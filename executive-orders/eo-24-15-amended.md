## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

     AMENDED EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-15

     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     LONE ROCK FIRE IN GILLIAM COUNTY AND BONEYARD FIRE IN
     GRANT COUNTY

     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

     The fire known as the “Lone Rock Fire” is burning in Gilliam, Morrow, and
     Wheeler counties and the “Boneyard Fire” is burning in Grant County.

     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Lone Rock and
     Boneyard Fires are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and
     structural fire protection was requested by the Gilliam and Morrow County Fire
     Defense Board and the Grant County Fire Defense Board and the State Fire
     Marshal concurs with that request.

     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Lone Rock Fire in Gilliam,
     Morrow, and Wheeler counties and the Boneyard Fire in Grant County and the
     threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting personnel and
     equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency Conflagration Act.

     These findings were made at 9:45 a.m. on July 15, 2024, with the Department of
     the State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management
     Team will be entering into unified command with Northwest Team Two. The
     Oregon State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management Team will be briefed at
     10:00 am on July 16, 2024, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

     1.     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding
            to the Lone Rock and Boneyard Fires may be redistributed by the State Fire
            Marshal.

     a      This conflagration is declared only for the Lone Rock and Boneyard Fires
            threatening structures in Gilliam, Grant, Morrow, and Wheeler counties.

     3:     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 9:45 a.m. on the 15" day of
            July 2024, and signed this 19" day of July 2024, in Salem, Oregon.
