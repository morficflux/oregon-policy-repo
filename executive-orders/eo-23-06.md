# Determination of a State of Winter Emergency in Multnomah County (Executive Order 23-06)

## At a glance

Executive Order 23-06: Determination of a State of Winter Emergency in Multnomah County. (Title as printed in the Governor's office listing of record; see Provenance.)

## Full text

Officeof theGovernor
Stateof Oregon

   EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23-06

   DETERMINATION OF A STATE OF WINTER EMERGENCY IN
   MULTNOMAH COUNTY.

   Today the Multnomah County Chair requested a State of Emergency be declared
   based on severe winter weather and below freezing temperatures. These winter
   conditions, including snow and ice are predicted to continue. The winter
   conditions pose a risk to the health and welfare of Oregonians, and it is necessary
   to act to minimize and reduce the risk of harm and loss of life.

   NOW, THEREFORE,IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

              A State of Emergency is declared fbr Multnomah County. All State
              agencies, including the Oregon Health Authority and the Oregon
              Department of Transportation are directed to coordinate and provide
              assistance while the Emergency Order remains in effect.

              The Oregon Department of Emergency Management is directed to
              coordinate and assist as needed with assessment and mitigation
              activities to address current and projected conditions in Multnomah
              County.

              All other state agencies are directed to coordinate with the above
              agencies and provide appropriate state resources as needed to assist
              Multnomah County.

              This Executive Order expires on March 4,2023.

      So Declared on February 25,2023 and signed, this 27tl'day of February, 2023.

                                                Tina Kotek
                                                GOVERNOR

                                                ATTEST:

                                                Shemia Fagan
                                                SECRETARY OF STATE
