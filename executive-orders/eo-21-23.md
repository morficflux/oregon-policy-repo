# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Grandview Fire in Jefferson County (Executive Order 21-23)

## At a glance

Executive Order 21-23: Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Grandview Fire in Jefferson County. (Title as printed in the Governor's office listing of record; see Provenance.)

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 21-23

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    GRANDVIEW FIRE IN JEFFERSON COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Grandview Fire” is burning in Jefferson County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Grandview Fire
    are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
    protection was requested by the Tri-County (Jefferson, Deschutes, and Crook) Fire
    Defense Board Chief and the State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Grandview Fire in Jefferson
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 9:16 am on July 12, 2021, with the Office of the State
    Fire Marshal, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order. The Office of
    State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management Team was briefed at 3:00 pm on July
    12, 2021, and entered into a unified command with an Oregon Department of
    Forestry Incident Management Team at 6:00 pm on July 12, 2021.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    ie     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to the Grandview Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
           Marshal.

    2.     This conflagration is declared only for the Grandview Fire threatening
           structures in Jefferson County and Deschutes County.

    Br     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 9:16 am on the 12th day of
           July 2021, and signed this 14" day of July 2021, in Salem, Oregon.

                                                                                           Contains
