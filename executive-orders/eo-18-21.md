# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Memaloose 2 Fire in Wasco County (Executive Order 18-21)

## At a glance

Executive Order 18-21: Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Memaloose 2 Fire in Wasco County. (Title as printed in the Governor's office listing of record; see Provenance.)

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-21

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    MEMALOOSE 2 FIRE IN WASCO COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Memaloose 2 Fire” is burning in Wasco County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Memaloose 2 Fire
    are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
    protection was requested by Chief Palmer, Wasco County Fire Defense Board
    Chief. The State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510 et seg., I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the Memaloose 2 Fire in Wasco
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 8:55 a.m. on August 11, 2018 and I now confirm them
    with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    Le     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to Memaloose 2 Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
           Marshal.

    2.     This emergency is declared only for the Memaloose 2 Fire threatening
           structures in Wasco County.

                                                                                         hi

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-21
    PAGE TWO

    3.   This order was made by verbal proclamation at 8:55 a.m. the 11th day of

         Done at Salem, Oregon, this Z29day of August, 2018.
