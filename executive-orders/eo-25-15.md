# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Cram Fire in Jefferson and Wasco Counties (Executive Order 25-15)

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25-15

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    CRAM FIRE IN JEFFERSON AND WASCO COUNTIES

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Cram Fire” is burning in Jefferson and Wasco Counties.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Cram Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by the Jefferson County Commissioners and the State Fire Marshal
    concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Cram Fire in Jefferson and
    Wasco Counties and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
    firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 2:23 pm on July 14, 2025, with the Department of the
    State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Green Incident Management
    Team will take command on July 14, 2025. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Green
    Incident Management Team will be briefed at 7 p.m. on July 14, 2025, and I now
    confirm them with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    Ih     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
           statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
           the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
           Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
           the Cram Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

    2      This conflagration is declared only for the Cram Fire threatening structures
           in Jefferson and Wasco counties.

    3h     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 2:23 pm on the 14th day of
           July 2025 and signed this 15" day of July 2025, in Salem, Oregon.

                                                                                              HE

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25-15
    PAGE TWO

    4,   This order is in effect as of this date and until which time the Department of
         the State Fire Marshal has demobilized all OSFM resources off this
         incident.

         Done at Salem, Oregon, this 15" day of July, 2025.

                                           O(a
                                       Tina Kotek
                                       GOVERNOR

                                                 bw Keel’
                                       Tobias Read
                                       SECRETARY OF STATE
