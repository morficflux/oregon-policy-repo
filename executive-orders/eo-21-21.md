# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Jack Fire in Douglas County (Executive Order 21-21)

## At a glance

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 21-21

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    JACK FIRE IN DOUGLAS COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Jack Fire” is burning in Douglas County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Jack Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by the Douglas County Fire Defense Board Chief and the State Fire
    Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Jack Fire in Douglas County
    and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting personnel
    and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 2:39 pm on July 6, 2021, with the Office of the State
    Fire Marshal, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order. The Office of the
    State Fire Marshal Blue Incident Management Team took command at 6:00 am on
    July 7.2021.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    ie     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to the Jack Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

    oe      This conflagration is declared only for the Jack Fire threatening structures
            in Douglas County.

    3       This order was made by verbal proclamation at 2:39 pm on the 6th day of
            July 2021, and signed this 7" day of July 2021, in Salem, Oregon.
