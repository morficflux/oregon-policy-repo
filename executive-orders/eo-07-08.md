# Conflagration Act - Harney County (Executive Order 07-08)

## At a glance

Executive Order 07-08: Conflagration Act - Harney County. (Title as printed in the Governor's office listing of record; see Provenance.)

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 07-08

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR EGLEY
    COMPLEX FIRES IN HARNEY COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor ofthe State of Oregon, | find that:

    Fires known as the “Egley Complex Fires”    are burning in Hamey County.

    ‘The resources necessary for protection oflife and property from the Egley Complex
    Fires are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
    protection was requested by the Hamey County Fire Defense Chief. The State Fire
    Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to fires known as the Egley Complex Fires in
    Hamey County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
    firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 11:12 p.m, on July 9, 2007 and I now confirm them
    with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    1.     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire.

    2.     This emergency is declared only for the Egley Complex Fires in Hamey
            County.

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 07-08
    PAGE TWO

    3.   This order was made by verbal proclamation at 11:12 p.m, the 9th day of
         July, 2007 and signed this 10th day of July, 2007, in Salem, Oregon.

                                             Vhedll
                                               Theodore R. Kulongoski
                                               GOVERNOR

                                               ATTE!

                                                ill Bradbury
                                               SECRETARY OF STATE.
