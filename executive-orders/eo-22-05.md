# Rescinding Executive Order 20-60 (Executive Order 22-05)

## At a glance

Executive Order 22-05: Rescinding Executive Order 20-60. (Title as printed in the Governor's office listing of record; see Provenance.)

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER 22-05

    RESCINDING EXECUTIVE ORDER 20-60

    On October 28, 2020, I adopted Executive Order 20-60, declaring an emergency to
    facilitate the critical response and recovery efforts in the aftermath of a series of
    devastating wildfires that began over the 2020 Labor Day holiday.

    Having consulted with individuals involved in the ongoing recovery efforts, | am
    satisfied that ongoing recovery efforts are at a point where they can continue
    without the need for an ongoing emergency declaration. Therefore, pursuant to my
    authority under ORS 401.204(1), I hereby proclaim and order that Executive Order
    20-60 is rescinded, and that the state of emergency declared therein is terminated.

    Done at Portland, Oregon, this 5 day of April, 2022.

                                           Kate Brown
