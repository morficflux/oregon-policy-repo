# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for Portions of the Smith River Complex in Josephine County (Executive Order 23-21)

## At a glance

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23-21

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR
    PORTIONS OF THE SMITH RIVER COMPLEX IN JOSEPHINE COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fires that threaten communities within the “Smith River Complex” are burning
    in Josephine County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from fires near
    communities within the Smith River Complex are beyond local capabilities.
    Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection was requested by the
    Josephine County Fire Defense Board Chief and the State Fire Marshal concurs
    with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fires within the Smith River Complex in
    Josephine County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
    firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 4:55 p.m. on August 28, 2023. The Oregon State Fire
    Marshal is mobilizing resources to the command of the local Fire Defense Board
    Chief for the fires near communties within the Smith River Complex. The Blue
    Incident Management Team will enter into unified command on the Smith River
    Complex. I now confirm these findings with this Executive Order.

    NOW, THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    ike    The Oregon Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources statewide and
           coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for the use of
           personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency Conflagration
           Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to the
           threatening communities within the Smith River Complex may be
           redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

    De     This conflagration is declared only for the fires within the Smith River
           Complex threatening structures in Josephine County.

                                                                                        Hie
