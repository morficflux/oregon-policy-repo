# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Golden Fire in Klamath County (Executive Order 23-16)

## At a glance

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23-16

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    GOLDEN FIRE IN KLAMATH COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Golden Fire” is burning in Klamath County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Golden Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by the Klamath County Fire Defense Board Chief, and the State Fire
    Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Golden Fire in Klamath
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 6:32 p.m. on July 22, 2023. The Oregon State Fire
    Marshal is mobilizing resources and the Blue Incident Mnaagement Team to the
    command of the local Fire Defense Board Chief for the Golden Fire. I now confirm
    these findings with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    le     The Oregon Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources statewide and
           coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for the use of
           personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency Conflagration
           Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to the Golden
           Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

    2      This conflagration is declared only for the Golden Fire threatening
           structures in Klamath County.

    3      This order was made by verbal proclamation at 6:32 p.m. on July 22, 2023,
           and signed on July 27, 2023, in Salem, Oregon.

                                                                                              HI

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23-16
    PAGE TWO

    4,   This order is in effect as of this date and until which time the Oregon State
         Fire Marshal has demobilized all OSFM resources off this incident.

         Done at Salem, Oregon, this 27th day of July 2023.

                                        Tina Kotek
                                        GOVERNOR
