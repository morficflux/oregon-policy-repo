# Extending Executive Order 21-02 for Clackamas, Marion, Multnomah, Polk, and Yamhill Counties (Executive Order 21-04)

## At a glance

Executive Order 21-04: Extending Executive Order 21-02 for Clackamas, Marion, Multnomah, Polk, and Yamhill Counties. (Title as printed in the Governor's office listing of record; see Provenance.)

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER 21-04

    EXTENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER 21-02 FOR CLACKAMAS, MARION,
    MULTNOMAH, POLK, AND YAMHILL COUNTIES

    On February 13, 2021, in Executive Order 21-02, I declared a state of emergency
    for Benton, Clackamas, Hood River, Linn, Marion, Multnomah, Polk, Washington,
    and Yamhill Counties, due to a severe winter storm that included heavy snow and
    ice accumulation, high winds, flooding, and landslides. That emergency was
    declared for seven days, through February 20, 2021.

    Following consultation with the Office of Emergency Management regarding the
    situation on the ground, I am extending the duration of the state of emergency for
    an additional seven days, through February 27, 2021, for Clackamas, Marion,
    Multnomah, Polk, and Yamhill Counties. Accordingly, paragraph three of
    Executive Order 21-02 is amended to provide as follows:

       3. This emergency shall extend through February 20, 2021, for Benton, Hood
          River, Linn, and Washington Counties. The emergency shall extend
          through February 27, 2021, for Clackamas, Marion, Multnomah, Polk, and
           Yamhill Counties, unless terminated earlier by the Governor.

       Done at Salem, Oregon, this 19" day of February, 2021.

                                                  —
                                           Kite Leo
                                         Kate Brown
                                         GOVERNOR

                                         Shemia Fagan
                                         SECRETARY OF STATE

                                                                                         Recycled
