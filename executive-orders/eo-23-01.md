# Determination of State of Emergency in Clackamas, Coos, Curry, Douglas, Hood River, Jackson, Josephine, Lane, Lincoln, Multnomah, Washington, and Umatilla counties due to a severe winter storm (Executive Order 23-01)

## Full text

Officeof theGovernor
Stateof Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23.01

   DETERMINATION OF STATE OF EMERGENCY IN CLACKAMAS,
   COOS, CURRY, DOUGLAS, HOOD RIVER, JACKSON, JOSEPHINE,
   LANE, LINCOLN, MULTNOMAH, WASHINGTON, AND UMATILLA
   COUNTIES DUE TO A SEVERE WINTER STORM THAT INCLUDES
   HIGH WINDS, HEAVY RAIN,ICE ACCUMULATION, FLOODING, AND
   LANDSLIDES

   Pursuant to ORS 401 .165, I find that a threat to life, safety, property, and
   significant damage to the state roads on the federal-aid highway system was caused
   by severe winter storms and have caused disaster conditions in Clackamas, Coos,
   Curry, Douglas, Hood River, Jackson, Josephine, Lane, Lincoln, Multnomah,
   Washington, and Umatilla counties. Beginning December 22,2022, and continuing
   through January 6,2023, these severe storms resulted in heavy rain, high winds,
   flooding, ice accumulation, landslides, and erosion at various locations within these
   counties, leading to critical transportation failures and disruption to energy and
   communications infrastructure. This storm system damaged state highways
   throughout these counties with scour, washouts, debris flows and mudslides.

   IT IS ORDERED AND DIRECTED:

       l.   Pursuant to ORS 401.165, and based on the facts described above, an
            emergency has occurred, and a state of emergency is declared for the above-
            listed counties from December 22,2022 through January 6,2023.

      2.    The Oregon Department of Transportation shall provide appropriate
            assistance and seek federal resources to effect repair and reconstruction of
            $2 million dollars or more in damages and impact to the federal aid
            highway system in the above-listed counties.

      J.    This declaration is limited to the above listed counties. It is limited to the
            provision of assistance by the Oregon Department of Transportation to
            repair and reconstruct eligible roadways that are part of the federal highway
