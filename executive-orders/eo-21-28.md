# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Middle Fork Complex in Lane County (Executive Order 21-28)

## At a glance

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 21-28

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    MIDDLE FORK COMPLEX IN LANE COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Middle Fork Complex” is burning in Lane County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Middle Fork
    Complex are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural
    fire protection was requested by the Lane County Fire Defense Board Chief and the
    State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Middle Fork Complex in
    Lane County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 4:35 pm on August 9, 2021, with the Office of the
    State Fire Marshal, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order. The Office
    of the State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management Team has entered into unified
    command with Pacific Northwest Team 3. The Office of State Fire Marshal Red
    Incident Management Team was briefed at 9:00 am on August 10, 2021

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    iP     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to the Middle Fork Complex may be redistributed by the State
           Fire Marshal.

   Za      This conflagration is declared only for the Middle Fork Complex
           threatening structures in Lane County.

    3      This order was made by verbal proclamation at 4:35 pm on the 9th day of
           August 2021, and signed this 10" day of August 2021, in Salem, Oregon.
