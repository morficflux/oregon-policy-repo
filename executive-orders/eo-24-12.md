# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Cow Valley Fire in Malheur County (Executive Order 24-12)

## At a glance

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-12

     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     COW VALLEY FIRE IN MALHEUR COUNTY

     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

     The fire known as the “Cow Valley Fire” is burning in Malheur County.

     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Cow Valley Fire
     are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
     protection was requested by the Malheur Fire Defense Board and the State Fire
     Marshal concurs with that request.

     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, J have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Cow Valley Fire in Malheur
     County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
     personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.

     These findings were made at 8:34 am on July 12, 2024, with the Department ofthe
     State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management Team
     will be entering into unified command with Northwest Team 6. The Oregon State
     Fire Marshal Red Incident Management Team will be briefed at 11:00 am on July
     13, 2024, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

     Ir     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding
            to the Cow Valley Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

     2,     This conflagration is declared only for the Cow Valley Fire threatening
            structures in Malheur County.

     3%     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 8:34 am on the 12th day of
            July 2024, and signed this 12" day of July 2024, in Salem, Oregon.

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-12
     PAGE TWO

     4,   This order is in effect as of this date and until which time the Department of
          the State Fire Marshal has demobilized all OSFM resources off this
          incident.

          Done at Salem, Oregon, this 12" day of July, 2024.

                                        Tina Kotek
                                        GOVERNOR

                                        ALLEEST:

                                        LaVonne Griffin-Valade
                                        SECRETARY OF STATE

                                                                                           LE
