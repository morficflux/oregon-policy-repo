# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for Portions of the Tyee Ridge Complex in Douglas County (Executive Order 23-19)

## At a glance

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

   EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23-19

   INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR
   PORTIONS OF THE TYEE RIDGE COMPLEX IN DOUGLAS COUNTY

   Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

   The fires that threaten communities within the “Tyee Ridge Complex” are burning
   in Douglas County.

   The resources necessary for protecting life and property from fires near
   communities within the Tyee Ridge Complex are beyond local capabilities.
   Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection was requested by the
   Douglas County Fire Defense Board Chief and the State Fire Marshal concurs with
   that request.

   In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
   safety, and property exists due to the fires within the Tyee Ridge Complex
   threatening homes and property in Douglas County and the threat exceeds the
   firefighting capabilities of local firefighting personnel and equipment.
   Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency Conflagration Act.

   These findings were made at 7:28 p.m. on August 25, 2023. The Oregon State Fire
   Marshal is mobilizing resources to the command of the local Fire Defense Board
   Chief for the fires near communities within the Tyee Ridge Complex. The Red
   Incident Management Team will enter into unified command on the morning of
   August 26, 2023 for fires threatening the built environment within the Tyee Ridge
   Complex. I now confirm these findings with this Executive Order.

   NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    IL;    The Oregon Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources statewide and
           coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for the use of
           personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency Conflagration
           Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to the fires
           threatening communities within the Tyee Ridge Complex may be
           redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

   De      This conflagration is declared only for the fires within the Tyee Ridge
           Complex threatening structures in Douglas County.
