# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Hat Rock and Mt. Hebron Fires in Umatilla County (Executive Order 23-14)

## At a glance

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

   EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23-14

   INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
   HAT ROCK AND MT. HEBRON FIRES IN UMATILLA COUNTY

   Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

   The fires known as the “Hat Rock and Mt. Hebron Fires” are burning in Umatilla
   County.

   The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Hat Rock & Mt
   Adam Fires are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and
   structural fire protection was requested by the Umatilla County Fire Defense Board
   Chief, and the State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

   In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
   safety, and property exists due to the fires known as the Hat Rock and Mt. Hebron
   Fires in Umatilla County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
   firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
   Conflagration Act.

   These findings were made at 4:51 p.m. on June 13, 2023. The Oregon State Fire
   Marshal is mobilizing resources to the command of the local Fire Defense Board
   Chief for the Hat Rock and Mt. Hebron Fires. I now confirm these findings with
   this Executive Order.

   NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

   Il,    The Oregon Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources statewide and
          coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for the use of
          personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency Conflagration
          Act to suppress and contain these fires. Resources responding to the Hat
          Rock and Mt. Hebron Fires may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

   2,     This conflagration is declared only for the Hat Rock and Mt. Hebron Fires
          threatening structures in Umatilla County.

   3.     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 4:51 p.m. on June 13, 2023,
          and signed on June 20, 2023, in Salem, Oregon.

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

   EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23-14
   PAGE TWO

   Al,   This order is in effect as of this date and until which time the Office of State
         Fire Marshal has demobilized all OSFM resources off this incident.

         Done at Salem, Oregon, this 20th day of June 2023.
