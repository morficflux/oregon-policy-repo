# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Rail Ridge Fire in Crook and Grant Counties (Executive Order 24-24)

## At a glance

Executive Order 24-24: Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Rail Ridge Fire in Crook and Grant Counties. (Title as printed in the Governor's office listing of record; see Provenance.)

## Full text

    Office of the Governor
    State of Oregon

         EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-24

         INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
         RAIL RIDGE FIRE IN CROOK AND GRANT COUNTIES

         Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, | find that:

         The fire known as the “Rail Ridge Fire” is burning in Crook and Grant counties.

         The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Rail Ridge Fire
         are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
         protection was requested by the Grant County Fire Defense Board and the State
{        Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

         In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
         safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Rail Ridge Fire in Crook
         and Grant counties and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
         firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the Emergency
         Conflagration Act.

         These findings were made at 10:47 am on September 6, 2024, with the Department
         ofthe State Fire Marshal. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Blue Incident
         Management Team will be entering into unified command with the California
         Team 10. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Blue Incident Management Team will be
         briefed at 5:30 pm on September 6, 2024, and I now confirm them with this
         Executive Order.

         NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

         Ik     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
                statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
                the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
                Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
                the Rail Ridge Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

         De     This conflagration is declared only for the Rail Ridge Fire threatening
                structures in Crook and Grant counties.

         3s     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 10:47 am on the 6" day of
                September 2024 and signed this 7" day of September 2024, in Salem,
                Oregon.
