# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Service Fire in Wheeler (Executive Order 24-27)

## At a glance

Executive Order 24-27: Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Service Fire in Wheeler. (Title as printed in the Governor's office listing of record; see Provenance.)

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-27

     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     SERVICE FIRE IN WHEELER COUNTY

     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, | find that:

     The fire known as the “Service Fire” is burning in Wheeler County.

     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Service Fire are
     beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
     was requested by the Wheeler County Fire Defense Board and the State Fire
     Marshal concurs with that request.

     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Service Fire in Wheeler
     County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
     personnel and equipment. Accordingly, ] have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.

     These findings were made at 9:15 pm on September 9, 2024, with the Department
     of the State Fire Marshal. The agency will be coordinating structural protection
     resources, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

     Ik     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding
            to the Service Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

     2.     This conflagration is declared only for the Service Fire threatening
            structures in Wheeler County.

     3;     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 9:15 pm on the 9"" day of
            September 2024 and signed this 10" day of September 2024, in Salem,
            Oregon.

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-27
     PAGE TWO

     4,   This order is in effect as of this date and until which time the Department of
          the State Fire Marshal has demobilized all OSFM resources off this
          incident.

          Done at Salem, Oregon, this 10" day of September, 2024.

                                          CLA
                                        Tina Kotek
                                        GOVERNOR

                                        ATTEST:

                                      : LaVonne Griffin-Valade
                                        SECRETARY OF STATE

                                                                                           HR
