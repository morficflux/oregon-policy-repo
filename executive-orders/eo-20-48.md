# Invocation of Emergency Conflagration Act for the Brattain Fire in Lake County (Executive Order 20-48)

## At a glance

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-48

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    BRATTAIN FIRE IN LAKE COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Brattain Fire” is burning in Lake County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Brattain Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by the Lake County Fire Defense Board and the State Fire Marshal
    concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the Brattain Fire in Lake County
    and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting personnel
    and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 3:25 pm on September 14, 2020 with Office of State
    Fire Marshal and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    ile    The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to the Brattain Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
           Marshal.

    ID,    This conflagration is declared only for the Brattain Fire threatening
           structures in Lake County.

    3.     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 3:25 pm the 14th day of
           September 2020 and signed this 15" day of September, 2020, in Salem,
           Oregon.
