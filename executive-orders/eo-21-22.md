# Invocation of the Emergency Conflagration Act for the Bootleg Fire in Klamath County. (Executive Order 21-22)

## Full text

Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 21-22

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    BOOTLEG FIRE IN KLAMATH COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Bootleg Fire” is burning in Klamath County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Bootleg Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by the Klamath County Fire Defense Board Chief and the State Fire
    Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Bootleg Fire in Klamath
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.

    These findings were made at 11:16 am on July 7, 2021, with the Office of the State
    Fire Marshal, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order. The Office of the
    State Fire Marshal Green Incident Management Team entered into a unified
    command with a Pacific Northwest Interagency Team at 7:00 pm on July 7, 2021.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    i      The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use ofpersonnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to the Bootleg Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
           Marshal.

    2       This conflagration is declared only for the Bootleg Fire threatening
            structures in Klamath County.

    oS)    This order was made by verbal proclamation at 11:16 am on the 7th day of
           July 2021, and signed this 9" day of July 2021, in Salem, Oregon.
