# Disclaimer

## Non-authoritative status

**Nothing in this repository is the official text of Oregon law, rule, or policy.**

This repository contains curated copies, excerpts, and summaries prepared for use by AI
agents and humans as a navigational reference. Errors, omissions, staleness, and formatting
differences from the official sources are possible at any time. Do not rely on this
repository for compliance, legal, or operational decisions — always consult the
authoritative source linked in each document's frontmatter (`source_url`) and body.

Oregon's own publication systems carry equivalent warnings. The Oregon Legislature's online
ORS database states that the official text is the printed published copy of the Oregon
Revised Statutes and that users "should verify the legal accuracy of all legal text against
official sources of Oregon law." This repository inherits that limitation and adds its own:
it is maintained by volunteers with AI assistance, on a best-effort cadence.

## Copyright posture

- **Statutes and administrative rules are not copyrightable.** Under the federal government
  edicts doctrine (U.S. Copyright Office Compendium § 313.6(C)(2); *Georgia v.
  Public.Resource.Org, Inc.*, 590 U.S. 255 (2020)), edicts of government are not subject to
  copyright. ORS and OAR text falls squarely within this.
- **Oregon does not assert ORS copyright.** On June 19, 2008 the Legislative Counsel
  Committee resolved unanimously that it "will not assert copyright on the Oregon Revised
  Statutes."
- **DAS statewide policy PDFs** carry no copyright notice or republication restriction; they
  are public records. This repository excerpts and summarizes them for an informational,
  non-authoritative purpose.
- **Third-party standards are different.** Copyrighted third-party works (e.g., ISO
  standards, CIS Controls) are never reproduced here — only summarized with a link to the
  official source. See `external-references/`.

This analysis is a good-faith reading of well-supported public authority, **not legal
advice**.
