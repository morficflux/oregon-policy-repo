# Issuance of bonds (ORS 267.345)

## At a glance

ORS 267.345 — Issuance of bonds. Chapter 267 (Chapter 267), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

267.345 Issuance of bonds. All general obligation and revenue bonds, including refunding bonds, issued under ORS 267.330 to 267.345 shall be issued as prescribed in ORS chapter 287A. [1969 c.643 §31; 2007 c.783 §84]
