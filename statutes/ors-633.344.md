# Label guarantees of additional plant nutrients (ORS 633.344)

## At a glance

ORS 633.344 — Label guarantees of additional plant nutrients. Chapter 633 (Chapter 633), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

633.344 Label guarantees of additional plant nutrients. In addition to the guarantees of plant nutrients required by ORS 633.321, label guarantees of other plant nutrients may be made from a list approved by the State Department of Agriculture.
