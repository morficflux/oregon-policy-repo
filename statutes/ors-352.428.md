# Sale to State of Oregon or United States Government (ORS 352.428)

## At a glance

ORS 352.428 — Sale to State of Oregon or United States Government. Chapter 352 (Chapter 352), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

352.428 Sale to State of Oregon or United States Government. The bonds may be sold to the State of Oregon or to the United States Government without advertisement thereof for public sale. [Formerly 351.440] 352.430 [1979 c.782 §3; repealed by 1987 c.246 §8]
