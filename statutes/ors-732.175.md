## Full text

732.175 Liability for acting as corporate insurer without authority. Any person who assumes to act as a corporate insurer without a permit to organize and without the issuance of a certificate of incorporation by the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services or who assumes to transact insurance business without a certificate of authority to transact such business issued by the director shall be jointly and severally liable for all liabilities created while so acting.
