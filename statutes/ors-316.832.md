## At a glance

## Full text

316.832 Travel expenses for loggers.

(1) In addition to the modifications to federal taxable income contained in this chapter, there shall be subtracted from federal taxable income traveling expenses incurred by a logger in job-related travel.

(2) The modification to federal taxable income by subsection

(1) of this section shall be substantiated by any proof required by the Department of Revenue by rule. The requirement for substantiation may be waived partially, conditionally or absolutely, as provided under ORS 315.063.
