## At a glance

## Full text

258.171 Full recount required to change results; exception for recount demand made by county clerk.

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, the person making a demand for a recount shall be bound by the original official returns unless the person demands a full recount.

(2) If a demand for a partial recount is made by a county clerk under ORS 258.161 (3), votes recounted in the precincts specified by the county clerk may be combined with votes in other precincts that were not recounted to determine the official returns of the election.
