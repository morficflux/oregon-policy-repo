## Full text

251.014 Electronic filing system; rules.

(1) The Secretary of State by rule shall adopt an electronic filing system to allow estimates, portraits, statements or arguments to be filed electronically using the Internet.

(2) The Secretary of State may adopt rules to administer this section. [2011 c.646 §9] Note: 251.014 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 251 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 251.015 [1957 c.217 §1; 1979 c.190 §314; renumbered 258.006]
