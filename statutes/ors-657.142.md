## At a glance

## Full text

657.142 Inclusion of split week in calendar quarter; rules. The Director of the Employment Department may prescribe by rule that a week shall be in, within or during the calendar quarter that includes the greater part of the week. [2021 c.43 §3] BENEFITS AND CLAIMS (Income Tax Matters)
