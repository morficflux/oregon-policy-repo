# Events or activities conducted by winery in exclusive farm use zone or mixed farm and forest zone (ORS 215.237)

## At a glance

ORS 215.237 — Events or activities conducted by winery in exclusive farm use zone or mixed farm and forest zone. Chapter 215 (Chapter 215), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

215.237 Events or activities conducted by winery in exclusive farm use zone or mixed farm and forest zone. If a winery sited on land zoned for exclusive farm use or mixed farm and forest use under ORS 215.452 conducts agri-tourism or other commercial events authorized in ORS 215.452 (5), the winery may not conduct agri-tourism or other commercial events or activities authorized by ORS 215.213

(11) or 215.283 (4). [2011 c.567 §3; 2013 c.554 §4] Note: 215.237 to 215.239 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 215 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
