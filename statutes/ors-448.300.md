# City ordinance authority (ORS 448.300)

## At a glance

ORS 448.300 — City ordinance authority. Chapter 448 (Chapter 448), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

448.300 City ordinance authority. Cities may prescribe by ordinance what acts constitute offenses against the purity of the water supply and the punishment or penalties therefor and may enforce those ordinances within their corporate limits and on property described in ORS 448.295. [Formerly 449.310]
