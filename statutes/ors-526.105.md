# Disposition of receipts under ORS (ORS 526.105)

## At a glance

ORS 526.105 — Disposition of receipts under ORS. Chapter 526 (Forestry Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

526.105 Disposition of receipts under ORS 526.090 and 526.095. All moneys received pursuant to the Acts of Congress mentioned in ORS 526.090 and 526.095 shall be paid into the State Treasury, credited to the State Forestry Department Account, and used exclusively for the purposes of the respective Acts. [1965 c.253 §17] 526.110 [Repealed by 1953 c.327 §6]
