# Required declaration of vacancy of public member office (ORS 571.412)

## At a glance

ORS 571.412 — Required declaration of vacancy of public member office. Chapter 571 (Chapter 571), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

571.412 Required declaration of vacancy of public member office. The Director of Agriculture shall immediately declare the office of a member of the Oregon Hemp Commission who is a member of the public vacant if the director finds that the member has become a producer or processor. [2021 c.216 §6a] Note: See note under 571.400.
