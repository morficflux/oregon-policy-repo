# Rules (ORS 197.728)

## At a glance

ORS 197.728 — Rules. Chapter 197 (Chapter 197), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

197.728 Rules. The Land Conservation and Development Commission shall administer regionally significant industrial areas and may adopt rules as necessary to implement ORS 197.722 to 197.728. [2011 c.564 §11] Note: See note under 197.722.
