# Rewards for information (Collections and Refunds) (ORS 323.255)

## At a glance

ORS 323.255 — Rewards for information (Collections and Refunds). Chapter 323 (Chapter 323), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

323.255 Rewards for information. The Department of Revenue may pay rewards to persons, other than officers or employees of the department, furnishing information that leads to the recovery of tax from other persons guilty of violating the provisions of ORS 323.005 to 323.482. Such rewards shall not exceed 10 percent of the net amount of tax, penalty and interest recovered by suit or otherwise and shall be paid only in cases where such evasions of tax would not be disclosed by the audit of reports or from other information available to the department. [1965 c.525 §64] (Collections and Refunds)
