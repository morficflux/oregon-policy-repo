# Single act sufficient to constitute professional real estate activity (ORS 696.040)

## At a glance

ORS 696.040 — Single act sufficient to constitute professional real estate activity. Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.040 Single act sufficient to constitute professional real estate activity. One act or transaction of professional real estate activity is sufficient to constitute engaging in professional real estate activity, within the meaning of this chapter.
