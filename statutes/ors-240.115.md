## Full text

240.115 Action to secure compliance with chapter. The Employment Relations Board may maintain such action or proceeding at law or in equity as it considers necessary or appropriate to secure compliance with this chapter and its rules and orders thereunder. 240.120 [Amended by 1969 c.80 §39a; repealed by 1973 c.794 §34]
