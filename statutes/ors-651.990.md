# Penalties GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 651.990)

## At a glance

ORS 651.990 — Penalties GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 651 (Bureau of Labor and Industries), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

651.990 Penalties.

(1) Willful neglect or refusal by any person subpoenaed under ORS 651.060 to attend or testify at the time and place named in the subpoena is a Class C misdemeanor.

(2) Violation of ORS 651.120

(2) is a Class C misdemeanor. [Amended by 1983 c.740 §240; 2011 c.597 §262] _______________
