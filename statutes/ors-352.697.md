# Graduate School of Social Work (ORS 352.697)

## At a glance

ORS 352.697 — Graduate School of Social Work. Chapter 352 (Chapter 352), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

352.697 Graduate School of Social Work.

(1) Pursuant to ORS 352.372, the Graduate School of Social Work is created, to be administered by Portland State University.

(2) The purpose of the Graduate School of Social Work is to provide a center for specialized education and research in the social services for the betterment of communities in the Portland metropolitan area, the state and the Pacific Northwest. [Formerly 352.213]
