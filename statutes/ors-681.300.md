# Waiver of examination requirement (ORS 681.300)

## At a glance

ORS 681.300 — Waiver of examination requirement. Chapter 681 (Chapter 681), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

681.300 Waiver of examination requirement. The State Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology may waive the examination described in ORS 681.290 and grant a license to:

(1) An applicant who holds the Certificate of Clinical Competence of the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association in the area for which the person is applying for a license.

(2) An audiology applicant who is certified by the American Board of Audiology. [Formerly 694.385; 1985 c.754 §1; 1995 c.280 §11; 2005 c.698 §9; 2011 c.133 §6] 681.310 [Formerly 694.395; repealed by 1995 c.280 §37]
