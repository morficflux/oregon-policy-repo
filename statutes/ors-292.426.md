# Tax court judge (ORS 292.426)

## At a glance

ORS 292.426 — Tax court judge. Chapter 292 (Salaries and Expenses of State Officers and Employees), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

292.426 Tax court judge.

(1) The annual salary of the judge of the Oregon Tax Court shall be $146,048 for the period beginning on January 1, 2019, and ending on June 30, 2020.

(2) On July 1, 2020, the annual salary of the judge of the Oregon Tax Court, as adjusted under ORS 292.428, shall be increased by $5,000.

(3) On July 1, 2025, the annual salary of the judge of the Oregon Tax Court shall be $209,532.

(4) On July 1, 2027, the annual salary of the judge of the Oregon Tax Court shall be 110 percent of the salary specified in subsection

(3) of this section, as adjusted under ORS 292.428. [2009 c.899 §4; 2013 c.722 §§44,44a; 2016 c.118 §14; 2018 c.98 §22; 2019 c.643 §18; 2025 c.613 §4] Note: See note under 292.311.
