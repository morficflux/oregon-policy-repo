# Local plastic straw laws (ORS 616.894)

## At a glance

ORS 616.894 — Local plastic straw laws. Chapter 616 (General and Miscellaneous Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

616.894 Local plastic straw laws. A local government or municipality may not, after June 13, 2019, enact an ordinance, resolution, regulation, rule or other law with requirements that differ from the provisions set forth in ORS 616.892. [2019 c.362 §2] Note: See note under 616.892. 616.900 [1989 c.1025 §8; 1991 c.734 §55; 2001 c.320 §9; repealed by 2001 c.320 §10]
