# Applicability of certain statutes to public improvements under lease purchase agreements (ORS 276.071)

## At a glance

ORS 276.071 — Applicability of certain statutes to public improvements under lease purchase agreements. Chapter 276 (Public Facilities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

276.071 Applicability of certain statutes to public improvements under lease purchase agreements. ORS 276.073 to 276.090, 279A.005 to 279A.030, 279A.050 to 279A.075, 279A.100, 279A.105, 279A.110, 279A.120, 279A.125, 653.268 and 653.269 and ORS chapter 279C, except ORS 279C.600 to 279C.625 apply to all public improvements that are being constructed, reconstructed or renovated for use by a state agency under a lease-purchase agreement or under any other agreement whereby ultimate state ownership is contemplated or expected. [1993 c.476 §2; 2003 c.794 §225] Note: 276.071 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 276 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
