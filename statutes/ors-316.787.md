# Payments to Japanese and Aleuts under Civil Liberties Act of 1988 (ORS 316.787)

## At a glance

ORS 316.787 — Payments to Japanese and Aleuts under Civil Liberties Act of 1988. Chapter 316 (Chapter 316), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

316.787 Payments to Japanese and Aleuts under Civil Liberties Act of 1988. Amounts paid to an eligible individual (persons of Japanese ancestry and Aleut civilian residents of the Pribilof Islands and the Aleutian Islands) under section 1989b-4, Title I, or 1989c-5, Title II, of the Civil Liberties Act of 1988 (P.L. 100-383) shall be treated for purposes of this chapter as damages for human suffering and shall be exempt from the taxes imposed under this chapter.
