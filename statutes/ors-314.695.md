# Application of ORS (ORS 314.695)

## At a glance

ORS 314.695 — Application of ORS. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.695 Application of ORS 314.280 and 314.605 to 314.675. The provisions of ORS 314.280 and 314.605 to 314.675 apply to the allocation and apportionment of the income of corporations and nonresident individuals, and do not apply to the income of resident individuals, resident estates, and resident trusts taxable as provided in the Personal Income Tax Act of 1969. [1967 c.60 §2; 1969 c.493 §90] (Controlled Foreign Corporations)
