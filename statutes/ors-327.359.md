# Small School District Supplement Fund (Public Charter School Grants) (ORS 327.359)

## At a glance

ORS 327.359 — Small School District Supplement Fund (Public Charter School Grants). Chapter 327 (Chapter 327), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

327.359 Small School District Supplement Fund.

(1) There is established the Small School District Supplement Fund, separate and distinct from the General Fund.

(2) The moneys in the Small School District Supplement Fund are appropriated continuously to the Department of Education for purposes of the grant program created by ORS 327.358. [2013 c.735 §3] Note: See note under 327.356. 327.360 [2005 c.803 §3; repealed by 2007 c.846 §18] (Public Charter School Grants)
