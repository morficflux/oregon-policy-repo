# Peace officers to assist the department VACCINES AND TREATMENTS (ORS 596.060)

## At a glance

ORS 596.060 — Peace officers to assist the department VACCINES AND TREATMENTS. Chapter 596 (Disease Control Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

596.060 Peace officers to assist the department. The State Department of Agriculture or any of its officers, employees or deputies may at any time call upon any peace officer for assistance in the discharge of duties, and such peace officer shall give the assistance requested. VACCINES AND TREATMENTS
