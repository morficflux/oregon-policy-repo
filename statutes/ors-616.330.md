# Alcoholic beverage exemption (ORS 616.330)

## At a glance

ORS 616.330 — Alcoholic beverage exemption. Chapter 616 (General and Miscellaneous Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

616.330 Alcoholic beverage exemption. ORS 616.205 to 616.215, 616.225 to 616.256, 616.286, 616.295, 616.310, 616.315, 616.325, 616.341, 616.350 to 616.366, 616.790, 616.992, rules adopted by the Oregon Health Authority under ORS 616.077

(1) and this section do not apply to alcoholic beverages. [1973 c.227 §25a; 1985 c.519 §4; 2009 c.595 §995] Note: 616.330 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 616 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
