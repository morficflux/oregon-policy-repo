# Processors required to pay for commodities within 30 days after delivery; interest on late payments (ORS 576.705)

## At a glance

ORS 576.705 — Processors required to pay for commodities within 30 days after delivery; interest on late payments. Chapter 576 (Agricultural Marketing Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

576.705 Processors required to pay for commodities within 30 days after delivery; interest on late payments. Notwithstanding any other provision of law:

(1) In the absence of a contract providing otherwise, any processor who purchases a commodity from a producer shall make full payment therefor not later than the 30th day after the day the processor takes delivery of the harvested commodity.

(2) Any processor who fails to make payment as required by subsection

(1) of this section shall pay, in addition to the amount due, interest thereon at the rate of one percent per month. [1971 c.531 §2]
