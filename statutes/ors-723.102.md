# Rulemaking authority (ORS 723.102)

## At a glance

ORS 723.102 — Rulemaking authority. Chapter 723 (Chapter 723), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

723.102 Rulemaking authority. In accordance with ORS chapter 183, the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may adopt rules for the purpose of carrying out this chapter. [1975 c.652 §11; 1985 c.762 §90; 1991 c.331 §123; 1991 c.635 §4; 1999 c.185 §6] 723.104 [1977 c.135 §43; repealed by 1985 c.762 §196]
