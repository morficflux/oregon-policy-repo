# Emergency response plan (ORS 466.620)

## At a glance

ORS 466.620 — Emergency response plan. Chapter 466 (Chapter 466), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

466.620 Emergency response plan. In accordance with the applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Environmental Quality Commission shall adopt an oil and hazardous material emergency response master plan consistent with the plan adopted by the Department of Transportation under ORS 453.825 and 453.835, and after consultation with the Department of the State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Police, the Oregon Fire Chiefs Association and any other appropriate agency or organization. [1985 c.733 §4; 1989 c.833 §92; 2005 c.825 §16; 2021 c.539 §119]
