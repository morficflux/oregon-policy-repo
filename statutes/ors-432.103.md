# Full faith and credit (ORS 432.103)

## At a glance

ORS 432.103 — Full faith and credit. Chapter 432 (Chapter 432), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

432.103 Full faith and credit. A determination of paternity or parentage by another state is entitled to full faith and credit. [Formerly 432.289; 2017 c.651 §47] 432.105 [Amended by 1973 c.829 §35; 1983 c.709 §32; 1997 c.783 §11; repealed by 2013 c.366 §88]
