## Full text

316.859 Addition for amount deducted as qualified business income from pass-through entity. There shall be added to federal taxable income for Oregon tax purposes the amount allowable as a deduction under section 199A(a) of the Internal Revenue Code for the tax year. [2018 c.108 §10] 316.860 [1953 c.304 §109; repealed by 1969 c.493 §99]
