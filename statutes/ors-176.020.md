# Cessation of term (ORS 176.020)

## At a glance

ORS 176.020 — Cessation of term. Chapter 176 (Governor), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

176.020 Cessation of term. The term of office of the Governor ceases when the successor of the Governor, having been declared elected by the Legislative Assembly as provided in the Constitution, is inaugurated by taking the oath of office. 176.030 [Renumbered 176.510] 176.040 [1959 c.672 §1; 1969 c.391 §13; repealed by 2009 c.77 §6] 176.050 [1959 c.672 §2; 2005 c.22 §123; 2007 c.70 §40; repealed by 2009 c.77 §6]
