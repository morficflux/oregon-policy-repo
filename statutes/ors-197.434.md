# Traffic impacts of speedway destination DESTINATION RESORTS (ORS 197.434)

## At a glance

ORS 197.434 — Traffic impacts of speedway destination DESTINATION RESORTS. Chapter 197 (Chapter 197), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

197.434 Traffic impacts of speedway destination.

(1) The private developer of the speedway destination site is financially responsible for addressing, through traffic infrastructure improvements and upgrades, adverse traffic impacts that cannot be adequately mitigated, in the judgment of road authorities, through the use of temporary traffic management measures.

(2) The private developer, or the organizer of a specific event or activity at the speedway destination site, is financially responsible for temporary traffic management measures required to mitigate the adverse traffic impacts of events or activities at the speedway destination site.

(3) Notwithstanding subsections

(1) and

(2) of this section, transportation infrastructure projects required by the establishment and use of the major motor speedway may receive funding from any source of moneys for transportation infrastructure projects. [2005 c.842 §3] Note: See note under 197.431. DESTINATION RESORTS
