# Authority of Governor during state of public health emergency (ORS 433.446)

## At a glance

ORS 433.446 — Authority of Governor during state of public health emergency. Chapter 433 (Chapter 433), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

433.446 Authority of Governor during state of public health emergency. The Governor may seek assistance under the Emergency Management Assistance Compact during a state of public health emergency to obtain additional resources for providing services directly related to mitigation of the crisis. [2003 c.555 §4; 2007 c.445 §25]
