# Considerations for rules regarding certain rural hospitals (ORS 656.256)

## At a glance

ORS 656.256 — Considerations for rules regarding certain rural hospitals. Chapter 656 (Chapter 656), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

656.256 Considerations for rules regarding certain rural hospitals. Whenever the Workers’ Compensation Division of the Department of Consumer and Business Services adopts any rule affecting a type A or B rural hospital, the division shall take into consideration the risk assessment formula set forth in ORS 442.520 (2). [1991 c.947 §19]
