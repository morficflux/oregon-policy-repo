# Hospital to maintain records (ORS 441.780)

## At a glance

ORS 441.780 — Hospital to maintain records. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.780 Hospital to maintain records. A hospital shall keep and maintain records necessary to demonstrate compliance with ORS 441.761 to 441.795. A hospital must provide records kept and maintained under this section to the Oregon Health Authority upon request. [Formerly 441.173] Note: See note under 441.761.
