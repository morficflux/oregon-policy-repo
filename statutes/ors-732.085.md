# Incorporators (ORS 732.085)

## At a glance

ORS 732.085 — Incorporators. Chapter 732 (Chapter 732), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

732.085 Incorporators. One or more natural persons of the age of 21 years or older or one or more financially responsible corporations may act as incorporators of a domestic insurer upon compliance with the provisions of the Insurance Code. [1967 c.359 §154] 732.090 [Repealed by 1967 c.359 §704]
