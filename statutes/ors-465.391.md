# Effect of certain laws on liability of person (ORS 465.391)

## At a glance

ORS 465.391 — Effect of certain laws on liability of person. Chapter 465 (Chapter 465), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

465.391 Effect of certain laws on liability of person. Nothing in ORS 453.396 to 453.408, 453.414, 459.236 and 459.311, including the limitation on the amount a local government unit must contribute under ORS 459.236 and 459.311, shall be construed to affect or limit the liability of any person. [1993 c.707 §9 (enacted in lieu of 465.390)]
