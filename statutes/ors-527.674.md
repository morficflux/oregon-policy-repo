# Rules requiring approval of written plan prohibited (ORS 527.674)

## At a glance

ORS 527.674 — Rules requiring approval of written plan prohibited. Chapter 527 (Chapter 527), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

527.674 Rules requiring approval of written plan prohibited. The State Board of Forestry may not adopt or enforce a rule under ORS 527.610 to 527.770 that requires that the board or the State Forester approve written plans as a required precedent to conducting a forest practice or operation. [2003 c.740 §13] 527.675 [1995 s.s. c.3 §39g; repealed by 1996 c.9 §8 (527.676 enacted in lieu of 527.675)]
