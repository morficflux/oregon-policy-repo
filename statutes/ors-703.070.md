## Full text

703.070 License applications; form; nonrefundable fee.

(1) Applications for initial renewal or replacement licenses as polygraph examiners or trainees under ORS 703.010 to 703.310 shall be submitted in writing and under oath to the Department of Public Safety Standards and Training, accompanied by the proper fee therefor specified in ORS 703.110.

(2) A fee submitted to the department may not be refunded. Each application shall be in such form and require such information as the department prescribes. [1975 c.608 §13; 1997 c.853 §46; 1999 c.59 §210]
