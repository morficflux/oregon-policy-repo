# Definitions for ORS 271.005 to (ORS 271.005)

## At a glance

ORS 271.005 — Definitions for ORS 271.005 to. Chapter 271 (Chapter 271), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

271.005 Definitions for ORS 271.005 to 271.540. As used in ORS 271.005 to 271.540:

(1) “Governing body” means the board or body in which the general legislative power of a political subdivision is vested.

(2) “Governmental body” means the State of Oregon, a political subdivision, the United States of America or an agency thereof.

(3) “Political subdivision” means any local government unit, including, but not limited to, a county, city, town, port, dock commission or district, that exists under the laws of Oregon and has power to levy and collect taxes.
