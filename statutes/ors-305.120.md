# Enforcement of tax laws (ORS 305.120)

## At a glance

ORS 305.120 — Enforcement of tax laws. Chapter 305 (Chapter 305), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

305.120 Enforcement of tax laws.

(1) The Department of Revenue shall see that revenue officers comply with the tax and revenue laws, that all taxes are collected, that complaint is made against any person violating such laws and that penalties prescribed by such laws are enforced.

(2) The Director of the Department of Revenue may call upon the district attorney or Attorney General to institute and conduct prosecutions for violations of the laws in respect to the assessment and taxation of property and the collection of public taxes and revenues. [Formerly 306.140]
