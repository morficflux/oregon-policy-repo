# Traumatic brain injury (ORS 743A.175)

## At a glance

ORS 743A.175 — Traumatic brain injury. Chapter 743A (Chapter 743A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743A.175 Traumatic brain injury.

(1) A health benefit plan, as defined in ORS 743B.005, shall provide coverage of medically necessary therapy and services for the treatment of traumatic brain injury.

(2) This section is exempt from ORS 743A.001. [2009 c.423 §2]
