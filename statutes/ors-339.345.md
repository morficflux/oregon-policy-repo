# Requirement to have automated external defibrillator (Prohibitions on Discrimination) (ORS 339.345)

## At a glance

ORS 339.345 — Requirement to have automated external defibrillator (Prohibitions on Discrimination). Chapter 339 (Chapter 339), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

339.345 Requirement to have automated external defibrillator. Each school campus in a school district, private school campus and public charter school campus shall have on the premises at least one automated external defibrillator. [2010 c.62 §1] (Prohibitions on Discrimination)
