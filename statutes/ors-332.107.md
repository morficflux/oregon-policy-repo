# Rules for school government (ORS 332.107)

## At a glance

ORS 332.107 — Rules for school government. Chapter 332 (Chapter 332), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

332.107 Rules for school government. Each district school board shall establish rules for the government of the schools and pupils consistent with the rules of the State Board of Education.
