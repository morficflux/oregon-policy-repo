# Compliance statement (ORS 180.550)

## At a glance

ORS 180.550 — Compliance statement. Chapter 180 (Attorney General; Department of Justice), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

180.550 Compliance statement. A seller or extender of credit may state the following on a consumer contract determined by the reviewing agency to comply with the plain language standards under ORS 180.545: “The form of this contract meets Oregon plain language guidelines.” [1985 c.587 §3] Note: See note under 180.540.
