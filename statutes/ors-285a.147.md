# Oregon-Ireland Sister State Committee authority (ORS 285A.147)

## At a glance

ORS 285A.147 — Oregon-Ireland Sister State Committee authority. Chapter 285A (Chapter 285A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285A.147 Oregon-Ireland Sister State Committee authority.

(1) The Oregon-Ireland Sister State Committee established under ORS 285A.146: (a) Organize activities for and host visiting delegations from Ireland; (b) Organize activities for and participate in delegations visiting Ireland; (c) Provide for the exchange of information between the State of Oregon and Ireland; (d) Work with representatives of Ireland on joint projects; and (e) Take all actions necessary to coordinate and promote Oregon exports to Ireland and Irish investments within Oregon.

(2) The Oregon-Ireland Sister State Committee may visit Ireland at least biennially for the purpose of fulfilling the objectives described in subsection

(1) of this section. [2025 c.543 §2] Note: See note under 285A.146.
