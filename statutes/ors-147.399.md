# Sexual Assault Victims’ Emergency Medical Response Fund (ORS 147.399)

## At a glance

## Full text

147.399 Sexual Assault Victims’ Emergency Medical Response Fund.

(1) The Sexual Assault Victims’ Emergency Medical Response Fund is established, separate and distinct from the General Fund. All moneys in the Sexual Assault Victims’ Emergency Medical Response Fund are continuously appropriated to the Department of Justice to be used for the purpose of carrying out the provisions of ORS 147.397.

(2) The Department of Justice may accept moneys from any source for the purpose of carrying out the provisions of ORS 147.397. The department shall deposit moneys accepted under this subsection in the Sexual Assault Victims’ Emergency Medical Response Fund. [2003 c.789 §3] Note: See note under 147.395.
