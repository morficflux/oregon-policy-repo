# “Balance trailer” (ORS 801.140)

## At a glance

ORS 801.140 — “Balance trailer”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.140 “Balance trailer.” “Balance trailer” means every trailer, other than a self-supporting trailer, pole trailer or semitrailer, designed so that its weight and that of its load is substantially balanced upon its axle or axles and so that it couples to the towing vehicle with a device other than a fifth wheel hitch. The definition in this section is based upon design features and, except as otherwise provided in this section, does not prohibit a balance trailer from fitting into another classification of trailer based on use. [1983 c.338 §20; 1985 c.16 §9] 801.145 [1983 c.338 §21; 1999 c.1051 §278; repealed by 2011 c.597 §112]
