# Agreements with State Fire Marshal representatives (ORS 476.851)

## At a glance

ORS 476.851 — Agreements with State Fire Marshal representatives. Chapter 476 (State Fire Marshal; Protection From Fire Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

476.851 Agreements with State Fire Marshal representatives. The State Fire Marshal may enter into agreements with any state or local agency to allow the agency to act as a representative of the State Fire Marshal for purposes of ORS 476.841 and 476.846. [2009 c.6 §5] Note: See note under 476.831. 476.855 [1973 c.667 §14; 1985 c.118 §11; 1993 c.185 §19; renumbered 476.033 in 1999]
