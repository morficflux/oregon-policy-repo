# Authority of Governor to carry out agreement (ORS 477.190)

## At a glance

ORS 477.190 — Authority of Governor to carry out agreement. Chapter 477 (Chapter 477), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

477.190 Authority of Governor to carry out agreement. The Governor may take any action necessary to carry out the Northwest Wildland Fire Protection Agreement as set forth in ORS 477.200. The Governor may delegate the authority granted under this section or ORS 477.180 and 477.185 to the State Forester. [1999 c.258 §6] Note: See note under 477.175.
