# Notification to law enforcement agency when kit collected (ORS 181A.326)

## At a glance

ORS 181A.326 — Notification to law enforcement agency when kit collected. Chapter 181A (State Police; Public Safety Standards and Training), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

181A.326 Notification to law enforcement agency when kit collected. A medical facility collecting sexual assault forensic evidence kits shall, within seven days after the collection of a kit, notify the law enforcement agency with jurisdiction over a possible sexual assault criminal investigation that the kit has been collected. [2016 c.89 §5] Note: See note under 181A.322.
