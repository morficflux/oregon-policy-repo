# “Crime” described (ORS 161.515)

## At a glance

ORS 161.515 — “Crime” described. Chapter 161 (General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

161.515 “Crime” described.

(1) A crime is an offense for which a sentence of imprisonment is authorized.

(2) A crime is either a felony or a misdemeanor. [1971 c.743 §66]
