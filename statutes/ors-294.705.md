# Tax supervising and conservation commission fund (ORS 294.705)

## At a glance

ORS 294.705 — Tax supervising and conservation commission fund. Chapter 294 (Chapter 294), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

294.705 Tax supervising and conservation commission fund. There is established the tax supervising and conservation commission fund in the county treasury. The fund shall consist of any penalties recovered under ORS 294.700.
