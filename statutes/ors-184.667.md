# Autonomous vehicles; lead agency (Cooperation and Assistance on Transportation Projects) (ORS 184.667)

## At a glance

ORS 184.667 — Autonomous vehicles; lead agency (Cooperation and Assistance on Transportation Projects). Chapter 184 (Certain Executive Branch Departments (incl. DAS)), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

184.667 Autonomous vehicles; lead agency. The Department of Transportation is the lead agency responsible for coordination of autonomous vehicle programs and policies. [2018 c.94 §1] (Cooperation and Assistance on Transportation Projects)
