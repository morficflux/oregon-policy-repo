# Sports facility owned by large city (Alternative Energy Systems) (ORS 307.171)

## At a glance

ORS 307.171 — Sports facility owned by large city (Alternative Energy Systems). Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.171 Sports facility owned by large city. Any sports facility owned by a city with a population of at least 500,000 is exempt from taxation, even if leased to or operated by a taxpaying entity. [2001 c.931 §2] (Alternative Energy Systems)
