# Records and accounts of commission (ORS 578.190)

## At a glance

ORS 578.190 — Records and accounts of commission. Chapter 578 (Chapter 578), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

578.190 Records and accounts of commission. The Oregon Wheat Commission shall keep accurate books, records and accounts of all its dealings which shall be open to inspection and audit by the Secretary of State. [1959 c.596 §§46,84; 1973 c.794 §32] 578.210 [Amended by 1959 c.596 §41; 1975 c.174 §1; 1981 c.58 §1; 1991 c.972 §1; repealed by 2003 c.604 §75 (578.211 enacted in lieu of 578.210)]
