# “Traffic crime” (ORS 801.545)

## At a glance

ORS 801.545 — “Traffic crime”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.545 “Traffic crime.” “Traffic crime” means any traffic offense that is punishable by a jail sentence. [1983 c.338 §100] 801.550 [1983 c.338 §102; 1999 c.1051 §83; renumbered 801.557 in 1999]
