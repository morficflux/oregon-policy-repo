# Refund of moneys (ORS 273.125)

## At a glance

ORS 273.125 — Refund of moneys. Chapter 273 (Chapter 273), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

273.125 Refund of moneys. Whenever it appears to the Department of State Lands that any moneys have been erroneously paid to it, the department may make an appropriate refund, or may deliver the moneys to the State Treasurer if the moneys appear to have been misdelivered to the department and to be unclaimed property described under ORS 98.302 to 98.436 or escheated funds, including those described under ORS 116.203, 652.405, 708A.430 or 723.466. [Formerly 273.470; 2019 c.678 §57] 273.130 [Amended by 1967 c.421 §58; renumbered 273.290]
