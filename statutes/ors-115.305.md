# Survival of causes of action (ORS 115.305)

## At a glance

ORS 115.305 — Survival of causes of action. Chapter 115 (Chapter 115), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

115.305 Survival of causes of action. All causes of action or suit, by one person against another, survive to the personal representative of the former and against the personal representative of the latter. [Formerly 121.020] 115.310 [Repealed by 1969 c.591 §305]
