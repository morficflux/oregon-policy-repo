# License requirement (ORS 703.405)

## At a glance

ORS 703.405 — License requirement. Chapter 703 (Chapter 703), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

703.405 License requirement. A person may not act as an investigator or represent that the person is an investigator unless that person is licensed under ORS 703.430. [1997 c.870 §2; 2001 c.838 §2] 703.406 [1989 c.1055 §§2,3,7(2); 1995 c.386 §11; renumbered 8.435 in 1995]
