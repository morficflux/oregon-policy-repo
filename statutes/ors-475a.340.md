# Psilocybin services; rules (ORS 475A.340)

## At a glance

ORS 475A.340 — Psilocybin services; rules. Chapter 475A (Chapter 475A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475A.340 Psilocybin services; rules. The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt by rule the requirements, specifications and guidelines for:

(1) Providing psilocybin services to a client;

(2) Holding and verifying the completion of a preparation session;

(3) Having a client complete, sign, and deliver a client information form to a psilocybin service center operator and a psilocybin service facilitator;

(4) Holding and verifying the completion of an administration session; and

(5) Holding and verifying the completion of an integration session. [2021 c.1 §33] Note: As originally enacted by the people, the leadline to ORS 475A.340 read “Psilocybin services.” The leadline was changed by editorial action.
