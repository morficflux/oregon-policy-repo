# Agricultural Development Division (ORS 576.009)

## At a glance

ORS 576.009 — Agricultural Development Division. Chapter 576 (Agricultural Marketing Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

576.009 Agricultural Development Division. There is established within the State Department of Agriculture an Agricultural Development Division which shall have the powers and duties conferred by ORS 576.006 to 576.022, and as specified by the Director of Agriculture. The Agricultural Development Division shall consist of a market development service and a commodity development service. [1955 c.572 §2; 1983 c.740 §221; 1985 c.623 §3] 576.010 [Repealed by 1953 c.119 §2]
