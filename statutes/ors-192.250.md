# Director of Oregon Department of Administrative Services to report to legislature on ORS 192.230 to (ORS 192.250)

## At a glance

## Full text

192.250 Director of Oregon Department of Administrative Services to report to legislature on ORS 192.230 to 192.250. The Director of the Oregon Department of Administrative Services shall report to the Legislative Assembly by appearing at least once during each biennium before the appropriate interim committees designated by the Speaker of the House of Representatives and the President of the Senate. The director shall testify as to the effectiveness of ORS 171.206, 192.230 to 192.250 and 292.956, including any cost savings realized or projected and any recommendations for further legislative action. [1991 c.842 §5; 2003 c.803 §4] Note: See note under 192.230. (Distribution)
