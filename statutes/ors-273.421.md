# Relinquishing title to United States Government or governmental body or subdivision; continued public use requirement (ORS 273.421)

## At a glance

ORS 273.421 — Relinquishing title to United States Government or governmental body or subdivision; continued public use requirement. Chapter 273 (Chapter 273), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

273.421 Relinquishing title to United States Government or governmental body or subdivision; continued public use requirement. The state hereby is granted express power to relinquish the title to any of its property not needed for public use to the United States Government or any other governmental body or political subdivision within the state, providing such property shall be used and continue to be used, for a public purpose by the United States Government, or such other governmental body or political subdivision in the State of Oregon.
