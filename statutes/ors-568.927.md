# Law inapplicable to certain forest practices (ORS 568.927)

## At a glance

ORS 568.927 — Law inapplicable to certain forest practices. Chapter 568 (Chapter 568), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

568.927 Law inapplicable to certain forest practices. The provisions of ORS 568.900 to 568.933 shall not apply to any forest practice conducted on forestland as defined in ORS 527.620. [1993 c.263 §12]
