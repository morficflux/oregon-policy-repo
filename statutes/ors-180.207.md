# Requirements for Department of Justice agency request budget (Powers and Duties) (ORS 180.207)

## At a glance

ORS 180.207 — Requirements for Department of Justice agency request budget (Powers and Duties). Chapter 180 (Attorney General; Department of Justice), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

180.207 Requirements for Department of Justice agency request budget.

(1) Each biennium, the Department of Justice shall include in its agency request budget filed under ORS 291.208 discrete amounts necessary to fund the projected costs of continuing state support for the following currently authorized programs and activities for the next biennium: (a) Children’s advocacy centers as defined in ORS 418.782; (b) The Survivor Housing Funds grant program, or any successor program that provides funds to assist victims of domestic violence, sexual assault, stalking, dating violence and human trafficking to access and maintain safe permanent housing; and (c) The Oregon Domestic and Sexual Violence Services Fund established under ORS 147.453.

(2) When calculating the amounts described in subsection

(1) of this section, the department may include in the total up to 10 percent for department administrative costs. [2024 c.86 §1] Note: 180.207 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 180 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. (Powers and Duties)
