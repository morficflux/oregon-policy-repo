# Regulatory procedure OPERATOR SERVICE PROVIDERS (ORS 759.675)

## Full text

759.675 Regulatory procedure. The procedures of the Public Utility Commission for petition, regulation and enforcement relative to attachments, including any rights of appeal from any decision thereof, shall be the same as those applicable to the commission. [1987 c.447 §27; 1989 c.5 §21] OPERATOR SERVICE PROVIDERS
