# “Public notice” defined (ORS 174.104)

## At a glance

ORS 174.104 — “Public notice” defined. Chapter 174 (Construction of Statutes; General Definitions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

174.104 “Public notice” defined. As used in the statute laws of this state, unless the context or a specially applicable definition requires otherwise, “public notice” means any legal publication which requires an affidavit of publication as required in ORS 193.070, or is required by law to be published.
