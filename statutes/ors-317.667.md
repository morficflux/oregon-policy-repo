# Tax treatment of captive insurers UNITARY TAX (ORS 317.667)

## At a glance

ORS 317.667 — Tax treatment of captive insurers UNITARY TAX. Chapter 317 (Chapter 317), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

317.667 Tax treatment of captive insurers. A captive insurer, as defined in ORS 735.150, making a valid election under section 831(b) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended, shall be afforded the same tax treatment on receipt of premiums and tax on investment earnings for state income tax purposes as exists for federal income tax purposes except that the income tax rates on taxable income of the captive insurer shall be those identified under state law rather than federal law. [2012 c.84 §24] UNITARY TAX
