# Application of definitions to construction of chapter (ORS 656.003)

## At a glance

ORS 656.003 — Application of definitions to construction of chapter. Chapter 656 (Chapter 656), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

656.003 Application of definitions to construction of chapter. Except where the context otherwise requires, the definitions given in this chapter govern its construction. [1975 c.556 §2 (enacted in lieu of 656.002)] 656.004 [Repealed by 1981 c.535 §28 (656.012 enacted in lieu of 656.004)]
