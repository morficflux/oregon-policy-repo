# Definitions OUTDOOR RECREATION RESOURCES (ORS 390.005)

## At a glance

ORS 390.005 — Definitions OUTDOOR RECREATION RESOURCES. Chapter 390 (Chapter 390), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

390.005 Definitions. As used in this chapter, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “Commission” means the State Parks and Recreation Commission.

(2) “Department” means the State Parks and Recreation Department.

(3) “Director” means the State Parks and Recreation Director. [1989 c.904 §2] OUTDOOR RECREATION RESOURCES
