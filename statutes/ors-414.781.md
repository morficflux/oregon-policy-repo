# Fee-for-service reimbursement of co-occurring mental health and substance use disorder treatment services (ORS 414.781)

## At a glance

ORS 414.781 — Fee-for-service reimbursement of co-occurring mental health and substance use disorder treatment services. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.781 Fee-for-service reimbursement of co-occurring mental health and substance use disorder treatment services. The Oregon Health Authority shall reimburse the cost of co-occurring mental health and substance use disorder treatment services paid for on a fee-for-service basis at an enhanced rate based on:

(1) Existing reimbursement codes used for co-occurring disorder treatments;

(2) Clinical complexity; and

(3) The education level of the provider. [2021 c.667 §2] Note: 414.781 and 414.782 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 414 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
