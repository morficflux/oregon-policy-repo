# Definitions for ORS (ORS 105.925)

## At a glance

ORS 105.925 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 105 (Chapter 105), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

105.925 Definitions for ORS 105.925 to 105.945. As used in ORS 105.925 to 105.945:

(1) “Event data recorder” has the meaning given that term in 49 C.F.R 563.5, as in effect on January 1, 2008.

(2) “Owner” means a person: (a) In whose name a motor vehicle is registered or titled; (b) Who leases a motor vehicle for at least three months; (c) Who is entitled to possession of a motor vehicle as the purchaser under a security agreement; or (d) Who is the attorney in fact, conservator or personal representative for a person described in paragraphs (a) to (c) of this subsection. [2007 c.644 §1]
