# Application (Indemnification) (ORS 404.205)

## At a glance

ORS 404.205 — Application (Indemnification). Chapter 404 (Chapter 404), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

404.205 Application. ORS 404.200 to 404.215 apply only to a qualified search and rescue volunteer who is performing search and rescue activities without compensation other than reimbursement for food, lodging, costs of transportation and other expenses. [2009 c.718 §12] (Indemnification)
