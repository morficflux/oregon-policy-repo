# Construction of ORS (ORS 162.121)

## At a glance

ORS 162.121 — Construction of ORS. Chapter 162 (Chapter 162), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

162.121 Construction of ORS 162.117 to 162.121. The provisions of ORS 30.862 and 162.117 to 162.121 shall be liberally construed to effectuate its remedial purposes. [1993 c.768 §5] Note: See note under 162.117. 162.130 [Repealed by 1971 c.743 §432] ESCAPE, SUPPLYING CONTRABAND AND FAILURE TO APPEAR
