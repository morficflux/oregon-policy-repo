# Definitions for ORS (ORS 414.667)

## At a glance

ORS 414.667 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.667 Definitions for ORS 414.667 to 414.671. As used in ORS 414.667 to 414.671:

(1) “Doula” has the meaning given that term in ORS 414.025.

(2) “Lactation counselor” has the meaning given that term in ORS 676.665.

(3) “Lactation educator” has the meaning given that term in ORS 676.665. [2017 c.281 §2; 2025 c.539 §6]
