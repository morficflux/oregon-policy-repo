# Indemnification of officers, employees and agents (ORS 707.758)

## At a glance

ORS 707.758 — Indemnification of officers, employees and agents. Chapter 707 (Chapter 707), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

707.758 Indemnification of officers, employees and agents. Unless a banking institution’s articles of incorporation provide otherwise:

(1) An officer of the banking institution is entitled to mandatory indemnification under ORS 707.748 and is entitled to apply for court-ordered indemnification under ORS 707.754, in each case to the same extent as a director under ORS 707.748 and 707.754.

(2) The banking institution may indemnify and advance expenses under ORS 707.744 to 707.762 to an officer, employee or agent of the banking institution to the same extent as to a director. [1989 c.324 §23; 1997 c.631 §112] 707.760 [1985 c.786 §22; renumbered 707.860 in 1989]
