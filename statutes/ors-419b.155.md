# Protective custody not arrest (ORS 419B.155)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.155 — Protective custody not arrest. Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.155 Protective custody not arrest.

(1) Protective custody shall not be deemed an arrest so far as the child is concerned.

(2) A peace officer taking a child into protective custody has all the privileges and immunities of a peace officer making an arrest. [1993 c.33 §62; 1993 c.546 §28]
