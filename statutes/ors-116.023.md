## Full text

116.023 Bond or other security. The court may require a bond or other security of any distributee for the protection of creditors and other interested persons who might suffer loss or injury because of the distribution of property under ORS 116.013. [1969 c.591 §170] 116.025 [Repealed by 1969 c.591 §305]
