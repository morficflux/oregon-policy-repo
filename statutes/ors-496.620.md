# Nonliability of law enforcement officers (ORS 496.620)

## At a glance

ORS 496.620 — Nonliability of law enforcement officers. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.620 Nonliability of law enforcement officers. No person authorized to enforce the wildlife laws shall suffer any civil liability for the enforcement or attempted enforcement of any provisions of the wildlife laws or for the exercise or attempted exercise of any of the duties or privileges granted to or imposed by law upon the State Fish and Wildlife Commission or such persons. [Amended by 1971 c.658 §11; 1973 c.723 §20] 496.625 [Amended by 1971 c.658 §12; repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
