# Bridge Program Fund PRESCRIPTION DRUGS (Oregon Prescription Drug Program) (ORS 414.245)

## At a glance

ORS 414.245 — Bridge Program Fund PRESCRIPTION DRUGS (Oregon Prescription Drug Program). Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.245 Bridge Program Fund. The Bridge Program Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund, consisting of federal funds received by the Oregon Health Authority to administer the bridge program described in ORS 414.241. Moneys in the Bridge Program Fund are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Health Authority to carry out ORS 414.241.
