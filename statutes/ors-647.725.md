# Relationship to other laws (ORS 647.725)

## At a glance

ORS 647.725 — Relationship to other laws. Chapter 647 (Chapter 647), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

647.725 Relationship to other laws. The rights, remedies and prohibitions in ORS 647.700 to 647.730 are in addition to and cumulative to any other right, remedy or prohibition accorded by common law, federal law or the statutes of this state. ORS 647.700 to 647.730 do not deny, abrogate or impair any common law or statutory right, remedy or prohibition. [1997 c.236 §6; 2018 c.28 §9] Note: See note under 647.700.
