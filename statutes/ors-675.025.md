# Application of ORS 58.325 to 58.365 (Licensing) (ORS 675.025)

## At a glance

## Full text

675.025 Application of ORS 58.325 to 58.365. ORS 58.325 to 58.365 shall not apply to professional corporations rendering psychological services. [1985 c.90 §11] (Licensing)
