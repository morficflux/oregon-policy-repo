## Full text

421.614 Corrections facilities; determining location.

(1) The Department of Corrections shall determine locations for corrections facilities pursuant to the provisions of ORS 421.611 to 421.630.

(2) The department shall establish, by rule, mandatory and desirable criteria to be used in the nominations made under ORS 421.616. [1995 c.745 §3] Note: See note under 421.611. 421.615 [Formerly 421.030; 1969 c.502 §25; repealed by 1971 c.212 §6]
