# Penalties (ORS 460.990)

## At a glance

ORS 460.990 — Penalties. Chapter 460 (Chapter 460), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

460.990 Penalties.

(1) Violation of any of the provisions of ORS 460.005 to 460.175 is a Class C misdemeanor.

(2) Violation of any provisions of ORS 460.310 to 460.370 is a Class B misdemeanor. [Subsection

(2) enacted as 1959 c.619 §10; 1961 c.427 §25; subsection

(1) enacted as 1961 c.427 §21; 1985 c.705 §9; 2011 c.597 §204] _______________
