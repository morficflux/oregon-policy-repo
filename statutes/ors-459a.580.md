# Prohibited disposal of used oil (ORS 459A.580)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.580 — Prohibited disposal of used oil. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.580 Prohibited disposal of used oil. Unless permitted pursuant to ORS 468B.050, no person shall dispose of used oil by discharge into sewers, drainage systems or the waters of this state as defined by ORS 468B.005, or by incineration other than for energy generating purposes. [Formerly 468.865; 2003 c.469 §3] Note: See note under 459A.552.
