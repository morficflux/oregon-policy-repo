# Joint production of state and county voters’ pamphlet (ORS 251.008)

## At a glance

ORS 251.008 — Joint production of state and county voters’ pamphlet. Chapter 251 (Chapter 251), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

251.008 Joint production of state and county voters’ pamphlet. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Secretary of State and a county may enter into an intergovernmental agreement as provided in ORS 190.003 to 190.130 under which the Secretary of State and the county clerk of the county may jointly produce a voters’ pamphlet for any election as provided under this chapter or any other law relating to production and distribution of a voters’ pamphlet. [1993 c.3 §2 and 1993 c.604 §9] 251.010 [Repealed by 1957 c.217 §9]
