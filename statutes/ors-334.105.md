# Definitions for ORS (ORS 334.105)

## At a glance

ORS 334.105 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 334 (Chapter 334), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

334.105 Definitions for ORS 334.108 to 334.115. As used in ORS 334.108 to 334.115, “pilot education service district” means:

(1) The Willamette Education Service District;

(2) The High Desert Education Service District; and

(3) The Northwest Regional Education Service District. [2005 c.828 §10; 2007 c.589 §1; 2010 c.59 §1] Note: 334.105 to 334.115 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 334 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
