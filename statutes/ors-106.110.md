# Unlawful issuance of marriage license prohibited (ORS 106.110)

## At a glance

ORS 106.110 — Unlawful issuance of marriage license prohibited. Chapter 106 (Marriage; Domestic Partnership), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

106.110 Unlawful issuance of marriage license prohibited. No county clerk shall issue a license contrary to the provisions of ORS 106.041 to 106.077 or 106.100.
