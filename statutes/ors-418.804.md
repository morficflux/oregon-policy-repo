# Short title (ORS 418.804)

## At a glance

ORS 418.804 — Short title. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.804 Short title. ORS 418.806 to 418.816 and 419B.023 shall be known and may be cited as “Karly’s Law.” [Formerly 419B.022] Note: 418.804 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 418 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 418.805 [1969 c.641 §1; 1977 c.717 §20; 1979 c.745 §1; 1987 c.794 §1; renumbered 657A.250 in 1993]
