# Civil penalties (ORS 675.336)

## At a glance

ORS 675.336 — Civil penalties. Chapter 675 (Chapter 675), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

675.336 Civil penalties.

(1) In addition to any other liability or penalty provided by law, the Occupational Therapy Licensing Board may impose a civil penalty on a person who violates any provision of ORS 675.210 to 675.340 or 675.850 in an amount not to exceed $1,000 for each violation.

(2) The board shall impose civil penalties under this section in the manner provided by ORS 183.745.

(3) All penalties recovered under this section shall be paid into the Occupational Therapy Licensing Board Account established in ORS 675.330 and shall be used only for the administration and enforcement of ORS 675.210 to 675.340.
