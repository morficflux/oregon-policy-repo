## Full text

475C.644 Civil penalty for violating ORS 475C.600 to 475C.648.

(1) In addition to any other liability or penalty provided by law, the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission may impose for each violation of a provision of ORS 475C.600 to 475C.648, or a rule adopted under a provision of ORS 475C.600 to 475C.648, a civil penalty that does not exceed $500 for each day that the violation occurs.

(2) The commission shall impose civil penalties under this section in the manner provided by ORS 183.745.

(3) Moneys collected under this section shall be deposited in the Marijuana Control and Regulation Fund established under ORS 475C.297 and are continuously appropriated to the commission for the purpose of carrying out the duties, functions and powers of the commission under ORS 475C.600 to 475C.648. [Formerly 475B.655; 2025 c.2 §33]
