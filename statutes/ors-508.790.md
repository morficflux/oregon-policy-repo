## Full text

508.790 Fee; application form.

(1) The annual fee for the vessel permit required by ORS 508.775 is: (a) $140 for resident applicants. (b) $196 for nonresident applicants.

(2) Applications shall be in such form and contain such information as the State Department of Fish and Wildlife, by rule, may prescribe.
