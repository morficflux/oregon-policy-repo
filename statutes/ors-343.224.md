# School district liability for expense of noneducational care (ORS 343.224)

## At a glance

ORS 343.224 — School district liability for expense of noneducational care. Chapter 343 (Chapter 343), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

343.224 School district liability for expense of noneducational care. School districts are not financially responsible for noneducational care of a child with a disability unless that district has participated in development of the child’s individualized education program that clearly documents that the care is prerequisite to the child receiving a free and appropriate education and the placement is for educational program needs, rather than care needs.
