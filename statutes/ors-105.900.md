# “Wind energy easement” defined for ORS (ORS 105.900)

## At a glance

ORS 105.900 — “Wind energy easement” defined for ORS. Chapter 105 (Chapter 105), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

105.900 “Wind energy easement” defined for ORS 105.905 and 105.910. As used in ORS 105.905 and 105.910, “wind energy easement” means any easement, covenant or condition designed to insure the undisturbed flow of wind across the real property of another. [1981 c.590 §1]
