## At a glance

## Full text

358.617 Rules. Pursuant to ORS chapter 183, the State Historic Preservation Officer shall adopt rules to carry out the duties and functions of the officer, including rules governing cultural resource management programs and grants-in-aid program categories. [1983 c.268 §4] 358.620 [1953 c.475 §4; 1955 c.547 §2; renumbered 390.430]
