# Maximum consecutive sentences (ORS 137.121)

## At a glance

ORS 137.121 — Maximum consecutive sentences. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.121 Maximum consecutive sentences. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, but subject to ORS 161.605, the maximum consecutive sentences which may be imposed for felonies committed on or after November 1, 1989, whether as terms of imprisonment, probation or both, shall be as provided by rules of the Oregon Criminal Justice Commission. [1989 c.790 §14] Note: 137.121 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 137 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 137.122 [1985 c.722 §2; repealed by 1991 c.67 §28]
