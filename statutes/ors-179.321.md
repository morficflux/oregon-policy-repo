# Responsibility to supervise state institutions (ORS 179.321)

## At a glance

ORS 179.321 — Responsibility to supervise state institutions. Chapter 179 (Administration of State Institutions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

179.321 Responsibility to supervise state institutions.

(1) The Oregon Health Authority shall operate, control, manage and supervise the Oregon State Hospital campuses.

(2) The Department of Corrections shall operate, control, manage and supervise those institutions defined as Department of Corrections institutions in ORS 421.005.
