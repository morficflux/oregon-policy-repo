# Requirements for persons accepting delivery sale purchase orders (ORS 323.706)

## At a glance

ORS 323.706 — Requirements for persons accepting delivery sale purchase orders. Chapter 323 (Chapter 323), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

323.706 Requirements for persons accepting delivery sale purchase orders. Subject to ORS 180.441, a person accepting a purchase order for a delivery sale, prior to the first mailing, shipment or other delivery of tobacco to a consumer, shall comply with:

(1) The age verification requirements set forth in ORS 323.709;

(2) The license requirements set forth in ORS 323.712;

(3) The disclosure requirements set forth in ORS 323.715;

(4) The reporting requirements set forth in ORS 323.721; and

(5) All other laws of this state applicable to sales of tobacco that occur entirely within Oregon, including but not limited to ORS 323.005 to 323.482, 323.500 to 323.645, 323.806 and 323.816. [2003 c.804 §75; 2009 c.717 §23; 2021 c.179 §8]
