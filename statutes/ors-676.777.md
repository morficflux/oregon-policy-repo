# Discipline (ORS 676.777)

## At a glance

ORS 676.777 — Discipline. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.777 Discipline. In the manner prescribed in ORS chapter 183 for contested cases, and at the direction of the State Board of Sign Language Interpreters, the Health Licensing Office may impose a form of discipline listed in ORS 676.612 for a violation of ORS 676.750 to 676.789 or rules adopted under ORS 676.750 to 676.789. [2023 c.414 §10]
