# Format for notices and summaries (ORS 294.433)

## At a glance

ORS 294.433 — Format for notices and summaries. Chapter 294 (Chapter 294), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

294.433 Format for notices and summaries. Format for publication of notices and summaries required by ORS 294.438 shall be prescribed by the Department of Revenue.
