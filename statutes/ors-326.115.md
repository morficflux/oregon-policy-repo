# Department of Education Account (ORS 326.115)

## At a glance

ORS 326.115 — Department of Education Account. Chapter 326 (State Administration of Education), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

326.115 Department of Education Account. The Department of Education Account is established separate and distinct from the General Fund. All moneys received by the Department of Education, other than appropriations from the General Fund, shall be deposited into the account and are continuously appropriated to the department to carry out the duties, functions and powers of the department. [2001 c.716 §12] 326.120 [Amended by 1965 c.100 §9; renumbered 326.071]
