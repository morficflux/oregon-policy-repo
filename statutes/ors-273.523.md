# Sale of forest products on state lands (ORS 273.523)

## At a glance

ORS 273.523 — Sale of forest products on state lands. Chapter 273 (Chapter 273), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

273.523 Sale of forest products on state lands. The Department of State Lands may sell and dispose of the forest products on lands of the State of Oregon that have not been designated Common School Forest Lands under ORS 530.460, regardless of acreage and in such quantities to each purchaser as the department shall prescribe. If the value of the forest products, as appraised under ORS 273.525, exceeds $25,000, the department shall sell the forest products to the highest and best bidder at public auction, or through sealed bids, as the department may determine prior to offering the forest products for sale. [Formerly 273.521]
