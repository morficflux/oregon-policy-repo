# License required (ORS 691.415)

## At a glance

ORS 691.415 — License required. Chapter 691 (Chapter 691), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

691.415 License required. A person who is not licensed under ORS 691.435 may not practice or attempt to practice as a licensed dietitian using in connection with the name of the person the words or letters “L.D.,” or “Licensed Dietitian.” [1989 c.487 §2; 2011 c.630 §5] 691.425 [1989 c.487 §3; 2005 c.21 §10; repealed by 2011 c.630 §24]
