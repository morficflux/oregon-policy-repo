# Alternative mechanism for disclosure of costs and charges (ORS 743B.284)

## At a glance

ORS 743B.284 — Alternative mechanism for disclosure of costs and charges. Chapter 743B (Chapter 743B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743B.284 Alternative mechanism for disclosure of costs and charges. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may waive the requirements of ORS 743B.281 or 743B.282 to allow an insurer to use an alternative disclosure mechanism, provided that the mechanism enables enrollees to access information substantially similar to or more extensive than the information disclosed in ORS 743B.281 or 743B.282. [Formerly 743.883] Note: See note under 743B.275.
