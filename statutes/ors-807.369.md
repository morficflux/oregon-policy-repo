# Driving at night FEES (ORS 807.369)

## At a glance

ORS 807.369 — Driving at night FEES. Chapter 807 (Chapter 807), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

807.369 Driving at night.

(1) Notwithstanding ORS 807.363, a person with a limited vision condition may operate a vehicle at night if the person: (a) Is issued a driver license under ORS 807.363; (b) Provides a certificate issued by a rehabilitation training specialist certifying that the person has successfully completed a rehabilitation training program and is able to safely operate a motor vehicle at night; and (c) Is examined every two years by a licensed vision specialist who certifies that the person is able to safely operate a motor vehicle at night.

(2) As used in this section, “limited vision condition,” “rehabilitation training specialist” and “rehabilitation training program” have the meanings given those terms in ORS 807.355. [2007 c.588 §8] FEES
