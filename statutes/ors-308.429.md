# Relief not allowed in case of arson by property owner (ORS 308.429)

## At a glance

ORS 308.429 — Relief not allowed in case of arson by property owner. Chapter 308 (Chapter 308), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308.429 Relief not allowed in case of arson by property owner. No relief under ORS 308.146

(5) or

(6) or 308.425 shall be given to any person who is convicted of arson with regard to the property for which relief is sought. [Formerly 308.440] 308.430 [1971 c.497 §2; repealed by 1974 s.s. c.14 §3]
