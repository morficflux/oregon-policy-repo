## At a glance

## Full text

632.211 Establishment of grades and standards; grading and inspection fee.

(1) The State Department of Agriculture shall, in accordance with the applicable provisions of ORS 632.900 to 632.935, establish grades and standards for onions which shall conform, in so far as practicable, to the official grades and standards prescribed by the United States Department of Agriculture.

(2) The department may fix and collect a fee for grading and inspecting of onions in accordance with ORS 632.940.
