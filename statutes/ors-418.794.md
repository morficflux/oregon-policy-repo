# Confidentiality of video recordings (ORS 418.794)

## At a glance

ORS 418.794 — Confidentiality of video recordings. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.794 Confidentiality of video recordings. Video recordings produced pursuant to ORS 418.746 to 418.801 shall remain in the custody of the children’s advocacy center or the regional children’s advocacy center and shall remain confidential and not subject to public disclosure except under a lawfully issued subpoena and protective order. [1991 c.898 §9; 1993 c.33 §336; 2005 c.562 §16; 2019 c.141 §21] Note: See note under 418.746.
