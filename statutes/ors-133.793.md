# Penalty for disobedience to ORS (ORS 133.793)

## At a glance

ORS 133.793 — Penalty for disobedience to ORS. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.793 Penalty for disobedience to ORS 133.787. Any officer who shall deliver to the agent for extradition of the demanding state a person in the custody of the officer under the Governor’s warrant in disobedience to ORS 133.787 commits a Class B misdemeanor. [Formerly 147.110]
