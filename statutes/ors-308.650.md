# Companies to maintain principal office and agent within state (ORS 308.650)

## At a glance

ORS 308.650 — Companies to maintain principal office and agent within state. Chapter 308 (Chapter 308), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308.650 Companies to maintain principal office and agent within state. Every company specified in ORS 308.515, doing business as such within this state, shall establish and maintain at some fixed point within the state a principal office and shall maintain thereat a secretary or managing agent.
