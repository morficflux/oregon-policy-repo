# Definitions for ORS 417.705 to (ORS 417.705)

## At a glance

ORS 417.705 — Definitions for ORS 417.705 to. Chapter 417 (Chapter 417), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

417.705 Definitions for ORS 417.705 to 417.800. As used in ORS 417.705 to 417.800:

(1) “Early Learning Hub” means an entity designated under ORS 417.827.

(2) “Outcome” means the measure of a desired result.

(3) “Services for children and families” does not include services provided by the Department of Education or school districts that are related to curriculum or instructional programs.

(4) “Target” means a specific level of achievement desired for a specific time, expressed numerically. [1993 c.676 §30; 1999 c.1053 §3; 2003 c.148 §1; 2003 c.553 §1; 2012 c.37 §§43,79; 2013 c.728 §21]
