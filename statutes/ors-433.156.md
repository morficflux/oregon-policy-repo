# Enforcement of isolation or quarantine by law enforcement authorities (ORS 433.156)

## At a glance

ORS 433.156 — Enforcement of isolation or quarantine by law enforcement authorities. Chapter 433 (Chapter 433), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

433.156 Enforcement of isolation or quarantine by law enforcement authorities. All state and local law enforcement authorities shall cooperate with any officer authorized to impose isolation or quarantine in the enforcement thereof.
