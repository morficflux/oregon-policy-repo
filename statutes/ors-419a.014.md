# Reports by juvenile department (ORS 419A.014)

## At a glance

ORS 419A.014 — Reports by juvenile department. Chapter 419A (Chapter 419A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419A.014 Reports by juvenile department. The juvenile department of a county shall report annually to the Youth Development Council the frequency with which the following persons are held in preadjudicative detention and the duration of the detention:

(1) Out-of-state runaways, as defined in ORS 419C.156; or

(2) Runaway youths and adjudicated youths. [1993 c.33 §6; 2001 c.904 §2; 2001 c.905 §3; 2003 c.396 §3; 2019 c.382 §3; 2021 c.489 §32]
