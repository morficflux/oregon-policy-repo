# Exemption of journeyman plumber for certain activities (ORS 480.634)

## At a glance

ORS 480.634 — Exemption of journeyman plumber for certain activities. Chapter 480 (Chapter 480), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

480.634 Exemption of journeyman plumber for certain activities.

(1) A person who has a valid journeyman plumber license does not have to obtain a license under ORS 480.630

(2) to work as an employee of a business engaged in installing or replacing by nonwelded means a potable domestic water heater that: (a) Is not used for space heating; (b) Has a capacity that does not exceed 180 gallons; (c) Has a water temperature that does not exceed 210 degrees Fahrenheit; (d) Has a pressure that does not exceed 150 pounds per square inch gauge pressure; and (e) Has a heat input that does not exceed 750,000 Btu per hour.

(2) Subsection

(1) of this section does not allow construction, repair or alteration of the domestic potable water heater. [1991 c.518 §15; 2005 c.758 §38] 480.635 [1973 c.830 §5; 1983 c.676 §21; repealed by 2005 c.758 §56]
