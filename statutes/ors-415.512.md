## At a glance

ORS 415.512 — Fees; rules. Chapter 415 (Chapter 415), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

415.512 Fees; rules.

(1) The Oregon Health Authority shall prescribe by rule a fee to be paid under ORS 415.501 (3), proportionate to the size of the parties to the transaction, sufficient to reimburse the costs of administering ORS 415.501.

(2) Moneys received by the authority under this section shall be deposited to the Oregon Health Authority Fund established in ORS 413.101 to be used for carrying out ORS 415.501. [2021 c.615 §4]
