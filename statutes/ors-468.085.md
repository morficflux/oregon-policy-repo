# Sovereign immunity defense (ORS 468.085)

## At a glance

ORS 468.085 — Sovereign immunity defense. Chapter 468 (Chapter 468), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468.085 Sovereign immunity defense. The defense of sovereign immunity is applicable in any action or other proceeding brought under ORS 468.076 to 468.087 only to the extent that it would apply to a person injured or threatened with injury in Oregon. [1991 c.826 §8] Note: See note under 468.076.
