# Criminal penalty GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 700.995)

## At a glance

ORS 700.995 — Criminal penalty GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 700 (Chapter 700), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

700.995 Criminal penalty. Violation of ORS 700.020 is a Class B misdemeanor. [2003 c.547 §89] Note: 700.995 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 700 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. _______________
