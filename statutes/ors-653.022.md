# “Piece-rate-work-day” defined for ORS (ORS 653.022)

## At a glance

ORS 653.022 — “Piece-rate-work-day” defined for ORS. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.022 “Piece-rate-work-day” defined for ORS 653.020. As used in ORS 653.020 (1), “piece-rate-work-day” means any day during which an employee performs any agricultural labor on a piece-rate basis for not less than one hour. For the purposes of this section, “employee” does not include any individual employed by an employer in agriculture if such individual is the parent, spouse, child or other member of the employer’s immediate family. [1971 c.758 §2; 1989 c.446 §3]
