# Sports bribery (ORS 165.085)

## At a glance

ORS 165.085 — Sports bribery. Chapter 165 (Chapter 165), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

165.085 Sports bribery.

(1) A person commits the crime of sports bribery if the person: (a) Offers, confers or agrees to confer any benefit upon a sports participant with intent to influence the sports participant not to give the best effort of the sports participant in a sports contest; or (b) Offers, confers or agrees to confer any benefit upon a sports official with intent to influence the sports official to improperly perform duties of a sports official.

(2) Sports bribery is a Class C felony. [1971 c.743 §164]
