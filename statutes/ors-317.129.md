# Tax payments of long term enterprise zone facilities credit claimants (ORS 317.129)

## At a glance

ORS 317.129 — Tax payments of long term enterprise zone facilities credit claimants. Chapter 317 (Chapter 317), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

317.129 Tax payments of long term enterprise zone facilities credit claimants. Notwithstanding ORS 317.850, corporate income or excise tax payments of a taxpayer allowed a tax credit under ORS 317.124 shall be deposited in the Long Term Enterprise Zone Fund established in ORS 317.127, to the extent those payments do not exceed an amount estimated by the Department of Revenue to equal 30 percent of the tax credit threshold amount determined under ORS 317.124

(7) plus 30 percent of any remaining qualified tax liability of the taxpayer under ORS 317.124 after allowance of the credit. [2001 c.292 §10]
