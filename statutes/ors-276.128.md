# Fire and other insurance (ORS 276.128)

## At a glance

ORS 276.128 — Fire and other insurance. Chapter 276 (Public Facilities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

276.128 Fire and other insurance. The Director of the Oregon Department of Administrative Services, as an operating cost, shall insure the office buildings against fire and other hazards in such sums as required to protect the value of buildings. Such insurance shall be provided under the provisions of ORS chapter 278.
