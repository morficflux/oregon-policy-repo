## Full text

381.605 City construction, operation and financing of interstate bridges. Incorporated cities may construct, maintain and operate toll bridges over rivers and bodies of water forming interstate boundaries, and for such purpose may borrow money and issue and sell bridge bonds to evidence such indebtedness. 381.610 [Repealed by 1983 c.350 §250 (381.611 enacted in lieu of 381.610)]
