# Qualifications for directors (ORS 478.050)

## At a glance

ORS 478.050 — Qualifications for directors. Chapter 478 (Chapter 478), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

478.050 Qualifications for directors. A director of a district shall be an elector or an owner within the district. A district may determine, by ordinance that takes effect at least one year prior to the date of a regular district election, that firefighters of the district, volunteer or otherwise, and other district employees shall not serve as directors.
