## Full text

678.392 Nurse intern license; rules.

(1) The Oregon State Board of Nursing shall issue a nurse internship license to an applicant who: (a) Is a student who is enrolled in and has successfully completed at least one academic term of a nursing education program that: (A) Is approved by the board or accredited or approved by another state or United States territory as described under ORS 678.040 and approved by the board; and (B) Includes a clinical component; and (b) Meets any other requirements established by the board by rule.

(2) The board may require a licensure fee for a nurse internship license.

(3) The board may adopt by rule the duration for which a nurse internship license is valid and any requirements and fees for license renewal. [2022 c.38 §2; 2025 c.124 §16]
