# Civil penalty (ORS 435.256)

## At a glance

ORS 435.256 — Civil penalty. Chapter 435 (Chapter 435), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

435.256 Civil penalty. The Oregon Health Authority may impose a civil penalty against a hospital for each violation of the rules adopted under ORS 435.254. A civil penalty imposed under this section may not exceed $1,000 for each violation upon inspection or each substantiated complaint filed.
