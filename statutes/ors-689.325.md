# Required reports (ORS 689.325)

## At a glance

ORS 689.325 — Required reports. Chapter 689 (Chapter 689), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

689.325 Required reports.

(1) All registered drug outlets shall report to the State Board of Pharmacy the occurrence of any of the following changes within the times specified by the board by rule: (a) Permanent closing; (b) Change of ownership, management, location or pharmacist in charge; or (c) Any and all other matters and occurrences as the board may require by rule.

(2) Disasters, accidents and emergencies which may affect the strength, purity or labeling of drugs, medications, devices or other materials used in the diagnosis or the treatment of injury, illness and disease shall be immediately reported to the board. [1979 c.777 §32; 1993 c.571 §10] 689.330 [Amended by 1955 c.94 §1; 1957 c.598 §1; 1963 c.96 §6; 1969 c.514 §18; repealed by 1977 c.842 §45 and 1979 c.777 §59]
