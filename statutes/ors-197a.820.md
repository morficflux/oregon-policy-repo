# Housing Accountability and Production Office Fund Note: Definitions in 197.015 apply to ORS chapter 197A. GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 197A.820)

## At a glance

ORS 197A.820 — Housing Accountability and Production Office Fund Note: Definitions in 197.015 apply to ORS chapter 197A. GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 197A (Chapter 197A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

197A.820 Housing Accountability and Production Office Fund.

(1) The Housing Accountability and Production Office Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund.

(2) The Housing Accountability and Production Office Fund consists of moneys appropriated, allocated, deposited or transferred to the fund by the Legislative Assembly or otherwise.

(3) Interest earned by the fund shall be credited to the fund.

(4) Moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Department of Land Conservation and Development to administer the fund, to operate the Housing Accountability and Production Office and to implement ORS 197A.800 to 197A.820.
