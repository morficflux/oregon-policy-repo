# Prohibition against interfering with premium financing recommendation (ORS 746.470)

## At a glance

ORS 746.470 — Prohibition against interfering with premium financing recommendation. Chapter 746 (Chapter 746), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

746.470 Prohibition against interfering with premium financing recommendation. No insurer shall interfere in any way with the right of any person soliciting or procuring an application for its insurance policies to recommend to an insured any premium finance company. [1983 c.239 §5]
