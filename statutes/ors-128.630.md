# Application of ORS (ORS 128.630)

## At a glance

ORS 128.630 — Application of ORS. Chapter 128 (Chapter 128), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

128.630 Application of ORS 128.610 to 128.769.

(1) ORS 128.610 to 128.769 apply to all charitable organizations holding property for charitable purposes over which the state or the Attorney General has enforcement or supervisory powers.

(2) ORS 128.610 to 128.769 shall apply regardless of any contrary provisions of any instrument. [1963 c.583 §§2,14; 1971 c.589 §3; 1981 c.593 §3; 1985 c.729 §27; 1985 c.730 §6; 2007 c.571 §3]
