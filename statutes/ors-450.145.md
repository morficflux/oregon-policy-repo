# Entry of assessments in lien docket; lien docket as public record (ORS 450.145)

## At a glance

ORS 450.145 — Entry of assessments in lien docket; lien docket as public record. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.145 Entry of assessments in lien docket; lien docket as public record.

(1) When assessments are made they shall be entered into a permanent lien docket which shall be kept in the office of the district and wherein shall be shown the amount of each lien, property against which it has been assessed, the owner thereof and such additional information as is required to keep a permanent and complete record of the lien and the payments thereon.

(2) The lien docket shall be a public record kept by the secretary and shall be open to inspection during all business hours established by the district.
