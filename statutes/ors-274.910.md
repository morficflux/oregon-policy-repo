# Application and effect (ORS 274.910)

## At a glance

ORS 274.910 — Application and effect. Chapter 274 (Chapter 274), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

274.910 Application and effect.

(1) ORS 274.905 to 274.940 do not apply to submersible, submerged or new lands fronting upon the Pacific Ocean.

(2) Nothing contained in ORS 274.905 to 274.940 shall divest the State of Oregon of its rights to minerals, oil, gas and sulfur. [1963 c.376 §§2,9; 1967 c.421 §176; 2015 c.804 §2]
