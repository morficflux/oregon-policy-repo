# Right of action (ORS 673.825)

## At a glance

ORS 673.825 — Right of action. Chapter 673 (Chapter 673), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

673.825 Right of action.

(1) A person that claims to have suffered damage because an exchange facilitator violated a provision of ORS 673.815 or 673.820 has a right of action on the bonds or deposits described in ORS 673.810. An action under this subsection does not limit the remedies available to the person under this section or under other provisions of law.

(2) An exchange facilitator that violates a provision of ORS 673.815 or 673.820 is subject to an action for damages brought in a circuit court of this state. [2013 c.392 §6]
