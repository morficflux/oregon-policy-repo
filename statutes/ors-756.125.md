# Interference with commission equipment ENFORCEMENT AND REMEDIES (ORS 756.125)

## At a glance

ORS 756.125 — Interference with commission equipment ENFORCEMENT AND REMEDIES. Chapter 756 (Public Utility Commission), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

756.125 Interference with commission equipment. No person shall destroy, injure or interfere with any apparatus or appliance owned or operated by or in charge of the Public Utility Commission, or any apparatus or appliance sealed by the commission.
