# Courts having jurisdiction (ORS 686.290)

## At a glance

ORS 686.290 — Courts having jurisdiction. Chapter 686 (Chapter 686), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

686.290 Courts having jurisdiction. Justice courts and circuit courts have concurrent jurisdiction of prosecutions for the violation of this chapter. [Amended by 1979 c.562 §31; 1993 c.491 §11] 686.350 [1975 c.619 §7; 1987 c.651 §8; 1989 c.171 §78; 1993 c.491 §12; repealed by 2003 c.178 §11]
