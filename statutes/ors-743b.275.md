# Definitions for ORS 743B.275 to (ORS 743B.275)

## At a glance

ORS 743B.275 — Definitions for ORS 743B.275 to. Chapter 743B (Chapter 743B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743B.275 Definitions for ORS 743B.275 to 743B.285. As used in ORS 743B.275 to 743B.285:

(1) “In-network” means performed by a provider or provider group that has directly contracted with the insurer.

(2) “Out-of-network” means performed by a provider or provider group that has not contracted or has indirectly contracted with the insurer. [Formerly 743B.280] Note: 743B.275 to 743B.285 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 743B or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
