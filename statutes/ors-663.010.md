# “Collective bargaining” defined (ORS 663.010)

## At a glance

ORS 663.010 — “Collective bargaining” defined. Chapter 663 (Chapter 663), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

663.010 “Collective bargaining” defined. For the purposes of this chapter, “collective bargaining” is the performance of the mutual obligation of the employer and the representative of the employees to meet at reasonable times and confer in good faith with respect to wages, hours and other terms and conditions of employment, or the negotiation of an agreement, or any question arising thereunder, and the execution of a written contract incorporating any agreement reached if requested by either party. However, this obligation does not compel either party to agree to a proposal or require the making of a concession. [Formerly 662.515]
