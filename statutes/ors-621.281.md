# Suspension or revocation of licenses issued under ORS (ORS 621.281)

## At a glance

ORS 621.281 — Suspension or revocation of licenses issued under ORS. Chapter 621 (Chapter 621), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

621.281 Suspension or revocation of licenses issued under ORS 621.266. If a person licensed under ORS 621.266 does not fully and faithfully comply with all provisions of ORS 621.122, 621.266 or 621.276 or standards and regulations adopted under ORS 621.181 and 621.261, the license of the person may be revoked, suspended or limited.
