# Ranking of inventory according to risk; rules (ORS 465.410)

## At a glance

ORS 465.410 — Ranking of inventory according to risk; rules. Chapter 465 (Chapter 465), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

465.410 Ranking of inventory according to risk; rules. In addition to the rules adopted under ORS 465.405, the Environmental Quality Commission shall adopt by rule a procedure for ranking facilities on the inventory based on the short-term and long-term risks they pose to present and future public health, safety, welfare or the environment. [1989 c.485 §8]
