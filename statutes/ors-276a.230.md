# Definitions (ORS 276A.230)

## At a glance

ORS 276A.230 — Definitions. Chapter 276A (Information Technology), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

276A.230 Definitions. As used in ORS 276A.233 and 276A.236:

(1) “Executive department” has the meaning given that term in ORS 174.112.

(2) “Information technology” includes, but is not limited to, all present and future forms of hardware, software and services for data processing, office automation and telecommunications.

(3) “State agency” means a board, commission, department, division, office or other entity within the executive department of state government, except: (a) The Secretary of State; (b) The State Treasurer; (c) The Oregon State Lottery; and (d) A public university that is listed in ORS 352.002. [Formerly 184.473]
