# Revocation, suspension or nonrenewal of permit (ORS 390.666)

## At a glance

ORS 390.666 — Revocation, suspension or nonrenewal of permit. Chapter 390 (Chapter 390), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

390.666 Revocation, suspension or nonrenewal of permit. If the State Parks and Recreation Director finds that a person or governmental body holding a permit issued under ORS 390.650 or 390.657 is making an improvement on property within the ocean shore contrary to the conditions set out in the permit, the director may revoke, suspend or refuse to renew such permit. The director may revoke a permit only after giving notice and opportunity for a hearing as provided in ORS 183.415 to 183.430, 183.440 to 183.460 and 183.470. [1999 c.373 §14; 2025 c.494 §7] 390.668 [Formerly 274.090 and then 390.730; renumbered 390.678 in 1999]
