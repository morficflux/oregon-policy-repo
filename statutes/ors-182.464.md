# Financial review by Secretary of State (ORS 182.464)

## At a glance

ORS 182.464 — Financial review by Secretary of State. Chapter 182 (State Administrative Agencies Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

182.464 Financial review by Secretary of State. The Secretary of State shall enter into agreements with each of the boards to set appropriate financial review schedules for those boards. The financial review schedules shall be set to allow board compliance with ORS 182.472. In lieu of conducting a financial review, the Secretary of State may elect to contract for the financial review with, or accept a financial review conducted by, an independent certified public accountant. [1999 c.1084 §7; 2007 c.218 §1] Note: See note under 182.454.
