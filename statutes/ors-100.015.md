# Regulation by Real Estate Commissioner; rules (ORS 100.015)

## At a glance

ORS 100.015 — Regulation by Real Estate Commissioner; rules. Chapter 100 (Chapter 100), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

100.015 Regulation by Real Estate Commissioner; rules. The Real Estate Commissioner has the exclusive right to regulate the submission of property to the provisions of this chapter and may adopt such rules as are necessary for the administration of this chapter. [Formerly 94.333; 2023 c.223 §10] Note: 100.015 was added to and made a part of 100.635 to 100.910 by legislative action but was not added to any other series. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
