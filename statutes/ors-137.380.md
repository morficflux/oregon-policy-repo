# Discipline, treatment and employment of prisoners (ORS 137.380)

## At a glance

ORS 137.380 — Discipline, treatment and employment of prisoners. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.380 Discipline, treatment and employment of prisoners. A judgment of commitment to the custody of the Department of Corrections need only specify the duration of confinement as provided in ORS 137.120. Thereafter the manner of the confinement and the treatment and employment of a person shall be regulated and governed by whatever law is then in force prescribing the discipline, treatment and employment of persons committed. [Amended by 1955 c.32 §1; 1955 c.660 §17; 1959 c.687 §1; 1973 c.836 §268; 1987 c.320 §37]
