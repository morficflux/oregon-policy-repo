# Encouragement of federal actions (ORS 468B.164)

## At a glance

ORS 468B.164 — Encouragement of federal actions. Chapter 468B (Chapter 468B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468B.164 Encouragement of federal actions. In carrying out its coordination activities under ORS 468B.162, the Department of Environmental Quality shall encourage federal agency actions that are consistent with the water policies of the State of Oregon. [Formerly 536.112] Note: See note under 468B.150.
