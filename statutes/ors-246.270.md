# Office hours of county clerk on election days PRECINCTS (ORS 246.270)

## At a glance

ORS 246.270 — Office hours of county clerk on election days PRECINCTS. Chapter 246 (Administration of Election Laws; Vote Recording Systems), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

246.270 Office hours of county clerk on election days. On the day of any primary election, general election or special election held throughout the county, the county clerk’s office shall remain open for business pertaining to the election from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m. of the same day.
