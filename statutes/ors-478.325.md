# District may levy taxes for purposes of ORS (ORS 478.325)

## At a glance

ORS 478.325 — District may levy taxes for purposes of ORS. Chapter 478 (Chapter 478), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

478.325 District may levy taxes for purposes of ORS 478.335 to 478.370. Expenses incurred by a district in establishing programs or providing benefits authorized by ORS 478.335 to 478.370 are expenses for which a district may levy taxes as provided by ORS 478.410. [1963 c.366 §8]
