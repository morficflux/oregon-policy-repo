## At a glance

ORS 646A.452 — Enforcement by Attorney General (Warranties on Assistive Devices). Chapter 646A (Chapter 646A), 2025 Edition.

646A.452 Enforcement by Attorney General. The Attorney General may enforce violations of ORS 646A.430 to 646A.450 under ORS 646.608. [2007 c.685 §12] (Warranties on Assistive Devices)
