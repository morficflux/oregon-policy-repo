# Commercial fishing permit surcharges (ORS 508.288)

## At a glance

ORS 508.288 — Commercial fishing permit surcharges. Chapter 508 (Chapter 508), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

508.288 Commercial fishing permit surcharges. In addition to the fees otherwise prescribed by law, the issuer of each of the following permits shall charge and collect the following surcharges each time the permit is issued:

(1) Ocean Troll Salmon Fishery permit issued under ORS 508.816, $65.

(2) Columbia River Gillnet Fishery permit issued under ORS 508.790, $74. [1989 c.512 §6; 1991 c.184 §2; 1997 c.8 §10; 2003 c.643 §2; 2009 c.765 §3; 2019 c.458 §5] 508.290 [Formerly 508.230; 1967 c.322 §2; repealed by 1975 c.130 §2 and 1975 c.253 §40]
