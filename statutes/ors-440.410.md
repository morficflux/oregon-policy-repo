# Annual audit PORT HOSPITALS (ORS 440.410)

## At a glance

ORS 440.410 — Annual audit PORT HOSPITALS. Chapter 440 (Chapter 440), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

440.410 Annual audit. An annual audit of the district shall be made by an auditor. A true and complete copy of the auditor’s report of such audit shall be filed in the office of the county clerk of the principal county, as defined by ORS 198.705, and shall remain a public record therein.
