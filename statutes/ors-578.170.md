# Commission exempt from certain financial administration laws (ORS 578.170)

## At a glance

ORS 578.170 — Commission exempt from certain financial administration laws. Chapter 578 (Chapter 578), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

578.170 Commission exempt from certain financial administration laws. Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, ORS 291.026, 291.201 to 291.222, 291.232 to 291.260, 291.322 to 291.334, 292.210 to 292.250, 293.260 to 293.280, 293.295 to 293.346 and 293.590 to 293.640 do not apply to the Oregon Wheat Commission or to the administration and enforcement of this chapter. [1959 c.596 §50] 578.180 [1959 c.596 §83; repealed by 2003 c.604 §73 (578.181 enacted in lieu of 578.180)]
