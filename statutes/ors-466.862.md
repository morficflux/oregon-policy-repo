# License required to provide heating oil tank services (ORS 466.862)

## At a glance

ORS 466.862 — License required to provide heating oil tank services. Chapter 466 (Chapter 466), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

466.862 License required to provide heating oil tank services. No person shall perform or offer to perform heating oil tank services without first obtaining the license required under ORS 466.868. [1999 c.979 §3] 466.865 [1997 c.838 §4; repealed by 1999 c.880 §8]
