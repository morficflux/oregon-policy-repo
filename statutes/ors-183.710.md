# Definitions for ORS 183.710 to (ORS 183.710)

## At a glance

ORS 183.710 — Definitions for ORS 183.710 to. Chapter 183 (Administrative Procedures Act), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

183.710 Definitions for ORS 183.710 to 183.730. As used in ORS 183.710 to 183.730, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “Interim committee” means a committee of the Legislative Assembly that is scheduled to meet when the Legislative Assembly is not in session and that has subject-matter jurisdiction over the state agency that has adopted a rule, as set forth in the subject-matter jurisdiction list developed under ORS 183.724.

(2) “Rule” has the meaning given that term in ORS 183.310.

(3) “State agency” means an agency as defined in ORS 183.310. [Formerly 171.705; 2009 c.81 §1] Note: 183.710 to 183.730 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 183 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
