# Restrictions on issuing certificates (ORS 822.022)

## At a glance

ORS 822.022 — Restrictions on issuing certificates. Chapter 822 (Chapter 822), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

822.022 Restrictions on issuing certificates. The Department of Transportation may not issue a vehicle dealer certificate authorizing a person to deal exclusively in motorcycles, mopeds, Class I all-terrain vehicles or snowmobiles or any combination of those vehicles. [2017 c.530 §3] Note: 822.022 was added to and made a part of the Oregon Vehicle Code by legislative action but was not added to ORS chapter 822 or any series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
