# Maximum maritime pilot license fee (ORS 776.357)

## At a glance

ORS 776.357 — Maximum maritime pilot license fee. Chapter 776 (Maritime Pilots and Pilotage), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

776.357 Maximum maritime pilot license fee.

(1) The maximum annual license fee for a maritime pilot is $2,500 for the biennium beginning July 1, 2007.

(2) The Oregon Board of Maritime Pilots shall adjust the amount of the maximum annual license fee for a maritime pilot for each subsequent biennium by a proportional amount equal to the percentage change in the 24-month period prior to the beginning of the biennium in the Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers, West Region (All Items), as published by the Bureau of Labor Statistics of the United States Department of Labor. [2007 c.621 §2; 2019 c.57 §34] Note: 776.357 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 776 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 776.360 [Repealed by 1957 c.448 §27]
