## At a glance

## Full text

267.410 Certain districts authorized to impose employer payroll tax on state agencies and political subdivisions. ORS 267.300, 267.380 and 267.410 to 267.430 apply only to a mass transit district situated in a metropolitan statistical area with a population exceeding 400,000. [1989 c.869 §7; 2009 c.11 §31]
