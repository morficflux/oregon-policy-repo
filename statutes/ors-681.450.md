# Executive director (ORS 681.450)

## At a glance

ORS 681.450 — Executive director. Chapter 681 (Chapter 681), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

681.450 Executive director. The executive director of the State Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology may, within guidelines established by the Oregon Department of Administrative Services, hire employees and enter into contracts for specific services necessary to carry out the administration of this chapter. [Formerly 694.495; 1995 c.280 §21; 2005 c.698 §19; 2009 c.756 §45]
