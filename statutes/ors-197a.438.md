# Restriction on parks in commercial or industrial zones (ORS 197A.438)

## At a glance

ORS 197A.438 — Restriction on parks in commercial or industrial zones. Chapter 197A (Chapter 197A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

197A.438 Restriction on parks in commercial or industrial zones.

(1) Except as provided by ORS 446.105, a mobile home or manufactured dwelling park shall not be established on land, within an urban growth boundary, which is planned or zoned for commercial or industrial use.

(2) Notwithstanding the provisions of subsection

(1) of this section, if no other access is available, access to a mobile home or manufactured dwelling park may be provided through a commercial or industrial zone. [Formerly 197.490]
