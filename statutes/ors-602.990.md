# Penalties (ORS 602.990)

## At a glance

ORS 602.990 — Penalties. Chapter 602 (Chapter 602), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

602.990 Penalties. Subject to ORS 153.022, violation of any of the provisions of ORS 602.090 or 602.190, or any rule adopted pursuant thereto, is a Class B misdemeanor. [Amended by 1993 c.350 §5; 1999 c.1051 §320; 2011 c.597 §245] _______________
