# Transfer of case; transportation of child or ward (Protective Custody) (ORS 419B.135)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.135 — Transfer of case; transportation of child or ward (Protective Custody). Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.135 Transfer of case; transportation of child or ward. If the child or ward who is the subject of the proceeding is, at the time of a transfer or temporary transfer provided for in ORS 419B.127, 419B.130 and 419B.132, in shelter care or for other reason needs transportation to the other county, the county in which the child or ward resides shall make such order or provision for the transportation and safekeeping of the child or ward as is appropriate in the circumstances, including an order directing any peace officer of the county in which the child or ward resides to transfer the child or ward in the manner directed. [1993 c.33 §60; 2003 c.396 §39] (Protective Custody)
