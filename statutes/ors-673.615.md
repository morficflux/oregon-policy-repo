# Prohibited acts; preparation of tax returns; refund anticipation loans (Licensing and Certification) (ORS 673.615)

## At a glance

ORS 673.615 — Prohibited acts; preparation of tax returns; refund anticipation loans (Licensing and Certification). Chapter 673 (Chapter 673), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

673.615 Prohibited acts; preparation of tax returns; refund anticipation loans. Except as otherwise provided in ORS 673.605 to 673.740:

(1) A person may not prepare or advise or assist in the preparation of personal income tax returns for another and for valuable consideration or represent that the person is so engaged unless the person is licensed as a tax consultant under ORS 673.605 to 673.740.

(2) A tax preparer may prepare or advise or assist in the preparation of tax returns only under the supervision of a tax consultant, or a person described in ORS 673.610

(2) or (4), and subject to such conditions and limitations as the State Board of Tax Practitioners by rule may impose.

(3) A registered tax aide may assist a tax consultant in the preparation of tax returns only under the supervision of a tax consultant and subject to such conditions and limitations as the board by rule may impose.

(4) A person may not be a facilitator of a refund anticipation loan without first being issued a license as a tax consultant or tax preparer under ORS 673.640.

(5) A tax consultant may employ and supervise no more than two registered tax aides at the same time under the conditions and limitations prescribed by the board by rule. [1973 c.387 §2; 1975 c.464 §6; 1977 c.100 §4; 2005 c.331 §5; 2025 c.347 §5] (Licensing and Certification)
