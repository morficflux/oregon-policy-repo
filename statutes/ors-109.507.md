## At a glance

## Full text

109.507 Access to Department of Human Services records required; access to private agency records discretionary.

(1) The Department of Human Services shall allow an Oregon licensed adoption agency or a third party individual or entity with whom the department or agency has contracted to examine confidential adoption records maintained by the department as part of a search conducted under ORS 109.502.

(2) A private adoption agency may allow the department, an Oregon licensed adoption agency or a third party individual or entity to examine confidential adoption records maintained by the agency as part of a search conducted under ORS 109.502. [1993 c.410 §8; 1995 c.730 §16; 2015 c.200 §15] Note: See note under 109.425. 109.510 [Amended by 1973 c.827 §14; 2005 c.22 §91; renumbered 109.621 in 2025] DONOR REGISTRY
