# Applicability (ORS 837.390)

## At a glance

ORS 837.390 — Applicability. Chapter 837 (Chapter 837), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

837.390 Applicability. ORS 837.300 to 837.390 and 837.995 and section 11, chapter 686, Oregon Laws 2013, do not apply to the Armed Forces of the United States as defined in ORS 352.313. [2013 c.686 §16] Note: See note under 837.300. PENALTIES
