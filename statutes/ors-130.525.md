# Applicability of ORS 130.530, (ORS 130.525)

## At a glance

ORS 130.525 — Applicability of ORS 130.530,. Chapter 130 (Chapter 130), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

130.525 Applicability of ORS 130.530, 130.535 and 130.540 to 130.575.

(1) ORS 130.530 and 130.535 apply only to a trust, or portion of a trust: (a) That comes into existence during the settlor’s lifetime; and (b) Is a revocable trust on the occurrence of any of the events described in ORS 130.530 or 130.535.

(2) ORS 130.540 to 130.575 apply only to a trust, or a portion of a trust, that comes into existence during the settlor’s lifetime and that was a revocable trust at the time of the settlor’s death. [2005 c.348 §110; 2013 c.529 §10]
