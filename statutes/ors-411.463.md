# Availability of information on all licensed healing arts (ORS 411.463)

## At a glance

ORS 411.463 — Availability of information on all licensed healing arts. Chapter 411 (Chapter 411), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

411.463 Availability of information on all licensed healing arts. When giving information concerning medical assistance, the Oregon Health Authority and the Department of Human Services shall make available to applicants or recipients materials which include at least a listing of all the healing arts licensed in this state.
