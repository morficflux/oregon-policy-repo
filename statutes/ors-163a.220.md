# Internet website (ORS 163A.220)

## At a glance

ORS 163A.220 — Internet website. Chapter 163A (Chapter 163A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

163A.220 Internet website. The Department of State Police shall consider:

(1) Contracting with a private vendor to build and maintain the website required by ORS 163A.215 (2)(a).

(2) Adding links on the website required by ORS 163A.215 (2)(a) that connect to other sex offender websites run by Oregon counties and by the federal government. [Formerly 181.836]
