# Municipally owned utility exempt from requirements (ORS 757.969)

## At a glance

ORS 757.969 — Municipally owned utility exempt from requirements. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.969 Municipally owned utility exempt from requirements. ORS 757.963 and 757.995 do not apply to municipally owned utilities organized under ORS chapter 225. [2021 c.592 §6b] Note: See note under 757.968.
