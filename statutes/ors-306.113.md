# Legislative findings (ORS 306.113)

## At a glance

ORS 306.113 — Legislative findings. Chapter 306 (Chapter 306), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

306.113 Legislative findings. The Legislative Assembly finds that for the property tax system to function appropriately it is essential that administration be results-based, innovative and efficient. Any direction to, or review of, county administration by the State of Oregon shall carry out this finding. [1997 c.782 §1] Note: 306.113 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 306 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
