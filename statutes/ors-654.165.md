# Employees not required to work bare-handed or rubber-gloved on high voltage lines (ORS 654.165)

## At a glance

ORS 654.165 — Employees not required to work bare-handed or rubber-gloved on high voltage lines. Chapter 654 (Chapter 654), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

654.165 Employees not required to work bare-handed or rubber-gloved on high voltage lines. No employer shall require an employee to perform bare-handed or rubber-gloved work on a live electrical line with a voltage of 5,000 volts or greater. [1991 c.549 §2] Note: 654.165 was added to and made a part of 654.001 to 654.295 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
