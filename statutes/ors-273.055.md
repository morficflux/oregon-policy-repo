# Power to acquire and dispose of real property (ORS 273.055)

## At a glance

ORS 273.055 — Power to acquire and dispose of real property. Chapter 273 (Chapter 273), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

273.055 Power to acquire and dispose of real property. If it finds it advisable in carrying out its duties, the Department of State Lands in the name of the State of Oregon may take title to real and personal property in fee simple or absolutely, in trust or under such other conditions as it considers advisable, and may convey title thereto or execute agreements necessary to carry out its duties. [Formerly 273.710; 1969 c.594 §3]
