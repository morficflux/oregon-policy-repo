# Conviction or acquittal of one or more of several defendants (ORS 136.470)

## At a glance

ORS 136.470 — Conviction or acquittal of one or more of several defendants. Chapter 136 (Chapter 136), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

136.470 Conviction or acquittal of one or more of several defendants. Upon an accusatory instrument against several defendants, any one or more may be convicted or acquitted. [Formerly 136.670]
