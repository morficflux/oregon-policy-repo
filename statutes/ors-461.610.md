## Full text

461.610 Transfer of winning tickets or shares; rules.

(1) Subject to subsection

(2) of this section, a person may not: (a) Sell, or offer to sell, a winning ticket or share; (b) Purchase, or offer to purchase, a winning ticket or share; or (c) Claim, or offer to claim, a winning ticket or share for another person for compensation.

(2) This section does not apply to any prize that the Oregon State Lottery Commission authorizes lottery game retailers to pay under ORS 461.250.

(3) The commission shall adopt rules necessary to enforce the provisions of this section. [2025 c.46 §2]
