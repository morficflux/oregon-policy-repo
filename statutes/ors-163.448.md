# Definitions for ORS 163.452 and 163.454 (ORS 163.448)

## At a glance

## Full text

163.448 Definitions for ORS 163.452 and 163.454. As used in ORS 163.452 and 163.454, “correctional facility” has the meaning given that term in ORS 162.135. [2005 c.488 §2] 163.450 [Repealed by 1971 c.743 §432]
