## At a glance

## Full text

404.350 Rescue of companion animal.

(1) A person engaged in search and rescue operations may make every practicable attempt under the circumstances to rescue a companion animal.

(2) This section does not impose liability on or expand liability of a person engaged in search and rescue operations. [Formerly 401.273] OUTDOOR RECREATION SEARCH AND RESCUE CARDS
