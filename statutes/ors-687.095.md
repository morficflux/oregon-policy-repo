# Duty to report prohibited conduct (State Board) (ORS 687.095)

## At a glance

ORS 687.095 — Duty to report prohibited conduct (State Board). Chapter 687 (Chapter 687), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

687.095 Duty to report prohibited conduct. Unless state or federal laws relating to confidentiality or the protection of health information prohibit disclosure, a massage therapist who has reasonable cause to believe that a licensee of another board has engaged in prohibited conduct as defined in ORS 676.150 shall report the prohibited conduct in the manner provided in ORS 676.150.
