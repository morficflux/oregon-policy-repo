# Authority to require general liability insurance (ORS 475C.165)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.165 — Authority to require general liability insurance. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.165 Authority to require general liability insurance. As is necessary to protect the public health and safety, the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission may require a licensee to maintain general liability insurance in an amount that the commission determines is reasonably affordable and available for the purpose of protecting the licensee against damages resulting from a cause of action related to activities undertaken pursuant to the license held by the licensee. [Formerly 475B.163]
