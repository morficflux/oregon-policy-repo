# Termination if property held for future development or other purpose; additional taxes (ORS 307.530)

## At a glance

ORS 307.530 — Termination if property held for future development or other purpose; additional taxes. Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.530 Termination if property held for future development or other purpose; additional taxes. An exemption granted under ORS 307.515 to 307.523 shall be immediately terminated and additional taxes imposed as provided in ORS 307.531 if the exempt property:

(1) Is being held for future development of low income rental housing; and

(2) Is used for any purpose other than the provision of low income rental housing. [1997 c.752 §10]
