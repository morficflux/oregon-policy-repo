# Appointment of agent to return fugitive from this state who waives extradition (ORS 133.837)

## At a glance

## Full text

133.837 Appointment of agent to return fugitive from this state who waives extradition. In the event a fugitive from this state shall waive extradition, an agent or agents to secure the return of the fugitive may be appointed by the district attorney of the county in which the offense was committed, and the account of such agent or agents embracing necessary expenses incurred in performing the service, shall be audited and paid in the same manner as accounts presented under ORS 133.857. [Formerly 147.235]
