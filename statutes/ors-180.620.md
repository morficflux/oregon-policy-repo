# Investigators to have authority of peace officers (ORS 180.620)

## At a glance

ORS 180.620 — Investigators to have authority of peace officers. Chapter 180 (Attorney General; Department of Justice), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

180.620 Investigators to have authority of peace officers. All investigators employed pursuant to ORS 180.600 to 180.630 shall have all statutory powers and authority of peace officers and police officers of the State of Oregon. [1977 c.754 §3]
