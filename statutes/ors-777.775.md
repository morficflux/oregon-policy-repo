# Status of export trading corporation; application of certain laws (ORS 777.775)

## At a glance

ORS 777.775 — Status of export trading corporation; application of certain laws. Chapter 777 (Chapter 777), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

777.775 Status of export trading corporation; application of certain laws.

(1) An export trading corporation is not a contracting agency for the purposes of ORS 279A.055, 279A.065, 279A.070, 279A.075, 279A.100, 279A.105, 279A.120, 279C.005, 279C.100 to 279C.125, 279C.300 to 279C.470 and 279C.570 and ORS chapter 279B, except ORS 279B.025, 279B.235, 279B.240, 279B.270, 279B.275 and 279B.280.

(2) An export trading corporation is not a public employer for the purposes of ORS chapters 238 and 238A. [1983 c.200 §16; 2003 c.733 §81; 2003 c.794 §331]
