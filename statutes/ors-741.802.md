# Authority to produce written informational materials (Penalties) (ORS 741.802)

## At a glance

ORS 741.802 — Authority to produce written informational materials (Penalties). Chapter 741 (Chapter 741), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

741.802 Authority to produce written informational materials. The Oregon Health Authority shall produce written materials containing information for consumers about the requirements for paying the premiums for qualified health plans. The authority shall distribute the materials to health care providers upon request. [2015 c.580 §4; 2021 c.569 §35] (Penalties)
