# Definitions BUREAU OF LABOR AND INDUSTRIES; COMMISSIONER (ORS 651.010)

## At a glance

ORS 651.010 — Definitions BUREAU OF LABOR AND INDUSTRIES; COMMISSIONER. Chapter 651 (Bureau of Labor and Industries), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

651.010 Definitions. As used in this chapter, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “Bureau” means the Bureau of Labor and Industries.

(2) “Commissioner” means the Commissioner of the Bureau of Labor and Industries. [Amended by 1979 c.659 §1; 1987 c.158 §123] BUREAU OF LABOR AND INDUSTRIES; COMMISSIONER
