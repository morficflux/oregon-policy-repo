# Issuance of relocation credits for removal of sign (ORS 377.762)

## At a glance

ORS 377.762 — Issuance of relocation credits for removal of sign. Chapter 377 (Chapter 377), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

377.762 Issuance of relocation credits for removal of sign. The Department of Transportation shall issue a relocation credit upon the owner’s request if a sign is removed, the owner has lost the lease for that sign site and the sign and permit meet the requirements of ORS 377.700 to 377.844. [2009 c.463 §3] Note: See note under 377.759.
