# Agreements with United States to perform security functions (ORS 190.112)

## At a glance

ORS 190.112 — Agreements with United States to perform security functions. Chapter 190 (Chapter 190), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

190.112 Agreements with United States to perform security functions. A public body as defined in ORS 174.109 may enter into an agreement with the United States to perform security functions at a military installation or facility in the United States and to receive payment for performing the functions. [2003 c.6 §1] Note: 190.112 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 190 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
