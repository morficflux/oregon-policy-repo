# Employment districts INFORMATION SYSTEMS (ORS 657.725)

## At a glance

ORS 657.725 — Employment districts INFORMATION SYSTEMS. Chapter 657 (Chapter 657), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

657.725 Employment districts. The Director of the Employment Department may divide the state into such number of employment districts as the director finds necessary and maintain a district office in each of said districts. INFORMATION SYSTEMS
