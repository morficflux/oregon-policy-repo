## Full text

737.330 Contracts to comply with effective filings; exception.

(1) No insurer shall make or issue a policy except in accordance with the filings which are in effect for the insurer as provided in this chapter.

(2) This section does not apply to policies for inland marine risks as to which filings are not required. [Amended by 1967 c.359 §312; 1969 c.690 §6] 737.335 [Repealed by 1967 c.359 §704]
