# Rules (Oregon Housing Needs Analysis) (ORS 456.599)

## At a glance

ORS 456.599 — Rules (Oregon Housing Needs Analysis). Chapter 456 (Chapter 456), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

456.599 Rules. The Housing and Community Services Department may adopt by rule policies and procedures for the administration and enforcement of ORS 456.594 to 456.599. [2017 c.727 §13] Note: See note under 456.594. 456.600 [1971 c.505 §7; 1977 c.98 §1; 1979 c.327 §17; renumbered 456.571] (Oregon Housing Needs Analysis)
