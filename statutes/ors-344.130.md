# Cooperation by district school boards to establish career and technical education (ORS 344.130)

## At a glance

ORS 344.130 — Cooperation by district school boards to establish career and technical education. Chapter 344 (Chapter 344), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

344.130 Cooperation by district school boards to establish career and technical education. Any district school board may cooperate with the State Board of Education in establishment of career and technical education schools or classes giving instruction in agricultural subjects, the trade or industrial subjects, or in home economics subjects, and may use any moneys raised by public taxation in the same manner as moneys for other school purposes are used for the maintenance and support of public schools. [Amended by 1993 c.45 §250; 2009 c.94 §20] 344.140 [Repealed by 1979 c.570 §4]
