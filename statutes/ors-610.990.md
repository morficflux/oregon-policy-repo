# Penalties (ORS 610.990)

## At a glance

ORS 610.990 — Penalties. Chapter 610 (Chapter 610), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

610.990 Penalties.

(1) Violation of ORS 610.045 is a Class A violation.

(2) Violation of ORS 610.050 is a Class A misdemeanor. [Amended by 1959 c.299 §3; subsection

(2) enacted as 1959 c.299 §2(3); 1971 c.743 §401; 1981 c.95 §4; 1999 c.1051 §209; 2011 c.597 §251] CHAPTERS 611 TO 615 [Reserved for expansion] _______________
