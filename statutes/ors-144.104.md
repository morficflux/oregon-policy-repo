## Full text

144.104 Supervisory authority; revising conditions.

(1) Upon release from prison, the person shall be supervised by the Department of Corrections or other supervisory authority.

(2) During the period of post-prison supervision, the supervisory authority may adjust the level of supervision and recommend to the State Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision revisions to the conditions of supervision appropriate to the released person’s conduct in the community. [1989 c.790 §§33,34; 1995 c.423 §24] Note: See note under 144.096. 144.105 [1967 c.560 §4; repealed by 1969 c.597 §281]
