# International application of ORS (ORS 109.714)

## At a glance

ORS 109.714 — International application of ORS. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.714 International application of ORS 109.701 to 109.834.

(1) A court of this state shall treat a foreign country as if it were a state of the United States for the purpose of applying ORS 109.701 to 109.771.

(2) Except as otherwise provided in subsection

(3) of this section, a child custody determination made in a foreign country under factual circumstances in substantial conformity with the jurisdictional standards of ORS 109.701 to 109.834 must be recognized and enforced under ORS 109.774 to 109.827.

(3) A court of this state need not apply ORS 109.701 to 109.834 if the child custody law of a foreign country violates fundamental principles of human rights. [1999 c.649 §5] Note: See note under 109.701.
