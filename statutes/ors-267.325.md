## At a glance

ORS 267.325 — Lease purchase agreements. Chapter 267 (Chapter 267), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

267.325 Lease purchase agreements. For the purpose of financing the design, engineering, acquisition, construction and installation of any mass transit system, a district may enter into lease purchase agreements for such term of years as the board may determine. The lease purchase payments payable by a district under any such lease purchase agreement may be payable out of any funds of the district, including without limitation any operating revenues, tax revenues or grants. [1989 c.899 §2]
