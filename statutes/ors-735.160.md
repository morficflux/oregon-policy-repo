# Business name (ORS 735.160)

## At a glance

ORS 735.160 — Business name. Chapter 735 (Chapter 735), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

735.160 Business name. A captive insurer may assume a business name only if consistent with the provisions of ORS 731.430. [2012 c.84 §7] Note: See note under 735.150.
