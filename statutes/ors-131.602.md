# Prohibited conduct for purposes of instrumentalities of crime (ORS 131.602)

## At a glance

ORS 131.602 — Prohibited conduct for purposes of instrumentalities of crime. Chapter 131 (Preliminary Provisions; Limitations; Jurisdiction; Venue; Criminal Forfeiture; Crime Prevention), 2025 Edition.
