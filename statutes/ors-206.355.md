# Unauthorized use of uniform prohibited PENALTIES (ORS 206.355)

## At a glance

ORS 206.355 — Unauthorized use of uniform prohibited PENALTIES. Chapter 206 (Chapter 206), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

206.355 Unauthorized use of uniform prohibited. No person other than a county sheriff, person designated by a county sheriff, or regularly salaried sheriff’s deputy shall wear, use, copy or imitate in any manner the uniform of that county sheriff. [1979 c.492 §2] 206.360 [1967 c.258 §§1,2,3,4; repealed by 1979 c.492 §1] 206.990 [Repealed by 1979 c.492 §1] PENALTIES
