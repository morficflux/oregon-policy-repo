# Rewards for information (ORS 314.855)

## At a glance

ORS 314.855 — Rewards for information. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.855 Rewards for information. The Department of Revenue may pay rewards to persons, other than officers or employees of the department, furnishing information that leads to the recovery of tax from other persons guilty of violating the provisions of income tax laws. Such rewards shall not exceed 10 percent of the net amount of tax, penalty and interest recovered by suit or otherwise and shall be paid only in cases where such evasions of tax would not be disclosed by the audit of returns or from other information available to the department. [1957 c.632 §38 (enacted in lieu of 316.760 and 317.550)] 314.860 [1979 c.690 §18; 1997 c.170 §27; 2013 c.688 §28; repealed by 2015 c.348 §1]
