# National Guard assistance for inspections (ORS 571.284)

## At a glance

ORS 571.284 — National Guard assistance for inspections. Chapter 571 (Chapter 571), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

571.284 National Guard assistance for inspections. The Governor may order that the Oregon National Guard provide assistance and support to the State Department of Agriculture and law enforcement in carrying out inspections of industrial hemp crops under ORS 571.281

(7) and other duties of the department related to the enforcement of ORS 571.260 to 571.348. [2024 c.16 §6] Note: See note under 571.260.
