# Powers conveyed to public bodies (General Obligation Bonds) (ORS 287A.010)

## At a glance

ORS 287A.010 — Powers conveyed to public bodies (General Obligation Bonds). Chapter 287A (Chapter 287A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

287A.010 Powers conveyed to public bodies. The powers conveyed to public bodies by ORS 287A.001 to 287A.380 are in addition to any other powers possessed by public bodies and do not limit those other powers. [2007 c.783 §68] (General Obligation Bonds)
