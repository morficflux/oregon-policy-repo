# Statute of limitation (ORS 180.765)

## At a glance

ORS 180.765 — Statute of limitation. Chapter 180 (Attorney General; Department of Justice), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

180.765 Statute of limitation. An action under ORS 180.760 must be brought within five years after the date that the Attorney General discovers the violation of ORS 180.755. In no event may an action under ORS 180.760 be brought more than 10 years after the date on which the violation is committed. [2009 c.292 §4; 2023 c.104 §2] Note: See note under 180.750.
