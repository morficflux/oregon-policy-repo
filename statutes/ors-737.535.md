# Withholding or giving false information prohibited (ORS 737.535)

## At a glance

ORS 737.535 — Withholding or giving false information prohibited. Chapter 737 (Chapter 737), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

737.535 Withholding or giving false information prohibited. No person shall willfully withhold information from or knowingly give false or misleading information to the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services, to any statistical agency designated by the director, to any rating organization, or to any insurer, which will affect the rates or premiums chargeable under this chapter. [Amended by 1967 c.359 §332; 1969 c.690 §24] 737.540 [Repealed by 1967 c.359 §704]
