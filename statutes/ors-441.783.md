# Information to be posted to Oregon Health Authority’s website (ORS 441.783)

## At a glance

ORS 441.783 — Information to be posted to Oregon Health Authority’s website. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.783 Information to be posted to Oregon Health Authority’s website. The Oregon Health Authority shall post on a website maintained by the authority:

(1) The hospital staffing plans received by the authority under ORS 441.761 to 441.795;

(2) Any report, described in ORS 441.791 (2)(d), made pursuant to an investigation of a complaint for which the authority issued a warning or imposed a civil penalty under ORS 441.792;

(3) Any order suspending or revoking the license of a hospital pursuant to ORS 441.793; and

(4) Any other matter recommended by the Nurse Staffing Advisory Board established under ORS 441.761. [Formerly 441.177] Note: See note under 441.761.
