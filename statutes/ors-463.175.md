## Full text

463.175 Objection to event by city or county; filing with commission. Nothing in this chapter prevents a county or city from objecting to the holding of, or participating in, an unarmed combat sports or entertainment wrestling event. Objections must be filed in writing with the Oregon State Athletic Commission. [1987 c.789 §13; 1991 c.211 §6; 2007 c.585 §15; 2017 c.235 §14] 463.180 [Repealed by 1987 c.789 §24]
