## At a glance

## Full text

466.030 Designation of classes of facilities subject to certain provisions. The Environmental Quality Commission may, by rule, designate classes of facilities designed to treat or dispose of hazardous waste or PCB that shall be subject to the provisions of ORS 466.025 to 466.065, 466.250, 466.255

(2) and

(3) and 466.260 to 466.320. [1985 c.670 §8]
