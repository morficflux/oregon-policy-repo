# Compensation and expenses (Sign Language Interpreters) (ORS 185.200)

## At a glance

ORS 185.200 — Compensation and expenses (Sign Language Interpreters). Chapter 185 (Oregon Disabilities Commission; Commissions on Hispanic Affairs, Black Affairs, and Asian and Pacific Islander Affairs; Commission for Women), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

185.200 Compensation and expenses. Members of the Oregon Disabilities Commission shall receive compensation and expenses as provided in ORS 292.495. Travel and per diem for state employees shall be compensated by the commission. [1983 c.726 §11; 1989 c.224 §24] 185.220 [1991 c.365 §3; repealed by 2005 c.663 §14] (Sign Language Interpreters)
