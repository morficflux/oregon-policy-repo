## At a glance

## Full text

734.710 Administration of delinquency proceeding claims and expenses; application of ORS 734.014, 734.026 and 734.110 to 734.440 to insurers.

(1) In any delinquency proceeding involving a member insurer, the claims and expenses of the insurer shall be administered as provided in ORS 734.510 to 734.710.

(2) Except as otherwise provided in ORS 734.510 to 734.710, ORS 734.014, 734.026 and 734.110 to 734.440 apply to a member insurer. [1971 c.616 §23] OREGON LIFE AND HEALTH INSURANCE GUARANTY ASSOCIATION
