# Maintenance of facilities and equipment; standards (ORS 632.756)

## At a glance

ORS 632.756 — Maintenance of facilities and equipment; standards. Chapter 632 (Chapter 632), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

632.756 Maintenance of facilities and equipment; standards. The plant facilities and equipment utilized in processing eggs or egg products shall be constructed, maintained and utilized in accordance with the rules promulgated under the federal Act or promulgated by the State Department of Agriculture. [1975 c.748 §9 (enacted in lieu of 632.755)] 632.760 [Formerly 632.097; repealed by 1975 c.748 §10 (632.761 enacted in lieu of 632.760)]
