# Finance elections PENALTIES (ORS 267.650)

## At a glance

ORS 267.650 — Finance elections PENALTIES. Chapter 267 (Chapter 267), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

267.650 Finance elections. Elections for the purpose of voting on the question of borrowing funds by issuance and sale of general obligation bonds or revenue bonds shall be called by the governing body. [1974 c.9 §15] PENALTIES
