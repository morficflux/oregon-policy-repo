# Restitution ordered to insurance carrier (ORS 137.102)

## At a glance

ORS 137.102 — Restitution ordered to insurance carrier. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.102 Restitution ordered to insurance carrier. Notwithstanding ORS 137.106, 137.108, 419C.450 and 811.706, upon collection, 50 percent of awards of restitution as defined in ORS 137.103, awards of restitution under ORS 419C.450 and money awards made under ORS 811.706 ordered to an insurance carrier shall be paid to the Department of Justice for purposes of victim services funding. [2025 c.360 §2]
