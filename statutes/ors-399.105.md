# Militia unit facilities (ORS 399.105)

## At a glance

ORS 399.105 — Militia unit facilities. Chapter 399 (Chapter 399), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

399.105 Militia unit facilities. Unless furnished by the United States, the state shall provide adequate armory accommodations, bases, camps, target ranges and other facilities and shall maintain such facilities for units of the Oregon National Guard allotted to the state under the laws of the United States, accepted by the Governor and organized under the authority of this chapter and ORS chapters 396 and 398. [1961 c.454 §46] 399.110 [Repealed by 1961 c.454 §213]
