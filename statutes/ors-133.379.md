# Duty of peace officer to arrest and prosecute violators of cruelty to animals laws (ORS 133.379)

## At a glance

ORS 133.379 — Duty of peace officer to arrest and prosecute violators of cruelty to animals laws. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.379 Duty of peace officer to arrest and prosecute violators of cruelty to animals laws. It shall be the duty of any peace officer to arrest and prosecute any violator of ORS 167.315 to 167.333, 167.340, 167.355, 167.365 or 167.428 for any violation which comes to the knowledge or notice of the officer. [Formerly 770.240; 1983 c.648 §3; 1985 c.662 §13; 2001 c.926 §17; 2009 c.550 §5; 2012 c.89 §9] 133.380 [Repealed by 1971 c.743 §432]
