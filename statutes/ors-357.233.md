# Election laws applicable (ORS 357.233)

## At a glance

ORS 357.233 — Election laws applicable. Chapter 357 (Chapter 357), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

357.233 Election laws applicable.

(1) ORS chapter 255 governs the following: (a) The nomination and election of district board members. (b) The conduct of district elections.

(2) The electors of a district may exercise the powers of the initiative and referendum regarding a district measure, in accordance with ORS 255.135 to 255.205. [1983 c.350 §220]
