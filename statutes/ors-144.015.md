# Confirmation by Senate (ORS 144.015)

## At a glance

ORS 144.015 — Confirmation by Senate. Chapter 144 (Chapter 144), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

144.015 Confirmation by Senate. Except as provided in ORS 144.005 (2)(b), the appointment of a member of the State Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision is subject to confirmation by the Senate as provided in ORS 171.562 and 171.565. [1969 c.597 §107; 1973 c.836 §282; 1985 c.565 §15; 2015 c.820 §38] 144.020 [Repealed by 1969 c.597 §281]
