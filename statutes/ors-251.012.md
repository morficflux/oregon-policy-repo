# Size, format and distribution of voters’ pamphlet; rules (ORS 251.012)

## At a glance

ORS 251.012 — Size, format and distribution of voters’ pamphlet; rules. Chapter 251 (Chapter 251), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

251.012 Size, format and distribution of voters’ pamphlet; rules. The Secretary of State by rule shall prescribe the size, format and method of distribution of any state voters’ pamphlet, subject to the limits described in this chapter. [1999 c.318 §7]
