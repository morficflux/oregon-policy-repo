# Notice of change of district boundary (ORS 255.045)

## At a glance

ORS 255.045 — Notice of change of district boundary. Chapter 255 (Chapter 255), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

255.045 Notice of change of district boundary. If the boundary of a district is changed, the district board immediately shall send a certified copy of the order, resolution or other action changing the boundary to the elections officer.
