# Fee for inspection (ORS 803.215)

## At a glance

ORS 803.215 — Fee for inspection. Chapter 803 (Chapter 803), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

803.215 Fee for inspection. A fee of $9 shall be charged for an inspection of a vehicle required by ORS 803.210. [1983 c.338 §201; 1985 c.736 §1; 1987 c.146 §6; 1987 c.790 §2; 2003 c.618 §47; 2023 c.232 §3]
