# Inoculation certificate (ORS 433.370)

## At a glance

ORS 433.370 — Inoculation certificate. Chapter 433 (Chapter 433), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

433.370 Inoculation certificate. Every veterinarian inoculating an animal against rabies shall supply to the owner evidence of inoculation which shall consist of a certificate issued and signed by the veterinarian. The form of the certificate shall be prescribed by the Oregon Health Authority. [1971 c.413 §8; 1975 c.750 §3; 1977 c.189 §6; 1985 c.793 §1; 2009 c.595 §669]
