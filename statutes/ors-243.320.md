# Copy of policy to be provided to unrepresented party (ORS 243.320)

## At a glance

ORS 243.320 — Copy of policy to be provided to unrepresented party. Chapter 243 (Chapter 243), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

243.320 Copy of policy to be provided to unrepresented party. For purposes of a mediation related to claims or allegations of workplace harassment, a mediator in the mediation shall provide a person who is a party to the mediation and who is not represented by an attorney with a copy of the policy described in ORS 243.319. [2022 c.107 §6] Note: See note under 243.317.
