# Department to prescribe methods of destruction of property; indemnity (ORS 596.406)

## At a glance

ORS 596.406 — Department to prescribe methods of destruction of property; indemnity. Chapter 596 (Disease Control Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

596.406 Department to prescribe methods of destruction of property; indemnity. When any real or personal property is ordered destroyed, the State Department of Agriculture shall prescribe the methods and procedures by which the destruction shall be effectuated and shall supervise any destruction so ordered. Indemnity shall be paid to the owner of such property as otherwise provided by law. However, no indemnity will be paid if the livestock that created the condition requiring the property destruction were imported into this state without a permit. [1953 c.689 §8; 1955 c.557 §28; 1973 c.2 §5] 596.410 [Repealed by 1953 c.689 §13]
