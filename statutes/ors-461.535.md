# Intercollegiate Athletic Fund (ORS 461.535)

## At a glance

ORS 461.535 — Intercollegiate Athletic Fund. Chapter 461 (Chapter 461), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

461.535 Intercollegiate Athletic Fund. The Intercollegiate Athletic Fund is created separate and distinct from the General Fund. The fund shall consist of revenues credited to the Sports Lottery Account under ORS 461.543

(4) and any other revenues available to the fund. [1989 c.828 §1; 1991 c.461 §81; 1991 c.962 §3; 2005 c.810 §1]
