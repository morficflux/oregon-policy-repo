# Disposition of penalties collected CRIMINAL PENALTIES (ORS 634.925)

## At a glance

ORS 634.925 — Disposition of penalties collected CRIMINAL PENALTIES. Chapter 634 (Chapter 634), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

634.925 Disposition of penalties collected. All penalties recovered under ORS 634.900 to 634.915 shall be deposited by the State Treasurer in the Department of Agriculture Service Fund. Such moneys are continuously appropriated to the State Department of Agriculture to be used for information and education related to pesticide application. [1989 c.943 §8] 634.990 [Amended by 1961 c.294 §13; repealed by 1973 c.341 §37] CRIMINAL PENALTIES
