## At a glance

## Full text

419C.067 Case transferred to juvenile court after verdict in criminal court. When a case is transferred to the juvenile court under ORS 137.707, the juvenile court shall enter an order finding the youth within the jurisdiction of the court under ORS 419C.005 based on the verdict in the criminal court. The juvenile court’s order has the same effect as an adjudication under ORS 419C.400. [1995 c.422 §82] CUSTODY
