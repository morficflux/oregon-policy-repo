# Policy regarding parents and their children (ORS 107.149)

## At a glance

ORS 107.149 — Policy regarding parents and their children. Chapter 107 (Chapter 107), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

107.149 Policy regarding parents and their children. It is the policy of this state to assure minor children of frequent and continuing contact with parents who have shown the ability to act in the best interest of the child and to encourage parents to share in the rights and responsibilities of raising their children after the parents have separated or dissolved their marriage.
