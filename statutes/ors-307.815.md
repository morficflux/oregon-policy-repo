# Exemption limited to taxes of district adopting ORS (ORS 307.815)

## At a glance

ORS 307.815 — Exemption limited to taxes of district adopting ORS. Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.815 Exemption limited to taxes of district adopting ORS 307.811. The exemption provided in ORS 307.811 applies only to the taxes of a taxing district the governing body of which has adopted, by ordinance or resolution, the provisions of ORS 307.811. [1999 c.476 §2a] (Public Beach Access Sites)
