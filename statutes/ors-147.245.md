## Full text

147.245 Disposition of moneys recovered from assailant; disposition of gifts or grants.

(1) Any moneys recovered by the Department of Justice under ORS 147.281 to 147.298 and 147.345 shall be credited to the Criminal Injuries Compensation Account.

(2) Any gifts, contributions, grants or federal funds specifically given to the department for the benefit of victims of crimes shall be credited to the Criminal Injuries Compensation Account. [1977 c.376 §24; 2005 c.383 §11] 147.250 [Renumbered 133.839] 147.253 [Renumbered 133.843]
