475.980 Affirmative defense to ORS 475.969, 475.971, 475.975

(1) and 475.976 (1). It is an affirmative defense to a charge of violating ORS 475.969, 475.971, 475.975

(1) or 475.976

(1) that the person possessed the precursor substance for a lawful purpose.
