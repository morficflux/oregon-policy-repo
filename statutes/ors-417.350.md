# Family support services as social benefits (ORS 417.350)

## At a glance

ORS 417.350 — Family support services as social benefits. Chapter 417 (Chapter 417), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

417.350 Family support services as social benefits. Funds, goods and services provided to families under ORS 417.340 to 417.348 are social benefits for the promotion of general welfare. The benefits may not be used to replace or reduce other state or federal benefits provided the families under Oregon law. [1993 c.241 §1]
