# Creation of district; use of revenues within district (ORS 367.820)

## At a glance

ORS 367.820 — Creation of district; use of revenues within district. Chapter 367 (Chapter 367), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

367.820 Creation of district; use of revenues within district. An agreement among the Department of Transportation and other units of government may create a new district, or designate a previously existing district, that includes any or all of the territory within the geographic boundaries of any or all Oregon counties in which a transportation project is located, and may require that all revenues from franchise fees, other user fees or other revenue sources collected within the district in connection with the transportation project be used exclusively for the benefit of the district. [2003 c.790 §11] Note: See note under 367.800.
