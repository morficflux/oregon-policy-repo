# Effect of approval of final account (ORS 116.123)

## At a glance

ORS 116.123 — Effect of approval of final account. Chapter 116 (Chapter 116), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

116.123 Effect of approval of final account. To the extent that the final account is approved, the personal representative and the surety of the personal representative, subject to the right of appeal, to the power of the court to vacate its final orders and to the provisions of ORS 116.213, are relieved from liability for the administration of the trust. The court may disapprove the account in whole or in part, surcharge the personal representative for any loss caused by any breach of duty and deny in whole or in part the right of the personal representative to receive compensation.
