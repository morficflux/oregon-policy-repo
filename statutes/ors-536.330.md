# Water Resources Act as supplemental to and including existing statutes (ORS 536.330)

## At a glance

ORS 536.330 — Water Resources Act as supplemental to and including existing statutes. Chapter 536 (Water Resources Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

536.330 Water Resources Act as supplemental to and including existing statutes. Chapter 707, Oregon Laws 1955, shall be construed by the Water Resources Commission as supplemental to existing statutes and not in lieu thereof except to the extent that existing statutes are expressly amended or repealed by chapter 707, Oregon Laws 1955. ORS 536.220 to 536.540 and the authority of the Water Resources Department thereunder shall include all laws now existing or hereinafter enacted that relate to or affect the use and control of the water resources of this state. [1955 c.707 §10(5); 1963 c.415 §1; 1975 c.581 §24; 1985 c.673 §180] Note: See note under 536.310.
