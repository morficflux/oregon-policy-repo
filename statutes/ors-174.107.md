# “Person with a disability” defined (Public Bodies) (ORS 174.107)

## At a glance

ORS 174.107 — “Person with a disability” defined (Public Bodies). Chapter 174 (Construction of Statutes; General Definitions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

174.107 “Person with a disability” defined.

(1) As used in the statute laws of this state, “person with a disability” means any person who: (a) Has a physical or mental impairment which substantially limits one or more major life activities; (b) Has a record of such an impairment; or (c) Is regarded as having such an impairment.

(2) Specific types of disabilities shall be considered subcategories under the definition of person with a disability. [1989 c.224 §2a; 2003 c.14 §70; 2007 c.70 §39] (Public Bodies)
