# Organization of program operator (ORS 459A.206)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.206 — Organization of program operator. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.206 Organization of program operator. A program operator of a drug take-back program must be organized as an entity that is exempt from income taxes under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, as amended and in effect on September 29, 2019. [2019 c.659 §3] Note: See note under 459A.200.
