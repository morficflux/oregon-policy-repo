# Placing substances in water or using explosives to take or destroy food fish (ORS 509.130)

## At a glance

ORS 509.130 — Placing substances in water or using explosives to take or destroy food fish. Chapter 509 (Chapter 509), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

509.130 Placing substances in water or using explosives to take or destroy food fish. It is unlawful, for the purpose of taking or destroying any food fish, to:

(1) Throw, cast or pass, or cause or permit to be thrown, cast or passed, in any waters of this state in which food fishes are wont to be, any substance deleterious to fish; or

(2) Explode or cause to be exploded in any waters of this state, any explosives. [Amended by 1963 c.112 §1; 1965 c.570 §112] 509.135 [Repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
