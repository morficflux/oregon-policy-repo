# Review of rate of interest by Legislative Assembly (ORS 311.506)

## At a glance

ORS 311.506 — Review of rate of interest by Legislative Assembly. Chapter 311 (Chapter 311), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

311.506 Review of rate of interest by Legislative Assembly. During each odd-numbered year regular session, the Legislative Assembly shall review the rate of interest, as specified under ORS 311.505

(2) that is charged and collected on property taxes that are due and unpaid. [1989 c.796 §10a; 2001 c.114 §29; 2011 c.545 §25]
