# POLST not required; revocation (ORS 127.672)

## At a glance

ORS 127.672 — POLST not required; revocation. Chapter 127 (Chapter 127), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

127.672 POLST not required; revocation. Nothing in ORS 127.663 to 127.684 is intended to require an individual to have a POLST or to require a health professional to authorize or execute a POLST. A POLST may be revoked at any time. [2009 c.595 §1183] 127.675 [2009 c.595 §1186; 2011 c.703 §24; repealed by 2017 c.101 §49]
