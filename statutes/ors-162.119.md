# Public fraud as racketeering activity (ORS 162.119)

## At a glance

ORS 162.119 — Public fraud as racketeering activity. Chapter 162 (Chapter 162), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

162.119 Public fraud as racketeering activity.

(1) Conduct constituting a violation of ORS 162.117 shall be an incident of racketeering activity for purposes of criminal actions brought under ORS 166.715 to 166.735.

(2) Conduct giving rise to the civil cause of action described in ORS 30.862 shall be an incident of racketeering activity for purposes of civil actions brought under ORS 166.715 to 166.735. [1993 c.768 §3] Note: See note under 162.117. 162.120 [Repealed by 1971 c.743 §432]
