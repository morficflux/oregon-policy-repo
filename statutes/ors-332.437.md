# Insurance reserve fund TRAFFIC REGULATION (ORS 332.437)

## At a glance

ORS 332.437 — Insurance reserve fund TRAFFIC REGULATION. Chapter 332 (Chapter 332), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

332.437 Insurance reserve fund. Any school district board by resolution may establish an insurance reserve fund by making transfers from the district’s general fund. Transfers to the insurance reserve fund shall be included in the district budget prepared and published in accordance with ORS 294.305 to 294.565. If at any time conditions arise which dispense with the necessity for further transfers to or expenditures from a fund established pursuant to this section, the district board shall so declare by resolution. The resolution shall order the balance remaining in such fund to be transferred to the general fund of the district and shall declare the insurance reserve fund closed.
