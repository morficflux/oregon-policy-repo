# Adequate funding required (ORS 329.065)

## At a glance

ORS 329.065 — Adequate funding required. Chapter 329 (Chapter 329), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

329.065 Adequate funding required. Nothing in this chapter is intended to be mandated without adequate funding support. Therefore, those features of this chapter which require significant additional funds shall not be implemented statewide until funding is available. [Formerly 326.740] 329.070 [Repealed by 1963 c.544 §52]
