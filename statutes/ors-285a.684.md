# Payment from fund upon loan approval (ORS 285A.684)

## At a glance

ORS 285A.684 — Payment from fund upon loan approval. Chapter 285A (Chapter 285A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285A.684 Payment from fund upon loan approval. If the Oregon Infrastructure Finance Authority approves a loan for a project, the authority shall pay moneys for the project from the Oregon Port Revolving Fund, in accordance with the terms of the loan contract as prescribed by the authority. [Formerly 285.887; 2001 c.883 §14; 2009 c.830 §42]
