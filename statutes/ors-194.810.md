# Applicability (ORS 194.810)

## At a glance

ORS 194.810 — Applicability. Chapter 194 (Chapter 194), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

194.810 Applicability.

(1) ORS 194.800 to 194.835 apply to an unsworn declaration by a declarant who at the time of making the declaration is physically located outside the boundaries of the United States whether or not the location is subject to the jurisdiction of the United States.

(2) ORS 194.800 to 194.835 do not apply to a declaration by a declarant who is physically located on property that is within the boundaries of the United States and subject to the jurisdiction of another country or a federally recognized Indian tribe. [2013 c.218 §3] Note: See note under 194.800.
