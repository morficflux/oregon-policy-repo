# Action affecting covenant (ORS 456.295)

## At a glance

ORS 456.295 — Action affecting covenant. Chapter 456 (Chapter 456), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

456.295 Action affecting covenant. An action affecting an affordable housing covenant may be commenced or intervened in by:

(1) The owner of an interest in the real property burdened by the covenant;

(2) An eligible covenant holder of the benefit of the covenant;

(3) A person that has a third-party right of enforcement; or

(4) A public body, as defined in ORS 174.109, in the jurisdiction of which the real property burdened by the covenant is located. [2007 c.691 §5] Note: See note under 456.270. STATE AND LOCAL COOPERATION WITH HOUSING PROJECTS
