## Full text

723.572 Insurance for members.

(1) A credit union may purchase or make available life savings, loan protection and other forms of insurance for its members in amounts related to their respective ages, shares, deposits or loan balances or to any combination of them.

(2) A credit union may enter into cooperative marketing arrangements to facilitate its members’ voluntary purchases of insurance. [1975 c.652 §60; 1999 c.185 §45]
