# Consolidated registration (ORS 732.562)

## At a glance

ORS 732.562 — Consolidated registration. Chapter 732 (Chapter 732), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

732.562 Consolidated registration. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may require or allow two or more affiliated insurers subject to registration to file a consolidated registration statement. [Formerly 732.665]
