# Environmental Data Management System Fund (ORS 468.054)

## At a glance

ORS 468.054 — Environmental Data Management System Fund. Chapter 468 (Chapter 468), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468.054 Environmental Data Management System Fund. The Environmental Data Management System Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. The Environmental Data Management System Fund consists of moneys deposited in the fund under ORS 468.053 and may include moneys appropriated, allocated, deposited or transferred to the fund by the Legislative Assembly or otherwise. Moneys in the Environmental Data Management System Fund are continuously appropriated to the Department of Environmental Quality for the maintenance and operations costs associated with the department’s environmental data management system. [2021 c.568 §4] Note: 468.054 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 468 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
