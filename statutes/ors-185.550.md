# Compensation and expenses COMMISSION ON ASIAN AND PACIFIC ISLANDER AFFAIRS (ORS 185.550)

## At a glance

ORS 185.550 — Compensation and expenses COMMISSION ON ASIAN AND PACIFIC ISLANDER AFFAIRS. Chapter 185 (Oregon Disabilities Commission; Commissions on Hispanic Affairs, Black Affairs, and Asian and Pacific Islander Affairs; Commission for Women), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

185.550 Compensation and expenses.

(1) Members of the Commission for Women who are not legislators shall be paid compensation and expenses as provided in ORS 292.495 from funds appropriated to the Oregon Advocacy Commissions Office.

(2) Members of the commission who are legislators shall be paid compensation and expense reimbursement as provided in ORS 171.072, payable from funds appropriated to the Legislative Assembly.
