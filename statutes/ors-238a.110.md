# Termination of membership (Vesting) (ORS 238A.110)

## At a glance

ORS 238A.110 — Termination of membership (Vesting). Chapter 238A (Chapter 238A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

238A.110 Termination of membership. Membership under the pension program terminates when:

(1) A member dies;

(2) A member withdraws under ORS 238A.375; or

(3) A member forfeits retirement credit under ORS 238A.145. [2003 c.733 §6; 2019 c.355 §4] (Vesting)
