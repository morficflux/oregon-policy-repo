# Policy (ORS 496.562)

## At a glance

ORS 496.562 — Policy. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.562 Policy. The purposes of this section and ORS 496.558, 496.566 and 497.153 are to:

(1) Authorize the State Fish and Wildlife Commission to issue to hunters an upland bird stamp for a specified fee;

(2) Establish a fund to be financed by the sale of upland bird stamps and any art works and prints related to the upland bird stamps for the purposes of promoting the propagation and conservation of upland birds and acquiring, developing, managing, enhancing, purchasing or acquiring through lands exchange upland bird habitat; and

(3) Provide the State Fish and Wildlife Commission with improved data on the location and number of upland bird hunters. [1989 c.406 §1; 2015 c.779 §47] Note: See note under 496.558.
