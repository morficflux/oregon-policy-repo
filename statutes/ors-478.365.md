# Employee contributions to retirement fund (ORS 478.365)

## At a glance

ORS 478.365 — Employee contributions to retirement fund. Chapter 478 (Chapter 478), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

478.365 Employee contributions to retirement fund. The district may collect, as a contribution from any employee, that percentage of the salary received by the employee which is necessary to fund on an actuarial reserve basis the cost of retirement benefits which the employee is required to provide pursuant to the provisions of a retirement plan. [1963 c.366 §5]
