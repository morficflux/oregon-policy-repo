# State taxation of bond interest (ORS 287A.345)

## At a glance

ORS 287A.345 — State taxation of bond interest. Chapter 287A (Chapter 287A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

287A.345 State taxation of bond interest. Interest on bonds of a public body is exempt from personal income tax under ORS chapter 316. [2007 c.783 §65]
