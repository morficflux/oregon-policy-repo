## Full text

820.220 Operation of low-speed vehicle in prohibited area; penalty.

(1) A person commits the offense of operation of a low-speed vehicle in a prohibited area if the person is a person with a disability and the person operates a golf cart or substantially similar motor vehicle on any highway with a speed designation greater than 25 miles per hour.

(2) The offense described in this section, operation of low-speed vehicle in prohibited area, is a Class D traffic violation. [1983 c.338 §781; 2007 c.70 §350] AMBULANCES AND EMERGENCY VEHICLES (Application of Traffic Laws)
