# Civil action for injuries caused by criminal acts (ORS 164.866)

## At a glance

ORS 164.866 — Civil action for injuries caused by criminal acts. Chapter 164 (Chapter 164), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

164.866 Civil action for injuries caused by criminal acts. Nothing in ORS 164.864, 164.865, 164.868, 164.869, 164.872, 164.873 or 164.875 or this section limits or impairs the right of a person injured by the criminal acts of a defendant to sue and recover damages from the defendant in a civil action. [1993 c.95 §11; 2001 c.666 §§31,43; 2005 c.830 §23] Note: See note under 164.864.
