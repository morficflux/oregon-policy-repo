# Conferences to coordinate work (ORS 561.366)

## At a glance

ORS 561.366 — Conferences to coordinate work. Chapter 561 (State Department of Agriculture), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

561.366 Conferences to coordinate work. The Director of Agriculture, the director of the agricultural experiment station and the director of the agricultural extension service of Oregon State University shall meet in conference at such times as may be necessary to eliminate any causes of overlapping and friction which may arise in connection with the conduct of their work; and they are authorized to invite representatives of collaborating federal agencies to participate in such conferences. [Formerly 561.100] 561.370 [Repealed by 1961 c.425 §20]
