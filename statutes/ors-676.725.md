# Civil penalties GENETIC COUNSELING (ORS 676.725)

## At a glance

ORS 676.725 — Civil penalties GENETIC COUNSELING. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.725 Civil penalties.

(1) The Health Licensing Office may impose on a temporary staffing agency a civil penalty not to exceed $500 per day per violation of ORS 676.698, 676.704, 676.707, 676.710, 676.716, 676.719 or 676.722. The office shall consider as a separate violation each assignment of an individual personnel member in violation of ORS 676.707 (1).

(2) Moneys from civil penalties imposed under this section shall be deposited in the Health Licensing Office Account established under ORS 676.625 for the administration of ORS 676.695 to 676.725. [2022 c.92 §7; 2023 c.506 §§5,14] GENETIC COUNSELING
