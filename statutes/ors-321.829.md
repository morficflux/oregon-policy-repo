# Exemption of timber from property taxation (ORS 321.829)

## At a glance

ORS 321.829 — Exemption of timber from property taxation. Chapter 321 (Chapter 321), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

321.829 Exemption of timber from property taxation. All timber in eastern Oregon shall be exempt from ad valorem property taxation. [Formerly 321.420] 321.830 [1991 c.714 §8; 1997 c.541 §409; 1999 c.314 §72; 2003 c.621 §60; renumbered 321.855 in 2003]
