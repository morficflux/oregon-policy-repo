# Fees (ORS 462.334)

## At a glance

ORS 462.334 — Fees. Chapter 462 (Chapter 462), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

462.334 Fees.

(1) The Oregon Racing Commission may require an exchange wagering licensee to: (a) Pay a fee to the commission of not more than two percent of the exchange wagering licensee’s exchange revenue; and (b) Pay a portion of the exchange wagering licensee’s exchange revenues as may be required by ORS 462.307.

(2) Moneys collected under this section shall be deposited in the General Fund in the State Treasury to the credit of the Oregon Racing Commission Account. [2019 c.313 §13]
