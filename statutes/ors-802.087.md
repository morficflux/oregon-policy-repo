# Rules for assisting offenders in obtaining driver license or identification card (ORS 802.087)

## At a glance

ORS 802.087 — Rules for assisting offenders in obtaining driver license or identification card. Chapter 802 (Chapter 802), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

802.087 Rules for assisting offenders in obtaining driver license or identification card. The Department of Transportation and the Department of Corrections jointly shall adopt rules and enter into interagency agreements necessary to assist offenders in obtaining a driver license or identification card prior to an offender’s release from a Department of Corrections institution. [2009 c.138 §2] 802.090 [1983 c.338 §870; 1985 c.16 §439; 1989 c.979 §1; 1993 c.713 §4; 1993 c.751 §11; renumbered 247.017 in 1993]
