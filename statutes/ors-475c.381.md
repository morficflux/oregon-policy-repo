# Immunity for person making report of violation (ORS 475C.381)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.381 — Immunity for person making report of violation. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.381 Immunity for person making report of violation. A person who, in good faith, makes a report of a violation of ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525, and who has reasonable grounds for making the report, is immune from any civil or criminal liability that otherwise might be incurred or imposed with respect to making the report or to the content of the report. The person has the same immunity with respect to participating in a judicial proceeding resulting from the report. [Formerly 475B.385]
