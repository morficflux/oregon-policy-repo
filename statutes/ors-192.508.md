# Rules (ORS 192.508)

## At a glance

ORS 192.508 — Rules. Chapter 192 (Records; Reports and Meetings), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

192.508 Rules. The committees or interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to the judiciary may adopt rules applicable to deliberations of the committees under ORS 192.485 to 192.513. The rules may enhance the opportunity for public participation in the deliberations of the committees, including through the live-streaming of meetings. [2017 c.654 §5; 2025 c.294 §3] Note: See note under 192.485.
