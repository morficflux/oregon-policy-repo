# Hunting or trapping on refuge prohibited (ORS 501.015)

## At a glance

## Full text

501.015 Hunting or trapping on refuge prohibited. Except as the State Fish and Wildlife Commission by rule may provide otherwise, no person shall hunt or trap any wildlife on any wildlife refuge created by any law of this state or any rule promulgated pursuant thereto. [1973 c.723 §105] 501.020 [Amended by 1955 c.63 §1; repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
