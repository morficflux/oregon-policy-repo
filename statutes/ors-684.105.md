## Full text

684.105 Discipline procedure; review of board orders.

(1) Where the State Board of Chiropractic Examiners proposes to refuse to issue a license, or proposes to revoke or suspend a license, opportunity for hearing shall be accorded as provided in ORS chapter 183.

(2) Judicial review of orders under subsection

(1) of this section shall be in accordance with ORS chapter 183. [1971 c.734 §131]
