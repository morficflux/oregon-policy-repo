# Definitions (ORS 835.005)

## At a glance

ORS 835.005 — Definitions. Chapter 835 (Aviation Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

835.005 Definitions. As used in this chapter and ORS chapters 836 and 837, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “Board” means the State Aviation Board.

(2) “Department” means the Oregon Department of Aviation.

(3) “Director” means the Director of the Oregon Department of Aviation. [Formerly 491.002; 1993 c.741 §90; 1999 c.935 §32] 835.009 [1993 c.741 §92b; repealed by 2001 c.378 §1] 835.010 [Formerly 491.005; repealed by 1993 c.741 §147]
