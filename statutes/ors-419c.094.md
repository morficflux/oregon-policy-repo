# Jurisdiction attaches at time youth taken into custody (ORS 419C.094)

## At a glance

ORS 419C.094 — Jurisdiction attaches at time youth taken into custody. Chapter 419C (Chapter 419C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419C.094 Jurisdiction attaches at time youth taken into custody. Except as otherwise provided in ORS 419C.103

(3) and (4), the jurisdiction of the juvenile court of the county in which a youth is taken into custody under ORS 419C.080 and 419C.088 shall attach from the time the youth is taken into custody. [1993 c.33 §162; 1993 c.546 §62; 1997 c.727 §4; 1999 c.577 §9]
