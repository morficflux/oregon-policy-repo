# Testing weights and measures at institutions supported by state funds (ORS 618.061)

## At a glance

ORS 618.061 — Testing weights and measures at institutions supported by state funds. Chapter 618 (Chapter 618), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

618.061 Testing weights and measures at institutions supported by state funds. The State Department of Agriculture shall, from time to time, test all weights and measures used in checking the receipt or disbursement of supplies in every institution for the maintenance of which moneys are appropriated by the Legislative Assembly, and report its findings in writing to the supervisory board or to the executive officer of the institution concerned. [1973 c.293 §7] 618.065 [Formerly 618.750; repealed by 1973 c.293 §55]
