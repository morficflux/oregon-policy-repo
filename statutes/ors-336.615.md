# Definition for ORS (ORS 336.615)

## At a glance

ORS 336.615 — Definition for ORS. Chapter 336 (Chapter 336), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

336.615 Definition for ORS 336.615 to 336.665. As used in ORS 336.615 to 336.665, “alternative education program” means a school or separate class group designed to best serve students’ educational needs and interests and assist students in achieving the academic standards of the school district and the state. [Formerly 339.605; 2001 c.490 §1] 336.620 [Formerly 336.430; renumbered 339.880 in 1993]
