# Persons authorized to conduct mechanical and electrical repairs in industrial plant (ORS 460.047)

## At a glance

ORS 460.047 — Persons authorized to conduct mechanical and electrical repairs in industrial plant. Chapter 460 (Chapter 460), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

460.047 Persons authorized to conduct mechanical and electrical repairs in industrial plant. Notwithstanding ORS 460.045 (2), the following persons may conduct electrical and mechanical repairs on an elevator located in an industrial plant:

(1) A limited supervising electrician licensed under ORS 479.630 (3).

(2) A limited journeyman electrician licensed under ORS 479.630 (5). [1999 c.1031 §8] 460.048 [1973 c.528 §6; repealed by 2009 c.696 §26] 460.050 [Amended by 1957 c.398 §7; repealed by 1961 c.427 §22]
