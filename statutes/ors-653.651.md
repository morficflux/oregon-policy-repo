## Full text

653.651 Enforcement.

(1) An employee asserting a violation of ORS 653.641

(2) or

(3) may file a complaint with the Commissioner of the Bureau of Labor and Industries under ORS 659A.820 or a civil action as provided in ORS 659A.885.

(2) The commissioner has the same enforcement powers with respect to the rights established under ORS 653.601 to 653.661 as are established in ORS chapters 652 and 653. [2015 c.537 §13] 653.655 [1973 c.564 §13; repealed by 1975 c.114 §1]
