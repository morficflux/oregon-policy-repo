# “State agency” defined (ORS 459A.775)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.775 — “State agency” defined. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.775 “State agency” defined. As used in ORS 459A.775 to 459A.785, “state agency” means any state officer, department, board, commission or court created by the Constitution or statutes of this state, including the Legislative Assembly, its committees, officers and employees. [Formerly 468.967] Note: 459A.775 to 459A.785 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 459A or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
