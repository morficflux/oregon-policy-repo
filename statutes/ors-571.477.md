# Cancellation of uncollectible assessment (ORS 571.477)

## At a glance

ORS 571.477 — Cancellation of uncollectible assessment. Chapter 571 (Chapter 571), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

571.477 Cancellation of uncollectible assessment. The Oregon Hemp Commission may cancel an uncollectible assessment consistent with ORS 293.240. Subsequent collection of debt written off under ORS 293.240 is governed by ORS 293.245. [2021 c.216 §28] Note: See note under 571.400.
