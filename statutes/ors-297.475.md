# Cancellation of request for Secretary of State assistance (ORS 297.475)

## At a glance

ORS 297.475 — Cancellation of request for Secretary of State assistance. Chapter 297 (Chapter 297), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

297.475 Cancellation of request for Secretary of State assistance. Whenever any municipal corporation has made a request to the Secretary of State, pursuant to ORS 297.425, for the audit of its accounts and fiscal affairs, the municipal corporation may cancel that request by ordinance or resolution adopted and furnished to the Secretary of State at least 90 days prior to the end of a fiscal year. [1977 c.774 §9; 2007 c.184 §4; 2023 c.10 §13] 297.480 [Repealed by 1977 c.774 §27]
