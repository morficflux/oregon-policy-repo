## Full text

686.375 Veterinary technician license; fee; rules. Upon application, accompanied by payment of the fee adopted by the Oregon State Veterinary Medical Examining Board by rule, the board shall issue a veterinary technician license to any applicant who furnishes satisfactory evidence that the applicant has met the requirements for licensure as a veterinary technician established by the board by rule. [2003 c.178 §1] Note: 686.375 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 686 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 686.380 [1975 c.619 §10; 1977 c.399 §8; repealed by 1985 c.112 §8] 686.390 [1975 c.619 §11; repealed by 1977 c.399 §9]
