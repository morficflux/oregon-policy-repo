# Commission to amend regulations to facilitate periodic review (ORS 197.631)

## At a glance

ORS 197.631 — Commission to amend regulations to facilitate periodic review. Chapter 197 (Chapter 197), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

197.631 Commission to amend regulations to facilitate periodic review. In order to use state and local periodic review resources most efficiently and effectively and to concentrate periodic review on adequate provision of economic development, needed housing, transportation, public facilities and services and urbanization, the Land Conservation and Development Commission shall adopt, amend or repeal the statewide land use planning goals, guidelines and corresponding rules as necessary to facilitate periodic review and to provide for compliance by local governments with those goals not described in ORS 197.628

(2) through the post-acknowledgment procedures of ORS 197.610 to 197.625. [1999 c.622 §11; 2005 c.829 §3; 2015 c.261 §3]
