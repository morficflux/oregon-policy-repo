# Local regulation of wells and water well constructors (Regulation of Ground Water Wells) (ORS 537.769)

## At a glance

ORS 537.769 — Local regulation of wells and water well constructors (Regulation of Ground Water Wells). Chapter 537 (Chapter 537), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

537.769 Local regulation of wells and water well constructors. The Legislative Assembly finds that ground water protection is a matter of statewide concern. No ordinance, order or regulation shall be adopted by a local government to regulate the inspection of wells, construction of wells or water well constructors subject to regulation by the Water Resources Commission or the Water Resources Department under ORS 537.747 to 537.795 and 537.992. [1989 c.129 §3] 537.770 [1955 c.708 §30; 1957 c.341 §9; repealed by 1961 c.334 §12] (Regulation of Ground Water Wells)
