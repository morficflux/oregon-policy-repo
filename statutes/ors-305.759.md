# Contribution to political party on income tax return not to be claimed as tax credit REFUNDS (ORS 305.759)

## At a glance

ORS 305.759 — Contribution to political party on income tax return not to be claimed as tax credit REFUNDS. Chapter 305 (Chapter 305), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

305.759 Contribution to political party on income tax return not to be claimed as tax credit. A contribution made under ORS 305.754 may not be claimed as a credit by the taxpayer under ORS 316.102. [2009 c.911 §5] Note: See note under 305.754. REFUNDS
