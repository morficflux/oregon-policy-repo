# Availability of writ of habeas corpus (ORS 426.380)

## At a glance

ORS 426.380 — Availability of writ of habeas corpus. Chapter 426 (Persons With Mental Illness; Dangerous Persons; Commitment; Housing), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

426.380 Availability of writ of habeas corpus. Any individual committed pursuant to ORS 426.005 to 426.223 and 426.273 to 426.380 shall be entitled to the writ of habeas corpus upon proper petition by the individual or a friend to any court generally empowered to issue the writ of habeas corpus in the county in which the state hospital in which the person is detained is located. [1967 c.460 §6]
