# Payment of commission expenditures (ORS 506.316)

## At a glance

ORS 506.316 — Payment of commission expenditures. Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.316 Payment of commission expenditures. Except as otherwise provided in ORS 506.321, all expenditures of the State Fish and Wildlife Commission pursuant to the commercial fishing laws shall be made from moneys appropriated for the purposes for which such moneys are used, upon claims presented and warrants drawn pursuant to law. [1965 c.570 §30; 1967 c.454 §108] 506.320 [Repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
