# Definitions for ORS (ORS 109.774)

## At a glance

ORS 109.774 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.774 Definitions for ORS 109.774 to 109.827. As used in ORS 109.774 to 109.827:

(1) “Petitioner” means a person who seeks enforcement of an order for return of a child under the Hague Convention on the Civil Aspects of International Child Abduction or enforcement of a child custody determination.

(2) “Respondent” means a person against whom a proceeding has been commenced for enforcement of an order for return of a child under the Hague Convention on the Civil Aspects of International Child Abduction or enforcement of a child custody determination. [1999 c.649 §23] Note: See note under 109.701.
