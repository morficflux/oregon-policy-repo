# Executive director (ORS 185.010)

## At a glance

ORS 185.010 — Executive director. Chapter 185 (Oregon Disabilities Commission; Commissions on Hispanic Affairs, Black Affairs, and Asian and Pacific Islander Affairs; Commission for Women), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

185.010 Executive director.

(1) The Oregon Advocacy Commissions Office shall be staffed by an executive director, who is responsible for the performance of the duties, functions and powers of the office.

(2) A board consisting of the chairpersons of the commissions served by the office and the Governor shall establish the qualifications for and appoint the executive director.

(3) The executive director shall receive a salary as prescribed by law, or as prescribed by the board described in subsection

(2) of this section if a salary is not prescribed by law.

(4) The executive director is in the unclassified service.

(5) The executive director shall provide each commission served by the office with the administrative support needed by the commission to carry out the statutory duties of the commission. Subject to any applicable provisions of the State Personnel Relations Law, the executive director shall employ all persons necessary for the operation of the office, prescribe the duties of those employees and establish the compensation payable to those employees.
