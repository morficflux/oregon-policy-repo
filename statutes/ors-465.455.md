# Construction of ORS 465.425 to (ORS 465.455)

## At a glance

ORS 465.455 — Construction of ORS 465.425 to. Chapter 465 (Chapter 465), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

465.455 Construction of ORS 465.425 to 465.455. Nothing in ORS 465.425 to 465.455 or any rule adopted under ORS 465.435 or 465.440 shall be construed to impose liability on a security interest holder or fiduciary or to expand the liability of a security interest holder or fiduciary beyond that which might otherwise exist. [1991 c.680 §8] (Oregon Environmental Cleanup Assistance)
