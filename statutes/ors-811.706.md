# Money damages resulting from violation of ORS 811.700 or (ORS 811.706)

## At a glance

ORS 811.706 — Money damages resulting from violation of ORS 811.700 or. Chapter 811 (Chapter 811), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

811.706 Money damages resulting from violation of ORS 811.700 or 811.705. When a person is convicted of violating ORS 811.700 or 811.705, the court, in addition to any other sentence it may impose, may order the person to pay an amount of money equal to the amount of any damages caused by the person as a result of the incident that created the duties in ORS 811.700 or 811.705. [1995 c.782 §2] Note: 811.706 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 811 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
