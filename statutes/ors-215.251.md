# Relationship to other farm uses (ORS 215.251)

## At a glance

ORS 215.251 — Relationship to other farm uses. Chapter 215 (Chapter 215), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

215.251 Relationship to other farm uses. Nothing in ORS 215.213 (1)(y), 215.246 to 215.249 or 215.283 (1)(v) affects whether the land application of a substance not described in ORS 215.213 (1)(y), 215.246 to 215.249 or 215.283 (1)(v) is a farm use as defined in ORS 215.203. [2001 c.488 §7; 2003 c.14 §100; 2009 c.850 §7] Note: See note under 215.246.
