# Written findings required (ORS 419C.355)

## At a glance

ORS 419C.355 — Written findings required. Chapter 419C (Chapter 419C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419C.355 Written findings required. The juvenile court shall make a specific, detailed, written finding of fact to support the findings made under ORS 419C.349 (2). [1993 c.33 §215; 2019 c.634 §18]
