## At a glance

## Full text

508.410 Contents of license applications. All applications for licenses under ORS 508.406 shall be made on blanks furnished by the State Fish and Wildlife Director and shall contain such information as the State Fish and Wildlife Commission determines to be necessary for proper administration and enforcement of the commercial fishing laws. [Amended by 1957 c.208 §1; 1963 c.196 §2; 1965 c.570 §67] 508.413 [1963 c.246 §1; repealed by 1965 c.67 §4]
