# Rules for ORS 743.022, (ORS 743B.127)

## At a glance

ORS 743B.127 — Rules for ORS 743.022,. Chapter 743B (Chapter 743B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743B.127 Rules for ORS 743.022, 743B.125 and 743B.126. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services shall adopt all rules necessary for the implementation and administration of ORS 743.022, 743B.125 and 743B.126. [Formerly 743.773]
