# Permit to take food fish for educational and scientific purposes (ORS 508.111)

## At a glance

ORS 508.111 — Permit to take food fish for educational and scientific purposes. Chapter 508 (Chapter 508), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

508.111 Permit to take food fish for educational and scientific purposes. The State Fish and Wildlife Director may issue to any person a permit to take food fish solely for educational and scientific purposes. [1965 c.570 §64a] 508.115 [Repealed by 1961 c.156 §1]
