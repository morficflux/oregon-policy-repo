# Preemption ROBBERY (ORS 164.388)

## At a glance

ORS 164.388 — Preemption ROBBERY. Chapter 164 (Chapter 164), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

164.388 Preemption. The provisions of ORS 137.131, 164.381 to 164.386 and 419C.461 are not intended to preempt any local regulation of graffiti or graffiti-related activities or any prosecution under ORS 164.345, 164.354 or 164.365. [1995 c.615 §7; 1999 c.1040 §6] Note: See note under 164.381. 164.390 [1959 c.626 §§1,4; repealed by 1971 c.743 §432] 164.392 [1959 c.626 §§2,3; repealed by 1971 c.743 §432] ROBBERY
