# Citizen involvement for wildlife management on private lands THREATENED OR ENDANGERED WILDLIFE SPECIES (ORS 496.169)

## At a glance

ORS 496.169 — Citizen involvement for wildlife management on private lands THREATENED OR ENDANGERED WILDLIFE SPECIES. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.169 Citizen involvement for wildlife management on private lands. The Legislative Assembly finds, in the interest of all Oregonians, a necessity to improve Oregon’s resource access and wildlife habitat through the further involvement of its citizens, through voluntary partnership between the State Department of Fish and Wildlife and landowners to manage wildlife on private lands and through support by additional financial revenues.
