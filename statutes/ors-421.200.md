# Disciplinary segregation unit data dashboard (ORS 421.200)

## At a glance

ORS 421.200 — Disciplinary segregation unit data dashboard. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.200 Disciplinary segregation unit data dashboard. The Department of Corrections shall establish, maintain and publish on the department’s website a publicly accessible data dashboard detailing the following information:

(1) The race, age and misconduct status of each adult in custody placed in a disciplinary segregation unit.

(2) The duration for which each adult in custody placed in a disciplinary segregation unit stays in the disciplinary segregation unit.

(3) The duration for which each adult in custody, after completing a placement in a disciplinary segregation unit, remains in the general population before returning to a disciplinary segregation unit.

(4) The level of misconduct providing the basis for the adult in custody to return to the disciplinary segregation unit under subsection

(3) of this section.

(5) The amount of time, in the aggregate and for each occasion, that an adult in custody placed in a disciplinary segregation unit is allowed to spend outside of a cell. [2023 c.245 §1] Note: 421.200 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 421 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. TRANSFER OF ADULTS IN CUSTODY
