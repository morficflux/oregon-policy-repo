## Full text

357.256 Board as district governing body; selection of president.

(1) The district board shall be the governing body of the district and shall exercise all powers thereof.

(2) At its first meeting or as soon thereafter as may be practicable, the board shall choose one of its members as president. [1981 c.226 §9] 357.260 [Repealed by 1975 c.476 §34]
