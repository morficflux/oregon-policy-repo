# Applicable standard (ORS 469A.050)

## At a glance

ORS 469A.050 — Applicable standard. Chapter 469A (Chapter 469A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469A.050 Applicable standard.

(1) Electric utilities must comply with the applicable renewable portfolio standard described in ORS 469A.052 or 469A.055.

(2) Electricity service suppliers must comply with the renewable portfolio standard established under ORS 469A.065. [2007 c.301 §5]
