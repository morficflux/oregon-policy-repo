# Cleanup of hazardous substance contaminating ground water (ORS 459.248)

## At a glance

ORS 459.248 — Cleanup of hazardous substance contaminating ground water. Chapter 459 (Chapter 459), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459.248 Cleanup of hazardous substance contaminating ground water. In addition to any other authority granted by law, if the Department of Environmental Quality finds that ground water is contaminated with a hazardous substance originating at a land disposal site, the department may require cleanup of the hazardous substance pursuant to authority under ORS 465.200 to 465.485. As used in this section, “hazardous substance” has the meaning given that term in ORS 465.200. [1993 c.526 §3]
