# Liability (ORS 459A.248)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.248 — Liability. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.248 Liability. An authorized collector, covered manufacturer, drug take-back organization, drug take-back program and program operator may not be held criminally or civilly liable for any function, duty or power performed for the purpose of complying with ORS 459A.200 to 459A.266, unless the function, duty or power was performed with gross negligence or willful and wanton misconduct. [2019 c.659 §17] Note: See note under 459A.200.
