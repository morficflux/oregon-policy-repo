## At a glance

## Full text

465.305 Application fees; rules. The Environmental Quality Commission may establish by rule reasonable fees for applicants for financial assistance sufficient to pay for the costs of the Department of Environmental Quality of carrying out the provisions of ORS 465.265 to 465.310. [1989 c.833 §110]
