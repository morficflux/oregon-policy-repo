# Receipt of prescription monitoring information by pharmacist (ORS 431A.862)

## At a glance

ORS 431A.862 — Receipt of prescription monitoring information by pharmacist. Chapter 431A (Chapter 431A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

431A.862 Receipt of prescription monitoring information by pharmacist. A pharmacist who dispenses a prescription drug for which reporting is required under ORS 431A.860 may receive prescription monitoring information regarding:

(1) A patient who is an individual to whom a prescription drug is dispensed on behalf of an animal; and

(2) An animal for which a prescription drug is prescribed. [2023 c.438 §2]
