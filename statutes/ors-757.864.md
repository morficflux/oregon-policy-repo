# Conduct of business after acquisition (ORS 757.864)

## At a glance

ORS 757.864 — Conduct of business after acquisition. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.864 Conduct of business after acquisition. If Oregon Community Power acquires an incumbent utility under ORS 757.812 to 757.950, all electric utility operations undertaken by Oregon Community Power after the acquisition shall be conducted under the name of Oregon Community Power. [2007 c.807 §15] Note: See note under 757.812.
