# Effect of allowance of motion (ORS 136.505)

## At a glance

ORS 136.505 — Effect of allowance of motion. Chapter 136 (Chapter 136), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

136.505 Effect of allowance of motion. The effect of allowing a motion in arrest of judgment is to place the defendant in the same situation in which the defendant was before indictment was found. [Formerly 136.820] 136.510 [Amended by 1973 c.836 §237; renumbered 136.430]
