# Local ordinances; fees (ORS 602.045)

## At a glance

ORS 602.045 — Local ordinances; fees. Chapter 602 (Chapter 602), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

602.045 Local ordinances; fees. A local government may:

(1) Adopt ordinances consistent with the best practices described in ORS 602.035; and

(2) Charge a reasonable fee for registering hives in residential areas. The fee that a local government charges for registering hives in residential areas may not exceed the amount charged under State Department of Agriculture rules for the registration of colonies that are subject to fees under ORS 602.090 (3).
