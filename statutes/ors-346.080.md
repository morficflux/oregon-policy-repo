# Notice to employees of reasonable assurance of continued employment DEAF STUDENTS’ BILL OF RIGHTS (ORS 346.080)

## At a glance

ORS 346.080 — Notice to employees of reasonable assurance of continued employment DEAF STUDENTS’ BILL OF RIGHTS. Chapter 346 (Chapter 346), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

346.080 Notice to employees of reasonable assurance of continued employment. The Department of Education shall give the notice required by ORS 332.554 to all classified employees of the Oregon School for the Deaf in the same manner and to the same effect as notice given under ORS 332.554.
