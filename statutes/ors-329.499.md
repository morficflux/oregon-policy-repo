# Report to Legislative Assembly on physical education (ORS 329.499)

## At a glance

ORS 329.499 — Report to Legislative Assembly on physical education. Chapter 329 (Chapter 329), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

329.499 Report to Legislative Assembly on physical education. Prior to February 1 of each odd-numbered year, the Department of Education shall report to the Legislative Assembly on the data collected under ORS 329.498 for the prior two school years. [2007 c.839 §2] Note: See note under 329.498. 329.500 [Repealed by 1953 c.306 §18]
