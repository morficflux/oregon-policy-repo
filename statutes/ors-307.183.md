# Summer homes on federal land occupied under permit (ORS 307.183)

## At a glance

ORS 307.183 — Summer homes on federal land occupied under permit. Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.183 Summer homes on federal land occupied under permit. Notwithstanding ORS 307.060, there shall be exempt from property taxation real property of the United States used and occupied for summer homes under a permit issued pursuant to the Act of March 4, 1915, ch. 144 (16 U.S.C. 497), as amended, but improvements thereon are subject to taxation. [1975 c.649 §1] Note: See second note under 307.182.
