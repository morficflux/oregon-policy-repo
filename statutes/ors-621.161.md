# Operation of dairy products plant without license prohibited (ORS 621.161)

## At a glance

ORS 621.161 — Operation of dairy products plant without license prohibited. Chapter 621 (Chapter 621), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

621.161 Operation of dairy products plant without license prohibited. A person shall not operate a dairy products plant unless that person has a valid dairy products plant license. A separate license is required for each dairy products plant operated. A person is not required to obtain a dairy products plant license to act as a milk hauler or to operate receiving or transfer stations in conjunction with a milk processing plant. [1953 c.686 §2; 1971 c.769 §4; 1999 c.197 §24] 621.165 [Repealed by 1953 c.686 §37]
