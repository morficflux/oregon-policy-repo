# Prohibited conduct by professional strikebreaker (ORS 662.225)

## At a glance

ORS 662.225 — Prohibited conduct by professional strikebreaker. Chapter 662 (Chapter 662), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

662.225 Prohibited conduct by professional strikebreaker. No professional strikebreaker shall knowingly become employed or offer to become employed for the purpose of replacing an employee involved in a strike or lockout, for the duration of that strike or lockout.
