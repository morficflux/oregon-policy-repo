# Installments (ORS 723.516)

## At a glance

ORS 723.516 — Installments. Chapter 723 (Chapter 723), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

723.516 Installments. A member may receive a loan in installments, or in one sum, and may pay the whole or any part of the member’s loan on any day on which the office of the credit union is open for business. [1975 c.652 §55] 723.520 [Repealed by 1959 c.106 §23]
