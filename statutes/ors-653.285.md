## At a glance

ORS 653.285 — Liability of employer; attorney fees. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.285 Liability of employer; attorney fees. If an employee’s trade equipment is damaged upon or stolen from premises under the employer’s control as a proximate cause of the employer’s failure to comply with ORS 653.280 the employee has a right to file an action against the employer, and the employer is liable, for financial settlement as is needed to repair or replace the equipment. The court may award reasonable attorney fees to the prevailing party in an action under this section. [1975 c.488 §8; 1981 c.897 §91; 1991 c.67 §158; 1995 c.618 §112]
