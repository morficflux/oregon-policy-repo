# Duty to prevent spread of communicable diseases; rules (ORS 433.110)

## At a glance

ORS 433.110 — Duty to prevent spread of communicable diseases; rules. Chapter 433 (Chapter 433), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

433.110 Duty to prevent spread of communicable diseases; rules. Every physician, physician associate, naturopathic physician or nurse attending a person affected with any communicable disease shall use all precautionary measures to prevent the spread of the disease as the Oregon Health Authority may prescribe by rule.
