# Term of licenses, tags and permits (ORS 497.016)

## At a glance

ORS 497.016 — Term of licenses, tags and permits. Chapter 497 (Chapter 497), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

497.016 Term of licenses, tags and permits. Unless otherwise provided by law, all licenses, tags and permits issued by the State Fish and Wildlife Commission shall be valid for such period of time as the commission prescribes. [1973 c.723 §44; 1981 c.445 §1] 497.020 [Repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
