# Interference with an unmanned aircraft system; unauthorized control (ORS 837.375)

## At a glance

ORS 837.375 — Interference with an unmanned aircraft system; unauthorized control. Chapter 837 (Chapter 837), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

837.375 Interference with an unmanned aircraft system; unauthorized control. In addition to any other remedies allowed by law, a person who intentionally interferes with, or gains unauthorized control over, an unmanned aircraft system licensed by the Federal Aviation Administration, or operated by the Armed Forces of the United States as defined in ORS 352.313, an agency of the United States or a federal, state or local law enforcement agency, is liable to the owner of the unmanned aircraft system in an amount of not less than $5,000. The court shall award reasonable attorney fees to a prevailing plaintiff in an action under this section. [2013 c.686 §14; 2015 c.315 §10] Note: See note under 837.300.
