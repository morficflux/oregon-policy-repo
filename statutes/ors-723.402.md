# Shares (ORS 723.402)

## At a glance

ORS 723.402 — Shares. Chapter 723 (Chapter 723), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

723.402 Shares.

(1) Shares may be subscribed to, paid for and transferred in such manner as the bylaws prescribe.

(2) A certificate need not be issued to denote ownership of a share in a credit union. [1975 c.652 §42; 1985 c.801 §4; 1987 c.158 §153; 1987 c.650 §7]
