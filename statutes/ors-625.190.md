# Disposition of fees BREAD, (ORS 625.190)

## At a glance

## Full text

625.190 Disposition of fees. All fees received by the State Department of Agriculture under ORS 625.010 to 625.270 shall be deposited in the Department of Agriculture Service Fund and are continuously appropriated to the department for the purpose of carrying out ORS 625.010 to 625.270. [Amended by 1979 c.499 §25] BREAD, ROLLS AND BUNS
