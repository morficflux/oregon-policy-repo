# Registration of aircraft (ORS 837.015)

## At a glance

ORS 837.015 — Registration of aircraft. Chapter 837 (Chapter 837), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

837.015 Registration of aircraft. Unless exempted by ORS 837.005, no person shall fail to register any aircraft when required by ORS 837.040 to 837.070.
