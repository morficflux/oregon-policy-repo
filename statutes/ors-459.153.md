# Intent not to discourage recycling DISPOSAL SITES (ORS 459.153)

## At a glance

ORS 459.153 — Intent not to discourage recycling DISPOSAL SITES. Chapter 459 (Chapter 459), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459.153 Intent not to discourage recycling. It is not the intent of the Legislative Assembly that Marion County, under ORS 459.125 and 459.135, take any action that would hinder or discourage recycling activities in the county.
