# Agency expenses (ORS 675.172)

## At a glance

ORS 675.172 — Agency expenses. Chapter 675 (Chapter 675), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

675.172 Agency expenses. The expenses of the Mental Health Regulatory Agency shall be paid by the regulated boards as established by rule by the regulated boards in consultation with each other. [2017 c.104 §5] Note: See note under 675.160.
