# Duties of council (ORS 694.170)

## At a glance

ORS 694.170 — Duties of council. Chapter 694 (Chapter 694), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

694.170 Duties of council.

(1) The Advisory Council on Hearing Aids shall have the responsibility and duty of advising the Health Licensing Office in all matters relating to ORS 694.015 to 694.170 including standards of practice and professional conduct, shall prepare the examinations required by ORS 694.015 to 694.170 subject to the approval of the office and shall assist the office in carrying out the provisions of ORS 694.015 to 694.170.

(2) The office shall consider and be guided by the recommendations of the council in all matters relating to ORS 694.015 to 694.170.
