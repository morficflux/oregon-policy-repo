# Temporary manager as agent of state agency (ORS 441.338)

## At a glance

ORS 441.338 — Temporary manager as agent of state agency. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.338 Temporary manager as agent of state agency. In any action or proceeding brought against a temporary manager in the temporary manager’s official capacity for acts committed while carrying out the powers and duties authorized by the Department of Human Services by rule or as agreed upon between the department and the licensee, the temporary manager shall be considered an agent of the department under ORS 30.260 to 30.300. [2009 c.539 §17] Note: See note under 441.331. 441.340 [Repealed by 1971 c.647 §149]
