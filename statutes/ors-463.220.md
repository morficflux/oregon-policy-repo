# Deposit of revenues TAX ON GROSS RECEIPTS (ORS 463.220)

## At a glance

ORS 463.220 — Deposit of revenues TAX ON GROSS RECEIPTS. Chapter 463 (Chapter 463), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

463.220 Deposit of revenues. All revenue obtained under this chapter from license fees, bonds and gross receipts taxes, and any other income received under this chapter, shall be deposited in the subaccount of the State Police Account established under ORS 181A.020.
