## Full text

479.995 Civil penalty for violation of ORS 479.510 to 479.945. The Electrical and Elevator Board may impose a civil penalty for a violation of ORS 479.510 to 479.945 or rules adopted for the administration or enforcement of ORS 479.510 to 479.945 and this section. The board shall impose a civil penalty authorized by this section as provided in ORS 455.895. [2001 c.411 §8; 2003 c.14 §335] _______________
