# Agency may be compelled to act (ORS 183.490)

## At a glance

ORS 183.490 — Agency may be compelled to act. Chapter 183 (Administrative Procedures Act), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

183.490 Agency may be compelled to act. The court may, upon petition as described in ORS 183.484, compel an agency to act where it has unlawfully refused to act or make a decision or unreasonably delayed taking action or making a decision. [1957 c.717 §13; 1979 c.593 §28] 183.495 [1975 c.759 §16a; repealed by 1985 c.757 §7]
