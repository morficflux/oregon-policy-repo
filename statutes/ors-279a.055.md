# Personal services contracts (ORS 279A.055)

## At a glance

ORS 279A.055 — Personal services contracts. Chapter 279A (Public Contracting - General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

279A.055 Personal services contracts.

(1) Except as provided in ORS 279A.140, a contracting agency may enter into personal services contracts. The provisions of this section do not relieve a contracting agency of the duty to comply with ORS 279A.140, any other law applicable to state agencies or applicable city or county charter provisions.

(2) A state contracting agency with procurement authority under ORS 279A.050 or a local contract review board by ordinance, resolution, administrative rule or other regulation may designate certain service contracts or classes of service contracts as personal services contracts. [2003 c.794 §8; 2005 c.103 §4]
