# Full faith and credit (ORS 419B.663)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.663 — Full faith and credit. Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.663 Full faith and credit. The juvenile court shall give full faith and credit to the public acts, records and judicial proceedings of an Indian tribe applicable to an Indian child custody proceeding. [2020 s.s.1 c.14 §63] Note: See note under 419B.600.
