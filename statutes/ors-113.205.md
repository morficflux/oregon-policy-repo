# Powers of surviving personal representative (ORS 113.205)

## At a glance

ORS 113.205 — Powers of surviving personal representative. Chapter 113 (Chapter 113), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

113.205 Powers of surviving personal representative.

(1) Every power exercisable by copersonal representatives may be exercised by the survivors or survivor of them when the appointment of one is terminated, unless the will provides otherwise.

(2) Where one of two or more persons named as copersonal representatives is not appointed, those appointed may exercise all the powers incident to the office, unless the will provides otherwise. [1969 c.591 §100; 2017 c.169 §20] 113.210 [Repealed by 1969 c.591 §305]
