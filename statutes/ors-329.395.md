# Definitions for ORS (ORS 329.395)

## At a glance

ORS 329.395 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 329 (Chapter 329), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

329.395 Definitions for ORS 329.395 to 329.425. As used in ORS 329.395 to 329.425:

(1) “Child development program” means an educational program funded by ORS 329.395 to 329.425 that conforms to standards adopted by the State Board of Education and that consists of an education component and a child care component.

(2) “Teenage parent program” means an educational program funded by ORS 329.395 to 329.425 that conforms to standards adopted by the State Board of Education and that consists of education for the student-parent and child care for the student-parent’s child.
