# Terminology in individualized education program for child with an intellectual disability (ORS 343.152)

## At a glance

ORS 343.152 — Terminology in individualized education program for child with an intellectual disability. Chapter 343 (Chapter 343), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

343.152 Terminology in individualized education program for child with an intellectual disability.

(1) An individualized education program may not use the words “retardation” or “mental retardation” to refer to a child with an intellectual disability.

(2) If an individualized education program developed, reviewed or revised before, on or after June 28, 2011, uses the words “retardation” or “mental retardation,” those words shall be substituted with other words designating “intellectual disability” wherever those words occur in the individualized education program. [2011 c.544 §6] Note: 343.152 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 343 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 343.153 [1979 c.423 §2 (enacted in lieu of 343.077); 1985 c.555 §11; 1989 c.491 §33; 1991 c.795 §2; repealed by 1993 c.45 §203]
