# Service of documents on defendant PROBATION AND PAROLE BY COMMITTING MAGISTRATE (ORS 137.482)

## At a glance

ORS 137.482 — Service of documents on defendant PROBATION AND PAROLE BY COMMITTING MAGISTRATE. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.482 Service of documents on defendant. A copy of any document filed in any of the following proceedings shall be served personally on the defendant, even if the defendant is represented by counsel, by providing the copy to the custodian of the defendant, who shall ensure that the copy is provided promptly to the defendant:

(1) A death warrant hearing under ORS 137.463.

(2) A proceeding in which a person other than the defendant seeks to stay execution of the defendant’s sentence of death.

(3) A petition for post-conviction relief filed under ORS 138.510 (2). [1999 c.1055 §16] Note: See note under 137.464. 137.510 [Amended by 1955 c.660 §18; 1955 c.688 §1; repealed by 1971 c.743 §432] PROBATION AND PAROLE BY COMMITTING MAGISTRATE
