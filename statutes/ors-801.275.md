# “Fifth wheel hitch” (ORS 801.275)

## At a glance

ORS 801.275 — “Fifth wheel hitch”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.275 “Fifth wheel hitch.” “Fifth wheel hitch” means a coupling device for vehicles that is commonly known as a kingpin and fifth wheel assembly. [1983 c.338 §46]
