# Report by person responsible for collecting assessment (ORS 576.335)

## At a glance

ORS 576.335 — Report by person responsible for collecting assessment. Chapter 576 (Agricultural Marketing Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

576.335 Report by person responsible for collecting assessment.

(1) A person responsible for collecting an assessment for a commodity commission shall make a report to the commission at the time and in the manner required by the commission.

(2) A person responsible for collecting an assessment for a commission who fails to deduct an assessment at the time of sale shall report and pay the assessment to the commission. A producer required to report and pay an assessment is subject to this section. [1953 c.489 §§24,26; 1959 c.596 §14; subsection

(4) enacted as 1965 c.211 §3; 2003 c.604 §40]
