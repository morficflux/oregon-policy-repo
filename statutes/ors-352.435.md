# Repealed statutes continued effective until bonds redeemed (Funds and Accounts) (ORS 352.435)

## At a glance

ORS 352.435 — Repealed statutes continued effective until bonds redeemed (Funds and Accounts). Chapter 352 (Chapter 352), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

352.435 Repealed statutes continued effective until bonds redeemed. Outstanding bonds issued under article 3, chapter 36, title 111, O.C.L.A., and chapter 41, title 111, O.C.L.A., shall continue to be valid obligations until they are redeemed in full, and authority granted in connection with said bonds shall continue in effect until they have been so redeemed. [Formerly 351.500] 352.440 [1979 c.782 §4; repealed by 1987 c.246 §8] (Funds and Accounts)
