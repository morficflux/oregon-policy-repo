# Eligibility criteria; rules (ORS 343.513)

## At a glance

ORS 343.513 — Eligibility criteria; rules. Chapter 343 (Chapter 343), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

343.513 Eligibility criteria; rules. The State Board of Education shall establish by rule procedures prescribing the eligibility criteria for early childhood special education and early intervention services. [1991 c.749 §15] 343.515 [1971 c.602 §4; repealed by 1975 c.621 §17]
