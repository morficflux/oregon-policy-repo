# Certificate authorizing branch to conduct business (ORS 714.059)

## At a glance

## Full text

714.059 Certificate authorizing branch to conduct business. At a banking institution’s request, the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services shall issue and deliver a certificate to authorize each branch for which the banking institution has given notice under ORS 714.025 to conduct business. [1997 c.631 §318; 2015 c.244 §88e] 714.060 [Amended by 1973 c.797 §325; 1985 c.12 §7; 1993 c.229 §16; 1993 c.255 §3; repealed by 1997 c.631 §567]
