# Equipment and facilities requirements (ORS 603.055)

## At a glance

ORS 603.055 — Equipment and facilities requirements. Chapter 603 (Chapter 603), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

603.055 Equipment and facilities requirements. Each establishment, other than those subject to ORS 603.045, shall maintain and only utilize an establishment and equipment in accordance with rules promulgated by the State Department of Agriculture. [1973 c.175 §7]
