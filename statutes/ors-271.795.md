# Construction of Act GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 271.795)

## At a glance

ORS 271.795 — Construction of Act GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 271 (Chapter 271), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

271.795 Construction of Act. ORS 271.715 to 271.795 shall be applied and construed to effectuate the general purpose to make uniform the laws with respect to the subject of ORS 271.715 to 271.795 among states enacting it. [1983 c.642 §6; 1997 c.249 §82] _______________
