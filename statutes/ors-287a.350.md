# Public records REFUNDING BONDS (ORS 287A.350)

## At a glance

ORS 287A.350 — Public records REFUNDING BONDS. Chapter 287A (Chapter 287A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

287A.350 Public records. The records of registered bond ownership, whether maintained by a public body or otherwise, are not public records within the meaning of ORS 192.311. [2007 c.783 §69; 2017 c.456 §10] REFUNDING BONDS
