# Report to legislative committee CLEANUP OF TOXIC CONTAMINATION FROM ILLEGAL DRUG MANUFACTURING (ORS 453.835)

## At a glance

## Full text

453.835 Report to legislative committee. The Department of Transportation shall submit regular reports on progress made toward completion of the plans to a committee designated by the Speaker of the House and the President of the Senate. If no such committee is designated, the reports shall be submitted to the Emergency Board.
