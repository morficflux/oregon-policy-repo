# Liability of art dealer for violation of ORS (ORS 359.250)

## At a glance

ORS 359.250 — Liability of art dealer for violation of ORS. Chapter 359 (Chapter 359), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

359.250 Liability of art dealer for violation of ORS 359.220.

(1) An art dealer who violates ORS 359.220 is liable to the consignor for $100 plus actual damages, including incidental damages sustained as a result of the violation.

(2) If an art dealer violates ORS 359.220, the consignor’s obligation for compensation to the art dealer is voidable by the consignor. [1985 c.830 §7; 2011 c.230 §8]
