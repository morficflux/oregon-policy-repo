## Full text

458.478 Home Start Lands Fund.

(1) The Home Start Lands Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the Home Start Lands Fund shall be credited to the fund. Moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Department of Administrative Services for the purpose of implementing ORS 197A.447 and 458.461 to 458.478, including providing grants and loans under ORS 458.469 (4).

(2) The Home Start Lands Fund consists of: (a) All moneys received under ORS 458.471

(1) and (2); (b) Moneys appropriated or otherwise transferred to the fund by the Legislative Assembly; and (c) Other moneys deposited in the fund from any source. [2025 c.481 §9] Note: See note under 458.461. LOCAL INNOVATION AND FAST TRACK HOUSING PROGRAM
