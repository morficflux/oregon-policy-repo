# Prepayment of additional taxes; extending taxes on tax roll; collection; distribution (ORS 308A.324)

## At a glance

ORS 308A.324 — Prepayment of additional taxes; extending taxes on tax roll; collection; distribution. Chapter 308A (Chapter 308A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308A.324 Prepayment of additional taxes; extending taxes on tax roll; collection; distribution.

(1) The amount determined to be due under ORS 308A.318 or 308A.321 may be paid to the tax collector prior to the completion of the next general property tax roll, pursuant to ORS 311.370.

(2) The amounts under ORS 308A.318 or 308A.321 shall be added to the tax extended against the land on the next general property tax roll, to be collected and distributed in the same manner as the remainder of the real property taxes. [Formerly 308.780]
