## Full text

407.490 Mortgage insurance claims; liability of department. After assignment transfer and delivery by the Department of Veterans’ Affairs of all rights in the insured property to the organization or person providing insurance, the department shall not be liable to any borrower for any claim or grievance arising out of the insurance agreement or the loan agreement. [1989 c.677 §4; 2005 c.625 §48] FINANCES
