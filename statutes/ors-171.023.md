# When member-elect may resign (Vacancies) (ORS 171.023)

## At a glance

ORS 171.023 — When member-elect may resign (Vacancies). Chapter 171 (State Legislature), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

171.023 When member-elect may resign. Any person who receives a certificate of election as a member of the Legislative Assembly is at liberty to resign the office, though the person may not have entered upon the execution of its duties or taken the requisite oath of office.
