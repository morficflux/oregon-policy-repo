# Notice to adjacent governments of potential increased infrastructure use (ORS 285C.142)

## At a glance

ORS 285C.142 — Notice to adjacent governments of potential increased infrastructure use. Chapter 285C (Chapter 285C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285C.142 Notice to adjacent governments of potential increased infrastructure use. The sponsor of an enterprise zone intending to enter into an agreement with a business firm for purposes of ORS 285C.050 to 285C.250 that may increase the use of infrastructure located outside the zone sponsor’s boundaries shall provide timely notice of the intent to all adjacent local governments within whose boundaries infrastructure may be so affected. [2023 c.298 §24]
