# Penalties; rules PENALTIES (ORS 676.890)

## At a glance

ORS 676.890 — Penalties; rules PENALTIES. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.890 Penalties; rules. The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt rules necessary to carry out ORS 676.870 to 676.890, including but not limited to penalties for violation of ORS 676.870 to 676.890 or rules adopted under ORS 676.870 to 676.890. [2015 c.373 §5] PENALTIES
