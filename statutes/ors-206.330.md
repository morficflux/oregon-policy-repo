# Sheriff entitled to rewards (ORS 206.330)

## At a glance

ORS 206.330 — Sheriff entitled to rewards. Chapter 206 (Chapter 206), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

206.330 Sheriff entitled to rewards. The sheriff is entitled to demand and receive to the sheriff’s own use any reward offered in pursuance of law for the apprehension of any person charged with or suspected of crime, when the sheriff has earned the same by a compliance with such offer. [Formerly 206.150] 206.340 [Formerly 206.160; repealed by 1981 c.808 §6]
