# Criminal penalty GENERALLY (ORS 348.992)

## At a glance

ORS 348.992 — Criminal penalty GENERALLY. Chapter 348 (Chapter 348), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

348.992 Criminal penalty. Violation of any of the provisions of ORS 348.594 to 348.615 by any person individually or on behalf of an organization or group is a Class B misdemeanor. [1997 c.652 §15; 1999 c.59 §97] CHAPTER 349 [Reserved for expansion] _______________
