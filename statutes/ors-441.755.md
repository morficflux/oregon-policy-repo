## Full text

441.755 Report form; contents.

(1) The Oregon Health Authority shall prescribe a form to be used by hospitals to make the report required by ORS 441.750 (1)(b) and shall prescribe the frequency of such reports.

(2) The report form may include the name of the hospital reporting, the date of birth, race and sex of person described in subsection

(1) of this section, the suicide method used by the person and known prior attempts in the past 12 months.

(3) The authority shall compile the results from the reports and report the results to the public. [1987 c.189 §2; 2009 c.595 §744] Note: See note under 441.750. HOSPITAL STAFFING (Staffing Committees and Plans)
