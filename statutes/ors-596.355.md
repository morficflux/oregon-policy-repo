# Prohibition against importation of livestock from quarantined area (ORS 596.355)

## At a glance

ORS 596.355 — Prohibition against importation of livestock from quarantined area. Chapter 596 (Disease Control Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

596.355 Prohibition against importation of livestock from quarantined area. No person shall import into this state any livestock from an area of any other state, territory or country which has been placed under quarantine by the United States Department of Agriculture or by the state, territory or country of origin in violation of the rules of the United States Department of Agriculture or of the state, territory or country of origin. [1955 c.557 §34] 596.360 [Repealed by 1955 c.557 §36]
