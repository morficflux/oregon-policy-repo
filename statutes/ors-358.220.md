# Annual historical society report of fund moneys expended (ORS 358.220)

## At a glance

ORS 358.220 — Annual historical society report of fund moneys expended. Chapter 358 (Chapter 358), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

358.220 Annual historical society report of fund moneys expended. On or before January 1 of each year, every historical society specified in ORS 358.200 which has received moneys from a county historical fund shall submit a report in writing to the governing body of the county showing in detail how such moneys have been expended during the preceding fiscal year ending June 30. [Amended by 1977 c.325 §3; 2023 c.173 §7]
