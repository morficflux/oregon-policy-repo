# Advance for expenses of travel and subsistence (ORS 292.280)

## At a glance

ORS 292.280 — Advance for expenses of travel and subsistence. Chapter 292 (Salaries and Expenses of State Officers and Employees), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

292.280 Advance for expenses of travel and subsistence. Notwithstanding ORS chapters 291, 292 and 293, any officer or employee of any state agency may receive an advance for approved necessary expenses of travel and subsistence arising out of official duties or employment, in the manner provided in ORS 292.286 and 292.288. [1955 c.765 §1; 1973 c.158 §1] 292.283 [1955 c.765 §2; 1971 c.244 §2; repealed by 1973 c.158 §5]
