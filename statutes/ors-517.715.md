# Exemptions from permit requirement (ORS 517.715)

## At a glance

ORS 517.715 — Exemptions from permit requirement. Chapter 517 (Chapter 517), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

517.715 Exemptions from permit requirement.

(1) When exploration will result in less than one acre of surface disturbance or drilling to 50 feet or less, any person conducting exploration is exempted from the requirements of the permit procedure described in ORS 517.702 to 517.740. However, nothing in this section exempts a person from the requirements of ORS chapter 273 or the requirements of other departments.

(2) All mineral exploration drill holes shall comply with the abandonment procedures specified in ORS 517.705 (2)(f). [Formerly 517.966; 1999 c.492 §12; 2020 s.s.2 c.4 §11]
