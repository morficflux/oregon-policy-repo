# Report on productivity improvement program RULES GOVERNING AGENCY-PROVIDED HOUSING (ORS 182.400)

## At a glance

ORS 182.400 — Report on productivity improvement program RULES GOVERNING AGENCY-PROVIDED HOUSING. Chapter 182 (State Administrative Agencies Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

182.400 Report on productivity improvement program. Prior to January 1 of each odd-numbered year, the Oregon Department of Administrative Services shall report to the Legislative Assembly on activities of the productivity improvement program in the manner prescribed in ORS 192.245.
