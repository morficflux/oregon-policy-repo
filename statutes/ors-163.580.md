## At a glance

ORS 163.580 — Display of sign concerning sale of smoking devices. Chapter 163 (Chapter 163), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

163.580 Display of sign concerning sale of smoking devices.

(1) A person who sells any of the smoking devices listed in ORS 163.575 (1)(d) shall display a sign clearly stating that the sale of such devices to persons under 18 years of age is prohibited by law.

(2) A person who violates this section commits a Class B violation.
