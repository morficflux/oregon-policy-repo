# Application of insider trading regulation (ORS 732.425)

## At a glance

ORS 732.425 — Application of insider trading regulation. Chapter 732 (Chapter 732), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

732.425 Application of insider trading regulation. The provisions of ORS 732.430, 732.435 and 732.440 do not apply to equity securities of a domestic stock insurer if:

(1) Such securities are registered, or are required to be registered, pursuant to section 12 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended; or

(2) Such insurer does not have any class of its equity securities held of record by 100 or more persons on the last business day of the year next preceding the year in which equity securities of the insurer would be subject to the provisions of ORS 732.430, 732.435 and 732.440 except for the provisions of this subsection. [Formerly 738.720]
