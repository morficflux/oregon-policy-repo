# State Archivist (ORS 357.815)

## At a glance

ORS 357.815 — State Archivist. Chapter 357 (Chapter 357), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

357.815 State Archivist. The office of State Archivist hereby is created. It shall be under the control and supervision of the Secretary of State, who shall, subject to any applicable provisions of the State Personnel Relations Law, appoint and fix the compensation of the archivist and such assistants as may be necessary. No person who has not had at least five years’ experience as an archivist shall be eligible for the office of State Archivist. [Formerly 358.010; 1973 c.439 §3] 357.820 [Renumbered 357.965]
