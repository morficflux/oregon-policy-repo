# Levying board to submit audit report or financial statements annually (ORS 294.665)

## At a glance

ORS 294.665 — Levying board to submit audit report or financial statements annually. Chapter 294 (Chapter 294), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

294.665 Levying board to submit audit report or financial statements annually. The levying board of each municipal corporation under the jurisdiction of the commission shall annually submit a copy of the full report of its independent auditor under ORS 297.425, or a copy of the financial statements submitted to the Secretary of State under ORS 297.435, as soon as practicable after the close of each fiscal year. [Amended by 1999 c.632 §19]
