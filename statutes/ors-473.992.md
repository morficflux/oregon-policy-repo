# Penalty upon failure to pay agricultural products tax (ORS 473.992)

## At a glance

ORS 473.992 — Penalty upon failure to pay agricultural products tax. Chapter 473 (Chapter 473), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

473.992 Penalty upon failure to pay agricultural products tax. Failure to pay a tax under ORS 473.045

(5) is a Class C misdemeanor. [2003 c.797 §9; 2011 c.597 §216] Note: 473.992 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 473 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. _______________
