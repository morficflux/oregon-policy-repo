# Adoption of basin plans (ORS 496.475)

## At a glance

ORS 496.475 — Adoption of basin plans. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.475 Adoption of basin plans. The plans adopted pursuant to ORS 496.470 shall:

(1) Incorporate sound science;

(2) Be based upon adaptive management, incorporating monitoring and evaluation and clearly defined objectives and outcomes;

(3) Benefit fish and wildlife;

(4) Be consistent with efforts of the State of Oregon to recover salmonid populations listed under the federal Endangered Species Act, 16 U.S.C. 1531 to 1544; and

(5) Include a risk versus benefit analysis to wild fish. [1999 c.671 §2; 2001 c.97 §2] Note: See note under 496.470.
