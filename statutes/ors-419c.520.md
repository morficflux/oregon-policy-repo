# Definitions (ORS 419C.520)

## At a glance

ORS 419C.520 — Definitions. Chapter 419C (Chapter 419C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419C.520 Definitions. As used in ORS 419C.411, 419C.522 to 419C.527 and 419C.529 to 419C.542:

(1) “Conditional release” includes but is not limited to the monitoring of mental and physical health treatment.

(2) “Qualifying mental disorder” does not include an abnormality: (a) Manifested only by repeated criminal or otherwise antisocial conduct; (b) Constituting solely a personality disorder; or (c) Constituting solely a conduct disorder.

(3) “Serious mental condition” means a condition that requires supervision and treatment services for the safety of others and is: (a) A mental illness of major depression; (b) A mental illness of bipolar disorder; or (c) A mental illness of psychotic disorder. [2005 c.843 §3; 2007 c.889 §3; 2017 c.634 §22; 2025 c.56 §15]
