# Agency’s responsibility (Permanency Hearing) (ORS 419B.460)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.460 — Agency’s responsibility (Permanency Hearing). Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.460 Agency’s responsibility. Where a child has been surrendered for adoption and the agency has not physically placed the child for adoption or initiated adoption proceedings within six months of receiving the child, the agency shall file a petition alleging that the child comes within the jurisdiction of the court. [1993 c.33 §134] (Permanency Hearing)
