# Access of telecommunications customers to 2-1-1 system (ORS 403.422)

## At a glance

ORS 403.422 — Access of telecommunications customers to 2-1-1 system. Chapter 403 (Chapter 403), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

403.422 Access of telecommunications customers to 2-1-1 system. Upon written request of the 2-1-1 provider, telecommunications service providers shall enable 2-1-1 for customers accessing telecommunications services through local exchange service, cellular service or other wired or wireless means. [2011 c.205 §7; 2021 c.111 §6]
