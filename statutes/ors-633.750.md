# Disposition of fees and charges paid under ORS (ORS 633.750)

## At a glance

ORS 633.750 — Disposition of fees and charges paid under ORS. Chapter 633 (Chapter 633), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

633.750 Disposition of fees and charges paid under ORS 633.511 to 633.750. All fees paid to the State Department of Agriculture pursuant to ORS 633.511 to 633.750, other than the fees and charges specified in ORS 633.610 and 633.630, shall be deposited in the Department of Agriculture Service Fund. All such moneys are continuously appropriated to the department for the purpose of carrying out those sections. [Amended by 1979 c.499 §32; 2003 c.14 §388] MISCELLANEOUS
