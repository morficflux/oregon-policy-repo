# Charges related to firearms (ORS 726.395)

## At a glance

ORS 726.395 — Charges related to firearms. Chapter 726 (Chapter 726), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

726.395 Charges related to firearms.

(1) A pawnbroker may charge a pledgor or customer a $5 fee if the pledgor or customer places a firearm with the pawnbroker. The pawnbroker may charge a $3 fee to renew the pledgor’s or customer’s placement of the firearm with the pawnbroker.

(2) In addition to the fees described in subsection

(1) of this section, a pawnbroker may charge a pledgor or customer any fee that a government entity imposes on the pawnbroker for holding or transferring a firearm. [1973 c.449 §3; 1997 c.842 §4; 2009 c.372 §4; 2014 c.54 §1; 2023 c.7 §2]
