# Subtraction of urban renewal increment from assessed value (ORS 310.232)

## At a glance

ORS 310.232 — Subtraction of urban renewal increment from assessed value. Chapter 310 (Chapter 310), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

310.232 Subtraction of urban renewal increment from assessed value. The assessed value of taxable property of a taxing district shall be further adjusted by the assessor for purposes of determining the district’s amount of taxes before compression under ORS 310.242 by subtracting any assessed value in the district attributable to an urban renewal increment in the district. [1997 c.541 §31]
