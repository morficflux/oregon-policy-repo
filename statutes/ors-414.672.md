## At a glance

ORS 414.672 — Tribal-based practices for mental health and substance abuse prevention, counseling and treatment. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.672 Tribal-based practices for mental health and substance abuse prevention, counseling and treatment. A medical assistance program shall consider tribal-based practices for mental health and substance abuse prevention, counseling and treatment services for members who are Native American or Alaska Native as equivalent to evidence-based practices for purposes of meeting standards of care and shall reimburse for those tribal-based practices.
