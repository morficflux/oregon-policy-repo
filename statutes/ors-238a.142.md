# Accrual by academic employees of community college (ORS 238A.142)

## At a glance

ORS 238A.142 — Accrual by academic employees of community college. Chapter 238A (Chapter 238A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

238A.142 Accrual by academic employees of community college. For purposes of calculating hours of service of an academic employee of a community college operated under ORS chapter 341 or a public university listed in ORS 352.002, each hour assigned to the academic employee as an hour of lecture time or as an hour of classroom time constitutes 2.67 hours of service. [2005 c.332 §20; 2007 c.769 §10; 2023 c.512 §2]
