## Full text

811.127 Organizing a speed racing event; penalty.

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, a person commits the offense of organizing a speed racing event if the person, to facilitate or aid another individual who is participating in an activity described in ORS 811.125 or 811.140 (1)(b): (a) Obstructs or places a barricade on a highway; or (b) Assists in placing an obstruction or barricade upon a highway.

(2) A person who organizes a speed racing event is not in violation of this section if the person has a permit from the road authority for the highway on which the event takes place.

(3) The offense described in this section, organizing a speed racing event, is: (a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this subsection, a Class A misdemeanor. (b) For a person’s second or subsequent conviction within a five-year period preceding the date of the person’s current conviction, a Class C felony. [2003 c.550 §2; 2023 c.158 §1] (Impeding Traffic)
