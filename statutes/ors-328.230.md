# Issuance of bonds upon favorable vote (ORS 328.230)

## At a glance

ORS 328.230 — Issuance of bonds upon favorable vote. Chapter 328 (Chapter 328), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

328.230 Issuance of bonds upon favorable vote. If the electors of the district approve the contracting of bonded indebtedness, the bonds shall be issued as prescribed in ORS chapter 287A. [Amended by 1965 c.100 §53; 1971 c.140 §1; 1983 c.350 §139; 2007 c.783 §128] 328.235 [Amended by 1971 c.140 §2; 1977 c.311 §1; 1981 c.94 §27; 1995 c.333 §10; repealed by 2007 c.783 §234]
