# Applications for amendment convention withdrawn ELECTION DATES (ORS 171.150)

## At a glance

ORS 171.150 — Applications for amendment convention withdrawn ELECTION DATES. Chapter 171 (State Legislature), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

171.150 Applications for amendment convention withdrawn. An application for an amendment convention under Article V of the United States Constitution that was submitted by the Legislative Assembly to Congress before September 24, 2023, is hereby withdrawn and is null and void. [2023 c.349 §1] ELECTION DATES
