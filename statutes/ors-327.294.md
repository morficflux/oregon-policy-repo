# School Improvement Fund (ORS 327.294)

## At a glance

ORS 327.294 — School Improvement Fund. Chapter 327 (Chapter 327), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

327.294 School Improvement Fund.

(1) There is established the School Improvement Fund, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the School Improvement Fund shall be credited to the fund.

(2) The moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Department of Education for purposes of the grant program created by ORS 327.297.

(3) Each fiscal year, the department shall distribute: (a) 95.25 percent of the moneys in the fund as grants to school districts, the Youth Corrections Education Program and the Juvenile Detention Education Program; and (b) 4.75 percent of the moneys in the fund as grants to education service districts. [2001 c.794 §2; 2007 c.578 §2]
