# Department not required to provide training for certification of reserve officers (ORS 181A.425)

## At a glance

ORS 181A.425 — Department not required to provide training for certification of reserve officers. Chapter 181A (State Police; Public Safety Standards and Training), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

181A.425 Department not required to provide training for certification of reserve officers. Nothing in ORS 181A.355, 181A.395, 181A.405 (1), 181A.410, 181A.420

(1) and (2), 181A.425, 181A.430, 181A.470, 181A.490, 181A.500, 181A.570, 181A.580, 181A.590, 181A.630, 181A.640 and 181A.650 requires:

(1) A law enforcement unit to certify individuals who are utilized by the law enforcement unit to perform the duties of a reserve officer; or

(2) The Department of Public Safety Standards and Training to provide the training for, or to fund, certification of reserve officers. [Formerly 181.711]
