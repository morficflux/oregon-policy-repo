# Department inspection (ORS 517.930)

## At a glance

ORS 517.930 — Department inspection. Chapter 517 (Chapter 517), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

517.930 Department inspection. Notwithstanding ORS 517.850, if the State Department of Geology and Mineral Industries has reason to believe that the provisions of an operating permit are being violated or that a surface mining operation is being conducted without a valid operating permit, it may inspect such surface mining areas without prior notice.
