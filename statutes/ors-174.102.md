# “Agricultural commodity,” “agricultural product” defined; harvesting or baling of straw as farming practice (ORS 174.102)

## At a glance

ORS 174.102 — “Agricultural commodity,” “agricultural product” defined; harvesting or baling of straw as farming practice. Chapter 174 (Construction of Statutes; General Definitions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

174.102 “Agricultural commodity,” “agricultural product” defined; harvesting or baling of straw as farming practice. As used in the statute laws of this state and in any administrative rule adopted pursuant thereto unless the context or a specifically applicable definition requires otherwise:

(1) The term “agricultural commodity” or “agricultural product” includes straw.

(2) The harvesting or baling of straw is a farming practice. [1995 c.601 §1] 174.103 [1987 c.162 §§1,2; 1989 c.264 §1; 2001 c.90 §1; repealed by 2003 c.242 §7]
