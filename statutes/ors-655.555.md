## At a glance

## Full text

655.555 Rules for administration of fund for injuries of adults in custody. The Oregon Department of Administrative Services, with the approval of the Department of Corrections and the Oregon Youth Authority, shall adopt rules necessary to administer the adult in custody injury fund. The rules may provide for procedures, benefits and coverage that vary from the workers’ compensation system.
