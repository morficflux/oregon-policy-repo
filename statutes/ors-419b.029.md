# Power to subpoena documents and records in child abuse investigations (ORS 419B.029)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.029 — Power to subpoena documents and records in child abuse investigations. Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.029 Power to subpoena documents and records in child abuse investigations. In connection with an investigation under ORS 419B.020, the Director of Human Services may by subpoena compel the production of documents and records, including audio records, video records, photographs and student records, that the director deems relevant or material to the investigation. [2023 c.195 §1] Note: 419B.029 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 419B or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
