# Report of marriage to county clerk (ORS 106.170)

## At a glance

ORS 106.170 — Report of marriage to county clerk. Chapter 106 (Marriage; Domestic Partnership), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

106.170 Report of marriage to county clerk. A person solemnizing a marriage shall, within five calendar days of the marriage ceremony, complete the original application, license and record of marriage form and deliver the form to the county clerk who issued the marriage license. The person solemnizing the marriage may keep a copy of the application, license and record of marriage form. [Amended by 1981 c.176 §2; 2001 c.501 §3; 2007 c.703 §7; 2015 c.168 §4] 106.180 [Amended by 1999 c.776 §2; repealed by 2007 c.703 §11]
