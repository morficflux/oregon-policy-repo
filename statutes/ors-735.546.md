# Pharmacy claims audits; auditors (ORS 735.546)

## At a glance

ORS 735.546 — Pharmacy claims audits; auditors. Chapter 735 (Chapter 735), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

735.546 Pharmacy claims audits; auditors. An entity that contracts with an independent third party to conduct audits may not:

(1) Agree to compensate the independent third party based on a percentage of the amount of overpayments recovered; or

(2) Disclose information obtained during an audit except to the contracting entity, the pharmacy subject to the audit or the holder of the policy or certificate of insurance that paid the claim. [2013 c.570 §7] Note: See note under 735.530.
