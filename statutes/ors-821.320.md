# Snowmobile registration and permit fees (All-Terrain Vehicle Highway Access Routes) (ORS 821.320)

## At a glance

ORS 821.320 — Snowmobile registration and permit fees (All-Terrain Vehicle Highway Access Routes). Chapter 821 (Chapter 821), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

821.320 Snowmobile registration and permit fees. The following fees are established relating to snowmobiles:

(1) Registration under ORS 821.080, $10.

(2) Renewal of registration under ORS 821.080, $10.

(3) Issuance of out-of-state permit under ORS 821.130, $7. [1983 c.338 §737; 1985 c.16 §354; 1985 c.459 §13; 1987 c.261 §5; 1989 c.991 §21; 1995 c.774 §23; 1999 c.977 §37] (All-Terrain Vehicle Highway Access Routes)
