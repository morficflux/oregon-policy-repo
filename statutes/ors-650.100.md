# Disposition of civil penalties MOTOR VEHICLE DEALERSHIPS (ORS 650.100)

## At a glance

ORS 650.100 — Disposition of civil penalties MOTOR VEHICLE DEALERSHIPS. Chapter 650 (Chapter 650), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

650.100 Disposition of civil penalties. All penalties recovered under ORS 650.095 shall be paid into the State Treasury and credited to the General Fund and are available for general government expenses. [2005 c.339 §5] MOTOR VEHICLE DEALERSHIPS
