# Requirements for individualized family service plans and individualized education programs for children who are deaf or deafblind or who are hard of hearing (ORS 343.148)

## At a glance

ORS 343.148 — Requirements for individualized family service plans and individualized education programs for children who are deaf or deafblind or who are hard of hearing. Chapter 343 (Chapter 343), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

343.148 Requirements for individualized family service plans and individualized education programs for children who are deaf or deafblind or who are hard of hearing. In addition to any other requirements prescribed by the State Board of Education by rule for individualized family service plans or individualized education programs, the board shall require that whenever a plan or program is developed, revised or reviewed for a child who is deaf or deafblind or who is hard of hearing, the parents of the child must be provided information about relevant services and placements offered by the school district, the education service district, regional programs and the Oregon School for the Deaf. [2021 c.255 §2] Note: 343.148 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 343 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 343.149 [1999 c.989 §22; repealed by 2005 c.209 §41 and 2005 c.662 §17] 343.150 [Repealed by 1965 c.100 §456]
