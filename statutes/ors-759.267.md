# Service promotion activities (ORS 759.267)

## At a glance

ORS 759.267 — Service promotion activities. Chapter 759 (Chapter 759), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

759.267 Service promotion activities. A telecommunications utility may promote the use of its services by offering a waiver of part or all of a recurring or a nonrecurring charge, a redemption coupon or a premium with the purchase of a service. ORS 759.260 and 759.265 do not apply to promotions under this section, but the customer group to which the promotion is available must be based on reasonable distinctions among customers. [1993 c.204 §4]
