# Application of ORS (ORS 673.350)

## At a glance

ORS 673.350 — Application of ORS. Chapter 673 (Chapter 673), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

673.350 Application of ORS 673.010 to 673.465 to employees of registered business organizations. Nothing contained in ORS 673.010 to 673.465 shall prohibit any person not a certified public accountant or public accountant from serving as an employee of a business organization registered under ORS 673.160. However, such an employee shall not issue any report on an accounting or financial statement over that employee’s name. [Amended by 1975 c.440 §19; 1985 c.605 §13; 1993 c.431 §9; 1999 c.322 §20]
