# Liability of department (ORS 607.339)

## At a glance

ORS 607.339 — Liability of department. Chapter 607 (Chapter 607), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

607.339 Liability of department. Except for gross negligence the State Department of Agriculture is not liable for the payment of the proceeds of sale or return of custody of an estray animal to a person claiming to be the true owner of the animal. [1953 c.367 §7; 2009 c.336 §14] 607.340 [Repealed by 1953 c.367 §13]
