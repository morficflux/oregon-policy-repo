# Applicability of chapter to reauthorization of existing hydroelectric project (ORS 543.012)

## At a glance

ORS 543.012 — Applicability of chapter to reauthorization of existing hydroelectric project. Chapter 543 (Chapter 543), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

543.012 Applicability of chapter to reauthorization of existing hydroelectric project.

(1) Except for the provisions of ORS 543.300, 543.310, 543.430, 543.440, 543.610, 543.650 to 543.685, 543.710, 543.720, 543.730 and 543.990, nothing in this chapter shall apply to the reauthorization of an existing project.

(2) All references in ORS 543.300, 543.310, 543.430, 543.440, 543.610, 543.650 to 543.685, 543.710, 543.720, 543.730 and 543.990 to a license or a license issued under ORS 543.010 to 543.610 also shall be considered a reference to a water right issued under ORS 468.065, 468B.040, 468B.045, 468B.046, 536.015, 536.050, 543.012 and 543.710 and ORS chapter 543A. [1997 c.449 §42]
