# Regulation in addition to other aerosolizing, smoking or vaporizing regulations (ORS 433.870)

## At a glance

## Full text

433.870 Regulation in addition to other aerosolizing, smoking or vaporizing regulations. ORS 433.835 to 433.875 and rules adopted under ORS 433.835 to 433.875 are in addition to and not in lieu of any other law regulating the smoking, aerosolizing or vaporizing of inhalants. [1981 c.384 §11; 2001 c.104 §164; 2001 c.990 §5; 2007 c.602 §6; 2015 c.158 §20]
