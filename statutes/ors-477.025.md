## At a glance

## Full text

477.025 Variability of wildland-urban interface fire protection problems. The Legislative Assembly recognizes that the wildland-urban interface in Oregon varies by condition, situation, fire hazard and risk, that different wildland-urban interface fire protection problems exist across the state because of this variability, and that these different problems necessitate varied fire prevention and protection practices.
