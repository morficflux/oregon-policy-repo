# Public sale of copies of city comprehensive plan and land use regulations (ORS 227.187)

## At a glance

ORS 227.187 — Public sale of copies of city comprehensive plan and land use regulations. Chapter 227 (Chapter 227), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

227.187 Public sale of copies of city comprehensive plan and land use regulations. A city shall maintain copies of its comprehensive plan and land use regulations, as defined in ORS 197.015, for sale to the public. [1991 c.363 §3]
