# Definitions (ORS 305.005)

## At a glance

ORS 305.005 — Definitions. Chapter 305 (Chapter 305), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

305.005 Definitions. As used in the revenue and tax laws of this state, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “Department” means the Department of Revenue.

(2) “Director” means the Director of the Department of Revenue. [1969 c.520 §2; 1995 c.79 §105; 1995 c.650 §105] Note: The definition of “manufactured structure” provided in 307.021 applies to ORS chapter 305. 305.010 [Formerly 306.010; repealed by 1969 c.520 §49]
