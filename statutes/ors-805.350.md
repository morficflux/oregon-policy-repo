## Full text

805.350 Violation of farm registration limits; penalty.

(1) A person commits the offense of violation of farm registration limits if the person uses or owns and permits to be used a vehicle registered under ORS 805.300 for purposes other than purposes described under ORS 805.390.

(2) The offense described in this section, violation of farm registration limits, is a Class A misdemeanor. [1983 c.338 §775; 1985 c.16 §372]
