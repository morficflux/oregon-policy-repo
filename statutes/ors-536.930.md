# Consequence of failure to follow order (ORS 536.930)

## At a glance

ORS 536.930 — Consequence of failure to follow order. Chapter 536 (Water Resources Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

536.930 Consequence of failure to follow order. Any owner or operator who fails without sufficient cause to take corrective action as required by an order of the Water Resources Commission shall be liable for damages not to exceed the amount of all expenses incurred by the Water Resources Department in carrying out the department’s enforcement duties related to the corrective action. [1989 c.618 §7; 1991 c.734 §51] Note: See note under 536.900.
