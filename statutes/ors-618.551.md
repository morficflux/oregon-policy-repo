# Remedies supplementary to existing statutory or common law remedies PENALTIES (ORS 618.551)

## At a glance

ORS 618.551 — Remedies supplementary to existing statutory or common law remedies PENALTIES. Chapter 618 (Chapter 618), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

618.551 Remedies supplementary to existing statutory or common law remedies. The remedies provided in ORS 618.506 to 618.541 and 618.995 are in addition to all other remedies, civil or criminal, existing at common law or under the laws of this state.
