# Appearance by respondent; affirmative defenses abolished (ORS 107.055)

## At a glance

ORS 107.055 — Appearance by respondent; affirmative defenses abolished. Chapter 107 (Chapter 107), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

107.055 Appearance by respondent; affirmative defenses abolished. The respondent shall not be required to answer a petition for annulment or dissolution of a marriage or for separation except by filing a general appearance or a general appearance with counterclaims relating to matters other than the grounds for annulment, dissolution or separation. Affirmative defenses are abolished.
