# Reciprocal sales to nonresidential electricity consumers (ORS 757.629)

## At a glance

ORS 757.629 — Reciprocal sales to nonresidential electricity consumers. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.629 Reciprocal sales to nonresidential electricity consumers. An electric utility that sells electricity, either directly or through a related party, to a nonresidential electricity consumer of another electric utility in this state shall permit any other electricity service supplier to sell electricity to nonresidential electricity consumers of the electric utility. [1999 c.865 §11] 757.630 [1961 c.691 §6; renumbered 758.425] 757.631 [Formerly 758.090; renumbered 165.840]
