# Rejection of bids (Competitive Proposals) (ORS 279C.395)

## At a glance

ORS 279C.395 — Rejection of bids (Competitive Proposals). Chapter 279C (Public Contracting - Public Improvements), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

279C.395 Rejection of bids. A contracting agency may reject any bid not in compliance with all prescribed public bidding procedures and requirements, and may, for good cause, reject all bids upon a finding of the contracting agency it is in the public interest to do so. In any case where competitive bids are required and all bids are rejected, and the proposed project is not abandoned, new bids may be called for as in the first instance. [2003 c.794 §121] (Competitive Proposals)
