# Prohibited acts (ORS 285A.106)

## At a glance

ORS 285A.106 — Prohibited acts. Chapter 285A (Chapter 285A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285A.106 Prohibited acts. The Oregon Infrastructure Finance Authority may not direct, supervise or control the policy, programs, personnel or administration of any program or entity unless specifically authorized by statute. [2009 c.830 §6]
