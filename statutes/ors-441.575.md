# Authorities may act jointly (ORS 441.575)

## At a glance

ORS 441.575 — Authorities may act jointly. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.575 Authorities may act jointly. All powers and responsibilities provided in ORS 441.525 to 441.595 may be exercised and discharged by two or more authorities acting jointly to effectuate the purposes of ORS 441.525 to 441.595. [1973 c.153 §11]
