# Rules ENVIRONMENTAL LABORATORIES (ORS 438.450)

## At a glance

ORS 438.450 — Rules ENVIRONMENTAL LABORATORIES. Chapter 438 (Chapter 438), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

438.450 Rules. The Oregon Health Authority shall make such rules as are necessary for carrying out ORS 438.010 to 438.450.
