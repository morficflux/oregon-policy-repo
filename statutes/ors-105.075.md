# Notice to quit; action to recover possession not affected by forcible entry or wrongful detainer (ORS 105.075)

## At a glance

ORS 105.075 — Notice to quit; action to recover possession not affected by forcible entry or wrongful detainer. Chapter 105 (Chapter 105), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

105.075 Notice to quit; action to recover possession not affected by forcible entry or wrongful detainer. In any action to recover the possession of real property, as provided for in ORS 105.005, notice to quit, when necessary, may be given as prescribed in ORS 91.050 to 91.110 and 105.120. Nothing in ORS 105.100 to 105.168 prevents such action from being maintained for the recovery of the possession of real property although the entry of the defendant is forcible or the holding is unlawful and with force as defined in ORS 105.105.
