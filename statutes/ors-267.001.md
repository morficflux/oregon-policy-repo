# Authority of certain mass transit and transportation districts to impose vehicle registration fees MASS TRANSIT DISTRICTS (Generally) (ORS 267.001)

## At a glance

ORS 267.001 — Authority of certain mass transit and transportation districts to impose vehicle registration fees MASS TRANSIT DISTRICTS (Generally). Chapter 267 (Chapter 267), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

267.001 Authority of certain mass transit and transportation districts to impose vehicle registration fees. Subject to ORS 801.040, 801.042, 801.237 and 803.445, for the purpose of exercising any power the district, as defined in ORS 801.237, is authorized to exercise, the district may impose registration fees on vehicles under ORS 803.445. [1989 c.864 §11; 2009 c.865 §40b] MASS TRANSIT DISTRICTS (Generally)
