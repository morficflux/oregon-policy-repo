# Application of fluoride varnishes to teeth of children by certain counselors and health assistants (Licensing) (ORS 680.026)

## At a glance

ORS 680.026 — Application of fluoride varnishes to teeth of children by certain counselors and health assistants (Licensing). Chapter 680 (Chapter 680), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

680.026 Application of fluoride varnishes to teeth of children by certain counselors and health assistants. Counselors and health assistants who have been trained in the application of fluoride varnishes to the teeth of children may apply fluoride varnishes to the teeth of children enrolled in or receiving services or benefits from the Women, Infants and Children Program, the Oregon Prenatal to Kindergarten Program or a federal Head Start grant program. [2007 c.379 §6; 2023 c.547 §30] Note: 680.026 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 680 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 680.030 [Amended by 1963 c.266 §3; repealed by 1983 c.169 §34] (Licensing)
