# Nonliability of peace officers enforcing commercial fishing laws (ORS 506.560)

## At a glance

ORS 506.560 — Nonliability of peace officers enforcing commercial fishing laws. Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.560 Nonliability of peace officers enforcing commercial fishing laws. The officers described in ORS 506.521 shall not suffer civil or criminal liability for any arrest, seizure or search as provided under the commercial fishing laws.
