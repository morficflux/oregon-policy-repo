# Board to establish policies and criteria for assessment (State Board) (ORS 680.550)

## At a glance

ORS 680.550 — Board to establish policies and criteria for assessment (State Board). Chapter 680 (Chapter 680), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

680.550 Board to establish policies and criteria for assessment. The State Board of Denture Technology shall establish policies and criteria for the assessment of the quality of the practice of denture technology based on practice standards subject to the approval of the Health Licensing Office.
