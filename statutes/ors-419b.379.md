# Guardian is not conservator (Authority Over Parents) (ORS 419B.379)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.379 — Guardian is not conservator (Authority Over Parents). Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.379 Guardian is not conservator. A person appointed guardian of the ward by the court is guardian only and not a conservator of the estate of the ward, unless that person is appointed conservator of the ward’s estate in a protective proceeding as provided in ORS chapter 125. [1993 c.33 §117; 1995 c.664 §94; 2003 c.396 §67] (Authority Over Parents)
