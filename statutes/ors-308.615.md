# Keeping roll as public record (ORS 308.615)

## At a glance

ORS 308.615 — Keeping roll as public record. Chapter 308 (Chapter 308), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308.615 Keeping roll as public record. When the review of the assessment roll is complete, the Department of Revenue shall keep the roll as a public record. [Amended by 2009 c.128 §11] 308.620 [Amended by 1955 c.735 §4; 1961 c.533 §48; repealed by 1977 c.870 §59]
