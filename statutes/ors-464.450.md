## Full text

464.450 Oregon Gaming Account; uses.

(1) There is hereby created in the General Fund of the State Treasury an account known as the Oregon Gaming Account.

(2) The Department of Justice shall pay into the State Treasury all moneys received by the department under ORS 167.117 and 167.118 and this chapter. The State Treasurer shall deposit the moneys in the General Fund to the credit of the Oregon Gaming Account. The moneys in the Oregon Gaming Account are continuously appropriated to the department to pay its expenses in administering and enforcing its rules and in otherwise performing its lawful duties under ORS 167.117 and 167.118 and this chapter. [1987 c.914 §16] DISCIPLINE
