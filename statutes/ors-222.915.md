# Application of ORS (ORS 222.915)

## At a glance

ORS 222.915 — Application of ORS. Chapter 222 (Chapter 222), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

222.915 Application of ORS 222.840 to 222.915. The provisions of ORS 222.840 to 222.915 do not apply to proceedings to annex territory to any city if the charter or ordinances of the city conflict with or are inconsistent with ORS 222.840 to 222.915. [1967 c.624 §12; 1971 c.673 §5] PENALTIES
