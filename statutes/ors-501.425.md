# John Day River Wildlife Refuge (ORS 501.425)

## At a glance

ORS 501.425 — John Day River Wildlife Refuge. Chapter 501 (Chapter 501), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

501.425 John Day River Wildlife Refuge. There is created a wildlife refuge within the area that is one-fourth mile from the high-water flowline along the John Day River from the Columbia River south to its junction with Thirty Mile Creek. [Formerly 498.215] 501.430 [Repealed by 1955 c.77 §1]
