# Transfer to State School Fund (ORS 305.794)

## At a glance

ORS 305.794 — Transfer to State School Fund. Chapter 305 (Chapter 305), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

305.794 Transfer to State School Fund. Amounts certified by the Department of Revenue to the State Treasurer under ORS 305.792 shall be transferred by the department to the State School Fund for the purposes for which State School Fund moneys may be used. [1999 c.960 §3; 2001 c.114 §7; 2005 c.755 §15]
