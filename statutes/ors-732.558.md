# Termination of registration by director (ORS 732.558)

## At a glance

ORS 732.558 — Termination of registration by director. Chapter 732 (Chapter 732), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

732.558 Termination of registration by director. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services shall terminate the registration of any insurer which demonstrates that it no longer is a member of an insurance holding company system. [Formerly 732.655] 732.560 [1967 c.359 §205; 1983 c.498 §13; 1993 c.447 §34; renumbered 732.536 in 1993]
