# Powers of ports created by special laws (ORS 777.180)

## At a glance

ORS 777.180 — Powers of ports created by special laws. Chapter 777 (Chapter 777), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

777.180 Powers of ports created by special laws. All ports created prior to April 17, 1920, under any special law of the Legislative Assembly of this state shall, in addition to the powers they possessed on that date, have all the powers possessed by ports under the general laws of this state, except the power to borrow money and issue bonds under ORS 777.410 and 777.415.
