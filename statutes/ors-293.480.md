# Adoption of uniform procedure for issuing duplicate instruments (ORS 293.480)

## At a glance

## Full text

293.480 Adoption of uniform procedure for issuing duplicate instruments. State officers, boards, departments or commissions lawfully issuing instruments upon the State Treasurer may adopt the uniform procedure of issuing and delivering to all parties entitled thereto duplicate instruments to replace those lost, stolen or destroyed, in accordance with ORS 293.475. [Formerly 291.522; 1969 c.142 §2; 1979 c.763 §3; 2005 c.110 §4]
