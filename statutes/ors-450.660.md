# Water authority formation (ORS 450.660)

## At a glance

ORS 450.660 — Water authority formation. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.660 Water authority formation. A water authority may be formed by any of the methods provided for in ORS 450.665 to 450.680 or 450.785. [1987 c.863 §2; 1993 c.577 §24]
