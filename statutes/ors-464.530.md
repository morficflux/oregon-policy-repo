## Full text

464.530 Jurisdiction of Circuit Court for Marion County; liability of department personnel.

(1) No trial court of the State of Oregon other than the Circuit Court of Marion County shall have jurisdiction over any action or proceeding against the Department of Justice or any employee of the department, for anything done, omitted to be done in or arising out of the performance of the duties of the department.

(2) Neither the department nor any employee thereof shall be personally liable in any action for damages sustained by any person because of any act done or omitted by the department, or any employee of the department, in the performance of the duties of the department.
