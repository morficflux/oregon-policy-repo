# “Travel trailer” (ORS 801.565)

## At a glance

ORS 801.565 — “Travel trailer”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.565 “Travel trailer.” “Travel trailer” means:

(1) A recreational vehicle without motive power that is eight and one-half feet or less in width and is not being used for commercial or business purposes; and

(2) A prefabricated structure that is eight and one-half feet or less in width and that is not being used for commercial or business purposes. [1983 c.338 §104; 1993 c.696 §8; 2003 c.655 §93] 801.570 [1983 c.338 §105; repealed by 2001 c.522 §12]
