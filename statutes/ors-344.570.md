# Action upon application (ORS 344.570)

## At a glance

ORS 344.570 — Action upon application. Chapter 344 (Chapter 344), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

344.570 Action upon application. Whenever the Department of Human Services receives an application for vocational rehabilitation under ORS 344.560, it shall promptly cause to be obtained and recorded, with respect to such applicant, all essential, pertinent information concerning the circumstances, health condition, vocational aptitudes and experience of the applicant, and such other information as may be necessary for the determination of the eligibility of the applicant and of the nature and amount of vocational rehabilitation services needed. 344.573 [1963 c.522 §7; 1971 c.779 §5; repealed by 2001 c.900 §261] 344.575 [1963 c.522 §3a; repealed by 2001 c.900 §261] 344.577 [1963 c.522 §8; 1971 c.779 §6; repealed by 2001 c.900 §261]
