# Exemptions (ORS 441.119)

## At a glance

ORS 441.119 — Exemptions. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.119 Exemptions. Any requirement in ORS 441.111 to 441.119 and 441.993 may not be applied to a care facility if the requirement is incompatible with:

(1) The professionally reasonable clinical judgment of the management or staff of the care facility; or

(2) A state or federal statute, federal regulation or administrative rule that applies to the care facility. [2023 c.567 §8] 441.120 [Repealed by 1971 c.730 §25] 441.121 [1981 c.534 §7; 2013 c.717 §6; renumbered 441.409 in 2015]
