# Immunity of officer acting in good faith (ORS 163.753)

## At a glance

ORS 163.753 — Immunity of officer acting in good faith. Chapter 163 (Chapter 163), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

163.753 Immunity of officer acting in good faith. A law enforcement officer acting in good faith shall not be liable in any civil action for issuing or not issuing a citation under ORS 163.735. [1993 c.626 §11; 1995 c.353 §9] Note: See note under 163.730.
