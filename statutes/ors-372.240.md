# District employees; expenses of board (ORS 372.240)

## At a glance

ORS 372.240 — District employees; expenses of board. Chapter 372 (Chapter 372), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

372.240 District employees; expenses of board. The district board may employ engineers, superintendents, mechanics, clerks, secretaries or other persons as requisite, necessary or convenient, in carrying on any of its work, at a rate of remuneration fixed by the board. [Amended by 1971 c.403 §6; 1971 c.514 §23] 372.250 [Repealed by 1969 c.344 §8]
