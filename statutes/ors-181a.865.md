# Qualifications for executive manager or supervisory manager (ORS 181A.865)

## At a glance

ORS 181A.865 — Qualifications for executive manager or supervisory manager. Chapter 181A (State Police; Public Safety Standards and Training), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

181A.865 Qualifications for executive manager or supervisory manager. An applicant for licensure as an executive manager or supervisory manager must meet the qualifications set forth in ORS 181A.855. [Formerly 181.877]
