## At a glance

## Full text

824.042 Department to participate in contested abandonment proceedings. The Department of Transportation shall participate before the appropriate federal agency in all contested railroad line abandonment proceedings involving the proposed abandonment of any railroad line in this state. Prior to such participation, the department shall consult with public entities and users of railroad service affected by the proposed abandonment. [Formerly 760.630] SAFETY PROGRAM STANDARD
