# Severability (ORS 147.415)

## At a glance

ORS 147.415 — Severability. Chapter 147 (Chapter 147), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

147.415 Severability. If any section, portion, clause or phrase of chapter 2, Oregon Laws 1987, is for any reason held to be invalid or unconstitutional, the remaining sections, portions, clauses and phrases shall not be affected but shall remain in full force in effect. [1987 c.2 §18] Note: See note under 147.405.
