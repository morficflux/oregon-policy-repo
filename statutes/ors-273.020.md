# Governor as land commissioner STATE LAND BOARD (ORS 273.020)

## At a glance

ORS 273.020 — Governor as land commissioner STATE LAND BOARD. Chapter 273 (Chapter 273), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

273.020 Governor as land commissioner. The Governor is Land Commissioner for the State of Oregon, and shall locate or select the lands to which the state is entitled under the laws of the United States, or otherwise. The Governor shall be allowed all necessary expenses incurred in the performance of duties in this capacity prescribed by law. The Department of State Lands shall assist the Governor as necessary in the performance of these duties.
