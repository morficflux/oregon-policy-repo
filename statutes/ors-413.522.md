# Problem Gambling Treatment Fund (ORS 413.522)

## At a glance

ORS 413.522 — Problem Gambling Treatment Fund. Chapter 413 (Chapter 413), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

413.522 Problem Gambling Treatment Fund.

(1) There is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund, the Problem Gambling Treatment Fund. All moneys in the Problem Gambling Treatment Fund are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Health Authority to be expended for programs for the prevention and treatment of gambling addiction and other emotional and behavioral problems related to gambling and for the administration of the programs.

(2) The Problem Gambling Treatment Fund shall consist of: (a) The net proceeds from the Oregon State Lottery allocated to the fund under ORS 461.549; (b) Moneys appropriated to the fund by the Legislative Assembly; and (c) Interest earnings on moneys in the fund. [Formerly 409.435] Note: See note under 413.520. HEALTH CARE INTERPRETERS
