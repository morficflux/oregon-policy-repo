# Penalty for intentional violation of restraining order (ORS 105.590)

## At a glance

ORS 105.590 — Penalty for intentional violation of restraining order. Chapter 105 (Chapter 105), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

105.590 Penalty for intentional violation of restraining order. An intentional violation of a restraining order, preliminary injunction or order of abatement under ORS 105.550 to 105.600 is a Class B misdemeanor. [1989 c.846 §9; 1999 c.168 §9; 2011 c.597 §159]
