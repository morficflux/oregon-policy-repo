# Simulated election for individuals under 18 years of age (ORS 254.068)

## At a glance

ORS 254.068 — Simulated election for individuals under 18 years of age. Chapter 254 (Chapter 254), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

254.068 Simulated election for individuals under 18 years of age. On the date of any election, the county clerk may conduct a simulated election. As used in this section, “simulated election” means a demonstration election held for individuals under 18 years of age for the purpose of encouraging future voter participation.
