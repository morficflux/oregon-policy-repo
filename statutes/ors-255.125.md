# Application of ORS (ORS 255.125)

## At a glance

ORS 255.125 — Application of ORS. Chapter 255 (Chapter 255), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

255.125 Application of ORS 255.135 to 255.205. ORS 255.135 to 255.205 carry out the provisions of section 1, Article IV, Oregon Constitution, and shall apply to the exercise of initiative or referendum powers by the people of a district regarding a district measure. [1979 c.190 §291]
