# Exhibition of license on boarding vessel (ORS 776.455)

## At a glance

ORS 776.455 — Exhibition of license on boarding vessel. Chapter 776 (Maritime Pilots and Pilotage), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

776.455 Exhibition of license on boarding vessel. On boarding a vessel and if required by the master thereof, a licensee shall exhibit the license before the licensee is authorized to perform a piloting assignment.
