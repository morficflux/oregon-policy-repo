## Full text

444.320 Physician report of childhood diabetes.

(1) As used in this section, “child” means an individual 18 years of age or younger.

(2) In accordance with ORS 444.300, upon diagnosing or first treating a child with Type I or Type II diabetes, a physician shall report to the Oregon Health Authority: (a) The name and address of the child; (b) The gender of the child; (c) The date of birth of the child; (d) The type of diabetes the child has; and (e) The date of diagnosis or first treatment by the reporting physician. [2001 c.719 §3; 2009 c.595 §799]
