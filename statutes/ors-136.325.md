# Jury not to be informed of and not to consider punishment that may be imposed (ORS 136.325)

## At a glance

ORS 136.325 — Jury not to be informed of and not to consider punishment that may be imposed. Chapter 136 (Chapter 136), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

136.325 Jury not to be informed of and not to consider punishment that may be imposed. Except as required in ORS 161.313 and 163.150, the jury in a criminal proceeding may not be informed of, and may not consider, any punishment that the court may impose if the defendant is convicted of the charge. [1997 c.852 §10] Note: 136.325 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 136 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
