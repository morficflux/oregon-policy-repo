## At a glance

## Full text

441.778 Variances to staffing plan requirements. Upon request of a hospital, the Oregon Health Authority may grant a variance to the written hospital-wide staffing plan requirements described in ORS 441.761 to 441.795 if the variance is necessary to ensure that the hospital is staffed to meet the health care needs of patients. [Formerly 441.164] Note: See note under 441.761.
