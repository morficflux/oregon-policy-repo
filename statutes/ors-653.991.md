# Penalties (ORS 653.991)

## At a glance

ORS 653.991 — Penalties. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.991 Penalties. Violation of any provision of this section or ORS 653.010 to 653.565 or of any rule adopted by the Bureau of Labor and Industries under ORS 653.307 is punishable as a misdemeanor. [1971 c.626 §6; 1993 c.18 §138; 2013 c.296 §28] 653.992 [1973 c.564 §16; repealed by 1975 c.114 §1] _______________
