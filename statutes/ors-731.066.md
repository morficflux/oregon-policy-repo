# “Authorized,” “unauthorized” insurer (ORS 731.066)

## At a glance

## Full text

731.066 “Authorized,” “unauthorized” insurer.

(1) An “authorized” insurer is one authorized by a subsisting certificate of authority to transact insurance in this state.

(2) An “unauthorized” insurer is one not so authorized. [1967 c.359 §14]
