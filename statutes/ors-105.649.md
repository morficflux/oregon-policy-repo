# Uniformity of application and construction (ORS 105.649)

## At a glance

ORS 105.649 — Uniformity of application and construction. Chapter 105 (Chapter 105), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

105.649 Uniformity of application and construction. In applying and construing ORS 105.623 to 105.649, consideration must be given to the need to promote uniformity of the law with respect to disclaimers among states that enact versions of the Uniform Disclaimer of Property Interests Act.
