# Recourse of owners of unpaid checks (ORS 293.460)

## At a glance

ORS 293.460 — Recourse of owners of unpaid checks. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.460 Recourse of owners of unpaid checks. The lawful owner of any check or order included in the report referred to in ORS 293.450 and not paid may file a claim with the State Treasurer in the manner provided by ORS 98.392 and 98.396. [Formerly 291.514; 1967 c.454 §65; 1981 c.188 §2; 1993 c.500 §63; 1993 c.694 §32; 2019 c.678 §63]
