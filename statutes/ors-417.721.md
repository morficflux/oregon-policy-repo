# Collaboration with coordinated care organizations (ORS 417.721)

## At a glance

ORS 417.721 — Collaboration with coordinated care organizations. Chapter 417 (Chapter 417), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

417.721 Collaboration with coordinated care organizations. The Oregon Health Authority, the Health Plan Quality Metrics Committee and the Early Learning Council shall work collaboratively with coordinated care organizations to develop performance metrics for prenatal care, delivery and infant care that align with early learning outcomes. [2013 c.728 §8; 2015 c.389 §12] Note: 417.721 and 417.723 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 417 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
