# Enforcement of ORS (ORS 166.422)

## At a glance

ORS 166.422 — Enforcement of ORS. Chapter 166 (Chapter 166), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

166.422 Enforcement of ORS 166.412. Where appropriate, a person may enforce the legal duties imposed by ORS 166.412 (7)(a) or (b), by the provisions of ORS 30.260 to 30.300 and ORS chapter 183. [1989 c.839 §12; 1995 c.729 §8; 2015 c.50 §5] Note: 166.422 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 166 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
