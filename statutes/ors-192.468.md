# Discretion of Public Records Advocate in dispute resolution services (ORS 192.468)

## At a glance

ORS 192.468 — Discretion of Public Records Advocate in dispute resolution services. Chapter 192 (Records; Reports and Meetings), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

192.468 Discretion of Public Records Advocate in dispute resolution services. Consistent with ORS 192.464 and rules adopted thereunder, the Public Records Advocate possesses sole discretion over the conduct of facilitated dispute resolution sessions. [2017 c.728 §3] 192.470 [1973 c.794 §10; renumbered 192.422 in 2017]
