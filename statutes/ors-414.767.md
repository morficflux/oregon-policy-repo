# Survey of medical assistance recipients regarding experience with behavioral health care and services (ORS 414.767)

## At a glance

ORS 414.767 — Survey of medical assistance recipients regarding experience with behavioral health care and services. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.767 Survey of medical assistance recipients regarding experience with behavioral health care and services. The Oregon Health Authority shall contract with a third-party vendor to survey medical assistance recipients about their experiences with behavioral health care and services using a standardized survey tool. [2021 c.667 §9] Note: 414.767 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 414 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 414.768 [2017 c.281 §3; renumbered 414.669 in 2019]
