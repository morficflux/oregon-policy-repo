# Nonparticipation in impaired health professional program (ORS 678.112)

## At a glance

ORS 678.112 — Nonparticipation in impaired health professional program. Chapter 678 (Chapter 678), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

678.112 Nonparticipation in impaired health professional program. Persons licensed or certified by the Oregon State Board of Nursing who elect not to participate in the impaired health professional program established under ORS 676.190 or who fail to comply with the terms of participation shall be reported by the program to the board for formal disciplinary action under ORS 678.111. [1991 c.193 §2; 2007 c.335 §1; 2009 c.697 §7; 2009 c.756 §§32,94; 2025 c.499 §8]
