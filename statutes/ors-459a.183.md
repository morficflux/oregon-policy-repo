# Mattress Stewardship Fund (ORS 459A.183)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.183 — Mattress Stewardship Fund. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.183 Mattress Stewardship Fund. The Mattress Stewardship Fund is established, separate and distinct from the General Fund. All moneys in the Mattress Stewardship Fund are continuously appropriated to the Department of Environmental Quality and may be used only to pay the costs of administering, implementing and enforcing ORS 459A.150 to 459A.189. [2022 c.102 §12] Note: See note under 459A.150.
