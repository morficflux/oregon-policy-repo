# Expenditures prohibited unless budget procedure complied with (ORS 576.420)

## At a glance

ORS 576.420 — Expenditures prohibited unless budget procedure complied with. Chapter 576 (Agricultural Marketing Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

576.420 Expenditures prohibited unless budget procedure complied with. An expenditure of moneys for a fiscal year may not be made or incurred by or under the authority of a commodity commission under ORS 576.051 to 576.455 unless the commission complies with ORS 576.416.
