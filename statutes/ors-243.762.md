# Alternative arbitration procedure under collective bargaining agreement (Miscellaneous) (ORS 243.762)

## At a glance

ORS 243.762 — Alternative arbitration procedure under collective bargaining agreement (Miscellaneous). Chapter 243 (Chapter 243), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

243.762 Alternative arbitration procedure under collective bargaining agreement. Nothing in ORS 240.060, 240.065, 240.080, 240.123, 243.650 to 243.809 and 341.290 is intended to prohibit a public employer and the exclusive representative of its employees from entering into a collective bargaining agreement which provides for a compulsory arbitration procedure which is substantially equivalent to ORS 243.742 to 243.756. [1973 c.536 §22; 2019 c.429 §16] (Miscellaneous)
