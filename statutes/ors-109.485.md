# Registry information to be maintained permanently (ORS 109.485)

## At a glance

ORS 109.485 — Registry information to be maintained permanently. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.485 Registry information to be maintained permanently. Any affidavits filed and other information collected by a registry shall be permanently maintained. [1983 c.672 §13] Note: See note under 109.425.
