# Department disposal of sited property BRANCH INSTITUTIONS (ORS 421.670)

## At a glance

ORS 421.670 — Department disposal of sited property BRANCH INSTITUTIONS. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.670 Department disposal of sited property. Notwithstanding ORS 421.611 to 421.630 or 421.635 to 421.657 or any action taken under ORS 421.611 to 421.630 or 421.635 to 421.657, the Department of Corrections may sell, lease, transfer or otherwise dispose of all or part of real property or any improvements to real property that have been sited for the construction or operation of a correctional facility. [2021 c.642 §4] Note: 421.670 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 421 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 421.705 [Formerly 421.080; 1965 c.616 §75; 1983 c.505 §8; repealed by 1987 c.320 §246] 421.710 [Formerly 421.083; 1983 c.505 §9; repealed by 1987 c.320 §246] BRANCH INSTITUTIONS
