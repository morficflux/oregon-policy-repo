# Use of proceeds of bond sale (ORS 450.115)

## At a glance

ORS 450.115 — Use of proceeds of bond sale. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.115 Use of proceeds of bond sale. The proceeds of the sale of bonds shall be paid to the district. The proceeds shall be used for the purpose indicated in the order calling for election upon the question of the issuance of the bonds, and for no other purpose. However, if those purposes are entirely fulfilled, any remaining proceeds shall be used for payment of the principal and interest of the bonds. [Amended by 2001 c.373 §4]
