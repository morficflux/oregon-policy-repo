# Amount of bond when twice license tax is less than $1,000 (ORS 319.053)

## At a glance

ORS 319.053 — Amount of bond when twice license tax is less than $1,000. Chapter 319 (Chapter 319), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

319.053 Amount of bond when twice license tax is less than $1,000. If the amount that is equivalent to twice the amount of a dealer’s estimated monthly license tax, as determined by the Department of Transportation, is an amount that is less than $1,000, the bond amount required by ORS 319.050, 319.051 or 319.052 shall be $1,000. [2003 c.52 §5]
