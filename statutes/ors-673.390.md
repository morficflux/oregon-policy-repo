# Solicitation permitted (Civil Penalties) (ORS 673.390)

## At a glance

ORS 673.390 — Solicitation permitted (Civil Penalties). Chapter 673 (Chapter 673), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

673.390 Solicitation permitted. The Oregon Board of Accountancy may not adopt a rule prohibiting an Oregon licensee from soliciting an engagement to perform professional services. [1981 c.89 §16; 1993 c.431 §11; 1999 c.322 §24; 2025 c.132 §34] (Civil Penalties)
