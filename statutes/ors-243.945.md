# Employees not eligible for assistance PUBLIC SAFETY MEMORIAL FUND (ORS 243.945)

## At a glance

ORS 243.945 — Employees not eligible for assistance PUBLIC SAFETY MEMORIAL FUND. Chapter 243 (Chapter 243), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

243.945 Employees not eligible for assistance. Notwithstanding ORS 243.910 to 243.945, any person who is hired on or after September 9, 1995, is not eligible to be assisted by the board under the provisions of ORS 243.910 to 243.945. [1995 c.600 §4; 2015 c.767 §70] PUBLIC SAFETY MEMORIAL FUND
