# Production exempt from compliance GRADES, STANDARDS, (ORS 632.849)

## At a glance

ORS 632.849 — Production exempt from compliance GRADES, STANDARDS,. Chapter 632 (Chapter 632), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

632.849 Production exempt from compliance. ORS 632.835 to 632.849 do not apply to:

(1) A commercial farm owner or operator with annual egg production from a flock of not more than 3,000 egg-laying hens; or

(2) Eggs produced by a commercial farm owner or operator described in subsection

(1) of this section. [2019 c.686 §5] 632.850 [2011 c.436 §3; repealed by 2019 c.686 §12] GRADES, STANDARDS, INSPECTION AND CLASSIFICATION OF HORTICULTURAL AND AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS
