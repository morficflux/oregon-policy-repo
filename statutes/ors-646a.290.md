## At a glance

## Full text

646A.290 Construction; other remedies.

(1) The provisions of ORS 646A.280 to 646A.290 shall be liberally construed to effectuate its remedial purposes.

(2) The remedy provided by ORS 646A.280 to 646A.290 is in addition to any other remedy, civil or criminal, that may be available under any other provision of law. Claims based on remedies available under other provisions of law may be joined in an action under ORS 646A.280 to 646A.290 or may be asserted in a separate action. [Formerly 646.302] (Automatic Renewal and Continuous Service Offers)
