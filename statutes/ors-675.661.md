# Employer to pay supervision costs for certain behavioral health care providers (ORS 675.661)

## At a glance

ORS 675.661 — Employer to pay supervision costs for certain behavioral health care providers. Chapter 675 (Chapter 675), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

675.661 Employer to pay supervision costs for certain behavioral health care providers. A public or private entity that employs mental health care providers who supervise associates, interns or other individuals who must have supervised clinical experience as a condition of licensure as a mental health care provider shall pay all costs incurred by the supervisor in providing supervision and the costs of the individual receiving supervision if the supervisor is:

(1) A licensed psychologist, as defined in ORS 675.010;

(2) A clinical social worker licensed under ORS 675.530;

(3) A master’s social worker licensed under ORS 675.533;

(4) A licensed marriage and family therapist, as defined in ORS 675.705; or

(5) A licensed professional counselor, as defined in ORS 675.705. [2021 c.677 §6] Note: See note under 675.650.
