# Port may collect certain fees from port users (ORS 777.220)

## At a glance

ORS 777.220 — Port may collect certain fees from port users. Chapter 777 (Chapter 777), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

777.220 Port may collect certain fees from port users. A port may collect from vessels using any port facility, wharfage, dockage and drydockage; and collect from owners or consignees of goods passing over the docks and warehouses, wharfage and storage charges for goods so handled. [1971 c.728 §24 (enacted in lieu of 777.130)] 777.225 [1963 c.209 §5; repealed by 1971 c.727 §203]
