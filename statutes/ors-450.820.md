# Authority may maintain garbage collection system and engage in insect control activities (ORS 450.820)

## At a glance

ORS 450.820 — Authority may maintain garbage collection system and engage in insect control activities. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.820 Authority may maintain garbage collection system and engage in insect control activities. In addition to other powers granted to it under ORS 450.600 to 450.989, the authority may:

(1) Maintain and operate disposal sites and garbage collection systems in compliance with the requirements of ORS 459.005 to 459.437, 459.992

(1) and

(2) and 466.995 (1).

(2) Engage in mosquito and other insect control activities. [1955 c.614 §27; 1967 c.428 §15; 1969 c.593 §40; 1971 c.648 §27]
