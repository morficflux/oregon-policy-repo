# Immunity from legal action in carrying out duties CAPTIVE INSURERS (ORS 735.145)

## At a glance

ORS 735.145 — Immunity from legal action in carrying out duties CAPTIVE INSURERS. Chapter 735 (Chapter 735), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

735.145 Immunity from legal action in carrying out duties. No person shall have a cause of action against the Oregon FAIR Plan Association or its employees or servicing facilities, any member of the board, or the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services or the employees of the director for any action taken by them in carrying out ORS 735.005 to 735.145. [1971 c.321 §16] CAPTIVE INSURERS
