# Reports by council to Governor and legislature (ORS 293.771)

## At a glance

ORS 293.771 — Reports by council to Governor and legislature. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.771 Reports by council to Governor and legislature. The Oregon Investment Council shall report to the Governor and Legislative Assembly on the investment funds investment program at each odd-numbered year regular session of the Legislative Assembly and at other times as the council considers in the public interest. [1967 c.335 §16; 2011 c.545 §38] 293.775 [1963 c.520 §3; repealed by 1967 c.335 §60]
