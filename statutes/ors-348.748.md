# Short title (ORS 348.748)

## At a glance

ORS 348.748 — Short title. Chapter 348 (Chapter 348), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

348.748 Short title. ORS 348.748 to 348.757 shall be known and may be cited as Oregon’s Open Educational Resources (OER) Program. [2017 c.708 §2] Note: 348.748 to 348.757 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 348 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
