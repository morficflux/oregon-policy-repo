# Effect of failure of petition circulator to certify signature sheet (ORS 250.042)

## At a glance

ORS 250.042 — Effect of failure of petition circulator to certify signature sheet. Chapter 250 (Chapter 250), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

250.042 Effect of failure of petition circulator to certify signature sheet. If a signature sheet of a petition is not certified by the circulator as required under ORS 198.430, 198.750, 221.031, 248.008, 249.061, 249.740, 249.865, 250.045, 250.165, 250.265, 255.135, 261.115 and 545.025, signatures contained on the signature sheet may not be counted for purposes of determining whether the petition contains the required number of signatures of electors. [2007 c.848 §8b]
