# Authority to order arrest for crime committed in presence of magistrate (ORS 133.340)

## At a glance

## Full text

133.340 Authority to order arrest for crime committed in presence of magistrate. When a crime is committed in the presence of a magistrate, the magistrate may, by a verbal or written order, command any person to arrest the offender and may thereupon proceed as if the offender had been brought before the magistrate upon a warrant of arrest. [Amended by 1973 c.836 §73; 1983 c.661 §8] 133.350 [Repealed by 1973 c.836 §358]
