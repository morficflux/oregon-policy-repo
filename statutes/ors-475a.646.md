# Authority of Oregon Health Authority to inspect (ORS 475A.646)

## At a glance

ORS 475A.646 — Authority of Oregon Health Authority to inspect. Chapter 475A (Chapter 475A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475A.646 Authority of Oregon Health Authority to inspect. To ensure compliance with ORS 475A.626 to 475A.654 and any rule adopted under ORS 475A.626 to 475A.654, the Oregon Health Authority may inspect the premises of a person that holds a license under ORS 475A.290 or 475A.305. [2021 c.1 §110]
