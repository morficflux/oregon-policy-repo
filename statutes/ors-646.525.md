# Cooperative bargaining associations authorized (ORS 646.525)

## At a glance

ORS 646.525 — Cooperative bargaining associations authorized. Chapter 646 (Chapter 646), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646.525 Cooperative bargaining associations authorized. Producers shall have the right to join voluntarily and belong to cooperative bargaining associations. [1963 c.514 §2] 646.530 [Repealed by 1953 c.391 §2]
