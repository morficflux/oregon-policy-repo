## At a glance

## Full text

578.080 Meetings of commission.

(1) The Oregon Wheat Commission shall meet at least four times each year regularly and at such other times as called by the chairperson. The chairperson may call special meetings of the commission at any time.

(2) The commission may participate in meetings outside this state for the purpose of advancing the work of the commission. [Amended by 2003 c.604 §95; 2009 c.193 §11]
