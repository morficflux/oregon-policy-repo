# Rules (Public Transportation Development Program) (ORS 184.725)

## At a glance

ORS 184.725 — Rules (Public Transportation Development Program). Chapter 184 (Certain Executive Branch Departments (incl. DAS)), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

184.725 Rules. The Department of Transportation shall adopt such rules as are necessary to carry out the provisions of ORS 184.670, 184.675 and 184.685 to 184.733. [1977 c.230 §12] 184.727 [1981 c.224 §3; repealed by 1989 c.867 §1] (Public Transportation Development Program)
