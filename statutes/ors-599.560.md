## Full text

599.560 Affidavits as to time of milking. Any person selling or offering for sale any cows in milk at public auction, intended to be used for dairy purposes, shall file with the auctioneer of the sale the affidavits of two freeholders to the effect that they have knowledge that such cows have been milked entirely dry within the time specified by ORS 599.550. TEMPORARY HORSE SALE LICENSES
