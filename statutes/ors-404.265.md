# Imposition of civil penalties by county REIMBURSEMENT BY BENEFITED PERSONS (ORS 404.265)

## At a glance

ORS 404.265 — Imposition of civil penalties by county REIMBURSEMENT BY BENEFITED PERSONS. Chapter 404 (Chapter 404), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

404.265 Imposition of civil penalties by county. A county may adopt an ordinance or resolution that imposes civil penalties for failure to comply with the provisions of ORS 404.255 to 404.265. [2025 c.200 §5] REIMBURSEMENT BY BENEFITED PERSONS
