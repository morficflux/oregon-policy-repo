# “Search and rescue” not to include urban search and rescue (ORS 404.095)

## At a glance

ORS 404.095 — “Search and rescue” not to include urban search and rescue. Chapter 404 (Chapter 404), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

404.095 “Search and rescue” not to include urban search and rescue. As used in ORS 404.095 to 404.270, “search and rescue” does not include urban search and rescue, as defined in ORS 476.615. [2023 c.305 §4]
