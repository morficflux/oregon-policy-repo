# Periodic financial reports required (ORS 297.415)

## At a glance

ORS 297.415 — Periodic financial reports required. Chapter 297 (Chapter 297), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

297.415 Periodic financial reports required. The Secretary of State shall require that periodic reports of financial condition and financial operations be prepared and submitted to the Secretary of State by municipal corporations in such form and at such times as the Secretary of State considers necessary. The periodic reports may be required of all municipal corporations. [1977 c.774 §3] 297.420 [Repealed by 1977 c.774 §27]
