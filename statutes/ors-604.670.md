# Rules by department (ORS 604.670)

## At a glance

ORS 604.670 — Rules by department. Chapter 604 (Chapter 604), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

604.670 Rules by department. The State Department of Agriculture, under the provisions of ORS chapter 183 may promulgate such rules as are necessary to administer and enforce the provisions of ORS 604.610 to 604.670, including but not limited to:

(1) The time and manner of maintaining inventory records; and

(2) Controls and minimum standards of construction and methods of operation by licensees. [1975 c.162 §4] 604.990 [Subsection

(6) of 1963 Replacement Part enacted as 1961 c.267 §10; subsection

(7) of 1963 Replacement Part enacted as 1961 c.462 §7 and amended by 1963 c.87 §4; repealed by 1967 c.129 §19]
