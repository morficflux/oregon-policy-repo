# Construction of Act (ORS 133.853)

## At a glance

ORS 133.853 — Construction of Act. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.853 Construction of Act. ORS 133.743 to 133.833 and 133.839 to 133.855 shall be so interpreted and construed as to effectuate their general purpose to make uniform the law of those states which enact the Uniform Criminal Extradition Act. [Formerly 147.270]
