# Cellular counterfeiting in the third degree (ORS 165.577)

## At a glance

ORS 165.577 — Cellular counterfeiting in the third degree. Chapter 165 (Chapter 165), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

165.577 Cellular counterfeiting in the third degree.

(1) A person commits the crime of cellular counterfeiting in the third degree if the person knowingly possesses a cloned cellular telephone and knows that the telephone is unlawfully cloned.

(2) Cellular counterfeiting in the third degree is a Class A misdemeanor. [1995 c.524 §2]
