# Permits for salmon hatcheries; fees (ORS 508.700)

## At a glance

ORS 508.700 — Permits for salmon hatcheries; fees. Chapter 508 (Chapter 508), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

508.700 Permits for salmon hatcheries; fees.

(1) The State Fish and Wildlife Commission may issue a permit, subject to such restrictions and regulations as the commission deems desirable, to any person to construct and operate a hatchery for: (a) Chinook salmon, also known as Oncorhynchus tshawytscha; or (b) Chum salmon, also known as Oncorhynchus keta or dog salmon; or (c) Silver salmon, also known as Oncorhynchus kisutch or coho salmon; or (d) Pink salmon, also known as Oncorhynchus gorbuscha or humpback salmon.

(2) The application for a permit to construct and operate a chum salmon, chinook salmon, silver salmon, or pink salmon hatchery shall include an application fee of $3,000. [1971 c.203 §1; 1973 c.356 §1; 1979 c.556 §1; 2009 c.832 §26]
