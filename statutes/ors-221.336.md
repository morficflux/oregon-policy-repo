# Establishment of municipal court (ORS 221.336)

## At a glance

ORS 221.336 — Establishment of municipal court. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.336 Establishment of municipal court. Any city of this state may establish a municipal court by charter or by ordinance. [1999 c.788 §46] 221.337 [1995 c.532 §1; 1997 c.801 §150; 1999 c.1051 §266; renumbered 221.357 in 1999]
