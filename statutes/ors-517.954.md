# Application of ORS (ORS 517.954)

## At a glance

ORS 517.954 — Application of ORS. Chapter 517 (Chapter 517), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

517.954 Application of ORS 517.952 to 517.989. ORS 517.952 to 517.989 apply only to mining operations as defined in ORS 517.952. ORS 517.952 to 517.989 do not apply to placer mining. [1991 c.735 §3a; 2013 c.371 §8] 517.955 [1981 c.622 §16; renumbered 517.951 in 1991]
