# Rules; contracts (ORS 357.209)

## At a glance

ORS 357.209 — Rules; contracts. Chapter 357 (Chapter 357), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

357.209 Rules; contracts. The State Library Board shall administer the provisions of ORS 357.203 to 357.212 and shall adopt rules governing the application for and granting of funds. Funds granted for reference services by one library for other libraries may be arranged by contract. [1993 c.685 §2; 2003 c.582 §2; 2015 c.328 §24] 357.210 [Amended by 1953 c.136 §4; 1963 c.49 §3; repealed by 1975 c.476 §34]
