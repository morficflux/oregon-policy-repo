# Duties of Oregon Health Authority (ORS 336.227)

## At a glance

ORS 336.227 — Duties of Oregon Health Authority. Chapter 336 (Chapter 336), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

336.227 Duties of Oregon Health Authority. To assist school districts to formulate the programs described in ORS 336.222 (1), the Oregon Health Authority shall:

(1) Devise a public information program directed toward students, parents, teachers, administrators and school board members at the school district level; and

(2) Contact advocacy associations of the target groups described in subsection

(1) of this section to facilitate outreach programs and disseminate alcohol and drug abuse prevention information. [1989 c.1076 §2; 2009 c.595 §209] 336.230 [Amended by 1965 c.100 §225; renumbered 336.057]
