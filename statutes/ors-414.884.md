# Applicability of managed care organization assessment PENALTIES (ORS 414.884)

## At a glance

ORS 414.884 — Applicability of managed care organization assessment PENALTIES. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.884 Applicability of managed care organization assessment. ORS 414.880, 414.882 and 414.902 apply to any payments made to a managed care organization by the Oregon Health Authority for the period beginning January 1, 2020, and ending December 31, 2032. [2017 c.538 §12; 2019 c.2 §10; 2025 c.4 §9] Note: 414.884 is repealed January 2, 2038. See section 10, chapter 4, Oregon Laws 2025. Note: Section 11, chapter 4, Oregon Laws 2025, provides: Sec. 11. Nothing in the repeal of ORS 414.880, 414.882, 414.884 and 414.902 by section 10 of this 2025 Act affects the imposition and collection of a managed care organization assessment under ORS 414.880 for a calendar quarter ending on or before December 31, 2032. [2025 c.4 §11] Note: See second note under 414.880. PENALTIES
