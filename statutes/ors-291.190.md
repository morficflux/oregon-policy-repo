# Short title (ORS 291.190)

## At a glance

ORS 291.190 — Short title. Chapter 291 (State Financial Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

291.190 Short title. ORS 291.195, 291.201 and 291.203 may be cited as the Budget Accountability Act. [1995 c.746 §61] Note: 291.190 and 291.195 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 291 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
