## Full text

807.168 Commercial driving privileges; Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration; conformance; rules.

(1) The Department of Transportation may adopt rules necessary to administer regulations promulgated by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration related to regulating commercial driving privileges.

(2) If a person is not in compliance with the rules adopted under this section, the department may deny the issuance or renewal of commercial driving privileges and may cancel, suspend or revoke commercial driving privileges.

(3) A person impacted by a rule adopted under this section is entitled to an administrative review or administrative hearing pursuant to ORS 809.140 and 809.440. [2025 c.415 §12] (Endorsements)
