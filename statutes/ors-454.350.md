# Effect of ORS (ORS 454.350)

## At a glance

ORS 454.350 — Effect of ORS. Chapter 454 (Chapter 454), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

454.350 Effect of ORS 454.317 to 454.350 on contracts between municipalities. Nothing in ORS 454.317 to 454.350 prohibits contracts between municipalities under which a municipality may provide treatment facilities or services to another municipality. [1983 c.235 §5] 454.355 [1973 c.424 §6; repealed by 1975 c.167 §13]
