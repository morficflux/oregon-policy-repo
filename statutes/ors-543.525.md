# ORS 543.530 to (ORS 543.525)

## At a glance

ORS 543.525 — ORS 543.530 to. Chapter 543 (Chapter 543), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

543.525 ORS 543.530 to 543.550 not applicable to regulated utilities. The provisions of ORS 543.530 to 543.550 shall not apply to any licensee which is a utility as defined in ORS 757.005 and regulated by the Public Utility Commission of Oregon. [1965 c.333 §1] Note: 543.525 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 543 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
