# Delivery of warrant when place of trial changed (ORS 137.467)

## At a glance

ORS 137.467 — Delivery of warrant when place of trial changed. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.467 Delivery of warrant when place of trial changed. If the place of trial has been changed, the death warrant shall be delivered to the sheriff of the county in which the defendant was tried. [1984 c.3 §6] 137.470 [1979 c.2 §6; repealed by 1981 c.873 §9]
