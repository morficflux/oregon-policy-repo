# Remedy not exclusive (Immigration) (ORS 180.785)

## At a glance

ORS 180.785 — Remedy not exclusive (Immigration). Chapter 180 (Attorney General; Department of Justice), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

180.785 Remedy not exclusive. The remedies provided under ORS 180.760 are in addition to any other remedy, civil or criminal, that may be available under any other provision of law. Claims based on remedies available under other provisions of law may be joined in an action under ORS 180.760. [2009 c.292 §8] Note: See note under 180.750. (Immigration)
