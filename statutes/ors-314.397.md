# Manner of payment (ORS 314.397)

## At a glance

ORS 314.397 — Manner of payment. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.397 Manner of payment. The tax may be paid with uncertified check under any rules as the Department of Revenue shall adopt, but if a check so received is not paid by the financial institution on which it is drawn, the taxpayer by whom the check is tendered remains liable for the payment of the tax and for all legal penalties the same as if the check had not been tendered. [1989 c.625 §61 (enacted in lieu of 316.407); 1997 c.631 §451]
