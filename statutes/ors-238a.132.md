# Shared work benefits to be treated as salary (ORS 238A.132)

## At a glance

ORS 238A.132 — Shared work benefits to be treated as salary. Chapter 238A (Chapter 238A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

238A.132 Shared work benefits to be treated as salary. Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter or ORS chapter 238, benefits received by a member under ORS 657.370 to 657.390 shall be treated as salary. [2023 c.404 §24] Note: 238A.132 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 238A by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
