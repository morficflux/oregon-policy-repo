# Disposition of moneys received BIOPHARMACEUTICAL CROPS (ORS 561.730)

## At a glance

ORS 561.730 — Disposition of moneys received BIOPHARMACEUTICAL CROPS. Chapter 561 (State Department of Agriculture), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

561.730 Disposition of moneys received. All moneys received by the New Crops Development Board shall be deposited in the Department of Agriculture Service Fund created by ORS 561.144 (1). All moneys received and so deposited are continuously appropriated to the board to carry out the provisions of ORS 561.700 to 561.720. [1983 c.501 §5] BIOPHARMACEUTICAL CROPS
