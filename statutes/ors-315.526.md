# Short title (ORS 315.526)

## At a glance

ORS 315.526 — Short title. Chapter 315 (Chapter 315), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

315.526 Short title. ORS 285C.650, 285C.653, 285C.656 and 315.529 to 315.536 shall be known and may be cited as the Oregon Low Income Community Jobs Initiative. [2011 c.732 §1] Note: 315.526, 315.529 and 315.536 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 315 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
