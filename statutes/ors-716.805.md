# Determining earnings (ORS 716.805)

## At a glance

ORS 716.805 — Determining earnings. Chapter 716 (Chapter 716), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

716.805 Determining earnings. Earnings of a savings bank shall be calculated on an accrual basis according to generally accepted accounting principles. [1973 c.797 §389] 716.810 [Repealed by 1973 c.797 §428] 716.820 [Repealed by 1973 c.797 §428]
