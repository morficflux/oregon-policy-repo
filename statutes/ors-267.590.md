# Interagency agreements (ORS 267.590)

## At a glance

ORS 267.590 — Interagency agreements. Chapter 267 (Chapter 267), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

267.590 Interagency agreements. A district may contract with any public or private agency for the agency to operate any facility or perform any function that the district is authorized to operate or perform. By contract the district may assume any function of any public corporation, city or county in the district that the district has power to assume under ORS 267.510 to 267.650.
