# Criminal penalties DENTAL HYGIENISTS (Generally) (ORS 680.990)

## At a glance

ORS 680.990 — Criminal penalties DENTAL HYGIENISTS (Generally). Chapter 680 (Chapter 680), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

680.990 Criminal penalties.

(1) Violation of any of the provisions of ORS 680.010 to 680.205 is a Class C misdemeanor.

(2) Violation of any provision of ORS 680.505 and 743A.028 is a Class B misdemeanor. [Amended by 1977 c.192 §11; subsection

(2) enacted as 1979 c.1 §17; 1983 c.169 §32; 2003 c.547 §19] _______________
