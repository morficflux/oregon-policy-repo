# Conclusive presumption of plan validity (ORS 457.135)

## At a glance

ORS 457.135 — Conclusive presumption of plan validity. Chapter 457 (Chapter 457), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

457.135 Conclusive presumption of plan validity. After October 3, 1979, any urban renewal plan purported to be adopted in conformance with applicable legal requirements shall be conclusively presumed valid for all purposes 90 days after adoption of the plan by ordinance of the governing body of the municipality. No direct or collateral attack on the action may thereafter be commenced.
