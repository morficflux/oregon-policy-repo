# Median sales price (ORS 307.661)

## At a glance

ORS 307.661 — Median sales price. Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.661 Median sales price. Prior to January 1 of each assessment year, the governing body of a city that adopts ORS 307.651 to 307.687 shall adopt by resolution the median sales price to be used for purposes of determining if dwelling units are qualified under ORS 307.651 to 307.687. In determining the median sales price, the governing body, assisted by the county assessor, shall use the sales data collected under ORS 309.200 in the county in which the greater portion of the taxable assessed value of single-unit housing in the city is located, as of the period ending the prior November 30. [2005 c.470 §5]
