# Agency authority to regulate air contaminants (ORS 184.891)

## At a glance

ORS 184.891 — Agency authority to regulate air contaminants. Chapter 184 (Certain Executive Branch Departments (incl. DAS)), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

184.891 Agency authority to regulate air contaminants. ORS 184.617 does not limit the authority of an agency, as defined in ORS 183.310, to regulate air contaminants. [2010 c.85 §10; 2017 c.750 §28b]
