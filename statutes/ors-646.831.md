# Fees and mileage for persons testifying (ORS 646.831)

## At a glance

ORS 646.831 — Fees and mileage for persons testifying. Chapter 646 (Chapter 646), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646.831 Fees and mileage for persons testifying. Any person appearing for oral examination pursuant to a demand served under ORS 646.750 shall be entitled to the fees and mileage provided for witnesses in ORS 44.415 (2). [1977 c.729 §5; 1989 c.980 §16]
