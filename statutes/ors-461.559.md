# Reversion of lottery allocations (ORS 461.559)

## At a glance

ORS 461.559 — Reversion of lottery allocations. Chapter 461 (Chapter 461), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

461.559 Reversion of lottery allocations.

(1) On December 31 in each odd-numbered year, all amounts remaining unspent by a state agency from statutory allocations made from the Administrative Services Economic Development Fund to that agency for the prior biennium shall revert to the Administrative Services Economic Development Fund.

(2) Subsection

(1) of this section does not apply to allocations for debt service, capital construction, continuing contracts, contested claims or special allocations designated by legislative action. [2013 c.722 §86; 2015 c.840 §29] Note: 461.559 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 461 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
