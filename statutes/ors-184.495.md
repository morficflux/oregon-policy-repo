# Report (ORS 184.495)

## At a glance

ORS 184.495 — Report. Chapter 184 (Certain Executive Branch Departments (incl. DAS)), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

184.495 Report. Each CASA Volunteer Program may report biannually to committees of the Legislative Assembly related to the provision of court appointed special advocate services. The report may include information regarding the program’s efforts to improve culturally specific and equitable services, partnerships with community-based organizations, volunteer recruitment efforts, percentage of need for CASA Volunteer Program services met by the program and demographic information relative to volunteers and children in foster care. The statewide coordinating entity contracted with by the Oregon Department of Administrative Services under ORS 184.492 may present the biannual reports required under this section on behalf of the CASA Volunteer Programs in this state. [2017 c.630 §4; 2021 c.459 §3]
