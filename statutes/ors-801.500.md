# “Special use trailer” (ORS 801.500)

## At a glance

ORS 801.500 — “Special use trailer”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.500 “Special use trailer.”

(1) “Special use trailer” means a trailer described under any of the following: (a) A trailer that is eight and one-half feet or less in width and of any length and that is used for commercial or business purposes. (b) A trailer that is used temporarily on a construction site for office purposes only. (c) A mobile modular unit.

(2) “Special use trailer” does not include any travel trailer. [1985 c.16 §26; 1993 c.696 §7; 2003 c.655 §90a]
