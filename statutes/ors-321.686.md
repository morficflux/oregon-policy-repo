## At a glance

## Full text

321.686 Penalty for violation of ORS 321.682. Violation of ORS 321.682 is subject to a fine not exceeding $5,000 or, if committed by an officer or employee of the state, dismissal or removal from office or employment, or both fine and dismissal or removal from office or employment. [1989 c.1083 §20; 2005 c.94 §106] SMALL TRACT FORESTLAND PROGRAM
