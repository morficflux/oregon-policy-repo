# Cooperation with other agencies to provide vocational training (ORS 412.044)

## At a glance

ORS 412.044 — Cooperation with other agencies to provide vocational training. Chapter 412 (Chapter 412), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

412.044 Cooperation with other agencies to provide vocational training. The Department of Human Services and any other state agency, which administers, supervises or participates in the administration of programs of public vocational or adult education services available in this state, may enter into and execute cooperative arrangements looking toward maximum utilization of such services to encourage the training or retraining of individuals participating in a community work and training program as defined in ORS 411.855, and otherwise to assist such individuals in preparing for regular employment. [Formerly 418.097] 412.045 [1961 c.633 §4; 1965 c.556 §19; repealed by 2005 c.381 §30]
