## At a glance

## Full text

329.501 Grants for physical education; rules.

(1) The Department of Education shall award grants to school districts and public charter schools for the purpose of meeting the physical education requirements of ORS 329.496.

(2) A district or school that receives a grant may use the grant to: (a) Hire teachers who specialize in physical education; and (b) Provide in-service training to teachers on the academic content standards for physical education and the requirements of ORS 329.496.

(3) The department shall determine: (a) The amount of a grant; (b) The criteria for awarding a grant; and (c) The process for awarding grants.

(4) The State Board of Education may adopt any rules necessary to administer this section. [2007 c.839 §10] Note: See note under 329.498.
