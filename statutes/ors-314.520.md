# State agency authority over certain electronic funds transfer payments (ORS 314.520)

## At a glance

ORS 314.520 — State agency authority over certain electronic funds transfer payments. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.520 State agency authority over certain electronic funds transfer payments. ORS 314.505, 314.518 and 316.198 do not alter the authority under ORS 293.525 of a state agency to require by rule that certain payments to the agency be made by electronic funds transfer. [1997 c.299 §6; 2001 c.114 §39; 2005 c.28 §2] Note: 314.520 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 314 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
