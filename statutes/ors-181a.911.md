# Interagency agreement (ORS 181A.911)

## At a glance

ORS 181A.911 — Interagency agreement. Chapter 181A (State Police; Public Safety Standards and Training), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

181A.911 Interagency agreement. The Department of Public Safety Standards and Training may enter into an interagency agreement with the Bureau of Labor and Industries to assist with the licensure, technical assistance and compliance services related to ORS 181A.900 to 181A.918. [2021 c.618 §10] 181A.912 [2019 c.152 §2; 2019 c.152 §3; renumbered 181A.972 in 2021]
