# Application of ORS (ORS 759.325)

## At a glance

ORS 759.325 — Application of ORS. Chapter 759 (Chapter 759), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

759.325 Application of ORS 759.375. ORS 759.375 does not apply to any mortgage or other encumbrance upon any real or personal property given to secure payment of any evidence of indebtedness issued under ORS 759.315. [1987 c.447 §33]
