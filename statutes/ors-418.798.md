# Legislative findings (ORS 418.798)

## At a glance

ORS 418.798 — Legislative findings. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.798 Legislative findings. The Legislative Assembly finds that children’s advocacy centers rely upon state funding and it is necessary and in the public interest to provide children’s advocacy centers and employees of children’s advocacy centers with certain immunities from civil liability arising out of children’s advocacy centers’ good faith participation in conducting statutorily mandated child abuse assessments. [2024 c.68 §2] Note: See note under 418.746.
