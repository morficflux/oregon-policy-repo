# Consideration of net gain or net loss upon sale of coal-fired resource for allocation to certain retail electricity consumers (ORS 757.519)

## At a glance

ORS 757.519 — Consideration of net gain or net loss upon sale of coal-fired resource for allocation to certain retail electricity consumers. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.519 Consideration of net gain or net loss upon sale of coal-fired resource for allocation to certain retail electricity consumers. The Public Utility Commission may consider the net gain or net loss upon the sale of any coal-fired resource, as defined in ORS 757.518, for allocation to the retail electricity consumers, as defined in ORS 757.600, of an electric company that makes sales of electricity to 25,000 or more retail electricity consumers in this state. [2016 c.28 §2] Note: See note under 757.518. 757.520 [Repealed by 1971 c.655 §250]
