# Trust fund; continuous appropriation (ORS 293.117)

## At a glance

ORS 293.117 — Trust fund; continuous appropriation. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.117 Trust fund; continuous appropriation.

(1) Moneys in a trust fund that are not otherwise appropriated by law are continuously appropriated to the agency that administers the trust in order to carry out the purposes of the trust.

(2) As used in this section, “trust fund” has the meaning given that term in ORS 291.002. [2003 c.81 §5] Note: 293.117 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 293 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 293.120 [Formerly 291.358; repealed by 2003 c.81 §14]
