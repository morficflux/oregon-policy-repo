# Notification of plant closing or mass layoff under Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act (ORS 285A.516)

## At a glance

ORS 285A.516 — Notification of plant closing or mass layoff under Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act. Chapter 285A (Chapter 285A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285A.516 Notification of plant closing or mass layoff under Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act. The Higher Education Coordinating Commission is the state agency that shall be notified when an employer is required to provide written notice of a plant closing or mass layoff under section 3 of the Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act (P.L. 100-379). [Formerly 285.457; 2001 c.684 §18; 2015 c.366 §69; 2023 c.557 §3]
