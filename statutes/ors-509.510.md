# Taking shellfish from marked beds without permission; disturbing beds (Krill) (ORS 509.510)

## At a glance

ORS 509.510 — Taking shellfish from marked beds without permission; disturbing beds (Krill). Chapter 509 (Chapter 509), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

509.510 Taking shellfish from marked beds without permission; disturbing beds. It is unlawful, without the permission of the legal occupants, to take up shellfish from natural or artificially planted beds, which beds have been lawfully and plainly marked. It is unlawful willfully to disturb the shellfish in such beds, the surfaces of such beds, or the markers. [Formerly 509.475] (Krill)
