# Source of revenue to repay loan (ORS 476.915)

## At a glance

ORS 476.915 — Source of revenue to repay loan. Chapter 476 (State Fire Marshal; Protection From Fire Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

476.915 Source of revenue to repay loan. A city or rural fire protection district that enters into an agreement with the State Fire Marshal for a fire protection equipment acquisition loan may obtain moneys for repayment thereof in the same manner as other moneys are obtained for purposes of the payment of expenses of the city or rural fire protection district. [1991 c.587 §4] Note: See note under 476.900.
