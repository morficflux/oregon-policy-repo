# Violation of registration limits on racing activity vehicles; penalty (ORS 805.037)

## At a glance

## Full text

805.037 Violation of registration limits on racing activity vehicles; penalty.

(1) A person commits the offense of violation of registration limits on a racing activity vehicle if a vehicle is permanently registered under ORS 805.035 and the person uses the vehicle other than for: (a) Exhibitions, parades or club activities; (b) Driving the vehicle from the person’s home to a race track that is within a 90-mile radius of the person’s home; or (c) Test driving the vehicle for maintenance or repair purposes within a 30-mile radius of where the vehicle is maintained or repaired.

(2) The offense described in this section, violation of registration limits on a racing activity vehicle, is a Class B traffic violation. [2007 c.693 §3b]
