# No right of action created by ORS (ORS 182.168)

## At a glance

ORS 182.168 — No right of action created by ORS. Chapter 182 (State Administrative Agencies Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

182.168 No right of action created by ORS 182.162 to 182.168. Nothing in ORS 182.162 to 182.168 creates a right of action against a state agency or a right of review of an action of a state agency.
