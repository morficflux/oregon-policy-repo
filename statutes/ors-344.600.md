# Unauthorized use of official rehabilitation data (ORS 344.600)

## At a glance

ORS 344.600 — Unauthorized use of official rehabilitation data. Chapter 344 (Chapter 344), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

344.600 Unauthorized use of official rehabilitation data. Except for purposes directly connected with the administration of vocational rehabilitation, and in accordance with the rules and regulations of the Department of Human Services, no person shall solicit, disclose, receive, make use of or authorize, knowingly permit, participate in or acquiesce in the use of, any list of or names of, or any information concerning persons applying for or receiving vocational rehabilitation directly or indirectly derived from the records, papers, files or communications of the state or subdivisions or agencies thereof, or acquired in the course of the performance of official duties. [Amended by 1967 c.552 §7] 344.610 [Amended by 1963 c.522 §5; repealed by 1965 c.100 §456]
