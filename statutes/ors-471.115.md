# Limitations on purchasing may be imposed (ORS 471.115)

## At a glance

ORS 471.115 — Limitations on purchasing may be imposed. Chapter 471 (Alcoholic Liquors Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

471.115 Limitations on purchasing may be imposed. The Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission may limit the quantity of alcoholic liquor purchased at any one time by any person. It may limit the amount of purchases within any length of time so as effectually to prevent the resale of such liquors.
