# Procedure for issuing bonds under ORS (ORS 367.715)

## At a glance

ORS 367.715 — Procedure for issuing bonds under ORS. Chapter 367 (Chapter 367), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

367.715 Procedure for issuing bonds under ORS 367.700 to 367.750. All bonds issued under ORS 367.700 to 367.750 must be issued in accordance with ORS chapter 286A.
