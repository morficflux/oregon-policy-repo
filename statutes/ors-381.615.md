# Majority vote required (ORS 381.615)

## At a glance

ORS 381.615 — Majority vote required. Chapter 381 (Chapter 381), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

381.615 Majority vote required. The council may issue and sell bonds for the purpose mentioned in ORS 381.605 only upon the approval of a majority of those voting on the question.
