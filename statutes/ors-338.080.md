## At a glance

## Full text

338.080 Cooperative agreement to provide educational services.

(1) A sponsor and a public charter school may enter into a cooperative agreement with other school districts for the purpose of forming a partnership to provide educational services.

(2) A cooperative agreement entered into as provided by this section must: (a) Be incorporated into the charter of the public charter school; and (b) Describe the terms of the partnership between the sponsor, the public charter school and other school districts. [2011 c.682 §2] OPERATION
