# Disallowance of subtraction for amounts included in calculation of net operating loss (ORS 316.029)

## At a glance

ORS 316.029 — Disallowance of subtraction for amounts included in calculation of net operating loss. Chapter 316 (Chapter 316), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

316.029 Disallowance of subtraction for amounts included in calculation of net operating loss. Notwithstanding ORS 316.739, a subtraction from federal taxable income is not allowed for amounts included in the calculation of an Oregon net operating loss under ORS 316.028. [2011 c.685 §2] 316.030 [1953 c.304 §6; repealed by 1957 c.632 §1 (314.075 and 314.080 enacted in lieu of 316.025, 316.030, 317.015 and 317.020)]
