## Full text

167.370 Participation in dogfighting.

(1) A person commits the crime of participation in dogfighting if the person knowingly: (a) Attends or has paid admission at any place for the purpose of viewing or betting upon a dogfight. (b) Advertises or otherwise offers to sell equipment that the person knows or reasonably should know will be used for the purpose of training and handling a fighting dog.

(2) Participation in dogfighting is a Class C felony. [1987 c.249 §3; 2008 c.42 §1]
