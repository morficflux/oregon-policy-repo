# Meetings (ORS 326.556)

## At a glance

ORS 326.556 — Meetings. Chapter 326 (State Administration of Education), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

326.556 Meetings. The State Council for Educational Opportunity for Military Children shall meet at least once each calendar year through the use of telephone or other electronic means and in accordance with ORS 192.610 to 192.705 and 192.672. [2014 c.110 §4; 2019 c.339 §2]
