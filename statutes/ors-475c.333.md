## At a glance

## Full text

475C.333 Prohibition against giving marijuana item as prize; penalty.

(1) A marijuana item may not be given as a prize, premium or consideration for a lottery, contest, game of chance, game of skill or competition of any kind.

(2) Violation of this section is a Class A misdemeanor. [Formerly 475B.333]
