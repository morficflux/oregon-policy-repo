# Governor may recall warrant (ORS 133.825)

## At a glance

ORS 133.825 — Governor may recall warrant. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.825 Governor may recall warrant. The Governor may recall the Governor’s warrant of arrest or may issue another warrant whenever the Governor deems proper. [Formerly 147.210]
