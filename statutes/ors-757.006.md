# People’s utility districts and electric cooperatives excluded from term “public utility” (ORS 757.006)

## At a glance

ORS 757.006 — People’s utility districts and electric cooperatives excluded from term “public utility”. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.006 People’s utility districts and electric cooperatives excluded from term “public utility.” For purposes of ORS chapter 757, the term “public utility” does not include a people’s utility district organized under ORS chapter 261 or an electric cooperative organized under ORS chapter 62. [2016 c.28 §18c] Note: 757.006 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 757 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
