# Online sexual corruption of a child in the first degree (ORS 163.433)

## At a glance

ORS 163.433 — Online sexual corruption of a child in the first degree. Chapter 163 (Chapter 163), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

163.433 Online sexual corruption of a child in the first degree.

(1) A person commits the crime of online sexual corruption of a child in the first degree if the person violates ORS 163.432 and intentionally takes a substantial step toward physically meeting with or encountering the child.

(2) Online sexual corruption of a child in the first degree is a Class B felony. [2007 c.876 §3] Note: See note under 163.431.
