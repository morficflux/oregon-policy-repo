# Services provided by denturist (ORS 743A.028)

## At a glance

ORS 743A.028 — Services provided by denturist. Chapter 743A (Chapter 743A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743A.028 Services provided by denturist. Notwithstanding any provisions of any policy of insurance covering dental health, whenever such policy provides for reimbursement for any service that is within the lawful scope of practice of a denturist, the insured under such policy shall be entitled to reimbursement for such service, whether the service is performed by a licensed dentist or a licensed denturist as defined in ORS 680.500. [Formerly 743.713] Note: 743A.028 was added to and made a part of the Insurance Code by law but was not added to ORS chapter 743A or any series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
