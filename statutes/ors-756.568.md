# Rescission, suspension and amendment of orders (ORS 756.568)

## At a glance

ORS 756.568 — Rescission, suspension and amendment of orders. Chapter 756 (Public Utility Commission), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

756.568 Rescission, suspension and amendment of orders. The Public Utility Commission may at any time, upon notice to the public utility or telecommunications utility and after opportunity to be heard as provided in ORS 756.500 to 756.610, rescind, suspend or amend any order made by the commission. Copies of the same shall be served and take effect as provided in ORS 756.558 for original orders. [Formerly 757.540; 1973 c.776 §30; 1987 c.447 §92a; 1995 c.733 §70] 756.570 [Amended by 1971 c.655 §51; renumbered 756.561]
