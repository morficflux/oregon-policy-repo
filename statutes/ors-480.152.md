# Publication of advertisement for sale of unlawful fireworks prohibited (ORS 480.152)

## At a glance

ORS 480.152 — Publication of advertisement for sale of unlawful fireworks prohibited. Chapter 480 (Chapter 480), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

480.152 Publication of advertisement for sale of unlawful fireworks prohibited.

(1) No person shall publish or cause to be published: (a) Any advertisement for the sale of fireworks the use or possession of which is declared unlawful by ORS 480.111 to 480.165. (b) Any advertisement for the sale of fireworks in any county, municipality or fire protection district that by law or ordinance has declared the sale or use of the fireworks is prohibited.

(2) Subsection

(1) of this section does not apply to any advertising medium that accepts such advertising in good faith, without knowledge of the violation of law. [1985 c.789 §7; 2013 c.24 §6]
