# Publicly available list of authorized temporary staffing agencies (ORS 676.701)

## At a glance

ORS 676.701 — Publicly available list of authorized temporary staffing agencies. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.701 Publicly available list of authorized temporary staffing agencies. The Health Licensing Office shall publish and maintain on a publicly available website operated by or on behalf of the office a list of temporary staffing agencies authorized under ORS 676.698. [2022 c.92 §3]
