# Designation of Oregon Institute of Technology as Oregon’s Polytechnic University (ORS 352.014)

## At a glance

ORS 352.014 — Designation of Oregon Institute of Technology as Oregon’s Polytechnic University. Chapter 352 (Chapter 352), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

352.014 Designation of Oregon Institute of Technology as Oregon’s Polytechnic University. Oregon Institute of Technology is designated and shall be known as Oregon’s Polytechnic University. [2021 c.224 §1] 352.015 [1991 c.935 §§1,2; 2007 c.70 §147; 2011 c.637 §240; renumbered 352.268 in 2015]
