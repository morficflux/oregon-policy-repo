# Court to which appeal is taken (ORS 221.367)

## At a glance

ORS 221.367 — Court to which appeal is taken. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.367 Court to which appeal is taken.

(1) If a municipal court has become a court of record under ORS 221.342: (a) An appeal in a proceeding involving a violation shall be taken to the Court of Appeals as provided in ORS 138.057. (b) An appeal in a proceeding involving a misdemeanor shall be taken to the Court of Appeals as provided in ORS 138.010 to 138.310 for appeals from a circuit court.

(2) If a municipal court has not become a court of record under ORS 221.342, an appeal shall be taken to the circuit court of the county in which the municipal court is located and in the manner provided in ORS 221.369 to 221.407. [2025 c.268 §38]
