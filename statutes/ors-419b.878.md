# Judicial inquiry and finding regarding whether child is Indian child (ORS 419B.878)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.878 — Judicial inquiry and finding regarding whether child is Indian child. Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.878 Judicial inquiry and finding regarding whether child is Indian child.

(1) When a court conducts a hearing, the court shall inquire and make a finding, subject to the procedures under ORS 419B.636 (4), regarding whether a child is an Indian child.

(2) If the court enters an order described in ORS 419B.636 (4)(b) directing that the child be treated as an Indian child, the court shall enter an order requiring the Department of Human Services to comply with the inquiry and notice provisions of ORS 419B.636 and 419B.639. [2001 c.622 §22; 2020 s.s.1 c.14 §40; 2021 c.398 §76]
