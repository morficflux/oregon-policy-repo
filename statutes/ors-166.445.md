# Short title (ORS 166.445)

## At a glance

ORS 166.445 — Short title. Chapter 166 (Chapter 166), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

166.445 Short title. ORS 166.432 to 166.445 and the amendments to ORS 166.416, 166.418 and 166.460 by sections 9, 10 and 11, chapter 1, Oregon Laws 2001, shall be known as the Gun Violence Prevention Act. [2001 c.1 §2] Note: See note under 166.432.
