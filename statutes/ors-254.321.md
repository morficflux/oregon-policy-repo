# Providing map of proposed boundaries for election on establishing or changing county or city boundaries VOTING (ORS 254.321)

## At a glance

ORS 254.321 — Providing map of proposed boundaries for election on establishing or changing county or city boundaries VOTING. Chapter 254 (Chapter 254), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

254.321 Providing map of proposed boundaries for election on establishing or changing county or city boundaries. At any election in which the question of establishing or changing the exterior boundaries of a county or city is submitted to a vote, the county clerk shall:

(1) Include with every mailed ballot a map indicating the proposed boundaries; or

(2) Print in a voters’ pamphlet prepared for the election a map indicating the proposed boundaries.
