## At a glance

## Full text

463.155 Compliance audits and verification; duties of superintendent; rules.

(1) The Superintendent of State Police or the authorized representatives of the superintendent shall: (a) Conduct compliance audits for unarmed combat sports or entertainment wrestling events; (b) Oversee ticket sales to the events; and (c) Verify compliance with ticketing, tax on gross receipts and other requirements of this chapter.

(2) The authorized representative of the superintendent for an unarmed combat sports or entertainment wrestling event shall verify the accuracy of the promoter’s account and the amount of the taxed gross receipts for the event.

(3) The superintendent shall establish by rule limitations on complimentary tickets for unarmed combat sports and entertainment wrestling events. [1987 c.789 §6; 1987 c.788 §7; 2003 c.142 §5; 2007 c.585 §13; 2017 c.235 §12] 463.160 [Repealed by 1959 c.160 §1]
