# Link on agency websites (ORS 408.504)

## At a glance

ORS 408.504 — Link on agency websites. Chapter 408 (Chapter 408), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

408.504 Link on agency websites. An agency, as defined in ORS 183.310, shall include on the agency’s website a link to the website established by the Department of Veterans’ Affairs under ORS 408.503. [2009 c.851 §2] Note: See note under 408.503.
