# Fish screening program report (ORS 496.141)

## At a glance

ORS 496.141 — Fish screening program report. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.141 Fish screening program report. On or before February 1 of each odd-numbered year, the State Department of Fish and Wildlife shall provide to the Joint Committee on Ways and Means a complete annual report regarding activities initiated by the department in regard to the fish screening program. The report shall include a complete budget analysis of all costs, including in-kind costs associated with the program, the number of screening or by-pass devices installed and the size of the diversions on which such devices were installed. The budget analysis shall identify all costs associated with the construction and installation of screening or by-pass devices, administrative costs and research and development costs associated with the program. [1995 c.426 §14] 496.145 [Repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
