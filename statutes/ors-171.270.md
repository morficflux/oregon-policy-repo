# Legislative materials furnished to public officers without charge are public property OREGON REVISED STATUTES (ORS 171.270)

## At a glance

ORS 171.270 — Legislative materials furnished to public officers without charge are public property OREGON REVISED STATUTES. Chapter 171 (State Legislature), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

171.270 Legislative materials furnished to public officers without charge are public property. Except as otherwise provided by or pursuant to law, the copies of the legislative materials referred to in ORS 171.236 furnished free of charge to the public officers of this state pursuant to law are public property and shall be delivered over by them to their successors in office. [Amended by 1969 c.174 §3; 1981 c.517 §18] 171.272 [1953 c.546 §1; 1961 c.167, §27; renumbered 173.152] OREGON REVISED STATUTES
