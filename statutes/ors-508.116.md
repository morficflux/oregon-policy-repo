# Permit to take animals living intertidally on bottom; fees (ORS 508.116)

## At a glance

ORS 508.116 — Permit to take animals living intertidally on bottom; fees. Chapter 508 (Chapter 508), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

508.116 Permit to take animals living intertidally on bottom; fees. (1)(a) The State Fish and Wildlife Director may issue to any person a permit to take animals living intertidally on the bottom. (b) The annual fee for a resident permit issued under this section is $140. (c) The annual fee for a nonresident permit issued under this section is $196.

(2) It is unlawful to take animals living intertidally on the bottom for commercial purposes by a permit issued under subsection

(1) of this section without first having obtained a license under ORS 508.035.
