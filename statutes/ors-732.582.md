# Determination of reasonableness and adequacy of capital and surplus EXAMINATIONS, CONFIDENTIALITY, (ORS 732.582)

## At a glance

ORS 732.582 — Determination of reasonableness and adequacy of capital and surplus EXAMINATIONS, CONFIDENTIALITY,. Chapter 732 (Chapter 732), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

732.582 Determination of reasonableness and adequacy of capital and surplus. For purposes of ORS 732.517 to 732.596, in order to determine whether the combined capital and surplus is reasonable in relation to the outstanding liabilities of the insurer and adequate to its financial needs, the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services must consider at least the applicable factors stated in ORS 731.554 for determining the reasonableness and adequacy of the insurer’s capital and surplus.
