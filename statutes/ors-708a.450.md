# Certified checks (ORS 708A.450)

## At a glance

ORS 708A.450 — Certified checks. Chapter 708A (Chapter 708A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

708A.450 Certified checks.

(1) An Oregon commercial bank shall certify a check only if the amount of the check actually stands to the credit of the drawer in collected funds on the books of the Oregon commercial bank.

(2) The amount of any certified check shall be immediately charged to the drawer’s account. [1997 c.631 §171]
