# Conditions for disclosure of drug test results (ORS 802.202)

## At a glance

ORS 802.202 — Conditions for disclosure of drug test results. Chapter 802 (Chapter 802), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

802.202 Conditions for disclosure of drug test results. The Department of Transportation shall disclose information about a drug test result that is made to the department under ORS 825.410 or 825.415 only if the person who requests the information provides the department with written permission from the person who is the subject of the report. [1999 c.1099 §9; 2013 c.163 §5]
