# Recycling and recovery of used oil; goal (ORS 459A.552)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.552 — Recycling and recovery of used oil; goal. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.552 Recycling and recovery of used oil; goal. It is the goal of the State of Oregon that the amount of recycling and recovery of used oil from households in Oregon shall be at least 70 percent. [1993 c.527 §2; 2005 c.22 §337] Note: 459A.552 to 459A.599 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 459A or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
