# Compensation and expenses; rules (ORS 681.410)

## At a glance

ORS 681.410 — Compensation and expenses; rules. Chapter 681 (Chapter 681), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

681.410 Compensation and expenses; rules. A member of the State Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology shall receive compensation and expenses as provided in ORS 292.495. The board may provide by rule for compensation to board members for the performance of official duties at a rate that is greater than the rate provided in ORS 292.495. [Formerly 694.455; 1985 c.64 §8; 1995 c.280 §17; 2005 c.698 §17; 2009 c.535 §17; 2009 c.756 §44]
