# Use of processed, source-separated waste tires for energy recovery (ORS 459.772)

## At a glance

ORS 459.772 — Use of processed, source-separated waste tires for energy recovery. Chapter 459 (Chapter 459), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459.772 Use of processed, source-separated waste tires for energy recovery. Notwithstanding any other provision of ORS 459.015, for purposes of encouraging the use of waste tires under ORS 459.705 to 459.790, the use of processed, source-separated waste tires having a positive market value as a new product to recover energy shall be considered recycling under ORS 459.015 (2)(c)(C). [1991 c.882 §4; 2021 c.681 §56] Note: 459.772 was added to and made a part of 459.705 to 459.790 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
