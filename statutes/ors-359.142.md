# Rules ART TRANSACTIONS (Consignments) (ORS 359.142)

## At a glance

ORS 359.142 — Rules ART TRANSACTIONS (Consignments). Chapter 359 (Chapter 359), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

359.142 Rules. The Director of the Oregon Business Development Department, in accordance with ORS chapter 183, may adopt such rules for the operation of the Arts Program as the director determines necessary or convenient for the program to perform its duties and functions. [1993 c.209 §4] 359.150 [1981 c.411 §4; 1993 c.209 §15; repealed by 1993 c.209 §23] ART TRANSACTIONS (Consignments)
