# Unspecified treatment and experimentation prohibited (ORS 444.230)

## At a glance

ORS 444.230 — Unspecified treatment and experimentation prohibited. Chapter 444 (Chapter 444), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

444.230 Unspecified treatment and experimentation prohibited.

(1) No child under the terms of ORS 444.110 to 444.240 shall be treated for any ailment except as is described by the order of the court under ORS 444.120, unless permission for such treatment is granted by the parents or guardians.

(2) No child shall be used for the purpose of experimentation.
