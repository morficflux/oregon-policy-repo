# Standard form for reporting salaries and other benefits (ORS 332.534)

## At a glance

ORS 332.534 — Standard form for reporting salaries and other benefits. Chapter 332 (Chapter 332), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

332.534 Standard form for reporting salaries and other benefits. In reporting the compensation of school district employees, the Department of Education shall prepare a standard form for the purpose of reporting the salary plus other benefits including their dollar value.
