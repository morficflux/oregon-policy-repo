# Deceased individual (ORS 109.198)

## At a glance

ORS 109.198 — Deceased individual. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.198 Deceased individual. If an individual seeking genetic testing demonstrates good cause, the court may order genetic testing of a deceased individual. [2025 c.592 §49] 109.200 [Amended by 1961 c.338 §4; repealed by 1969 c.619 §15]
