# Rulemaking authority (ORS 671.415)

## At a glance

ORS 671.415 — Rulemaking authority. Chapter 671 (Chapter 671), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

671.415 Rulemaking authority. The State Landscape Architect Board, subject to ORS chapter 183, may adopt rules necessary for the board to perform its duties under ORS 671.310 to 671.459 and 671.995. [1981 c.536 §11; 1987 c.414 §42d; 2003 c.14 §421] 671.420 [1961 c.431 §16; repealed by 1977 c.842 §10]
