# Small restaurant requirements (ORS 447.023)

## At a glance

ORS 447.023 — Small restaurant requirements. Chapter 447 (Chapter 447), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

447.023 Small restaurant requirements. Rules adopted under ORS 447.020 shall allow restaurants with an occupancy capacity of no more than 15 persons, including employees and patrons, to have only one toilet fixture and adjacent lavatory on the premises. [1979 c.390 §4; 1981 c.438 §30]
