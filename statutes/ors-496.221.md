# ORS (ORS 496.221)

## At a glance

ORS 496.221 — ORS. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.221 ORS 496.201 to 496.221 not intended to extend Indian legal or political rights. Nothing in ORS 496.201 to 496.221 is intended to extend legal or political recognition to any Indians described in ORS 496.201

(1) for any purpose other than provided in ORS 496.201 to 496.216. [1981 c.575 §6; 1987 c.99 §4] 496.225 [Repealed by 1973 c.723 §130] ACCESS AND HABITAT BOARD
