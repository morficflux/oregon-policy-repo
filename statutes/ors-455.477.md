# Requirement for suit filed by licensed specialty code inspector or plan reviewer (ORS 455.477)

## At a glance

## Full text

455.477 Requirement for suit filed by licensed specialty code inspector or plan reviewer. A person carrying on, conducting or transacting specialty code inspections or plan reviews or a business employing specialty code inspectors or plan reviewers may not maintain any suit or action relating to specialty code inspections or plan reviews in any of the courts of this state without alleging and proving that the person or business was licensed under ORS 455.457 at the time of performing such work. [1999 c.1045 §11] Note: See note under 455.455.
