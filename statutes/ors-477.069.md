# Negligence in origin and in failure to control fire may be united in one complaint (ORS 477.069)

## At a glance

ORS 477.069 — Negligence in origin and in failure to control fire may be united in one complaint. Chapter 477 (Chapter 477), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

477.069 Negligence in origin and in failure to control fire may be united in one complaint. Notwithstanding any other law, in any action authorized by ORS 477.068 to collect the costs incurred, the plaintiff may unite in the same complaint causes of action based upon any or all of the grounds therein mentioned.
