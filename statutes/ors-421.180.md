# Disciplinary procedures; rules (ORS 421.180)

## At a glance

ORS 421.180 — Disciplinary procedures; rules. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.180 Disciplinary procedures; rules. The Department of Corrections by rule shall adopt procedures to be utilized in disciplining persons committed to the physical and legal custody of the department. [1973 c.621 §4; 1983 c.211 §1; 1987 c.320 §177]
