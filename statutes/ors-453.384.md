## At a glance

## Full text

453.384 Immunity of team members from liability. During operations authorized under ORS 453.374 to 453.390, members of regional hazardous materials response teams shall be protected and defended from liability under ORS 30.260 to 30.300. [1989 c.833 §85] 453.385 [1971 c.609 §11; 1975 c.606 §30; renumbered 469.390]
