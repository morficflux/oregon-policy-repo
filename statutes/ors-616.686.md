# Farm direct marketing rules (ORS 616.686)

## At a glance

ORS 616.686 — Farm direct marketing rules. Chapter 616 (General and Miscellaneous Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

616.686 Farm direct marketing rules.

(1) The State Department of Agriculture may adopt rules for the administration and enforcement of ORS 616.683.

(2) The State Department of Agriculture may not adopt rules to decrease an acidified food sales limit previously established by the department by rule. [2011 c.288 §3; 2019 c.57 §30; 2023 c.181 §4] Note: See note under 616.680. SANITARY REGULATIONS FOR FOOD AND FOOD ESTABLISHMENTS
