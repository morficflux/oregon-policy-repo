# Policies to support and promote independence and self-determination (ORS 430.071)

## At a glance

ORS 430.071 — Policies to support and promote independence and self-determination. Chapter 430 (Chapter 430), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

430.071 Policies to support and promote independence and self-determination. The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt policies that support and promote independence and self-determination for persons receiving mental health, addiction or substance use disorder services. The policies shall be designed to remove barriers that:

(1) Segregate persons with disabilities from full participation in the community in the most integrated setting in accordance with the United States Supreme Court decision in Olmstead v. L.C., 527 U.S. 581 (1999); and

(2) Prevent persons with disabilities from enjoying a meaningful life, the benefits of community involvement and citizen rights guaranteed by law. [2007 c.805 §1; 2009 c.595 §462; 2022 c.5 §1] Note: 430.071 to 430.078 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 430 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
