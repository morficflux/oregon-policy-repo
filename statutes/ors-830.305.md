# Unsafe operation (ORS 830.305)

## At a glance

ORS 830.305 — Unsafe operation. Chapter 830 (Small Watercraft _______________ 2025 EDITION SMALL WATERCRAFT SMALL WATERCRAFT GENERAL PROVISIONS 830.005 Definitions for chapter 830.015 Application of ORS 83), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

830.305 Unsafe operation. A person commits the crime of unsafe operation of a boat if the person operates a boat in a manner that endangers or would be likely to endanger any person or property. [Formerly 488.099]
