# Preemption of municipal charter amendments and local ordinances (ORS 475C.429)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.429 — Preemption of municipal charter amendments and local ordinances. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.429 Preemption of municipal charter amendments and local ordinances. The provisions of ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525 are designed to operate uniformly throughout the state and are paramount and superior to and fully replace and supersede any municipal charter amendment or local ordinance inconsistent with the provisions of ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525. Amendments and ordinances that are inconsistent with the provisions of ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525 are repealed. [Formerly 475B.454]
