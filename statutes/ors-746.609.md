# Exemptions for health insurers (ORS 746.609)

## At a glance

ORS 746.609 — Exemptions for health insurers. Chapter 746 (Chapter 746), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

746.609 Exemptions for health insurers. ORS 746.620, 746.630, 746.640, 746.645 and 746.665 do not apply to health insurers. [2003 c.87 §5]
