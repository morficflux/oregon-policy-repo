# Inspection of records more than 25 years old (Health Records) (ORS 192.390)

## At a glance

ORS 192.390 — Inspection of records more than 25 years old (Health Records). Chapter 192 (Records; Reports and Meetings), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

192.390 Inspection of records more than 25 years old. Notwithstanding ORS 192.338, 192.345 and 192.355 and except as otherwise provided in ORS 192.398, public records that are more than 25 years old shall be available for inspection. [Formerly 192.495] Note: 192.390 was added to and made a part of 192.311 to 192.478 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. (Health Records)
