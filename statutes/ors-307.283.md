## Full text

307.283 Homesteads of unmarried surviving spouses of veterans of Civil War or Spanish War. The surviving spouse remaining unmarried of any honorably discharged veteran of the Civil War or the Spanish War, who is pensioned and actually resides in a homestead, is entitled to an exemption of $2,000 of the taxable value of such homestead, in addition to the exemption from taxes on real property otherwise provided by law for such surviving spouse. [Formerly 307.300] 307.285 [1981 c.530 §2; 1982 s.s.1 c.33 §5; repealed by 1991 c.459 §81] (Active Duty Military Service)
