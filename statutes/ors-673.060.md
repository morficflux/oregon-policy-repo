## Full text

673.060 C.P.A. examination; scope; rules.

(1) The certified public accountant examination must test a person’s knowledge of the subjects of accounting and auditing and other related subjects as provided by the Oregon Board of Accountancy by rule.

(2) The board may contract with any organization, governmental or private, for examination development and administration.
