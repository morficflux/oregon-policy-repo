# Penalties (ORS 401.990)

## At a glance

ORS 401.990 — Penalties. Chapter 401 (Chapter 401), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

401.990 Penalties. Any person knowingly violating any provision of this chapter, or any of the rules, regulations or orders adopted and promulgated under this chapter, shall, upon conviction thereof, be guilty of a Class C misdemeanor. [1967 c.480 §8; 1977 c.248 §4; 1983 c.586 §41; 2009 c.718 §48] _______________
