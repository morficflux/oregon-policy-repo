# Exemption for tobacco products not subject to taxation by state (ORS 323.515)

## At a glance

ORS 323.515 — Exemption for tobacco products not subject to taxation by state. Chapter 323 (Chapter 323), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

323.515 Exemption for tobacco products not subject to taxation by state. The tax imposed by ORS 323.505 and 323.508 does not apply with respect to any tobacco products which under the Constitution and laws of the United States may not be made the subject of taxation by this state. [1985 c.816 §18; 2025 c.581 §4c]
