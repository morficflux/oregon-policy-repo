## Full text

446.005 “Issuing authority” defined. As used in ORS 446.003 to 446.200, 446.225 to 446.285 and 446.425, unless the context requires otherwise, or unless administration and enforcement by Oregon under the existing or revised National Manufactured Housing Construction and Safety Standards Act would be adversely affected, “issuing authority” means with respect to mobile home or manufactured dwelling park plans review and construction, the Department of Consumer and Business Services.
