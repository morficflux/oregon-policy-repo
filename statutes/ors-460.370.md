# Deposit of fees PENALTIES (ORS 460.370)

## At a glance

ORS 460.370 — Deposit of fees PENALTIES. Chapter 460 (Chapter 460), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

460.370 Deposit of fees. All moneys from fees collected by the Department of Consumer and Business Services under ORS 460.310 to 460.370 shall be paid into the Consumer and Business Services Fund created by ORS 705.145 and are continuously appropriated to the department for use as provided in ORS 455.022.
