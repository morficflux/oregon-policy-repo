# Annual report to Governor and Legislative Assembly (Accounts for Qualified Higher Education Expenses) (ORS 178.330)

## At a glance

ORS 178.330 — Annual report to Governor and Legislative Assembly (Accounts for Qualified Higher Education Expenses). Chapter 178 (State Treasurer), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

178.330 Annual report to Governor and Legislative Assembly. The Oregon 529 Savings Board shall publish an annual report to the Governor and the Legislative Assembly detailing the board’s activities under ORS 178.300 to 178.360, 178.375 and 178.380. The board shall submit the report to the Governor and the Legislative Assembly on or before March 15 of each year. [Formerly 348.873] (Accounts for Qualified Higher Education Expenses)
