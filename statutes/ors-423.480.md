# Adoption of supervision rules (ORS 423.480)

## At a glance

ORS 423.480 — Adoption of supervision rules. Chapter 423 (Chapter 423), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

423.480 Adoption of supervision rules. The Department of Corrections, in consultation with county community corrections agencies, community members, including persons currently or formerly under supervision, and organizations that provide culturally specific services, shall adopt rules for standards concerning the location of supervision visits, the frequency of visits and the manner of reporting, for persons on supervision. The rules must take into account evidence-based practices and must require consideration of the risks, needs and responsivity of each supervised person and the goals for completion of supervision. The rules must include a reporting process that is designed to minimize disruptions to the life of the supervised person and avoid unnecessary hardships, while offering the supervised person a broad array of reporting options, and that is focused on the success of the person on supervision. [2022 c.78 §12]
