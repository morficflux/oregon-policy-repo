# No right or benefit created (ORS 182.152)

## At a glance

ORS 182.152 — No right or benefit created. Chapter 182 (State Administrative Agencies Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

182.152 No right or benefit created. ORS 182.151 is intended to improve the internal management of state agencies in Oregon and is not intended to create any right or benefit, substantive or procedural, enforceable at law by a party against the State of Oregon, its agencies, its officers or any person. [1999 c.523 §2] Note: See note under 182.151. 182.160 [1959 c.501 §6; repealed by 1959 c.501 §10]
