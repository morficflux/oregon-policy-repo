# Community service (ORS 419C.462)

## At a glance

ORS 419C.462 — Community service. Chapter 419C (Chapter 419C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419C.462 Community service. The court may order an adjudicated youth to perform appropriate community service for a number of hours not to exceed that which could be required under ORS 137.129 if the adjudicated youth were an adult. [1993 c.33 §234; 2003 c.396 §115; 2021 c.489 §77]
