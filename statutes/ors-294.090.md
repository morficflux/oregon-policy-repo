# County orders and vouchers to be numbered to correspond to warrants drawn (ORS 294.090)

## At a glance

ORS 294.090 — County orders and vouchers to be numbered to correspond to warrants drawn. Chapter 294 (Chapter 294), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

294.090 County orders and vouchers to be numbered to correspond to warrants drawn. The county clerk and clerk of the county court shall number all orders and vouchers with numbers to correspond with warrants drawn.
