## Full text

438.620 Accreditation fees; disposition of fees.

(1) In conjunction with the environmental laboratory accreditation program established under ORS 438.615, the Oregon Health Authority may establish and collect a fee for laboratory accreditation under the program. A fee imposed under this section shall not exceed the cost of administering the program.

(2) Prior to imposing the fee under subsection

(1) of this section, the authority shall obtain the approval of the Oregon Department of Administrative Services and report to the appropriate legislative committee.

(3) All moneys collected by the Oregon Health Authority under this section shall be deposited in a dedicated account of the authority. Such moneys are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Health Authority to pay the costs of the authority, the State Department of Agriculture and the Department of Environmental Quality in administering the environmental laboratory accreditation program established under ORS 438.615. [1999 c.1063 §4; 2009 c.595 §717]
