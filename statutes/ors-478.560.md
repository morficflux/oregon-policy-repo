## Full text

478.560 Deposit and disbursement of funds of districts located in two or more counties. Funds accruing to a district, located in two or more counties, from any source shall be deposited in accordance with ORS 295.001 to 295.108 and shall be drawn out only upon proper order and warrant or check bearing the signature of those persons authorized to sign warrants or checks by resolution of the board.
