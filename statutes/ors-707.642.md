# Organizational meeting of directors; notice (ORS 707.642)

## At a glance

ORS 707.642 — Organizational meeting of directors; notice. Chapter 707 (Chapter 707), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

707.642 Organizational meeting of directors; notice. After the issuance of the certificate of incorporation, an organizational meeting of the board of directors named in the articles of incorporation shall be held, either within or without this state, at the call of a majority of the incorporators, for the purpose of adopting bylaws, electing officers and transacting such other business as may come before the meeting. The incorporators who called the meeting shall give at least three days’ notice thereof by mail to each director so named, which notice shall state the time, place and purpose of the meeting. [1989 c.324 §27; 1997 c.631 §89]
