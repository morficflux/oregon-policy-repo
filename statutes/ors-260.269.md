## Full text

260.269 Rules for synthetic media disclosures. The Secretary of State may adopt rules necessary to implement ORS 260.268. [2024 c.62 §4] Note: 260.269 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 260 or any series therein by legislative action.
