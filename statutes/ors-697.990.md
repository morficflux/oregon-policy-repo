# Penalties (ORS 697.990)

## At a glance

ORS 697.990 — Penalties. Chapter 697 (Chapter 697), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

697.990 Penalties.

(1) Violation of ORS 697.015 or 697.058 by an individual is a Class A violation.

(2) Violation of ORS 697.015 or 697.058 by a corporation or association is a Class A violation. Any officer or agent of a corporation or association who personally participates in any violation of ORS 697.015 or 697.058 by the corporation or association is subject to the penalty prescribed in subsection

(1) of this section.

(3) Violation of ORS 697.612 or 697.642 to 697.702 is punishable, upon conviction, as a Class A misdemeanor. [Amended by 1959 c.525 §35; subsection
