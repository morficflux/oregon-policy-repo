# Record keeping requirements for wet marine and transportation insurance contracts (ORS 731.833)

## At a glance

ORS 731.833 — Record keeping requirements for wet marine and transportation insurance contracts. Chapter 731 (Administration and General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

731.833 Record keeping requirements for wet marine and transportation insurance contracts.

(1) Each producer of nonadmitted wet marine and transportation insurance shall keep a full and true record of each nonadmitted wet marine and transportation insurance contract placed on an Oregon home state risk. The record must include a copy of the policy, certificate, cover note or other evidence of insurance that the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services specifies by rule.

(2) A producer shall keep the record described in subsection

(1) of this section open at all reasonable times to the director’s examination, without notice, for a period of not less than five years after termination of the nonadmitted wet marine and transportation insurance contract. [2019 c.493 §7]
