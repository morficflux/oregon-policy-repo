# City’s authority to control and collect charges for use of rights of way (ORS 757.954)

## At a glance

ORS 757.954 — City’s authority to control and collect charges for use of rights of way. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.954 City’s authority to control, and collect charges for, use of rights of way. ORS 757.812 to 757.950 do not diminish, or authorize the adoption of rules that diminish, the authority of a city to control the use of the city’s rights of way or to collect license fees, privilege taxes, rent or other charges for the use of the rights of way of the city. [2007 c.807 §42] Note: See note under 757.812. WILDFIRE PROTECTION AND MITIGATION
