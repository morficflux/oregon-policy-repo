# Federal tax credits allowable only as specified (ORS 315.054)

## At a glance

ORS 315.054 — Federal tax credits allowable only as specified. Chapter 315 (Chapter 315), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

315.054 Federal tax credits allowable only as specified. No credits applied directly to the income tax calculated for federal purposes pursuant to the Internal Revenue Code shall be applied in calculating the tax due under ORS chapter 314, 316, 317 or 318 except those prescribed in this chapter or ORS chapter 314, 316, 317 or 318. [1993 c.730 §4 (enacted in lieu of 316.107)] 315.055 [Repealed by 1965 c.26 §6]
