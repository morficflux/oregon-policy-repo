# Call of special elections (ORS 440.350)

## At a glance

ORS 440.350 — Call of special elections. Chapter 440 (Chapter 440), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

440.350 Call of special elections. The board, at any regular meeting, may call a special election of the electors of the district. [Formerly 441.305] 440.355 [Formerly 441.315; repealed by 1979 c.190 §431]
