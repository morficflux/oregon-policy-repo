## Full text

716.574 Purchase of real estate sale contract as loan; authority to acquire contracts.

(1) The purchase of a bona fide contract covering a sale of real estate is a loan on real estate within the meaning of ORS 716.552 to 716.574.

(2) A savings bank may acquire contracts covering a sale of real estate if all other requirements of ORS 716.552 to 716.574 are satisfied.
