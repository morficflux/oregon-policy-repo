# Prohibition on uses of map of wildfire risk or exposure HOME PROTECTION INSURANCE (ORS 742.278)

## At a glance

ORS 742.278 — Prohibition on uses of map of wildfire risk or exposure HOME PROTECTION INSURANCE. Chapter 742 (Chapter 742), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

742.278 Prohibition on uses of map of wildfire risk or exposure. An insurance company may not use a map published by an agency of this state that identifies areas of wildfire risk or exposure as a basis for:

(1) Canceling or declining to renew a homeowner insurance policy; or

(2) Increasing a premium for a homeowner insurance policy. [2023 c.67 §4] Note: See note under 742.277. HOME PROTECTION INSURANCE
