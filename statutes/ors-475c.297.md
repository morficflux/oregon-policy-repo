# Marijuana Control and Regulation Fund (Regulatory Specialists) (ORS 475C.297)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.297 — Marijuana Control and Regulation Fund (Regulatory Specialists). Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.297 Marijuana Control and Regulation Fund. The Marijuana Control and Regulation Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the Marijuana Control and Regulation Fund shall be credited to the fund. Moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission to administer and enforce ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525, 475C.540 to 475C.586, 475C.600 to 475C.648, 475C.925, 571.280, 571.282, 571.295 to 571.298 and 571.309. [Formerly 475B.296; 2024 c.16 §§7,15] (Regulatory Specialists)
