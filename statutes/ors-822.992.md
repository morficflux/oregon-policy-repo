# Civil penalties for violations related to dismantlers (ORS 822.992)

## At a glance

ORS 822.992 — Civil penalties for violations related to dismantlers. Chapter 822 (Chapter 822), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

822.992 Civil penalties for violations related to dismantlers.

(1) The Department of Transportation may levy a civil penalty on, and collect the civil penalty from, any person holding a dismantler certificate issued under ORS 822.110 or identification card or supplemental dismantler certificate issued under ORS 822.125 in an amount not to exceed $1,000 for each violation if the person holding the certificate has violated ORS 803.140, 819.012, 819.016, 819.040, 822.120, 822.125, 822.130, 822.135 or 822.150.

(2) The department shall impose civil penalties under this section in the manner provided in ORS 183.745. [2011 c.433 §5] Note: 822.992 was added to and made a part of the Oregon Vehicle Code by legislative action but was not added to ORS chapter 822 or any series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
