## Full text

410.632 Certain covenants between employer and home care worker or personal support worker voidable by worker. A covenant between an employee and an employer not to solicit employees of the employer or solicit or transact business with customers of the employer made in the context of an employment relationship or contract with a home care worker or personal support worker, as defined in ORS 410.600, is voidable by the home care worker or personal support worker and may not be enforced by a court of this state.
