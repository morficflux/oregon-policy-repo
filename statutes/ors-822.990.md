## At a glance

## Full text

822.990 Civil penalties for violations of ORS 822.500.

(1) The Department of Transportation may levy and collect a civil penalty in the amount of $1,000 for each violation against any person who violates ORS 822.500.

(2) Civil penalties under this section shall be imposed in the manner provided in ORS 183.745. [2001 c.727 §3; 2023 c.400 §28]
