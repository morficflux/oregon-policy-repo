# Inspection authority (ORS 459A.752)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.752 — Inspection authority. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.752 Inspection authority. In addition to the authority granted under ORS 459.992, inspectors and investigators employed by the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission have authority to inspect any space occupied by a dealer for compliance with ORS 459A.738. [Formerly 459A.742]
