## Full text

676.645 Renewal of certificate; rules.

(1) A certificate issued under ORS 676.640

(1) or

(3) must be renewed annually. To renew a certificate under this section, a certified advanced esthetician must submit to the Health Licensing Office: (a) A renewal application; (b) Proof that the certified advanced esthetician has completed any continuing education requirements established by the Board of Certified Advanced Estheticians by rule; and (c) The applicable renewal fee established under ORS 676.576.

(2) A temporary certificate issued under ORS 676.640

(4) expires as prescribed by the office by rule. [2015 c.722 §4]
