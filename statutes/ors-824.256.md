# Expense contributed by public held in trust by railroad company EMPLOYEE SAFETY REGULATIONS (ORS 824.256)

## At a glance

ORS 824.256 — Expense contributed by public held in trust by railroad company EMPLOYEE SAFETY REGULATIONS. Chapter 824 (Chapter 824), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

824.256 Expense contributed by public held in trust by railroad company. Any portion of the cost or expense that is contributed or borne by any public authority under ORS 824.200, 824.204, 824.206, 824.210 to 824.218 and 824.226 shall forever be considered as held in trust by the railroad company receiving the same or the benefits thereof, and no part thereof shall be considered a part of the value of the property of the railroad company upon which it is entitled to receive a return.
