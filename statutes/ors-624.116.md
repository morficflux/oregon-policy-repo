## Full text

624.116 When alternative food service criteria applicable. The Director of the Oregon Health Authority may declare that an extraordinary situation exists and may apply alternative food service criteria in an establishment operated to prepare or serve food or beverages to indigent or needy persons by a benevolent organization, as defined by ORS 624.101, without charge or solicitation from those served. [Formerly 624.035; 2009 c.595 §1015] 624.120 [Amended by 1957 c.672 §7; 1973 c.427 §13; 1987 c.905 §34; renumbered 624.029 in 2007]
