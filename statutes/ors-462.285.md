## Full text

462.285 Confidentiality of identity of persons making certain reports.

(1) If a person makes a report to the Oregon Racing Commission of a violation or potential violation of a statute, rule or order relating to racing, the identity of the person making the report is confidential and is not subject to disclosure as a public record under ORS 192.311 to 192.478.

(2) A person making a report described in subsection

(1) of this section may waive the confidentiality otherwise granted under this section.
