# Guidelines for districts DIRECT AND CONTRACT SERVICES (ORS 341.015)

## At a glance

## Full text

341.015 Guidelines for districts. The Higher Education Coordinating Commission shall adopt guidelines for the orderly development and management of community college districts, including guidelines for personnel policy formulation, student record keeping and privacy procedures.
