# Trustee as public employee (ORS 441.309)

## At a glance

ORS 441.309 — Trustee as public employee. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.309 Trustee as public employee.

(1) In any action or special proceeding brought against a trustee in the trustee’s official capacity for acts committed while carrying out the powers granted and duties imposed by ORS 441.277 to 441.323, the trustee shall be considered a public employee.

(2) A trustee may be held liable in a personal capacity only for the trustee’s own gross negligence, intentional acts or breach of fiduciary duty. [1981 c.868 §9] 441.310 [Amended by 1959 c.616 §1; 1969 c.343 §17; repealed by 1971 c.647 §149]
