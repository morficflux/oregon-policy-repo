# State Board of Forestry rules (ORS 526.335)

## At a glance

ORS 526.335 — State Board of Forestry rules. Chapter 526 (Forestry Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

526.335 State Board of Forestry rules. The State Board of Forestry may adopt rules as necessary to implement ORS 526.305 to 526.340. [2009 c.69 §11]
