# Sports bribe receiving (ORS 165.090)

## At a glance

ORS 165.090 — Sports bribe receiving. Chapter 165 (Chapter 165), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

165.090 Sports bribe receiving.

(1) A person commits the crime of sports bribe receiving if: (a) As a sports participant the person solicits, accepts or agrees to accept any benefit from another person with the intent that the person will thereby be influenced not to give the best effort of the person in a sports contest; or (b) As a sports official the person solicits, accepts or agrees to accept any benefit from another person with the intent that the person will improperly perform duties of a sports official.

(2) Sports bribe receiving is a Class C felony. [1971 c.743 §165]
