# Penalties OPTOMETRISTS (Generally) (ORS 683.990)

## At a glance

ORS 683.990 — Penalties OPTOMETRISTS (Generally). Chapter 683 (Chapter 683), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

683.990 Penalties. Violation of any of the provisions of ORS 683.010 to 683.340 is a Class A misdemeanor. [Amended by 1971 c.743 §408; 2005 c.379 §5] _______________
