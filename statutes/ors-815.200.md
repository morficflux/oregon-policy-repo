# Violation of visible emission limits; penalty (ORS 815.200)

## At a glance

ORS 815.200 — Violation of visible emission limits; penalty. Chapter 815 (Chapter 815), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

815.200 Violation of visible emission limits; penalty.

(1) A person commits the offense of violation of visible emission limits if the person operates, drives and causes or permits to be driven on any highway: (a) A motor vehicle, other than one described in paragraph (b) of this subsection, that has visible emissions exceeding visible emissions allowed under Visible Emission Standard I under ORS 815.195. (b) A motor vehicle powered by compression ignition, two cycle or diesel cycle engines or a vehicle excluded by order of the Environmental Quality Commission under ORS 468A.075 and the vehicle has visible emissions exceeding visible emissions allowed under Visible Emission Standard II under ORS 815.195.

(2) The exemptions from this section are established under ORS 815.205.

(3) The offense described in this section, violation of visible emission limits, is a Class D traffic violation. [1983 c.338 §485; 1985 c.393 §21]
