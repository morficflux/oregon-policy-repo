## Full text

147.508 Reconsideration of release decision.

(1) At the request of a victim, the prosecuting attorney may request that the court schedule a hearing to reconsider a release decision if: (a) The victim did not have notice of, or an opportunity to be heard at, a hearing in which the court released the defendant from custody or reduced the defendant’s security amount; and (b) The victim’s request is made no later than 30 days after the victim knew or reasonably should have known of the release decision that is to be reconsidered.

(2) As used in this section, “release decision” includes: (a) Decisions made at arraignment; and (b) Decisions made at hearings described in ORS 419C.273 (1)(b)(A) to (C). [2009 c.178 §4; 2011 c.659 §2; 2023 c.182 §11] Note: See note under 147.500.
