# Rulemaking authority (ORS 671.670)

## At a glance

ORS 671.670 — Rulemaking authority. Chapter 671 (Chapter 671), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

671.670 Rulemaking authority. In accordance with any applicable provision of ORS chapter 183, the State Landscape Contractors Board may adopt rules the board considers reasonable for the administration and enforcement of ORS 671.510 to 671.760 and 671.997. [1971 c.764 §19; 1981 c.536 §26; 2005 c.609 §17] 671.675 [1987 c.461 §7; repealed by 1995 c.645 §6]
