# Criteria and standards for evaluation of projects (ORS 184.720)

## At a glance

ORS 184.720 — Criteria and standards for evaluation of projects. Chapter 184 (Certain Executive Branch Departments (incl. DAS)), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

184.720 Criteria and standards for evaluation of projects. For the purpose of aiding the development of public transportation systems, the Department of Transportation shall establish criteria and standards for the evaluation of transportation projects considered for assistance under ORS 184.670, 184.675 and 184.685 to 184.733. [1977 c.230 §11]
