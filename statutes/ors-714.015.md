# Branches not in compliance with ORS (ORS 714.015)

## At a glance

ORS 714.015 — Branches not in compliance with ORS. Chapter 714 (Chapter 714), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

714.015 Branches not in compliance with ORS 714.025 to 714.079 prohibited. Banking institutions shall not establish or maintain branches except as expressly authorized in ORS 714.025 to 714.079 and 714.995. [1997 c.631 §312] 714.020 [Repealed by 1973 c.797 §428]
