# Distribution and availability of registration cards (ORS 247.178)

## At a glance

ORS 247.178 — Distribution and availability of registration cards. Chapter 247 (Chapter 247), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

247.178 Distribution and availability of registration cards. Any person may distribute a registration card in any reasonable manner that facilitates elector registration, including but not limited to distribution of the card door to door. The card shall be available at any field office of the Department of Transportation where applications for driver licenses or vehicle registrations are accepted and at any office of an agency designated a voter registration agency under ORS 247.208. [Formerly 247.045; 1993 c.713 §12; 1993 c.741 §20] 247.180 [Repealed by 1957 c.608 §231]
