# Unsuccessful candidate not eligible as candidate (ORS 249.048)

## At a glance

ORS 249.048 — Unsuccessful candidate not eligible as candidate. Chapter 249 (Chapter 249), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

249.048 Unsuccessful candidate not eligible as candidate. A candidate for nomination of a major political party to a public office who fails to receive the nomination may not be the candidate of any other political party or a nonaffiliated candidate for the same office at the succeeding general election. The filing officer may not certify the name of the candidate. [Formerly 249.420; 2005 c.797 §62] 249.050 [Repealed by 1957 c.608 §231] 249.051 [1957 c.608 §70; 1979 c.190 §115; renumbered 249.076]
