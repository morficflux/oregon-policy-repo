# De minimis tax payment not required (ORS 314.401)

## At a glance

ORS 314.401 — De minimis tax payment not required. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.401 De minimis tax payment not required. Notwithstanding ORS 314.395 and 314.400, if the balance of tax due as shown on the report or return filed by the taxpayer for the tax year is less than $1, payment of the amount shown shall not be required. [1999 c.73 §2]
