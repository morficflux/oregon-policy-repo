# Unclean slaughterhouses prohibited (ORS 603.059)

## At a glance

ORS 603.059 — Unclean slaughterhouses prohibited. Chapter 603 (Chapter 603), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

603.059 Unclean slaughterhouses prohibited. No owner or occupier of premises where animals are slaughtered shall permit the same to remain unclean, to the extent that it constitutes a health hazard. [Formerly 433.710] 603.060 [Amended by 1955 c.724 §8; 1959 c.239 §2; 1967 c.396 §2; repealed by 1969 c.565 §48]
