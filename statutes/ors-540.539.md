# Exchange subject to beneficial use requirements (ORS 540.539)

## At a glance

ORS 540.539 — Exchange subject to beneficial use requirements. Chapter 540 (Chapter 540), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

540.539 Exchange subject to beneficial use requirements. An exchange of water under ORS 540.533 to 540.537 is subject to the requirements of beneficial use under ORS 540.610. [1987 c.815 §5] 540.540 [Renumbered 540.545 in 1987]
