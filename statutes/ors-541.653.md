# Purpose and application of ORS (ORS 541.653)

## At a glance

ORS 541.653 — Purpose and application of ORS. Chapter 541 (Chapter 541), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

541.653 Purpose and application of ORS 541.651 to 541.696.

(1) The purpose of ORS 541.651 to 541.696 is to establish a means for state government to support the development of water resource projects having economic, environmental and community benefits.

(2) The Legislative Assembly intends that any conditions or requirements described in ORS 541.651 to 541.696 apply only to projects that receive loans or grants from the Water Supply Development Account established in ORS 541.656. [2013 c.784 §2] Note: See note under 541.651. 541.655 [1967 c.567 §11; 1971 c.754 §11; 1973 c.330 §7; 1973 c.674 §10; 1985 c.414 §2; renumbered 196.730 and then 196.865 in 1989]
