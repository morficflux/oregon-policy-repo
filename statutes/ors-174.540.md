# Parts of printed statute editions not part of the law (ORS 174.540)

## At a glance

ORS 174.540 — Parts of printed statute editions not part of the law. Chapter 174 (Construction of Statutes; General Definitions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

174.540 Parts of printed statute editions not part of the law. Title heads, chapter heads, division heads, section and subsection heads or titles and explanatory notes, in the statute laws described in ORS 174.510

(1) and in parts of Oregon Revised Statutes, do not constitute any part of the law. [1953 c.3 §4; 1961 c.90 §5; 2013 c.1 §15]
