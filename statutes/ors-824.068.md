## At a glance

## Full text

824.068 Water quality standards for locomotives.

(1) The Department of Transportation shall prescribe standards for water quality on railroad locomotives in this state.

(2) The department may for good cause shown permit variances from the standards so prescribed. [Formerly 761.365; 2021 c.630 §9] HAZARDOUS MATERIALS
