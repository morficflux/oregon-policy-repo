# Civil penalty exemption for unlawful employment of minors by parents or persons standing in place of parents (ORS 653.365)

## At a glance

ORS 653.365 — Civil penalty exemption for unlawful employment of minors by parents or persons standing in place of parents. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.365 Civil penalty exemption for unlawful employment of minors by parents or persons standing in place of parents. The provisions of ORS 653.370 do not apply when minors under 18 years of age are employed under the following circumstances:

(1) The minor is employed by the parent of the minor; or

(2) The minor is employed by a person standing in the place of the parent of the minor and who has custody of the minor. [1981 c.820 §3; 1997 c.103 §1]
