## At a glance

## Full text

571.438 Exemption from certain financial administration laws. ORS 291.026, 291.201 to 291.222, 291.232 to 291.260, 291.322 to 291.334, 292.210 to 292.250, 293.260 to 293.280, 293.295 to 293.346 and 293.590 to 293.640 do not apply to the Oregon Hemp Commission or to the administration and enforcement of ORS 571.400 to 571.501. [2021 c.216 §15] Note: See note under 571.400.
