# Required content of candidate’s statement (ORS 251.085)

## At a glance

ORS 251.085 — Required content of candidate’s statement. Chapter 251 (Chapter 251), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

251.085 Required content of candidate’s statement. The candidate’s statement shall begin with a summary of the following: Occupation, educational and occupational background, and prior governmental experience. [Formerly 255.027]
