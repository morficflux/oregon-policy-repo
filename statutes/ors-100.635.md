## Full text

100.635 Filing with commissioner; fee.

(1) Except as provided by ORS 100.660 and 100.665, prior to negotiating within this state for the sale of a condominium unit located in another state, or prior to the sale of any condominium unit located within this state, the developer shall file with the Real Estate Commissioner, in any form prescribed by the commissioner: (a) General information on the condominium, including: (A) The name and address of the condominium and the county in which the condominium is located; and (B) The name, address and telephone number of the developer. (b) Two copies of the disclosure statement for the condominium prepared in accordance with ORS 100.655. (c) The documents for and other information on the condominium as required by ORS 100.640.

(2) The filing must include the fee required by ORS 100.670. [Formerly 94.331; 2019 c.69 §21]
