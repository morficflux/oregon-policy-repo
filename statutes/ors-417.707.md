# Duty of state agencies providing services for children and families (ORS 417.707)

## At a glance

ORS 417.707 — Duty of state agencies providing services for children and families. Chapter 417 (Chapter 417), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

417.707 Duty of state agencies providing services for children and families. The purpose of ORS 417.705 to 417.800, as described in ORS 417.708 to 417.725, shall be implemented by all state agencies providing services for children and families to guide the providing of those services. [Formerly 417.755; 2012 c.97 §20]
