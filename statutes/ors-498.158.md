# Hunting or trapping wildlife in certain governmental districts restricted (ORS 498.158)

## At a glance

ORS 498.158 — Hunting or trapping wildlife in certain governmental districts restricted. Chapter 498 (Chapter 498), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

498.158 Hunting or trapping wildlife in certain governmental districts restricted.

(1) Except as provided in ORS 448.305 and in subsection

(2) of this section, no person shall hunt or trap any wildlife within the boundaries of any city, public park, cemetery or on any school lands.

(2) No hunting or trapping shall be allowed on any lands within the boundaries of any city, public park or on any school lands unless: (a) The governing body or other agency that administers the affairs of the city, public park or school, after notice and hearing, authorizes such hunting or trapping by ordinance or resolution; and (b) The State Fish and Wildlife Commission, after notice and hearing, determines that such hunting or trapping would not adversely affect public safety or unreasonably interfere with other authorized uses of such lands. [1973 c.723 §90] 498.160 [1971 c.223 §3; repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
