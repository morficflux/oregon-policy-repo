## Full text

571.282 Interagency agreement regarding inspections; rules.

(1) The State Department of Agriculture may enter into an interagency agreement with the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission to allow representatives of the commission to carry out inspections of industrial hemp crops under ORS 571.281

(7) throughout this state.

(2) The department and the commission may adopt rules to carry out this section. [2024 c.16 §4] Note: See note under 571.260.
