# Number of jurors required for verdict (ORS 136.450)

## At a glance

ORS 136.450 — Number of jurors required for verdict. Chapter 136 (Chapter 136), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

136.450 Number of jurors required for verdict.

(1) A jury in a criminal action may render a verdict of guilty only by unanimous agreement.

(2) A jury in a criminal action may render a verdict of not guilty only by a concurrence of at least 10 of 12 jurors. [Formerly 136.610; 1997 c.313 §25; 2019 c.635 §8; 2021 c.478 §11]
