# Short title (ORS 106.300)

## At a glance

ORS 106.300 — Short title. Chapter 106 (Marriage; Domestic Partnership), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

106.300 Short title. ORS 106.300 to 106.340 may be cited as the Oregon Family Fairness Act. [2007 c.99 §1] Note: 106.300 to 106.340 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 106 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
