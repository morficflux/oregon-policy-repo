# Contributions by state and counties for medical assistance demonstration projects under ORS (ORS 411.137)

## At a glance

ORS 411.137 — Contributions by state and counties for medical assistance demonstration projects under ORS. Chapter 411 (Chapter 411), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

411.137 Contributions by state and counties for medical assistance demonstration projects under ORS 411.135. Exclusive of all sums of money contributed by the federal government for medical assistance and administration under ORS 414.033

(2) the State of Oregon shall contribute 40 percentum and the several counties of the state in which demonstration projects are operating shall each contribute 60 percentum of all sums, including administrative costs, required to be expended for such purposes in and for such respective counties. [1975 c.509 §8; 1991 c.66 §3]
