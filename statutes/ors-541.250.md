# Cession to United States not rescinded SUITS FOR DETERMINATION OF WATER RIGHTS UNDER 1905 ACT (ORS 541.250)

## At a glance

ORS 541.250 — Cession to United States not rescinded SUITS FOR DETERMINATION OF WATER RIGHTS UNDER 1905 ACT. Chapter 541 (Chapter 541), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

541.250 Cession to United States not rescinded. Nothing in ORS 541.220 to 541.240 shall be construed as rescinding the cession by the state to the United States of lands, as provided in chapter 5, Oregon Laws 1905. SUITS FOR DETERMINATION OF WATER RIGHTS UNDER 1905 ACT
