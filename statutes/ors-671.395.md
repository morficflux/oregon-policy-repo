# Continuing education; fee (ORS 671.395)

## At a glance

ORS 671.395 — Continuing education; fee. Chapter 671 (Chapter 671), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

671.395 Continuing education; fee.

(1) The State Landscape Architect Board may establish or approve programs of continuing education that contribute to the competency of landscape architects. The board may charge a fee for programs of continuing education it establishes.

(2) The board may require completion of a program of continuing education established or approved under this section as a condition for the issuance or renewal of registration as a landscape architect under ORS 671.310 to 671.459. [1981 c.536 §9; 2001 c.950 §15; 2003 c.14 §418] 671.400 [1961 c.431 §13; repealed by 1977 c.842 §10]
