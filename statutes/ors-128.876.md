# Rules (ORS 128.876)

## At a glance

ORS 128.876 — Rules. Chapter 128 (Chapter 128), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

128.876 Rules. The Attorney General shall make rules as to the filing and execution of reports and registration statements required by ORS 128.610 to 128.650, 128.680, 128.710, 128.801 to 128.898, 128.995 and 646.608 and to the contents thereof. The Attorney General may make additional rules and amend existing rules as necessary for the proper administration of the Charitable Solicitations Act. [1985 c.729 §18; 2003 c.14 §46] 128.880 [1959 c.599 §9; 1967 c.359 §130; renumbered 731.724]
