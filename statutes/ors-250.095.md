# State measures affecting a county or district (ORS 250.095)

## At a glance

ORS 250.095 — State measures affecting a county or district. Chapter 250 (Chapter 250), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

250.095 State measures affecting a county or district. A law enacted by the Legislative Assembly relating only to a county or district may be referred by the Legislative Assembly or by petition to the people of the county or district. The percentage of signatures required under section 1, Article IV, Oregon Constitution, for a referendum petition filed under this section shall be based on the vote for Governor within the county or district. [1979 c.190 §148] 250.100 [Repealed by 1957 c.608 §231]
