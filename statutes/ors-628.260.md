# Furnishing copies of law to applicants for licenses; posting copy (ORS 628.260)

## At a glance

ORS 628.260 — Furnishing copies of law to applicants for licenses; posting copy. Chapter 628 (Chapter 628), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

628.260 Furnishing copies of law to applicants for licenses; posting copy. Two copies of ORS 628.210 to 628.370 and 628.990

(2) shall be furnished each applicant for a license. One copy shall be posted in a conspicuous place in the plant operated by the licensee.
