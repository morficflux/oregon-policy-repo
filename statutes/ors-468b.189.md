# Actions by State Department of Agriculture (ORS 468B.189)

## At a glance

ORS 468B.189 — Actions by State Department of Agriculture. Chapter 468B (Chapter 468B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468B.189 Actions by State Department of Agriculture. After a declaration of a ground water quality management area under ORS 468B.180, and consistent with the action plan developed under ORS 468B.184 and 468B.186, the State Department of Agriculture, as necessary to control a relevant contaminant of concern:

(1) Shall adopt and implement area-specific rules, as provided in ORS 561.191 and 568.900 to 568.933, to regulate the contaminant of concern.

(2) May make available stewardship agreement opportunities, pursuant to ORS 541.973. [2025 c.605 §14] Note: 468B.189 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 468B or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
