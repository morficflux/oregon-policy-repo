# Assignments of property to wildfire hazard zones null and void FIRE PREVENTION (Wildfire Risk) (ORS 477.491)

## At a glance

ORS 477.491 — Assignments of property to wildfire hazard zones null and void FIRE PREVENTION (Wildfire Risk). Chapter 477 (Chapter 477), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

477.491 Assignments of property to wildfire hazard zones null and void.

(1) As used in this section, “order” has the meaning given that term in ORS 183.310.

(2) An order issued by the State Forestry Department under ORS 477.490 (2023 Edition) that assigns property to a wildfire hazard zone is null and void. [2025 c.590 §8] Note: 477.491 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 477 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. FIRE PREVENTION (Wildfire Risk)
