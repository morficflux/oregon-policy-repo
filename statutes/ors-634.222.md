# Determination of lawful establishment of protected area in actions or proceedings; certified copy of filed order as evidence (ORS 634.222)

## At a glance

ORS 634.222 — Determination of lawful establishment of protected area in actions or proceedings; certified copy of filed order as evidence. Chapter 634 (Chapter 634), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

634.222 Determination of lawful establishment of protected area in actions or proceedings; certified copy of filed order as evidence. In any suit, action or proceeding involving the validity or enforcement of any proceeding or action of a protected area, the protected area shall be deemed to have been established in accordance with the provisions of this chapter upon proof of the filing of an order as required by ORS 634.216. A copy of such order, certified as filed by the Secretary of State, shall be admissible evidence in any such suit, action or proceeding and shall be proof of the filing and contents thereof. [1973 c.341 §31] 634.225 [1953 c.685 §12; 1961 c.294 §6; repealed by 1973 c.341 §37]
