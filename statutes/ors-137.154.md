# Level II obligations in local court judgments (ORS 137.154)

## At a glance

ORS 137.154 — Level II obligations in local court judgments. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.154 Level II obligations in local court judgments. Level II obligations in criminal judgments entered in local courts include all fines and other monetary obligations payable to the state, a city or a county, after payment of the amount provided for in ORS 153.633 (2). [2013 c.685 §18; 2022 c.57 §8]
