# Determining value per mile of property of companies using wire, pipe or pole lines or operational routes (ORS 308.575)

## At a glance

ORS 308.575 — Determining value per mile of property of companies using wire, pipe or pole lines or operational routes. Chapter 308 (Chapter 308), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308.575 Determining value per mile of property of companies using wire, pipe or pole lines or operational routes. The Department of Revenue may apportion the assessed value of the property of any company owning or using wire, pipe or pole lines, or operating over operational routes, over the wire, pipe or pole lines in such manner and at such rate or rates per mile as the department determines to be reasonable and fair. [Amended by 1981 c.804 §65; 1991 c.459 §152; 1997 c.541 §209; 2009 c.128 §10]
