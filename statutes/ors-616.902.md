# Use of consumer-owned containers (ORS 616.902)

## At a glance

ORS 616.902 — Use of consumer-owned containers. Chapter 616 (General and Miscellaneous Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

616.902 Use of consumer-owned containers.

(1) As used in this section: (a) “Beverage” means a liquid for drinking, including water. (b) “Food” means a raw, cooked or processed edible substance, ice, beverage or ingredient used or intended for use or for sale in whole or in part for human consumption, or chewing gum. (c) “Restaurant” has the meaning given that term in ORS 624.010.

(2) The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt rules allowing for a restaurant to allow a consumer to fill a consumer-owned container with food. [2023 c.74 §1] Note: 616.902 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 616 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
