# Application of federal law to determination of taxable income (ORS 316.024)

## At a glance

ORS 316.024 — Application of federal law to determination of taxable income. Chapter 316 (Chapter 316), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

316.024 Application of federal law to determination of taxable income. Section 243 of the Tax Reform Act of 1986 (P.L. 99-514) does not apply for purposes of determining taxable income under this chapter. [1987 c.293 §12a; 2003 c.46 §36] 316.025 [1953 c.304 §5; repealed by 1957 c.632 §1 (314.075 and 314.080 enacted in lieu of 316.025, 316.030, 317.015 and 317.020)]
