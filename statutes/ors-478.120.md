# Inclusion of forestland in district (ORS 478.120)

## At a glance

ORS 478.120 — Inclusion of forestland in district. Chapter 478 (Chapter 478), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

478.120 Inclusion of forestland in district. The authority to include forestland within a rural fire protection district pursuant to ORS 478.010 (2)(b) applies to forestland within the exterior boundaries of an existing district. [1973 c.337 §3; 2025 c.581 §37]
