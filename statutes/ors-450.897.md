# Bancroft Bonding Act applicable (ORS 450.897)

## At a glance

ORS 450.897 — Bancroft Bonding Act applicable. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.897 Bancroft Bonding Act applicable. Sanitary authorities organized under ORS chapter 450 that are in existence on November 1, 1981, or thereafter, are authorized to use the bonding provisions of ORS 223.205 to 223.316. [1981 c.351 §1; 1991 c.902 §113; 1997 c.249 §151] Note: 450.897 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 450 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
