# County clerk to record instruments affecting realty for state boards and commissions (ORS 182.060)

## At a glance

ORS 182.060 — County clerk to record instruments affecting realty for state boards and commissions. Chapter 182 (State Administrative Agencies Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

182.060 County clerk to record instruments affecting realty for state boards and commissions. When requested by a state board or commission, the county clerk shall record in the office of the clerk any instrument affecting real property and shall return to the board or commission a receipt for the instrument showing the legal charge for the recording of the instrument. [Amended by 1999 c.654 §12] 182.065 [1977 c.739 §1; 1979 c.593 §5; renumbered 183.025] 182.070 [1953 c.527 §2; 1961 c.167 §21; 1979 c.215 §1; repealed by 1995 c.69 §12]
