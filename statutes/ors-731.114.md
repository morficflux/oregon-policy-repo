# “Limited benefit coverage” (ORS 731.114)

## At a glance

ORS 731.114 — “Limited benefit coverage”. Chapter 731 (Administration and General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

731.114 “Limited benefit coverage.” “Limited benefit coverage” means:

(1) Health insurance that provides: (a) Coverage for accident only, specific disease or condition only, credit or disability income; (b) Dental only coverage; or (c) Vision only coverage; and

(2) Independent, noncoordinated, hospital-only indemnity insurance or other fixed indemnity insurance. [2016 c.11 §2] Note: 731.114 was added to and made a part of the Insurance Code by legislative action but was not added to ORS chapter 731 or any series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
