# Application of ORS 354.605 to (ORS 354.615)

## At a glance

ORS 354.615 — Application of ORS 354.605 to. Chapter 354 (Chapter 354), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

354.615 Application of ORS 354.605 to 354.715. Except as provided in ORS 354.690 (5), nothing in ORS 354.605 to 354.715 shall apply to the construction or operation of community antenna systems or the redistribution of any signals, writings, images, sounds or intelligence of any nature by cable. [1975 c.286 §2(2); 1979 c.108 §8; 1985 c.445 §3]
