## Full text

604.076 Authority to issue citations.

(1) In addition to the authority set forth in ORS 604.015, in the enforcement of this chapter, the Director of Agriculture has the authority to issue and serve citations to any person for violation of any provision of ORS 604.071.

(2) The director may delegate the authority to issue citations to investigative officers and brand inspection supervisors.

(3) A citation shall contain the information and substantially conform to the requirements for a citation under ORS chapter 153.
