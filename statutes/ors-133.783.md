# Authority of arresting officer to command assistance (ORS 133.783)

## At a glance

ORS 133.783 — Authority of arresting officer to command assistance. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.783 Authority of arresting officer to command assistance. Every such officer or other person empowered to make the arrest shall have the same authority in arresting the accused to command assistance therein as sheriffs and other officers have by law in the execution of any criminal process directed to them, with the like penalties against those who refuse their assistance. [Formerly 147.090]
