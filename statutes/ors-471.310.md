# Cities, counties as licensees (Application for License) (ORS 471.310)

## At a glance

ORS 471.310 — Cities, counties as licensees (Application for License). Chapter 471 (Alcoholic Liquors Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

471.310 Cities, counties as licensees. Any city or county may, without further charter authority, become a licensee under this chapter. [Amended by 1995 c.301 §61; 1999 c.351 §49; 2021 c.596 §4] (Application for License)
