# Service of petition and order (ORS 109.801)

## At a glance

ORS 109.801 — Service of petition and order. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.801 Service of petition and order. Except as otherwise provided in ORS 109.807, the petition and order for enforcement of a child custody determination must be served by the petitioner, by any method authorized for service of process within this state, upon the respondent and any person who has physical custody of the child. [1999 c.649 §31] Note: See note under 109.701.
