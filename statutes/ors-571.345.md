# Remediation for unintentional violation; rules (ORS 571.345)

## At a glance

## Full text

571.345 Remediation for unintentional violation; rules. The State Department of Agriculture may by rule or order establish a process providing for the remediation of a violation of ORS 571.330 or 571.333 that is committed by a grower or handler licensed under ORS 571.281 and is not committed intentionally. [2018 c.116 §9; 2021 c.542 §33] Note: See note under 571.260.
