# Duties of State Forester INSTITUTIONAL INDUSTRIES AND COMMODITIES (ORS 421.298)

## At a glance

ORS 421.298 — Duties of State Forester INSTITUTIONAL INDUSTRIES AND COMMODITIES. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.298 Duties of State Forester. The State Forester shall make reasonable efforts to use local available crews within Oregon before calling on fire suppression units from other states. [1991 c.302 §4] INSTITUTIONAL INDUSTRIES AND COMMODITIES
