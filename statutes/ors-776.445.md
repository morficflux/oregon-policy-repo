# Liability of certain persons for licensee’s compensation (ORS 776.445)

## At a glance

ORS 776.445 — Liability of certain persons for licensee’s compensation. Chapter 776 (Maritime Pilots and Pilotage), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

776.445 Liability of certain persons for licensee’s compensation. In addition to the lien of the licensee upon the vessel for any sum due for piloting, the master, owner and consignee or agent are jointly and severally liable to the licensee therefor. [1957 c.448 §21; 1993 c.796 §15] 776.450 [Repealed by 1957 c.448 §27]
