# Providing basic information to employees (ORS 654.780)

## At a glance

ORS 654.780 — Providing basic information to employees. Chapter 654 (Chapter 654), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

654.780 Providing basic information to employees. Agriculture employers shall give all employees a copy of the basic information developed by the Department of Consumer and Business Services for the purpose of informing employees pursuant to ORS 654.770. The information shall be provided in the employee’s own language if the department has produced it in that language. The information shall be provided to persons at the time of hire. [1987 c.832 §5; 2005 c.22 §461] 654.990 [Amended by 1959 c.516 §5; 1961 c.485 §28; 1967 c.150 §1; repealed by 1973 c.833 §37 (654.991 enacted in lieu of 654.990)]
