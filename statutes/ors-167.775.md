# Local regulation of vending machines (ORS 167.775)

## At a glance

ORS 167.775 — Local regulation of vending machines. Chapter 167 (Chapter 167), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

167.775 Local regulation of vending machines. Cities and counties by ordinance or resolution may not regulate vending machines that dispense tobacco products or inhalant delivery systems and that are in any manner accessible to persons under 21 years of age. [Formerly 167.404]
