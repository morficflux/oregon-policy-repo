# Penalty for presenting nonstandard instruments for recording (ORS 205.327)

## At a glance

ORS 205.327 — Penalty for presenting nonstandard instruments for recording. Chapter 205 (Chapter 205), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

205.327 Penalty for presenting nonstandard instruments for recording. When an instrument required or permitted by law to be recorded is presented to a county clerk for recording, if the instrument does not comply with the requirements of ORS 205.232 or 205.234, the county clerk shall record the instrument, but shall charge and collect in advance a penalty of $20. The penalty authorized and collected under this section is in addition to and not in lieu of the fees charged under ORS 205.320 for recording the instrument.
