## Full text

247.298 Extension of time for updates under ORS 247.292. If there are fewer than 22 days between the date of an election and the registration deadline for the next succeeding election, the county clerk may update registrations as required under ORS 247.292 after the next succeeding election. [1993 c.713 §17e; 1999 c.410 §13] 247.300 [1957 c.608 §44; 1961 c.115 §2; repealed by 1975 c.678 §25]
