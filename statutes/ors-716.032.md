# Investigation and ruling on application (ORS 716.032)

## At a glance

## Full text

716.032 Investigation and ruling on application. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services shall investigate and rule on the application for authority to organize in the manner specified for institutions in ORS 707.080, 707.090 and 707.145. [1973 c.797 §347; 1977 c.135 §30; 1997 c.631 §333] 716.036 [1973 c.797 §348; repealed by 1997 c.631 §567]
