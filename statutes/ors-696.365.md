# City or county business license tax (ORS 696.365)

## At a glance

ORS 696.365 — City or county business license tax. Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.365 City or county business license tax.

(1) A city or county may not impose a business license tax on or collect a business license tax from an individual licensed as a real estate broker or a timeshare sales agent who engages in professional real estate activity only as an agent of a managing principal broker.

(2) As used in this section, “business license tax” has the meaning given that term in ORS 701.015. [1987 c.581 §4; 2001 c.300 §78; 2007 c.319 §12; 2025 c.39 §17; 2025 c.389 §24] Note: 696.365 was added to and made a part of ORS 696.010 to 696.495 but was not added to any smaller series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
