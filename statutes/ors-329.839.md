# School District Collaboration Grant Account (Statewide Education Plans) (ORS 329.839)

## At a glance

ORS 329.839 — School District Collaboration Grant Account (Statewide Education Plans). Chapter 329 (Chapter 329), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

329.839 School District Collaboration Grant Account. The School District Collaboration Grant Account is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the School District Collaboration Grant Account shall be credited to the account. Moneys in the School District Collaboration Grant Account are continuously appropriated to the Department of Education for the School District Collaboration Grant Program established by ORS 329.838.
