# Agreements between primary sponsor and United States (ORS 285B.509)

## At a glance

ORS 285B.509 — Agreements between primary sponsor and United States. Chapter 285B (Chapter 285B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285B.509 Agreements between primary sponsor and United States. The primary sponsors of projects listed in ORS 285B.503 are authorized to enter into agreements with agencies of the United States for the project and, notwithstanding any other provision of law, may each agree to be bound by any requirement imposed by an Act of the United States Congress as a condition of federal participation in the project. [1997 c.644 §11] Note: See note under 285B.500.
