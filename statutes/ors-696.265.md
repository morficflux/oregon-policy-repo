# Recognition of nonresident real estate licensee; rules (Miscellaneous) (ORS 696.265)

## At a glance

## Full text

696.265 Recognition of nonresident real estate licensee; rules. Notwithstanding ORS 696.040 to 696.232 and 696.255, the Real Estate Commissioner may prescribe by rule the terms and conditions for license recognition of a nonresident real estate broker or salesperson and for reciprocity agreements with other states and countries, including but not limited to application procedures, license qualifications, license maintenance, limitations on activities and license renewal requirements. [1989 c.532 §4; 1999 c.470 §3; 2001 c.300 §24] Note: 696.265 was added to and made a part of 696.010 to 696.495 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. (Miscellaneous)
