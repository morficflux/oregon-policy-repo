# Retention of property to answer order of court (ORS 133.495)

## At a glance

ORS 133.495 — Retention of property to answer order of court. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.495 Retention of property to answer order of court. The proceeds of the sale mentioned in ORS 133.485 and other property seized shall be retained by liens, if not released on bond, to answer any order that may be entered by the court upon the trial of the person arrested. [Formerly 142.130] 133.510 [Repealed by 1965 c.508 §8]
