# Establishing reserves for variable life insurance and annuity policies (ORS 733.115)

## At a glance

ORS 733.115 — Establishing reserves for variable life insurance and annuity policies. Chapter 733 (Chapter 733), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

733.115 Establishing reserves for variable life insurance and annuity policies. Reserves for variable life insurance and annuity policies shall be established in accordance with actuarial procedures that recognize the variable nature of the benefits provided, any mortality guarantees, and the valuation requirements of the Standard Valuation Law.
