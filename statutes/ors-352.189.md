# Political or sectarian tests prohibited in appointment of faculty or employees (ORS 352.189)

## At a glance

## Full text

352.189 Political or sectarian tests prohibited in appointment of faculty or employees. A political or sectarian test may not be allowed or applied in the appointment of faculty and other employees of a public university listed in ORS 352.002.
