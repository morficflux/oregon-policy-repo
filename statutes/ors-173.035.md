# Submission of statements to Legislative Assembly (ORS 173.035)

## Full text

173.035 Submission of statements to Legislative Assembly. The Legislative Fiscal Officer and Legislative Revenue Officer shall submit the statement prepared under ORS 173.025 or 173.029 to the Legislative Assembly at a time set by the rules of the house where the measure was introduced. [1977 c.414 §2; 1987 c.854 §3; 2016 c.117 §3] 173.040 [Repealed by 1953 c.492 §16]
