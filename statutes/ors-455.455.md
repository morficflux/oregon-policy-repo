# Building inspection and plan review; license required; exception (ORS 455.455)

## At a glance

ORS 455.455 — Building inspection and plan review; license required; exception. Chapter 455 (Building Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

455.455 Building inspection and plan review; license required; exception. (1)(a) A person may not employ an individual to perform specialty code inspections in any specialty area unless the individual has a license issued in that specialty area under ORS 455.457. (b) A person may not engage in specialty code inspections without having a license issued under ORS 455.457 in the specialty area for which the inspection is provided. (c) A person may not employ an individual to perform plan reviews unless the individual has a license issued under ORS 455.457. (d) A person may not engage in reviewing plans without having a license issued under ORS 455.457.

(2) The requirements in subsection

(1) of this section do not apply to a person who is an employee of the state or of a municipality. [1999 c.1045 §2] Note: 455.455 to 455.465, 455.467 and 455.469 to 455.477 were added to and made a part of ORS chapter 455 by legislative action but were not added to any smaller series therein.
