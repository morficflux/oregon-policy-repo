# Construction of ORS (ORS 222.310)

## At a glance

ORS 222.310 — Construction of ORS. Chapter 222 (Chapter 222), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

222.310 Construction of ORS 222.210 to 222.310; substantial compliance sufficient. ORS 222.210 to 222.310 shall be construed liberally, and substantial compliance with the provisions of those sections shall be sufficient.
