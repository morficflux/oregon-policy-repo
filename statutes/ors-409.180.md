# Consolidation of internal audit units (ORS 409.180)

## At a glance

ORS 409.180 — Consolidation of internal audit units. Chapter 409 (Department of Human Services), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

409.180 Consolidation of internal audit units. The Director of Human Services may consolidate the internal audits units within the Department of Human Services into an office of audits within the director’s office, in order to achieve a higher level of independence and economy of management. [1991 c.321 §1] 409.182 [2011 c.293 §1; repealed by 2015 c.70 §2]
