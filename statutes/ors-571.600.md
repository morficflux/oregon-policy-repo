# Definitions for ORS (ORS 571.600)

## At a glance

ORS 571.600 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 571 (Chapter 571), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

571.600 Definitions for ORS 571.605 to 571.620. As used in ORS 571.605 to 571.620:

(1) “Business development project” has the meaning given that term in ORS 285B.050.

(2) “Western juniper” means trees of the species Juniperus occidentalis. [2015 c.636 §1] Note: 571.600 to 571.625 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 571 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
