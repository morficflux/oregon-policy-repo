## Full text

215.412 Adoption of hearing procedure and rules.

(1) The governing body of a county by ordinance or order shall adopt one or more procedures for the conduct of hearings.

(2) The governing body of a county by ordinance or order shall adopt rules stating that all decisions made by the governing body will be based on factual information, including adopted comprehensive plans and land use regulations. [1973 c.552 §14; 1977 c.766 §11; 1997 c.452 §2] 215.415 [1953 c.662 §5; repealed by 1971 c.13 §1]
