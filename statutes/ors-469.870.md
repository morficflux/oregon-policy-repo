# Application of ORS (ORS 469.870)

## At a glance

ORS 469.870 — Application of ORS. Chapter 469 (Chapter 469), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469.870 Application of ORS 469.865, 469.870 and 469.900

(1) to electric utility. ORS 469.865, 469.900

(1) and this section shall not apply to an electric utility if the Public Utility Commission determines that its existing commercial energy conservation services program meets or exceeds the requirements of those sections. [1981 c.708 §4]
