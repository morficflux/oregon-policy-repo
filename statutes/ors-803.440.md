# Failure to submit declaration of weight; penalty (ORS 803.440)

## At a glance

ORS 803.440 — Failure to submit declaration of weight; penalty. Chapter 803 (Chapter 803), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

803.440 Failure to submit declaration of weight; penalty.

(1) A person commits the offense of failure to submit a declaration of weight if the person does not submit a declaration of weight for a vehicle described in this subsection when the person applies for registration of the vehicle or has the vehicle registered in the person’s name and the vehicle has been altered or reconstructed. This section applies to the following vehicles: (a) Any vehicle required to establish registration weight under ORS 803.430. (b) Any commercial bus. (c) Any vehicle registered as a farm vehicle under ORS 805.300. (d) Any vehicle registered under the proportional registration provisions of ORS 826.009 or 826.011.

(2) The offense described in this section, failure to submit a declaration of weight, is a Class D traffic violation. [1983 c.338 §232; 1989 c.43 §24; 1989 c.723 §11; 1993 c.751 §26]
