# Continuing education (ORS 688.565)

## At a glance

ORS 688.565 — Continuing education. Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.565 Continuing education.

(1) The Board of Medical Imaging shall approve programs of continuing education in medical imaging modalities and for limited X-ray machine operators to meet the requirements of ORS 688.505.

(2) The board may seek volunteers from the various medical imaging modalities to review and assist in the approval of continuing education credits for their respective medical imaging modalities.

(3) Continuing education programs for limited X-ray machine operators shall be reviewed by a radiographer. [1977 c.534 §13; 2005 c.151 §5; 2009 c.833 §19] 688.575 [1977 c.534 §§16,17; 1979 c.449 §2; repealed by 1981 c.603 §8]
