# Tax levy to finance programs under ORS (ORS 450.977)

## At a glance

ORS 450.977 — Tax levy to finance programs under ORS. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.977 Tax levy to finance programs under ORS 450.947 to 450.977. Expenses incurred by a district in establishing programs or providing benefits authorized by ORS 450.947 to 450.977 are expenses for which an authority may levy taxes as provided in ORS 450.885. [1973 c.399 §7] (Miscellaneous)
