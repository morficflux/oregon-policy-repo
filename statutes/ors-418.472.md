# Siting of shelter-care home INDEPENDENT RESIDENCE FACILITIES (ORS 418.472)

## At a glance

ORS 418.472 — Siting of shelter-care home INDEPENDENT RESIDENCE FACILITIES. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.472 Siting of shelter-care home. The governing body of a county or its designee in a county with a population of less than 400,000 may allow the operation of a shelter-care home, as defined in ORS 418.470, upon a lot or parcel in any zone, including an exclusive farm use or forest use zone, if the shelter-care home is an existing use on that lot or parcel on September 20, 1985. [1985 c.791 §2] INDEPENDENT RESIDENCE FACILITIES
