# “Stocks” defined for ORS 757.400 to (ORS 757.400)

## At a glance

ORS 757.400 — “Stocks” defined for ORS 757.400 to. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.400 “Stocks” defined for ORS 757.400 to 757.463. As used in ORS 757.400 to 757.463, “stocks” means stocks, stock certificates or other evidence of interest or ownership.
