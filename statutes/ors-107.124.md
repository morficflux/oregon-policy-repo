# Effect of revocation (ORS 107.124)

## At a glance

ORS 107.124 — Effect of revocation. Chapter 107 (Chapter 107), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

107.124 Effect of revocation. If a designation of beneficiary is revoked under ORS 107.121, the designation of beneficiary must be given effect as if the spouse or the relative of the spouse had predeceased the principal. [2005 c.285 §4] Note: See note under 107.118. 107.125 [1965 c.386 §3; repealed by 1971 c.280 §28] 107.126 [1971 c.280 §15; 1993 c.716 §4; 1993 c.763 §7; 1997 c.801 §148; repealed by 2003 c.576 §580]
