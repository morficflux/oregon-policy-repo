# Registration requirement (ORS 697.015)

## At a glance

ORS 697.015 — Registration requirement. Chapter 697 (Chapter 697), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

697.015 Registration requirement. A person shall not operate as a collection agency in this state unless the person registers with the Department of Consumer and Business Services under ORS 697.031 and maintains the registration in accordance with that section.
