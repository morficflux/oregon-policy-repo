# Juvenile court’s retention of authority over parent (ORS 419C.346)

## At a glance

ORS 419C.346 — Juvenile court’s retention of authority over parent. Chapter 419C (Chapter 419C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419C.346 Juvenile court’s retention of authority over parent. If the juvenile court waives a youth to another court under ORS 419C.349 (1)(b) or 419C.370 for disposition as an adult, the juvenile court nevertheless may retain jurisdiction over the youth’s parents or guardians under ORS 419C.570. However, if the court enters an order of waiver under ORS 419C.349 (1)(a) or 419C.364, jurisdiction over the parents or guardians under ORS 419C.570 shall terminate. [1993 c.33 §212; 1993 c.546 §77; 2019 c.634 §16]
