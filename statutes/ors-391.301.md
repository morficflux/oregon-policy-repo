# Ratification of compact (ORS 391.301)

## At a glance

ORS 391.301 — Ratification of compact. Chapter 391 (Chapter 391), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

391.301 Ratification of compact. The Legislative Assembly of the State of Oregon hereby adopts and ratifies the Columbia River Light Rail Transit Compact set forth in ORS 391.306, and the provisions of the compact are hereby declared to be the law of this state upon such compact becoming effective as provided in Article XXII of the compact. [1996 c.13 §1] 391.305 [1995 s.s. c.3 §33; repealed by 1996 c.13 §4]
