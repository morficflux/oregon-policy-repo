## Full text

105.905 Wind energy easement appurtenant; termination.

(1) A wind energy easement shall be appurtenant to and run with the real property benefited and burdened by the easement.

(2) A wind energy easement shall terminate: (a) Upon occurrence of the conditions stated in the creating instrument; (b) By judgment of a court based upon abandonment or changed conditions; or (c) At any time by agreement of all the owners of the benefited and burdened property. [1981 c.590 §2; 2003 c.576 §371]
