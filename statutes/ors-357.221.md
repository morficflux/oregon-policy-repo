# District formation; petition requirements (ORS 357.221)

## At a glance

ORS 357.221 — District formation; petition requirements. Chapter 357 (Chapter 357), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

357.221 District formation; petition requirements.

(1) A library district may be created as provided in ORS 198.705 to 198.955 and 357.216 to 357.286.

(2) In addition to other required matters, a petition for formation of a district shall state the method of election of the board of the proposed district from among the methods described in ORS 357.241. [1981 c.226 §2]
