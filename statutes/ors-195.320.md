## At a glance

## Full text

195.320 Ombudsman.

(1) The Governor shall appoint an individual to serve, at the pleasure of the Governor, as the Compensation and Conservation Ombudsman.

(2) The ombudsman must be an individual of recognized judgment, objectivity and integrity who is qualified by training and experience to: (a) Analyze problems of land use planning, real property law and real property valuation; and (b) Facilitate resolution of complex disputes. [2007 c.424 §17]
