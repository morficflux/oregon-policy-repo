# Pre-1953 Public Employees Retirement System abolished (ORS 237.950)

## At a glance

ORS 237.950 — Pre-1953 Public Employees Retirement System abolished. Chapter 237 (Chapter 237), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

237.950 Pre-1953 Public Employees Retirement System abolished. The Public Employees Retirement System of the State of Oregon, established by chapter 401, Oregon Laws 1945, as amended, hereby is abolished, subject to the provisions of ORS 237.950 to 237.980. [1953 c.180 §2]
