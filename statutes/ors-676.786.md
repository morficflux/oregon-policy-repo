# Health Licensing Office rules (ORS 676.786)

## At a glance

ORS 676.786 — Health Licensing Office rules. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.786 Health Licensing Office rules. The Health Licensing Office shall adopt rules with the advice of the State Board of Sign Language Interpreters to carry out the office’s duties under ORS 676.750 to 676.789. [2023 c.414 §13; 2025 c.238 §9]
