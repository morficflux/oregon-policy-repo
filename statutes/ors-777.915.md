# Definitions for ORS (ORS 777.915)

## At a glance

ORS 777.915 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 777 (Chapter 777), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

777.915 Definitions for ORS 777.915 to 777.953. As used in ORS 777.915 to 777.953, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “Board” means the board of commissioners of the Oregon International Port of Coos Bay.

(2) “Coos Bay rail link” means a railroad right-of-way, including spur lines, appurtenances, facilities and related property within the railroad right-of-way, that connects the station of Danebo in Lane County with the station of Coquille in Coos County.

(3) “Port” means the Oregon International Port of Coos Bay. [1987 c.565 §2; 2011 c.437 §1]
