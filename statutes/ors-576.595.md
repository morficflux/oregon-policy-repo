# Sales are in commercial channels MEDIATION OF MARKETING DISPUTES (ORS 576.595)

## At a glance

ORS 576.595 — Sales are in commercial channels MEDIATION OF MARKETING DISPUTES. Chapter 576 (Agricultural Marketing Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

576.595 Sales are in commercial channels. Any sale of a commodity by a grower or producer is a sale in commercial channels for the purposes of ORS 576.051 to 576.455 and 576.991 (2). [1965 c.211 §1; 2003 c.604 §49; 2011 c.597 §236] MEDIATION OF MARKETING DISPUTES
