# Amount of penalty; rules (ORS 536.910)

## At a glance

## Full text

536.910 Amount of penalty; rules. After public hearing, the Water Resources Commission by rule shall adopt a schedule establishing the civil penalty that may be imposed under ORS 536.900 and the time allowed to correct each violation. However, the civil penalty may not exceed $5,000 for each violation. [1989 c.618 §3] Note: See note under 536.900.
