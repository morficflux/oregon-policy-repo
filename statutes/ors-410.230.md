# Expenditure of local funds not required (Type A Agencies) (ORS 410.230)

## At a glance

ORS 410.230 — Expenditure of local funds not required (Type A Agencies). Chapter 410 (Chapter 410), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

410.230 Expenditure of local funds not required. Nothing in ORS 409.010 and 410.040 to 410.300 requires an area agency or local governmental unit to expend local funds for the purpose of maintaining or expanding services to elderly persons and persons with disabilities. [1981 c.784 §37; 1989 c.224 §77; 2005 c.22 §275; 2007 c.70 §171; 2011 c.720 §86] (Type A Agencies)
