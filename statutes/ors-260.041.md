# Principal campaign committee (ORS 260.041)

## At a glance

ORS 260.041 — Principal campaign committee. Chapter 260 (Chapter 260), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

260.041 Principal campaign committee.

(1) Notwithstanding ORS 260.005

(18) and except as provided in ORS 260.043, a candidate shall designate a political committee as the candidate’s principal campaign committee. A candidate may designate only one political committee as the candidate’s principal campaign committee.

(2) A political committee may not be designated as the principal campaign committee of more than one candidate. [1979 c.190 §343; 1999 c.999 §7; 2005 c.809 §23; 2009 c.818 §3]
