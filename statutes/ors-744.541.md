# Adjustment of claim under policy issued by unauthorized insurer (ORS 744.541)

## At a glance

## Full text

744.541 Adjustment of claim under policy issued by unauthorized insurer. An adjuster may adjust a loss claimed under an insurance policy issued by an unauthorized insurer other than a surplus line insurer. The adjuster shall notify the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services thereof not later than the 20th day after adjusting the loss.
