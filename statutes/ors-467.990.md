# Penalties (ORS 467.990)

## At a glance

ORS 467.990 — Penalties. Chapter 467 (Chapter 467), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

467.990 Penalties. Violation of any provision of this chapter or rules or orders made under the provisions of this chapter is a Class B misdemeanor. Each day of violation shall be considered a separate offense. [1971 c.452 §6; 1973 c.835 §161] _______________
