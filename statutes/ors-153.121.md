# Appeal TRAFFIC OFFENSES (ORS 153.121)

## At a glance

ORS 153.121 — Appeal TRAFFIC OFFENSES. Chapter 153 (Chapter 153), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

153.121 Appeal. An appeal from a judgment in a violation proceeding may be taken by either party as follows:

(1) From a proceeding in justice court or municipal court, as provided in ORS 138.057 for appeals of violations.

(2) From a proceeding in circuit court, as provided in ORS chapter 19, except that the standard of review is the same as for an appeal from a judgment in a proceeding involving a misdemeanor or felony.
