# Definitions for ORS (ORS 407.480)

## At a glance

ORS 407.480 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 407 (Chapter 407), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

407.480 Definitions for ORS 407.480 to 407.490. As used in ORS 407.480 to 407.490:

(1) “Insurance” means any mortgage guarantee or mortgage insurance against financial loss by reason of: (a) Collection activity; (b) Foreclosure; or (c) Foreclosure by the Department of Veterans’ Affairs under ORS 407.135.

(2) “Mortgage” includes a trust deed. [1989 c.677 §2; 2005 c.625 §46]
