# Defenses for hindering or compounding limited (ORS 162.345)

## At a glance

ORS 162.345 — Defenses for hindering or compounding limited. Chapter 162 (Chapter 162), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

162.345 Defenses for hindering or compounding limited. It is no defense to a prosecution for hindering prosecution or compounding that the principal offender is not apprehended, prosecuted, convicted or punished. [1971 c.743 §209] 162.350 [Amended by 1955 c.660 §22; repealed by 1961 c.649 §9]
