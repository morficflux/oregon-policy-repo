# Disposition of fees; administrative expenses; other uses (ORS 465.127)

## At a glance

ORS 465.127 — Disposition of fees; administrative expenses; other uses. Chapter 465 (Chapter 465), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

465.127 Disposition of fees; administrative expenses; other uses. All moneys received by the Department of Revenue under ORS 465.101 to 465.131 shall be deposited in the State Treasury and credited to a suspense account established under ORS 293.445. After payment of administration expenses incurred by the department in the administration of ORS 465.101 to 465.131 and of refunds or credits arising from erroneous overpayments, the balance of the money shall be credited to the appropriate accounts as approved by the Legislative Assembly to carry out the state’s oil, hazardous material and hazardous substance emergency response program as it relates to the maintenance, operation and use of the public highways, roads, streets and roadside rest areas in this state as allowed by section 3a, Article IX of the Oregon Constitution. [1989 c.833 §147; 1989 c.935 §4; 1993 c.707 §1] 465.130 [Repealed by 1989 c.846 §15]
