# Judicial review of penalties (ORS 443.815)

## At a glance

ORS 443.815 — Judicial review of penalties. Chapter 443 (Chapter 443), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

443.815 Judicial review of penalties. Judicial review of civil penalties imposed under ORS 443.790 shall be as provided under ORS 183.480, except that the court may, in its discretion, reduce the amount of the penalty. [1987 c.431 §8]
