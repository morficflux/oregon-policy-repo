# Legislative report POWERS AND DUTIES (ORS 334.115)

## At a glance

## Full text

334.115 Legislative report. Each pilot education service district shall biennially report to the interim legislative committees relating to education on the governance structure of the board of the pilot education service district prior to October 1 of each even-numbered year. [2005 c.828 §13; 2007 c.589 §4] Note: See note under 334.105. 334.120 [Amended by 1957 c.678 §10; 1963 c.544 §35; 1965 c.100 §183; renumbered 334.225] POWERS AND DUTIES
