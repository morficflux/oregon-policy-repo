# Rule against perpetuities; inapplicability CREATION OF UNIT OWNERSHIP (ORS 100.025)

## At a glance

ORS 100.025 — Rule against perpetuities; inapplicability CREATION OF UNIT OWNERSHIP. Chapter 100 (Chapter 100), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

100.025 Rule against perpetuities; inapplicability. The rule against perpetuities may not be applied to defeat any provisions of a declaration, supplemental declaration, bylaw or rule for a condominium adopted under ORS 100.005 to 100.627. [Formerly 94.016] CREATION OF UNIT OWNERSHIP
