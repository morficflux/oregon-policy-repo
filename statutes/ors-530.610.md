# Levy of taxes prohibited (ORS 530.610)

## At a glance

ORS 530.610 — Levy of taxes prohibited. Chapter 530 (Chapter 530), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

530.610 Levy of taxes prohibited. A community forest authority created under ORS 530.606 does not have the power or authority to levy taxes. [2005 c.500 §6] Note: See note under 530.600.
