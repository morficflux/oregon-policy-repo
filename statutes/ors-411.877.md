# Definitions for ORS 411.877 to (ORS 411.877)

## At a glance

ORS 411.877 — Definitions for ORS 411.877 to. Chapter 411 (Chapter 411), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

411.877 Definitions for ORS 411.877 to 411.896. As used in ORS 411.877 to 411.896:

(1) “Job opportunities and basic skills program” means the program described in ORS 412.009.

(2) “JOBS Plus” or “program” means the JOBS Plus Program established in ORS 411.878.

(3) “Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program” has the meaning given that term in ORS 411.806. [1995 c.561 §2 and 1995 c.816 §15; 2009 c.21 §47; 2009 c.599 §9a; 2019 c.602 §8; 2025 c.405 §9] Note: Chapter 561, Oregon Laws 1995, and sections 14 to 33, chapter 816, Oregon Laws 1995, were enacted by the Legislative Assembly as duplicate JOBS Plus Programs. Legislative Counsel has compiled the relevant sections of both chapters as ORS 316.680, 411.877 to 411.896 and 657.925. Legislative Counsel has not substituted ORS section references for provisions in chapter 816, Oregon Laws 1995, that technically are not related to the JOBS Plus Program.
