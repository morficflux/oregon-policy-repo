# Rules PENALTIES (ORS 679.730)

## At a glance

ORS 679.730 — Rules PENALTIES. Chapter 679 (Chapter 679), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

679.730 Rules. The Health Licensing Office may adopt rules to carry out ORS 679.700 to 679.720. [2025 c.199 §7] Note: 679.730 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 679 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 679.990 [Repealed by 1957 c.552 §6 (679.991 enacted in lieu of 679.990)] PENALTIES
