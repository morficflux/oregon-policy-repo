# Duty to report prohibited conduct (ORS 675.815)

## At a glance

ORS 675.815 — Duty to report prohibited conduct. Chapter 675 (Chapter 675), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

675.815 Duty to report prohibited conduct. Unless state or federal laws relating to confidentiality or the protection of health information prohibit disclosure, a licensee who has reasonable cause to believe that a licensee of another board has engaged in prohibited conduct shall report the prohibited conduct as defined in ORS 676.150 in the manner provided in ORS 676.150. [2009 c.536 §6]
