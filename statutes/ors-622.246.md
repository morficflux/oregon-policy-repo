# Purpose of water quality monitoring and analysis (ORS 622.246)

## At a glance

ORS 622.246 — Purpose of water quality monitoring and analysis. Chapter 622 (Chapter 622), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

622.246 Purpose of water quality monitoring and analysis. The Legislative Assembly finds and declares that it is the purpose of the pilot program established by ORS 622.243 to expand on the valuable work completed through the pilot project by the State Department of Agriculture authorized under section 7, chapter 814, Oregon Laws 2015, to increase the frequency of water quality monitoring and analysis related to the closure and opening of shellfish harvesting in Tillamook Bay. [2017 c.711 §2] Note: See note under 622.243.
