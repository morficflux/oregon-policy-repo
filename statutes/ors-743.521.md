# Leased workers; offering group health insurance (ORS 743.521)

## At a glance

ORS 743.521 — Leased workers; offering group health insurance. Chapter 743 (Chapter 743), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743.521 Leased workers; offering group health insurance.

(1) A leasing company may offer group health insurance to its leased workers. If the leasing company does not offer group health insurance to its leased workers, the client employer may offer group health insurance to the leased workers.

(2) If a leasing company offers group health insurance to its leased workers, the leasing company shall offer group health insurance to all its leased workers in the same manner. [Formerly 743.533] Note: The amendments to 743.521 by section 9, chapter 78, Oregon Laws 2025, become operative July 1, 2027. See section 23, chapter 78, Oregon Laws 2025. The text that is operative on and after July 1, 2027, is set forth for the user’s convenience. 743.521. (1)(a) A professional employer organization may offer group health insurance to its clients’ covered employees. (b) If the professional employer organization does not offer group health insurance to its clients’ covered employees, the client employer may offer group health insurance to the covered employees.

(2) If a professional employer organization offers group health insurance to any of its clients’ covered employees, the professional employer organization shall offer group health insurance to all its clients’ covered employees in the same manner.
