# Creation of reserves (Other Funds) (ORS 656.640)

## At a glance

ORS 656.640 — Creation of reserves (Other Funds). Chapter 656 (Chapter 656), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

656.640 Creation of reserves. The State Accident Insurance Fund Corporation may set aside such other reserves within the Industrial Accident Fund as are deemed necessary. [Formerly 656.468; 1981 c.854 §46] (Other Funds)
