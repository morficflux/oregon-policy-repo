# Records of notification of approaching expiration of registration or license not required (ORS 802.210)

## At a glance

ORS 802.210 — Records of notification of approaching expiration of registration or license not required. Chapter 802 (Chapter 802), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

802.210 Records of notification of approaching expiration of registration or license not required. The Department of Transportation is not required to maintain records on any of the following:

(1) The preparation and notification required on approaching expiration of registration under ORS 803.450.

(2) The preparation and notification required on approaching expiration of driver license or driver permit under ORS 807.140. [1983 c.338 §125; 1985 c.16 §35; 2015 c.404 §4; 2019 c.312 §21]
