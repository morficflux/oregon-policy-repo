# Effect of repeal of validating or curative Act (ORS 174.070)

## At a glance

ORS 174.070 — Effect of repeal of validating or curative Act. Chapter 174 (Construction of Statutes; General Definitions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

174.070 Effect of repeal of validating or curative Act. The repeal of a validating or curative Act shall not affect any validation or cure theretofore accomplished.
