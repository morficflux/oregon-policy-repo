# Department to make dependent care payments directly to providers (ORS 411.122)

## At a glance

ORS 411.122 — Department to make dependent care payments directly to providers. Chapter 411 (Chapter 411), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

411.122 Department to make dependent care payments directly to providers. The Department of Human Services shall provide dependent care payments directly to dependent care providers for eligible families. [1989 c.1047 §1; 1995 c.278 §47] Note: 411.122 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 411 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 411.125 [1969 c.597 §228b; 1971 c.779 §71; 1981 c.545 §8; 1991 c.10 §1; 1999 c.422 §1; 2001 c.900 §87; renumbered 411.075 in 2009]
