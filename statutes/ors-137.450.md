# Enforcement of money judgment in criminal action (ORS 137.450)

## At a glance

ORS 137.450 — Enforcement of money judgment in criminal action. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.450 Enforcement of money judgment in criminal action. A judgment against the defendant or complainant in a criminal action, so far as it requires the payment of a fine, fee, assessment, costs and disbursements of the action or restitution, may be enforced as a judgment in a civil action. [Amended by 1973 c.836 §269; 1987 c.709 §1]
