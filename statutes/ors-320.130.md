## Full text

320.130 Law enforcement officers to enforce tax and assist department. The state police, sheriffs, constables, police and other law enforcement officers within the State of Oregon shall enforce all provisions of ORS 320.005 to 320.150 and shall assist the Department of Revenue. [Amended by 2005 c.94 §96]
