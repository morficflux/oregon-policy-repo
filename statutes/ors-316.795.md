## Full text

316.795 Exemption for payments to tenants of manufactured dwelling parks upon termination of rental agreement. Amounts received by a taxpayer under ORS 90.645

(1) are exempt from the taxes imposed by this chapter. [2007 c.906 §12] (First-time Home Buyer Savings Accounts)
