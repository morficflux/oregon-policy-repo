# Adjuster license required (ORS 744.505)

## At a glance

ORS 744.505 — Adjuster license required. Chapter 744 (Chapter 744), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

744.505 Adjuster license required. Except as provided in ORS 744.515, a person may not engage in business as an adjuster unless the person has obtained a license to engage in business as an adjuster under ORS 744.521. [Formerly 736.485; 1983 c.76 §10; 1987 c.774 §139; 1989 c.413 §14; 1989 c.701 §43; 1991 c.810 §12; 2019 c.151 §24] 744.510 [Repealed by 1967 c.359 §704]
