# Mental health wellness policies for law enforcement agencies (Certification) (ORS 181A.487)

## At a glance

ORS 181A.487 — Mental health wellness policies for law enforcement agencies (Certification). Chapter 181A (State Police; Public Safety Standards and Training), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

181A.487 Mental health wellness policies for law enforcement agencies.

(1) As used in this section, “law enforcement agency” means: (a) A city or municipal police department. (b) A county sheriff’s office. (c) The Oregon State Police. (d) A police department established by a university under ORS 352.121 or 353.125.

(2) A law enforcement agency shall establish a mental health wellness policy for addressing issues related to the mental health wellness of law enforcement officers employed by the agency. [Formerly 181A.832] Note: 181A.487 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 181A or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. (Certification)
