# Road work plans, specifications and bids (ORS 370.020)

## At a glance

ORS 370.020 — Road work plans, specifications and bids. Chapter 370 (Chapter 370), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

370.020 Road work plans, specifications and bids. The county court:

(1) Shall prepare plans and specifications of roads mentioned in ORS 370.010.

(2) Shall invite bids in conformity to such plans and specifications.

(3) May also receive and consider any and all bids in conformity to any plans and specifications furnished by any individual, firm or corporation offering to bid on such roads.

(4) May reject any and all bids. 370.030 [Repealed by 1983 c.350 §232 (370.031 enacted in lieu of 370.030)]
