# Definitions (ORS 826.001)

## At a glance

ORS 826.001 — Definitions. Chapter 826 (Chapter 826), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

826.001 Definitions. As used in this chapter:

(1) “Combined weight” means the total empty weight of all vehicles in a combination plus the total weight of the load carried on that combination of vehicles.

(2) “Commercial vehicle” means a vehicle that: (a) Is used for the transportation of persons for compensation or profit; or (b) Is designed or used primarily for the transportation of property.

(3) “Department” means the Department of Transportation. [Formerly 768.001]
