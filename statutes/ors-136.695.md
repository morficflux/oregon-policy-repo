# Evidence obtained in violation of ORS (ORS 136.695)

## At a glance

ORS 136.695 — Evidence obtained in violation of ORS. Chapter 136 (Chapter 136), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

136.695 Evidence obtained in violation of ORS 136.675 or 136.685 inadmissible. No evidence secured in violation of ORS 136.675 or 136.685 shall be admissible in any criminal proceeding in this state.
