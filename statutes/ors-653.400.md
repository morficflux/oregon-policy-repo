# Rules (ORS 653.400)

## At a glance

ORS 653.400 — Rules. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.400 Rules. The Bureau of Labor and Industries may prepare, adopt and promulgate rules for the carrying into effect of this section and ORS 653.305 to 653.370, 653.560 and 653.565. [Formerly 653.525]
