## At a glance

## Full text

673.075 Credit for examination sections taken in other states.

(1) The Oregon Board of Accountancy may, in accordance with its rules, grant credit to an applicant for one or more sections of an examination for which the applicant received passing grades from the licensing authority in any other state.

(2) Any examination for which credit is granted an applicant under subsection

(1) of this section shall be equivalent to examination requirements described in ORS 673.060.
