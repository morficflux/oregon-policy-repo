## At a glance

## Full text

350.510 Administration of Forestry Education Council.

(1) The Forestry Education Council shall select one of its members as chairperson and another as vice chairperson, for such terms and with duties and powers necessary for the performance of the functions of such offices as the President of Oregon State University determines.

(2) Except as provided in ORS 350.530, a majority of the members of the council constitutes a quorum for the transaction of business.

(3) The council shall meet at times and places specified by the call of: (a) The chairperson; (b) A majority of the members of the council; or (c) The Dean of the College of Forestry at Oregon State University after consultation with the president. [Formerly 351.677]
