# Restrictions on discarding, storing or transporting infectious waste (ORS 459.388)

## At a glance

ORS 459.388 — Restrictions on discarding, storing or transporting infectious waste. Chapter 459 (Chapter 459), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459.388 Restrictions on discarding, storing or transporting infectious waste.

(1) No person who generates infectious waste shall discard or store such waste except as provided in ORS 459.390.

(2) No person shall transport infectious waste other than infectious waste that is an incidental part of other solid waste except as provided in ORS 459.390

(6) and 825.256. [1989 c.763 §4]
