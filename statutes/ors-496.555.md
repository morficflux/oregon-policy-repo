# Contract on migratory waterfowl stamp matters UPLAND BIRD STAMPS (ORS 496.555)

## At a glance

ORS 496.555 — Contract on migratory waterfowl stamp matters UPLAND BIRD STAMPS. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.555 Contract on migratory waterfowl stamp matters. In carrying out its duties, functions and powers with regard to the migratory waterfowl stamps, the State Fish and Wildlife Commission may contract for the performance of those duties, functions and powers. The contract may include, among other matters, provisions for advance payment or reimbursement for services performed pursuant to any such contract. All costs and expenses incurred pursuant to this section shall be paid from the Migratory Waterfowl Subaccount established under ORS 496.303.
