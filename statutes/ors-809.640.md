# Procedures on habitual offender determination VEHICLE IMPOUNDMENT AND IMMOBILIZATION; (ORS 809.640)

## At a glance

## Full text

809.640 Procedures on habitual offender determination. When the Department of Transportation determines from the driving record of a person as maintained by the department that a person’s driving privileges are required to be revoked as a habitual offender under ORS 809.600, the department shall revoke the driving privileges of the person. A person is entitled to administrative review of a revocation under this section.
