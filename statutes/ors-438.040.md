# Clinical laboratory qualifications and certifications (ORS 438.040)

## At a glance

ORS 438.040 — Clinical laboratory qualifications and certifications. Chapter 438 (Chapter 438), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

438.040 Clinical laboratory qualifications and certifications.

(1) The Oregon Health Authority shall verify the qualifications of each clinical laboratory in this state and shall issue or facilitate the issuance of a certificate to a clinical laboratory that satisfies the requirements of the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments of 1988 (P.L. 100-578, 42 U.S.C. 201 and 263a).

(2) A clinical laboratory may not operate in this state without a certificate issued by the authority or the federal government under this section.
