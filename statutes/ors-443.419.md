## Full text

443.419 License revocations, suspensions or conditions; rules.

(1) The Department of Human Services or the Oregon Health Authority may revoke, suspend or impose conditions on the license of any residential training facility or residential training home based on criteria adopted by the licensing agency by rule, including but not limited to when: (a) There exists a threat to the health, safety or welfare of a resident; (b) There is reliable evidence of abuse, neglect or exploitation of a resident; or (c) The facility or home is out of compliance with ORS 443.002 or 443.400 to 443.455 or the rules of the licensing agency.

(2) In cases where an imminent danger to the health or safety of the residents exists, a license may be suspended immediately. [2021 c.316 §2]
