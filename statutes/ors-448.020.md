# Plan approval required to construct or alter pool facilities (ORS 448.020)

## At a glance

ORS 448.020 — Plan approval required to construct or alter pool facilities. Chapter 448 (Chapter 448), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

448.020 Plan approval required to construct or alter pool facilities. A person may not construct or perform a major alteration or reconstruction of a public swimming pool, public spa pool, public wading pool or bathhouse without plan approval to do so from the Oregon Health Authority. [Amended by 1961 c.566 §4; 1973 c.215 §3; 1979 c.453 §2; 1999 c.929 §1; 2009 c.595 §829; 2019 c.456 §118]
