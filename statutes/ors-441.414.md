# Effect of ORS (ORS 441.414)

## At a glance

ORS 441.414 — Effect of ORS. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.414 Effect of ORS 441.402 to 441.419 on right to visitors. Nothing in ORS 441.402 to 441.419 shall affect the right of residents of a long term care facility to see visitors of their choice. [Formerly 441.133; 2017 c.441 §19] 441.415 [1973 c.837 §2; 1977 c.751 §40; renumbered 442.400]
