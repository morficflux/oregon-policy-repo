# Consent of Department of State Lands for state lands (ORS 777.347)

## At a glance

ORS 777.347 — Consent of Department of State Lands for state lands. Chapter 777 (Chapter 777), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

777.347 Consent of Department of State Lands for state lands. The Department of State Lands is authorized to consent to annexation with respect to any land owned by the State of Oregon, under the jurisdiction of the department, which is located in the territory proposed to be annexed. [1963 c.268 §12; 1971 c.727 §189] 777.350 [Repealed by 1963 c.268 §21]
