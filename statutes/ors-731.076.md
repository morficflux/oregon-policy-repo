# “Department,” “director” (ORS 731.076)

## At a glance

ORS 731.076 — “Department,” “director”. Chapter 731 (Administration and General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

731.076 “Department,” “director.”

(1) “Department” means the Department of Consumer and Business Services.

(2) “Director” means the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services. [1967 c.359 §16; 1987 c.373 §76; 1993 c.744 §28] 731.080 [Repealed by 1965 c.241 §3]
