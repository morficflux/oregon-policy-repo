# Performance of lead-based paint activities and renovation without certification prohibited (ORS 431A.358)

## At a glance

ORS 431A.358 — Performance of lead-based paint activities and renovation without certification prohibited. Chapter 431A (Chapter 431A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

431A.358 Performance of lead-based paint activities and renovation without certification prohibited.

(1) An individual may not perform or offer to perform lead-based paint activities unless the individual is certified as provided under ORS 431A.355.

(2) A firm may not perform or offer to perform a renovation for compensation unless the firm is certified as provided under ORS 431A.355.

(3) A firm may not perform or offer to perform lead-based paint activities unless the firm is certified as provided under ORS 431A.355. [Formerly 431.922; 2021 c.191 §5]
