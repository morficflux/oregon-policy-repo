# Notice of availability of short-acting opioid antagonist; rules (ORS 689.806)

## At a glance

ORS 689.806 — Notice of availability of short-acting opioid antagonist; rules. Chapter 689 (Chapter 689), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

689.806 Notice of availability of short-acting opioid antagonist; rules.

(1) A retail or hospital outpatient pharmacy shall provide written notice in a conspicuous manner that a short-acting opioid antagonist, as defined in ORS 689.800, and the necessary medical supplies to administer the short-acting opioid antagonist are available at the pharmacy.

(2) The State Board of Pharmacy may adopt rules to carry out this section. [Formerly 689.686]
