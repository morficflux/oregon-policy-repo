# Business income tax (ORS 671.760)

## At a glance

ORS 671.760 — Business income tax. Chapter 671 (Chapter 671), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

671.760 Business income tax.

(1) As used in this section, “business license tax” has the meaning given that term in ORS 671.755.

(2) A city that imposes a business license tax based on or measured by adjusted net income earned by conducting business within the city is exempt from ORS 671.755.
