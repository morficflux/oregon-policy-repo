# Exemptions applicable to payments (ORS 414.095)

## At a glance

ORS 414.095 — Exemptions applicable to payments. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.095 Exemptions applicable to payments. Neither medical assistance nor amounts payable to vendors out of medical assistance funds are transferable or assignable at law or in equity and none of the money paid or payable under the provisions of this chapter is subject to execution, levy, attachment, garnishment or other legal process.
