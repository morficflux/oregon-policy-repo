# Resolution to exercise powers (ORS 456.325)

## At a glance

ORS 456.325 — Resolution to exercise powers. Chapter 456 (Chapter 456), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

456.325 Resolution to exercise powers. The exercise by a state public body of the powers granted in ORS 456.305 to 456.325 may be authorized by resolution of the governing body of such state public body adopted by a majority of the members of its governing body present at a meeting of the governing body. This resolution may be adopted at the meeting at which it is introduced. Such a resolution shall take effect immediately and need not be laid over or published or posted.
