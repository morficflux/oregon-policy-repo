# Manufacture and sale of handiwork; disposition of sale price (ORS 421.142)

## At a glance

ORS 421.142 — Manufacture and sale of handiwork; disposition of sale price. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.142 Manufacture and sale of handiwork; disposition of sale price.

(1) The Department of Corrections may establish a program to allow the manufacture of small articles of handiwork by the adults in custody in Department of Corrections institutions, out of raw materials purchased by the adults in custody with their own funds, which articles may be sold to the public. State-owned property may not be sold or given to adults in custody under this section.

(2) The department may provide that all or a part of the sales price of the articles be deposited to the account of the adults in custody manufacturing the article. [1953 c.537 §1; 1969 c.502 §12; 1987 c.320 §170; 2017 c.195 §1; 2019 c.213 §70]
