# Remedies cumulative (ORS 319.120)

## At a glance

ORS 319.120 — Remedies cumulative. Chapter 319 (Chapter 319), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

319.120 Remedies cumulative. Except as otherwise provided in ORS 319.180 and 319.200, the remedies of the state provided in ORS 319.090, 319.100 and 319.110 are cumulative. No action taken pursuant to those statutes shall relieve any person from the penal provisions of ORS 319.010 to 319.420 and 319.990. [Amended by 1967 c.54 §1; 1999 c.769 §7]
