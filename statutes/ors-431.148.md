## Full text

431.148 Evidence-based best practices; rules. The Oregon Health Authority may adopt by rule evidence-based best practices for the purpose of assisting local public health authorities in implementing the foundational programs established under ORS 431.141 and any other public health program or activity established under ORS 431.147. Rules adopted under this section are only for the purpose of assisting local public health authorities and are not mandatory guidelines for the implementation of the programs or activities. [2015 c.736 §23; 2017 c.627 §8] RULES
