# Withdrawal of territory not subject to boundary commission review (ORS 267.263)

## At a glance

ORS 267.263 — Withdrawal of territory not subject to boundary commission review. Chapter 267 (Chapter 267), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

267.263 Withdrawal of territory not subject to boundary commission review. The alteration of the boundaries of a district under ORS 267.250 to 267.263 is not subject to the jurisdiction or review of a local government boundary commission. [1987 c.799 §7; 2007 c.239 §14]
