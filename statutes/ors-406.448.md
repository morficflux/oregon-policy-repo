# Definitions (ORS 406.448)

## At a glance

ORS 406.448 — Definitions. Chapter 406 (Department of Veterans’ Affairs), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

406.448 Definitions. As used in ORS 406.087, 406.450, 406.452, 406.454 and 406.456:

(1) “Survivor of a veteran” means the spouse or a dependent of a deceased veteran.

(2) “Veteran” has the meaning given that term in ORS 408.225. [2007 c.357 §1; 2009 c.41 §12; 2018 c.30 §3] Note: 406.448 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 406 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
