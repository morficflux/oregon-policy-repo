## Full text

744.053 Requirements to be licensed as insurance producer for class of insurance. A person may not sell, solicit or negotiate insurance in this state for any class or classes of insurance unless the person is licensed as an insurance producer for that class or those classes in accordance with ORS 744.052 to 744.089.
