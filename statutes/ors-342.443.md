# Reports of longitudinal data (ORS 342.443)

## At a glance

ORS 342.443 — Reports of longitudinal data. Chapter 342 (Chapter 342), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

342.443 Reports of longitudinal data.

(1) The Education and Workforce Policy Advisor shall report biennially to the Legislative Assembly longitudinal data on the number and percentage of: (a) Diverse students enrolled in community colleges; (b) Diverse students enrolled in public universities; (c) Diverse students graduated from public universities; (d) Diverse candidates enrolled in public approved educator preparation programs; (e) Diverse candidates who have completed public approved educator preparation programs; (f) Diverse candidates receiving Oregon teaching or administrative licenses or registrations based on preparation in this state and preparation in other states; (g) Diverse educators who are newly employed in the public schools in this state; and (h) Diverse educators already employed in the public schools.

(2) The advisor also shall report comparisons of scores achieved by diverse persons and nondiverse persons on basic skills, pedagogy and subject matter tests.

(3) The Higher Education Coordinating Commission, the public universities listed in ORS 352.002, the Department of Education, the Teacher Standards and Practices Commission, community colleges and school districts shall cooperate with the advisor in collecting data and preparing the report.
