# Criminal penalties GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 634.992)

## At a glance

ORS 634.992 — Criminal penalties GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 634 (Chapter 634), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

634.992 Criminal penalties. Violation of any of the provisions of this chapter, or any rules adopted by the State Department of Agriculture related to restricted-use pesticides, is a Class A misdemeanor. [1973 c.341 §36; 2011 c.597 §256; 2021 c.177 §5] _______________
