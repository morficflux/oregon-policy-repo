# Appointment of financial institution examiners (ORS 705.610)

## At a glance

ORS 705.610 — Appointment of financial institution examiners. Chapter 705 (Department of Consumer and Business Services _______________ 2025 EDITION DEPARTMENT OF CONSUMER AND BUSINESS SERVICES INSURANCE AND FINANCE ADMINISTRATION ADMI), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

705.610 Appointment of financial institution examiners. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services shall appoint examiners to conduct, as required by law, all examinations of entities subject to ORS chapters 706 to 725. [1987 c.373 §20] 705.620 [1987 c.171 §2; 1993 c.264 §§3,3a; 1995 c.192 §1; repealed by 2009 c.541 §49]
