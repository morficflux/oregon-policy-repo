# Oregon Health Authority to provide laboratory examination; rules (ORS 433.012)

## At a glance

## Full text

433.012 Oregon Health Authority to provide laboratory examination; rules. The Oregon Health Authority shall provide the necessary laboratory examinations requested by local health departments for the diagnosis of those communicable diseases identified by rule of the authority to be a reportable disease. [1987 c.600 §7; 2009 c.595 §629] 433.015 [Repealed by 1973 c.259 §20]
