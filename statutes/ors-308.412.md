# Effect of election to exclude income approach to value under prior law (ORS 308.412)

## At a glance

ORS 308.412 — Effect of election to exclude income approach to value under prior law. Chapter 308 (Chapter 308), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308.412 Effect of election to exclude income approach to value under prior law. An owner that made an election that was in effect under ORS 308.411 (1997 Edition) shall be considered to have chosen ORS 308.411 (2)(a) to apply to the election. The owner may revise or revoke the election pursuant to ORS 308.411 (6). [1999 c.579 §31] Note: See note under 308.408.
