# Duty to report change of name or contact information (Discipline) (ORS 688.138)

## At a glance

ORS 688.138 — Duty to report change of name or contact information (Discipline). Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.138 Duty to report change of name or contact information. A physical therapist or physical therapist assistant shall report to the Oregon Board of Physical Therapy a change to the physical therapist’s or physical therapist assistant’s name, business or residential address, contact email address or contact telephone number within 30 days after the date of the change. [2025 c.627 §4] Note: 688.138 becomes operative July 1, 2027. See section 24, chapter 627, Oregon Laws 2025. Note: 688.138 was added to and made a part of 688.010 to 688.201 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. (Discipline)
