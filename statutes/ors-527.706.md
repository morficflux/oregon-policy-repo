# Program of photogrammetric mapping; rules (ORS 527.706)

## At a glance

ORS 527.706 — Program of photogrammetric mapping; rules. Chapter 527 (Chapter 527), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

527.706 Program of photogrammetric mapping; rules.

(1) As used in this section, “photogrammetric mapping” has the meaning given that term in ORS 672.002.

(2) To aid in monitoring compliance with ORS 527.610 to 527.770 and rules adopted thereunder, the State Board of Forestry shall adopt rules that: (a) Require persons that file notifications pursuant to ORS 527.670

(6) to inform the State Forester when the operations are complete, within a reasonable time after completion; (b) Authorize the State Forester to use a program of photogrammetric mapping to determine whether operations for which notifications have been filed pursuant to ORS 527.670

(6) have been completed; or (c) Otherwise establish a program for determining when operations for which notifications have been filed pursuant to ORS 527.670

(6) have been completed.

(3) Rules described in subsection (2)(b) or (c) of this section must limit the discretion of the person conducting the compliance monitoring. [2022 c.33 §44] Note: 527.706 is repealed only if certain conditions are met. See sections 54 and 60 to 64, chapter 33, Oregon Laws 2022 (third note following 527.711).
