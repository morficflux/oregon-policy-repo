# Proximity of psilocybin service center to school (ORS 475A.310)

## At a glance

ORS 475A.310 — Proximity of psilocybin service center to school. Chapter 475A (Chapter 475A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475A.310 Proximity of psilocybin service center to school. Notwithstanding ORS 475A.305 (2)(e), a psilocybin service center may be located within 1,000 feet of a school if:

(1) The psilocybin service center is not located within 500 feet of: (a) A public elementary or secondary school for which attendance is compulsory under ORS 339.020; or (b) A private or parochial elementary or secondary school, teaching children as described in ORS 339.030 (1)(a); and

(2) The Oregon Health Authority determines that there is a physical or geographic barrier capable of preventing children from traversing to the premises of the psilocybin service center. [2021 c.1 §27]
