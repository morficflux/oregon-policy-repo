## At a glance

ORS 685.990 — Penalties. Chapter 685 (Chapter 685), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

685.990 Penalties.

(1) Violation of any provision of this chapter is a Class A misdemeanor. (2)(a) In addition to any other sanctions authorized by law, the Oregon Board of Naturopathic Medicine may impose a civil penalty not to exceed $5,000 for each violation of any provision of this chapter, or of any rule adopted by the board. (b) Civil penalties imposed under this subsection shall be imposed as provided in ORS 183.745. [Amended by 1999 c.1051 §218; 2005 c.215 §2; 2009 c.43 §35] _______________
