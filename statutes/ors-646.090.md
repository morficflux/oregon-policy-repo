## At a glance

## Full text

646.090 Inducing or receiving price discrimination prohibited. No person engaged in commerce or food commerce, or both, in the course of such commerce, shall knowingly induce or receive a discrimination in price which is prohibited by ORS 646.040 to 646.080. 646.100 [Amended by 1963 s.s. c.2 §3; repealed by 1975 c.255 §17] 646.105 [1963 s.s. c.2 §2; repealed by 1975 c.255 §17] 646.110 [Repealed by 1975 c.255 §17] 646.120 [Repealed by 1975 c.255 §17]
