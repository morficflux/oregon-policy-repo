# Applicability of ORS 711.190, 711.197 and 711.199 to merging or converting trust company (ORS 709.530)

## At a glance

## Full text

709.530 Applicability of ORS 711.190, 711.197 and 711.199 to merging or converting trust company. ORS 711.190, 711.197 and 711.199 shall be applicable to a merging or converting Oregon trust company. [1997 c.631 §232]
