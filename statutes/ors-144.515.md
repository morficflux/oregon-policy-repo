# Release terminates enrollment; continued employment (ORS 144.515)

## At a glance

ORS 144.515 — Release terminates enrollment; continued employment. Chapter 144 (Chapter 144), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

144.515 Release terminates enrollment; continued employment. A person’s enrollment in the work release program terminates upon the release of the person from confinement pursuant to law. To the extent possible, the Department of Corrections shall cooperate with employers in making possible the continued employment of persons released.
