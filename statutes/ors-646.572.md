# Administration of telephone solicitation program through contract or by designation of federal registry; contract provisions; duty of Attorney General (ORS 646.572)

## At a glance

## Full text

646.572 Administration of telephone solicitation program through contract or by designation of federal registry; contract provisions; duty of Attorney General.

(1) The Attorney General shall either: (a) Advertise for bids and enter into a contract with a person to act as the administrator of the telephone solicitation program described in ORS 646.574; or (b) Designate a federal “do not call” registry, including but not limited to the registry maintained by the Federal Trade Commission under 16 C.F.R. 310, in lieu of an Oregon do not call registry.

(2) The Attorney General may include in a contract with the administrator any provision that the Attorney General determines is in the public interest.

(3) If a party requests, the Attorney General shall instruct the party on how to register the party’s telephone number on the federal “do not call” registry designated under subsection (1)(b) of this section. [1999 c.564 §3; 2007 c.441 §6]
