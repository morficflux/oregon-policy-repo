# Industrial hemp propagation (ORS 571.290)

## At a glance

ORS 571.290 — Industrial hemp propagation. Chapter 571 (Chapter 571), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

571.290 Industrial hemp propagation. A grower licensed under ORS 571.281 may use any propagation method, including planting seeds or starts or the use of clones or cuttings, to produce industrial hemp. [Formerly 571.288] Note: See note under 571.260.
