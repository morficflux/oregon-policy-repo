# Civil liability for bison running at large on land STOCK RUNNING AT LARGE ON OPEN RANGE (ORS 607.085)

## At a glance

## Full text

607.085 Civil liability for bison running at large on land. A person is liable to the owner or lawful possessor of land for resulting economic damages if:

(1) The person permits bison to run at large upon the land; or

(2) Bison for which the person is the owner or possessor are running at large or uncontrolled upon the land.
