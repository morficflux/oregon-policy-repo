# Certain loans excludable from maximum loan amount (ORS 407.215)

## At a glance

ORS 407.215 — Certain loans excludable from maximum loan amount. Chapter 407 (Chapter 407), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

407.215 Certain loans excludable from maximum loan amount. No applicant is entitled to borrow more than the maximum amount allowed under ORS 407.205 other than for reasons specified in ORS 407.205 except that when the property on which the loan was made becomes the property of the applicant’s spouse as a result of a judgment declaring a marriage void or dissolved and the loan is repaid, or remains unrepaid and there is an assumption of primary liability on the loan by a party other than the applicant, the loan may be excluded from consideration in computing the maximum loan allowable under ORS 407.205. [Formerly 407.045; 2003 c.576 §440; 2019 c.223 §11]
