# Disposition of revenues (ORS 633.371)

## At a glance

ORS 633.371 — Disposition of revenues. Chapter 633 (Chapter 633), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

633.371 Disposition of revenues. The State Department of Agriculture shall deposit revenues received under ORS 633.311 to 633.479 and 633.994 in the Department of Agriculture Service Fund. The revenues deposited under this section are continuously appropriated to the department for the purpose of administering and enforcing ORS 633.311 to 633.479 and 633.994. [2001 c.914 §11; 2009 c.97 §10] 633.380 [Amended by 1965 c.268 §11; 1977 c.799 §5; repealed by 2001 c.914 §30]
