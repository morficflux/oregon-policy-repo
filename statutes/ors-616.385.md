# Public hearing required for rules (ORS 616.385)

## At a glance

ORS 616.385 — Public hearing required for rules. Chapter 616 (General and Miscellaneous Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

616.385 Public hearing required for rules. All rules promulgated under ORS 616.335 to 616.385 shall only be promulgated after public hearing and shall be in accordance with the applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183.
