# Wagering on dog races prohibited (ORS 462.400)

## At a glance

ORS 462.400 — Wagering on dog races prohibited. Chapter 462 (Chapter 462), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

462.400 Wagering on dog races prohibited. No person may wager money or any other thing of value on the outcome of a greyhound race or other dog race, or accept or facilitate such wagering, regardless of the location at which the race takes place. [2025 c.26 §2] Note: 462.400 becomes operative July 1, 2027. See section 19, chapter 26, Oregon Laws 2025.
