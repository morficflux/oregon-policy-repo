# Information required on enrollment agreement (ORS 345.117)

## At a glance

ORS 345.117 — Information required on enrollment agreement. Chapter 345 (Chapter 345), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

345.117 Information required on enrollment agreement. Any enrollment agreement used within this state as a contract for instruction between a career school and a student shall have printed or stamped upon it: “Any inquiry a student may have regarding this contract may be made in writing to the school (name and address), or to the Higher Education Coordinating Commission (current address).” [1975 c.478 §22; 1989 c.333 §11; 1995 c.343 §10; 2012 c.104 §31]
