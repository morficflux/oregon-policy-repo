# Civil penalties (ORS 709.980)

## At a glance

ORS 709.980 — Civil penalties. Chapter 709 (Chapter 709), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

709.980 Civil penalties.

(1) Any person who violates ORS 709.270 or 709.280 or any rule adopted under ORS 709.170

(3) shall forfeit a civil penalty in an amount determined by the director of not more than $2,500 for each offense.

(2) All money forfeited under this section shall be paid to the State Treasurer to be deposited in the Consumer and Business Services Fund.

(3) The civil penalty may be recovered as provided in ORS 706.980. [1975 c.544 §29c] 709.990 [Repealed by 1973 c.797 §428] _______________ CHAPTER 710 [Reserved for expansion]
