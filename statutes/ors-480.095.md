# Penalty and civil liability for violation of ORS (ORS 480.095)

## At a glance

ORS 480.095 — Penalty and civil liability for violation of ORS. Chapter 480 (Chapter 480), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

480.095 Penalty and civil liability for violation of ORS 480.085. Persons violating ORS 480.085 are subject to the penalty provided in ORS 480.990

(4) and are liable in civil action for damages to any person suffering injury from handling or otherwise coming in contact with unused explosives that are left in an area of use in violation of ORS 480.085, regardless of any negligence or lack of negligence on the part of the defendant.
