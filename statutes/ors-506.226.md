# Use of electric shock to take adult salmonids for private hatchery permittees prohibited (ORS 506.226)

## At a glance

ORS 506.226 — Use of electric shock to take adult salmonids for private hatchery permittees prohibited. Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.226 Use of electric shock to take adult salmonids for private hatchery permittees prohibited. Notwithstanding any other provision of law the State Department of Fish and Wildlife shall not use in any body of water any electric current or electric shock device for the purpose of capturing any adult salmonids for a person granted a permit pursuant to ORS 508.700 to 508.745. [1981 c.646 §2] 506.230 [Repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
