# Payment of tax by seller (ORS 319.681)

## At a glance

ORS 319.681 — Payment of tax by seller. Chapter 319 (Chapter 319), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

319.681 Payment of tax by seller. The seller of fuel for use in a motor vehicle shall remit to the Department of Transportation with each report required by ORS 319.675 all the tax due on the amount of fuel sold less four percent, which the seller shall retain. [1959 c.188 §15; 1977 c.429 §6]
