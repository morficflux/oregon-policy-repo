# Definition of “facility” for ORS (ORS 441.331)

## At a glance

ORS 441.331 — Definition of “facility” for ORS. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.331 Definition of “facility” for ORS 441.331 to 441.341. As used in ORS 441.331 to 441.341, “facility” has the meaning given that term in ORS 441.277. [2009 c.539 §14] Note: 441.331 to 441.341 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 441 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
