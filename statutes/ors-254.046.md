# Expense of city election (ORS 254.046)

## At a glance

ORS 254.046 — Expense of city election. Chapter 254 (Chapter 254), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

254.046 Expense of city election. If a city holds a special election on a date other than the primary election or general election, it shall bear the expense of the election. [1979 c.190 §228; 1987 c.267 §38; 1995 c.712 §52] 254.050 [Repealed by 1957 c.608 §231] 254.055 [1973 c.481 §2; 1979 c.190 §145; 1979 c.345 §3; renumbered 250.065]
