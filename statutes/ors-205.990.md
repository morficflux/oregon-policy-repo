# Penalties (ORS 205.990)

## At a glance

## Full text

205.990 Penalties. Any officer who violates ORS 205.510

(1) shall be deemed guilty of official misconduct and punished therefor as provided by ORS 162.415. [Amended by 1959 c.552 §15; 1971 c.743 §346] _______________
