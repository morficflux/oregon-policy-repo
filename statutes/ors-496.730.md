# Legislative intent (ORS 496.730)

## At a glance

ORS 496.730 — Legislative intent. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.730 Legislative intent. It is the intent of the Legislative Assembly to protect wildlife from becoming habituated to humans and to protect the public against the serious health and safety risk posed by wildlife that are drawn into contact with humans and infrastructure by individuals who knowingly feed wildlife. [2011 c.284 §2]
