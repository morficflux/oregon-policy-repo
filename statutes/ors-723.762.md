# Participation in government programs (ORS 723.762)

## At a glance

## Full text

723.762 Participation in government programs. The participation by a credit union in any government program providing unemployment, Social Security, old age pension or other benefits shall not be deemed a waiver of the taxation exemption hereby granted. [1975 c.652 §79] 723.802 [1975 c.652 §80; repealed by 1985 c.762 §196]
