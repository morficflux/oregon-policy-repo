# Choice of amortization period for certain lump sum payments to side accounts (ORS 238.232)

## At a glance

ORS 238.232 — Choice of amortization period for certain lump sum payments to side accounts. Chapter 238 (Chapter 238), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

238.232 Choice of amortization period for certain lump sum payments to side accounts. If a participating public employer makes a lump sum payment from moneys not borrowed by the employer to an account established under ORS 238.229 in an amount equal to or greater than $10 million, the Public Employees Retirement Board shall allow the participating public employer to choose an amortization period of six years, 10 years, 16 years or 20 years for the use of the lump sum payment to offset contributions to the system that the public employer would otherwise be required to make for the liabilities against which the lump sum payment is applied. [2018 c.105 §3b; 2019 c.355 §§20,21]
