# Continuation of action without claim presentation (ORS 115.315)

## At a glance

ORS 115.315 — Continuation of action without claim presentation. Chapter 115 (Chapter 115), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

115.315 Continuation of action without claim presentation. An action against a decedent commenced before and pending on the date of death of the decedent may be continued as provided in ORCP 34 B(2) without presentation of a claim against the estate of the decedent. [1969 c.591 §166; 1979 c.284 §107] 115.320 [Repealed by 1969 c.591 §305]
