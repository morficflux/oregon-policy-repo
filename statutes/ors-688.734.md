# Disciplinary authority of Health Licensing Office (ORS 688.734)

## At a glance

ORS 688.734 — Disciplinary authority of Health Licensing Office. Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.734 Disciplinary authority of Health Licensing Office. In the manner prescribed in ORS chapter 183 for contested cases and in consultation with the Board of Athletic Trainers, the Health Licensing Office may impose a form of discipline listed in ORS 676.612 against any person engaged in the practice of athletic training for any of the grounds listed in ORS 676.612 and for any violation of the provisions of ORS 688.701 to 688.734 or the rules adopted under ORS 688.701 to 688.734. [2003 c.547 §11; 2005 c.648 §22; 2013 c.568 §68; 2019 c.378 §7]
