# Consultation with State Treasurer (Revenue Bonds) (ORS 757.945)

## At a glance

ORS 757.945 — Consultation with State Treasurer (Revenue Bonds). Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.945 Consultation with State Treasurer. Oregon Community Power may consult with and obtain advice from the State Treasurer on proposed or executed financing agreements. The State Treasurer may recover from Oregon Community Power any costs incurred by the State Treasurer in providing consultation and advice. [2007 c.807 §38] Note: See note under 757.812. (Revenue Bonds)
