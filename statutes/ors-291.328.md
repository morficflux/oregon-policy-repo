# Board may require presentation of evidence to support requests for action; filing of orders (ORS 291.328)

## At a glance

## Full text

291.328 Board may require presentation of evidence to support requests for action; filing of orders.

(1) Before the Emergency Board makes any allocation, grants any authorization or approves any budget under ORS 291.326, the board may require a state agency to submit written evidence to justify the allocation, authorization or approval or may require the head of the agency to appear before it in support thereof.

(2) The Emergency Board may require the Director of the Oregon Department of Administrative Services to submit a written report regarding the need and justification for the allocation, authorization or approval.

(3) Upon making an allocation, granting an authorization or approving a budget, the Emergency Board shall file with the department and the Secretary of State a copy of the order of allocation, grant of authorization or approved budget. [1953 c.386 §4; 2016 c.117 §30]
