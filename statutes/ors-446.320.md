## At a glance

ORS 446.320 — Tourist facility license required. Chapter 446 (Chapter 446), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

446.320 Tourist facility license required.

(1) No person shall establish, operate, manage or maintain a tourist facility, without a license from the Director of the Oregon Health Authority.

(2) Organizational camps operated under rental or leasehold agreements may be licensed either to the landlord or to the tenant provided that the license holder shall be responsible for compliance with ORS 446.310 to 446.350 and the rules adopted thereunder. [1969 c.533 §3; 1973 c.560 §14; 1979 c.696 §4; 1979 c.789 §2a; 1983 c.707 §9; 2009 c.595 §813]
