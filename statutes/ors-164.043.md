# Theft in the third degree (ORS 164.043)

## At a glance

ORS 164.043 — Theft in the third degree. Chapter 164 (Chapter 164), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

164.043 Theft in the third degree.

(1) A person commits the crime of theft in the third degree if: (a) By means other than extortion, the person commits theft as defined in ORS 164.015; and (b) The total value of the property in a single or an aggregate transaction is less than $100.

(2) Theft in the third degree is a Class C misdemeanor. [1987 c.907 §2; 2009 c.11 §11; 2009 c.16 §1]
