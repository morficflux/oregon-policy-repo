# Prohibitions against keeping of wild or exotic animals (Generally) (ORS 609.205)

## At a glance

ORS 609.205 — Prohibitions against keeping of wild or exotic animals (Generally). Chapter 609 (Chapter 609), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

609.205 Prohibitions against keeping of wild or exotic animals. Notwithstanding the provisions of ORS chapters 496, 497 and 498 relating to wildlife, and ORS 609.305 to 609.355 and 609.992 relating to exotic animals, a city or county may prohibit by ordinance the keeping of wildlife, as defined in ORS 496.004, and may prohibit by ordinance the keeping of exotic animals as defined in ORS 609.305. [1977 c.802 §3; 1985 c.437 §9] (Generally)
