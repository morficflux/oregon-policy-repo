# Services to other local governments authorized (ORS 450.693)

## At a glance

ORS 450.693 — Services to other local governments authorized. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.693 Services to other local governments authorized. A water authority may provide any of the services that it is authorized to provide under ORS 450.600 to 450.989 to other local governments as defined in ORS 174.116 situated within the boundaries of the authority at wholesale, retail or a combination of wholesale and retail. [1993 c.577 §13; 2003 c.802 §118]
