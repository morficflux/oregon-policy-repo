# Investments authorized to fund length of service awards for volunteer firefighters REVENUES AND FINANCES (Generally) (ORS 478.390)

## At a glance

ORS 478.390 — Investments authorized to fund length of service awards for volunteer firefighters REVENUES AND FINANCES (Generally). Chapter 478 (Chapter 478), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

478.390 Investments authorized to fund length of service awards for volunteer firefighters. In addition to the investments authorized by ORS 294.035, the board of directors of a rural fire protection district organized under ORS chapter 478 may invest or cause to be invested any surplus funds of the district in contracts described in ORS 294.035 (3)(f) for the purpose of funding length of service awards for the volunteer firefighters of the district. [1993 c.452 §2; 1995 c.245 §13; 2005 c.443 §30] Note: 478.390 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 478 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. REVENUES AND FINANCES (Generally)
