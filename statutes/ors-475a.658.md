# Definitions for ORS 475A.658 to (ORS 475A.658)

## At a glance

ORS 475A.658 — Definitions for ORS 475A.658 to. Chapter 475A (Chapter 475A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475A.658 Definitions for ORS 475A.658 to 475A.714. As used in ORS 475A.658 to 475A.714:

(1) “Retail sale” means any transfer, exchange, gift or barter of a psilocybin product by any person to a client.

(2) “Retail sales price” means the price paid for a psilocybin product, excluding tax, to a psilocybin service center operator by or on behalf of a client. [2021 c.1 §113]
