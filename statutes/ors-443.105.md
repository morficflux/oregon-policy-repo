# Caregiver registries; rules WORKER SAFETY IN HOME HEALTH CARE SETTINGS (ORS 443.105)

## At a glance

ORS 443.105 — Caregiver registries; rules WORKER SAFETY IN HOME HEALTH CARE SETTINGS. Chapter 443 (Chapter 443), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

443.105 Caregiver registries; rules. The Oregon Health Authority may adopt rules governing caregiver registries, including but not limited to:

(1) The minimum qualifications of individuals whose services are offered through a caregiver registry;

(2) Standards for the organization and quality of client care;

(3) Procedures for maintaining records;

(4) Requirements for contractual arrangements for professional and ancillary services;

(5) Requiring criminal background checks on individuals placed on a roster by a caregiver registry;

(6) Procedures for complaints against caregiver registries; and

(7) Procedures for inspection of caregiver registries. [2009 c.792 §14c; 2009 c.792 §65; 2011 c.70 §16] WORKER SAFETY IN HOME HEALTH CARE SETTINGS
