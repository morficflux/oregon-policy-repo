# Purpose of ORS (ORS 343.391)

## At a glance

ORS 343.391 — Purpose of ORS. Chapter 343 (Chapter 343), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

343.391 Purpose of ORS 343.391 to 343.413. The purpose of ORS 343.391 to 343.413 is to facilitate the identification and education of talented and gifted children. [1959 c.528 §1; 1963 c.570 §21; 1971 c.613 §1; 1979 c.385 §1] 343.393 [1959 c.528 §11; repealed by 1961 c.500 §2]
