# Disclaimer of power held in fiduciary capacity (ORS 105.641)

## At a glance

ORS 105.641 — Disclaimer of power held in fiduciary capacity. Chapter 105 (Chapter 105), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

105.641 Disclaimer of power held in fiduciary capacity.

(1) If a fiduciary disclaims a power held in a fiduciary capacity that has not been exercised, the disclaimer takes effect as of the time the instrument creating the power becomes irrevocable.

(2) If a fiduciary disclaims a power held in a fiduciary capacity that has been exercised, the disclaimer takes effect immediately after the last exercise of the power.

(3) A disclaimer under this section applies to another fiduciary if the disclaimer so provides and the fiduciary disclaiming has the authority to bind the estate, trust or other person for whom the fiduciary is acting. [2001 c.245 §11]
