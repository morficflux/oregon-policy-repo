# Oregon Youth Employment Fund (Development of Green Jobs) (ORS 660.354)

## At a glance

ORS 660.354 — Oregon Youth Employment Fund (Development of Green Jobs). Chapter 660 (Chapter 660), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

660.354 Oregon Youth Employment Fund. The Oregon Youth Employment Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the Oregon Youth Employment Fund shall be credited to the fund. Moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Higher Education Coordinating Commission for the purposes of ORS 660.353. [2011 c.702 §5; 2015 c.366 §68] (Development of Green Jobs)
