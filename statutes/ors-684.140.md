# Officers of board (ORS 684.140)

## At a glance

ORS 684.140 — Officers of board. Chapter 684 (Chapter 684), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

684.140 Officers of board. The State Board of Chiropractic Examiners shall annually elect a president, a vice president and a secretary-treasurer from its membership. The executive director of the board shall keep a record of the proceedings of the board which shall be open to public inspection at all times during business hours. [Amended by 1991 c.892 §9; 2009 c.756 §53]
