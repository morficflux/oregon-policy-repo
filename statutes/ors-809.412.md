# Authority of juvenile court for suspension or revocation (ORS 809.412)

## At a glance

ORS 809.412 — Authority of juvenile court for suspension or revocation. Chapter 809 (Chapter 809), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

809.412 Authority of juvenile court for suspension or revocation. If a juvenile court finds a youth to be within the jurisdiction of the juvenile court under ORS 419C.005 for committing an offense that is a ground for suspension or revocation upon conviction under ORS 809.409, 809.411, 809.510 to 809.545 or 813.400 or any other law requiring suspension or revocation of driving privileges upon conviction of an offense, the juvenile court shall order the suspension or revocation of driving privileges that is required upon conviction of the offense. [Formerly 809.370; 2008 c.49 §3; 2011 c.355 §30; 2013 c.237 §19] Note: 809.412 was added to and made a part of the Oregon Vehicle Code by legislative action but was not added to ORS chapter 809 or any series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 809.413 [2003 c.402 §4; 2005 c.649 §20; 2009 c.395 §9; 2009 c.783 §11; 2011 c.470 §4; repealed by 2013 c.237 §12]
