# Common law application to statutory obligations and remedies (ORS 696.855)

## At a glance

ORS 696.855 — Common law application to statutory obligations and remedies. Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.855 Common law application to statutory obligations and remedies.

(1) ORS 696.301, 696.392, 696.600 to 696.785, 696.890 and 696.995 do not directly, indirectly or by implication limit or alter any preexisting common law or statutory right or remedy including actions for fraud, negligence or equitable relief.

(2) Common law and statutory remedies are not affected by ORS 696.301, 696.392, 696.600 to 696.785, 696.890 and 696.995. [1993 c.570 §12; 2001 c.300 §49a; 2011 c.158 §2] Note: See note under 696.800.
