# Tax does not legalize ownership, display or operation in violation of law (ORS 320.140)

## At a glance

## Full text

320.140 Tax does not legalize ownership, display or operation in violation of law. Nothing in ORS 320.005 to 320.150 shall be construed as licensing, authorizing or legalizing the ownership, possession, display or operation, in violation of any law of this state, of any amusement device. [Amended by 1993 c.270 §64; 1993 c.803 §14; 2005 c.94 §97]
