# Adverse party contact information (ORS 221.363)

## At a glance

ORS 221.363 — Adverse party contact information. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.363 Adverse party contact information.

(1) When entering a judgment in an action for a violation or a misdemeanor, a municipal court shall, by including a notification in the judgment document or by another effective manner, notify the defendant that the defendant may request the name and contact information of the government official or entity upon which service of a notice of appeal is required under ORS 221.374, with instruction on how to make such a request.

(2) Upon the defendant making a request described in this subsection, the municipal court shall provide the defendant with the name and contact information of the government official or entity upon which service of the notice of appeal is required. [2025 c.268 §37a]
