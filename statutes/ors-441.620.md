# Disclosure of business information required (Enforcement of Nursing Home Laws) (ORS 441.620)

## At a glance

ORS 441.620 — Disclosure of business information required (Enforcement of Nursing Home Laws). Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.620 Disclosure of business information required. Each facility shall disclose to the resident in writing its legal name and business address, and the name and business address of the administrator of the facility, at the time of admission of a resident. Information required to be disclosed by this section shall be kept current. [1979 c.261 §6] 441.623 [1987 c.428 §1; repealed by 2001 c.900 §261] (Enforcement of Nursing Home Laws)
