# Angling or hunting for compensation in violation of wildlife laws or rules prohibited (ORS 498.032)

## At a glance

ORS 498.032 — Angling or hunting for compensation in violation of wildlife laws or rules prohibited. Chapter 498 (Chapter 498), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

498.032 Angling or hunting for compensation in violation of wildlife laws or rules prohibited. No person shall angle for or hunt, or offer to angle for or hunt, for compensation, any wildlife in violation of any provision of the wildlife laws or any rule promulgated pursuant thereto. [1973 c.723 §78] 498.035 [Repealed by 1959 c.352 §5]
