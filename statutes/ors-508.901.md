## Full text

508.901 Fee; application form; rules.

(1) The annual fee for the vessel permit required by ORS 508.880 is: (a) $224 for resident applicants. (b) $280 for nonresident applicants.

(2) Applications shall be in such form and contain such information as the State Department of Fish and Wildlife, by rule, may prescribe.
