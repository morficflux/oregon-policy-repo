# Nursing Education Grant Fund GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 353.612)

## At a glance

ORS 353.612 — Nursing Education Grant Fund GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 353 (Chapter 353), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

353.612 Nursing Education Grant Fund.

(1) The Nursing Education Grant Fund is established separate and distinct from the General Fund. All moneys received by the Oregon Department of Administrative Services or Oregon Health and Science University for purposes of ORS 353.600 to 353.612 shall be deposited into the State Treasury and credited to the Nursing Education Grant Fund.

(2) Interest earned from the Nursing Education Grant Fund shall be credited to the fund.

(3) All moneys in the Nursing Education Grant Fund are continuously appropriated to the department for allocation to the university for the purposes of ORS 353.600 to 353.612. [2003 c.697 §3a] _______________
