## Full text

390.260 Application to Willamette River Greenway; restriction on condemnation to acquire lands.

(1) Nothing in ORS 390.250 to 390.260 applies to the Willamette River Greenway created pursuant to ORS 390.310 to 390.368.

(2) No land to which ORS 390.250 to 390.260 are applicable shall be acquired by the exercise of the power of eminent domain. [1973 c.668 §3] (Oregon Main Street Revitalization Grant Program)
