# Duties of ombudsman (ORS 195.322)

## At a glance

ORS 195.322 — Duties of ombudsman. Chapter 195 (Chapter 195), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

195.322 Duties of ombudsman.

(1) For the purpose of helping to ensure that a claim is complete, as described in ORS 195.312, the Compensation and Conservation Ombudsman may review a proposed claim if the review is requested by a claimant that intends to file a claim under ORS 195.305 and 195.310 to 195.314.

(2) At the request of the claimant or the public entity reviewing a claim, the ombudsman may facilitate resolution of issues involving a claim under ORS 195.305 to 195.336 and sections 5 to 11, chapter 424, Oregon Laws 2007, sections 2 to 9 and 17, chapter 855, Oregon Laws 2009, and sections 2 to 7, chapter 8, Oregon Laws 2010. [2007 c.424 §18]
