# Health benefit plan credentialing for applied behavior analysis providers CULTURAL COMPETENCY CONTINUING EDUCATION (ORS 676.830)

## At a glance

ORS 676.830 — Health benefit plan credentialing for applied behavior analysis providers CULTURAL COMPETENCY CONTINUING EDUCATION. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.830 Health benefit plan credentialing for applied behavior analysis providers. A health benefit plan as defined in ORS 743B.005 may establish credentialing requirements for the provision of applied behavior analysis by licensed health care professionals, behavior analysts or assistant behavior analysts licensed under ORS 676.810 or by behavior analysis interventionists registered by the Health Licensing Office under ORS 676.815. [2015 c.674 §17; 2023 c.500 §4] CULTURAL COMPETENCY CONTINUING EDUCATION
