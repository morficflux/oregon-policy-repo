# Consultation with certain groups on production and posting of signs (ORS 471.553)

## At a glance

ORS 471.553 — Consultation with certain groups on production and posting of signs. Chapter 471 (Alcoholic Liquors Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

471.553 Consultation with certain groups on production and posting of signs. The Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission shall consult with representatives of business and industry as well as interested citizens groups, including the March of Dimes and the Junior League, to determine the most cost-effective, convenient method to produce and post the sign described in ORS 471.551, which shall be distributed by the commission.
