# Definitions for ORS 357.805 to (ORS 357.805)

## At a glance

ORS 357.805 — Definitions for ORS 357.805 to. Chapter 357 (Chapter 357), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

357.805 Definitions for ORS 357.805 to 357.895. As used in ORS 357.805 to 357.895, unless the context requires otherwise, “photocopy,” “political subdivision,” “public record” and “state agency” are defined by ORS 192.005. [Formerly 358.005; 1999 c.55 §2] 357.810 [Renumbered 357.955]
