# Warehouseman’s reports to department (ORS 586.555)

## At a glance

ORS 586.555 — Warehouseman’s reports to department. Chapter 586 (Chapter 586), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

586.555 Warehouseman’s reports to department. Each warehouseman shall report information to the State Department of Agriculture at such times and as may be required by the department for the necessary enforcement and supervision of a sound, reasonable and efficient warehouse inspection program for the protection of depositors of grain and for persons or agencies who lend money against such grains. [1961 c.445 §21] 586.560 [Repealed by 1955 c.731 §34]
