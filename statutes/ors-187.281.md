# Organ, Eye and Tissue Donor Appreciation Week (ORS 187.281)

## At a glance

ORS 187.281 — Organ, Eye and Tissue Donor Appreciation Week. Chapter 187 (Chapter 187), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

187.281 Organ, Eye and Tissue Donor Appreciation Week. The second week of April of each year is designated as Organ, Eye and Tissue Donor Appreciation Week. [2018 c.13 §1]
