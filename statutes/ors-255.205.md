# Retention of petition materials (ORS 255.205)

## At a glance

ORS 255.205 — Retention of petition materials. Chapter 255 (Chapter 255), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

255.205 Retention of petition materials. The elections officer shall retain the signature sheets of a filed initiative or referendum petition with a copy of the district measure. If the measure is approved by the district electors, a copy of the measure shall be preserved as a permanent public record, and the signature sheets shall be preserved for six years.
