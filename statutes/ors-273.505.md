# Object of ORS (ORS 273.505)

## At a glance

ORS 273.505 — Object of ORS. Chapter 273 (Chapter 273), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

273.505 Object of ORS 273.511. The object of ORS 273.511 is to ascertain and determine the title of the State of Oregon to the beds of shallow and dry lakes and the lands around lakes exposed to reliction or otherwise. [1967 c.421 §76] 273.510 [Amended by 1967 c.421 §37; repealed by 1967 c.616 §29]
