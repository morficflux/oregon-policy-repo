# Nonapplicability of Uniform Controlled Substances Act (ORS 459A.257)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.257 — Nonapplicability of Uniform Controlled Substances Act. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.257 Nonapplicability of Uniform Controlled Substances Act. The provisions of the Uniform Controlled Substances Act do not apply to a program operator or authorized collector, insofar as the program operator is collecting, transporting and disposing of covered drugs pursuant to ORS 459A.200 to 459A.266. [2019 c.659 §20] Note: See note under 459A.200.
