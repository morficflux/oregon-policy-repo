# Provision of immunization against influenza virus (ORS 421.113)

## At a glance

ORS 421.113 — Provision of immunization against influenza virus. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.113 Provision of immunization against influenza virus. Each year, in accordance with medically accepted standards for timing and administration of immunization against the influenza virus:

(1) The Department of Corrections shall offer each inmate in the physical custody of the department immunization against the influenza virus at no charge to the inmate.

(2) The department shall schedule, for each inmate who does not decline the immunization in writing, an appointment to receive immunization against the influenza virus. An inmate may decline to receive the immunization at any time, including at the appointment. [2019 c.550 §1] Note: 421.113 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 421 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 421.115 [Repealed by 1955 c.532 §3]
