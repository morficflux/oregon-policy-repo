# Withdrawal of consent (ORS 109.216)

## At a glance

ORS 109.216 — Withdrawal of consent. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.216 Withdrawal of consent.

(1) An individual who consents to assisted reproduction, other than under a surrogacy agreement, may withdraw consent any time before a transfer that results in a pregnancy by giving written notice of the withdrawal of consent to the person who agreed to give birth to a child conceived by the assisted reproduction.

(2) An individual who withdraws consent under subsection

(1) of this section: (a) Is not a parent of a child conceived by the assisted reproduction under ORS 109.206 to 109.218; and (b) Is a donor if the individual provided the gametes that resulted in the pregnancy.

(3) An individual who withdraws consent as provided in this section shall provide a copy of the withdrawal to the clinic or health care provider facilitating the assisted reproduction. Failure to give notice to the clinic or health care provider does not affect the validity of the withdrawal. [2025 c.592 §61]
