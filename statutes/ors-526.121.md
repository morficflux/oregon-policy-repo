# Reimbursement of revolving account; keeping records (ORS 526.121)

## At a glance

ORS 526.121 — Reimbursement of revolving account; keeping records. Chapter 526 (Forestry Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

526.121 Reimbursement of revolving account; keeping records.

(1) Reimbursement vouchers for claims paid from the revolving account created by ORS 526.111 shall be approved by the forester. Warrants in payment of the vouchers shall be drawn in favor of the forester and deposited with the State Treasurer to reimburse the revolving account.

(2) The forester shall maintain such records as readily disclose the true status of vouchers payable from, and checks outstanding against, the revolving account and the balance to the credit thereof. [1953 c.327 §2; 1957 c.83 §23; 1965 c.253 §19; 1983 c.740 §207]
