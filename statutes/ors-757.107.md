# Supplemental budgets and orders (ORS 757.107)

## At a glance

ORS 757.107 — Supplemental budgets and orders. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.107 Supplemental budgets and orders. Adjustment and additions to such budget expenditures may be made from time to time during the year by filing supplementary budgets with the Public Utility Commission. The provisions of ORS 757.105

(3) apply to adjustments and additions to budgets. [Amended by 1971 c.655 §83]
