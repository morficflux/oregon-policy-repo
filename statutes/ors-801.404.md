# “Racing activity vehicle” (ORS 801.404)

## At a glance

ORS 801.404 — “Racing activity vehicle”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.404 “Racing activity vehicle.” “Racing activity vehicle” means a motor vehicle that:

(1) Is primarily used for racing on a race track and that has: (a) A bodiless tubular steel chassis that forms the main structural component of the vehicle; (b) High side rails; (c) Integral front and rear rollover tubes; (d) A suspension with both front and rear double unequal length wishbones and inboard, pushrod operated dampers; and (e) A product identification number instead of a vehicle identification number; and

(2) Is not a replica or an assembled vehicle. [2007 c.693 §2] 801.405 [1983 c.338 §75; 1985 c.402 §4; renumbered 801.408 in 2007]
