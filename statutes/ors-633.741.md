# Jackson County exemption from ORS (ORS 633.741)

## At a glance

ORS 633.741 — Jackson County exemption from ORS. Chapter 633 (Chapter 633), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

633.741 Jackson County exemption from ORS 633.738. ORS 633.738 does not apply to any local measure that was:

(1) Proposed by initiative petition and, on or before January 31, 2013, qualified for placement on the ballot in a county; and

(2) Approved by the electors of the county at an election held on May 20, 2014. [2013 s.s. c.4 §4] Note: 633.741 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 633 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
