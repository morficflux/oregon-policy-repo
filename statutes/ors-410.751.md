# Reports to Legislative Assembly (ORS 410.751)

## At a glance

ORS 410.751 — Reports to Legislative Assembly. Chapter 410 (Chapter 410), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

410.751 Reports to Legislative Assembly. During each odd-numbered year regular session of the Legislative Assembly, the Department of Human Services shall report to the Joint Committee on Ways and Means and the committees of the Legislative Assembly related to health or human services, in the manner provided in ORS 192.245, on the:

(1) Types of services provided under ORS 410.750;

(2) Number of individuals with brain injuries who received services described in ORS 410.750 (2);

(3) Barriers faced by individuals with brain injuries in accessing needed services; and

(4) Needs of individuals with brain injuries that are not being met by any known provider or other entity in this state. [2023 c.573 §2] 410.850 [1985 c.647 §2; repealed by 1987 c.523 §1 (410.851 enacted in lieu of 410.850)]
