## Full text

466.635 Report of spill or release of reportable quantity of hazardous material. Any person owning or having control over any oil or hazardous material who has knowledge of a spill or release shall immediately notify the Oregon Department of Emergency Management as soon as that person knows the spill or release is a reportable quantity. [1985 c.733 §7; 2007 c.740 §39; 2021 c.539 §76]
