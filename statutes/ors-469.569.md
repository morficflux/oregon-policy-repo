# Definitions for ORS (ORS 469.569)

## At a glance

ORS 469.569 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 469 (Chapter 469), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469.569 Definitions for ORS 469.566 to 469.583. As used in ORS 469.566 to 469.583:

(1) “Board” means the Oregon Hanford Cleanup Board.

(2) “High-level radioactive waste” means fuel or fission products from a commercial nuclear reactor after irradiation that is packaged and prepared for disposal.

(3) “United States Department of Energy” means the federal Department of Energy established under 42 U.S.C.A. 7131 or any successor agency assigned responsibility for the long-term disposal of high-level radioactive waste. [1987 c.514 §3; 2003 c.186 §34] Note: See note under 469.566. 469.570 [Formerly 453.575; 1995 c.505 §23; renumbered 469.563 in 1997]
