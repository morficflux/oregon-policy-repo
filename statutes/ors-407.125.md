## At a glance

ORS 407.125 — Loans to qualified person. Chapter 407 (Chapter 407), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

407.125 Loans to qualified person. All moneys in the Oregon War Veterans’ Fund created by ORS 407.495 may be advanced by the Department of Veterans’ Affairs as loans to any person qualified for loans under the provisions of Article XI-A, section 3, Oregon Constitution, for the acquisition of farms and homes, as provided in this chapter.
