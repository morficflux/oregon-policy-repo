# Issuance of subpoena by magistrate for witnesses at preliminary examination (ORS 136.557)

## At a glance

ORS 136.557 — Issuance of subpoena by magistrate for witnesses at preliminary examination. Chapter 136 (Chapter 136), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

136.557 Issuance of subpoena by magistrate for witnesses at preliminary examination. A magistrate before whom an information is laid or complaint made may issue subpoenas subscribed by the magistrate for witnesses within the state, either on behalf of the state or of the defendant. [Formerly 139.020] 136.560 [Amended by 1957 c.551 §1; 1959 c.302 §1; repealed by 1971 c.743 §432]
