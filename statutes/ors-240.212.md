# Management service (ORS 240.212)

## At a glance

ORS 240.212 — Management service. Chapter 240 (State Personnel Relations), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

240.212 Management service. The management service shall comprise all positions not in the unclassified or exempt service that have been determined to be confidential employees, supervisory employees or managerial employees, as defined in ORS 243.650. [1981 c.409 §6; 1995 c.286 §25]
