## At a glance

## Full text

417.723 Grant program to support alignment of systems. The Oregon Health Authority and the Early Learning Council shall establish a grant program to provide funding to support effective and scalable strategies that align statewide early learning systems and health systems for the purpose of improving the developmental outcomes for children from zero through three years of age. [2013 c.728 §9; 2021 c.631 §2] Note: See note under 417.721.
