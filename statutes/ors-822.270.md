## Full text

822.270 State Board of Towing Account.

(1) The State Board of Towing Account is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund.

(2) The account consists of the following: (a) Moneys collected from fees imposed under ORS 822.285. (b) Moneys appropriated to the account by the Legislative Assembly. (c) Moneys from any other source.

(3) Moneys in the account are continuously appropriated to the Department of Transportation to carry out the provisions of ORS 822.250 to 822.290 and 822.995. [2021 c.578 §6] Note: See note under 822.250.
