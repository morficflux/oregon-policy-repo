# Carrying of concealed weapons (ORS 166.240)

## At a glance

ORS 166.240 — Carrying of concealed weapons. Chapter 166 (Chapter 166), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

166.240 Carrying of concealed weapons.

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, any person who carries concealed upon the person any knife having a blade that projects or swings into position by force of a spring or by centrifugal force, any dirk, dagger, ice pick, slungshot, metal knuckles, or any similar instrument by the use of which injury could be inflicted upon the person or property of any other person, commits a Class B misdemeanor.

(2) Nothing in subsection

(1) of this section applies to any peace officer as defined in ORS 133.005, whose duty it is to serve process or make arrests. Justice courts have concurrent jurisdiction to try any person charged with violating any of the provisions of subsection

(1) of this section. [Amended by 1977 c.454 §1; 1985 c.543 §2; 1989 c.839 §21; 1999 c.1040 §15] 166.245 [1989 c.839 §38; repealed by 1995 s.s. c.1 §7]
