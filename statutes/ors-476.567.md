# Biennial report on State Fire Marshal Mobilization Fund (ORS 476.567)

## At a glance

ORS 476.567 — Biennial report on State Fire Marshal Mobilization Fund. Chapter 476 (State Fire Marshal; Protection From Fire Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

476.567 Biennial report on State Fire Marshal Mobilization Fund. On or before March 31 of each odd-numbered year, the Department of the State Fire Marshal shall report to the Joint Committee on Ways and Means, or to the interim Joint Committee on Ways and Means, on:

(1) The amounts appropriated, allocated, deposited or transferred to the State Fire Marshal Mobilization Fund, from any source, on or after July 1 of the previous odd-numbered year; and

(2) Actual and anticipated expenditures from the fund on or after July 1 of the previous odd-numbered year. [2025 c.178 §2] Note: 476.567 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 476 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
