## At a glance

## Full text

508.778 Prohibition on issuance of new permits. On or after March 27, 2024, new vessel permits may not be issued. [1979 c.679 §3; 1981 c.365 §40; 1989 c.940 §3; 1995 c.602 §5; 2024 c.48 §2]
