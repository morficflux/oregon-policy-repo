# Questions of livestock ownership and possession (ORS 599.273)

## At a glance

ORS 599.273 — Questions of livestock ownership and possession. Chapter 599 (Chapter 599), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

599.273 Questions of livestock ownership and possession. After livestock enter the premises of a livestock auction market, if a question of the right of ownership or possession arises, the livestock shall be subject to the provisions of ORS 604.056. [1967 c.368 §3; 1981 c.248 §17]
