# Certain sales practices prohibited (ORS 743.523)

## At a glance

ORS 743.523 — Certain sales practices prohibited. Chapter 743 (Chapter 743), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743.523 Certain sales practices prohibited.

(1) No person selling group health insurance is authorized to sell membership in an association, including a labor union, for the purpose of qualifying an applicant who is an individual for group health insurance.

(2) No person selling membership in an association, including a labor union, is authorized to offer group health insurance for the purpose of selling membership in the association. [1989 c.784 §10]
