# Diversity, equity and inclusion policy (ORS 284.372)

## At a glance

ORS 284.372 — Diversity, equity and inclusion policy. Chapter 284 (Organizations for Economic Development), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

284.372 Diversity, equity and inclusion policy. In addition to any applicable requirements under ORS 284.368, a company seeking reimbursement under ORS 284.368 must:

(1) Have a written diversity, equity and inclusion policy as of the date on which the company submits an application for reimbursement;

(2) In accordance with the written policy described in subsection

(1) of this section, actively engage in good faith efforts to hire or contract with individuals from underrepresented groups for the production for which the reimbursement is sought;

(3) Report diversity statistics related to the production to the Oregon Film and Video Office after completion of the production; and

(4) Establish a process for addressing claims of harassment, discrimination and other misconduct related to the production. [2021 c.247 §1] Note: 284.372 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 284 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
