## Full text

538.230 Vested water rights not affected; condemnation for park not prevented. ORS 538.220 shall not affect vested water rights or prevent condemnation for public park purposes of lands through which Mill and Barr Creeks or their tributaries flow. 538.240 [Repealed by 1963 c.95 §1] 538.250 [Repealed by 1953 c.222 §3]
