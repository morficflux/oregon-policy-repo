# Civil penalty for violations of chapter WEIGHT (ORS 818.450)

## At a glance

ORS 818.450 — Civil penalty for violations of chapter WEIGHT. Chapter 818 (Chapter 818), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

818.450 Civil penalty for violations of chapter. In addition to any penalty provided in a specific statute in this chapter, any person who violates a provision of this chapter is subject to civil penalty as provided in ORS 825.950. [1997 c.722 §3] _______________
