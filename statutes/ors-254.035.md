## At a glance

## Full text

254.035 Time and place of elections for city officers.

(1) It is the intention of the Legislative Assembly to carry out the provisions of section 14a, Article II, Oregon Constitution.

(2) All elections for city officers shall be held at the same time and place as elections for state and county officers. Unless a city charter or ordinance provides otherwise, the ballots used for state and county elections, if the county clerk considers it practicable, shall be arranged to include city offices and measures.
