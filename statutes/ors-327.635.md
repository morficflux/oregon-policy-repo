## Full text

327.635 Labor standards required on federally financed school construction. The Superintendent of Public Instruction shall provide, in the construction of school facilities financed in part through federal grants, for the enforcement of labor standards not less beneficial to employees on such projects than those required under sections 1 and 2 of the Act of Congress of August 30, 1935, as amended. 327.640 [Repealed by 1965 c.100 §456] FINANCING OF STATE AND FEDERAL REQUIREMENTS
