# Application of Uniform Child Custody Jurisdiction and Enforcement Act (ORS 107.707)

## At a glance

ORS 107.707 — Application of Uniform Child Custody Jurisdiction and Enforcement Act. Chapter 107 (Chapter 107), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

107.707 Application of Uniform Child Custody Jurisdiction and Enforcement Act. The Uniform Child Custody Jurisdiction and Enforcement Act, ORS 109.701 to 109.834, applies to proceedings under ORS 107.700 to 107.735. [2005 c.536 §5] Note: 107.707 was added to and made a part of 107.700 to 107.735 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
