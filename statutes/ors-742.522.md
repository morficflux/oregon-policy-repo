# Binding arbitration under ORS (ORS 742.522)

## At a glance

ORS 742.522 — Binding arbitration under ORS. Chapter 742 (Chapter 742), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

742.522 Binding arbitration under ORS 742.520; costs.

(1) Costs to the insured of the arbitration proceeding under ORS 742.520

(6) shall not exceed $100 and all other costs of arbitration shall be borne by the insurer.

(2) As used in this section, “costs” does not include attorney fees or expenses incurred in the production of evidence or witnesses or the making of transcripts of the arbitration proceedings. [Formerly 743.802; 2007 c.328 §9]
