# Scope (ORS 105.626)

## At a glance

ORS 105.626 — Scope. Chapter 105 (Chapter 105), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

105.626 Scope. ORS 105.623 to 105.649 apply to disclaimers of any interest in or power over property without regard to when the interest or power that is disclaimed was created. [2001 c.245 §3] 105.627 [1975 c.622 §1; 1981 c.56 §1; repealed by 2001 c.245 §19]
