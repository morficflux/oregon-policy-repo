# Definitions for ORS (ORS 182.456)

## At a glance

ORS 182.456 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 182 (State Administrative Agencies Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

182.456 Definitions for ORS 182.456 to 182.472. As used in ORS 182.456 to 182.472:

(1) “Board” means a semi-independent state agency listed in ORS 182.454.

(2) “License” includes licenses, registrations, certifications, permits or other forms of permission required by law to pursue an occupation or engage in a business regulated by a board. [1999 c.1084 §§4,55; 2003 c.686 §§14,15; 2007 c.71 §60] Note: See note under 182.454.
