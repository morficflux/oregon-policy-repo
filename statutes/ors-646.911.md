# Sale of gasoline with oxygenate other than ethanol (ORS 646.911)

## At a glance

ORS 646.911 — Sale of gasoline with oxygenate other than ethanol. Chapter 646 (Chapter 646), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646.911 Sale of gasoline with oxygenate other than ethanol. Notwithstanding ORS 646.910, a person may sell, supply or offer to sell or supply gasoline in this state that contains any oxygenate other than ethanol, if the California Air Resources Board, the California Environmental Policy Council or the United States Environmental Protection Agency allows the use of the oxygenate. [2007 c.739 §23] 646.912 [2007 c.739 §17; repealed by 2015 c.203 §28]
