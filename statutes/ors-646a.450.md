# Rules; investigative powers of department (ORS 646A.450)

## At a glance

ORS 646A.450 — Rules; investigative powers of department. Chapter 646A (Chapter 646A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646A.450 Rules; investigative powers of department.

(1) The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may adopt rules to implement and enforce ORS 646A.430 to 646A.450.

(2) The director may investigate warrantors or other persons as reasonably necessary to enforce ORS 646A.430 to 646A.450 and to protect consumers in this state. [2007 c.685 §11]
