# Immunity for reporting in good faith (ORS 686.465)

## At a glance

ORS 686.465 — Immunity for reporting in good faith. Chapter 686 (Chapter 686), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

686.465 Immunity for reporting in good faith. A veterinarian who acts in good faith and has reasonable grounds for making a report of suspected aggravated animal abuse under ORS 686.455 is not liable in any civil or criminal proceeding brought as a result of making the report. [2003 c.275 §5] Note: See note under 686.450.
