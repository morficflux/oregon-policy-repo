# Use of contractor (ORS 830.940)

## At a glance

ORS 830.940 — Use of contractor. Chapter 830 (Small Watercraft _______________ 2025 EDITION SMALL WATERCRAFT SMALL WATERCRAFT GENERAL PROVISIONS 830.005 Definitions for chapter 830.015 Application of ORS 83), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

830.940 Use of contractor. An authorized agency may enter into a contract with any person to carry out the provisions of ORS 830.908 to 830.948 on behalf of the authorized agency. [2013 c.680 §11; 2025 c.90 §9]
