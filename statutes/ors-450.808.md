# General duties of authority manager (ORS 450.808)

## At a glance

ORS 450.808 — General duties of authority manager. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.808 General duties of authority manager. The manager of the authority shall, subject to the direction and control of the board, serve as the administrator and supervisor of the functions and operations of the authority and shall perform all duties prescribed by the board. [1955 c.614 §24]
