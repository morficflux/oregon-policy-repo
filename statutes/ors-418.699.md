# Additional duties or liabilities not imposed on youth sports providers (ORS 418.699)

## At a glance

ORS 418.699 — Additional duties or liabilities not imposed on youth sports providers. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.699 Additional duties or liabilities not imposed on youth sports providers. Nothing in ORS 418.691 to 418.701 imposes any additional duty or liability on any youth sports provider by reason of the youth sports provider not performing a duty that is encouraged by ORS 418.696. [2001 c.550 §4] Note: See note under 418.691.
