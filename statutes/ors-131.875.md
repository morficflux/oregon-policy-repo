# Effect of appointment of special officers on salary of regular officers (ORS 131.875)

## At a glance

ORS 131.875 — Effect of appointment of special officers on salary of regular officers. Chapter 131 (Preliminary Provisions; Limitations; Jurisdiction; Venue; Criminal Forfeiture; Crime Prevention), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

131.875 Effect of appointment of special officers on salary of regular officers. The regularly elected, qualified and acting officers shall, during any appointment of a special officer, receive the salary provided by law, to the same extent as though no special officer had been appointed. [Formerly 148.180]
