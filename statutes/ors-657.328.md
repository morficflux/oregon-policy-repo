# Eligibility period adopted by rule (ORS 657.328)

## At a glance

ORS 657.328 — Eligibility period adopted by rule. Chapter 657 (Chapter 657), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

657.328 Eligibility period adopted by rule. For purposes of ORS 657.321 to 657.329, the Employment Department may adopt rules providing for an eligibility period that begins in an extended benefit period after an individual’s benefit year has ended if federal law provides for funding that is greater than 50 percent of extended benefits. [2010 c.6 §6] Note: 657.328 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 657 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
