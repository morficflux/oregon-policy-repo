# Printing inaugural address of Governor PUBLIC PRINTING GENERALLY (ORS 282.150)

## At a glance

## Full text

282.150 Printing inaugural address of Governor. At the inauguration of a Governor, the Oregon Department of Administrative Services shall cause to be printed such number of copies of the inaugural address as the Governor-elect directs. [Amended by 1957 c.230 §1; 1975 c.605 §10; 2001 c.539 §9] PUBLIC PRINTING GENERALLY
