# Filing boundary change with county assessor and Department of Revenue (ORS 450.988)

## At a glance

ORS 450.988 — Filing boundary change with county assessor and Department of Revenue. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.988 Filing boundary change with county assessor and Department of Revenue. For purposes of ad valorem taxation, a boundary change must be filed in final approved form with the county assessor and the Department of Revenue as provided in ORS 308.225. [2001 c.138 §32]
