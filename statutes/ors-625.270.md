# Unlawful sale prohibited MISCELLANEOUS (ORS 625.270)

## At a glance

ORS 625.270 — Unlawful sale prohibited MISCELLANEOUS. Chapter 625 (Chapter 625), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

625.270 Unlawful sale prohibited. A person may not sell, offer for sale, hold for sale or bake a loaf of bread in violation of the provisions of ORS 625.010 to 625.270 or orders thereunder.
