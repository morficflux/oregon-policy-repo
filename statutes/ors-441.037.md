## Full text

441.037 Hearings; procedures; judicial review.

(1) When the Oregon Health Authority or the Department of Human Services proposes to refuse to issue or renew a license, or proposes to revoke or suspend a license, opportunity for hearing shall be accorded as provided in ORS chapter 183.

(2) Adoption of rules, conduct of hearings, issuance of orders and judicial review of rules and orders shall be in accordance with ORS chapter 183. [1971 c.734 §56; 1977 c.582 §47; 1987 c.428 §7; 2001 c.900 §165; 2009 c.595 §724]
