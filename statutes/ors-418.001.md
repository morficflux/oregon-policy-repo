# Definition for ORS (ORS 418.001)

## At a glance

ORS 418.001 — Definition for ORS. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.001 Definition for ORS 418.005 to 418.030. As used in ORS 418.005 to 418.030, “child” or “juvenile” means an individual under 21 years of age. [1973 c.827 §34] 418.003 [1973 c.463 §§2,3; repealed by 2001 c.900 §261]
