# Statute revision of 1953 substituted for statutes repealed by ORS (ORS 174.550)

## At a glance

ORS 174.550 — Statute revision of 1953 substituted for statutes repealed by ORS. Chapter 174 (Construction of Statutes; General Definitions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

174.550 Statute revision of 1953 substituted for statutes repealed by ORS 174.520. The provisions of the statute laws described in ORS 174.510

(1) are considered as substituted in a continuing way for the provisions of the prior statute laws repealed by ORS 174.520. [1953 c.3 §5; 1961 c.90 §6] 174.560 [1953 c.3 §8; repealed by 1961 c.90 §7]
