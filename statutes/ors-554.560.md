# Quorum for meeting (ORS 554.560)

## At a glance

ORS 554.560 — Quorum for meeting. Chapter 554 (Chapter 554), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

554.560 Quorum for meeting. Notwithstanding the provision of ORS 554.070

(2) providing for a quorum to do business, the members of the corporation present shall constitute a quorum to approve or reject the application to include land in, or exclude land from, the corporation. [1963 c.103 §7]
