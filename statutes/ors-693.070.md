# License number; issue and expiration dates (ORS 693.070)

## At a glance

ORS 693.070 — License number; issue and expiration dates. Chapter 693 (Chapter 693), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

693.070 License number; issue and expiration dates. All licenses shall bear:

(1) The date of issue and date of expiration; and

(2) An identification number assigned by the State Plumbing Board.
