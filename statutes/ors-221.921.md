# Interest of officers in city contracts (ORS 221.921)

## At a glance

ORS 221.921 — Interest of officers in city contracts. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.921 Interest of officers in city contracts. No mayor, council member or any other officer of any city referred to in ORS 221.906, during the period for which the officer is elected, shall be interested in any contract the expenses of which are to be paid out of the city treasury. 221.922 [Repealed by 1983 c.350 §331a] 221.923 [Amended by 1961 c.290 §1; repealed by 2011 c.597 §309]
