# Separate accounting for funds (ORS 293.756)

## At a glance

ORS 293.756 — Separate accounting for funds. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.756 Separate accounting for funds. The investment officer shall keep, for each fund included in the investment funds for which investments are made, a separate account, which shall record the individual amounts and the totals of all investments of moneys in the fund. [1967 c.335 §13] 293.760 [Formerly 291.616; repealed by 1967 c.335 §60]
