# Successor bound by agreement (ORS 474.025)

## At a glance

ORS 474.025 — Successor bound by agreement. Chapter 474 (Chapter 474), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

474.025 Successor bound by agreement. A successor to a supplier or wholesaler, whether by way of merger, purchase of corporate shares, purchase of assets or otherwise, shall be bound by each distribution agreement the predecessor was a party to at the time of transfer with respect to each brand the successor continues to make available for sale in this state. [1989 c.529 §5] 474.030 [Repealed by 1977 c.745 §54]
