# Services for state agencies and political subdivisions (ORS 662.435)

## At a glance

ORS 662.435 — Services for state agencies and political subdivisions. Chapter 662 (Chapter 662), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

662.435 Services for state agencies and political subdivisions. The services and facilities of the State Conciliation Service and the conciliator shall be made available to the State of Oregon or any of its agencies, boards, commissions or other branches or any of the political subdivisions of the state and to the public employees of the State of Oregon in all its agencies, boards, commissions or other branches or its political subdivisions in the same manner as such facilities are available to private employers and their employees. [1957 c.122 §4; 1959 c.184 §1; 1969 c.671 §15] 662.440 [Repealed by 1957 c.122 §5]
