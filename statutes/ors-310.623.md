## At a glance

ORS 310.623 — Floating home eligible as homestead (Property Tax Work-Off Programs). Chapter 310 (Chapter 310), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

310.623 Floating home eligible as homestead. A floating home, as defined in ORS 830.700, assessed under the property tax laws of this state shall be eligible to be a homestead for the purposes of all tax laws of this state giving a right or privilege to a homestead.
