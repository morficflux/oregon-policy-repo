# Enumeration of duties not exclusive (ORS 420A.014)

## At a glance

ORS 420A.014 — Enumeration of duties not exclusive. Chapter 420A (Chapter 420A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

420A.014 Enumeration of duties not exclusive. The enumeration of duties, functions and powers in ORS 420A.010 is not intended to be exclusive nor limit the duties, functions and powers imposed on or vested in the Oregon Youth Authority by other statutes. [1995 c.422 §3]
