# Possession of a false law enforcement identification card (ORS 162.369)

## At a glance

## Full text

162.369 Possession of a false law enforcement identification card.

(1) A person commits the crime of possession of a false law enforcement identification card if the person possesses a false law enforcement identification card.

(2) Possession of a false law enforcement identification card is a Class A misdemeanor.

(3) As used in this section, “false law enforcement identification card” means an identification card that: (a) Identifies the possessor of the card as a member of a law enforcement unit; and (b) Was not lawfully issued to the possessor by the law enforcement unit. [1993 c.243 §3] Note: See note under 162.367. 162.370 [Repealed by 1961 c.649 §9]
