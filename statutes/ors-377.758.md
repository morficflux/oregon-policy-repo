# Notification by federal authorities of illegal sign; consequences (ORS 377.758)

## At a glance

ORS 377.758 — Notification by federal authorities of illegal sign; consequences. Chapter 377 (Chapter 377), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

377.758 Notification by federal authorities of illegal sign; consequences. If appropriate federal authorities notify the Department of Transportation that the erection of a sign pursuant to any of the provisions of ORS 377.756 to 377.758 is contrary to any federal law, the department shall cease issuing permits and shall cause any signs erected pursuant to ORS 377.756 to 377.758 to be removed. [1987 c.631 §5; 2001 c.104 §128]
