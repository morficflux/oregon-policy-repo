# Criminal penalties (ORS 345.990)

## At a glance

ORS 345.990 — Criminal penalties. Chapter 345 (Chapter 345), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

345.990 Criminal penalties. Violation of any provision of ORS 345.020, 345.030, 345.070, 345.115 or 345.325 is a Class B misdemeanor. [Amended by 1975 c.478 §26; subsection

(2) enacted as 1975 c.557 §9; 1993 c.45 §280; 2011 c.301 §8]
