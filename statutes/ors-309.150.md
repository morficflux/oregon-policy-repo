# Appeals of personal property value upon summary or accelerated collection of taxes (ORS 309.150)

## At a glance

## Full text

309.150 Appeals of personal property value upon summary or accelerated collection of taxes. Appeals of the value of personal property, on which the tax is required to be paid as provided in ORS 311.465 and 311.480, shall be heard by a property value appeals board in the same manner that other assessments of property are heard. [Amended by 1975 c.365 §2; 1981 c.804 §22; 1991 c.459 §201; 1995 c.226 §12; 1997 c.541 §238; 2023 c.29 §31] 309.160 [1979 c.241 §32; 1981 c.804 §1; 1983 s.s. c.5 §18; repealed by 1985 c.613 §31]
