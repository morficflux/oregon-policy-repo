# Ex officio members of Oregon Hemp Commission (ORS 571.408)

## At a glance

ORS 571.408 — Ex officio members of Oregon Hemp Commission. Chapter 571 (Chapter 571), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

571.408 Ex officio members of Oregon Hemp Commission. The Director of Agriculture and the Dean of the College of Agricultural Sciences of Oregon State University are ex officio nonvoting members of the Oregon Hemp Commission. [2021 c.216 §5] Note: See note under 571.400.
