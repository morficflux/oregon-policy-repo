## Full text

586.415 Approval of holder of receipt required before sale, shipment or other transaction. No warehouseman shall sell, encumber, ship, transfer or in any manner remove or permit to be shipped, transferred or removed from a warehouse any grain received by the warehouseman for deposit or handling, without the written approval of the holder of the warehouse receipt or load slip.
