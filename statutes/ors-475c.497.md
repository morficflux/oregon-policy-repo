# Exceptions from applicability of ORS 475C.005 to (ORS 475C.497)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.497 — Exceptions from applicability of ORS 475C.005 to. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.497 Exceptions from applicability of ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525. Except for ORS 475C.489 and 475C.493, ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525:

(1) Do not apply to the extent a person acts within the scope of and in compliance with the Oregon Medical Marijuana Act; and

(2) Do not amend or affect duties, functions and powers of the Oregon Health Authority under the Oregon Medical Marijuana Act. [Formerly 475B.531]
