# Requirement that licensee be qualified to conduct business in this state LICENSING (ORS 725.110)

## At a glance

ORS 725.110 — Requirement that licensee be qualified to conduct business in this state LICENSING. Chapter 725 (Chapter 725), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

725.110 Requirement that licensee be qualified to conduct business in this state. A license shall not be granted to any person under this chapter unless the person is legally qualified to conduct business in this state. [Amended by 1999 c.469 §1] LICENSING
