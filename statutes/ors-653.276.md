# Economic forecast to include economic and wage data relating to state agricultural economy (ORS 653.276)

## At a glance

ORS 653.276 — Economic forecast to include economic and wage data relating to state agricultural economy. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.276 Economic forecast to include economic and wage data relating to state agricultural economy. The division of the Oregon Department of Administrative Services that serves as the office of economic analysis shall include in the economic forecast for a biennium any available relevant economic and wage data that relates to the agricultural economy in this state as impacted by the operation of ORS 315.133 and 653.272. [2022 c.115 §6b] Note: 653.276 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 653 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
