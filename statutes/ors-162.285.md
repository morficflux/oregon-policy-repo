# Tampering with a witness (ORS 162.285)

## At a glance

ORS 162.285 — Tampering with a witness. Chapter 162 (Chapter 162), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

162.285 Tampering with a witness.

(1) A person commits the crime of tampering with a witness if: (a) The person knowingly induces or attempts to induce a witness or a person the person believes may be called as a witness in any official proceeding to offer false testimony or unlawfully withhold any testimony; or (b) The person knowingly induces or attempts to induce a witness to be absent from any official proceeding to which the person has been legally summoned.

(2) Tampering with a witness is a Class C felony. [1971 c.743 §203; 1979 c.231 §1]
