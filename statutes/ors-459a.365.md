# City and county regulation of collection of solid waste SPECIFIC RECYCLING REQUIREMENTS (State Agencies) (ORS 459A.365)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.365 — City and county regulation of collection of solid waste SPECIFIC RECYCLING REQUIREMENTS (State Agencies). Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.365 City and county regulation of collection of solid waste. ORS 459A.305 to 459A.355 do not supersede any authority under ORS chapter 459 or 459A for cities and counties to regulate the collection of solid waste. [2007 c.302 §14a] Note: See note under 459A.300. SPECIFIC RECYCLING REQUIREMENTS (State Agencies)
