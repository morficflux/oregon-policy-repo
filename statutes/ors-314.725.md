# Privilege tax applicable to partnerships (ORS 314.725)

## At a glance

ORS 314.725 — Privilege tax applicable to partnerships. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.725 Privilege tax applicable to partnerships. Each partnership transacting business in this state shall, for the privilege of carrying on or doing business by it within this state, include with the filing of the return required under ORS 314.724 payment of a minimum tax of $150. [2009 c.745 §3]
