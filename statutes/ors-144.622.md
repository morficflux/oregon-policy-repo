## Full text

144.622 “Parole” and “parolee” defined for Uniform Act for Out-of-State Supervision. For purposes of ORS 144.610 and 144.613 to 144.617, “parole” includes but is not limited to post-prison supervision, and “parolee” includes but is not limited to persons on post-prison supervision under rules adopted by the Oregon Criminal Justice Commission. [1989 c.790 §37] Note: 144.622 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 144 or any series therein by legislative action.
