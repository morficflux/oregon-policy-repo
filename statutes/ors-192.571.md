# No right of action (ORS 192.571)

## At a glance

ORS 192.571 — No right of action. Chapter 192 (Records; Reports and Meetings), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

192.571 No right of action. Nothing in ORS 192.556 or 192.558 may be construed to create a new private right of action against a health care provider or a state health plan. [Formerly 192.524] Note: See note under 192.553.
