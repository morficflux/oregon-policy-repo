# Working hours, holidays, leaves of absence and vacations of employees in state classified service (ORS 240.551)

## At a glance

ORS 240.551 — Working hours, holidays, leaves of absence and vacations of employees in state classified service. Chapter 240 (State Personnel Relations), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

240.551 Working hours, holidays, leaves of absence and vacations of employees in state classified service. The Personnel Division shall establish the hours of work, holidays, leaves of absence with and without pay and vacations of employees in the state classified service. The division may delegate this responsibility to individual operating agencies where appropriate. [1979 c.468 §21]
