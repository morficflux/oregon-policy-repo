# Retirement benefits not to be funded for individual not employee of district when membership in system created BENEFITS FOR DISTRICT VOLUNTEERS (ORS 478.370)

## At a glance

ORS 478.370 — Retirement benefits not to be funded for individual not employee of district when membership in system created BENEFITS FOR DISTRICT VOLUNTEERS. Chapter 478 (Chapter 478), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

478.370 Retirement benefits not to be funded for individual not employee of district when membership in system created. Nothing in ORS 478.325 and 478.355 to 478.370 authorizes the district to budget, provide for payments or collect contributions to fund retirement benefits for an individual who is not in the employment of the district at the time of the creation of a membership status under a retirement plan. [1963 c.366 §7] BENEFITS FOR DISTRICT VOLUNTEERS
