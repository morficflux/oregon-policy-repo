# Terms of seed purchase contract (ORS 576.792)

## At a glance

ORS 576.792 — Terms of seed purchase contract. Chapter 576 (Agricultural Marketing Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

576.792 Terms of seed purchase contract.

(1) The terms of a seed purchase contract must include: (a) The estimated date for seed delivery; (b) The terms and estimated date for the seed dealer to pay the seed grower; (c) The amount of other seed to be purchased; and (d) The species, cultivars and quality standards of the other seed to be purchased.

(2) If the seed purchase contract does not settle the price of the other seed, the contract is enforceable and price shall be determined as described in ORS 72.3050

(1) to (3). A seed purchase contract must require the seed dealer to make payment to the seed grower within 30 days after seed delivery. However, upon written mutual agreement of the seed grower and the seed dealer, the grower may extend the period available for the dealer to make payment.
