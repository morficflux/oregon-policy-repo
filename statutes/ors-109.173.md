# Attorney fees (Genetic Testing) (ORS 109.173)

## At a glance

ORS 109.173 — Attorney fees (Genetic Testing). Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.173 Attorney fees. In any proceeding brought to modify or compel compliance with an order of the court issued under ORS 109.124 to 109.171, the court may render judgment awarding to a party, or directly to the party’s attorney, a sum of money determined to be reasonable as an attorney fee and costs and expenses of suit, which judgment may include expert witness fees, in preparation for and at trial and on appeal. [Formerly 109.237] Note: 109.173 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 109 or any series therein by legislative action.
