## At a glance

## Full text

179.370 Residence of superintendents at institutions. The Director of the Department of Corrections or the Director of the Oregon Health Authority may require that a superintendent reside in state-provided housing at the institution under the jurisdiction of the superintendent. The rental shall be determined pursuant to ORS 182.425. [Amended by 1959 c.80 §1; 1969 c.597 §35; 1977 c.583 §1; 1987 c.320 §120a; 1989 c.171 §21; 2009 c.595 §134; 2013 c.36 §42] (Staff)
