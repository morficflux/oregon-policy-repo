# Improvement without permit or contrary to permit conditions as public nuisance (ORS 390.661)

## At a glance

ORS 390.661 — Improvement without permit or contrary to permit conditions as public nuisance. Chapter 390 (Chapter 390), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

390.661 Improvement without permit or contrary to permit conditions as public nuisance. The improvement on any property within the ocean shore without a permit issued under ORS 390.650 or 390.657, or in a manner contrary to the conditions set out in the permit, is a public nuisance. [1999 c.373 §12; 2025 c.494 §5]
