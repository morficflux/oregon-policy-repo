# Rate recovery bond not security for certain purposes (ORS 757.465)

## At a glance

ORS 757.465 — Rate recovery bond not security for certain purposes. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.465 Rate recovery bond not security for certain purposes. A rate recovery bond, as described under ORS 757.457, 757.459, 757.461 and 757.463, that is exempt under ORS 59.025 from ORS 59.049 and 59.055 is not a security for purposes of ORS 59.115, 59.135 or 59.137. [2023 c.529 §7] Note: 757.465 and 757.467 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 757 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
