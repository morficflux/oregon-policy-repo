# Oregon Health Authority duties regarding behavioral health care workforce training, endorsements, certifications (ORS 675.650)

## At a glance

## Full text

675.650 Oregon Health Authority duties regarding behavioral health care workforce training, endorsements, certifications. The Oregon Health Authority shall create workforce training and establish endorsements or certifications for behavioral health providers of co-occurring disorder treatment. [2021 c.667 §10] Note: 675.650, 675.653, 675.655, 675.658, 675.661 and 675.664 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 675 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
