# Execution for payment of fines and costs (Seizure and Disposition of Unlawful Equipment and Fish Unlawfully Taken) (ORS 506.635)

## At a glance

ORS 506.635 — Execution for payment of fines and costs (Seizure and Disposition of Unlawful Equipment and Fish Unlawfully Taken). Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.635 Execution for payment of fines and costs. Any fishing gear used in violation of the commercial fishing laws, including boats or vehicles of any kind and other appliances, is subject to execution for the payment of fines and costs provided for in the commercial fishing laws.
