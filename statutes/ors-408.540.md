# Use of buildings by veterans’ organizations (ORS 408.540)

## At a glance

ORS 408.540 — Use of buildings by veterans’ organizations. Chapter 408 (Chapter 408), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

408.540 Use of buildings by veterans’ organizations. All buildings acquired or constructed by county courts pursuant to chapter 595, Oregon Laws 1947, shall be made available for the use of any veterans’ organizations and their auxiliaries located in the city, town or community where any such building is located. 408.550 [Repealed by 1961 c.454 §213]
