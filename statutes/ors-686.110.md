# Renewal of licenses or permits (ORS 686.110)

## At a glance

ORS 686.110 — Renewal of licenses or permits. Chapter 686 (Chapter 686), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

686.110 Renewal of licenses or permits.

(1) A license or permit shall be renewed when the holder: (a) Requests renewal; (b) Pays a fee to the Oregon State Veterinary Medical Examining Board to be determined pursuant to ORS 686.255; and (c) Presents evidence of participation in approved professional educational activities during the previous year to the extent established by the board.

(2) Upon failure to pay renewal fees due to absence from the state or for other reasons, a license may be renewed upon payment of late fees set by the board. [Amended by 1963 c.133 §1; 1967 c.191 §2; 1975 c.619 §4; 1979 c.65 §1; 1979 c.743 §1; 1987 c.651 §6; 1993 c.491 §5]
