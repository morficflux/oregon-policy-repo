## Full text

814.200 Unlawful operation of motorcycle or moped; penalty.

(1) A person operating a moped or motorcycle commits the offense of unlawful moped or motorcycle operation if the person: (a) Fails to sit astride, or to stand astride, the moped or motorcycle seat while facing forward; or (b) Carries a package, bundle or other article that prevents the person from keeping both hands on the handlebars.

(2) The offense described in this section, unlawful moped or motorcycle operation, is a Class B traffic violation. [1983 c.338 §686; 1987 c.138 §6; 2015 c.502 §1]
