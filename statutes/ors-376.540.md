# Logging roads MISCELLANEOUS WAYS (ORS 376.540)

## At a glance

ORS 376.540 — Logging roads MISCELLANEOUS WAYS. Chapter 376 (Chapter 376), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

376.540 Logging roads. Any logging road which is necessary for the transportation of a single tract of timber is within ORS 376.505 to 376.540, whether it is a common carrier or otherwise. Such road is not under the jurisdiction of the Department of Transportation unless the owners thereof declare it a common carrier.
