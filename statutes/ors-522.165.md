## Full text

522.165 Request for permit modification; fee.

(1) A permittee must make a request to modify a permit before changing the location, number or designation specified for any geothermal well or before undertaking to alter in any manner the casing of a geothermal well.

(2) A request to modify a permit under this section, except for changes to a well name, shall be accompanied by the nonrefundable fee specified in ORS 522.115. [1975 c.552 §10; 2009 c.794 §16] 522.170 [1971 c.776 §5; repealed by 1975 c.552 §55]
