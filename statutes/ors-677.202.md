# When procedure inapplicable (ORS 677.202)

## At a glance

ORS 677.202 — When procedure inapplicable. Chapter 677 (Chapter 677), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

677.202 When procedure inapplicable. ORS 677.200 does not apply in cases where the license of a person to practice under this chapter has been suspended automatically as provided in ORS 677.225. [1967 c.470 §33; 1983 c.486 §24; 1989 c.830 §12; 1991 c.485 §6]
