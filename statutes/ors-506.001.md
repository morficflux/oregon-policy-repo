# Contents of commercial fishing laws (ORS 506.001)

## At a glance

ORS 506.001 — Contents of commercial fishing laws. Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.001 Contents of commercial fishing laws. All laws enacted for the protection, propagation and preservation of food fish or for the protection and development of commercial fisheries in this state, including but not limited to ORS chapters 506, 507, 508, 509, 511 and 513, may be cited as the commercial fishing laws. [1965 c.570 §2] 506.005 [Repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
