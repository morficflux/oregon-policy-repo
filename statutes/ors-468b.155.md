# State goal to prevent ground water contamination (ORS 468B.155)

## At a glance

ORS 468B.155 — State goal to prevent ground water contamination. Chapter 468B (Chapter 468B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468B.155 State goal to prevent ground water contamination. The Legislative Assembly declares that it is the goal of the people of the State of Oregon to prevent contamination of Oregon’s ground water resource while striving to conserve and restore this resource and to maintain the high quality of Oregon’s ground water resource for present and future uses. [Formerly 468.692] Note: See note under 468B.150.
