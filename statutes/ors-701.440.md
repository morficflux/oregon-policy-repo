# Applicability to federal projects SPECIAL TRADES (Home Inspectors) (ORS 701.440)

## At a glance

ORS 701.440 — Applicability to federal projects SPECIAL TRADES (Home Inspectors). Chapter 701 (Chapter 701), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

701.440 Applicability to federal projects. ORS 279C.555, 701.410, 701.420 and 701.430 do not apply when the owner is the United States or any agency thereof or when the construction is paid for, in whole or in part, with federal moneys. [1975 c.772 §6; 2003 c.794 §321] Note: See note under 701.410. SPECIAL TRADES (Home Inspectors)
