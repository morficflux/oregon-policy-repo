# Return of instrument after recordation (ORS 205.238)

## At a glance

ORS 205.238 — Return of instrument after recordation. Chapter 205 (Chapter 205), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

205.238 Return of instrument after recordation. In every county, the county clerk shall return any instrument presented for recording to the person authorized to receive the instrument. The county clerk shall return the instrument by personally delivering or mailing the instrument not later than the 10th business day, not counting days on which the recording office is closed, after the date of recordation. [1991 c.230 §6] 205.240 [1957 c.669 §§1,2; repealed by 1971 c.267 §16]
