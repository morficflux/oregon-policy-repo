# Chapter does not apply to certain trusts CLAIMS AGAINST ESTATES (ORS 115.335)

## At a glance

ORS 115.335 — Chapter does not apply to certain trusts CLAIMS AGAINST ESTATES. Chapter 115 (Chapter 115), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

115.335 Chapter does not apply to certain trusts. The provisions of this chapter do not apply to claims against trusts that are subject to the provisions of ORS 130.350 to 130.450.
