# License to practice music therapy; fees (ORS 681.710)

## At a glance

ORS 681.710 — License to practice music therapy; fees. Chapter 681 (Chapter 681), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

681.710 License to practice music therapy; fees. The Health Licensing Office shall issue a license to practice music therapy to an applicant who:

(1) Is at least 18 years of age;

(2) Is in good standing in any other states where the applicant is licensed or certified to practice music therapy;

(3) Submits sufficient proof, as determined by the office, of: (a) Having passed the Certification Board for Music Therapists examination; (b) Current certification with the Certification Board for Music Therapists; or (c) A professional designation of “registered music therapist,” “certified music therapist” or “advanced certified music therapist” issued by the National Music Therapy Registry;

(4) Pays a licensure fee; and

(5) Meets other qualifications specified by the office under ORS 681.730. [2015 c.632 §2] Note: See note under 681.700.
