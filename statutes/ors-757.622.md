# Commission to establish terms and conditions for default electricity service to nonresidential consumers (ORS 757.622)

## At a glance

ORS 757.622 — Commission to establish terms and conditions for default electricity service to nonresidential consumers. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.622 Commission to establish terms and conditions for default electricity service to nonresidential consumers. The Public Utility Commission shall establish the terms and conditions for providing default electricity service for nonresidential electricity consumers in an emergency. The commission also shall establish reasonable terms and conditions for providing default service to a nonresidential electricity consumer in circumstances when the consumer is receiving electricity services through direct access and elects instead to receive such services through the default service. The terms and conditions for default service established by the commission shall provide for viable competition among electricity service suppliers.
