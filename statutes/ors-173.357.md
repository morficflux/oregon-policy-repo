# Disposition of moneys collected or received by commission LEGISLATIVE FISCAL OFFICER (ORS 173.357)

## At a glance

ORS 173.357 — Disposition of moneys collected or received by commission LEGISLATIVE FISCAL OFFICER. Chapter 173 (Chapter 173), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

173.357 Disposition of moneys collected or received by commission. All moneys collected or received by the Oregon Law Commission shall be paid into the General Fund of the State Treasury. Such moneys are continuously appropriated for and shall be used by the commission in carrying out the purposes for which the funds are received. [1997 c.661 §11] LEGISLATIVE FISCAL OFFICER
