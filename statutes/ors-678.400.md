## At a glance

## Full text

678.400 Disciplinary action for failure to maintain good academic standing. In addition to the grounds listed in ORS 678.111, the Oregon State Board of Nursing may revoke or suspend a nurse internship license issued under ORS 678.392 if the nurse intern is not in good academic standing, as determined by the board. [2022 c.38 §6] (Fees)
