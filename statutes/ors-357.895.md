# Rules STATE REFERENCE COORDINATING COUNCIL (ORS 357.895)

## At a glance

ORS 357.895 — Rules STATE REFERENCE COORDINATING COUNCIL. Chapter 357 (Chapter 357), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

357.895 Rules. In accordance with ORS chapter 183, the State Archivist shall issue rules and regulations to carry out the powers and duties of the State Archivist under ORS 192.005 to 192.170 and 357.805 to 357.895. [Formerly 358.090] STATE REFERENCE COORDINATING COUNCIL
