# Deputy Raymond Williams and Deputy Michael Cheney Memorial Highway (ORS 366.926)

## At a glance

ORS 366.926 — Deputy Raymond Williams and Deputy Michael Cheney Memorial Highway. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.926 Deputy Raymond Williams and Deputy Michael Cheney Memorial Highway.

(1) The portion of Oregon Highway 82 between mileposts 18 and 25 shall also be known as the Deputy Raymond Williams and Deputy Michael Cheney Memorial Highway.

(2) The Department of Transportation shall place and maintain suitable markers along the highway described in this section that indicate the designation of the highway as the Deputy Raymond Williams and Deputy Michael Cheney Memorial Highway. (3)(a) The department may accept moneys from and may enter into agreements with law enforcement groups, other civic organizations or individuals to create, install and maintain the markers. (b) The department may not use public funds for the installation and maintenance of the markers. [2023 c.107 §1] Note: 366.926 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 366 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
