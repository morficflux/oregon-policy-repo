# Overpayment of salary or compensation (Student Policies) (ORS 352.244)

## At a glance

ORS 352.244 — Overpayment of salary or compensation (Student Policies). Chapter 352 (Chapter 352), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

352.244 Overpayment of salary or compensation. When an employee of a public university listed in ORS 352.002 receives payment of salary or compensation in an amount greater than the employee’s entitlement, the amount of the overpayment may be deducted from salary or compensation earned by the employee. The deduction may be in such form and manner as the governing board of the public university may prescribe.
