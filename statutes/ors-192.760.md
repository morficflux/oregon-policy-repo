# Relationship of ORS 192.715 to (ORS 192.760)

## At a glance

## Full text

192.760 Relationship of ORS 192.715 to 192.760 to federal law. ORS 192.715 to 192.760 modify, limit, and supersede the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act, 15 U.S.C. 7001 et seq., but do not modify, limit or supersede section 101(c) of that Act, 15 U.S.C. 7001(c), or authorize electronic delivery of any of the notices described in section 103(b) of that Act, 15 U.S.C. 7003(b). [2013 c.221 §9] Note: See note under 192.715. FINANCIAL INSTITUTION RECORD DISCLOSURES
