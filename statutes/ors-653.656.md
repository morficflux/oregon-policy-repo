# Rules (ORS 653.656)

## At a glance

ORS 653.656 — Rules. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.656 Rules. The Commissioner of the Bureau of Labor and Industries:

(1) Shall enforce the provisions of ORS 653.601 to 653.661; and

(2) May adopt rules necessary for the implementation and enforcement of ORS 653.601 to 653.661. [2015 c.537 §14]
