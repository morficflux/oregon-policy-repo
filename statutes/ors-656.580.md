# Payment of compensation notwithstanding cause of action for damages; lien on cause of action for compensation paid (ORS 656.580)

## At a glance

## Full text

656.580 Payment of compensation notwithstanding cause of action for damages; lien on cause of action for compensation paid.

(1) The worker or beneficiaries of the worker, as the case may be, shall be paid the benefits provided by this chapter in the same manner and to the same extent as if no right of action existed against the employer or third party, until damages are recovered from such employer or third party.

(2) The paying agency has a lien against the cause of action as provided by ORS 656.591 or 656.593, which lien shall be preferred to all claims except the cost of recovering such damages. [Formerly 656.314] 656.582 [Renumbered 656.384]
