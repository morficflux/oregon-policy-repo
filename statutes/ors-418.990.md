# Criminal penalties (ORS 418.990)

## At a glance

ORS 418.990 — Criminal penalties. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.990 Criminal penalties.

(1) A person who violates ORS 418.255, 418.290 or 418.300 commits a Class D violation.

(2) A person who violates ORS 418.630 commits a Class B misdemeanor.

(3) Violation of ORS 418.215, 418.250 or 418.327

(4) is a Class A misdemeanor. Each day of violation is a separate offense. [Formerly part of 419.990; subsection
