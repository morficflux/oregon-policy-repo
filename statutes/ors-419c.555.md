# Authority to appoint guardian (ORS 419C.555)

## At a glance

ORS 419C.555 — Authority to appoint guardian. Chapter 419C (Chapter 419C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419C.555 Authority to appoint guardian. Except when the court grants legal custody to the Oregon Youth Authority, the court may grant guardianship of the adjudicated youth to a private institution or agency to which the adjudicated youth is committed or to some suitable person or entity if it appears necessary to do so in the interests of the adjudicated youth. [1993 c.33 §249; 2003 c.396 §126; 2021 c.489 §93]
