# Action against landlord for failure to reduce rent (ORS 307.525)

## At a glance

ORS 307.525 — Action against landlord for failure to reduce rent. Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.525 Action against landlord for failure to reduce rent. In addition to any other provision of law, if a landlord violates ORS 307.517 (1)(c), a tenant may recover damages in an amount triple the actual damages sustained as a result of the violation. The court may award reasonable attorney fees to the prevailing party in an action under this section. [1989 c.803 §6; 1995 c.618 §62]
