# Reinstatement of license (State Board) (ORS 688.148)

## At a glance

ORS 688.148 — Reinstatement of license (State Board). Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.148 Reinstatement of license.

(1) The Oregon Board of Physical Therapy may reinstate a revoked or suspended physical therapist license or physical therapist assistant license if the person whose license was revoked or suspended: (a) Meets the requirements for initial licensure; and (b) Satisfactorily completes any requirements or meets any conditions for reinstatement of the license stipulated in a consent order at the time the license was revoked or suspended.

(2) The board may require a person described in subsection

(1) of this section to demonstrate competence to practice physical therapy or as a physical therapist assistant by one or more of the following: (a) Under any reasonable licensure conditions imposed by the board, completing a supervised clinical practice as prescribed by the board; (b) Completing, or demonstrating completion of, continuing competence that was required during the period when the license was revoked or suspended; (c) Passing an examination required by the board; or (d) Successfully completing any practice assessment tools or evaluations required by the board. [2025 c.627 §6] Note: 688.148 becomes operative July 1, 2027. See section 24, chapter 627, Oregon Laws 2025. 688.150 [1959 c.461 §15; 1969 c.339 §16; 1971 c.585 §15; repealed by 1971 c.734 §21] (State Board)
