## At a glance

## Full text

128.993 Penalty for signing false document.

(1) A person commits the crime of signing a false document for filing if the person: (a) Knows the document is false in any material respect; and (b) Signs the document with an intent that the document be delivered to the office of the Secretary of State for filing under ORS 128.560 to 128.600.

(2) Signing a false document for filing is a Class A misdemeanor. [2013 c.158 §14]
