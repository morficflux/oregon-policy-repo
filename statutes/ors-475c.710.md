# Authority to retain portion of tax to pay expenses incurred (ORS 475C.710)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.710 — Authority to retain portion of tax to pay expenses incurred. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.710 Authority to retain portion of tax to pay expenses incurred. For the purpose of compensating marijuana retailers for expenses incurred in collecting the tax imposed under ORS 475C.674, each marijuana retailer is permitted to deduct and retain two percent of the amount of taxes that are collected by the marijuana retailer from all retail sales of marijuana items conducted by the marijuana retailer. [Formerly 475B.745]
