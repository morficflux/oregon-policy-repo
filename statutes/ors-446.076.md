## Full text

446.076 Consumer and Business Services Fund; sources; uses. All moneys received by the Department of Consumer and Business Services under ORS 446.003 to 446.200 and 446.225 to 446.285 shall be paid into the Consumer and Business Services Fund created by ORS 705.145. The moneys received under this section are continuously appropriated to the department for use as provided in ORS 446.423.
