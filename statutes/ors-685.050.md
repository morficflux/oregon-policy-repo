# Execution of reports of live birth and death (ORS 685.050)

## At a glance

ORS 685.050 — Execution of reports of live birth and death. Chapter 685 (Chapter 685), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

685.050 Execution of reports of live birth and death. Licensees under this chapter are authorized to sign reports of live birth and death. Reports signed under this section shall be accepted as fulfilling all the requirements of the laws of this state dealing with such reports. [Amended by 2001 c.526 §7; 2013 c.366 §79]
