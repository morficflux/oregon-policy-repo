# Protection of witnesses at hearings (ORS 656.285)

## At a glance

ORS 656.285 — Protection of witnesses at hearings. Chapter 656 (Chapter 656), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

656.285 Protection of witnesses at hearings. ORCP 36 C shall apply to workers’ compensation cases, except that the Administrative Law Judge shall make the determinations and orders required of the court in ORCP 36 C, and in addition attorney fees shall not be declared as a matter of course but only in cases of harassment or hardship. [1973 c.652 §1; 1977 c.358 §11; 1979 c.284 §187]
