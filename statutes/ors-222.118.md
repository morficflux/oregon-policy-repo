# Provision of city services to airport without requiring annexation (ORS 222.118)

## At a glance

ORS 222.118 — Provision of city services to airport without requiring annexation. Chapter 222 (Chapter 222), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

222.118 Provision of city services to airport without requiring annexation. A city and an airport may enter into an agreement pursuant to which the city provides sewer and water services to the airport without requiring the annexation, or consent to eventual annexation, to the city of the territory on which the airport is situated. [2015 c.787 §1] Note: 222.118 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 222 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
