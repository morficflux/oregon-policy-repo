# Construction of chapter (ORS 725.015)

## At a glance

ORS 725.015 — Construction of chapter. Chapter 725 (Chapter 725), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

725.015 Construction of chapter. Nothing in this chapter shall be construed or held to limit the rights, powers or privileges granted to any person by any law of this state or of the United States whereby the loaning of money or extending of credit is regulated, provided that such person is operating in compliance with the provisions of such law. [Formerly 725.040]
