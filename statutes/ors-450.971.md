# Employee contribution (ORS 450.971)

## At a glance

ORS 450.971 — Employee contribution. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.971 Employee contribution. The authority may collect, as a contribution from any employee, that percentage of the salary received by the employee which is necessary to fund on an actuarial reserve basis the cost of retirement benefits which the employee is required to provide pursuant to the provisions of the retirement plan. [1973 c.399 §12]
