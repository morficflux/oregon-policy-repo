# ORS (ORS 317.351)

## At a glance

ORS 317.351 — ORS. Chapter 317 (Chapter 317), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

317.351 ORS 317.349 not applicable to finance leases. Notwithstanding ORS 317.349, finance leases as described in section 168(f)(8) of the Internal Revenue Code, as that section was amended and in effect for purposes of ORS 317.349, shall be accorded the same treatment for Oregon tax purposes as they are for federal tax purposes. [1987 c.293 §45; 2003 c.77 §22] 317.355 [Repealed by 1957 c.632 §1 (314.385 enacted in lieu of 316.545 and 317.355)]
