# Status of reports under ORS 435.496; rules (ORS 432.153)

## At a glance

## Full text

432.153 Status of reports under ORS 435.496; rules. The reports required under ORS 435.496 are statistical reports to be used only for medical and health purposes and shall not be incorporated into the permanent official records of the system of vital statistics. A schedule for the disposition of these reports may be provided by rule of the State Registrar of the Center for Health Statistics. [Formerly 432.337] 432.155 [Repealed by 1979 c.696 §14]
