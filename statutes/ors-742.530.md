# Exclusions from coverage (ORS 742.530)

## At a glance

ORS 742.530 — Exclusions from coverage. Chapter 742 (Chapter 742), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

742.530 Exclusions from coverage.

(1) The insurer may exclude from the coverage for personal injury protection benefits any injured person who: (a) Intentionally causes self-injury; (b) Is participating in any prearranged or organized racing or speed contest or practice or preparation for any such contest; or (c) Willfully conceals or misrepresents any material fact in connection with a claim for personal injury protection benefits.

(2) The insurer may exclude from the coverage for the benefits required by ORS 742.524 (1)(b) and (c) any person injured as a pedestrian in an accident outside this state, other than the insured person or a member of that person’s family residing in the same household. [Formerly 743.815; 2005 c.341 §2]
