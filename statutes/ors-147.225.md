# Criminal Injuries Compensation Account (ORS 147.225)

## At a glance

ORS 147.225 — Criminal Injuries Compensation Account. Chapter 147 (Chapter 147), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

147.225 Criminal Injuries Compensation Account. There is established the Criminal Injuries Compensation Account. All moneys in the account are continuously appropriated for and may be used by the Department of Justice for the purposes authorized in ORS 147.005 to 147.367, 147.390 and 147.397. [1977 c.376 §22; 2003 c.789 §§4,6; 2007 c.23 §2; 2007 c.268 §4; 2012 c.81 §15; 2020 s.s.2 c.10 §30]
