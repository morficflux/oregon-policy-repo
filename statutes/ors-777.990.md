# Penalties (ORS 777.990)

## At a glance

ORS 777.990 — Penalties. Chapter 777 (Chapter 777), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

777.990 Penalties.

(1) Failure by a port treasurer, or county treasurer charged with the duties provided by ORS 777.515, to comply with the requirements of that section for a period of 10 days is a Class A violation.

(2) Subject to ORS 153.022, any person violating a regulation adopted by a port board under ORS 777.120 or 777.190 commits a Class A misdemeanor. [Amended by 1971 c.728 §102; 1999 c.1051 §226; 2011 c.597 §95] _______________
