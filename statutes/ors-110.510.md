## Full text

110.510 Application to certain support proceedings.

(1) A tribunal of this state shall apply ORS 110.500 to 110.641 and, as applicable, ORS 110.645 to 110.669 to a support proceeding involving: (a) A foreign support order; (b) A foreign tribunal; or (c) An obligee, obligor or child residing in a foreign country.

(2) A tribunal of this state that is requested to recognize and enforce a support order on the basis of comity may apply the procedural and substantive provisions of ORS 110.500 to 110.641.

(3) ORS 110.645 to 110.669 apply only to a support proceeding under the Convention. In such a proceeding, if a provision of ORS 110.645 to 110.669 is inconsistent with ORS 110.500 to 110.641, ORS 110.645 to 110.669 control. [2015 c.298 §6] JURISDICTION
