# Parties designated “appellant” and “respondent”; title of action (Appealable Judgments and Orders) (ORS 138.030)

## At a glance

ORS 138.030 — Parties designated “appellant” and “respondent”; title of action (Appealable Judgments and Orders). Chapter 138 (Chapter 138), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

138.030 Parties designated “appellant” and “respondent”; title of action. The party appealing is known as the appellant and the adverse party as the respondent; but the title of the action is not changed in consequence of the appeal. (Appealable Judgments and Orders)
