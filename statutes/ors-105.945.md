# Exempted data RULE AGAINST PERPETUITIES (ORS 105.945)

## At a glance

ORS 105.945 — Exempted data RULE AGAINST PERPETUITIES. Chapter 105 (Chapter 105), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

105.945 Exempted data. ORS 105.925 to 105.945 do not apply to data that is stored or transmitted pursuant to a subscription service agreement for the use of a recording device to record a history of where a motor vehicle travels or for the transmission of data to a central communications system. [2007 c.644 §6] RULE AGAINST PERPETUITIES
