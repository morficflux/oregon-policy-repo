# Inventory listing not prerequisite to other remedial action (ORS 465.240)

## At a glance

ORS 465.240 — Inventory listing not prerequisite to other remedial action. Chapter 465 (Chapter 465), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

465.240 Inventory listing not prerequisite to other remedial action. Nothing in ORS 465.225 to 465.240, 465.405 and 465.410 or placement of a facility on the list under ORS 465.215 shall be construed to be a prerequisite to or otherwise affect the authority of the Director of the Department of Environmental Quality to undertake, order or authorize a removal or remedial action under ORS 465.200 to 465.485 and 465.900. [1989 c.485 §6]
