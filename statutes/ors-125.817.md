# Exclusive basis (ORS 125.817)

## At a glance

ORS 125.817 — Exclusive basis. Chapter 125 (Chapter 125), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

125.817 Exclusive basis. ORS 125.815 to 125.835 provide the exclusive jurisdictional basis for a court of this state to appoint a guardian or issue a conservatorship order for an adult under ORS 125.800 to 125.852. [2009 c.179 §8] Note: See note under 125.800.
