# Giving false information to a peace officer in connection with a citation or warrant (ORS 162.385)

## At a glance

ORS 162.385 — Giving false information to a peace officer in connection with a citation or warrant. Chapter 162 (Chapter 162), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

162.385 Giving false information to a peace officer in connection with a citation or warrant.

(1) A person commits the crime of giving false information to a peace officer in connection with a citation or warrant if the person knowingly uses or gives a false or fictitious name, address or date of birth to any peace officer when: (a) The peace officer is issuing or serving the person a citation under authority of ORS 133.055 to 133.076 or ORS chapter 153; or (b) There is an outstanding warrant for the person’s arrest.

(2) Giving false information to a peace officer in connection with a citation or warrant is a Class A misdemeanor. [1983 c.661 §11; 1999 c.1051 §70; 2003 c.777 §1; 2007 c.771 §1; 2017 c.99 §1] Note: 162.385 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 133 by legislative action.
