# Application of ORS (ORS 498.412)

## At a glance

ORS 498.412 — Application of ORS. Chapter 498 (Chapter 498), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

498.412 Application of ORS 498.406. ORS 498.406 does not apply to any landowner offering to sell recreational access to property the landowner owns. [1973 c.749 §4] 498.415 [Repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
