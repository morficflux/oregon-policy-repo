# Intent of prohibited interest provisions (ORS 756.034)

## At a glance

ORS 756.034 — Intent of prohibited interest provisions. Chapter 756 (Public Utility Commission), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

756.034 Intent of prohibited interest provisions. Nothing in ORS 756.022 to 756.032 is intended to authorize any act otherwise prohibited by law. [1971 c.655 §7]
