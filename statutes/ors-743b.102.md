# Certifications and disclosure of coverage (ORS 743B.102)

## At a glance

ORS 743B.102 — Certifications and disclosure of coverage. Chapter 743B (Chapter 743B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743B.102 Certifications and disclosure of coverage. All carriers that offer individual or group health benefit plans shall provide certifications and disclosure of coverage in accordance with 42 U.S.C. 300gg(e) and 300gg-43 as amended and in effect on July 1, 1997. [Formerly 743.749]
