# Terms of office of first city council (ORS 221.090)

## At a glance

ORS 221.090 — Terms of office of first city council. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.090 Terms of office of first city council.

(1) Members of the first city council shall serve the following terms: (a) The two members receiving the two highest number of votes shall hold office until the first Monday in January next following the second general election held after incorporation of the city; and (b) The three members receiving the three next highest number of votes shall hold office until the first Monday in January next following the first general election held after the incorporation.

(2) The county clerk shall prepare and deliver a certificate of election to each candidate elected to the city council. [Amended by 1983 c.350 §19]
