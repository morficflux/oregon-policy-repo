# Reporting information to university (ORS 190.590)

## At a glance

ORS 190.590 — Reporting information to university. Chapter 190 (Chapter 190), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

190.590 Reporting information to university. Any state agency, or officer thereof, and any city, or department, officer or employee thereof, shall, upon request of Portland State University, furnish such available information as may be required by the university in securing accurate data and information upon which to base its estimates. The university may prescribe the form for reporting such information. [Formerly 221.885; 2013 c.768 §106f] 190.600 [Formerly 221.890; repealed by 1963 c.115 §2]
