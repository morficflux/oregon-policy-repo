# Manufactured structure trip permits (ORS 446.636)

## At a glance

ORS 446.636 — Manufactured structure trip permits. Chapter 446 (Chapter 446), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

446.636 Manufactured structure trip permits.

(1) For a new manufactured structure, the manufactured structure dealer must obtain the trip permit on behalf of the owner. If the dealer fails to obtain the trip permit prior to the scheduled moving date, the vehicle transporter must obtain the trip permit on behalf of the owner.

(2) For a used manufactured structure, the owner must obtain a trip permit from the county assessor for the county in which the manufactured structure is sited.

(3) Notwithstanding subsections

(1) and

(2) of this subsection, if a dealer or vehicle transporter refuses to obtain a trip permit as required by subsection

(1) of this section, or if a county assessor refuses to issue a trip permit to an owner who has complied with ORS 446.631, the owner may apply directly to the Department of Consumer and Business Services for a trip permit.

(4) The department may issue trip permits in bulk to a manufactured structure dealer or vehicle transporter. [2003 c.655 §22a] Note: See note under 446.561. 446.640 [1959 c.314 §17; repealed by 1973 c.833 §48]
