## Full text

455.098 Minimum standards and statewide consistency in electronic processes; rules. The Department of Consumer and Business Services shall adopt rules to establish minimum standards and statewide consistency in electronic processes for reviewing plans, issuing permits and performing field inspections. [2021 c.223 §1] Note: 455.098 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 455 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. (Director)
