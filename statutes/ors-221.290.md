# Application of ORS (ORS 221.290)

## At a glance

ORS 221.290 — Application of ORS. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.290 Application of ORS 221.275 to 221.290. ORS 221.275 to 221.290 apply to any city with a population exceeding 300,000. [1995 c.533 §9]
