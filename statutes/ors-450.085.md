# Adoption of regulations and ordinances (District Finances) (ORS 450.085)

## At a glance

ORS 450.085 — Adoption of regulations and ordinances (District Finances). Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.085 Adoption of regulations and ordinances. Any general regulation or ordinance of a district board shall be adopted in accordance with ORS 198.510 to 198.600. Orders not establishing a general regulation need not be posted or published. [Amended by 1971 c.268 §16] (District Finances)
