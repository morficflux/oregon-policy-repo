# Electors’ mileage expenses (ORS 248.380)

## At a glance

ORS 248.380 — Electors’ mileage expenses. Chapter 248 (Chapter 248), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

248.380 Electors’ mileage expenses. An elector of President and Vice President of the United States who attends at the time and place appointed and votes for President and Vice President shall be entitled to receive from this state mileage expenses at the rate allowed to members of the Legislative Assembly.
