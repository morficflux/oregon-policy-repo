## At a glance

ORS 705.155 — Petty cash funds. Chapter 705 (Department of Consumer and Business Services _______________ 2025 EDITION DEPARTMENT OF CONSUMER AND BUSINESS SERVICES INSURANCE AND FINANCE ADMINISTRATION ADMI), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

705.155 Petty cash funds. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services and the Workers’ Compensation Board may in their discretion establish and maintain petty cash funds for the purpose of making change and paying other petty incidental expenses in the administration of the functions of the Department of Consumer and Business Services and the board. [1987 c.373 §19] 705.160 [1985 c.762 §8; repealed by 1987 c.373 §85]
