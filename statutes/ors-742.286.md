# Mortgage insurance; who may write SURETY INSURANCE (ORS 742.286)

## At a glance

ORS 742.286 — Mortgage insurance; who may write SURETY INSURANCE. Chapter 742 (Chapter 742), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

742.286 Mortgage insurance; who may write. All policies and contracts of insurance covering liens or security interests in real property shall be written by authorized mortgage insurers. No other class of insurer may write any form of mortgage insurance.
