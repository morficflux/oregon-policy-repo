## At a glance

## Full text

652.615 Remedy for violation of ORS 652.610. There is hereby created a private cause of action for a violation of ORS 652.610

(3) for actual damages or $200, whichever is greater. In any such action the court may award to the prevailing party, in addition to costs and disbursements, reasonable attorney fees. [1981 c.594 §7]
