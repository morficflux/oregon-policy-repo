## Full text

676.647 Prohibition on use of unregistered device; rules.

(1) A person may not use a device that is not registered with the United States Food and Drug Administration to perform advanced nonablative esthetics procedures.

(2) The Board of Certified Advanced Estheticians may adopt rules to carry out this section. [2021 c.366 §2]
