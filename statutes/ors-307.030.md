# Property subject to assessment generally (ORS 307.030)

## At a glance

ORS 307.030 — Property subject to assessment generally. Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.030 Property subject to assessment generally.

(1) All real property within this state and all tangible personal property situated within this state, except as otherwise provided by law, shall be subject to assessment and taxation in equal and ratable proportion.

(2) Except as provided in ORS 308.505 to 308.674, intangible personal property is not subject to assessment and taxation. [Amended by 1993 c.353 §2; 1997 c.154 §28]
