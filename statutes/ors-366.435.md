# Auditing and allowing claims (ORS 366.435)

## At a glance

ORS 366.435 — Auditing and allowing claims. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.435 Auditing and allowing claims. The Department of Transportation may allow all claims legally payable out of the highway fund. The department shall, if satisfied as to the correctness and validity of a claim, indorse approval thereon. When claims have been approved and indorsed by the fiscal officer of the department, they shall be filed with the fiscal officer of the department, who shall audit and pay the same out of the highway fund. [Amended by 1953 c.252 §2; 1967 c.454 §39] 366.440 [Repealed by 2015 c.138 §11]
