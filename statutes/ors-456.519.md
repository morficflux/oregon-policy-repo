# General obligation bonds authorized (ORS 456.519)

## At a glance

ORS 456.519 — General obligation bonds authorized. Chapter 456 (Chapter 456), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

456.519 General obligation bonds authorized. In order to provide funds for the purposes specified in Article XI-I(2) of the Oregon Constitution, including those specified in ORS 456.539, the Director of the Housing and Community Services Department may request the State Treasurer to issue bonds in accordance with the provisions of ORS chapter 286A.
