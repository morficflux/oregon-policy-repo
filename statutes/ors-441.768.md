# Staffing ratios for certified nursing assistants (ORS 441.768)

## At a glance

ORS 441.768 — Staffing ratios for certified nursing assistants. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.768 Staffing ratios for certified nursing assistants. A hospital may not assign a certified nursing assistant to more than seven patients at a time during a day or evening shift or to more than 11 patients at a time during a night shift. [2023 c.507 §8] Note: See note under 441.761.
