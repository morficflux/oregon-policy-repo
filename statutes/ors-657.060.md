# Employment; family service and foster care excluded (ORS 657.060)

## At a glance

ORS 657.060 — Employment; family service and foster care excluded. Chapter 657 (Chapter 657), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

657.060 Employment; family service and foster care excluded.

(1) “Employment” does not include service performed by a person in the employ of a son, daughter, or spouse, and service performed by a child under the age of 18 in the employ of the father or mother.

(2) “Employment” does not include service performed as foster care parents certified by the Department of Human Services or approved by a child-caring agency as defined in ORS 418.205. [Amended by 1975 c.334 §1; 1987 c.857 §1; 2016 c.106 §55]
