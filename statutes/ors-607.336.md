# Title of purchaser at sale (ORS 607.336)

## At a glance

ORS 607.336 — Title of purchaser at sale. Chapter 607 (Chapter 607), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

607.336 Title of purchaser at sale. The purchaser of an animal sold in accordance with ORS 607.328 shall have clear and absolute title to the animal. [1953 c.367 §8; 1975 c.65 §8; 2009 c.336 §11]
