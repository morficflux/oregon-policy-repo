# Redemption of bonds before maturity dates (ORS 450.930)

## At a glance

ORS 450.930 — Redemption of bonds before maturity dates. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.930 Redemption of bonds before maturity dates. In its discretion, the board may issue bonds of the authority with reservation of the right to redeem them for retirement or refunding purposes prior to the final dates of maturity.
