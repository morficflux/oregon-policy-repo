# Contingency reserve liability for mortgage insurance (ORS 733.100)

## At a glance

ORS 733.100 — Contingency reserve liability for mortgage insurance. Chapter 733 (Chapter 733), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

733.100 Contingency reserve liability for mortgage insurance. A mortgage insurer shall establish a contingency reserve liability for the protection of policyholders against the effect of adverse economic cycles according to accounting procedures approved or required by the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services. [1967 c.359 §217; 1969 c.692 §7; 2001 c.318 §10] 733.110 [1967 c.359 §218; 1977 c.320 §9; 1981 c.609 §3; repealed by 1991 c.401 §30]
