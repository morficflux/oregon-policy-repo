# Program to assist environmental justice communities (ORS 469.166)

## At a glance

ORS 469.166 — Program to assist environmental justice communities. Chapter 469 (Chapter 469), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469.166 Program to assist environmental justice communities. The State Department of Energy shall establish a program to provide assistance related to energy projects and activities to environmental justice communities, as defined in ORS 469A.400. At a minimum, the program must provide environmental justice communities with information regarding:

(1) Funding resources.

(2) Technical assistance.

(3) Other support that may be available.
