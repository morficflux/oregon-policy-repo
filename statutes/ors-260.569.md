# Prohibition on payment based on signatures obtained on nominating petition or voter registration card (ORS 260.569)

## At a glance

ORS 260.569 — Prohibition on payment based on signatures obtained on nominating petition or voter registration card. Chapter 260 (Chapter 260), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

260.569 Prohibition on payment based on signatures obtained on nominating petition or voter registration card. A person may not pay or receive money or another thing of value based on the number of:

(1) Signatures a person obtains for purposes of nominating a candidate for elective public office; or

(2) Signed voter registration cards a person collects. [2007 c.314 §2]
