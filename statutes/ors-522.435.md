# Rule, order to supersede previous board action (ORS 522.435)

## At a glance

ORS 522.435 — Rule, order to supersede previous board action. Chapter 522 (Chapter 522), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

522.435 Rule, order to supersede previous board action. Any rule adopted or order entered under ORS 522.405 shall supersede any right or privilege previously granted by the governing board of the State Department of Geology and Mineral Industries to the same person or state or local governing body, special district or agency with respect to the reservoir. [1981 c.588 §11] 522.440 [1971 c.776 §38; repealed by 1973 c.388 §8]
