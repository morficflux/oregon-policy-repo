# Addition for unused qualified business credits (ORS 317.304)

## At a glance

ORS 317.304 — Addition for unused qualified business credits. Chapter 317 (Chapter 317), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

317.304 Addition for unused qualified business credits. There shall be added to federal taxable income the amount taken as a deduction on the taxpayer’s federal return for unused qualified business credits under section 196 of the Internal Revenue Code. [1995 c.556 §16] 317.305 [1957 c.74 §2; repealed by 1983 c.162 §57]
