# Effect of void marriage (ORS 108.730)

## At a glance

ORS 108.730 — Effect of void marriage. Chapter 108 (Chapter 108), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

108.730 Effect of void marriage. If a marriage is determined to be void, an agreement that would otherwise have been a premarital agreement is enforceable only to the extent necessary to avoid an inequitable result. [1987 c.715 §7] Note: See note under 108.700.
