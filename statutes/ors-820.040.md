## At a glance

ORS 820.040 — Inspection of vehicles. Chapter 820 (Chapter 820), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

820.040 Inspection of vehicles.

(1) The Department of Transportation, in enforcing the safety code established under ORS 820.030, may inspect any vehicle that is described under ORS 820.010 or 820.020.

(2) Upon request, the Superintendent of State Police shall assist the department in these inspections. [1983 c.338 §741]
