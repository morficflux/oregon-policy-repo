# Authority of local public health administrator to restrain violation of public health laws (ORS 431.157)

## At a glance

ORS 431.157 — Authority of local public health administrator to restrain violation of public health laws. Chapter 431 (State and Local Administration and Enforcement of Public Health Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

431.157 Authority of local public health administrator to restrain violation of public health laws. A local public health administrator has the same powers granted to the Oregon Health Authority under ORS 431.155. [1983 c.370 §4; 2003 c.309 §5; 2009 c.595 §538; 2015 c.736 §34]
