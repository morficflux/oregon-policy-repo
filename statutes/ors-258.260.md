# Costs to be collected for multicounty or statewide election recounts (ORS 258.260)

## At a glance

ORS 258.260 — Costs to be collected for multicounty or statewide election recounts. Chapter 258 (Chapter 258), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

258.260 Costs to be collected for multicounty or statewide election recounts. If the demand for recount is made for a multicounty or statewide election, the Secretary of State also may collect those costs allowed in ORS 258.231

(1) which the secretary incurs as a result of the recount. [Formerly 251.635] 258.265 [1965 c.586 §20; repealed by 1979 c.190 §431]
