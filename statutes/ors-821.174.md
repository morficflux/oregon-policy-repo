# Prohibition on operating Class I, Class III or Class IV all-terrain vehicle while driving privileges suspended (ORS 821.174)

## At a glance

## Full text

821.174 Prohibition on operating Class I, Class III or Class IV all-terrain vehicle while driving privileges suspended. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, a person may not operate a Class I, Class III or Class IV all-terrain vehicle while the person’s driving privileges are suspended or revoked. A person who violates this section is in violation of ORS 811.175 or 811.182, as appropriate. [1995 c.775 §7; 2011 c.360 §23] Note: 821.174 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 821 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 821.175 [1987 c.587 §6; 1989 c.661 §3; 1989 c.991 §11a; 1995 c.774 §4; renumbered 821.195 in 1995]
