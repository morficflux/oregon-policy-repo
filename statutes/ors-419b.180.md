# Shelter facilities (ORS 419B.180)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.180 — Shelter facilities. Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.180 Shelter facilities. The juvenile court of each county shall designate the place or places in which children are to be placed in shelter care when taken into protective custody. [1993 c.33 §68; 1993 c.546 §34; 2019 c.382 §14]
