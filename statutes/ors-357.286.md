357.286 Retirement system for employees. A district may establish an employees’ retirement system as provided for rural fire protection districts under ORS 478.355 to 478.370. [1981 c.226 §15] 357.290 [1965 c.378 §§1,2,3; repealed by 1975 c.476 §34] INTERSTATE LIBRARY COMPACT
