# “Basic research” defined for ORS (ORS 352.365)

## At a glance

ORS 352.365 — “Basic research” defined for ORS. Chapter 352 (Chapter 352), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

352.365 “Basic research” defined for ORS 352.365 to 352.379. As used in ORS 352.365 to 352.379, “basic research” is defined as scholarly investigation conducted to obtain new knowledge for its own sake. [Formerly 351.865] 352.370 [Formerly 351.765; 1965 c.100 §347; 1993 c.45 §294; 2011 c.637 §245; renumbered 350.250 in 2015]
