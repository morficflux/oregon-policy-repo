# Selling or transporting food fish taken by angling (ORS 509.025)

## At a glance

ORS 509.025 — Selling or transporting food fish taken by angling. Chapter 509 (Chapter 509), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

509.025 Selling or transporting food fish taken by angling. It is unlawful to sell within this state, or transport out of this state for the purpose of sale, food fish taken from any waters of this state by means of angling. [Amended by 1965 c.570 §94; subsection

(2) enacted as 1965 c.570 §107; 1977 c.242 §6] 509.030 [Amended by 1965 c.570 §99; 1969 c.411 §3; repealed by 1975 c.1 §1 (509.031 enacted in lieu of 509.030)]
