# Placement of children in institutions in other states (ORS 417.240)

## At a glance

ORS 417.240 — Placement of children in institutions in other states. Chapter 417 (Chapter 417), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

417.240 Placement of children in institutions in other states. Any court having jurisdiction pursuant to ORS 419B.100 or 419C.005 to place children may place a child in an institution in another state pursuant to Article VI of the Interstate Compact on the Placement of Children and shall retain jurisdiction as provided in Article V thereof. [1975 c.482 §5; 1993 c.33 §324]
