# Department duties for transition services; rules (ORS 343.955)

## At a glance

ORS 343.955 — Department duties for transition services; rules. Chapter 343 (Chapter 343), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

343.955 Department duties for transition services; rules. The Department of Education shall ensure that transition services, as defined in ORS 343.035, provided to students with intellectual or developmental disabilities do not occur in a sheltered work setting or a mock sheltered work setting, as those terms are defined by rule by the department. [2025 c.228 §6] Note: 343.955 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 343 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 343.960 [Formerly 343.950 and then 326.510; 1975 c.620 §1; 1977 c.251 §1; 1977 c.586 §1; 1979 c.700 §1; 1981 c.916 §1; repealed by 1985 c.555 §19 (343.961 enacted in lieu of 343.960 and 343.965)]
