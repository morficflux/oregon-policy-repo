# Severability clause (ORS 110.677)

## At a glance

ORS 110.677 — Severability clause. Chapter 110 (Chapter 110), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

110.677 Severability clause. If any provision of this chapter or its application to any person or circumstance is held invalid, the invalidity does not affect other provisions or applications of this chapter that can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this chapter are severable. [2015 c.298 §79] _______________
