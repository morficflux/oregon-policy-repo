# Application of certain provisions to authorities (ORS 450.685)

## At a glance

ORS 450.685 — Application of certain provisions to authorities. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.685 Application of certain provisions to authorities.

(1) Except as provided by subsection

(2) of this section, ORS 450.070, 450.084, 450.085 and 450.600 to 450.989 apply to a water authority.

(2) ORS 450.810 (1), 450.815 (7),

(8) and (9), 450.820 and 450.835 do not apply to a water authority.

(3) ORS 264.240, 264.250 (2), 264.300 to 264.320 and 264.505 to 264.840 are applicable to a water authority. [1971 c.504 §5; 1989 c.809 §4; 1993 c.577 §28]
