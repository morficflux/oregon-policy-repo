# Sale or management of foreclosed property under personal services contract (ORS 407.377)

## At a glance

ORS 407.377 — Sale or management of foreclosed property under personal services contract. Chapter 407 (Chapter 407), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

407.377 Sale or management of foreclosed property under personal services contract. If the Department of Veterans’ Affairs enters into any personal services contract under which any other person is authorized to sell or manage homes or farms obtained for and in behalf of this state under ORS 407.135 and 407.145 (1), then ORS 407.375 may not apply to the sale of such homes or farms by that person. [1987 c.661 §4; 2005 c.625 §41] 407.380 [Amended by 1967 c.335 §51; repealed by 1980 c.17 §1 and by 1981 c.660 §18]
