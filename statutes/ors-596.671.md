# Amount of indemnity (ORS 596.671)

## At a glance

ORS 596.671 — Amount of indemnity. Chapter 596 (Disease Control Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

596.671 Amount of indemnity. The indemnity shall be a sum, which added to the salvage, shall not exceed 80 percent of the appraised value of each animal or article of property. [1965 c.217 §5 (enacted in lieu of 596.670); 1971 c.705 §1; 1981 c.334 §3] 596.680 [1953 c.633 §7; repealed by 1965 c.217 §6 (596.681 enacted in lieu of 596.680)]
