# Continuing authority of board upon lapse, suspension, revocation or voluntary surrender of license (ORS 684.156)

## At a glance

ORS 684.156 — Continuing authority of board upon lapse, suspension, revocation or voluntary surrender of license. Chapter 684 (Chapter 684), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

684.156 Continuing authority of board upon lapse, suspension, revocation or voluntary surrender of license. The lapse, suspension or revocation of a license issued under ORS chapter 684 by the operation of law or by order of the State Board of Chiropractic Examiners or by the decision of a court of law, or the voluntary surrender of a license by a licensee, shall not deprive the board of jurisdiction to proceed with any investigation of or any action or disciplinary proceeding against the licensee or to revise or render null and void an order of disciplinary action against the licensee. [2001 c.598 §5] Note: 684.156 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 684 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 684.157 [1997 c.264 §2; 2009 c.756 §54; repealed by 2009 c.697 §14]
