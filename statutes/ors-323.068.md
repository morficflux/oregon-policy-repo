# Prepayment of tax (ORS 323.068)

## At a glance

ORS 323.068 — Prepayment of tax. Chapter 323 (Chapter 323), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

323.068 Prepayment of tax. Every distributor who sells or possesses for sale unstamped cigarettes in this state shall prepay all taxes imposed under ORS 323.005 to 323.482 prior to the sale of the cigarettes to any person in Oregon by purchasing cigarette tax stamps sold pursuant to ORS 323.005 to 323.482 and affixing those stamps to the unstamped packages of cigarettes. [2003 c.804 §3] 323.070 [1965 c.525 §26; repealed by 1971 c.416 §3]
