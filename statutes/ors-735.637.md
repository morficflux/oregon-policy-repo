# License term; renewal; rules (ORS 735.637)

## At a glance

ORS 735.637 — License term; renewal; rules. Chapter 735 (Chapter 735), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

735.637 License term; renewal; rules. A license obtained under ORS 735.634 is effective for one year, or for a longer period if the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services so prescribes by rule. The director shall prescribe by rule conditions and procedures under which a licensee may renew a license that has expired. [Formerly 742.430] Note: See note under 735.631.
