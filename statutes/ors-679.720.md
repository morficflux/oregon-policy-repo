# Discipline (ORS 679.720)

## At a glance

ORS 679.720 — Discipline. Chapter 679 (Chapter 679), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

679.720 Discipline. In the manner prescribed in ORS chapter 183 for contested cases, the Health Licensing Office may impose a form of discipline listed in ORS 676.612 against a dental laboratory registered under ORS 679.710 for any violation of ORS 679.700 to 679.720 or rules adopted under ORS 679.700 to 679.720 or for the commission of a prohibited act described in ORS 676.612. [2025 c.199 §5] Note: 679.720 becomes operative July 1, 2026. See section 19, chapter 199, Oregon Laws 2025. Note: See second note under 679.700.
