# Exemption of minors serving as soccer referees (ORS 653.362)

## At a glance

ORS 653.362 — Exemption of minors serving as soccer referees. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.362 Exemption of minors serving as soccer referees. The provisions of ORS 653.305 to 653.370 do not apply to a person under 18 years of age serving as a referee or assistant referee in a youth or adult recreational soccer match. [2001 c.765 §2]
