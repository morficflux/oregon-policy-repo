# Modifications generally (ORS 317.259)

## At a glance

ORS 317.259 — Modifications generally. Chapter 317 (Chapter 317), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

317.259 Modifications generally. Federal taxable income, adopted under ORS 317.013 and 317.018, shall be modified as provided by law. Each modification authorized under law shall be allowed only to the extent that the modification is allocated and apportioned to Oregon income, except as otherwise specifically provided by law.
