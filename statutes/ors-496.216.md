# Disposition of salmon remaining after ceremony (ORS 496.216)

## At a glance

ORS 496.216 — Disposition of salmon remaining after ceremony. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.216 Disposition of salmon remaining after ceremony. Any salmon remaining after the ceremony may be distributed to tribal members without charge for their subsistence consumption only and not for sale, barter or gift to others, or may be donated to a nonprofit institution or agency. [1981 c.575 §5] 496.220 [Repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
