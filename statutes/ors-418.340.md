# Rules (ORS 418.340)

## At a glance

ORS 418.340 — Rules. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.340 Rules. The Department of Human Services shall make all necessary rules for payments to subsidize adoptions or guardianships. [1971 c.129 §5; 2011 c.141 §3; 2015 c.840 §18] 418.342 [1989 c.306 §2; renumbered 657A.100 in 1993] 418.344 [1989 c.306 §1; renumbered 657A.110 in 1993]
