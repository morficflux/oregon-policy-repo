# Definitions for ORS (ORS 414.532)

## At a glance

ORS 414.532 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.532 Definitions for ORS 414.534 to 414.538. As used in ORS 414.534 to 414.538:

(1) “Medical assistance” has the meaning given that term in ORS 414.025.

(2) “Provider” has the meaning given that term in ORS 743B.001. [2001 c.902 §1] Note: 414.532 to 414.540 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 414 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
