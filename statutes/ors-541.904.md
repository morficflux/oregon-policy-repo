# Authority of executive director to enter into interagency agreements (ORS 541.904)

## At a glance

## Full text

541.904 Authority of executive director to enter into interagency agreements. In addition to any other authority granted to the executive director of the Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board, the executive director, on behalf of the board, may enter into interagency agreements necessary to carry out the duties and responsibilities of the board. [Formerly 541.363] Note: See note under 541.890.
