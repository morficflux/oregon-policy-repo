# Prohibition on operation without authorization (ORS 676.704)

## At a glance

ORS 676.704 — Prohibition on operation without authorization. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.704 Prohibition on operation without authorization.

(1) A temporary staffing agency may not operate in this state without an authorization issued or renewed under ORS 676.698.

(2) Each separate physical location of a temporary staffing agency must obtain an authorization under ORS 676.698. [2022 c.92 §4]
