# Prohibition on acceptance of physical currency over $100 as contribution (ORS 260.012)

## At a glance

ORS 260.012 — Prohibition on acceptance of physical currency over $100 as contribution. Chapter 260 (Chapter 260), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

260.012 Prohibition on acceptance of physical currency over $100 as contribution. A candidate, political committee or a petition committee may not accept aggregate contributions from a single source of more than $100 per calendar year in the form of physical currency of the United States or of any other country. [2023 c.600 §6] Note: 260.012 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 260 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
