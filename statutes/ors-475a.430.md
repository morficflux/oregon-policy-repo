# Prohibition against manufacturing psilocybin products outdoors (ORS 475A.430)

## At a glance

ORS 475A.430 — Prohibition against manufacturing psilocybin products outdoors. Chapter 475A (Chapter 475A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475A.430 Prohibition against manufacturing psilocybin products outdoors. A psilocybin product manufacturer that holds a license under ORS 475A.290 may not manufacture psilocybin products outdoors. [2021 c.1 §52]
