# Compensation of licensees determined by law (ORS 776.415)

## At a glance

ORS 776.415 — Compensation of licensees determined by law. Chapter 776 (Maritime Pilots and Pilotage), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

776.415 Compensation of licensees determined by law. No licensee shall demand or receive any greater, lesser or different compensation for piloting a vessel upon any of the pilotage grounds than is allowed by law. [1957 c.448 §25(2); 1993 c.796 §12] 776.420 [Amended by 1953 c.141 §2; 1953 c.142 §2; 1955 c.698 §1; repealed by 1957 c.448 §27]
