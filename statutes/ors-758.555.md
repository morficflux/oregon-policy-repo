# Effect of energy sales on qualifying facility (ORS 758.555)

## At a glance

ORS 758.555 — Effect of energy sales on qualifying facility. Chapter 758 (Chapter 758), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

758.555 Effect of energy sales on qualifying facility. A qualifying facility shall not become a public utility within the meaning of ORS 757.005 on account of sales made under ORS 543.610, 757.005 and 758.505 to 758.555. [1983 c.799 §6] 758.990 [Renumbered 757.992] _______________
