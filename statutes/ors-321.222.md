# Jurisdiction of board of property tax appeals SPECIAL ASSESSMENT OF WESTERN OREGON FORESTLAND (ORS 321.222)

## At a glance

ORS 321.222 — Jurisdiction of board of property tax appeals SPECIAL ASSESSMENT OF WESTERN OREGON FORESTLAND. Chapter 321 (Chapter 321), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

321.222 Jurisdiction of board of property tax appeals. Any appeal of forestland value that does not involve an appeal of a specially assessed value certified under ORS 321.216 shall be made to the property value appeals board in the manner prescribed in ORS 309.100.
