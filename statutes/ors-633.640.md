# Dean may employ assistants (ORS 633.640)

## At a glance

ORS 633.640 — Dean may employ assistants. Chapter 633 (Chapter 633), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

633.640 Dean may employ assistants. For the purposes of performing the duties assigned to the dean, in carrying out ORS 633.511 to 633.750, the dean may employ necessary assistance and delegate to such assistants, analysts and inspectors so employed the duties assigned to the dean by those sections. [Amended by 2003 c.14 §383] 633.650 [Repealed by 1955 c.379 §23]
