# Environmentally sound practices; rules (ORS 692.410)

## At a glance

ORS 692.410 — Environmentally sound practices; rules. Chapter 692 (Chapter 692), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

692.410 Environmentally sound practices; rules. The State Mortuary and Cemetery Board shall adopt rules promoting environmentally sound death care practices. [2009 c.709 §7] Note: 692.410 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 692 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
