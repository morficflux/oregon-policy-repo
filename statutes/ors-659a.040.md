# Discrimination against worker applying for or inquiring about workers’ compensation benefits prohibited (ORS 659A.040)

## At a glance

ORS 659A.040 — Discrimination against worker applying for or inquiring about workers’ compensation benefits prohibited. Chapter 659A (Chapter 659A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

659A.040 Discrimination against worker applying for or inquiring about workers’ compensation benefits prohibited. It is an unlawful employment practice for any person acting on behalf of an employer to discriminate against a worker with respect to hire or tenure or any term or condition of employment because the worker has:

(1) Applied for or inquired about benefits under ORS chapter 656;

(2) Invoked or utilized the procedures provided for in ORS chapter 656; or

(3) Given testimony under the provisions of ORS chapter 656. [2001 c.621 §32; 2022 c.6 §4]
