# Application and construction (ORS 109.831)

## At a glance

ORS 109.831 — Application and construction. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.831 Application and construction. In applying and construing ORS 109.701 to 109.834, consideration must be given to the need to promote uniformity of the law with respect to its subject matter among states that enact it. [1999 c.649 §40] Note: See note under 109.701.
