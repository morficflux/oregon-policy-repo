# Public body; issuance of revenue bonds (ORS 450.690)

## At a glance

ORS 450.690 — Public body; issuance of revenue bonds. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.690 Public body; issuance of revenue bonds. A water authority is a public body for the purposes of ORS chapter 287A, and revenue bonds issued by a water authority shall be issued in accordance with ORS chapter 287A. [1987 c.863 §8; 1993 c.577 §29; 2007 c.783 §187]
