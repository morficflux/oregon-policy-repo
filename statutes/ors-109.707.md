# Proceedings governed by other law (ORS 109.707)

## At a glance

ORS 109.707 — Proceedings governed by other law. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.707 Proceedings governed by other law. ORS 109.701 to 109.834 do not govern a proceeding pertaining to the authorization of emergency medical care for a child. [1999 c.649 §3] Note: See note under 109.701. 109.710 [1973 c.375 §2; 1997 c.707 §23; repealed by 1999 c.649 §55]
