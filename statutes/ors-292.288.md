# State claim on advances (ORS 292.288)

## At a glance

ORS 292.288 — State claim on advances. Chapter 292 (Salaries and Expenses of State Officers and Employees), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

292.288 State claim on advances. The state shall have a prior claim against and a right to withhold any and all funds payable, or to become payable, by the state to any officer or employee up to the amount of such advance.
