# “Motor vehicle” defined for ORS (ORS 278.200)

## At a glance

ORS 278.200 — “Motor vehicle” defined for ORS. Chapter 278 (Insurance for Public Bodies), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

278.200 “Motor vehicle” defined for ORS 278.205 and 278.322. As used in ORS 278.205 and 278.322, “motor vehicles” includes commercial buses, as defined in ORS 801.200. [1979 c.842 §5; 1983 c.338 §905; 1985 c.16 §454; 1987 c.158 §37]
