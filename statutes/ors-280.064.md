# Period for use of revenues raised by local option tax (ORS 280.064)

## At a glance

ORS 280.064 — Period for use of revenues raised by local option tax. Chapter 280 (Chapter 280), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

280.064 Period for use of revenues raised by local option tax. Subject to ORS 294.305 to 294.565 and the applicable provisions of a charter, ordinance or resolution of a subdivision, a subdivision may use revenues raised by a local option tax beyond the period of years during which the subdivision is authorized to levy the local option tax if the revenue is used for the purpose authorized by the electors. [2003 c.195 §6] Note: 280.064 was added to and made a part of 280.040 to 280.145 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
