# Term of office for members of a governing board (ORS 352.084)

## At a glance

ORS 352.084 — Term of office for members of a governing board. Chapter 352 (Chapter 352), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

352.084 Term of office for members of a governing board. Notwithstanding the term of office specified in ORS 352.076, the term of a member appointed to a governing board by the Governor may be adjusted so that one-half, as nearly as possible, of the members of the board are appointed biennially. [2013 c.768 §7; 2017 c.440 §11]
