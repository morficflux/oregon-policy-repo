# Definitions (ORS 421.612)

## At a glance

ORS 421.612 — Definitions. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.612 Definitions. As used in ORS 421.611 to 421.630, unless the context otherwise requires:

(1) “Authority” means the Corrections Facilities Siting Authority as established in ORS 421.621.

(2) “Department” means the Department of Corrections. [1995 c.745 §2] Note: See note under 421.611.
