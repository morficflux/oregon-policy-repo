# Department of Transportation Human Trafficking Awareness Fund (ORS 377.885)

## At a glance

ORS 377.885 — Department of Transportation Human Trafficking Awareness Fund. Chapter 377 (Chapter 377), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

377.885 Department of Transportation Human Trafficking Awareness Fund.

(1) The Department of Transportation Human Trafficking Awareness Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the Department of Transportation Human Trafficking Awareness Fund shall be credited to the fund. Moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Department of Transportation to pay for the expenses incurred in carrying out the provisions of ORS 377.880. The fund consists of moneys appropriated or transferred to the fund and moneys received under subsection

(2) of this section.

(2) The department may receive gifts, grants or contributions from any source, whether public or private, to carry out the provisions of ORS 377.880. Moneys received under this subsection shall be deposited in the Department of Transportation Human Trafficking Awareness Fund. [2017 c.646 §3]
