# Proof of age; when affidavit required (ORS 106.050)

## At a glance

ORS 106.050 — Proof of age; when affidavit required. Chapter 106 (Marriage; Domestic Partnership), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

106.050 Proof of age; when affidavit required. The county clerk may accept any reasonable proof of the age of an applicant for a marriage license satisfactory to the clerk. The clerk may require proof of age by affidavit of some person other than either of the parties seeking the license if the clerk deems it necessary in order to determine the age of an applicant to the clerk’s satisfaction.
