# Branch captive insurer as pure captive insurer; rules (ORS 735.180)

## At a glance

ORS 735.180 — Branch captive insurer as pure captive insurer; rules. Chapter 735 (Chapter 735), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

735.180 Branch captive insurer as pure captive insurer; rules. Except as otherwise provided in ORS 735.150 to 735.190, a branch captive insurer must be a pure captive insurer with respect to business operations in this state, unless otherwise permitted by rule of the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services. [2012 c.84 §17] Note: See note under 735.150.
