# Definitions for ORS (ORS 169.155)

## At a glance

ORS 169.155 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 169 (Chapter 169), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

169.155 Definitions for ORS 169.155 and 169.166. As used in ORS 169.166 and this section:

(1) “Local correctional facility” includes lockups and temporary hold facilities.

(2) “Reasonable efforts to collect the charges and expenses” means that the provider has billed the individual to whom the emergency medical services were provided or the insurer or health care service contractor of the individual before seeking to collect from the keeper of the local correctional facility. [1979 c.530 §4; 1993 c.196 §6] 169.160 [Repealed by 1971 c.743 §432] 169.165 [1979 c.530 §2; 1981 c.690 §1; repealed by 1993 c.196 §12]
