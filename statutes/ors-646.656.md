# Remedies supplementary to existing statutory or common law remedies ANTITRUST LAW (ORS 646.656)

## At a glance

## Full text

646.656 Remedies supplementary to existing statutory or common law remedies. The remedies provided in ORS 336.184 and 646.605 to 646.652 are in addition to all other remedies, civil or criminal, existing at common law or under the laws of this state.
