## Full text

821.070 Failure to title; exemptions; penalty.

(1) A person commits the offense of failure to title a snowmobile if the person is the owner of a snowmobile that is in this state or is operating a snowmobile at any place in this state and the snowmobile has not been issued a title as provided under ORS 821.060.

(2) The requirement to title a snowmobile under this section does not apply if the snowmobile is any of the following: (a) Owned and operated by the United States, another state or political subdivision thereof. (b) Owned and operated by this state or by any city, district or political subdivision thereof. (c) Exempted from registration requirements by ORS 821.090. (d) A new snowmobile that is in the possession of a dealer for purposes of sale or display.

(3) The offense described in this section, failure to title a snowmobile, is a Class D traffic violation. [1985 c.16 §346; 1985 c.459 §6; 1989 c.991 §7; 1995 c.383 §105; 1995 c.774 §16; 1999 c.977 §27] (Registration for Snowmobiles)
