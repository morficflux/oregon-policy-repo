# Certification requirement (ORS 688.630)

## At a glance

ORS 688.630 — Certification requirement. Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.630 Certification requirement.

(1) It is unlawful for any person to act as a hemodialysis technician without being certified by the Oregon Health Authority.

(2) It is unlawful for any outpatient renal dialysis facility to authorize a person to act for it as a hemodialysis technician without being certified by the authority. [1997 c.580 §2; 2009 c.595 §1095; 2023 c.199 §6]
