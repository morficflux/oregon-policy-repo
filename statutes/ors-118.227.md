# Time period for refund (ORS 118.227)

## At a glance

ORS 118.227 — Time period for refund. Chapter 118 (Chapter 118), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

118.227 Time period for refund. The period prescribed for the Department of Revenue to allow or make a refund of any tax, or portion of tax, paid under this chapter shall be as provided in ORS 314.415. [2009 c.358 §3] Note: 118.227 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 118 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
