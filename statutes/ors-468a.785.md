## At a glance

## Full text

468A.785 Pilot programs.

(1) Upon the advice of the Indoor Air Pollution Task Force, the Environmental Quality Commission may establish a pilot program for any product designed for household or office use that is not adequately regulated by federal law that may be a threat to human health by contaminating indoor air.

(2) The Environmental Quality Commission may establish a voluntary product-labeling pilot program to identify products with a low potential for causing indoor air pollution. [Formerly 468.359] Note: See note under 468A.775. AGRICULTURAL OPERATIONS AND EQUIPMENT
