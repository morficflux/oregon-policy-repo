## At a glance

ORS 293.611 — Accounts and records of Secretary of State as Auditor of Public Accounts. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.611 Accounts and records of Secretary of State as Auditor of Public Accounts. The Secretary of State shall cause to be maintained accounts and records the Secretary of State considers necessary in the performance of constitutional functions as Auditor of Public Accounts. [1967 c.454 §72] 293.615 [Formerly 291.556; repealed by 1967 c.454 §119]
