# End of Lewis and Clark Trail (ORS 366.910)

## At a glance

ORS 366.910 — End of Lewis and Clark Trail. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.910 End of Lewis and Clark Trail. That portion of Broadway Street in Seaside which meets the Pacific Ocean shall be known as the end of the Lewis and Clark Trail. 366.915 [Repealed by 2015 c.138 §14]
