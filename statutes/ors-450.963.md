# Employees’ retirement system (ORS 450.963)

## At a glance

ORS 450.963 — Employees’ retirement system. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.963 Employees’ retirement system.

(1) An authority may establish an employees’ retirement system pursuant to ORS 450.963 to 450.973. The authority may enter into agreements necessary to establish the system and carry out the plan and may agree to modifications of such agreements from time to time.

(2) The retirement plan may provide for retirement benefits measured on the basis of services rendered or to be rendered by an employee, either before or after the date on which such employee first becomes a member of the retirement plan. The retirement plan may provide for a minimum of years of service and a minimum and maximum age of retirement for the employee. [1973 c.399 §10] 450.965 [1955 c.614 §55; repealed by 1971 c.727 §203]
