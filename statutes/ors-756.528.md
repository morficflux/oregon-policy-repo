# Segregation of issues (ORS 756.528)

## At a glance

ORS 756.528 — Segregation of issues. Chapter 756 (Public Utility Commission), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

756.528 Segregation of issues. At any time before the conclusion of the taking of evidence in a proceeding, the Public Utility Commission may segregate the issues involved and order separate hearings thereon at such times and places as the commission may prescribe. [1971 c.655 §43; 2005 c.638 §4] 756.530 [Amended by 1971 c.655 §38; renumbered 756.512]
