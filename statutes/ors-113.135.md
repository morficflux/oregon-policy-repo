## At a glance

## Full text

113.135 Designation of attorney to be filed. If the personal representative has employed an attorney to represent the personal representative in the administration of the estate, the personal representative shall file in the estate proceeding the name and post-office address of the attorney unless that information appears in the petition or the order appointing the personal representative. [1969 c.591 §93] 113.140 [Repealed by 1969 c.591 §305]
