# Criminal liability of an individual for corporate conduct JUSTIFICATION (ORS 161.175)

## At a glance

ORS 161.175 — Criminal liability of an individual for corporate conduct JUSTIFICATION. Chapter 161 (General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

161.175 Criminal liability of an individual for corporate conduct. A person is criminally liable for conduct constituting an offense which the person performs or causes to be performed in the name of or in behalf of a corporation to the same extent as if such conduct were performed in the person’s own name or behalf. [1971 c.743 §17] JUSTIFICATION
