## Full text

446.440 Application of condominium provisions to parks.

(1) The provisions of ORS 100.005 to 100.910 may be applied to a mobile home or manufactured dwelling park as defined in this chapter.

(2) Notwithstanding the provisions of subsection

(1) of this section a mobile home or manufactured dwelling park is not a condominium for purposes of local zoning and planning provisions.
