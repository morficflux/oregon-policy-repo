# “Credit union” defined for ORS (ORS 723.008)

## At a glance

ORS 723.008 — “Credit union” defined for ORS. Chapter 723 (Chapter 723), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

723.008 “Credit union” defined for ORS 723.136 and 723.464 to 723.498. As used in ORS 723.136 and 723.464 to 723.498, “credit union” means a credit union organized under this chapter, an interstate credit union doing business in this state or a federal credit union.
