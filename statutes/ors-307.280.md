# Effect of exemption under ORS (ORS 307.280)

## At a glance

ORS 307.280 — Effect of exemption under ORS. Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.280 Effect of exemption under ORS 307.250 on prior tax levied. Allowance of the exemption, under ORS 307.250, in any year shall not have the effect of canceling or permitting the cancellation of any tax levied in any prior year.
