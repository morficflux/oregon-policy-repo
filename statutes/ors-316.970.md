# Effect of chapter 493, Oregon Laws 1969 (ORS 316.970)

## At a glance

## Full text

316.970 Effect of chapter 493, Oregon Laws 1969. This chapter is intended to supersede any conflicting provisions of law in effect on August 22, 1969, to the extent of such conflict. [Formerly 316.802] 316.990 [1953 c.304 §115; repealed by 1957 c.632 §1 (314.991 enacted in lieu of 316.990 and 317.990)]
