## Full text

188.025 Apportionment duties of Secretary of State; rules. The Secretary of State shall adopt rules the secretary considers necessary in carrying out the secretary’s reapportionment duties under ORS 188.010 to 188.305 and section 6, Article IV of the Oregon Constitution. [Formerly 188.015] CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICTS
