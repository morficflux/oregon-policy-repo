# “Primary ownership record” (ORS 801.402)

## At a glance

ORS 801.402 — “Primary ownership record”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.402 “Primary ownership record.” “Primary ownership record” means:

(1) The manufacturer’s certificate of origin or equivalent record as determined by the Department of Transportation by rule;

(2) The current title issued for the vehicle by the State of Oregon or another jurisdiction; or

(3) Any other record determined by the department by rule to be a primary ownership record. [1989 c.927 §3; 1993 c.233 §9] 801.403 [2001 c.522 §2; renumbered 801.406 in 2007]
