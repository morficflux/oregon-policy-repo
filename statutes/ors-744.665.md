# Continuing education; rules THIRD PARTY ADMINISTRATORS (ORS 744.665)

## At a glance

ORS 744.665 — Continuing education; rules THIRD PARTY ADMINISTRATORS. Chapter 744 (Chapter 744), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

744.665 Continuing education; rules. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services by rule may establish requirements for continuing education that each insurance consultant must satisfy as a condition for continuation of the license. [1985 c.697 §14] THIRD PARTY ADMINISTRATORS
