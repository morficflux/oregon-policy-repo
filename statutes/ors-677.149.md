# Report on provisional licensure of internationally trained physicians CHANGING LOCATION OF PRACTICE (ORS 677.149)

## At a glance

## Full text

677.149 Report on provisional licensure of internationally trained physicians. Not later than February 1 of each odd-numbered year, the Oregon Medical Board shall report, in the manner provided in ORS 192.245, to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to health care, on the provisional licensure of internationally trained physicians under ORS 677.146. The report must include the following information from the two immediately preceding calendar years:

(1) The number of applications for provisional licensure received;

(2) The numbers of provisional licenses issued or denied;

(3) An aggregated list of employers that employed provisionally licensed internationally trained physicians; and

(4) Any disciplinary or remedial actions taken against provisionally licensed internationally trained physicians.
