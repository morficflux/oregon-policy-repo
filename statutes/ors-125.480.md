# Approval of accounting (Liabilities) (ORS 125.480)

## At a glance

ORS 125.480 — Approval of accounting (Liabilities). Chapter 125 (Chapter 125), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

125.480 Approval of accounting. Subject to appeal or vacation within the time allowed by law, an order, made upon notice and hearing, allowing an intermediate accounting of a conservator, is final as to the liabilities of the conservator concerning the matters considered in connection with the intermediate accounting. An order, made upon notice and hearing, allowing a final accounting is final as to all previously unsettled liabilities of the conservator to the protected person or successors relating to the conservatorship. [1995 c.664 §50] (Liabilities)
