# Rules (ORS 292.036)

## At a glance

ORS 292.036 — Rules. Chapter 292 (Salaries and Expenses of State Officers and Employees), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

292.036 Rules. The Oregon Department of Administrative Services may prescribe such rules and regulations as are necessary to carry out the provisions of ORS 292.014 to 292.036. [1955 c.495 §10] 292.038 [Formerly 292.020; 1959 c.588 §18; 1959 c.687 §5; 1967 c.346 §3; repealed by 1969 c.378 §11]
