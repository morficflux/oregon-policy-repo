# Modification of agreement; consideration not required (ORS 108.720)

## At a glance

ORS 108.720 — Modification of agreement; consideration not required. Chapter 108 (Chapter 108), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

108.720 Modification of agreement; consideration not required. After marriage, a premarital agreement may be amended or revoked only by a written agreement signed by the parties. The amended agreement or the revocation is enforceable without consideration. [1987 c.715 §5] Note: See note under 108.700.
