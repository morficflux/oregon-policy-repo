# Funding and assistance for planning projects (ORS 285B.460)

## At a glance

ORS 285B.460 — Funding and assistance for planning projects. Chapter 285B (Chapter 285B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285B.460 Funding and assistance for planning projects.

(1) The Oregon Infrastructure Finance Authority may provide financial or other assistance to a municipality for a planning project.

(2) The planning project may be a stand-alone project.

(3) The planning project may include an environmental action on a brownfield. For purposes of this subsection: (a) “Brownfield” has the meaning given that term in ORS 285A.185. (b) “Environmental action” has the meaning given that term in ORS 285A.188.
