# Fees for notice; rules (ORS 197.649)

## At a glance

ORS 197.649 — Fees for notice; rules. Chapter 197 (Chapter 197), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

197.649 Fees for notice; rules. The Land Conservation and Development Commission may establish by rule fees to cover the cost of notice given to persons by the Director of the Department of Land Conservation and Development under ORS 197.610

(4) and 197.615 (3). [1983 c.827 §11f; 1985 c.565 §28; 1991 c.612 §15; 2013 c.1 §20] Note: See note under 197.646.
