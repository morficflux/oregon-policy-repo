## Full text

716.185 Registered agent; registered office.

(1) Each Oregon savings bank shall continuously maintain in this state a registered agent and registered office that may be, but need not be, the same as any of its places of business.

(2) A registered agent shall be: (a) An individual who resides in this state and whose business office is identical to the registered office; (b) A domestic corporation, domestic limited liability company, domestic professional corporation or domestic nonprofit corporation whose business office is identical to the registered office; or (c) A foreign corporation, foreign limited liability company, foreign professional corporation or foreign nonprofit corporation authorized to transact business in this state whose business office is identical to the registered office. [2005 c.192 §8]
