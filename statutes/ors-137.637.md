# Determining length of determinate sentences (ORS 137.637)

## At a glance

ORS 137.637 — Determining length of determinate sentences. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.637 Determining length of determinate sentences. When a determinate sentence of imprisonment is required or authorized by statute, the sentence imposed shall be the determinate sentence or the sentence as provided by the rules of the Oregon Criminal Justice Commission, whichever is longer. [1989 c.790 §82; 1995 c.520 §2] Note: 137.637 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 137 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 137.640 [Repealed by 1961 c.359 §1] 137.650 [Repealed by 1961 c.359 §1]
