# Apprentices; authority to work (ORS 693.040)

## At a glance

ORS 693.040 — Apprentices; authority to work. Chapter 693 (Chapter 693), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

693.040 Apprentices; authority to work. An apprentice plumber may work at the trade of plumbing if the apprentice works under the supervision of a licensed journeyman plumber. [Amended by 1981 c.438 §13; 1993 c.397 §2; 2005 c.758 §45] 693.050 [Amended by 1969 c.540 §3; 1975 c.429 §14; 1977 c.873 §13; 1981 c.438 §14; 1987 c.414 §50a; 1993 c.744 §152; repealed by 2005 c.758 §56]
