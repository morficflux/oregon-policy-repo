# Establishment of school or other building after registration (ORS 475C.847)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.847 — Establishment of school or other building after registration. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.847 Establishment of school or other building after registration. If a building described in ORS 475C.833 (3)(d) that has not previously been attended by children is established within 1,000 feet of a medical marijuana dispensary, the medical marijuana dispensary may remain at its current location unless the Oregon Health Authority revokes the registration of the medical marijuana dispensary. [Formerly 475B.870; 2022 c.81 §27]
