# Civil penalty for violations of ORS 447.065 or (ORS 693.994)

## At a glance

ORS 693.994 — Civil penalty for violations of ORS 447.065 or. Chapter 693 (Chapter 693), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

693.994 Civil penalty for violations of ORS 447.065 or 693.111. The State Plumbing Board may impose a civil penalty on a person who violates ORS 447.065 or 693.111 or a board rule adopted thereunder. A civil penalty may not exceed $5,000. The imposition of civil penalties under this section is subject to ORS chapter 183. [2001 c.683 §24] Note: 693.994 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 693 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. _______________
