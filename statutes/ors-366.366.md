# Removal of trees (ORS 366.366)

## At a glance

ORS 366.366 — Removal of trees. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.366 Removal of trees. Notwithstanding any city, county or other local government charter or ordinance, the Department of Transportation may cut down or remove trees located within a state highway right of way without first obtaining a permit.
