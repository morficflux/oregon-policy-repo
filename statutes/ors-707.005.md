# Organization required to engage in banking or trust business (ORS 707.005)

## At a glance

ORS 707.005 — Organization required to engage in banking or trust business. Chapter 707 (Chapter 707), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

707.005 Organization required to engage in banking or trust business. It is unlawful for any person to engage in or transact a banking or trust business within this state except by means of an entity duly organized for the purpose. [1973 c.797 §50; 1997 c.631 §29]
