# Building permits; content (ORS 455.050)

## At a glance

ORS 455.050 — Building permits; content. Chapter 455 (Building Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

455.050 Building permits; content. All building permits issued in this state shall contain the following information:

(1) The name and address of the owner of the building or structure to be constructed or altered under the permit;

(2) The name and address of the builder or contractor, if known, who will perform the construction or alteration; and

(3) The street address and legal description or tax lot number of the property on which construction or alteration will occur. [Formerly 456.887] Note: 455.050 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 455 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
