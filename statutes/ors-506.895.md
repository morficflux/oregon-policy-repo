# Person aiding in commercial fishing law violation punishable as principal (ORS 506.895)

## At a glance

ORS 506.895 — Person aiding in commercial fishing law violation punishable as principal. Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.895 Person aiding in commercial fishing law violation punishable as principal. Any person who counsels, aids or assists in any violation of the commercial fishing laws shall incur the penalties provided for the person guilty of such violation. [1981 c.365 §18]
