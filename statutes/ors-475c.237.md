# Requirement that marijuana items comply with minimum standards (ORS 475C.237)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.237 — Requirement that marijuana items comply with minimum standards. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.237 Requirement that marijuana items comply with minimum standards.

(1) A marijuana item may not be sold or offered for sale within this state unless the marijuana item complies with the minimum standards prescribed by the statutory laws of this state.

(2) The Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission may prohibit the sale of a marijuana item by a marijuana retailer for a reasonable period of time for the purpose of determining whether the marijuana item complies with the minimum standards prescribed by the statutory laws of this state. [Formerly 475B.236]
