# Power of Oregon Health Authority to condition, suspend, revoke or refuse to renew radiology inspector accreditation; rules (ORS 453.790)

## At a glance

ORS 453.790 — Power of Oregon Health Authority to condition, suspend, revoke or refuse to renew radiology inspector accreditation; rules. Chapter 453 (Chapter 453), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

453.790 Power of Oregon Health Authority to condition, suspend, revoke or refuse to renew radiology inspector accreditation; rules. The Oregon Health Authority may condition, suspend, revoke or refuse to renew accreditation of a radiology inspector for the following reasons:

(1) Knowingly falsifying information included on the inspection report form supplied by the authority.

(2) Substantially failing to comply with authority procedures.

(3) Failing to meet authority accuracy requirements.

(4) Such other grounds as the authority may establish by rule. [1995 c.444 §10; 2009 c.595 §917]
