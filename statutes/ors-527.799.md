## Full text

527.799 Implementing pesticide application provisions; rules. The State Forestry Department may adopt rules to implement the provisions of ORS 527.786 to 527.793, 527.794, 527.795, 527.796, 527.797 and 527.798. [2022 c.33 §51] Note: 527.799 is repealed only if certain conditions are met.
