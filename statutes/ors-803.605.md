# Erroneous issuance of trip permit; refund of fee (ORS 803.605)

## At a glance

ORS 803.605 — Erroneous issuance of trip permit; refund of fee. Chapter 803 (Chapter 803), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

803.605 Erroneous issuance of trip permit; refund of fee. When the Department of Transportation determines that it has erroneously issued a trip permit to a person who did not require the permit, the department may refund to the person any fee the person paid for the permit. [1985 c.313 §6] Note: 803.605 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 803 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
