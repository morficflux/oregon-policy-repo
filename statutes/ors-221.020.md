# Authority to incorporate (ORS 221.020)

## At a glance

ORS 221.020 — Authority to incorporate. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.020 Authority to incorporate. The people of an area, no part of which lies in an incorporated city and in which 150 persons reside, may incorporate a city by approving at an election called and held according to ORS 221.031 to 221.061 a proposition provided by those sections for incorporating the city. 221.030 [Amended by 1965 c.579 §2; 1973 c.432 §2; repealed by 1981 c.890 §2 (221.031 enacted in lieu of 221.030)]
