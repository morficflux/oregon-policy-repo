# Election of board members at regular district election (Dissolution) (ORS 198.915)

## At a glance

ORS 198.915 — Election of board members at regular district election (Dissolution). Chapter 198 (Chapter 198), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

198.915 Election of board members at regular district election. At the first regular election held in the surviving or successor district, two or three district board members shall be elected as provided by ORS 198.910 (3). [1971 c.727 §48; 1993 c.424 §5] (Dissolution)
