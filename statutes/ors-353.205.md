## Full text

353.205 Credit for education and training received while serving in Armed Forces; rules. The Oregon Health and Science University shall give credit for education and training obtained by a person while serving in the Armed Forces of the United States, as defined in ORS 352.313. The education and training for which credit may be given must meet the standards adopted by the Oregon Health and Science University Board of Directors by rule. [2005 c.518 §5] (Physical Access Committee)
