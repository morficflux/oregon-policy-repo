# Agency assistance and advice regarding plan (ORS 496.273)

## At a glance

ORS 496.273 — Agency assistance and advice regarding plan. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.273 Agency assistance and advice regarding plan. All agencies of state government, as defined in ORS 174.111, are directed to assist and advise the State Department of Fish and Wildlife in the development of the Wildlife Corridor Action Plan and in the five-year reviews of the plan required under ORS 496.272. [2019 c.272 §2] Note: See note under 496.272.
