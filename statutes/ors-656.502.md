# “Fiscal year” defined (ORS 656.502)

## At a glance

ORS 656.502 — “Fiscal year” defined. Chapter 656 (Chapter 656), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

656.502 “Fiscal year” defined. As used in ORS 656.502 to 656.526, “fiscal year” means the period of time commencing on July 1 and ending on the succeeding June 30.
