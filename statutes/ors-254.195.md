## Full text

254.195 Ballot printing specifications; sample ballots.

(1) Official ballots shall be printed in black ink upon good quality material.

(2) The governing body of a city, county or district may mail sample ballots to all electors within the city, county or district to assist the electors’ preparation for voting.
