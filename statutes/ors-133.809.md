# Release (ORS 133.809)

## At a glance

ORS 133.809 — Release. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.809 Release. Unless the offense with which the prisoner is charged is shown to be an offense punishable by death or life imprisonment under the laws of the state in which it was committed, the judge or magistrate must make a release decision concerning the person arrested under ORS 135.230 to 135.290, for the appearance of the person at a time specified in the security release or in the release agreement. [Formerly 147.160] 133.810 [Amended by 1973 c.836 §141; renumbered 135.175]
