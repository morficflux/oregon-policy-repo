# Rules (ORS 468A.230)

## At a glance

ORS 468A.230 — Rules. Chapter 468A (Chapter 468A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468A.230 Rules. The Oregon Climate Action Commission may adopt by rule such standards and procedures as it considers necessary for the operation of the commission. [2007 c.907 §8; 2023 c.442 §43] Note: See note under 468A.200.
