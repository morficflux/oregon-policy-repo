## Full text

401.307 Emergency management agency appropriation; tax levy.

(1) Each county and city may make appropriations, in the manner provided by law for making appropriations for the expenses of the county or city, for the payment of expenses of its emergency management agency and may levy taxes upon the taxable property within the county or city.

(2) An appropriation made under subsection

(1) of this section shall be budgeted so that it is possible to identify it as a distinguishable expense category. [Formerly 401.325] (Declaration of State of Emergency)
