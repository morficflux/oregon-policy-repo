# Collection of certain registration fees for State Parks and Recreation Department Fund (ORS 366.512)

## At a glance

ORS 366.512 — Collection of certain registration fees for State Parks and Recreation Department Fund. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.512 Collection of certain registration fees for State Parks and Recreation Department Fund.

(1) The Department of Transportation shall collect all registration fees for campers, motor homes and travel trailers. Such fees shall be paid into the State Parks and Recreation Department Fund.

(2) As used in this section: (a) “Camper” has the meaning given that term in ORS 801.180. (b) “Motor home” has the meaning given that term in ORS 801.350. (c) “Travel trailer” has the meaning given that term in ORS 801.565.
