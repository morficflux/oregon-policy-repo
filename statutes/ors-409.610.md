# Legislative goal MISCELLANEOUS (ORS 409.610)

## At a glance

ORS 409.610 — Legislative goal MISCELLANEOUS. Chapter 409 (Department of Human Services), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

409.610 Legislative goal. It is the goal of the Legislative Assembly to provide programs to make child care services more affordable, to improve the quality of services offered and to increase the number of child care providers. Programs should be tailored to the needs of local communities and should include a combination of actions that will address both targeted populations, such as teen parents or children with disabilities, and low-income working or student parents.
