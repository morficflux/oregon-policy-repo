# Penalties (ORS 462.990)

## At a glance

ORS 462.990 — Penalties. Chapter 462 (Chapter 462), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

462.990 Penalties.

(1) Except as provided in this section, violations of any provision of this chapter is a Class A misdemeanor.

(2) Any person violating the provisions of ORS 462.420, 462.430, 462.450, 462.460, 462.470 or 462.415

(2) commits a Class C felony.

(3) Any person who conspires or attempts to commit or commits any act of touting as defined in ORS 462.510 commits a Class B misdemeanor.

(4) Any person violating the provisions of ORS 462.140

(1) commits a Class A misdemeanor. [Part of subsection

(1) derived from 1957 c.313 §22; subsection

(2) enacted as 1953 c.498 §8; subsection

(3) enacted as 1953 c.499 §5; 1955 c.538 §2; 1969 c.356 §34; 1969 c.528 §§1,2; 1987 c.320 §235; 2011 c.597 §206]
