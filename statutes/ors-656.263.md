# To whom notices sent under ORS 656.262, (ORS 656.263)

## At a glance

ORS 656.263 — To whom notices sent under ORS 656.262,. Chapter 656 (Chapter 656), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

656.263 To whom notices sent under ORS 656.262, 656.265, 656.268 to 656.289, 656.295 to 656.325 and 656.382 to 656.388. All notices of proceedings required to be sent under ORS 656.262, 656.265, 656.268 to 656.289, 656.295 to 656.325, 656.382 to 656.388 and this section shall be sent to the employer and the insurer, if any. [1967 c.97 §2; 1975 c.556 §42]
