# Effect of chapter on Oregon Securities Law PENALTIES (ORS 645.320)

## At a glance

ORS 645.320 — Effect of chapter on Oregon Securities Law PENALTIES. Chapter 645 (Commodity Transactions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

645.320 Effect of chapter on Oregon Securities Law. Nothing in this chapter shall impair, derogate or otherwise affect the authority or powers of the director under the Oregon Securities Law or the application of any provision thereof to any person or transaction. [1987 c.148 §9] PENALTIES
