# Notice of prohibition in policies and by adjusters (ORS 746.290)

## At a glance

ORS 746.290 — Notice of prohibition in policies and by adjusters. Chapter 746 (Chapter 746), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

746.290 Notice of prohibition in policies and by adjusters.

(1) An adjuster establishing loss under a motor vehicle liability insurance policy shall advise the insured of the provisions of ORS 746.280.

(2) Every motor vehicle liability insurance policy issued in this state after December 31, 1977, and any extension or renewal after that date of a policy issued before that date shall be accompanied by a statement in clear and conspicuous language approved by the director of: (a) The rights and responsibilities of the insured when a claim is submitted; and (b) The provisions of ORS 746.280. [1977 c.785 §4] Note: See note under 746.275.
