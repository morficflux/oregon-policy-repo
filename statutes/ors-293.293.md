# Funds held in trust by court APPROVING AND PAYING CLAIMS (Approval) (ORS 293.293)

## At a glance

ORS 293.293 — Funds held in trust by court APPROVING AND PAYING CLAIMS (Approval). Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.293 Funds held in trust by court.

(1) Whenever a court is required to hold funds in trust for the benefit of any party, all such funds shall be deposited with the State Treasurer in an account separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest on such funds shall be credited to the General Fund.

(2) Notwithstanding subsection

(1) of this section, if the amount in trust is $10,000 or more, upon motion of a party, the court may order that the amount deposited with the State Treasurer be placed in an interest bearing account and that the interest be credited or paid to a party or parties, as the court deems appropriate. [1987 c.117 §1; 1989 c.966 §20] Note: 293.293 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 293 or any series therein by legislative action.
