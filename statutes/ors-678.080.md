# Evidence of licensure (ORS 678.080)

## At a glance

ORS 678.080 — Evidence of licensure. Chapter 678 (Chapter 678), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

678.080 Evidence of licensure. A person to whom a license is issued under ORS 678.010 to 678.448, whenever requested to do so in relation to employment as a registered or practical nurse or nurse intern or in relation to enforcement of ORS 678.010 to 678.448, shall provide evidence of current licensure.
