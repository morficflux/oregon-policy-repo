# Authority of board to require fingerprints PRACTICE OF PHARMACY (ORS 689.207)

## At a glance

ORS 689.207 — Authority of board to require fingerprints PRACTICE OF PHARMACY. Chapter 689 (Chapter 689), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

689.207 Authority of board to require fingerprints. For the purpose of requesting a state or nationwide criminal records check under ORS 181A.195, the State Board of Pharmacy may require the fingerprints of a person who is:

(1) Applying for a license or certificate that is issued by the board;

(2) Applying for renewal of a license or certificate that is issued by the board; or

(3) Under investigation by the board. [2005 c.730 §68] Note: 689.207 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 689 or any series therein by legislative action.
