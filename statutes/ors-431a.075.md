# Liability of provider (ORS 431A.075)

## At a glance

ORS 431A.075 — Liability of provider. Chapter 431A (Chapter 431A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

431A.075 Liability of provider.

(1) A provider may not be held liable for acting in accordance with approved trauma system plans.

(2) A person who in good faith provides data or other information to the Oregon Trauma Registry in accordance with ORS 431A.085 to 431A.105 is immune from any civil or criminal liability that might otherwise be incurred or imposed with respect to provision of the data. [Formerly 431.617] Note: 431A.075 is repealed January 1, 2027. See sections 43 and 44, chapter 32, Oregon Laws 2024.
