# Definitions for ORS (ORS 127.002)

## At a glance

ORS 127.002 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 127 (Chapter 127), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

127.002 Definitions for ORS 127.005 to 127.045. For the purposes of ORS 127.005 to 127.045:

(1) “Agent” includes an attorney-in-fact.

(2) “Financially incapable” has the meaning given that term in ORS 125.005.

(3) “Incapacitated” has the meaning given that term in ORS 125.005. [2009 c.46 §1; 2021 c.272 §6; 2023 c.9 §8]
