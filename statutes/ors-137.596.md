# Probation violations; custodial sanctions; rules (ORS 137.596)

## At a glance

ORS 137.596 — Probation violations; custodial sanctions; rules. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.596 Probation violations; custodial sanctions; rules. The Oregon Criminal Justice Commission shall amend its rules to increase the jail and nonjail custody units that can be imposed as custodial sanctions for probation violations under ORS 137.595. The commission shall base the amendments on the existing rule structure and may not increase existing sanction limits by more than 60 days. [2001 c.737 §1] Note: See note under 137.592.
