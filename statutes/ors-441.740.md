# Judicial review (Quality Care Fund) (ORS 441.740)

## At a glance

ORS 441.740 — Judicial review (Quality Care Fund). Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.740 Judicial review. Judicial review of civil penalties imposed under ORS 441.710, shall be as provided under ORS 183.480, except that the court may, in its discretion, reduce the amount of the penalty. [1975 c.328 §7] Note: See note under 441.705. (Quality Care Fund)
