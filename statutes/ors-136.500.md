# Motion in arrest of judgment; basis and time for making (ORS 136.500)

## At a glance

ORS 136.500 — Motion in arrest of judgment; basis and time for making. Chapter 136 (Chapter 136), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

136.500 Motion in arrest of judgment; basis and time for making. A motion in arrest of judgment is an application on the part of the defendant that no judgment be rendered on a plea or verdict of guilty. It may be founded on either or both of the grounds specified in ORS 135.630

(1) and (4), and not otherwise. The motion must be made within the time allowed to file a motion for a new trial, and both such motions may be made and heard as the court directs. [Formerly 136.810]
