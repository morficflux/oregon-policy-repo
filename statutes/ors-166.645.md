## Full text

166.645 Hunting in cemeteries prohibited.

(1) Hunting in cemeteries is prohibited.

(2) As used in subsection

(1) of this section “hunting” has the meaning for that term provided in ORS 496.004.

(3) Violation of subsection

(1) of this section is a misdemeanor. [1973 c.468 §2; 1987 c.158 §30]
