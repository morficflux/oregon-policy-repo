# ORS 377.700 to (ORS 377.740)

## At a glance

ORS 377.740 — ORS 377.700 to. Chapter 377 (Chapter 377), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

377.740 ORS 377.700 to 377.844 not intended to authorize signs prohibited by other governmental units. Nothing in ORS 377.700 to 377.844 and 377.992 is intended to permit a person to erect or maintain any sign that is prohibited by any governmental unit. [1971 c.770 §25]
