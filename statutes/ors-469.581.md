# Advisory and technical committees (ORS 469.581)

## At a glance

ORS 469.581 — Advisory and technical committees. Chapter 469 (Chapter 469), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469.581 Advisory and technical committees. The Oregon Hanford Cleanup Board may establish any advisory and technical committee it considers necessary. Members of any advisory or technical committee established under this section may receive reimbursement for travel expenses incurred in the performance of their duties in accordance with ORS 292.495. [1987 c.514 §14; 1991 c.562 §2] Note: See note under 469.566.
