# Authority to repay certain loans and to manage Salem office buildings (ORS 276.112)

## At a glance

ORS 276.112 — Authority to repay certain loans and to manage Salem office buildings. Chapter 276 (Public Facilities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

276.112 Authority to repay certain loans and to manage Salem office buildings. The Oregon Department of Administrative Services may:

(1) Use moneys in the Oregon Department of Administrative Services Operating Fund to repay loans made from investing funds to acquire buildings under ORS 276.013, 276.015 and 276.110 to 276.137 before July 1, 1969.

(2) Alter, repair and equip buildings acquired for office buildings under ORS 276.013, 276.015 and 276.110 to 276.137 before July 1, 1969.
