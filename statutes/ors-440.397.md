# Filing of boundary change (ORS 440.397)

## At a glance

ORS 440.397 — Filing of boundary change. Chapter 440 (Chapter 440), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

440.397 Filing of boundary change. For purposes of ad valorem taxation, a boundary change must be filed in final approved form with the county assessor and the Department of Revenue as provided in ORS 308.225. [2001 c.138 §28]
