# Solicitations of moneys by commission APPRENTICESHIP AND TRAINING (ORS 660.523)

## At a glance

ORS 660.523 — Solicitations of moneys by commission APPRENTICESHIP AND TRAINING. Chapter 660 (Chapter 660), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

660.523 Solicitations of moneys by commission. The OregonServes Commission may apply for and accept grants, contributions and assistance from any federal, state or local government agency and any foundation, individual or organization for the purposes of:

(1) Performing commission duties under ORS 660.515;

(2) Implementing programs and policies necessary to carry out the duties, functions and powers of the commission; or

(3) Assisting residents and public and private entities in implementing commission programs and policies. [Formerly 458.575] 660.990 [Amended by 1967 c.6 §26; repealed by 1981 c.764 §20] _______________
