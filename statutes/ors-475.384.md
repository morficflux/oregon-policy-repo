# Trade association list of marketed products containing dextromethorphan; ability to request (ORS 475.384)

## At a glance

ORS 475.384 — Trade association list of marketed products containing dextromethorphan; ability to request. Chapter 475 (Chapter 475), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475.384 Trade association list of marketed products containing dextromethorphan; ability to request. Any trade association representing manufacturers of over-the-counter finished drug products containing dextromethorphan must provide to any requesting business that makes retail sales a list of the finished drug products containing dextromethorphan marketed by the trade association’s members. A business may make a request pursuant to this section only once per year. [2017 c.345 §3] Note: See note under 475.380.
