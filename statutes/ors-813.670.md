# Complaint process (ORS 813.670)

## At a glance

ORS 813.670 — Complaint process. Chapter 813 (Chapter 813), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

813.670 Complaint process. The Department of State Police shall adopt a procedure for a person to file a complaint with the department concerning the failure of a service center or manufacturer’s representative to comply with a requirement of ORS 813.660 to 813.680. The department shall:

(1) Provide a response to the complainant no later than 14 days after the date the complaint is filed;

(2) Complete an investigation of the complaint no later than 90 days after the date the complaint is filed; and

(3) Provide a written report of the results of the investigation to the service center or manufacturer’s representative and to the complainant. [2017 c.655 §5; 2017 c.655 §17; 2019 c.200 §§11,12]
