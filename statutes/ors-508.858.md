# Fee (ORS 508.858)

## At a glance

ORS 508.858 — Fee. Chapter 508 (Chapter 508), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

508.858 Fee. The annual fee for the vessel permit required by ORS 508.840 is:

(1) $140 for resident applicants.

(2) $196 for nonresident applicants. [1981 c.365 §31; 1991 c.701 §14; 2009 c.832 §36; 2011 c.613 §7; 2025 c.564 §67] Note 1: The amendments to 508.858 by section 69, chapter 564, Oregon Laws 2025, become operative January 1, 2028. See section 70, chapter 564, Oregon Laws 2025. The text that is operative from January 1, 2028, until January 1, 2030, is set forth for the user’s convenience. 508.858. The annual fee for the vessel permit required by ORS 508.840 is:

(1) $146 for resident applicants.

(2) $204 for nonresident applicants. Note 2: The amendments to 508.858 by section 71, chapter 564, Oregon Laws 2025, become operative January 1, 2030. See section 72, chapter 564, Oregon Laws 2025. The text that is operative on and after January 1, 2030, including amendments by section 69, chapter 564, Oregon Laws 2025, is set forth for the user’s convenience. 508.858. The annual fee for the vessel permit required by ORS 508.840 is:

(1) $152 for resident applicants.

(2) $212 for nonresident applicants.
