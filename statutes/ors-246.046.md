# Secretary of State and county clerks to seek out evidence of violations SECRETARY OF STATE (ORS 246.046)

## At a glance

ORS 246.046 — Secretary of State and county clerks to seek out evidence of violations SECRETARY OF STATE. Chapter 246 (Administration of Election Laws; Vote Recording Systems), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

246.046 Secretary of State and county clerks to seek out evidence of violations. The Secretary of State and each county clerk shall diligently seek out any evidence of violation of any election law.
