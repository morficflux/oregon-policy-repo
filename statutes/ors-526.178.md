# Going upon private property (ORS 526.178)

## At a glance

ORS 526.178 — Going upon private property. Chapter 526 (Forestry Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

526.178 Going upon private property. The State Board of Forestry, or any duly authorized representative of the board, may go upon private property in the manner provided by ORS 35.220 to determine the advisability or practicability of acquiring real property or any interest in real property.
