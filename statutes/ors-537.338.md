## At a glance

## Full text

537.338 Rules for state agency request for in-stream water right. The Water Resources Commission by rule shall establish standards, criteria and procedures by which a state agency included under ORS 537.336 may request an in-stream water right to be issued under ORS 537.336. [1987 c.859 §5] 537.340 [Formerly 537.290; renumbered 537.395 in 1987]
