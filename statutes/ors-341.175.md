# Adjustment of zone boundaries (ORS 341.175)

## At a glance

ORS 341.175 — Adjustment of zone boundaries. Chapter 341 (Chapter 341), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

341.175 Adjustment of zone boundaries. Except as provided in ORS 255.400 to 255.424, the board shall adjust the boundaries of zones established within a district as necessary to make them as nearly equal in population as is feasible according to the latest federal census. The board also shall adjust boundaries of zones as necessary to reflect boundary changes of the district. [Formerly 341.916; 1969 c.220 §3; 1971 c.513 §28; 1983 c.350 §199; 2019 c.449 §38]
