# Annual proof of qualification for farm vehicle registration (ORS 805.322)

## At a glance

ORS 805.322 — Annual proof of qualification for farm vehicle registration. Chapter 805 (Chapter 805), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

805.322 Annual proof of qualification for farm vehicle registration. The owner of a vehicle or combination that has four or more axles and that is registered as a farm vehicle under ORS 805.300 or has a farm device issued pursuant to ORS 805.400 shall annually show to the satisfaction of the Department of Transportation that the owner meets the qualifications of ORS 805.320. [1993 c.368 §4] 805.330 [1983 c.338 §771; 1985 c.16 §370; repealed by 1993 c.751 §106]
