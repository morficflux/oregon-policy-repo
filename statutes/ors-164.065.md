# Theft of lost, mislaid property (ORS 164.065)

## At a glance

ORS 164.065 — Theft of lost, mislaid property. Chapter 164 (Chapter 164), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

164.065 Theft of lost, mislaid property. A person who comes into control of property of another that the person knows or has good reason to know to have been lost, mislaid or delivered under a mistake as to the nature or amount of the property or the identity of the recipient, commits theft if, with intent to deprive the owner thereof, the person fails to take reasonable measures to restore the property to the owner. [1971 c.743 §126] 164.070 [Amended by 1965 c.253 §131; repealed by 1971 c.743 §432]
