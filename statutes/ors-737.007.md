# “Rating organization” defined (ORS 737.007)

## At a glance

ORS 737.007 — “Rating organization” defined. Chapter 737 (Chapter 737), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

737.007 “Rating organization” defined.

(1) As used in this chapter, unless the context requires otherwise, “rating organization” means: (a) Every person, other than an insurer, whether located within or outside this state who has as the person’s object or purpose the making of rates, rating plans or rating systems; or (b) Two or more insurers which act in concert for the purpose of making rates, rating plans or rating systems.

(2) Subsection

(1) of this section does not include, apply to or affect two or more insurers operating within the specific authorizations contained in ORS 737.275, 737.312, 737.365, 737.390 and 737.526. [1969 c.690 §14] 737.010 [Amended by 1967 c.359 §303; renumbered 737.280]
