## Full text

412.059 Investigation of eligibility of child; rules. Whenever the Department of Human Services receives a notification of the dependency of a child or an application for aid, a record shall promptly be made of the circumstances, relating the facts supporting the application and such other information as may be required by the rules and regulations of the department. [Formerly 418.115] 412.060 [Repealed by 1961 c.633 §14]
