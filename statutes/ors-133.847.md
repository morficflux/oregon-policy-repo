# Trial of extradited person for other crimes (ORS 133.847)

## At a glance

ORS 133.847 — Trial of extradited person for other crimes. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.847 Trial of extradited person for other crimes. After a person has been brought back to this state upon extradition proceedings, the person may be tried in this state for other crimes which the person may be charged with having committed here as well as that specified in the requisition for extradition. [Formerly 147.260] 133.850 [Renumbered 135.215]
