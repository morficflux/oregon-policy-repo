# Penalties (ORS 354.990)

## At a glance

ORS 354.990 — Penalties. Chapter 354 (Chapter 354), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

354.990 Penalties. Any property owner who knowingly makes any false verified declaration in order to obtain a service charge exemption under the provisions of ORS 354.690 is guilty of unsworn falsification and upon conviction shall be punished as provided in ORS 162.085.
