# Members who leave field of membership (ORS 723.186)

## At a glance

ORS 723.186 — Members who leave field of membership. Chapter 723 (Chapter 723), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

723.186 Members who leave field of membership. A member who leaves the credit union’s field of membership may be permitted to retain the member’s membership in the credit union. [1975 c.652 §21; 2009 c.234 §4] 723.188 [1989 c.550 §2; 1999 c.59 §226; 1999 c.316 §9; repealed by 2015 c.458 §4]
