# Definition of “livestock” (ORS 609.125)

## At a glance

ORS 609.125 — Definition of “livestock”. Chapter 609 (Chapter 609), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

609.125 Definition of “livestock.” As used in ORS 609.135 to 609.190, “livestock” means ratites, psittacines, horses, hinnies, mules, donkeys, cattle, llamas, alpacas, sheep, goats, swine, domesticated fowl and any fur-bearing animal bred and maintained commercially or otherwise, within pens, cages and hutches. [1999 c.756 §11; 2025 c.170 §2] 609.130 [Repealed by 1977 c.802 §15]
