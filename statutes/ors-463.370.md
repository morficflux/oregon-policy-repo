# Distribution of tax revenues (ORS 463.370)

## At a glance

ORS 463.370 — Distribution of tax revenues. Chapter 463 (Chapter 463), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

463.370 Distribution of tax revenues. After deduction of administrative costs of the Oregon State Athletic Commission established under ORS 463.113, 75 percent of the remaining gross receipts tax, as described in ORS 463.320 and deposited in the subaccount of the State Police Account established under ORS 181A.020, shall be credited to and deposited in the subaccount established pursuant to section 36 (2), chapter 1084, Oregon Laws 1999, or a successor subaccount, account or fund.
