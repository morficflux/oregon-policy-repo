# Representation before tax court magistrate by officer or employee of local government or department (ORS 305.245)

## At a glance

ORS 305.245 — Representation before tax court magistrate by officer or employee of local government or department. Chapter 305 (Chapter 305), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

305.245 Representation before tax court magistrate by officer or employee of local government or department. Notwithstanding ORS 8.690, 9.160, 9.320, ORS chapter 180, ORS 203.145 or other law, in any conference or proceeding before a tax court magistrate with respect to the administration of any tax, a local government or the Department of Revenue may be represented by any officer or authorized employee of the local government or department. [1985 c.761 §39; 1995 c.650 §14; 2024 c.52 §31] 305.250 [1969 c.520 §12; repealed by 1975 c.705 §12]
