# Donation and distribution of prohibited cosmetics (ORS 646A.017)

## At a glance

ORS 646A.017 — Donation and distribution of prohibited cosmetics. Chapter 646A (Chapter 646A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646A.017 Donation and distribution of prohibited cosmetics. Notwithstanding ORS 646A.009:

(1) A cosmetic that does not meet the requirements of ORS 646A.009 may be donated to a food bank, homeless shelter, hospital, animal shelter, corrections facility or emergency shelter.

(2) An entity described in subsection

(1) of this section that receives a cosmetic donated pursuant to subsection

(1) of this section may distribute the cosmetic to an individual who is receiving services from the entity. [2023 c.536 §4] 646A.018 [Formerly 646.193; repealed by 2009 c.170 §4] 646A.020 [Formerly 646.195; repealed by 2009 c.170 §4]
