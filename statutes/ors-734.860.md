# Exemption of association from payment of fees and taxes (ORS 734.860)

## At a glance

ORS 734.860 — Exemption of association from payment of fees and taxes. Chapter 734 (Chapter 734), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

734.860 Exemption of association from payment of fees and taxes. The Oregon Life and Health Insurance Guaranty Association shall be exempted from payment of all fees and all taxes levied by this state or any of its political subdivisions, except taxes levied on real property. [1975 c.251 §17]
