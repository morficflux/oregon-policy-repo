# Partial apportionments (ORS 328.030)

## At a glance

ORS 328.030 — Partial apportionments. Chapter 328 (Chapter 328), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

328.030 Partial apportionments. The executive officer of the administrative office for the county, upon the written request of any district school board, may make a partial apportionment to any district of any money due it at the time of making a regular apportionment under ORS 328.015, and apportion the remainder at the next regular apportionment. The county treasurer shall pay any partial apportionment made under this section. [Amended by 1963 c.544 §19; 1965 c.100 §48] 328.035 [1971 c.449 §4; 1985 c.555 §15; repealed by 2001 c.36 §3]
