# Powers of director (ORS 456.565)

## At a glance

ORS 456.565 — Powers of director. Chapter 456 (Chapter 456), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

456.565 Powers of director. The Director of the Housing and Community Services Department, in addition to the director’s other powers, shall have the power, subject to the applicable provisions of the State Personnel Relations Law, to appoint all subordinate officers and employees of the Housing and Community Services Department and prescribe their duties and fix their compensation. [1991 c.739 §13]
