# Legislative findings (ORS 463.018)

## At a glance

ORS 463.018 — Legislative findings. Chapter 463 (Chapter 463), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

463.018 Legislative findings. The Legislative Assembly finds that the unarmed combat sports and entertainment wrestling industries in this state should be regulated in order to protect the best interests of entertainment wrestlers, unarmed combat sports competitors and the public.
