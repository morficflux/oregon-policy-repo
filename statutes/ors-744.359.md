# Disclosure by life settlement broker or provider to insurer (ORS 744.359)

## At a glance

ORS 744.359 — Disclosure by life settlement broker or provider to insurer. Chapter 744 (Chapter 744), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

744.359 Disclosure by life settlement broker or provider to insurer. Prior to the initiation of a plan, transaction or series of transactions, a life settlement broker or life settlement provider shall fully disclose to an insurer the plan, transaction or series of transactions to which the life settlement broker or life settlement provider is a party, to originate, renew, continue or finance a life insurance policy with the insurer for the purpose of engaging in the business of life settlements at anytime prior to, or during the first five years after, issuance of the policy. [2009 c.711 §12] 744.360 [Repealed by 1967 c.359 §704]
