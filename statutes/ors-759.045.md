# Special rules for utilities exempted from regulation under ORS (ORS 759.045)

## At a glance

ORS 759.045 — Special rules for utilities exempted from regulation under ORS. Chapter 759 (Chapter 759), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

759.045 Special rules for utilities exempted from regulation under ORS 759.040. The Public Utility Commission shall adopt specific rules to apply to telecommunications utilities which are exempted from certain regulation under ORS 759.040. An objective of these rules shall be to minimize the regulatory burden on these utilities to the extent this objective is feasible and consistent with the public interest. These rules shall not pertain to the statutes from which these utilities are exempted under ORS 759.040. [1991 c.658 §2]
