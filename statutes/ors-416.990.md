# Penalties (ORS 416.990)

## At a glance

ORS 416.990 — Penalties. Chapter 416 (Chapter 416), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

416.990 Penalties. Any person who makes, renders, signs or verifies any false or fraudulent statement, or supplies any false or fraudulent information with intent to evade any lawful requirement of the Department of Human Services or the Oregon Health Authority is guilty of a misdemeanor. [Formerly part of 411.990; 2001 c.900 §225; 2009 c.595 §356] _______________
