# Initiative and referendum in districts (ORS 372.280)

## At a glance

ORS 372.280 — Initiative and referendum in districts. Chapter 372 (Chapter 372), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

372.280 Initiative and referendum in districts. The electors of a district may exercise the initiative and referendum powers with reference to legislation of the district, in accordance with ORS 255.135 to 255.205. [Amended by 1971 c.514 §26; 1983 c.350 §247] 372.290 [Repealed by 1971 c.514 §32 and 1971 c.727 §203; (372.450, 372.460, 372.470 and 372.480 enacted in lieu of 372.290)]
