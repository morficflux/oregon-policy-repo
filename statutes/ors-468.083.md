# Right conferred under ORS 468.076 to (ORS 468.083)

## At a glance

ORS 468.083 — Right conferred under ORS 468.076 to. Chapter 468 (Chapter 468), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468.083 Right conferred under ORS 468.076 to 468.087 in addition to other rights. The right provided in ORS 468.076 to 468.087 is in addition to, and not in derogation of, any other right. [1991 c.826 §7] Note: See note under 468.076.
