# Reporting lost boat (ORS 830.050)

## At a glance

ORS 830.050 — Reporting lost boat. Chapter 830 (Small Watercraft _______________ 2025 EDITION SMALL WATERCRAFT SMALL WATERCRAFT GENERAL PROVISIONS 830.005 Definitions for chapter 830.015 Application of ORS 83), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

830.050 Reporting lost boat. If any person finds a boat which is lost or adrift the person shall report the whereabouts of the boat to the owner or to any peace officer at the earliest possible time. [Formerly 488.124]
