# Certain remedial statutes to be construed liberally (ORS 476.720)

## At a glance

ORS 476.720 — Certain remedial statutes to be construed liberally. Chapter 476 (State Fire Marshal; Protection From Fire Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

476.720 Certain remedial statutes to be construed liberally. ORS 476.010 to 476.090, 476.155 to 476.170, 476.210 to 476.270, 476.990 (1)(a) and 479.168 to 479.190 are remedial in nature and shall be construed liberally. [Amended by 2011 c.97 §2; 2023 c.347 §5]
