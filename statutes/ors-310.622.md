## At a glance

## Full text

310.622 Manufactured structures eligible as homesteads under tax laws of state. A manufactured structure assessed under the ad valorem tax laws of this state shall be eligible to be a homestead for the purposes of all tax laws of this state giving a right or privilege to a homestead. For those manufactured structures assessed as real property, the manufactured structure homestead includes land and improvements to the same extent that a homestead would be recognized if the manufactured structure were a conventional home. [1971 c.529 §11; 1977 c.884 §16] Note: The definition of “manufactured structure” provided in 307.021 applies to ORS chapter 310. (Floating Homes)
