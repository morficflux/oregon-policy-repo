# Communication between courts (ORS 125.807)

## At a glance

ORS 125.807 — Communication between courts. Chapter 125 (Chapter 125), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

125.807 Communication between courts.

(1) A court of this state may communicate with a court in another state concerning a proceeding arising under ORS 125.800 to 125.852. The court may allow the parties to participate in the communication. Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, the court shall make a record of the communication. The record may be limited to the fact that the communication occurred.

(2) Courts may communicate concerning schedules, calendars, court records and other administrative matters without making a record. [2009 c.179 §4] Note: See note under 125.800.
