# Printing or furnishing of ballots by county clerk (ORS 254.185)

## At a glance

ORS 254.185 — Printing or furnishing of ballots by county clerk. Chapter 254 (Chapter 254), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

254.185 Printing or furnishing of ballots by county clerk. The county clerk shall print or furnish all the required ballots and shall provide them for use by electors in the county. Only ballots printed or furnished under this section may be used in an election. [Formerly 250.080; 2007 c.154 §30] 254.190 [Repealed by 1979 c.190 §431]
