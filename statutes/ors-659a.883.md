# Contractual shortening of statute of limitations prohibited (ORS 659A.883)

## At a glance

ORS 659A.883 — Contractual shortening of statute of limitations prohibited. Chapter 659A (Chapter 659A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

659A.883 Contractual shortening of statute of limitations prohibited. It is an unlawful employment practice for an employer to enter into an agreement with a former, current or prospective employee that has the effect of shortening a statute of limitations with respect to a violation over which the Bureau of Labor and Industries has enforcement authority. [2025 c.379 §2]
