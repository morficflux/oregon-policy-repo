# Boating safety policy (ORS 830.100)

## At a glance

ORS 830.100 — Boating safety policy. Chapter 830 (Small Watercraft _______________ 2025 EDITION SMALL WATERCRAFT SMALL WATERCRAFT GENERAL PROVISIONS 830.005 Definitions for chapter 830.015 Application of ORS 83), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

830.100 Boating safety policy. It is the policy of this state to promote safety for persons and property in and connected with the use, operation and equipment of boats and to promote uniformity of laws relating thereto. [Formerly 488.710]
