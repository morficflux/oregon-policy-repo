# Superintendent to administer statutes related to state financing of education; board rules (ORS 327.125)

## At a glance

ORS 327.125 — Superintendent to administer statutes related to state financing of education; board rules. Chapter 327 (Chapter 327), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

327.125 Superintendent to administer statutes related to state financing of education; board rules. The Superintendent of Public Instruction shall administer the provisions of ORS 327.006 to 327.173, 327.348, 327.356 to 327.359 and 327.731. The State Board of Education shall adopt all necessary rules not inconsistent with ORS 327.006 to 327.173, 327.348, 327.356 to 327.359 and 327.731 to carry into effect the provisions of those statutes. [Amended by 1963 c.570 §8; 1965 c.100 §29; 1989 c.491 §6; 1991 c.780 §17; 2007 c.846 §14; 2013 c.735 §§10,11; 2021 c.355 §7]
