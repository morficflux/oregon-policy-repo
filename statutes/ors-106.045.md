## Full text

106.045 Fee for marriage license; purpose.

(1) In addition to any other fees provided by law, the county clerk shall collect a fee of $25 upon the application for a marriage license.

(2) The county clerk shall regularly pay over to the Department of Human Services all moneys collected under subsection

(1) of this section to be credited to the Domestic Violence Fund pursuant to ORS 409.300. [1981 c.357 §1; 1983 c.480 §6; 1987 c.740 §1; 2009 c.595 §65a; 2011 c.720 §55] Note: 106.045 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 106 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
