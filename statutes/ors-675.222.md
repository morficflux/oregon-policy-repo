# Employing unlicensed person prohibited (Licensing) (ORS 675.222)

## At a glance

ORS 675.222 — Employing unlicensed person prohibited (Licensing). Chapter 675 (Chapter 675), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

675.222 Employing unlicensed person prohibited. No person shall hire or employ a person to practice as an occupational therapist or as an occupational therapy assistant unless the person hired or employed is licensed in accordance with ORS 675.210 to 675.340. [1991 c.791 §4] (Licensing)
