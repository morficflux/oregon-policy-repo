# Disclosure of information (ORS 743B.550)

## At a glance

ORS 743B.550 — Disclosure of information. Chapter 743B (Chapter 743B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743B.550 Disclosure of information. Nothing in ORS 743.008, 743A.012, 743B.195, 743B.197, 743B.200, 743B.202, 743B.204, 743B.250, 743B.400, 743B.403, 743B.420, 743B.423 and 743B.550 shall be construed to require disclosure of information that is otherwise privileged or confidential under any other provision of law. [Formerly 743.839; 2021 c.97 §83]
