# “Funeral escort vehicle”; “funeral lead vehicle”; “funeral procession” (ORS 801.288)

## At a glance

ORS 801.288 — “Funeral escort vehicle”; “funeral lead vehicle”; “funeral procession”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.288 “Funeral escort vehicle”; “funeral lead vehicle”; “funeral procession.”

(1) “Funeral escort vehicle” means any two-wheel or three-wheel vehicle that is accompanying a funeral procession and is properly equipped under ORS 811.800.

(2) “Funeral lead vehicle” means any vehicle that is properly equipped under ORS 811.800 and is used to lead and facilitate the movement of a funeral procession.

(3) “Funeral procession” means two or more vehicles, including any funeral lead vehicle or funeral escort vehicle, accompanying the body or cremated or reduced remains of a deceased person. [1991 c.482 §§2,3,4; 2021 c.296 §31]
