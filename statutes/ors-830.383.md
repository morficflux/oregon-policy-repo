## Full text

830.383 Person required to remedy especially hazardous condition. A person may not intentionally disobey a lawful order to take immediate and reasonable steps to remedy an especially hazardous condition that is issued under ORS 830.380 by another person known by the person to be a peace officer. [1997 c.568 §3] Note: See note under 830.380.
