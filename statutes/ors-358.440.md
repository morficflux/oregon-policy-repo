# Notice to lenders HERITAGE DISTRICTS (ORS 358.440)

## At a glance

ORS 358.440 — Notice to lenders HERITAGE DISTRICTS. Chapter 358 (Chapter 358), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

358.440 Notice to lenders. When a museum accepts a loan of property, the museum shall inform the lender in writing of the provisions of ORS 358.420 to 358.440. [1985 c.580 §6] HERITAGE DISTRICTS
