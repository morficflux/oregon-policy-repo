# Collateral not required for deferred compensation funds (ORS 295.022)

## At a glance

## Full text

295.022 Collateral not required for deferred compensation funds. Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, when a depository receives moneys of the Deferred Compensation Fund established under ORS 243.411 from the state for deposit or investment, the depository shall not have to maintain the collateral required under this chapter for those deferred compensation moneys.
