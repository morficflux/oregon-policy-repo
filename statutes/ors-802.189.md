# Criminal penalty for violation of ORS (ORS 802.189)

## At a glance

ORS 802.189 — Criminal penalty for violation of ORS. Chapter 802 (Chapter 802), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

802.189 Criminal penalty for violation of ORS 802.175 to 802.187. Knowingly obtaining or using personal information from a motor vehicle record in violation of ORS 802.175 to 802.187 is a Class A misdemeanor. [1997 c.678 §9]
