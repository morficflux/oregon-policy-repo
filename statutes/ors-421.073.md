# Housing of Adults in Custody from Other Jurisdictions Account (ORS 421.073)

## At a glance

ORS 421.073 — Housing of Adults in Custody from Other Jurisdictions Account. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.073 Housing of Adults in Custody from Other Jurisdictions Account. The Housing of Adults in Custody from Other Jurisdictions Account is created within the General Fund. Moneys credited to the account are continuously appropriated to the Department of Corrections for costs of incarceration. The Department of Corrections shall deposit all moneys received by the department as reimbursement under ORS 169.053

(3) into the account. [1996 c.1 §2; 2019 c.213 §131] Note: 421.073 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 421 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
