# Authority to require segregation of premises (ORS 475C.161)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.161 — Authority to require segregation of premises. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.161 Authority to require segregation of premises. As is necessary to protect the public health and safety, the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission may require a premises licensed under ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525 to be segregated into separate areas:

(1) For conducting the activities permitted under each license, if the licensee holds more than one license issued under ORS 475C.005 to 475C.525 for the same premises;

(2) For conducting activities related to processing marijuana into different types of cannabinoid products, cannabinoid concentrates or cannabinoid extracts, if the licensee is a marijuana processor that holds a license issued under ORS 475C.085 and that processes marijuana into any combination of different types of products, concentrates and extracts; or

(3) For producing marijuana and processing marijuana as described in ORS 475C.089 if the licensee is a marijuana producer that holds a license issued under ORS 475C.065 and that processes marijuana as described in ORS 475C.089. [Formerly 475B.158]
