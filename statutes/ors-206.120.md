## Full text

206.120 Disposition of money in custody when office vacant. When the official term of office of any sheriff ends by expiration of the term, death, resignation, removal from office or otherwise, the money in the custody of the sheriff by virtue of the office of the sheriff, belonging to the county or litigants, shall be turned over immediately to the successor in office, and duplicate itemized receipts therefor immediately shall be filed with the county treasurer. 206.130 [Renumbered 206.310] 206.140 [Renumbered 206.320] 206.150 [Renumbered 206.330] 206.160 [Renumbered 206.340] 206.170 [Repealed by 1963 c.331 §13]
