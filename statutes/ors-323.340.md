## At a glance

ORS 323.340 — Reporting requirements for distributors. Chapter 323 (Chapter 323), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

323.340 Reporting requirements for distributors.

(1) On or before the 20th day of January, April, July and October, every distributor shall file on forms prescribed by the Department of Revenue a report containing any information the department may require to carry out the purposes of ORS 323.005 to 323.482.

(2) A distributor holding more than one distributor’s license and having centralized accounting may file one composite report combining the information required of each license location under subsection

(1) of this section. [1965 c.525 §73; 1971 c.416 §1; 1999 c.62 §15]
