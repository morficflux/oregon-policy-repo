# Issuance or use of proceeds contrary to commission order (ORS 759.355)

## At a glance

ORS 759.355 — Issuance or use of proceeds contrary to commission order. Chapter 759 (Chapter 759), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

759.355 Issuance or use of proceeds contrary to commission order. No telecommunications utility shall directly or indirectly, issue or cause to be issued any stock or bond, note or other evidence of indebtedness in nonconformity with the order of the Public Utility Commission authorizing the same or contrary to the provisions of ORS 759.300 to 759.360, or of the Constitution of this state, or apply the proceeds from the sale thereof, or any part thereof, to any purpose other than the purposes specified in the commission’s order, or to any purpose specified in the commission’s order, in excess of the amount in the order authorized for such purpose. [1987 c.447 §39]
