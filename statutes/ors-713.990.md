# Civil penalties (ORS 713.990)

## At a glance

ORS 713.990 — Civil penalties. Chapter 713 (Chapter 713), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

713.990 Civil penalties. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may impose on any person that violates any provision of this chapter a civil penalty of up to $1,000 for each day during which the offense continues. The penalty shall be assessed and collected in the manner prescribed in ORS 706.570 (3). [1997 c.631 §308; 2015 c.244 §88] _______________
