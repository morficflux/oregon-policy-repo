# Determination of ordinary high and low water (ORS 274.015)

## At a glance

ORS 274.015 — Determination of ordinary high and low water. Chapter 274 (Chapter 274), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

274.015 Determination of ordinary high and low water. For purposes of this chapter, when the lines of ordinary high or low water cannot be determined by survey or inspection, then such lines shall be determined by the use of the annual mean high or mean low water for the preceding year. [1967 c.421 §99] 274.020 [Renumbered 274.890]
