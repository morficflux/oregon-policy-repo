# Issuance of licenses (ORS 686.095)

## At a glance

ORS 686.095 — Issuance of licenses. Chapter 686 (Chapter 686), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

686.095 Issuance of licenses. If upon an examination judged by the Oregon State Veterinary Medical Examining Board sufficiently comprehensive to enable the applicant to actively engage in the practice of veterinary medicine in this state, the applicant demonstrates a scientific and practical knowledge of the art of veterinary medicine, and the applicant complies with the other provisions of this chapter, the board shall issue a license to the applicant to practice veterinary medicine in this state. The license shall be signed by the president and executive director of the board, and attested by the board’s seal. [1953 c.624 §8; 2003 c.163 §2] 686.100 [Repealed by 1953 c.624 §9]
