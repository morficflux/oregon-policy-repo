# Public referral and information telephone number (ORS 403.410)

## At a glance

ORS 403.410 — Public referral and information telephone number. Chapter 403 (Chapter 403), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

403.410 Public referral and information telephone number. 2-1-1 is created as the official state dialing code for public referral to and information about health and human services and services after an emergency. [Formerly 401.286] 403.415 [Formerly 401.288; 2011 c.205 §3; 2021 c.539 §70; repealed by 2021 c.111 §4]
