## Full text

294.755 Payment on quarterly basis; remedies for collection. Political subdivisions electing to participate in the Local Government Employer Benefit Trust Fund shall pay into the fund on a quarterly basis and are subject to all remedies for the collection of delinquent taxes provided in ORS chapter 657. [1977 c.491 §10] LOCAL AND TRIBAL GOVERNMENT INVESTMENT POOL
