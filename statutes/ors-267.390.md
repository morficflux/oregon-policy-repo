# Acceptance of funds from United States (ORS 267.390)

## At a glance

ORS 267.390 — Acceptance of funds from United States. Chapter 267 (Chapter 267), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

267.390 Acceptance of funds from United States. The district may:

(1) Accept, without limitation by any other provision of ORS 267.010 to 267.394 requiring approval of indebtedness, any contributions or loans from the United States for the purpose of carrying out any provision of ORS 267.010 to 267.394; and

(2) Do anything that the board considers necessary in order to avail itself of aid, assistance or cooperation under this section under any federal law. [1969 c.643 §37]
