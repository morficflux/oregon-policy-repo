# Maximum amount of fee; adjustment of fee PENALTIES (ORS 776.810)

## At a glance

ORS 776.810 — Maximum amount of fee; adjustment of fee PENALTIES. Chapter 776 (Maritime Pilots and Pilotage), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

776.810 Maximum amount of fee; adjustment of fee.

(1) Subject to subsection

(2) of this section, the maximum board operations fee for a vessel is $100.

(2) The Oregon Board of Maritime Pilots shall adjust the amount of the maximum board operations fee for a vessel each biennium beginning July 1, 2015, by a proportional amount equal to the percentage change in the 24-month period prior to the beginning of the biennium in the Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumers, West Region (All Items), as published by the Bureau of Labor Statistics of the United States Department of Labor. [2013 c.539 §4; 2019 c.57 §35] PENALTIES
