# Application of Health Care Quality Improvement Act of 1986 (ORS 441.089)

## At a glance

ORS 441.089 — Application of Health Care Quality Improvement Act of 1986. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.089 Application of Health Care Quality Improvement Act of 1986. The provisions of the Health Care Quality Improvement Act of 1986, P.L. No. 99-660, 100 Stat. p. 3743, 3784 (1986) apply within this state for professional review actions pursuant to ORS 441.015 to 441.119 and 441.993. [1987 c.850 §5] Note: 441.089 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 441 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 441.090 [1971 c.730 §15; 1973 c.358 §6; 1973 c.840 §14; 1975 c.485 §1; 1977 c.751 §28; renumbered 442.320] 441.092 [1975 c.485 §4; 1977 c.751 §29; renumbered 442.330]
