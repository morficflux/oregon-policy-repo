# Energy Conservation Clearinghouse for Commerce and Industry (ORS 469.135)

## At a glance

ORS 469.135 — Energy Conservation Clearinghouse for Commerce and Industry. Chapter 469 (Chapter 469), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469.135 Energy Conservation Clearinghouse for Commerce and Industry. The State Department of Energy shall expand the Energy Conservation Clearinghouse for Commerce and Industry so that it provides:

(1) Current information to business and industry on: (a) State and federal financing mechanisms; (b) Tax advantages of energy conservation investments; and (c) General economic advantages of energy conservation investments.

(2) Teaching on conservation techniques and management of energy by corporations. [1981 c.865 §2]
