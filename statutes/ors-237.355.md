# Establishment of retirement system (ORS 237.355)

## At a glance

ORS 237.355 — Establishment of retirement system. Chapter 237 (Chapter 237), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

237.355 Establishment of retirement system. A city in which any pension system may be established by charter, may establish a retirement system pursuant to ORS 237.350 to 237.380. The council of the city may enact such ordinances as are necessary to put the system into effect and may supplement or change the ordinances from time to time. [Formerly 238.520]
