## At a glance

## Full text

813.136 Consequence of refusal or failure to submit to field sobriety tests. If a person refuses or fails to physically submit to field sobriety tests as required by ORS 813.135 after the person has been informed of the consequences of refusing to submit, evidence of the person’s refusal or failure to physically submit is admissible in any criminal or civil action or proceeding arising out of allegations that the person was driving while under the influence of intoxicants. [1989 c.576 §14; 2019 c.475 §6] CHEMICAL TESTS; METHODS AND REQUIREMENTS
