# Definitions for ORS 285B.530 to (ORS 285B.530)

## At a glance

ORS 285B.530 — Definitions for ORS 285B.530 to. Chapter 285B (Chapter 285B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285B.530 Definitions for ORS 285B.530 to 285B.548. As used in ORS 285B.530 to 285B.548, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “Infrastructure lottery bonds” means the bonds authorized to be issued under ORS 285B.533 for the purpose of financing infrastructure projects.

(2) “Infrastructure projects” includes: (a) A water project defined in ORS 285B.560; and (b) Payment of any state financial obligations to the federal government under the Safe Drinking Water Act. [1997 c.800 §16; 1999 c.44 §24] Note: 285B.530 to 285B.548 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 285B by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
