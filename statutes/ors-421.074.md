# Online access to educational and development programs; rules (ORS 421.074)

## At a glance

ORS 421.074 — Online access to educational and development programs; rules. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.074 Online access to educational and development programs; rules.

(1) No later than September 1, 2023, the Department of Corrections shall ensure that adults in custody in the Coffee Creek Correctional Facility and the Snake River Correctional Institution have online access to: (a) The adult basic skills development program described in ORS 421.084; (b) The professional and technical program described in ORS 421.081; and (c) Education programs that provide college credit, a college degree, a college certification, an industry-recognized certification or license or an apprenticeship.

(2) Nothing in this section establishes a legally enforceable right to online access to the programs described in this section for adults in custody.

(3) The department shall adopt any rules necessary for establishing program eligibility and participation criteria for online access for adults in custody. [2022 c.81 §17] Note: 421.074 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 421 or any series therein by legislative action.
