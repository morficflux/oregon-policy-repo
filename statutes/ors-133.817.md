# Persons under criminal prosecution in this state at time of requisition (ORS 133.817)

## At a glance

ORS 133.817 — Persons under criminal prosecution in this state at time of requisition. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.817 Persons under criminal prosecution in this state at time of requisition. If a criminal prosecution has been instituted against such person under the laws of this state and is still pending, the Governor, at the discretion of the Governor, either may surrender the person on the demand of the executive authority of another state or may hold the person until the person has been tried and discharged, or convicted and punished in this state. [Formerly 147.190] 133.820 [Amended by 1973 c.836 §142; renumbered 135.185]
