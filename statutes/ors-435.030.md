# Wholesale and manufacturer license (ORS 435.030)

## At a glance

ORS 435.030 — Wholesale and manufacturer license. Chapter 435 (Chapter 435), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

435.030 Wholesale and manufacturer license. Wholesale and manufacturer licenses shall be issued only to persons authorized to sell or distribute the appliances, drugs or medicinal preparations mentioned in ORS 435.010.
