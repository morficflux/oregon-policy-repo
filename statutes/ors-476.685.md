# Biennial reports WILDFIRE PROGRAMS (ORS 476.685)

## At a glance

## Full text

476.685 Biennial reports. The Governor’s Fire Service Policy Council shall provide a biennial report to the Governor on the council’s progress in supporting the mission of the Department of the State Fire Marshal. The report shall identify significant accomplishments, current challenges and opportunities for improvement. [2001 c.647 §2; 2021 c.539 §132; 2025 c.177 §9] Note: See note under 476.680.
