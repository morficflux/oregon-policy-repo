# Disbursal of money in county historical fund (ORS 358.210)

## At a glance

ORS 358.210 — Disbursal of money in county historical fund. Chapter 358 (Chapter 358), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

358.210 Disbursal of money in county historical fund. Upon the filing of the detailed estimate and approval thereof by the governing body of the county, all moneys in the county historical fund are subject to disbursal by warrants drawn by the historical society and signed by the president and secretary of the society. No money withdrawn from the fund shall be expended except for purposes set forth in ORS 358.200 and included in the detailed estimate. [Amended by 2023 c.173 §6]
