# General evidentiary privileges inapplicable (ORS 441.675)

## At a glance

ORS 441.675 — General evidentiary privileges inapplicable. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.675 General evidentiary privileges inapplicable. In the case of abuse of a resident in a long term care facility, the privileges extended under ORS 40.225 to 40.295 shall not be a ground for excluding evidence regarding the abuse of a resident, or the cause thereof, in any judicial proceeding resulting from a report made pursuant to ORS 441.640. [1979 c.770 §10; 1983 c.740 §158; 1993 c.759 §12]
