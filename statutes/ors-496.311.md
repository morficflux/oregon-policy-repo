# Limitation on size of revolving fund (ORS 496.311)

## At a glance

ORS 496.311 — Limitation on size of revolving fund. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.311 Limitation on size of revolving fund. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the revolving fund referred to in ORS 496.300 shall not exceed $40,000.
