# Appointments to fill vacancies OREGON PROJECT INDEPENDENCE (ORS 410.340)

## At a glance

ORS 410.340 — Appointments to fill vacancies OREGON PROJECT INDEPENDENCE. Chapter 410 (Chapter 410), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

410.340 Appointments to fill vacancies. In case of a vacancy on the Governor’s Commission on Senior Services, the appointing authority shall appoint a successor for the remainder of the unexpired term. [Formerly 184.910; 1983 c.740 §132] OREGON PROJECT INDEPENDENCE
