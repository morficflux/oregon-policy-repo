# Buyer and seller responsibilities (ORS 696.835)

## At a glance

ORS 696.835 — Buyer and seller responsibilities. Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.835 Buyer and seller responsibilities. None of the affirmative obligations of a real estate licensee or agent in a real estate transaction under ORS 696.805, 696.810 or 696.815 relieves a seller or a buyer from the responsibility to protect the seller’s or buyer’s own interests respectively. [1993 c.570 §9] Note: See note under 696.800.
