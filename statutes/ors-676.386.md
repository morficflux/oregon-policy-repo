# Requirement that doula, lactation counselor or lactation educator provide culturally specific services (ORS 676.386)

## At a glance

ORS 676.386 — Requirement that doula, lactation counselor or lactation educator provide culturally specific services. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.386 Requirement that doula, lactation counselor or lactation educator provide culturally specific services.

(1) As used in this section: (a) “Doula” has the meaning given that term in ORS 414.025. (b) “Lactation counselor” and “lactation educator” have the meanings given those terms in ORS 676.665.

(2) To the extent practicable, a doula, lactation counselor or lactation educator shall strive to provide services that are culturally specific, as defined in ORS 413.256, to a client or patient. [2025 c.539 §15]
