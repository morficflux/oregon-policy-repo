# Definition for ORS (ORS 285A.300)

## At a glance

ORS 285A.300 — Definition for ORS. Chapter 285A (Chapter 285A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285A.300 Definition for ORS 285A.300 to 285A.312. As used in ORS 285A.300 to 285A.312, “fund” means the Title I Bank Fund. [Formerly 285.680; 1999 c.509 §12]
