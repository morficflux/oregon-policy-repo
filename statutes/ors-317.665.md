# Oregon net losses of insurer in prior years (ORS 317.665)

## At a glance

ORS 317.665 — Oregon net losses of insurer in prior years. Chapter 317 (Chapter 317), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

317.665 Oregon net losses of insurer in prior years. In computing Oregon taxable income, an insurer shall be allowed as a deduction an amount equal to the aggregate Oregon net losses of prior years as defined in ORS 317.476. [Formerly 317.299; 1995 c.786 §18]
