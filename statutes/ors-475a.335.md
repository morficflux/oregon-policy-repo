# Requirement to verify person’s age; rules (Psilocybin Services) (ORS 475A.335)

## At a glance

ORS 475A.335 — Requirement to verify person’s age; rules (Psilocybin Services). Chapter 475A (Chapter 475A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475A.335 Requirement to verify person’s age; rules. The Oregon Health Authority may adopt rules establishing the circumstances under which the authority may require a psilocybin service facilitator that holds a license issued under ORS 475A.325 to use an age verification scanner or any other equipment used to verify a person’s age for the purpose of ensuring that the psilocybin service facilitator does not provide psilocybin services to a person under 21 years of age. Information obtained under this section may not be retained after verifying a person’s age and may not be used for any purpose other than verifying a person’s age. [2021 c.1 §32] (Psilocybin Services)
