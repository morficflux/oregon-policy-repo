# Collection of information about district employees by department (ORS 327.134)

## At a glance

## Full text

327.134 Collection of information about district employees by department.

(1) The Department of Education shall collect the same information that the department uses to administer ORS 327.013 (1)(b)(B) for all individuals who are employed by a school district or by an education service district.

(2) Upon receipt of a request to collect the information described in subsection

(1) of this section, the school district or education service district shall in a timely manner provide the requested information to the department. [2025 c.75 §1] Note: 327.134 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 327 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 327.135 [Amended by 1955 c.660 §27; 1957 c.612 §15; repealed by 1963 c.570 §33]
