# Oregon Health Authority officers and employees (ORS 413.034)

## At a glance

## Full text

413.034 Oregon Health Authority officers and employees. Subject to any applicable provisions of ORS chapter 240, the Director of the Oregon Health Authority shall appoint all subordinate officers and employees of the Oregon Health Authority, prescribe their duties and fix their compensation. [2009 c.595 §13] 413.035 [Formerly 413.211; repealed by 1973 c.651 §11]
