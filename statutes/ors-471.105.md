# Purchaser’s qualifications (ORS 471.105)

## At a glance

ORS 471.105 — Purchaser’s qualifications. Chapter 471 (Alcoholic Liquors Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

471.105 Purchaser’s qualifications. Before being qualified to purchase alcoholic liquor from the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission, a person must be at least 21 years of age. [Amended by 1961 c.687 §5; 1967 c.577 §1; 1971 c.159 §1; 2005 c.22 §343; 2021 c.351 §42] 471.110 [Amended by 1961 c.259 §3; repealed by 1967 c.577 §10]
