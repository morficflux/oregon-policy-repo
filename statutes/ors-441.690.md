# Complainant may accompany investigator (ORS 441.690)

## At a glance

ORS 441.690 — Complainant may accompany investigator. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.690 Complainant may accompany investigator. Upon the request of any person filing a complaint to be investigated by the Department of Human Services against a long term care facility, as defined in ORS 442.015, or against a residential care facility, as defined in ORS 443.400, the complainant or a designee thereof, or both, shall be allowed to accompany an investigator to the site of the alleged violation. [1981 c.241 §1; 1987 c.428 §33; 1987 c.548 §6; 2001 c.900 §176]
