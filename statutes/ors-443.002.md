# Providers with multiple facilities (ORS 443.002)

## At a glance

ORS 443.002 — Providers with multiple facilities. Chapter 443 (Chapter 443), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

443.002 Providers with multiple facilities.

(1) As used in this section: (a) “Facility” means: (A) A residential training facility as defined in ORS 443.400; (B) A residential training home as defined in ORS 443.400; (C) A residential treatment facility as defined in ORS 443.400; (D) A residential treatment home as defined in ORS 443.400; or (E) An adult foster home as defined in ORS 443.705. (b) “Provider” means a person licensed or a person applying for a license to operate a facility.

(2) The Department of Human Services or the Oregon Health Authority may deny, suspend, revoke or refuse to renew a license of a provider if the department or authority finds that the provider operates a separate facility that is not or has not been in substantial compliance with rules adopted under ORS 443.400 to 443.455 or 443.705 to 443.825. [2009 c.418 §1; 2009 c.828 §30] Note: See second note under 443.001.
