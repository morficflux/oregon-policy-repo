## Full text

475C.900 Immunity for state agencies, officers, employees and agents in performance of duties. The Oregon Health Authority, the State Department of Agriculture and the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission, and the officers, employees and agents of the authority, department and commission, are immune from any cause of action for the performance of, or the failure to perform, duties required by ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919. [Formerly 475B.934]
