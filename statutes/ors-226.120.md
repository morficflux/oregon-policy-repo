# Composition and selection of board of park commissioners (ORS 226.120)

## At a glance

ORS 226.120 — Composition and selection of board of park commissioners. Chapter 226 (Chapter 226), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

226.120 Composition and selection of board of park commissioners. The board of park commissioners of the city shall be composed of the mayor and city engineer, if there is one, and if not, the city auditor, together with five citizens thereof, to be appointed by the circuit court in which the city is located. If there is more than one circuit judge in the circuit in which the city is located, the appointment shall be made by all the judges acting together. Not more than three citizens so appointed to the board shall be of the same political party. 226.130 [Repealed by 1983 c.350 §53 (226.131 enacted in lieu of 226.130)]
