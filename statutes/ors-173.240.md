# Committee’s staff prohibited from influencing legislation (ORS 173.240)

## At a glance

ORS 173.240 — Committee’s staff prohibited from influencing legislation. Chapter 173 (Chapter 173), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

173.240 Committee’s staff prohibited from influencing legislation. Neither the Legislative Counsel nor any employee of the committee shall oppose, urge or attempt to influence legislation. [1953 c.492 §14] 173.250 [1953 c.492 §15; 1957 c.60 §1; subsection

(4) enacted as 1961 c.159 §2; 1969 c.256 §5; 1981 c.517 §24; renumbered 171.305] 173.310 [1963 c.292 §2 (173.310 to 173.340 enacted in lieu of 173.155); 1969 c.314 §10; 1971 c.638 §7; 1977 c.186 §1; repealed by 1979 c.472 §2]
