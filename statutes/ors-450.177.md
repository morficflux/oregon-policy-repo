# Filing boundary change with county assessor and Department of Revenue (Annexation) (ORS 450.177)

## At a glance

## Full text

450.177 Filing boundary change with county assessor and Department of Revenue. For purposes of ad valorem taxation, a boundary change must be filed in final approved form with the county assessor and the Department of Revenue as provided in ORS 308.225. [2001 c.138 §30] Note: 450.177 was added to and made a part of 450.005 to 450.245 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein.
