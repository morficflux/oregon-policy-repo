# Reimbursements to Department of Human Services (ORS 344.850)

## At a glance

## Full text

344.850 Reimbursements to Department of Human Services. All reimbursements to the Department of Human Services made by the Department of Consumer and Business Services in connection with rehabilitation services shall be deposited in the State Vocational Rehabilitation Account and be included in the biennial budget of the Department of Human Services. [Formerly 344.680; 1965 c.100 §443; 1983 c.297 §2] 344.990 [Repealed by 1965 c.100 §456] _______________
