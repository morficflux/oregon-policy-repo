# Tobacco Use Reduction Account (ORS 431A.153)

## At a glance

ORS 431A.153 — Tobacco Use Reduction Account. Chapter 431A (Chapter 431A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

431A.153 Tobacco Use Reduction Account.

(1) There is established in the General Fund the Tobacco Use Reduction Account.

(2) Amounts credited to the Tobacco Use Reduction Account are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Health Authority for the funding of prevention and education programs designed to reduce cigarette and tobacco use. [Formerly 431.832]
