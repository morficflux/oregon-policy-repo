# “Nonresident estate or trust” defined (ORS 316.302)

## At a glance

ORS 316.302 — “Nonresident estate or trust” defined. Chapter 316 (Chapter 316), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

316.302 “Nonresident estate or trust” defined. For purposes of this chapter, a “nonresident estate or trust” means an estate or trust that is not a resident.
