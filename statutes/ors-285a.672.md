# Review of application (ORS 285A.672)

## At a glance

ORS 285A.672 — Review of application. Chapter 285A (Chapter 285A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285A.672 Review of application.

(1) Upon receipt of an application filed as provided in ORS 285A.669, the Oregon Infrastructure Finance Authority shall determine whether the plans and specifications for the proposed project set forth in or accompanying the application are satisfactory.

(2) If the authority determines that the plans and specifications are not satisfactory, the authority may: (a) Reject the application. (b) Require the applicant to submit additional information of the plans and specifications as may be necessary. [Formerly 285.875; 2001 c.883 §10; 2009 c.830 §39; 2023 c.41 §1]
