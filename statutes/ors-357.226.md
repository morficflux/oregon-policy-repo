## Full text

357.226 District board members; appointment of librarian.

(1) The officers of the district shall be a board of five members, to be elected by the electors of the district. The district board shall appoint a district librarian, who shall be the secretary for the district.

(2) Any elector residing within the district shall be qualified to serve as a district board member. [1981 c.226 §3] 357.230 [Amended by 1975 c.476 §8; renumbered 357.015]
