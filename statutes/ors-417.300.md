# Purpose of ORS 417.305 (ORS 417.300)

## At a glance

ORS 417.300 — Purpose of ORS 417.305. Chapter 417 (Chapter 417), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

417.300 Purpose of ORS 417.305. The purpose of ORS 417.305 is to establish a state policy for serving Oregon’s children and families, in recognition that addressing the needs, strengths and assets of children necessarily requires addressing the needs, strengths and assets of families and communities, and to direct state agencies to work in partnership with local communities to plan, coordinate and provide programs accordingly. [1989 c.834 §2; 1999 c.1053 §1]
