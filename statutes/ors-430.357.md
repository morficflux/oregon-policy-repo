# Minimum standards; rules (ORS 430.357)

## At a glance

ORS 430.357 — Minimum standards; rules. Chapter 430 (Chapter 430), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

430.357 Minimum standards; rules.

(1) The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt rules to implement ORS 430.338 to 430.380 and to establish minimum standards for alcohol and drug prevention and treatment programs in accordance with the comprehensive substance use prevention, substance use disorder treatment and recovery support services plan developed by the Alcohol and Drug Policy Commission under ORS 430.223.

(2) All standards and guidelines adopted by the authority to implement programs authorized under ORS 430.338 to 430.380 shall be adopted as rules pursuant to ORS chapter 183 regardless of whether they come within the definition of rule in ORS 183.310 (9). [Formerly 430.360; 1985 c.565 §70; 1987 c.53 §5; 2009 c.595 §486; 2011 c.673 §23; 2018 c.44 §6; 2025 c.495 §6]
