# Board meetings (ORS 680.560)

## At a glance

ORS 680.560 — Board meetings. Chapter 680 (Chapter 680), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

680.560 Board meetings. The State Board of Denture Technology shall hold a meeting at least once each year and shall annually elect a chairperson from its members. [1979 c.1 §10; 1981 c.313 §6; 1991 c.921 §13; 1993 c.142 §13; 1999 c.885 §29; 2003 c.547 §18]
