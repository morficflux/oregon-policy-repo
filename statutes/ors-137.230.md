# Definitions for ORS (ORS 137.230)

## At a glance

ORS 137.230 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.230 Definitions for ORS 137.260. As used in ORS 137.260, “conviction” or “convicted” means an adjudication of guilt upon a verdict or finding entered in a criminal proceeding in a court of competent jurisdiction.
