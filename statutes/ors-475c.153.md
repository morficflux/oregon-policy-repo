# Oregon Health Authority rule requirement (Powers and Duties of Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission with Respect to Licensees) (ORS 475C.153)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.153 — Oregon Health Authority rule requirement (Powers and Duties of Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission with Respect to Licensees). Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.153 Oregon Health Authority rule requirement. Notwithstanding the provisions of ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919, rules adopted by the Oregon Health Authority under ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919 must allow for the provision, transfer and sale of usable marijuana as described in ORS 475C.137. [Formerly 475B.149] (Powers and Duties of Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission with Respect to Licensees)
