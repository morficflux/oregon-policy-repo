## Full text

506.241 Commercial fishing vessel fleet reduction program; rules. The State Fish and Wildlife Commission, by rule, may prescribe a commercial fishing vessel fleet reduction program that complies with the federal Salmon and Steelhead Conservation and Enhancement Act of 1980.
