# Disposition of fees (Laying Conditions) (ORS 632.815)

## At a glance

ORS 632.815 — Disposition of fees (Laying Conditions). Chapter 632 (Chapter 632), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

632.815 Disposition of fees. All fees collected by the State Department of Agriculture under ORS 632.705 to 632.815 shall be paid to the State Treasurer by the department who shall deposit them in the Department of Agriculture Service Fund. Such funds are continuously appropriated to the department for the purposes of the administration and enforcement of such sections. [Formerly 632.110; 1975 c.748 §18; 1979 c.499 §28] (Laying Conditions)
