# Limitation of competition (ORS 279A.142)

## At a glance

ORS 279A.142 — Limitation of competition. Chapter 279A (Public Contracting - General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

279A.142 Limitation of competition. A contracting agency may, by appropriate ordinance, resolution, rule or other appropriate legislative action, limit competition for a public contract to emerging small businesses certified under ORS 200.055 if the contract price is estimated at $250,000 or less and is funded by the Emerging Small Business Account established under ORS 200.180. [2007 c.344 §2; 2015 c.565 §14; 2021 c.630 §46]
