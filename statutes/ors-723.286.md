## Full text

723.286 Executive committee. From the persons elected to the board, the board may appoint an executive committee of not less than three directors who may be authorized to act for the board in all respects, subject to such conditions and limitations as are prescribed by the board. [1975 c.652 §32] 723.290 [Amended by 1959 c.106 §12; repealed by 1975 c.652 §88]
