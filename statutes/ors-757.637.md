# Comparable access to transmission and distribution facilities (ORS 757.637)

## At a glance

ORS 757.637 — Comparable access to transmission and distribution facilities. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.637 Comparable access to transmission and distribution facilities. To the extent permissible under federal law, the Public Utility Commission shall ensure that an electric company that offers direct access:

(1) Provides electricity service suppliers and retail electricity consumers access to its transmission facilities and distribution system comparable to that provided for its own use; and

(2) Provides electricity service suppliers and retail electricity consumers timely access to information about its transmission facilities and distribution system, metering and loads comparable to that provided to its own nondistribution divisions, affiliates and related parties. [1999 c.865 §10] 757.640 [1961 c.691 §8; renumbered 758.435] 757.641 [Formerly 758.110; renumbered 165.850]
