# Grant to Central Oregon Intergovernmental Council (ORS 568.973)

## At a glance

ORS 568.973 — Grant to Central Oregon Intergovernmental Council. Chapter 568 (Chapter 568), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

568.973 Grant to Central Oregon Intergovernmental Council. The Oregon Department of Administrative Services shall provide a grant to the Central Oregon Intergovernmental Council to:

(1) In rural areas of this state, coordinate efforts to identify and apply for sources of funding for western juniper treatment, or other water conservation projects, among cities, counties or other organizations; and

(2) Coordinate activities among grant recipients described in ORS 568.972. [2024 c.107 §12] Note: 568.973 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 568 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 568.974 [2023 c.606 §37; repealed by 2024 c.107 §11]
