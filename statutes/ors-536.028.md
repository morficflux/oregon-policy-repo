## Full text

536.028 Rules pertaining to human or livestock consumption uses within or above scenic waterway. In accordance with applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Water Resources Commission may adopt rules necessary to administer the provisions of ORS 390.835 pertaining to the issuance of a water right within or above a scenic waterway.
