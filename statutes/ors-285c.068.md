## Full text

285C.068 Port cosponsorship of zones.

(1) A port located in whole or in part within an existing enterprise zone may be added as a cosponsor of the enterprise zone by resolution of the governing body of the port, subject to the consent of the existing zone sponsor and with notice to the Oregon Business Development Department.

(2) The addition of a port as a cosponsor of an existing enterprise zone under this section does not change the termination date of the enterprise zone under ORS 285C.245. [2005 c.704 §14; 2015 c.648 §6; 2023 c.298 §30]
