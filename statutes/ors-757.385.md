# Allowance of fair and reasonable rates (ORS 757.385)

## At a glance

ORS 757.385 — Allowance of fair and reasonable rates. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.385 Allowance of fair and reasonable rates. Nothing in ORS 276.910 and 757.360 to 757.380 affects the authority of the Public Utility Commission to set fair and reasonable rates as authorized under ORS 756.040 (1). [2009 c.748 §8] Note: See note under 757.360.
