# Payment of principal and interest (ORS 441.570)

## At a glance

ORS 441.570 — Payment of principal and interest. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.570 Payment of principal and interest. The board of directors shall establish rentals, selling prices, and other charges at least adequate to pay the principal of and interest on the obligations of the authority as the same become due, including payments to any special fund or funds, together with the financing and other costs of the authority. [1973 c.153 §10]
