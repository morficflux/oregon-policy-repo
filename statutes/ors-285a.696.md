# Duties of director (ORS 285A.696)

## At a glance

ORS 285A.696 — Duties of director. Chapter 285A (Chapter 285A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

285A.696 Duties of director. The Oregon Business Development Commission may appoint the Director of the Oregon Business Development Department as their representative and agent in all matters pertaining to ORS 285A.666 to 285A.732. The director shall ensure that all provisions of ORS 285A.666 to 285A.732 are complied with and that appropriately trained personnel are employed pursuant to ORS 285A.070 to properly administer the fiscal and other portions of ORS 285A.666 to 285A.732. [Formerly 285.905; 2009 c.830 §45a]
