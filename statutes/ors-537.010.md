# Definition of “Water Rights Act” (ORS 537.010)

## At a glance

ORS 537.010 — Definition of “Water Rights Act”. Chapter 537 (Chapter 537), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

537.010 Definition of “Water Rights Act.” As used in this chapter, “Water Rights Act” means and embraces ORS 536.050, 537.120, 537.130, 537.140 to 537.252, 537.390 to 537.400, 538.420, 540.010 to 540.120, 540.210 to 540.230, 540.310 to 540.430, 540.443 to 540.491, 540.505 to 540.586 and 540.710 to 540.750.
