## At a glance

ORS 685.125 — Discipline procedure; rules. Chapter 685 (Chapter 685), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

685.125 Discipline procedure; rules.

(1) The Oregon Board of Naturopathic Medicine shall give opportunity for hearing as provided in ORS chapter 183 when the board proposes to: (a) Refuse to issue a license; (b) Refuse to renew a license; or (c) Impose any of the sanctions set forth in ORS 685.110.

(2) In accordance with applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the board may adopt rules necessary for the administration of the laws that the board is charged with administering. [1971 c.734 §134; 1985 c.624 §14; 2009 c.43 §25; 2009 c.396 §2]
