# Contents of license (ORS 508.450)

## At a glance

ORS 508.450 — Contents of license. Chapter 508 (Chapter 508), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

508.450 Contents of license. Each license issued under ORS 508.406 shall be numbered and dated by the State Fish and Wildlife Director or an authorized agent and contain the site or address where the appliance or business is located and the name of the person to whom the license is granted. [Amended by 1957 c.132 §1; 1963 c.196 §6] 508.455 [Amended by 1955 c.122 §1; repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
