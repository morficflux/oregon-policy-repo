# Use of live birds for competitive shooting prohibited (ORS 498.112)

## At a glance

ORS 498.112 — Use of live birds for competitive shooting prohibited. Chapter 498 (Chapter 498), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

498.112 Use of live birds for competitive shooting prohibited. No person shall use any live bird as a target for the purpose of competitive shooting. [1973 c.723 §86] 498.115 [Amended by 1953 c.178 §2; repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
