## At a glance

## Full text

346.567 Collection of percentage of net proceeds of operation of vending facilities; rules.

(1) The Commission for the Blind shall collect a percentage of net proceeds earned by a vending facility. The moneys collected under this section shall be deposited into the fund established under ORS 346.569.

(2) The commission shall determine by rule, and shall state in an agreement with a vending facility manager, the percentage of net proceeds to be collected under this section. [2017 c.717 §3; 2017 c.717 §4]
