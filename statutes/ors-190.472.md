# Mutual interstate law enforcement assistance agreements (ORS 190.472)

## At a glance

ORS 190.472 — Mutual interstate law enforcement assistance agreements. Chapter 190 (Chapter 190), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

190.472 Mutual interstate law enforcement assistance agreements. A full-time, fully compensated police officer commissioned by the State of Washington, Idaho or California or any full-time, fully compensated police officer commissioned by a unit of local government of the State of Washington, Idaho or California may exercise any authority that the officer’s commission vests in the officer throughout the territorial bounds of Oregon if the officer is acting pursuant to a mutual law enforcement assistance agreement between a law enforcement agency of the neighboring state and a law enforcement agency of Oregon. [1997 c.855 §1] Note: 190.472 to 190.478 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 190 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
