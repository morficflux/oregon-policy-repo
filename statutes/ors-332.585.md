# Determination of student census by school districts (ORS 332.585)

## At a glance

ORS 332.585 — Determination of student census by school districts. Chapter 332 (Chapter 332), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

332.585 Determination of student census by school districts. The district school board may conduct a student census to determine the number of pupils between the ages of 4 and 20 resident within the district.
