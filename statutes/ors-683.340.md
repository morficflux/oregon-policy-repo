# Duty to report prohibited conduct OPTICIANS (ORS 683.340)

## At a glance

ORS 683.340 — Duty to report prohibited conduct OPTICIANS. Chapter 683 (Chapter 683), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

683.340 Duty to report prohibited conduct. Unless state or federal laws relating to confidentiality or the protection of health information prohibit disclosure, an optometrist who has reasonable cause to believe that a licensee of another board has engaged in prohibited conduct as defined in ORS 676.150 shall report the prohibited conduct in the manner provided in ORS 676.150. [2009 c.536 §17] OPTICIANS
