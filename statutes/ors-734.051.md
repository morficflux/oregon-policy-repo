# Actions by director during period of supervision (ORS 734.051)

## At a glance

## Full text

734.051 Actions by director during period of supervision. During the period of supervision of an insurer, the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may institute rehabilitation or liquidation proceedings, extend the period of supervision or take any other action under the authority of the director with respect to the insurer. [1989 c.425 §8]
