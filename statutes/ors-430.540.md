## At a glance

## Full text

430.540 Designation of and standards for evaluation sites.

(1) The county mental health program director shall designate sites for evaluation in the county plan of individuals who may be or are known to be drug dependent. The Oregon Health Authority shall establish standards for such sites, consistent with ORS 430.357, and periodically publish a list of approved sites.

(2) The costs of evaluation shall be borne by the county of appropriate jurisdiction. [1977 c.871 §6; 2009 c.595 §501; 2011 c.673 §34]
