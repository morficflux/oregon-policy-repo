# Definitions for ORS 305.690 to (ORS 305.690)

## At a glance

ORS 305.690 — Definitions for ORS 305.690 to. Chapter 305 (Chapter 305), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

305.690 Definitions for ORS 305.690 to 305.753. As used in ORS 305.690 to 305.753, unless the context otherwise requires:

(1) “Commission” means the Oregon Charitable Checkoff Commission.

(2) “Department” means the Department of Revenue.

(3) “Internal Revenue Code” means the federal Internal Revenue Code as amended and in effect on December 31, 2023.
