# Allocation to this state of interest and dividends (ORS 314.640)

## At a glance

ORS 314.640 — Allocation to this state of interest and dividends. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.640 Allocation to this state of interest and dividends. Interest and dividends are allocable to this state if the taxpayer’s commercial domicile is in this state. [1965 c.152 §8]
