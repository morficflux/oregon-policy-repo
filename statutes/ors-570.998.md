# Civil penalty for firewood violation GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 570.998)

## At a glance

ORS 570.998 — Civil penalty for firewood violation GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 570 (Chapter 570), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

570.998 Civil penalty for firewood violation. Violation of ORS 570.720 or a State Department of Agriculture rule adopted under ORS 570.720 is punishable by a civil penalty, not to exceed $10,000. The imposition of a civil penalty under this section is subject to ORS 183.745. Any civil penalty recovered under this section shall be deposited in the Invasive Species Control Account established under ORS 570.810. [2011 c.439 §2] _______________
