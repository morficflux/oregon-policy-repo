# Effective date for Act of Legislative Assembly (Resignation) (ORS 171.022)

## At a glance

ORS 171.022 — Effective date for Act of Legislative Assembly (Resignation). Chapter 171 (State Legislature), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

171.022 Effective date for Act of Legislative Assembly. Except as otherwise provided in the Act, an Act of the Legislative Assembly takes effect on January 1 of the year after passage of the Act. [1999 c.1012 §1] (Resignation)
