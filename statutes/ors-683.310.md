# Professional corporation regulation not required (Enforcement) (ORS 683.310)

## At a glance

ORS 683.310 — Professional corporation regulation not required (Enforcement). Chapter 683 (Chapter 683), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

683.310 Professional corporation regulation not required. ORS 58.345 does not apply to professional corporations engaged in the practice of optometry. [1985 c.103 §16] (Enforcement)
