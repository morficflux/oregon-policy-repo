# Rules regarding health and safety STATE HIGHWAY FUND (ORS 366.493)

## At a glance

ORS 366.493 — Rules regarding health and safety STATE HIGHWAY FUND. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.493 Rules regarding health and safety. The Oregon Transportation Commission may adopt rules governing health and safety in roadside rest areas and scenic overlooks under the jurisdiction of the Department of Transportation. [2009 c.99 §2] STATE HIGHWAY FUND
