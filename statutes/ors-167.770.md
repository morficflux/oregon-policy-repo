## Full text

167.770 Display of sign; penalty.

(1) A person who sells tobacco products or inhalant delivery systems shall display a sign clearly stating that the sale of the tobacco products or inhalant delivery systems to persons under 21 years of age is prohibited by law.

(2) Failure to display a sign required by this section is a Class A violation. [2017 c.701 §3]
