## At a glance

## Full text

171.590 Cooperation of state agencies.

(1) In carrying out specific evaluations and reviews, the Legislative Fiscal Office may request the services of the Division of Audits, the Oregon Department of Administrative Services and other statutory agencies of the Legislative Assembly.

(2) The Division of Audits shall, for each audit recommended by the Joint Legislative Audit Committee and included in the audit plan described in ORS 297.076: (a) Verify with the committee that the identified scope of the audit meets the goals of the committee; and (b) Provide periodic updates to the committee on the status of the audit.
