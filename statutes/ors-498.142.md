## Full text

498.142 Hunting with artificial light restricted; rules.

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, no person shall hunt wildlife with the aid of any artificial light.

(2) The State Fish and Wildlife Commission, by rule, may authorize hunting with the aid of an artificial light for the purpose of taking raccoon, opossum or bobcat or to alleviate damage by wildlife to other resources. [1973 c.723 §88a; 1977 c.136 §3] 498.145 [Renumbered 498.272]
