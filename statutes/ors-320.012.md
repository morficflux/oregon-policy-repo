# Increase in tax when net receipts exceed specified amounts; rules (ORS 320.012)

## At a glance

ORS 320.012 — Increase in tax when net receipts exceed specified amounts; rules. Chapter 320 (Chapter 320), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

320.012 Increase in tax when net receipts exceed specified amounts; rules.

(1) If at any point during the tax year, net receipts from one or more amusement devices operating at a single location exceed $104,000, the tax imposed under ORS 320.011 shall be increased by an additional $50 for each device at the location.

(2) If at any point during the tax year, net receipts from one or more amusement devices operating at a single location exceed $260,000, the tax imposed under ORS 320.011 and subsection

(1) of this section shall be increased by an additional $75 for each device at the location.

(3) The department may adopt rules defining the term “location” for purposes of this section. [1993 c.803 §4; 1995 c.79 §173; 1995 c.255 §3; 1999 c.501 §3]
