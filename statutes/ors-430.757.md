# Reports of abuse to be maintained by Department of Human Services (ORS 430.757)

## At a glance

ORS 430.757 — Reports of abuse to be maintained by Department of Human Services. Chapter 430 (Chapter 430), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

430.757 Reports of abuse to be maintained by Department of Human Services. A proper record of all reports of abuse made under ORS 430.743 and 430.765 shall be maintained by the Department of Human Services. [1991 c.744 §9; 2021 c.97 §52] Note: See note under 430.735. 430.760 [1969 c.253 §1; repealed by 1985 c.555 §26]
