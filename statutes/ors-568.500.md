# Order of dissolution; effect (ORS 568.500)

## At a glance

## Full text

568.500 Order of dissolution; effect. Upon issuance of an order of dissolution by the State Department of Agriculture, all ordinances and regulations adopted and in force within the former soil and water conservation district shall be of no further force and effect. [Amended by 1981 c.92 §24; 2009 c.220 §21]
