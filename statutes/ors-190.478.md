# Effect on federal officers INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION (ORS 190.478)

## At a glance

ORS 190.478 — Effect on federal officers INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION. Chapter 190 (Chapter 190), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

190.478 Effect on federal officers. ORS 190.472 does not confer on federal officers, as defined in ORS 133.005, any additional powers. The powers of federal officers are limited to those powers conferred by other laws. [1997 c.855 §4] Note: See note under 190.472. INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION
