# Penalties STATE PRINTING (ORS 282.990)

## At a glance

ORS 282.990 — Penalties STATE PRINTING. Chapter 282 (Public Printing), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

282.990 Penalties. The provisions of ORS 291.990 apply to ORS 282.020 and 282.050. Any violation of ORS 282.040, 282.050 and 282.060 shall, upon conviction, be punished as prescribed in ORS 291.990. _______________
