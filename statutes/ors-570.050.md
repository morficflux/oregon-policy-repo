## Full text

570.050 Cooperation of peace officers in enforcing quarantines. All peace officers in this state shall enforce all quarantine measures promulgated by the United States Department of Agriculture and the State Department of Agriculture. 570.055 [Repealed by 2011 c.597 §118] INSPECTION AND QUARANTINE GENERALLY; ABATEMENT OF NUISANCES
