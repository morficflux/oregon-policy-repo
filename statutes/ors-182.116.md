# Identification of race or ethnicity on forms issued by public bodies (ORS 182.116)

## At a glance

ORS 182.116 — Identification of race or ethnicity on forms issued by public bodies. Chapter 182 (State Administrative Agencies Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

182.116 Identification of race or ethnicity on forms issued by public bodies. A form or document issued by a public body, as defined in ORS 174.109, that asks a person to identify the person’s race or ethnicity must allow the person to select multiple races or ethnicities. [2019 c.352 §1] Note: 182.116 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 182 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
