# Notifying department upon ceasing to use fuel in connection with motor vehicle (ORS 319.650)

## At a glance

ORS 319.650 — Notifying department upon ceasing to use fuel in connection with motor vehicle. Chapter 319 (Chapter 319), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

319.650 Notifying department upon ceasing to use fuel in connection with motor vehicle. If any person ceases using fuel within this state in connection with a motor vehicle with respect to which an emblem has been issued pursuant to ORS 319.600 but continues using fuel within this state in connection with another motor vehicle or other motor vehicles, the person shall immediately notify the Department of Transportation.
