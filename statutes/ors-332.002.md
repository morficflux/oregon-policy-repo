# Definitions for chapter BOARD OF DIRECTORS (ORS 332.002)

## At a glance

ORS 332.002 — Definitions for chapter BOARD OF DIRECTORS. Chapter 332 (Chapter 332), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

332.002 Definitions for chapter. As used in this chapter, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “District school board” means the board of directors of a common school district or a union high school district.

(2) “School district” means a common or union high school district. [1965 c.100 §126; 1983 c.350 §167] BOARD OF DIRECTORS
