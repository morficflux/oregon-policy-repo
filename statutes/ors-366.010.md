# “County court” and “road” or “highway” defined (ORS 366.010)

## At a glance

ORS 366.010 — “County court” and “road” or “highway” defined. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.010 “County court” and “road” or “highway” defined. As used in this Act:

(1) “County court” includes all county officers or boards charged by law with the duty of building, constructing, repairing, altering or maintaining roads or bridges, or both.

(2) “Road” or “highway” includes necessary bridges and culverts, and city streets, subject to such restrictions and limitations as are provided. 366.015 [Repealed by 2015 c.138 §9] 366.105 [Amended by 1969 c.599 §17; 1971 c.598 §1; 1973 c.249 §29; 1979 c.186 §9; repealed by 1993 c.741 §147] 366.110 [Amended by 1969 c.314 §29; repealed by 1973 c.249 §91]
