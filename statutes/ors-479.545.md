# License required of state employee; letter of authority (ORS 479.545)

## At a glance

ORS 479.545 — License required of state employee; letter of authority. Chapter 479 (Chapter 479), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

479.545 License required of state employee; letter of authority.

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, no person is exempted by ORS 479.540 from the requirements under ORS 479.510 to 479.945 to have a license to make electrical installations solely on the basis the person is employed by an agency of this state.

(2) Any person issued a letter of authority under ORS 479.545 (1985 Replacement Part) may continue to make electrical installations under ORS 479.510 to 479.945 on property owned or controlled by an agency of the state. [1981 c.815 §7; 1985 c.568 §1; 1987 c.414 §34a; 2003 c.14 §322]
