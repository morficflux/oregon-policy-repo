# Risk management framework (ORS 732.656)

## At a glance

ORS 732.656 — Risk management framework. Chapter 732 (Chapter 732), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

732.656 Risk management framework. An insurer shall maintain a risk management framework to assist the insurer with identifying, assessing, monitoring, managing and reporting on the insurer’s material and relevant risks. The insurer satisfies this requirement if the insurance group of which the insurer is a member maintains a risk management framework applicable to the operations of the insurer. [2015 c.547 §4] Note: See note under 732.650.
