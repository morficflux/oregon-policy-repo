# Extraordinary costs (ORS 297.495)

## At a glance

ORS 297.495 — Extraordinary costs. Chapter 297 (Chapter 297), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

297.495 Extraordinary costs. Any extraordinary work required to be performed by the Secretary of State in connection with any of the audits or the reports thereon filed pursuant to ORS 297.465 shall be compensated by an additional payment to be agreed upon by the Secretary of State and the municipal corporation for which the audit is made or to be made. [1977 c.774 §11] 297.500 [Repealed by 1977 c.774 §27]
