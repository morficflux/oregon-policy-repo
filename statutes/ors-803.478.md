# Donation to Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs Veterans Suicide Prevention and Outreach Program (Cards) (ORS 803.478)

## At a glance

ORS 803.478 — Donation to Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs Veterans Suicide Prevention and Outreach Program (Cards). Chapter 803 (Chapter 803), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

803.478 Donation to Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs Veterans Suicide Prevention and Outreach Program.

(1) A person that applies for the renewal of vehicle registration online may make a contribution of $1 or more to the Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs Veterans Suicide Prevention and Outreach Program at the time the online application for renewal of vehicle registration is made.

(2) The vehicle registration renewal website and online application form must state that the owner has the option of making a contribution to the Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs Veterans Suicide Prevention and Outreach Program.

(3) Moneys contributed to the Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs Veterans Suicide Prevention and Outreach Program in accordance with this section shall be deposited in the Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs Veterans Suicide Prevention and Outreach Program Fund. [2013 c.779 §6] Note: 803.478 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 803 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. (Cards)
