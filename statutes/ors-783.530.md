# Receipt of compensation from seaman for furnishing employment (ORS 783.530)

## At a glance

ORS 783.530 — Receipt of compensation from seaman for furnishing employment. Chapter 783 (Chapter 783), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

783.530 Receipt of compensation from seaman for furnishing employment. No person shall demand or receive, either directly or indirectly, from any seaman or apprentice, or from any person seeking employment as a seaman or apprentice, or from any person on behalf of the seaman or apprentice, any remuneration whatever for providing the seaman or apprentice with employment on board any seagoing vessel. 783.540 [Repealed by 1953 c.113 §2]
