# Prohibition against charging fee for collection, transportation or recycling of covered electronic devices; exception (ORS 459A.330)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.330 — Prohibition against charging fee for collection, transportation or recycling of covered electronic devices; exception. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.330 Prohibition against charging fee for collection, transportation or recycling of covered electronic devices; exception.

(1) Except as authorized in subsection

(2) of this section, an electronics producer responsibility program or a collector participating in an electronics producer responsibility program may not charge a fee to covered entities for the collection, transportation or recycling of covered electronic devices.

(2) A collector that provides a premium service to a covered entity may charge for the additional cost of providing the premium service. [2007 c.302 §8; 2023 c.259 §17] Note: See note under 459A.300.
