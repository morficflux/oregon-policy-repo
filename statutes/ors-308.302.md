# Disposition of penalties (ORS 308.302)

## At a glance

ORS 308.302 — Disposition of penalties. Chapter 308 (Chapter 308), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308.302 Disposition of penalties. All penalties collected pursuant to ORS 308.030, 308.295, 308.296 or 308.300 shall be credited to the general fund of the county.
