# Certificate of election required before taking oath of office (ORS 254.568)

## At a glance

ORS 254.568 — Certificate of election required before taking oath of office. Chapter 254 (Chapter 254), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

254.568 Certificate of election required before taking oath of office. When a candidate elected to public office is required by law to take, file, subscribe or indorse an oath of office before entering upon the duties of the office, the candidate shall not take, file, subscribe or indorse the oath until the candidate has been granted a certificate of election. [1993 c.493 §101] 254.570 [Repealed by 1979 c.190 §431]
