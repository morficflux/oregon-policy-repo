# Implementation of program by publicly owned utility (Miscellaneous) (ORS 469.659)

## At a glance

ORS 469.659 — Implementation of program by publicly owned utility (Miscellaneous). Chapter 469 (Chapter 469), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469.659 Implementation of program by publicly owned utility. After the publicly owned utility has submitted to the Director of the State Department of Energy the residential energy conservation program required by ORS 469.651, the publicly owned utility promptly shall implement that program.
