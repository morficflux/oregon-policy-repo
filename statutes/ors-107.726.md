# Standing to petition for relief of person under 18 years of age (ORS 107.726)

## At a glance

ORS 107.726 — Standing to petition for relief of person under 18 years of age. Chapter 107 (Chapter 107), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

107.726 Standing to petition for relief of person under 18 years of age. A person who is under 18 years of age may petition the circuit court for relief under ORS 107.710 if:

(1) The person is: (a) The spouse of the respondent; (b) The former spouse of the respondent; or (c) A person who has been in a sexually intimate relationship with the respondent; and

(2) The respondent is 18 years of age or older. [1993 c.643 §4]
