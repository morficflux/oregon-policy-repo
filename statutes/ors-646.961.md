# Motor Vehicle Fuel Inspection Program Account (ORS 646.961)

## At a glance

ORS 646.961 — Motor Vehicle Fuel Inspection Program Account. Chapter 646 (Chapter 646), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646.961 Motor Vehicle Fuel Inspection Program Account. The Motor Vehicle Fuel Inspection Program Account is created in the Department of Agriculture Service Fund. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, all moneys in the account are appropriated continuously to the State Department of Agriculture for the administration of ORS 646.947 to 646.963. [1997 c.310 §9]
