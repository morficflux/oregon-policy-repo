# Existing development plans and coordination plans (ORS 276.057)

## At a glance

ORS 276.057 — Existing development plans and coordination plans. Chapter 276 (Public Facilities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

276.057 Existing development plans and coordination plans. Any development plan or coordination plan for the areas described by ORS 276.054 that is in effect on January 1, 2010, remains in effect until modified or repealed by the Capitol Planning Commission under ORS 276.056. [2009 c.896 §5] Note: See note under 276.055.
