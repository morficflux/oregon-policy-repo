# Rules (ORS 835.112)

## At a glance

ORS 835.112 — Rules. Chapter 835 (Aviation Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

835.112 Rules. In accordance with applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Director of the Oregon Department of Aviation may adopt rules necessary for the administration of the laws that the department is charged with administering. [1999 c.935 §5]
