# General prohibition (ORS 165.659)

## At a glance

ORS 165.659 — General prohibition. Chapter 165 (Chapter 165), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

165.659 General prohibition. Except as provided in ORS 133.545, 133.575, 133.595, 133.617, 133.619, 133.721, 133.724, 133.729, 133.731, 133.735, 133.737, 133.739, 165.540 and 165.657 to 165.673, no person may install or use a pen register or trap and trace device. [1989 c.983 §16] 165.660 [Formerly 74.510; repealed by 1971 c.743 §432]
