# Rules (ORS 192.860)

## At a glance

ORS 192.860 — Rules. Chapter 192 (Records; Reports and Meetings), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

192.860 Rules. The Attorney General may adopt rules the Attorney General considers necessary to carry out the provisions of ORS 192.820 to 192.868. [2005 c.821 §15] Note: See note under 192.820.
