# Limitation on administrative expenses VOLUNTEER PROGRAM (ORS 409.304)

## At a glance

ORS 409.304 — Limitation on administrative expenses VOLUNTEER PROGRAM. Chapter 409 (Department of Human Services), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

409.304 Limitation on administrative expenses. Grants awarded through funding from the Criminal Fine Account for domestic violence programs shall be used to support direct services, with no more than five percent of each grant to be spent for administration.
