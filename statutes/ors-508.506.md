# Additional payments on certain fish species Note Additional fee through 2036--2015 c.734 §§4,8(1)(b) (ORS 508.506)

## At a glance

## Full text

508.506 Additional payments on certain fish species. In addition to the ad valorem fee prescribed by law, there shall be paid for each fish species referred to in ORS 508.505 (1)(a), an additional fee of four cents per pound. The ad valorem fee referred to in this section is subject to ORS 508.505 to 508.540. [1989 c.512 §8; 1991 c.184 §3; 1997 c.8 §11; 2003 c.643 §3; 2009 c.765 §4; 2015 c.734 §5; 2019 c.458 §6] Note: Sections 4 and 8 (1)(b), chapter 734, Oregon Laws 2015, provide: Sec. 4. Additional fee through 2036.

(1) In addition to the ad valorem fee prescribed by law, there shall be paid for each fish species referred to in ORS 508.505 (1)(a), an additional fee of four cents per pound. The ad valorem fee referred to in this section is subject to ORS 508.505 to 508.540.

(2) Notwithstanding ORS 506.306, all moneys received by the State Fish and Wildlife Commission pursuant to subsection

(1) of this section shall be deposited in the Oregon Hatchery Monitoring and Maintenance Fund. [2015 c.734 §4; 2025 c.437 §3] Sec. 8. (1)(b) Section 4, chapter 734, Oregon Laws 2015, as amended by section 3 of this 2025 Act, is repealed on January 2, 2037. [2015 c.734 §8(1); 2015 c.779 §53(1)(a); 2017 c.120 §3(1)(a); 2025 c.437 §6(1)(b)] Note: Section 4 (2), chapter 437, Oregon Laws 2025, provides: Sec. 4.

(2) The additional fee imposed by section 4, chapter 734, Oregon Laws 2015, as amended by section 3 of this 2025 Act, applies to fish caught on or before December 31, 2036. [2025 c.437 §4(2)] 508.507 [1971 c.540 §10; repealed by 1991 c.701 §3]
