# Record of board business (Appeals of Value) (ORS 309.072)

## At a glance

ORS 309.072 — Record of board business (Appeals of Value). Chapter 309 (Chapter 309), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

309.072 Record of board business. The meetings, qualification, sittings and adjournment of the property value appeals boards shall be recorded in the journal of the county governing body.
