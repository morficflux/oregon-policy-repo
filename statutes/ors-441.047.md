# Requirement to provide resident with information about Long Term Care Ombudsman services (ORS 441.047)

## At a glance

ORS 441.047 — Requirement to provide resident with information about Long Term Care Ombudsman services. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.047 Requirement to provide resident with information about Long Term Care Ombudsman services. Upon the admission of a resident to a long term care facility, the facility shall provide the resident with information developed by the Long Term Care Ombudsman describing the availability and services of the ombudsman. [2019 c.117 §2] Note: 441.047 was added to and made a part of 441.015 to 441.119 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
