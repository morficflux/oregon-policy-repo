# Liability of health care providers and plans (ORS 414.745)

## At a glance

ORS 414.745 — Liability of health care providers and plans. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.745 Liability of health care providers and plans. Any health care provider or plan contracting to provide services to the eligible population under ORS 414.591, 414.631 and 414.688 to 414.745 shall not be subject to criminal prosecution, civil liability or professional disciplinary action for failing to provide a service which the Legislative Assembly has not funded or has eliminated from its funding pursuant to ORS 414.735.
