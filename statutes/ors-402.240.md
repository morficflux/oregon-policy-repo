## Full text

402.240 Indemnification of employee providing assistance.

(1) Assistance rendered by an employee of a responding participant is a governmental function.

(2) Employees of a responding participant are agents of the requesting participant.

(3) The requesting participant shall defend, save harmless and indemnify an employee of a responding participant to the same extent the requesting participant is required to do for its employees as provided in ORS 30.285 and 30.287.
