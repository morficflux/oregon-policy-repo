# Validation of mineral leases executed and delivered before August 9, 1961 PENALTIES (ORS 273.920)

## At a glance

ORS 273.920 — Validation of mineral leases executed and delivered before August 9, 1961 PENALTIES. Chapter 273 (Chapter 273), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

273.920 Validation of mineral leases executed and delivered before August 9, 1961. All leases and conveyances granting the right to explore or prospect for minerals or oil and gas, and for the drilling, mining and removal of the same on or from lands or mineral rights under the jurisdiction and control of any state agency, board or commission, which were executed and delivered by such agency, board or commission prior to August 9, 1961, are hereby validated and declared to be legal and enforceable. [Formerly part of 517.410] PENALTIES
