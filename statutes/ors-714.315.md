# Creation of rebuttable presumption PENALTIES (ORS 714.315)

## At a glance

ORS 714.315 — Creation of rebuttable presumption PENALTIES. Chapter 714 (Chapter 714), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

714.315 Creation of rebuttable presumption. Compliance with ORS 714.280 to 714.300 shall create a rebuttable presumption that the operator of the ATM or night deposit facility in question has provided adequate measures for the safety of users of the ATM or night deposit facility. [1993 c.381 §17] 714.990 [Repealed by 1973 c.797 §428] PENALTIES
