# Civil penalties (ORS 716.991)

## At a glance

ORS 716.991 — Civil penalties. Chapter 716 (Chapter 716), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

716.991 Civil penalties. Any person who violates ORS 716.850 shall forfeit and pay to the State Treasurer to be deposited in the Consumer and Business Services Fund a civil penalty in an amount determined by the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services of not more than $2,500. The civil penalty may be recovered as provided in ORS 706.980. [Formerly 716.855] _______________
