# Judgment in criminal action that effects release of defendant; delivery to sheriff (ORS 137.175)

## At a glance

## Full text

137.175 Judgment in criminal action that effects release of defendant; delivery to sheriff. Whenever a judgment in a criminal action will effect the immediate release of a defendant by discharge, probation, sentence to time served, or otherwise, the court shall cause the prompt delivery of a copy of the judgment to the sheriff no later than three calendar days after the judgment is entered. [1987 c.251 §3; 1991 c.111 §15; 1997 c.801 §65c] 137.180 [Amended by 1987 c.709 §2; 1989 c.472 §5; 1995 c.658 §77; 1997 c.801 §62; 1999 c.1051 §126; repealed by 2003 c.576 §580]
