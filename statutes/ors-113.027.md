# Limitation on admission of will to probate (ORS 113.027)

## At a glance

ORS 113.027 — Limitation on admission of will to probate. Chapter 113 (Chapter 113), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

113.027 Limitation on admission of will to probate. A will may not be admitted to probate or an estate reopened to admit a will to probate more than one year after the estate of the decedent has been administered in Oregon and closed. [1973 c.506 §21] 113.030 [Amended by 1963 c.308 §1; repealed by 1969 c.591 §305]
