## Full text

250.149 Determination of sufficient funds for commission and citizen panels.

(1) Not later than the date that is four months before the date of the general election in an even-numbered year, the Citizens’ Initiative Review Commission shall determine whether moneys in sufficient amount are available in the account established under ORS 182.470 to carry out all the duties, functions and powers of the commission, implement ORS 250.139 to 250.143 and pay for any statements to be printed in the voters’ pamphlet under ORS 251.185. (2)(a) If the commission determines that the account has sufficient moneys under subsection

(1) of this section, the commission shall carry out all the duties, functions and powers of the commission, implement ORS 250.139 to 250.143 and may submit statements to be printed in the voters’ pamphlet under ORS 251.185. (b) If the commission determines that the account has insufficient moneys under subsection

(1) of this section, for the general election in that even-numbered year, the commission may not carry out all the duties, functions and powers of the commission, implement ORS 250.139 to 250.143 or submit statements to be printed in the voters’ pamphlet under ORS 251.185.
