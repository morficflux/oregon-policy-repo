# Authority to sign reports of death (ORS 677.518)

## At a glance

ORS 677.518 — Authority to sign reports of death. Chapter 677 (Chapter 677), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

677.518 Authority to sign reports of death. A physician associate may complete and sign reports of death. Reports of death signed by a physician associate shall fulfill all of the laws dealing with reports of death. A physician associate who prepares a report of death shall comply with all provisions of ORS 432.133. [2003 c.104 §1; 2010 c.43 §5; 2013 c.366 §76; 2021 c.349 §13; 2024 c.73 §138] Note: 677.518 was added to and made a part of 677.495 to 677.535 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
