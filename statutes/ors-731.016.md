# Construction of Insurance Code APPLICATION OF INSURANCE CODE (ORS 731.016)

## At a glance

## Full text

731.016 Construction of Insurance Code. The Insurance Code shall be liberally construed and shall be administered and enforced by the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services to give effect to the policy stated in ORS 731.008. [1967 c.359 §4] 731.020 [Repealed by 1965 c.241 §3] APPLICATION OF INSURANCE CODE
