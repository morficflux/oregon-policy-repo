# Registration fee (ORS 106.330)

## At a glance

ORS 106.330 — Registration fee. Chapter 106 (Marriage; Domestic Partnership), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

106.330 Registration fee.

(1) In addition to any other fees provided by law, the county clerk shall collect a fee of $25 for registering a Declaration of Domestic Partnership.

(2) The county clerk shall regularly pay over to the Department of Human Services all moneys collected under subsection

(1) of this section to be credited to the Domestic Violence Fund pursuant to ORS 409.300. [2007 c.99 §7; 2009 c.595 §65c; 2011 c.720 §56] Note: See note under 106.300.
