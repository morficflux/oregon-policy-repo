# Creation of tax-exempt entity by university (ORS 353.117)

## At a glance

ORS 353.117 — Creation of tax-exempt entity by university. Chapter 353 (Chapter 353), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

353.117 Creation of tax-exempt entity by university.

(1) Pursuant to ORS 353.050, Oregon Health and Science University may create and maintain an entity that is exempt from federal income tax under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code, as amended, for the purpose of conducting clinical care and practice and advancing other university missions by the faculty.

(2) Any entity created by the university under subsection

(1) of this section shall be considered: (a) A public employer for purposes of ORS 236.605 to 236.640 and ORS chapters 238 and 238A; (b) A unit of local government for purposes of ORS 190.003 to 190.130; (c) A public body for purposes of ORS 30.260 to 30.300 and 307.112; (d) A public agency for purposes of ORS 200.090; and (e) A public corporation for purposes of ORS 307.090. [2001 c.921 §26; 2003 c.86 §9; 2003 c.733 §75]
