## Full text

358.590 Advisory and technical committees; reimbursement of commission member expenses; solicitation of funds.

(1) To aid and advise the Oregon Heritage Commission in the performance of its functions, the commission may establish such advisory and technical committees as it considers necessary. These committees may be continuing or temporary. The commission shall determine the representation, membership, terms and organization of the committees and shall appoint their members.

(2) Members of the committees are not entitled to compensation, but in the discretion of the commission may be reimbursed from funds available to the commission for actual and necessary travel and other expenses incurred by them in the performance of their official duties, subject to ORS 292.495.

(3) The commission may raise and expend funds, including grants from federal, state and private sources. [1995 c.428 §6] Note: See note under 358.570.
