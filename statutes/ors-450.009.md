# Formation purposes; petition (ORS 450.009)

## At a glance

ORS 450.009 — Formation purposes; petition. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.009 Formation purposes; petition. Sanitary districts may be formed for the purpose of providing sanitation facilities and services. In addition to the other matters, a petition for formation of a sanitary district shall state the number of members, three or five, on the district board.
