# List of highly toxic and restricted-use pesticides (ORS 634.316)

## At a glance

ORS 634.316 — List of highly toxic and restricted-use pesticides. Chapter 634 (Chapter 634), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

634.316 List of highly toxic and restricted-use pesticides. The State Department of Agriculture may establish, maintain and amend lists of pesticides and devices which are highly toxic or restricted-use pesticides or devices. [1973 c.341 §8] 634.320 [1969 c.268 §10; repealed by 1973 c.341 §37]
