# Examination (ORS 672.575)

## At a glance

ORS 672.575 — Examination. Chapter 672 (Chapter 672), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

672.575 Examination. The State Board of Geologist Examiners shall determine the scope, form and content of the examinations, provided for under ORS 672.505 to 672.705. [1977 c.612 §9] 672.580 [Repealed by 1971 c.751 §39]
