# Policy (ORS 314.647)

## At a glance

ORS 314.647 — Policy. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.647 Policy. The Legislative Assembly finds and declares it to be the policy of this state to carry out a comprehensive review of income apportionment whenever federal legislation changes the nexus standard for state imposition of taxes based on business activity within state borders. [2001 c.793 §12; 2017 c.43 §3] Note: 314.647 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 314 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
