457.025 Powers supplemental to other laws. The powers conferred by this chapter are in addition and supplemental to the powers conferred by any other law. [Formerly 457.110] 457.030 [Amended by 1957 c.456 §18; repealed by 1979 c.621 §28] URBAN RENEWAL AGENCIES; PLANS; ACTIVITIES
