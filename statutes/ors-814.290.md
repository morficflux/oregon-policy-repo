# Exemptions from motorcycle helmet requirements (ORS 814.290)

## At a glance

ORS 814.290 — Exemptions from motorcycle helmet requirements. Chapter 814 (Chapter 814), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

814.290 Exemptions from motorcycle helmet requirements. This section establishes exemptions from the requirements and penalties relating to the use of motorcycle helmets under ORS 814.260 to 814.280. A person is not in violation of ORS 814.260, 814.269, 814.275 or 814.280 if the person is any of the following:

(1) Within an enclosed cab.

(2) Operating or riding a vehicle designed to travel with three wheels in contact with the ground at speeds of less than 15 miles per hour. [1983 c.338 §693; 1987 c.910 §6; 1995 c.492 §7] 814.300 [1983 c.338 §694; repealed by 1999 c.179 §1]
