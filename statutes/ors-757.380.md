# Applicability of ORS 757.360 to (ORS 757.380)

## At a glance

ORS 757.380 — Applicability of ORS 757.360 to. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.380 Applicability of ORS 757.360 to 757.380. ORS 757.360 to 757.380 apply only to qualifying systems that are solar photovoltaic energy systems. [2009 c.748 §5] Note: See note under 757.360.
