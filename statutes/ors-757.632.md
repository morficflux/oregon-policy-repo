# Electricity service supplier’s access to electric company’s distribution facilities (ORS 757.632)

## At a glance

ORS 757.632 — Electricity service supplier’s access to electric company’s distribution facilities. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.632 Electricity service supplier’s access to electric company’s distribution facilities. Every electricity service supplier is authorized to use the distribution facilities of an electric company on a nondiscriminatory basis after the retail electricity consumers of the electricity service supplier are afforded direct access pursuant to ORS 757.601. [1999 c.865 §7] 757.635 [1961 c.691 §7; renumbered 758.430] 757.636 [Formerly 758.100; renumbered 165.845]
