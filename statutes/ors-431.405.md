# Purpose of ORS 431.380 to (ORS 431.405)

## At a glance

ORS 431.405 — Purpose of ORS 431.380 to. Chapter 431 (State and Local Administration and Enforcement of Public Health Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

431.405 Purpose of ORS 431.380 to 431.510. It is the purpose of ORS 431.380 to 431.510 to encourage improvement and standardization of health departments in order to provide a more effective and more efficient public health service throughout the state.
