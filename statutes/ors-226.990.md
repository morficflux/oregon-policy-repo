# Penalties GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 226.990)

## At a glance

ORS 226.990 — Penalties GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 226 (Chapter 226), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

226.990 Penalties. Violation of ORS 226.190 is punishable, upon conviction, in the same manner and to the same extent as for a crime defined in ORS 164.043, 164.045, 164.055 and 164.057. [Amended by 1971 c.743 §347; 1987 c.907 §14] _______________
