# Application of health laws (ORS 685.040)

## At a glance

ORS 685.040 — Application of health laws. Chapter 685 (Chapter 685), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

685.040 Application of health laws. Licensees under this chapter shall observe and are subject to all state, county and municipal laws and regulations relating to public health. [Amended by 2001 c.526 §6]
