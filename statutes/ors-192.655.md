# Recording requirements for certain entities (ORS 192.655)

## At a glance

## Full text

192.655 Recording requirements for certain entities.

(1) This section applies to any meeting of the following: (a) The board of directors of a common or union high school district; (b) The board of directors of an education service district; (c) The board of education of a community college district; or (d) The board of trustees of a public university described in ORS 352.002.

(2) A public body subject to this section must: (a) Notwithstanding ORS 192.650, make a video recording of the meetings of the entity described in subsection

(1) of this section that governs the public body; and (b) Within seven days following the meeting, post a video recording of the meeting on the public body’s website or social media site.

(3) If the facilities of the public body lack broadband Internet access, the public body may comply with this section by posting on the public body’s website or social media site an audio recording of meetings of the entity described in subsection

(1) of this section that governs the public body.

(4) Subsections

(2) and

(3) of this section do not apply to: (a) Any meeting or portion of a meeting that is conducted in executive session; or (b) A school district with a resident average daily membership of less than 50 students. [2024 c.47 §2; 2025 c.244 §1]
