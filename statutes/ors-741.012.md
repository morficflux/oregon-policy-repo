# State-administered technology platform (Financing Operations of Exchange) (ORS 741.012)

## At a glance

## Full text

741.012 State-administered technology platform. The Oregon Health Authority shall procure and administer an information technology platform or service, separate from the federal platform, to provide electronic access to the health insurance exchange in this state on and after November 1, 2026.
