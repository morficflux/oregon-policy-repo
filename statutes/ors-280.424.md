# Bond-financed single room occupancy rental housing to conform with federal law (ORS 280.424)

## At a glance

ORS 280.424 — Bond-financed single room occupancy rental housing to conform with federal law. Chapter 280 (Chapter 280), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

280.424 Bond-financed single room occupancy rental housing to conform with federal law. Single room occupancy rental housing financed with bonds authorized by ORS 280.410, 280.415, 280.422 to 280.425 and 280.431 must conform to the requirements of the Federal Mortgage Subsidy Bond Tax Act of 1980. [1981 c.368 §8; 2003 c.286 §8]
