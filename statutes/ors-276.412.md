# Payment of rent by state agencies (ORS 276.412)

## At a glance

ORS 276.412 — Payment of rent by state agencies. Chapter 276 (Public Facilities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

276.412 Payment of rent by state agencies.

(1) Each month the Oregon Department of Administrative Services may bill state agencies to which quarters in any state building have been assigned an amount fixed by the department as rent for the preceding month for the quarters assigned to such agencies.

(2) Moneys so collected shall be deposited in the Oregon Department of Administrative Services Operating Fund.

(3) This section applies to quarters occupied by the Department of State Lands and to quarters leased with option to purchase or under installment purchase agreement under ORS 276.429 (1).
