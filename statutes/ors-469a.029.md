## Full text

469A.029 Eligibility; registration date for certain generating facilities with tracking system. To be eligible for renewable energy certificates, the owner or operator of a generating facility that qualifies under ORS 469A.020

(5) and

(6) and 469A.025

(6) and

(7) must register the generating facility with the Western Renewable Energy Generation Information System or other regional system or trading program designated by the State Department of Energy before January 1, 2011. [2010 c.17 §5] Note: See note under 469A.027.
