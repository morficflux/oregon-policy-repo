## Full text

453.728 Tanning device to comply with federal requirements; exception for certain phototherapy devices.

(1) Any tanning device used by a tanning facility shall comply with all applicable federal laws and regulations.

(2) ORS 453.726 to 453.734 do not apply to a phototherapy device used by or under the direct supervision of a physician licensed under ORS chapter 677. [Formerly 431.935]
