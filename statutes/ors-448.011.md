## Full text

448.011 Powers of Oregon Health Authority; rules. The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt rules pertaining to construction plan submission, plan approval, design, construction, size, shape, purification equipment, piping, operation, sanitation and accident prevention for public swimming pools, public spa pools, public wading pools and bathhouses as the authority deems necessary. [1961 c.566 §3 (enacted in lieu of 448.010); 1971 c.650 §24; 1973 c.215 §2; 1999 c.929 §8; 2009 c.595 §828; 2019 c.456 §117]
