# Classifying facilities (ORS 426.035)

## At a glance

ORS 426.035 — Classifying facilities. Chapter 426 (Persons With Mental Illness; Dangerous Persons; Commitment; Housing), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

426.035 Classifying facilities. The Oregon Health Authority may assign classifications, as defined by rule of the authority, to facilities that provide care and treatment for persons committed to the authority under ORS 426.130 or provide emergency care or treatment for persons pursuant to ORS 426.070, 426.077 or 426.228 to 426.235. The authority may authorize a facility to retake custody of a person who unlawfully leaves a facility as provided in ORS 426.223. [Formerly 426.238] Note: 426.035 was added to and made a part of 426.005 to 426.390 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
