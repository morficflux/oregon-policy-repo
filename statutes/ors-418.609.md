# Applicability of Indian Child Welfare Act FOSTER HOMES NOT SUPERVISED BY CHILD-CARING AGENCIES (ORS 418.609)

## At a glance

ORS 418.609 — Applicability of Indian Child Welfare Act FOSTER HOMES NOT SUPERVISED BY CHILD-CARING AGENCIES. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.609 Applicability of Indian Child Welfare Act. Nothing in ORS 418.606 to 418.608 affects the application of the Indian Child Welfare Act (25 U.S.C. 1901 et seq.). [2017 c.36 §4] Note: See note under 418.606. 418.610 [Formerly 419.356; repealed by 1983 c.537 §7 and 1983 c.740 §142] FOSTER HOMES NOT SUPERVISED BY CHILD-CARING AGENCIES
