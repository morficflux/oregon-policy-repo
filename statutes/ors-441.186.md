# Rights, privileges or remedies of nursing staff (ORS 441.186)

## At a glance

ORS 441.186 — Rights, privileges or remedies of nursing staff. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.186 Rights, privileges or remedies of nursing staff.

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, nothing in ORS 441.183 and 441.184 shall be deemed to diminish any rights, privileges or remedies of a nursing staff under federal or state law or regulation or under any collective bargaining agreement or employment contract.

(2) ORS 441.183 and 441.184 provide the only remedies under state law for a nursing staff for an alleged violation of ORS 441.181 committed by a hospital. [Formerly 441.182] Note: See note under 441.179.
