# Wildlife as state property; taking, angling, hunting or trapping in violation of wildlife law or rules prohibited (ORS 498.002)

## At a glance

ORS 498.002 — Wildlife as state property; taking, angling, hunting or trapping in violation of wildlife law or rules prohibited. Chapter 498 (Chapter 498), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

498.002 Wildlife as state property; taking, angling, hunting or trapping in violation of wildlife law or rules prohibited.

(1) Wildlife is the property of the state. No person shall angle for, take, hunt, trap or possess, or assist another in angling for, taking, hunting, trapping or possessing any wildlife in violation of the wildlife laws or of any rule promulgated pursuant thereto.

(2) No person shall angle for, take, hunt or trap, or assist another in angling for, taking, hunting or trapping any wildlife while intentionally violating ORS 164.245 to 164.270 or 498.120.
