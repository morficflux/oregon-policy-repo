# Certain terms definable by rule (ORS 320.302)

## At a glance

ORS 320.302 — Certain terms definable by rule. Chapter 320 (Chapter 320), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

320.302 Certain terms definable by rule. The Department of Revenue may by rule define “dwelling unit,” “nonprofit facility,” “temporary human occupancy” and other terms for purposes of ORS 320.300 to 320.365. [2005 c.187 §5]
