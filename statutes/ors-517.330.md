## Full text

517.330 Accounting for fees. All fees collected under ORS 517.290 and 517.310 are the property of the county in which they are collected, and shall be accounted for by the officer collecting them as other recording fees are accounted for. 517.410 [Amended by 1961 c.419 §1; part renumbered 273.920; remainder renumbered 273.355] 517.420 [Amended by 1955 c.528 §1; 1961 c.419 §2; 1983 c.740 §206; repealed by 1993 c.340 §2] MINING LEASES
