# Use of warrant to collect amount of judgment (ORS 657.646)

## At a glance

ORS 657.646 — Use of warrant to collect amount of judgment. Chapter 657 (Chapter 657), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

657.646 Use of warrant to collect amount of judgment. In any case in which a judgment is rendered in favor of the Employment Department for amounts due under this chapter, the Director of the Employment Department may issue a warrant to collect the amount of the judgment. [2005 c.336 §2] 657.650 [Amended by 1983 c. 522 §8; repealed by 1993 c.344 §11]
