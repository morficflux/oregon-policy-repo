# Authority of Oregon Health Authority to discipline registrants of authority (ORS 475C.574)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.574 — Authority of Oregon Health Authority to discipline registrants of authority. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.574 Authority of Oregon Health Authority to discipline registrants of authority. Subject to the applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, if a person violates a provision of ORS 475C.540 to 475C.586 or a rule adopted under a provision of ORS 475C.540 to 475C.586, the Oregon Health Authority may:

(1) Refuse to register the person under ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919;

(2) Suspend activities conducted by a registrant pursuant to ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919; or

(3) Remove a registrant from a registry kept pursuant to ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919. [Formerly 475B.580]
