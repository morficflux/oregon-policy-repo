# Directives, instructions and assistance to county clerks (ORS 246.120)

## At a glance

ORS 246.120 — Directives, instructions and assistance to county clerks. Chapter 246 (Administration of Election Laws; Vote Recording Systems), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

246.120 Directives, instructions and assistance to county clerks. In carrying out the responsibility under ORS 246.110, the Secretary of State shall prepare and distribute to each county clerk detailed and comprehensive written directives, and shall assist, advise and instruct each county clerk, on registration of electors and election procedures which are under the direction and control of the county clerk. The directives and instructions shall include relevant sample forms of ballots, documents, records and other materials and supplies required by the election laws. A county clerk affected thereby shall comply with the directives or instructions. [1957 c.608 §3; 1965 c.464 §1; 1979 c.190 §6] 246.130 [1957 c.608 §4; repealed by 1979 c.190 §431]
