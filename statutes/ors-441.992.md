# Civil penalty for violation of ORS (ORS 441.992)

## At a glance

ORS 441.992 — Civil penalty for violation of ORS. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.992 Civil penalty for violation of ORS 441.048 or 441.049.

(1) In addition to any other penalty or remedy provided by law, the Oregon Health Authority may impose a civil penalty of up to $10,000 for each violation of ORS 441.048 or 441.049.

(2) Moneys received under this section shall be paid into the General Fund.

(3) Civil penalties shall be imposed as provided in ORS 183.745. [2020 s.s.1 c.20 §3] Note: 441.992 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 441 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
