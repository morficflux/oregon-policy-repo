# Drawing warrants (ORS 293.346)

## At a glance

ORS 293.346 — Drawing warrants. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.346 Drawing warrants. Warrants shall be drawn in payment of claims for payment from any moneys in the State Treasury. All warrants shall be drawn by the Oregon Department of Administrative Services, and ORS 291.015

(2) does not apply to the drawing of warrants by the department. [1967 c.454 §20; 1971 c.80 §1; 1993 c.741 §27]
