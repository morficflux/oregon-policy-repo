# Addition of flavoring to drug; conditions (ORS 689.608)

## At a glance

ORS 689.608 — Addition of flavoring to drug; conditions. Chapter 689 (Chapter 689), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

689.608 Addition of flavoring to drug; conditions. The addition of flavoring to a drug intended for dispensation may not be considered compounding if the flavoring:

(1) Is inert, nonallergenic and has no effect other than imparting a flavor to the drug or modifying the flavor of the drug; and

(2) Does not constitute more than five percent of the total volume of the drug. [2024 c.73 §2] 689.610 [Amended by 1969 c.514 §41; repealed by 1977 c.842 §45 and 1979 c.777 §59]
