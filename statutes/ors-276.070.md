# Contract relating to building owned by Employment Department (Lease Purchase) (ORS 276.070)

## At a glance

## Full text

276.070 Contract relating to building owned by Employment Department. The Employment Department and the Oregon Department of Administrative Services may contract for the operation, maintenance and insuring by the Oregon Department of Administrative Services of any office building located in the capitol area, as defined in ORS 276.010, that is owned wholly or in part by the Employment Department. [1969 c.706 §42] (Lease Purchase)
