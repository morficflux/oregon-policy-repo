# Notice following impoundment under city or county ordinance (ORS 809.725)

## At a glance

ORS 809.725 — Notice following impoundment under city or county ordinance. Chapter 809 (Chapter 809), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

809.725 Notice following impoundment under city or county ordinance.

(1) When a motor vehicle is impounded under authority of a city or county ordinance, the city or county shall give notice of the impoundment to the owners of the motor vehicle and to any lessors or security interest holders as shown on the records of the Department of Transportation. The notice shall be given within 48 hours of impoundment.

(2) The notice required by subsection

(1) of this section shall be given to the same parties, in the same manner and within the same time limits as provided in ORS 819.180 for notice after removal of a vehicle. [1997 c.514 §3]
