# Application of chapter to estates and certain trusts (ORS 316.267)

## At a glance

ORS 316.267 — Application of chapter to estates and certain trusts. Chapter 316 (Chapter 316), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

316.267 Application of chapter to estates and certain trusts. The tax imposed by this chapter on individuals applies to the taxable income of estates and trusts, except for trusts taxed as corporations under ORS chapter 317 or 318. [1969 c.493 §39; 1973 c.115 §3] 316.270 [1953 c.304 §32; repealed by 1969 c.493 §99]
