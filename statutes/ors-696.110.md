# Exclusive nature of regulation of real estate licensees (ORS 696.110)

## At a glance

ORS 696.110 — Exclusive nature of regulation of real estate licensees. Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.110 Exclusive nature of regulation of real estate licensees. The provisions of ORS 696.010 to 696.495, 696.600 to 696.785, 696.800 to 696.870 and 696.995 shall be exclusive and no political subdivision or agency of this state shall require or issue any license or charge any fee for licensing or regulation of individuals licensed under ORS 696.022. Nothing in this section shall limit the authority of a county, city or town to levy and collect a general and nondiscriminatory license fee upon all businesses in the county, city or town or to levy a tax based upon the business conducted by any licensee or firm within the county, city or town. [Amended by 1955 c.322 §11; 1961 c.309 §3; 1965 c.617 §2; 1975 c.746 §1b; 2001 c.300 §12; 2007 c.319 §22] 696.120 [Amended by 1977 c.649 §18; repealed by 2001 c.300 §84]
