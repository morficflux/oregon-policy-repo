# Regulation by department of testing, treating and examining of livestock (ORS 599.315)

## At a glance

## Full text

599.315 Regulation by department of testing, treating and examining of livestock. The State Department of Agriculture may require such testing, treating and examining of livestock sold, traded, exchanged or handled at or through livestock auction markets as in its judgment may be necessary to prevent the spread of brucellosis, tuberculosis or paratuberculosis, hog cholera and other infectious, contagious or communicable diseases among the livestock of this state.
