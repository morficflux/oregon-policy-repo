# Change of adopted child’s name (ORS 109.360)

## At a glance

ORS 109.360 — Change of adopted child’s name. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.360 Change of adopted child’s name. If in a petition for the adoption of a child a change of the child’s name is requested, the court, upon entering a judgment granting the adoption, may also provide in the judgment for the change of the name without the notice required by ORS 33.420.
