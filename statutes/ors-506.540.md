# Payment of rewards (ORS 506.540)

## At a glance

ORS 506.540 — Payment of rewards. Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.540 Payment of rewards. The State Fish and Wildlife Commission may offer and authorize payment of rewards for the arrest and conviction of any person who has violated any of the commercial fishing laws, but no reward of more than $100 shall be offered or paid for any one arrest or conviction. [Amended by 1965 c.570 §48] 506.545 [Repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
