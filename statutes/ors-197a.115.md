# City report on implementation of housing production strategy (ORS 197A.115)

## At a glance

ORS 197A.115 — City report on implementation of housing production strategy. Chapter 197A (Chapter 197A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

197A.115 City report on implementation of housing production strategy. Between 12 and 18 months before a city’s deadline for completing a housing capacity determination under ORS 197A.270 (2), 197A.280

(2) or 197A.335 (1), each city with a population of 10,000 or greater shall submit to the Department of Land Conservation and Development a report setting forth the actions that a city has taken since its most recent housing capacity determination to implement its housing production strategy or to otherwise encourage the development of needed housing, to increase the affordability of housing, to reduce rent burdens, to affirmatively further fair housing or to otherwise meet the purposes of ORS 197A.100 (2). [2023 c.13 §38]
