# Converted facilities to house felony or misdemeanant prisoners (ORS 169.677)

## At a glance

ORS 169.677 — Converted facilities to house felony or misdemeanant prisoners. Chapter 169 (Chapter 169), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

169.677 Converted facilities to house felony or misdemeanant prisoners. If a Department of Corrections institution is made to operate as a regional correctional facility pursuant to agreement under ORS 169.673, the purposes of the institution shall include the imprisonment of either felony or misdemeanant prisoners, or both, of the parties to the agreement under which the facility is operated. [1985 c.708 §7; 1987 c.320 §104] 169.680 [1971 c.636 §8; repealed by 1985 c.708 §9]
