# Policy (ORS 677.500)

## At a glance

ORS 677.500 — Policy. Chapter 677 (Chapter 677), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

677.500 Policy. It is the intent of the Legislative Assembly in requiring the licensure of physician associates to encourage appropriate use of physician associates in the delivery of health care services to the extent of a physician associate’s education and experience. [1981 c.220 §9; 1989 c.830 §31; 1999 c.582 §2; 2010 c.43 §2; 2011 c.550 §2; 2024 c.73 §132]
