# Adverse possession (ORS 326.543)

## At a glance

ORS 326.543 — Adverse possession. Chapter 326 (State Administration of Education), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

326.543 Adverse possession.

(1) As used in this section: (a) “Education service district” has the meaning given that term in ORS 334.003. (b) “Facility” means the school operated under ORS 346.010. (c) “Post-secondary institution” means: (A) A community college as defined in ORS 341.005; (B) A public university listed in ORS 352.002; and (C) The Oregon Health and Science University. (d) “School district” has the meaning given that term in ORS 330.005.

(2) The interest or estate of an education service district, a facility, a post-secondary institution or a school district in any real property may not be extinguished or diminished by adverse possession. [2007 c.552 §1; 2009 c.562 §19; 2011 c.637 §104]
