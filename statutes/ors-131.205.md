# Definition for ORS 131.205 to (ORS 131.205)

## At a glance

ORS 131.205 — Definition for ORS 131.205 to. Chapter 131 (Preliminary Provisions; Limitations; Jurisdiction; Venue; Criminal Forfeiture; Crime Prevention), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

131.205 Definition for ORS 131.205 to 131.235. As used in ORS 131.205 to 131.235, “this state” means the land and water and the airspace above the land and water with respect to which the State of Oregon has legislative jurisdiction. [1973 c.836 §13] 131.210 [Repealed by 1973 c.836 §358]
