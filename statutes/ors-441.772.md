# Leaving a patient care assignment (ORS 441.772)

## At a glance

ORS 441.772 — Leaving a patient care assignment. Chapter 441 (Chapter 441), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

441.772 Leaving a patient care assignment. A registered nurse at a hospital may not place a patient at risk of harm by leaving a patient care assignment during an agreed upon shift or an agreed upon extended shift without authorization from the appropriate supervisory personnel. [Formerly 441.168] Note: See note under 441.761.
