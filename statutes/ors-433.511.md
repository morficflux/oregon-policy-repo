# Public information program (ORS 433.511)

## At a glance

ORS 433.511 — Public information program. Chapter 433 (Chapter 433), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

433.511 Public information program. Subject to available funds, the Oregon Health Authority may establish a broad public information program to educate the public on indoor air pollutants, their identities, causes and effects, and on effective practical methods for preventing, detecting and correcting the causes of indoor air pollution.
