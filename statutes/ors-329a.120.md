# Services by resource and referral entities (ORS 329A.120)

## At a glance

ORS 329A.120 — Services by resource and referral entities. Chapter 329A (Chapter 329A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

329A.120 Services by resource and referral entities.

(1) The Department of Early Learning and Care shall implement, administer and maintain the resource and referral system.

(2) Resource and referral entities must provide services including, but not limited to: (a) Training and technical assistance for existing and potential child care providers that includes, but is not limited to, coaching, mentoring, consulting and advising on professional development; (b) Referrals for parents or guardians seeking child care providers; or (c) Recruitment of qualified individuals to meet the child care needs of a community. [Formerly 657A.120; 2017 c.187 §4; 2019 c.148 §1; 2021 c.631 §38; 2022 c.27 §§3,4]
