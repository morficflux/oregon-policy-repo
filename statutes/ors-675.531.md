## Full text

675.531 Temporary authorizations; rules.

(1) The State Board of Licensed Social Workers may adopt rules providing for: (a) The temporary licensure of clinical social workers; (b) The temporary registration of baccalaureate social workers; (c) The temporary licensure of master’s social workers; and (d) The temporary certification of clinical social work associates.

(2) The board may establish by rule fees for temporary registration, certification and licensure under this section. [2015 c.106 §2]
