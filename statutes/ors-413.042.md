# Rules (ORS 413.042)

## At a glance

ORS 413.042 — Rules. Chapter 413 (Chapter 413), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

413.042 Rules. In accordance with applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Director of the Oregon Health Authority may adopt rules necessary for the administration of the laws that the Oregon Health Authority is charged with administering. [2009 c.595 §14] 413.045 [1961 c.620 §22; 1963 c.609 §5; repealed by 1965 c.556 §28 and 1969 c.203 §13]
