## At a glance

## Full text

417.798 State Director of Head Start Collaboration. For purposes of the statutory laws of this state and all state operations of the federal Head Start program, the director of state operations of the federal Head Start program may be referenced as the State Director of Head Start Collaboration. [2012 c.37 §139] Note: See note under 417.796. (Runaway and Homeless Youth)
