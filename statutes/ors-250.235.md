# Retention of petition materials CITY MEASURES (ORS 250.235)

## At a glance

ORS 250.235 — Retention of petition materials CITY MEASURES. Chapter 250 (Chapter 250), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

250.235 Retention of petition materials. The county clerk shall retain the signature sheets of a filed initiative or referendum petition with a copy of the county measure. If the measure is approved by the electors, a copy of the measure shall be preserved as a permanent public record, and the signature sheets shall be preserved for six years. [1979 c.190 §161] CITY MEASURES
