# Warrant to agent to return fugitive from this state (ORS 133.827)

## At a glance

ORS 133.827 — Warrant to agent to return fugitive from this state. Chapter 133 (Chapter 133), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

133.827 Warrant to agent to return fugitive from this state. Whenever the Governor of this state shall demand a person charged with crime or with escaping from confinement or breaking the terms of security release, probation or parole in this state from the chief executive of any other state, or from the Chief Justice or an Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of the District of Columbia authorized to receive such demand under the laws of the United States, the Governor shall issue a warrant under the seal of this state to some agent or agents, commanding the agent to receive the person so charged if delivered to the agent and convey the person to the proper officer of the county in this state in which the offense was committed. [Formerly 147.220; 1999 c.1051 §248] 133.830 [Amended by 1973 c.836 §143; renumbered 135.195]
