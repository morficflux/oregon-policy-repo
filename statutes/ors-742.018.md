# Provision for construction according to foreign law prohibited (ORS 742.018)

## At a glance

ORS 742.018 — Provision for construction according to foreign law prohibited. Chapter 742 (Chapter 742), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

742.018 Provision for construction according to foreign law prohibited. No policy of insurance shall contain any condition, stipulation or agreement requiring such policy to be construed according to the laws of any other state or country. Any such condition, stipulation or agreement shall be invalid. [Formerly 736.315 and then 743.048] 742.020 [Amended by 1965 c.611 §4; repealed by 1967 c.359 §704]
