# Witnesses; subpoena (ORS 419C.405)

## At a glance

ORS 419C.405 — Witnesses; subpoena. Chapter 419C (Chapter 419C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419C.405 Witnesses; subpoena.

(1) Witnesses or other persons necessary for the conduct of the hearing may be subpoenaed. The youth, parents, guardian or any person appearing in the youth’s behalf may have compulsory attendance of witnesses in the youth’s or their behalf in the same manner as provided in ORS 136.567 to 136.603. The form of the subpoena shall be substantially as provided in ORS 136.575

(4) or (6), but shall describe the action as a “juvenile court proceeding” and the appearance as on behalf of “the court,” “the youth,” and so on, as the case may be.

(2) In addition to the subpoena available under subsection

(1) of this section, when the petition alleges that the youth is within the jurisdiction of the court by reason of a ground set forth in ORS 419C.005, the youth or any person appearing in behalf of the youth or the state may secure the attendance of out-of-state witnesses in the same manner as provided in ORS 136.623 to 136.637. [1993 c.33 §224]
