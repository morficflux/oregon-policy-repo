# Criminal penalties (ORS 688.990)

## At a glance

ORS 688.990 — Criminal penalties. Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.990 Criminal penalties. Violation of any provision of ORS 688.020, 688.120, 688.415 or 688.425 is a Class A misdemeanor. [1959 c.461 §25; subsection

(2) enacted as 1977 c.534 §21; 1989 c.171 §80; 2005 c.627 §14; 2011 c.597 §283]
