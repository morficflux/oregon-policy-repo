# Allocation of moneys to counties and cities generally (ORS 366.739)

## At a glance

ORS 366.739 — Allocation of moneys to counties and cities generally. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.739 Allocation of moneys to counties and cities generally. Except as otherwise provided in ORS 366.744, the taxes collected under ORS 319.020, 319.530, 803.090, 803.420, 818.225, 825.476 and 825.480 and the special use fuel license fees collected under ORS 319.535, minus $71.2 million per biennium, shall be allocated 24.38 percent to counties under ORS 366.762 and 15.57 percent to cities under ORS 366.800. [Formerly 366.524; 2014 c.13 §7] 366.740 [Renumbered 367.160]
