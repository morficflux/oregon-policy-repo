# Notice of allotment (ORS 291.248)

## At a glance

ORS 291.248 — Notice of allotment. Chapter 291 (State Financial Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

291.248 Notice of allotment. Upon the granting of any allotment, the Oregon Department of Administrative Services shall transmit a notice of the allotment to the agency concerned.
