# Transporting or possessing salmon unlawfully taken by net in certain waters prohibited (ORS 509.375)

## At a glance

ORS 509.375 — Transporting or possessing salmon unlawfully taken by net in certain waters prohibited. Chapter 509 (Chapter 509), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

509.375 Transporting or possessing salmon unlawfully taken by net in certain waters prohibited. No person shall transport through the waters of this state wherein net fishing for salmon is prohibited or have in possession anywhere within this state any salmon which were taken by any type of net within the international waters of the Pacific Ocean or within the territorial waters of this state or of another state, territory or country wherein such fishing is prohibited and which are not accompanied by a certificate issued under the authority of this state or of another state, territory or country showing that such salmon were lawfully taken. [1957 c.152 §4] 509.380 [1957 c.152 §5; repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
