# Bases of parole decisions to be in writing (ORS 144.135)

## At a glance

ORS 144.135 — Bases of parole decisions to be in writing. Chapter 144 (Chapter 144), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

144.135 Bases of parole decisions to be in writing. The board shall state in writing the detailed bases of its decisions under ORS 144.110 to 144.125. [1977 c.372 §9] Note: See note under 144.110.
