## At a glance

## Full text

522.315 Final order of department; delivery to operator. Whenever the State Department of Geology and Mineral Industries gives any written direction concerning any geothermal well and the operator requests in writing that a final order for purposes of ORS chapter 183 be made, the department shall, within 15 days after receipt of the notice, deliver such final written order to the operator. [1975 c.552 §24] 522.320 [1971 c.776 §§25,26; repealed by 1975 c.552 §55]
