# Rules (ORS 426.236)

## At a glance

ORS 426.236 — Rules. Chapter 426 (Persons With Mental Illness; Dangerous Persons; Commitment; Housing), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

426.236 Rules. The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt rules necessary to carry out the provisions of ORS 426.035, 426.155, 426.228 to 426.235 and 426.237. [1993 c.484 §8; 2001 c.481 §4; 2009 c.595 §408; 2025 c.559 §33] Note: See note under 426.228.
