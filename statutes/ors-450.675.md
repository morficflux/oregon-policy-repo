# Formation of authorities from areas within one or more counties (ORS 450.675)

## At a glance

ORS 450.675 — Formation of authorities from areas within one or more counties. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.675 Formation of authorities from areas within one or more counties. Any portion of one or more counties, including both incorporated and unincorporated areas as well as areas within domestic water supply districts, county service districts for water supply works and other districts may be formed into a water authority under ORS 450.600 to 450.989. Such areas need not be contiguous. [1971 c.504 §3; 1993 c.577 §26]
