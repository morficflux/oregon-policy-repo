# Stock running at large on highways in Washington County (ORS 607.520)

## At a glance

ORS 607.520 — Stock running at large on highways in Washington County. Chapter 607 (Chapter 607), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

607.520 Stock running at large on highways in Washington County. No person, either as owner or in possession or control of any horses, mules, cattle, swine, sheep or goats shall permit them to run at large on any highway or public road in Washington County. 607.525 [Repealed by 1957 c.604 §41]
