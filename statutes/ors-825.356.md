# Courts to forward copies of record on conviction for violation of chapter MOTOR CARRIER EDUCATION PROGRAM (ORS 825.356)

## At a glance

ORS 825.356 — Courts to forward copies of record on conviction for violation of chapter MOTOR CARRIER EDUCATION PROGRAM. Chapter 825 (Chapter 825), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

825.356 Courts to forward copies of record on conviction for violation of chapter. The courts having jurisdiction of this chapter shall, upon a conviction of anyone for violation of this chapter, immediately forward a copy of the record of such conviction to the office of the Department of Transportation. [Formerly 767.065] MOTOR CARRIER EDUCATION PROGRAM
