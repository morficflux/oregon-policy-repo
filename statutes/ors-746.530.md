# Attorney fees (ORS 746.530)

## At a glance

ORS 746.530 — Attorney fees. Chapter 746 (Chapter 746), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

746.530 Attorney fees. In any action to enforce any right created by ORS 746.405 to 746.530, the prevailing party may be awarded a reasonable amount, to be fixed by the court, as attorney fees. The amount may be taxed as part of the cost of the action and any appeal thereon. [1983 c.239 §6]
