# “Metropolitan planning organization” defined (ORS 184.888)

## At a glance

ORS 184.888 — “Metropolitan planning organization” defined. Chapter 184 (Certain Executive Branch Departments (incl. DAS)), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

184.888 “Metropolitan planning organization” defined. As used in ORS 184.617, 184.893, 184.895, 184.897 and 184.899, “metropolitan planning organization” has the meaning given that term in ORS 197.629.
