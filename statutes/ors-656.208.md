# Death during permanent total disability (ORS 656.208)

## At a glance

ORS 656.208 — Death during permanent total disability. Chapter 656 (Chapter 656), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

656.208 Death during permanent total disability. If an injured worker dies during the period of the worker’s permanent total disability, whatever the cause of death, and the worker leaves a beneficiary, payment must be made in the same manner and in the same amounts as provided in ORS 656.204. [Amended by 1957 c.453 §2; 1959 c.450 §2; 1965 c.285 §22b; 1969 c.521 §2; 1971 c.415 §2; 1973 c.497 §3; 1975 c.497 §2; 1985 c.108 §2; 2017 c.71 §3]
