# Authority to require fingerprints of individuals listed on application (ORS 475C.277)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.277 — Authority to require fingerprints of individuals listed on application. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.277 Authority to require fingerprints of individuals listed on application. For the purpose of requesting a state or nationwide criminal records check under ORS 181A.195, the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission may require the fingerprints of any individual listed on an application submitted under ORS 475C.273. [Formerly 475B.271]
