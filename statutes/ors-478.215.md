# Position numbers for director election (ORS 478.215)

## At a glance

ORS 478.215 — Position numbers for director election. Chapter 478 (Chapter 478), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

478.215 Position numbers for director election.

(1) Each office of director shall be designated by number as Position No. 1, Position No. 2 and so forth.

(2) The secretary of a district shall assign a position number to each office on the board. The number so assigned shall be certified by the secretary to the director in office holding that position. A copy of the certification shall be filed in the records of the elections officer for the district. [1977 c.301 §4; 1983 c.350 §284] 478.220 [Repealed by 1957 s.s. c.10 §6 (478.221 enacted in lieu of 478.220)]
