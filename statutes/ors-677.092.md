# Duty to report prohibited conduct (ORS 677.092)

## At a glance

ORS 677.092 — Duty to report prohibited conduct. Chapter 677 (Chapter 677), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

677.092 Duty to report prohibited conduct. Unless state or federal laws relating to confidentiality or the protection of health information prohibit disclosure, a physician who has reasonable cause to believe that a licensee of another board has engaged in prohibited conduct as defined in ORS 676.150 shall report the prohibited conduct in the manner provided in ORS 676.150. [2009 c.536 §20; 2013 c.129 §7]
