# Rules establishing standards and guidelines (ORS 660.520)

## At a glance

ORS 660.520 — Rules establishing standards and guidelines. Chapter 660 (Chapter 660), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

660.520 Rules establishing standards and guidelines. In accordance with applicable provisions in ORS chapter 183, the Higher Education Coordinating Commission, in consultation with the OregonServes Commission, may adopt rules:

(1) Establishing standards and guidelines for applications for grants; and

(2) Establishing standards and requirements for administration of programs funded by grants. [Formerly 458.573]
