# Form of petition; numbering of signature sheets; rules (ORS 250.015)

## At a glance

ORS 250.015 — Form of petition; numbering of signature sheets; rules. Chapter 250 (Chapter 250), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

250.015 Form of petition; numbering of signature sheets; rules. The Secretary of State by rule shall:

(1) Design the form of the prospective petition, and the initiative and the referendum petition, including the signature sheets, to be used in any initiative or referendum in this state.

(2) Designate the quality of paper to be used for signature sheets in order to ensure the legibility of the signatures.

(3) Prescribe a system for numbering the signature sheets to be used in any initiative or referendum in this state.
