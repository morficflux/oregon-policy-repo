# Restricted uses during closure (ORS 477.545)

## At a glance

ORS 477.545 — Restricted uses during closure. Chapter 477 (Chapter 477), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

477.545 Restricted uses during closure.

(1) Regulated closures require entrants into designated areas to comply with the requirements set forth in the proclamation under ORS 477.535, which requirements in the judgment of the forester are necessary to prevent danger to life, forest resources or property.

(2) Absolute closures restrict the areas to all forms of use and shall be designated only during periods of extreme fire hazard conditions endangering life, forest resources or property. [Formerly 477.158; 1967 c.429 §47; 1969 c.204 §6; 1997 c.274 §16; 2025 c.59 §3]
