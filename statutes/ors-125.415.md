# Termination of bond by surety (Conservator’s Powers and Duties) (ORS 125.415)

## At a glance

ORS 125.415 — Termination of bond by surety (Conservator’s Powers and Duties). Chapter 125 (Chapter 125), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

125.415 Termination of bond by surety.

(1) A surety may cancel the obligation of the surety upon notice in writing to the conservator and the court. The notice shall specify an effective date for the cancellation that is not less than 30 days after the date of the notice.

(2) A surety’s obligation under a bond shall continue until court approval of an accounting covering the period during which the bond was effective and an order is entered releasing the sureties. [1995 c.664 §37] (Conservator’s Powers and Duties)
