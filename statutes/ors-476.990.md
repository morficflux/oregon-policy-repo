# Penalties (ORS 476.990)

## At a glance

ORS 476.990 — Penalties. Chapter 476 (State Fire Marshal; Protection From Fire Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

476.990 Penalties.

(1) The following are Class A misdemeanors: (a) Violation of ORS 476.150 (2). (b) Violation of ORS 476.380

(1) or 476.510 to 476.610.

(2) Subject to ORS 153.022, violation of ORS 476.710 or 476.715 or of any rule or regulation of the State Parks and Recreation Department promulgated thereunder is a Class B misdemeanor.

(3) Violation of ORS 476.410 to 476.440 is a Class C misdemeanor. (4)(a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this subsection, violation of ORS 476.715 is a Class A violation. (b) Violation of ORS 476.715 is a Class B misdemeanor if the conduct constituting the offense: (A) Results in the ignition of a fire; or (B) Occurs within a forest protection district organized under ORS 477.225, or a part thereof, for which a fire season has been designated pursuant to ORS 477.508.
