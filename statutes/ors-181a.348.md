# Telecommunicators as first responders (ORS 181A.348)

## At a glance

ORS 181A.348 — Telecommunicators as first responders. Chapter 181A (State Police; Public Safety Standards and Training), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

181A.348 Telecommunicators as first responders. The Legislative Assembly finds and declares that telecommunicators are first responders. [2021 c.278 §1] Note: 181A.348 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 181A or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
