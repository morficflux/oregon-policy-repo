# Legislative findings and declarations (ORS 321.204)

## At a glance

ORS 321.204 — Legislative findings and declarations. Chapter 321 (Chapter 321), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

321.204 Legislative findings and declarations. The Legislative Assembly finds that an accurate system of annually determining forestland values in this state is vital to achieving a fair and equitable system of taxing the forest resources of this state. The Legislative Assembly declares that an annual determination of forestland values is the process that best achieves an accurate assessment of forestland in this state. [2001 c.860 §14] 321.205 [1953 c.375 §23; repealed by 1961 c.297 §12]
