# Rules (ORS 420A.025)

## At a glance

ORS 420A.025 — Rules. Chapter 420A (Chapter 420A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

420A.025 Rules. In accordance with applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Director of the Oregon Youth Authority may adopt rules necessary for the administration of the laws that the Oregon Youth Authority is charged with administering. [1995 c.422 §8]
