# Revocation, suspension or refusal to issue license (ORS 599.630)

## At a glance

ORS 599.630 — Revocation, suspension or refusal to issue license. Chapter 599 (Chapter 599), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

599.630 Revocation, suspension or refusal to issue license. The State Department of Agriculture may revoke or suspend or refuse to issue the temporary horse sale license of any person who does not or has not complied with the provisions of ORS 599.610, 599.620 and 599.640 or regulations promulgated thereunder. [1959 c.63 §4]
