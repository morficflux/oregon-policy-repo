# Rules to be adopted in consultation with affected parties (ORS 517.125)

## At a glance

ORS 517.125 — Rules to be adopted in consultation with affected parties. Chapter 517 (Chapter 517), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

517.125 Rules to be adopted in consultation with affected parties. Any rule pertaining to recreational or small scale mining adopted after June 28, 1999, shall be adopted in consultation with affected parties. [1999 c.354 §3] Note: See note under 517.120.
