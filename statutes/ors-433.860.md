# Enforcement (ORS 433.860)

## At a glance

ORS 433.860 — Enforcement. Chapter 433 (Chapter 433), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

433.860 Enforcement. The Oregon Health Authority or local public health authority, as defined in ORS 431.003, may institute an action in the circuit court of the county where the violation occurred to enjoin repeated violations of ORS 433.850.
