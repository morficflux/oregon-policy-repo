# Oregon Climate Corps Fund (ORS 468A.292)

## At a glance

ORS 468A.292 — Oregon Climate Corps Fund. Chapter 468A (Chapter 468A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468A.292 Oregon Climate Corps Fund. The Oregon Climate Corps Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the Oregon Climate Corps Fund shall be credited to the fund. Moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the University of Oregon for the purposes specified in ORS 468A.290. [2009 c.480 §2] Note: See note under 468A.290.
