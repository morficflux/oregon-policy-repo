# Effect of consolidating districts (ORS 568.471)

## At a glance

ORS 568.471 — Effect of consolidating districts. Chapter 568 (Chapter 568), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

568.471 Effect of consolidating districts. If two or more soil and water conservation districts are consolidated, the corporate existence of the districts and, except as provided in ORS 568.545, the terms of office for the board of directors of the former districts expire upon the Secretary of State issuing and recording a certificate of formation for the consolidated district. Upon consolidation, the consolidated district shall assume and be vested with all rights and liabilities of the former districts. [2009 c.220 §17] 568.480 [Amended by 1973 c.656 §8; 1981 c.92 §22; repealed by 2009 c.220 §34]
