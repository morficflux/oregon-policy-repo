# Application (ORS 723.506)

## At a glance

ORS 723.506 — Application. Chapter 723 (Chapter 723), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

723.506 Application. Every application for a loan shall be made in the form prescribed by the credit committee, credit manager or loan officer. The application shall state the security, if any offered. Each loan shall be evidenced by a written document. [1975 c.652 §53; 1991 c.635 §14] 723.510 [Amended by 1959 c.106 §20; 1971 c.681 §11; repealed by 1975 c.652 §88]
