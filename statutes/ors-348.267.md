# Marketing of tax credit (ORS 348.267)

## At a glance

ORS 348.267 — Marketing of tax credit. Chapter 348 (Chapter 348), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

348.267 Marketing of tax credit. The Higher Education Coordinating Commission may hire or contract with a marketer to market the tax credits described in ORS 315.643 to taxpayers. [2018 c.108 §5] Note: See note under 348.266.
