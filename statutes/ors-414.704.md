# Advisory committee SCOPE OF COVERED HEALTH SERVICES (ORS 414.704)

## At a glance

ORS 414.704 — Advisory committee SCOPE OF COVERED HEALTH SERVICES. Chapter 414 (Chapter 414), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

414.704 Advisory committee. The Health Evidence Review Commission shall consult with an advisory committee in determining priorities for mental health care and chemical dependency. The advisory committee shall include mental health and chemical dependency professionals who provide inpatient and outpatient mental health and chemical dependency care. [Formerly 414.730] 414.705 [1989 c.836 §2; 1991 c.753 §4; 2003 c.735 §1; 2003 c.810 §7; repealed by 2011 c.602 §§64,70] SCOPE OF COVERED HEALTH SERVICES
