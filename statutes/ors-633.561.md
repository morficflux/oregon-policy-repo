# Preparation of list of prohibited noxious weed seeds and restricted noxious weed seeds (ORS 633.561)

## At a glance

ORS 633.561 — Preparation of list of prohibited noxious weed seeds and restricted noxious weed seeds. Chapter 633 (Chapter 633), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

633.561 Preparation of list of prohibited noxious weed seeds and restricted noxious weed seeds. The director shall prepare a list of prohibited noxious weed seeds and a list of restricted noxious weed seeds. On the list of restricted noxious weed seeds the director, with the concurrence of the dean, shall specify the number of such seeds per pound that may be present in agricultural, flower or vegetable seed. [1955 c.379 §15; 1981 c.196 §3; 2007 c.281 §6] 633.570 [Repealed by 1955 c.379 §23]
