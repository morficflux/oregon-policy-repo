# Prohibition against producing, processing or storing homemade cannabinoid extracts, industrial hemp extracts (Prohibited Conduct) (ORS 475C.313)

## At a glance

## Full text

475C.313 Prohibition against producing, processing or storing homemade cannabinoid extracts, industrial hemp extracts.

(1) A person other than a marijuana processor that holds a license issued under ORS 475C.085 may not process cannabinoid extracts into a cannabinoid product.

(2) A person may not produce, process or store homemade industrial hemp extracts.

(3) Violation of this section is a Class A misdemeanor. [Formerly 475B.311] (Prohibited Conduct)
