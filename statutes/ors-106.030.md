# Voidable marriages (ORS 106.030)

## At a glance

ORS 106.030 — Voidable marriages. Chapter 106 (Marriage; Domestic Partnership), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

106.030 Voidable marriages. When either party to a marriage is incapable of making such contract or consenting thereto for want of legal age or sufficient understanding, or when the consent of either party is obtained by force or fraud, such marriage shall be void from the time it is so declared by judgment of a court having jurisdiction thereof. [Amended by 2003 c.576 §372] 106.040 [Repealed by 1953 c.143 §9]
