# Interagency agreements (ORS 568.924)

## At a glance

ORS 568.924 — Interagency agreements. Chapter 568 (Chapter 568), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

568.924 Interagency agreements. The State Department of Agriculture may enter into agreements with any agency of this state, including but not limited to a soil and water conservation district, or with any agency of the federal government, for the purposes of carrying out the provisions of ORS 568.900 to 568.933 including the development of a plan. [1993 c.263 §11]
