# Fee schedule (ORS 837.045)

## At a glance

ORS 837.045 — Fee schedule. Chapter 837 (Chapter 837), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

837.045 Fee schedule.

(1) The registration fee to be paid under ORS 837.040, and the fee to be paid upon renewal of registration under ORS 837.060, is as follows: ______________________________________________________________________________ AIRCRAFT CLASSIFICATION AND FEE SCHEDULE Single engine fixed wing, piston $ 65 Single engine fixed wing, turboprop 250 Multiengine fixed wing, piston 150 Multiengine fixed wing, turboprop 300 Turbojet fixed wing 700 Helicopter piston engine 65 Helicopter turbine engine 175 Lighter than air, home built, sailplane, experimental or gyrocopter 55 Ultralight aircraft 55 Ex-military multiengine or turbojet/ ex-air carrier 300 ______________________________________________________________________________

(2) Any registration fees and penalties due in a previous year but not paid remain due and payable and may be collected by the Oregon Department of Aviation prior to renewal of registration of the aircraft in the current year. [Formerly 493.090; 1991 c.186 §2; 1997 c.585 §8; 2005 c.75 §3; 2009 c.693 §1; 2015 c.450 §2]
