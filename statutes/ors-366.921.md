# Wilbur C. Moore Bridge (ORS 366.921)

## At a glance

ORS 366.921 — Wilbur C. Moore Bridge. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.921 Wilbur C. Moore Bridge.

(1) The bridge that crosses Euchre Creek on U.S. Highway 101 near milepost 317 shall be known as the Wilbur C. Moore Bridge.

(2) The Department of Transportation shall place and maintain suitable markers along U.S. Highway 101 indicating the designation of the bridge. [2011 c.441 §1] Note: See note under 366.918.
