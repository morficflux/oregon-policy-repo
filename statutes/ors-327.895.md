## Full text

327.895 Financial and program audits by Secretary of State; reports to Legislative Assembly and Governor.

(1) Not later than December 31, 2020, and every two years thereafter, the Secretary of State shall conduct financial and program audits of the uses of the High School Graduation and College and Career Readiness Fund and the effectiveness of the fund in achieving the purposes of the fund.

(2) The Secretary of State shall submit the audit reports to the Legislative Assembly and the Governor. [2017 c.1 §11] Note: See note under 327.850. AGRICULTURAL EDUCATION
