## Full text

714.210 Use of ATMs; verification; application of branch banking law.

(1) A banking institution, through its own facilities or those of an entity described in ORS 708A.160, may make available for use by its customers and others one or more ATMs. Every transaction initiated through an ATM shall be subject to verification by the banking institution either by direct wire transmission or otherwise.

(2) ORS 714.025 does not apply to the establishment and maintenance of ATMs. [1975 c.193 §3; 1993 c.381 §2; 1997 c.631 §309] 714.220 [1975 c.193 §4; 1993 c.381 §3; repealed by 1997 c.631 §567] 714.230 [1975 c.193 §5; repealed by 1989 c.245 §1] 714.240 [1975 c.193 §6; 1979 c.810 §1; 1989 c.245 §2; 1993 c.381 §4; repealed by 1997 c.631 §567] 714.250 [1975 c.193 §7; 1979 c.810 §2; 1993 c.381 §5; repealed by 1997 c.631 §567] 714.260 [1975 c.193 §8; 1993 c.381 §6; repealed by 1997 c.631 §567]
