# Statement of premium (Individual Life Insurance Policies) (ORS 743.156)

## At a glance

ORS 743.156 — Statement of premium (Individual Life Insurance Policies). Chapter 743 (Chapter 743), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743.156 Statement of premium. A life insurance policy shall contain a provision separately stating the premium for each benefit provision of the policy for which such separate statement is necessary, as determined by the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services, to give adequate disclosure of the terms of the policy. [1967 c.359 §374] (Individual Life Insurance Policies)
