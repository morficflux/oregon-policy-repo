# Authority of lending institution (ORS 407.181)

## At a glance

ORS 407.181 — Authority of lending institution. Chapter 407 (Chapter 407), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

407.181 Authority of lending institution. In exercising the authority granted to it by ORS 407.177 to 407.181, a lending institution shall perform only the services that are delegated to it by contract entered into under ORS 407.177, and shall comply with the terms of the contract and applicable laws. [1989 c.746 §3] 407.183 [1967 s.s. c.1 §2; 1967 s.s. c.19 §1; 1969 c.615 §6; renumbered 407.535]
