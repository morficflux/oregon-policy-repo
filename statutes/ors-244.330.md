# Distribution of manual on government ethics (ORS 244.330)

## At a glance

ORS 244.330 — Distribution of manual on government ethics. Chapter 244 (Chapter 244), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

244.330 Distribution of manual on government ethics. The Oregon Government Ethics Commission shall distribute, insofar as is practicable, copies of its ethics manual to every public official. The commission shall seek the assistance of professional associations that represent public officials in its efforts to comply with this section. [1993 c.714 §4] Note: 244.330 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 244 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
