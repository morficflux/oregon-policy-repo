# Fiduciary responsibilities (ORS 646A.787)

## At a glance

ORS 646A.787 — Fiduciary responsibilities. Chapter 646A (Chapter 646A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646A.787 Fiduciary responsibilities.

(1) A person that is subject to ORS 646A.770 to 646A.787 shall act in a fiduciary capacity with respect to funds the person receives or holds for the benefit of another person.

(2) A person that sells a guaranteed asset protection waiver in connection with a retail sale of a motor vehicle may not: (a) Charge more for the guaranteed asset protection waiver than five percent of the amount the borrower finances under a finance agreement; or (b) Vary the benefits, terms, conditions or price of the guaranteed asset protection waiver on account of a borrower’s race, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, income or education. [2015 c.523 §7; 2021 c.367 §28]
