# Criminal penalty for rule violation (ORS 526.990)

## At a glance

ORS 526.990 — Criminal penalty for rule violation. Chapter 526 (Forestry Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

526.990 Criminal penalty for rule violation. Violation of any rule promulgated under ORS 526.041

(1) is, upon conviction, punishable as a misdemeanor. [1969 c.249 §4]
