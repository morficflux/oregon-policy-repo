# Authority to adopt policies (ORS 326.558)

## At a glance

ORS 326.558 — Authority to adopt policies. Chapter 326 (State Administration of Education), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

326.558 Authority to adopt policies. The State Council for Educational Opportunity for Military Children may adopt policies to carry out the duties assigned to the state council. [2014 c.110 §6] 326.560 [1985 c.464 §1; repealed by 1993 c.45 §18]
