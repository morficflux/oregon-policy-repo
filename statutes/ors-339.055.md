# Duties of attendance supervisors (ORS 339.055)

## At a glance

ORS 339.055 — Duties of attendance supervisors. Chapter 339 (Chapter 339), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

339.055 Duties of attendance supervisors. The attendance supervisor when notified of a truancy or unexcused absence shall investigate the truancy or nonattendance at school. If the child is not exempt from compulsory school attendance, the attendance supervisor shall proceed as provided in ORS 339.080 and 339.090. [Formerly 339.100] 339.060 [Repealed by 1965 c.100 §456]
