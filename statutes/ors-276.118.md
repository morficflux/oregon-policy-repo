# Policy concerning location and manner of construction of buildings (ORS 276.118)

## At a glance

ORS 276.118 — Policy concerning location and manner of construction of buildings. Chapter 276 (Public Facilities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

276.118 Policy concerning location and manner of construction of buildings. It is declared to be public policy that any building acquired or erected pursuant to ORS 276.005 shall be located and constructed in such a manner that it shall constitute a maximum value investment with emphasis on:

(1) Structural function and utility of the building.

(2) Access by the public.

(3) Resale value of the building.

(4) Compliance with the public policy of the state as declared in ORS 276.426. [1957 c.727 §13; 1977 c.598 §8]
