# Rules (ORS 466.907)

## At a glance

ORS 466.907 — Rules. Chapter 466 (Chapter 466), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

466.907 Rules. The Environmental Quality Commission may adopt rules to implement the financial assistance program established by ORS 466.903 and 466.905. However, to the maximum extent possible, the Department of Environmental Quality shall rely on existing rules adopted by the commission to carry out other programs providing financial assistance to owners or operators of underground storage tank facilities. [1997 c.788 §4] Note: See note under 466.901.
