# Definitions (ORS 705.010)

## At a glance

ORS 705.010 — Definitions. Chapter 705 (Department of Consumer and Business Services _______________ 2025 EDITION DEPARTMENT OF CONSUMER AND BUSINESS SERVICES INSURANCE AND FINANCE ADMINISTRATION ADMI), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

705.010 Definitions. As used in this chapter:

(1) “Board” means the Workers’ Compensation Board.

(2) “Department” means the Department of Consumer and Business Services.

(3) “Director” means the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services. [1987 c.373 §1; 1993 c.744 §9; 1999 c.59 §213] 705.100 [1985 c.762 §3; repealed by 1987 c.373 §85]
