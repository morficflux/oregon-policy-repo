# Report of temporary fiduciary (ORS 125.610)

## At a glance

ORS 125.610 — Report of temporary fiduciary. Chapter 125 (Chapter 125), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

125.610 Report of temporary fiduciary.

(1) A temporary fiduciary shall file a report with the court setting out all activities of the temporary fiduciary under the authority of the appointment. Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, the report must be filed: (a) When the temporary fiduciary completes the duties of the fiduciary; (b) When the temporary appointment expires; or (c) When the court orders the termination of the temporary fiduciary’s authority.

(2) If the person appointed as temporary fiduciary is appointed to act as a permanent fiduciary for the protected person, the report of the activities of the temporary fiduciary may be included in the first annual report of the guardian or in the first accounting of the conservator. [1995 c.664 §65]
