## At a glance

## Full text

469A.027 Certification of eligibility for certain generating facilities; generation date of electricity. The State Department of Energy may certify as eligible for renewable energy certificates a facility that qualifies under ORS 469A.020

(5) and

(6) and 469A.025

(6) and

(7) only for electricity generated on or after January 1, 2011. [2010 c.17 §4] Note: 469A.027 to 469A.031 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 469A or any series therein by legislative action.
