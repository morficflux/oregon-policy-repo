# Procedures and rules (ORS 703.230)

## At a glance

ORS 703.230 — Procedures and rules. Chapter 703 (Chapter 703), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

703.230 Procedures and rules.

(1) The Department of Public Safety Standards and Training shall conduct all proceedings under ORS 703.010 to 703.310 in accordance with ORS chapter 183. Judicial review of an action of the department shall be provided in ORS 183.480, 183.485, 183.490 and 183.500.

(2) The Board on Public Safety Standards and Training, or the department with the board’s approval, shall adopt, in accordance with ORS chapter 183, rules for the administration and enforcement of ORS 703.010 to 703.310. [1975 c.608 §§11,12; 1997 c.853 §57]
