# Duties to be performed at meetings on record (ORS 332.057)

## At a glance

ORS 332.057 — Duties to be performed at meetings on record. Chapter 332 (Chapter 332), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

332.057 Duties to be performed at meetings on record. Any duty imposed upon the district school board as a body must be performed at a regular or special meeting and must be made a matter of record. [Formerly 332.060 and then 332.108; 1993 c.45 §39] 332.060 [Renumbered 332.108 and then 332.057]
