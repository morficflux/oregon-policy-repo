# Additional legal holidays (ORS 187.020)

## At a glance

ORS 187.020 — Additional legal holidays. Chapter 187 (Chapter 187), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

187.020 Additional legal holidays. In addition to those specified in ORS 187.010, the following days are legal holidays in this state:

(1) Every day appointed by the Governor as a holiday.

(2) Every day appointed by the President of the United States as a day of mourning, rejoicing or other special observance only when the Governor also appoints that day as a holiday.
