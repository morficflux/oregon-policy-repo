# Liability for destruction of property by wildfire (ORS 477.092)

## At a glance

ORS 477.092 — Liability for destruction of property by wildfire. Chapter 477 (Chapter 477), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

477.092 Liability for destruction of property by wildfire.

(1) As used in this section, “wildfire” has the meaning given that term in ORS 477.089.

(2) A person is not liable in a civil action for injury to or destruction of property arising out of a wildfire, except to the extent evidence demonstrates that: (a) An action or inaction of the person constituted negligence or a higher degree of fault; and (b) The action or inaction caused or contributed to the cause of the wildfire or caused or contributed to the spreading of the wildfire. [2013 c.307 §3] Note: See note under 477.089.
