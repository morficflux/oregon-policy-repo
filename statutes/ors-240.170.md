# Oregon Department of Administrative Services Operating Fund (ORS 240.170)

## At a glance

ORS 240.170 — Oregon Department of Administrative Services Operating Fund. Chapter 240 (State Personnel Relations), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

240.170 Oregon Department of Administrative Services Operating Fund. All moneys received by the Personnel Division pursuant to the state personnel management program shall be deposited in the State Treasury to the credit of the Oregon Department of Administrative Services Operating Fund and are appropriated continuously out of that fund for the payment of all expenses incurred by the division for administration of the state personnel management program.
