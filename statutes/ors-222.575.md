# Agreements for joint operation by city and district (ORS 222.575)

## At a glance

ORS 222.575 — Agreements for joint operation by city and district. Chapter 222 (Chapter 222), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

222.575 Agreements for joint operation by city and district. The agreements referred to in ORS 222.530 (5), 222.540

(4) and 222.560

(4) may be entered into between the city and a district prior to and contingent upon the withdrawal of the annexed or incorporated area from the district under the provisions of ORS 222.524, or they may be made after such withdrawal. [1957 c.401 §5]
