## At a glance

## Full text

305.525 Notice to taxpayer of right to appeal. At the same time that a notice of assessment, letter of refund denial or determination or an order of the property value appeals board is given to any taxpayer, the Department of Revenue or property value appeals board, as the case may be, shall in writing also notify the taxpayer of the right of the taxpayer to appeal to the tax court under ORS 305.404 to 305.560.
