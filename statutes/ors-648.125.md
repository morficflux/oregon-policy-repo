# Rules (ORS 648.125)

## At a glance

ORS 648.125 — Rules. Chapter 648 (Chapter 648), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

648.125 Rules. In accordance with any applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Secretary of State may adopt rules the secretary considers necessary or proper for the administration of this chapter. [1963 c.551 §14; 2003 c.14 §393] 648.130 [1971 c.194 §4; repealed by 1985 c.728 §110]
