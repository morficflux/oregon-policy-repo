## At a glance

## Full text

255.325 Legislative intent to promote regularity of special district elections; rulemaking and enforcement by Secretary of State. Except as provided in ORS 255.400 to 255.424, the Secretary of State by rule shall require the districts that are not in compliance with ORS 255.335 to so comply. For this purpose, the rule may require adjusting or staggering terms of board members. [Formerly 259.235; 1981 c.173 §36; 2019 c.449 §43]
