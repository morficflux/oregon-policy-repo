# Purpose of district unique identifiers (ORS 478.970)

## At a glance

ORS 478.970 — Purpose of district unique identifiers. Chapter 478 (Chapter 478), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

478.970 Purpose of district unique identifiers. The purpose of ORS 478.970 to 478.982 is to establish a unique identifier for each district to be used for statistical purposes by the State Fire Marshal and in the process of insurance rating. ORS 478.970 to 478.982 do not alter or add to the corporate title or identification of a district organized or established by law. [1953 c.164 §1; 1969 c.667 §59; 2001 c.426 §2; 2025 c.179 §5]
