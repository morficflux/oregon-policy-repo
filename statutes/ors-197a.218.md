# Availability of urban facilities and services (Urban and Rural Reserves) (ORS 197A.218)

## At a glance

ORS 197A.218 — Availability of urban facilities and services (Urban and Rural Reserves). Chapter 197A (Chapter 197A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

197A.218 Availability of urban facilities and services.

(1) Lands within urban growth boundaries shall be available for urban development concurrent with the provision of key urban facilities and services in accordance with locally adopted development standards.

(2) Notwithstanding subsection

(1) of this section, lands not needed for urban uses during the planning period may be designated for agricultural, forest or other nonurban uses. [Formerly 197.752] (Urban and Rural Reserves)
