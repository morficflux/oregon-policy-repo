# Application to inspection and plan review for prefabricated structures (ORS 455.481)

## At a glance

ORS 455.481 — Application to inspection and plan review for prefabricated structures. Chapter 455 (Building Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

455.481 Application to inspection and plan review for prefabricated structures. Nothing in ORS 455.455, 455.457, 455.459, 455.461, 455.467, 455.475 or 455.477 is intended to limit, supersede or otherwise affect the rights, obligations or professional activities of an inspector engaged in the business of providing prefabricated structure plan approvals and inspections, as defined in ORS 455.715, pursuant to ORS 455.715 to 455.740. [1999 c.1045 §28] Note: See note under 455.479.
