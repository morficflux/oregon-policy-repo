# Selling frozen dessert with excessive bacteria count prohibited (ORS 621.340)

## At a glance

ORS 621.340 — Selling frozen dessert with excessive bacteria count prohibited. Chapter 621 (Chapter 621), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

621.340 Selling frozen dessert with excessive bacteria count prohibited. A frozen dessert wholesaler shall not sell, offer for sale or possess with intent to sell a frozen dessert or frozen dessert mix that has an excessive bacteria count as established by rule of the State Department of Agriculture. Frozen desserts in which cultured milk is an ingredient shall not be required to meet any bacteria count standards. [Amended by 1953 c.684 §12; 1969 c.152 §3; 1999 c.197 §46]
