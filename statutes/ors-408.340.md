# Limitation of liability (Hepatitis C) (ORS 408.340)

## At a glance

ORS 408.340 — Limitation of liability (Hepatitis C). Chapter 408 (Chapter 408), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

408.340 Limitation of liability.

(1) A physician, physician associate, nurse practitioner, hospital or clinic subject to ORS 408.300 to 408.340 shall not be subject to any criminal or civil liability for providing information required under ORS 408.300 to 408.340.

(2) Nothing in this section shall prevent, however, any action for negligence by a physician, physician associate, nurse practitioner, hospital or clinic in choosing or providing medical treatment. [1983 c.658 §4; 2014 c.45 §36; 2024 c.73 §56] (Hepatitis C)
