# Retention of moneys for administrative costs of school districts (ORS 327.889)

## At a glance

ORS 327.889 — Retention of moneys for administrative costs of school districts. Chapter 327 (Chapter 327), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

327.889 Retention of moneys for administrative costs of school districts.

(1) A school district may not use more than five percent of an apportionment under ORS 327.859 for administrative costs.

(2) A school district may not use an apportionment to administer activities not directly related to the programs, opportunities and strategies described in ORS 327.865, 327.868 and 327.871.

(3) From the portion of the apportionment used for administrative costs, a school district must conduct an annual analysis of: (a) Student attendance in grades 9 through 12; and (b) Disciplinary referrals, suspensions and expulsions in grades 9 through 12 disaggregated by race and ethnicity. [2017 c.1 §15; 2025 c.498 §7] Note: See note under 327.850.
