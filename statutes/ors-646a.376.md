# Enforcement; civil penalty WARRANTY REGULATION AND ENFORCEMENT (Enforcement of Express Warranties on New Motor Vehicles) (ORS 646A.376)

## At a glance

ORS 646A.376 — Enforcement; civil penalty WARRANTY REGULATION AND ENFORCEMENT (Enforcement of Express Warranties on New Motor Vehicles). Chapter 646A (Chapter 646A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646A.376 Enforcement; civil penalty. Violation of ORS 646A.372 or 646A.374 is an unlawful trade practice that is subject to investigation under ORS 646.618 and is subject to enforcement under ORS 646.632. Notwithstanding the provisions of ORS 646.642, a civil penalty imposed for a violation of ORS 646A.372 or 646A.374 may not exceed $5,000.
