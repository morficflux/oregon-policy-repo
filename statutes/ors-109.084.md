# Relation to Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act (ORS 109.084)

## At a glance

ORS 109.084 — Relation to Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.084 Relation to Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act. ORS 109.181 to 109.203, 109.206 to 109.218, 109.222 to 109.244 and 109.518 to 109.525 modify, limit and supersede the Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act, 15 U.S.C. 7001, et seq, but do not modify, limit or supersede 15 U.S.C. 7001(c), or authorize electronic delivery of any notice described in 15 U.S.C. 7003(b). [2025 c.592 §105] Note: See note under 109.082.
