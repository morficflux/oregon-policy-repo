# Date of election (ORS 250.221)

## At a glance

ORS 250.221 — Date of election. Chapter 250 (Chapter 250), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

250.221 Date of election. If an initiative or referendum petition contains the required number of verified signatures, the election on the county measure shall be held on the next available election date in ORS 203.085 that is not sooner than the 90th day after the measure was filed with the county clerk.
