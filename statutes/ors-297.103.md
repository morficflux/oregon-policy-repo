# Audit information in Secretary of State budget INVESTIGATION OF LOSS OF PUBLIC FUNDS OR PROPERTY (ORS 297.103)

## At a glance

## Full text

297.103 Audit information in Secretary of State budget. In the agency request budget submitted by the Secretary of State under ORS 291.208, the Secretary of State shall specify the amounts estimated to be expended in the biennium during which the agency request budget is prepared for categories of audit and related services, including fiscal audits, performance audits, information technology audits, the Government Waste Hotline and audit follow-up reviews. [2019 c.567 §10] Note: See note under 297.100. INVESTIGATION OF LOSS OF PUBLIC FUNDS OR PROPERTY
