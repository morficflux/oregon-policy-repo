# Reports by companies of mileage to county assessors (ORS 308.645)

## At a glance

ORS 308.645 — Reports by companies of mileage to county assessors. Chapter 308 (Chapter 308), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308.645 Reports by companies of mileage to county assessors. Upon request by the county assessor, a company assessed by the Department of Revenue under ORS 308.505 to 308.674 shall furnish a report to the county assessor, under oath, showing the length, as of January 1 at 1:00 a.m. of the assessment year, in each city, town, school district, road district, port or other municipal taxing agency or district, or in lieu thereof the length in each tax code area in the county, of main and branch railroad lines, and of main tracks, spurs, yard tracks and sidetracks and also of wire, pipe or pole lines and operational routes. [Amended by 1973 c.402 §12; 1997 c.154 §42; 2009 c.128 §14]
