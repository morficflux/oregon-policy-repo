# Issuance of bonds (ORS 450.915)

## At a glance

ORS 450.915 — Issuance of bonds. Chapter 450 (Chapter 450), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

450.915 Issuance of bonds. If, at the bond election, a majority of the votes cast is in favor of the issuance of bonds, the board may issue the bonds as prescribed in ORS chapter 287A. [1955 c.614 §45; 1969 c.694 §11; 1975 c.642 §24; 1977 c.188 §4; 1981 c.526 §6; 2001 c.215 §10; 2007 c.783 §188]
