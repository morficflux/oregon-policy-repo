## Full text

260.034 Deadline for incumbent seeking reelection to file petition or declaration. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, an incumbent holder of a public office who intends to seek reelection shall file a nominating petition or a declaration of candidacy for an additional term of the same public office at least seven days prior to the corresponding filing deadline for nonincumbent candidates for that public office. [2024 c.9 §17b] (Treasurers, Statements of Organization and Accounts)
