# Activities relating to oysters exempted WHEN LICENSE REQUIRED (ORS 508.011)

## At a glance

ORS 508.011 — Activities relating to oysters exempted WHEN LICENSE REQUIRED. Chapter 508 (Chapter 508), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

508.011 Activities relating to oysters exempted. This chapter does not apply to oysters or to any activity of any person relating to oysters.
