# Validity of conservation or highway scenic preservation easement (ORS 271.745)

## At a glance

ORS 271.745 — Validity of conservation or highway scenic preservation easement. Chapter 271 (Chapter 271), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

271.745 Validity of conservation or highway scenic preservation easement. A conservation easement or highway scenic preservation easement is valid even though:

(1) It is not appurtenant to an interest in real property;

(2) It can be or has been assigned to another holder;

(3) It is not of a character that has been recognized traditionally at common law;

(4) It imposes a negative burden;

(5) It imposes affirmative obligations upon the owner of an interest in the burdened property or upon the holder;

(6) The benefit does not touch or concern real property; or

(7) There is no privity of estate or of contract. [1983 c.642 §4; 1985 c.160 §4] 271.750 [1967 c.318 §5; 1975 c.511 §2; 1981 c.787 §43; repealed by 1983 c.642 §11]
