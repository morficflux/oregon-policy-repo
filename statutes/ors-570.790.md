# Advisory and technical committees (ORS 570.790)

## At a glance

ORS 570.790 — Advisory and technical committees. Chapter 570 (Chapter 570), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

570.790 Advisory and technical committees.

(1) The Invasive Species Council may establish advisory and technical committees that it considers necessary to aid and advise the council in the performance of its functions. The committees may be continuing or temporary committees. The council shall determine the representation, membership, terms and organization of the committees and appoint their members.

(2) Members of the committees are not entitled to compensation, but at the discretion of the council may be reimbursed from funds available to the council for actual and necessary travel and other expenses incurred by members of the committees in the performance of their official duties, subject to ORS 292.495. [Formerly 561.693]
