# Determination of waste of oil or gas (ORS 520.045)

## At a glance

ORS 520.045 — Determination of waste of oil or gas. Chapter 520 (Chapter 520), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

520.045 Determination of waste of oil or gas. The State Department of Geology and Mineral Industries may determine whether waste of oil or gas over which it has jurisdiction exists or is imminent. In the exercise of such power the department may:

(1) Collect data.

(2) Make investigations and inspections.

(3) Examine properties, leases, papers, books and records, including drilling records and logs.

(4) Examine, check, test and gauge oil and gas wells and tanks.

(5) Hold hearings.

(6) Provide for the keeping of records and the making of reports.

(7) Take such action as may be reasonably necessary to enforce this chapter. [1953 c.667 §6; 2007 c.672 §8] 520.050 [Repealed by 1953 c.667 §21]
