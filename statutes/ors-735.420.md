# Declaration of ineligibility of surplus lines insurer (ORS 735.420)

## At a glance

ORS 735.420 — Declaration of ineligibility of surplus lines insurer. Chapter 735 (Chapter 735), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

735.420 Declaration of ineligibility of surplus lines insurer.

(1) The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may declare a surplus lines insurer described in ORS 735.415

(1) ineligible if the director has reason to believe that the surplus lines insurer: (a) Is in unsound financial condition; (b) Is no longer eligible under ORS 735.415; (c) Has willfully violated the laws of this state; or (d) Does not make reasonably prompt payment of just losses and claims in this state or elsewhere.

(2) The director shall promptly mail notice of all such declarations to each surplus lines licensee. [1987 c.774 §121; 2001 c.191 §44d]
