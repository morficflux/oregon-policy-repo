# Definitions for ORS (ORS 136.760)

## At a glance

ORS 136.760 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 136 (Chapter 136), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

136.760 Definitions for ORS 136.765 to 136.785. As used in ORS 136.765 to 136.785:

(1) “Accusatory instrument” has the meaning given that term in ORS 131.005.

(2) “Enhancement fact” means a fact that is constitutionally required to be found by a jury in order to increase the sentence that may be imposed upon conviction of a crime. [2005 c.463 §1] Note: 136.760 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 136 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
