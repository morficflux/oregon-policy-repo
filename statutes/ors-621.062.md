# Deviation from standard of identity prohibited GRADE DESIGNATION USE (ORS 621.062)

## At a glance

## Full text

621.062 Deviation from standard of identity prohibited. A person shall not process, distribute, sell or offer or expose for sale fluid milk that does not conform to a standard of identity established by the State Department of Agriculture.
