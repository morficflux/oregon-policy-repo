# Level IV obligations in local court judgments (ORS 137.158)

## At a glance

ORS 137.158 — Level IV obligations in local court judgments. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.158 Level IV obligations in local court judgments. Level IV obligations in criminal judgments entered in local courts are amounts payable for reward reimbursement under ORS 131.897. [2013 c.685 §20]
