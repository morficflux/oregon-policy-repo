# Addition of purchase price of purchased winning lottery ticket (Wildfire-related Litigation) (ORS 316.861)

## At a glance

ORS 316.861 — Addition of purchase price of purchased winning lottery ticket (Wildfire-related Litigation). Chapter 316 (Chapter 316), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

316.861 Addition of purchase price of purchased winning lottery ticket. If the amount of the purchase price of a lottery ticket that is purchased in violation of ORS 461.610 is allowed as a deduction for federal tax purposes, the amount of the purchase price shall be added to federal taxable income for Oregon tax purposes. [2025 c.46 §4] 316.863 [1985 c.802 §3; repealed by 1997 c.839 §69] 316.865 [1953 c.304 §110; repealed by 1969 c.493 §99] (Wildfire-related Litigation)
