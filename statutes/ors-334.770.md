# Power of new board generally GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 334.770)

## At a glance

ORS 334.770 — Power of new board generally GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 334 (Chapter 334), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

334.770 Power of new board generally. The board of directors of the new education service district shall have the same duties and exercise the same authority over the district as does the board of directors of every education service district.
