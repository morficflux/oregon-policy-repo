# Laws applicable to Oregon Community Power (ORS 757.822)

## At a glance

ORS 757.822 — Laws applicable to Oregon Community Power. Chapter 757 (Chapter 757), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

757.822 Laws applicable to Oregon Community Power.

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, the provisions of ORS chapters 35, other than ORS 35.550 to 35.575, 180, 190, 192 and 244 and ORS 30.260 to 30.460, 200.005 to 200.025, 200.045 to 200.090, 221.450, 236.605 to 236.640, 243.650 to 243.809, other than 243.696, 297.040, 307.090 and 307.112 apply to Oregon Community Power under the same terms as they apply to any other subdivision of state government.

(2) Except as otherwise provided by law, the provisions of ORS chapters 182, 183, 238, 238A, 240, 270, 273, 276, 279A, 279B, 279C, 283, 286A, 291, 292, 293, 294, 295 and 297 and ORS 35.550 to 35.575, 183.710 to 183.730, 183.745, 183.750, 184.305 to 184.345, 190.430, 190.480, 190.490, 192.105, 200.035, 243.105 to 243.585, 243.696, 243.853 to 243.855, 278.011 to 278.120, 278.315 to 278.415, 279.835 to 279.855, 282.010 to 282.150, 283.085 to 283.092, 287A.140, 287A.150, 287A.472 and 656.017

(2) do not apply to Oregon Community Power.

(3) Oregon Community Power is not a participating public employer in the Public Employees Retirement System.

(4) Any funds held by or under the control of Oregon Community Power are not public funds, as defined in ORS 295.001. [2007 c.807 §4; 2009 c.11 §96; 2009 c.538 §13; 2012 c.107 §70] Note: See note under 757.812.
