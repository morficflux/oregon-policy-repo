# Contracts in violation of law prohibited (ORS 658.174)

## At a glance

ORS 658.174 — Contracts in violation of law prohibited. Chapter 658 (Chapter 658), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

658.174 Contracts in violation of law prohibited. No employment agency shall knowingly issue a contract for employment containing any term or condition that, if complied with, would be in violation of law, or attempt to fill an order for help to be employed in violation of law. [1973 c.678 §7b] 658.175 [Repealed by 1961 c.380 §16 (658.176 enacted in lieu of 658.175)]
