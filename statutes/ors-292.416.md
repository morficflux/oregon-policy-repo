# Circuit court judges (ORS 292.416)

## At a glance

ORS 292.416 — Circuit court judges. Chapter 292 (Salaries and Expenses of State Officers and Employees), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

292.416 Circuit court judges.

(1) The annual salary of each judge of a circuit court shall be $142,136 for the period beginning on January 1, 2019, and ending on June 30, 2020.

(2) On July 1, 2020, the annual salary of each judge of a circuit court, as adjusted under ORS 292.428, shall be increased by $5,000.

(3) On July 1, 2025, the annual salary of each judge of a circuit court shall be $204,060.

(4) On July 1, 2027, the annual salary of each judge of a circuit court shall be 110 percent of the salary specified in subsection

(3) of this section, as adjusted under ORS 292.428.
