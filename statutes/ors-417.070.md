# Juvenile court jurisdiction (ORS 417.070)

## At a glance

ORS 417.070 — Juvenile court jurisdiction. Chapter 417 (Chapter 417), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

417.070 Juvenile court jurisdiction. The juvenile courts of this state have jurisdiction of juveniles within the operation of ORS 417.010 to 417.080. [1959 c.434 §7]
