# Practice of clinical pharmacy REGULATION OF DRUG OUTLETS (ORS 689.295)

## At a glance

ORS 689.295 — Practice of clinical pharmacy REGULATION OF DRUG OUTLETS. Chapter 689 (Chapter 689), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

689.295 Practice of clinical pharmacy. In accordance with rules adopted by the State Board of Pharmacy under ORS 689.205, a pharmacist may engage in the practice of clinical pharmacy. [2015 c.362 §2] REGULATION OF DRUG OUTLETS
