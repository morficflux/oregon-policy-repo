# Voluntary liquidation CIVIL PENALTIES (ORS 716.915)

## At a glance

## Full text

716.915 Voluntary liquidation. An Oregon stock savings bank may go into voluntary liquidation in accordance with the procedures and subject to the provisions set forth for institutions in ORS 711.215 to 711.250.
