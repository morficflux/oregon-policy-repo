# Payment on shares and deposits; restriction on class of shares (ORS 723.450)

## At a glance

## Full text

723.450 Payment on shares and deposits; restriction on class of shares. Shares, share certificates, deposits and deposit certificates may be withdrawn for payment to the account holder or to third parties in accordance with the manner and procedures established by the board of directors. The board of directors may restrict one class of shares so that a share in the class may not be redeemed, withdrawn or transferred except upon termination of membership in the credit union. [1985 c.801 §7] 723.452 [1981 c.298 §1; renumbered 723.463]
