## Full text

807.620 Giving false information to police officer; penalty.

(1) A person commits the offense of giving false information to a police officer if the person knowingly uses or gives a false or fictitious name, address or date of birth to any police officer who is enforcing motor vehicle laws.

(2) The offense described in this section, giving false information to a police officer, is a Class A misdemeanor. [1983 c.338 §343; 1985 c.16 §160; 1985 c.597 §22] 807.630 [1987 c.744 §9; repealed by 1989 c.636 §54] MISCELLANEOUS
