## At a glance

ORS 743B.285 — Rules. Chapter 743B (Chapter 743B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743B.285 Rules. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services shall adopt rules necessary to carry out the purposes of ORS 743B.275 to 743B.285. [Formerly 743.893] Note: See note under 743B.275.
