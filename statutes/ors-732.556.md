# Requirement that certain persons provide information to insurers (ORS 732.556)

## At a glance

ORS 732.556 — Requirement that certain persons provide information to insurers. Chapter 732 (Chapter 732), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

732.556 Requirement that certain persons provide information to insurers. Any person within an insurance holding company system subject to registration shall provide complete and accurate information to an insurer when such information is necessary to enable the insurer to comply with the registration requirements of ORS 732.517 to 732.596. [1993 c.447 §41]
