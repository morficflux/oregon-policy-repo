# Criteria for development and operation of special programs; rules (ORS 343.045)

## At a glance

ORS 343.045 — Criteria for development and operation of special programs; rules. Chapter 343 (Chapter 343), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

343.045 Criteria for development and operation of special programs; rules. The State Board of Education shall establish by rule criteria to guide the development and operation of special programs authorized by this chapter. The Superintendent of Public Instruction shall apply these criteria in certifying such programs for reimbursement specifically provided by law for such programs. The criteria shall be limited to educational services and educational programs and shall not include treatment. [Formerly 343.235; 1975 c.621 §1; 1977 c.714 §10; 1989 c.491 §31] 343.050 [Repealed by 1953 c.110 §2]
