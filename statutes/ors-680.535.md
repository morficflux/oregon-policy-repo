# Grounds for imposing discipline (Practice) (ORS 680.535)

## At a glance

ORS 680.535 — Grounds for imposing discipline (Practice). Chapter 680 (Chapter 680), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

680.535 Grounds for imposing discipline. In the manner prescribed in ORS chapter 183 for contested cases, the Health Licensing Office may impose a form of discipline listed in ORS 676.612 against any person practicing denture technology for any of the grounds listed in ORS 676.612 and for any violation of the provisions of ORS 680.500 to 680.565, or the rules adopted thereunder.
