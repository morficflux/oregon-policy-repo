# Fees; how determined (ORS 677.525)

## At a glance

ORS 677.525 — Fees; how determined. Chapter 677 (Chapter 677), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

677.525 Fees; how determined. Every physician associate shall pay to the Oregon Medical Board nonrefundable fees as determined by the board pursuant to ORS 677.265.
