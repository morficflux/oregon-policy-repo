ORS 732.450 — Arbitrage transactions exempt; rules. Chapter 732 (Chapter 732), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

732.450 Arbitrage transactions exempt; rules. The provisions of ORS 732.430, 732.435 and 732.440 do not apply to foreign or domestic arbitrage transactions unless made in contravention of such rules as the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may adopt in order to carry out the purposes of ORS 732.420 to 732.455. [Formerly 738.770]
