# Sample tastings authorized PROHIBITIONS RELATING TO LIQUOR (ORS 471.402)

## At a glance

ORS 471.402 — Sample tastings authorized PROHIBITIONS RELATING TO LIQUOR. Chapter 471 (Alcoholic Liquors Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

471.402 Sample tastings authorized. The holder of a brewery license issued under ORS 471.221, a winery license issued under ORS 471.223, a grower sales privilege license issued under ORS 471.227, a brewery-public house license issued under ORS 471.200, a warehouse license issued under ORS 471.242 or a manufacturer certificate of approval issued under ORS 471.244 may provide or pay for sample tastings of wine, cider or malt beverages for the public on premises licensed under a full or limited on-premises sales license or under an off-premises sales license. [1995 c.58 §4; 1999 c.351 §33; 2016 c.3 §6] Note: 471.402 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 471 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. PROHIBITIONS RELATING TO LIQUOR
