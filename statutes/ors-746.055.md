# Title insurance commissions, rebates and discounts (ORS 746.055)

## At a glance

ORS 746.055 — Title insurance commissions, rebates and discounts. Chapter 746 (Chapter 746), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

746.055 Title insurance commissions, rebates and discounts. With respect to title insurance, no commissions, rebates or discounts shall be paid, allowed or permitted to any person having an interest in or lien upon real property which is the subject of the title insurance involved, or to any person acting for or on behalf of a person with such an interest or lien. [Formerly 748.086] 746.060 [Repealed by 1961 c.256 §5]
