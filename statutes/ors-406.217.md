# Duties of director concerning advisory committee (ORS 406.217)

## At a glance

ORS 406.217 — Duties of director concerning advisory committee. Chapter 406 (Department of Veterans’ Affairs), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

406.217 Duties of director concerning advisory committee. The Director of Veterans’ Affairs shall:

(1) Solicit recommendations from the Advisory Committee on the administration of all federal and state laws affecting veterans, their spouses, dependents and survivors in this state.

(2) Instruct members of the Advisory Committee concerning the operation of all veterans’ programs administered by the director. [1991 c.798 §3; 2007 c.44 §3]
