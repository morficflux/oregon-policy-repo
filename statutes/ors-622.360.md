# Cultivation does not affect ocean shore activities PENALTIES (ORS 622.360)

## At a glance

ORS 622.360 — Cultivation does not affect ocean shore activities PENALTIES. Chapter 622 (Chapter 622), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

622.360 Cultivation does not affect ocean shore activities. Nothing in ORS 622.210, 622.220, 622.240, 622.250, 622.270, 622.280, 622.290, 622.320 or 622.340 is intended to affect activities on the ocean shore, as defined in ORS 390.605.
