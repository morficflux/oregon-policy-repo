# Policy (ORS 454.784)

## At a glance

ORS 454.784 — Policy. Chapter 454 (Chapter 454), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

454.784 Policy. It is the policy of the State of Oregon to encourage innovative technology for subsurface and alternative sewage disposal systems and nonwater-carried sewage disposal facilities consistent with the protection of the public health and safety and the quality of the waters of this state. [1999 c.978 §2] Note: See note under 454.782. 454.785 [1974 c.30 §4; repealed by 1975 c.309 §6]
