# Disclosure and sharing of information between Department of Revenue and Oregon Health Authority (ORS 323.404)

## At a glance

ORS 323.404 — Disclosure and sharing of information between Department of Revenue and Oregon Health Authority. Chapter 323 (Chapter 323), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

323.404 Disclosure and sharing of information between Department of Revenue and Oregon Health Authority.

(1) Notwithstanding the confidentiality provisions of ORS 323.403, the Department of Revenue may disclose information received under ORS 323.005 to 323.482 to the Oregon Health Authority to carry out the provisions of ORS 167.750 to 167.780, 431A.175 or 431A.183.

(2) The authority may disclose information obtained pursuant to ORS 431A.175 or 431A.183 to the department for the purpose of carrying out the provisions of ORS 323.005 to 323.482, provided that the authority does not disclose personally identifiable information. [2019 c.525 §19] 323.405 [1965 c.525 §81; repealed by 1977 c.870 §55 (323.416 enacted in lieu of 323.405, 323.410 and 323.415)]
