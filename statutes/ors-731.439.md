# Satisfaction of requirements of ORS (ORS 731.439)

## At a glance

ORS 731.439 — Satisfaction of requirements of ORS. Chapter 731 (Administration and General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

731.439 Satisfaction of requirements of ORS 731.438

(1) by certain title plants. A title plant that conforms on December 31, 1999, with ORS 731.438

(1) and

(2) (1997 Edition) satisfies the requirements of ORS 731.438

(1) as amended by section 1, chapter 183, Oregon Laws 1999, if it also contains after that date a general index, adequate maps and currently posted tract or geographic indexes for all lands in the county. [1999 c.183 §3]
