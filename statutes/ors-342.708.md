# ORS (ORS 342.708)

## At a glance

ORS 342.708 — ORS. Chapter 342 (Chapter 342), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

342.708 ORS 342.700 and 342.704 not limitation on or prerequisite for other rights and remedies. Nothing in ORS 342.700 and 342.704 is intended to limit or operate as a prerequisite to pursuing any rights or remedies provided under other statutes or the common law. [1997 c.272 §3] Note: See note under 342.700. 342.710 [1971 c.582 §1; repealed by 1973 c.536 §39] 342.720 [1971 c.582 §§2,7; repealed by 1973 c.536 §39] STEROIDS AND PERFORMANCE-ENHANCING SUBSTANCES
