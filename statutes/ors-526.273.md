# Cooperation with federal agencies (ORS 526.273)

## At a glance

ORS 526.273 — Cooperation with federal agencies. Chapter 526 (Forestry Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

526.273 Cooperation with federal agencies. The State Forestry Department shall cooperate with federal agencies to increase the effectiveness of activities undertaken pursuant to ORS 526.271, 526.274 and 526.275. [2021 c.592 §27a] Note: 526.273 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 526 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
