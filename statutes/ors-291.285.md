# Payment of accounting charges from Economic Development Fund (ORS 291.285)

## At a glance

ORS 291.285 — Payment of accounting charges from Economic Development Fund. Chapter 291 (State Financial Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

291.285 Payment of accounting charges from Economic Development Fund. Moneys in the Administrative Services Economic Development Fund are continuously appropriated for the payment of accounting charges of the Oregon Department of Administrative Services in order to maintain accountability of the fund. [1985 c.828 §16] Note: 291.285 and 291.290 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 291 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
