# False return, statement or document prohibited (ORS 305.815)

## At a glance

ORS 305.815 — False return, statement or document prohibited. Chapter 305 (Chapter 305), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

305.815 False return, statement or document prohibited. No person shall willfully make and subscribe any return, statement or other document that contains or is verified by a declaration under ORS 305.810 that it is made under penalties for false swearing if the person does not believe the return, statement or other document is true and correct as to every material matter. [Formerly 306.420; 1997 c.84 §3]
