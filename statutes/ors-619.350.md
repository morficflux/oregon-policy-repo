# “Fryer” defined (ORS 619.350)

## At a glance

## Full text

619.350 “Fryer” defined. As used in ORS 619.350 to 619.380, unless the context requires otherwise, “fryer” means a chicken of any breed or variety, slaughtered under the age of six months, produced for sale for human consumption as a fryer, broiler or fryer-roaster, or the cut-up parts of such a chicken. [1959 c.385 §1; 1997 c.462 §1; 2009 c.367 §4]
