# Criminal penalties (ORS 100.990)

## At a glance

ORS 100.990 — Criminal penalties. Chapter 100 (Chapter 100), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

100.990 Criminal penalties. Subject to ORS 153.022, any person who violates any of the provisions of ORS 100.015, 100.635 to 100.730 and 100.740 to 100.780 or any rules adopted thereunder or any alternative requirements of the Real Estate Commissioner prescribed pursuant to ORS 100.720 (3), commits a Class C felony. [Formerly 94.991; 1999 c.1051 §300; 2011 c.597 §158] _______________
