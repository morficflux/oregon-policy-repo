# Commissioner’s order against unlicensed agent (Escrow Property) (ORS 696.577)

## At a glance

ORS 696.577 — Commissioner’s order against unlicensed agent (Escrow Property). Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.577 Commissioner’s order against unlicensed agent. Whenever the Real Estate Commissioner finds that any person is offering to engage in or engaging in the business of an escrow agent without a license as an escrow agent as required under ORS 696.505 to 696.590, the commissioner may order the person to cease and desist from offering to engage in or engaging in the business of an escrow agent. Any proceeding under this section is subject to the requirements of ORS chapter 183. [1981 c.617 §36] (Escrow Property)
