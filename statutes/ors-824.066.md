# Helper unit operation restrictions (ORS 824.066)

## At a glance

ORS 824.066 — Helper unit operation restrictions. Chapter 824 (Chapter 824), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

824.066 Helper unit operation restrictions.

(1) Except for operation in its yards or terminals, no railroad shall permit or require a helper unit that is not attached to a train to be operated with a crew of fewer than two people.

(2) As used in this section, “helper unit” means a locomotive power unit placed near the middle of or at the rear of a train to help the train traverse steep grades.

(3) A violation of this section is a Class A violation. [Formerly 761.331; 1999 c.1051 §231]
