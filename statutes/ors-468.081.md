# Rights of injured person (ORS 468.081)

## At a glance

ORS 468.081 — Rights of injured person. Chapter 468 (Chapter 468), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468.081 Rights of injured person. ORS 468.076 to 468.087 do not accord a person injured or threatened with injury in another jurisdiction any rights superior to those that the person would have if injured or threatened with injury in Oregon. [1991 c.826 §6] Note: See note under 468.076.
