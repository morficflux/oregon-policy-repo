# Business trusts and trustees not subject to bank and trust company regulations (ORS 128.570)

## At a glance

ORS 128.570 — Business trusts and trustees not subject to bank and trust company regulations. Chapter 128 (Chapter 128), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

128.570 Business trusts and trustees not subject to bank and trust company regulations. Neither a business trust nor the trustees of such trust are subject to the provisions of ORS chapters 706, 707 and 709. [1971 c.491 §3; 1997 c.631 §418]
