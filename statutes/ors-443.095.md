# Applicability of laws to domestic service (ORS 443.095)

## At a glance

ORS 443.095 — Applicability of laws to domestic service. Chapter 443 (Chapter 443), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

443.095 Applicability of laws to domestic service. No provision of ORS 443.014 to 443.105 shall be construed to prevent repair or domestic services by any person. [1977 c.738 §10; 2005 c.22 §304]
