# Definitions for ORS (ORS 196.200)

## At a glance

ORS 196.200 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 196 (Chapter 196), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

196.200 Definitions for ORS 196.200 to 196.240 and 196.993. As used in ORS 196.200 to 196.240 and 196.993, “Willamette Falls Locks project” means:

(1) The project for navigation, Willamette Falls Locks, Willamette River, Oregon, authorized by the Act of June 25, 1910 (61 P.L. 264, 36 Stat. 630, 664, chapter 382);

(2) Any land in which the federal government had a property interest for the project described in subsection

(1) of this section as of June 11, 2021; and

(3) Any improvements on the land described in subsection

(2) of this section. [2021 c.229 §1] Note: 196.200 to 196.240 and 196.993 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 196 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
