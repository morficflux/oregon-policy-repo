# Concealing the birth of an infant (ORS 167.820)

## At a glance

ORS 167.820 — Concealing the birth of an infant. Chapter 167 (Chapter 167), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

167.820 Concealing the birth of an infant.

(1) A person commits the crime of concealing the birth of an infant if the person conceals the corpse of a newborn child with intent to conceal the fact of its birth or to prevent a determination of whether it was born dead or alive.

(2) Concealing the birth of an infant is a Class A misdemeanor. [1971 c.743 §286]
