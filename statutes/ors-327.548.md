# Hunger Free Schools Account (ORS 327.548)

## At a glance

ORS 327.548 — Hunger Free Schools Account. Chapter 327 (Chapter 327), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

327.548 Hunger Free Schools Account.

(1) The Hunger Free Schools Account is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund.

(2) Moneys in the Hunger Free Schools Account are continuously appropriated to the Department of Education for the purpose of making reimbursements to school districts under ORS 327.545.
