# Rules (ORS 413.014)

## At a glance

ORS 413.014 — Rules. Chapter 413 (Chapter 413), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

413.014 Rules. In accordance with applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Oregon Health Policy Board may adopt rules necessary for the administration of the laws that the board is charged with administering. [2009 c.595 §6] 413.015 [Formerly 413.030; repealed by 1969 c.69 §8]
