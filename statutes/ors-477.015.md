# Definitions (ORS 477.015)

## At a glance

ORS 477.015 — Definitions. Chapter 477 (Chapter 477), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

477.015 Definitions. As used in this section and ORS 477.025:

(1) “Wildland” means: (a) Forestland, as defined in ORS 477.001; or (b) An unimproved area that contains enough unmanaged vegetation, at any time of the year, to constitute a fire hazard, in the judgment of the forester, regardless of how the area is zoned or taxed.

(2) “Wildland-urban interface” means a geographic area in which there is a concentration of dwellings in an urban or suburban setting near wildland.
