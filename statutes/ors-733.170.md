# Accounts and records (ORS 733.170)

## At a glance

ORS 733.170 — Accounts and records. Chapter 733 (Chapter 733), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

733.170 Accounts and records. An insurer shall keep its books, records, accounts and transaction source data in such manner that the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may readily verify its statements of financial condition and ascertain whether the insurer is unimpaired, has given proper treatment to policyholders and has complied with the Insurance Code.
