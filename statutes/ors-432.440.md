# County registrar sale of certified copies of records of live birth and death; rules (ORS 432.440)

## At a glance

ORS 432.440 — County registrar sale of certified copies of records of live birth and death; rules. Chapter 432 (Chapter 432), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

432.440 County registrar sale of certified copies of records of live birth and death; rules. The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt, taking into consideration local service needs and interests, rules to allow a county registrar to sell, within six months of the date of the event occurring in the county, certified copies of records of live birth and death. [Formerly 432.085]
