# Application of time limitations to public bodies (ORS 115.008)

## At a glance

## Full text

115.008 Application of time limitations to public bodies. Notwithstanding ORS 12.250, and except as otherwise specifically provided in this chapter, all statutes of limitation and other time limitations imposed under this chapter apply to actions brought in the name of the state, or brought in the name of any county or public corporation, and to actions brought for the benefit of the state or for the benefit of any county or public corporation.
