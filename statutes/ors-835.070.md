# Accounts (ORS 835.070)

## At a glance

ORS 835.070 — Accounts. Chapter 835 (Aviation Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

835.070 Accounts. The Director of the Oregon Department of Aviation shall keep a true and accurate account of all sums received and of vouchers issued by the Oregon Department of Aviation.
