# Political subdivisions to establish rules (ORS 526.821)

## At a glance

ORS 526.821 — Political subdivisions to establish rules. Chapter 526 (Forestry Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

526.821 Political subdivisions to establish rules. All political subdivisions engaged in selling public timber shall establish, by rule, the standards and procedures to implement the provisions of ORS 526.801 to 526.831 and 526.992. [1991 c.942 §8] 526.825 [1963 c.298 §4; 1967 c.34 §4; repealed by 1981 c.823 §3]
