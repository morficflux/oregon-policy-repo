# Commission subject to state budget procedures and laws governing supervision of expenditures (ORS 462.265)

## At a glance

ORS 462.265 — Commission subject to state budget procedures and laws governing supervision of expenditures. Chapter 462 (Chapter 462), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

462.265 Commission subject to state budget procedures and laws governing supervision of expenditures.

(1) The Oregon Racing Commission is subject to the provisions of ORS 291.201 to 291.222, including but not limited to the provisions of those sections relating to changes and revisions by the Governor in agency request budgets.

(2) The commission and its officers and employees are subject to the provisions of ORS 291.232 to 291.260 and 291.990. [1959 c.284 §§1,2; 1969 c.356 §35; 2016 c.117 §69]
