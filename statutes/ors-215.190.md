## At a glance

## Full text

215.190 Violation of ordinances or regulations. No person shall locate, construct, maintain, repair, alter, or use a building or other structure or use or transfer land in violation of an ordinance or regulation authorized by ORS 215.010 to 215.190 and 215.402 to 215.438. [1955 c.439 §9; 1963 c.619 §13] 215.200 [1957 s.s. c.11 §1; renumbered 215.285] AGRICULTURAL LAND USE (Exclusive Farm Use Zones)
