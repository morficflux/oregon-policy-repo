# State shared revenues not available to historic ghost town THE 1893 INCORPORATION ACT (ORS 221.872)

## At a glance

ORS 221.872 — State shared revenues not available to historic ghost town THE 1893 INCORPORATION ACT. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.872 State shared revenues not available to historic ghost town. An historic ghost town shall not receive any portion of liquor revenues, highway funds or other state funds distributed to incorporated cities.
