# Use of Ring Test or Whey Test for brucellosis ICE CREAM AND OTHER FROZEN DESSERTS (ORS 621.300)

## At a glance

ORS 621.300 — Use of Ring Test or Whey Test for brucellosis ICE CREAM AND OTHER FROZEN DESSERTS. Chapter 621 (Chapter 621), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

621.300 Use of Ring Test or Whey Test for brucellosis. The State Department of Agriculture may approve the use of the Ring Test or the Whey Test as alternatives to blood testing of milk producing animals for brucellosis.
