# Reissuance of revoked certificate (Civil Penalties) (ORS 672.685)

## At a glance

ORS 672.685 — Reissuance of revoked certificate (Civil Penalties). Chapter 672 (Chapter 672), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

672.685 Reissuance of revoked certificate. The State Board of Geologist Examiners may reissue a certificate of registration to any person whose certificate has been revoked if the person:

(1) Submits a written application to the board showing good cause to justify the reissuance; and

(2) Demonstrates compliance with the continuing education requirements established under ORS 672.588. [1977 c.612 §20; 2019 c.125 §4] (Civil Penalties)
