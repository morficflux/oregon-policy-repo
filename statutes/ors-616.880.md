# Written warning notice for minor violation (ORS 616.880)

## At a glance

ORS 616.880 — Written warning notice for minor violation. Chapter 616 (General and Miscellaneous Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

616.880 Written warning notice for minor violation. Nothing in ORS 616.850 to 616.890 shall be construed as requiring the State Department of Agriculture to cite incidental or minor violations of ORS 616.860 to 616.870 whenever the department believes that the public interest will be served adequately in the circumstances by issuance of an alleged written warning notice. Each such notice issued shall include the name and address of the grocery store or food market, the date of the notice issuance, a description of the alleged violation and a statement of the penalties for a continued course of violation. [1977 c.181 §9; 2009 c.175 §9] 616.885 [1977 c.181 §10; 1991 c.734 §54; renumbered 616.996 in 2001]
