# Disclosure of S corporation items to shareholder (ORS 314.770)

## At a glance

ORS 314.770 — Disclosure of S corporation items to shareholder. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.770 Disclosure of S corporation items to shareholder. The Department of Revenue may disclose to the shareholder of an S corporation those items of S corporation gain, loss or other particulars relating to the S corporation that are necessary to administer the tax imposed by ORS chapter 316, 317 or 318 if the department considers the disclosure necessary to facilitate the audit of the shareholder’s income tax return. [Formerly 314.749] Note: 314.770 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 314 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
