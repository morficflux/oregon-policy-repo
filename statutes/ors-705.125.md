## Full text

705.125 Deputy director; subordinate officers and personnel.

(1) With the approval of the Governor, the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may appoint a deputy director who shall serve at the pleasure of the director, not be subject to the State Personnel Relations Law and have full authority to act for the director, subject to the control of the director. The appointment of the deputy director shall be by written order filed with the Secretary of State.

(2) Except as provided in ORS 705.115 and subsection

(1) of this section, the director, subject to applicable provisions of the State Personnel Relations Law, shall appoint all subordinate officers and employees of the Department of Consumer and Business Services, prescribe their functions and fix their compensation. [1987 c.373 §5] 705.130 [1985 c.762 §6; repealed by 1987 c.373 §85]
