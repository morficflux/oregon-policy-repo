# Stock at large or grazing on highway from Canyonville to Umpqua National Forest (ORS 607.515)

## At a glance

ORS 607.515 — Stock at large or grazing on highway from Canyonville to Umpqua National Forest. Chapter 607 (Chapter 607), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

607.515 Stock at large or grazing on highway from Canyonville to Umpqua National Forest. No person shall permit any cattle, horses, mules, sheep, goats, hogs or other livestock, to run at large, be pastured, staked or tethered upon the state highway extending from the easterly boundary of the incorporated City of Canyonville easterly to the west boundary of the Umpqua National Forest.
