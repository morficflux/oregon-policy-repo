# Continuing authority of board upon lapse, suspension, revocation or voluntary surrender of license (ORS 683.278)

## At a glance

ORS 683.278 — Continuing authority of board upon lapse, suspension, revocation or voluntary surrender of license. Chapter 683 (Chapter 683), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

683.278 Continuing authority of board upon lapse, suspension, revocation or voluntary surrender of license. The lapse, suspension or revocation of a license by operation of law, by order of the Oregon Board of Optometry or by decision of a court of law, or the voluntary surrender of a license by a licensee, shall not deprive the board of jurisdiction to proceed with any investigation of or any action or disciplinary proceeding against the licensee, or to revise or render null and void an order suspending or revoking the license. [2001 c.456 §2] Note: 683.278 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 683 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 683.280 [Amended by 1973 c.829 §66; 1985 c.103 §11; repealed by 1997 c.643 §34]
