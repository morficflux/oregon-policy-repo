# Petty cash fund ENFORCEMENT (ORS 677.305)

## At a glance

## Full text

677.305 Petty cash fund. The Oregon Medical Board may maintain a petty cash fund in compliance with ORS 293.180 in the amount of $5,000. [1955 c.282 §1; 1967 c.470 §57; 1983 c.486 §39; 1989 c.830 §24] 677.310 [Amended by 1967 c.470 §58; repealed by 1989 c.830 §49] ENFORCEMENT
