# When personal service of process required (ORS 650.080)

## At a glance

ORS 650.080 — When personal service of process required. Chapter 650 (Chapter 650), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

650.080 When personal service of process required. The method of service referred to in ORS 650.075 may not be used if personal service can be used. [1973 c.509 §11]
