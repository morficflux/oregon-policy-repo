# Holder of school nurse certificate qualified to conduct and coordinate health services program (ORS 342.495)

## At a glance

ORS 342.495 — Holder of school nurse certificate qualified to conduct and coordinate health services program. Chapter 342 (Chapter 342), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

342.495 Holder of school nurse certificate qualified to conduct and coordinate health services program.

(1) The holder of a school nurse certificate issued under ORS 342.475

(1) to

(3) is qualified to accept employment to conduct and coordinate the health services programs of any public school in the State of Oregon. A person licensed as a registered nurse may use the term “nurse” as part of a title when employed by a school.

(2) No school or school district is required to employ as a nurse a person certified under ORS 342.475

(1) to (3). [Formerly 342.982] 342.505 [Amended by 1955 c.219 §1; 1961 c.383 §1; 1965 c.100 §367; repealed by 1993 c.45 §177] 342.508 [1957 c.446 §1; 1965 c.100 §368; 1965 c.608 §20; repealed by 1973 c.298 §9] 342.510 [Amended by 1965 c.100 §380; renumbered 342.965]
