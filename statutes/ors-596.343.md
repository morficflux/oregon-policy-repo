# Testing sheep for Akabane Virus (ORS 596.343)

## At a glance

ORS 596.343 — Testing sheep for Akabane Virus. Chapter 596 (Disease Control Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

596.343 Testing sheep for Akabane Virus. In carrying out the provisions of this chapter, unless the State Department of Agriculture determines, upon the advice of appropriate federal animal health authorities, that no significant threat to the health of the livestock of this state exists, the department shall cause all sheep entering this state that are not exempt pursuant to ORS 596.341

(3) to be examined or tested for Akabane Virus. [1987 c.909 §6]
