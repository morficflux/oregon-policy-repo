# Sewage treatment works violation penalties (ORS 448.992)

## At a glance

ORS 448.992 — Sewage treatment works violation penalties. Chapter 448 (Chapter 448), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

448.992 Sewage treatment works violation penalties.

(1) Any person who knowingly and willfully violates ORS 448.415

(2) commits a Class B misdemeanor.

(2) Any person who knowingly makes any false statement, representation, or certification in any application, record, report, plan or other document filed or required to be maintained under ORS 448.410 to 448.430, or by any rule adopted under ORS 448.410 to 448.430, commits a Class B misdemeanor. [1987 c.635 §8; 2011 c.597 §200]
