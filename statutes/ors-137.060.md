# Form of bench warrant (ORS 137.060)

## At a glance

ORS 137.060 — Form of bench warrant. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.060 Form of bench warrant. The bench warrant shall be substantially in the following form: ______________________________________________________________________________ CIRCUIT COURT FOR THE COUNTY OF ______, STATE OF OREGON IN THE NAME OF THE STATE OF OREGON To any peace officer in the State of Oregon, greeting: A B having been on the ______ day of ______, 2___, convicted in this court of the crime of (designating it generally), you are commanded to arrest the above-named defendant forthwith and bring the defendant before such court for judgment or, if the court has adjourned, deliver the defendant into the custody of the jailor of this county. By order of the court. Witness my hand and seal of said circuit court, affixed at ______, in said county, this ______ day of ______, 2___. [L. S.] C D, Clerk of the Court ______________________________________________________________________________ [Amended by 1957 c.659 §1; 1971 c.423 §1; 2015 c.212 §16]
