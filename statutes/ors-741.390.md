# False or misleading filings prohibited (ORS 741.390)

## At a glance

ORS 741.390 — False or misleading filings prohibited. Chapter 741 (Chapter 741), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

741.390 False or misleading filings prohibited. A person may not file or cause to be filed with the Oregon Health Authority any article, certificate, report, statement, application or any other information related to the health insurance exchange required or permitted by the authority to be filed, that is known by the person to be false or misleading in any material respect. [2011 c.415 §24; 2015 c.3 §28; 2021 c.569 §29]
