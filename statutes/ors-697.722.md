# Execution or attachment of trust account funds (ORS 697.722)

## At a glance

ORS 697.722 — Execution or attachment of trust account funds. Chapter 697 (Chapter 697), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

697.722 Execution or attachment of trust account funds. Funds in a trust account that a debt management service provider maintains under ORS 697.682 are not subject to execution or attachment on a claim against the debt management service provider. [1983 c.17 §14; 2009 c.604 §12] 697.725 [1959 c.635 §24; repealed by 1973 c.794 §34] 697.730 [1959 c.635 §25; repealed by 1981 c.631 §2]
