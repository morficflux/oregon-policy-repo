# Inquiries from director to premium finance company (ORS 746.422)

## At a glance

ORS 746.422 — Inquiries from director to premium finance company. Chapter 746 (Chapter 746), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

746.422 Inquiries from director to premium finance company. In the manner provided in ORS 731.296, the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may address inquiries to a premium finance company, and a premium finance company shall reply to such inquiries. [1993 c.265 §11]
