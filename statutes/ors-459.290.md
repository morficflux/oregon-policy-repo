# Disposal site rehabilitation and enhancement advisory committee (ORS 459.290)

## At a glance

ORS 459.290 — Disposal site rehabilitation and enhancement advisory committee. Chapter 459 (Chapter 459), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459.290 Disposal site rehabilitation and enhancement advisory committee. Each local government unit that apportions money under ORS 459.284 shall establish a citizens advisory committee to select plans, programs and projects for the rehabilitation and enhancement of the area around disposal sites for which the local government unit has apportioned moneys under ORS 459.284. If any local government unit establishes a citizens advisory committee under this section, another local government unit may not also establish a local citizens advisory committee under this section for the same disposal site.
