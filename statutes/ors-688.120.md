# Fraudulent representations prohibited (Miscellaneous) (ORS 688.120)

## At a glance

ORS 688.120 — Fraudulent representations prohibited (Miscellaneous). Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.120 Fraudulent representations prohibited. A person may not obtain or attempt to obtain a license as a physical therapist or as a physical therapist assistant or a temporary permit under ORS 688.110 by any fraudulent representation. [1959 c.461 §13; 1969 c.339 §13; 1971 c.585 §12; 1975 c.111 §9; 2005 c.21 §8; 2005 c.627 §10] (Miscellaneous)
