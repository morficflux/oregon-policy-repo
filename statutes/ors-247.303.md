# Deadline for elector to update registration (ORS 247.303)

## At a glance

ORS 247.303 — Deadline for elector to update registration. Chapter 247 (Chapter 247), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

247.303 Deadline for elector to update registration. Notwithstanding ORS 247.025, an elector whose registration is active or inactive may update the registration at any time before 8 p.m. on the day of the election.
