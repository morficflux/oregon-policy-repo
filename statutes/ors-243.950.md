# Public Safety Memorial Fund (ORS 243.950)

## At a glance

ORS 243.950 — Public Safety Memorial Fund. Chapter 243 (Chapter 243), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

243.950 Public Safety Memorial Fund. The Public Safety Memorial Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned, if any, shall inure to the benefit of the Public Safety Memorial Fund. All moneys deposited in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Department of Public Safety Standards and Training for the purposes of ORS 243.954 to 243.974, to be expended by the Public Safety Memorial Fund Board, established by ORS 243.952, as provided in ORS 243.954 to 243.974. However, the board may not expend more than $60,000 per biennium of the moneys for administrative costs of the board incurred under ORS 243.954 to 243.974. [1999 c.981 §3] Note: 243.950 to 243.974 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 243 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
