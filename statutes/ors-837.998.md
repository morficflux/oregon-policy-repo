# Civil penalties GENERALLY (ORS 837.998)

## At a glance

ORS 837.998 — Civil penalties GENERALLY. Chapter 837 (Chapter 837), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

837.998 Civil penalties.

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this section, in addition to any other penalty provided by law, the Director of the Oregon Department of Aviation may impose a civil penalty not to exceed $720 for each violation of any provision of this chapter or any rule adopted, or order issued, under this chapter.

(2) The director may impose a civil penalty not to exceed $2,500 for violation of ORS 837.080 or any rule adopted, or order issued, under this chapter to enforce ORS 837.080.

(3) The director shall impose civil penalties under this section in the manner provided in ORS 183.745. [2013 c.403 §2] _______________
