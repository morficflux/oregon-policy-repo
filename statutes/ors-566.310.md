# Definition for ORS (ORS 566.310)

## At a glance

ORS 566.310 — Definition for ORS. Chapter 566 (Chapter 566), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

566.310 Definition for ORS 566.310 to 566.360. As used in ORS 566.310 to 566.360, “secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture of the United States. [Formerly 414.040 and then 414.810; 1995 c.152 §1; 2003 c.253 §25]
