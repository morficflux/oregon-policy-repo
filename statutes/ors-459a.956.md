# Certification program (ORS 459A.956)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.956 — Certification program. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.956 Certification program.

(1) The Department of Environmental Quality shall establish a program or approve a program established by a third party to certify commingled recycling processing facilities located outside of Oregon. The department may issue certificates under the program or develop a list of approved contractors to issue certificates.

(2) A commingled recycling processing facility certified under this section must satisfy the requirements of ORS 459A.955 (2). [2021 c.681 §38]
