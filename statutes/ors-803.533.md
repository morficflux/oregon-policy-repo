# Period of validity for Oregon National Guard member or military reservist (ORS 803.533)

## At a glance

ORS 803.533 — Period of validity for Oregon National Guard member or military reservist. Chapter 803 (Chapter 803), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

803.533 Period of validity for Oregon National Guard member or military reservist.

(1) Notwithstanding ORS 803.530, a registration plate assigned to a vehicle registered to a member of the Oregon National Guard or a military reservist ordered on active duty and deployed to a location outside the United States that expires while the person is on active duty shall remain valid for 90 days following the termination of active duty.

(2) The court shall dismiss the charge of improper display of validating stickers under ORS 803.560 if, when charged, the person charged was the registered owner of the vehicle and a member of the Oregon National Guard or a military reservist, and the registration plate was valid pursuant to subsection

(1) of this section. [2005 c.257 §4] Note: 803.533 was added to and made a part of the Oregon Vehicle Code by legislative action but was not added to ORS chapter 803 or any series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
