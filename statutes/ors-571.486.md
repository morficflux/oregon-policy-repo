# Requirement to comply with ORS (ORS 571.486)

## At a glance

ORS 571.486 — Requirement to comply with ORS. Chapter 571 (Chapter 571), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

571.486 Requirement to comply with ORS 571.483 in order to make, incur expenditure. An expenditure of moneys for a fiscal year may not be made or incurred by or under the authority of the Oregon Hemp Commission under ORS 571.400 to 571.501 unless the commission complies with ORS 571.483. [2021 c.216 §31] Note: See note under 571.400.
