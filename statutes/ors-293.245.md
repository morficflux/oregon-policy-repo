# Subsequent collection of debt written off under ORS (ORS 293.245)

## At a glance

ORS 293.245 — Subsequent collection of debt written off under ORS. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.245 Subsequent collection of debt written off under ORS 293.240. If a debt previously written off pursuant to ORS 293.240 subsequently becomes collectible, the state agency to which the money is owed shall proceed to collect the money due. The agency shall credit the money so collected to the accounts or funds to which the debt was originally owing. [1965 c.448 §3; 1971 c.604 §4; 2001 c.233 §2] RECEIVING AND HANDLING MONEYS
