# Definitions for ORS 137.126 to (ORS 137.126)

## At a glance

ORS 137.126 — Definitions for ORS 137.126 to. Chapter 137 (Chapter 137), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

137.126 Definitions for ORS 137.126 to 137.131. As used in ORS 137.126 to 137.131:

(1) “Community service” means uncompensated labor for an agency whose purpose is to enhance physical or mental stability, environmental quality or the social welfare.

(2) “Agency” means a nonprofit organization or public body agreeing to accept community service from offenders and to report on the progress of ordered community service to the court or its delegate. [1981 c.551 §2] 137.127 [1955 c.660 §5; repealed by 1967 c.585 §8]
