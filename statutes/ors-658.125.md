# Judicial review (ORS 658.125)

## At a glance

ORS 658.125 — Judicial review. Chapter 658 (Chapter 658), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

658.125 Judicial review. A person aggrieved by an order of the Commissioner of the Bureau of Labor and Industries may obtain judicial review thereof in accordance with ORS chapter 183.
