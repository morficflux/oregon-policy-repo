# Limitations on payments and contributions (ORS 237.375)

## At a glance

ORS 237.375 — Limitations on payments and contributions. Chapter 237 (Chapter 237), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

237.375 Limitations on payments and contributions. Nothing in ORS 237.350 to 237.380 authorizes the city to budget, provide for payments or collect contributions to fund retirement benefits for an individual who is not in the employment of the city at the time of the creation of a membership status under a retirement plan. [Formerly 238.560]
