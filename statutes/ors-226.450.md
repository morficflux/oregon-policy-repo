## Full text

226.450 Authority to expend funds for maintenance of cemeteries. All cities owning and controlling lands, lots or parcels of ground used for cemetery purposes may expend, for the purpose of beautifying, caring for and keeping up all such lands and premises, such public funds as have been included for that purpose in their annual budget. 226.480 [1953 c.51 §1; repealed by 1981 c.48 §8] 226.490 [1971 c.38 §1; repealed by 1981 c.48 §8] ABANDONED CEMETERIES
