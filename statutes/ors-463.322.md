# Persons exempt from tax (ORS 463.322)

## At a glance

ORS 463.322 — Persons exempt from tax. Chapter 463 (Chapter 463), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

463.322 Persons exempt from tax. Nothing in ORS 463.015, 463.035, 463.113, 463.320 and 463.322 is intended to tax subscribers to pay-per-view telecasts or cable system operators in this state. [1993 c.587 §6; 2017 c.235 §18] Note: 463.322 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 463 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
