# Disqualification (ORS 321.848)

## At a glance

ORS 321.848 — Disqualification. Chapter 321 (Chapter 321), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

321.848 Disqualification. ORS 308A.700 to 308A.733 apply whenever land designated as forestland as a result of an application being filed therefor under ORS 321.805 to 321.855 thereafter becomes disqualified. [Formerly 321.825]
