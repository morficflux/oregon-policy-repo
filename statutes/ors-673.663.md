# Use of title “tax consultant” (ORS 673.663)

## At a glance

ORS 673.663 — Use of title “tax consultant”. Chapter 673 (Chapter 673), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

673.663 Use of title “tax consultant.”

(1) An individual may not assume or use the title or designation “tax consultant” unless the person is licensed as a tax consultant under ORS 673.605 to 673.740.

(2) A sole proprietorship, partnership, corporation or other legal entity may not assume or use the title or designation “tax consultant” unless the entity is in compliance with ORS 673.643. [1991 c.297 §2] 673.665 [1973 c.387 §12; repealed by 1975 c.464 §26]
