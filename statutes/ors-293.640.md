# Period covered by biennial reports (ORS 293.640)

## At a glance

ORS 293.640 — Period covered by biennial reports. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.640 Period covered by biennial reports. The biennial report of any state officer or agency required to be submitted to the Legislative Assembly or the Governor shall cover the biennial period closing on June 30 next preceding the odd-numbered year regular session of the Legislative Assembly.
