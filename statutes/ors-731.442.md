# Prohibition on transacting life insurance business on mutual assessment plan (ORS 731.442)

## At a glance

ORS 731.442 — Prohibition on transacting life insurance business on mutual assessment plan. Chapter 731 (Administration and General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

731.442 Prohibition on transacting life insurance business on mutual assessment plan. An insurer may not transact a life insurance business upon a mutual assessment plan within this state. [Formerly 739.105; 2005 c.185 §1]
