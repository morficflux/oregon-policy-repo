# When list of persons issued electrical permits supplied (ORS 308.310)

## At a glance

ORS 308.310 — When list of persons issued electrical permits supplied. Chapter 308 (Chapter 308), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

308.310 When list of persons issued electrical permits supplied. The Electrical and Elevator Board in the Department of Consumer and Business Services shall furnish any county assessor upon request a complete list of those persons who have been issued electrical permits in such county within one year of the date of the request, together with the location of the electrical installations requested thereby. The board shall have 30 days to prepare the list after the board has received the request. [Amended by 1983 c.740 §88; 1987 c.414 §149; 1993 c.744 §107] 308.315 [Repealed by 1955 c.610 §1]
