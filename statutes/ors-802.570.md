# Compensation and reimbursement for legislative representative under Multistate Highway Transportation Agreement AGREEMENTS WITH PRIVATE CONTRACTORS (ORS 802.570)

## At a glance

ORS 802.570 — Compensation and reimbursement for legislative representative under Multistate Highway Transportation Agreement AGREEMENTS WITH PRIVATE CONTRACTORS. Chapter 802 (Chapter 802), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

802.570 Compensation and reimbursement for legislative representative under Multistate Highway Transportation Agreement. A legislator who is a designated representative under ORS 802.560 is entitled to compensation and expense reimbursement under ORS 171.072, payable from funds appropriated to the Legislative Assembly. [1987 c.879 §21; 2001 c.610 §2] AGREEMENTS WITH PRIVATE CONTRACTORS
