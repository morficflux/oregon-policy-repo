# Duty to report prohibited conduct (ORS 688.838)

## At a glance

ORS 688.838 — Duty to report prohibited conduct. Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.838 Duty to report prohibited conduct. Unless state or federal laws relating to confidentiality or the protection of health information prohibit disclosure, a respiratory care practitioner or polysomnographic technologist who has reasonable cause to believe that a licensee of another board has engaged in prohibited conduct as defined in ORS 676.150 shall report the prohibited conduct in the manner provided in ORS 676.150. [2009 c.536 §30; 2011 c.715 §15]
