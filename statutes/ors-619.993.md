# Criminal penalty for meat inspection law violations (ORS 619.993)

## At a glance

ORS 619.993 — Criminal penalty for meat inspection law violations. Chapter 619 (Chapter 619), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

619.993 Criminal penalty for meat inspection law violations. Violation of any of the provisions of ORS 619.010 to 619.071 and 619.370, or rules adopted under ORS 619.010 to 619.071 or for the enforcement of ORS 619.370 is a Class A misdemeanor. [1973 c.174 §16; 1975 c.304 §8; 2009 c.175 §3]
