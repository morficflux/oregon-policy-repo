# Performance-based incentive payments for primary care (ORS 743B.458)

## At a glance

ORS 743B.458 — Performance-based incentive payments for primary care. Chapter 743B (Chapter 743B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743B.458 Performance-based incentive payments for primary care. An insurer offering a health benefit plan, as defined in ORS 743B.005, that reimburses the costs of services provided by a national primary care medical home payment model, conducted by the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Innovation in accordance with 42 U.S.C. 1315a, that includes performance-based incentive payments for primary care, shall offer similar alternative payment methodologies to reimburse the costs of services provided by patient centered primary care homes identified in accordance with ORS 413.259 that serve beneficiaries of the health benefit plan. [2017 c.489 §7] Note: 743B.458 was added to and made a part of the Insurance Code by legislative action but was not added to ORS chapter 743B or any series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
