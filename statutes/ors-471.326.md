# Refund of civil penalty if suspension not sustained on judicial review (ORS 471.326)

## At a glance

ORS 471.326 — Refund of civil penalty if suspension not sustained on judicial review. Chapter 471 (Alcoholic Liquors Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

471.326 Refund of civil penalty if suspension not sustained on judicial review. If the action of the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission in suspending a license or permit issued under this chapter is not sustained upon judicial review under ORS chapter 183, the commission shall promptly refund the amount paid pursuant to ORS 471.322

(1) by check or order drawn on the State Treasurer from the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission Account. [1969 c.67 §4; 1973 c.144 §2; 1975 c.735 §2; 1979 c.264 §11; 1991 c.61 §2; 1995 c.301 §66; 1999 c.351 §53; 2021 c.351 §80]
