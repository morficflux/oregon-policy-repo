# Governor’s budget to include amount needed to pay amounts due on unpaid financing agreements (ORS 283.091)

## At a glance

ORS 283.091 — Governor’s budget to include amount needed to pay amounts due on unpaid financing agreements. Chapter 283 (Interagency Services), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

283.091 Governor’s budget to include amount needed to pay amounts due on unpaid financing agreements. The Oregon Department of Administrative Services shall include in the Governor’s budget for each fiscal period amounts sufficient to permit the payment of all amounts which will be due on unpaid financing agreements during that fiscal period. [1989 c.1032 §4; 2016 c.117 §46] Note: See note under 283.085.
