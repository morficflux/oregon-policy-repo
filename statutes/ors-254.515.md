# Counting ballots marked “Federal only” (ORS 254.515)

## At a glance

ORS 254.515 — Counting ballots marked “Federal only”. Chapter 254 (Chapter 254), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

254.515 Counting ballots marked “Federal only.” Ballots marked “Federal only” may be counted only for the offices for which the elector is entitled to vote. Votes on the ballot for other offices may not be counted. [Formerly 250.520; 1999 c.410 §60; 2005 c.797 §57; 2017 c.749 §4] 254.520 [Repealed by 1979 c.190 §431]
