# Materials regarding opportunities to work as apprentice; provision of materials to schools, students, parents and guardians (ORS 344.742)

## At a glance

## Full text

344.742 Materials regarding opportunities to work as apprentice; provision of materials to schools, students, parents and guardians.

(1) The Department of Education shall, in conjunction with the Apprenticeship and Training Division of the Bureau of Labor and Industries, prepare written material in print or electronic form regarding opportunities to work as an apprentice, as defined in ORS 660.010, in this state. The department shall update the material at least once per calendar year.

(2) The department shall provide the material described in subsection

(1) of this section to public high schools in this state.

(3) A public high school shall provide the material described in subsection

(1) of this section to students enrolled in the public high school, and to parents or guardians of students enrolled in the public high school, during events or other times when the public high school provides materials regarding higher education opportunities to students and parents or guardians of students. [2017 c.405 §1]
