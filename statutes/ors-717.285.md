## At a glance

## Full text

717.285 Limit on responsibility of licensee; costs and attorney fees.

(1) The responsibility of a licensee to any person for a money transmission conducted on that person’s behalf by the licensee or the licensee’s authorized delegate shall be limited to the amount of money transmitted or the face amount of the payment instrument purchased plus statutory interest.

(2) In addition to any amounts under subsection

(1) of this section, the court may award a prevailing party reasonable costs and attorney fees. [1999 c.571 §19]
