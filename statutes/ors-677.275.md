# Administrative law judges (ORS 677.275)

## At a glance

ORS 677.275 — Administrative law judges. Chapter 677 (Chapter 677), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

677.275 Administrative law judges. Each administrative law judge conducting hearings on behalf of the Oregon Medical Board is vested with the full authority of the board to schedule and conduct hearings on behalf and in the name of the board on all matters referred by the board, including issuance of licenses, proceedings for placing licensees on probation and for suspension and revocation of licenses, and shall cause to be prepared and furnished to the board, for decision thereon by the board, the complete written transcript of the record of the hearing. This transcript shall contain all evidence introduced at the hearing and all pleas, motions and objections, and all rulings of the administrative law judge. Each administrative law judge may administer oaths and issue summonses, notices and subpoenas, but may not place any licensee on probation or issue, refuse, suspend or revoke a license. [1967 c.470 §53; 1983 c.486 §36; 1989 c.830 §21; 1999 c.849 §§157,158; 2003 c.75 §57; 2009 c.756 §27; 2025 c.344 §2]
