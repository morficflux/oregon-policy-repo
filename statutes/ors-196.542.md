# Use of local resources by State Department of Fish and Wildlife (ORS 196.542)

## At a glance

ORS 196.542 — Use of local resources by State Department of Fish and Wildlife. Chapter 196 (Chapter 196), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

196.542 Use of local resources by State Department of Fish and Wildlife. In implementing the activities described in ORS 196.540 pursuant to ORS 196.545, the State Department of Fish and Wildlife shall use local resources where feasible and practical. [2012 c.27 §3; 2024 c.38 §3] Note: See note under 196.540.
