# Application of Internal Revenue Code to certain property tax laws (ORS 305.842)

## At a glance

ORS 305.842 — Application of Internal Revenue Code to certain property tax laws. Chapter 305 (Chapter 305), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

305.842 Application of Internal Revenue Code to certain property tax laws. As used in ORS 307.130, 307.147, 307.580, 308A.450, 310.140, 310.800 and 311.666, “Internal Revenue Code” means the federal Internal Revenue Code as amended and in effect on December 31, 2023. [2014 c.52 §15; 2015 c.348 §27; 2015 c.442 §22; 2016 c.33 §15; 2017 c.527 §16; 2018 c.101 §16; 2019 c.319 §16; 2021 c.456 §17; 2022 c.83 §17; 2023 c.97 §2; 2023 c.171 §17; 2024 c.75 §17]
