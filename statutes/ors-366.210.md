# Limit on administration and engineering expenditure FREIGHT ADVISORY COMMITTEE (ORS 366.210)

## At a glance

ORS 366.210 — Limit on administration and engineering expenditure FREIGHT ADVISORY COMMITTEE. Chapter 366 (State Highways and State Highway Fund), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

366.210 Limit on administration and engineering expenditure. The total cost in any one year for administration and engineering of highway construction shall not exceed 10 percent of the total funds available to the Department of Transportation during that year for its highway activities. [Amended by 1973 c.249 §38; 1993 c.741 §39] FREIGHT ADVISORY COMMITTEE
