# Management of state radio station at Oregon Institute of Technology (ORS 354.090)

## At a glance

ORS 354.090 — Management of state radio station at Oregon Institute of Technology. Chapter 354 (Chapter 354), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

354.090 Management of state radio station at Oregon Institute of Technology. The Oregon Institute of Technology is declared the managing agency of the FM radio station KTEC, licensed to the Oregon Institute of Technology, and as such shall prescribe standards in conformity with the regulations and laws of the United States Government relating to educational FM radio stations. By such standards the Oregon Institute of Technology shall make the facilities of the radio station available in the training programs of the Oregon Institute of Technology.
