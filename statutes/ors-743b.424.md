# Applicability (ORS 743B.424)

## At a glance

ORS 743B.424 — Applicability. Chapter 743B (Chapter 743B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

743B.424 Applicability. The provisions of ORS 743B.001, 743B.220, 743B.405 and 743B.422 do not apply to medical services contracts for services to be provided under ORS chapter 656. [Formerly 743.811] Note: See note under 743B.405.
