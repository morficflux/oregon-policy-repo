# Penalties (ORS 532.990)

## At a glance

ORS 532.990 — Penalties. Chapter 532 (Chapter 532), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

532.990 Penalties.

(1) Violation of any of the provisions of ORS 532.130 is a Class B misdemeanor.

(2) Violation of any of the provisions of ORS 532.140 is a Class C felony. [Subsection

(3) enacted as 1957 c.668 §22; 1983 c.89 §16; 1987 c.320 §238; 1993 c.47 §3; 2011 c.597 §224] CHAPTERS 533 TO 535 [Reserved for expansion]_ ______________
