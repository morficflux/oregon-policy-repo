# Exemptions from ORS (ORS 696.609)

## At a glance

ORS 696.609 — Exemptions from ORS. Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.609 Exemptions from ORS 696.392, 696.600 to 696.785 and 696.995. ORS 696.392, 696.600 to 696.785 and 696.995 do not apply to an individual licensed under ORS 696.022 or to those persons exempt under ORS 696.030 from licensing under ORS 696.020. [1995 c.217 §5; 2001 c.300 §42; 2007 c.319 §17] 696.610 [Repealed by 1981 c.617 §41]
