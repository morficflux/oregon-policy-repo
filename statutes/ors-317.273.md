# Dividend income received by domestic corporation from certain foreign corporations (ORS 317.273)

## At a glance

ORS 317.273 — Dividend income received by domestic corporation from certain foreign corporations. Chapter 317 (Chapter 317), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

317.273 Dividend income received by domestic corporation from certain foreign corporations. To derive Oregon taxable income, there shall be subtracted from federal taxable income dividend income with respect to the “gross-up” provisions of section 78 of the Internal Revenue Code.
