# Interference with department equipment HAZARDOUS MATERIALS GENERALLY (ORS 823.051)

## At a glance

ORS 823.051 — Interference with department equipment HAZARDOUS MATERIALS GENERALLY. Chapter 823 (Chapter 823), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

823.051 Interference with department equipment. No person shall destroy, injure or interfere with any apparatus or appliance owned or operated by or in charge of the Department of Transportation, or any apparatus or appliance sealed by the department.
