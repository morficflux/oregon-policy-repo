## Full text

174.510 Statute revision of 1953 enacted as law; Oregon Revised Statutes; citation.

(1) The statute laws set forth after section 8 of enrolled House Bill No. 2 of the Forty-seventh Legislative Assembly were enacted as law of the State of Oregon, effective December 31, 1953.

(2) The statute laws described in subsection

(1) of this section, together with sections compiled in parts bearing the certificate of the Legislative Counsel pursuant to ORS 171.285, may be cited as Oregon Revised Statutes. In citing a specific section of Oregon Revised Statutes, the designation “ORS (number of section)” may be used. [1953 c.3 §§1,7; 1961 c.90 §2]
