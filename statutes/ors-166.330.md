# Use of firearms with other than incombustible gun wadding (ORS 166.330)

## At a glance

## Full text

166.330 Use of firearms with other than incombustible gun wadding. Any person who uses in any firearms discharged on lands within this state, not owned by the person, anything other than incombustible gun wadding, commits a Class C misdemeanor. [Amended by 2011 c.597 §165] 166.340 [1965 c.20 §§2,3; 1969 c.351 §1; repealed by 1981 c.41 §3]
