# Competitive field trials for hunting dogs (ORS 498.106)

## At a glance

ORS 498.106 — Competitive field trials for hunting dogs. Chapter 498 (Chapter 498), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

498.106 Competitive field trials for hunting dogs. Competitive field trials for hunting dogs may be held at such times and places and under such conditions as the State Fish and Wildlife Commission may prescribe by rule. [1973 c.723 §85] 498.110 [Amended by 1953 c.157 §2; repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
