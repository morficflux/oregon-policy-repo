# Investigating notices; soliciting comments (ORS 714.049)

## At a glance

ORS 714.049 — Investigating notices; soliciting comments. Chapter 714 (Chapter 714), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

714.049 Investigating notices; soliciting comments. The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services shall investigate each notice that a banking institution files under ORS 714.025. With respect to notices for branches that the banking institution will locate outside this state, the director shall promptly provide the local bank supervisory agency or regulator with a copy of the notice and an opportunity to comment on the notice. The director is not bound by the local bank supervisory agency’s or regulator’s comments. [1997 c.631 §316; 2015 c.244 §88c] 714.050 [Amended by 1973 c.797 §323; repealed by 1979 c.826 §1]
