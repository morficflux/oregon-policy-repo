# Authority to require fingerprints (ORS 688.643)

## At a glance

ORS 688.643 — Authority to require fingerprints. Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.643 Authority to require fingerprints. For the purpose of requesting a state or nationwide criminal records check under ORS 181A.195, the Oregon Health Authority may require the fingerprints of a person who is:

(1) Applying for a certificate, or a renewal of a certificate, under ORS 688.640 or 688.650; or

(2) Under investigation by the authority under ORS 688.655. [2023 c.199 §4]
