# County financing construction of interstate bridges (ORS 381.420)

## At a glance

ORS 381.420 — County financing construction of interstate bridges. Chapter 381 (Chapter 381), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

381.420 County financing construction of interstate bridges. Counties may borrow money for the purpose of constructing interstate bridges and issue bonds to evidence such indebtedness. 381.425 [Repealed by 1991 c.220 §15] 381.430 [Repealed by 1991 c.220 §15] 381.435 [Repealed by 1991 c.220 §15]
