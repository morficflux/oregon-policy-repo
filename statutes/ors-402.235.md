# Applicable benefits for injuries to employee providing assistance (ORS 402.235)

## At a glance

ORS 402.235 — Applicable benefits for injuries to employee providing assistance. Chapter 402 (Chapter 402), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

402.235 Applicable benefits for injuries to employee providing assistance. If a person is an employee of a responding participant and the person sustains injury in the course of providing requested assistance, the person is entitled to all applicable benefits, including workers’ compensation, normally available to the employee while performing regular duties for the responding participant. [Formerly 190.168; 2017 c.192 §8]
