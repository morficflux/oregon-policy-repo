# Effective date of local option (ORS 471.515)

## At a glance

ORS 471.515 — Effective date of local option. Chapter 471 (Alcoholic Liquors Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

471.515 Effective date of local option. In each county or city that returns a majority vote for or against prohibition, as to any classes of alcoholic liquor, the law shall take effect on January 1 following the day of election. [Amended by 1983 c.350 §278] 471.520 [Amended by 1979 c.190 §422; repealed by 1983 c.350 §331a]
