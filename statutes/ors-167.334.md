# Evaluation of person convicted of violating ORS (ORS 167.334)

## At a glance

ORS 167.334 — Evaluation of person convicted of violating ORS. Chapter 167 (Chapter 167), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

167.334 Evaluation of person convicted of violating ORS 167.333. Upon the conviction of a defendant for violation of ORS 167.333, the court may order a psychiatric or psychological evaluation of the defendant for inclusion in the presentence report as described in ORS 137.077. [2001 c.926 §5c] Note: 167.334 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 167 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
