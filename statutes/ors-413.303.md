## Full text

413.303 Council chairperson; quorum; meetings.

(1) The Health Information Technology Oversight Council shall select one of the council’s members as chairperson, for such term and with such duties and powers necessary for the performance of the functions of the chairperson as the Oregon Health Policy Board determines.

(2) A majority of the members of the council constitutes a quorum for the transaction of business.

(3) The council shall meet at least quarterly at a place, day and hour determined by the council. The council may also meet at other times and places specified by the call of the chairperson or of a majority of the members of the council. [2009 c.595 §1172; 2015 c.243 §5] 413.306 [2009 c.595 §1173; repealed by 2015 c.243 §9]
