## At a glance

## Full text

803.053 Expedited titling; vehicle dealers; rules; fee.

(1) At the request of a vehicle dealer, the Department of Transportation shall provide expedited titling services if the vehicle dealer pays the fee imposed under this section. A request under this section must be made in the manner required by the department. The department shall adopt rules establishing criteria and procedures for providing expedited titling services under this section.

(2) The fee for providing an expedited title under this section is $100. [2014 c.14 §2] Note: 803.053 was added to and made a part of the Oregon Vehicle Code by legislative action but was not added to ORS chapter 803 or any series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
