# Conveyance or release of contingent or expectant interests (ORS 107.131)

## At a glance

ORS 107.131 — Conveyance or release of contingent or expectant interests. Chapter 107 (Chapter 107), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

107.131 Conveyance or release of contingent or expectant interests. In addition to the revocation of designation of beneficiary under ORS 107.121, a judgment of dissolution, separation or annulment may require conveyance or release of contingent or expectant interests, including right of survivorship, that are necessary to effectuate a division of assets between the principal and the spouse in the marital dissolution, separation or annulment. [2005 c.285 §6] Note: See note under 107.118.
