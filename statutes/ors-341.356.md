# Election laws applicable (ORS 341.356)

## At a glance

ORS 341.356 — Election laws applicable. Chapter 341 (Chapter 341), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

341.356 Election laws applicable. Except as provided in ORS 255.400 to 255.424:

(1) ORS chapter 255 governs the following: (a) The nomination and election of board members. (b) The conduct of district elections.

(2) The electors of a community college district may exercise the powers of the initiative and referendum regarding a district measure, in accordance with ORS 255.135 to 255.205.

(3) ORS 249.865 to 249.877 govern the recall of board members. [1983 c.350 §206; 2019 c.449 §27]
