# Position numbers for election of directors (ORS 440.345)

## At a glance

ORS 440.345 — Position numbers for election of directors. Chapter 440 (Chapter 440), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

440.345 Position numbers for election of directors.

(1) Each office of director of a health district shall be designated by number as Position No. 1, Position No. 2 and so forth.

(2) The secretary of a district shall assign a position number to each office on the board. The secretary shall certify the number so assigned to the director in office holding that position and shall file one copy of the certification in the records of the elections officer for the district. [Formerly 441.305; 1983 c.350 §259]
