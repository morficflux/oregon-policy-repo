# Definitions for ORS (ORS 353.600)

## At a glance

ORS 353.600 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 353 (Chapter 353), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

353.600 Definitions for ORS 353.600 to 353.612. As used in ORS 353.600 to 353.612:

(1) “Committee” means the Oregon Nursing Shortage Coalition Committee created in ORS 353.606.

(2) “Post-secondary education institution” means: (a) A public university listed in ORS 352.002; (b) A community college operated under ORS chapter 341; (c) A school or division of Oregon Health and Science University; or (d) An Oregon-based, generally accredited, not-for-profit private institution of higher education. [2003 c.697 §2; 2011 c.637 §260]
