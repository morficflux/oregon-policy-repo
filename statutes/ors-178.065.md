# Authority of State Treasurer to require fingerprints (ORS 178.065)

## At a glance

ORS 178.065 — Authority of State Treasurer to require fingerprints. Chapter 178 (State Treasurer), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

178.065 Authority of State Treasurer to require fingerprints. For the purpose of requesting a state or nationwide criminal records check under ORS 181A.195, the State Treasurer may require the fingerprints of a person who:

(1) Is employed or applying for employment by the State Treasurer;

(2) Provides services or seeks to provide services to the State Treasurer as a contractor, vendor or volunteer; or

(3) Has been appointed or is being considered for appointment to a board or commission by the State Treasurer. [Formerly 181.541] Note: 178.065 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 178 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 178.070 [Amended by 1981 c.660 §19; repealed by 1989 c.569 §6] 178.080 [Amended by 1969 c.591 §290; 1971 c.186 §2; renumbered 305.830]
