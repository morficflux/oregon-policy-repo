# Contested case hearing (ORS 537.622)

## At a glance

ORS 537.622 — Contested case hearing. Chapter 537 (Chapter 537), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

537.622 Contested case hearing. A contested case proceeding under ORS 537.621

(8) shall be conducted in accordance with the applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183 and ORS 536.076 and 536.077, except that an interlocutory appeal under ORS 183.480

(3) is not allowed. [1957 c.341 §2; 1983 c.740 §213; 1985 c.673 §56; 1995 c.416 §27; 2003 c.75 §97; 2025 c.575 §17]
