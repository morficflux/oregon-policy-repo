# Conflicts of interest (ORS 723.272)

## At a glance

ORS 723.272 — Conflicts of interest. Chapter 723 (Chapter 723), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

723.272 Conflicts of interest. No director, committee member, officer, agent or employee of the credit union shall in any manner, directly or indirectly, participate in the deliberation upon or the determination of any question affecting pecuniary interest or the pecuniary interest of any corporation, partnership or association (other than the credit union) in which the director, committee member, officer, agent or employee is directly or indirectly interested. [1975 c.652 §29] 723.275 [1959 c.106 §5; repealed by 1975 c.652 §88]
