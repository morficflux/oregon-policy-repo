## Full text

478.240 Special elections; electors.

(1) At any regular meeting, the district board may call a special election.

(2) In any district in which there are no electors registered in the district and the property is used for business, industrial or farming purposes and is nonresidential in character, all owners of property located within the district may vote, and the authorized officer or representative of any corporation owning land in the district may vote for the corporation landowner.
