# Limitation on compliance requirements (ORS 475.382)

## At a glance

ORS 475.382 — Limitation on compliance requirements. Chapter 475 (Chapter 475), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475.382 Limitation on compliance requirements. ORS 475.380 may not be construed to impose on a business that makes retail sales of a finished drug product containing dextromethorphan any compliance requirement other than manually obtaining and verifying proof of age as a condition of sale. For purposes of this section, compliance requirements include any requirement to place a finished drug product in a specific location within the business, any restriction on an individual’s direct access to the finished drug product and any requirement to keep and maintain records of transactions involving the finished drug product. [2017 c.345 §2] Note: See note under 475.380.
