# Disclosure of partnership items to partner (Centralized Partnership Audit Regime) (ORS 314.727)

## At a glance

ORS 314.727 — Disclosure of partnership items to partner (Centralized Partnership Audit Regime). Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.727 Disclosure of partnership items to partner. The Department of Revenue may disclose to a partner of a partnership those items of partnership gain, loss or other particulars relating to the partnership that are necessary to determine or administer the tax imposed by ORS chapter 316, 317 or 318 if the department considers the disclosure necessary to facilitate the audit of the partner’s income or excise tax return. [1997 c.100 §5] 314.730 [1989 c.625 §41; renumbered 314.761 in 2019] (Centralized Partnership Audit Regime)
