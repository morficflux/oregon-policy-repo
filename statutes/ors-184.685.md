# Purpose of department (ORS 184.685)

## At a glance

ORS 184.685 — Purpose of department. Chapter 184 (Certain Executive Branch Departments (incl. DAS)), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

184.685 Purpose of department. The Department of Transportation may conduct statewide coordinating, financing, planning, research and development of public transportation systems in this state to insure the most orderly, efficient and economical development of such systems. [1969 c.599 §57; 1973 c.249 §25; 1977 c.230 §7]
