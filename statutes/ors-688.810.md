# Use of titles (ORS 688.810)

## At a glance

ORS 688.810 — Use of titles. Chapter 688 (Chapter 688), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

688.810 Use of titles.

(1) A respiratory care practitioner may use the title “Licensed Respiratory Care Practitioner” and the abbreviation “LRCP.”

(2) A polysomnographic technologist may use the title “Licensed Polysomnographic Technologist” and the abbreviation “LPSGT.” [1997 c.792 §8; 2011 c.715 §3]
