# Financial report (ORS 344.730)

## At a glance

ORS 344.730 — Financial report. Chapter 344 (Chapter 344), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

344.730 Financial report. All rehabilitation programs which receive state aid under the provisions of ORS 344.710 to 344.730, on or before January 15, shall file with the Department of Human Services a financial report on the preceding year in the form prescribed by the department. [1963 c.506 §4; 1991 c.93 §8; 2025 c.228 §13]
