## Full text

475C.895 Civil penalty for violating ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919.

(1) In addition to any other liability or penalty provided by law, the Oregon Health Authority may impose for each violation of a provision of ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919, or for each violation of a rule adopted under a provision of ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919, a civil penalty that does not exceed $500 for each day that the violation occurs.

(2) The authority shall impose civil penalties under this section in the manner provided by ORS 183.745.

(3) All moneys collected pursuant to this section shall be deposited in the Oregon Health Authority Fund established under ORS 413.101 and are continuously appropriated to the authority for the purpose of carrying out the duties, functions and powers of the authority under ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919. [Formerly 475B.925] (Regulation by Cities and Counties of Cannabis for Medical Use)
