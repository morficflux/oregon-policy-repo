# Enhancement of penalty for violation of ORS (ORS 815.233)

## At a glance

ORS 815.233 — Enhancement of penalty for violation of ORS. Chapter 815 (Chapter 815), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

815.233 Enhancement of penalty for violation of ORS 815.232. A person otherwise convicted of a violation under ORS 815.232

(4) commits a misdemeanor if:

(1) The person has been convicted of three or more violations of ORS 815.232

(1) within 12 months immediately preceding the commission of the offense; and

(2) The prior convictions are admitted by the defendant or alleged in the accusatory pleading. [1991 c.601 §4; 1999 c.1051 §92] Note: See note under 815.232. (Mirrors)
