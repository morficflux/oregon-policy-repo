## Full text

181A.350 Eligibility of towing business to be placed on department list; rules. The Superintendent of State Police may make rules governing the eligibility of towing businesses to be placed and remain on any list of such businesses used by the Department of State Police when it requests towing services on behalf of any person. [Formerly 181.440] PUBLIC SAFETY STANDARDS AND TRAINING (Definitions)
