# Definitions for ORS 619.411 to (ORS 619.411)

## At a glance

ORS 619.411 — Definitions for ORS 619.411 to. Chapter 619 (Chapter 619), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

619.411 Definitions for ORS 619.411 to 619.426. As used in ORS 619.411 to 619.426:

(1) “Commission” means the Oregon Sheep Commission.

(2) “Lamb” means animals defined as lamb by the United States Department of Agriculture grading standards.

(3) “Immediate container” means the box, carton, bag or wrapper or other receptacle used by any person in transporting lamb, or in which lamb is offered for sale. “Immediate container” does not include package liners.

(4) “Label” means the information required by ORS 619.411 to 619.426 to be placed on the lamb or on the immediate container by means of a stamp, stencil or printing by machine or by attaching to the immediate container, by means of glue or paste, a machine-printed label. [1989 c.616 §2]
