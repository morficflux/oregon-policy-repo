# Willful detention of library property PENALTIES (ORS 357.975)

## At a glance

ORS 357.975 — Willful detention of library property PENALTIES. Chapter 357 (Chapter 357), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

357.975 Willful detention of library property. It shall be unlawful for any person willfully or maliciously to detain any library materials belonging to a publicly supported library or privately supported school, academic or research library or incorporated library for 30 days after notice in writing from the librarian of such library, given after the expiration of time which by regulations of such library such materials may be kept. The notice shall bear upon its face a copy of this section and of ORS 357.990. [Formerly 357.830; 1975 c.476 §30] PENALTIES
