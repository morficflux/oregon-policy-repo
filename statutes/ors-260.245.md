## Full text

260.245 Withholding certificate of election or certificate of nomination for failure to file statement. The Secretary of State, county clerk or chief city elections officer may not grant a certificate of election or certificate of nomination to any candidate until the candidate has filed the statements relating to the election that the candidate is required to file under ORS 260.057. [1971 c.749 §22; 1973 c.744 §22; 1977 c.829 §21; 1979 c.190 §362; 1981 c.234 §15; 2003 c.542 §20; 2005 c.809 §43] 260.250 [Amended by 1971 c.749 §41; renumbered 260.442]
