# Confidentiality of information MEDICAL USE OF CANNABIS (Generally) (ORS 475C.758)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.758 — Confidentiality of information MEDICAL USE OF CANNABIS (Generally). Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.758 Confidentiality of information. Information received by a financial institution under ORS 475C.746, 475C.750 or 475C.754 is confidential for purposes of ORS 717.200 to 717.320, 717.900 and 717.905, the Bank Act and ORS chapter 723. Except as otherwise required or permitted by the provisions of ORS 192.583 to 192.607, 717.200 to 717.320, 717.900 and 717.905, or the Bank Act or ORS chapter 723, or by other state law or rule or federal law or regulation, a financial institution may not make the information available to any person other than:

(1) The customer to whom the information applies; and

(2) A trustee, conservator, guardian, personal representative or agent of the customer to whom the information applies. [Formerly 475B.778] MEDICAL USE OF CANNABIS (Generally)
