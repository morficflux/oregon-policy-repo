# Disincorporation of cities; effective date (ORS 221.610)

## At a glance

ORS 221.610 — Disincorporation of cities; effective date. Chapter 221 (Organization and Government of Cities), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

221.610 Disincorporation of cities; effective date. Any city not liable for any debt or other obligation, may surrender its charter, disincorporate and cease to exist if a majority of the electors of the city authorize the surrender and disincorporation as provided in ORS 221.621 and 221.650. The surrender and disincorporation shall become effective 60 days after the city has authorized surrender and disincorporation. [Amended by 1983 c.350 §25] 221.620 [Repealed by 1983 c.350 §26 (221.621 enacted in lieu of 221.620, 221.630, 221.640 and 221.660)]
