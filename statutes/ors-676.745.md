# Discipline (ORS 676.745)

## At a glance

ORS 676.745 — Discipline. Chapter 676 (Chapter 676), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

676.745 Discipline. In the manner described in ORS chapter 183 for contested cases, the Health Licensing Office may impose a form of discipline listed in ORS 676.612 against a person for any violation of ORS 676.736 and for any violation of a rule adopted under ORS 676.730 to 676.748. [2021 c.231 §6]
