# Soccer referees considered independent contractors (ORS 314.015)

## At a glance

## Full text

314.015 Soccer referees considered independent contractors. Notwithstanding ORS 670.600, for purposes of ORS chapter 316, a person serving as a referee or assistant referee in a youth or adult recreational soccer match shall be considered to be an independent contractor.
