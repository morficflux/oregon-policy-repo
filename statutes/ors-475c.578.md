# Civil penalty for violating ORS (ORS 475C.578)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.578 — Civil penalty for violating ORS. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.578 Civil penalty for violating ORS 475C.540 to 475C.586.

(1) In addition to any other liability or penalty provided by law, the Oregon Health Authority may impose for each violation of a provision of ORS 475C.540 to 475C.586, or a rule adopted under a provision of ORS 475C.540 to 475C.586, a civil penalty that does not exceed $500 for each day that the violation occurs.

(2) The authority shall impose civil penalties under this section in the manner provided by ORS 183.745.

(3) Moneys collected under this section shall be deposited in the Oregon Health Authority Fund established under ORS 413.101 and are continuously appropriated to the authority for the purpose of carrying out the duties, functions and powers of the authority under ORS 475C.540 to 475C.586. [Formerly 475B.585]
