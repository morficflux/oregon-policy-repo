# Tax credit limit for biennium HEALTH (ORS 315.603)

## At a glance

ORS 315.603 — Tax credit limit for biennium HEALTH. Chapter 315 (Chapter 315), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

315.603 Tax credit limit for biennium. The total amount of potential tax credits allowed under ORS 315.593 at the time of preliminary certification under ORS 315.595 may not exceed $4 million for any biennium.
