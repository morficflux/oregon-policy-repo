# Applicability of ORS (ORS 164.867)

## At a glance

ORS 164.867 — Applicability of ORS. Chapter 164 (Chapter 164), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

164.867 Applicability of ORS 164.868, 164.869 and 164.872. The provisions of ORS 164.868, 164.869 and 164.872 apply only to persons operating commercial enterprises. [1993 c.95 §2] Note: See note under 164.864.
