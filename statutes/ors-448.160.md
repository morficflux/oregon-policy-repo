## At a glance

ORS 448.160 — Emergency plans. Chapter 448 (Chapter 448), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

448.160 Emergency plans.

(1) The Oregon Health Authority shall maintain a plan outlining actions to be taken by the authority during emergencies relating to water systems.

(2) The authority may require that a water supplier compile an emergency plan if it appears necessary to the Director of the Oregon Health Authority. [1981 c.749 §12; 2009 c.595 §847]
