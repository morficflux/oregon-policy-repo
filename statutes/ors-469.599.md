# Public Utility Commission’s duty (ORS 469.599)

## At a glance

ORS 469.599 — Public Utility Commission’s duty. Chapter 469 (Chapter 469), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469.599 Public Utility Commission’s duty. The Public Utility Commission shall not authorize the issuance of stocks, bonds or other evidences of indebtedness to finance any nuclear-fueled thermal power plant pursuant to ORS 757.400 to 757.463 until the Energy Facility Siting Council has made the finding required under ORS 469.595. [1981 c.1 §6]
