# Persons insurable under ORS (ORS 407.475)

## At a glance

ORS 407.475 — Persons insurable under ORS. Chapter 407 (Chapter 407), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

407.475 Persons insurable under ORS 407.465. Any person receiving or assuming a loan or making a contract under this chapter for the acquisition of a home or farm, and the spouse or former spouse of that person, may be insured under the insurance provided for by ORS 407.465 upon complying with such rules relating to that insurance as the director may adopt. [Formerly 407.076; 1985 c.221 §2; 1987 c.132 §2]
