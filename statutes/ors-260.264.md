## At a glance

## Full text

260.264 Education and assistance regarding campaign finance laws.

(1) The Secretary of State shall hire a full-time employee to provide voter education, support and outreach regarding Oregon’s campaign finance laws, including the laws regarding political contributions set forth in ORS 260.006 to 260.021 and 260.416.

(2) The Secretary of State shall provide technical assistance to candidates, campaigns and the public to ensure a smooth transition to the newly enacted contribution limit requirements set forth in ORS 260.006 to 260.021 and 260.416. [2024 c.9 §21] Note: 260.264 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 260 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 260.265 [1995 c.1 §9; repealed by 1999 c.999 §59] (Disclosure of Source of Communication or Donation)
