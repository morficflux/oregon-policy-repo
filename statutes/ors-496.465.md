# Interference with project prohibited ADOPTION OF PLANS FOR NATURAL PRODUCTION OF ANADROMOUS FISH RUNS (ORS 496.465)

## At a glance

ORS 496.465 — Interference with project prohibited ADOPTION OF PLANS FOR NATURAL PRODUCTION OF ANADROMOUS FISH RUNS. Chapter 496 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Wildlife Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

496.465 Interference with project prohibited. Except for activities or projects authorized by a unit of municipal, state or federal government, no person shall disturb, damage, destroy or interfere with the operation of a salmon and trout enhancement project referred to in ORS 496.450.
