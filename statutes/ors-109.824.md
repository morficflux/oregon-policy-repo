# Role of law enforcement officer (ORS 109.824)

## At a glance

ORS 109.824 — Role of law enforcement officer. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.824 Role of law enforcement officer. At the request of a district attorney acting under ORS 109.821, a law enforcement officer may take any lawful action reasonably necessary to locate a child or a party and assist a district attorney with responsibilities under ORS 109.821. [1999 c.649 §38] Note: See note under 109.701.
