# Authorization to request federal waivers (ORS 413.071)

## At a glance

ORS 413.071 — Authorization to request federal waivers. Chapter 413 (Chapter 413), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

413.071 Authorization to request federal waivers. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, federal laws shall govern the administration of federally granted funds. The Director of the Oregon Health Authority may request a waiver of any federal law in order to fully implement provisions of state law using federally granted funds. [2011 c.720 §36] Note: 413.071 and 413.072 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 413 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
