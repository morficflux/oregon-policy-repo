# Assignment of certificates of sale; deed to assignee (ORS 273.295)

## At a glance

ORS 273.295 — Assignment of certificates of sale; deed to assignee. Chapter 273 (Chapter 273), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

273.295 Assignment of certificates of sale; deed to assignee. All assignments of certificates of sale shall be executed and acknowledged in the same manner as a deed to real estate. The assignee, upon full payment of the amount due on the purchase price, and delivery to the Department of State Lands of such certificate and assignment, shall receive a deed for the land described in such certificate, in the name of the assignee. [Formerly 273.140]
