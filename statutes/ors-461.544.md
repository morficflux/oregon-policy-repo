# Use of proceeds of video lottery games (ORS 461.544)

## At a glance

ORS 461.544 — Use of proceeds of video lottery games. Chapter 461 (Chapter 461), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

461.544 Use of proceeds of video lottery games. All net proceeds from video lottery games shall be allocated to the Administrative Services Economic Development Fund. [1991 c.461 §§1,78; 1993 c.18 §118; 1995 c.814 §9] Note: 461.544 to 461.549, 461.555, 461.725 and 461.740 were enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but were not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 461 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 461.545 [1989 c.828 §7; repealed by 1991 c.461 §79 and 1991 c.962 §15] 461.546 [1991 c.962 §2a; 1993 c.18 §119; repealed by 1995 c.814 §4]
