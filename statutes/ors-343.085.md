# Tuition prohibited (Procedures) (ORS 343.085)

## At a glance

ORS 343.085 — Tuition prohibited (Procedures). Chapter 343 (Chapter 343), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

343.085 Tuition prohibited. No tuition shall be charged to any resident student participating in any special program authorized by this chapter.
