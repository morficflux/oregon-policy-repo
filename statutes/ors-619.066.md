## At a glance

## Full text

619.066 Labeling of meat products to conform to ORS chapters 616 and 618. The labeling requirements for meat or meat products shall be those prescribed in ORS chapter 616 and the labeling requirements as to weight or measure of meat or meat products shall be those prescribed in ORS chapter 618. [1973 c.174 §11; 1975 c.304 §18] 619.070 [Repealed by 1957 c.104 §21]
