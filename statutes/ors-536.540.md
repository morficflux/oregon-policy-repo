# Approval of voucher claims (ORS 536.540)

## At a glance

ORS 536.540 — Approval of voucher claims. Chapter 536 (Water Resources Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

536.540 Approval of voucher claims. All voucher claims for indebtedness or expenses authorized and incurred by the Water Resources Commission in carrying out its functions as provided by law shall be approved by the commission or as provided in ORS 293.330.
