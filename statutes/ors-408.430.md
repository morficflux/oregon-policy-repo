# Free services to veterans rendered by court administrator or county clerk MISCELLANEOUS VETERANS’ BENEFITS (ORS 408.430)

## At a glance

ORS 408.430 — Free services to veterans rendered by court administrator or county clerk MISCELLANEOUS VETERANS’ BENEFITS. Chapter 408 (Chapter 408), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

408.430 Free services to veterans rendered by court administrator or county clerk. The county clerk or a court clerk or court administrator shall administer all oaths required in matters of pensions, certify pension vouchers, affix the seal of the court whenever required, and make and prepare copies of any documents of record in the office and certify thereto any matter required by veterans seeking benefits from any agency or department of the State of Oregon or federal government, without any charge, fee or compensation. [Amended by 1993 c.223 §9] MISCELLANEOUS VETERANS’ BENEFITS
