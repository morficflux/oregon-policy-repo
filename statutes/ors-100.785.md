## Full text

100.785 Blanket encumbrance prohibited.

(1) Subject to the provisions of ORS 100.720, a condominium unit may not be conveyed by a developer subject to a blanket encumbrance.

(2) Notwithstanding subsection

(1) of this section, the developer shall conform to an alternative requirement or method which the Real Estate Commissioner may deem acceptable to afford a purchaser the protection provided by the prohibition in subsection

(1) of this section. [Formerly 94.465; 2001 c.756 §56] ENFORCEMENT
