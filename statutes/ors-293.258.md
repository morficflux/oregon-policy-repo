# Delinquent Accounts Administration Fund (ORS 293.258)

## At a glance

ORS 293.258 — Delinquent Accounts Administration Fund. Chapter 293 (Administration of Public Funds), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

293.258 Delinquent Accounts Administration Fund.

(1) The Delinquent Accounts Administration Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the Delinquent Accounts Administration Fund shall be credited to the fund. Moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Department of Administrative Services for purposes of administering ORS 293.226 and 293.252 to 293.256.

(2) The fund shall consist of: (a) Moneys deposited in the fund pursuant to ORS 293.256; (b) Amounts donated to the fund; (c) Amounts appropriated or otherwise transferred to the fund by the Legislative Assembly; and (d) Other amounts deposited in the fund from any source. [2015 c.766 §9] Note: See note under 293.252.
