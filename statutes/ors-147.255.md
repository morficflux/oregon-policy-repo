# Recovery of moneys paid on fraudulent claims; recovery of fees (ORS 147.255)

## At a glance

ORS 147.255 — Recovery of moneys paid on fraudulent claims; recovery of fees. Chapter 147 (Chapter 147), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

147.255 Recovery of moneys paid on fraudulent claims; recovery of fees. The Department of Justice may institute suit:

(1) To recover any awards made because of fraudulent claims.

(2) On behalf of the applicant or recipients, to recover all fees paid to a counsel or agent in violation of ORS 147.315.
