# Contracts to provide fiscal data LEGISLATIVE COUNSEL COMMITTEE (ORS 173.055)

## At a glance

ORS 173.055 — Contracts to provide fiscal data LEGISLATIVE COUNSEL COMMITTEE. Chapter 173 (Chapter 173), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

173.055 Contracts to provide fiscal data. The Legislative Fiscal Officer and Legislative Revenue Officer are authorized to contract with other agencies or persons to provide fiscal data necessary to carry out the provisions of ORS 173.025 to 173.055.
