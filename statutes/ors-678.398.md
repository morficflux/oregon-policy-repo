## At a glance

ORS 678.398 — Supervision of nurse intern; rules. Chapter 678 (Chapter 678), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

678.398 Supervision of nurse intern; rules.

(1) A registered nurse who is responsible for the supervision of a nurse intern: (a) Is accountable at all times for the safety and well-being of patients to whom the nurse intern provides care; (b) Shall maintain an awareness of the nursing tasks and functions performed by the nurse intern; (c) Shall be immediately available to the nurse intern through methods determined by the Oregon State Board of Nursing; and (d) Shall have knowledge of the tasks and functions that the nurse intern may perform.

(2) The board may adopt rules to carry out this section. [2022 c.38 §5; 2025 c.124 §18]
