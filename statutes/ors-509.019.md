# Consumption of catch at sea lawful without payment of fee (ORS 509.019)

## At a glance

ORS 509.019 — Consumption of catch at sea lawful without payment of fee. Chapter 509 (Chapter 509), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

509.019 Consumption of catch at sea lawful without payment of fee. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, an individual lawfully engaged in commercial fishing, while at sea, may consume a portion of the lawful commercial catch, without payment of the fees required under ORS 508.505. [1987 c.178 §2; 1991 c.701 §18] 509.020 [Repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
