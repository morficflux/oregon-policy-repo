## At a glance

## Full text

743.008 Reporting requirements; rules. The Department of Consumer and Business Services shall develop by rule reporting requirements as necessary for the consistent and efficient implementation of ORS 743B.200, 743B.202, 743B.250 and 743B.423. In order to minimize duplicative reporting requirements, the department shall accept copies of reports prepared for national accreditation organizations as sufficient to meet the reporting requirements developed pursuant to this section to the extent that the reports include the information required by the department pursuant to this section. [Formerly 743.819] Note: Definitions for 743.008 may be found in 743B.001. 743.009 [1967 c.359 §337; 1969 c.336 §11; 1973 c.608 §1; renumbered 742.005 in 1989] (Filing and Approval of Rates, Schedules and Forms)
