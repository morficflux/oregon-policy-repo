# Illegal conduct by State Treasury not a defense (ORS 162.118)

## At a glance

ORS 162.118 — Illegal conduct by State Treasury not a defense. Chapter 162 (Chapter 162), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

162.118 Illegal conduct by State Treasury not a defense. Illegal conduct by the State Treasury or any of its employees or agents shall not be a defense for any person charged with the crime of public investment fraud or to any person against whom any civil action is brought under ORS 30.862 and 162.117 to 162.121. [1993 c.768 §2] Note: See note under 162.117.
