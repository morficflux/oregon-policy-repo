# Mode of review; abolition of writs of error and certiorari (ORS 138.010)

## At a glance

ORS 138.010 — Mode of review; abolition of writs of error and certiorari. Chapter 138 (Chapter 138), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

138.010 Mode of review; abolition of writs of error and certiorari. Writs of error and of certiorari in criminal actions are abolished. The only mode of reviewing a judgment or order in a criminal action is that prescribed by ORS 138.010 to 138.310. 138.012 [1999 c.1055 §5; 2001 c.306 §2; renumbered 138.052 in 2017]
