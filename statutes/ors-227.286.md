# Regulations applicable to public property (ORS 227.286)

## At a glance

ORS 227.286 — Regulations applicable to public property. Chapter 227 (Chapter 227), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

227.286 Regulations applicable to public property. City ordinances regulating the location, construction, maintenance, repair, alteration, use and occupancy of land and buildings and other structures shall apply to publicly owned property, except as the ordinances prescribe to the contrary. [1969 c.460 §3 (enacted in lieu of 227.285); 1975 c.767 §12]
