# Breast-feeding in public place (ORS 109.001)

## At a glance

ORS 109.001 — Breast-feeding in public place. Chapter 109 (Chapter 109), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

109.001 Breast-feeding in public place. A woman may breast-feed her child in a public place. [1999 c.306 §1] Note: 109.001 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 109 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
