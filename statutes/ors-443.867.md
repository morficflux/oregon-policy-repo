# In-home care agency license not required for licensed hospice program to provide palliative care (ORS 443.867)

## At a glance

ORS 443.867 — In-home care agency license not required for licensed hospice program to provide palliative care. Chapter 443 (Chapter 443), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

443.867 In-home care agency license not required for licensed hospice program to provide palliative care. Notwithstanding ORS 443.305 to 443.350 requiring an in-home care agency to obtain a license to provide services that are necessary to assist an individual in meeting the individual’s daily needs but are not curative or rehabilitative, a hospice program licensed under ORS 443.860 is not required to be licensed as an in-home care agency under ORS 443.315 to provide palliative care, as defined by the Oregon Health Authority by rule. [2019 c.238 §2]
