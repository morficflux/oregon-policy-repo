# Applicability of ORS (ORS 475C.910)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.910 — Applicability of ORS. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.910 Applicability of ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919 to criminal causes of action. The provisions of ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919 do not protect a person from a criminal cause of action based on possession, delivery or manufacture of marijuana that is not described in ORS 475C.770 to 475C.919. [Formerly 475B.943]
