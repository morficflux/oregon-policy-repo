# Sale of real property; notice of loan repayment charge required (Loan Offset Grants) (ORS 470.695)

## At a glance

ORS 470.695 — Sale of real property; notice of loan repayment charge required (Loan Offset Grants). Chapter 470 (Chapter 470), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

470.695 Sale of real property; notice of loan repayment charge required. A person entering into an agreement to sell, rent, lease or otherwise confer a right in the person’s real property that is benefited by an energy efficiency and sustainable technology loan for which a loan repayment charge or other repayment obligation applies or for which a fixture filing, lien or other form of security exists shall, prior to any party signing the agreement, give notice of the loan repayment charge, repayment obligation, filing, lien or other security affecting the property to the other parties to the agreement. [2009 c.753 §39] (Loan Offset Grants)
