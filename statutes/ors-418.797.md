## Full text

418.797 Biennial certified expense and funding reports to Department of Justice.

(1) The director of a children’s advocacy center shall submit to the Department of Justice, by no later than April 1 of each even-numbered year, projected costs of the center for the next biennium, along with estimates for all sources of funding other than the state, including but not limited to insurance reimbursements, court witness fees, private donations and governmental grants.

(2) Each director of a children’s advocacy center submitting information under subsection

(1) of this section shall certify that the submitted information is true and correct and that the accuracy of all numerical information has been verified. [2024 c.86 §3] Note: See note under 418.746.
