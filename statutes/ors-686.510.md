## Full text

686.510 Euthanasia Task Force; fees; use of fees. The Oregon State Veterinary Medical Examining Board may establish a certified Euthanasia Task Force and may assess application and certification fees against certified euthanasia technicians and their employers. The fees so assessed are continuously appropriated to the board to support activities of the task force. [1987 c.651 §10] VETERINARY FACILITIES
