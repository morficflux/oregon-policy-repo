# Limitation on qualification for discount (Uninsured Motorist Coverage) (ORS 742.496)

## At a glance

ORS 742.496 — Limitation on qualification for discount (Uninsured Motorist Coverage). Chapter 742 (Chapter 742), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

742.496 Limitation on qualification for discount. No person shall receive a discount under ORS 742.490 to 742.494 if the person takes the approved course as a punishment, specified by a court or other government entity, for a moving traffic violation. [1989 c.379 §6] (Uninsured Motorist Coverage)
