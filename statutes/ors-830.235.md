# Carburetors (ORS 830.235)

## At a glance

ORS 830.235 — Carburetors. Chapter 830 (Small Watercraft _______________ 2025 EDITION SMALL WATERCRAFT SMALL WATERCRAFT GENERAL PROVISIONS 830.005 Definitions for chapter 830.015 Application of ORS 83), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

830.235 Carburetors. Carburetors on all engines of motorboats, other than outboard motors, shall be fitted with a U. S. Coast Guard approved device for arresting backfire. [Formerly 488.071]
