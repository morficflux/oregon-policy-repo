## At a glance

## Full text

162.165 Escape in the first degree.

(1) A person commits the crime of escape in the first degree if: (a) Aided by another person actually present, the person uses or threatens to use physical force in escaping from custody or a correctional facility; or (b) The person uses or threatens to use a dangerous or deadly weapon escaping from custody or a correctional facility.

(2) Escape in the first degree is a Class B felony. [1971 c.743 §192]
