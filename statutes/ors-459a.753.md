# Certification and withdrawal procedures (ORS 459A.753)

## At a glance

ORS 459A.753 — Certification and withdrawal procedures. Chapter 459A (Chapter 459A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

459A.753 Certification and withdrawal procedures. The procedures for certification or withdrawal provided for in ORS 459A.725 to 459A.735 shall be in accordance with ORS chapter 183. [Formerly 459A.743]
