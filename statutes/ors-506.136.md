# Commission to study and classify food fish and fishing gear (ORS 506.136)

## At a glance

ORS 506.136 — Commission to study and classify food fish and fishing gear. Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.136 Commission to study and classify food fish and fishing gear. The State Fish and Wildlife Commission shall:

(1) Investigate the habits, supply and economic uses of, and classify all food fish.

(2) Classify all fishing gear and such classification shall be final. [1965 c.570 §21] 506.140 [Repealed by 1965 c.570 §152] 506.141 [1965 c.570 §22; repealed by 1975 c.253 §40]
