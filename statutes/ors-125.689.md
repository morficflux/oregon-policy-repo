# Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator Fund (ORS 125.689)

## At a glance

ORS 125.689 — Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator Fund. Chapter 125 (Chapter 125), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

125.689 Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator Fund.

(1) The Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator Fund is established in the State Treasury, separate and distinct from the General Fund. Interest earned by the Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator Fund shall be credited to the fund. The fund consists of: (a) Moneys appropriated or otherwise transferred to the fund by the Legislative Assembly; (b) Moneys received from federal, state or local sources; (c) Amounts donated to the fund; (d) Investment earnings received on moneys in the fund; and (e) Other amounts deposited in the fund from any source.

(2) Moneys in the fund are continuously appropriated to the Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator for the purposes of ORS 125.675 to 125.691.

(3) Moneys in the fund may be invested and reinvested as provided in ORS 293.701 to 293.857.

(4) Moneys in the fund may be used by the Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator for administrative costs and expenses of the Oregon Public Guardian and Conservator in performing the duties and functions under ORS 125.675 to 125.691. [2017 c.310 §7]
