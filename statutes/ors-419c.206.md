# Compensation for counsel (ORS 419C.206)

## At a glance

ORS 419C.206 — Compensation for counsel. Chapter 419C (Chapter 419C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419C.206 Compensation for counsel. When the court appoints counsel for a youth, the compensation for counsel and reasonable fees and expenses of investigation, preparation and presentation paid or incurred shall be determined and paid as provided in ORS 135.055. [1993 c.33 §184; 2001 c.962 §51; 2003 c.449 §31; 2021 c.597 §14]
