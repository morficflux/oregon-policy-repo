## Full text

757.264 Annual forecast of certain projected production tax credits required; inclusion in rates. Each public utility that makes sales of electricity shall forecast on an annual basis the projected state and federal production tax credits received by the public utility due to variable renewable electricity production, and the Public Utility Commission shall allow those forecasts to be included in rates through any variable power cost forecasting process established by the commission. [2016 c.28 §18b] 757.265 [Repealed by 1971 c.655 §250]
