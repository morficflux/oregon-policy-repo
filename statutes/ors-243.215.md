# Certain eligible employees permitted to receive employer contributions for health benefit plans of their choice; rules (Miscellaneous) (ORS 243.215)

## At a glance

ORS 243.215 — Certain eligible employees permitted to receive employer contributions for health benefit plans of their choice; rules (Miscellaneous). Chapter 243 (Chapter 243), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

243.215 Certain eligible employees permitted to receive employer contributions for health benefit plans of their choice; rules. Any eligible employee unable to participate in one or more of the plans described in ORS 243.135

(1) solely because the employee is assigned to perform duties outside the state may be eligible to receive the monthly state or local government contribution, less administrative expenses, as payment of all or part of the cost of a health benefit plan of choice, subject to the approval of the Public Employees’ Benefit Board and such rules as the board may adopt. [1971 c.527 §13; 2013 c.731 §10] 243.220 [1977 c.675 §1; renumbered 243.345] (Miscellaneous)
