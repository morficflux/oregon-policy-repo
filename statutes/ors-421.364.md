# Provision of legal services to Oregon Corrections Enterprises (ORS 421.364)

## At a glance

ORS 421.364 — Provision of legal services to Oregon Corrections Enterprises. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.364 Provision of legal services to Oregon Corrections Enterprises. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Attorney General, at the request of Oregon Corrections Enterprises, shall identify one or more assistant attorneys general to provide legal services related to the adult in custody work programs of Oregon Corrections Enterprises. At least one assistant attorney general shall have an office located at the main office of Oregon Corrections Enterprises as a primary office location. [1999 c.955 §14; 2019 c.213 §83] 421.365 [Repealed by 1981 c.380 §4]
