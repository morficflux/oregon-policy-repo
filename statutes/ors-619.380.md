# Enjoining violations LABELING OF LAMB (ORS 619.380)

## At a glance

ORS 619.380 — Enjoining violations LABELING OF LAMB. Chapter 619 (Chapter 619), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

619.380 Enjoining violations. In addition to other penalties and enforcement provisions contained in ORS 619.350 to 619.380, circuit courts may, upon petition of the State Department of Agriculture, enjoin by temporary or permanent injunction any violation of the provisions of ORS 619.350 to 619.380. [1959 c.385 §8; 2009 c.367 §5] 619.410 [1957 c.104 §1; repealed by 1973 c.174 §20] LABELING OF LAMB
