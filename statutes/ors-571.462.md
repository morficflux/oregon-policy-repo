## Full text

571.462 Penalty for delayed payment of assessment.

(1) In addition to the penalties prescribed in ORS 571.501, a person who delays transmittal of funds beyond the time set by the Oregon Hemp Commission shall pay a penalty of 10 percent of the amount due and one and one-half percent interest per month on the unpaid balance of the assessment.

(2) The commission may waive the penalty and interest described in subsection

(1) of this section upon a showing of good cause, as determined by the commission by rule.

(3) Notwithstanding subsection

(1) of this section, if an assessment is collected pursuant to a federal marketing order or agreement, the commission may establish a penalty or interest rate that is consistent with the federal marketing order or agreement. [2021 c.216 §23] Note: See note under 571.400.
