# Emergency preparedness functions (ORS 401.114)

## At a glance

ORS 401.114 — Emergency preparedness functions. Chapter 401 (Chapter 401), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

401.114 Emergency preparedness functions. The Oregon Department of Emergency Management shall carry out the following functions:

(1) Coordinate emergency management functions on a regional basis within this state;

(2) Coordinate training related to emergency response on a regional basis within this state; and

(3) Develop and carry out emergency preparedness exercises on a statewide basis. [2021 c.539 §25a] Note: 401.114 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 401 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 401.115 [Formerly 401.530; renumbered 401.175 in 2009]
