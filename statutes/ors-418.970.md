# and (ORS 418.970)

## At a glance

ORS 418.970 — and. Chapter 418 (Chapter 418), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

418.970 ORS 418.950 to 418.970 inapplicable to existing facilities. The provisions of ORS 418.950 to 418.970 do not apply to child-caring facilities in existence and operating on October 3, 1979. [1979 c.597 §6] 418.975 [2009 c.540 §1; 2011 c.720 §158; 2012 c.37 §58; 2013 c.623 §16; 2013 c.624 §33; repealed by 2019 c.616 §7] SYSTEMS OF CARE
