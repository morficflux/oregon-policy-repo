# Response to notices from local governments (ORS 196.676)

## At a glance

ORS 196.676 — Response to notices from local governments. Chapter 196 (Chapter 196), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

196.676 Response to notices from local governments. The Department of State Lands shall respond to the notice received from local governments pursuant to ORS 215.418

(1) and 227.350

(1) within 30 days of receipt of the notice. The response shall state whether a permit is or in the future will be required or whether a permit has been issued by the department for the activity which is subject to notice. [1989 c.837 §7]
