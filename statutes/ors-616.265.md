# When advertisement deemed false (ORS 616.265)

## At a glance

ORS 616.265 — When advertisement deemed false. Chapter 616 (General and Miscellaneous Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

616.265 When advertisement deemed false. An advertisement of a food shall be deemed to be false if it is false or misleading in any particular.
