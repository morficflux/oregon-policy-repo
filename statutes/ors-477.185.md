# Use of local fire protection resources (ORS 477.185)

## At a glance

ORS 477.185 — Use of local fire protection resources. Chapter 477 (Chapter 477), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

477.185 Use of local fire protection resources. The Governor shall make reasonable efforts to use local available fire protection resources within Oregon before calling on forces from other members of the Northwest Wildland Fire Protection Agreement.
