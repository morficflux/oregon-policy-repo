# Jurisdiction and authority to enforce commercial fishing laws (ORS 506.501)

## At a glance

ORS 506.501 — Jurisdiction and authority to enforce commercial fishing laws. Chapter 506 (Application, Administration and Enforcement of Commercial Fishing Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

506.501 Jurisdiction and authority to enforce commercial fishing laws. The State Fish and Wildlife Commission has jurisdiction and authority to enforce the commercial fishing laws, except as provided in ORS 506.506 to 506.516. [1965 c.570 §34] 506.505 [Repealed by 1965 c.570 §152]
