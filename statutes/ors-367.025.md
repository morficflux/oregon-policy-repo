## Full text

367.025 Issuance of infrastructure bonds.

(1) If the Department of Transportation determines that it is necessary or desirable to issue infrastructure bonds to provide moneys for the Oregon Transportation Infrastructure Fund, the department shall ask the State Treasurer to issue infrastructure bonds.

(2) Infrastructure bonds shall be issued as provided in ORS chapter 286A. [1997 c.679 §5; 2001 c.536 §9; 2003 c.794 §266; 2007 c.783 §163]
