# Election from zones (ORS 332.126)

## At a glance

ORS 332.126 — Election from zones. Chapter 332 (Chapter 332), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

332.126 Election from zones. Except as provided in ORS 255.400 to 255.424:

(1) If a majority of the district school board of a zoned common school district or a zoned union high school district so decides, the board may provide that directors of the district school board who are nominated from zones also shall be elected from the zones from which they are nominated.

(2) At any time after a district school board decides that directors shall be elected by zone: (a) The district school board may rescind the decision and provide that the directors who are nominated by zone shall be elected at large. (b) Zones may be abolished pursuant to ORS 332.128. [1983 c.284 §3a; 1993 c.45 §44; 2019 c.449 §13]
