# Commission for the Blind Account (ORS 346.290)

## At a glance

ORS 346.290 — Commission for the Blind Account. Chapter 346 (Chapter 346), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

346.290 Commission for the Blind Account.

(1) There is established in the General Fund of the State Treasury an account to be known as the Commission for the Blind Account. Except for funds made available to the Commission for the Blind under ORS 346.270 or deposited pursuant to ORS 346.569, all moneys received by the commission for promoting the welfare of persons with visual impairments shall be paid into the State Treasury and credited to the Commission for the Blind Account.

(2) All moneys in the Commission for the Blind Account are continuously appropriated to the commission for use by the commission for the respective purposes authorized by law.

(3) The commission shall keep a record of all moneys deposited in the Commission for the Blind Account. The record shall indicate by separate cumulative accounts the source from which the moneys are derived and the individual activity or program against which each withdrawal is charged.
