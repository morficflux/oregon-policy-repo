# Work camps ALTERNATIVE INCARCERATION PROGRAM (ORS 421.490)

## At a glance

ORS 421.490 — Work camps ALTERNATIVE INCARCERATION PROGRAM. Chapter 421 (Chapter 421), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

421.490 Work camps. In addition to camps established under ORS 421.455 to 421.480 the Department of Corrections may execute agreements for the establishment and operation of work camps for adults in custody in minimum custody of Department of Corrections institutions in cooperation with all public agencies. [1963 c.157 §2; 1987 c.320 §208; 2019 c.213 §141] ALTERNATIVE INCARCERATION PROGRAM
