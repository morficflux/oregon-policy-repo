# Treatment of moneys collected under ORS (ORS 314.469)

## At a glance

ORS 314.469 — Treatment of moneys collected under ORS. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.469 Treatment of moneys collected under ORS 314.406. Moneys collected under ORS 314.406 shall be considered net revenue from the tax imposed under ORS chapter 316 for purposes of ORS 316.502. [2007 c.568 §13] 314.470 [1957 c.632 §26 (enacted in lieu of 316.675 and 317.480); repealed by 1961 c.20 §1] ESTIMATED TAX PROCEDURE
