# Regulation of mileage records; distinguishing marks (Rate Regulation) (ORS 825.212)

## At a glance

ORS 825.212 — Regulation of mileage records; distinguishing marks (Rate Regulation). Chapter 825 (Chapter 825), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

825.212 Regulation of mileage records; distinguishing marks. The Department of Transportation may:

(1) Prescribe such methods and means as the department determines to be necessary for checking, verifying and ascertaining the number of miles traveled by each motor vehicle operated by for-hire carrier and private carrier and insure that the mileage charged for is computed on basis of extreme mileage traveled.

(2) Prescribe distinguishing marks, such as signs, colors, lights, tags and plates as may be convenient or necessary for distinguishing classes of carriers or for protective or regulatory purposes; but not inconsistent with the Oregon Vehicle Code. [Formerly 767.895] (Rate Regulation)
