## Full text

450.640 Powers of joint authority; issuance of revenue bonds.

(1) Except as otherwise provided in this section, a joint water and sanitary authority possesses all the duties, functions and powers granted to water authorities and to sanitary authorities under ORS 450.600 to 450.989.

(2) Revenue bonds issued by a joint water and sanitary authority shall be issued as prescribed in ORS chapter 287A. [1993 c.577 §10; 2007 c.783 §186]
