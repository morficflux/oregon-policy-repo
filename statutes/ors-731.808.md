# “Gross amount of premiums” defined (ORS 731.808)

## At a glance

ORS 731.808 — “Gross amount of premiums” defined. Chapter 731 (Administration and General Provisions), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

731.808 “Gross amount of premiums” defined. As used in ORS 731.804, 731.812, 731.820, 731.830 and 731.834, “gross amount of premiums” means the consideration paid by insureds to an insurer for policies of insurance, and includes all premiums, assessments, dues and fees received or derived, or obligations taken therefor, by whatever term known. [1967 c.359 §132; 1989 c.413 §5; 2019 c.493 §4]
