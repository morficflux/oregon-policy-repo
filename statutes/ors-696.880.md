# Licensee not required to disclose proximity of registered sex offender DUTIES OF REAL ESTATE PROPERTY MANAGERS (ORS 696.880)

## At a glance

ORS 696.880 — Licensee not required to disclose proximity of registered sex offender DUTIES OF REAL ESTATE PROPERTY MANAGERS. Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.880 Licensee not required to disclose proximity of registered sex offender. Nothing in ORS 163A.005 to 163A.235, 696.301, 696.805, 696.810, 696.815 or 696.855 creates an obligation on the part of a person licensed under this chapter to disclose to a potential purchaser of residential property that a sex offender registered under ORS 163A.010, 163A.015, 163A.020 or 163A.025 resides in the area. [1999 c.732 §2; 2001 c.300 §73; 2011 c.271 §24; 2013 c.708 §28] Note: 696.880 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 696 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. DUTIES OF REAL ESTATE PROPERTY MANAGERS
