# Authority of Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission to inspect (ORS 475C.628)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.628 — Authority of Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission to inspect. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.628 Authority of Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission to inspect. To ensure compliance with ORS 475C.600 to 475C.648 and any rule adopted under ORS 475C.600 to 475C.648, the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission may inspect the premises of a person that holds a license under ORS 475C.065, 475C.085, 475C.093 or 475C.097. [Formerly 475B.635]
