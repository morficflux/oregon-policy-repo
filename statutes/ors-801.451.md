# “Roundabout” (ORS 801.451)

## At a glance

ORS 801.451 — “Roundabout”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.451 “Roundabout.” “Roundabout” means an intersection characterized by a circulatory roadway, channelized approaches and yield control of entering traffic. A roundabout encompasses the area bounded by the outermost curb line or, if there is no curb, the edge of the pavement, and includes crosswalks on any entering or exiting roadway. [2001 c.464 §2] 801.453 [1987 c.887 §2; repealed by 2003 c.819 §19]
