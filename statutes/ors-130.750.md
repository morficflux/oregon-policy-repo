# Trustee’s duty to comply with prudent investor rule (ORS 130.750)

## At a glance

ORS 130.750 — Trustee’s duty to comply with prudent investor rule. Chapter 130 (Chapter 130), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

130.750 Trustee’s duty to comply with prudent investor rule.

(1) Except as otherwise provided in subsection

(2) of this section, a trustee who invests and manages trust assets owes a duty to the beneficiaries of the trust to comply with the prudent investor rule set forth in ORS 130.755.

(2) The prudent investor rule is a default rule that may be expanded, restricted, eliminated or otherwise altered by the provisions of a trust. A trustee is not liable to a beneficiary to the extent that the trustee acted in reasonable reliance on the provisions of the trust. [2005 c.348 §77]
