## Full text

282.060 Exception to application of state laws where federal funds are involved. Notwithstanding any provision in ORS 282.020 or 282.050 to the contrary, in all cases where federal granted funds are involved, the federal laws, rules and regulations applicable thereto shall govern. 282.065 [2001 c.539 §11; repealed by 2005 c.33 §8] 282.070 [Amended by 1975 c.605 §7; 1995 c.452 §14; repealed by 1995 c.612 §24] 282.075 [1977 c.693 §1; repealed by 1995 c.612 §24; amended by 1995 c.776 §3; amendment treated as reenactment, see 282.076]
