# Application of ORS 250.265 to (ORS 250.255)

## At a glance

ORS 250.255 — Application of ORS 250.265 to. Chapter 250 (Chapter 250), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

250.255 Application of ORS 250.265 to 250.346. ORS 250.265 to 250.346 apply to the exercise of initiative or referendum powers regarding a city measure under section 1, Article IV, Oregon Constitution, unless the city charter or ordinance provides otherwise. [1979 c.190 §162]
