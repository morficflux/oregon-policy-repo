# Removing or defacing seizure or embargo notice unlawful (ORS 561.630)

## At a glance

ORS 561.630 — Removing or defacing seizure or embargo notice unlawful. Chapter 561 (State Department of Agriculture), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

561.630 Removing or defacing seizure or embargo notice unlawful. No person shall remove or deface any notice placed upon products seized or embargoed by the State Department of Agriculture, or move any such products from the place designated in the notice without written permission from the department.
