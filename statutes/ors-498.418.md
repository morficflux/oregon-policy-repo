## Full text

498.418 License application; form; fee.

(1) A person who is required to obtain a license from the State Fish and Wildlife Commission to operate an outdoor club shall submit to the commission an application for such license, on a form approved by the commission, that contains such information as the commission may require regarding the ownership, financial condition and operation of the club and promotional plans for sale of membership therein.

(2) The application shall be accompanied by the applicable fee under the fee schedule in ORS 497.061. [1973 c.749 §5; 2015 c.779 §31] 498.420 [Repealed by 1973 c.723 §130]
