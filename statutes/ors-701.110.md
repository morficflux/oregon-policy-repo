# License suspension for unpaid determined wage claim (ORS 701.110)

## At a glance

ORS 701.110 — License suspension for unpaid determined wage claim. Chapter 701 (Chapter 701), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

701.110 License suspension for unpaid determined wage claim. Upon receipt of the notification provided by the Commissioner of the Bureau of Labor and Industries under ORS 652.333 stating that a contractor or a business has failed to pay the amounts due as ordered in a final determination order within 60 days of the issuance of the order, the Construction Contractors Board shall, after notice and opportunity for a hearing under ORS chapter 183, suspend the license of the contractor or the business. [2019 c.444 §11]
