# Term of renewed license; rules (ORS 475C.047)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.047 — Term of renewed license; rules. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.047 Term of renewed license; rules. The Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission may adopt rules to establish a term for a license issued under ORS 475C.065, 475C.085, 475C.093, 475C.097 or 475C.548 that is up to five years if the commission finds that adoption of such a rule is consistent with public safety and the objectives of the licensing requirements. The rules adopted under this section may apply only to the renewal of a license. [2025 c.285 §3]
