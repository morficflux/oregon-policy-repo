# Application of ORS 167.060 to (ORS 167.100)

## At a glance

ORS 167.100 — Application of ORS 167.060 to. Chapter 167 (Chapter 167), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

167.100 Application of ORS 167.060 to 167.100. ORS 167.060 to 167.100 shall be applicable and uniform throughout the state and all political subdivisions and municipalities therein, and no local authority shall enact any ordinances, rules or regulations in conflict with the provisions thereof. [1971 c.743 §262a] 167.105 [Repealed by 1971 c.743 §432]
