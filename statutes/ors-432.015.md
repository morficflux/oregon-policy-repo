# Rules (ORS 432.015)

## At a glance

ORS 432.015 — Rules. Chapter 432 (Chapter 432), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

432.015 Rules. The State Registrar of the Center for Health Statistics, under the supervision of the Director of the Oregon Health Authority, shall adopt rules in accordance with ORS chapter 183 that are necessary to the installation and efficient performance of an adequate system of vital statistics. [Amended by 1961 c.191 §4; 1983 c.709 §3; 1997 c.783 §3; 2009 c.595 §606; 2013 c.366 §3]
