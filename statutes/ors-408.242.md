# Public employees in Active Guard Reserve Program (ORS 408.242)

## At a glance

ORS 408.242 — Public employees in Active Guard Reserve Program. Chapter 408 (Chapter 408), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

408.242 Public employees in Active Guard Reserve Program. ORS 408.240 (4)(a) does not apply to a public employee who leaves a position under ORS 408.240

(1) to enter or reenter the Active Guard Reserve Program under 32 U.S.C. 502(f). [2005 c.38 §1] Note: 408.242 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 408 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
