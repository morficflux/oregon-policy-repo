# Dropping from rolls PENALTIES (ORS 399.530)

## At a glance

ORS 399.530 — Dropping from rolls PENALTIES. Chapter 399 (Chapter 399), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

399.530 Dropping from rolls. When an enlisted person of the organized militia absents self without leave and there is reason to believe that the person does not intend to return, the person may be discharged in accordance with military department regulations issued pursuant to ORS 396.305. [1961 c.454 §68] PENALTIES
