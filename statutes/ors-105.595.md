## At a glance

## Full text

105.595 Action to abate nuisance not to affect other remedies; exception. Except to the extent that a judgment has been entered in the action for damages under ORS 105.560 (2), the abatement of a nuisance under ORS 105.550 to 105.600 does not prejudice the right of any person to recover damages for its past existence. [1989 c.846 §10; 1999 c.168 §5]
