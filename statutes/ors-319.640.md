# Cancellation of license on request of user (ORS 319.640)

## At a glance

ORS 319.640 — Cancellation of license on request of user. Chapter 319 (Chapter 319), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

319.640 Cancellation of license on request of user. If any person to whom a license has been issued pursuant to ORS 319.550 to 319.600 ceases using fuel within this state for a period of six months, the person shall immediately request in writing that the Department of Transportation cancel the license. On receipt of the request the department shall cancel the license.
