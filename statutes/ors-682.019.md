# Receipt and disbursement of federal funds (ORS 682.019)

## At a glance

ORS 682.019 — Receipt and disbursement of federal funds. Chapter 682 (Chapter 682), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

682.019 Receipt and disbursement of federal funds. The Oregon Health Authority may receive and disburse such federal funds as may be available for carrying out any of the provisions of ORS 820.330 to 820.380 or this chapter. [Formerly 682.295; 2009 c.595 §1064] 682.020 [Amended by 1961 c.248 §2; 1969 c.276 §2; 1983 c.486 §60; renumbered 677.810]
