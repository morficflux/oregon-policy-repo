# Notice of provisions of ORS 646.567 to (ORS 646.578)

## At a glance

ORS 646.578 — Notice of provisions of ORS 646.567 to. Chapter 646 (Chapter 646), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

646.578 Notice of provisions of ORS 646.567 to 646.578; rulemaking by Public Utility Commission. The Public Utility Commission shall by rule require that telecommunications companies inform parties of the provisions of ORS 646.567 to 646.578 and 646.608. Notification may be by:

(1) Annual inserts in the billing statements mailed to parties; or

(2) Conspicuous publication of the notice in the consumer information pages of local telephone directories. [Formerly 646.571] 646.580 [Repealed by 1953 c.391 §2] 646.590 [Repealed by 1953 c.391 §2] 646.600 [Repealed by 1953 c.391 §2]
