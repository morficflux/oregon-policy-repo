# Good cause; determination (ORS 650.310)

## At a glance

ORS 650.310 — Good cause; determination. Chapter 650 (Chapter 650), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

650.310 Good cause; determination. When determining whether good cause exists for an action, a person shall consider:

(1) Concerning the dealer affected by the action: (a) The extent of the dealer’s sales and leases of recreational vehicles in the area of sales responsibility; (b) The nature and extent of the dealer’s investment in the dealer’s business; (c) Whether the dealer’s service facilities, equipment, parts, supplies and personnel are adequate to carry out the responsibilities assigned to the dealer in the dealership agreement; (d) The extent and quality of the warranty service performed by the dealer; and (e) The extent to which the dealer performed the obligations imposed by the dealership agreement.

(2) The economic effect the action may have on communities located within the affected dealer’s area of sales responsibility. [2003 c.377 §2]
