# Certain agreements void (Farm Labor Contractor Indorsement) (ORS 658.720)

## At a glance

ORS 658.720 — Certain agreements void (Farm Labor Contractor Indorsement). Chapter 658 (Chapter 658), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

658.720 Certain agreements void. Agreements by workers purporting to modify their rights under ORS 658.705 to 658.850 shall be void as contrary to public policy. [1989 c.962 §11; 1991 c.67 §167] (Farm Labor Contractor Indorsement)
