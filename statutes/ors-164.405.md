# Robbery in the second degree (ORS 164.405)

## At a glance

ORS 164.405 — Robbery in the second degree. Chapter 164 (Chapter 164), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

164.405 Robbery in the second degree.

(1) A person commits the crime of robbery in the second degree if the person violates ORS 164.395 and the person: (a) Represents by word or conduct that the person is armed with what purports to be a dangerous or deadly weapon; or (b) Is aided by another person actually present.

(2) Robbery in the second degree is a Class B felony. [1971 c.743 §149] 164.410 [Repealed by 1971 c.743 §432]
