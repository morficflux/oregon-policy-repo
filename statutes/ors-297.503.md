# Spending constituting “expenditures”; rules (ORS 297.503)

## At a glance

## Full text

297.503 Spending constituting “expenditures”; rules. The Secretary of State shall, by rule, specify categories and types of spending that constitute expenditures for purposes of ORS 297.435 and 297.485. [2023 c.10 §3] Note: 297.503 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 297 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
