# Department to work with other states to avoid disruption of waste flows (ORS 465.378)

## At a glance

## Full text

465.378 Department to work with other states to avoid disruption of waste flows. The Department of Environmental Quality shall work cooperatively with other states to avoid disrupting or changing waste flows between states that may be caused by the establishment or adjustment of state disposal fees. [1995 c.552 §4] 465.380 [Formerly 466.590; 1991 c.703 §47; 1991 c.721 §2; repealed by 1993 c.707 §4 (465.381 enacted in lieu of 465.380)]
