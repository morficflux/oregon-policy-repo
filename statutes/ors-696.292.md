# Limitation on demand for compensation (ORS 696.292)

## At a glance

ORS 696.292 — Limitation on demand for compensation. Chapter 696 (Chapter 696), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

696.292 Limitation on demand for compensation. An individual listed in ORS 696.030 may not make a demand for the compensation of a real estate licensee under ORS 696.290. [2017 c.234 §7] Note: 696.292 was added to and made a part of ORS chapter 696 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
