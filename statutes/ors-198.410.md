# Definition for ORS (ORS 198.410)

## At a glance

ORS 198.410 — Definition for ORS. Chapter 198 (Chapter 198), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

198.410 Definition for ORS 198.425 and 198.430. As used in ORS 198.425 and 198.430, unless the context requires otherwise, “district officer” means a member of the governing body of a district who serves as such by virtue of election to such position. [1969 c.325 §1; 1971 c.23 §6; 1981 c.173 §2; 1983 c.83 §4] 198.420 [1969 c.325 §2; repealed by 1971 c.23 §12]
