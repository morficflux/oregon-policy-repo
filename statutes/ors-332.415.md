# Transportation of students attending private or parochial schools (ORS 332.415)

## At a glance

ORS 332.415 — Transportation of students attending private or parochial schools. Chapter 332 (Chapter 332), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

332.415 Transportation of students attending private or parochial schools. Whenever any district school board lawfully provides for transportation for pupils attending public schools, all children attending any private or parochial school under the compulsory school attendance laws shall, where the private or parochial school is along or near the route designated by said board, be entitled equally to the same rights, benefits and privileges as to transportation so provided for.
