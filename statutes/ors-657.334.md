# Limitation on period for which benefits paid DISLOCATED WORKER PROGRAM (ORS 657.334)

## At a glance

ORS 657.334 — Limitation on period for which benefits paid DISLOCATED WORKER PROGRAM. Chapter 657 (Chapter 657), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

657.334 Limitation on period for which benefits paid. Additional benefits may be paid under the provisions of ORS 657.331 to 657.334 only with respect to weeks not within an extended benefit period, and not within a period covered by any federal law allowing the filing of new claims extending benefits beyond those provided for as regular or extended benefits. [1983 c.818 §8; 1985 c.194 §1; 1987 c.126 §1; 1989 c.818 §1] DISLOCATED WORKER PROGRAM
