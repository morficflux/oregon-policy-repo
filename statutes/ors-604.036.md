# Copy of recordation certificate; brand books (ORS 604.036)

## At a glance

ORS 604.036 — Copy of recordation certificate; brand books. Chapter 604 (Chapter 604), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

604.036 Copy of recordation certificate; brand books. Upon recording of a brand under ORS 604.027, the State Department of Agriculture may:

(1) Issue a certified copy of its certificate of recordation upon the payment of a fee therefor to be established by the department; and

(2) Periodically publish brand books or supplements thereto, and distribute them upon payment of a fee to be established by the department sufficient to cover its costs therefor. [1981 c.248 §7] 604.040 [Amended by 1953 c.358 §15; 1961 c.267 §2; repealed by 1967 c.129 §19]
