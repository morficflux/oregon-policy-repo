# Short title (ORS 468B.550)

## At a glance

ORS 468B.550 — Short title. Chapter 468B (Chapter 468B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

468B.550 Short title. This section and ORS 468B.555 shall be known as the “Willamette Watershed Improvement Trading Act.” [2001 c.758 §1] Note: 468B.550 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 468B or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
