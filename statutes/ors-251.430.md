# Temporary exemption from public records law (ORS 251.430)

## At a glance

ORS 251.430 — Temporary exemption from public records law. Chapter 251 (Chapter 251), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

251.430 Temporary exemption from public records law. Notwithstanding ORS 192.311 to 192.478 relating to public records, materials filed by a candidate for inclusion in a county voters’ pamphlet and arguments supporting or opposing a measure filed by any person for inclusion in a county voters’ pamphlet are exempt from public inspection until the fourth business day after the final date for filing the materials. [1989 c.1031 §15; 1993 c.493 §23]
