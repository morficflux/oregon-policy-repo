# Elections conducted under this chapter (ORS 254.016)

## At a glance

ORS 254.016 — Elections conducted under this chapter. Chapter 254 (Chapter 254), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

254.016 Elections conducted under this chapter. Any primary election, general election or special election held in this state shall be conducted under the provisions of this chapter, unless specifically provided otherwise in the statute laws of this state. [1979 c.190 §225; 1983 c.350 §69a; 1995 c.712 §51; 1999 c.999 §45] 254.020 [Repealed by 1957 c.608 §231]
