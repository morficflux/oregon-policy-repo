# Bobcat and red fox control permitted (ORS 610.003)

## At a glance

## Full text

610.003 Bobcat and red fox control permitted. Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the State Department of Agriculture, after consultation with the State Department of Fish and Wildlife, may implement bobcat and red fox control procedures as authorized under this chapter, for a specified period of time and within a specified area, if the State Department of Agriculture determines such action is necessary to protect domestic mammals or birds. [Formerly subsection

(2) of 610.002; 1979 c.399 §4]
