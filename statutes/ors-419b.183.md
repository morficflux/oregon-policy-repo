# Speedy hearing required (ORS 419B.183)

## At a glance

ORS 419B.183 — Speedy hearing required. Chapter 419B (Chapter 419B), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

419B.183 Speedy hearing required. A child or ward may not be held in shelter care more than 24 hours, excluding Saturdays, Sundays and judicial holidays, except on order of the court made pursuant to a hearing. [1993 c.33 §70; 2003 c.396 §41; 2019 c.382 §15]
