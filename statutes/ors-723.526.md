# Other loan programs (ORS 723.526)

## At a glance

ORS 723.526 — Other loan programs. Chapter 723 (Chapter 723), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

723.526 Other loan programs.

(1) Loans to credit union members may be shared with other credit unions, corporations or financial organizations.

(2) A credit union may participate in guaranteed loan programs of the federal government, the State of Oregon or any other state.

(3) A credit union may purchase the conditional sales contracts, notes and similar instruments of its members.

(4) A credit union may purchase the leases of its members if the leases satisfy the requirements of ORS 723.152 (9). [1975 c.652 §57; 1999 c.185 §43] 723.530 [Amended by 1959 c.106 §21; 1971 c.367 §1; repealed by 1975 c.652 §88]
