# Violations punishable by fine (ORS 569.990)

## At a glance

ORS 569.990 — Violations punishable by fine. Chapter 569 (Chapter 569), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

569.990 Violations punishable by fine.

(1) Violation of a provision of ORS 569.175 to 569.195 or a rule adopted under ORS 569.175 to 569.195 is a Class B violation.

(2) Violation of a provision of ORS 569.360 to 569.495 is a Class A violation. [2009 c.98 §9(1),(3); 2011 c.9 §77]
