# Disposition of receipts RECOVERY FOR SERVICES (ORS 693.165)

## At a glance

ORS 693.165 — Disposition of receipts RECOVERY FOR SERVICES. Chapter 693 (Chapter 693), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

693.165 Disposition of receipts. All moneys received by the Department of Consumer and Business Services or the State Plumbing Board under ORS 447.010 to 447.156, 447.992 and 455.895 (1)(a) and this chapter shall be paid into the Consumer and Business Services Fund created by ORS 705.145. Such moneys shall be used only for the administration and enforcement of ORS 447.010 to 447.156 and 447.992 and this chapter. [1981 c.438 §26; 1993 c.744 §161; 2001 c.411 §30] RECOVERY FOR SERVICES
