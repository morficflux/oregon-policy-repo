# Effect of law on other community development corporations HOUSING REVITALIZATION PROGRAM (ORS 458.240)

## At a glance

ORS 458.240 — Effect of law on other community development corporations HOUSING REVITALIZATION PROGRAM. Chapter 458 (Chapter 458), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

458.240 Effect of law on other community development corporations. Nothing in ORS 456.550 and 458.210 to 458.240 shall limit the authority or powers of community development corporations authorized pursuant to ORS 708A.150. [1989 c.1030 §9; 1997 c.631 §475] HOUSING REVITALIZATION PROGRAM
