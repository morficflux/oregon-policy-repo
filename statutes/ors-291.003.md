# Federal laws and rules govern use of federal funds; exceptions FISCAL DUTIES OF DEPARTMENT (ORS 291.003)

## At a glance

ORS 291.003 — Federal laws and rules govern use of federal funds; exceptions FISCAL DUTIES OF DEPARTMENT. Chapter 291 (State Financial Administration), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

291.003 Federal laws and rules govern use of federal funds; exceptions. Notwithstanding any provision of ORS 291.001 to 291.034, 291.201 to 291.222, 291.232 to 291.260, 291.261, 291.307 and 291.990, but subject to any applicable judgment or settlement agreement, in all cases where federal funds are involved, the federal laws, rules and regulations applicable to the federal funds shall govern.
