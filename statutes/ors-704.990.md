# Criminal penalties GENERAL PROVISIONS (ORS 704.990)

## At a glance

ORS 704.990 — Criminal penalties GENERAL PROVISIONS. Chapter 704 (Chapter 704), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

704.990 Criminal penalties.

(1) Failure to comply with ORS 704.020, 704.021 or 704.065 is a Class A misdemeanor.

(2) Violation of ORS 704.030 is a Class A misdemeanor. [1983 c.655 §6; 2005 c.438 §10; 2013 c.422 §13] _______________
