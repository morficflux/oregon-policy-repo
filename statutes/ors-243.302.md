## At a glance

## Full text

243.302 Grouping retired and nonretired employees for health insurance coverage. The Public Employees’ Benefit Board may group retired state employees and state employees who are not retired for the purpose of entering into contracts for health insurance coverage. [1991 c.969 §1; 1997 c.222 §42] Note: 243.302 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 243 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. LOCAL GOVERNMENT HEALTH COVERAGE CONTRACTS
