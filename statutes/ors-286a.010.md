# Information required by State Treasurer (ORS 286A.010)

## At a glance

ORS 286A.010 — Information required by State Treasurer. Chapter 286A (Chapter 286A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

286A.010 Information required by State Treasurer. A related agency shall, at the direction of the State Treasurer, provide the State Treasurer with:

(1) The information that the State Treasurer considers necessary to determine whether to issue the requested bonds, including assumptions underlying cash flow projections associated with the repayment of the bonds; and

(2) After the requested bonds are issued, the information that the State Treasurer considers necessary to: (a) Administer the bonds; and (b) Comply with federal and state securities law and bond covenants. [2007 c.783 §4]
