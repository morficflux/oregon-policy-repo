# Oregon Health Authority to adopt rules for ORS (ORS 431.350)

## At a glance

ORS 431.350 — Oregon Health Authority to adopt rules for ORS. Chapter 431 (State and Local Administration and Enforcement of Public Health Laws), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

431.350 Oregon Health Authority to adopt rules for ORS 431.330 to 431.350. Upon receipt of written approval from the Conference of Local Health Officials the Oregon Health Authority shall adopt rules necessary for the administration of ORS 431.330 to 431.350.
