# Application of ORS 759.400 to (ORS 759.420)

## At a glance

ORS 759.420 — Application of ORS 759.400 to. Chapter 759 (Chapter 759), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

759.420 Application of ORS 759.400 to 759.455 to wholesale transactions regulated under federal law. Nothing in ORS 759.400 to 759.455 is intended to affect, alter or in any way modify wholesale transactions regulated by the federal Telecommunications Act of 1996 (Public Law 104-104) as in effect on September 1, 1999, and regulations adopted thereunder. [1999 c.1093 §26] (Universal Service Fund)
