# Right to possess marijuana items for personal use (ORS 475C.445)

## At a glance

ORS 475C.445 — Right to possess marijuana items for personal use. Chapter 475C (Chapter 475C), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

475C.445 Right to possess marijuana items for personal use. ORS 475C.433 does not prevent any person residing in the county or city from having, for personal use, a marijuana item purchased from a marijuana retailer licensed under ORS 475C.097. [Formerly 475B.479]
