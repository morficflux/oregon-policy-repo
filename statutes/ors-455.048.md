# Rules (ORS 455.048)

## At a glance

ORS 455.048 — Rules. Chapter 455 (Building Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

455.048 Rules. In accordance with the applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services may adopt rules necessary for the implementation of ORS 455.044 and 455.046. [Formerly 455.846] Note: See note under 455.044.
