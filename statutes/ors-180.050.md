# Location of office (Powers and Duties) (ORS 180.050)

## At a glance

ORS 180.050 — Location of office (Powers and Duties). Chapter 180 (Attorney General; Department of Justice), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

180.050 Location of office. The Attorney General shall keep and attend the office of Attorney General at the capital of the state. The state shall provide and furnish the office. (Powers and Duties)
