# Commission authority (ORS 759.036)

## At a glance

ORS 759.036 — Commission authority. Chapter 759 (Chapter 759), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

759.036 Commission authority. Except as otherwise provided by law, the Public Utility Commission shall have authority to determine the manner and extent of the regulation of telecommunications services within the State of Oregon. [2005 c.232 §7]
