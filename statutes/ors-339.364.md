# Victim may seek redress under other laws (Teen Dating Violence and Domestic Violence) (ORS 339.364)

## At a glance

ORS 339.364 — Victim may seek redress under other laws (Teen Dating Violence and Domestic Violence). Chapter 339 (Chapter 339), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

339.364 Victim may seek redress under other laws. ORS 339.351 to 339.364 may not be interpreted to prevent a victim of harassment, intimidation or bullying or a victim of cyberbullying from seeking redress under any other available law, whether civil or criminal. ORS 339.351 to 339.364 do not create any statutory cause of action. [2001 c.617 §7; 2007 c.647 §6] (Teen Dating Violence and Domestic Violence)
