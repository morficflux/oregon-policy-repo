## At a glance

## Full text

130.905 UTC 1102. Electronic records and signatures. The legal effect, validity or enforceability of electronic records or electronic signatures under ORS chapter 130, and of contracts formed or performed with the use of such records or signatures, are governed by ORS 84.001 to 84.061. [2005 c.348 §97] Note: See note under 130.900.
