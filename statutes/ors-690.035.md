# Application for certificate or permit (ORS 690.035)

## At a glance

ORS 690.035 — Application for certificate or permit. Chapter 690 (Chapter 690), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

690.035 Application for certificate or permit. A person desiring to obtain a certificate or demonstration permit shall apply in writing to the Health Licensing Office on a form approved by the office. Each application shall contain an affirmation by the applicant that the information contained therein is accurate. The application must also include evidence establishing to the satisfaction of the office that the applicant possesses the necessary qualifications.
