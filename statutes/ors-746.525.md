# Agreement effective as security interest (ORS 746.525)

## At a glance

ORS 746.525 — Agreement effective as security interest. Chapter 746 (Chapter 746), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

746.525 Agreement effective as security interest. No filing of the premium finance agreement shall be necessary to perfect the validity of such agreement as a secured transaction as against creditors, subsequent purchasers, pledgees, encumbrancers, successors or assigns. [1969 c.639 §14]
