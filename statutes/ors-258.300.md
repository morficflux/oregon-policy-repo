## Full text

258.300 Elections officials to notify Secretary of State when automatic full recount required. Immediately following the completion of the official canvass of votes for any election, the elections officer who prepared the canvass shall notify the Secretary of State of any election subject to an automatic full recount under ORS 258.280 and 258.290.
