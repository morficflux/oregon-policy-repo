## At a glance

ORS 114.065 — Limitations on support. Chapter 114 (Chapter 114), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

114.065 Limitations on support. If it appears to the court that after provision for support under ORS 114.015 is made the estate will be insolvent, the provision for support ordered by the court shall not exceed one-half of the estimated value of the property of the estate, and any periodic payment of moneys so ordered shall not continue for more than one year after the date of death of the decedent. [1969 c.591 §109] 114.070 [1957 c.345 §1; repealed by 1969 c.591 §305]
