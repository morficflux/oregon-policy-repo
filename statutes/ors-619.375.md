# Certain fryers excepted from provisions of ORS (ORS 619.375)

## At a glance

ORS 619.375 — Certain fryers excepted from provisions of ORS. Chapter 619 (Chapter 619), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

619.375 Certain fryers excepted from provisions of ORS 619.350 to 619.380. The provisions of ORS 619.350 to 619.380 do not apply to fryers being transported from the grower’s residence or place of business to a warehouse for storage, or to any place for inspection, grading, packing or processing. [1959 c.385 §6]
