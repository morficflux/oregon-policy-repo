## Full text

432.994 Unlawful use of vital record or report; civil penalty.

(1) The Director of the Oregon Health Authority may impose a civil penalty in an amount not to exceed $10,000 for each violation described in ORS 432.993. Moneys received by the authority from civil penalties imposed under this section shall be deposited in the General Fund and are available for general governmental expenses.

(2) Civil penalties under this section shall be imposed in the manner provided in ORS 183.745. [2013 c.366 §44]
