# Effect on other laws (ORS 653.559)

## At a glance

ORS 653.559 — Effect on other laws. Chapter 653 (Chapter 653), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

653.559 Effect on other laws. Nothing in ORS 653.555 to 653.557 relieves an employer of the obligation to comply with any other provision of law, including the provisions of ORS chapter 652, 653 or 654. [2024 c.36 §6] Note: 653.559 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 653 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. ADMINISTRATION
