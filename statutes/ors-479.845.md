# Limitation on use of local government fees (ORS 479.845)

## At a glance

ORS 479.845 — Limitation on use of local government fees. Chapter 479 (Chapter 479), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

479.845 Limitation on use of local government fees. Fees collected by a city or county for the enforcement or administration of the electrical specialty code and rules adopted under ORS 479.730

(1) shall be used only for the enforcement and administration of those laws. [1977 c.874 §5; 1981 c.815 §34; 2001 c.573 §18; 2005 c.435 §7]
