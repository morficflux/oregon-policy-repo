# Decedent’s probate estate (Decedent’s Nonprobate Estate) (ORS 114.650)

## At a glance

ORS 114.650 — Decedent’s probate estate (Decedent’s Nonprobate Estate). Chapter 114 (Chapter 114), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

114.650 Decedent’s probate estate. For purposes of ORS 114.600 to 114.725, a decedent’s probate estate is the value of all estate property that is subject to probate and that is available for distribution after payment of claims and expenses of administration. A decedent’s probate estate includes all property that could be administered under a simple estate affidavit pursuant to ORS 114.505 to 114.560. A decedent’s probate estate does not include any property that constitutes a probate transfer to the decedent’s surviving spouse under ORS 114.685. [2009 c.574 §10; 2023 c.17 §19] (Decedent’s Nonprobate Estate)
