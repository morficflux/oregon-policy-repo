# Enforcement officers to enforce prohibition on gray machines (ORS 461.725)

## At a glance

ORS 461.725 — Enforcement officers to enforce prohibition on gray machines. Chapter 461 (Chapter 461), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

461.725 Enforcement officers to enforce prohibition on gray machines. Out of the funds available to the Oregon State Lottery Commission to meet the administrative expenses of the Oregon State Lottery, the lottery commission shall employ or contract for at least three undercover enforcement officers to enforce the prohibition on gray machines imposed by ORS 162.305, 164.377, 166.715, 167.117, 167.164, 167.166, 461.210, 461.215, 461.217, 461.400, 461.445, 461.535, 461.548, 461.725 and 464.250. [1991 c.962 §18; 1995 c.814 §3] Note: See note under 461.544. 461.730 [1989 c.909 §49; renumbered 447.255 in 1991]
