# When hearing not required (ORS 561.625)

## At a glance

ORS 561.625 — When hearing not required. Chapter 561 (State Department of Agriculture), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

561.625 When hearing not required. Nothing in ORS 561.605 to 561.630, 616.225, 632.485 and 633.670 shall be construed as requiring that a hearing be held in connection with the seizure or embargoing of illegal or suspected products or as prohibiting the release, destruction or other disposition of such products by agreement between the State Department of Agriculture and the owner or person in possession of such products. [Subsection

(1) of 1965 Replacement Part formerly 616.100; subsection

(2) of 1965 Replacement Part formerly part of 616.095; 1967 c.437 §2]
