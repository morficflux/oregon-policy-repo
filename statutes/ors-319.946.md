# General rulemaking authority for per-mile road usage charge program (ORS 319.946)

## At a glance

ORS 319.946 — General rulemaking authority for per-mile road usage charge program. Chapter 319 (Chapter 319), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

319.946 General rulemaking authority for per-mile road usage charge program. In accordance with applicable provisions of ORS chapter 183, the Department of Transportation may adopt any rules the department considers necessary or convenient for the administration of ORS 319.883 to 319.946. [2019 c.428 §11] Note: See second note under 319.883.
