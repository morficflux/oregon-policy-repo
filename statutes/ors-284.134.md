# Financial review by Secretary of State (ORS 284.134)

## At a glance

ORS 284.134 — Financial review by Secretary of State. Chapter 284 (Organizations for Economic Development), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

284.134 Financial review by Secretary of State. The Secretary of State shall enter into agreements with the Oregon Tourism Commission to set an appropriate financial review schedule for the commission. In lieu of conducting a financial review, the Secretary of State may elect to contract for the financial review with, or accept a financial review conducted by, an independent certified public accountant. [Formerly 285A.277] 284.135 [1989 c.1015 §4; repealed by 1991 c.11 §19]
