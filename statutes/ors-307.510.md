# Appeal to tax court by taxpayer (Filing Deadline for Certain Programs) (ORS 307.510)

## At a glance

ORS 307.510 — Appeal to tax court by taxpayer (Filing Deadline for Certain Programs). Chapter 307 (Chapter 307), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

307.510 Appeal to tax court by taxpayer. Any taxpayer aggrieved by any decision under ORS 307.480 to 307.510 may appeal to the tax court within the time provided and in the manner specified by ORS 305.404 to 305.560. [1973 c.382 §7; 1995 c.650 §76] (Filing Deadline for Certain Programs)
