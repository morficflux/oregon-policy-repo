# Electric company serving 25,000 or fewer consumers not subject to requirements RENEWABLE PORTFOLIO STANDARDS (Definitions) (ORS 469A.480)

## At a glance

ORS 469A.480 — Electric company serving 25,000 or fewer consumers not subject to requirements RENEWABLE PORTFOLIO STANDARDS (Definitions). Chapter 469A (Chapter 469A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

469A.480 Electric company serving 25,000 or fewer consumers not subject to requirements. ORS 469A.400 to 469A.475 do not apply to an electric company, as defined in ORS 757.600, that serves electricity to 25,000 or fewer retail electricity consumers, as defined in ORS 757.600, located in this state. [2021 c.508 §17] _______________
