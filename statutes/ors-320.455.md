# When purchasers required to remit use tax and excise tax (ORS 320.455)

## At a glance

ORS 320.455 — When purchasers required to remit use tax and excise tax. Chapter 320 (Chapter 320), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

320.455 When purchasers required to remit use tax and excise tax. Any purchaser liable for the use tax imposed under ORS 320.410 or the excise tax imposed under ORS 320.415 and from whom the tax has not been collected shall, on or before the 30th day following the date on which the tax became due, file with the Department of Revenue a report of the amount of tax due from the purchaser in a form and manner prescribed by the department. The purchaser shall remit the amount of tax due with the report. [2017 c.750 §100; 2018 c.93 §14]
