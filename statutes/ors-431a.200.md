# Proof of licensure (ORS 431A.200)

## At a glance

ORS 431A.200 — Proof of licensure. Chapter 431A (Chapter 431A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

431A.200 Proof of licensure. A person to which a license or other authorization has been issued under ORS 431A.198 or 431A.220 must post proof of licensure or other authorization in a clear and conspicuous place at the premises for which the license or other authorization has been issued. [2021 c.586 §6]
