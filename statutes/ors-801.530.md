# “Tow vehicle” (ORS 801.530)

## At a glance

ORS 801.530 — “Tow vehicle”. Chapter 801 (General Provisions and Definitions for Oregon Vehicle Code), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

801.530 “Tow vehicle.” “Tow vehicle” means a motor vehicle that is:

(1) Altered or designed for, equipped for and used in the business of towing vehicles; and

(2) Used to tow vehicles by means of a crane, hoist, tow bar, tow line or dolly or otherwise used to render assistance to other vehicles. [1983 c.338 §97; 1985 c.71 §2] 801.535 [1983 c.338 §98; renumbered 801.524 in 1991]
