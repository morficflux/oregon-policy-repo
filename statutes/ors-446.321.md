## Full text

446.321 Fee for license; rules.

(1) Every applicant for a license under ORS 446.320 shall pay to the Oregon Health Authority a fee established by the authority by rule. The fee may not exceed $100, except that recreation parks shall pay an additional fee not to exceed $3 for each space.

(2) Rules adopted pursuant to subsection

(1) of this section shall be adopted in accordance with ORS chapter 183. [1983 c.707 §12; 2005 c.22 §317; 2009 c.595 §814; 2019 c.510 §1]
