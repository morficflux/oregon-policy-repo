# Commission meetings (ORS 173.328)

## At a glance

ORS 173.328 — Commission meetings. Chapter 173 (Chapter 173), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

173.328 Commission meetings. The Oregon Law Commission shall meet regularly pursuant to a schedule established by the commission. The commission also shall meet at other times and places specified by the call of the chairperson or of a majority of the members of the commission. [1997 c.661 §5; 2009 c.114 §4] 173.330 [1963 c.292 §4 (173.310 to 173.340 enacted in lieu of 173.155); repealed by 1979 c.472 §2]
