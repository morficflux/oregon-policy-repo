## At a glance

## Full text

107.795 Availability of other remedies. Nothing in ORS 107.615 and 107.755 to 107.795 shall preclude a party from obtaining any orders available under ORS 107.700 to 107.735 or ORS 124.005 to 124.040 before or during mediation.
