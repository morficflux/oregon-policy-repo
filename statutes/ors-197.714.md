## At a glance

## Full text

197.714 Cooperation of county and city concerning industrial development.

(1) Notwithstanding the authority granted in ORS 197.713 and 197.716 to allow industrial or other employment use development, when a county or its designee considers action under ORS 197.713 or 197.716 for land within 10 miles of the urban growth boundary of a city, the county or its designee shall give notice to the city at least 21 days prior to taking action.

(2) If the city objects to the authorization of industrial or other employment use development under ORS 197.713 or 197.716, the city and county shall negotiate to establish conditions on the industrial or other employment use designation, development or changes in the development necessary to mitigate concerns raised by the city’s objection. [2003 c.688 §2; 2019 c.170 §3] Note: See note under 197.713. 197.715 [1973 c.482 §2; repealed by 1977 c.665 §24]
