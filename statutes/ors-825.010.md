# Compliance with chapter required ECONOMIC AND SAFETY REGULATION (Applicability) (ORS 825.010)

## At a glance

ORS 825.010 — Compliance with chapter required ECONOMIC AND SAFETY REGULATION (Applicability). Chapter 825 (Chapter 825), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

825.010 Compliance with chapter required. No for-hire carrier or private carrier shall operate any motor vehicle for the transportation of persons or property, or both, on any public highway in this state except in accordance with the provisions of this chapter. [Formerly 767.040; 1997 c.275 §35] ECONOMIC AND SAFETY REGULATION (Applicability)
