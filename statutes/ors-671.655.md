# Deposit of moneys (ORS 671.655)

## At a glance

ORS 671.655 — Deposit of moneys. Chapter 671 (Chapter 671), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

671.655 Deposit of moneys. Except as the State Landscape Contractors Board may otherwise provide under ORS 182.462 (1)(e), the board shall deposit moneys received as fees or civil penalties into the account created by the board pursuant to ORS 182.470. [2005 c.609 §6; 2011 c.110 §5]
