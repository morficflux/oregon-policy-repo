## Full text

358.683 Oregon Property Management Program; rules.

(1) The State Parks and Recreation Director, with the advice of the State Advisory Committee on Historic Preservation shall formulate and implement an Oregon Property Management Program. The program shall include, but need not be limited to: (a) Policies and plans for accepting and preserving historic sites and property in Oregon; (b) Criteria for selecting sites and property according to the provisions of ORS 358.680 to 358.690; and (c) Any other provision necessary to administer the program.

(2) The director and the State Advisory Committee on Historic Preservation shall coordinate activities concerning historic properties with the State Historic Preservation Officer.

(3) In accordance with any applicable provision of ORS chapter 183, the director may promulgate rules to carry out the provisions of the Oregon Property Management Program. [1983 c.231 §3; 1989 c.904 §56]
