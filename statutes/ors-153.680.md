# Costs MISCELLANEOUS (ORS 153.680)

## At a glance

ORS 153.680 — Costs MISCELLANEOUS. Chapter 153 (Chapter 153), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

153.680 Costs. Any amount collected by a circuit court, justice court or municipal court as costs in a criminal action shall be retained by the court.
