# Connection to federal law; meaning of terms (ORS 118.007)

## At a glance

ORS 118.007 — Connection to federal law; meaning of terms. Chapter 118 (Chapter 118), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

118.007 Connection to federal law; meaning of terms. Any term used in ORS 118.005 to 118.540 has the same meaning as when used in a comparable context in the laws of the federal Internal Revenue Code relating to federal estate taxes, unless a different meaning is clearly required or the term is specifically defined in ORS 118.005 to 118.540. Any reference in ORS 118.005 to 118.540 to the Internal Revenue Code means the federal Internal Revenue Code as amended and in effect on December 31, 2010, except where the Legislative Assembly has specifically provided otherwise. [2003 c.806 §2; 2011 c.526 §2] 118.009 [2003 c.806 §1a; repealed by 2011 c.526 §29]
