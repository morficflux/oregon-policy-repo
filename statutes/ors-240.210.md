# Classified service (ORS 240.210)

## At a glance

ORS 240.210 — Classified service. Chapter 240 (State Personnel Relations), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

240.210 Classified service. The classified service comprises all positions in the state service existing on June 16, 1945, or thereafter created and which are not listed in ORS 240.200, 240.205 or 240.212. [Amended by 1955 c.738 §7; 1981 c.409 §2]
