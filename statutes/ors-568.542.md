# Payment of expenses for director election from county funds (ORS 568.542)

## At a glance

ORS 568.542 — Payment of expenses for director election from county funds. Chapter 568 (Chapter 568), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

568.542 Payment of expenses for director election from county funds. The expenses incurred for the election of directors of a soil and water conservation district under ORS 568.210 to 568.808 and 568.900 to 568.933 shall be paid out of county funds by the county or counties within which the territory of the district lies. [1995 c.243 §2] Note: 568.542 was added to and made a part of 568.210 to 568.808 by legislative action but was not added to any smaller series therein. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
