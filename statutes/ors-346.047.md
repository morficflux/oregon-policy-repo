# Authority of state board to hold property (ORS 346.047)

## At a glance

ORS 346.047 — Authority of state board to hold property. Chapter 346 (Chapter 346), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

346.047 Authority of state board to hold property. The State Board of Education may receive, take and hold property, both real and personal for the school operated under ORS 346.010 and may sell, transfer, assign, allot, set over or convey the property pursuant to legislative authority.
