# Cancellation of uncollectible assessment; subsequent collection (ORS 577.525)

## At a glance

ORS 577.525 — Cancellation of uncollectible assessment; subsequent collection. Chapter 577 (Chapter 577), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

577.525 Cancellation of uncollectible assessment; subsequent collection. The Oregon Beef Council may cancel an uncollectible assessment consistent with ORS 293.240. Subsequent collection of debt written off under ORS 293.240 is governed by ORS 293.245.
