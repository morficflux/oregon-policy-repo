# Attorney General as legal advisor and counsel to commission (ORS 294.695)

## At a glance

ORS 294.695 — Attorney General as legal advisor and counsel to commission. Chapter 294 (Chapter 294), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

294.695 Attorney General as legal advisor and counsel to commission. The Attorney General shall be the legal advisor and counsel of the commission and shall represent it in all suits and actions and other legal proceedings in any court in this state. The Attorney General shall receive compensation for any services rendered in such capacity. [Amended by 1969 c.363 §2; 1973 c.775 §6]
