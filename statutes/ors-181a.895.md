# and (ORS 181A.895)

## At a glance

ORS 181A.895 — and. Chapter 181A (State Police; Public Safety Standards and Training), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

181A.895 State preemption of local laws relating to private security providers. The provisions of ORS 181A.360, 181A.840 to 181A.918 and 181A.995 preempt any laws of the political subdivisions of this state relating to the regulation of private security providers. [Formerly 203.090] Note: 181A.895 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 181A or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
