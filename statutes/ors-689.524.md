# Approval of coverage for biological product (ORS 689.524)

## At a glance

ORS 689.524 — Approval of coverage for biological product. Chapter 689 (Chapter 689), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

689.524 Approval of coverage for biological product. ORS 689.522 does not prohibit an insurer or other health care payer from requiring prior authorization or imposing other appropriate utilization controls in approving coverage for any biological product. [2016 c.43 §4] Note: 689.524 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 689 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation.
