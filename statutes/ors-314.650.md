# Apportionment of income (ORS 314.650)

## At a glance

ORS 314.650 — Apportionment of income. Chapter 314 (Chapter 314), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

314.650 Apportionment of income. All apportionable income shall be apportioned to this state by multiplying the income by the sales factor. [1965 c.152 §10; 1989 c.626 §5; 1989 c.1088 §1; 1995 c.79 §156; 2001 c.793 §1; 2003 c.739 §§1,5; 2005 c.832 §§48,49; 2009 c.842 §1; 2017 c.43 §4]
