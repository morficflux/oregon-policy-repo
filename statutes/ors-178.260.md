## Full text

178.260 Final orders relating to compliance.

(1) The Commissioner of the Bureau of Labor and Industries shall inform the Oregon Retirement Savings Board of any final orders issued under ORS 178.255.

(2) The board shall include the commissioner’s final order determinations related to employers’ compliance in the annual reports described in ORS 178.235. [2019 c.149 §4] OREGON 529 SAVINGS NETWORK (Generally)
