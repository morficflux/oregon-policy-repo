## At a glance

## Full text

284.565 Development of process for certifying sites ready for industrial or traded sector development. The Oregon Business Development Department, in coordination with the Economic Revitalization Team established pursuant to ORS 284.555, shall develop and administer a process for certifying sites throughout the state that are ready for industrial or traded sector development. [2003 c.800 §21]
