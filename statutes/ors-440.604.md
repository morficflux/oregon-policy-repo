## Full text

440.604 Rules relating to regional health improvement plan.

(1) By September 1, 2011, the Oregon Health Authority shall adopt by rule requirements for the regional health improvement plan adopted under ORS 440.603.

(2) The Oregon Health Authority shall adopt rules: (a) Necessary to implement ORS 440.600 to 440.603; and (b) That allow for the consolidation of planning and reporting requirements of the authority under ORS 440.603. [2011 c.418 §17] _______________
