# Definitions for ORS (ORS 443.205)

## At a glance

ORS 443.205 — Definitions for ORS. Chapter 443 (Chapter 443), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

443.205 Definitions for ORS 443.215 and 443.225. As used in ORS 443.215 and 443.225, “domiciliary care facilities” means facilities providing residential care to adults, including adult foster homes, group care facilities or residential treatment, training or care facilities, established, contracted for or operated by the Department of Human Services or the Oregon Health Authority.
