# Approval of indemnification required (ORS 596.625)

## At a glance

ORS 596.625 — Approval of indemnification required. Chapter 596 (Disease Control Generally), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

596.625 Approval of indemnification required. No application, contract or agreement for the payment of indemnity under ORS 596.615 to 596.681 is enforceable unless approved and signed by the Director of Agriculture. [1981 c.334 §6] 596.630 [1953 c.633 §2; 1955 c.557 §32; repealed by 1963 c.208 §4]
