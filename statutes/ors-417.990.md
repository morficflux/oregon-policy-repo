# Penalty for placement of children in violation of compact THE INTERSTATE COMPACT FOR JUVENILES (ORS 417.990)

## At a glance

ORS 417.990 — Penalty for placement of children in violation of compact THE INTERSTATE COMPACT FOR JUVENILES. Chapter 417 (Chapter 417), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

417.990 Penalty for placement of children in violation of compact. The sending, bringing, or causing to be sent or brought into any receiving state of a child in violation of the terms of the Interstate Compact on the Placement of Children is a Class A misdemeanor. [1975 c.482 §8] _______________
