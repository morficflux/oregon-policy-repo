# Creation of education service districts (ORS 334.010)

## At a glance

ORS 334.010 — Creation of education service districts. Chapter 334 (Chapter 334), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

334.010 Creation of education service districts. There is created in each region a district to be known as the education service district to consist of the counties and the area of the common school districts as listed in ORS 334.013, with a governing body thereof to be known as the education service district board.
