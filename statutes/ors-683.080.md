# Validity of certificates issued under former law (ORS 683.080)

## At a glance

ORS 683.080 — Validity of certificates issued under former law. Chapter 683 (Chapter 683), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

683.080 Validity of certificates issued under former law. Any person holding an unrevoked certificate issued under chapter 139, Oregon Laws 1905, as amended, shall be entitled to continue the practice of optometry as though it were issued under ORS 683.010 to 683.340 and such certificate shall be subject to renewal, revocation and suspension the same as though it has been issued under ORS 683.010 to 683.340. 683.090 [Repealed by 1971 c.102 §11]
