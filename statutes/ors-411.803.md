# When spouse may be compensated for in-home care (ORS 411.803)

## At a glance

ORS 411.803 — When spouse may be compensated for in-home care. Chapter 411 (Chapter 411), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

411.803 When spouse may be compensated for in-home care. When a married recipient of public assistance provided under ORS 411.706 requires in-home care, the Department of Human Services shall provide that such care be compensated even though provided by the spouse, in the manner and to the extent specified by rule of the department based on the extent of need and the availability of funds therefor. [1985 c.638 §2; 2001 c.900 §95; 2005 c.381 §11] 411.805 [1961 c.526 §1; repealed by 1963 c.599 §2 (411.806 enacted in lieu of 411.805)]
