# Effect of ORS 280.410 to (ORS 280.485)

## Full text

280.485 Effect of ORS 280.410 to 280.485 on powers of city. ORS 280.410 to 280.485 is additional, alternative and supplemental authority for cities with a population of 70,000 or more and shall not abrogate any power, right or authority otherwise granted by law or charter to such cities. [1977 c.772 §16; 2003 c.286 §7] (Counties)
