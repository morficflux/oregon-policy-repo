# Standards for clustered mailboxes in county roads and rights-of-way (ORS 215.606)

## At a glance

ORS 215.606 — Standards for clustered mailboxes in county roads and rights-of-way. Chapter 215 (Chapter 215), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

215.606 Standards for clustered mailboxes in county roads and rights-of-way. Each county in this state shall adopt standards and specifications for clustered mailboxes within the boundaries of county roads and rights-of-way that conform to the standards and specifications for such mailboxes contained in the State of Oregon Structural Specialty Code. [2011 c.488 §3] Note: 215.606 was enacted into law by the Legislative Assembly but was not added to or made a part of ORS chapter 215 or any series therein by legislative action. See Preface to Oregon Revised Statutes for further explanation. 215.610 [1969 c.418 §2; 1979 c.190 §407; repealed by 1983 c.327 §16]
