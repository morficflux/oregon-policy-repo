# Composition of education service districts (ORS 334.013)

## At a glance

## Full text

334.013 Composition of education service districts.

(1) Except where the boundaries of an education service district are changed by merger under ORS 334.710 to 334.770 or other provision of law, the education service districts of this state are as follows: (a) Region 1. Clatsop, Columbia, Tillamook and Washington Counties. (b) Region 2. Multnomah County. (c) Region 3. Marion, Polk and Yamhill Counties. (d) Region 4. Lincoln, Linn and Benton Counties. (e) Region 5. Lane County. (f) Region 6. Douglas County. (g) Region 7. Coos and Curry Counties and the area lying within the Reedsport School District. (h) Region 8. Jackson, Josephine and Klamath Counties. (i) Region 9. Hood River and Wasco Counties. (j) Region 10. Crook and Deschutes Counties. (k) Region 11. Lake County. (L) Region 12. Umatilla, Morrow, Union and Baker Counties. (m) Region 14. Malheur County and the area comprising the Huntington School District. (n) Region 15. Clackamas County. (o) Region 17. Harney County. (p) Region 18. Wallowa County. (q) Region 19. Sherman, Gilliam and Wheeler Counties. (r) Region 20. Grant County. (s) Region 21. Jefferson County and the area comprising the Warm Springs Reservation.

(2) Where a boundary change or formation of a component school district results in a joint school district, the joint school district shall be included in the education service district in which the joint district’s administrative office is located. [Formerly 334.020; 2019 c.258 §1]
