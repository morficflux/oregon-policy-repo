# Notice of application for exchange (ORS 540.535)

## At a glance

ORS 540.535 — Notice of application for exchange. Chapter 540 (Chapter 540), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

540.535 Notice of application for exchange. After receipt of an exchange application, the Water Resources Commission shall give notice of the application in the weekly public notice of the Water Resources Department. [1987 c.815 §3; 2011 c.52 §7; 2025 c.282 §6] Note: The amendments to 540.535 by section 6, chapter 282, Oregon Laws 2025, become operative April 1, 2026, and apply to applications and petitions submitted on or after April 1, 2026. See section 30, chapter 282, Oregon Laws 2025, as amended by section 21b, chapter 575, Oregon Laws 2025, and see section 48, chapter 282, Oregon Laws 2025, as amended by section 21c, chapter 575, Oregon Laws 2025. The text that is operative until April 1, 2026, is set forth for the user’s convenience. 540.535. After receipt of an exchange application, the Water Resources Commission shall give notice of the application by publishing notice of the application in a newspaper having general circulation in the area in which the water uses are located at least once each week for at least two successive weeks. The cost of providing the notice by publication shall be paid in advance by the applicant to the commission.
