# Exceptions to final order (ORS 543A.135)

## At a glance

ORS 543A.135 — Exceptions to final order. Chapter 543A (Chapter 543A), 2025 Edition.

## Full text

543A.135 Exceptions to final order.

(1) Within 20 days after the Water Resources Director issues a final order under ORS 543A.130 after the conclusion of a contested case hearing, any party may file exceptions to the order with the Water Resources Commission.

(2) The commission shall issue a modified order, if the exceptions are allowed, or deny the exceptions within 60 days after the close of the exception period under subsection

(1) of this section. [1997 c.449 §26]
