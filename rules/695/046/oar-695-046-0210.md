## Full text

695-046-0210 Board Approval and Delegation of Authority The Board shall conditionally approve grants with funding conditions made available to the public. The Director is delegated the responsibility of ensuring that funding conditions required by the Board are fully satisfied by the grant applicant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.906 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.932(9)
