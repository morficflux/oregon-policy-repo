## Full text

695-046-0225 Subsequent Conveyances A water interest acquired with OWEB grant funds shall not be conveyed to another party unless the conveyance is approved by the Board and may not be conveyed for the purpose of consumptive uses.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.906 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.932(9)
