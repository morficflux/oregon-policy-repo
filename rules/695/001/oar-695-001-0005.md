## Full text

695-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure The Oregon Attorney General’s Model and Uniform Rules of Procedure, January 2004 edition, were adopted by the Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board on September 14, 2004 and will be followed in all matters except where a different procedure is prescribed by statute. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Watershed Enhancement Board.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 541 & Ch. 734 & OL 1987
