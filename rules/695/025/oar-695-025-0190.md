## Full text

695-025-0190 Waiver and Periodic Review of Rules The Director may waive the requirements of Division 25 for individual grant applications unless required by statute, when doing so will result in more efficient or effective implementation of the Board’s monitoring grant program. Any waiver must be in writing and included in the grant file to which the waiver applies. The administrative rules for monitoring grants shall be periodically reviewed by the Board and revised as necessary and appropriate.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.906 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.890-541.969
