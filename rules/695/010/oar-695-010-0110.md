## Full text

695-010-0110 Waiver of Rules The Director may waive the requirements of division 10, unless they are required by statute, for individual grants, when doing so will result in more efficient or effective implementation of the Board's grant program. Any waiver granted shall be in writing and included in the permanent file of the individual grant for which the waiver was granted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.906 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.890 - 541.969
