## Full text

695-015-0120 Waiver and Periodic Review of Rules The Director may waive the requirements of Division 15 for individual grant applications unless required by statute, when doing so will result in more efficient or effective implementation of the Board’s engagement grant program. Any waiver must be in writing and included in the grant file to which the waiver applies.
