# Purpose (OAR 695-015-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

695-015-0010 Purpose The Board shall provide outreach grants, as funds are available, for Engagement Projects that are necessary for carrying out eligible restoration and acquisition projects, or programs that lead to development of eligible projects, as required by ORS 541.956(4) and OR CONST Art. XV, §4b(2)(d).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.906 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.956
