## Full text

695-015-0100 Board Funding Decision

(1) The Board may fund a project in whole or in part.

(2) Projects not funded may be resubmitted during application submission periods prescribed by the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.906 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.956

History: OWEB 2-2017, f. & cert. ef. 7-31-17
