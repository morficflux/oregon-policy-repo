## Full text

695-015-0040 Eligible and Ineligible Project Types

(1) The Board will only consider projects that: (a) Are focused on a specific project site or sites, or that support resource assessment and planning that results in identification of a specific project site or sites; and (b) Will lead to eligible restoration or acquisition projects within a specific timeframe.

(2) Projects whose primary purpose is education are not eligible.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.906 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.956
