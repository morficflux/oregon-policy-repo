## Full text

695-005-0080 Periodic Rules Review and Program Evaluation OWEB and the Board shall review the grant program and rules at least once every five years and make changes as needed to carry out a high quality and effective program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.906 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.890 - 541.969
