## Full text

695-047-0090 Board Funding Decision For Focused Investment Partnership Initiatives, the Board shall award funds on a biennial basis. Subject to Board evaluation and future appropriations, the Board seeks investment for up to six years for each Initiative. Prior to soliciting for Initiatives, the Board shall determine the maximum amount for biennial awards.
