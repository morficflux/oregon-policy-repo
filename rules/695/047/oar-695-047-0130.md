## Full text

695-047-0130 Reporting (1 ) R eporting for project-level grants shall be determined in each project grant agreement.

(2) At the end of each biennium, the Focused Investment Initiative partnerships shall, in collaboration with OWEB staff, report the following to the Board: (a) Initiative progress for the biennium; (b) Additional investments generated through the Initiative beyond match used for project-level applications; (c) Updated Work Plan and budget proposed for the next biennium; and (d) Any proposed changes to the geography, scope, or Core Partners of the Initiative.
