## Full text

586-010-0045 Executive Secretary The Superintendent of Public Instruction shall appoint an executive secretary from the staff of the State Department of Education who will serve as administrative officer. The executive secretary shall serve at the pleasure of the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 342.805 - 342.934

History: FDA 1-1992, f. & cert. ef. 3-5-92 FDA 1-1985, f. 3-25-85, ef. 3-27-85 FDA 4, f. 3-11-74, ef. 4-11-74 FDA 2(Temp), f. & ef. 1-4-74
