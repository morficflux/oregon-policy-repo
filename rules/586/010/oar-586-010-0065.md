## Full text

586-010-0065 Expenses for Professional and Other Special Assistance The Board shall be furnished by the State Department of Education at the Department’s expense appropriate professional and other special assistance reasonably required in the performance of the Board’s duties.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 342.805 - 342.934
