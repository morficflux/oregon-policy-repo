## Full text

416-420-0000 Purpose

(1) These rules define the process by which a person may access Oregon Youth Authority (OYA) facility premises.

(2) All youth in custody, except as specifically provided in these rules, are eligible for visitation while residing in an OYA facility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 162.135, ORS 162.185, ORS 419C.478, ORS 420A.010 & ORS 420A.100
