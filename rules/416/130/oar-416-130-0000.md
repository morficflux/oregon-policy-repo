## Full text

416-130-0000 Definitions

(1) Person: Any natural person, corporation, partnership, firm, or association.

(2) Public Record: Any writing containing information relating to the conduct of the public's business prepared, owned, used or retained by a public body regardless of physical form or characteristics.

(3) Writing: Handwriting, typewriting, printing, photographing and every means of recording including letters, words, pictures, sounds, or symbols, and all papers, maps, files, facsimiles, or electronic recordings.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192 & ORS 419A.255
