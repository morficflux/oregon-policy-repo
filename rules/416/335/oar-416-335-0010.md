# Purpose (OAR 416-335-0010)

## Full text

416-335-0010 Purpose The purpose of the Behavior Rehabilitation Services (BRS) Program is to remediate the BRS Client’s debilitating psychosocial, emotional and behavioral disorders by providing such Services as behavioral intervention, counseling, and skills-training. Services are delivered in a way that integrates a gender-responsive, culturally-sensitive, trauma-informed, and age-appropriate or developmentally-appropriate approach. These rules supplement the BRS Program general rules with additional requirements for BRS Programs provided through contract with OYA.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 420A.010 & 420A.014
