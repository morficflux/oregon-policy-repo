## Full text

416-800-0090 Record Keeping, Confidentiality Any information obtained in the Criminal Records Check is confidential. OYA must restrict the dissemination of information obtained in the Criminal Records Check. Only those persons, as identified by OYA, with a demonstrated and legitimate need to know the information, may have access to Criminal Records Check records information.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.021 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.557, 420A.010, 420A.020 & 420A.021
