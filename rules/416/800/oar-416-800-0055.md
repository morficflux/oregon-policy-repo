## Full text

416-800-0055 Types of Crimes Relevant to a Fitness Determination

(1) Crimes Relevant to a Fitness Determination. (a) All felonies; (b) All misdemeanors; (c) Any United States Military crime or international crime; (d) Any civil restraining order entered for spousal abuse, domestic violence, harassment, or stalking.

(2) Evaluation of Crimes. OYA must evaluate a crime on the basis of the laws of the jurisdiction in which the crime or offense occurred.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.021 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 420A.010, 420A.020 & 420A.021
