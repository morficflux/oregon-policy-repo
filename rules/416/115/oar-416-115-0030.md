## Full text

416-115-0030 Administration of the Interstate Compact for Juveniles The Oregon Youth Authority (OYA) Deputy Juvenile Compact Administrator will provide interpretation of the ICJ and coordination of all referrals or requests to:

(1) Permit out-of-state supervision of a juvenile who should be sent to another state when eligible for parole or probation;

(2) Permit out-of-state travel for an Adjudicated Delinquent;

(3) Provide for the return of Accused Delinquents, Absconders, and Escapees to the states they left;

(4) Provide for return of Runaways to their Home States who have not as yet been adjudged delinquent; and

(5) Return juveniles to Oregon when ICJ placement fails.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.010 - 417.080
