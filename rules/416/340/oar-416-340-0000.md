## Full text

416-340-0000 Purpose This rule provides for consistent practice in the storage, control and administration of medication to youth in OYA custody in close-custody facilities or community substitute care placements.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.517, 420A.010, 420A.014 & 420.054
