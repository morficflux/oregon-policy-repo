# Financial Accounting (OAR 416-260-0050)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

416-260-0050 Financial Accounting OYA must complete financial accountings as required for legal proceedings, audits, and when a maintenance account balance exists and the youth in custody's OYA custody has been terminated.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 420A.030 & 420A.032

History: OYA 15-2022, amend filed 12/13/2022, effective 12/14/2022 OYA 2-2015, f. & cert. ef. 2-19-15 OYA 5-2001, f. & cert. ef. 4-20-01
