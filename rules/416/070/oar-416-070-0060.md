## Full text

416-070-0060 Variances

(1) The Supervising Clinical Psychologist may grant a variance to the criteria used to determine the certification status of a QMHA, QMHP, Treatment Services Supervisor if the Supervising Clinical Psychologist documents the reason for the variance and the proposed timeline for the variance.

(2) Signed documentation from the Supervising Clinical Psychologist indicating support of the variance must be retained in the subject QMHA, QMHP, or Treatment Services Supervisor employee’s personnel file.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.010, ORS 420A.025 & ORS 420A.022 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 420A.022
