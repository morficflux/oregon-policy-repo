## Full text

416-320-0030 Exceptions These rules do not apply to persons placed in OYA supervision via an interstate compact, to persons placed in OYA facilities under ORS 137.124, or to persons transferred to a youth correctional facility from a county sheriff. If an APB or arrest warrant is necessary in one of these cases, OYA will immediately notify the appropriate party (interstate compact administrator, Oregon Department of Corrections, or county sheriff).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 420A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 420.905 – 420.915
