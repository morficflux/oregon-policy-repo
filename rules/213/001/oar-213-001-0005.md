## Full text

213-001-0005 Rulemaking Procedure

(1) All new and revised rules will be adopted in accordance with the provisions of ORS Chapter 183 and the Model Rules of Procedure adopted by the Oregon Attorney General.

(2) Amendments to the following divisions take effect on January 1, 2004: OAR 213-001-0000 and 0005; 213-003-0001(14), (15), (16); 213-005-0001, 0004, and 0007; 213-011-0003; 213-017-0001, 0002, 0003, 0004, 0005, 0006, 0007, 0008, 0009, 0010, and 0011, and 213-018-0038, 0050, and 0090; and 213-019-0007, 0008, 0010, 0011, 0012, and 0015.

(3) Adoption of rules OAR 213-018-0047 and 0048 take effect on January 1, 2004.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.596, 137.667, 421.412, 475.986, 475.998 & 811.707 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669 & 2001 OL Ch. 147, 387, 502, 510, 615, 635, 696, 737, 804, 828, 857, 870, 884, 919, 926 & 2003 OL Ch. 383, 453, 464, 484, 543, 550, 577, 632, 633, 804 & 815

History: CJC 2-2003, f. 12-31-03, cert. ef. 1-1-04 CJC 2-2001, f. 12-26-01, cert. ef. 1-1-02 CJC 1-1999, f. & cert. ef. 11-1-99 CJC 3-1997, f. 10-29-97, cert. ef. 11-1-97 CJC 1-1996, f. 3-6-96, cert. ef. 3-8-96, Renumbered from 253-001-0005 SSGB 1-1988, f. & cert. ef. 11-16-88
