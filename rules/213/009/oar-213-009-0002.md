## Full text

213-009-0002 Offenders Found Guilty Except for Insanity The rules of the Criminal Justice Commission shall not apply to any offender found guilty except for insanity pursuant to ORS 161.295. The disposition of such an offender shall be as provided by the provisions of ORS 161.295 to 161.400.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
