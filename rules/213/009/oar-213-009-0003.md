## Full text

213-009-0003 Other Sanctions In addition to the presumptive or departure sentence, the sentencing judge may impose any restitution, fine, fee or other monetary payment authorized or required by law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
