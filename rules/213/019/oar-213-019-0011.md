## Full text

213-019-0011 Crime Category 5 — Drug Offenses

(1) ORS 164.338 – ARSON INCIDENT TO THE MANUFACTURE OF A CONTROLLED SUBSTANCE II – (C): If the value of the property destroyed was $10,000 or more but less than $50,000.

(2) ORS 475.910(3) Unlawful Administration of a Controlled Substance. Unlawful Administration of a Controlled Substance shall be ranked at Crime Category 5 when the substance is a Schedule IV controlled substance. [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
