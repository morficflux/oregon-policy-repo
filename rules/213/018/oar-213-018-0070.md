## Full text

213-018-0070 Supplying Contraband (ORS 162.185)

(1) CRIME CATEGORY 7: Supplying Contraband shall be ranked at Crime Category 7 if the offender supplied a firearm or firearms as contraband.

(2) CRIME CATEGORY 6: Supplying Contraband shall be ranked at Crime Category 6 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 7 and the offender supplied one or more dangerous weapons (not including firearms) as contraband.

(3) CRIME CATEGORY 5: Supplying Contraband shall be ranked at Crime Category 5 if it cannot be ranked at either Crime Category 6 or 7 and the offender supplied a controlled substance or substances as contraband.

(4) CRIME CATEGORY 4: Supplying Contraband shall be ranked at Crime Category 4 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 5, 6 or 7.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
