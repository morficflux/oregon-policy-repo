## Full text

213-018-0005 Abandonment of a Child (ORS 163.535)

(1) CRIME CATEGORY 7: Abandonment of a Child shall be ranked at Crime Category 7 if the child victim was placed in immediate danger as a consequence of the offender’s criminal conduct.

(2) CRIME CATEGORY 3: Abandonment of a Child shall be ranked at Crime Category 3, if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 7.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
