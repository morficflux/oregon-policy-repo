## Full text

213-018-0055 Rape I (ORS 163.375)

(1) CRIME CATEGORY 10: Rape I shall be ranked at Crime Category 10 if one or more of the following factors was included in the commission of the offense: (a) The offender used or threatened to use a weapon; (b) The offender caused or threatened to cause serious physical injury; (c) The victim was under the age of twelve; or (d) The victim was incapable of consent by reason of mental defect, mental incapacitation or physical helplessness.

(2) CRIME CATEGORY 9: Rape I shall be ranked at Crime Category 9 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 10.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669

History: CJC 1-1999, f. & cert. ef. 11-1-99
