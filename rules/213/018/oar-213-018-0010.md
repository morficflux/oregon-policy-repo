## Full text

213-018-0010 Aggravated Theft (ORS 164.057)

(1) CRIME CATEGORY 6: Aggravated Theft shall be ranked at Crime Category 6 if the amount of loss caused by the criminal conduct was more than $50,000.

(2) CRIME CATEGORY 5: Aggravated Theft shall be ranked at Crime Category 5 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 6.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669

History: CJC 1-1999, f. & cert. ef. 11-1-99
