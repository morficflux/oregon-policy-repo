## Full text

213-018-0020 Assault I (ORS 163.185)

(1) CRIME CATEGORY 10: Assault I shall be ranked at Crime Category 10 if the offender violated ORS 163.185(1)(b) or if the victim(s) did not substantially contribute to the commission of the offense by precipitating the attack.

(2) CRIME CATEGORY 9: Assault I shall be ranked at Crime Category 9 if the victim(s) substantially contributed to the commission of the offense by precipitating the attack.
