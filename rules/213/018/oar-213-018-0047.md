## Full text

213-018-0047 Manslaughter II (ORS 163.125)

(1) CRIME CATEGORY 9: Manslaughter II shall be ranked at Crime Category 9 if the manslaughter resulted from the operation of a motor vehicle and the driver of the motor vehicle was driving while under the influence of intoxicants.

(2) CRIME CATEGORY 8: Manslaughter II shall be ranked at Crime Category 8 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 9.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 & 2003 OL Ch. 815 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 & OL Ch. 815
