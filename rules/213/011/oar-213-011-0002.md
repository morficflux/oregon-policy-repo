## Full text

213-011-0002 Responsibility for Post-Prison Supervision Upon release from prison, the offender shall be supervised by the Department or the corrections agency designated by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
