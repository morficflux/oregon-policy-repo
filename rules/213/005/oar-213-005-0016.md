## Full text

213-005-0016 Departure Probationary Sentences A probationary sentence which exceeds the applicable limitation on the use of custodial supervision as part of a probationary sentence is a departure.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
