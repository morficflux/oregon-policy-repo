# Other (OAR 213-004-0004)

## At a glance

## Full text

213-004-0004 Other Unranked Offenses Except for Aggravated Murder, Murder I, and Murder II, when a person is convicted of any other felony which is omitted from the Crime Seriousness Scale, the sentencing judge shall determine the appropriate crime category for the current crime of conviction and shall state on the record the reasons for the offense classification.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669 & SB 1013 (2019)
