## Full text

213-004-0008 Person Class A Adult Misdemeanor Convictions Every two prior adult convictions of person Class A misdemeanors in the offender’s criminal history shall be counted as one adult conviction of a person felony for criminal history purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
