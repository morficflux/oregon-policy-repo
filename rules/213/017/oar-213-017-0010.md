## Full text

213-017-0010 Crime Category 2 The following offenses are classified at crime category 2 on the Crime Seriousness Scale:

(1) CONTROLLED SUBSTANCE OFFENSES – See division 19.

(2) CANNABIS OFFENSES – See division 21.

(3) ORS Chapter 59 — BLUE SKY LAWS & SECURITIES LAWS* — (C).

(4) ORS 164.055 — THEFT I* — (C).

(5) ORS 164.065 — THEFT OF LOST/MISLAID PROPERTY * — (C).

(6) ORS 164.075 — THEFT BY EXTORTION* — (B).

(7) ORS 164.085 — THEFT BY DECEPTION* — (C).

(8) ORS 164.125 — THEFT OF SERVICES* — (C).

(9) ORS 164.135 — UNAUTHORIZED USE OF VEHICLE* — (C).

(10) ORS 164.138 — CRIMINAL POSSESSION OF A RENTED OR LEASED MOTOR VEHICLE* — (C).

(11) ORS 164.140(4) — POSSESSION OF RENTED PROPERTY* — (C).

(12) ORS 164.215 — BURGLARY II* — (C).

(13) ORS 164.315 — ARSON II* — (C).

(14) ORS 164.365 — CRIMINAL MISCHIEF I* — (C).

(15) ORS 164.377(5) — COMPUTER FRAUD (LOTTERY)* — (C).

(16) ORS 164.377(5) — COMPUTER CRIME* — (C).

(17) ORS 164.868 — UNLAWFUL LABEL SOUND RECORDING* — (C).

(18) ORS 164.869 — UNLAWFUL RECORD LIVE PERFORMANCE* — (C).

(19) ORS 164.872 — UNLAWFUL LABEL VIDEOTAPE* — (C).

(20) ORS 164.889 — INTERFERE W/AGRICULTURAL RESEARCH* — (C).

(21) ORS 165.013 — FORGERY I — (C).

(22) ORS 165.022 — CRIMINAL POSSESSION FORGED INSTRUMENT I* — (C).

(23) ORS 165.055(3)(A) — CREDIT CARD FRAUD* — (C).

(24) ORS 165.065 — NEGOTIATING BAD CHECKS* — (C).

(25) ORS 165.074 — UNLAWFUL FACTORING PAYMENT CARD * — (C).

(26) ORS 165.085 — SPORTS BRIBERY — (C).

(27) ORS 165.090 — RECEIVING SPORTS BRIBE — (C).

(28) ORS 165.579 — CELLULAR COUNTERFEIT II — (C).

(29) ORS 165.800 — IDENTITY THEFT* — (C).

(30) ORS 165.813 — UNLAWFUL POSSESSION FICTICIOUS ID — (C).

(31) ORS 166.642 — FELON POSSESS SOFT BODY ARMOR — (C).

(32) ORS 167.164 — POSSESS GRAY MACHINE — (C)

(33) ORS 167.167 — CHEATING AT GAMBLING — (C).

(34) ORS 167.388 — INTERFERE LIVESTOCK PRODUCTION* — (C).

(35) ORS 305.091 — UNAUTHORIZED USE OR DISCLOSURE BY STATE OFFICER OR EMPLOYEE OF INFORMATION PERTAINING TO DELINQUENT DEBTOR TRANSMITTED TO OR FROM DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE THROUGH DATA MATCH SYSTEM — (C). (If offender knowingly causes economic loss of less than $1,000.)

(36) ORS 411.630 — OBTAINING PUBLIC ASSISTANCE UNLAWFULLY — (C).

(37) ORS 411.840 — OBTAIN/USE FOOD STAMPS UNLAWFULLY — (C).

(38) ORS 496.992(10) — VIOLATION OF WILDLIFE LAWS — (C).

(39) ORS 647.145 — TRADEMARK COUNTERFEITING II* — (C).

(40) ORS 647.150 — TRADEMARK COUNTERFEITING I* — (B).

(41) ORS 807.500 — UNLAWFUL PRODUCTION ODOT ID CARDS — (C).

(42) ORS 811.125 — ORGANIZE SPEED RACING EVENT — (C).

(43) ORS 811.540 — FELONY ATTEMPTING TO ELUDE (in a vehicle) — (C).

(44) ORS 819.300 — POSSESSION OF STOLEN VEHICLE* — (C).

(45) * Property offenses marked with an asterisk shall be ranked at Crime Category 2 if the value of the property stolen or destroyed was less than $1,000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 & 2003 OL Ch. 453 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669, 164.889, 166.642, 2003 OL Ch. 383, 453, 550, 632, 633, 2007 OL Ch. 684, SB 254 (2017), SB 302 (2017), SB 323 (2017) & HB 2328 (2019)
