## Full text

213-007-0003 Stipulated Grid Block

(1) Subject to the provisions of OAR 213-007-0002 and the approval of the sentencing judge, the district attorney and defense may stipulate to the grid block classification within the Sentencing Guidelines Grid which will provide the presumptive sentence for the offender.

(2) If the sentencing judge accepts the stipulated grid block classification and imposes a sentence other than the presumptive sentence for the stipulated grid block, the sentence is a departure.
