## Full text

213-007-0001 Permissible Plea Agreements The sentencing judge shall comply with the rules of this division when accepting a negotiated plea as a plea agreement for any offense committed on or after the effective date of these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
