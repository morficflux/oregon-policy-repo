## Full text

213-008-0006 Departure Limitations on the Use of Sanction Units

(1) A departure on the number of sanction units imposed as part of a probationary sentence shall not total more than double the maximum number of sanction units permitted as part of the sentence as described in OAR 213-005-0011(2) and (3).

(2) Notwithstanding the provisions of section

(1) of this rule, the maximum number of sanction units that may be used to impose a jail term as part of a probationary sentence shall be limited to the maximum number of sanction units included in the sentence as provided by OAR 213-005-0011(2) and (3).
