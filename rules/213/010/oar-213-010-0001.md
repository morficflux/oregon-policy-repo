## Full text

213-010-0001 Revocation of Probation The decision to revoke probation is discretionary and may be exercised upon a finding that the offender has violated one or more of the conditions of probation, or that the offender has participated in new criminal activity.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.667 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.667 - 137.669
