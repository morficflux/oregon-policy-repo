# Purpose (OAR 678-005-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

678-005-0005 Purpose The Oregon Wheat Commission (OWC) occasionally requires the services of an outside party to accomplish all or part of a project. The purpose of these rules is to specify the screening and selection procedures which will be used for personal service contracts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 578

History: WC 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 7-11-91
