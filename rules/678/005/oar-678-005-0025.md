## Full text

678-005-0025 Maintenance of RFP Mailing List The Commission Office shall maintain a Request for Proposals (RFP) Mailing List consisting of persons, businesses, organizations and other entities which have indicated the desire to be notified of contracting opportunities that are available.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 578

History: WC 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 7-11-91
