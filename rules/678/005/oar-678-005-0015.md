## Full text

678-005-0015 Definitions

(1) “Competitive Negotiation” (formal bidding) is a procurement method whereby proposals are requested from a number of sources and the Request for Proposal is publicized.

(2) “Non-Competitive Negotiation” (sole source) is a procurement through solicitation of a proposal from only one source.

(3) “Small Purchase Procedures” (informal bidding) are those relatively simple and informal procurement methods whereby price and rate quotations are obtained from a number of sources and selection made on the basis of cost and other applicable criteria.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 578
