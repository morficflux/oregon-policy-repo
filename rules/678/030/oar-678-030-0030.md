# Term Limits (OAR 678-030-0030)

## At a glance

## Full text

678-030-0030 Term Limits Each grower member and public member of the commission shall be appointed to a four-year term. Should a vacancy occur, the Director of the ODA would appoint a replacement for the remainder of the term.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 578 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 578
