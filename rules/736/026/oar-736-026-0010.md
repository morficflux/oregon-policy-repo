## Full text

736-026-0010 State Park Beaches The taking of driftwood in small amounts by visitors for souvenirs, for personal use or as gifts is permitted. The removal of large amounts of driftwood, or any wood with mechanized loading equipment, shall be prohibited except as approved by the park manager. Chainsaws must be approved by the park manager. No wood shall be removed which is imbedded in the beach or in sand dune banks.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 390 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.660

History: PR 10-1984, f. & ef. 12-12-84
