## Full text

736-005-0005 Purpose of Rule This rule establishes the standards the State Parks and Recreation Department, Office of the State Liaison Officer, shall use when performing statewide comprehensive outdoor recreation planning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.545, 183.550 & 390.180 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.180
