## Full text

736-056-0060 Disbursement of Oregon Main Street Revitalization Grant Funds The department will distribute grant funds to Grantees on a reimbursable basis after submission of billings on approved schedules specified in grant agreements. When requested by the department, Grantees shall supply additional information to substantiate billings. The department may disburse grant funds in advance if Grantees can demonstrate a compelling need.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124(1) & 390.262(9) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.262 & 390.264
