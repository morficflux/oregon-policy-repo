## Full text

736-045-0412 Dedication and Management of a State Natural Area (Reserve): Reports At regular intervals the department may make or cause to be made a record of management activities and other influences affecting each state natural area and state natural area reserve.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
