## Full text

736-045-0400 Dedication and Management of a State Natural Area (Reserve): Purpose The purpose and scope of dedication is to establish and maintain the integrity of the plan, and the program, by means of written formal recognition and protection of a natural area of land, water, or both land and water for natural heritage conservation purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
