# Purpose (OAR 736-045-0006)

## At a glance

## Full text

736-045-0006 Purpose The purpose of this division is to provide a central and continuing register of areas in Oregon that contain significant natural heritage resources and special species, and describe the process used to register properties on the Oregon Register of Natural Areas.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
