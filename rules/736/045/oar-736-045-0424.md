## Full text

736-045-0424 Dedication and Management of a State Natural Area (Reserve): Visitor Protection Owners of land may provide guard rails, fences, steps, and bridges when essential to the safety of a reasonable alert and cautious visitor.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
