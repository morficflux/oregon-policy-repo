# Purpose (OAR 736-053-0100)

## At a glance

## Full text

736-053-0100 Purpose The purpose of OAR 736-053-0100 to 736-053-0140 is to establish the procedures and criteria that the Oregon Heritage Commission, with the advice of the Oregon Historical Society and the Oregon Museums Association, will use when awarding Museum Grant Funds as provided in ORS 358.583.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124 & 358.585 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 358.583
