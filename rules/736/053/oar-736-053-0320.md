## Full text

736-053-0320 Application Procedure To apply for an event to be designated a statewide anniversary commemoration, an application must be submitted by the specified deadline. Applications must meet the format and criteria prescribed by the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 358.585 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 358.595 (2)(f)
