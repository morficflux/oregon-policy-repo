## Full text

736-053-0200 Purpose The purpose of OAR 736-053-0200 to 736-053-0235 is to establish the procedures and criteria that the Oregon Heritage Commission will use when awarding Oregon Heritage Grants as provided in ORS 358.590(3)

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 358.585 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 358.590
