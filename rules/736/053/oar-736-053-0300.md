## Full text

736-053-0300 Purpose The purpose of OAR 736-053-0300 to 736-053-0325 is to adopt regulations establishing procedures that the Oregon Heritage Commission shall use when coordinating statewide anniversary commemorations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 358.585 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 358.595(2f)
