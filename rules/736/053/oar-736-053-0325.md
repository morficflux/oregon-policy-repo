## Full text

736-053-0325 Commission Coordination of Statewide Anniversary Commemorations Commission coordination of statewide anniversary commemorations designated under OAR 736-053-0315, may include providing communication, technical and financial assistance to government and non-profit organizations that implement the commemorations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 358.585 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 358.595(2f)
