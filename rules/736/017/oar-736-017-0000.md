## Full text

736-017-0000 Purpose The purpose of this division is to establish the procedures and criteria that the Oregon Parks and Recreation Department (OPRD) will use in recommending Veterans and War Memorial Grants for funding to the commission pursuant to ORS 390.180(1)(d).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.180(1)(d)
