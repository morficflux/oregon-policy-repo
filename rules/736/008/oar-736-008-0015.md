## Full text

736-008-0015 Federal Requirements The Land and Water Conservation Fund Act of 1965, as amended, provides matching grants to states and local units of government for acquisition and development of public outdoor recreation areas and facilities. All applicants for federal funding assistance must also satisfy the requirements delineated in the “National Park Service’s Land and Water Conservation Fund Grants Manual,” Volume 71 (March 11, 2021).
