## Full text

736-027-0025 Preservation of Salvage The Parks and Recreation Department does not have the resources to preserve and protect salvage from vandalism, theft and natural forces. It shall be the responsibility of the salvage owner to preserve and protect any and all salvage belonging to the owner.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.545, 184 & 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.660

History: PR 13-1992, f. & cert. ef. 11-12-92 PR 15-1984, f. & ef. 12-12-84
