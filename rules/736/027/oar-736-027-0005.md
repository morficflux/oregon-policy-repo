# Policy (OAR 736-027-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 736-027-0005 — Policy. Chapter 736, Division 027. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

736-027-0005 Policy This rule is adopted pursuant to ORS 390.660 in order to ensure that salvageable objects and materials which are washed up on Oregon ocean shores do not constitute a hazard to beach users or to the beach environment, and that they shall be returned when possible to their rightful owners.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 390 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.660

History: PR 15-1984, f. & ef. 12-12-84
