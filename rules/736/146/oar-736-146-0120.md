## Full text

736-146-0120 Ethics in Sole Source The department may not select a sole-source procurement pursuant to ORS 279B.075 and avoid a competitive procurement if the purpose of the selection is to primarily benefit the contractor, directly or indirectly, to the detriment of the department or the best interest of the state.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.0656)(a) & ORS 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.0656)(a), ORS 279A.070, ORS 279B.075 & ORS 279A.140
