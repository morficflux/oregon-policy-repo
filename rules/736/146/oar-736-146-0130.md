## Full text

736-146-0130 Fragmentation The department may not artificially divide or fragment a procurement so as to constitute a small procurement, pursuant to ORS 279B.065, or an intermediate procurement, pursuant to ORS 279B.070.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065(6)(a) & ORS 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065(6)(a), ORS 279A.070 & 279B.065
