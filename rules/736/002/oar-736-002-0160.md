## Full text

736-002-0160 Fees

(1) The Department may charge a fee for acquiring criminal offender information for use in making a fitness determination. The fee shall not exceed the actual cost of aquiring criminal offender information on the subject individual.

(2) The Department shall not charge a fee if the subject individual is a Department employee, a Department volunteer, or an applicant for employment or a volunteer position with the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181A.195, 180.267 & 390.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181A.195 & 390.200
