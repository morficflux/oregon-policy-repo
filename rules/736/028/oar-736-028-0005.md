# Purpose (OAR 736-028-0005)

## Full text

736-028-0005 Purpose Pursuant to Chapter 673, Oregon Laws 1973 and Chapter 395, Oregon Laws 1985 (ORS 390.270–390.290), the Department of Parks and Recreation shall provide matching fund grants to cities, counties, and Rural Fire Protection Districts (RFPD’s) providing lifesaving services on the ocean shore. These grants shall be distributed in the amounts and under the conditions set forth in Chapter 673 and in these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.545 & 390.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.280
