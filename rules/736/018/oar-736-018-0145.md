## Full text

736-018-0145 Log Export Rules: Enforcement Investigation of suspected violations of these rules and/or surveillance of unprocessed timber in transit and at port facilities may be conducted by the Department, or contracted by the Department to other state or federal agencies. Any alleged violations of the export prohibition provisions of this section will be referred by the Department to the appropriate federal or state agency for prosecution or other legal action.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.121(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.121
