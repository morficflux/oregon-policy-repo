## Full text

736-018-0120 Log Export Rules: Applicable State Timber All unprocessed timber, as defined in OAR 736-018-0105, which originates from state lands is prohibited from export.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.121(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.121
