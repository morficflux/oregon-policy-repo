## Full text

736-018-0125 Log Export Rules: Surplus Timber The prohibitions against export contained in OAR 736-018-0105 to 736-018-0145 shall not apply to specific quantities of grades and species of unprocessed timber originating from state land which the United States Secretary of Agriculture or Interior has determined by rule to be surplus to the needs of timber manufacturing facilities in the United States.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.121(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.121
