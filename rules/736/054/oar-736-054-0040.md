## Full text

736-054-0040 Permits to Restore, Maintain and Preserve an Abandoned Cemetery Purpose This division establishes the procedures, criteria and requirements that the Oregon Parks and Recreation Department (OPRD) will use in issuing a permit to restore, maintain, and preserve an abandoned cemetery.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 226.640(6) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 226.640
