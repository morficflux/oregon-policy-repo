## Full text

736-054-0000 Purpose The purpose of this division is to establish the procedures and criteria that the Oregon Commission on Historic Cemeteries will use in recommending Historic Cemetery Grants pursuant to ORS 97.780(3).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124(1) & 390.131 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 97.780
