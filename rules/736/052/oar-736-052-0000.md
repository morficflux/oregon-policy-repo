## Full text

736-052-0000 Purpose The department is required to adopt administrative rules by ORS 358.640(4). This division provides the department procedure for considering a request for approval from a state agency to transfer, sell, demolish, substantially alter or otherwise dispose of any historic artifacts pursuant to ORS 358.640(3) and 358.647. This division specifically excludes real property matters governed by ORS 358.653 and archaeological objects provided for in ORS 358.905 to 358.961.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 358.640(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 358.640(3) & 358.647
