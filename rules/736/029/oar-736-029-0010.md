## Full text

736-029-0010 Purpose

(1) This division governs the use of portions of the ocean shore as well as areas adjacent to state parks that the State Land Board has designated as a Marine Reserve or Marine Protected Area (OAR chapter 141, division 142). The Fish and Wildlife Commission has adopted prohibitions and allowances on harvest of fish and wildlife resources (OAR chapter 635, division 12). The Department of State Lands (DSL) has adopted rules regarding authorized uses within the marine reserves and marine protected areas.

(2) This division shall go into effect on June 30, 2011 to coincide with the effective date of Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife (ODFW) prohibitions and allowances.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124 & 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.635; 390.660 & 2009 OL Ch. 847
