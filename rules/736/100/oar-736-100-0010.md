## Full text

736-100-0010 Goal Two — Promote Outdoor Recreation in Oregon The Oregon Parks and Recreation Department is the state’s principal advocate, leader and source of expertise and support for outdoor recreation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.010 & 390.117
