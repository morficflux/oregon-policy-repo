## Full text

736-100-0070 Goal Seven — Provide Varied, High-quality Camping and Other Overnight Experiences Camping is a cornerstone of the Oregon state park experience. Demand for camping and other overnight stays in parks is increasing and becoming more varied, and the Department must respond quickly and creatively to that demand.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.010 & 390.117
