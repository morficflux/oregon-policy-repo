## Full text

736-100-0030 Goal Four — Preserve Oregon's Rich Cultural Heritage and Broaden Public Understanding of Oregon's Historic Places and Events The cultural history of Oregon — structures, landmarks, and special places — extends beyond the boundaries of park properties. The Heritage Conservation Division safeguards this public trust.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.010 & 390.117
