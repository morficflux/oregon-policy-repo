## Full text

736-100-0040 Goal One — Acquire Properties that Build Upon the Diversity and Strength of Our Current System The Oregon state park system is a rich mosaic of Oregon's natural resources, scenic landscapes and history. In 1998, voters sent a clear message of support for the enhancement of that mosaic through the development, conservation and expansion of Oregon’s park system for future generations.
