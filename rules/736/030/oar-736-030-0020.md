## Full text

736-030-0020 Prohibition of Camping Overnight camping, including overnight sleeping in tents, driftwood shelters, sleeping bags, recreational vehicles, trailers or automobiles, on the ocean shore within the city limits of Cannon Beach, Lincoln City, Seaside, Newport, Bandon, Gold Beach, Brookings, Rockaway Beach and Manzanita is prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.124 & 390.660
