## Full text

736-030-0005 Prohibition of Horses and Other Livestock Horses and other livestock are prohibited on the ocean shore between the south city limits of Rockaway and northward to the mouth of the Nehalem River in Tillamook County.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.124 & 390.660
