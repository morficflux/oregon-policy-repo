## Full text

736-030-0070 Prohibition of Launching Boats, Watercraft The launching of any rigid watercraft such as metal or fiberglass canoes and boats and jet skis on the ocean shore within the city limits of Seaside and Cannon Beach is prohibited except by permit from the city. This does not apply to inflatable rafts and boats, inner tubes or swimmer safety devices. The launching of surfboards from Avenue “U” north in Seaside is also prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.124 & 390.660

History: PR 11-1994, f. 11-29-94, cert. ef. 12-1-94
