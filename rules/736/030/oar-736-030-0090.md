# Beach (OAR 736-030-0090)

## Full text

736-030-0090 Beach Lifeguard Authority Within the city limits of Cannon Beach, city lifeguards shall have the authority to restrict the launching of inflatable devices by individuals, on a case-by-case basis, when necessary for public safety or the safety of an individual. No person shall disregard a city lifeguard’s instructions relating to the launching of inflatable devices within the city limits of Cannon Beach.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.124 & 390.660

History: PRD 11-1998, f. 7-30-98, cert. ef. 7-31-98
