## Full text

736-030-0050 Prohibition of Fireworks There shall be no fireworks permitted on the ocean shore within the city limits of Seaside, except by joint permit from the respective city and the Area One Park Manager. The definition of fireworks is found in OAR 837-012-0072(19).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.124 & 390.660

History: PR 11-1994, f. 11-29-94, cert. ef. 12-1-94
