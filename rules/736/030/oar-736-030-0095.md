## Full text

736-030-0095 Prohibition of Camping on Beaches in Clatsop County Camping on the ocean shore between the Necanicum River and the South Jetty of the Columbia River is prohibited. For the purposes of this rule, camping includes, but is not limited to, erecting a tent or temporary shelter or arranging bedding, or both, or parking a recreation vehicle or other vehicle in such a manner as will permit remaining overnight, or for other extended periods.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.635 & 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS
