## Full text

736-030-0060 Prohibition of Playing Golf Playing golf on the ocean shore within the city limits of Seaside is prohibited except by permit from the city.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.124 & 390.660

History: PR 11-1994, f. 11-29-94, cert. ef. 12-1-94
