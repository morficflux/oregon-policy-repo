# Purpose (OAR 736-023-0000)

## At a glance

## Full text

736-023-0000 Purpose These rules set forth the criteria and procedure by which the Parks and Recreation Department will periodically review the vegetation line established under ORS 390.770 so that the Parts and Recreation Commission may recommend adjustments to that line to the legislature pursuant to ORS 390.755.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 390.124 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 390.755

History: PR 10-1994, f. 11-29-94, cert. ef. 12-1-94
