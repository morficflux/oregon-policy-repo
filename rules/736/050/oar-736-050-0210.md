## Full text

736-050-0210 State Advisory Committee on Historic Preservation: Statutory Authority ORS 358.617 requires the State Historic Preservation Officer to adopt rules to carry out the duties and functions of the officer, including rules governing cultural resource management programs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 358.617 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 358.617
