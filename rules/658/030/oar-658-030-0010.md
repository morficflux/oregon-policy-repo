## Full text

658-030-0010 Number of Commissioners, Terms The Oregon Potato Commission will consist of ten

(10) commissioners appointed by the Director of the Oregon Department of Agriculture for a term of four years or, if for a term following a prior term for that person, ending four years from the date of expiration of any prior term.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576
