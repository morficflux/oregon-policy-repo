## Full text

658-020-0025 Maintenance of RFP Mailing List The Commission Office shall maintain a Request for Proposals (RFP) Mailing List consisting of persons, businesses, organizations and other entities which have indicated the desire to be notified of contracting opportunities that are available. An OPC business form will be used to place persons on the list.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.304
