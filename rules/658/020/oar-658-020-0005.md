# Purpose (OAR 658-020-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

658-020-0005 Purpose The Oregon Potato Commission (OPC) occasionally requires the services of an outside party to accomplish all or part of a project. The purpose of these rules is to specify the screening and selection procedures which will be used for personal service contracts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.304

History: PC 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 6-19-91
