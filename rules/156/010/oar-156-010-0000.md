## Full text

156-010-0000 Comparability of Work

(1) The Legislative Administration Committee shall use a neutral and objective method to determine the comparability of the value of work performed by Legislative Branch employees. The Legislative Administration Committee shall use this method to determine the compensation and classification system for these employees pursuant to ORS 292.951.

(2) The chosen methodology shall include an equal pay analysis which ensures the chosen method eliminates unlawful wage differentials required by equal pay laws.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 292 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 292.956
