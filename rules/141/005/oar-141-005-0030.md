# Emergencies (OAR 141-005-0030)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

141-005-0030 Emergencies In case of an emergency, a meeting of the State Land Board may be canceled, or a meeting may be held upon such notice as is appropriate and reasonable under the circumstances.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192 & 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.035
