## Full text

141-005-0040 Executive Sessions An executive session of the board may be held during a regular, special, or emergency meeting when authorized under ORS 192.660, after identifying the specific authorization for holding the executive session. When an executive session only is to be held as a regular, special or emergency meeting, notice shall be given to the members of the board, interested persons, wire services, newspapers, radio and television stations as appropriate for a special, emergency, or regular meeting. The notice shall state the specific provision of law authorizing the executive session.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192 & 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.035
