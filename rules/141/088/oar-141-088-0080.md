## Full text

141-088-0080 Restrictions for the State-Owned Banks of the Columbia River in the Vicinity of the Gary Island All state-owned land that is under the jurisdiction of the Department that is between the line of ordinary high water and the line of ordinary low water on the south bank of the Columbia River in the vicinity of Gary Island between River Mile 123.5 and 124.5 is closed to any use without prior written authorization from the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 273 & 274 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273 & 274

History: DSL 2-2004, f. & cert. ef. 6-11-04
