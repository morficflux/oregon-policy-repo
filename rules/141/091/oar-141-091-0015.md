# Billing (OAR 141-091-0015)

## At a glance

## Full text

141-091-0015 Billing Charges shall be paid in cash at the time of request for copies except that at the discretion of the Director or any Assistant Director, billing may accompany mailed copies. State agencies or other governmental bodies may be billed for copies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192 & 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192 & 273
