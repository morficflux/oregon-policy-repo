## Full text

141-070-0170 Leases Issued Prior to the Effective Date of These Rules All upland oil and gas lease agreements issued prior to the effective date of these administrative rules remain subject to the provisions of OAR 141-072-0205 et seq., under division 72 of chapter 141, Division of State Lands.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790
