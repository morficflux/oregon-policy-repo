## Full text

141-070-0160 Lease Termination At the expiration of the lease or upon sooner termination, the lessee shall execute and deliver to the state a release or a recorded quitclaim of the leased premises if the original lease was recorded.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790
