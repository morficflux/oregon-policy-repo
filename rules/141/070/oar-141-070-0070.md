## Full text

141-070-0070 Rejection The Director may reject any application that is not in compliance with these rules or that is not in the public interest. If rejected, all fees shall be returned to applicant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790
