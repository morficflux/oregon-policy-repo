## Full text

141-142-0030 Otter Rock Marine Reserve

(1) All state-owned submerged and submersible land bounded by the following points is within the Otter Rock Marine Reserve: (a) Beginning from a point at Latitude 44° 45.17' (44° 45’10” N ), Longitude -124° 3.86’ (124° 3’52” W) (Point A); (b) Then following the MHWL south to Latitude 44° 43.31' (44° 45' 10.5" N), Longitude -124° 3.66' (124° 3' 51.5" W) (Point B); (c) Then west to Latitude 44° 43.31' (44° 44' 47.6" N), Longitude -124° 4.2' (124° 4' 12" W) (Point C); (d) Then north to Latitude 44° 45.17’ (44° 45’10” N), Longitude -124° 4.53’ (124° 4’32” W) (Point D); (e) Then in an easterly direction back to the point of beginning (Point A).

(2) All coordinate information is provided in WGS84 coordinate system (geographic latitude/longitude) and has not been verified by a licensed surveyor.
