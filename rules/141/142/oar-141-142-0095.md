## Full text

141-142-0095 Cape Falcon Shoreside Marine Protected Area

(1) All state-owned submerged and submersible land bounded by the following points is within the Cape Falcon Shoreside Marine Protected Area: (a) Beginning from a point at Latitude 45° 48.00' (45° 48' 0" N), Longitude -123° 58.10' (123° 58' 6" W) (Point A); (b) Then south along the ELWL to Latitude 45° 46.80' (45° 46' 48" N), Longitude -123° 58.17' (123° 58' 10.2" W) (Point B); (c) Then west to a point at Latitude 45° 46.79’ (45° 46’ 47“ N), Longitude -123° 58.37’ (123° 58’ 22“ W) (Point C); (d) Then north to a point at Latitude 45° 48.00 (45° 48’ 0” N), Longitude -123° 58.25’ (123° 58’ 15” W) (Point D); (e) Then in an easterly direction back to the point of beginning (Point A).

(2) All coordinate information is provided in WGS84 coordinate system (geographic latitude/longitude) and has not been verified by a licensed surveyor.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.555 & ORS 273.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.555 & ORS 196.540
