## Full text

141-142-0040 Redfish Rocks Marine Protected Area

(1) All state-owned submerged and submersible land bounded by the following points is within the Redfish Rocks Marine Protected Area: (a) Beginning from a point at Latitude 42° 41.16' (42° 41' 9.6" N), Longitude -124° 28.86' (124° 28' 51.6" W) (Point A); (b) Then north to Latitude 42° 42.54' (42° 42' 32.4" N), Longitude -124° 29.64' (124° 29' 38.4" W) (Point B); (c) Then west to Latitude 42° 41.90' (42° 41' 54" N), Longitude -124° 32.50' (124° 32' 30" W) (Point C); (d) Then south to Latitude 42° 40.25' (42° 40' 15" N), Longitude -124° 32.50' (124° 32' 30" W) (Point D); (e) Then in an easterly direction back to the point of beginning (Point A).

(2) All coordinate information is provided in WGS84 coordinate system (geographic latitude/longitude) and has not been verified by a licensed surveyor.
