## Full text

141-142-0075 Cascade Head South Marine Protected Area

(1) All state-owned submerged and submersible land bounded by the following points is within the Cascade Head South Marine Protected Area: (a) Beginning from a point at Latitude 44° 56.50' (45° 56' 30" N), Longitude -124° 1.47' (124° 1' 28.2" W) (Point A); (b) Then west to Latitude 44° 56.50' (45° 56' 30" N), Longitude -124° 5.00' (124° 5' 0" W) (Point B); (c) Then south to Latitude 44° 59.25' (44° 59' 15" N), Longitude -124° 5.00' (124° 5' 0" W) (Point C); (d) Then east to Latitude 44° 59.25' (44° 59' 15" N), Longitude -124° 0.89' (124° 0’ 53” W) (Point D); (e) Then in a southerly direction along the MHWL back to the point of beginning (Point A).

(2) All coordinate information is provided in WGS84 coordinate system (geographic latitude/longitude) and has not been verified by a licensed surveyor.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.555 & ORS 273.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.555 & ORS 196.540
