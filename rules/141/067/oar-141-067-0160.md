## Full text

141-067-0160 Eligibility to Apply to Purchase or Exchange Land Unless otherwise indicated in these rules, any person who is at least 18 years of age and who is a citizen of the United States or who has declared an intention to become a citizen, may apply to purchase or exchange state land
