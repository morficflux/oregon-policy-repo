## Full text

141-016-0040 Log Branding and Marking Requirements All unprocessed state timber or logs originating from state timber sales shall be branded with an assigned and registered brand before removal from the sale area. Unless prevented by the size or condition of the wood one end of all logs originating from state timber sales shall be hammer branded and one end shall be painted with a paint type and color determined by the State Forester or Director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.521

History: DSL 4-2000, f. & cert. ef. 4-24-00
