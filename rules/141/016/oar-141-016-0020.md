## Full text

141-016-0020 Applicable State Timber All unprocessed timber, as defined in OAR 141-016-0005(16), which originates from state lands is prohibited from export.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.521

History: DSL 4-2000, f. & cert. ef. 4-24-00
