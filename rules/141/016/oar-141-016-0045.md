# Enforcement (OAR 141-016-0045)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

141-016-0045 Enforcement Investigation of suspected violations of these rules and/or surveillance of unprocessed timber in transit and at port facilities may be conducted by the State Forester or Director, or contracted by the State Forester or the Director to other state or federal agencies. Any alleged violations of the export prohibition provisions of this section will be referred by the State Forester or Director to the appropriate federal or state agency for prosecution or other legal action.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.521

History: DSL 4-2000, f. & cert. ef. 4-24-00
