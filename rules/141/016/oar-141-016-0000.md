## Full text

141-016-0000 Purpose and Applicability These rules apply to the sale of timber and logs from common school forest lands and to other Board-managed lands. The State Forester shall have the responsibility to apply these rules on common school forest lands and the Director of the Division of State Lands shall apply them to other Board-managed land as described in these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.521
