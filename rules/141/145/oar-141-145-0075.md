## Full text

141-145-0075 Removal of Unauthorized Structures The Department may pursue the removal of unauthorized structures on state-owned submerged and submersible land through Division 82; Rules Governing the Management of, and Issuing of Leases, Licenses and Registrations for Structures on, and Uses of State-Owned Submerged and Submersible Land.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 273 & 274 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 274
