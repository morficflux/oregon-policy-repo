# Pre-Application (OAR 141-145-0020)

## At a glance

OAR 141-145-0020 — Pre-Application. Chapter 141, Division 145. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

141-145-0020 Pre-Application Requirements Prospective applicants for an easement, conservation easement, or lease shall meet with Department staff to discuss the proposed project and use before submitting an application to the Department. This meeting may be in person or through other means acceptable to the Department. The Department may invite other government entities and affected stakeholders to take part in a pre-application meeting.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 273 & 274 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 274

History: DSL 2-2014, f. 1-13-14, cert. ef. 2-1-14
