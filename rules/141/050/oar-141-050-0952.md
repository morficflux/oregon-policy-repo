## Full text

141-050-0952 Publicity Information about a Natural Heritage Conservation Area and appropriate descriptive material may be developed and made available to interested persons. However, publicity which would tend to encourage the general public to visit a Natural Heritage Conservation Area in greater numbers than its carrying capacity shall be avoided.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
