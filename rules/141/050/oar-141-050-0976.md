## Full text

141-050-0976 Introduction and Management of Special Species The introduction into or the management of a Natural Heritage Conservation Area for special wildlife species shall be by agreement between the Board and the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife, Oregon Department of Agriculture or other agency as provided in the management scheme.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
