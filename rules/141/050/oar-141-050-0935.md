# Purpose (OAR 141-050-0935)

## At a glance

## Full text

141-050-0935 Purpose The purpose and scope of dedication is to establish and maintain the integrity of the Plan, and the Program, by means of written formal recognition and protection of an area of land and/or water for natural heritage conservation purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591

History: LB 9-1982, f. & ef. 10-1-82
