## Full text

141-050-0960 Natural Heritage Conservation Area Manager Notwithstanding the instruments of dedication, managers of a Natural Heritage Conservation Area shall not take any action or fail to take any action which is in conflict with a statute, rule regulation or policy relating to an agency having an interest in or responsibility for the Natural Heritage Conservation Area.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591

History: LB 9-1982, f. & ef. 10-1-82 LB 18, f. 7-29-74, ef. 8-25-74
