## Full text

141-050-0999 Waiver of Rules The State Land Board may waive all or any part of these rules which would prevent the establishment, management, or protection of a Natural Heritage Conservation Area if such rule is in conflict with a statute, rule, regulation, or policy relating to an agency having an interest in or responsibility for the Natural Heritage Conservation Area.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591

History: LB 9-1982, f. & ef. 10-1-82 LB 18, f. 7-29-74, ef. 8-25-74
