# Fire (OAR 141-050-0962)

## At a glance

## Full text

141-050-0962 Fire Prescribed fire may only be used as a management tool in such areas or situations where fire is needed to maintain or protect a Natural Heritage Conservation Area as an ecosystem type specified in the management scheme.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591

History: LB 9-1982, f. & ef. 10-1-82 LB 18, f. 7-29-74, ef. 8-25-74
