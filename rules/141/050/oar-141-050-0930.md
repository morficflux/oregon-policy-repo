## Full text

141-050-0930 Register Withdrawal Registered natural areas may be withdrawn from the Register by the State Land Board upon the recommendation of the Council. Specific reasons must be given for withdrawal.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
