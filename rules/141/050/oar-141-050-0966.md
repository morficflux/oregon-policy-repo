## Full text

141-050-0966 Visitor Protection Guard rails, fences, steps, and bridges may be provided when essential to the safety of a reasonable alert and cautious visitor.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
