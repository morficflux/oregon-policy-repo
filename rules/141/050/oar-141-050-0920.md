## Full text

141-050-0920 Location and Maintenance of Register The data files of the Oregon Register of Natural Heritage Resources will be located at the Oregon Natural Heritage Information Center office in Portland, OR, and will be maintained by the Council.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
