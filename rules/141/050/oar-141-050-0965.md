## Full text

141-050-0965 Disturbance of Natural Features The management of Natural Heritage Conservation Areas shall not include the cutting or removal of vegetation or the disturbance of other natural features, except that which is essential to carry out the management scheme enumerated in these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
