## Full text

141-050-0996 Other Structures and Improvements Signs and temporary research installations may be permitted within a Natural Heritage Conservation Area. No other structures or facilities shall be located within a Natural Heritage Conservation Area except as provided in the management scheme on these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
