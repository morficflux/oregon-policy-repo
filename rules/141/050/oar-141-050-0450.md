## Full text

141-050-0450 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide a central and continuing register of areas in Oregon which contain significant natural heritage resources and special species, and describe the process used to register properties on the Oregon Register of Natural Heritage Resources. Ref. ORS 273.581, Chapter 208, Oregon Laws 1981.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273 .563 - 273 .591
