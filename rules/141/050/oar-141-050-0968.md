## Full text

141-050-0968 Scenic or Landscape Management No measures or actions shall be taken to alter the natural growth or features of a Natural Heritage Conservation Area for the purpose of enhancing its neatness, beauty, or amenities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.563 - 273.591
