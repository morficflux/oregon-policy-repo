## Full text

141-122-0080 Assignment of Easements

(1) An easement in good standing is transferable. Prior written consent of the Department is required prior to any transfer of the easement. No transfer may increase the burden on the estate or detract from the value of the underlying state land.

(2) The Holder of an easement wanting to transfer their easement must submit to the Department: (a) Notice of proposed transfer on a form provided by the Department at least 60 calendar days prior to the date that the transfer is to occur; and (b) Non-refundable transfer processing fee of $1,000.00 payable to the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.761, 274.040, 274.720, 376.620, 530.050, 530.490 & 758.010
