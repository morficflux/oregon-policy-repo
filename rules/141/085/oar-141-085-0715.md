## Full text

141-085-0715 Mitigation for Temporary Impacts Applicants for projects that involve temporary impacts to waters of this state must provide a rehabilitation plan for rectification of temporary impacts. Rectification must include re-establishment of pre-existing contours and pre-existing vegetation. A monitoring plan to confirm the reestablishment of waters of this state, or reestablishment of vegetation may be required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.600-196.692 & ORS 196.825 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.600-196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990
