# Purpose (OAR 141-060-0005)

## Full text

141-060-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide criteria and procedures to be used by the Oregon State Land Board and the Division of State Lands in distributing monies from the Oregon Rural Rehabilitation (ORR) Fund. The rules are to be construed in accordance with ORS 566.310 through 566.360 and with the 1975 Use Agreement between the Board and the U.S. Department of Agriculture, Farmers Home Administration Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 566 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 566.310 - 566.360

History: LB 3-1987, f. & ef. 5-6-87
