## Full text

141-089-0620 General

(1) Special Headings and Fonts. Where headings, special fonts or double-spacing are used, they are for convenience only and have no substantive effect.

(2) Applicability of OAR 141-085. Unless otherwise specified under OAR 141-089, the provisions of OAR 141-085 apply to General Authorizations (GA).
