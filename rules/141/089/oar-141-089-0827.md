## Full text

141-089-0827 Non-motorized In-stream Placer Mining within ESH - Expiration and No Transfer

(1) Expiration of In-stream Placer Mining Authorizations. Authorizations issued under this GA expire on December 31 of each calendar year.

(2) No Transfer of Authorization. An authorization cannot be transferred to another person.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.600-196.692 & 196.795-196.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.600-196.692 & 196.795-196.990
