# The Purpose of the Protection Category (OAR 141-120-0050)

## At a glance

## Full text

141-120-0050 The Purpose of the Protection Category The purpose of placing wetlands or portions of wetlands into the Protection Category is to protect important wetland resources in perpetuity except as specified in OAR 141-120-0020(7), and preserve them for the functions and values they provide for the people of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.692 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.678 - 196.692, 215 & 227.350
