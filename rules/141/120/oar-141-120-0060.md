# Management (OAR 141-120-0060)

## At a glance

OAR 141-120-0060 — Management. Chapter 141, Division 120. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

141-120-0060 Management Directive for the Protection Category Local governments shall ensure that areas in the Protection Category are managed to:

(1) Allow each site’s natural ecological processes to occur; or

(2) Maintain or enhance the existing wetland type, functions or values.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.692 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.678 - 196.692, 215 & 227.350

History: LB 6-1994, f. & cert. ef. 10-20-94
