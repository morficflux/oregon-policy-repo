## Full text

141-120-0140 The Purpose of the Development Category The purpose of placing wetlands or portions of wetlands into the Development Category is to help balance the public’s need for development with the need to protect and conserve important wetland resources.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.692 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.678 - 196.692, 215 & 227.350
