## Full text

141-120-0230 Citation of Statutory Authority ORS 196.692 authorizes the Division of State Lands to adopt rules to carry out the provisions of ORS 196.668 through 196.692, Wetland Inventory and Wetland Conservation Plans.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.692 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.678 - 196.692, 215 & 227.350
