## Full text

141-120-0170 Uses Allowed in Development Category Wetlands A local government may allow the following uses after reviewing the proposed use against criteria and standards set out in the WCP implementing ordinances, that ensure that the uses meet the management directives in OAR 141-120-0150 and are intended to achieve WCP Goals:

(1) All uses allowed in the WCP and implementing zoning ordinances;

(2) Construction of committed infrastructure; and

(3) Biofiltration or other wetland water treatment systems provided the proposed project meets all applicable water quality standards.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.692 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.678 - 196.692, 215 & 227.350
