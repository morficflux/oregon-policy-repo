# Confidentiality (OAR 141-010-0280)

## Full text

141-010-0280 Confidentiality All proprietary information submitted by an applicant or permittee pursuant to these rules shall be treated as confidential trade secrets under ORS 192.500(1)(b) and shall not be disclosed to the public unless released by the permittee. Proprietary information includes, but is not limited to, the exact location of a survey and survey results.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 274 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 274.735

History: LB 7-1986, f. & ef. 7-18-86
