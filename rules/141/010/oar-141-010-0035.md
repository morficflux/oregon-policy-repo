# Refund (OAR 141-010-0035)

## Full text

141-010-0035 Refund Checks enclosed with the bids will be returned to the bidders upon request, except in the case of the successful bidder. Such requests are to be submitted on a form supplied by the Board. The execution of a request for refund shall be determined a withdrawal of the bidder’s offer and a waiver of all rights the bidder may have in connection with or by virtue of said bid.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 274 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 274.735

History: LB 6, f. & ef. 9-17-64
