# Authorization (OAR 141-010-0025)

## At a glance

## Full text

141-010-0025 Authorization Bids submitted by corporations or partnerships shall contain an affidavit or a certificate of authority verifying that the company official, agent, or partner signing the lease and bid offer is duly authorized to enter into such a contract for the bidder.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 274 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 274.735
