## Full text

141-082-0275 Application Requirements for a Lease or Public Facility License

(1) Any person wanting to use state-owned submerged and submersible land that is subject to a lease or public facility license must, using a form provided by the Department, apply for and obtain the required authorization prior to using the submerged and submersible land.

(2) All applications for a lease or public facility license must be fully completed and accompanied by a non-refundable fee payable to the Department in the amount of $750.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 273 & 274 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 274
