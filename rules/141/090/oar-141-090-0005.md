## Full text

141-090-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish standards and procedures by which the Department of State Lands makes jurisdictional determinations for the purpose of regulating fill and removal within waters of this state. These rules also establish minimum standards for wetland delineation reports submitted to the Department for review and the procedures for Department review and approval.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.845 & 196.692 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.800 – 196.990, 196.600 – 196.665, 196.668 – 196.692 & 197.279
