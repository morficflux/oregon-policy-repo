## Full text

141-141-0150 Request for Qualifications (RFQ)

(1) The OOST may request qualifications in order to support a project or activity that has not been proposed for funding through the competitive grants process but may be necessary for the OOST to address one or more funding priorities.

(2) Proposals submitted by qualified applicants shall be managed in the same manner as outlined in OAR 141-141-0130 and 141-141-0140 above.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.565(6) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.565–196.570
