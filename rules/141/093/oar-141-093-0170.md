## Full text

141-093-0170 Minor Removal-Fill Impacts - Authorized Activities This GP authorizes a person to conduct removal-fill activity in certain non-tidal wetlands in an amount equal to or less than two-tenths (0.20) of an acre per project.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990
