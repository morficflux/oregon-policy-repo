## Full text

141-093-0125 Enforcement Failure to adhere to the terms of any authorization issued under this division is a violation of the Removal-Fill Law and may be subject to appropriate enforcement in accordance with OAR 141-085.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990
