## Full text

141-093-0100 General

(1) Special Headings and Fonts. Where headings, special fonts or double-spacing are used, they are for the convenience of the user only and have no substantive effect.

(2) Applicability of OAR 141-085. Unless otherwise specified under 141-093, the provisions of 141-085 apply to General Permits (GPs).
