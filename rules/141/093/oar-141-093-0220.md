# Maintenance (OAR 141-093-0220)

## Full text

141-093-0220 Maintenance Drainage - Purpose This General Permit (GP) authorizes removal of up to 100 cubic yards of material and fill of up to 100 cubic yards of material for the purpose of maintaining drainage to protect agricultural lands.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990
