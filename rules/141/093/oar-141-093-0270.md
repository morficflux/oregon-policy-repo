## Full text

141-093-0270 Maintenance Dredging - Authorized Activities This General Permit authorizes maintenance dredging in accordance with the requirements of this Division, including the removal of material, transport of material to a placement site, and placement of material in an upland, flowlane or territorial sea location.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.817 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.600-196.990
