## Full text

141-093-0145 Transportation-Related Structures - GP-Specific Application Requirements for Authorizing Projects

(1) Compensatory Mitigation. Unless otherwise approved by the Department, compensatory mitigation options are: (a) Mitigation bank credit purchase from a Department-approved mitigation bank with a service area that includes the proposed removal-fill site and that meets the eligibility requirements for CM outlined in ORS 141-085-0690; (b) In-lieu fee credit purchase that meets the eligibility requirements for CM outlined in ORS 141-085-0690; and (c) Cash payment to the Department’s payment in-lieu program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990
