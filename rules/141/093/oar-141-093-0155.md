## Full text

141-093-0155 Minor Removal-Fill Impacts - Purpose Non-Tidal Wetland Impacts. This General Permit (GP) authorizes removal-fill activity in certain non-tidal wetlands subject to the terms and conditions contained herein.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990
