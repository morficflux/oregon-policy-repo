# Rental Rate (OAR 141-071-0600)

## At a glance

OAR 141-071-0600 — Rental Rate. Chapter 141, Division 071. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

141-071-0600 Rental Rate Rentals at the rate of $1 per acre per year shall be due and payable in advance on or before the annual anniversary of the effective date of the lease. Rentals paid each year will be deducted from royalties due accruing during the rental year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
