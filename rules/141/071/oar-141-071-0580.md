## Full text

141-071-0580 Forfeiture of Competitive Bid Leases If a successful bidder fails to execute the lease or otherwise fails to comply with these rules, his deposit will be forfeited and the bidding unit will be reoffered for competitive leasing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
