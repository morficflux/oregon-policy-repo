# Commingling (OAR 141-071-0625)

## At a glance

## Full text

141-071-0625 Commingling All production shall be maintained segregated until an approved system of measurement has accounted for the quantity and quality of the leased minerals. Approval may be obtained to commingle production upon proof that royalty payable to the state shall not be diminished.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
