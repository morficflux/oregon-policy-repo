# Bidding Requirements (OAR 141-071-0560)

## At a glance

OAR 141-071-0560 — Bidding Requirements. Chapter 141, Division 071. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

141-071-0560 Bidding Requirements Each bidder shall submit with his bid an application to lease mineral rights, the filing fee required by OAR 141-071-0420, and a certified or cashier’s check, bank draft, or money order in the amount of one-half of the amount of the applicant’s bonus bid.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
