# Bonds (OAR 141-071-0430)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

141-071-0430 Bonds Applicants for permits or mining leases shall furnish corporate surety bonds in amounts and with sureties acceptable to the Division. Bonds will be conditioned on compliance with all the terms of the permit or lease.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
