## Full text

141-071-0550 Notice of Bidding The Division will publish a notice of opportunity to bid for leases within the discovery area in a newspaper of general circulation in the county or counties where the discovery is located. The Division will provide written notice to any person or organization which has requested in writing of the Division that such notices be provided to it directly.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
