## Full text

141-071-0525 Application Rejection If application supplements are not received by the Division within the time limit specified in OAR 141-071-0520 or if such application supplements are not complete, the entire application shall be rejected and returned to the applicant with an explanation of the reasons for rejection.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790
