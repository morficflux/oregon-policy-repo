## Full text

141-071-0470 Priority Applications will be accepted in the order received and priority of permit issuance will be established on the same basis except as provided in the case of simultaneous filings.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790
