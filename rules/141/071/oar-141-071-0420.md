## Full text

141-071-0420 Fees and Service Charges The Division shall collect nonrefundable service charges to cover the expense of processing the following matters:

(1) Original application — $50 for each lease or permit.

(2) Assignment — $25 for each assignment.

(3) Mineral prospecting permit fee — $.50/acre annually.

(4) Renewal prospecting permit fee — $5/acre annually.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
