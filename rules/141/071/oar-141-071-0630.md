## Full text

141-071-0630 Assignment

(1) A mineral lease shall not be assigned, in total or in part, without written approval of the Director.

(2) Each request for total or partial assignment of lease must be accompanied by the following: (a) A completed “Assignment of Lease” form (available from the Division); (b) A fee in the amount of $50 payable by check; (c) If a corporation, written verification that the assignee is registered to conduct business in Oregon. Registration must be through the Corporation Division, Secretary of State’s Office, Salem, OR 97310.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790
