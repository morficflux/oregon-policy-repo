## Full text

141-071-0465 Application for Mineral Prospecting Permit Any qualified applicant wishing to obtain an original, amended, or renewal mineral prospecting permit from the Division shall submit at least two copies of a mineral prospecting application on a form provided by the Division together with the required fees. All such forms shall be completed in full, signed by the applicant of his (its) authorized representative with proof of authorization, and shall include two copies of all necessary exhibits. Maps shall be in reproducible form. The Division may require submission of such other information as it deems necessary to make a decision on granting, modifying, or denying the permit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
