## Full text

141-071-0585 Restoration of Bidding Units If no bids are received on a bidding unit, the Division, at its discretion, may offer the lands and mineral rights for non-competitive leasing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.780 - 273.790

History: LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
