# Purpose (OAR 141-015-0000)

## At a glance

## Full text

141-015-0000 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide a uniform procedure governing defaults on Common School timber sale contracts involving only Common School Lands or contracts in which the timber on the Common School Lands predominates in value.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.521

History: LB 1-1983, f. & ef. 4-29-83 LB 10-1982(Temp), f. & ef. 11-18-82
