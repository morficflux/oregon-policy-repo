## Full text

141-086-0045 Notice of Opportunity for Comment on Amendments The Division shall, in writing, provide notice of the opportunity for public comment on proposed amendments to a wetland conservation plan, as required by ORS 196.684(3). Such notice shall be as described in OAR 141-086-0015 and comments must be submitted as described in 141-086-0020.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 196 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.668 - 196.692

History: LB 4-1990, f. & cert. ef. 11-7-90
