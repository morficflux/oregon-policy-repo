## Full text

141-086-0030 Review of Wetland Conservation Plans As described in ORS 196.684(9), an order by the Director regarding approval, amendment or review of a wetland conservation plan shall be reviewable by the Land Use Board of Appeals as a land use decision of a state agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 196 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.668 - 196.692

History: LB 4-1990, f. & cert. ef. 11-7-90
