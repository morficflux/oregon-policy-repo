## Full text

141-086-0050 Hearing on Amendments At the Director’s discretion, a public informational hearing may be held before taking final action on proposed amendments to a wetland conservation plan. Notice of such hearing, required by ORS 196.684(3), shall be given as described in OAR 141-086-0021. Such hearing shall be held as described in 141-086-0022.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 196 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.668 - 196.692
