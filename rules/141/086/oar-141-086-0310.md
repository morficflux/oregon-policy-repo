## Full text

141-086-0310 Policy To protect the state’s wetland resources, the functions and services they provide, and all interests, it is important that clear and consistent criteria be used to identify significant wetlands for planning purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.299
