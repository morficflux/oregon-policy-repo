# Purpose (OAR 141-086-0300)

## Full text

141-086-0300 Purpose ORS 197.279(3) directs the Division of State Lands to establish these criteria and procedures for the identification of significant wetlands under Statewide Planning Goal 5. Local governments will use these technical standards to complete their planning responsibilities for wetlands, which are established by the Land Conservation and Development Commission (OAR 660-023-0100).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.299
