# License Renewals (OAR 339-010-0023)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

339-010-0023 License Renewals Each applicant for license renewal shall submit to the Board on or before May 31 of each even numbered year, a completed license renewal application and appropriate renewal fee. The renewal fees are non-refundable.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.320(10)(11) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.336 & 675.337
