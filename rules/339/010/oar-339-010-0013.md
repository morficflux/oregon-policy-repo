## Full text

339-010-0013 Law Ethics Exam Requirement Applicants for licensure for an Oregon Occupational Therapy license shall be required to take and successfully pass an Oregon Law/ethics examination with a passing score set by the board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.320 & 675.230
