## Full text

339-001-0006 Time for Requesting a Contested Case Hearing A request for a contested case hearing must be in writing and must be received by the Board within thirty days from the date the contested case notice was served.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.320(10) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
