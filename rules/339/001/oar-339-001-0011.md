## Full text

339-001-0011 Filing Exceptions and Argument to the Board After a proposed order has been served on a party, the Board shall notify the party when written exceptions must be filed to be considered by the Board, and the Board shall notify the party when the party may appear before the Board to present argument regarding the proposed order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.320(10) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
