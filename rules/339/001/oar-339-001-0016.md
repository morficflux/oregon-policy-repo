## Full text

339-001-0016 Petition for Reconsideration or Rehearing as Condition for Judicial Review All parties, including limited parties, must file a petition for reconsideration or rehearing with the Board as a condition for obtaining judicial review of any order of the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.320(10) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
