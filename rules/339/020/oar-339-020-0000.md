## Full text

339-020-0000 Continuing Education Defined Continuing education (CE) is participation in courses, classes, workshops and other means for the purpose of developing and updating professional skills to provide appropriate occupational therapy services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.320(11) &

(12)
