## Full text

339-020-0090 Waiver The Board may, in individual cases involving physical disability or illness, or undue hardship, grant waivers of the minimum continuing education requirements or extensions of time within which to fulfill the same or make the required reports. Applications for waiver shall be made to the Board in writing at least two months prior to license expiration.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.320(11) &

(12)
