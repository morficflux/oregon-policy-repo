## Full text

617-010-0056 Exemptions from Reporting and Assessment

(1) "Casual Sales" or on-farm sales of milk by a producer as provided for in Title 49 section 621.012 shall be exempt from Commission reporting and assessment requirements.

(2) "Casual Sales" or on-farm sales of milk by producer not meeting the criteria of Title 49 section 621.012 are not exempt from Commission reporting and assessment requirements.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576
