## Full text

820-020-0055 Unprofessional Conduct Incompetence, negligence or gross negligence in the practice engineering, land surveying, or photogrammetric mapping is unprofessional conduct.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, ORS 672.255 & ORS 672.325 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 672.325
