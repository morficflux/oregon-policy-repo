## Full text

820-080-0005 Refunds and Charges

(1) Application fees are non-refundable and will not be applied to future applications.

(2) Rescore fees are non-refundable.

(3) If the Board receives payment of any fees by check and the check is deposited and returned to the Board, the payor of the fees will be assessed a charge of $20 in addition to the required payment of the fees.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 672.255 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 672.325
