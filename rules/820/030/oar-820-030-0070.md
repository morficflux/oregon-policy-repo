# Surveyor's (OAR 820-030-0070)

## At a glance

## Full text

820-030-0070 Surveyor’s Seal and Signature Required In both ORS 209.250(2)(c) and (3)(g) the intent is that each page of the map and the last page of the narrative, if the narrative is a separate document, shall bear the seal and original signature of the professional land surveyor.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 672.255 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 672.325
