# Survey Monument Marking (OAR 820-030-0080)

## At a glance

OAR 820-030-0080 — Survey Monument Marking. Chapter 820, Division 030. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

820-030-0080 Survey Monument Marking A monument set by a registered professional land surveyor to mark or reference a point on a property or land line, or to mark or reference a geodetic control survey point, must be durably and visibly marked or tagged with the registered business name (Oregon only) or the letters “L.S.” followed by the registration number (Oregon only) of the surveyor in charge or, if the monument is set by a public officer, the monument must be marked with the official title of the office.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 672.255 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 672.325

History: BEELS 7-2015, f. & cert. ef. 9-16-15
