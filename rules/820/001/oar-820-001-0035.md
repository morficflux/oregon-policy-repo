## Full text

820-001-0035 Notice of Intent to Award At least 10 business days before the award of a contract under OAR 820-001-0030(3), the Board shall provide to each proposer notice of the Board’s intent to award a contract.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 672.255 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 672.325
