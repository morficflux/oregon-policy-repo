# Multiple Registrant (OAR 820-005-0055)

## At a glance

## Full text

820-005-0055 Multiple Registrant “Multiple registrant” means a person who is registered as both a land surveyor and an engineer or is registered as an engineer in two or more disciplines.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 672.255 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 372.325
