# Digital Signature (OAR 820-005-0025)

## At a glance

OAR 820-005-0025 — Digital Signature. Chapter 820, Division 005. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

820-005-0025 Digital Signature "Digital signature" means a type of electronic signature that transforms a message through the use of an algorithm or series of algorithms that provide a key pair, private and public, for signor verification, document security and authentication.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 672.255 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 372.325
