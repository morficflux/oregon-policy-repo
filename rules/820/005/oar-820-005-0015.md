# Certificate Authority (OAR 820-005-0015)

## At a glance

## Full text

820-005-0015 Certificate Authority “Certificate Authority” is the third party that issues and manages digital certificates (private and public keys) for digital signatures.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 672.255 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 372.325
