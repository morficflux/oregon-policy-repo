# Digital Certificate (OAR 820-005-0020)

## At a glance

OAR 820-005-0020 — Digital Certificate. Chapter 820, Division 005. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

820-005-0020 Digital Certificate “Digital certificate” is required to affix a digital signature, for the recipient to verify the identity of the signor, and for the recipient to verify that the contents of the document have not been altered since the signature was affixed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 672.255 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.002 - 372.325
