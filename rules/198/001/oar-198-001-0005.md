## Full text

198-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure The Judicial Department Personnel Division, pursuant to rulemaking authority granted to the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court and in accordance with ORS 183.341, adopts, by reference, Division 1 of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act. This adoption applies only to rules required by ORS 292.956.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341 & 292.956 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 292.956

History: JDG 1-1988, f. 4-15-88, cert. ef. 4-20-88
