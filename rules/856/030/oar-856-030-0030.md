## Full text

856-030-0030 Notification of Orders The Board shall mail, by regular first class mail with a certificate of service, a copy of the final order and accompanying findings and conclusions to each party to the proceeding and, if applicable, to the party’s attorney of record.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 776 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.470
