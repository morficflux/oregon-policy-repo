## Full text

856-030-0045 Rates in Coos Bay and Yaquina Bay for Vessels in an Emergency Regular rates set by the current rate order shall be used for a vessel in an emergency under ORS 776.600(1)(b).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 776.115 & ORS 776.600 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 776.115(7) & ORS 776.600(1)(b)
