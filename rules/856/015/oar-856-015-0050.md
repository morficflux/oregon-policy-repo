## Full text

856-015-0050 Reduced License Fees Individuals licensed by this Board as pilots for Yaquina Bay bar pilotage ground, who anticipate less than full-time employment as pilots, may pay a reduced annual license fee of $50.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 776 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 776.355
