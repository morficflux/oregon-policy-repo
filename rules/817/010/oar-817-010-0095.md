## Full text

817-010-0095 Pets in Facilities Pets or other animals are not permitted in the business facility. This prohibition does not apply to service animals recognized by the American with Disabilities Act or to fish in aquariums.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 690.165, ORS 690.055, ORS 690.205 & ORS 676.615 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 690.165, ORS 690.055 & ORS 690.205
