## Full text

817-005-0010 Elections The chairperson of the Board must be elected annually. Elections must be held during the last regularly scheduled Board meeting of the year or if canceled, the next regularly scheduled meeting. Terms of office run from January 1 to December 31.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 690.175 & ORS 676.615 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 690.175

History: BOC 3-2019, adopt filed 12/06/2019, effective 01/01/2020
