## Full text

817-120-0005 Practice Standards

(1) Authorization holders must adhere to all applicable Office laws and rules listed in ORS 676.560 to 676.660 and OAR Chapter 331 divisions 1 through 30.

(2) Authorization holders must practice in a manner that safeguards the public's health, safety, and welfare.

(3) An authorization holder is responsible for determining if any practice would be prohibited by law.

(4) Services provided to clients by an authorization holder that are prohibited, unsafe, dangerous or causes harm may result in a violation of incompetence, negligence or unprofessional conduct pursuant to ORS 676.612(2)(j) and under Office rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.605, ORS 676.615 & ORS 690.165 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.605, ORS 676.615 & ORS 690.165
