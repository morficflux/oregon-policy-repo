## Full text

817-015-0075 Head Lice Head lice may be treated at the discretion of the authorization holder. Compliance with OAR Chapter 817, Division 10 Safety and Infection Control Rules and Facility Standards, must be observed and followed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 690.165, ORS 690.205, ORS 690.055 & ORS 676.615 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 690.165, ORS 690.205 & ORS 690.055
