## Full text

407-010-0001 Policies and Procedures to Guide Use of Information on OYA and DHS Employees Who are Subjects of a CPS Assessment The DHS Office of Human Resources will develop policies and procedures to guide use of information provided by CPS supervisors in relation to the requirements of OAR 413-015-0405(9).
