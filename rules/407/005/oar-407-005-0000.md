# Purpose (OAR 407-005-0000)

## At a glance

## Full text

407-005-0000 Purpose These rules (407-005-0000 through 407-005-0030) establish a Department policy of non-discrimination on the basis of disability in accordance with the Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 (ADA) and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.050
