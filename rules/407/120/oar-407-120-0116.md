## Full text

407-120-0116 Web Portal Submitter

(1) Any provider activating their web portal access for web portal submission may be a web portal submitter. The provider will be referred to as the web portal submitter when functioning in that capacity, and shall be required to comply with these rules governing web portal submitters.

(2) The authorized signer of the provider enrollment agreement shall be the individual who is responsible for the provider’s DDE claims submission process. (a) If a provider submits their own claims directly, the provider will be referred to as the web portal submitter when functioning in that capacity and shall be required to comply with these rules governing web portal submitters. (b) If a provider uses an agent or clinic to submit DDE claims using the Department’s web portal, the agent or clinic will be referred to as the web portal submitter when functioning in that capacity and shall be required to comply with these rules governing web portal submitters.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 & 414.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
