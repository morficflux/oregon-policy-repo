## Full text

407-014-0310 Information Access The organization or user shall utilize the Department access control process for all requested and approved access. The Department shall notify the user of each approval or denial. When approved, the Department shall provide the user with a unique login identifier to access the network and information system or information asset. The Department may authorize the use of a generic login identifier..
