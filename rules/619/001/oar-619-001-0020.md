## Full text

619-001-0020 Model Rules of Procedure The Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, in effect on January 1, 2005, are adopted as the rules of procedure for the Oregon Wine Board and shall be followed in all matters except where a different procedure is prescribed by statute or in OAR chapter 619.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576.766(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.768(2)(b)

History: OWB 1-2005, f. 12-5-05, cert. ef. 12-15-05
