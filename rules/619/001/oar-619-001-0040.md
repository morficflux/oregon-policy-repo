## Full text

619-001-0040 Development of Draft Annual Plans

(1) Each fiscal year, the Oregon Wine Board will prepare draft annual plans for promotion and research during the next fiscal year.

(2) The draft annual plan for promotion will include, but are not limited to, the following elements: (a) A description of how the Board will support the promotion of Oregon’s wine grape growing and winemaking industries; and (b) Any other elements the Board considers appropriate to include in the draft plan for promotion.

(3) The draft annual plan for research will include, but are not limited to, the following elements: (a) A description of how the Board will support enological and viticultural research; (b) A description of how the Board will support economic research to develop sustainable business practices for wine grape growing and winemaking in Oregon; and (c) Any other elements the Board considers appropriate to include in the draft plan for research.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576.766(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.768(2)(b)
