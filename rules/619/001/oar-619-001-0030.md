## Full text

619-001-0030 Definitions For purposes of these rules, the following definitions apply.

(1) “Board” means the Oregon Wine Board established pursuant to ORS 576.753.

(2) “Director” means the director of the Oregon Economic and Community Development Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576.766(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.768(2)(b)
