## Full text

860-036-1580 Disconnection, Reconnection, and Field Visit Charges If authorized by the water utility's tariffs or statement of rates, a water utility may assess and require payment of one or both of the following as applicable prior to reconnecting service:

(1) A reasonable disconnection and reconnection charge if the water service was involuntarily disconnected, or

(2) A reasonable field visit charge if the water utility visits a service address intending to disconnect or reconnect service, but due to customer action, the utility is unable to disconnect or reconnect service.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 757.225

History: PUC 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-17
