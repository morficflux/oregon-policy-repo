## Full text

860-036-2160 Application for Authority to Guarantee Indebtedness An application by a rate-regulated water utility to assume any obligation or liability as guarantor, endorser, surety or otherwise must comply with rules set forth in OAR 860 027-0035 governing energy and telecommunication utilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 757.440
