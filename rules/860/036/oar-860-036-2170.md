## Full text

860-036-2170 Use of Deferred Accounting An application by a rate-regulated water utility to defer amounts under ORS 757.259 must comply with rules set forth in OAR 860-027-0300 governing energy and telecommunication utilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 756.105 & 757.259
