## Full text

860-036-1800 Service Territory Allocation Definitions For purposes of service territory allocation OAR 860-036-1800 through 860 036-1850:

(1) "Allocated territory" means an approved area with boundaries set out by Commission order granting an exclusive service territory.

(2) "Community water supply system" has the meaning provided by ORS 758.300.

(3) "Water utility" has the meaning provided in ORS 758.300.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756, 757 & 758 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 758.300-758.320
