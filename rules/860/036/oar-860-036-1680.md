## Full text

860-036-1680 Cross Connection Control

(1) All customers must comply with the water utility’s Cross Connection Control Program to protect the water system from contamination. A customer's failure to comply is grounds for disconnection under OAR 860-036-1500.

(2) The water utility’s cross connection protection rules will be included, as necessary, in the water utility's tariffs or statement of rates.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 757.020

History: PUC 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-17
