## Full text

860-036-1540 Disconnection of Service on Weekends and Holidays A water utility may not disconnect service for non-payment on a non-business day, or the day immediately preceding a non-business day, of either the utility or the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040

History: PUC 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-17
