## Full text

860-036-1410 Application of Partial Payments Absent written instructions from the customer, and consistent with OAR 860-036-1240(6), the water utility must apply payments in the following order:

(1) Past due regulated tariff or statement of rates services;

(2) Currently due regulated tariff or statement of rates services; and

(3) Non-regulated services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040

History: PUC 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-17
