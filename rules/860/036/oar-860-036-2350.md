## Full text

860-036-2350 Annual Report of Results of Operations

(1) By May 1 of each year, all rate-regulated water utilities must submit a financial Annual Report of Results of Operations for the preceding calendar year using the current form approved by the Commission. The annual report form is available at http://www.puc.state.or.us/Pages/Information-for-Water-Utility-Companies.aspx.

(2) The report must be filed electronically with the Commission's Filing Center at puc.filingcenter@state.or.us.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 756.105, 757.120, 757.125 & 757.135

History: PUC 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-17
