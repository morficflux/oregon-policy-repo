## Full text

860-036-1320 Temporary Service A water utility may provide temporary service to a customer and charge all costs of installing and removing the temporary service, in excess of any salvage realized, if the costs are stated in the water utility's tariff or statement of rates.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040

History: PUC 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-17
