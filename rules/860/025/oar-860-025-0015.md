# Applications for (OAR 860-025-0015)

## At a glance

## Full text

860-025-0015 Applications for Approval of Amendments to Contracts to Avoid or Eliminate Duplicate Utility Service Applications under ORS 758.430 for a Commission order approving an amendment to a contract approved pursuant to ORS 758.410 to 758.420 or 759.560 shall contain:

(1) The amendatory contract;

(2) Reference to the Commission order approving the initial contract; and

(3) Such information required by OAR 860-025-0010 as may assist the Commission in reviewing the application.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 758 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 758.400 - 758.475
