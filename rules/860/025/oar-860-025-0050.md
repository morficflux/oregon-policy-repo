## Full text

860-025-0050 Application by an Unserved Person for Service from a Large Telecommunications Utility An application under ORS 759.590 for an order of the Commission directing another telecommunications utility to provide local exchange service to an unserved person shall comply with OAR 860-032-0220.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 759.580, 759.585, 759.590 & 759.595
