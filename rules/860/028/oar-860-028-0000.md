## Full text

860-028-0000 Applicability

(1) The rules contained in this Division apply to every pole or conduit owner and every pole or conduit occupant, as defined in OAR 860-028-0020.

(2) Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any of the division 28 rules for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.035, 757.270, 759.045 & 759.650
