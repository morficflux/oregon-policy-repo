## Full text

860-037-0210 Voluntary Water Disconnection for Wastewater Service A customer who wishes to have service discontinued, shall provide the wastewater utility with a five-business-day notice in advance of the requested date of discontinuance of service. Until the wastewater utility receives such notice, the customer is responsible for all service rendered.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005 & 757.061
