## Full text

860-037-0220 Disconnection of Water Service on Weekends and Holidays Water service shall not be disconnected for non-emergencies on a weekend or a state or wastewater utility-recognized holiday. Water service shall not be disconnected for non-emergencies on a Friday or the day before a state or wastewater utility-recognized holiday unless mutually agreed upon by the customer, the wastewater utility, and the Commission’s Consumer Services Section.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005, 757.061 & 757.760
