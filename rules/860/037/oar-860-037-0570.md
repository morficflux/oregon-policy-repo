## Full text

860-037-0570 Accounting for Construction Work In Progress (CWIP) This rule applies to wastewater utilities that provide service inside the boundaries of a city. The Commission may allow into rates the costs of a specific capital improvement project in progress if:

(1) The wastewater utility uses the additional revenues solely for the purpose of completing the capital improvement project;

(2) The wastewater utility demonstrates that its access to capital is limited and it is in the public interest to provide funding for the capitol improvement through rates; and

(3) Such costs are approved through tariffs filed with the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.355 & Ch. 202 & OL 2003
