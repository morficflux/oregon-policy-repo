## Full text

860-037-0020 Designation of Third Party to Receive Notices A wastewater utility shall offer its customer or applicant the option to designate a third party to receive bills and notices set forth in these rules. When a wastewater utility receives such designation, it shall send bills and notices required under these rules to the customer’s representative, with duplicate copies of disconnect notices also served on the customer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005 & 757.061
