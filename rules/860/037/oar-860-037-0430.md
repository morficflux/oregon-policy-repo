## Full text

860-037-0430 Applications to Make Tariffs or Rate Schedules Effective on Less Than Statutory Notice A wastewater utility seeking authority to make tariffs or rate schedules effective on less than statutory notice must use application forms approved by the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005, 757.061 & 757.220
