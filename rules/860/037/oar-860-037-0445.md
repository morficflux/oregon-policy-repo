## Full text

860-037-0445 Tariff Changes Effective with Service Rendered All tariff changes apply to service rendered on and after the effective date of the changes, unless the Commission by order provides otherwise. As used in this rule, "service rendered" means basic service provided, or likewise as the context requires.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005, 757.007, 757.061 & 757.220
