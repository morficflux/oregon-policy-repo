## Full text

860-037-0545 Applications for Waiver of Requirements Under OARs 860-037-0530 and 860-037-0535 The Commission will not waive the requirements of OAR 860-037-0530 or 860-037-0535 for any transactions exceeding 0.1 percent of the previous calendar year’s Oregon utility operating revenues unless the transaction or transactions can be demonstrated in advance to be fair and reasonable and in the public interest. .

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005 & 757.061
