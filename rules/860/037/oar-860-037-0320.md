## Full text

860-037-0320 Location of Underground Facilities A wastewater utility and its customers shall comply with requirements of OAR chapter 952 regarding the prevention of damage to underground facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005 & 757.542 - 757.562
