## Full text

860-037-0240 Reconnection Fee When a wastewater service is disconnected for wastewater service, pursuant to OAR 860-037-0245, the wastewater utility may charge the reconnection fee in its tariff.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005, 757.061 & 757.225
