## Full text

860-034-0230 Reconnection Fee for Utility Service Where a utility service is disconnected pursuant to OAR 860-034-0180, the small telecommunications utility may charge the reconnection fee set forth in its tariff.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 759.045
