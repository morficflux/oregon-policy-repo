## Full text

860-034-0080 Restrictions on Entering a Customer Residence No employee of a small telecommunications utility shall enter the residence of a customer without proper authorization except in an emergency endangering life or property.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 759.045
