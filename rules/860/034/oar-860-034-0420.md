## Full text

860-034-0420 Location of Underground Facilities A small telecommunications utility or telecommunications cooperative and its customers shall comply with requirements of chapter 952 regarding the prevention of damage to underground facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.542 - 757.562 & 759.045
