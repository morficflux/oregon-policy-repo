## Full text

860-034-0670 Extension of Time for a Request for an Exception The Commission or a Hearings Officer may grant, upon a showing of good cause, a request to modify the filing dates set forth in OARs 860-034-0640 through 860-034-0660.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 759.220 & 759.225
