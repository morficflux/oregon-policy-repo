860-034-0325 Requests to Abandon, Exempt from Regulation, or Price-List Regulated Telecommunications Services A small telecommunications utility is subject to the requirements set forth in OARs 860-032-0020, 860-032-0023, 860-032-0025, and 860-032-0035.
