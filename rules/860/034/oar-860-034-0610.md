## Full text

860-034-0610 Revision of Depreciation Guidelines During February of 1995 and every three years thereafter, the Commission will open a docket to consider changes to the guidelines. The Commission will hold a prehearing conference to establish dates to resolve the issues.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 759.135, 759.220 & 759.225
