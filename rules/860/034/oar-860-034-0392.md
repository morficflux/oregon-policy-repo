# Accounting for (OAR 860-034-0392)

## Full text

860-034-0392 Accounting for Directors’ Fees by Small Telecommunications Utilities Directors’ fees paid by a small telecommunications utility to members of its board of directors, who are also paid as officers of the utility, shall not be recognized as a charge to operating expenses in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 759.045
