## Full text

860-032-0000 Waiver Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any of the Division 032 rules for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS. 756.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 759.005 & 759.020

History: PUC 6-2011, f. & cert. ef. 9-14-11
