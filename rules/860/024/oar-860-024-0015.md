# Ground Return (OAR 860-024-0015)

## At a glance

OAR 860-024-0015 — Ground Return. Chapter 860, Division 024. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

860-024-0015 Ground Return Every Operator with either alternating or direct current power lines or equipment within Oregon may use a connection to ground only for protection purposes. A ground connection shall not be used for the purpose of providing a return conductor for power purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, ORS 756, ORS 757 & ORS 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.035, ORS 757.649 & ORS 759.045
