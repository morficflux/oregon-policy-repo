## Full text

860-024-0007 Location of Underground Facilities An Operator and its customers shall comply with requirements of OAR chapter 952 regarding the prevention of damage to underground facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, ORS 756, ORS 757 & ORS 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.542 - 757.562, ORS 757.649 & ORS 759.045
