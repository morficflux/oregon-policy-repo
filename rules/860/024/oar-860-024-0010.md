## Full text

860-024-0010 Construction, Operation, and Maintenance of Electrical Supply and Communication Lines Every Operator shall construct, operate, and maintain electrical supply and communication lines in compliance with the standards prescribed by the 2023 Edition of the National Electrical Safety Code approved April 25, 2022, by the American National Standards Institute. [Publications: Publications referenced are available for review from the Commission.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, ORS 756, ORS 757 & ORS 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.035
