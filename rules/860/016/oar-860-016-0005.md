## Full text

860-016-0005 Waiver Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any of the Division 016 rules for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission or Arbitrator.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 756.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040
