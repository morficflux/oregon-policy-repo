## Full text

860-021-0051 Main Extensions for Gas Service Each gas utility shall develop, with the Commission’s approval, a uniform policy governing the amount of main extension which will be made free to connect a new customer. This policy should be related to the investment that can prudently be made for the probable revenue.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 756 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040
