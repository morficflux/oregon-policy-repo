## Full text

860-021-0017 Designation of Third Party to Receive Notices Each energy or large telecommunications utility shall offer its customers the option to designate a third party to receive bills and notices set forth in these rules. When an energy or large telecommunications utility receives such designation, it shall send bills and notices required under these rules to the customer’s representative, with duplicate copies of disconnect notices also served on the customer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 756.500 & 757.760
