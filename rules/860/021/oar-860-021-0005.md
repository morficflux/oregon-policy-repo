## Full text

860-021-0005 Scope of the Rules Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any of the Division 021 rules for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 756 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040
