## Full text

860-026-0000 Applicability of Division 26

(1) The rules contained in this Division apply to energy utilities and large telecommunications utilities, as defined in OAR 860-026-0005.

(2) Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any of the division 26 rules for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 759.030, 759.040 & 759.045
