## Full text

860-001-0530 Admissions A party may serve a request for admission on any other party under ORCP 45. Responses to each request must be served within 7 days of receipt. Requests not denied within 7 days are deemed admitted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 756.040 & 756.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORCP 45, ORS 756.040 & 756.500 – 756.575

History: PUC 5-2010, f. & cert. ef. 10-22-10
