## Full text

860-001-0160 Filing Requirements in Rulemaking Proceedings

(1) Written comments on proposed rules and other documents submitted in rulemaking proceedings must be filed with the Filing Center at the address listed in OAR 860-001-0140. Filing by electronic mail is preferred, but physical documents will be accepted. (a) To be considered by the Commission, a document must be received by the deadline for the submission of written comments specified in the notice of proposed rulemaking. (b) Documents must include the docket number assigned by the Commission to the rulemaking proceedings.

(2) Written comments on a proposed rule must comply with OAR 860-001-0210(3).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 756.040 & 756.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 756.500 - 756.575
