## Full text

860-001-0650 Legal Argument Parties may request the opportunity to file briefs in any proceedings. The Commission or ALJ may require a party to file a brief, or to present oral arguments instead of or in addition to briefs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 756.040 & 756.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 756.500 – 756.575

History: PUC 5-2010, f. & cert. ef. 10-22-10
