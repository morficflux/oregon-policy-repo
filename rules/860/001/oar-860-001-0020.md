860-001-0020 Hours of Operation, Location, and Contact Information

(1) Office Hours: Commission offices are open to the public between 8:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday, except on legal holidays as defined in ORS 187.010 or when the Commission’s office is closed by a Department of Administrative Services directive.

(2) Location and general contact information: The information included in this section is current at the time of rule adoption, but may change. Current information and additional contact information is available on the Commission's website: http://www.puc.state.or.us (a) Physical Location: 201 High Street SE, Suite 100, Salem, OR 97301 (b) Mailing Address: PO Box 1088, Salem, OR 97308-1088. (c) Telephone: (A) Local to Salem: (503) 373-7394, TTY (Oregon Relay Service): (800)-735-2900; (B) Consumer Services: (800) 522-2404; (C) Telephone Assistance Programs: (800) 848-4442, TTY (800) 648-3458.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 756.040 & 756.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 756.500 – 756.575
