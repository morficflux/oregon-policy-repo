## Full text

860-001-0200 Public Participation The Commission may informally seek public input before giving notice of intent to adopt, amend, or repeal a rule. A person may request to be notified of informal opportunities for public input by requesting to be placed on a notification list described in OAR 860-001-0030(1)(b).
