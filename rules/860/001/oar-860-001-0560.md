## Full text

860-001-0560 Public Meetings Except in cases of emergency, all Commissioners are required to participate in a decision of the Commission at a public meeting that proposes a major rate change for an electric or natural gas utility under ORS 757.205. For purposes of this rule, a major rate change is an increase of two percent or more for any customer class.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 756.040 & 756.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.610 et seq. & 756.040
