## Full text

860-001-0490 Records in Other Proceedings If a party offers in evidence all or part of the record from another Commission proceeding, then the party must provide a copy of the offered record to the ALJ and copies to other parties upon request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 756.040 & 756.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.450, 756.040 & 756.500 – 756.575

History: PUC 5-2010, f. & cert. ef. 10-22-10
