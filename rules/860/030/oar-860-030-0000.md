## Full text

860-030-0000 Exemptions

(1) Except as provided in section

(2) of this rule, the rules contained in this Division do not apply to unincorporated associations and cooperative corporations or to investor-owned electric utilities that satisfy their public purpose obligations under ORS 757.612.

(2) These rules apply to investor-owned electric utilities to the extent required by ORS 469.860 through 469.900.

(3) Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any of the Division 030 rules for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 757.612
