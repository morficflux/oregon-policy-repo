## Full text

860-030-0040 Commercial Energy Conservation Purpose, Statutory Authorization, and Policy. The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards, and criteria for electric and gas utilities to present energy audit programs for commercial buildings as required by Oregon Laws 1981, Chapter 708, Sections

(1) through

(11) (ORS 469.865 et seq.).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 469, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 469.631 - 469.645
