## Full text

860-030-0013 Approval of EE Avoided Costs for Energy Efficiency Measures The third-party nongovernmental entity identified in OAR 860-030-0011 may not use utility-specific EE avoided cost data in the administration of such measures and programs until the data is approved by the Commission. The Commission will generally consider approval of EE avoided costs for this purpose within 60 days from the filing deadline for the EE avoided cost report.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 756.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 756.070

History: PUC 5-2019, adopt filed 05/22/2019, effective 05/22/2019
