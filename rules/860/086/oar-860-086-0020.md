## Full text

860-086-0020 Electric Company Customer Information An electric company must file and maintain a tariff with the Commission that specifies the types of proprietary customer information, along with the prices, terms, conditions, and consent procedures associated with the transfer of such information to its competitive operations, electricity service suppliers, affiliates and aggregators. The provisions of this rule do not apply to information transferred under OAR 860-086-0030 or 860-086-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 757.600 - 757.667

History: PUC 6-2012, f. & cert. ef. 8-24-12
