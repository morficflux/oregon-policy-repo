# Net Metering Insurance (OAR 860-039-0080)

## Full text

860-039-0080 Net Metering Insurance A public utility will not require a customer-generator whose net metering facility is in compliance with the standards in paragraphs (a) and (b) of ORS 757.300(4) and the safety standards contained in these rules to purchase additional liability insurance or to name the utility as an additional insured on the customer-generator’s liability insurance policy.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040 & 757.300

History: PUC 8-2007, f. & cert. ef. 7-27-07
