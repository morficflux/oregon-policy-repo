## Full text

860-084-0200 Capacity Reservation, Timing, and Volumetric Incentive Rates A retail electricity consumer who has made a capacity reservation and who has executed all required agreements with the electric company must be paid the effective volumetric incentive rate at the time of enrollment for 100 percent of payable generation. Capacity reservation applications and standard contracts must provide the volumetric incentive rate in effect on the capacity reservation date.
