# Solar Photovoltaic Pilot Capacity Limit (OAR 860-084-0150)

## Full text

860-084-0150 Solar Photovoltaic Pilot Capacity Limit New capacity reservations will not be accepted after March 31, 2016, or after the cumulative capacity of contracted systems in pilot programs reaches 27.5 megawatts of nameplate capacity, whichever is earlier.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 757.360 - 757.380 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.360 - 757.380
