## Full text

860-084-0450 Reports to the Legislature The Commission must open a docket on or before November 1 of 2010, 2012, and 2014 to receive public comment and recommendations on the draft reports prepared by Commission staff regarding the pilot programs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 757.360 - 757.380 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.360 - 757.380
