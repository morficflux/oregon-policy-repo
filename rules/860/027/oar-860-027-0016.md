## Full text

860-027-0016 Accounting for Director’s Fees Directors’ fees paid by an energy or large telecommunications utility to members of its board of directors, who are also paid as officers of the energy or large telecommunications utility, shall not be recognized as a charge to operating expenses in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.110 & 759.115

History: PUC 16-2001, f. & cert. ef. 6-21-01 PUC 9-1998, f. & cert. ef. 4-28-98 PUC 164, f. 4-18-74, ef. 5-11-74 (Order No. 74-307)
