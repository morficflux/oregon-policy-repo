## Full text

860-027-0031 When the Commission May Reregulate Financings of Exempt Telecommunications Utilities If any bond rating made by Standard and Poor’s, Moody’s, or Fitch for a large telecommunications utility exempt under ORS 759.315(5) from the requirements of ORS 759.310 and 759.315(2) falls below "A," the Commission may find that reregulation of the large telecommunications utility under ORS 759.310 and 759.315(2) is necessary to prevent the impairment of service to customers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 756.060 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 759.040 & 759.045

History: PUC 27-2001, f. & cert. ef. 12-28-01 PUC 16-2001, f. & cert. ef. 6-21-01 PUC 8-1994, f. & cert. ef. 4-15-94 (Order No. 94-555)
