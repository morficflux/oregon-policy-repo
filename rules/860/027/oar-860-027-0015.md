# New (OAR 860-027-0015)

## At a glance

## Full text

860-027-0015 New Construction Budget Each energy utility operating within Oregon is required to file annually on or before March 31 on forms approved by the Commission information on new construction, extensions, and additions to the utility’s property.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.105 & 757.105
