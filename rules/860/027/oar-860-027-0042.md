## Full text

860-027-0042 Timeliness of Application Made Under OAR 860-027-0040 and Filings Made Under OAR 860-027-0041 Applications made under OAR 860-027-0040 and filings made under OAR 860-027-0041 shall occur no later than 90 days after the execution of the contract giving rise to the application or filing. The contract shall be deemed to be executed on the date the parties sign a written contract or on the date the parties begin to transact business under the contract, whichever date is earlier.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 756.040, 757.005, 757.015, 757.490, 757.495, 759.005, 759.010, 759.385 & 759.390
