## Full text

860-038-0500 Code of Conduct Purpose The Code of Conduct rules (OAR 860-038-0500 through 860-038-0640) govern the interactions and transactions among the electric company, its Oregon affiliates, and its competitive operations. The Code of Conduct is designed to protect against market abuses and anti-competitive practices by electric companies in the Oregon retail electricity markets.
