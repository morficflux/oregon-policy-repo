## Full text

860-085-0500 Voluntary Emission Reduction Projects OAR 860-085-0500 through 860-085-0750 are established under ORS 757.539 and are to be read in conjunction with that statute. For purposes of these rules, “Emission Reduction Project” or “Project” means a single or set of voluntary measures, including all labor, equipment, materials, items, or actions, designed to reduce anthropogenic greenhouse gas emissions within a defined boundary that would not otherwise occur.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS Ch. 183, 756 & 757 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.539
