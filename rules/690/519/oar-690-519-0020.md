## Full text

690-519-0020 Out-of-State Appropriations To support present and proposed resource development in Oregon no out-of-state appropriations of water shall be made or granted by any agency or public corporation of the state for the waters of the Columbia River or its tributaries.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 537

History: WRD 5-1983, f. & cert. ef. 8-19-83
