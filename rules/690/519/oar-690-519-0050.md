## Full text

690-519-0050 Existing Rights This program does not modify, set aside or alter any existing right to use water or the priority of such use established under existing law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 537

History: WRD 5-1983, f. & cert. ef. 8-19-83
