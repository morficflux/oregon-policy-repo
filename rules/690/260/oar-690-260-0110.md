## Full text

690-260-0110 Notice of Violation to Water Providing Organizations

(1) When a member of a water providing organization is notified of a violation, a duplicate notice of violation shall be served in person or by registered or certified mail on the organization.

(2) The purpose of notification to the organization is to encourage the organization to assist in obtaining compliance from the individual member.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 540
