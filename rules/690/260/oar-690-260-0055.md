## Full text

690-260-0055 Repeat Violations and Continuing Notice of Violation Any similar violation for which the person or agency responsible has received a notice of violation within the last three years is a repeat violation. Such notice shall serve as a continuing notice of the violation. In the case of repeat violations, a civil penalty may be imposed without providing additional time for correction.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 540
