## Full text

690-512-0090 Whitehorse and Willow Creeks Willow Creek and tributaries, and Whitehorse Creek and tributaries are withdrawn from future appropriations except as described in the order of the Water Resources Commission effective April 24, 1992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.410 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 536.410
