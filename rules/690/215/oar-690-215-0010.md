# Maintenance of an (OAR 690-215-0010)

## Full text

690-215-0010 Maintenance of an Existing Well Following Construction of Replacement Well Any time a new water supply well is constructed to replace an existing well which is a source of contamination, or is wasting the ground water resource by allowing loss of artesian pressure or commingling of aquifers, the existing well shall be repaired in compliance with these rules or abandoned in accordance with OAR 690-220-0030 through 690-220-0140.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795
