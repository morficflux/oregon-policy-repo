# Well (OAR 690-215-0050)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

690-215-0050 Well Cover All water supply wells shall be securely covered to prevent any foreign substance from entering the well, including any material which might contaminate the ground water. The well cover shall meet the requirements of OAR 690-220-0005.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540

History: WRD 7-2001, f. & cert. ef. 11-15-01 WRD 13-1986, f. 10-7-86, ef. 11-1-86
