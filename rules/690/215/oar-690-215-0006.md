## Full text

690-215-0006 Well Alterations

(1) Well alterations as defined in OAR 690-200-0050(7) shall be performed by a licensed Water Supply Well Constructor, or a landowner with a Landowner’s Well Construction Permit and bond.

(2) Water Supply Well Constructors or a permitted and bonded landowner shall record the following data, if available, on the Water Supply Well Report as required under OAR 690-205-0210 before completing alteration work on a well: (a) Pre-alteration static water level and date taken. (b) Pre-alteration casing diameter. (c) Pre-alteration casing gauge. (d) Pre-alteration well depth. (e) Pre-alteration seal material.

(3) Well alteration work shall be completed in accordance with OAR 690-215. The Water Supply Well Constructor shall only be responsible for the alteration work they performed under OAR 690-215.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795
