## Full text

690-215-0015 Accessibility to Well for Reconditioning, Repair or Abandonment To enable drilling equipment future access to the water supply well for reconditioning, repair, or abandonment, the property owner should maintain a minimum five-foot separation distance between the well and any permanent structure.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540

History: WRD 7-2001, f. & cert. ef. 11-15-01 WRD 7-1988, f. & cert. ef. 6-29-88
