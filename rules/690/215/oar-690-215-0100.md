## Full text

690-215-0100 Drilling in a Dug Well In no case shall a dug well be deepened by drilling methods.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540

History: WRD 13-1986, f. 10-7-86, ef. 11-1-86, Renumbered from 690-061-0241 WRD 9-1978, f. 12-12-78, ef. 1-1-79
