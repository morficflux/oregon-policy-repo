# Sealing (OAR 690-215-0025)

## At a glance

## Full text

690-215-0025 Sealing Pitless Adapter and Pitless Units The sealing area around pitless adapter or pitless unit installations shall be replaced with unhydrated bentonite as required by OAR 690-210-0330 and shall be at least one and one-half inches thick around the casing and pitless device.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540

History: WRD 3-2008, f. 12-22-08, cert. ef. 1-2-09
