## Full text

690-215-0090 Conversion to an Artesian Well If a water supply well becomes artesian upon deepening, the well shall be cased, sealed and completed in accordance with OAR 690-210-0155.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
