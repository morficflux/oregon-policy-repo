690-502-0190 Sherwood-Dammasch-Wilsonville Ground Water Limited Area Groundwater from the basalt aquifers in the Sherwood-Dammasch-Wilsonville Groundwater Limited Area as described and shown in Exhibit 6 is classified for exempt uses only.
