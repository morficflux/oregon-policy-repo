## Full text

690-382-0450 Existing Documentation of Modifications If documentation of a modification under a ground water registration was on file with the Department prior to July 22, 2005, the Department shall provide an application for recognition of the modification described in the notice priority with respect to processing other applications for recognition of ground water registration modifications. An application filed under OAR 690-382-0400 may incorporate by reference information about the modification that is on file with the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025, ORS 536.027 & ORS 537.610 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.610 & ORS 540.505-540.532
