## Full text

690-504-0110 West Fork Hood River Subbasin Reservation Nine thousand (9,000) acre-feet of unappropriated water of the Lake Branch of the West Fork Hood River or its tributaries upstream of the confluence with the West Fork Hood River are reserved for storage in multipurpose reservoirs. The priority date of the reservation is November 6, 1992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.249 & 537.356

History: WRD 8-1996, f. & cert. ef. 11-14-96
