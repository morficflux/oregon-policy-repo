## Full text

690-504-0130 Neal Creek Subbasin Reservation Five thousand (5,000) acre-feet of unappropriated water of Neal Creek or its tributaries are reserved for storage in multipurpose reservoirs. The priority date of the reservation is November 6, 1992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.249 & 537.356

History: WRD 8-1996, f. & cert. ef. 11-14-96
