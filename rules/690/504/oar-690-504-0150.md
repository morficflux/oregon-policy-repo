## Full text

690-504-0150 Eightmile Creek Subbasin Reservation Four thousand (4,000) acre-feet of unappropriated water of Eightmile Creek or its tributaries upstream of the confluence of Pine Hollow with Eightmile Creek are reserved for storage in multipurpose reservoirs. The priority date of the reservation is November 6, 1992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.249 & 537.356

History: WRD 8-1996, f. & cert. ef. 11-14-96
