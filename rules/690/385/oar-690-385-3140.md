## Full text

690-385-3140 Temporary Change in Point of Diversion or Appropriation

(1) As provided in ORS 540.570, a temporary change in point of diversion or appropriation may be made in combination with, or as necessary to facilitate, a change in place of use.

(2) A change in point of diversion is restricted to the same source of surface water. A change in point of appropriation is restricted to the same aquifer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025 & 536.027 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 540.570

History: WRD 9-2004, f. & cert. ef. 11-16-04
