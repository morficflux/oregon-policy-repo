# Practice in Suits in Equity Governs Taking of Testimony (OAR 690-030-0080)

## Full text

690-030-0080 Practice in Suits in Equity Governs Taking of Testimony The practice in the Circuit Courts of the state governing the taking of testimony in suits in equity, in so far as applicable, shall govern the taking of testimony on the hearing of said contest; but the evidence shall be confined to the subjects enumerated in the statement of contest; and the Water Resources Director or his authorized assistant may, in their discretion, reject testimony and evidence offered, which is not so confined.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 543

History: WRD 3, f. & ef. 2-18-77
