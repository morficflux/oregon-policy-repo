## Full text

690-030-0040 Service of Amended Statement Service of such amended statement of contest shall be made on the contestee, his agent or attorney, prior to the filing of the same with the Water Resources Director and no amendment of the statement of contest will be received or filed which does not bear an endorsement thereon of service upon said contestee, his agent, or attorney.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 543

History: WRD 3, f. & ef. 2-18-77
