## Full text

690-315-0100 Department Action on Extension Applications by Holders of Municipal Water Supply Permits If the Department finds an applicant has submitted a completed application as required under OAR 690-315-0070, the Department shall process the application as established under OARs 690-315-0050 and 690-315-0060.
