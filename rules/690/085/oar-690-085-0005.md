# Purpose (OAR 690-085-0005)

## Full text

690-085-0005 Purpose These rules establish procedures and requirements for the annual reporting of water use by governmental entities pursuant to ORS 537.099. These rules also establish procedures for designation of serious water management problem areas pursuant to ORS 540.435.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 & 540

History: WRD 13-1988, f. & cert. ef. 8-10-88
