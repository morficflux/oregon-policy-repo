## Full text

690-600-0000 Purpose These rules establish procedures for the Oregon Water Resources Department in accepting applications and considering proposals for funding under the provisions of Senate Bill 1069 (Chapter 13, 2008 Laws). The Water Conservation, Reuse, and Storage Grant Program includes funding for project planning studies performed to evaluate the feasibility of developing a water conservation, reuse, or storage project.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2008 OL Ch. 13
