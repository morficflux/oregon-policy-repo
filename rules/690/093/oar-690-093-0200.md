## Full text

690-093-0200 Program Review

(1) The Water Resources Department shall review the loan and grant program on a biennial basis to assess to what extent the outcomes described in ORS 541.677 are being achieved, and shall report the review findings to the Water Resources Commission.

(2) The Commission shall modify the project selection process as necessary to better achieve the outcomes described in ORS 541.677.
