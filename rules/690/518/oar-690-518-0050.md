## Full text

690-518-0050 Out-of-Basin Appropriations To support present and proposed Mid-Coast Basin water resource development, no out-of-basin appropriation of the waters of the Mid-Coast Basin shall be granted without prior approval of, and following a public hearing by, the Water Resources Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 537
