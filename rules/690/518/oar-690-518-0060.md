# Water Quality (OAR 690-518-0060)

## At a glance

OAR 690-518-0060 — Water Quality. Chapter 690, Division 518. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

690-518-0060 Water Quality Rights to use of water granted by any state agency in accordance with this water use program shall be issued only on the condition that any effluents or return flows from such uses shall not interfere with other beneficial uses of water.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 537
