## Full text

690-217-0055 Pump Test Reports Results of pump tests shall be reported on the pump test report form supplied by the Water Resources Department. The person conducting the pump test shall be responsible for completing and certifying the pump test report. The well owner shall sign and submit the pump test report.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537

History: WRD 25-1988, f. & cert. ef. 12-20-88
