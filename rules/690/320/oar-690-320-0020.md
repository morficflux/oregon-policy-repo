## Full text

690-320-0020 Cancellation of Permit When it appears from an onsite examination by the Water Resources Department that no appropriation has been made under the terms of the permit, or that use once made has undergone a period of five successive years of nonuse, a certified letter of intent to cancel the permit shall be sent to the permittee, allowing 60 days from the date of the letter for response. Failure to respond during the 60-day period shall result in cancellation of the permit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025 & 536.027 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 536.220, 536.300, 536.310, 537.260, 537.410 - 537.450, 540 & 543
