## Full text

690-511-0110 Owyhee Reservations Sixty thousand (60,000) acre-feet of unappropriated water of the Owyhee River and tributaries are reserved. The water is reserved for storage in multipurpose reservoirs to be constructed in the future. The priority date of the reservations is November 6, 1992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.249 & 537.356

History: WRD 2-1997, f. & cert. ef. 2-25-97
