## Full text

690-003-0200 Certification of Copies of Records Certification of both hard copies and electronic copies of records will be provided for the fee identified in ORS 536.050. The Department will certify only that on the date copied, the copy was a true and correct copy of the original record. The Department cannot certify as to any subsequent changes or manipulation of the record.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.430, 536.025 & 536.027 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.440 & 536.480
