## Full text

690-200-0041 Water Used Must be Potable All water used in the construction, alteration, repair or abandonment of water supply wells shall be potable.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
