# Organic Materials (OAR 690-200-0042)

## At a glance

OAR 690-200-0042 — Organic Materials. Chapter 690, Division 200. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

690-200-0042 Organic Materials Organic materials which foster or promote undesired organic growth or have the potential to degrade water quality shall not be employed in the construction of a water supply well. This includes, but is not limited to, brans, hulls, grains, starches, and proteins.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
