## Full text

690-200-0047 Unattended Wells All wells, when unattended during construction, shall be covered to protect public health and safety.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
