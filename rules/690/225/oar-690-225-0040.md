## Full text

690-225-0040 Multiple Violations and Consolidation of Proceedings In cases of multiple or continuing violations, each occurrence of substantially the same activity and each days continuance of a violation after the responsible party has been notified is a separate and distinct violation. Administrative enforcement proceedings for multiple violations may be consolidated into a single proceeding.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
