## Full text

690-508-0120 Middle Grande Ronde Subbasin Reservation Nine thousand (9,000) acre-feet of unappropriated water of Catherine Creek and tributaries above Ames Creek are reserved. The water is reserved for storage in multipurpose reservoirs to be constructed in the future. The priority date of the reservations is November 6, 1992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.249 & 537.356

History: WRD 2-1997, f. & cert. ef. 2-25-97
