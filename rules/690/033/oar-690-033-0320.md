## Full text

690-033-0320 Statewide Rules: Rule Review These rules, OAR 690-033-0310 through 690-033-0340, shall be reviewed and may be revised when:

(1) Additional species are listed as sensitive, threatened, or endangered; or

(2) Basin programs are revised by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025 & 536.027 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.140 - 537.190

History: WRD 6-1996, f. & cert. ef. 7-11-96
