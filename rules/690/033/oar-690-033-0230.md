## Full text

690-033-0230 Lower Columbia Rules (Below Bonneville Dam): Flow Management The Department shall consult with the affected fish and wildlife agencies and the appropriate Indian tribes to assist in resolving the flow management conflicts, if the flow management objectives for protection of threatened and endangered fish species cannot be achieved because of differing requirements between fish species in the management area.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027 & 537.140 - 537.190

History: WRD 4-1995, f. & cert. ef. 6-16-95
