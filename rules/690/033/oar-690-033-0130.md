## Full text

690-033-0130 Upper Columbia Rules (Above Bonneville Dam): Flow Management If the flow management objectives cannot be achieved because of differing requirements between fish species in the management area, the Department shall consult with the affected fish and wildlife agencies and the appropriate Indian tribes to assist in resolving the flow management conflicts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027 & 537.140 - 537.190 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.140 - 537.190
