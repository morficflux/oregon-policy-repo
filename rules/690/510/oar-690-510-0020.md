# Water Quality (OAR 690-510-0020)

## At a glance

OAR 690-510-0020 — Water Quality. Chapter 690, Division 510. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

690-510-0020 Water Quality Rights to use of water for industrial or mining purposes shall be issued only on condition that any effluents or return flows from such uses shall not interfere with other beneficial uses of water.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 537

History: WRD 7-2016, f. & cert. ef. 10-18-16 WRD 2-1986, f. & cert. ef. 2-20-86 WRD 2-1985, f. & cert. ef. 3-28-85 WRD 1-1981, f. & cert. ef. 4-20-81 WRB 44, f. 10-29-70
