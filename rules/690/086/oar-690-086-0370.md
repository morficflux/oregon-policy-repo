## Full text

690-086-0370 Alternate Municipal WMCP: Water Supply Element for Small Municipal Water Suppliers The water supply element of an Alternate Municipal WMCP shall include the following:

(1) A description of the current and future service areas, including population projection estimates or other relevant growth projections;

(2) Based on the information provided in section

(1) of this rule, an estimate of the water supplier’s water demand projections for 10 and 20 years; and

(3) A comparison of the projected water needed and the quantity of water currently available to the Small Municipal Water Supplier , and a description of how the Small Municipal Water Supplier will meet future system demand.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027, 537.211 & 540.572 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.230, 537.630 & 539.010

History: WRD 7-2018, adopt filed 12/21/2018, effective 12/21/2018
