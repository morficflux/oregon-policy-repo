## Full text

690-014-0030 Certified Water Right Examiner Required for Claim of Beneficial Use In addition to other rules contained in OAR chapter 690 requiring specified information to be prepared by a CWRE, the water right holder or person responsible for a permit or transfer final order must have a CWRE prepare the Claim of Beneficial Use if the permit or transfer application was filed after July 9, 1987.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536, ORS 537, ORS 536.025, ORS 536.027 & ORS 537.797 - 537.799 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 536 & ORS 537
