## Full text

690-250-0140 Special Problem Area Regulation The watermaster may prohibit the diversion or use of water by anyone who has failed to comply with a Commission rule or order pursuant to a declaration of a serious water management problem area under ORS 540.435.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 540

History: WRD 24-1988, f. & cert. ef. 12-14-88
