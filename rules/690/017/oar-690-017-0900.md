## Full text

690-017-0900 Time Period of Non-Use Where the rebutted nonuse occurs as an intervening period within an unrebutted period of nonuse totaling five or more years, the remaining unrebutted years of nonuse will be treated as consecutive and cancellation will be initiated.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025 & ORS 536.027 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 540.610 - 540.670
