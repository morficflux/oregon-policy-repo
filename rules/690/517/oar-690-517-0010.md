# Reservations (OAR 690-517-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

690-517-0010 Reservations Water in the amounts specified is reserved in the following streams for municipal use:

(1) Chetco River — three cfs, downstream from the confluence with the North Fork Chetco River.

(2) Winchuck River — one cfs, downstream from the confluence with Bear Creek. [ED. NOTE: The South Coast Basin is delineated on agency Map, File 17.6, available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 537
