# Out-of-Basin Appropriations (OAR 690-517-0040)

## At a glance

## Full text

690-517-0040 Out-of-Basin Appropriations No out-of-basin diversion of South Coast Basin water shall be granted without the prior approval of, and following a public hearing by, the Water Resources Commission. [ED. NOTE: The South Coast Basin is delineated on agency Map, File 17.6, available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 537
