## Full text

690-077-0055 Disposition of Minimum Perennial Stream-flows Following the conversion of a minimum streamflow, the Commission shall retain the original minimum streamflow until it determines through basin program amendment that no public benefit is derived by maintaining both an instream water right and a minimum streamflow.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536 & 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 536 & 537
