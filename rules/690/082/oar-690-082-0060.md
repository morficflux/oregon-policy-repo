## Full text

690-082-0060 Priority Date The date of priority for all applications received under OAR 690-082-0040 shall be the date the application is accepted by the Director after review and approval of the proposed water use under 690-082-0050.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 536

History: WRD 2-1988, f. & cert. ef. 1-19-88
