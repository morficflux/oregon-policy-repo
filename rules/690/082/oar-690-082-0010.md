# Purpose (OAR 690-082-0010)

## Full text

690-082-0010 Purpose These rules authorize the Director of the Water Resources Department to accept applications to appropriate water for a use in addition to the uses for which particular waters are classified in the applicable basin program. These rules apply in all basins for which the Water Resources Commission has adopted a basin program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 536

History: WRD 2-1988, f. & cert. ef. 1-19-88
