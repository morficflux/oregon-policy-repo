## Full text

690-230-0075 Disposal of Low-Temperature Geothermal Fluids Produced During Drilling and Testing Low-temperature geothermal fluids produced during drilling or testing of a low-temperature geothermal production or injection well shall be disposed of in a manner that minimizes hazards. For additional requirements on the disposal of low-temperature geothermal fluids produced during well drilling or testing, contact the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537
