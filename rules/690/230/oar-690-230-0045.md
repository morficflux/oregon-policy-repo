## Full text

690-230-0045 Location of Low-Temperature Geothermal Injection Wells Exceeding 15,000 Gallons Per Day The owner of any low-temperature geothermal injection well having an anticipated injection rate of greater than 15,000 gallons per day is required to have a separation distance between the low-temperature geothermal injection and production wells that is adequate to protect the production wells from substantial thermal interference. The Director shall make a decision on the proposed separation distance based on information supplied by the owner as per OAR 690-230-0115.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537
