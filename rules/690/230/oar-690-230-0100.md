# Additional (OAR 690-230-0100)

## At a glance

## Full text

690-230-0100 Additional Standards for Low-Temperature Geothermal Injection Wells Procedures required to inject effluent into a low-temperature geothermal injection well shall not cause failure of the well casing and/or seal materials or other components of the well structure, including but not limited to, movement, displacement or fracturing of the overburden.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
