## Full text

690-507-0840 Stage Gulch CGWA: Violation Policy Whenever the Department has reason to believe a violation of a rule in OAR 690, division 507 has occurred, it shall investigate. If a violation has occurred, the Director may take enforcement action.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.900 - 536.935 & 537.505 - 537.745
