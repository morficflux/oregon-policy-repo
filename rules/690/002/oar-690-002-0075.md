## Full text

690-002-0075 Scope of Hearing for Hearings Not Governed by OAR 690-002-0005(2) The issues to be considered in a contested case hearing are limited to issues timely raised by the parties in any protests, requests for hearing, or requests for standing or party status as allowed by applicable law.
