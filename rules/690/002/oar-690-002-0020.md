## Full text

690-002-0020 Authorized Non-Attorney Representation A party or limited party participating in a contested case hearing may be represented by an authorized representative in the manner and to the extent provided for in OAR 137-003-0555 of the Attorney General's Model Rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341, 536.025 & 536.027 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341 & 183.457
