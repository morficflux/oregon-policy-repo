# Purpose (OAR 690-029-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 690-029-0005 — Purpose. Chapter 690, Division 029. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

690-029-0005 Purpose This rule establishes the methods by which the Water Resources Department (Department) provides opportunity for public notice and comment regarding negotiations between the Director of the Water Resources Department (Director) and the representatives for the Warm Springs Tribes and federal government, as trustee for the tribes, held to establish the scope and attributes of the water right claimed by the tribes, pursuant to ORS 539.300, et seq.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 539

History: WRD 23-1988, f. & cert. ef. 12-14-88
