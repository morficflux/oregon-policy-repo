## Full text

690-012-0010 Purpose These rules prescribe the definitions, standards, information requirements, and fees for processing an application proposing an out-of-basin diversion of water. The Water Resources Commission shall use these rules to evaluate the merits of applications to divert water from one basin to another.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537

History: WRD 2-1991, f. & cert. ef. 2-8-91
