## Full text

690-509-0120 North Fork Burnt River Reservation Six thousand five hundred (6,500) acre-feet of unappropriated water of the North Fork Burnt River and tributaries upstream of Unity Reservoir are reserved for storage in multipurpose reservoirs to be constructed in the future. The priority date of the reservation is November 6, 1992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537.249 & 537.356
