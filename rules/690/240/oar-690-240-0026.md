## Full text

690-240-0026 Well Identification Label Maintenance The well identification label shall not be removed from the wellhead and shall be maintained by the land owner in an accessible location and in a readable condition.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.090, ORS 537.505-537.795, ORS 536.027, ORS 536.900 & ORS 537.992 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 536.090, ORS 537.505-537.795, ORS 536.900 & ORS 537.992
