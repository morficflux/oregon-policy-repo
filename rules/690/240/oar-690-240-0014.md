## Full text

690-240-0014 Water Used Must be Potable All water used in the construction, alteration, repair or abandonment of monitoring wells and geotechnical holes shall be potable.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795
