# Public Safety (OAR 690-240-0012)

## At a glance

## Full text

690-240-0012 Public Safety No monitoring well, geotechnical hole, or other hole shall be constructed, maintained, or abandoned in such a manner as to constitute a health threat, or health hazard or a menace to public safety.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 737.780

History: WRD 7-2001, f. & cert. ef. 11-15-01 WRD 2-1995, f. 5-17-95, cert. ef. 7-1-95
