## Full text

690-240-0630 Assessment of Civil Penalties Under OAR 690-240-0580(1) the Director may at any time select the most appropriate enforcement tool, including assessment of civil penalties, to gain compliance. However, the Director shall not impose a civil penalty if compliance has been achieved in another manner prior to final decision in the proceeding.
