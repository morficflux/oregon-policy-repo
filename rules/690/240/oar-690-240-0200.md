## Full text

690-240-0200 Continuing Education Committee A Continuing Education Program and Continuing Education Committee are established under chapter 496, Oregon Laws 2001 (ORS 537.765). The duties of the Well Constructors Continuing Education Committee are to review and approve continuing education courses and assign continuing education credits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 537 & 742 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 537 & 742
