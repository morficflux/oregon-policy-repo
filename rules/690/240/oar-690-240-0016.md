## Full text

690-240-0016 Unattended Wells All wells, when unattended during construction, shall be covered to protect public health and safety.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795
