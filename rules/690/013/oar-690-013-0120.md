## Full text

690-013-0120 Supplemental Permits When supplemental permits apply to lands having perfected water rights, the supplemental permit shall be appurtenant to the land listed in the petition.

Statutory/Other Authority: Ch. 1000 & OL 1989 Statutes/Other Implemented: Ch. 1000 & OL 1989
