# Steel Casing Joints (OAR 690-210-0200)

## At a glance

## Full text

690-210-0200 Steel Casing Joints All steel casing joints shall be welded or thread coupled and shall be water tight. If welded casing joints are used, the weld shall be a full penetrating weld at least equal in thickness to the wall thickness of the casing. Welded casing joints shall have a tensile strength equal to or greater than that of the casing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
