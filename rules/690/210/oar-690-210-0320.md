# Methods of Placement of Cement Grout or Concrete (OAR 690-210-0320)

## Full text

690-210-0320 Methods of Placement of Cement Grout or Concrete Cement grout or concrete used as a sealing material in a well shall be placed or forced upward from the bottom to completely fill the annular space to be grouted and shall be placed in one continuous operation without significant interruption. If temporary outer surface casing is used in the construction of the well, it shall be withdrawn as the grout or concrete is placed. (For acceptable methods of placement, see Appendix 210-3 and Figure 210-1). [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
