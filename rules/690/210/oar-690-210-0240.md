## Full text

690-210-0240 Casing Shall be Centered In all instances, casings shall be centered in sealed intervals. Casing centralizers may be used to ensure centering. When sealing a well by Method E, casing centralizers shall be used. (See Figure 210-11). [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
