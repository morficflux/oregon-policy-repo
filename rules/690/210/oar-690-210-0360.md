## Full text

690-210-0360 Movement of Casing after Cement Grouting In no case shall the permanent well casing be moved or driven following the placement and initial set of the cement grout.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540

History: WRD 13-1986, f. 10-7-86, ef. 11-1-86, Renumbered from 690-061-0106 WRD 9-1978, f. 12-12-78, ef. 1-1-79
