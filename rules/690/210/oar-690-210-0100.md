## Full text

690-210-0100 Mineralized or Contaminated Groundwater All formations which yield contaminated or mineralized water shall be adequately cased and sealed off to prevent contamination of the overlying or underlying water-bearing zones.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
