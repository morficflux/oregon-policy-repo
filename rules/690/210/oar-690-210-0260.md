# Openings in the Casing (OAR 690-210-0260)

## At a glance

OAR 690-210-0260 — Openings in the Casing. Chapter 690, Division 210. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

690-210-0260 Openings in the Casing There shall be no opening in the casing wall between the top of the casing and the bottom of the required casing seal except for pitless adapters, measurement access ports, and grout nipples installed in conformance with these standards. In no case shall holes be cut in the casing wall for the purpose of lifting or lowering casing into the well bore unless such holes are properly welded closed and watertight prior to placement into the well bore.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540

History: WRD 13-1986, f. 10-7-86, ef. 11-1-86, Renumbered from 690-061-0046 WRD 9-1978, f. 12-12-78, ef. 1-1-79
