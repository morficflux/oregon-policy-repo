# Drill (OAR 690-210-0300)

## At a glance

OAR 690-210-0300 — Drill. Chapter 690, Division 210. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

690-210-0300 Drill Cuttings or Chips In no case shall drill cuttings or drill chips be used or allowed to fill, partially fill, or fall into the required sealing interval of a well during the construction or the completion of a well.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540

History: WRD 13-1986, f. 10-7-86, ef. 11-1-86, Renumbered from 690-061-0110 WRD 9-1978, f. 12-12-78, ef. 1-1-79 Repealed by WRD 9-1978, f. 12-12-78, ef. 1-1-79 WRD 3, f. & ef. 2-18-77
