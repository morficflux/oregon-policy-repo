# Injection (OAR 690-210-0070)

## Full text

690-210-0070 Injection Wells No water supply well subject to these rules shall be used for the injection of surface or ground waters, or chemically or thermally altered waters, unless the injection installation, well design, and receiving formations are approved by the Water Resources Department. For additional regulations on the use of wells for injection purposes, contact the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
