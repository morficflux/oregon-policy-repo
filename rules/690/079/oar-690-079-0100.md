## Full text

690-079-0100 Review by Director Following the comment period established by OAR 690-079-0090, the Department shall review the reservation request. The Director shall reject the reservation request if:

(1) The reservation request does not contain all the information required by OAR 690-079-0060 after the applicant has been given an opportunity to cure any defects in the request according to OAR 690-079-0040(2); or

(2) The Department concludes the reservation request would impair or be detrimental to the public interest based on the public interest review standards of OAR 690, division 11.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025, 536.027, 536.220, 536.300, 536.310, 537.338 & 537.356 - 537.358

History: WRD 9-1992, f. & cert. ef. 7-1-92
