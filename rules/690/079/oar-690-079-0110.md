## Full text

690-079-0110 Appeal of Director’s Decision The agency requesting the reservation may file a request for Commission review of the Director’s rejection within 60 days of notification of rejection of a reservation request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025, 536.027, 536.220, 536.300, 536.310, 537.338 & 537.356 - 537.358
