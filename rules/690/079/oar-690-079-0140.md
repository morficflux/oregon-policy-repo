## Full text

690-079-0140 Conditions on a Reservation When approving a reservation, the Commission shall include any conditions deemed necessary to protect and promote the public interest.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197, 536.025, 536.027, 536.220, 536.300, 536.310, 537.338 & 537.356 - 537.358
