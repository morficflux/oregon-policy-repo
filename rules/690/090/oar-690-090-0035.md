## Full text

690-090-0035 Notice and Public Meetings

(1) The director shall make public notice of each application within one week of receipt and at least 14 days prior to the public meeting as provided in OAR 690-090-0019. The notice shall contain a summary of the application including the names of the applicants, the location, nature and any new water appropriations required by the proposed project.

(2) The notice shall be mailed to all persons and organizations which have filed a written request for notices with the department and shall be posted at the department.

(3) Individuals or groups wishing to comment on an application shall do so to the director in writing within 14 days following the date of mailing the notice if they wish their comments to be considered. (4)(a) The director shall call a public meeting on an application as provided in OAR 690-090-0019. (b) Such meeting shall be conducted according to procedures described in ORS Chapter 192 and OAR 690-090-0019.
