## Full text

690-052-0150 Disposition of the Water Right The hydroelectric water right for a project is subject to the provisions of ORS 543A.305 regarding the conversion of a hydroelectric water right to an instream right. Nothing in these rules or in 543A.305 prevents the operator from transferring a hydroelectric water right to an instream right according to 540.505 through 540.530, prior to conversion.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027 & ORS 543A.300(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 543 & ORS 543A
