## Full text

690-052-0160 Modification of Time Limits The Department, for good cause, may extend the time limits set forth in this division for a reasonable period of time.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027 & ORS 543A.300(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 543 & ORS 543A
