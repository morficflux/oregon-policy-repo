# Preliminary (OAR 690-051-0040)

## Full text

690-051-0040 Preliminary Permit not Required No License or Permit shall be issued within six months of either approving the Preliminary Permit or acceptance of the License or Permit application. A Preliminary Permit is unnecessary where the project is a minor project.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025, 536.027, 537 & 543

History: WRD 2-1993, f. & cert. ef. 5-19-93 WRD 14-1986, f. & ef. 10-13-86
