## Full text

690-051-0140 Time and Place of Hearings The conduct of contested hearings shall be as provided in OAR 137-003-0501 through -0700. The contested case hearing may be heard at the Director's office in Salem.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025, 536.027, 537 & 543
