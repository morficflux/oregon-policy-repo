## Full text

690-019-0060 Potential Liability Issuance of an emergency use permit, temporary drought transfer or a grant of preference of use by order of the Commission may not protect the person receiving the permit, transfer or grant of preference from liability for improper use of the water under the permit, transfer or grant of preference.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.700 - 536.780 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 536.700 - 536.780
