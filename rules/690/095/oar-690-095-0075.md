## Full text

690-095-0075 Conditions to Disbursement of Loan Funds The director shall withhold payment of funds under a loan contract until any necessary federal and state environmental impact approval processes have been completed for the project and any relevant approvals and permits from any regulatory authority pertaining to the construction or operation of the project have been obtained.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027 & 541.646 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541-600 - 541.646
