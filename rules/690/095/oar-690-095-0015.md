## Full text

690-095-0015 Eligibility: Applicant and Project

(1) To be eligible, an applicant must be a qualified water developer seeking to finance a water development project in the Columbia River Basin under the loan program.

(2) To be eligible, projects shall meet the definition of a “water development project” as defined in ORS 541.600.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027 & 541.646 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541-600 - 541.646
