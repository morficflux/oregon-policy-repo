## Full text

690-095-0085 Issuance of Bonds Bonds may be issued at the request of the director and administered as provided in ORS chapter 286A and 541.780 to 541.840. Bonds may be issued prior to and after the execution of any loan contracts entered into with respect to the bonds.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.027 & 541.646 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541-600 - 541.646
