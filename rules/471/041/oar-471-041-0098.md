## Full text

471-041-0098 Request to Withdraw Application for Review An applicant’s written request to withdraw an application for review will be allowed unless the request is received after notice of EAB’s decision has been issued.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 657.610 & 657.685 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.685(6)
