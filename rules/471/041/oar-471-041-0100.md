## Full text

471-041-0100 Notice of Decision

(1) Notice of EAB’s decision shall be by United States Postal Service mail or electronic means to each party or its representative at the physical or electronic address of record with EAB.

(2) Notice of EAB’s decision shall include: (a) A caption identifying the parties and their representatives, (b) The date the notice was issued, (c) A statement of appeal rights, and (d) Findings of fact and conclusions and reasons, or a statement adopting the findings of fact and conclusions and reasons set forth in the ALJ Order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 657.610 & 657.685 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.275 & 657.685(6)
