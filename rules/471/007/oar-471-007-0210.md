## Full text

471-007-0210 Definitions

(1) “Authorized Designee” means a Department employee authorized to obtain and/or review criminal offender information and other records about a subject individual through criminal records checks and other means, and to conduct a fitness determination in accordance with these rules.

(2) "Department" means the Oregon Employment Department (OED) or any subdivision thereof.

(3) "Family Member" means a spouse, domestic partner, natural parent, foster parent, adoptive parent, stepparent, child, foster child, adopted child, stepchild, sibling, stepbrother, stepsister, father-in-law, mother-in-law, son-in-law, daughter-in-law, brother-in-law, sister-in-law, grandparent, grandchild, aunt, uncle, niece, nephew or first cousin.

(4) “Subject Individual” means an individual identified in ORS 181A.200 as someone from whom the Department may require fingerprints for the purpose of conducting a criminal records check.

(5) See OAR 125-007-0210 for additional definitions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181A.195, 181A.200 184.340 & 184.365 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181A.195
