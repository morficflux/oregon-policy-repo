# Jury Pay (OAR 471-031-0030)

## At a glance

## Full text

471-031-0030 Jury Pay Compensation, reimbursement, fees, lodging, meals, or other remuneration paid or provided to an individual for services performed as juror are not wages.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.095 & 657.105 - 657.140

History: 1DE 150, f. & ef. 2-9-76
