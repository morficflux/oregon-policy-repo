## Full text

471-031-0135 Participants of Federal Training Programs In accordance with ORS 657.067(2), “employment” includes services performed by an individual as a participant in an on-the-job training (OJT) program of the Work Incentive Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.067
