## Full text

471-031-0050 Gifts Gifts, other than tips or gratuities, received by an employee during the course of employment from persons other than the employer are not wages. The Director, however, reserves the right, based on the facts in each particular case, to determine whether or not the gift is in fact a bonus, fee, or prize given as a reward or added remuneration for services rendered.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.095 & 657.105 - 657.140
