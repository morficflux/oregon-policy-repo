# Accident (OAR 471-031-0040)

## At a glance

## Full text

471-031-0040 Accident Compensation When an employer continues the payment of wages during a disability period, or pays to the employee all or part of the difference between benefits or compensation received from an insurance carrier or State Accident Insurance Fund and the employee’s regular or usual wage, the sums so paid by the employer are wages unless excluded from the term wages by ORS 657.115 and 657.125. Lump sum or other special payments to compensate an employee for an accident sustained in the course of employment are not wages.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.115 & 657.125
