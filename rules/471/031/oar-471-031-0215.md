## Full text

471-031-0215 Registered Domestic Partnerships For the purposes of ORS Chapter 657, “spouse” includes any person in a registered domestic partnership considered valid under the laws of the State of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657.610 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.610
