## Full text

471-031-0125 In-Home Sales For the purposes of ORS 657.087(2), “In-person solicitation” means a face-to-face contact at the customer’s residence and does not include mail, telephone or other non-personal contacts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.087
