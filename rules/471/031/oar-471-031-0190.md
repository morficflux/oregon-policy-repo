## Full text

471-031-0190 Shopping News For the purposes of ORS 657.080(1), “shopping news” includes, but is not limited to, publications that consist primarily of classified ads and/or coupons and does not include publications that are primarily phone listings.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657.610 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.610
