# Payments by an (OAR 471-031-0060)

## At a glance

OAR 471-031-0060 — Payments by an. Chapter 471, Division 031. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

471-031-0060 Payments by an Employer to Persons in the Military Service Payments made by an employer voluntarily and without contractual obligation, to or in behalf of a person for periods during which such person performs military services in the Army, Navy, Air Forces, National Guard, or similar units of the United States or any state, do not constitute wages in determining benefit rights or for tax purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.065

History: 1DE 150, f. & ef. 2-9-76
