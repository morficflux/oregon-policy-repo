## Full text

471-010-0005 Director As used in these rules and unless the context requires otherwise, the title “Administrator” or “Assistant Director” means the Director of the Employment Department of the State of Oregon. Where appropriate, “Administrator” or “Director” shall also mean any duly authorized representative of the Director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657.176, 657.260, 657.265 & 657.610 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.010(5)
