## Full text

471-010-0090 Customer Information and Disclosure: Disclosure Charges The department may charge a reasonable fee to reimburse it for the cost of providing records, including the cost of preparing the information and costs associated with implementing and maintaining written disclosure agreements.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657.610 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.665
