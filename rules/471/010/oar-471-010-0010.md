## Full text

471-010-0010 Rules Procedure Prior to the adoption, amendment, or repeal of any permanent rule, the Employment Department shall give notice of the proposed adoption, amendment, or repeal:

(1) In the Secretary of State's Bulletin referred to in ORS 183.360 at least twenty-one

(21) days prior to the effective date.

(2) By providing a copy of the notice to persons on the Employment Department’s mailing list established pursuant to ORS 183.335(7) at least twenty-eight

(28) days prior to the effective date.

(3) By providing a copy of the notice to the legislators specified in ORS 183.335(15) of this section at least 49 days before the effective date.

(4) By providing a copy of the notice to news agencies, to a newspaper of statewide circulation, and other members of the media who have requested notification.
