## Full text

471-030-0022 “Full-Time Work” Defined “Full-time work,” for the purposes of ORS 657.100, is 40 hours of work in a week except in those industries, trades or professions in which most employers due to custom, practice, or agreement utilize a normal work week of more or less than 40 hours in a week.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.100
