## Full text

471-030-0043 Customary Occupation In applying the provisions of ORS 657.325(8), customary occupation means any work that the individual is capable of performing based on experience and training with respect to the individual’s work history during the most recent three-year period prior to the failure to accept an offer of or referral to a job.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.325(8)
