## Full text

471-030-0012 Standard Base Year Defined As used in ORS 657.173(1)(b), “standard base year” means a regular base year or period that is not expanded in any way.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657.610 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.610
