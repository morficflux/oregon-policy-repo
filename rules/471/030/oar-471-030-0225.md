## Full text

471-030-0225 Extended Benefits Look Back As used in ORS 657.321 to ORS 657.329, when an individual’s benefit year ends before the start of an Extended Benefits period and federal law provides funding greater than 50% of Extended Benefits, the Director shall allow for an eligibility period that begins within the Extended Benefits period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657.610
