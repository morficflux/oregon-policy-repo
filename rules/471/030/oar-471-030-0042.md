## Full text

471-030-0042 Good Prospects In applying the provisions of ORS 657.325(8), an individual has good prospects for obtaining work if the Director finds that the individual could be reasonably expected to obtain work in the individual’s customary occupation within four weeks from the week-ending date of the week in which the job referral or job offer was made.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.325(8)
