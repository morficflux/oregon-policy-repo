## Full text

471-030-0097 Labor Disputes The term “labor dispute” as used in the Employment Department law means any concerted or deliberate action by two or more individuals or by an employing unit resulting in either a strike or lockout in which wages, hours, working conditions or terms or employment of the individuals are involved.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.200
