## Full text

471-030-0100 Maximum Benefit Amount For purposes of ORS 657.176(4) the term “maximum benefit amount” shall be the amount of benefit entitlement established under 657.150(5).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.176(4)
