## Full text

471-030-0010 Assignment of Wages For purposes of ORS 657.150(2) and

(4) wages shall be assigned to the calendar quarter in which they are paid, in the same manner that taxes are payable pursuant to OAR 471-031-0070(1).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 657 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 657.150(2) & 657.150(4)
