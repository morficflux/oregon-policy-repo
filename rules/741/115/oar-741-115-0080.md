## Full text

741-115-0080 Vegetation Control at Grade Crossings

(1) The railroad shall control vegetation on its right-of-way for a distance of 250 feet in each direction from the edge of the crossing surface and for a distance of 50 feet in each direction from the centerline of the nearest track or to the edge of the railroad’s right-of-way, whichever is less, so that the vegetation does not obstruct motorists’ view of approaching trains.

(2) The public authority shall control vegetation on its right-of-way within the SSD and within its right-of-way.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 824.202 & 824.220 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.212

History: RD 3-2003, f. 9-18-03, cert. ef. 10-1-03
