# Hand Cleaning Facilities (OAR 741-335-0160)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

741-335-0160 Hand Cleaning Facilities Facilities for cleaning of hands shall be provided on cabooses and locomotives in service at a location where the use thereof will not result in the contamination of the drinking water dispensing system. An adequate supply of hand soap or other hand cleaning agents shall be provided in appropriate dispensers or crew packs and shall be capable of removing oil-based substances. Individual disposable or launderable towels and waste receptacles for used towels shall be provided.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.068
