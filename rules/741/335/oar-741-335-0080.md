## Full text

741-335-0080 Toilet Room Ventilation Toilet rooms on cabooses shall be ventilated. If not practical to ventilate by windows, ventilation shall be provided by some other means.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.068
