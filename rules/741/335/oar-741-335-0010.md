## Full text

741-335-0010 Applicability of Rules OAR 741-335-0020 through 741-335-0200 apply to each railroad operating locomotives and/or cabooses in this state.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.068
