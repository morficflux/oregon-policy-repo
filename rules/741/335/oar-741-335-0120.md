## Full text

741-335-0120 Toilet Room Supplies An adequate supply of toilet paper with a convenient holder shall be supplied for each toilet. Crew packs adequately supplied with toilet paper are acceptable.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.068
