# Application for Variances (OAR 741-335-0030)

## At a glance

## Full text

741-335-0030 Application for Variances Applications for variances from OAR 741-335-0010 through 741-335-0180 shall contain a photo and/or drawing of the facility for which the variance is requested and shall describe the measures to be followed in lieu of the applicable rule. Each application shall be accompanied by at least five copies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.068
