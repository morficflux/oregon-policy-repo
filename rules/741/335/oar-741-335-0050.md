## Full text

741-335-0050 Caboose Sanitation Facilities Each caboose which employees are required to occupy shall have a toilet in a toilet room. The enclosure of every toilet room shall be of solid construction from floor to ceiling with interior illumination, and have a solid door or a door with nontransparent windows. Doors to toilet rooms shall be latchable from the inside. The walls and ceilings in every toilet room shall be of suitable nonabsorbent material and have a light-colored water repellent finish at least equal to that of a good quality paint or varnish. The finish of the floor of every toilet room and side walls up to a height not less than 6 inches shall be smooth, cleanable and of a nonabsorbent material.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.068
