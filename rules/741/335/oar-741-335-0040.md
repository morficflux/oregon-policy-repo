# Exempt Equipment (OAR 741-335-0040)

## At a glance

OAR 741-335-0040 — Exempt Equipment. Chapter 741, Division 335. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

741-335-0040 Exempt Equipment Toilets are not required on cabooses used exclusively in yards or in transfer service where railroad provided and maintained stationary toilet facilities are available to railroad employees and notice of such facilities is provided to employees.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.068
