# Walkways on (OAR 741-320-0160)

## Full text

741-320-0160 Walkways on Curves Except where OAR 741-320-0080 (Standard No. 1) applies, a walkway located on the inside of a curve exceeding 17 degrees shall have a minimum width of 7 feet as measured from the outside edge of rail. See OAR 741-315-0010(3).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.056 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.050 & 824.056
