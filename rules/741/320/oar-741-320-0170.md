# Walkways for (OAR 741-320-0170)

## Full text

741-320-0170 Walkways for Trackside Warning Devices A walkway conforming to OAR 741-320-0080 (Standard No. 1) or 741-320-0130 (Walkways on Structures) as appropriate, shall be constructed and maintained, upon order of the Department, for such distance and in each location as is necessary to facilitate inspection of a train stopped by a trackside warning device, only after notice to the affected railroad and hearing (in case of dispute). NOTE: The Department may order the walkways prescribed in this rule only after notice to the affected railroad and hearing (in case of dispute).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.056 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.050 & 824.056
