741-400-0010 Standards for Cabooseless Train Operations The Public Utility Commission adopts the following orders as standards for the operation by Class I railroads of freight trains without an occupied caboose as the rear cars: Case Order Railroad Number Number Southern Pacific RC-47 84-904 86-787 Union Pacific RC-58 86-1119 87-039 Burlington Northern RC-59 85-1214 RC-78 86-764
