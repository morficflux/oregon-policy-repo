## Full text

741-200-0040 Form of Applications

(1) Applications shall be filed on forms approved by the Department.

(2) An original application and attachments must be filed with the Rail Division of the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 824.202 & 824.220 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.204, 824.206 & 824.210
