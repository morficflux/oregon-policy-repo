## Full text

741-200-0080 Assignment of Responsibility for Continuing Costs Cost responsibility for the maintenance of facilities associated with any project for which an application is filed pursuant to these rules shall be assigned to a railroad or the public authority in interest.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823 & 824 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.244
