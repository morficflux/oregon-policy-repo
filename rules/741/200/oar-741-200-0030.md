## Full text

741-200-0030 Persons Who May Make Application Pursuant to ORS Chapter 824, only a railroad company or the public authority in interest may file an application for authority to construct, relocate, alter or close a crossing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 824.202 & 824.220 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.202
