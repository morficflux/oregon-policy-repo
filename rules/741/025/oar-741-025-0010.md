# Purpose (OAR 741-025-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

741-025-0010 Purpose The Short Line Credit Premium Account is established in ORS 367.067. The purpose of division 25 rules is to establish the Short Line Railroad Rail Infrastructure Improvement Program as well as procedures, standards and criteria for its operation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 367.066, 367.067 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 367.066(2) & 367.067
