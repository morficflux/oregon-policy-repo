## Full text

741-025-0030 Application Requirements Applications for infrastructure assistance from the Short Line Credit Premium Account shall be in a format prescribed by the Department and shall contain or be accompanied by such information as the Department may require.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 367.066, 367.067 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 367.066(2) & 367.067
