# Notification Required (OAR 741-040-0030)

## At a glance

OAR 741-040-0030 — Notification Required. Chapter 741, Division 040. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

741-040-0030 Notification Required Unless an exception applies pursuant to OAR 741-040-0040, at least 30 days before listing or placing any Real Property for sale, exchange or conveyance, a Political subdivision shall notify the Rail Division Administrator of its intent to undertake such listing or placement if the Real Property is within:

(1) 100 feet of a Railroad Right of Way;

(2) 500 feet of an At-Grade crossing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & Ch. 446 OL 2011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 271.310

History: RD 1-2012, f. & cert. ef. 1-27-12
