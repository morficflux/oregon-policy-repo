## Full text

741-040-0040 Exempt from Notification The requirement for notification does not apply:

(1) To listing or placing any Real Property for sale, exchange or conveyance within a Light Rail Corridor;

(2) If the proposed sale, exchange or conveyance of the real property is to a provider of rail service; or

(3) To the proposed sale, exchange or conveyance of easements.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 271.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 271

History: RD 2-2013, f. & cert. ef. 12-20-13 RD 1-2012, f. & cert. ef. 1-27-12
