## Full text

741-040-0070 Railroad Notification

(1) Division shall solicit from each provider of rail service operating within the State first-class U.S. and electronic mail addresses to which Division shall send notifications required hereunder.

(2) Division will notify any provider of rail service using the Railroad Right of Way within the area specified by ORS 271.310(3) of the Real Property being sold, exchanged or conveyed by at least one of the following methods: (a) Mailed notice; (b) Electronic mail notice.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & Ch. 446 OL 2011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 271.310
