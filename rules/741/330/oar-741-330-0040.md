# Size and Design of Signs (OAR 741-330-0040)

## At a glance

## Full text

741-330-0040 Size and Design of Signs Signs which are not in compliance with OAR 741-330-0010 and 741-330-0020, shall be replaced with signs of the required size and design.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.050 & 824.052
