## Full text

741-030-0065 Fees for Certification

(1) When submitting an application for preliminary certification, applicants must include a payment of $250 to the Department.

(2) When submitting an application for final certification, applicants shall include a payment not to exceed one half of one percent (0.5%) of the estimated tax credit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 823.011, 315.593 & 315.597 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 315.591 - 315.603
