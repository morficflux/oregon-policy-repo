# Purpose (OAR 741-030-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

741-030-0010 Purpose The purpose of division 30 rules is to implement the Short Line Railroad Rehabilitation Tax Credit program, as well as procedures, standards and criteria for its operation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 823.011, 315.593 & 315.597 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 315.591 - 315.603
