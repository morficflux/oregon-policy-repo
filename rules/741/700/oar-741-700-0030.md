# Other (OAR 741-700-0030)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

741-700-0030 Other Than Class I Railroads Annual reports will be submitted on the most current form approved by the Department. Reportings are to be minimal so as to create no undue burden on the railroads yet provide information necessary to carry out the duties of the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823 & 824 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 824.030

History: RS 2-1996, f. & cert. ef. 3-14-96, Renumbered from 860-048-0055 PUC 3-1982, f. & ef. 3-4-82, Order No. 82-133
