# Purpose (OAR 741-020-0010)

## At a glance

OAR 741-020-0010 — Purpose. Chapter 741, Division 020. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

741-020-0010 Purpose Chapter 741, Oregon Laws 2003 created the Industrial Rail Spur Fund, allowing for the issuance of lottery bonds to for the purpose of financing grants and loans to fund industrial rail spurs. The purpose of division 20 rules is to establish the Industrial Spur Track Program for the distribution of the funds as well as procedures, standards and criteria for its operation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 367.070 & Sec. 10 & Ch. 741 OL 2003
