## Full text

741-020-0030 Application Requirements Persons or entities interested in receiving funds from the Industrial Rail Spur Fund may submit to the Department a written application. The application shall be in a format prescribed by the Department and shall contain or be accompanied by such information as the Department may require.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 367.070 & Sec. 10 & Ch. 741 OL 2003
