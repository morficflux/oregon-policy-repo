## Full text

309-120-0265 Extension of Transfer

(1) If the Oregon Health Authority determines that the administrative commitment must exceed 180 days in order to stabilize the adult in custody, the administrative commitment must be renewed in a subsequent administrative commitment hearing held in accordance with these rules.

(2) Notwithstanding this rule, an administrative commitment may not continue beyond the term of incarceration to which the adult in custody was sentenced.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 179.473, 179.479 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.471-179.486 & 179.495-179.508
