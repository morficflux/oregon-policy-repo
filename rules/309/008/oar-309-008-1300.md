## Full text

309-008-1300 Hearings

(1) An applicant or provider who issued a notice of intent to revoke, impose conditions, or refuse to renew its certificate under these rules shall be entitled to request a hearing in accordance with ORS Chapter 183.

(2) When the Division orders the imposition of a condition or orders immediate suspension of a certificate under the provisions of this rule, the provider shall be entitled to request a hearing in accordance with ORS Chapter 183.
