## Full text

309-118-0000 Purpose and Scope These rules prescribe a process to address the informal complaints and grievances that an Oregon State Hospital patient, or a representative on the patient's behalf, might have in regards to the hospital or its staff. These rules also establish a review process for patients or their representatives who are not satisfied with the resolution of their grievances.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 413.042, 179.321 & 179.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 426.385, 179.321 & 179.360
