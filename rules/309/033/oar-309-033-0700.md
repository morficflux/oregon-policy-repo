## Full text

309-033-0700 Purpose and Scope

(1) This section establishes rules pursuant to ORS 426.072, 426.236, 426.228, 426.232, 426.233 and 426.234, 426.385 for certification of hospitals and facilities which provide care, custody, and treatment to persons under civil commitment and to persons in custody or on diversion.

(2) Seclusion or restraint may be used only for the management of violent or self-destructive behavior that jeopardizes the immediate physical safety of the patient, a staff member, or other individuals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042, 426.236, 426.385 & 430.021 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 426.005 - 426.395
