## Full text

309-033-0400 Statement of Purpose and Statutory Authority

(1) Purpose. These rules prescribe standards and procedures relating to the care, custody, and treatment of persons under civil commitment and persons alleged to have a mental illness.

(2) Statutory authority. These rules are authorized by ORS 426.005 through 426.395 and carry out the provisions of ORS 426.005 through 426.395.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 426.005 - 426.395
