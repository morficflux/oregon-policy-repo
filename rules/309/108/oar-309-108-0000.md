## Full text

309-108-0000 Purpose and Statutory Authority These rules prescribe the standards and procedures for the handling of patient’s personal, excess or storage property and the security inspection and search procedures for Oregon State Hospital patients.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 413.042 & ORS 179.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.321 & 426.385
