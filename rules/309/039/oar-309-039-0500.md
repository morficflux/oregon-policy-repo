## Full text

309-039-0500 Purpose and Scope These rules apply to certifications of provider organizations that render non-inpatient mental health treatment services. The certifications exist solely for the purpose of qualifying for insurance reimbursement. Agencies that contract with the Oregon Health Authority (OHA), subcontract with OHA, or contract with a Community Mental Health Program are not eligible for the “non-inpatient” certification.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 743A.168 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743A.160 & 743.168
