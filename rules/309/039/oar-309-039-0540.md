## Full text

309-039-0540 General Standards Each provider is required to meet all applicable standards from the following standards:

(1) Specific Staff Qualifications and Compentencies, detailed in OAR 309-019-0125; and

(2) Personnel Documentation, Training and Supervision, detailed in OAR 309-019-0130.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 743A.168 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743A.160 & 743.168
