## Full text

309-014-0010 Purpose of a Community Mental Health Program The purpose of a CMHP is to provide a system of appropriate, accessible, coordinated, effective, efficient safety net services to meet the mental health needs of the citizens of the community.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 430.640 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 430.610 – 430.640
