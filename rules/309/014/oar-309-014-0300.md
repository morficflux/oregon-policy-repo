## Full text

309-014-0300 Purpose and Scope These rules relate to the implementation of Chapter 418, Oregon Laws 2011 sections 13 through 20. The scope is limited to the creation of the Central Health Council and the implementation of the Central Oregon Health Improvement Plan.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2011 OL Ch. 418 & Sec. 13-19
