## Full text

309-112-0020 Use of Security Transportation The chief medical officer or designee may authorize the use of secure transportation for patients of a secure program when outside the security area.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 426.385 & 427.031
