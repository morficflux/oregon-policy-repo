## Full text

309-112-0025 Use of Restraint for Acute Medical Conditions

(1) During medical treatment for acute physical conditions, personal and physical restraint may be used to prevent a patient from injuring himself or herself.

(2) Use of a restraint in the presence of a physician may be authorized verbally; ongoing or continuing use of personal or physical restraint must be ordered in writing by a physician.

(3) Treatment staff shall: (a) Attend to the patient’s basic personal needs and exercise needs in accordance with general medical practice; and (b) To the extent practicable, accommodate the patient’s mental disabilities treatment and training regimen.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 426.385
