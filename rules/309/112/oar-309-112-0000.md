## Full text

309-112-0000 Purpose and Statutory Authority

(1) Purpose. These rules prescribe policies and procedures concerning the use of restraint or seclusion in the treatment and in the behavior management of patients in state institutions operated by the Division. In addition to these general rules, other more specific requirements established by federal regulations must be followed where applicable.

(2) Statutory Authority. These rules are authorized by ORS 179.040 and 413.042 and carry out the provisions of 426.385.

(3) The rules set out in Chapter 309, Division 112 are applicable on and after September 23, 2020.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 426.385
