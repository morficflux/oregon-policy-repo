## Full text

309-040-0435 Criminal Penalties

(1) Operating an AFH without a license is punishable as a Class C misdemeanor.

(2) Refusing to allow any of the following is punishable as a Class B misdemeanor: (a) Division access to the AFH for inspection or investigation; (b) Division access to residents in order to interview residents privately or to review records; or (c) State and local fire inspector access to the AFH regarding fire safety.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 443.705 - 443.825
