## Full text

309-013-0105 Disciplinary Action Fraud or embezzlement of Division resources and/or patient or resident funds committed by Division employees shall constitute grounds for disciplinary action. The type and extent of disciplinary action will be determined in accordance with the Division’s collective bargaining agreements and “Personnel Relations Law, Personnel Rules and Personnel Policies.” Notwithstanding any portion of these rules, existing agreements with unions representing the employee(s) involved, governing complaint investigation, shall be observed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 430.021
