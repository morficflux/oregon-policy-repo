## Full text

309-012-0115 Earned Income Exclusion The Division allows a patient or resident to retain a portion of any income earned while in a mental health institution. The amount of earned income to be excluded in the calculation of ability-to-pay is determined by subtracting $65 from earned income. An additional $25 will be subtracted from the total income (both earned and unearned) as an allowance for personal need.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.770 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.610 – 179.770

History: MHD 11-1985, f. & ef. 6-19-85
