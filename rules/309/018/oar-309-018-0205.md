## Full text

309-018-0205 Quality Assessment and Performance Improvement Providers shall develop and implement a structured and ongoing process to assess, monitor, and improve the quality and effectiveness of services provided to individuals and their families.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042, 428.205 - 428.270, 430.640 & 443.450 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 430.010, 430.205 - 430.210, 430.254 - 430.640, 430.850 - 430.955, 443.400 - 443.460, 443.991, 461.549 & 743A.168
