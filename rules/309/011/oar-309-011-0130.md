# Policy (OAR 309-011-0130)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

309-011-0130 Policy The Addictions and Mental Health (AMH) Division shall adopt a policy that supports and promotes self-determination for persons receiving mental health services. The policy shall be designed to remove barriers that:

(1) Segregate persons with disabilities from full participation in the community in the most integrated setting in accordance with the United States Supreme Court decision in Olmstead v. L.C., 527 U.S. 581 (1999); and

(2) Prevent persons with disabilities from enjoying a meaningful life, the benefits of community involvement and citizen rights guaranteed by law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 430.078 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 430.078
