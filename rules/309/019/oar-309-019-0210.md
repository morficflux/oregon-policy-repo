## Full text

309-019-0210 Quality Assessment and Performance Improvement Providers shall develop and implement a structured and ongoing process to assess, monitor, and improve the quality and effectiveness of services provided to individuals and their families.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 430.640 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 430.010, 430.205 - 430.210, 430.254 - 430.640 & 430.850 - 430.955
