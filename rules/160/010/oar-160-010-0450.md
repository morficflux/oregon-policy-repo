## Full text

160-010-0450 Conversion of a Business Entity When a domestic business entity is converting to a foreign business entity, the articles of conversion shall include the jurisdiction of the foreign business entity.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 56.022 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 60.472, 62.607, 63.470, 67.342 & 70.505
