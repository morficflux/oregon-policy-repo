## Full text

160-010-0230 Renewal of Trade/Service Mark A trade or service mark specimen cannot be altered through renewal. A new application must be filed if the design or words that comprise the trade or service mark is altered.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 56.022 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 647.055

History: CORP 3-2009, f. 12-22-09 cert. ef. 1-1-10
