## Full text

160-100-0240 Information Required to Be Recorded in Record of Protests A notary public shall maintain a record of information about each protest of commercial paper performed by the notary public consisting of copies of source originals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 194.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 194.300
