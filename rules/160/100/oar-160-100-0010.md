# Date of (OAR 160-100-0010)

## Full text

160-100-0010 Date of Mailing Whenever ORS Chapter 194.205 to 194.990 and OAR 160-100-0000 to 160-100-1150 require or permit a record or object to be mailed to the Secretary of State, the date of mailing shall be the date the record or object was actually received by the Secretary of State.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 194.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 194.360
