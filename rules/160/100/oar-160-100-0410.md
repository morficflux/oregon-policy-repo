## Full text

160-100-0410 Displaying List of Notary Fees A notary public who charges a fee for a notarial act shall either display a list, in English, of notary fees specified in OAR 160-100-0400 in a conspicuous location in the notary public's place of business or give a copy of the notice to any person requesting a notarial act to read before having the notarial act performed. A place of business is the notary public's residence, business office or any other location in which the notary public performs a notarial act.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 194.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 194.300 & 194.400
