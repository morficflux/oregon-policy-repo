## Full text

160-100-1080 Secretary of State Attending Approved Course of Study An approved provider must permit the Secretary of State or representatives of the Secretary of State to attend any approved course of study, without prior notice and at no charge, for the purpose of observation, monitoring, auditing, and investigating the instruction given.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 194.325 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 194.325
