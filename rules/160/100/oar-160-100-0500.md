## Full text

160-100-0500 Notification of Secretary of State of Conviction Within 30 days of the notification of conviction of any felony or crime involving fraud, dishonesty or deceit by a notary public, the Secretary of State shall conduct an investigation of the notary public, in accordance with OAR 160-100-0430(2)–(8).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 194.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 194.360
