## Full text

160-040-0504 Notice of Changes Changes in standard search logic. If the filing office changes its standard search logic or the implementation of its standard search logic in a manner that could alter search results, the filing office will provide public notice of such change.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 79.0526 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 79.0506 & 79.0523
