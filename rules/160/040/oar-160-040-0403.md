## Full text

160-040-0403 Notice of Bankruptcy The filing officer shall take no action upon receipt of a notification, formal or informal, of a bankruptcy proceeding involving a debtor named in the UCC information management system. Accordingly, financing statements will lapse as scheduled unless properly continued.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 79.0526 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 79.0522
