## Full text

160-040-0404 Redaction of Certain Information The filing officer is obliged to redact certain information from the information it provides to searchers and bulk data purchasers in accordance with ORS 56.037. Such information should not be included in UCC records and will be redacted in accordance with such laws.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 79.0526 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 56.037

History: CORP 4-2008, f. 7-15-08, cert. ef. 8-1-08
