## Full text

160-040-0204 Refusal Errors If a secured party or a remitter demonstrates to the satisfaction of the filing officer that a UCC record that was refused for filing should not have been refused, the filing officer will file the UCC record with the filing date and time the UCC record was originally tendered for filing. The filing officer shall also file a filing officer statement noting when and why the record was changed.
