## Full text

160-040-0401 Errors of the Filing Officer The filing office may correct the data entry and indexing errors of filing officer personnel in the UCC information management system at any time. If the correction occurs after the filing officer has issued a certification date that includes the filing date of the corrected document, the filing officer shall enter a filing officer statement in the UCC information management system identifying the record to which it relates, stating the date of the correction, and explaining the nature of the corrective action taken. The record shall be preserved as long as the record of the initial financing statement is preserved in the UCC information management system.
