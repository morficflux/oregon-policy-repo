## Full text

160-070-0030 Withdrawal of Complaint

(1) A complainant may withdraw a complaint at any time after filing it and before the first draft of a report prepared under the provisions of ORS 56.206(6) by submitting a written and signed or otherwise acknowledged request for withdrawal.

(2) In the event that a complaint is withdrawn before the first draft of a report is prepared, the Office will not release a report.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 56.206 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 56.200 to 56.209

History: CORP 1-2018, adopt filed 03/20/2018, effective 04/01/2018
