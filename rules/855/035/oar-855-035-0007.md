## Full text

855-035-0007 Sales Sales by drug outlets except itinerant vendors shall be made only from the premises at the location registered by the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 475.035 & 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.305, 689.315 & 689.325
