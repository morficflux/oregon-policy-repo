## Full text

855-035-0023 Disposal of Drugs Drugs that are outdated, damaged, deteriorated, misbranded, or adulterated shall be quarantined and physically separated from other drugs until they are destroyed or returned to their supplier.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 475.035, 689.155, 689.205, 689.305 & 689.315

History: PB 1-1992, f. & cert. ef. 1-31-92 1PB 2-1984, f. & ef. 3-7-84
