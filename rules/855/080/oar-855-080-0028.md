## Full text

855-080-0028 Excluded or Exempted Substances

(1) The board adopts the excluded substances list found in 21 CFR 1308.22 (v. 04/01/2025).

(2) The board adopts the exempt chemical preparations list found in 21 CFR 1308.24 (v. 04/01/2025).

(3) The board adopts the exempted prescription products list in the Table of Exempted Prescription Products (v. 12/03/2025) pursuant to 21 CFR 1308.32 (v. 04/01/2025). [Publications: Publications referenced are available for review at the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 & ORS 475.035 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.155 & ORS 475.035
