## Full text

855-043-0530 Dispensing Practitioner Drug Outlets - Drug Procurement The registered DPDO may only receive drugs from an Oregon Registered Drug Outlet (e.g. Wholesaler, Manufacturer or Pharmacy).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.155 & ORS 689.305
