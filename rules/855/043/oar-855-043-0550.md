## Full text

855-043-0550 Dispensing Practitioner Drug Outlets - Disposal of Drugs Drugs that are recalled, outdated, damaged, deteriorated, misbranded, adulterated, or identified as suspect or illegitimate must be documented, quarantined and physically separated from other drugs until they are destroyed or returned to the supplier.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.155 & ORS 689.305

History: BP 27-2021, minor correction filed 10/21/2021, effective 10/21/2021 BP 4-2017, adopt filed 11/30/2017, effective 12/01/2017
