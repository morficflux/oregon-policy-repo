## Full text

855-043-0535 Dispensing Practitioner Drug Outlets - Drug Storage All drugs must be stored according to manufacturer’s published guidelines and be stored in appropriate conditions of temperature, light, humidity, sanitation, ventilation, and space.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.155 & ORS 689.305
