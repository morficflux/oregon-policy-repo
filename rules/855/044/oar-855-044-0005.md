## Full text

855-044-0005 Definitions

(1) “Charitable Pharmacy” means a facility registered with the Oregon Board of Pharmacy for the purpose of receiving and distributing donated drugs.

(2) “Point-of-Contact” means an individual designated by a charitable pharmacy who serves as the primary contact person for the charitable pharmacy and who is responsible for managing the charitable pharmacy at that location.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.772 & 689.774
