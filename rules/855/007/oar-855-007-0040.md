## Full text

855-007-0040 Delegation of Authority When these rules are in effect, any authority vested in the Board may be exercised by the Executive Director (ED), any person acting as Executive Director in the ED’s absence or incapacity, or any person the ED designates to make such decisions on the ED’s behalf.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.165
