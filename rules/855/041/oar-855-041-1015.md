## Full text

855-041-1015 Operation of Pharmacy (Both Retail and Institutional Drug Outlets)

(1) Supervision. A pharmacy may only be operated when a pharmacist licensed to practice in this state is present. This means that the pharmacist must be physically present in the pharmacy or institutional facility.

(2) Sanitation: (a) Pharmacies shall be kept clean. (b) Persons working in a pharmacy shall practice appropriate infection control.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.305 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.305
