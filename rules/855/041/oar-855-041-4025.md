## Full text

855-041-4025 Purpose and Scope - Home Dialysis A Retail Drug Outlet for Home Dialysis supplies may provide dialysis solutions under the general supervision and direction of a pharmacist with special training in renal disease and dialysis to end stage renal disease (ESRD) patients who have chosen the option of home dialysis therapy and who have been appropriately trained.
