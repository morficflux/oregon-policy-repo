# Registration (OAR 855-041-5015)

## At a glance

## Full text

855-041-5015 Registration All residential drug outlets shall register annually with the Board of Pharmacy. Residential drug outlets which also provide outpatient pharmacy services shall also register as retail drug outlets.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.155
