## Full text

855-041-1145 New Containers Each pharmacy must dispense a drug in a new container that complies with the current provisions of the Poison Prevention Packaging Act in 16 CFR 1700 (v. 01/01/2025), 16 CFR 1701 (v. 01/01/2025), and 16 CFR 1702 (v. 01/01/2025). [Publications: Publications referenced are available for review at the agency.]
