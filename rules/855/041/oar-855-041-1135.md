## Full text

855-041-1135 Defines Labeling and Container Requirements for Repackage Drugs

(1) Drugs prepackaged by a pharmacy for later own use dispensing on prescription shall be in a container meeting USP standards and labeled to identify at a minimum: (a) Brand name, or generic name and manufacturer; (b) Strength; (c) Lot number; (d) Manufacturer’s expiration date, or any earlier date which, in the pharmacist’s professional judgment, is preferable.

(2) An internal control number which references manufacturer and lot number may be utilized.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689
