## Full text

855-041-1025 Disposal of Drugs Drugs that are outdated, damaged, deteriorated, misbranded, or adulterated shall be quarantined and physically separated from other drugs until they are destroyed or returned to their supplier.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 475.035, 689.155, 689.205, 689.305 & 689.315
