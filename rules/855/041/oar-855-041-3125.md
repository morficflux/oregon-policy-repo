## Full text

855-041-3125 Remote Processing - Prescription or Drug Order Processing A prescription or drug order for a controlled substance may be processed by a remote processing pharmacy when permitted by law and consistent with federal rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.155
