## Full text

855-041-6300 Absence of the Pharmacist The CPO must make appropriate arrangements for provision of drugs to the medical staff and other authorized personnel by use of a night cabinet or by access to the pharmacy, or both, for situations when hospital pharmacy services are not available.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.155 & ORS 689.605
