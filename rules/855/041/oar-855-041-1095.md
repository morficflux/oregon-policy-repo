## Full text

855-041-1095 Pharmacy Advertising No person shall advertise or otherwise purport to operate as a pharmacy or to advertise or purport to provide pharmacy services unless the person is registered with the Board pursuant to ORS 689.305.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 475.035, 689.155, 689.205, 689.305 & 689.315
