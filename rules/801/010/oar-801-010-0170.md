## Full text

801-010-0170 Publication of Disciplinary Action The Board in its discretion will publicize disciplinary action taken under ORS 673.170 in such manner and for such period as it may direct.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 673.410 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 673.170
