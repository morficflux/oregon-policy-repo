## Full text

801-040-0070 CPE Audit

(1) The Board may audit CPE reports submitted by licensees. When a licensee is selected for audit of CPE hours, licensees are required to submit the following: (a) All appropriate documentation that confirms attendance of all CPE programs and the number of eligible CPE hours; and (b) Any additional information required.

(2) Licensees must comply with all requests for information and any applicable deadlines for submitting information as required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 673.410 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 673.165
