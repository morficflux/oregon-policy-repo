## Full text

801-050-0070 Application for Administration of Peer Review Program

(1) Application. Applications for administration of a peer review program shall be submitted to the Board in writing and shall be accompanied by materials describing the operation of the proposed peer review program. Materials submitted by the sponsor must be sufficient to demonstrate that the proposed peer review program meets the minimum standards for performing and reporting on peer reviews.

(2) Sponsors that are over sighted by and report to the AICPA Peer Review Board are not required to submit an application for approval to the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL 2001, Ch. 638, Sec. 12 & ORS 673.455 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 673.455
