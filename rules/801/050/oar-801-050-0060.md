## Full text

801-050-0060 Confidentiality Client records that are received in the course of a peer review shall be treated as confidential in accordance with the provisions of Oregon Public Records Law (ORS Chapter 192).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 673.455 & OL 2001, Ch. 638 & Sec. 12 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 673.455
