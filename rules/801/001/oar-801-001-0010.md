## Full text

801-001-0010 Screening and Selection Procedures for Personal Services Contracts The Oregon Board of Accountancy adopts by this reference the Personal Contract Rules as promulgated by the Department of Administrative Services for procedures to be followed when entering into personal services contracts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279.051
