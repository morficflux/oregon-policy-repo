# Required (OAR 437-004-1440)

## At a glance

OAR 437-004-1440 — Required. Chapter 437, Division 004. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

437-004-1440 Required Postings Post signs reading, “No Smoking or Open Flame,” in all areas:

(1) For fueling;

(2) For receiving or dispensing flammable or liquids;

(3) For use or storage of flammable liquids; or

(4) Where there may be flammable or explosive gases, vapors, mists, dust, fibers or flyings. NOTE: Signs reading “FLAMMABLE — KEEP FIRE AWAY” will also be in compliance with this rule.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
