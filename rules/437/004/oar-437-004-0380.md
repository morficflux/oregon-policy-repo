## Full text

437-004-0380 Manually Propelled Mobile Ladder Stands and Scaffolds (Towers) Standards for the use of mobile work platforms and scaffolds are found in Division 2, Subdivision D, 1910.27 which applies to agricultural places of employment.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
