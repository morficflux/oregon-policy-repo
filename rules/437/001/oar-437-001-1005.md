## Full text

437-001-1005 Authority and Applicability of Rules

(1) OAR 437-001-1005 through 437-001-1065 are promulgated under the Director’s authority contained in ORS 654.097.

(2) The Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services delegates to the Administrator of Oregon OSHA the authority to enforce these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
