## Full text

437-001-1010 Purpose and Scope

(1) Nothing in these rules is intended to impose a duty upon the insurer or to transfer from the employer to the insurer responsibility set forth in ORS 654.001 to 654.991, or to impose liability other than these rules upon the insurer for failure to identify any unsafe conditions or occupational health and safety hazard.

(2) The purpose of these rules is to promote workplace health and safety by: (a) Establishing insurer and self-insured employer loss prevention services designed to advise employers on regulations, laws, means and methods for improving health and safety at their places of employment; and (b) Providing for the evaluation of insurers’ and self-insured employers’ loss prevention activities by Oregon OSHA to ensure compliance with the law and these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
