## Full text

437-001-0455 Application for Consultative Services Employers may make a verbal or written request for consultative services. A request must:

(1) Be made by an employer to a representative of the Division;

(2) Identify the employer and the location where the consultation is desired; and

(3) Define the specific problem or hazard, or other reason for the request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001-654.295
