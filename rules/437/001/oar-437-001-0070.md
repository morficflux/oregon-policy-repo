## Full text

437-001-0070 Inspection Warrants If a Compliance Officer is denied entry, Oregon OSHA may institute action to obtain an inspection warrant, as provided for in ORS 654.202 to 654.216.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
