## Full text

437-001-1055 Self-Insured and Group Self-Insured Employer Loss Prevention Programs Each self-insured employer and each member of a group self-insured program shall establish and implement a written occupational health and safety loss prevention program for each establishment. As a minimum requirement, the program shall:

(1) Provide for a loss prevention effort within the normal functions of the business for prevention or reduction of health and safety injuries and illnesses; and

(2) Inform its managers and workplace locations of the availability and the process for requesting loss prevention assistance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
