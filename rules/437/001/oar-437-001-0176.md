## Full text

437-001-0176 Determination of Penalty — Failure to Notify Employees of Advance Notice The Administrator, after considering the related facts, may assess a penalty not to exceed $1,000 for each violation of the employer’s failure to give notification by posting to employees of advanced notice.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
