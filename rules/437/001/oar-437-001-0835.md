## Full text

437-001-0835 Purpose of Rules These rules explain what assistance is available from the Worksite Redesign Program, who is qualified and how to receive assistance. The department may solicit applications for worksite redesign grants and product grants in order to prevent the recurrence of on-the-job injuries and illnesses.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.622(9) & 656.622 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.622
