## Full text

437-007-0315 Eye and Face Protection Where chips, sawdust or flying particles present a hazard, the employer must provide and require personnel to wear eye and face protection meeting the requirement of Division 2/I. NOTE: Logger-type mesh screen may be used for chain saw operators.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: OSHA 5-2003, f. 6-2-03, cert. ef. 12-1-03
