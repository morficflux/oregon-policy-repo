# Leg Protection (OAR 437-007-0325)

## At a glance

## Full text

437-007-0325 Leg Protection The employer must provide and require each employee who operates a chain saw to wear flexible ballistic nylon pads, chaps or other equivalent protection in a manner that protects their legs from the top of the thigh to the top of the boot from contact with the moving saw chain. EXCEPTION: This does not apply to an employee working aloft in trees when supported by climbing spurs and climbing belt.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: OSHA 5-2003, f. 6-2-03, cert. ef. 12-1-03
