## Full text

437-007-1525 Belayed (Snubbing) Climbing System

(1) The belay line must be a dynamic rope with a minimum 5400 pounds breaking strength.

(2) Do not use a body belay.

(3) Do not use a chest harness as the tie-in point.

(4) Follow the requirements of Appendix 7-K. [ED. NOTE: Appendix referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
