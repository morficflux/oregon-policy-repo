## Full text

437-007-0505 Bridges

(1) All bridge structures and surfaces must be: (a) Adequate to support the maximum imposed loads. (b) Maintained in good repair.

(2) All bridges must have rub rails constructed of wood, concrete or equivalent materials that: (a) Have a minimum height of 9 inches (6-inch by 6-inch timbers set on 4-inch by 6-inch blocks). (b) Are secured to the bridge deck.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: OSHA 5-2003, f. 6-2-03, cert. ef. 12-1-03
