## Full text

437-002-2022 Additional Oregon Rules for Powered Platforms

(1) Scope and application. This rule applies to powered platforms not regulated by 1910.66 Powered platforms for building maintenance, 1910.67 Vehicle-mounted elevating and rotating work platforms, and 1910.68 Manlifts.

(2) Powered work platforms identified in (2)(a) — (c) of this rule, used in general industry, must meet the requirements of Division 3, Subdivision L Scaffolds. (a) Manually Propelled Elevating Aerial Platforms covered by the scope of ANSI/SIA A92.3 – 1990. (b) Scissor lifts — Self-Propelled Elevating Work Platforms covered by the scope of ANSI/SIA A92.6 — 1990. (c) Boom Supported Elevating Work Platform covered by the scope of ANSI/SIA A92.5 — 1996.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
