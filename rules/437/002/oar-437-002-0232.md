## Full text

437-002-0232 Additional Oregon Rule for Derricks Rated Load Marking. A radius indicator or boom angle indicator, compatible with the load rating chart, shall be provided where it is clearly visible to the operator in his or her normal operating position on all derricks equipped with a movable working boom.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
