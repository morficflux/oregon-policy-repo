## Full text

437-002-0371 Scope and Application 29 CFR 1910.1025 applies to all occupational exposures to lead, except Construction (see 1926.62 in division 3, Construction). NOTE: §1910.1025(a)(1) and

(2) were not adopted. In Oregon, OAR 437-002-0371 applies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
