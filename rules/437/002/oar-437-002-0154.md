## Full text

437-002-0154 Individual Locks In addition to and not instead of the definition of "lockout device" in this section, the user must have the only key to each lock(s) or only the user may have the combination to each lock. NOTE: This exemption of construction, agriculture and maritime employment was not adopted in Oregon. Lockout/ tagout rules continues to apply to all Oregon employers.) NOTE: The following Oregon-initiated rule relates to 29 CFR 1910.147(c)(5):
