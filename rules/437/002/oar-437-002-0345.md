## Full text

437-002-0345 Inland Emergency Aid If conducting inland dive operation, the telephone or call numbers of the nearest local sheriff’s office shall be included on the “Emergency Aid” list.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: OSHA 5-1993, f. 5-3-93, cert. ef. 6-1-93
