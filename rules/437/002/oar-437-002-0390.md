## Full text

437-002-0390 Oregon Effective Dates

(1) The effective date for 29 CFR 1910.1450 in Oregon is August 8, 1990.

(2) Start up dates in Oregon: (a) Employers shall have developed and implemented a written Chemical Hygiene Plan no later than May 8, 1991; (b) 29 CFR 1910.1450(a)(2) shall not take effect until the employer has developed and implemented a written Chemical Hygiene Plan. NOTE: The Oregon-initiated rule is adopted in place of 1910.1450(k).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: OSHA 9-1990, f. 5-8-90, ef. 8-8-90
