## Full text

437-003-0925 Powder-Actuated Tools Powder-actuated tools used by employees shall meet all other applicable requirements of American National Standards Institute, ANSI A10.3-1985, Safety Requirements for Powder-Actuated Fastening Systems. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
