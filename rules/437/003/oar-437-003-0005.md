## Full text

437-003-0005 Additional Applicability If a specific type of equipment, process or practice is not limited to the construction industry, the provisions contained in other divisions of OAR chapter 437, Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Code, shall apply.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
