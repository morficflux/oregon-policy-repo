## Full text

437-003-1101 Asbestos Respiratory Protection Program The employer must implement a respiratory protection program in accordance with Division 2/I, 1910.134(b) through (d) (except (d)(1)(iii)), and (e) through (m) and (o), which covers each employee required by Division 3/Z, 1926.1101 Asbestos, to use a respirator. NOTE: This is in addition to other respiratory protection and medical surveillance requirements specified in these Asbestos rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: OSHA 5-2011, f. 12-8-11, cert. ef. 7-1-12
