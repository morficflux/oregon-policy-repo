## Full text

437-003-0071 Manually Propelled Elevating Aerial Platforms When using manually propelled elevating aerial platforms as covered by ANSI/SIA A92.3-1990, the manufacturer’s operating manual must be with the equipment. You must follow all operating and maintenance instructions and recommendations of the manufacturer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: OSHA 2-1997, f. & cert. ef. 3-12-97
