# Extension (OAR 437-003-0065)

## At a glance

## Full text

437-003-0065 Extension Ladders Extension ladders shall be equipped with necessary guide irons, locks, and hooks and shall be assembled so that the sliding (upper) section shall be on top of the base (lower) section.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: APD 8-1989, f. & ef. 7-7-89 APD 5-1989, f. 3-31-89, ef. 5-1-89
