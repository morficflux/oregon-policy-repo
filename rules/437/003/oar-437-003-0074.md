## Full text

437-003-0074 Scissor Lifts — Self-Propelled Elevating Work Platforms When using self-propelled elevating aerial platforms, scissor lifts, as covered by ANSI/SIA A92.6-1990, the manufacturer’s operating manual must be with the equipment. You must follow all operating and maintenance instructions and recommendations of the manufacturer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
