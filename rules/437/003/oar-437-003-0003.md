# Purpose (OAR 437-003-0003)

## Full text

437-003-0003 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to prescribe minimum safety requirements for employees engaged in construction work, including demolition, blasting and use of explosives, and power transmission distribution and maintenance work.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
