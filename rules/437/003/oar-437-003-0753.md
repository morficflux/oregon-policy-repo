## Full text

437-003-0753 Tag Lines Tag lines shall be used to control loads except when it is determined, by a qualified rigger, that they create a hazard.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295
