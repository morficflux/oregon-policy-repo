# Applicable Rules (OAR 437-003-0027)

## Full text

437-003-0027 Applicable Rules Whenever any employee is exposed to noise in the workplace, the requirements of OAR chapter 437, division 2/G, 1910.95, Occupational Noise Exposure shall apply. NOTE: §1926.52 was not adopted by the Department. In Oregon, 437-003-0027 applies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.001 - 654.295

History: OSHA 6-1994, f. & cert. ef. 9-30-94 APD 8-1989, f. & ef. 7-7-89
