## Full text

411-100-0000 Purpose and Scope The purpose of these rules is to provide for the administration and implementation of ORS 410.730 and 410.732, relating to the establishment of the Self-Sufficiency Trust Fund and the Disability Trust Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.730 and 410.732 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.730
