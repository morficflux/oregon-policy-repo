## Full text

411-305-0200 Statement of Purpose

(1) The rules in OAR chapter 411, division 305 prescribe standards, responsibilities, and procedures for Community Developmental Disabilities Programs to partner with families and other community-based networks in the coordination of supports and services to: (a) Maximize independence and increase the ability of a child to engage in a life that is fully integrated into the community; (b) Increase the ability of a family to care for their child in the family home; and (c) Strengthen the role of the family as the primary caregiver.

(2) Family support services include the supports and services described in OAR 411-305-0235.

(3) Family support services are individualized and built on family support and child and family-centered planning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 417.342 & 430.662 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.342, 427.005, 427.007, 427.104, 430.610, 430.620 & 430.662-430.670
