## Full text

411-310-0010 Statement of Purpose The purpose of these rules is to:

(1) Prescribe the operational procedures for the Developmental Disabilities Community Housing Program and the Community Housing Fund; and

(2) Implement and describe the acquisition, construction, rehabilitation, maintenance, and disposal of Community Housing established under the authority of ORS 427.330 to 427.345.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 & 410.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 427.330 - 427.345
