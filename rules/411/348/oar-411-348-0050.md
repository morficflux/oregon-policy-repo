## Full text

411-348-0050 License Expiration, Termination of Operations, and License Return

(1) Unless revoked, suspended, or terminated earlier, each license to operate a Host Home expires two years following the date of issuance.

(2) If the operation of a Host Home is discontinued for any reason, the license is considered to have been terminated.

(3) Each license is considered void immediately if the operation of a Host Home is discontinued by voluntary action of the licensee, if there is a change in ownership, or if there is a change in the in-residence caregiver.

(4) The license must be returned to the Department immediately upon suspension or revocation of the license or when operation is discontinued.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 427.104, 441.715, 443.450 & 443.455 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 427.104, 441.705-441.720, 441.740, 441.745, 443.384, 443.392, 443.400-443.445, 443.450, 443.455, 443.880, 443.881 & 443.991
