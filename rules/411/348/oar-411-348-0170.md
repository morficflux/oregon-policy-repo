## Full text

411-348-0170 Staffing Requirements

(1) A Host Home must have in-residence caregivers or staff present appropriate to the number of all of the following: (a) Children receiving services to meet the needs of the children in accordance with each child's ISP; (b) Other children in the Host Home; and (c) Care-dependent adults.

(2) There must be at least one in-residence caregiver or staff member present in the Host Home when a child enrolled to receive service in the Host Home is in the Host Home.

(3) A child may not be left alone in a vehicle, unless identified as appropriate in accordance with the child’s ISP.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 427.104, 441.715, 443.450 & 443.455 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 427.104, 441.705-441.720, 441.740, 441.745, 443.384, 443.392, 443.400-443.445, 443.450, 443.455, 443.880, 443.881 & 443.991
