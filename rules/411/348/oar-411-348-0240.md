## Full text

411-348-0240 Assessment of Fire Evacuation Assistance and Fire Safety Evacuation Plan

(1) A program provider must assess, within 24 hours of a child's entry to a Host Home, the child's ability to evacuate the Host Home in response to an alarm or simulated emergency.

(2) A program provider must document the level of assistance needed by each child to safely evacuate the Host Home and the documentation must be maintained in the child's records.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 427.104, 441.715, 443.450 & 443.455 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 427.104, 441.705-441.720, 441.740, 441.745, 443.384, 443.392, 443.400-443.445, 443.450, 443.455, 443.880, 443.881 & 443.991
