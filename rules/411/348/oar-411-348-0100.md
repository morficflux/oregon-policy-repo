## Full text

411-348-0100 Capacity

(1) A Host Home may only have a capacity to serve up to two children enrolled in services.

(2) The capacity of a Host Home is based on consideration of the following factors: (a) The physical requirements as specified by these rules; and (b) The number of children not enrolled in services and care-dependent adults residing in the Host Home.

(3) The total number of children receiving services, children residing in the Host Home age 18 or younger, and care-dependent adults may not exceed five persons in a Host Home.
