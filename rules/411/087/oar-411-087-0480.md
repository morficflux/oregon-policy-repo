# Waste (OAR 411-087-0480)

## At a glance

## Full text

411-087-0480 Waste Processing Systems Storage and disposal. Space and facilities shall be provided for the sanitary storage and disposal of waste. Incinerator units must be a system approved by the Department of Environmental Quality. Compliance with OAR 333, division 18 is required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 & 441.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.055 & 441.615

History: SSD 22-1991, f. 12-31-91, cert. ef. 1-1-92
