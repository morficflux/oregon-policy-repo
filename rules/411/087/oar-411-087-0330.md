## Full text

411-087-0330 Employee Rooms

(1) Rooms Required. The facility shall have an employee lounge and room(s) for conferences, meetings and inservice training. This requirement may be met with a multi-purpose room, but must be in addition to space required for residents.

(2) Exceptions. Facilities continuously licensed since January 1, 1992 shall be exempt from section

(1) of this rule unless otherwise provided by OAR 411-087-0010.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 & 441.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.055 & 441.615

History: SSD 22-1991, f. 12-31-91, cert. ef. 1-1-92
