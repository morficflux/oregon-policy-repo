## Full text

411-087-0240 Personal Care Services Separate room or designated space and appropriate equipment shall be provided for hair care and grooming needs of residents.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 & 441.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.055 & 441.615
