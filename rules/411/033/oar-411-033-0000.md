## Full text

411-033-0000 Purpose and Scope The rules in OAR chapter 411, division 033 ensure in-home care agencies, as one of the Medicaid in-home service provider options, provide services to maximize independence, empowerment, dignity, and human potential through the provision of flexible, efficient, and suitable services. In-home services fill the role of complementing and supplementing an individual's personal abilities, so the individual is able to continue to live in their own home.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 410.070, 410.090 & 413.085 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.010, 410.020, 410.070 & 413.085
