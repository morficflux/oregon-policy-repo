## Full text

411-058-0050 Client Records A referral agent shall maintain client records sufficient to document compliance with ORS 443.370 to 443.376 for a period of three years from the date of any disclosure statement given to a client.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 410.070 & 443.373 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.050 & 410.070
