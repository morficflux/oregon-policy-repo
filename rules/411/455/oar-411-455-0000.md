## Full text

411-455-0000 Statement of Purpose

(1) The rules in OAR chapter 411, division 455 prescribe standards, responsibilities, and procedures for the delivery of State Plan personal care (SPPC) services to individuals who are eligible for services through Community Developmental Disabilities Programs, Brokerages, or Children’s Intensive In-Home Services.

(2) SPPC services are intended to supplement an individual's personal abilities and available alternative resources, including the individual's natural support system, for the individual to live independently at home and in the community.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 427.104 & 430.662 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 427.007, 430.610, 430.620 & 430.662-430.670
