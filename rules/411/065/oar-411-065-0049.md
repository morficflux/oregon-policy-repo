## Full text

411-065-0049 Reimbursement

(1) The total monthly rate for specialized living services must not exceed the amount approved for each resident in accordance with the terms of the contract signed by the Department.

(2) Any add-on costs for nursing services must not exceed the RN Contract fee rate.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.070
