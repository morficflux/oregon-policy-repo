## Full text

411-375-0000 Purpose

(1) The rules in OAR chapter 411, division 375 establish the standards and procedures governing independent providers and the fiscal services provided on behalf of individuals who employ or contract with an independent provider.

(2) Independent providers provide home and community-based waiver, state plan, and general fund services to individuals eligible for developmental disabilities services and receiving supports authorized by a case management entity.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.600, 410.606-619 & 427.007
