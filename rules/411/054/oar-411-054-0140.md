# Additional Authority (OAR 411-054-0140)

## Full text

411-054-0140 Additional Authority SPD may commence a suit in equity to enjoin operation of a facility when:

(1) A facility is operated without a valid license; or

(2) Notice of revocation has been given and a reasonable time has been allowed for placement of individuals in other facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 & 443.450 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 443.400 - 443.455 & 443.991

History: SPD 14-2007, f. 8-31-07, cert. ef. 11-1-07
