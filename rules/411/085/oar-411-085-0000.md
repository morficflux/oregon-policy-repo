## Full text

411-085-0000 Statement of Purpose The purpose of these rules (OAR 411, divisions 85–89) is to establish requirements for nursing facilities that promote quality care and maximization of personal choice and independence for residents. Whenever possible, care shall be directed toward returning the resident to his/her own residence or to the least restrictive alternative environment within the shortest time possible.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 & 441.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.055 & 441.615
