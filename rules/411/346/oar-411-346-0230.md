## Full text

411-346-0230 Appeals

(1) Upon written notice of denial, suspension, revocation, or non-renewal of a certificate from the Department, an applicant or foster provider may request a contested case hearing to appeal the decision pursuant to ORS 183.413 to 183.470.

(2) The written request must be submitted within 10 days of the denial, suspension, revocation, or non-renewal notification date and must specifically state the reasons for the appeal.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 & 443.835 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 430.215, 443.830 & 443.835
