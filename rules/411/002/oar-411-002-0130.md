## Full text

411-002-0130 Designation of a Type B1 Area Agency on Aging

(1) A designated Type B1 Area Agency on Aging shall meet the requirements of OAR 411-002-0110.

(2) Notwithstanding OAR 411-002-0110(4), a designated Type B1 Area Agency on Aging shall be: (a) An office or agency of a unit of general purpose local government which is designated by the Chief elected official of the unit to serve as an Area Agency; or (b) Any office or agency designated by the appropriate chief elected officials of any combination of units of general or general and special purpose local government formed in accordance with ORS Chapter 190.

(3) Designation as any category of Type B1 area agency on aging requires written notice to the Division at least six months prior to the proposed implementation of change of status. The Division will notify the Governor’s Commission on Senior Services and the Oregon Disabilities Commission.
