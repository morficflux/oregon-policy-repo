## Full text

411-071-0035 Certification Process

(1) Any hospital, agency, program, or Area Agency on Aging must obtain certification from the Department before providing admission assessment services.

(2) The certification issued to a program is not valid for use by any other program.

(3) Certification is valid for the length of the contract unless revoked or suspended by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.505 - 410.545 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.530
