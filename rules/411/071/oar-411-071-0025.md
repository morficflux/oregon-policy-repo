## Full text

411-071-0025 Recommendations for Placement/Prohibition on Conflict of Interest

(1) If the individual chooses to have long-term care information provided by a certified program, the certified program must provide information about appropriate care settings and services.

(2) A certified program must not recommend placement to a specific nursing facility, assisted living facility, residential care facility or adult foster home in which it has a financial interest.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.525 & 410.530
