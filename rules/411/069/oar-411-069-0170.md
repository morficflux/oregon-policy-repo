## Full text

411-069-0170 Sunset Provision The long term care assessment applies to long term care facility gross revenue received on or after June 2003 and before July 1, 2032.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 410.070 & 411.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.750, OL 2003, ch 736 & OL 2018, ch 66
