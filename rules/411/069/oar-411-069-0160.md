## Full text

411-069-0160 Limitations on the Imposition of the Long Term Care Facility Assessment The long term care facility assessment may be imposed only in a calendar quarter for which the long term care facility reimbursement rate that is part of the Oregon Medicaid reimbursement system was calculated according to the methodology described in Oregon Laws 2003, chapter 736, section 24.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 410.070 & 411.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.750 & OL 2003 Ch. 736
