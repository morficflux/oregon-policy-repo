# Days (OAR 411-070-0050)

## Full text

411-070-0050 Days Chargeable The Department will pay for the day of admission but not for the day of discharge, transfer, or death except as provided for in OAR 411-070-0110. When the day of admission is the same as the day of discharge, the Department will only pay for one day.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 & 414.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.070 & 414.065
