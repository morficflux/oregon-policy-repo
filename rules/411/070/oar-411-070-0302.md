## Full text

411-070-0302 Filing of Revised Financial Statements

(1) Revised Nursing Facility Financial Statements may only be filed with prior written authorization from the Department.

(2) An amended report must be postmarked within six months of the end of the fiscal reporting period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 414.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.070
