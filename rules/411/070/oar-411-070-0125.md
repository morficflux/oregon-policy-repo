# Medicare, (Title XVIII) SPD shall pay on behalf of eligible individuals the coinsurance rate established under (OAR 411-070-0125)

## Full text

411-070-0125 Medicare, (Title XVIII) SPD shall pay on behalf of eligible individuals the coinsurance rate established under Medicare, Part A, Hospital Care, for care rendered from the 21st day through the 100th day of care in a Medicare certified nursing facility. SPD shall pay the appropriate rate as described in these rules for care beyond the 100th day. Payment shall be subject to documentation required for the rate.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.070 & 414.065
