# Quality Assurance (OAR 411-425-0045)

## At a glance

OAR 411-425-0045 — Quality Assurance. Chapter 411, Division 425. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

411-425-0045 Quality Assurance A CME must submit all requested documentation to the Department and allow the Department or the Oregon Health Authority to observe assessors while conducting ONAs for quality assurance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 427.104, 427.105, 427.115 & 430.662 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 427.104, 427.105, 427.115 & 430.662
