## Full text

411-035-0000 Purpose

(1) These rules ensure individuals served by the Department of Human Services, Aging and People with Disabilities program have equal access to required and optional K-State Plan services that are not defined in other rules in OAR chapter 411. The services in these rules are intended to assist and empower an eligible individual to maximize their independence, dignity, and human potential through the provision of flexible, efficient, and suitable services.

(2) Services described in these rules include: (a) Backup Systems and Assistive Technology. (b) Chore Services. (c) Environmental Modification Services. (d) Transition Services. (e) Voluntary Consumer Training.

(3) Payments for the services in these rules are limited to the lowest possible cost which will adequately meet the individual's minimum necessary needs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.050, 410.040, 410.090, 410.210 to 410.300 & 441.520
