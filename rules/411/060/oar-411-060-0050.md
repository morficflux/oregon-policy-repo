# Reporting (OAR 411-060-0050)

## At a glance

OAR 411-060-0050 — Reporting. Chapter 411, Division 060. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

411-060-0050 Reporting All Facilities with Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments (CLIA) waivers must follow the reporting requirements contained in OARs 333-018-0000 thru 333-018-0145. This includes the requirements contained in OAR 333-018-0011 for reporting race, ethnicity, language and disability data.
