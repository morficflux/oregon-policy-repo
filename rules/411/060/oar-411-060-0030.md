# Outbreak (OAR 411-060-0030)

## Full text

411-060-0030 Outbreak Prevention Testing Effective November 1, 2020, a Facility must coordinate or complete specimen collection for COVID-19 testing of all Residents, Facility Staff and Associated Staff within 72 hours of identification of a new case of COVID-19 in either a Resident, Facility Staff or Associated Staff.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.025, 441.055, 441.615, 441.630, 441.637, 441.650, 443.400-443.455, 443.991 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.025, 441.055, 441.615, 441.630, 441.637, 441.650, 443.400-443.455, 443.991
