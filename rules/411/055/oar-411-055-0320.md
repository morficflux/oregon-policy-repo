## Full text

411-055-0320 Service Provider Requirements The service provider shall be a Residential Care Facility that must:

(1) Have a history of an unencumbered license from the Department and meet the requirements of OAR 411-054-0016.

(2) Meet all Home and Community-Based Service requirements set forth under OAR chapter 411, division 4.

(3) Meet the staffing requirements described in OAR 411-055-0330.

(4) Comply with all the rules in OAR chapter 411, division 54.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 410.070, 443.450 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.050, 410.070, 443.400 - 443.455, 443.991
