## Full text

411-046-0200 Compensation and Billing

(1) All billing and claims must comply with OAR 407-120-0330; OAR 407-120-0340; and chapter 410, division 120, as applicable.

(2) Compensation for Behavior Support Services in supplemental or specific needs setting contracts shall be defined through the Department contract.

(3) The Department may adjust rates in underserved areas to ensure individuals have access to services.

(4) Failure to comply with standards in this rule may result in determination of overpayment for which restitution may be sought.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 410.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 410.070
