## Full text

411-360-0280 Criminal Penalties

(1) Operating an AFH-DD without a license is punishable as a Class C misdemeanor pursuant to ORS 443.991(5).

(2) Refusing to allow the Department access and inspection to the AFH-DD or access to the AFH-DD regarding fire safety by state and local fire inspector, is punishable as a Class B misdemeanor pursuant ORS 443.991(6).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 410.070, 443.725, 443.730, 443.735, 443.738, 443.742, 443.760, 443.765, 443.767, 443.775 & 443.790 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 443.705 - 443.825
