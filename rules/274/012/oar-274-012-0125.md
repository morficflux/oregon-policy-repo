## Full text

274-012-0125 Misrepresentation of Emergency Financial Assistance The Department may require immediate reimbursement of funds, either in total or in part, if it is determined that applicant intentionally submitted invalid, incomplete, or fraudulent information when applying for funds from this Program, or if it is discovered that the funds were not used for the approved purpose(s).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.030, 406.050, 406.130, 408.010 & 408.500 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 406.030, 406.050, 406.130, 408.010 & 408.500
