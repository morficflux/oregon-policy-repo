## Full text

274-012-0115 Evidence of Need When an application is made for emergency financial assistance, the applicant shall provide the Department with the documentation required under Subsection 0105, together with statement(s) and other documentation satisfactory to the Department indicating how the desired financial assistance will benefit the veteran and his/her immediate family. The Department may require additional documentation or information from the applicant as it deems necessary or appropriate.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.030, 406.050, 406.130, 408.010 & 408.500 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 406.030, 406.050, 406.130, 408.010 & 408.500
