## Full text

274-012-0105 Evidence Required to Establish Eligibility The following documentation shall be submitted to the Department when applying for program funds:

(1) A copy of evidence of separation of military service under honorable conditions.

(2) Proof of current Oregon residence.

(3) Proof of any change in name: (a) Where a veteran's name has been legally changed since discharge, a certified copy of the Court Order, marriage certificate, or divorce decree must be furnished to the Department; (b) Where a veteran's name has been changed, but not legally, an affidavit from the veteran and affidavits from at least two disinterested persons must be furnished to the Department to show such change.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.030, 406.050, 406.130, 408.010 & 408.500 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 406.030, 406.050, 406.130, 408.010 & 408.500
