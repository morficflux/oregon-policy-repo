## Full text

274-012-0131 Waiver of Rules Subject to the limitations of the law, and at its sole discretion, the Department of Veterans' Affairs may waive all or part of these administrative rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.030, 406.050, 406.130, 408.010 & 408.500 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 406.030, 406.050, 406.130, 408.010 & 408.500
