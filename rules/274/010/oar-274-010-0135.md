## Full text

274-010-0135 Additional Information and Evidence The Department of Veterans' Affairs may, in addition to the information and evidence specified in the application, instructions, or rules and regulations, require such additional information and evidence as he deems necessary to establish the applicant’s eligibility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 408 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 408.010 - 408.070
