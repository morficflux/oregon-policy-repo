## Full text

274-010-0160 Reports by Executive Head of Educational Institution On or before the tenth day of each calendar month, the executive head of each institution of learning shall submit certified copies of Form 1006-M (Statement of Educational Aid), containing the following information.

(1) Name of each beneficiary;

(2) Amount of training; and

(3) Progress.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 408 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 408.050
