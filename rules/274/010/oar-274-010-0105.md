## Full text

274-010-0105 Who May Apply for Educational Aid Any person who qualifies as a beneficiary under the provisions of ORS 408.010 is eligible to apply for educational aid.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 408 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 408.010 - 408.030
