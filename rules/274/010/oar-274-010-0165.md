## Full text

274-010-0165 Lost, Stolen, or Destroyed Warrant If educational aid warrant is lost, stolen, or destroyed before negotiation, beneficiary may obtain payment of by furnishing to the State Treasurer evidence that value has not been received and an acceptable indemnity bond.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 408 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 408.060
