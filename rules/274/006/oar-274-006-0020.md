## Full text

274-006-0020 Recordkeeping Confidentiality Any information obtained in the criminal records check is confidential. The Department must restrict the dissemination of information obtained in the criminal records check. Only those persons, as identified by the Department, with a demonstrated and legitimate need to know the information, may have access to criminal records check records.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.534, 406.030 & 2009 SB 94 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.534, 406.030 & 2009 SB 94
