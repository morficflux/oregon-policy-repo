# Purpose (OAR 274-006-0001)

## At a glance

OAR 274-006-0001 — Purpose. Chapter 274, Division 006. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

274-006-0001 Purpose These rules provide for the Department of Veterans' Affairs (Department) acquisition of information about a subject individual's criminal history through criminal records checks and its use of that information to determine whether the individual is fit to provide services to the Department as an employee, volunteer, contractor or vendor. The fact that the Department determines that an individual is fit does not guarantee the individual a position as a Department employee, volunteer, contractor or vendor or that the individual will be hired by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.534 and 406.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.534 and 406.030

History: DVA 1-2009, f. 12-22-09 cert. ef. 1-1-10
