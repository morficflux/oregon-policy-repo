## Full text

274-045-0030 Modification of Application An application may be modified such as to the amount of the loan requested, legal description, amount of security or plans and specifications, but the modification must be requested in writing by the veteran and shall be subject to the approval of the Director. Substitution of security is not allowed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 407

History: DVA 2-2001, f. & cert. ef. 5-23-01
