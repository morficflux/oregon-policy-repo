## Full text

274-045-0471 Taxes, Hazard Insurance and Flood Insurance If delinquent taxes become a lien against the security, or the borrower fails to maintain the required insurance, the Director may pay the taxes or insurance and if so paid, will collect the amount paid by ODVA from the borrower through an escrow account per the provisions of OAR 274-045-0150.
