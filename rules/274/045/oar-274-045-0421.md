## Full text

274-045-0421 Interest Rate As provided by ORS 407.327, the interest rate on veterans' home improvement loans for which applications are received on or after June 29, 2001 shall be fixed and consistent with OAR 274-045-0070.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.030, 407.115, 407.125 & Art. XI-A OR Const. Statutes/Other Implemented: Art. XI-A OR Const.

History: DVA 10-2001, f. & cert. ef. 12-26-01
