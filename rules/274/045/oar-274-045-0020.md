## Full text

274-045-0020 Who May Apply for Loan

(1) A loan shall be made only to an individual veteran.

(2) Joint loans or loans to a cooperative shall not be valid.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 407

History: DVA 2-2001, f. & cert. ef. 5-23-01
