## Full text

274-045-0050 Appraisal of Property An appraisal shall be made to assist in establishing the loan value only after a complete application has been received.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.030, 407.115 & 407.225(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 407
