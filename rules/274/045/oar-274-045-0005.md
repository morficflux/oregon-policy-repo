## Full text

274-045-0005 Purpose and Objectives OAR 274, division 045, is established to administer and enforce ORS 407.075 through 407.595. These rules, together with the Post Vietnam Era Veterans' Home Loan Origination Guide/Mortgage Broker Loan Origination Guide, shall implement the Post Vietnam Era Veterans’ Home Loan Program. The program's objective is to provide funds to finance owner-occupied, residential housing for qualified veterans in the State of Oregon, thereby encouraging home ownership of residential housing by such veterans.
