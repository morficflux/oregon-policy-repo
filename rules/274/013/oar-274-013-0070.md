## Full text

274-013-0070 Waiver of Rules The Department may waive or modify any requirements of OAR 274, division 013, in order to achieve substantial justice and the purposes of the Program unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable federal or state law.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2008 OL Ch. 18 & ORS 406.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2008 OL Ch. 18

History: DVA 6-2008, f. 9-30-08, cert. ef. 10-1-08
