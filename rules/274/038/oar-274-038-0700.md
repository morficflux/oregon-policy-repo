## Full text

274-038-0700 Grant Administration The Department shall administer the grant program.

(1) The Department and recipient shall execute a written agreement, prior to the disbursal of funds.

(2) The Department will prepare the agreement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.030 & ORS 406.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 406.142 (2017 HB 2891)
