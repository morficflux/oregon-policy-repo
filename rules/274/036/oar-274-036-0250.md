## Full text

274-036-0250 Grant Amount

(1) Veteran Educational Bridge Grant total disbursement amounts will not exceed $10,000 per veteran, per lifetime.

(2) Grant award amounts will be dependent upon financial need as documented by the veteran in the application provided by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.005(4) & ORS 406.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 408.115, ORS 408.125 & HB 4132 (2026)
