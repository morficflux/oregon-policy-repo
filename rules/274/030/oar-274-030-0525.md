## Full text

274-030-0525 Fiscal Division of Funds for Veterans’ Organizations The Department shall not disburse more than one-half of the funds available for disbursement to veterans' organizations under ORS 406.310 during the first 12 months of the Department’s fiscal biennium.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 406.030, 406.310 & 406.330
