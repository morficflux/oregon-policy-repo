## Full text

274-037-0420 Use of Grant Funds Grant funds may be used subject to all allowances and restrictions set forth in ORS 406.530(3) and other expenses such as capital improvement and capital assets, as provided for in policy and guidance by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: SB 35 (chapter 731 Oregon Laws 2019) & ORS 406.005(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 406.530
