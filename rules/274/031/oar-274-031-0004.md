# Certification (OAR 274-031-0004)

## At a glance

OAR 274-031-0004 — Certification. Chapter 274, Division 031. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

274-031-0004 Certification Testing ODVA shall require that all new VSOs and VSOs being reestablished must take the following examinations within a timeframe as determined by the Director or the Director's designee per OAR 274-031-0003:

(1) Milestone examination; and

(2) Certification examination.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406.452 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 406.050 & 406.452

History: DVA 1-2011, f. & cert. ef. 3-24-11
