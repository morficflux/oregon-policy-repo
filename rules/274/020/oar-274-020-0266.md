## Full text

274-020-0266 Eligibility to Apply Eligibility to apply for a loan under this division is subject to the eligibility criteria of Article X1-A of the Oregon Constitution and qualification under the Internal Revenue Code as a qualified veteran. The acceptance of an application and granting of a loan is further subject to the provisions of ORS Chapter 407, OAR division 020, 025, 045, other applicable law, and the policies and procedures of the Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs (ODVA), and at the discretion of the director of ODVA.
