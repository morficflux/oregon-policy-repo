## Full text

274-020-0305 Filing Period An applicant may file to secure a veterans’ loan at any time after separation from the service, even though he has re-enlisted and is not separated at the time of application.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 407.115, 407.125 & 407.205

History: DVA 35, f. 12-19-68, ef. 1-11-69 DVA 26, f. 12-13-60 DVA 22-1957, f. 11-15-57, ef. 11-14-57
