## Full text

274-020-0375 Lost, Stolen, or Destroyed Warrants or Checks If loan proceeds in the form of a warrant or check issued by the Director are lost, stolen, or destroyed before negotiation, the payee may obtain payment by filing with the Director evidence that value has not been received.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 406 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 407.115 & 407.495
