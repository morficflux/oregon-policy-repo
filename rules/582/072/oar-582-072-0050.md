## Full text

582-072-0050 Supported employment post-employment services

(1) Shall be only those needed services that are unavailable from an extended services provider; and

(2) Shall meet rehabilitation needs that do not require a complex and comprehensive provision of services (34 CFR §361.46(c)); and

(3) Shall be limited in scope and duration (34 CFR §361.5(c)(42)).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 344.530 & 344.570 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 344.511 - 344.690 & 344.710 - 344.730
