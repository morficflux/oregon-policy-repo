## Full text

582-100-0005 Determination of Need for an Order of Selection

(1) The Program may implement an Order of Selection if it determines that it cannot provide the full range of vocational rehabilitation services to all eligible individuals, based on projected fiscal and personnel resources, and the rehabilitation needs of individuals eligible to be served by the Program.

(2) The Program shall determine whether an Order of Selection is necessary (in accordance with 34 CFR § 361.36): (a) Prior to the beginning of each federal fiscal year; or (b) Due to changed circumstances within the current federal fiscal year.

(3) The Program must consult with the State Rehabilitation Council ( 34 CFR 361.36(f) ) regarding: (a) Need to establish an order of selection, including any reevaluation of the need; (b) Priority categories of the particular order of selection; (c) Criteria for determining individuals with the most significant disabilities; and (d) Administration of the order of selection.
