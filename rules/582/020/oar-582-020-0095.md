## Full text

582-020-0095 Civil Action Any party who disagrees with the reviewing official’s final order may bring a civil action with respect to the disputed action. The action may be brought in any state court of competent jurisdiction or in a district court of the United States of competent jurisdiction without regard to the amount in controversy.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 344.530 & 344.590 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 344.530 & ORS 344.540 & 344.590
