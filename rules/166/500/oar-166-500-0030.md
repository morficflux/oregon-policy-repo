## Full text

166-500-0030 Notice of Proposed Rulemaking Filing Requirements

(1) Notice of proposed rulemaking filings, in addition to the requirements outlined in ORS 183.335, must include the following components: (a) Caption; (b) Rule number and title; (c) Proposed rule text, which must comply with OAR 166-500-0055; (d) Associated statutory/other authority and statutes/other implemented, as defined in OAR 166-500-0010; (e) Rule summary provided for each rule; and (f) If applicable, a PDF of all rule attachments containing items such as charts, drawings, forms, graphs, maps, seals, tables, and other non-text information that is directly related to the rule and is not included in the rule text. See OAR 166-500-0060.

(2) Agencies adopting or amending rules incorporating published standards by reference may omit copies of the publications if applicable, under ORS 183.355(2). Agencies must explain how the public may obtain referenced items.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.355 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.360, ORS 183.355 & ORS 183.335
