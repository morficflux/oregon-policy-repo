# Purpose (OAR 166-017-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

166-017-0005 Purpose Agencies must ensure access to all public records as defined by ORS 192.410 to 192.505 for the entire length of the retention period approved by the State Archivist. Electronic public records are particularly susceptible to accidental deletion, damage and obsolescence. These rules help to ensure that public records maintained in electronic format are accessible for their scheduled retention period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.050, 192.060 & 192.105 Statutes/Other Implemented: 357.825(2), 357.855 & 357.895
