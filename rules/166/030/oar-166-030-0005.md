# Purpose (OAR 166-030-0005)

## Full text

166-030-0005 Purpose This Division specifies requirements for agency records management programs, and specifies the lawful authority and methods to destroy or otherwise dispose of public records. They apply to all public records, regardless of medium or physical format, created and stored by state and local agencies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192 & 357 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192 & 357
