# Prior (OAR 166-030-0041)

## Full text

166-030-0041 Prior Audit of Fiscal Public Records Required Public records of fiscal transactions, regardless of medium or physical format, may not be destroyed until the minimum retention period has passed and the person charged with their audit has released them for destruction. If federal funds are involved, requirements of the United States government shall be observed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192 & 357 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192, 357.855 & 357.895
