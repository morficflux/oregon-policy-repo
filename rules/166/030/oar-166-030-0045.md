## Full text

166-030-0045 Suspension of Scheduled Public Records Destruction A scheduled destruction of records, regardless of medium or physical format, which are the subject of a public records request or pending litigation shall be suspended until the request or litigation has been resolved. Only those records which have been specifically requested need be retained.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192 & 357 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192, 357.855 & 357.895
