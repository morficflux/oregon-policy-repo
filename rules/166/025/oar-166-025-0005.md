## Full text

166-025-0005 Purpose and Application of Microfilm Standards Rules The authority for these rules is found in ORS 192.070, 357.825(2), and 357.895. State agencies and political subdivisions shall comply with these rules for microfilming public records. These rules contain the measures necessary to insure that the informational content of public records is protected for the life of the record.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.070, 357.825(2) & 357.895 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 357.825(2), 357.855 & 357.895
