## Full text

173-012-0000 Board Administration Fees For all Network Plans, the board may charge fees to account owners in order to pay for the board's and the network’s administrative expenses. Fees shall be collected on a predetermined basis and be paid to the board and the network. The board may from time to time review and adjust fees.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.853(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.857(6) & 348.853(2)
