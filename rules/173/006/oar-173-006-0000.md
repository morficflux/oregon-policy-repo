# Account (OAR 173-006-0000)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

173-006-0000 Account Owner Any person, including a custodian under UGMA/UTMA, who is legally able to contract under applicable state law and who meets federal and state legal requirements governing the network, is eligible to establish an account.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.853(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.841 & 348.873
