## Full text

173-016-0010 Waivers The executive director may waive non statutory requirements of OAR chapter 173 if such a waiver would serve the best interests of the network and the board and would not violate the Code.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.853(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.841 - 348.873
