# Investment Policies (OAR 173-014-0000)

## At a glance

## Full text

173-014-0000 Investment Policies The Board shall establish investment policies for the Network Plans. These policies shall be reviewed, and may be modified, from time to time as the Board, in its sole discretion, determines. Any change to a plan’s investment policy shall apply prospectively.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.853(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.841 - 348.873
