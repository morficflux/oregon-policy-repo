## Full text

173-014-0010 Investment Direction Investment direction by the account owner or designated beneficiary of an account is prohibited unless otherwise permitted by section 529.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.853(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.841 - 348.873
