## Full text

173-014-0005 Reassignment of Accounts under the Age-Based and Years-to-College Investment Options For all Network Plans, investment direction by the account owner or designated beneficiary of an account is prohibited unless otherwise permitted by the Code.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.853(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.841 - 348.873
