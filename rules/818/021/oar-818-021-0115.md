# Display of (OAR 818-021-0115)

## At a glance

## Full text

818-021-0115 Display of Licenses Every licensee of the Board shall have conspicuously displayed their current license in every office where that licensee practices in plain sight of the licensee’s patients.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 679.120, 679.250(7), 680.075 & 680.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 679.020, 679.025 & 680.020

History: DE 1-1993, f. & cert. ef. 1-29-93
