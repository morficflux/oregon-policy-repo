## Full text

818-021-0125 Reapplication Following Denial of Licensure A person whose application for licensure has been denied on grounds other than failure of the licensure examination may not reapply for five years from the date of the Board's Final Order denying licensure.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 679 & 680 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 679.060, 679.065, 679.070, 679.080, 680.040, 680.050, 680.060, 680.070 & 680.072
