## Full text

818-001-0011 Time for Requesting a Contested Case Hearing A request for a contested case hearing must be in writing and must be received by the Board within twenty-one days from the date the contested case notice was served.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 679.250 & 183.341 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 679.250 & 183.341 OL 1999 & Ch. 849
