## Full text

738-124-0090 SOAR Program

(1) SOAR Project List. The Department shall prepare, as approved by the Director, a list of proposed projects that serve at least one of the purposes described in OAR 738-124-0010 (“SOAR project list”). The Department shall provide the Board the SOAR project list.

(2) Approval by Board. The Board shall approve or reject projects listed in the SOAR project list at a public meeting. The Board may increase or decrease dollar amounts allocated to projects it approves from the SOAR project list.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 835.040, ORS 835.035, ORS 835.112 & ORS 319.023 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 835.040, ORS 835.025 & ORS 319.023
