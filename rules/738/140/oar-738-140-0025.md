## Full text

738-140-0025 Intentional Violation of Public Body Registration of Unmanned Aircraft Systems Requirement Violation of any provision of ORS 837.360 concerning public body registration of unmanned aircraft systems may incur a civil penalty of an amount computed using the formula described in OAR 738-140-0030, whichever is greater, where “BF” is the base fine and shall be equal to $111.00. The maximum civil penalty that may be imposed under these rules is $10,000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 153.022, 183.745, 835.035, 835.112 & 837.998 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 837.005 - 837.998

History: AVIA 6-2015, f. & cert. ef. 12-15-15
