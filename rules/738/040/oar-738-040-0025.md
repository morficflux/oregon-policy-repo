# Equipment (OAR 738-040-0025)

## At a glance

## Full text

738-040-0025 Equipment All seaplanes must be equipped as required by the regulations of the Federal Aviation Administration specifically for seaplanes that are in effect on November 1, 1996.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 835.035 & 835.080 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 835.035, 835.080 & 835.085

History: AERO 1-1997, f. & cert. ef. 2-28-97 1AD 5-1981, f. & ef. 11-18-81 1AD 3-1981, f. 7-23-81, ef. 8-1-81
