# Access (OAR 738-015-0055)

## At a glance

OAR 738-015-0055 — Access. Chapter 738, Division 015. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

738-015-0055 Access Permits Effective August 15, 2001, the date of approval of the Leasing Policy for Commercial Property by the State Aviation Board, the Department shall not issue any new access permits until all State-owned airport property has been developed, except at those airports where access permits already exist.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 835.035, 835.040 & 835.112 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 935.035, 935.040, 835.112 & 836.055

History: AVIA 1-2002, f. & cert. ef. 9-3-02
