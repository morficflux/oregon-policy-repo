# Enforcement (OAR 738-035-0030)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

738-035-0030 Enforcement The Department shall provide consistent, uniform, and fair enforcement of these minimum standards to accomplish the Department’s goals and promote successful commercial business operations at all State-owned airports. The Department may, therefore, provide in its written agreements any appropriate provisions to assist the Department in enforcing these operating minimum standards.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 835.035, 835.040 & 835.112 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 835.035, 835.040, 835.112 & 836.055
