# Agricultural Application (OAR 738-050-0080)

## At a glance

OAR 738-050-0080 — Agricultural Application. Chapter 738, Division 050. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

738-050-0080 Agricultural Application Such operations, whether performed by fixed-wing aircraft or helicopters, shall load and service in those areas of the airport designated for that purpose.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184, 835 & 836 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 836.095 & 836.505

History: 1AD 1-1982, f. & ef. 4-20-82 1AD 4-1981(Temp), f. & ef. 8-21-81
