# Effective Dates of Assessment (OAR 668-010-0025)

## Full text

668-010-0025 Effective Dates of Assessment The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser or handler, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and OAR 668-010-0010(1), or the reports required under OAR 668-010-0015, applies to and covers any purchase by him or delivery on or after June 1, 1967, of strawberries grown in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.325 & 576.335

History: SBY 4, f. 5-31-67
