## Full text

668-030-0010 Number of Commissioners and Terms The Oregon Strawberry Commission will consist of seven commissioners appointed by the Director of the Oregon Department of Agriculture for a term or three years or, if for a term following a prior term for that person, ending three years from the date of expiration of any prior term.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576
