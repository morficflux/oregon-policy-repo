# Wildlife Diversity Program Goal (OAR 635-100-0010)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

635-100-0010 Wildlife Diversity Program Goal It is the goal of the Wildlife Diversity Program to maintain Oregon’s wildlife diversity by protecting and enhancing populations and habitats of native wildlife at self-sustaining levels throughout natural geographic ranges. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]
