## Full text

635-006-0231 When Possession of Salmon Unlawful Except as otherwise provided by law or rule, it is unlawful to possess or distribute commercially caught salmon that has been taken by gear other than “troll,” unless authorized by the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129

History: DFW 38-1999, f. & cert. ef. 5-24-99
