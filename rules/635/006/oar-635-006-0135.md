## Full text

635-006-0135 Alteration, Borrow, or Loan License Unlawful It is unlawful for any person to alter, borrow, or loan any license issued by the Director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 508.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.306
