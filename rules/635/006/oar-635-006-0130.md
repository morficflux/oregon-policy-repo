## Full text

635-006-0130 Wholesale Fish Bait Dealer License The sale of food fish or shellfish under a wholesale fish bait dealer’s license for any other purpose than use as bait, for scientific or educational purposes, or for live public display is prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 508
