## Full text

635-006-0134 Bonding Requirements In accordance with ORS 508.415 and 508.550, the minimum bond amount is $25.00 for wholesale bait dealers; $1,000.00 for wholesale fish dealers, fish canners, and shellfish canners; and $200.00 for limited fish sellers. Bond amounts are determined by a total of the landing fees due for the three highest sales months of the previous year, and may be changed at the discretion of the Department. No bond is required of non-reporting fish dealers.
