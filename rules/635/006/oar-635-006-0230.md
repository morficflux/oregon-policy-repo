## Full text

635-006-0230 When Possession of Steelhead or Walleye Unlawful Except as otherwise provided by law or rule, it is unlawful for any person to have in possession either steelhead trout or walleye taken by any means other than by angling. Notwithstanding OAR 635-006-0225, it is lawful for any wholesale fish dealer, canner, buyer or retailer to possess and sell, in Oregon, legally purchased steelhead trout or walleye taken from outside the Columbia River Basin, consistent with reporting requirements contained within 635-006-0200.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138 & 509.031 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.138 & 509.031
