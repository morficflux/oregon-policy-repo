## Full text

635-006-1050 Evidence of Limited Entry Permit Eligibility In making determinations regarding issuance or renewal of any limited entry permit, the Department and the Commercial Fishery Permit Board may consider as evidence of permit qualifications or requirements Department records and such receipts, accounts, contracts and other business records of private parties as the Department or the Board considers reliable evidence of the qualifications or requirements in question.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109
