## Full text

635-006-1035 Eligibility Requirements for a Permit Eligibility for a limited entry permit is as follows:

(1) Gillnet salmon — see ORS 508.784.

(2) Troll salmon — see ORS 508.810.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109
