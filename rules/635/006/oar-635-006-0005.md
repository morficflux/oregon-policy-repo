# Fishing (OAR 635-006-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

635-006-0005 Fishing Gear Specifications and Operation It is unlawful for any individual to take or land food fish from the waters of this state or the Pacific Ocean with fishing gear which does not meet the specifications, or is not operated as provided or authorized by statute or Department of Fish and Wildlife regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506
