## Full text

635-006-0110 Food Fish Canner License A food fish canner license is required to perform any of the activities defined as a “food fish canner” under OAR 635-006-0001. This license is in lieu of, and not in addition to, a wholesale fish dealer license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 508
