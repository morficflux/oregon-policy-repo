## Full text

635-006-0233 Payment for Legal Commercial Fishing Overages For purposes of legally landed overages where proceeds are to be remitted to the state, payment shall be at fair market value as defined in OAR 635-006-0001.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & ORS 506.129
