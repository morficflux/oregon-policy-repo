## Full text

635-006-0105 Wholesale Fish Dealer License A wholesale fish dealer license is required to perform any of the activities defined as a “wholesale fish dealer” under OAR 635-006-0001.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 508
