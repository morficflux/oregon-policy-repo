## Full text

635-006-0400 Purpose of the Columbia River Gillnet Salmon Vessel Fleet Reduction Program The purpose of the Columbia River gillnet salmon vessel fleet reduction program is to obtain an effective and expeditious reduction of the number of vessels in the non-Indian Columbia River commercial gillnet salmon fishing fleet to reduce their overall fishing capacity.
