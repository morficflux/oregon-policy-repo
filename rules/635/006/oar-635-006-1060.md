## Full text

635-006-1060 Revocation and Refusal to Issue Permits The Commercial Fishery Permit Board may revoke and refuse subsequent issuance of limited entry fisheries permits as follows:

(1) Gillnet salmon permit — see ORS 508.787.

(2) Troll salmon permit — see ORS 508.813.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
