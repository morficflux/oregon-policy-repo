# Release Limits (OAR 635-040-0012)

## At a glance

## Full text

635-040-0012 Release Limits Each permit shall specify release limits. If limits were not specified in permits already issued, the Department has specified, in writing, applicable release limits for those permits, and any transferee is bound by those specified limits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, 506 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, 506 & 508

History: FWC 5-1990, f. 1-24-90, cert. ef. 1-26-90
