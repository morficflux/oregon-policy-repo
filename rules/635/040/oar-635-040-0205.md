# Policy (OAR 635-040-0205)

## At a glance

OAR 635-040-0205 — Policy. Chapter 635, Division 040. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

635-040-0205 Policy It is the policy of the Oregon Fish and Wildlife Commission to provide for the development of fish production in net pens where such operations meet the standards set by the Department of Fish and Wildlife for fish propagation and fish transfer; by the Department of Environmental Quality for waste discharge; and by the Division of State Lands for siting and use of surface area and installation of facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, 506 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, 506 & 508

History: FWC 5-1990, f. 1-24-90, cert. ef. 1-26-90
