## Full text

635-040-0202 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to:

(1) Provide for the orderly development of fish production in suitable areas of waterways and in quantities acceptable for those areas.

(2) Recognize that limited suitability of Oregon’s small, shallow estuaries for extensive net pen culture or cage culture will prevent extensive production with net pens.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, 506 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, 506 & 508
