## Full text

635-405-0015 Compliance with the Statewide Planning Goals and Compatibility with Acknowledged Comprehensive Plans and Land Use Regulations Prior to undertaking any action or program listed in OAR 635-405-0010, the Department shall find that the program or action complies with the statewide planning goals and is compatible with applicable acknowledged comprehensive plans and land use regulations. The Department shall make its goal compliance and plan compatibility findings in accordance with OAR 635-405-0000 through 635-405-0045 and other applicable rules, and the Department’s certified state agency coordination program, which is hereby adopted by reference.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197 & 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197 & 496
