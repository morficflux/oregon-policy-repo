## Full text

635-051-0035 Decoys and Calls Except for purposes of law enforcement or wildlife management, the use of decoys powered by motor, battery, or other self-powered methods is prohibited. Decoys with parts that are moved solely by human action (for example, via a cable or pull-string) or the wind are not affected by this prohibition.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
