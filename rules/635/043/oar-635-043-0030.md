## Full text

635-043-0030 Scientific Taking Permit Requirements Any person desiring to take wildlife for scientific purposes must first secure a Scientific Taking Permit by applying to the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife. The application shall list the wildlife species and numbers to be collected, the areas and methods of collecting, purpose for collecting, the date of application, the name, address, occupation, and signature of the applicant, and the signature and affiliation of the sponsor if the applicant is a student. This permit may not be used in lieu of a hunting license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 496
