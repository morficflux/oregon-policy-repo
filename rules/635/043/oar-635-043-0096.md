## Full text

635-043-0096 Harassing Wildlife Causing Damage Administrative Rules 635-043-0100 through 635-043-0115 govern harassment of wildlife to control damage, except for wolves, which are addressed in 635-110-0010 through 635-110-0030.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 496
