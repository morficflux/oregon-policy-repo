## Full text

635-043-0250 Purpose of the Urban Deer and Elk Population Control Pilot Program The purpose of these rules is to describe the procedures and requirements for issuing kill permits to take deer and elk within urban boundaries, thereby allowing cities the ability to reduce deer or elk populations in response to public nuisance as defined in OAR 498.012, health or safety concerns, and/or property damage.
