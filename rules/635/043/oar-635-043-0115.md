## Full text

635-043-0115 Cancellation of Harassment Permit The harassment permit may be cancelled if the wildlife damage ceases before the termination date or if permittee fails to comply with provisions on the permit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 496
