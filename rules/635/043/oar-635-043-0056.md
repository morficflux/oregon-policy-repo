## Full text

635-043-0056 Trapping of Wildlife For the purpose of alleviating a public nuisance or preventing property damage, the Department may trap and relocate wild turkeys found within the Roseburg city limits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 498.158 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 498.158
