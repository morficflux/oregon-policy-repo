## Full text

635-043-0023 Purpose of Scientific Taking Permits Rules to take wildlife for scientific study allow educational institutions and private and public scientists a means to study or gain knowledge of wildlife. The Department acts as coordinator and contact for wildlife research projects conducted in Oregon. Refer to division 7 for taking fish on a Scientific Taking Permits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 496
