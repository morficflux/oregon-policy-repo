# Purpose (OAR 635-001-0150)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

635-001-0150 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish the process and requirements for hunters to be awarded controlled hunt preference points (preference points) as an alternative to cash for providing information to the Oregon State Police (OSP) and Oregon Hunters Association (OHA) “Turn-In-Poachers” (TIP) program, pursuant to ORS 497.112. The program is intended to encourage hunters to report wildlife violations they witness involving antelope, bear, cougar, deer, elk, moose, mountain goat, mountain sheep or wolf.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146, ORS 496.012, 496.162 & 497.112 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.138, 496.146, ORS 496.012, 496.162 & 497.112

History: DFW 139-2018, adopt filed 09/17/2018, effective 09/17/2018
