# Definition (OAR 635-001-0100)

## At a glance

## Full text

635-001-0100 Definition For the purpose of ORS 498.279, “competition or contest in which prizes are offered” means any event where the total value of all cash or merchandise awards exceeds $25.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 299 & 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183, 299 & 496

History: FWC 96-1987, f. & ef. 11-17-87 FWC 3-1982, f. & ef. 1-11-82
