## Full text

635-090-0130 Landowner Preference Tags A landowner who is qualified to receive landowner preference tags from the commission may request from the board two additional tags for providing current public access and two additional tags for current wildlife habitat programs.

(1) This request shall be made with supporting evidence that the access is significant and the habitat programs benefit wildlife.

(2) The board may recommend that the commission grant the request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.232 & 496.242 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.232 & 496.242
