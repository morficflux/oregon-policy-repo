## Full text

635-070-0020 Controlled Western Oregon Elk Rifle Hunts Tags shall be issued by a controlled hunt drawing following the procedures established in OAR chapter 635, division 60. Except as provided in OAR 635-065-0015, a person successful in drawing a tag for a controlled elk season shall not hunt in any other elk season.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
