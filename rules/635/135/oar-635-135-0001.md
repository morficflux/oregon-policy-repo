## Full text

635-135-0001 Oregon Wild Turkey Management Plan Content and Purpose

(1) The 2018 Oregon Wild Turkey Management Plan establishes state policy and direction for Oregon’s wild turkey management program. The 2018 Plan replaces previous plans. Section IV: Management Guidelines and Appendix I: Trap and Transplant Guidelines of the 2018 Plan are incorporated here by reference as administrative rule. Copies of the Plan are available at the Salem headquarters office of the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife, 4034 Fairview Industrial Drive SE, Salem, OR 97302. The 2018 Plan also serves as an informational and historical document for the Department.

(2) To the extent practicable, at least once every five years the Department shall review the Oregon Wild Turkey Management Plan and make recommendations to the Commission for any changes to the plan. Review of the plan should include incorporation of the best available science and should seek comments from scientific experts and the public.
