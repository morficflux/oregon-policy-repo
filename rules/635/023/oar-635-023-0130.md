635-023-0130 Fall Sport Fishery The current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations provide requirements for the Columbia River Zone. However, additional regulations may be adopted in this rule division from time to time, and, to the extent of any inconsistency, they supersede the current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations .

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138 & ORS 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.162
