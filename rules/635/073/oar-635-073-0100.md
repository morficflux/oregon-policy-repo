## Full text

635-073-0100 Controlled Premium Hunt Regulations

(1) Tags shall be issued by a controlled hunt drawing following the procedures established in OAR chapter 635, division 060.

(2) “L” series Premium Hunt deer tags, “M” series Premium Hunt elk tags, and “N” series Premium Hunt pronghorn antelope tags are in addition to all other tags and permits approved by the commission. In addition to the number of deer, elk, and pronghorn antelope tags legally available to an individual, an individual is allowed one additional deer tag, one additional elk tag, and one additional pronghorn antelope tag annually provided these tags are Premium Hunt series tags.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
