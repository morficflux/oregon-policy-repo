## Full text

635-074-0000 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish the process and requirements for participation in a mentored youth hunter program pursuant to ORS 497.360. The program is intended to introduce youth to hunting by deferring the requirement for hunter education for youth hunters under certain circumstances.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS: 497.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: 497.360
