## Full text

635-074-0010 Supervising Hunter The supervising hunter shall:

(1) Have a valid hunting license and tag(s) valid for the dates, area and species being hunted.

(2) Maintain immediate control of the mentored youth hunter at all times while the youth is in possession of any legal weapon for the hunt.

(3) Ensure that all Oregon hunting regulations are followed. The supervising hunter shall be responsible and accountable for all actions of the mentored youth hunter while engaged in hunting.

(4) Supervise only one mentored youth hunter at any given time while engaged in hunting.

(5) Review and acknowledge understanding of information on safe hunting practices provided by the department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS: 497.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: 497.360
