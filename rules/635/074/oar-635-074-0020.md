## Full text

635-074-0020 Seasons and Bag Limits

(1) Any wildlife harvested by the mentored youth hunter shall be counted towards the supervising hunter’s bag limit.

(2) The mentored youth hunting program is not applicable to designated youth only seasons and hunts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS: 497.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 497.360
