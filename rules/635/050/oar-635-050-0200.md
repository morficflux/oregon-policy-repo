## Full text

635-050-0200 Refuges Open to Hunting or Trapping All state wildlife refuges, except those specified as closed to all hunting or trapping, are open to hunting or trapping of furbearing mammals and mammals unprotected by law or Department regulations during the respective open seasons within the period November 1 through March 31.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
