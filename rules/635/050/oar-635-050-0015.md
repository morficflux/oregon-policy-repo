# Purpose (OAR 635-050-0015)

## Full text

635-050-0015 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish season dates, bag limits and manner of harvest of furbearing and unprotected mammals pursuant to ORS Chapters 496, 497 and 498.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, ORS 497 & ORS 498 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, ORS 497 & ORS 498
