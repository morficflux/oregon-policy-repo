## Full text

635-400-0037 Public Involvement Any individual, organization or public agency may request the Department apply for an instream water right on a waterway.

(1) The Department Instream Water Right Coordinator shall review the request with the appropriate Department District and Region to determine the Department priority of the waterway requested.

(2) If the request is determined to be for a high priority waterway, as defined by OAR 635-400-0002, the Department Instream Water Right Coordinator may apply for the instream water right, after determining the instream flow requirement in accordance with OAR 635-400-0015

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 & 537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496 & 537

History: FWC 110-1989, f. 10-24-89, cert. ef. 10-28-89
