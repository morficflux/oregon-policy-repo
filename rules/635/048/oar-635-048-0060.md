# Trapper (OAR 635-048-0060)

## Full text

635-048-0060 Trapper Certificate of Course Completion Persons successfully completing the exam and submitting an appropriate application will be issued a trapping brand number. This number will serve as the person’s certificate of course completion.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 & 497 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496 & 497

History: FWC 15-1987, f. & ef. 4-15-87
