## Full text

635-048-0010 Hunter Education Course Fee A $10.00 application fee will be collected from students enrolled in a hunter education or bowhunter education course for materials and services furnished by the Hunter Education Program. This fee will be collected by the Department as part of the application to participate in the course, and used to provide stipends to instructors for materials or services authorized by the department and to defray the cost of equipment provided to instructors by the Department. In no instance shall the course application fee serve as a barrier to persons desiring to participate in the training course. The department may waive the application fee for students who cannot pay the class fee due to hardship.
