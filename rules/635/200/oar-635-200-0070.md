## Full text

635-200-0070 Moose Any person may sell, purchase, or exchange the following parts of moose (Alces alces):

(1) The hide;

(2) Any bone or other part of the skeletal structure, except the skull; and

(3) Antlers (if detached from the skull, if skull is split apart between antlers or if antlers are naturally shed).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 498.019, 498.022 & 498.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 498.019, 498.022 & 498.042
