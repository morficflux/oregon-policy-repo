## Full text

635-200-0080 Wildlife Not Native to Oregon

(1) Except as provided in ORS 498.022 and this rule, any person may sell, purchase or exchange the parts of any species other than those species listed in Appendix 5 ("Extant, Self-Sustaining Oregon Vertebrates by Physiographic Provinces, Selected Community Types and Selected Habitat Components") of the Oregon Wildlife Diversity Plan 1993-1998 (November 1993).

(2) Any person may sell, purchase, or exchange the inedible parts of bullfrogs ( Rana catesbeiana ).

(3) The sale, purchase, or exchange of nonnative cervid parts is governed by section 60 of this rule.

(4) Except as provided in ORS 498.022, this rule, or other rules of the commission, no person may purchase, sell, offer for sale, or possess with the intent to sell any “covered animal species” part or product.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 498.019, 498.022 & 498.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 498.019, 498.022 & 498.042
