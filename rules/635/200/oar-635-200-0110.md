## Full text

635-200-0110 Rendering Any person may sell, purchase, or exchange the fats, oils, and similar end products of the process of rendering wildlife carcasses or parts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 498.019, 498.022 & 498.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 498.019, 498.022 & 498.042
