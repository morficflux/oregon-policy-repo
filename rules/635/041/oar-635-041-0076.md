635-041-0076 Summer Salmon Season

(1) Salmon (all species), steelhead, shad, yellow perch, bass, walleye, catfish, and carp may be sold or retained for subsistence from the Columbia River Zone 6 Treaty Indian fisheries, as follows: (a) With gear restricted to set and drift gillnets with a 7-inch minimum mesh size restriction during the following periods: (A) 6:00 a.m. June 16 through 6:00 p.m. June 19; (B) 6:00 a.m. June 22 through 6:00 p.m. June 24; (C) 6:00 a.m. June 29 through 6:00 p.m. July 1; and (D) 6:00 a.m. July 6 through 6:00 p.m. July 8. (b) 12:01 a.m. June 16 through 11:59 p.m. July 31 with gear restricted to subsistence fishing gear which includes hoop nets, bag nets, dip nets, and rod and reel with hook-and-line; however, only steelhead caught after 6:00 a.m. June 16 may be sold or retained for subsistence. (c) Fish caught during any open period may be sold after the period concludes as long as the fish were landed during an open period. (d) Sturgeon may not be sold, but sturgeon between 38-54 inches in fork length caught in the Bonneville Pool and between 43-54 inches in fork length caught in The Dalles and John Day pools may be retained for subsistence use. (e) Closed areas as set forth in OAR 635-041-0020 and 635-041-0045 are in effect.

(2) Effective 6:00 a.m. June 16 through 11:59 p.m. July 31, commercial sales of fish caught by enrolled Yakama Nation members in Yakama Nation tributary fisheries are open during those days and hours when these tributaries are open under lawfully-enacted Yakama Nation subsistence fishery regulations as follows: (a) The Yakama Nation tributary fisheries include the Klickitat River, Wind River, and Drano Lake. (b) Allowable sales include salmon (all species), steelhead, walleye, shad, catfish, carp, bass and yellow perch. (c) Sturgeon may not be sold but sturgeon between 38-54 inches in fork length in Bonneville Pool may be kept for subsistence purposes. (d) Gear is restricted to subsistence fishing gear which includes hoop nets, bag nets, dip nets, and rod and reel with hook-and-line. Gillnets may only be used in Drano Lake. (e) Fish caught during any open period may be sold after the period concludes as long as the fish were landed during the open period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.118 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.129 & 507.030
