## Full text

635-430-0000 Purpose of Wildlife Habitat Conservation and Management Program The purpose of these rules is to implement the Wildlife Habitat Conservation and Management Program (Program), ORS 308A.400-308A.430. These rules:

(1) Establish priorities, criteria and standards for enrollment of parcels, tracts, or lots of eligible land into the Program;

(2) Specify the form and content of a wildlife habitat and conservation management plan and the conservation and management practices that are appropriate to preserve, enhance or improve the structure or function of wildlife habitat; and

(3) Establish processes for self-reporting, site inspection, and adding and removing eligible land from the Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.162 & 308A.400-308A.430 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012 & 308A.400-308A.430
