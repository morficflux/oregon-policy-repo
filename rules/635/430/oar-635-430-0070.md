## Full text

635-430-0070 Amendments to Approved Wildlife Habitat Conservation and Management Plans

(1) Landowners may request amendments to approved wildlife habitat conservation and management plans by contacting the appropriate Watershed District office.

(2) The landowner shall provide a copy of the approved plan and a description of the proposed amendments to the Department.

(3) The Department will follow the procedures in OAR 635-430-0040 when reviewing amendments to approved plans.

(4) Amendments shall meet the standards in OAR 635-430-0060.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.162 & 308A.400-308A.430 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012 & 308A.400-308A.430
