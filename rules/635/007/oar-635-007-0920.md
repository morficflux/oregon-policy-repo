# Report Requirement (OAR 635-007-0920)

## Full text

635-007-0920 Report Requirement Each person or entity issued a Scientific Taking Permit may be required to submit to the Department written reports describing the date(s), specific location(s) and the number of species of fish taken and/or released live under a permit during the permit period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 496.138, 497.252 & .298, 506.109, .119 & 508.111 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.335, 496.138, 497.252 & .298, 506.109, .119 & 508.111
