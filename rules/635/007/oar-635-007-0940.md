# Nongame Fish Protected (OAR 635-007-0940)

## At a glance

## Full text

635-007-0940 Nongame Fish Protected Notwithstanding OAR 635-011-0220, the Department may issue a Scientific Taking Permit for studies of any white amur, Warner Sucker, Borax Lake Chub, Foskett Springs Dace, Oregon Tui Chub of Hutton Springs, or Lost River and Shortnose Suckers of Klamath County.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 496.138, 497.252 & .298, 506.109, 506.119 & 508.111 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.335, 496.138, 497.252, 497.298, 506.109, 506.119 & 508.111
