635-007-0910 Scientific Taking Permit Application — Fish

(1) Any person or entity wishing to obtain a Scientific Taking Permit must complete and submit to the Department the appropriate permit application form. (a) No fee is charged for Fish Scientific Taking Permits issued for scientific or educational purposes as part of a program or course of study at a K-12 educational institution. (b) A fee of $119.50 (plus a $2.00 license agent fee) shall be charged for each Fish Scientific Taking Permit issued for any agency, corporation, association, or other such entity.

(2) The Department may prescribe such terms and conditions in a permit as it may deem necessary to ensure that fish taken pursuant to the permit will be used only for scientific or educational purposes.

(3) Permits will not be issued to any person or entity for the purpose of collecting fish to sell to scientific or educational supply houses or to any other person or entity.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, SB 247 (2015), ORS 496.146 & ORS 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 497.298 & ORS 508.111
