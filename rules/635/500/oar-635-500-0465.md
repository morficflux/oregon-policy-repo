## Full text

635-500-0465 Ghost and Mud Shrimp It is the objective of the Department to maintain self-sustaining populations of ghost and mud shrimp in sufficient abundance to support bait fisheries.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, 506 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, 506 & 508

History: FWC 110-1990, f. & cert. ef. 10-1-90
