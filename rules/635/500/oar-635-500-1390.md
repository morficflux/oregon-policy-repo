## Full text

635-500-1390 Summer Steelhead Summer steelhead shall no longer be stocked in the Molalla and Pudding subbasins.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
