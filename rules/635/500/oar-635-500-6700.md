## Full text

635-500-6700 Organization of Rules These rules (OAR 635-500-6700 through 635-500-6765) establish the Commission’s policy for the non-tribal Columbia River Recreational and Commercial Fisheries Management Framework.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
