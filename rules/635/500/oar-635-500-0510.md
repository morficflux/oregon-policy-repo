## Full text

635-500-0510 Coho Salmon

(1) The following operating principles apply to coho salmon in the Tenmile Lakes basin: (a) The Tenmile Lakes Basin shall be managed for the production of naturally produced coho salmon with an option for a hatchery program consistent with provisions in OAR 635-500-6500 (Oregon Coast Coho Conservation Plan for the State of Oregon); (b) Coho salmon stock approved for Tenmile Lakes system are Tenmile stock only; (c) Naturally produced stock shall be incorporated in all hatchery programs in every generation.

(2) In accordance with these operating principles, it is the objective of the Department to: (a) Recover the naturally produced coho population in the Tenmile Lakes Basin consistent with the six measurable criteria for desired status contained in OAR 635-500-6500 (Oregon Coast Coho Conservation Plan for the State of Oregon); (b) Recover the Tenmile Lakes Basin naturally produced coho salmon sufficiently to allow an in-basin fishery on naturally produced coho salmon consistent with OAR 635-500-6500 (Oregon Coast Coho Conservation Plan for the State of Oregon).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
