## Full text

635-500-4390 Pacific Lamprey Policy and objective for Pacific lamprey management in the Salmon River Basin.

(1) Policy: The Salmon River Basin shall be managed for wild production of Pacific lamprey.

(2) Objective: Maintain or increase Pacific lamprey production in rivers and streams in the Salmon River Basin where they naturally occur.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129

History: DFW 5-1998, f. & cert. ef. 1-12-98
