## Full text

635-500-6750 White Sturgeon

(1) Transition Period (2013–January 31, 2017). (a) In years when retention is allowed, allocate 90% of the harvestable surplus downstream from Bonneville Dam for use in non-tribal fisheries and hold 10% in reserve as an additional conservation buffer above the maximum harvest rate allowed in Oregon’s white sturgeon conservation plan. (b) Assign 80% of the white sturgeon available for harvest to the recreational fishery. (c) Assign 20% to off-channel and mainstem commercial fisheries.

(2) Long Term (February 1, 2017 and Beyond). (a) In years when retention is allowed, allocate 90% of the harvestable surplus downstream from Bonneville Dam for use in non-tribal fisheries and hold 10% in reserve as an additional conservation buffer above the maximum harvest rate allowed in Oregon’s white sturgeon conservation plan. (b) Assign 80% of the white sturgeon available for harvest to the recreational fishery. (c) Assign the balance (20%) to off-channel and mainstem commercial fisheries.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
