## Full text

635-500-0560 Angler Access It is the objective of the Department to:

(1) Develop access sites within the Tenmile Lakes Basin and investigate potential boat access points for North Tenmile Lake.

(2) Maintain and improve existing access sites.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, 506 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, 506 & 508

History: FWC 14-1991, f. 2-28-91, cert. ef. 3-1-91
