## Full text

635-500-0266 McKenzie Subbasin Fish Management — Applicability OAR 635-500-0266 through 635-500-0276 apply to the Mckenzie River subbasin which includes the mainstem McKenzie River, its tributaries, and Leaburg Pool. These rules are in addition to, and not in lieu of, rules which apply to the Willamette River Basin.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 & 506 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496 & 506
