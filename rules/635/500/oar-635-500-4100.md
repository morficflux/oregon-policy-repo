## Full text

635-500-4100 Public Access Policies and objective for public access management in the mid-coast small ocean tributary streams.

(1) Policies: (a) The Department shall seek to provide access for bank angling that will satisfy public need for a variety of angling opportunities and a dispersion of angling effort throughout the basins; (b) Acquisition and development of angler access sites shall be consistent with guidelines and objectives for management of fish species and habitat.

(2) Objective: Increase bank angling access in mid-coast small ocean tributary streams.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129

History: DFW 5-1998, f. & cert. ef. 1-12-98
