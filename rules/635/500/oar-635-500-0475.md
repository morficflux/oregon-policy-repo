## Full text

635-500-0475 Angler Access It is the objective of the Department to:

(1) Develop additional access sites around Coos Bay and in the upper basin.

(2) Maintain and improve existing access sites in the Coos River Basin.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, 506 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, 506 & 508

History: FWC 110-1990, f. & cert. ef. 10-1-90
