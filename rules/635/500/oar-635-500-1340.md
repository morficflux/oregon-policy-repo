## Full text

635-500-1340 Warmwater Game Fish

(1) The following operating principle applies to the Middle Fork Willamette Subbasin: No introductions of warmwater game fish shall be permitted in streams of the subbasin.

(2) In accordance with this operating principle, it is the objective of the Department to: (a) Minimize impacts of warmwater game fish on endemic fish species; (b) Provide a diversity of warmwater angling opportunities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720
