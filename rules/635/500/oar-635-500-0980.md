## Full text

635-500-0980 Coho Salmon The following operating principle applies to the Coast Fork Willamette Subbasin: Coho salmon shall not be released into the Coast Fork Willamette Subbasin.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720
