## Full text

635-500-3850 Devil Lake Policy and objectives for fish management in Devil Lake of the Klamath River Basin.

(1) Policy: Devil Lake shall be managed for hatchery production of rainbow trout under the Basic Yield Management Option.

(2) Objectives: (a) Provide a consumptive fishery for stocked rainbow trout; (b) Protect the wild redband trout population in Fishhole Creek from hatchery fish escaping from Devil Lake.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.162, 506.109 & 506.129

History: FWC 56-1997, f. 9-4-97, cert. ef. 9-15-97
