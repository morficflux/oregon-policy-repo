## Full text

635-500-3820 J. C. Boyle Reservoir Policies and objectives for fish management in J. D. Boyle Reservoir of the Klamath River Basin.

(1) Policies: (a) Redband trout in J. C. Boyle Reservoir shall be managed for natural production under the Wild Fish Management Option; (b) No hatchery reared fish shall be stocked in J. C. Boyle Reservoir.

(2) Objectives: (a) Maintain protection of genetic diversity, adaptiveness and abundance of wild redband trout; (b) Provide a consumptive fishery for warmwater game fish.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.162, 506.109 & 506.129
