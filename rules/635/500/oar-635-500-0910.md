## Full text

635-500-0910 Coast Fork Willamette Subbasin Fish Management — Habitat It is the objective of the Department to:

(1) Protect fish populations from impacts caused by land use activities.

(2) Restore and enhance riparian and in-stream fish habitats.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720
