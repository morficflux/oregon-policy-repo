## Full text

635-500-1663 Willamette River Basin Above the Mouth of the McKenzie River Operating Policy and Objectives Management policies, objectives, and operating principles for spring Chinook in the area above the mouth of the McKenzie River are under OAR 635-500-1290.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
