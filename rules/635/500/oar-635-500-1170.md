# Summer (OAR 635-500-1170)

## Full text

635-500-1170 Summer Steelhead It is the objective of the Department to increase the recreational catch of summer steelhead above Willamette Falls to an average annual minimum of 500 fish.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720
