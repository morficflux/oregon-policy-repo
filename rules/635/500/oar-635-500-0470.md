## Full text

635-500-0470 Other Shellfish Species It is the objective of the Department to maintain the abundance of crawfish, red rock crab, clams, mussels, and piddocks.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, 506 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, 506 & 508

History: FWC 110-1990, f. & cert. ef. 10-1-90
