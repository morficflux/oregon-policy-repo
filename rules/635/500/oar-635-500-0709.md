## Full text

635-500-0709 Hyatt Lake Hyatt Lake shall be managed for hatchery and natural production under two alternatives from the Warmwater Fish Plan and Oregon’s Trout Plan: Trout — Basic yield; Bass — Basic yield.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496, 506 & 508 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496, 506 & 508
