## Full text

635-098-0000 Purpose of the Fund The purpose of the Conservation and Recreation Fund appropriated to the State Department of Fish and Wildlife is to carry out activities that serve to protect, maintain, or enhance fish and wildlife resources in Oregon and to support nature-based recreation consistent with the Oregon Conservation Strategy.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.252 & 496.254 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
