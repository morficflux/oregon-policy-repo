## Full text

635-098-0010 Contributions to the Fund The Oregon Conservation and Recreation Fund shall consist of:

(1) Moneys appropriated to the Department for deposit in the Fund or otherwise transferred to the Fund; and

(2) Gifts, grants, contributions or other donations for use as described in 635-098-0020 (Expenditures from the Fund) that are received by the Department from any public or private source and caused to be deposited and credited to the Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.252 & 496.254 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
