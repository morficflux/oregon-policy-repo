# Documents Required in Field (OAR 635-060-0055)

## Full text

635-060-0055 Documents Required in Field A person hunting in any controlled game mammal hunt shall have on their person a hunting license valid for the calendar year in which the person is hunting, Hunter Education Certificate or a Department document which includes their Hunter Education Certificate Number (for persons less than 18 years old), and a controlled hunt tag (if applicable) for the area and season being hunted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
