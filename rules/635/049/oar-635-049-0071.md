## Full text

635-049-0071 Obligation to Report Disease Incidents Any cervid license holder with knowledge that a cervid in his or her licensed Type 1 facility has any of the diseases on the Cervid Disease Surveillance List (or has been exposed to any of those diseases) must promptly report that fact to the Department and the State Veterinarian:
