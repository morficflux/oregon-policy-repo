## Full text

635-049-0270 Application for Cervid Propagation License — Type 2

(1) Person desiring to hold or propagate live fallow deer or reindeer must first secure a Cervid Propagation License -- Type 2 by applying to the Department's headquarters office. (a) The application shall list the: (A) Cervid species and numbers to be held; (B) Date of application; (C) Location and size of the facility; and (D) Name, address, and signature of the applicant. (b) Provided review of the application indicates the proposed operation meets the requirements of OAR chapter 635, division 049, the Department will notify the applicant of preliminary approval and the need for a facility inspection.

(2) The fee for a Cervid Propagation License -- Type 2 shall be $62.50 (plus a $2.00 license agent fee) annually.
