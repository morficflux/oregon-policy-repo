## Full text

635-049-0145 Sale or Exchange

(1) The sale or exchange of live cervids, cervid embryos and cervid gametes is prohibited, except that live cervids, embryos and gametes may be sold or exchanged if legally held under an Oregon cervid license and only if: (a) Sold to others who are authorized to hold cervids legally outside the state of Oregon; or (b) Sold to or exchanged with the holder of an Oregon cervid license valid for that species or subspecies.

(2) Purchase, sale or exchange of cervid parts is governed by OAR chapter 635, division 200.

(3) Note the requirements of OAR 635-049-0265 governing transport of cervids.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 497.228, 498.002, 498.019, 498.052 & 174.106 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 497.228, 498.002, 498.019, 498.052 & 174.106
