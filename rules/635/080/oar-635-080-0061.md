## Full text

635-080-0061 Imnaha Unit The Imnaha Unit, Number 61, is that area beginning at Joseph, northeast on Imnaha Highway to Imnaha; south and west along Imnaha River and South Fork of Imnaha River to Hawkins Pass; north on West Fork Trail to Wallowa Lake; north on State Highway 82 to Joseph, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496
