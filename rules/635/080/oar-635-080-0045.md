# Fossil Unit (OAR 635-080-0045)

## Full text

635-080-0045 Fossil Unit The Fossil Unit, Number 45, is that area beginning at Fossil, north on State Highway 19 to Condon; east on State Highway 206 to State Highway 207; south on State Highway 207 to State Highway 19; southeast on State Highway 19 to U.S. Highway 26; west on U.S. Highway 26 to Bridge Creek near Mitchell; northwest along Bridge Creek to John Day River; northwest along John Day River to State Highway 218 at Clarno; northeast on State Highway 218 to Fossil, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496

History: FWC 72-1989, f. & cert. ef. 8-15-89 FWC 26-1978, f. & ef. 5-26-78 FWC 119, f. & ef. 6-3-77
