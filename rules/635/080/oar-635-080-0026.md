# Powers (OAR 635-080-0026)

## At a glance

## Full text

635-080-0026 Powers Unit The Powers Unit, number 26, is that area beginning at Grave Creek Bridge on Rogue River; west on Rogue River to Illahee; north on posted forest road to Powers; northwest on Powers Highway to State Highway 42; east on State Highway 42 to Tenmile; south on Benedict Rd. to Ireland Rd.; east on Ireland Rd. to Olalla Cr. Rd.; south on Olalla Creek Road through Horse Prairie to Live Oak Cr. Rd.; southwest on Live Oak Cr. Rd. to Union Creek Road; northeast on SPRR tracks to Dixonville-Nickle Mountain main power transmission line adjacent to Nickel Mountain Road; east along main power transmission line to Interstate Highway 5; south on Interstate Highway 5 to Wolf Creek; southwest on Wolf Creek-Grave Creek Road to Grave Creek Bridge, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
