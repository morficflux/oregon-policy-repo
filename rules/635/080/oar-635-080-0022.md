# Dixon (OAR 635-080-0022)

## At a glance

## Full text

635-080-0022 Dixon Unit The Dixon Unit, number 22, is that area beginning at Shady Cove; northwest on State Highway 227 to Dixonville-Hanna Nickel main power transmission line at a point approximately two miles west of Days Creek; north along main power transmission line to North Umpqua River; east along North Umpqua River to Toketee; south on Toketee Road to State Highway 138; east and south on State Highway 138 to State Highway 230; southwest on State Highway 230 to State Highway 62; southwest on State Highway 62 to Shady Cove, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162

History: DFW 47-1999, f. & cert. ef. 6-16-99 FWC 72-1989, f. & cert. ef. 8-15-89 FWC 26-1978, f. & ef. 5-26-78 FWC 119, f. & ef. 6-3-77
