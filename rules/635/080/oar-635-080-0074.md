# Warner (OAR 635-080-0074)

## At a glance

## Full text

635-080-0074 Warner Unit The Warner Unit, number 74, is that area beginning at Lakeview; north east on U.S. Highway 395 to Fort Bidwell-Warner Valley Road north of Abert Lake; south on Fort Bidwell-Warner Valley Road through Plush and Adel to Oregon-California state line; west along state line to U.S. Highway 395 at New Pine Creek; north on U.S. Highway 395 to Lakeview, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162

History: DFW 47-1999, f. & cert. ef. 6-16-99 FWC 72-1989, f. & cert. ef. 8-15-89 FWC 26-1978, f. & ef. 5-26-78 FWC 119, f. & ef. 6-3-77
