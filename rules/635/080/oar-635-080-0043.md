## Full text

635-080-0043 Biggs Unit The Biggs Unit, Number 43, is that area beginning at Arlington, south on State Highway 19 to Fossil; southwest along State Highway 218 to Shaniko; north on U.S. Highway 97 to Grass Valley; southwest on State Highway 216 to Tygh Valley; north on U.S. Highway 197 to the Columbia River; east along the Columbia River to Arlington, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496

History: FWC 72-1989, f. & cert. ef. 8-15-89 FWC 26-1978, f. & ef. 5-26-78 FWC 119, f. & ef. 6-3-77
