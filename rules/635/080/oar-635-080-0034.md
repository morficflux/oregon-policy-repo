## Full text

635-080-0034 Upper Deschutes Unit The Upper Deschutes Unit, number 34, is that area beginning at Bend; southwest on U.S. Highway 97 to Crescent; west on Klamath County Road 61to Crescent Creek; southwest on Crescent Creek to State Highway 58; northwest on State Highway 58 to Pacific Crest Trail; north along Pacific Crest Trail to U.S. Highway 242; east on State Highway 242 to Sisters; east on State Highway 126 to Redmond; southwest on U.S. Highway 97 to Bend, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
