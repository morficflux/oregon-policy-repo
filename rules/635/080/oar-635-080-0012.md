# Wilson Unit (OAR 635-080-0012)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

635-080-0012 Wilson Unit The Wilson Unit, number 12, is that area beginning on the north shore line at the mouth of Tillamook Bay; north along Pacific Ocean coastline to north shoreline of Nehalem Bay; northeast along north shoreline of Nehalem Bay and North Fork Nehalem River to State Highway 53 bridge; northeast on State Highway 53 to U.S. Highway 26 at Necanicum Junction; southeast on U.S. Highway 26 to Timber-Glenwood Highway; south on Timber-Glenwood Highway to Glenwood; southwest on State Highway 6 to Wilson River at Mills Bridge; west along Wilson River and north shoreline of Tillamook Bay to mouth, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162

History: FWC 17-1996, f. 4-10-96, cert. ef. 4-15-96 FWC 72-1989, f. & cert. ef. 8-15-89 FWC 26-1978, f. & ef. 5-26-78 FWC 119, f. & ef. 6-3-77
