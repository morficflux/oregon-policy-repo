## Full text

635-080-0059 Snake River Unit The Snake River Unit, number 59, is that area beginning at confluence of Imnaha and Snake rivers; southeast along Snake River to Thirty-two Point Creek; southwest along Thirty-two Point Creek to Summit Road; southwest and northwest on Summit Road to North Fork Dry Creek; southwest along North Fork Dry Creek and northwest along Dry Creek to Imnaha River; north along Imnaha River to Snake River, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162

History: DFW 47-1999, f. & cert. ef. 6-16-99 FWC 72-1989, f. & cert. ef. 8-15-89 FWC 26-1978, f. & ef. 5-26-78 FWC 119, f. & ef. 6-3-77
