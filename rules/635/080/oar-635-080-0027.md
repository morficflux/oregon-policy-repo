## Full text

635-080-0027 Chetco Unit The Chetco Unit, Number 27, is that area beginning at Gold Beach, east along Rogue River to Grants Pass, southwest on U.S. Highway 199 to Oregon-California state line; west along state line to Pacific Ocean; northwest along Pacific Ocean coastline to Gold Beach, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496
