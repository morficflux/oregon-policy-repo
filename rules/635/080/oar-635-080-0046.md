## Full text

635-080-0046 Murderers Creek Unit The Murderers Creek Unit, number 46, is that area beginning at Dayville; east on U.S. Highway 26 to Prairie City; southeast on County Road 62 to Summit Prairie; west on Logan Valley County Road 16 to Seneca; south on U.S. Highway 395 to Forest Road 37; southwest on Forest Road 37 to Forest Road 3750; west on Forest Road 3750 to South Fork John Day River; northwest along South Fork John Day River to Dayville, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
