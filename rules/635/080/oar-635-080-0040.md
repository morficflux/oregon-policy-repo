## Full text

635-080-0040 Maupin Unit The Maupin Unit, number 40, is that area beginning at Maupin; north on U.S. Highway 197 to Tygh Valley; northeast on State Highway 216 to Grass Valley; south on U.S. Highway 97 to Shaniko; southeast on State Highway 218 to Antelope; southwest on Antelope Creek Road to U.S. Highway 97; north on U.S. Highway 97 to junction of U.S. Highway 197 and South Junction Road; southwest on South Junction Road to Deschutes River at South Junction; northeast along Deschutes River to Maupin, point of beginning.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162

History: DFW 47-1999, f. & cert. ef. 6-16-99 FWC 72-1989, f. & cert. ef. 8-15-89 FWC 26-1978, f. & ef. 5-26-78 FWC 119, f. & ef. 6-3-77
