## Full text

635-065-0755 Trespass It is unlawful to hunt on the cultivated or enclosed land of another without permission. Enclosed land may be bounded by a fence, ditch, water, posted line or any other line indicating separation from surrounding territory.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496
