## Full text

635-065-0725 Other Weapons It is unlawful to hunt game mammals with a crossbow or any weapon other than those rifles, handguns, shotguns, muzzleloaders, and bows authorized.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496
