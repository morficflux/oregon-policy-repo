## Full text

635-160-0010 Plan Objectives for Elk Management The tables entitled “2016 Rocky Mt Elk Winter Population and Bull Ratio Management Objectives (MO)” and “2016 Roosevelt Elk Winter Population and Bull Ratio Management Objectives (MO)” are incorporated by reference as part of the Oregon Elk Management Plan.
