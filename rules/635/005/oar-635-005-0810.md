## Full text

635-005-0810 Revocation and Refusal to Issue Sea Urchin and Sea CucumberPermits The Commercial Fishery Permit Board may revoke and refuse subsequent issuance of a Sea Urchin and Sea Cucumber Permit pursuant to ORS 508.485 and 508.490.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.129 & 508.760
