## Full text

635-005-0650 Spot and Coonstripe Shrimp Fishery Defined “Spot and coonstripe shrimp fisheries” means the commercial fisheries for spot shrimp (Pandalus platyceros) and coonstripe shrimp (Pandalus danae) in the Pacific Ocean.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129

History: DFW 76-2012, f. 6-28-12, cert. ef. 7-1-12
