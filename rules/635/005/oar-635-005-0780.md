## Full text

635-005-0780 Closed Season — Weathervane Scallop Fishery There is no closed season on weathervane scallops taken for commercial purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
