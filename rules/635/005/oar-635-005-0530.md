## Full text

635-005-0530 Tanner and Box Crab Taken in Trawl Nets Notwithstanding OAR 635-005-0540, the Department may issue permits to land Tanner and box crab taken as incidental catch in trawl nets outside Oregon waters.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
