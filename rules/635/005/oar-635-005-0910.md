## Full text

635-005-0910 Oyster Seasons and Gear It is unlawful to take oysters for either personal use or commercial purposes from natural oyster beds located on unoccupied state lands.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.129 & 506.306
