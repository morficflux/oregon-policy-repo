## Full text

635-005-0725 Weathervane Scallop Fishery Defined “Weathervane scallop fishery” means the commercial fishery for weathervane scallops (Patinopecten caurinus) in the Pacific Ocean.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
