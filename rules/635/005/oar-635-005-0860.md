## Full text

635-005-0860 Closed Season — Crayfish Fishery It is unlawful to take crayfish for commercial purposes from November 1 of any year through March 31 of the following year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.129 & 506.306
