## Full text

635-005-0655 Closed Season — Spot and Coonstripe Shrimp There is no closed season for the taking of spot or coonstripe shrimp.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
