## Full text

635-005-0680 Brine Shrimp Fishery Defined "Brine shrimp fishery" means the commercial fishery for adult brine shrimp (Artemia spp.) from Lake Abert (Lake County).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
