## Full text

635-005-0315 Bay Clam Dive Permit Fee

(1) The annual fee for a Resident Bay Clam Dive Permit is $125.00 (plus a $2.00 license agent fee) for applicants.

(2) The annual fee for a Nonresident Bay Clam Dive Permit is $175.00 (plus a $2.00 license agent fee) for applicants.

(3) A fee of $100.00 shall be charged for each transfer of participation rights under OAR 635-005-0340.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 & Other SB 247 (2015) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
