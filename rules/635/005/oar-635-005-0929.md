## Full text

635-005-0929 Closed Season From 12:01 pm Friday through 11:59 am Sunday of any week, it is unlawful to:

(1) Take market squid for commercial purposes using purse seine net, dipnet, or trawl net gear; or

(2) Use light to attract market squid for commercial purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, ORS 508.036, ORS 506.109, ORS 506.119 & ORS 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, ORS 506.129, ORS 496.162, ORS 196.540, ORS 196.542, ORS 196.545, ORS 196.550 & ORS 196.555
