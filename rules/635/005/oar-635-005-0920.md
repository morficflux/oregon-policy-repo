## Full text

635-005-0920 Market Squid Fishery Market squid (Loligo opalescens) are federally managed by the Coastal Pelagic Species Fishery Management Plan, and are subject to all federal regulations adopted by reference in OAR 635-004-0375.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
