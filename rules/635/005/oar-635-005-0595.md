## Full text

635-005-0595 Revocation and Refusal to Issue Pink Shrimp Permits The Commercial Fishery Permit Board may revoke and refuse subsequent issuance of a Pink Shrimp Permit pursuant to ORS 508.898.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.129 & 508.898
