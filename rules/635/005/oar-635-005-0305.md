## Full text

635-005-0305 Bay Clam Dive Fishery Defined "Bay clam dive fishery" means the commercial fishery for bay clams (including: cockle clams, Clinocardium nuttallii; butter clams, Saxidomus gigantea; gaper clams, Tresus capax; and softshell clams, Mya arenaria) from subtidal areas in Oregon estuaries using dive gear.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129

History: DFW 164-2015, f. 12-9-15, cert. ef. 1-1-16 DFW 76-2012, f. 6-28-12, cert. ef. 7-1-12
