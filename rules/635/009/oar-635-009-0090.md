## Full text

635-009-0090 Salmon and Trout Enhancement Program These Rules guide the Department’s administration, including project evaluation and approval, of the Salmon and Trout Enhancement Program (STEP) as authorized under the provisions of ORS 496.430, et seq. These rules also govern the STEP Advisory Committee’s membership and meeting frequency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138 & ORS 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.430 et seq.
