## Full text

635-440-0015 Distribution of Funds by County Funds received by a county program from the Department may only be used to reimburse the following expenses or losses:

(1) Compensation to permit holders for documented economic harm

(2) Compensation to permit holders for gear changes.

(3) Compensation to the county for allowable expenditures necessary to implement the county program during the calendar year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 509.230 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 508.775 - 508.796
