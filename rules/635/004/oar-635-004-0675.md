## Full text

635-004-0675 Intertidal Animal Fishery Defined “Intertidal Animal Fishery” means the commercial fisheries for animals living intertidally on the bottom.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
