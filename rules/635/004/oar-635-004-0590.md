## Full text

635-004-0590 Coastal Rivers Shad Fishery Defined “Coastal rivers shad fishery” means the commercial fishery for American shad (Alosa sapidissima) in the bays and rivers specified in OAR 635-004-0595.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
