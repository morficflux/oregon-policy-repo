## Full text

635-004-0630 Sturgeon Fishery Defined “Sturgeon fishery” means the commercial fishery for white sturgeon (Acipenser transmontanus) in the areas outlined in OAR 635-004-0635.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
