## Full text

635-004-0615 Pacific Hagfish Harvest Guideline When landed annual catch approaches 1.6 million pounds of Pacific Hagfish, the Department Marine Resources Program will hold a public meeting to evaluate the fishery.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.162, 506.109 & 506.129
