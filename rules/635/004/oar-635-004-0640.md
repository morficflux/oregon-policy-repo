## Full text

635-004-0640 Sturgeon Fishing Gear It is unlawful to take sturgeon for commercial purposes by any means other than the commercial fishing gear authorized in the fishing area in which the sturgeon are taken, except setlines are permitted in the Columbia River.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.306
