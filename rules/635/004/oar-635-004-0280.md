## Full text

635-004-0280 Maintaining Records of Cumulative Catch Vessels landing groundfish under a cumulative catch limit shall keep copies of fish landing receipts on board for inspection by authorized enforcement officials for a minimum of 90 days from date of delivery. Receipts shall be signed and dated by both the dealer representative and vessel operator. Fish landing receipts may be in the form of Department electronic fish tickets; or fish dealer dock receipts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, ORS 496.162, ORS 506.036, ORS 506.109, ORS 506.119 & ORS 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & ORS 506.129
