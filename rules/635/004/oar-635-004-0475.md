## Full text

635-004-0475 Revocation and Refusal to Issue Permits The Commission may revoke and refuse subsequent issuance of a Yaquina Bay Roe-Herring Permit pursuant to ORS 508.485 and 508.490.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.129 & 508.765
