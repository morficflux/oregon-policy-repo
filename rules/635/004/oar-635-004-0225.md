## Full text

635-004-0225 Bait Restrictions It is unlawful to use lamprey as bait in any commercial fishery.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, ORS 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.126 & 506.306
