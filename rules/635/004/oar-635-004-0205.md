## Full text

635-004-0205 Licensing Requirements In addition to the regulations contained in this Division, fishers should consult regulations contained in OAR chapter 635, division 006 and Oregon Revised Statutes Chapter 508 for licensing requirements and fee information.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.129, 508.025, 508.035, 508.235, 508.260, 508.300, 508.306 & 508.312
