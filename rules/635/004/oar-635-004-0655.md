## Full text

635-004-0655 Closed Surfperch Season It is unlawful to take surfperch for commercial purposes from the Pacific Ocean from August 1 through September 30.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109, 506.129 & 506.306
