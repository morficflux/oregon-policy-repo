# Far Offshore Fishery Trip Limit (OAR 635-004-0260)

## Full text

635-004-0260 Far Offshore Fishery Trip Limit Except for the licensing requirements provided in OAR 635-004-0245, there are no trip limits or landing restrictions for fish or fish products taken in the far offshore fishery.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.162, 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.138, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129
