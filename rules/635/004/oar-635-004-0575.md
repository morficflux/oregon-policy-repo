635-004-0575 Organization of Other Finfish Rules The Other Finfish Section includes regulations for Halibut, Coastal Rivers Shad, Hagfish, Sturgeon, Surfperch, Minor Finfish and Intertidal Animal Fisheries.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
