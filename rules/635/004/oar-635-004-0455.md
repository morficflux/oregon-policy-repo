## Full text

635-004-0455 Yaquina Bay Roe-Herring Fishery Defined “Yaquina Bay Roe-Herring fishery” means the commercial fishery for Pacific herring (Clupea harengus pallasi) open annually between January 1 and April 15 in Yaquina Bay.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
