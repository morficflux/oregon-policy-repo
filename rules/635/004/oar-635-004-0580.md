## Full text

635-004-0580 Pacific Halibut Fishery Defined “Halibut fishery” means the commercial fishery for Pacific halibut (Hippoglossus stenolepis) in the Pacific Ocean.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.109 & 506.129
