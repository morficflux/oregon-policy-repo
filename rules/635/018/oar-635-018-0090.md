635-018-0090 Inclusions and Modifications The current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations provide requirements for the Central Zone. However, additional regulations may be adopted in this rule division from time to time and to the extent of any inconsistency, they supersede the current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations.

(1) The Deschutes River from the mouth at the I-84 Bridge upstream to Sherars Falls is open to angling for trout, hatchery steelhead, and hatchery Chinook salmon from May 5 through May 20, 2026. All wild Chinook salmon must be released unharmed. The daily catch limit is: (a) One

(1) adult hatchery Chinook salmon; and (b) Five

(5) hatchery jack salmon.

(2) It is unlawful to continue to angle in the area from Sherars Falls downstream to the upper railroad trestle after taking a daily bag limit of one

(1) adult Chinook salmon.

(3) All other catch limits and restrictions remain unchanged from those listed for Deschutes River in the current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138 & ORS 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.162
