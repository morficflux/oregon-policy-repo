# Area (OAR 635-003-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 635-003-0005 — Area. Chapter 635, Division 003. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

635-003-0005 Area As used in this division, the Pacific Ocean includes that area of the Columbia River seaward of a straight line from the knuckle of the south jetty on the Oregon shore to the inshore end of the north jetty on the Washington shore.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 & 506 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496 & 506
