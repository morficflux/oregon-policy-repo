## Full text

635-012-0100 Cape Perpetua Seabird Protection Area Prohibitions and Allowances

(1) Except as specified in section

(2) below, take of all species authorized by general Commission rule for this area is allowed.

(2) Take of the following species, used as seabird forage, are prohibited within the Cape Perpetua Seabird Protection Area: (a) Pacific herring; (b) Pacific sardine (pilchard); (c) Anchovies; (d) Smelt as defined by OAR 635-004-0215; (e) Pacific sand lance; (f) Mackerels.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506.119 & 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.540 - 196.555 & SB 1510 (2012)
