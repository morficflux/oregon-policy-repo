635-014-0090 Inclusions and Modifications The current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations provide requirements for the Northwest Zone. However, additional regulations may be adopted in this rule division from time to time and to the extent of any inconsistency, they supersede the current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations pamphlet.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138 & ORS 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.162
