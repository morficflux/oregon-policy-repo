## Full text

635-047-0035 Fees

(1) Fee for hunting preserve license shall be $230.00 (plus a $2.00 license agent fee) per year.

(2) Licenses issued under this rule shall be issued for a period of July 1 to June 30 of the following year.

Statutory/Other Authority: SB 247 (2015), ORS 496.112, ORS 496.138, ORS 496.146 & ORS 496.162 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.112, ORS 496.138, ORS 496.146 & ORS 496.162
