## Full text

635-010-0031 Cancelled Documents

(1) If cancellation of documents are required as a result of error or other unforeseen circumstances, an agent or customer must contact the Department to obtain a correction.

(2) Agents will not be able to cancel documents after the day of initial issue.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496 & 497 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496 & 497
