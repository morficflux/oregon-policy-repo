## Full text

635-010-0096 Issuing Combined Angling Harvest Tag to Youth When a person under 12 years of age purchases a combined angling harvest tag, the agent must issue at no charge a youth license before issuing a youth combined angling harvest tag.

Statutory/Other Authority: SB 247 (2015), ORS 496 & 497 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496 & 497
