## Full text

635-042-0027 Summer Salmon Season The summer commercial salmon season in the Columbia River downstream of Bonneville Dam is assessed annually based on stock status and allocation policies. Harvest opportunity and gear regulations will be established by inseason modification.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.118, ORS 506.109 & ORS 506.129 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.119 & ORS 507.030
