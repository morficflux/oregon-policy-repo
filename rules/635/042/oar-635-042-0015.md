# Closed (OAR 635-042-0015)

## Full text

635-042-0015 Closed Season It is unlawful to take any fish for commercial purposes from the Columbia River except during the times, in the areas, with the gear, and for the species as provided in these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 506 & 507 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506 & 507
