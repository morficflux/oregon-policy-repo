## Full text

635-042-0154 Carp and Other Nongame Fish It is unlawful to fish for or possess carp and other nongame fish from the Columbia River for commercial purposes except:

(1) As authorized by ORS 508.106 (permit to take carp or other foodfish).

(2) During open commercial fishing periods with gear authorized for those open commercial fishing periods.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 506, 507 & 509 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183, 506, 507 & 509

History: FWC 10-1988, f. & cert. ef. 3-4-88
