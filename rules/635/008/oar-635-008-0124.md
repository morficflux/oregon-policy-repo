## Full text

635-008-0124 Lostine Wildlife Area (Wallowa County) The Lostine Wildlife Area is open to wildlife-oriented public use unless otherwise excluded or restricted by the following rules:

(1) Camping is prohibited.

(2) Domestic sheep and goats are prohibited.

(3) Discharging firearms is prohibited except while hunting as authorized during game bird and game mammal seasons.

(4) Open fires are prohibited.

(5) The wildlife area is closed to the public between 10 pm and 4 am.

(6) ATV and snowmobile use is prohibited on all area lands except for administrative use.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
