## Full text

635-008-0060 Burns Gravel Pond (Harney County) The Burns Gravel Pond area is open to public use unless otherwise excluded or restricted by the following rules:

(1) Hunting is prohibited.

(2) Discharging firearms is prohibited.

(3) Camping is prohibited.

(4) Open fires are prohibited.

(5) The area is closed to the public between 10 pm and 4 am.

(6) Trapping is prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
