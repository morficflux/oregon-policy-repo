## Full text

635-008-0127 North Umpqua River, Winchester Ramp (Douglas County) In addition to the provisions of OAR 635-008-0200, it is unlawful to launch or retrieve any power boat from North Umpqua River Ramp area, located approximately one mile upstream from Winchester Dam on the south side of the river at T26S R5W Sec 30.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
