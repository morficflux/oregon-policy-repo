## Full text

635-008-0103 Gods Valley Wildlife Area (Clatsop County) The Gods Valley Wildlife Area is open to wildlife-oriented public use unless otherwise excluded or restricted by the following rules: Vehicles shall be restricted to travel only on county roads.
