## Full text

635-008-0154 Smith River, Lower Take Out (Douglas County) In addition to the provisions of OAR 635-008-0200, it is unlawful to launch boats from Smith River Lower Take Out area, located approximately seven road miles upstream from the mouth of North Fork Smith River on the north side of Smith River at T20S R10W Sec 35.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
