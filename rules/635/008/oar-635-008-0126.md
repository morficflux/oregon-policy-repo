## Full text

635-008-0126 Minam River Wildlife Area (Union/Wallowa Counties) The Minam River Wildlife Area is open to wildlife-oriented public use compatible with the goals and objectives in the 2026 Minam River Wildlife Area Management Plan unless otherwise excluded or restricted by the following rules:

(1) Discharging of firearms is prohibited except while hunting during authorized game bird and game mammal seasons.

(2) Camping is prohibited except at Wildlife Flat for Minam River Trail access, which shall not exceed 48 hrs, and at 2-Mile Flat on Big Canyon Rd, which is allowed April 1 through December 31 and shall not exceed 14 days during any 30-day period.

(3) Open fires are prohibited except at campsites. All open fires are prohibited on all lands during designated fire closures.

(4) The wildlife area lands are closed to public entry from January 1 through March 31. Exception: Year-round public access is permitted on the Minam River Trail and associated parking area along highway 82.

(5) ATV and snowmobile use is prohibited on all area lands except for administrative use.

(6) ODFW Wildlife Area Parking Permit is required. Vehicle parking at Wildlife Flat for Minam River Trail access shall not exceed 14 days during any 30-day period.

(7) Domestic sheep and goats are prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
