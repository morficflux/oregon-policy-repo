## Full text

635-008-0195 Winchester Viewing Chamber: Douglas County It is unlawful to trespass on the following Department property between the hours of 10p.m. and 4a.m.: Winchester viewing access, the viewing chamber and the fishway, all located at Winchester Dam on the North Umpqua River.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
