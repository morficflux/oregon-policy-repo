635-008-0015 Agreements to Restrict Use of Motor-Propelled Vehicles No person shall violate posted motor-propelled vehicle use restrictions established by agreements entered into pursuant to ORS 498.152. [ED. NOTE: This rule was Renumbered from 630-010-0515, 635-015-0015]
