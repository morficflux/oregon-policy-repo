## Full text

635-425-0015 Fish and Wildlife In-Water Blasting Permit Policy It is the policy of the Oregon Fish and Wildlife Commission to discourage in-water blasting unless it is the only practicable method to accomplish the project goals. The Department may issue in-water blasting permits only if they contain conditions for preventing injury to fish and wildlife and their habitat.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 506.109, 506.119 & 509.140 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 506.109, 506.119 & 509.140
