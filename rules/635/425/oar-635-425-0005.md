# Applicability (OAR 635-425-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 635-425-0005 — Applicability. Chapter 635, Division 425. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

635-425-0005 Applicability OAR 635-425-0000 through 635-425-0050 apply whenever the use of explosives is desired in the course of removing any obstruction in any waters of this state, in constructing any foundations for dams, bridges or other structures, or in carrying on any trade or business.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 506.109, 506.119 & 509.140 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 506.109, 506.119 & 509.140
