# Pre-License (OAR 812-006-0164)

## At a glance

## Full text

812-006-0164 Pre-License Training Alternative Applicants whose RMI’s complete and pass the Contractor Pre-License Business, Law, and Project Management, Training Institute course, developed by an Oregon community college Small Business Development Center, under an Intergovernmental Agreement, will satisfy the pre-licensure training requirement and testing requirement set forth in ORS 701.122.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, 701.122 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.122

History: CCB 3-2018, adopt filed 06/29/2018, effective 07/01/2018
