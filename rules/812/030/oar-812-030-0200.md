## Full text

812-030-0200 General Application Requirements An individual must submit the following to qualify for a locksmith certificate:

(1) An application on a form provided by the board;

(2) The fee established in OAR 812-030-0250; and

(3) If applicable, the CCB license number of the business owned by or employing the applicant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, 701.235 & 701.485 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.485

History: CCB 3-2010, f. & cert. ef. 2-1-10
