## Full text

812-003-0370 Renewal of Inactive Status To renew an inactive license in an inactive status:

(1) If the licensee was subject to discipline by the agency, the licensee must satisfy any conditions imposed by the agency as a result of the discipline;

(2) The licensee must submit the request to renew the license in inactive status on forms provided by the agency; and

(3) The licensee must submit the fees required under OAR 812-003-0142.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.056 & 701.063
