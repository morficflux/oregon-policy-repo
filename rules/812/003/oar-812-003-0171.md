## Full text

812-003-0171 Bond, Letter of Credit or Cash Deposit For all new and renewal license applications, a surety bond as required under ORS 701.068, or a surety bond, letter of credit or cash deposit as required under ORS 701.088, must be in one of the following amounts:

(1) Residential General Contractor — $25,000.

(2) Residential Specialty Contractor — $20,000.

(3) Residential Limited Contractor — $15,000.

(4) Residential Developer — $25,000.

(5) Residential Locksmith Services Contractor — $15,000.

(6) Home Inspector Services Contractor — $15,000.

(7) Home Services Contractor — $15,000.

(8) Home Energy Performance Score Contractor — $15,000.

(9) Residential Restoration Contractor — $15,000.

(10) Commercial General Contractor Level 1 — $80,000.

(11) Commercial Specialty Contractor Level 1 — $55,000.

(12) Commercial General Contractor Level 2 — $25,000.

(13) Commercial Specialty Contractor Level 2 — $25,000.

(14) Commercial Developer — $25,000.

(15) Construction Flagging Contractor — $25,000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 701.068, 701.088 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.068, 701.081, 701.084, 701.088, 701.470 & 701.540
