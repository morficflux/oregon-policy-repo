## Full text

812-020-0060 Effective Date — Continuing Education for Commercial Contractors OAR 812-020-0050 to 812-020-0073 and the amendment to OAR 812-003-0280(2) take effect upon passage, and apply to commercial contractors that renew their licenses on and after July 1, 2010.
