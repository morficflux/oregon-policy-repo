# Renewal "Renewal" (of license) as used in ORS 701 (OAR 812-002-0640)

## At a glance

OAR 812-002-0640 — Renewal "Renewal" (of license) as used in ORS 701. Chapter 812, Division 002. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

812-002-0640 Renewal “Renewal” (of license) as used in ORS 701.063, 701.068 and 701.131 includes but is not limited to the act of submitting a replacement bond, a bond rider, or letter of credit or cash deposit, a certificate of insurance, a fee, the renewal form, any employer account numbers and any pre-licensure training.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 670.310, 701.056, 701.063, 701.068, 701.073, 701.088, 701.131 & 701.238
