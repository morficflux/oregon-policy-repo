# General Contractor (OAR 812-002-0320)

## At a glance

OAR 812-002-0320 — General Contractor. Chapter 812, Division 002. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

812-002-0320 General Contractor "General contractor" means a contractor whose business operations require the use of more than two unrelated building trades or crafts that the contractor supervises, subcontracts to be performed or performs in whole or in part.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701
