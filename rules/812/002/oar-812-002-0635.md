## Full text

812-002-0635 Reissue A license is reissued when licensure is approved by the Board after a lapse that occurred because the licensee failed to renew the license and failed to provide proof of bonding, letter of credit, or cash deposit coverage and insurance coverage during the lapse. A reissued license is effective from the date that the lapse ends.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.063 & 701.068
