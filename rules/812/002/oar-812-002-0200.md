## Full text

812-002-0200 Date the Contractor Becomes Aware of a Lapse in License “Date the contractor becomes aware of a lapse in license” includes but is not limited to the date a notice is mailed to the address of record from the Construction Contractors Board that his/her license has been suspended, terminated or lapsed for any reason.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.063 & 701.131
