# Respondent "Respondent" means a contractor that a complaint is filed against under ORS 701 (OAR 812-002-0670)

## At a glance

OAR 812-002-0670 — Respondent "Respondent" means a contractor that a complaint is filed against under ORS 701. Chapter 812, Division 002. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

812-002-0670 Respondent “Respondent” means a contractor that a complaint is filed against under ORS 701.131 to 701.180 or that the board proposes to impose a civil penalty against under ORS Chapter 701, including but not limited to 701.992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.131 - 701.180
