## Full text

812-002-0420 Lapse in License “Lapse in license” as used in ORS 701.063(4), 701.131(2)(b), 701.225(6)(b) and OAR chapter 812 commences at the time that a license expires, is suspended or is terminated for any reason and ends when the license is renewed, reissued or reinstated by the agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.063, 701.131 & 701.225
