## Full text

812-002-0630 Reinstate A license is reinstated when licensure is approved by the Board after a lapse that occurred because the license was suspended. A reinstated license is effective from the date that the suspension ends.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.063, 701.068 & 701.098(4)
