# Work Period (OAR 812-002-0800)

## At a glance

OAR 812-002-0800 — Work Period. Chapter 812, Division 002. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

812-002-0800 Work Period “Work Period” as used in, OAR 812-003-0150, 812-004-0320, 812-004-0600, 812-004-1320 and 812-004-1600 means the time period from the date a contractor accepts a payment, offers a written proposal, enters into a contract or begins construction, whichever occurs first, until the date the contractual work is substantially completed by the contractor, or if not substantially completed, the date the work by the contractor ceased.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.133, 701.139, 701.140, 701.145 & 701.146
