## Full text

812-002-0143 Complainant “Complainant” means a person who files a complaint against a contractor under ORS 701.131 to 701.180.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.131 - 701.180
