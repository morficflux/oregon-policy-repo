## Full text

812-002-0500 Nurseryman "Nurseryman" as used in ORS 701.005 means any person who cares for nursery stock for sale, as defined in 571.005(5).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 571.005 & 701.005
