## Full text

812-002-0580 Person “Person” means:

(1) An individual, including, but not limited to a self-employed individual;

(2) A partnership, joint venture, limited liability partnership, or limited partnership;

(3) A corporation;

(4) A trust;

(5) A limited liability company; or

(6) Other entity.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 87.005, 87.093, 445.080, 656.021, 656.029 & 701
