# Structure (OAR 812-002-0700)

## At a glance

OAR 812-002-0700 — Structure. Chapter 812, Division 002. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

812-002-0700 Structure “Structure” means that which is built or constructed, an edifice or building of any kind, or any piece of work artificially built up or composed of parts joined together in some definite manner, or an improvement attached to real estate or any part thereof as described in ORS 701.005.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 87.058, 279C.460, 646.605 & 701
