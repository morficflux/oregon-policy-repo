## Full text

812-002-0040 Appurtenance “Appurtenance” means any accessory improvement to real estate associated with a structure.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.027, 701.005, 701.139, 701.145, 701.146 & 701.325
