## Full text

812-002-0280 For Compensation or With the Intent to Sell “For compensation or with the intent to sell” as used in ORS 701.005 is not intended to include real estate licensees engaged in professional real estate activities as defined in 696.010(13).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.005, 701.010, 701.021, 701.026 & 701.131
