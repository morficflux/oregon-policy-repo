## Full text

812-002-0720 Subcontractor “Subcontractor” is an entity who has a contract, either oral or written, with a contractor but not with the owner of the structure to perform work subject to ORS Chapter 701 and who is responsible for a specific portion of the entire project.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279C.555 & 701

History: CCB 7-2005, f. 12-7-05, cert. ef. 1-1-06 CCB 4-1998, f. & cert. ef. 4-30-98
