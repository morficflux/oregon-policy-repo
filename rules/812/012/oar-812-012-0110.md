## Full text

812-012-0110 Terms of Written Contract

(1) If a contractor is required to have a written contract under ORS 701.305, the written contract or attached addendum to the written contract must contain the following: (a) The contractor’s name, address, phone number and license number issued by the board as shown on board records. (b) An acknowledgment of a written offer of a warranty, if an offer is required by ORS 701.320, and indication of the acceptance or rejection of the offered warranty; (c) An explanation of the property owner’s rights under the contract, including, but not limited to, the ability to file a complaint with the board and the existence of any mediation or arbitration provision in the contract, set forth in a conspicuous manner as defined by the board by rule; (d) Customer’s name and address; (e) Address where the work is to be performed; (f) A description of the work to be performed; and (g) Price and payment terms.

(2) The information described in section

(1) of this rule must be legible and in dark ink.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, 701.235, 701.305, 701.315, 701.320, 701.330 & 701.335 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.305, 701.330 & 701.335
