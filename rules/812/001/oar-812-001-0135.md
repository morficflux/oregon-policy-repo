# Receipt Date of Documents (OAR 812-001-0135)

## Full text

812-001-0135 Receipt Date of Documents Any document delivered to the agency, including documents delivered by mail, fax, e-mail, or any other type of delivery, before or at 12:00:00 midnight will be considered received on the day of delivery ending at midnight. Any document delivered to the agency after 12:00:00 midnight will be considered received on the day of delivery beginning immediately after midnight.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701

History: CCB 15-2006, f. 12-12-06, cert. ef. 1-1-07
