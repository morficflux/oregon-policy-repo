## Full text

812-007-0260 Fees for Lead-Based Paint Activity Licenses — Contractors

(1) All license and renewal application fees are non-refundable and non-transferrable.

(2) The following fees are established: (a) Lead abatement contractor, $50/year. (b) Lead inspection contractor, $50/year.

(3) If the board receives payment of fees by check and the check is returned to the board as an NSF check, the board will charge the applicant $25 in addition to the required fees.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, 701.235 & 701.515 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.510 & 701.515

History: CCB 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 2-1-10
