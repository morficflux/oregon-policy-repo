## Full text

812-022-0018 Agreements to Offer Agency Courses — Continuing Education for Residential Contractors The agency may enter into agreements with approved providers to provide the agency’s courses on laws, regulations and business practices.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, 701.126 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.267
