## Full text

812-004-1500 Closure of Complaint After Settlement If the complainant and respondent agree to a settlement, within 30 days from the date the settlement agreement requires completion of the terms of the settlement, the complainant must notify the agency in writing whether the terms of the settlement have been fulfilled.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.145
