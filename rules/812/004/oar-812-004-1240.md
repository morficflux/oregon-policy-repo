## Full text

812-004-1240 Exhaustion of Surety Bond, Letter of Credit or Cash Deposit The agency may continue processing a complaint even though the surety bond, letter of credit or cash deposit related to that complaint is exhausted by prior complaints.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: OAR 701.068, 701.088, 701.145 & 701.150
