## Full text

812-004-1001 Application of Rules

(1) The rules in 812-004-1001 to 812-004-1600 apply to complaints filed under ORS 701.145.

(2) The following rules apply to complaints filed under ORS 701.146: (a) OAR 812-004-1001 through 812-004-1240; (b) OAR 812-004-1260 through 812-004-1320; (c) OAR 812-004-1340, except 812-004-1340(2)(c), (2)(i) and (8); (c) OAR 812-004-1420; (d) OAR 812-004-1520; and (e) OAR 812-004-1600.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: OAR 701.133, 701.139, 701.140, 701.145 & 701.146
