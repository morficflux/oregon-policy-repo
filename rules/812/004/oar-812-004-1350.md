## Full text

812-004-1350 Procedure if Information on Complaint Form is Incomplete If the agency receives a complaint form that does not meet the requirements of OAR 812-004-1340, the agency may close the complaint if the complainant does not provide the missing information in response to a written request for the information from the agency. The written request and closure must comply with OAR 812-004-1260.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.131, 701.133, 701.139, 701.140, 701.143, 701.145 & 701.146
