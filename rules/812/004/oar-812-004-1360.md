## Full text

812-004-1360 Addition of Complaint Items at On-Site Meeting If the agency holds an on-site meeting, the complainant may add new complaint items up to and through the initial on-site meeting. New items added to a timely filed complaint under this rule are considered timely filed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.140 & 701.145
