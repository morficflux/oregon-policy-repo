## Full text

812-004-1460 Agency Recommendation of Resolution If it appears that the respondent has breached a contract or performed work negligently or improperly, the agency may recommend to the complainant and respondent a resolution consistent with the terms of the contract, generally accepted building practices, and industry standards.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.130, 701.145 & 701.150
