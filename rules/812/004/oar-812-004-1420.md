## Full text

812-004-1420 Processing Owner and Primary Contractor Complaints Together If an owner complaint based on the same facts and issues is received at any time during the processing of a primary contractor complaint, the two complaints will be processed together.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.140 & 701.145
