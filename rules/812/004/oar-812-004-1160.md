## Full text

812-004-1160 Establishment of Co-Complainant The agency may allow a person to become a co-complainant, with the complainant’s permission, even though that person did not sign the complaint form if the person would otherwise qualify as a complainant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.140 & 701.145
