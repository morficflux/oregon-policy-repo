## Full text

812-009-0320 Entry of Agency Evidence Contested case enforcement hearings may be held before an administrative law judge. The agency's evidence may be entered into the record by the administrative law judge, or by another representative of the agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 701.235 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.133 & 701.145
