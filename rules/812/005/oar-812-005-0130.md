## Full text

812-005-0130 Hearing If the agency issues a notice of intent to take an action and an answer or a written request for hearing is timely received, the agency will set an administrative hearing to determine whether the respondent has violated any provision of ORS Chapter 701 or of these rules, and if so, the duration of any suspension and the amount of any civil penalty to be assessed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310 - 183.500, 670.310, 701.235 & 701.992 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.098 & 701.102
