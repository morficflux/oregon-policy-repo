## Full text

812-005-0100 Notice of Intent to Take Action Except as provided under authority of ORS 701.098(4), if the agency intends to revoke, suspend, or condition a license, or assess a civil penalty, it shall issue and serve on the respondent a notice of intent to take an action, giving the opportunity for hearing. For cases the agency determines as potentially complex, unique or otherwise significant, the notice may include the statement that an answer to the assertions or charges will be required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310 - 183.500, 701.235 & 701.992 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.102
