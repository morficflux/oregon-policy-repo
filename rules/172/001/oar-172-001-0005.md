# Model (OAR 172-001-0005)

## Full text

172-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure The Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedure Act, as promulgated by the Attorney General of the State of Oregon, effective as of February 12, 2010, are adopted as the rules of procedure for administrative rulemaking and other administrative law functions of the Authority. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Oregon Facilities Authority.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341(1) & 289.240(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341

History: OFA 1-2011, f. & cert. ef. 1-10-11 HECF 3-1990, f. & cert. ef. 6-14-90 HECF 1-1990(Temp), f. & cert. ef. 1-30-90
