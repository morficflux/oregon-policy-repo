## Full text

670-010-0020 Penalties Penalty for delaying transmittal of assessment moneys ORS 576.355 "In addition to the penalties prescribed in ORS 576.991, any person who delays transmittal of funds beyond the time set by a Commission shall pay a penalty of 10 percent of the amount due and shall also pay one and one-half percent interest per month on the unpaid balance of the assessment. A commission may waive the penalty and interest described in subsection

(1) of this section upon showing of good cause."
