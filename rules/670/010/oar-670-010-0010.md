## Full text

670-010-0010 Assessments

(1) Any producer, handler or first purchaser shall deduct and withhold an assessment of four-tenths of a cent ($.004) per pound or $8 per ton after June 1, 2001 for all blueberries grown in Oregon (See definition of “First Purchaser.)

(2) The maximum assessment for a reporting year (670-010-0005(8)) will be one percent of the total annual gross sales of the raw product of the berries delivered to the handler(s) during the reporting year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.325 & 576.335
