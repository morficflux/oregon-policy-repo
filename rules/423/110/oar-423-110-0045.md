## Full text

423-110-0045 Prohibitions An entity or person to which the Youth Development Division distributes funds under OAR 423-110-0001 to 423-110-0050 may not expend the funds on any of the following:

(1) Any activity or use prohibited by state law or rule or local ordinance;

(2) If the funds are federal funds, any activity or use prohibited by federal law or regulation;

(3) Campaigning for office or campaigning on behalf of a person who is running for office or who is currently in office;

(4) Religious instruction or recruitment;

(5) Any activity or use that falls outside of the scope of work described within any grant agreement pursuant to which the funds were distributed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 417.847 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.847
