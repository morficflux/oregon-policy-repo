## Full text

423-110-0030 Notice and Appeal

(1) Prior to awarding a grant under OAR 423-110-0025, the Youth Development Division shall send written notification of the initial determination of the distribution to any applicant that applied for the grant.

(2) If the applicant will receive a grant under OAR 423-110-0025 the written notification must take the form of a letter of intent to award. A letter of intent to award does not guarantee that the applicant will receive the grant. Receipt of a grant is contingent on satisfying any requirement imposed by or necessary to enter into a grant agreement.

(3) The division shall establish an appeals process by which an eligible applicant may appeal a decision to award a grant under OAR 423-110-0025. The process must be approved by the council.
