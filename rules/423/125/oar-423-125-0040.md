# Site (OAR 423-125-0040)

## Full text

423-125-0040 Site Visits If practicable, the division shall make a reasonable effort to visit a site where activities funded by gang prevention and intervention funds are conducted at least once a biennium.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 417.847 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.850 & ORS 417.855

History: YDD 2-2019, adopt filed 08/21/2019, effective 08/21/2019
