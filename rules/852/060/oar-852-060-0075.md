## Full text

852-060-0075 Discovery An order requiring discovery will be responded to pursuant to OAR 137-003-0025 included in the Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act and ORS 676.175. In addition, ORS 676.175 provides that contested case hearings are closed to members of the public.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 683 & 182 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 683.155 & 182.466
