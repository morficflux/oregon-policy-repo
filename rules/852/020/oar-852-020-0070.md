## Full text

852-020-0070 Optometric Physician Educational and Professional Standards To meet the expanded optometric scope of practice and current standard of care in Oregon, all optometric physicians must have demonstrated qualification and have obtained certification to use topical and nontopical therapeutic pharmaceutical agents for the practice of optometry.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 683 & 182 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 683.210(1), 683.270(1)(k) & 182.466
