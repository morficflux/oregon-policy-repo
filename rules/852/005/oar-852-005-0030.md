## Full text

852-005-0030 Contracting The Oregon Board of Optometry adopts by reference portions of the State of Oregon’s contracting policies. These rules are contained in the Oregon Administrative Rules, chapter 125, divisions 20, 300. The Board’s financial policies will govern any other contracting activities.
