## Full text

852-070-0005 Definitions “Hour” means clock hour of 60 minutes of instruction time, plus or minus 10 minutes. “Half-hour” means 30 minutes of instruction time, plus or minus five minutes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 683 & 182 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 683.210 & 182.466
