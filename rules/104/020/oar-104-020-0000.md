# Purpose (OAR 104-020-0000)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

104-020-0000 Purpose The purpose of the rule is to provide guidance for all private, state, local government agencies and non-governmental employers with 250 or more full-time employees to conduct mandatory annual earthquake drills for employees located within office buildings.

Statutory/Other Authority: Ch. 366 & OL 2001 Statutes/Other Implemented: Ch. 366 & OL 2001

History: OEM 1-2013, f. 12-17-13, cert. ef. 1-1-14 OEM 1-2002, f. & cert. ef. 4-15-02
