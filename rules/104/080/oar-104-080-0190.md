## Full text

104-080-0190 Funding the Emergency Communications System

(1) The Department distributes Emergency Communications Account monies to 9-1-1 jurisdictions quarterly, pursuant to ORS 403.240.

(2) The Department shall maintain a current list of 9-1-1 jurisdictions eligible to receive and expend distributed 9-1-1 tax funds from the Emergency Communications Account.

(3) The Department shall make payments for costs of the emergency communications system on behalf of a 9-1-1 jurisdiction as referenced in ORS 403.240

(3) (a), (b) and (c).
