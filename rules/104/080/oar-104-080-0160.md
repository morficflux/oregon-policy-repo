## Full text

104-080-0160 The Emergency Communications System

(1) The Department shall coordinate with all parties to ensure effective delivery of the emergency call using the emergency communications system, including providers, 9-1-1 Jurisdictions and PSAP staff.

(2) The delivery of an emergency call requires: (a) A secure network for delivery of ALI; (b) The secure delivery of ANI by the provider; (c) CPE at each PSAP for emergency call processing and display; (d) Mapping software and hardware showing the caller’s location; and (e) Equipment allowing direct communication with the caller.
