## Full text

611-010-0025 Effective Dates of Assessment The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser or handler, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and OAR 611-010-0010(1), or the reports required under 611-010-0015, applies to and covers any purchase by him or delivery on or after July 6, 1987, of caneberries grown in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.325 & 576.335

History: OCC 4-1987, f. & ef. 12-1-87 OCC 3-1987(Temp), f. & ef. 7-30-87 OCC 2-1981, f. 7-22-81, ef. 7-23-81
