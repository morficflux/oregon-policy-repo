# Penalties (OAR 611-010-0020)

## At a glance

## Full text

611-010-0020 Penalties Penalty for delaying transmittal of assessment moneys (ORS 576.355): "In addition to the penalties prescribed in ORS 576.991, any first purchaser or other person who delays transmittal of funds beyond the time set by the Commission shall pay ten percent of the amount due for the first month of delay and one and one-half percent of the amount due for each month of delay thereafter."

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.325 & 576.355
