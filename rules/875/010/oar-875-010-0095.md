## Full text

875-010-0095 Fee Waivers for Licenses or Permits The Board may waive a category of fees if, in its judgment, such waiver is necessary to ensure that the fees charged do not exceed the cost of administering the Board's regulatory program pursuant to ORS 656.255(c).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 686.210 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 686.020, 686.045 & 686.065
