## Full text

710-015-0000 Independent Expert Panelists According to the duties defined in ORS 250.139(6)(e), the Commission shall establish a panel of experts independent of campaigns supporting or campaigns opposing the measure to provide testimony or other information to the citizen panel.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 250.137(3)(a) & 250.139(6)(d) & (e) & 2014 OL Ch. 72 & Sec. 2 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 250.137(3)(a) & 250.139(6)(d) & (e) & 2014 OL Ch. 72 & Sec. 2
