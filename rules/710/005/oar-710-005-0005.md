## Full text

710-005-0005 Commission Budget The Citizens’ Initiative Review Commission hereby adopts by reference the Citizens’ Initiative Review Commission 2015–2017 Biennium Budget of $243,250 covering the period from July 1, 2017 through June 30, 2019. The Chair of the Commission, in consultation with the Commission, will amend budgeted accounts as necessary within the approved budget of $243,250 for the effective operation of the Commission. The Commission will not exceed the approved 2017–2019 Biennium Budget without amending this rule and holding a public hearing thereon as required, by ORS 182.462(1). The budget can be found on the Commission’s website. [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
