## Full text

331-475-0005 Definitions The following definitions apply to OAR 331-475-0010 to OAR 331-495-0005:

(1) “IBCLC” means an individual who is an International Board Certified Lactation Consultant certified by the International Board of Lactation Consultant Examiners as of June 29, 2017, or has met – at a minimum – the IBCLE standards in place on June 29, 2017.

(2) “IBLCE” means the International Board of Lactation Consultant Examiners.

(3) “Good standing” means no unresolved or outstanding disciplinary actions.

(4) “Office” means the Health Licensing Office.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565 & ORS 676.665-676.689 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565 & ORS 676.665-676.689
