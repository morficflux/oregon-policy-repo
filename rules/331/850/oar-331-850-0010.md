# Investigative Authority (OAR 331-850-0010)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

331-850-0010 Investigative Authority The Office may initiate and conduct investigations relating to the practice of sexual-abuse-specific treatment pursuant to ORS 676.608, and may take appropriate disciplinary action in accordance with the provisions of 675.385, 676.612 and 676.992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472
