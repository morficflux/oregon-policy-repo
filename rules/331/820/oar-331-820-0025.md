## Full text

331-820-0025 Certified Clinical Sexual Offense Therapists Without a Mental Health License A CCSOT who did not hold an active Oregon mental health professional license, or equivalent license as determined by the Office, upon initial certification, may continue to engage in the practice of sexual-abuse-specific treatment as a CCSOT only if the person obtains an Oregon mental health professional license or equivalent license as determined by the Office by Jan. 1, 2021.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472
