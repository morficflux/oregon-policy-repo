## Full text

331-130-0015 License Display and Posting Requirements

(1) Licensees must show proof of valid license upon request.

(2) Licensees must post the license in public view at the licensed athletic trainer's primary workplace in public view.

(3) If a licensee carries the license pocket card, it may be laminated.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.701-688.734 & 2019 Senate Bill 742 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.701-688.734 & 2019 Senate Bill 742
