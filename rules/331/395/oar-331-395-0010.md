# INVESTIGATIVE AUTHORITY (OAR 331-395-0010)

## Full text

331-395-0010 INVESTIGATIVE AUTHORITY The Office may initiate and conduct investigations relating to the practice of art therapy and may take appropriate disciplinary action in accordance with the provisions of ORS 676.612 and ORS 676.992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 681.740-681.758 & 2019 Senate Bill 29 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 681.740-681.758 & 2019 Senate Bill 29
