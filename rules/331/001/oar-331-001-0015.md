## Full text

331-001-0015 Applicability of Office Rules The provisions of OAR 331-001-0000 through 331-030-0040 shall apply in the administration and regulation of all programs administered by the Office pursuant to ORS 676.565 and ORS 681.700 to 676.730 except as otherwise provided.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.615, ORS 681.700-676.730 & ORS 676.565 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.615, ORS 681.700-676.730 & ORS 676.565
