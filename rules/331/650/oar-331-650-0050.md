## Full text

331-650-0050 Office-Administered Practical Examination Requirements Before an individual can apply to take the Office-administered practical examination, they must:

(1) Complete the training program pursuant to 331-640-0005; or

(2) Meet the requirements in ORS 694.065 (1)(b) or (1)(c).
