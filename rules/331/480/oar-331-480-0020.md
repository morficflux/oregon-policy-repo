## Full text

331-480-0020 License display and posting requirements

(1) Authorization holders must show proof of valid license upon request or post the license in public view at the lactation consultant’s primary workplace; the authorization holder may carry the license pocket card.

(2) An authorization holder may temporarily conceal the address printed on the license with a covering that is removable.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565 & ORS 676.665-676.689 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565 & ORS 676.665-676.689
