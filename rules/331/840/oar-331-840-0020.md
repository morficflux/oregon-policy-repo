## Full text

331-840-0020 Ethical Principles The Sexual Offense Treatment Board adopts the 2014 Association for the Treatment of Sexual Abusers (ATSA) Professional Code of Ethics and pages 1-15 of the Ethical Principles. Documents are available at www.atsa.com and can be obtained from the Office upon request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472
