## Full text

331-840-0010 Standards of Practice

(1) The standards set forth in OAR Chapter 331, Division 840 apply to all certificate holders as specified in ORS 675.365(3).

(2) Failure to comply with these standards constitutes unprofessional conduct, and a certificate holder engaging in such conduct may be subject to discipline under ORS 676.612.

(3) All certificate holders must abide by confidentiality requirements pursuant to ORS 675.390.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472
