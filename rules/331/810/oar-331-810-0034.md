## Full text

331-810-0034 Certified Sexual Offense Therapist Intern

(1) Only a CSOTI certified under ORS 675.375 may use the title, words or abbreviations that indicate the person is authorized to practice as a CSOTI.

(2) A CSOTI certification is valid for one year and becomes inactive on the last day of the month one year from the date of issuance.

(3) A CSOTI must be under the direct supervision of a CCSOT.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.365-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.365-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472
