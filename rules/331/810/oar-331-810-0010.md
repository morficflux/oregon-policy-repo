## Full text

331-810-0010 Certified Clinical Sexual Offense Therapist

(1) Only a CCSOT certified under ORS 675.375 may use the title, words or abbreviations that indicate the person is authorized to practice as a CCSOT.

(2) A certification is valid for one year and becomes inactive on the last day of the month one year from the date of issuance.
