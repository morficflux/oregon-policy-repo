## Full text

331-810-0025 Certified Associate Sexual Offense Therapist

(1) Only a CASOT certified under ORS 675.375 may use the title words or abbreviations that indicate the person is authorized to practice as a CASOT.

(2) A CASOT certification is valid for one year and becomes inactive on the last day of the month one year from the date of issuance.

(3) A CASOT must be under the direct supervision of a CCSOT.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472
