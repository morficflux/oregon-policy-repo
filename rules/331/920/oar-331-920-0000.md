## Full text

331-920-0000 Dermal Implanting Prohibitions Dermal implanting services defined under ORS 690.350 are prohibited until education and training programs can be implemented.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 345, 676.607, 676.615, 676.625, 690.365, 690.370, 690.385, 690.390, 690, 405, 690.407, 690.410 & 690.415 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.607, 676.608, 676.612, 676.615, 676.625, 690.350, 690.360, 690.365, 690.370, 690.380, 390.385, 690.390, 690.405, 690.407, 690.410, 690.415, 2011 OL Ch. 346 § 22 & 35
