## Full text

331-705-0025 Vacancies in Office If the chairperson or vice chairperson are unable to complete their term, the Board must elect another chairperson or vice chairperson.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.820 & ORS 688.825 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.615, ORS 688.820 & ORS 688.825
