## Full text

331-705-0015 Election of Chairperson and Vice Chairperson The chairperson and vice chairperson must be elected by the Board. The elected Board chairperson and vice chairperson may serve continuously until the Board elects another chairperson and vice chairperson.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.615, ORS 688.820 & ORS 688.825 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.820 & ORS 688.825
