## Full text

331-900-0110 Informed Consent for Certain Standard Body Piercing Procedures

(1) A licensee must document the verbal explanation and the written informed consent process in the client’s record. Informed consent information must include the following: (a) Definition of procedure or process; (b) Risk(s) of procedure or process; (c) Description and risk of adverse outcomes; (d) Alternative procedures and their risks; (e) Documentation of questions asked by the client; (f) Aftercare instructions; and (g) Signature from the licensee and the client.

(2) Informed consent including signatures from the licensee may be electronically signed and stored in accordance with

(1) of this rule.
