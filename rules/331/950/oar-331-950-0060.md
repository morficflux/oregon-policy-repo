## Full text

331-950-0060 Schedule of Civil Penalties for Electrologists The Office has adopted the following presumptive penalty schedule for the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd violation of Electrologist Practice Restrictions laws and rules. This schedule applies, except at the discretion of the Office pursuant to OAR 331-020-0060. For the 4th and subsequent offenses, the provisions of OAR 331-020-0060 apply.

(1) Performing a prohibited service is a violation of OAR 331-910-0065 excluding (4): (a) 1st offense: $1,000; (b) 2nd offense: $2,500; (c) 3rd offense: Monetary penalty and any other actions allowed by law including revocation of suspended authorization to practice and refusal to issue a new authorization to practice to a revoked authorization holder.

(2) Failing to adhere to towels or linens requirements listed in OAR 331-910-0065(4): (a) 1st offense: $300; (b) 2nd offense: $500; (c) 3rd offense: $1,000.
