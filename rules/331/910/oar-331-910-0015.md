## Full text

331-910-0015 Application Requirements for Electrology Temporary License – Limited Basis An individual applying for an electrology temporary license for a limited basis must:

(1) Meet the requirements of OAR 331, division 30; (a) Submit a completed application form prescribed by the Office, which must contain the information listed in OAR 331-030-0000, including one form of government issued identification which must be photographic and show proof of being 18 years of age. The completed application must be accompanied by payment of the required application and license fees and must be received at least 20 calendar days before electrology services are provided to clients; and (b) Attest to six months of training or experience, within the last two years, pertaining to performing electrology on a form prescribed by the Office.

(2) For the purpose of this rule training or experience includes attendance or participation at an instructional program presented, recognized, or under the sponsorship of any permanently organized institution, agency, or professional organization or association recognized by the Office.

(3) All applications received after the required 20th calendar day deadline will not be accepted by the Office unless otherwise approved.
