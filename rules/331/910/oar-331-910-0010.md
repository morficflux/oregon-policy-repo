## Full text

331-910-0010 Electrology Temporary License - Limited Basis

(1) An electrology temporary license is a temporary license, issued on a limited basis, to perform electrology services (guest spots, instruction) not to exceed 30 consecutive calendar days. An electrology temporary license holder: (a) May renew the license up to two times, in a 12-month period from the date the Office receives the initial application. License renewals can be done consecutively with no lapse in active license dates; (b) Must submit all requests to renew a license on a form prescribed by the Office. The completed form must be accompanied by payment of the required renewal fee. Requests to renew a license must be received at least 20 calendar days before electrology services are provided, unless the requested renewal period is consecutive to the previously authorized period with no lapse in active license; (c) Must submit notification of a change in work location at least 24 hours before services are performed; and (d) Must work in a licensed body art facility.

(2) An electrology temporary license holder must adhere to standards within OAR 331-910-0065, 331-910-0070, 331-910-0075, 331-910-0080, 331-910-0085 and all applicable rules listed in OAR 331 division 925.
