## Full text

440-020-0010 Purpose of the Rules The Department of Consumer and Business Services is required by ORS 25.785 to record an applicant’s social security number in order to issue or renew an occupational, professional, recreational or driver license, certification, permit or registration subject to suspension under 25.750 to 25.783. The purpose of these rules is to establish requirements and procedures for requiring the applicant to provide a social security number and requirements for applicants who have not been issued a social security number by the United States Social Security Administration.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 705.135 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 25.785
