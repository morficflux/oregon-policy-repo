## Full text

440-045-0020 Assessment Rate The assessment to be levied against insurers, self-insured employers and self-insured employer groups for Calendar Year 2026 shall be 9.8 percent of direct earned premium and the direct earned premium self-insured employers and self-insured employer groups would have paid had they been insured employers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.612, 656.726 & 705.135 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.612 & 656.614
