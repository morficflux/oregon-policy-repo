# Applicability of Rule (OAR 440-045-0015)

## At a glance

OAR 440-045-0015 — Applicability of Rule. Chapter 440, Division 045. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

440-045-0015 Applicability of Rule OAR 440-045-0010 to 440-045-0030 apply to the assessments levied by the director under the authority of ORS 656.612 and 656.614 against insurers' direct earned workers' compensation premiums and the direct earned premium self-insured employers and self-insured employer groups would have paid had they been insured employers in a manner as prescribed in OAR 436, division 085.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 705.135 & 656.612 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.612 & 656.614
