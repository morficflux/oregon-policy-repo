## Full text

440-045-0030 Payment Manner and Intervals The manner and intervals for payments of the Workers' Compensation Premium Assessment is delegated to the Workers' Compensation Division. OAR 436 division 085 prescribe such manner and intervals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 705.135 & 656.612 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.612 & 656.614
