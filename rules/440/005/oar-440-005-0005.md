# Applicability of (OAR 440-005-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

440-005-0005 Applicability of Rule OAR 440-005-0001 to 440-005-0030 apply to all public records of the Department of Consumer and Business Services (department), and govern the application of ORS 192.410 to 192.505 to these records.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.430, 192.440, 656.726, 697.732, 731.244, 731.282, 731.284 & 731.804 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.410 - 192.505
