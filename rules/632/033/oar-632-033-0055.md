# Penalties (l) (OAR 632-033-0055)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

632-033-0055 Penalties (l) Any person who conducts an exploration activity without a valid Exploration Permit as required by the Mined Land Reclamation Act shall be punished upon conviction, by a fine of not more than $10,000.

(2) Violation of any provision of the Mined Land Reclamation Act, or of any rule or order made pursuant to the Act, or of any conditions of an Exploration Permit, is punishable, upon conviction, by a fine of not more than $10,000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 517 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 517.990
