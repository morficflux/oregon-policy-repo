## Full text

632-033-0043 Expiration of an Exploration Permit Obligation An Exploration Permit obligation expires upon: (l) Transferring of the permit to another obligee.

(2) The area within the permit area coming under an Operating Permit.

(3) Completing the obligation of the Exploration Permit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 517 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 517.720

History: GE 1-1990, f. & cert. ef. 8-3-90
