# Notices (OAR 632-015-0035)

## Full text

632-015-0035 Notices The permittee must give the department a minimum of 24 hours advance notice of plugging operations. For seismic shot hole plugging operations that are not a continuation of the initial drilling of the shot hole or part of the plugging procedures immediately following seismic program operations, the permittee must give the department a minimum of 24 hours advance notice of the plugging operations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 516.090 & 520.095 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.027 & 520.095
