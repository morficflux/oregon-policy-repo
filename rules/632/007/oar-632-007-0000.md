## Full text

632-007-0000 Purpose The rules in this division implement the responsibilities of the Oregon Department of Geology and Mineral Industries under ORS 195.260(4)(a) relating to the mapping of further review areas for rapidly moving landslides.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 516.090 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 195.260 & 516.090
