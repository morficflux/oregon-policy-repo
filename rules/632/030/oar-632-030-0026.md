# On-Site (OAR 632-030-0026)

## Full text

632-030-0026 On-Site Construction Waiver The Department may, in its discretion, waive the requirements for preparation and approval of a reclamation plan if the requirements of ORS 517.790(2)(a) through (c) are satisfied and specific plans or functionally similar requirements approved and enforced by a governmental entity are sufficient to ensure that property will be converted to the new use in a timely fashion.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 516.090, 517.810 & 517.840 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 517.750 - 517.992

History: DGMI 1-2009, f. & cert. ef. 5-15-09
