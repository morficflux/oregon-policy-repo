## Full text

632-037-0120 Mine Operation Standards The Department shall require a mining operation to comply with the following mine operation standards:

(1) Mine facilities have been designed to handle the 100-year, 24-hour precipitation event, at a minimum.

(2) An interim vegetative cover of stockpiles of topsoil or overburden materials that will be used in reclamation shall be required to prevent erosion or fugitive dust release from the overburden storage or spoils area.

(3) Any standard adopted by rule by any permitting or cooperating agency related to the operation of a mining operation.

(4) No loss of existing critical habitat of any state or federally listed threatened or endangered plant species, as determined by the Department of Agriculture.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 517.750 - 517.995 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 517.956
