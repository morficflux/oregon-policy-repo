## Full text

632-038-0080 Coordination with Private Incentive Programs The department may cooperate with private foundations, industry associations, or other entities with respect to the establishment and administration of awards or incentive programs that promote mined land reclamation in a manner that is consistent with the objectives of ORS 517.760, 517.840(1), and this division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 517.840(1)(E) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 517.840(1)(E)
