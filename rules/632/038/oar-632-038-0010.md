## Full text

632-038-0010 Applicability The rules in this division apply to mined land reclamation except for activities related to chemical process mining regulated under OAR 632, division 037.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 517.840(1)(E) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 517.840(1)(E)
