## Full text

632-038-0070 Grants To the extent that funds are available from sources identified in ORS 516.090(2) or voluntary settlements of enforcement actions and there is a legislative approval of expenditures, the board may award grants for qualifying reclamation practices.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 517.840(1)(E) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 517.840(1)(E)
