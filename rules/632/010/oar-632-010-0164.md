## Full text

632-010-0164 Commingling of Production Prohibited The production from one pool must not be commingled with that from another pool in the same well before delivery to a purchaser, unless otherwise approved by the board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
