# Tubing (OAR 632-010-0132)

## At a glance

OAR 632-010-0132 — Tubing. Chapter 632, Division 010. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

632-010-0132 Tubing All wells must be equipped with tubing. Production must be exclusively through tubing, unless otherwise approved by the department. The bottom of tubing on flowing wells may not be higher than 100 feet above the top of the producing horizon, or as otherwise approved by the department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
