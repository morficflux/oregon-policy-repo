## Full text

632-010-0011 Active Permits On or before the anniversary of the issuance date of each active permit issued by the department, the permittee must pay to the department an annual nonrefundable fee in the amount specified in ORS 520.017.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
