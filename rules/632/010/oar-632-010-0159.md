## Full text

632-010-0159 Underground Reservoirs for Natural Gas Storage Rules providing for well spacing and proration of gas do not apply to gas storage wells, injection wells, or monitor wells.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095

History: DGMI 1-2013, f. & cert. ef 3-21-13 GMI 3-1980, f. 2-29-80, ef. 3-1-80
