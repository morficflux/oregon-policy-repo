## Full text

632-010-0146 Vacuum Pumps Prohibited The use of vacuum pumps or other devices for the purpose of putting a vacuum on any gas- or oil-bearing stratum is prohibited, unless approved by the department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
