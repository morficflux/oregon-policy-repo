## Full text

632-010-0178 Duration of Tests The permittee must test for open-flow and pressure of completed gas wells at such intervals and continue for such time as is necessary to effect accurate determination. The permittee must file a report of all tests with the department within 60 days of the completed test.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
