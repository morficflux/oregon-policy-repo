## Full text

632-010-0162 Illegal Production No purchaser, producer, operator, permittee, or any other person may produce any crude oil, natural gas, or waste oil from any spacing unit or pool in this state except in accordance with the rules, regulations, and orders of the board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
