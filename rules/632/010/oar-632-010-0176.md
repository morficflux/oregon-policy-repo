## Full text

632-010-0176 Supervision of Open-Flow and Pressure Tests All tests made in determining the potential flow and shut-in, well-head, or bottom hole pressure of a gas well must be conducted by the permittee and may be monitored by the department to ensure that:

(1) Initial flow to the surface is accomplished during daylight hours;

(2) Gas will be flared during tests when feasible; and

(3) Proper safety precautions are taken to prevent fire or explosion during tests.
