# Fire Walls (OAR 632-010-0138)

## At a glance

OAR 632-010-0138 — Fire Walls. Chapter 632, Division 010. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

632-010-0138 Fire Walls Every permanent oil or condensate tank, or battery of tanks, must be surrounded by a dike or firewall with a capacity of one and one-half times that of the tank or battery of tanks.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
