## Full text

632-010-0174 Measurement of Potential Open-Flow of Gas Wells The permittee must determine the potential open-flow of a gas well by U.S. Department of Energy back-pressure method unless the department approves in writing the use of an alternative method.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
