## Full text

632-010-0167 Maximum Efficient Rate Hearings A hearing to determine the maximum efficiency rate at which any pool in the state can produce oil and gas without waste may be held by the board on its own motion or at the request of any interested party.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 520.095
