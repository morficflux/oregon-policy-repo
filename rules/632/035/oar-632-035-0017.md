## Full text

632-035-0017 Limited Exemptions

(1) Limited exempt status is available only for operations that have received and maintained a certificate of limit exemption as provided in ORS 517.770 and 517.775.

(2) Operations under a certificate of limited exemption are subject to the provisions in OAR 632-030-0017.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 517 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 517.770 & 517.775
