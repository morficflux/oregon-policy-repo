## Full text

632-001-0000 Notice of Rule Making In addition to other hearing and notice of rule making requirements, the department will furnish notice of its intended agency action, depending on the sphere of interest, to interested parties named on a list maintained, with periodic revision, by the State Geologist.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 516.090
