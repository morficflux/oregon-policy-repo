## Full text

632-020-0100 Removal of Casings No person may remove a casing, or any part of a casing, from any geothermal well or prospect well without applying in advance and obtaining approval in writing from the department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 522 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 522.305
