## Full text

632-020-0158 Wellhead Equipment, Injection Wells Adequate wellhead equipment must be installed to control expected pressures. Where underground conditions are unknown, the same equipment must be used as required for exploration holes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 522 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 522.305
