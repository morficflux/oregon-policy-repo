# Production Reports (OAR 632-020-0120)

## At a glance

## Full text

632-020-0120 Production Reports The owner or operator of any well producing geothermal resources must file with the department, before the 20th day of each month, a statement of the geothermal resources production from the well during the preceding calendar month. The report must be in a form acceptable to the department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 522 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 522.305
