# Environmental Protection (OAR 632-020-0170)

## At a glance

## Full text

632-020-0170 Environmental Protection In conducting operations under this chapter, the permittee or operator must not pollute or otherwise damage land, water or air resources. The operator must comply with all federal and state air and water quality standards and requirements. Plans for the disposal of well effluents must be approved by the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality and department before any disposal actions are taken.
