## Full text

632-020-0134 Naming of Fields The board, on its own or upon request of any interested person, may, after a public hearing, name a geothermal field and set the boundaries of the field. Using the same procedure, the board may later change the boundaries of a field.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 522 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 522.305
