## Full text

632-020-0117 Spacing Plan The board may, on its own motion, or at the request of any interested party, hold a hearing to determine the maximum efficient rate at which any geothermal well in the state can produce a geothermal resource without waste. After the hearing, the board may set production rates by order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 522 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 522.305
