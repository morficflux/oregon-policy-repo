## Full text

632-020-0151 Handling of Test Fluids Prior to conducting formation and production tests, the operator must provide adequate storage for anticipated volumes of formation fluids and drilling mud. Arrangements for ultimate disposal of waste fluids must be made with the department and the Department of Environmental Quality.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 522 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 522.305
