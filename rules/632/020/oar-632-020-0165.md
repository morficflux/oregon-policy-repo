## Full text

632-020-0165 Judicial Review of Board Actions Any person adversely affected by any rule or order by the board may obtain judicial review thereof pursuant to ORS Chapter 183.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 522 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 522.305
