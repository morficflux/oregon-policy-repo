## Full text

114-006-0000 Interference with Ombudsman Program

(1) No person shall willfully interfere with the performance of the Ombudsman or a designee’s official duties.

(2) Individuals interfering with the Ombudsman or designee’s official duties will be referred to the appropriate agency for sanctions or other actions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441

History: LTCO 1-1989, f. 1-19-89, cert. ef. 2-1-89
