## Full text

114-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure Pursuant to ORS 183.341, the Long-Term Care Ombudsman adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedure Act as amended and effective March 3, 1988. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedures is available from the the office of the Attorney General or from the Long Term Care Ombudsman.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441 & 183

History: LTCO 1-1988, f. 3-20-88, cert. ef. 6-1-88
