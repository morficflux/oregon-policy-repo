## Full text

114-001-0010 Fee for Copies of Public Records The Ombudsman shall provide within a reasonable time copies of any public records in its files upon written request from any person and may charge a fee reasonably calculated to cover its costs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441

History: LTCO 1-1989, f. 1-19-89, cert. ef. 2-1-89
