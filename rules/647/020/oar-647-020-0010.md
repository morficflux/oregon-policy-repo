## Full text

647-020-0010 Purpose of Public Records Request Processing Rules

(1) To implement the provisions of ORS 192.324(7) that require each public body to publish its methods for the public to obtain information and in order to implement the provisions of ORS 192.318 to provide reasonable rules necessary for the protection of Oregon Processed Vegetable Commission records and to prevent interference with the regular discharge of the commission duties, the commission adopts the procedures set forth in this division. Procedures for review of any denial of public records requests, or fee waiver or reduction, shall be in accordance with the provisions of ORS 192.311 to 192.478.

(2) Capitalized terms in this division not specifically defined are as defined in ORS 192.311.

(3) Commodity Commission is as defined in ORS 576, ORS 577 and ORS 578.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.318(2), ORS 576.304(14), ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.324, ORS 192.329
