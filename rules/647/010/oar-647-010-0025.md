## Full text

647-010-0025 Effective Dates of Assessment The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser or handler, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and OAR 647-010-0010(1) — Assessments, or the reports required under OAR 647-010-0015 — Reports and Payment of Assessment Moneys, applies to and covers any purchases by him or delivery on or after July 25, 1985, of the above named vegetables for processing and grown in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576

History: PVC 2-1985, f. 7-17-85, ef. 7-22-85
