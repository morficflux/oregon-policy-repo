## Full text

647-015-0010 Number of Commissioners, Terms The Oregon Processed Vegetable Commission will consist of seven commissioners appointed by the Director of the Oregon Department of Agriculture for a term of three years or, if for a term following a prior term for that person, ending three years from the date of expiration of any prior term.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576
