## Full text

418-040-0000 Purpose The rules in OAR chapter 418, division 40 establish the rules for the Homecare Choice Program administered by the Oregon Home Care Commission. The purpose of the program is to provide private pay individuals with access to the Commission's statewide Registry to hire employees to provide in-home services and to assist participants in meeting the legal responsibilities of being a household employer.
