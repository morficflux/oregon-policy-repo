## Full text

419-430-0080 Day Treatment Agencies: Health Services A day treatment agency must provide oversight of the clinical aspects of health care provided to children in care and must provide psychiatric on-call consultation at all times.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, ORS 418.240 & ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.205 - 418.325
