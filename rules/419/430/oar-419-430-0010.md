## Full text

419-430-0010 Day Treatment Agencies: What Law Applies

(1) These rules, OAR 419-430-0010 to 419-430-0110, regulate a child caring agency that provides day treatment services. A day treatment agency must: (a) Comply with OAR 419-400-0005 to 419-400-0310 and OAR 419-430-0010 to 419-430-0110; and (b) Comply with all requirements in OAR chapter 309, division 22 applicable to providers of psychiatric day treatment.

(2) OAR 419-430-0010 to 419-430-0110 do not apply to a program that provides residential care under OAR 419-470-0010 to 419-470-0180, an academic boarding school (OAR 419-410-0010 to 419-410-0150), or a therapeutic boarding school (OAR 419-490-0010 to 419-490-0170.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, ORS 418.005, ORS 418.215 & ORS 418.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.205 - 418.325
