## Full text

419-460-0240 Outdoor Youth Programs: Animals and Pets An outdoor youth program must comply with all of the following requirements:

(1) Animals and pets must be free from disease and cared for in a safe and clean manner.

(2) An outdoor youth program must take reasonable measures to assure that children in care are not exposed to danger from animals.

(3) All domestic animals and pets must be vaccinated against rabies. Documentation of the vaccination against rabies must be available in the responsible employee's personnel file.
