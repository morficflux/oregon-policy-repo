# Confidentiality (OAR 419-220-0200)

## Full text

419-220-0200 Confidentiality Information gathered and records and reports compiled during an OTIS investigation are confidential and may be disclosed only as provided in ORS 419B.035. The identity of the person reporting abuse may not be disclosed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 419B.005 to 419B.050
