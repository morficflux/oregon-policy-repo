## Full text

419-470-0010 Residential Care Agencies: What Law Applies These rules, OAR 419-470-0010 to 419-470-0180, regulate a child-caring agency licensed as a residential care agency. A residential care agency must also comply with OAR 419-400-0005 to 419-400-0310.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, ORS 418.240 & ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.205 - 418.310
