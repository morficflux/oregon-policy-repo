## Full text

419-440-0100 Foster Care Agencies: Closures of Proctor Foster Homes If a foster care agency decertifies a proctor foster home, the foster care agency must provide the proctor foster home parent or parents a written notice of the specific reasons for the action, must retain a copy of the notification in the record of the proctor foster home.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, ORS 418.005, ORS 418.248 & ORS 418.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.205 - 418.325
