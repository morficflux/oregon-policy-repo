# Confidentiality (OAR 419-210-0200)

## At a glance

OAR 419-210-0200 — Confidentiality. Chapter 419, Division 210. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

419-210-0200 Confidentiality Information gathered and records and reports compiled during an OTIS investigation are confidential and may be disclosed only as provided in ORS 419B.035. The identity of the person reporting abuse may not be disclosed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 & 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.050, ORS 443.400 to 443.455, 409.185, 409.225, 418.015, 419B.005 - 419B.050 & 419B.035
