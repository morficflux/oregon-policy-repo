## Full text

419-490-0010 Therapeutic Boarding Schools: What Law Applies These rules, OAR 419-490-0010 to 419-490-0170, regulate a child-caring agency licensed as a therapeutic boarding school. A therapeutic boarding school must also comply with OAR 419-400-0005 to 419-400-0310.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, ORS 418.005, ORS 418.327 & ORS 418.215 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.327, ORS 409.010, ORS 418.005 & ORS 418.215
