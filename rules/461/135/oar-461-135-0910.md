## Full text

461-135-0910 Unaccompanied Minor Program; REF, REFM Refugees who entered the United States under the Unaccompanied Minor Program administered by the federal Office of Refugee Resettlement are wards of the court and are in the custody of a public agency. They are not eligible for REF or REFM.
