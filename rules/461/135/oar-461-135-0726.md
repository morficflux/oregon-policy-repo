## Full text

461-135-0726 Specific Requirements; OSIPM-ICP To be eligible for OSIPM-ICP (Independent Choices Program), a person must meet criteria for In-Home Services and the Independent Choices Program contained in OAR Chapter 411, Division 030.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060
