## Full text

461-135-0580 Prepared Meals; SNAP The following SNAP recipients and their spouses may use SNAP benefits to purchase meals prepared for them and delivered to them by a nonprofit meal-delivery service authorized by the United States Department of Agriculture:

(1) People age 60 or over.

(2) Housebound people.

(3) People who have physical or other disabilities which prevent them from adequately preparing their meals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060
