# Communal (OAR 461-135-0590)

## At a glance

OAR 461-135-0590 — Communal. Chapter 461, Division 135. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

461-135-0590 Communal Dining; SNAP For SNAP, recipients age 60 or over and their spouses, and those receiving SSI and their spouses, may use SNAP benefits issued to them to purchase meals prepared especially for them at communal dining facilities authorized by the United States Department of Agriculture for that purpose.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060
