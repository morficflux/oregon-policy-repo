## Full text

461-135-1250 Closure of Post-TANF Program Notwithstanding any other administrative rule in Chapter 461, effective April 30, 2012 the Post-TANF program funding ends. Continuation of Post-TANF benefits is not authorized after April 30, 2012, regardless of whether a hearing request on Post-TANF is submitted or pending.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060, 411.070, 412.006, 412.009, 412.049 & 412.124 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060, 411.070, 412.006, 412.009, 412.049 & 412.124
