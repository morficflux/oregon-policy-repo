## Full text

461-135-0350 Eligible People; EA To be eligible for EA, a client must be a dependent child (defined in OAR 461-135-0070), a caretaker relative (defined in OAR 461-135-0070(1)(a)(B) or (C)), or the mother of an unborn child whose pregnancy has reached the calendar month before the month in which the due date falls.
