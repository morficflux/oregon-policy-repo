## Full text

461-135-0080 TANF Eligibility for Minor Parents To be eligible for TANF, a minor parent applying for benefits for his or her child must live with the minor's parent, parents or legal guardian unless it is unsafe or impractical for the minor parent to live with those individuals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060 & 412.049 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060, 412.049 & 412.084
