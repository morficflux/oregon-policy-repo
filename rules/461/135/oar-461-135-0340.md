## Full text

461-135-0340 Work Requirements; EA To be eligible for EA, an adult filing group member or a dependent child, if required to participate in the JOBS program, must not have done any of the following without good cause (see OAR 461-130-0327) in the 30 days before receiving emergency assistance benefits:

(1) Quit or refused a job.

(2) Refused a referral to a job or training.

(3) Been discharged for misconduct in accordance with the unemployment insurance compensation laws of Oregon.

(4) Reduced his or her earnings.
