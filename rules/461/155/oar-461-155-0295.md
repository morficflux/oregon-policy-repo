461-155-0295 Income Standard; SLMB, QI

(1) Eligibility for the Specified Low Income Medicare Beneficiary (SLMB) program requires income greater than 100 percent (see OAR 461-155-0290) but less than 120 percent of the federal poverty level. The adjusted income standard for SLMB is 120 percent of the 2026 federal poverty level. SLMB Adjusted Income Standard No. in Need Group ..............................
