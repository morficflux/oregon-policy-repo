## Full text

461-155-0310 Special Shelter Allowance; REF, TANF The Special Shelter Allowance is included in the REF and TANF Payment. It is an advance of the ERA refund per ORS 412.155: Special Shelter Allowance: No. in Need Group — Amount 1 — $22.35. 2 — 22.35. 3 — 21.14. 4 — 20.34. 5 — 19.53. 6 — 18.73. 7 — 17.92. 8 — 17.12. 9 — 16.32. 10 or more — 14.71.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.081, 411.085, 412.006, 412.049 & 412.155 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.081, 411.085, 412.006, 412.049 & 412.155
