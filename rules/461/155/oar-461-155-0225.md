## Full text

461-155-0225 Income Standard; REFM In the REFM program, the adjusted income standard is 200 percent of the federal poverty level, as listed in OAR 461-155-0180, based on the size of the need group (see OAR 461-110-0630).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 411.060, 411.070, 411.404 & 414.231 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.010, 411.060, 411.070, 411.404 & 414.231
