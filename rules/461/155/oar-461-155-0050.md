## Full text

461-155-0050 Payment Standard for AFC and RCF; REF, TANF For REF and TANF, the payment standard for one person in AFC or RCF is the same as the adjusted income standard for a need group with one adult per OAR 461-155-0030. The payment, minus a $30 personal allowance for clothing and personal incidentals, is for room and board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060, 411.070 & 412.049 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060, 411.070 & 412.049
