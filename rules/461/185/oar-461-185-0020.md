## Full text

461-185-0020 Authorization of T&A

(1) The client, or their guardian or conservator, authorizes establishment of a T&A account on a form prescribed by the Department.

(2) Spouses must each sign the authorization form to have their funds administered in trust.

(3) Clients not able to sign their name may make a mark. Two people must witness the signing and give their addresses.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 411.060 & 411.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.050, 411.060 & 411.070
