# Radiation (OAR 461-145-0415)

## At a glance

## Full text

461-145-0415 Radiation Exposure Compensation Act Radiation Exposure Compensation Act payments are issued to compensate individuals for injuries or deaths resulting from exposure to radiation from nuclear testing or uranium mining. For all programs, these payments are excluded.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.816 & 412.049 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060, 411.700, 411.816, 414.042 & 412.049
