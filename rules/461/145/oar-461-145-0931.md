## Full text

461-145-0931 Additional Exclusions for Farming Costs; SNAP In the SNAP program, if gross self-employment income from farming is less than the costs calculated in accordance with OAR 461-145-0920, and the client receives or anticipates receiving annual gross farm income of $1,000 or more, then farming-related costs that exceed self-employment income from farming are allowed as an exclusion from nonfarm self-employment income, other earned income, and unearned income.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060, 418.040 & 411.816 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060 & 411.816

History: AFS 2-1999, f. 3-26-99, cert. ef. 4-1-99
