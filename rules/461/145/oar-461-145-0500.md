## Full text

461-145-0500 Social Secuirty Death Benefit Money remaining from Social Security death benefits after the payment of burial costs is treated as lump-sum income (see OAR 461-140-0120).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.816, 414.042 & 412.049 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.700, 411.816, 414.042 & 412.049
