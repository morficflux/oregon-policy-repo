## Full text

461-180-0044 Effective Dates; Income Cap Trust The effective date for an income cap trust that makes a client income-eligible for long term care or home and community-based care (see OAR 461-001-0030) under 461-135-0750 and 461-145-0540(9)(c) is the first day of the month in which the trust document is signed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060
