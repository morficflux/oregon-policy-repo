## Full text

461-105-0180 Nondiscrimination in Determining Eligibility Determine eligibility without discrimination on the basis of race, color, sex, national origin, disability, political beliefs, age, or religious creed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060
