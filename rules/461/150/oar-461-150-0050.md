## Full text

461-150-0050 Prospective Eligibility and Budgeting; OSIP, OSIPM, and QMB In the OSIP, OSIPM, and all QMB programs, the Department uses prospective eligibility (see OAR 461-001-0000) and budgeting (see OAR 461-001-0000) as follows:

(1) In the OSIP, OSIPM (except OSIPM-ICP), and all QMB programs: (a) For the initial month (see OAR 461-001-0000): (A) When a new source of income begins in the initial month , only income received or expected to be received in that month is counted. (B) Except as provided in paragraph (A) of this subsection, the Department converts and averages prospective income in accordance with OAR 461-150-0070 and OAR 461-150-0080. (b) For each ongoing month (see OAR 461-001-0000), the Department converts and averages prospective income in accordance with OAR 461-150-0070 and OAR 461-150-0080.

(2) In OSIPM-ICP, the budget month (see OAR 461-001-0000) is the initial month of eligibility.
