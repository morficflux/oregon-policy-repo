## Full text

461-165-0082 SNAP Cash-Out SNAP households in Clackamas, Columbia, Multnomah, and Washington counties receive SNAP benefits in cash if all persons in the filing group are 65 years of age or older or are eligible to receive SSI benefits under Title XVI of the Social Security Act.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.816 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.816

History: SSP 24-2004, f. 12-30-04, cert. ef. 1-1-05
