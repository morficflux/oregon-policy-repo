## Full text

461-160-0160 Earned Income Deduction; REF, REFM, and TANF In the REF, REFM, and TANF programs, the earned income deduction authorized in this division of rules is allowed for each person in the financial group who has earned income. The earned income deduction is 50 percent of the individual's gross earned income including self-employment income.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050, 411.060 & 412.049 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.010, 411.060 & 412.049
