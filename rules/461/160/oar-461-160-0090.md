## Full text

461-160-0090 Employment and Independence Expenses; OSIP-EPD and OSIPM-EPD In the OSIP-EPD and OSIPM-EPD programs, an employment and independence expense (see OAR 461-001-0035) must be approved by the branch office prior to its use as a deduction from countable income (see OAR 461-140-0010).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.060, 411.070 & 414.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.060, 411.070 & 414.042
