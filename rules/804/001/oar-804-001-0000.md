## Full text

804-001-0000 Notice Rule Before adoption, amendment or repeal of any permanent rule, the State Landscape Architect Board shall give notice of the intended rulemaking action:

(1) In the Secretary of State’s Bulletin referred to in ORS 183.360 at least 21 days prior to the effective date of the intended rulemaking action.

(2) By furnishing to persons on the State Landscape Architect Board notice list established under ORS 183.335(8) notice of the intended rulemaking action at least 28 days before the effective date of the intended rulemaking action.

(3) By furnishing a copy of the notice to the: (a) Oregon Chapter of the American Society of Landscape Architects; (b) Associated Press.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 & 670.310(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.335
