## Full text

804-001-0015 Election of Officers The Board may elect a Chair, Vice Chair and Treasurer once a year. The new Chair, Vice-Chair and Treasurer’s terms will begin at the end of the meeting at which they are elected.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, ORS 671.415 & ORS 671.459 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 670.300
