## Full text

804-020-0065 Revoked Registration Individuals whose registrations have been revoked must reapply and pass an appropriate examination, as determined by the Board, and establish to the Board’s satisfaction having met all requirements of ORS 671.425 to be re-registered.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 671 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.425
