## Full text

804-020-0040 Examination Grading The Board shall accept the recommended grading procedures and minimum passing scores for all sections of the Landscape Architect Registration Examination (LARE) as established by the Council of Landscape Architectural Registration Boards (CLARB).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 671. Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.335
