# Fraudulent Information (OAR 137-076-0055)

## At a glance

## Full text

137-076-0055 Fraudulent Information Any applicant who misrepresents information in an application for crime victims’ compensation or in any other materials requested by the Department, forfeits any eligibility for compensation and the applicant’s application for compensation shall be denied with prejudice.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 147.205(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 147.255
