## Full text

137-076-0065 Negotiated Settlements If the victim is successful in a claim or legal action against the assailant or another party and is able to recover monetary damages, the Department shall be subrogated for the full amount of payments made by the Department. However, the Department may, at its sole discretion, waive all or part of its recovery, if it is determined to be in the best interests of the Department and the victim.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 147.205(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 147.345
