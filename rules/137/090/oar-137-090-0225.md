## Full text

137-090-0225 Transition Procedures The handling of “hard-copy” criminal intelligence information submitted to the CIU prior to the effective date of these amended rules shall be governed by the provisions of former OAR 137, division 90, adopted in September 1989. Once the information has been entered into the electronic database in compliance with these amended rules, adopted on August 8, 2000, the hard copy files may be purged and destroyed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180 & 357.805 et seq. Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 180.610, 181.575 & 357.805 et seq.

History: DOJ 11-2000, f. & cert. ef. 8-9-00
