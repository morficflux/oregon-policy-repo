## Full text

137-090-0090 Information Input Information to be stored in the CIU’s criminal intelligence file must first undergo a review for relevancy and an evaluation for source reliability and information validity prior to filing:

(1) Relevancy Review: Incoming information is reviewed by the CIUS, or a designee of the Chief Counsel, to determine its relevancy to the CIU’s mission.

(2) Source Reliability: The term, source, relates to the individual, group, or organization providing the information to the CIU. Source reliability will be determined according to the criteria set forth in Table 1. [Table not included. See ED. NOTE.]

(3) Information Validity: The term, information, relates to written, oral, and/or pictorial materials provided to the CIU by the individual, group, or organization. Information validity will be determined according to the criteria set forth in Table 2. [Table not included. See ED. NOTE.] [ED. NOTE: Tables referenced are available from the agency.]
