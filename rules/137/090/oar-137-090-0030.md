## Full text

137-090-0030 Criminal Intelligence Unit Mission The mission of the Criminal Intelligence Unit is to provide the Department of Justice and Oregon law enforcement agencies with a statewide criminal information base and analyses which meets their needs to protect the public and suppress criminal activity.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 180.610(2), 180.610(3) & 180.610(4)

History: JD 2-1989, f. & cert. ef. 9-13-89
