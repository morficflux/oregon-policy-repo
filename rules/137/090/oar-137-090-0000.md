# Purpose (OAR 137-090-0000)

## Full text

137-090-0000 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide standards, policies and procedures for the operation of the Criminal Intelligence Unit (CIU) of the Organized Crime Section, and to ensure compliance with 28 CFR Part 23.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 180.610(2), 180.610(3), 180.610(4) & 28 CFR Part 23
