## Full text

137-055-2120 Rules for Contested Case Hearings in the Oregon Child Support Program Contested case hearings for the Oregon Child Support Program are conducted in accordance with the Attorney General's Model Rules at OAR 137-003-0501 through 137-003-0700 and with 137-055-2120 through 137-055-2180. The hearings are not open to the public and are closed to non-participants, except the administrative law judge may permit non-participants to attend subject to the parties' consent.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180.345 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 180.345
