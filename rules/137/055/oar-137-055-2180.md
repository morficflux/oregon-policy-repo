## Full text

137-055-2180 Reconsideration and Rehearing A petition for reconsideration or rehearing authorized by OAR 137-003-0675 must be filed with the administrative law judge who signed the final order. An administrative law judge will rule on the petition and take appropriate action if the petition is allowed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180.345 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 180.345
