## Full text

137-085-0090 Training Records Each qualifying victim services program shall maintain a roster of advocates who have completed the minimum training described required by OAR 137-085-0080 and who are thus “certified advocates.”

Statutory/Other Authority: 2015 HB 3476 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2015 HB 3476
