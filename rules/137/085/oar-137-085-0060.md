## Full text

137-085-0060 Purpose These rules set out the guidelines for minimum training required of persons providing services to victims of domestic violence, sexual assault and stalking in order to be certified advocates for purposes of the evidentiary privilege and confidentiality requirements of HB 3476.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2015 HB 3476 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2015 HB 3476
