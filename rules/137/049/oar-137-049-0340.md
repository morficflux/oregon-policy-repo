## Full text

137-049-0340 Late Bids, Late Withdrawals and Late Modifications Any Offer received after Closing is late. An Offeror's request for withdrawal or modification of an Offer received after Closing is late. A Contracting Agency shall not consider late Offers, withdrawals or modifications except as permitted in OAR 137-049-0350 or 137-049-0390.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279C.365, 279C.375 & 279C.395
