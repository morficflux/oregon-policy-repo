# Notice of (OAR 137-080-0015)

## Full text

137-080-0015 Notice of Establishment of an Escrow Account In the case where a victim of crime is deceased, the notice to be published by the Department for five years from the establishment of an escrow account, under Section 3, Chapter 552, Oregon Laws 1985, shall advise dependents of such victims of the escrow moneys’ availability to satisfy judgments for damages suffered as a result of the crime.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 147 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 147.275

History: JD 1-1986, f. & ef. 1-15-86 JD 3-1985(Temp), f. & ef. 9-20-85
