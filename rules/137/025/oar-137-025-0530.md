## Full text

137-025-0530 Effective Dates

(1) OAR 137-025-0010 is repealed, effective January 1, 1988.

(2) OAR 137-025-0020 to 137-025-0030, 137-025-0050 to 137-025-0110, 137-025-0190 to 137-025-0230 and 137-025-0330 shall take effect on November 1, 1987.

(3) OAR 137-025-0040, 137-025-0120 to 137-025-0180 and 137-025-0240 to 137-025-0320 shall take effect on January 1, 1988.

(4) The amended fee schedule, to take effect on January 1, 2007, shall apply as follows: (a) For licensees/permit application fees, for licensees/permits which expire after December 31, 2007; and (b) For report fees, for licensee reporting periods ending after December 31, 2006.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 464 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 464.250(1)
