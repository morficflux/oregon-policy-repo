## Full text

137-025-0100 Notice of Bingo Activities Prior to conducting bingo operations, each licensee shall file with the Department a schedule of bingo activities on a form provided by the Department. The form shall list the regular sessions conducted by the licensee, specifying the days and hours of the week. The licensee shall not conduct operations except during the times on file with the Department. A licensee desiring to change its scheduled bingo activities shall file an amended schedule with the Department on a form prescribed by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 464 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 464.250
