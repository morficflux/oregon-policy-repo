## Full text

137-025-0150 Bingo Fees

(1) All annual and quarterly bingo reports filed with the Department shall be accompanied by a fee, made payable to the Department of Justice, as follows: (a) Class D license — $20; (b) Class C license — A fee of $20 plus 0.5 of 1 percent of the bingo handle in excess of $20,000; (c) Class B license — A fee of 0.5 of 1 percent of the bingo handle up to $75,000 and 1 percent of the bingo handle in excess of $75,000; (d) Class A license — A fee of 1.20 percent of the bingo handle up to $3,000,000 and 1 percent of the bingo handle in excess of $3,000,000.

(2) A delinquency fee of $20 or 1 percent of the fee described above, whichever is greater, shall be paid by the licensee if the report or regular fee is not delivered to the Department by the due date. The minimum delinquency fee shall increase to $50 after 60 days from the due date of the report.

(3) When the filing date for reports and fees falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday, the due date is the next business day following the Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday.
