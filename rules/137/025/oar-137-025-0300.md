## Full text

137-025-0300 Raffle Prize Limits

(1) The total cash prize(s) offered or awarded in a raffle shall not exceed $5,000. Gift cards and gift certificates may exceed $5,000 as long as the holder cannot redeem the face value for cash.

(2) No prize shall be offered or awarded with a retail market value in excess of $75,000 and the cumulative retail value of all prizes offered or awarded at a raffle shall not exceed $100,000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 464.250 Statutes/Other Implemented: 1997, HB 2358 2017 & HB 3009
