## Full text

137-025-0520 Model APA Rules The Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, effective September 15, 1997, are by this reference adopted as the rules and procedures for carrying out ORS 167.117, 167.118 and ORS Chapter 464, except as otherwise specifically provided herein. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the Department of Justice.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 914 Statutes/Other Implemented: HB 3009 & 1997
