## Full text

137-045-0020 Mixed Contracts A mixed Public Contract requires the contractor to render certain services and provide the Agency with other kinds of services, goods or products. Classification of a mixed Public Contract as a Personal Services Contract, Architectural and Engineering Services Contract, Information Technology Contract, or other kind of Public Contract is determined by the mixed Public Contract's predominant purpose. A mixed Public Contract's predominant purpose is determined by whether the majority of the amounts paid or received under the mixed Public Contract will be for a particular kind of service (personal, architectural, engineering, land surveying or related services, Information Technology, or other kinds of service) or for the acquisition of goods or products.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 291.047(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 291.045, 291.047 & OL 1997 & Ch. 869
