## Full text

137-009-0140 Methods for Selecting Contractors

(1) The Department will use one of the following methods to select a Contractor: (a) The Department may select a Contractor from a list of individuals or entities established for a Designated Practice Area as set forth in OAR 137-009-0145. (b) The Department may select a Contractor from a group of proposers within a Designated Practice Area as set forth in OAR 137-009-0147. (c) The Department may select a Contractor from a group of proposers to a specific matter Solicitation as set forth in OAR 137-009-0150. (d) The Department may select a Contractor through direct negotiation as set forth in OAR 137-009-0160.

(2) Nothing in this section shall prevent the Department from entering into an amendment to a contract for legal services according to its terms.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180.140(5), 183.310(9) & 279A.025(2)(j) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 180.140(5) & 279A.025(2)(j)
