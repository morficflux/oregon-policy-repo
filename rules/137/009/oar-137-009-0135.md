## Full text

137-009-0135 Policy The policy of the Department is to select Contractors in an expeditious and efficient manner that is consistent with the goal of delivering highly competent legal services at the Lowest Overall Cost to the State of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180.140(5), 183.310(9) & 279A.025(2)(j) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 180.140(5) & 279A.025(2)(j)
