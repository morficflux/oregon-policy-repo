## Full text

137-009-0125 Purpose The Department may contract for the services of special legal assistants or private counsel to provide legal services otherwise required by law to be performed by the Attorney General. These rules specify the screening and selection procedures the Department will use to select individuals or entities to perform such services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180.140(5), 183.310(9) & 279A.025(2)(j) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 180.140(5) & 279A.025(2)(j)
