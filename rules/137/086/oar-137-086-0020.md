# Advisory Council (OAR 137-086-0020)

## At a glance

## Full text

137-086-0020 Advisory Council An Advisory Council of no fewer than fifteen and no more than twenty members shall be selected and serve terms in accordance with ORS 147.471 and with the by-laws established by the Advisory Council. Copies of the by-laws and other open records are available by contacting the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 147.465(3) & 2001 OL Ch. 870 (HB 2918) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 147.450 - 147.471 & 2001 OL Ch. 870 (HB 2918)
