## Full text

137-010-0040 Place of Filing Registration and annual reports required under ORS Chapter 128 shall be submitted to the Charitable Activities Section, Oregon Department of Justice, 1515 S.W. 5th, Suite 410, Portland, Oregon 97201-5451.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 128.876 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 128.650, 128.660, 128.670, 128.802, 128.804, 128.807, 128.812, 128.821, 128.826 & 128.841
