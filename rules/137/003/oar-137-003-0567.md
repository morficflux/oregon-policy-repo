## Full text

137-003-0567 Discovery in Contested Case Hearing — Standard Any discovery request must be reasonably likely to produce information that is generally relevant and necessary to the case, or is likely to facilitate resolution of the case.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341 & 183.630
