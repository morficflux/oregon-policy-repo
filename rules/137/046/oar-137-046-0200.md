## Full text

137-046-0200 Notice to Governor’s Policy Advisor for Economic and Business Equity In accordance with ORS 200.035, State Contracting Agencies shall provide timely notice of all Procurements and Contract Awards to the Governor’s Policy Advisor for Economic and Business Equity if the estimated Contract Price exceeds $10,000.
