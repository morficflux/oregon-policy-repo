137-046-0500 Repealed Rules As required by Or Laws 2003, Chapter 794, Section 334, OAR 137-030-0000, 137-030-0005, 137-030-0006, 137-030-0008, 137-030-0010, 137-030-0011, 137-030-0012, 137-030-0013, 137-030-0014, 137-030-0015, 137-030-0020, 137-030-0030, 137-030-0035, 137-030-0040,137-030-0050, 137-030-0055, 137-030-0060, 137-030-0065, 137-030-0070, 137-030-0075, 137-030-0080,137-030-0085, 137-030-0090, 137-030-0095, 137-030-0100, 137-030-0102, 137-030-0104, 137-030-0105, 137-030-0110, 137-030-0115, 137-030-0120, 137-030-0125, 137-030-0130, 137-030-0135, 137-030-0140, 137-030-0145, 137-030-0155, 137-035-0000, 137-035-0010, 137-035-0020, 137-035-0030, 137-035-0040,137-035-0050, 137-035-0060, 137-035-0065, 137-035-0070, 137-035-0080, 137-040-0000, 137-040-0005,137-040-0010, 137-040-0015, 137-040-0017, 137-040-0020, 137-040-0021, 137-040-0025, 137-040-0030,137-040-0031, 137-040-0035, 137-040-0045, 137-040-0500, 137-040-0510, 137-040-0520, 137-040-0530, 137-040-0540, 137-040-0550, 137-040-0560, 137-040-0565, 137-040-0570, 137-040-0590 are repealed effective March 1, 2005.
