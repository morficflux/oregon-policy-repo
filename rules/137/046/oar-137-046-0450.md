## Full text

137-046-0450 Interstate Cooperative Procurements A Contracting Agency that chooses to participate in, sponsor, conduct or administer an Interstate Cooperative Procurement may do so only in accordance with ORS 279A.220.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065 & 279A.220
