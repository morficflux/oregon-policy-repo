## Full text

137-046-0430 Permissive Cooperative Procurements A Contracting Agency that chooses to participate in, sponsor, conduct or administer a Permissive Cooperative Procurement may do so only in accordance with ORS 279A.215.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065 & 279A.215

History: DOJ 11-2004, f. 9-1-04, cert. ef. 3-1-05
