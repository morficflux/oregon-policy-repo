## Full text

137-110-0640 Confidentiality No videotaping, transcription or other recording of resolution conferences is permitted except by written agreement of the parties and the facilitator.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2013 OL Ch. 304, Sec. 6(1)(f) & 6(1)(g) Statutes/Other Implemented: 2013 OL Ch. 304, Sec. 3(5), 6(1)(f) & 6(1)(g)
