## Full text

137-047-0460 Late Offers, Late Withdrawals and Late Modifications Any Offer received after Closing is late. An Offeror's request for withdrawal or modification of an Offer received after Closing is late. An Agency shall not consider late Offers, withdrawals or modifications except as permitted in OAR 137-047-0470 or 137-047-0261.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 & 279B.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279B.055
