## Full text

137-047-0750 Judicial Review of Other Violations Any violation of ORS Chapter 279A or 279B by a Contracting Agency for which no judicial remedy is otherwise provided in the Public Contracting Code is subject to judicial review as set forth in 279B.420.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279B.420

History: DOJ 11-2004, f. 9-1-04, cert. ef. 3-1-05
