## Full text

137-047-0810 Termination of Price Agreements A Contracting Agency may terminate a Price Agreement as follows:

(1) As permitted by the Price Agreement;

(2) If the circumstances set forth in ORS 279B.140(2) exist; or

(3) As permitted by applicable law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065 & ORS 279B.140
