## Full text

137-047-0280 Emergency Procurements A Contracting Agency may Award a Contract as an Emergency Procurement pursuant to the requirements of ORS 279B.080. When an Emergency Procurement is authorized, the Procurement shall be made with competition that is reasonable and appropriate under the circumstances. However, for emergency Procurement of construction services, see 279B.080(2).
