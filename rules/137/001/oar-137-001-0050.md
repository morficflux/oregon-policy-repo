# Agency Rulemaking Action (OAR 137-001-0050)

## At a glance

## Full text

137-001-0050 Agency Rulemaking Action At the conclusion of the hearing, or after receipt of the presiding officer’s requested report and recommendation, if any, the agency may adopt, amend, or repeal rules covered by the notice of intended action. The agency shall fully consider all written and oral submissions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.335(3)
