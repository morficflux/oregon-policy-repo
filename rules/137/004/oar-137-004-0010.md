## Full text

137-004-0010 Unacceptable Conduct A presiding officer may expel a person from an agency proceeding if that person engages in conduct that disrupts the proceeding.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341(1)
