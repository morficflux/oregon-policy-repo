## Full text

858-010-0002 Guidelines for Supervising Technicians A licensee may delegate administration and scoring of tests to technicians as provided in ORS 675.010(4) if the licensee ensures the technicians are adequately trained to administer and score the specific test being used; and ensures that the technicians maintain standards for the testing environment and testing administration as set forth in the American Psychological Association Standards for Educational and Psychological Tests (1999) and Ethical Principles for Psychologists (2002).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.010 - 675.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.010
