## Full text

858-010-0039 Application for Independent Status — Psychologist Associate A licensed psychologist associate may apply to the Board for approval to function as an independent psychologist associate. Independent status will be granted only after at least three years of work as a licensed psychologist associate or a psychologist at a demonstrated high level of professional proficiency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.065 & 675.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.065 & 675.110
