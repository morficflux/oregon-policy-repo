## Full text

858-020-0105 Board’s Responsibility to the Public The Board may inform the public and the profession as to the ethics and nature of psychological activities, including the licensure law, what may constitute violations of the law, and how inquiry about possible violations or complaints may appropriately be made. The Board will refrain from any statement or action which might be construed as an official opinion or legal advice.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.110
