## Full text

858-040-0025 Continuing Education Reporting

(1) Before a license will be renewed under ORS 675.110, a licensee must submit a Renewal Notice and Reporting Affidavit.

(2) A copy of the Renewal Notice and Reporting Affidavit is available on the Board’s website.

(3) Renewal Notice and Reporting Affidavits must be postmarked by the due date printed on the notice. Burden of proof of mailing is on the licensee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.110(14)
