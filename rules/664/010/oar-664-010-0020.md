## Full text

664-010-0020 Penalties Penalty for delaying transmittal of assessment moneys (ORS 576.355) "In addition to the penalties prescribed in 576.991, any person who delays transmittal of funds beyond the time set by a Commission shall pay a penalty of 10 percent of the amount due and shall also pay one and one-half percent interest per month on the unpaid balance of the assessment."

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 & 2003 OL Ch. 604 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576 & 2003 OL Ch. 604
