# Purpose (OAR 644-005-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

644-005-0005 Purpose The Oregon Sheep Commission (OSC) occasionally requires the services of an outside party to accomplish all or part of a project. The purpose of these rules is to specify the screening and selection procedures which will be used for personal service contracts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576

History: SC 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 2-27-91
