## Full text

644-010-0025 Effective Dates of Assessment The assessment to be withheld and paid by a handler, processor, distributor, auction market, or other person engaged in handling or marketing sheep shall begin with sheep purchased or sold on or after July 1, 2014.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576
