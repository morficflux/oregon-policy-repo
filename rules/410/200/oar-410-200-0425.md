## Full text

410-200-0425 Specific Requirements; MAGI Pregnant Woman In addition to eligibility requirements applicable to the MAGI Pregnant Woman program in other rules in chapter 410 division 200, this rule describes specific eligibility requirements for the MAGI Pregnant Woman program.

(1) To be eligible for the MAGI Pregnant Woman program, an individual must be pregnant at least one day in the month for which eligibility is being determined, including months they are being evaluated for Retroactive Medical (OAR 410-200-0130).

(2) To be eligible for the MAGI Pregnant Woman program, an individual must: (a) Have household income that is at or below 185 percent of the federal poverty level (OAR 410-200-0315); or (b) Be eligible for protected eligibility according to the policy described in OAR 410-200-0135.

(3) To be eligible for the MAGI Pregnant Woman program, an individual must not be receiving Supplemental Security Income (SSI).
