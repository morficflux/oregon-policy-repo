## Full text

410-200-0405 Specific Requirements; Substitute Care In addition to eligibility requirements applicable to the Substitute Care program in other rules in chapter 410 division 200, this rule describes specific eligibility requirements for the Substitute Care program, effective 10/01/13.

(1) To be eligible for Substitute Care, an individual shall be under the age of 21 and live in a Behavioral Rehabilitation Services (BRS) facility or an intermediate Psychiatric Residential Treatment Facility (PRTF) for which a public agency of Oregon is assuming at least partial financial responsibility, including those placed in an intermediate psychiatric care facility by the Oregon Youth Authority.

(2) While living in an intermediate psychiatric care facility, an individual’s EDG consists of the individual only.

(3) There is no income test for Substitute Care.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.402, 411.404 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.400, 411.402, 411.404, 411.406, 413.032, 413.038, 414.025, 414.231 & 414.706
