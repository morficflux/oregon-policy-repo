## Full text

410-127-0065 Signature Requirements

(1) Physicians shall sign for services they order. This signature shall be handwritten or electronic, and it must be in the client's medical record.

(2) The ordering physician shall ensure the authenticity of the signature.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
