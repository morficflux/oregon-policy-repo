## Full text

410-125-0095 Hospitals Providing Specialized Inpatient Services

(1) Some hospitals provide specific highly specialized in patient services by arrangement with the Division.

(2) Reimbursement is made according to the terms of a contract between the Division and the hospital.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
