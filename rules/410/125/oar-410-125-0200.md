# Time Limitation for Submission of Claims (OAR 410-125-0200)

## At a glance

OAR 410-125-0200 — Time Limitation for Submission of Claims. Chapter 410, Division 125. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

410-125-0200 Time Limitation for Submission of Claims Division of Medical Assistance Programs (Division) will accept a claim up to 12 months after the date of service. The date of discharge is the date of service for an inpatient hospital claim.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
