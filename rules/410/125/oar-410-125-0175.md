## Full text

410-125-0175 Hospitals Providing Specialized Outpatient Services Some hospitals provide specific highly specialized outpatient services by arrangement with the Division. Reimbursement is made according to the terms of a written agreement or contract.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
