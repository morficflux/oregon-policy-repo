410-147-0180 Vaccines for Children (VFC) Program

(1) The Division of Medical Assistance Programs (Division) will reimburse Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs) and Rural Health Clinics (RHCs) for the administration of vaccines to eligible clients.

(2) The VFC program supplies federally purchased free vaccines for immunizing eligible client’s ages 0 through 18 at no cost to participating health care providers. For more information on how to enroll in the VFC program, contact the Oregon Health Authority (Authority) Immunization Program. Refer to the FQHC and RHC Supplemental Information for instructions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 414.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
