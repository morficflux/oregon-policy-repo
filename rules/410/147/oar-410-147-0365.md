## Full text

410-147-0365 Rural Health Clinic Obstetrics Care Delivery Procedures Reimbursement Reimbursement for obstetric delivery procedures by the Division of Medical Assistance Programs (Division) to eligible Medicare-certified Independent RHCs will be according to the physician fee schedule and outside of the Prospective Payment System (PPS).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 414.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
