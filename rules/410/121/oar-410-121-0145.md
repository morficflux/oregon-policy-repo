## Full text

410-121-0145 Prescription Requirements

(1) Division of Medical Assistance Programs (Division) will make payment for covered drugs supplied on drug order or prescription of a licensed practitioner and dispensed by a pharmacist. Dispensings include new prescriptions, refills of existing prescriptions, and over-the-counter (OTC) medications.

(2) Each drug order or prescription filled for a Division client must: (a) Be retained in the pharmacy's file at the pharmacy’s place of business; and, (b) All drug orders or prescriptions must comply with the Oregon State Board of Pharmacy rules and regulations as listed in OAR 855 Division 041.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 414.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
