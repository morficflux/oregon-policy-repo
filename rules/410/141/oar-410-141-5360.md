## Full text

410-141-5360 CCO INSOLVENCY AND DISSOLUTION: Access to Funds and Transition of Members and Records

(1) CCOs shall provide the Authority access to Restricted Reserve Funds if insolvency occurs.

(2) CCOs shall have written policies and procedures to ensure that if insolvency occurs, Members and related clinical records are transitioned to other CCOs or providers with minimal disruption.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042, 414.572, 414.591, 414.605, 415.013 & 415.101-415.430 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.570-414.686 & 415.001-415.430
