## Full text

410-141-3620 MCE Assessment: Determining the Date Filed

(1) For the purposes of these rules, any reports, requests, appeals, payments, or other response by the MCE must be either: (a) Received by the Authority before the close of business on the date due; or (b) If mailed, postmarked before midnight of the due date.

(2) When the due date falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday, the response is due on the next business day.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 414.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065

History: DMAP 55-2019, adopt filed 12/17/2019, effective 01/01/2020
