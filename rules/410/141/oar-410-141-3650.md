## Full text

410-141-3650 MCE Assessment: Final Order of Payment The Authority shall issue a final order of payment for deficiencies or penalties when:

(1) The MCE did not make a timely request for a hearing;

(2) Any part of the deficiency or penalty was upheld after a hearing;

(3) Upon agreement of the MCE and the Authority.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & ORS 414.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065 & 2017 HB 2391
