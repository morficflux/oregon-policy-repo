## Full text

410-146-0075 Client Copayments

(1) AI/AN clients who are members of a federally recognized Indian Tribe or Tribal Organization and receive Medicaid-covered services rendered through an IHCP are exempt from copayments. Refer to OAR 410-120-1230 Client Copayment.

(2) IHCPs may not charge copayments to eligible non-AI/AN Division clients receiving care at their facility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & ORS 414.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
