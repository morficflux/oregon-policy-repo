# Purpose (OAR 410-150-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

410-150-0005 Purpose The Administrative Examination and Report rules are provided for use in conjunction with the General Rules of the Division of Medical Assistance Programs (DMAP) to assist providers in completing examinations requested and in preparing claims for administrative evaluations and reports. Division 150 rules do not apply to managed care plans.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065

History: DMAP 41-2019, adopt filed 10/01/2019, effective 12/01/2019
