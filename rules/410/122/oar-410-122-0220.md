## Full text

410-122-0220 Pacemaker Monitor

(1) E0610 — Pacemaker monitor, self-contained, checks battery depletion, includes audible and visible check systems: (a) The Division will purchase; (b) Also covered for payment by the Division when client is a resident of a nursing facility.

(2) E0615 — Pacemaker monitor, self-contained, checks battery depletion and other pacemaker components, includes digital/visible check systems: (a) The Division will purchase; (b) Also covered for payment by the Division when client is a resident of a nursing facility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 414.065
