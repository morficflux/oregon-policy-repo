## Full text

410-050-0770 Determining the Date Filed

(1) For the purposes of these rules, any reports, requests, appeals, payments, or other response by the hospital shall be received by the Authority either: (a) Before the close of business on the date due; or (b) If mailed, postmarked before midnight of the due date.

(2) When the due date falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or a legal holiday, the date filed is on the next business day following the Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday.
