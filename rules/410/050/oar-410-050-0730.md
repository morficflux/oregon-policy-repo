## Full text

410-050-0730 Entities Subject to the Hospital Assessment Each hospital in Oregon is subject to the hospital assessment except:

(1) Hospitals operated by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs;

(2) Public academic medical centers;

(3) Specialty Psychiatric Hospitals;

(4) Long Term Acute Care Hospitals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042, 410.070 & 411.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: HB 2010 (2025)
