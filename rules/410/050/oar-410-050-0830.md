## Full text

410-050-0830 Hearing Process

(1) Any hospital that receives a notice of proposed action may request a contested case hearing under ORS 183.

(2) The hospital may request a hearing by submitting a written request within 20 days of the date of the notice of proposed action.

(3) Prior to the hearing, the hospital shall meet with the Authority for an informal conference: (a) The informal conference may be used to negotiate a written settlement agreement. (b) If the settlement agreement includes a reduction or waiver of penalties, the agreement shall be approved and signed by the Director.

(4) Except as provided in section

(5) of this rule, if the case proceeds to a hearing, the administrative law judge shall issue a proposed order. The Authority shall issue a final order.

(5) Nothing in this section shall preclude the Authority and the hospital from agreeing to informal disposition of the contested case at any time, consistent with ORS 183.415(5).
