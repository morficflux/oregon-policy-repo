## Full text

255-075-0040 Compelling of Witnesses: Contempt The Sanction Authority or Hearings Officer or party requesting a subpoena, may seek contempt proceedings in the circuit court of any county against any person refusing to honor the subpoena.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.050, 144.140 & 144.343 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.096, 144.098, 144.102, 144.106, 144.108, 144.346 & Ch. 525 OL 1997 (Enrolled SB 156)
