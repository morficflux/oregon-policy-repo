# Procedure (OAR 255-020-0020)

## At a glance

## Full text

255-020-0020 Procedure A business meeting under this division is a public meeting as defined in ORS 192.610 to 192.690. Adequate public notice, public access, and public minutes are required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.610 - 192.690 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.610 - 192.690
