## Full text

255-015-0003 Oral Record of Hearing A recording of the oral proceedings of any hearing shall be kept by the Board for at least four years.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.050 & ORS 144.140 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.130, ORS 144.185 & ORS 192.311 - 192.431
