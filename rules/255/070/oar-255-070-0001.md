255-070-0001 Conditions Not Limited by Exhibit J

(1) The Board may order parole conditions pursuant to OAR 255-070-0015.

(2) The Board shall approve post-prison supervision conditions pursuant to OAR 213-011-0001.

(3) Conditions of parole and post-prison supervision are not limited to those shown in Exhibit J. (Attached) [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
