## Full text

255-025-0005 Date Prison Term Starts to Run

(1) Generally, the date on which the inmate is delivered to custody of the Department of Corrections for the purpose of serving the sentence is the commencement date for the prison term.

(2) In the case of additional consecutive sentences, the board shall rescind the existing parole release date which shall become the commencement date for the new prison term unless the board unsums the consecutive ranges pursuant to OAR 255-035-0022. Upon unsumming the ranges, the board shall apply section three of this rule.

(3) In the case of additional concurrent sentences, the sentencing date shall be the commencement date for the new prison term.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.320, 137.370, 144.050, 144.079 & 144.783 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.320, 137.370, 144.050, 144.079 & 144.783
