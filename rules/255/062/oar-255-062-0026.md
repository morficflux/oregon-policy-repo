## Full text

255-062-0026 Interim Hearing Finding If the Board finds, based upon a properly-submitted request for an interim hearing, that there is reasonable cause to believe that the inmate may be granted a change in the terms of confinement or a firm release date, the Board shall conduct a hearing as soon as is reasonably convenient. An interim hearing may be granted by a majority of the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.054, 144.228, 144.232, 163.105, 163.115 & 2009 OL Ch. 660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.228, 144.232, 163.105, 163.115 & 2009 OL Ch 660
