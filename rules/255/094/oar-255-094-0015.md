## Full text

255-094-0015 Return to Active Supervision

(1) An offender is subject to arrest for violations of conditions of supervision while on either active or inactive supervision.

(2) The Releasing Authority may return an offender to active supervision for the remainder of the supervision period set by the sentencing court or set by law when the Releasing Authority receives a report from the supervising agency showing good cause why the inactive status is no longer in the offender's best interest or the best interest or safety of the community.

(3) If the supervising agency has good cause to return an offender to active supervision, and the whereabouts of the offender are unknown, the supervising agency may request a warrant from the Releasing Authority.

(4) When an offender is returned to active supervision status, all general conditions plus all previously imposed special conditions shall be in effect.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.085 & SB 1145 & OL 1995 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.085 & SB 1145 & OL 1995
