## Full text

255-010-0005 Membership The Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision shall consist of those members appointed by the Governor, pursuant to ORS 144.005.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.005 & 144.025
