## Full text

255-040-0026 Effect of Minimum Terms on Reductions

(1) If the inmate has a judicial minimum sentence greater than 36 months, which the Board previously upheld pursuant to ORS 144.110 or 163.115, the Board must overturn the minimum before it can grant a reduction from the previously established term.

(2) If the inmate has a mandatory minimum sentence pursuant to ORS 161.610, the Board cannot grant a reduction below the mandatory minimum sentence and the statutory reduction for good time, except as provided in OAR 255-040-0028.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.122, 144.126, 144.110, 163.115 & 161.610 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.110, 144.122, 144.126 & 163.115
