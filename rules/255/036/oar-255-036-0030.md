## Full text

255-036-0030 Parole Supervision A dangerous offender released to parole prior to the sentence expiration shall serve the remainder of the sentence term imposed under ORS 161.725 and 161.735 on parole. The inmate shall serve at least three years of supervised parole.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL 1993, Ch. 680 & Section 1(b) (SB 139) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.096, 144.098, 144.102, 144.106, 144.108, 144.346 & Ch. 51 & OL 1997 (Enrolled SB 156)
