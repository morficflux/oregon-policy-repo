## Full text

255-032-0010 Minimum Period of Confinement Pursuant to ORS 163.105 or 163.115

(1) The minimum period of confinement for an adult person convicted of Aggravated Murder as defined by ORS 163.105(1) shall be thirty

(30) years.

(2) The minimum period of confinement for an adult person convicted of Aggravated Murder as defined by ORS 163.105(2) prior to December 6, 1984, shall be twenty

(20) years.

(3) The minimum period of confinement for a person sentenced to life for Murder under ORS 163.115 committed on or after June 30, 1995, shall be twenty-five

(25) years.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.110, 163.105 & 163.115 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144, 163.105 & 163.115
