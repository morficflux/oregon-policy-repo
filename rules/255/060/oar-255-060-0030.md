## Full text

255-060-0030 Exit Interview Board Review Packet The exit interview Board Review Packet shall contain:

(1) Institution face sheet;

(2) All Board Action Forms since the prison term hearing, if any;

(3) Psychiatric and/or psychological evaluations (previous 6 months);

(4) Correspondence;

(5) Field parole analysis report, a pre-sentence investigation report or comparable report;

(6) Court orders;

(7) Misconduct reports; and

(8) Release plan.

(9) Inmate’s Notice of Rights and Board of Parole & Post-Prison Supervision Procedures.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.096, 144.098 & 144.185 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.096, 144.098 & 144.185
