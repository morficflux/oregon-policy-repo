# Postponement (OAR 255-060-0013)

## Full text

255-060-0013 Postponement Order Any order regarding the postponement of parole release shall be sent to the prisoner and shall set forth:

(1) The facts and specific reasons for the decision and the individual votes of the Board members.

(2) Notice of the right to administrative appeal pursuant to the procedures of division 80.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144

History: PAR 2-1990, f. & cert. ef. 4-5-90
