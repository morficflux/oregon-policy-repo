## Full text

255-060-0020 Out-of-State Parole Release Hearing Procedures An inmate in the Department of Corrections’ custody who is housed in an out-of-state facility may receive a teleconference exit interview in conformance with rule 255-060-0006.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.098 & 144.125 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.098 & 144.125
