## Full text

345-024-0640 Modification of the Standard for Nongenerating Energy Facilities The Council may by rule modify the carbon dioxide emissions standard for nongenerating energy facilities in OAR 345-024-0620 so that it remains equivalent to the standard for the net carbon dioxide emissions rate of a base load power plant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 & 469.501 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.501 & 469.503
