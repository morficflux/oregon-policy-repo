## Full text

345-024-0610 Modification of the Standard for Non-Base Load Power Plants The Council may by rule modify the carbon dioxide emissions standard for non-base load power plants in OAR 345-024-0590 so that the standard remains equivalent to the standard for the net carbon dioxide emissions rate of a base load gas plant, subject to the principles described in OAR 345-024-0510.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 & 469.503 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.501 & 469.503
