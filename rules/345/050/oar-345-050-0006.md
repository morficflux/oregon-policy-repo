## Full text

345-050-0006 Disposal Prohibited As provided under ORS 469.525, no radioactive waste shall be disposed of within this state, no person may arrange for disposal of radioactive waste within this state, no person may transport radioactive waste for disposal in this state, and no waste disposal facility for any radioactive waste shall be established, operated or licensed within this state, except as provided in ORS chapter 469 and this division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.525
