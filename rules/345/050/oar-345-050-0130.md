## Full text

345-050-0130 Ability to Construct and Operate A person shall not locate a waste disposal facility at a site un­less the person or firm proposing to dispose of the material has or can acquire the organizational, managerial, and technical expertise to construct, operate and retire the facility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.375
