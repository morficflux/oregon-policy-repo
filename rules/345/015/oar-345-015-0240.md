## Full text

345-015-0240 The Decision-Making Record The decision-making record on an application for a site certificate includes the decision record for the Department of Energy’s proposed order and the record of the contested case proceeding.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.370
