## Full text

345-015-0001 Purpose and Authority The rules in this division, authorized by ORS 469.040, 469.470 and 469.440, establish procedures governing Department of Energy and Energy Facility Siting Council review processes, including contested case proceedings.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470, ORS 469.370(5) & ORS 183.417(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.310-550, ORS 469.040, ORS 469.370, ORS 469.405 & ORS 469.440
