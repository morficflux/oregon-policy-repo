## Full text

345-095-0015 Scope and Construction

(1) The rules in this Division apply to all uranium mills that use conventional alkaline or acid leach technologies, together with their related and supporting facilities, for which the Council has issued a site certificate.

(2) If the Council finds that applicable Oregon statutes and rules, other than those involving federally delegated programs, would impose requirements inconsistent with the rules in this Division, the Council shall resolve the conflict consistent with the public interest. In resolving the conflict, the Council cannot waive any applicable state statute.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.556 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.556 & 469.505
