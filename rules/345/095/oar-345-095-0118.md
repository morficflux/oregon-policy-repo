# Mine Reclamation (OAR 345-095-0118)

## At a glance

OAR 345-095-0118 — Mine Reclamation. Chapter 345, Division 095. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

345-095-0118 Mine Reclamation Following the operational life of the facility, the certificate holder shall reclaim the mine site by modifying overburden and waste dump slopes to grades favorable to reclamation and by implementing surface water management measures to prevent water collection or erosion in the area and to aid in revegetating the site.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.556 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.470

History: EFSC 1-2007, f. & cert. ef. 5-15-07 EFSC 2-1980, f. & ef. 2-28-80
