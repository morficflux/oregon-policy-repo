## Full text

345-095-0005 Purpose It is the purpose of these rules to ensure that the construction, operation, and decommissioning of uranium mills and related and supporting facilities are accomplished in a manner consistent with ORS 469.310.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.310, 469.501 & 469.556
