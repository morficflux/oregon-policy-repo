## Full text

345-070-0010 Legislative Authority These rules are promulgated concurrently by the Council and the Director pursuant to their respective rulemaking authorities contained in ORS Chapter 183, ORS 469.470, 469.501 to, 469.507, 469.530, 469.560 and 192.500, in order to implement their joint responsibility under 469.530.
