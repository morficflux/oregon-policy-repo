# Purpose (OAR 345-070-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 345-070-0005 — Purpose. Chapter 345, Division 070. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

345-070-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to protect the confidentiality of information submitted to the Council and the Director regarding security programs for nuclear-fueled thermal power plants, nuclear installations, and the transportation of radioactive materials to and from such facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.530 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.530
