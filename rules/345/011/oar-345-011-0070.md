## Full text

345-011-0070 Council Requests for Information The Council may at any time request that any person subject to the Council’s jurisdiction submit information to the Council about any matter within the Council’s jurisdiction.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.470
