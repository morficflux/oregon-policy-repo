# Unacceptable (OAR 345-011-0080)

## At a glance

## Full text

345-011-0080 Unacceptable Conduct The chair may expel any person who engages in conduct that disrupts any Council proceeding. In any proceeding of a Council committee, the chairman or chairwoman of the committee may expel any person who engages in conduct that disrupts the proceeding.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.460
