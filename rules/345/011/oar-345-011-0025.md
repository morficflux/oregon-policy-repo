## Full text

345-011-0025 Consideration of Matters Not on Agenda A Council member or the Council Secretary may, with the approval of a majority of Council members, raise matters at a meeting that the Council Secretary did not place on the agenda. The Council shall not act on a matter not on the agenda unless a majority of the members present agree that the matter is so substantial and of such immediate concern that the Council should not defer action until the next regular Council meeting.
