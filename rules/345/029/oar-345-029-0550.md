## Full text

345-029-0550 Enforcement Conference If requested by the responsible party in its response to the Pre-Enforcement Notice provided under OAR 345-029-0520(2), the Director must provide the responsible party an opportunity for an enforcement conference to present information regarding the alleged violation and to discuss any corrective actions taken or proposed. The Director may use information discussed at the conference in determining the appropriate enforcement action.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.085, 469.540 & 469.992
