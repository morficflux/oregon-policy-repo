## Full text

345-026-0005 Purpose The purpose of the rules in this division is to ensure that the construction, operation and retirement of facilities are accomplished in a manner consistent with the protection of the public health, safety and welfare and the protection of the environment.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.310, 469.410, 469.430 & 469.507
