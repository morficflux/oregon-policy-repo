## Full text

345-060-0015 Vehicles, Operator, Equipment The carrier shall maintain all aspects of vehicles, operators and equipment in accordance with OAR 740, division 100. These Oregon Department of Transportation rules reference the requirements of 49 CFR 390 through 397 in effect as of the date of this rule.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 & 469.607 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.603, 469.605 & 469.607
