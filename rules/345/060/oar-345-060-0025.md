## Full text

345-060-0025 Packaging, Placarding, Labeling and Documentation The shipper shall maintain all packaging, placarding, labeling, shipment documentation and all other aspects of transporting any radioactive material in accordance with 10 CFR 71 and 73, and 49 CFR 171 through 179 in effect as of the date of this rule.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 & 469.607 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.607
