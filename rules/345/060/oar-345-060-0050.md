## Full text

345-060-0050 Weather and Road Conditions The carrier shall avoid movement of motor vehicles during a road condition advisory of the Oregon State Highway Division unless vehicles have the required chains or traction tires specified in OAR 734, division 17.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470 & 469.607 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.603 - 469.615
