## Full text

345-060-0007 Inspections The State of Oregon or its agents may inspect shipments under these rules for compliance with applicable rules and regulations. The State shall inspect all irradiated reactor fuel (defined in 10 CFR 73.37 in effect as of the date of this rule) and Highway Route Controlled Quantity shipments (defined in 49 CFR 173.403 in effect as of the date of this rule). The state may choose to waive inspection if the shipment is carrying a current Commercial Vehicle Safety Alliance inspection sticker. The state may inspect samplings of other shipments. The State may inspect highway shipments made under conditional permits described in OAR 345-060-0004(6). The State shall make arrangements for inspection when the carrier gives notice for inspection, as described in 345-060-0005.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.470, 469.605 & 469.607 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.603 - 469.615
