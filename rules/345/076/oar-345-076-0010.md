# Purpose (OAR 345-076-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

345-076-0010 Purpose The rules in this division supplement the general standards in OAR chapter 345, division 22, for the siting of energy facilities and apply to any nuclear-fueled thermal power plant or nuclear power plant, as defined in ORS 469.300 and their related and supporting facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.470
