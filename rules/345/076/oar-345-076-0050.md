## Full text

345-076-0050 Federal Waste Depository To issue a site certificate for a nuclear power facility, the Council must find that there is an adequate federal repository for the disposal of high-level radioactive waste as described in ORS 469.595.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.300
