# Effective Dates of Assessment (OAR 669-010-0040)

## At a glance

OAR 669-010-0040 — Effective Dates of Assessment. Chapter 669, Division 010. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

669-010-0040 Effective Dates of Assessment The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser or handler, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and 669-010-0020(4) of the reports required under 669-010-0025, applies to and covers any purchase by him or her or delivery on or after June 1, 1989, of any sweet cherries grown in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576
