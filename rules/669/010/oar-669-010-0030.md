# Penalties (OAR 669-010-0030)

## Full text

669-010-0030 Penalties Penalty for delaying transmittal of assessment moneys (ORS 576.355), "In addition to the penalties prescribed in 576.99, any person who delays transmittal of funds beyond the time set by a Commission shall pay a penalty of ten percent of the amount due and shall also pay one and one-half percent interest per month on the unpaid balance of the assessment. If seeking a waiver of the penalties, a written explanation of the circumstances that caused the payment delay must be submitted to the Commission office for review. Penalties may be waived by a majority vote of the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 & 2003 OL Ch. 604 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576 & 2003 OL Ch. 604
