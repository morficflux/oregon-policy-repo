# Exemption Based on Type of Commodity Sale (OAR 669-010-0050)

## Full text

669-010-0050 Exemption Based on Type of Commodity Sale No assessment will be deducted for any sale or sales of sweet cherries made by the producer direct to any consumer(s) and/or retailer(s) where the total accumulated sales during a fiscal year is not more than 2000 pounds. (See casual sales as defined in 669-010-0015.)

Statutory/Other Authority: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576

History: OSCC 1-2004, f. & cert. ef. 1-13-04
