## Full text

669-030-0030 Removal of Commissioners The Commission may recommend that the Director of the Department of Agriculture may remove a commissioner from office, and declare the position vacant for cause.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576
