## Full text

338-010-0050 Trainee Registration Extension and Information

(1) To extend a trainee registration an individual must submit a trainee extension form prescribed by the Office; and

(2) Pay trainee extension fees.

(3) A trainee must extend a trainee registration in consecutive, uninterrupted, one-year increments.

(4) A trainee may extend the trainee registration no more than three times without Board approval. Additional extensions must be approved by the Board.
