## Full text

105-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure The Chief Human Resources Office adopts the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act as amended and effective January 1, 2024. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the Attorney General or the Department of Administrative Services.]
