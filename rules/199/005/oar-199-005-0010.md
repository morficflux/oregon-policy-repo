## Full text

199-005-0010 Resale Value of Unsolicited Tokens or Awards

(1) The purpose of this rule is to assist public officials in determining the resale value of items provided under ORS 244.020( 7 [6] )(b)(C).

(2) Engraved or otherwise personalized items that include a public official's name are deemed to have a resale value under $25, unless the personalized item is made from gold or some other valuable material that would have value over $25 as a raw material.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 244.290 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 244.020
