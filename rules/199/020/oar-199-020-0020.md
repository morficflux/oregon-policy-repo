# Filing Deadlines (OAR 199-020-0020)

## At a glance

## Full text

199-020-0020 Filing Deadlines Effective in 2016, Statements of Economic Interest electronically filed online with the Commission by April 15, regardless of whether the 15th falls on a holiday or weekend, are considered timely.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 244.290 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 244.050
