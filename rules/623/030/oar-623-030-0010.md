## Full text

623-030-0010 Number of Commissioners, Terms The Oregon Hazelnut Commission will consist of eight members appointed by the Director of the Oregon Department of Agriculture for a term of three years or; if for a term following a prior term for that person, ending three years from the date of expiration of any prior term.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576. Statutes/Other Implemented: 2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576
