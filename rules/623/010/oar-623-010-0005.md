# Casual (OAR 623-010-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 623-010-0005 — Casual. Chapter 623, Division 010. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

623-010-0005 Casual Sale "Casual sale" means any sale or sales of hazelnuts made by the producer direct to the consumer where the total accumulated sales during a fiscal year is not more than two hundred and fifty (250) pounds, in-shell basis.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576

History: HZL 1-2000, f. 12-8-00, cert. ef. 1-1-01 OFC 2, f. 8-6-59
