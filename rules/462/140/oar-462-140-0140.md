# Director of (OAR 462-140-0140)

## At a glance

## Full text

462-140-0140 Director of Racing Commercial race meet licensees shall designate a person to act in the capacity of a director of racing. The designee shall act for the management of the race meet licensee in all matters pertaining to racing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270
