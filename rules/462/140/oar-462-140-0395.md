## Full text

462-140-0395 Owner

(1) The owner will be responsible that his or her thoroughbred, foaled in 2018 and after, has been microchipped and registered with the Jockey Club. Microchips may be implanted by a certified veterinarian or a trained Thoroughbred Racing Protective Bureau (TRPB) technician. The owner is responsible for all fees associated with the microchipping of their horse. During the transition period the owner may request a variance from the Commission Stewards, as long as the horse is able to be identified and has the proper paperwork.

(2) Each licensed owner is responsible for disclosure to the Commission or its designee of the true and entire ownership of each of his/her horses registered with the racing secretary. Any change in ownership or trainer of a horse registered with the racing secretary shall be approved by the stewards. Each owner shall comply with all licensing requirements.
