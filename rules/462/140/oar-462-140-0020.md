## Full text

462-140-0020 Mutuel Manager

(1) The mutuel manager shall be in charge of the mutuel department and shall enforce all pari-mutuel rules of the commission.

(2) The mutuel manager shall ensure that minors are not allowed to wager.

(3) The mutuel manager shall ensure that the wagering facilities and pari-mutuel clerks present a neat appearance and that the pari-mutuel clerks conduct themselves in an orderly, courteous and professional manner.
