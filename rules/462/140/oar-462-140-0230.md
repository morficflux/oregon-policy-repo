## Full text

462-140-0230 Clerk of Scales The clerk of scales shall:

(1) Weigh jockeys out and in, and ensure that riders carry the correct assigned weight.

(2) At the time of weighing out, record all overweights and cause these to be announced publicly and posted in a conspicuous place before the running of each race.

(3) Ensure that the scales have been certified to be accurate by a reputable company or governmental agency prior to the start of the race meet.

(4) Check the scales for accuracy every race day.

(5) Maintain accurate scale sheets for each race.

(6) Ensure that a tag is attached to the bridle of all horses sent to the test barn by the board of stewards or commission veterinarian.
