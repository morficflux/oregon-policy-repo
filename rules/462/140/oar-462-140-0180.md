# Track Superintendent (OAR 462-140-0180)

## Full text

462-140-0180 Track Superintendent The track superintendent is responsible for the condition of the track and shall see that all equipment under his or her care is in the best possible condition, and that the track and equipment are maintained by qualified personnel.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270
