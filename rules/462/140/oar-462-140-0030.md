# Vendor (OAR 462-140-0030)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

462-140-0030 Vendor Vendors must have a list of products they sell attached to the license application and a current copy of all state permits and licenses to dispense such products. The list of products and any changes to that list must be approved by a commission veterinarian and/or stewards.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270
