# Assistant (OAR 462-140-0330)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

462-140-0330 Assistant Trainer Assistant trainers have the same duties as trainers, and may be held equally responsible with the regular trainer as the absolute ensurer of the condition of racing animals in their care.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270

History: RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
