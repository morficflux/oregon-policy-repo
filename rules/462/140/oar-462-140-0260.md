# Patrol Judge (OAR 462-140-0260)

## At a glance

OAR 462-140-0260 — Patrol Judge. Chapter 462, Division 140. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

462-140-0260 Patrol Judge When required by the commission, there shall be one or more patrol judges, appointed by the race meet licensee, whose duties are to view each race from the vantage point to which the stewards have assigned them. At the end of each racing day the patrol judges shall report their observations of each race in writing to the stewards.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270
