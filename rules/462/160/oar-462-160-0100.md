## Full text

462-160-0100 Purpose To describe requirements and procedures used to ensure the health and welfare of racehorses and to safeguard the interests of the public and the participants in racing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270 & 462.415
