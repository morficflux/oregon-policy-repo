## Full text

462-200-0120 Minus Pool The “breakage” from each pool in a race shall be applied on any “minus” pool in that particular race. In the event a minus pool occurs after all breakage has been applied from that particular race, the expense of said minus pool for the race shall be borne by the race meet licensee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270(3)
