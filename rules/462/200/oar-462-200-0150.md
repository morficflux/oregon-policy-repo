# General Provisions (OAR 462-200-0150)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

462-200-0150 General Provisions Each race meet licensee shall conduct wagering in accordance with applicable laws and these rules. Such wagering shall employ a pari-mutuel system approved by the commission. The totalizator shall be tested prior to and during the meeting as required by the commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270(3)

History: RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
