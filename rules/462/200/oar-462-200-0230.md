## Full text

462-200-0230 Cancelled Contests If a contest is cancelled or declared “no contest” or “no race,” refunds shall be granted on valid wagers in accordance with these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270(3)
