# Totalizator (OAR 462-200-0080)

## At a glance

## Full text

462-200-0080 Totalizator Standards The Oregon Racing Commission shall set standards for procedure, equipment and personnel and any other areas the commission deems necessary to insure the operations and integrity of the tote system.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270(3)
