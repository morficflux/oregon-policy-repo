# Financial Reports (OAR 462-200-0350)

## At a glance

OAR 462-200-0350 — Financial Reports. Chapter 462, Division 200. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

462-200-0350 Financial Reports A race-track licensee shall provide the Oregon Racing Commission with an audited report of its pari-mutuel operations at the off-track wagering facility(ies) not more than 120 days following the end of the race meet’s fiscal year, and shall provide at the commission’s or their representative’s request all of the simulcast operator’s business records for examination.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.700 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.700

History: RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
