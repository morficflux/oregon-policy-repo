# Days (OAR 462-200-0040)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

462-200-0040 Days Racing Permitted Any race meeting properly licensed under ORS Chapter 462 by the Oregon Racing Commission may conduct racing with pari-mutuel wagering on any day of the week. No wagering or actual running of a race shall be permitted after midnight on any day except in the case of emergency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270(3)
