# Minimum (OAR 462-200-0020)

## At a glance

OAR 462-200-0020 — Minimum. Chapter 462, Division 200. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

462-200-0020 Minimum Payoff No mutuel ticket shall be paid off at less than 10 cents net for each dollar wagered except when the breaks compute to less than 10 cents on the first dollar, the license shall pay five cents for each dollar wagered. The infield board shall indicate the amount due on each minimum price winning mutuel ticket sold for each specific pool.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.140

History: RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
