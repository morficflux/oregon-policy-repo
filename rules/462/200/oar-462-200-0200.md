## Full text

462-200-0200 Advance Performance Wagering No race meet licensee shall permit wagering to begin before the mutuel windows open on the day of the performance unless it has first obtained the authorization of the commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.270(3)
