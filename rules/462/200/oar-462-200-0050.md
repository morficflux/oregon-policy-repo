# Underpayment (OAR 462-200-0050)

## At a glance

OAR 462-200-0050 — Underpayment. Chapter 462, Division 200. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

462-200-0050 Underpayment If during any race meet conducted under this law, there shall be underpayments of the amount actually due to the wagerers, the amount of such underpayments to wagerers shall revert and belong to the State of Oregon and paid to the commission and become a part of its fund, and shall not be retained by the race meet licensee under whose license such race meet was held.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 462.270(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 462.150

History: RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
