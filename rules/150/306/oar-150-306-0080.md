# Electronic Data Transfer (OAR 150-306-0080)

## At a glance

OAR 150-306-0080 — Electronic Data Transfer. Chapter 150, Division 306. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

150-306-0080 Electronic Data Transfer Any data required to be delivered in compliance with property taxation law may be sent and received electronically by agreement of the parties. All required data fields must be included in the transmission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 306.125
