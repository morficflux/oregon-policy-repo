## Full text

150-306-0090 Confidentiality of Appraisals of Industrial Property Made by and Contained in Department of Revenue Files The files of industrial property, appraised by the Department of Revenue and contained in DOR files, will be safeguarded in the manner stated in OAR 150-308-0440. Text available via filing PDF that is stored in ORMS
