## Full text

150-306-0200 Application Filing by Telephonic Facsimile (FAX) Application Filing by Electronic Means The Department of Revenue or any county may accept any application for property tax exemption or special assessment by telephonic facsimile (FAX) or other electronic means according to the policies and procedures of the receiving agency.
