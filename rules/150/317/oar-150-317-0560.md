## Full text

150-317-0560 Consolidated Oregon Return: Credits The amount of credit that may offset tax on a consolidated Oregon return is not limited to the tax attributable to the corporation earning the credit. This provision applies to credits carried forward from years in which a separate or combined report is filed to years in which a consolidated return is filed, as well as current year credits.
