## Full text

150-317-0290 Research Tax Credit: Notice of Election The election to compute the credit under ORS 317.152 or 317.154 shall be made on the Oregon return for the tax year in which the credit is claimed. The election can be changed on an amended return subject to the limitations provided in 314.410 and 314.415.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 317.153
