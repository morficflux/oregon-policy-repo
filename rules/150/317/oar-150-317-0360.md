## Full text

150-317-0360 Definition of “State” For purposes of ORS 317.309, the term “state” shall mean any state of the United States, the District of Columbia, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and a territory or possession of the United States, and any foreign country or political subdivision thereof.
