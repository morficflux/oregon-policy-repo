## Full text

150-317-0480 Definition of “Premiums” in the Insurance Sales Factor For purposes of computing the Insurance Sales Factor, the term “premiums” shall mean “premiums written” as required to be reported in the Annual Statement filed with the Department of Consumer and Business Services, Insurance Division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 317.660
