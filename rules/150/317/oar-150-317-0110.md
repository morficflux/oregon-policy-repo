## Full text

150-317-0110 Tax Reform Act of 1984 Adjustments The rule under OAR 150-316-0020 shall be followed in determining how adjustments due to adoption of effective dates in the federal Tax Reform Act of 1984 shall be reported.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 314.031
