## Full text

150-317-0530 Corporations Doing Business Outside the United States A corporation included in a consolidated federal return shall not be excluded from a consolidated Oregon return simply because its business operations are conducted outside the United States.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 317.705
