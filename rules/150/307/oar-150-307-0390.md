## Full text

150-307-0390 Veterans Property Held in Trust To receive an exemption on property that is held in a trust, the trust must be clearly identified as revocable for the specific property on which a claim for exemption is filed under ORS 307.250 and an application is made as required in 307.260

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 307.270
