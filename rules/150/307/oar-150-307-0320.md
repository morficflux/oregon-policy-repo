## Full text

150-307-0320 Definition of Elderly Funded exemptions are granted to qualifying non profit homes for the elderly under ORS 307.241 to 307.245. For purposes of 307.241 to 307.245 and 307.375, the term “elderly” refers to a person 62 years of age or older. This rule is to assist in determining the eligibility of a nonprofit home for a funded property tax exemption.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 307.241
