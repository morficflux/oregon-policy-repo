## Full text

150-315-0154 Pollution Control Facilities: Adjustment of Basis When computing adjusted basis for a facility certified in a tax year beginning before January 1, 1977, original basis is reduced by the amount of depreciation that was not deducted due to claiming the credit and by the amount of credit claimed in tax years beginning before January 1, 1977.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 315.304
