## Full text

150-315-0100 Child Care Division Contribution Credit The form required by this section should not be attached to the tax return, but kept with the taxpayer’s records. Upon audit or examination, the information must be made available to the department to verify any credit claimed under this section.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 315.213
