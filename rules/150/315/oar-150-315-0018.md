## Full text

150-315-0018 Reforestation Credit: Reasons Beyond the Taxpayer’s Control For purposes of reforestation credit, the Department of Revenue adopts the definition of "reasons beyond the taxpayer's control" defined in Department of Forestry rule OAR 629-23-420 filed 8-1-08 and certified effective 9-1-08.
