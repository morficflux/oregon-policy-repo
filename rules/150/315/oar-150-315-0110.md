# Scholarship (OAR 150-315-0110)

## At a glance

## Full text

150-315-0110 Scholarship Tax Credit The form required by this section should not be attached to the tax return, but must be kept with the taxpayer’s records. Upon audit or examination, the information must be made available to the department to verify any credit claimed under this section. [ED. NOTE: Forms referenced are available from the agency.]
