## Full text

150-267-0030 Employer-Employee Application Unless the context requires otherwise, for purposes of administration of transit district payroll taxes, determination whether an employer-employee relationship exists will be made under rules adopted pursuant to state withholding statutes. See OAR 150-316-0255 and 150-316-0241.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 267.380
