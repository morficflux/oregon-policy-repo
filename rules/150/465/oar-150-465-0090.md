# Due Dates for Payment of Fees (OAR 150-465-0090)

## Full text

150-465-0090 Due Dates for Payment of Fees After January 1, 1990, all fees collected during a calendar quarter are due on the last day of the month following the end of the calendar quarter. The due dates will be January 31, April 30, July 31, and October 31.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 465.104
