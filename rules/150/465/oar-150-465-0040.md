## Full text

150-465-0040 Payment of Fee by Seller The owner of the petroleum product is considered to be the seller. The seller must pay the fee when the product is withdrawn from the seller’s bulk facility for sale or use in the seller’s own distribution chain. For example, Company A owns a bulk facility in Portland and a chain of service stations throughout the state. Company A also owns its own truck-trailers. Company A must pay the fee at the time it withdraws product from its bulk facility into its truck-trailers for distribution to its service stations.
