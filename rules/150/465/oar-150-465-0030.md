## Full text

150-465-0030 Definition of “Petroleum Product” As used in ORS 465.101 through 465.131, “petroleum product” includes blends of petroleum products mandated by ORS 646.905 through 646.963, such as diesel that contains a percentage of biodiesel, or gasoline that contains a percentage of ethanol.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 465.101
