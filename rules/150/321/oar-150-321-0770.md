## Full text

150-321-0770 Definition of “Sound Management Practices” For the purpose of ORS 321.805(4), “Sound management practices,” in addition to growing trees may include but are not limited to: range management, fire protection, soil erosion control, stream protection, cooperative wildlife management, and road access control.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 321.805
