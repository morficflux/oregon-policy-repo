## Full text

150-321-0650 Definition of Owner for Small Tract Forestland Purposes “Owner” as used in ORS 321.706 to 321.716 means one or more individuals, a public or private corporation, a limited liability company, a limited liability partnership, an unincorporated association, a partnership, an estate, a trust, a government, a governmental instrumentality, or any combination of these or similar entities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 321.706
