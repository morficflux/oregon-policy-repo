## Full text

150-321-0210 Forestland Classification The forestland classification for western Oregon is as follows: [See PDF link below.] Site class is based on Bulletin #201 tables dated 1930 and James King 50 year index tables dated 1966, topographical features, vegetation and soil types. [Publications: The publication(s) referred to or incorporated by reference in this rule is available from the Department of Revenue pursuant to ORS 183.360(2) and 183.355(6).] [ED.
