## Full text

150-321-0730 Common Ownership for Sale of Small Tract Forestland “Person” means an individual, a public or private corporation, a limited liability company, a limited liability partnership, an unincorporated association, a partnership, a government, or a governmental instrumentality.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 & 321.609 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 321.719
