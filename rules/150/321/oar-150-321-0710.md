## Full text

150-321-0710 Notation on Assessment and Tax Roll The assessment and tax roll must show the notation “Small Tract Forestland — Potential Additional Tax Liability” for each parcel of land that is assessed as small tract forestland.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 & 321.609 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 321.709
