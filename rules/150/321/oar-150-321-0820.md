## Full text

150-321-0820 Notification by Assessor of Denial of Application The assessor will send the written notice denying the application, in whole or in part, for designation of forestland to the applicant by certified mail.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 321.839
