## Full text

150-321-0330 Date of Acquisition In answering the question on the application for designation as forestland concerning date of acquisition, the applicant must list the exact date of acquisition if the forestland was acquired within the five year period immediately preceding the date of the application. If the forestland was acquired more than five years before the date of the application, that fact must be stated, but dates are not required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 321.358
