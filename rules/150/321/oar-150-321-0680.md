## Full text

150-321-0680 Definition of Taxpayer for Appeal of Small Tract Forestland Application Denial “Taxpayer,” as used in ORS 321.706(7), means an owner of forestland that is the subject of an application for qualification as small tract forestland.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 321.706
