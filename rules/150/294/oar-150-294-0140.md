## Full text

150-294-0140 Transfers from the CATF to the CAFFA Account Each county must notify the Department of Revenue of the amount it deposits into the County Assessment Function Funding Assistance (CAFFA) account from the County Assessment and Taxation Fund (CATF) account at the time of deposit. The deposit must occur on or before the 10th working day of the month following the last day of the fiscal quarter.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 294.187
