# Budget Resources (OAR 150-294-0390)

## At a glance

OAR 150-294-0390 — Budget Resources. Chapter 150, Division 294. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

150-294-0390 Budget Resources Budget resources of a county shall not include proceeds and interest arising under ORS 275.090 to 275.310 which will be distributed to any municipal corporation. However, any proceeds and interest distributed under 275.090 to 275.310 shall be considered a budget resource for the municipal corporation receiving the distribution, including the county.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 294.361
