## Full text

150-294-0830 Duties of the Budget Committee of a Council of Government The budget committee shall notify the public of the time and place of public meetings on the budget and shall make the budget available. The budget committee must approve a budget document prior to any council of government hearings on that document.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 294.915
