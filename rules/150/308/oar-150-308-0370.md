## Full text

150-308-0370 Determining Taxable Value for Assessment Charges on Property Exempt from Taxation If a property that is exempt from ad valorem taxation is subject to assessment charges, the assessor shall determine the maximum amount of assessment charges by using the real market value of the property.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 308.232
