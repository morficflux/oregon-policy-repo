## Full text

150-308-1120 Definition of Specially Assessed Homesites

(1) “Homesite” as defined in ORS 308A.250(3) includes site developments as defined in OAR 150-307-0010 and amenities associated with the raw, undeveloped land such as topography that affords the site a particular view, river frontage, property access, and utility access.

(2) A forest homesite qualified under ORS 308A.253(1) must be located on a parcel of land with greater than 10 acres of specially assessed forestland, that is zoned exclusive farm use (EFU), forest use, or farm and forest use.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 308.229 & 308A.250
