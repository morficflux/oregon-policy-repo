# Definition of Affected (OAR 150-308-0180)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

150-308-0180 Definition of Affected “Affected property” means property that is subject to one or more of the following events: partitioned or subdivided; added to the account as omitted property; rezoned and used consistent with the rezoning; disqualified from a special assessment, exemption, or partial exemption.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 308.156
