## Full text

150-312-0030 Mailing of Notice of Foreclosure Proceeding Counties have the option to send the first-class mail notice prior to sending the certified mail notice, providing both notices are sent by August 15, or the date of application of judgment and decree, whichever is later.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 312.040
