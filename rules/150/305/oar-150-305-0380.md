## Full text

150-305-0380 Application and Signature Due Dates Completed applications and completed signature petitions must be received by the Charitable Checkoff Commission by July 1 of the first tax year for which the entity seeks to be included on a schedule of the individual tax return.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 305.725
