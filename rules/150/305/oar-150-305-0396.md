## Full text

150-305-0396 Signature Gathering Period The signature gathering period for any tax year for which an entity seeks to be included on a schedule of the individual tax return begins July 1 of the tax year two years prior to the year in which being included on a schedule is sought and extends through June 30 of the tax year being included on a schedule is sought. For example: if an entity seeks to be included on a schedule of the individual tax return for tax year 2021, the signature gathering period is from July 1, 2019, through June 30, 2021.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100, 305.720 & 305.753 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 305.720
