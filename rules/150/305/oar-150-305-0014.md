## Full text

150-305-0014 Applicable Dates Administrative rules adopted by the department, unless specified otherwise by statute or by rule, shall be applicable for all periods open to examination.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 305.100
