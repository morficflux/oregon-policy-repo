## Full text

150-305-0276 Appeal Time Frame If a taxpayer wishes to appeal denial of a request for cancellation of assessment or refund, the appeal shall be made within 90 days of notice of denial.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 305.295
