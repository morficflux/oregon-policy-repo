## Full text

150-314-0197 Failure to File Penalty A penalty of 20 percent of the tax required to be shown as tax on the return will be added if a return is not filed within three months after the due date. The three month period will be measured from the last day of any extension granted to file the return.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 314.400
