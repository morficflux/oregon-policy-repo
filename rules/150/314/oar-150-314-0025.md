# Pollution (OAR 150-314-0025)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

150-314-0025 Pollution Control Facilities: Revocation of Certificate Upon receipt of notice of revocation of a certificate, the Department shall issue a statement to the taxpayer of the amount of unpaid taxes due, together with interest from the time the taxes would have been due if the tax relief had not been granted. If, within 30 days of the date of such statement, the amount is not paid or protest received as to the accuracy of the billing, warrants will be issued pursuant to ORS 314.430, and such other collection procedures will be instituted as provided by law and deemed necessary by the Department.
