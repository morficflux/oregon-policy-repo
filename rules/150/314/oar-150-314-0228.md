## Full text

150-314-0228 Extension of Period for Assessment Once the provisions of ORS 314.410(6) have been exercised, the Department and the taxpayer may, by written agreement, extend beyond the original agreed period the period for mailing a notice of deficiency or assessing a deficiency, provided the subsequent agreement is made before the original agreement expires.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: 314.410
