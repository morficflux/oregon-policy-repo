## Full text

150-314-0187 Responsibility for Tax Payments When an employer fails to withhold income tax from an employee under ORS 316.167, the employee is not relieved from paying income tax under Chapter 305, 314 and 316.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 314.395
