# Corporation Tax Credits — Converting an S Corporation to a C Corporation (OAR 150-314-0497)

## Full text

150-314-0497 Corporation Tax Credits — Converting an S Corporation to a C Corporation Tax credits passed through to shareholders and not used entirely to offset the shareholders’ tax liabilities in the year of pass-through shall be carried forward by the shareholders. Such unused credits may not offset corporation tax liabilities in future years when C corporation status has been elected.
