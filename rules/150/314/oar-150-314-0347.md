## Full text

150-314-0347 Application of ORS 314.610 to 314.667: Allocation Any taxpayer subject to the taxing jurisdiction of this state shall allocate all of its nonapportionable income or loss within or without this state in accordance with ORS 314.625 to 314.645.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 314.615
