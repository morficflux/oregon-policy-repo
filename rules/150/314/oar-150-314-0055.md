## Full text

150-314-0055 Change in Methods of Accounting or Reporting A taxpayer’s method of accounting for state income tax purposes must be the same as for federal tax purposes and it must clearly reflect the taxpayer’s income. If the taxpayer’s method of accounting does not clearly reflect the taxpayer’s income, the department shall compute the taxpayer’s taxable income in a manner consistent with the Treasury Regulations set forth under Internal Revenue Code Sections 446 through 483.
