## Full text

150-311-0790 Refund Reserve Account For tax years beginning on or after July 1, 1992, the anticipated annual refunds shall be the total dollar amount of refunds issued for the prior fiscal year. This amount may be increased or decreased for anticipated changes in appeals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 311.807
