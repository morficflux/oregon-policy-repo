## Full text

150-311-0220 Date Roll Corrected For purposes of ORS 311.223(4) and 311.229 the "roll is corrected" on the date the assessor sends the notice to the taxpayer's last known address by first class mail as required in 311.223(2).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 311.223
