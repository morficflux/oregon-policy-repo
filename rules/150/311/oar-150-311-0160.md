## Full text

150-311-0160 Roll Correction for Nonexistent Property Property or improvements, which did not exist, but were included on the assessment roll at the time of the last appraisal shall be corrected, when discovered, under ORS 311.205(1)(b) and 311.206.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 311.205
