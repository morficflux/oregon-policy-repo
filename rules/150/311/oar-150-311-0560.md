## Full text

150-311-0560 Fee for Service of Warrant Under ORS 311.605 to ORS 311.635 The fee for service of a warrant is not property tax money. When money is collected for service of a warrant it shall be deposited to the County General Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 311.633
