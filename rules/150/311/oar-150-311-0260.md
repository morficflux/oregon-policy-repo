## Full text

150-311-0260 Prepayment of Property Taxes Unless authorized by law, no prepayments of property taxes which have not been certified by a taxing district, shall be collected or accepted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 311.250
