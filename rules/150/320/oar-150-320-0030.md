## Full text

150-320-0030 Waiver of Penalty The provisions of OAR 150-305-0068 shall be followed to determine when the department may waive a penalty charged for failure to file or pay tax by the due date.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 & 320.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 320.075 & 320.080
