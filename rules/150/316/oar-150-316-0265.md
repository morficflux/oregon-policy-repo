## Full text

150-316-0265 Withholding Payments: Cash Basis All withholding is on a cash basis and must be reported on a cash basis. Example: If services are performed in January but not compensated until April, withholding on the wages for those services is reported on the report for the quarter ending June 30.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 316.168
