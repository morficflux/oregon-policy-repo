## Full text

150-316-0519 Gain or Loss Upon the Sale of State and Municipal Bonds of Other States (Foreign States)

(1) Holders of state and municipal bonds of other states (foreign states) shall determine the gain or loss upon the sale or disposition of the bonds by following the federal rules for taxable securities under Internal Revenue Code sections 1271 to 1283 inclusive.

(2) Adjusted Issue Price: The adjusted issue price or basis of the bonds shall be the issue price increased by the total amount of original issue discount (OID) included in Oregon taxable income using the rules for federal taxable securities in section 1272 of the Internal Revenue Code and corresponding regulations. See OAR 150-316-0511 for example.
