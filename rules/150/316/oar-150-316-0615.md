## Full text

150-316-0615 Substantiation Required for Construction Worker and Loggers Expenses Upon audit, the taxpayer may be required to provide the same substantiation that would be necessary for a travel expense deduction allowable under IRC 162(a).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 316.818 & 316.832
