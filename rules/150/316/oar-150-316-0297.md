## Full text

150-316-0297 Where Taxpayer Reports on Fiscal Year Basis Taxes withheld during any calendar year shall be allowed as a credit for the taxable year of the taxpayer which begins in that calendar year. For example, where an employee is on a fiscal year ending June 30, 1969, he would credit the tax withheld on his wages for the calendar year 1968.
