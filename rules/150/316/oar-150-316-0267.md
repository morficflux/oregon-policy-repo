# Additional (OAR 150-316-0267)

## Full text

150-316-0267 Additional Time to File Reports Oregon does not allow additional 10 day federal extension to file the quarterly tax report when all payments are paid when due. Information provided by the taxpayer on the tax report is essential to providing timely payment of Unemployment Insurance benefits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 316.168
