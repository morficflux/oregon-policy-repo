## Full text

150-316-0557 Modification of Federal Taxable Income: Oregon Income Tax Claimed as an Itemized Deduction Beginning in tax year 1991, if the taxpayer itemizes deductions for Oregon, the itemized deductions will be subject to the same phase-out requirement as required for federal income tax purposes under IRC Section 68. Oregon law allows federal itemized deductions, after the phase-out, reduced by any Oregon income tax that has been itemized for federal income tax purposes.
