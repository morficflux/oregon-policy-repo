## Full text

150-316-0336 Employee’s Rights Recourse against an employer in regard to taxes on wages withheld and reported, but not paid to the Department of Revenue, is exclusively that of the state. An employee’s rights as to any such tax withheld, reported and unpaid are those of a tax credit or refund as provided in ORS 316.187 and OAR 150-316-0295.
