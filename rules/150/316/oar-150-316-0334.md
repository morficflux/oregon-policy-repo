## Full text

150-316-0334 Withholding Tax Payment Requirements for Agricultural Employers An employer of agricultural employees is required to pay Oregon taxes withheld at the same time as federal tax is deposited. Federal tax is the sum of withholding, FICA (social security) and Medicare taxes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 316.197
