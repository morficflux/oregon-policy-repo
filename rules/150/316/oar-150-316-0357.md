## Full text

150-316-0357 Waiver of Termination Reports For the purposes of waiver of termination reports, the Department of Revenue adopts the successor-in-interest definition as found in Employment Division OAR 471-031-0140, Filed 12-23-77 and Effective 1-1-78.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 316.202
