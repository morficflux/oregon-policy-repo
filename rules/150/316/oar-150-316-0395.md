## Full text

150-316-0395 Tax Treatment of Unincorporated Organization Except as otherwise provided by statute, regulations under Internal Revenue Code Section 7701 that allow unincorporated entities to elect to be classified as corporations or partnerships for federal tax purposes shall also be effective for Oregon tax purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 305.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 316.277
