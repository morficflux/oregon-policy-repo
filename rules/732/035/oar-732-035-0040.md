## Full text

732-035-0040 Program Development The Department shall:

(1) Study the effects of grants authorized under this program on the creation or maintenance of jobs in Oregon.

(2) File semiannual reports with the Commission on the findings of the studies.

(3) Adopt rules and develop policies and grant application procedures necessary to achieve the goals of the Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, Sec. 24 (2), Ch. 746 & OL 2007 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 184.670, 184.685, Ch. 746 & OL 2007
