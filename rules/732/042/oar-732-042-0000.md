## Full text

732-042-0000 Purpose These rules establish procedures and requirements necessary for the administration of the Statewide Transportation Improvement Fund (STIF) Formula Fund, pursuant to ORS 184.758(1)(a). The STIF Formula Fund is intended to improve public transportation services for current and potential future Oregon transit users by distributing moneys to Qualified Entities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, ORS 184.658 & ORS 184.761 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 184.751-184.766
