# Applications for (OAR 333-121-0050)

## At a glance

OAR 333-121-0050 — Applications for. Chapter 333, Division 121. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-121-0050 Applications for Exemptions Any application for a license or for amendment of a license authorizing use of a teletherapy-type unit for irradiation of materials or objects may include proposed alternatives for the requirements of this division. The Authority will approve the proposed alternatives if the applicant provides adequate rationale for the proposed alternatives and demonstrates that they are likely to provide an adequate level of safety for workers and the public.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.675 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.675
