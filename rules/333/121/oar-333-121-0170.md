## Full text

333-121-0170 Source Rack Protection If the product to be irradiated moves on a product conveyor system, the source rack and the mechanism that moves the rack must be protected by a carrier or guides to prevent products and product carriers from hitting or touching the rack or mechanism.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.675 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.675
