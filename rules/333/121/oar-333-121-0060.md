## Full text

333-121-0060 Request for Written Statements Each license is issued with the condition that the licensee will, at any time before expiration of the license, upon the Authority's request, submit a written statement to enable the Authority to determine whether the license should be modified, suspended, or revoked.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.675 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.675
