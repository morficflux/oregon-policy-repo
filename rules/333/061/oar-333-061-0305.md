## Full text

333-061-0305 Domestic Well Program: Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide a basis for implementing ORS 448.271. This law became effective on July 24, 1989, and establishes a program to provide water quality monitoring of underground aquifers that are used for domestic purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 448.131 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 448.271
