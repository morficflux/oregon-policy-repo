## Full text

333-061-0210 Operator Certification: Scope OAR 333-061-0210 through 333-061-0272 apply to community and non-transient non-community public water systems, water suppliers responsible for these types of water systems, and the operators of water treatment plants and distribution systems at community and non-transient non-community public water systems.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 448.131 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 448.450, 448.455, 448.460, 448.465 & 448.994
