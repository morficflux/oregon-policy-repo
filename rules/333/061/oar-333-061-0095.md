## Full text

333-061-0095 Severability These rules are severable, if any rule or part thereof or the application of such rule to any person or circumstance is declared invalid, that invalidity shall not affect the validity of any remaining portion of these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 448.131 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 448.131
