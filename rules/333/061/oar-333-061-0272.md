## Full text

333-061-0272 Suspension of Certification

(1) The Authority may immediately suspend an operator’s certification for violation of any portion of OAR 333-061-0005 to 333-061-0270 if the Authority finds that such violation(s) constitute a serious danger to the public health or safety. The Authority shall set forth specific reasons for such findings.

(2) An operator has 90 days from the date of notice to the operator to request a hearing. The hearing shall be held as soon as practicable if a request for hearing is received by the Authority.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 448.131 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431.110, 431.150, 448.450, 448.455 & 448.994
