## Full text

333-061-0031 Maximum Residual Disinfectant Levels MRDLs are enforceable in the same manner as maximum contaminant levels and are specified in Table 7. The MRDL for chlorine and chloramines shall be calculated as a running annual average according to OAR 333-061-0036(4)(i). The MRDL for chlorine dioxide is determined by follow-up monitoring according to OAR 333-061-0036(4)(i). [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
