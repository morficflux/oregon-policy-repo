## Full text

333-016-0130 Labeling Requirements for Accompanying Literature When any accompanying literature includes or bears any directions for use (by printed word, picture, design, or combination of such methods), such placard, pamphlet, booklet, book, sign, or other graphic or visual device shall bear all the information required by ORS 453.035(2).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.095 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.035

History: HD 40, f. 8-2-73, ef. 8-15-73
