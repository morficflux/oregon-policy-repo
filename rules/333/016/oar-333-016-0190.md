## Full text

333-016-0190 “Administrator” Intended to Include “State Public Health Officer” Wherever in the foregoing rules OAR 333-016-0005 through 333-016-0185 the term “Administrator” is used, it is intended to also include and shall be deemed to additionally refer to the “State Public Health Officer” as defined in ORS 431.045.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.095 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431.045

History: HD 40, f. 8-2-73, ef. 8-15-73
