## Full text

333-016-2035 Manufacturer Disclosure of High Priority Chemicals of Concern for Children’s Health Used in Children’s Products: Practical Quantification Limits

(1) The practical quantification limit for a chemical that is a contaminant is 100 parts per million.

(2) The practical quantification limits for intentionally added chemicals are the limits established in Exhibit A, incorporated by reference. [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
