## Full text

333-016-0120 Deceptive Use of Disclaimers A hazardous substance shall not be deemed to have met the requirements of ORS 453.035(2)(a) through (i) if there appears in or on the label or in accompanying literature, words, statements, designs, or other graphic material that in any manner negates or disclaims any of the label statements required by the Act; for example, a statement on a toxic or irritant substance, such as “harmless” or “safe around pets.”

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.095 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.035

History: HD 40, f. 8-2-73, ef. 8-15-73
