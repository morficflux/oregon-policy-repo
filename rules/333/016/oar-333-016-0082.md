## Full text

333-016-0082 Repurchase of Banned Hazardous Substances Banned hazardous toys and other banned hazardous substances shall be repurchased in accordance with ORS 453.075.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.095 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.055 & 453.075

History: HD 40-1973, f. 8-2-73, ef. 8-15-73
