## Full text

333-016-0170 Procedural Rules Procedure for the issuance, amendment, or repeal of rules declaring particular substances to be hazardous substances or banned hazardous substances shall be as specified in ORS Chapter 183 and the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure thereunder.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.095 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.095
