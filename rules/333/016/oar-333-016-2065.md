## Full text

333-016-2065 Formal Communications Regarding Toxic Free Kids Formal communication between manufacturers or trade associations and the Authority relating to waiver, chemical substitution, chemical removal, or exemption requests, and their fees, shall be to and from ToxicFreeKids.Program@dhsoha.state.or.us .

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & ORS 431A.258 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431A.258
