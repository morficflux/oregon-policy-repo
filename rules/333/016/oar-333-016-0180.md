## Full text

333-016-0180 Guaranty In case of the giving of a guaranty or undertaking referred to in ORS 453.085(5), each person signing such guaranty or undertaking or causing it to be signed, shall be considered to have given it. Each person causing a guaranty or undertaking to be false is chargeable with violation of ORS 453.085(5).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.095 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.085

History: HD 40, f. 8-2-73, ef. 8-15-73
