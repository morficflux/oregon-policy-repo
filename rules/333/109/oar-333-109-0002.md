## Full text

333-109-0002 Registration Requirements No person may receive, possess, use, transfer, own, or acquire a particle accelerator unless they apply for, and are granted, a registration issued pursuant to division 333-101.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
