## Full text

333-081-0080 NARRO Duties In addition to the requirements set out in these rules, a NARRO shall dispose of any anatomical material not returned to a relative or personal representative of the donor in accordance with all laws pertaining to the disposition of human remains. This requirement does not apply to anatomical material that the NARRO has recovered or distributed for research or educational purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 438.710 & 438.720 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 438.705-438.720 & 438.994

History: PH 6-2014, f. 1-30-14, cert. ef. 2-1-14
