## Full text

333-081-0025 Denial of License Application If the Division intends to deny a license application, it shall issue a Notice of Proposed Denial of License Application in accordance with ORS 183.411 through 183.470.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 438.710 & 438.720 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 438.705-438.720 & 438.994
