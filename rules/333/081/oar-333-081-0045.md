## Full text

333-081-0045 Return of License Each license certificate in the licensee's possession shall be returned to the Division immediately upon the suspension or revocation of the license, failure to renew the license by the date of expiration, loss of accreditation or if operation is discontinued by the voluntary action of the licensee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 438.705-438.720 & 438.994 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 438.705-438.720 & 438.994
