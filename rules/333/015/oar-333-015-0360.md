## Full text

333-015-0360 Verification of Packaging that is Child-Resistant Retailers shall provide verification of a manufacturer’s written laboratory testing report describing the results of whether packaging is child-resistant based on the protocol set forth in 16 CFR 1700.20 (1995) to the Oregon Health Authority (Authority) upon the Authority’s request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431A.175 & ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431A.175
