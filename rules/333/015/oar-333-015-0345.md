## Full text

333-015-0345 Packaging Requirements for Non-nicotine Liquid Containers A non-nicotine liquid container for sale to a consumer:

(1) Shall be child-resistant.

(2) Shall not be packaged in a manner that is attractive to minors.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431A.175 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431A.175
