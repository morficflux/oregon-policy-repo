## Full text

333-015-0064 Oregon Indoor Clean Air Act: Outdoor Smoking Areas

(1) The owner or entity in charge of a place of business may establish an outdoor smoking, aerosolizing or vaporizing of inhalants area if that area is: (a) Not within 10 feet of entrances, exits, windows that open, ventilation intakes that serve an enclosed area of any public place or place of employment, or any portion of an accessibility ramp; (b) Not, at any time, an enclosed area; and (c) In compliance with all other state, city, and county codes.

(2) Nothing in these rules shall prevent an employer from increasing the amount of property where smoking, aerosolizing or vaporizing is prohibited beyond the 10-foot requirement or from designating the entire premises as smokefree.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 433.855 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 433.835 - 433.870
