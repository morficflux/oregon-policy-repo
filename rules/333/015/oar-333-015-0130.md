## Full text

333-015-0130 Oregon Menu Labeling Act: Nutrition Information for Shared Menu Items or Shared Combination Meal Menu Items Nutrition information for menu items or combination meal menu items intended to serve multiple individuals must state the number of individuals intended to be served by the menu items or combination meal menu items and the total typical nutrient values per individual serving.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2009 OL Ch. 314 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2009 OL Ch. 314

History: PH 17-2009, f. 12-29-09, cert. ef. 1-1-10
