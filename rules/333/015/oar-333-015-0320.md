## Full text

333-015-0320 Labeling Requirements for Prefilled Inhalant Delivery Systems A label on a prefilled inhalant delivery system must conform to the labeling standards in OAR 333-015-0310, if the prefilled inhalant delivery system contains nicotine.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431A.175 & 21 CFR Part 1143.3 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431A.175
