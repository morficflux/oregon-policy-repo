## Full text

333-015-0357 Packaging Attractive to Minors An inhalant delivery system is packaged in a manner that is attractive to minors if it is packaged in a manner that is designed, intended, or reasonably appears to be attractive to minors. Features of the packaging that may reasonably appear be attractive to minors include but are not limited to the following:

(1) The shape of any animal, commercially recognizable toy, sports equipment, commercially recognizable candy, or images of minors; or

(2) A commercially recognizable toy, sports equipment, or commercially recognizable candy attached to the package.
