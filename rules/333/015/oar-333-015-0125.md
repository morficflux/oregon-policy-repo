## Full text

333-015-0125 Oregon Menu Labeling Act: Nutrition Labeling of Combination Meal Menu Items Labeling of combination meal menu items is not required as long as typical nutrient values are provided for the individual food products or menu items that comprise the combination meal menu item.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2009 OL Ch. 314 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2009 OL Ch. 314
