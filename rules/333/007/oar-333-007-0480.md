333-007-0480 Audit and Random Testing

(1) The Oregon Health Authority (Authority) may require a registrant to submit samples identified by the Authority to a laboratory of the Authority's choosing or the cannabis reference laboratory to be tested in order to determine whether a registrant is in compliance with OAR 333-007-0300 through 333-007-0600 or any other rule of the Authority.

(2) A laboratory doing audit testing under section

(1) of this rule must comply with these rules unless otherwise authorized by the Authority.

(3) The Authority may, at any time, require a registrant to permit the sampling of or submit a sample of a marijuana item to the Authority for testing. Such testing may include testing for: (a) Any microbiological contaminant. (b) Heavy metals; (c) Solvents; (d) Pesticides; (e) Mycotoxins; (f) Adulterants, additives, or other contaminants that may pose a risk to public health or safety, or is prohibited by law.

(4) The Authority may require any testing ordered under sections

(1) and

(3) of this rule to be paid for by the registrant.

(5) The Authority may obtain a marijuana item from a registrant at any time and have it tested to ensure compliance with these rules and OAR chapter 333, division 8, or to protect the public health and safety.
