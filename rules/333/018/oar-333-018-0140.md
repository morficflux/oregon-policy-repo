## Full text

333-018-0140 Health Care Acquired Infection Reporting and Public Disclosure: Prohibited Activities Unless specifically required by state or federal rules, regulations, or statutes, the Authority is prohibited from:

(1) Disclosing individually identifiable patient, health care professional, or health care facility employee information;

(2) Intentionally linking or attempting to link individual providers to individual HAI events; and

(3) Providing patient-level or provider-level reportable HAI data to any state agency for enforcement or regulatory actions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 442.420, 2007 OL Ch. 838 § 1–6 & 12 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.496, 192.502, 2007 OL Ch. 838 § 1–6 & 12
