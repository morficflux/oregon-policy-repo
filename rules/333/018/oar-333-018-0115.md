## Full text

333-018-0115 HAI Reporting for Ambulatory Surgery Centers All Ambulatory Surgical Centers must complete the Evidence-Based Elements of Patient Safety Performance (EBEPSP) Survey provided by the Authority, annually, no later than 30 days after receipt of the survey.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 442.420 & ORS 442.853-442.856 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 442.405 & ORS 442.853-442.856
