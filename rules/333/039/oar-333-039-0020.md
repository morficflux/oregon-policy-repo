## Full text

333-039-0020 Drainage

(1) The site selected for the outdoor mass gathering shall have good natural drainage. Areas which are swampy, or areas known to be susceptible to flash flooding are not acceptable.

(2) Roads at the outdoor mass gathering site shall be provided with culverts, tiles, and ditching wherever needed to protect such roads from erosion due to precipitation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 433.763 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 433.763
