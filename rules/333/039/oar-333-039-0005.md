## Full text

333-039-0005 Purpose These rules govern health and safety at outdoor mass gatherings pursuant to ORS 433.735 through 433.770. Organizers of such gatherings must apply for a permit to the county governing body of the county in which an outdoor mass gathering is to take place. Applications for permits must be accompanied by sufficiently detailed plans, specifications, and reports from which it can be determined by the county governing body and other reviewing public officials and agencies that there is or will be compliance with these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 433.740 & 433.760 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 433.740
