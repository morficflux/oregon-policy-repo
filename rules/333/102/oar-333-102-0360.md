# Special (OAR 333-102-0360)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-102-0360 Special Requirement for a Specific License to Manufacture, Assemble, Repair or Distribute Commodities, Products or Devices Which Contain Radioactive Material: Right to Cause the Withholding or Recall of Byproduct Material The Authority may cause the withholding or recall of byproduct material from any licensee who is not equipped to observe or fails to observe such safety standards to protect health as may be established by the Authority, or who uses such materials in violation of law or regulation of the Authority, or in a manner other than as disclosed in the application therefore or approved by the Authority.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 & 453.665 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
