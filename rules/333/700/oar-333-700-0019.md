## Full text

333-700-0019 Denial of License Application If the Division intends to deny a license application, it shall issue a Notice of Proposed Denial of License Application in accordance with ORS 183.411 through 183.470.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.015 & 441.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.025
