## Full text

333-700-0015 Annual License Fee For outpatient renal dialysis facilities, the annual licensing fee shall be $2,000. Each license shall be issued only for the facility named in the application and shall not be transferable. If the ownership of the agency changes, the new owner shall apply for a license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.015 & 442.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.020 & 442.025
