## Full text

333-700-0020 Expiration and Renewal of License Each license to operate an outpatient renal dialysis facility shall expire on December 31 following the date of issue, and if a renewal is desired, the licensee shall make application at least 30 days prior to the expiration date upon a form prescribed by the Division as described in OAR 333-700-0010.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.015 & 441.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.025
