## Full text

333-510-0045 Posting Requirements A hospital shall post an anti-retaliation notice on the premises that:

(1) Summarizes the provisions of ORS 441.181, 441.183, 441.184 and 441.192;

(2) Is clearly visible; and

(3) Is posted where notices to employees and applicants for employment are customarily displayed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & ORS 441.185 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.185
