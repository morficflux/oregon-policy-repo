## Full text

333-615-0000 General Complete application. To be ruled complete, an application for a psychiatric hospital must:

(1) Include a narrative organized in accordance with each major section of OAR 333-615-0000 through 333-615-0070;

(2) Satisfy the criteria specified in the Certificate of Need Application Instructions (OAR chapter 333, division 580); and

(3) Include the application fee as specified in OAR 333-565-0000, Table 4.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431.120, ORS 442.310 & ORS 442.315 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431.120 & ORS 442.315
