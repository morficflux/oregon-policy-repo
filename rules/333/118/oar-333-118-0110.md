## Full text

333-118-0110 General License: Fissile Material A general license is issued to any licensee of the Authority to transport fissile material, or to deliver fissile material to a carrier for transport, if the material is shipped in accordance with 10 CFR Part 71.22.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
