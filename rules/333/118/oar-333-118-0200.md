## Full text

333-118-0200 Quality Assurance Requirements

(1) This rule applies to general licensees only, and not to certificate of compliance holders (CoC) or applicants of CoC. Each licensee shall establish and maintain a Quality Assurance program specified by the Nuclear Regulatory Commission, 10 CFR, Subpart H, Parts 71.101 through 71.137.

(2) Licensees shall submit their Quality Assurance program or plans for review and approval to: Attn: Radiation Protection Services, 800 NE Oregon St., Suite 640, Portland Oregon, 97232.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
