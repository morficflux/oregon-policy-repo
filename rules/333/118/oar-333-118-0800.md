## Full text

333-118-0800 Referenced Materials

(1) This division of OAR chapter 333 incorporates by reference material originally published elsewhere. Certified copies of the complete text of incorporated materials referenced are available for public inspection during regular business hours at the Radiation Protection Services Office. Copies of referenced material will be provided at cost upon request. Information regarding how the incorporated material may be obtained or examined is available from Radioactive Materials Program, Radiation Protection Services, 800 NE Oregon Street, Portland, Oregon 97232.

(2) Material referenced in this division does not include amendments to or revised editions of the material published later than the effective date of the relevant section.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
