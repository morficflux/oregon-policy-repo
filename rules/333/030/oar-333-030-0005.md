# Purpose (OAR 333-030-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-030-0005 Purpose These rules prescribe the requirements for the construction, operation and use of organizational camps. They are for the purpose of protecting the health and welfare of persons using these camps. Various types of activities are found in organizational camps and the rules are designed to assure the protection of individuals consistent with those activities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.330 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.310 - 446.350
