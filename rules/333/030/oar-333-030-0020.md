## Full text

333-030-0020 Licensing Required

(1) No person shall establish, operate, manage or maintain an organizational camp without first securing a license from the Division or the local public health authority. Either the landlord or tenant may be issued a license for an organizational camp operated under contract, rental or leasehold arrangements. The license holder is responsible for compliance with these rules, with the exception of duties delegated to a tenant as specified in OAR 333-030-0023.

(2) All licenses issued under ORS 446.310 to 446.350 terminate and are renewable on December 31 of each year.
