## Full text

333-030-0130 Variance

(1) A license applicant, licensee or contracted renter may apply to the Division in writing for a variance from a requirement in OAR 333-030-0015 through 333-030-0125. In order to qualify for a variance an applicant or licensee must demonstrate, to the satisfaction of the Division, that: (a) Strict compliance with the rule would be highly burdensome or impractical due to special conditions or cause; (b) The public or private interest in granting the variance clearly outweighs the interest of the application of uniform rules; and (c) Alternative measures, if applicable, provide adequate public health and safety protection for camp participants.

(2) A variance may only be granted by the Division and not by a LPHA.
