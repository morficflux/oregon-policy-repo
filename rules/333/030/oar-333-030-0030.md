## Full text

333-030-0030 Required Fees Every applicant for an Organizational Camp license shall pay a fee of $100 unless another fee has been specified by the local public health authority.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.330 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.321
