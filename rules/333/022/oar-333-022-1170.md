## Full text

333-022-1170 CareAssist: Hearings A client who has benefits denied, restricted, or terminated has a right to a contested case hearing in accordance with ORS Chapter 183.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042, 431.250 & 431.830 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431.250 & 431.830
