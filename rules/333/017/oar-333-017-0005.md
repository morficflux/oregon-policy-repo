## Full text

333-017-0005 Reference Documents The following publication, which is available for inspection at the Public Health Division, is incorporated by reference in whole or in part in OAR chapter 333, divisions 12, 17, 18, and 19: "Investigative Guidelines": Investigative Guidelines for Reportable Diseases, published on an ongoing basis by the Division's Center for Public Health Practice.
