## Full text

333-049-0020 Purpose and Intent The purpose of these rules is to implement ORS 433.090 through 433.104, which:

(1) Authorizes the Public Health Division to develop an IIS for all children and adults born or living in Oregon; and

(2) Requires the Public Health Division to adopt rules to develop and implement the IIS, including a process by which a parent or guardian can control the transfer of information from the immunization record when such control is necessary to protect the health or safety of the family.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 433.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 433.092
