## Full text

333-049-0060 Access to Immunization Records

(1) Clients, parents and guardians of children less than 18 years of age may request a copy of their, or their child's, immunization record by submitting a request for the record, as prescribed by the program.

(2) All other authorized users shall access such records in a manner prescribed by the program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 433.094 & 433.096 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 433.094 & 433.096
