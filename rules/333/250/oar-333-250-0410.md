## Full text

333-250-0410 Civil Penalties A person that violates licensed ambulance service rules or laws, an administrative order, or settlement agreement is subject to the imposition of a civil penalty not to exceed $5,000 per violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 682.017 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 682.224

History: PH 7-2017, f. & cert. ef. 3-21-17
