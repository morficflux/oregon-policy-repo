# General Sanitation (OAR 333-029-0040)

## At a glance

OAR 333-029-0040 — General Sanitation. Chapter 333, Division 029. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-029-0040 General Sanitation All buildings, other facilities, equipment, fixtures, furnishings and the premises of travelers’ accommodations and hostels shall be kept clean, in good repair, and maintained so as to protect the health, safety, and well being of persons using those facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.330 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.330
