## Full text

333-029-0115 Fees Every applicant for a Travelers’ Accommodation or Hostel license shall pay a fee of $100 unless another fee has been specified by the local public health authority.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.321 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.310 - 446.350 & 446.990
