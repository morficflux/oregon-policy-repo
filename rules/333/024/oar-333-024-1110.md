## Full text

333-024-1110 Newborn Screening: Failure to Comply The Oregon State Public Health Laboratory may refer a hospital, freestanding birthing center or a practitioner to the appropriate licensing entity for failure to comply with these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.014, 431A.750 & 433.285 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 433.285, 433.290 & 433.295
