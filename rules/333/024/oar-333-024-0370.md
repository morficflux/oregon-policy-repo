## Full text

333-024-0370 Health Screen Testing: Purpose OAR 333-024-0370 through 333-024-0400 are for the purpose of carrying out ORS 438.010(6), ORS 438.060, and ORS 438.150. The purpose of the rules is to regulate the quality of health screen testing and to govern the issuance of permits to perform this type of testing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 438.010, 438.060 & 438.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 438.060, 438.150 & 438.310
