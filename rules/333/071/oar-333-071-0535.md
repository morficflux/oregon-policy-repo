## Full text

333-071-0535 Radiology Services An SICF, regardless of classification shall have on-site or contract radiology services that:

(1) Comply with ORS chapter 453 and rules adopted thereunder;

(2) Support the SICF’s medical and other services; and

(3) Are available on a timely basis.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.025
