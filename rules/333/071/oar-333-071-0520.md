# Laboratory (OAR 333-071-0520)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-071-0520 Laboratory Services The facility shall provide or shall have a written contract with a licensed clinical laboratory under ORS chapter 438 and OAR chapter 333, division 24, or its equivalent. A list of available tests and procedures shall be maintained.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.025
