## Full text

333-008-1680 Medical Marijuana Processors: Withdrawal of Processing Site Application An applicant for a processing site registration may withdraw an initial or renewal application at any time prior to the Authority acting on the application unless the Authority has determined that the applicant submitted false or misleading information or there is a pending investigation or enforcement action in which case the Authority may refuse to accept the withdrawal and may issue a notice of proposed denial in accordance with OAR 333-008-1670.
