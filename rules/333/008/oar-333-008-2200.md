## Full text

333-008-2200 General Requirements for Medical Marijuana Processing Sites and Dispensaries: Civil Penalties

(1) In addition to any other liability or penalty provided by law, the Authority may impose, against any person, a civil penalty that does not exceed $500 per day, for each violation of a provision of: (a) ORS 475C.833, 475C.843, or any rules adopted thereunder; (b) ORS 475C.815, 475C.824, 475C.827 or any rules adopted thereunder; or (c) OAR 333-008-1000 to 333-008-2180 or OAR chapter 333, division 7.

(2) The Authority shall impose civil penalties under this section in the manner provided by ORS 183.745.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431A.010, 475C.815, 475C.833, 475C.895 & 475C.919 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 475C.815, 475C.833 & 475C.895
