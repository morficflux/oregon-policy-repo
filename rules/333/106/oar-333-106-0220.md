## Full text

333-106-0220 Fluoroscopic X-ray Systems Requirements: Fluoroscopic Indication of Potential and Current During fluoroscopy and cinefluorography X-ray tube potential and current shall be continuously indicated. Deviation of X-ray tube potential and current from the indicated values shall not exceed the maximum deviation stated by the manufacturer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 - 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.625, 453.635 & 453.695
