## Full text

333-106-0235 Fluoroscopic X-ray Systems Requirements: Mobile Fluoroscopes In addition to the other requirements of OAR 333-106-0201 through 333-106-0245 of these rules, mobile fluoroscopes shall provide intensified imaging.
