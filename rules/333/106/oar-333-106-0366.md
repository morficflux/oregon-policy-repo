## Full text

333-106-0366 Computed Tomography X-ray Systems: Dose Limits The CTDIvol for the following CT examinations on standard phantoms shall not exceed the dose limits listed below:

(1) Adult head, 80 mGy;

(2) Adult abdomen, 30 mGy;

(3) Pediatric abdomen (five years of age or 40 pounds, 20 mGy; and

(4) Pediatric head, 40 mGy.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 - 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
