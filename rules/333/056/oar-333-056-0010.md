## Full text

333-056-0010 Purpose of Infectious Waste Administrative Rules The purpose of OAR 333-056-0020 through 333-056-0050 is to define terms related to infectious waste and to prescribe acceptable methods of storage and treatment of infectious waste.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431.110, 433.004 & 459.395 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 459.395
