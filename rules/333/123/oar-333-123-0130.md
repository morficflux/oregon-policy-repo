## Full text

333-123-0130 Superficial Electronic Brachytherapy: Refresher Training Authorized users, qualified medical physicists, and superficial electronic brachytherapy operators must complete at least four hours of annual continuing education specific to superficial electronic brachytherapy and retain copies of the continuing education records in accordance with OAR 333-100-0057 and be made available to the Authority for inspection.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 - 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
