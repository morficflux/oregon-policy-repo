## Full text

333-123-0135 Superficial Electronic Brachytherapy: Activation of the Superficial Electronic Brachytherapy X-ray Tube

(1) A superficial electronic brachytherapy machine may only be activated by a superficial electronic brachytherapy operator.

(2) An authorized user must provide personal supervision of the superficial electronic brachytherapy operator during the patient’s initial treatment when activating the X-ray tube and direct supervision of the superficial electronic brachytherapy operator for all subsequent treatments.

(3) A qualified medical physicist must be a part of all initial patient planning and provide general supervision (be available for direct communication either in person, telephone, or other electronic means) during the patient’s treatments.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 - 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
