## Full text

333-123-0001 Purpose and Scope This Division establishes requirements for registrants of therapeutic radiation machines. Nothing in this Division relieves the registrant from complying with other applicable federal, state and local regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
