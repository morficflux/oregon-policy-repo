# Recordkeeping (OAR 333-105-0710)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-105-0710 Recordkeeping Requirements: Records of Radiation Surveys Each licensee must maintain a record of each exposure device survey conducted before the device is placed in storage as specified in 333-105-0570(3). Each record must be maintained for three years after it is made.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
