## Full text

333-027-0029 Denial, Suspension, or Revocation of License

(1) The Authority may impose a civil penalty or deny, suspend or revoke an agency's license for failure to comply with ORS 443.004, 443.014 through 443.095 or these rules.

(2) If the Authority intends to impose a civil penalty or deny, suspend or revoke an agency license, it shall do so in accordance with ORS 183.411 through 183.470.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 443.085 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 443.045
