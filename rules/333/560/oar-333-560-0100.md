## Full text

333-560-0100 Facility Waiver Limitations A certificate of need facility waiver does not authorize a hospital to develop a new inpatient hospital facility or provide new long-term care services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431.120(6), 442.315 & 442.342 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431.120(6), 442.315 & 442.342
