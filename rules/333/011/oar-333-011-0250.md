## Full text

333-011-0250 Court Ordered Birth Records A certified copy of each order to establish a record of birth shall be forwarded to the State Registrar from the court clerk by the 5th and 15th working day of each month. The order shall be in the form specified by the State Registrar and shall be suitable for issuing certified copies of the birth record on a single sheet.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 432.188 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 432.188
