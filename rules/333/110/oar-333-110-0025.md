## Full text

333-110-0025 Waiver Upon application to the Authority, certain requirements of this Division may be waived or modified if it can be shown that the requirements are unnecessary or impractical in specific cases.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 - 453.745 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
