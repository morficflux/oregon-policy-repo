## Full text

333-076-0095 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish standards for the licensure of ambulatory surgical centers and extended stay centers to ensure the health and safety of individuals who receive services from these facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.025
