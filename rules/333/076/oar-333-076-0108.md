## Full text

333-076-0108 Expiration and Renewal of ASC License Each license to operate an ASC shall expire on December 31 following the date of issue, and if a renewal is desired, the licensee shall make application and pay the required renewal fee at least 30 days prior to the expiration date upon a form prescribed by the Authority as described in OAR 333-076-0102.
