## Full text

333-076-0800 Extended Stay Center (ESC) Applicability Effective January 7, 2019, an extended stay center (ESC) licensed in accordance with OAR 333-076-0820 may provide post-surgical and post-diagnostic medical and nursing services to a patient who is recovering from a surgical procedure performed in an affiliated ambulatory surgical center (ASC).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.025 & OL 2018 Chapter 50 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.015 & OL 2018 Chapter 50
