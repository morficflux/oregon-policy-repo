# Ventilation (OAR 333-170-0070)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-170-0070 Ventilation Bed and Breakfast Facilities shall be exempt from the provisions of OAR 333-150-0000 sections 6-304.11 and 6-501.14(A), however, in the event that the inspecting sanitarian determines that sufficient ventilation must be mechanical in nature, such ventilation shall be installed and operated according to state and local code.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 624.041 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 624.041
