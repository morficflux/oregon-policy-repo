# Employee Change Rooms (OAR 333-170-0040)

## At a glance

OAR 333-170-0040 — Employee Change Rooms. Chapter 333, Division 170. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-170-0040 Employee Change Rooms Bed and Breakfast Facilities shall be exempt from the provisions of OAR 333-150-0000 sections 6-305.11, 6-403.11 (B) and 6-501.110 provided, however, that no person shall change clothes, store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage or preparation of food or for utensil washing or storage.
