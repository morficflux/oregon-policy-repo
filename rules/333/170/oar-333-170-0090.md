# Utility Facilities (OAR 333-170-0090)

## At a glance

OAR 333-170-0090 — Utility Facilities. Chapter 333, Division 170. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-170-0090 Utility Facilities Bed and Breakfast Facilities shall be exempt from the provisions of OAR 333-150-0000 sections 5-203.13 and 6-306.10 provided, however, that hot water must be available for janitorial purposes. The use of handwashing lavatories, utensil-washing or equipment-washing or food preparation sinks for this purpose is prohibited.
