# Food (OAR 333-170-0110)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-170-0110 Food Source All food intended for consumption by guests shall meet the Oregon Department of Agriculture requirements as being obtained from an approved source. The use of home canned foods and meat and dairy products from unapproved sources is prohibited, and the storage of such food items shall not be allowed in any area where food is prepared or served to guests.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 624.041 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 624.041
