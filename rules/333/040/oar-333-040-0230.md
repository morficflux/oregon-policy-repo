## Full text

333-040-0230 Denial, Suspension, Revocation of License and Civil Penalties

(1) An applicant for an initial license as a Drug Laboratory Decontamination Contractor will be denied if the applicant fails to meet any of the qualifications or requirements of these rules.

(2) The Division may deny, suspend or revoke the license of any contractor pursuant to ORS 453.888, 183.310 to 183.550 and Oregon Laws 1999, Chapter 849.

(3) Denials, suspensions and revocations of licenses are contested cases subject to ORS 183 and Oregon Laws 1999, Chapter 849 and the model procedural rules of the Attorney General.
