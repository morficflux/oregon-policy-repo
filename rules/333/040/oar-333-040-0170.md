# Advice and (OAR 333-040-0170)

## At a glance

OAR 333-040-0170 — Advice and. Chapter 333, Division 040. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-040-0170 Advice and Consultation Between the dates of scheduled training for contractors as set forth in ORS 453.888, the Division shall be available to consult with contractors, as well as those planning to become contractors, on information pertinent to illegal drug manufacturing sites, including but not limited to chemicals found at such sites and their toxicity, new or revised decontamination procedures, personal protective equipment and applicable federal regulations and state rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.864 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.855 - 453.912
