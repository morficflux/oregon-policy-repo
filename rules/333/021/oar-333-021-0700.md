333-021-0700 Form for Reporting Written Request and Consent to Performance of Artificial Insemination The form, referred to as Exhibit 2 and by this reference made a part hereof, is adopted as the form for reporting written request and consent to performance of artificial insemination pursuant to ORS 677.365.
