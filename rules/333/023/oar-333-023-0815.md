# Notification to Patients (OAR 333-023-0815)

## Full text

333-023-0815 Notification to Patients Using language provided by the Authority, a pharmacy shall notify each patient receiving a controlled substance about the Prescription Drug Monitoring Program before or when the controlled substance is dispensed to the patient. The notification shall include that the prescription will be entered into the system.
