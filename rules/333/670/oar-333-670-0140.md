## Full text

333-670-0140 Burden of Proof Petitioners shall have the burden of proving by a preponderance of the evidence all adjudicative facts necessary to establish entitlement to reconsideration, or to the relief it seeks.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431.120(6) & 442.315 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431.120(6) & 442.315
