## Full text

333-125-0200 Physical Protection in Transit: Record Retention Licensees shall maintain the records that are required by the regulations in this division for the period specified by the appropriate regulation. If a retention period is not otherwise specified, these records must be retained until the Authority terminates the facility's license. All records related to this division may be destroyed upon the Authority terminating the facility’s license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.635
