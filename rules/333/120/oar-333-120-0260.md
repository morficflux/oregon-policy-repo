## Full text

333-120-0260 Storage and Control of Licensed Material: Control of Material Not in Storage The licensee must control and maintain constant surveillance of licensed material that is in a controlled or unrestricted area and that is not in storage.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
