## Full text

333-120-0540 Waste Disposal: Disposal of Specific Wastes

(1) A licensee may dispose of the following licensed material as if it were not radioactive: (a) 1.85 kBq (0.05 uCi), or less, of hydrogen-3 or carbon-14 per gram of medium used for liquid scintillation counting; and (b) 1.85 kBq (0.05 uCi), or less, of hydrogen-3 or carbon-14 per gram of animal tissue, averaged over the weight of the entire animal.

(2) A licensee may not dispose of tissue under subsection (1)(b) of this rule in a manner that would permit its use either as food for humans or as animal feed.

(3) The licensee must maintain records in accordance with OAR 333-120-0670.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
