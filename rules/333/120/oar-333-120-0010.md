# General (OAR 333-120-0010)

## Full text

333-120-0010 General Provisions: Scope The rules in this division apply to persons licensed or registered by the Authority to receive, possess, use, transfer, or dispose of licensed radioactive material or registered devices. The limits in this division do not apply to doses due to background radiation, to exposure of patients to radiation for the purpose of medical diagnosis or therapy, or to voluntary participation in medical research programs.
