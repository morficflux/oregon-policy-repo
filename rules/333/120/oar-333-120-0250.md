## Full text

333-120-0250 Storage and Control of Licensed Material: Security of Stored Material

(1) The licensee must secure from unauthorized removal or access licensed materials that are stored in controlled or unrestricted areas.

(2) The registrant must secure registered radiation machines from unauthorized removal.

(3) The registrant must use devices or administrative procedures to prevent unauthorized use of registered radiation machines.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
