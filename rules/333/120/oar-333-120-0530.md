333-120-0530 Waste Disposal: Treatment of Disposal by Incineration A licensee may treat or dispose of licensed material by incineration only in the amounts and forms specified in OAR 333-120-0540 or as specifically approved by the Authority pursuant to OAR 333-120-0510.
