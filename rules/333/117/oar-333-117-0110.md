## Full text

333-117-0110 Protection of Workers During Operations Each person subject to the general license in OAR 333-117-0100 shall conduct operations in compliance with the standards for radiation protection set out in division 111 and division 120, except for releases of radioactivity in effluents, which shall be governed by 333-117-0120 and disposal, which shall be governed by 333-117-0130.
