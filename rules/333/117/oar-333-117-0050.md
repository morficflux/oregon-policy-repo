# Effective Date (OAR 333-117-0050)

## At a glance

OAR 333-117-0050 — Effective Date. Chapter 333, Division 117. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-117-0050 Effective Date The provision and requirements of this division shall take effect January 1990 and shall apply to all facilities or sites owned or controlled by a person on that date. Products distributed and disposals made prior to that date are not subject to the provisions of this division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 - 453.745 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807

History: HD 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 1-8-91
