## Full text

333-117-0010 Purpose This division establishes radiation protection standards for the possession, use, transfer and disposal of naturally occurring radioactive materials (NORM) not subject to regulation under the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 - 453.775 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
