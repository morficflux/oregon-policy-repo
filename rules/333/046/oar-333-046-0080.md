# Record (OAR 333-046-0080)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-046-0080 Record Keeping Clinics must keep all records related to the Vaccines for Children (VFC) program or Vaccine Access Program (VAP) for a minimum of three years and make them available to the Oregon Health Authority for review upon request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 431.250 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 413.042 & 431.250
