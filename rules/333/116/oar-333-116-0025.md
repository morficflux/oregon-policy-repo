## Full text

333-116-0025 FDA, Other Federal, and State Requirements Nothing in this part relieves the licensee from complying with applicable FDA, other Federal, and State requirements governing radiopharmaceutical drugs or devices.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
