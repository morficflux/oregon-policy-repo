## Full text

333-116-0550 Viewing System A licensee must construct or equip each teletherapy room to permit continuous observation of the patient from the teletherapy unit console during irradiation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
