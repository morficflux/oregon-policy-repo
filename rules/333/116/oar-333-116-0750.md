## Full text

333-116-0750 Physician Training in a Three Month Program A physician who, before July 1, 1984, began a three month nuclear medicine training program approved by the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education and has successfully completed the program is exempted from the requirements of OAR 333-116-0660 or 333-116-0670.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
