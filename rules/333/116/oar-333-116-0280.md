## Full text

333-116-0280 Storage of Volatiles and Gases

(1) A licensee must store volatile radiopharmaceuticals and radioactive gases in the shipper’s radiation shield and container.

(2) A licensee must store and use a multidose container in a properly functioning fume hood.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
