## Full text

333-116-0650 Training for Experienced Radiation Safety Officer An individual identified as a Radiation Safety Officer on an Agency, Agreement State, Licensing State or U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission license on July 1, 2006 who oversees only the use of radioactive material for which the licensee was authorized on that date need not comply with the training requirements of OAR 333-116-0640.
