## Full text

333-116-0620 Reports of Teletherapy Surveys, Checks, Tests and Measurements A licensee must furnish a copy of the records required in OAR 333-116-0590, 333-116-0600, 333-116-0610 and the output from the teletherapy source expressed as rem (Sv) per hour at one meter from the source and determined during the full calibration required in OAR 333-116-0570 to the Authority within 30 days following completion of the action that initiated the record requirement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
