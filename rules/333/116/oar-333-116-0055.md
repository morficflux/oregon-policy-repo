## Full text

333-116-0055 Exemptions Regarding Type A Specific Licenses of Broad Scope A licensee possessing a Type A specific license of broad scope for medical use is exempt from:

(1) The provisions of OAR 333-116-0040(2);

(2) The provisions of OAR 333-116-0040(7) regarding additions to or changes in areas of use only at the addresses specified in the license;

(3) The provisions of OAR 333-116-0050(1);

(4) The provisions of OAR 333-116-0050(2)(a) for an authorized user, ophthalmic physicist or authorized nuclear pharmacist, and

(5) The provisions of OAR 333-116-0140(1).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
