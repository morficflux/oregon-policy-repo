# Rubidium-82 (OAR 333-116-0870)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-116-0870 Rubidium-82 Generator Rubidium-82 generators require quality assurance procedures for equipment, patient injection, waiting area, imaging, and post-imaging care. There also must be a procedure for spills, and a handling procedure for liquid quality assurance sources for early model PET cameras. Dose calibration procedures are the same as in OAR 333-116-0850(6).
