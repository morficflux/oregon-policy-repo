## Full text

333-116-0485 Other Medical Uses of Byproduct Material or Radiation from Byproduct Material A licensee shall use byproduct or a radiation source not specifically addressed in OAR 333-116-0300 through 333-116-0480 in accordance with the manufacturer’s radiation safety and operating instructions.
