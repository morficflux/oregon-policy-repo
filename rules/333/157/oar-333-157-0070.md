## Full text

333-157-0070 Licensing Any license issued by the Authority pursuant to ORS Chapter 624 shall expire and may be reinstated on December 31 of each year; except for temporary restaurant licenses issued pursuant to ORS 624.082, 624.084 and 624.086.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 624.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 624.100 – 624.130
