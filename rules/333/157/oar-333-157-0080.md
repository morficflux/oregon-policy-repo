## Full text

333-157-0080 Fees

(1) Fees for eating and drinking establishments and other food service activities subject to ORS Chapter 624 shall be as specified in ORS Chapter 624.

(2) Any restaurant providing food or beverage solely to children, elderly persons, indigent or other needy populations shall not be required to pay a restaurant license fee to the Authority if such restaurant is: (a) Operated by a benevolent organization as defined in ORS 624.101; and (b) The patrons or recipients are not required to pay the full cost of the food or beverage.

(3) A restaurant that meets the criteria in section

(2) of this rule must still obtain a restaurant license and must comply with OAR 333-150-0000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 624.100 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 624.100 – 624.130
