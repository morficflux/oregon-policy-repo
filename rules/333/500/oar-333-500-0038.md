## Full text

333-500-0038 Denial of License Application If the Oregon Health Authority intends to deny a license application, it shall issue a Notice of Proposed Denial of License Application in accordance with ORS 183.411 through 183.470.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.037
