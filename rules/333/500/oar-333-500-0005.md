# Applicability (OAR 333-500-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 333-500-0005 — Applicability. Chapter 333, Division 500. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-500-0005 Applicability Unless a specific rule provides otherwise, OAR chapter 333, divisions 500 through 535 apply to a hospital classified as general, low occupancy acute care, or psychiatric or mental and do not apply to a hospital classified as a special inpatient care facility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.025
