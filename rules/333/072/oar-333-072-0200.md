# Purpose (OAR 333-072-0200)

## Full text

333-072-0200 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish notice requirements for patient choice and financial interest as required in ORS 441.098 when health practitioners refer patients for diagnostic testing or health care treatment or services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 441.098 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 441.098

History: PH 15-2014, f. & cert. ef. 6-2-14
