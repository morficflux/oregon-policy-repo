# Records (OAR 333-100-0055)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-100-0055 Records Each licensee and registrant must maintain records showing the receipt, transfer, and disposal of all sources of radiation. Additional record requirements are specified elsewhere in these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
