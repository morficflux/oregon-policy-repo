# Interpretations (OAR 333-100-0015)

## Full text

333-100-0015 Interpretations Except as specifically authorized by the Agency in writing, no interpretation of the meaning of these rules by any officer or employee of the Agency, other than a written interpretation, will be recognized to be binding upon the Agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
