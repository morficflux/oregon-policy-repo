# Additional (OAR 333-100-0030)

## Full text

333-100-0030 Additional Requirements The Agency may, by rule, regulation, or order, impose upon any licensee or registrant such requirements in addition to those established in these rules as it deems appropriate or necessary to minimize danger to public health and safety or property.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
