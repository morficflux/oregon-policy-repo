## Full text

333-010-0080 Cancer Reporting Regulations: Training and Consultation The Authority shall provide annual continuing education for interested persons involved in cancer registry reporting. Continuing education content must include, but is not limited to, cancer diagnosis and management, epidemiology and statistics, and hardware and software registry applications. The central cancer registry staff must supplement the continuing education with one-on-one consultations to assist health care facilities and practitioners as needed in meeting the reporting requirements.
