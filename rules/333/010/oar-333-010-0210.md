## Full text

333-010-0210 Client Eligibility A person must meet the following WW Program eligibility criteria in order to be enrolled in the WW Program:

(1) Be a woman 40 to 64 years of age; and

(2) Be enrolled in the BCCP program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 413.042 & 431.250
