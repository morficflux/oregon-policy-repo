## Full text

333-010-0290 Provider Appeals (Level 2) — Contested Case Hearing An enrolling or ancillary provider may request a contested case hearing within 30 calendar days of the date of a decision affecting the provider. Contested case hearings will be held in accordance with ORS Chapter 183 and the Attorney General’s model rules, OAR 137-003-0501 through 137-003-0700.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 413.042 & 431.250
