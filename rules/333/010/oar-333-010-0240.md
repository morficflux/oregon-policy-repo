## Full text

333-010-0240 Excluded Services

(1) Services and laboratory tests not directly related to heart disease, stroke and diabetes, or not included in the ICD-10 and CPT code lists provided in the WW Program Manual are not covered by the WW Program.

(2) No payment will be made for any expense incurred for any other medical service or laboratory tests whose primary purpose is for a reason other than heart disease, stroke, or diabetes screening and prevention.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 413.042 & 431.250
