## Full text

333-010-0230 Submission of Information by Ancillary Providers Ancillary providers shall provide results of services to enrolled providers within 14 calendar days from the date of service.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 413.042 & 431.150
