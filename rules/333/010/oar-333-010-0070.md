## Full text

333-010-0070 Cancer Reporting Regulations: Advisory Committee The Authority shall appoint an advisory committee to review the operations of the central registry and to make recommendations regarding registry policy, and to review research protocols for which confidential and privileged data are requested. The composition of the advisory committee must generally represent those with a professional or personal interest in cancer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 432.510 & 432.520 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 432.510
