## Full text

333-010-0810 Dental Pilot Projects: Discontinuation or Completion of Project

(1) An approved project must notify the Authority in writing if it intends to discontinue its status as a Dental Pilot Project, at least 60 calendar days prior to discontinuation. Notification must include a closing report that includes, but is not limited to: (a) The reasons for discontinuation as a pilot project; (b) A summary of pilot project activities including the number of persons who entered the employment/utilization phase; and (c) A description of the plan to inform trainees of the project’s discontinuation and that they are precluded from performing the skills authorized under the pilot project after discontinuation unless the provider type has been legalized by the State of Oregon.

(2) The project must obtain written acknowledgement from trainees regarding notification of the project’s discontinuation and preclusion from performing skills authorized under the pilot project after discontinuation, unless the provider type has been legalized and the trainee has met necessary licensure requirements.

(3) Project completion. A project sponsor must provide a full report of findings to the Authority within 180 calendar days of the completion of the project in a format prescribed by the Authority.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2011 OL Ch. 716 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2011 OL Ch. 716
