# Purpose (OAR 333-031-0003)

## Full text

333-031-0003 Purpose These rules adopted pursuant to the provisions of ORS 446.330 prescribe the requirements for the construction and operation of recreation parks. They are for the purpose of protecting the health and welfare of persons using these facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.0315 Statutes/Other Implemented: 446.310 - 446.350, 446.435 & 446.990
