# Special (OAR 333-031-0072)

## At a glance

OAR 333-031-0072 — Special. Chapter 333, Division 031. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

333-031-0072 Special Conditions The Administrator may exempt any requirements of the rules for toilets, waste water disposals and spacing, to meet special short-term campground needs either arising annually during hunting season or other similar special situations if he finds that public health will not be endangered.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.330 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.330
