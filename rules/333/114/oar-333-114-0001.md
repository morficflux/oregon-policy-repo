# General (OAR 333-114-0001)

## Full text

333-114-0001 General The purpose of these rules is to insure that the response to a radioactive material accident be both swift and appropriate to minimize damage to any person, property, domestic animals, wildlife or the environment.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.755

History: HD 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 1-8-91 HD 10-1987, f. & ef. 7-28-87
