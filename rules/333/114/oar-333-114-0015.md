## Full text

333-114-0015 Radioactive/Hazardous Materials Emergency Response Exercise This is defined as a practice response to an accident involving radioactive or other hazardous materials. The exercise should involve all organizations that would normally be present in a real incident, including but not limited to: Firefighting, law enforcement, prehospital and hospital emergency medical care, highway maintenance and state technical support agencies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.775
