## Full text

333-101-0250 Hospital Radiology Inspector Accreditation Revocation The Authority may condition, suspend, revoke, or refuse to renew accreditation of a radiology inspector for the following reasons:

(1) Knowingly falsifying information included on the inspection report form supplied by the Authority.

(2) Substantially failing to comply with Authority procedures.

(3) Failing to meet Authority accuracy requirements.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.790 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 - 453.807
