## Full text

333-025-0150 Retention of Genetic Information for the Purpose of Testing to Benefit Blood Relatives of Deceased Individuals Any person may retain the genetic information of a deceased individual indefinitely for the sole purpose of benefiting blood relatives of the deceased individual without specific authorization.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.535 & 192.537 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.535 & 192.537
