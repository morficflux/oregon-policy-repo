## Full text

333-025-0115 Informed Consent for Non-Exempt Genetic Research

(1) Except as provided in OAR 333-025-0120, a researcher may use an identified human biological sample or genetic information obtained on or after June 25, 2001, for genetic research only with specific informed consent for genetic research.

(2) Except as provided in OAR 333-025-0120, a researcher may use an identified human biological sample or genetic information obtained prior to June 25, 2001, for genetic research with blanket informed consent or specific informed consent for genetic research.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.535 & 192.547 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.535 & 192.547
