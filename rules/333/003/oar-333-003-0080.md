## Full text

333-003-0080 Effect of Declaration Ending Immediately upon termination of the declaration of the public health emergency, all actions taken pursuant to these rules are terminated unless an emergency related to the declaration has been proclaimed under ORS 401.055 or continuation of the actions is otherwise authorized by law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 433.441 - 433.452
