## Full text

333-003-0100 Volunteer Registry: Scope The rules in OAR 333-003-0100 to 333-003-0210 pertain to the registration and deployment of health care providers and support and administrative personnel to provide health care, public health and supportive services and to the designation of emergency health care centers under ORS 401.651 to 401.670 during a declared emergency.
