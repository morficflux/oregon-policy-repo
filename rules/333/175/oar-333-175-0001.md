## Full text

333-175-0001 Purpose The food handler training program rules set fees and address requirements for successful completion of a food handler training program and issuance of the certificate. Additionally, these rules set out general provisions regarding food handler training programs and provide guidelines for the relationship between the Oregon Health Authority, Local Public Health Authorities and Designated Agents.
