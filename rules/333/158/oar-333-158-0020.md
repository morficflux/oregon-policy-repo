# Licenses and (OAR 333-158-0020)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-158-0020 Licenses and Permits Licenses and permits issued pursuant to these rules shall be subject to the statutes of the licensing and inspecting agency, including fees and legal remedies, and shall be deemed to satisfy the licensing statutes of the other agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 624.530 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 624.530
