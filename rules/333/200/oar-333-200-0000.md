## Full text

333-200-0000 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish the procedures and standards for the development and maintenance of a comprehensive statewide trauma system as set forth under ORS 431A.050 through 431A.105.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431A.050 - 431A.105
