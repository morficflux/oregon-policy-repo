# Authority Responsibilities (OAR 333-200-0275)

## Full text

333-200-0275 Authority Responsibilities When requested, the Authority shall provide statistical reports in formats prescribed by the Authority in consultation with the STAB, to the STAB and ATAB Quality Improvement Committees within 90 days of the close of the calendar quarter following receipt of the data submitted pursuant to OAR 333-200-0265(8).
