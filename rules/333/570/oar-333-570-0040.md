## Full text

333-570-0040 Additional Information Following receipt of a complete certificate of need application, the division may request or accept additional information only if such information is necessary for the division to make an equitable and informed decision through application of applicable rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 431.120(6) & 442.315 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 431.120(6) & 442.315
