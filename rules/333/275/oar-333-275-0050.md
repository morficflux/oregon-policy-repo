## Full text

333-275-0050 Hemodialysis Technician Certification Expiration Each certification issued, unless sooner suspended or revoked, shall expire on June 30 of odd numbered years.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.650 & 688.665 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.650
