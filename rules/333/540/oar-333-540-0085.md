## Full text

333-540-0085 Return of Agency License A caregiver registry shall return its license to the Division immediately if the registry:

(1) Voluntarily decides to stop operating a registry;

(2) Has its license suspended or revoked; or

(3) Fails to renew its license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 443.105 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 443.045
