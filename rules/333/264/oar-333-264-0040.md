## Full text

333-264-0040 Eligibility, Application and Approval of Emergency Medical Responder (EMR) Courses

(1) The Oregon Health Authority (Authority) is responsible for approving EMR courses.

(2) An ambulance service or any other entity in Oregon may conduct an EMR course that meets the requirements of OAR 333-264-0050, if approved by the Authority.

(3) An ambulance service or entity that wants to conduct an EMR course must submit an application to the Authority on a form prescribed by the Authority that includes all the information necessary to determine whether the course meets the standards prescribed in OAR 333-264-0050 and whether the course director meets the requirements in OAR 333-264-0100. The form must be received by the Authority at least 30 business days prior to the first day of class.

(4) The Authority shall return an application that is incomplete to the applicant.

(5) No entity shall conduct an EMR course until the Authority has approved the course.

(6) The Authority may deny or revoke the approval to conduct an EMR course in accordance with ORS chapter 183 and these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 682.017 & ORS 682.208 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 682.216, ORS 682.208 & ORS 682.017
