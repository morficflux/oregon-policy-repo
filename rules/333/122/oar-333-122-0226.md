## Full text

333-122-0226 General Requirements for All RGD: Records of Radiation Survey Instruments Each registrant must maintain records of the calibrations of its radiation survey instruments that are required under OAR 333-122-0210 and retain each record for three years after it is made.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
