## Full text

333-122-0065 General Requirements for All RGD: X-ray Tube Registration

(1) No RGD shall be operated unless the applicant satisfies the general requirements specified in OAR 333-101-0005 and 333-103-0005.

(2) Upon determination that an applicant meets the requirements of this division, the Authority must issue a registration and validation certificate as specified in OAR 333-101-0035.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
