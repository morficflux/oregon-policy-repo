## Full text

333-122-0001 Purpose OAR chapter 333, division 122 prescribes requirements for the non-healing arts radiation generating devices (RGD) use of X-ray machines and radiation safety requirements for persons using RGD X-ray equipment.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
