# General (OAR 333-122-0055)

## At a glance

## Full text

333-122-0055 General Requirements for All RGD: Labeling All RGD equipment shall be labeled with a readily discernible sign or signs bearing the radiation symbol and the words:

(1) CAUTION -- HIGH INTENSITY X-RAY BEAM , or words having a similar intent, on the X-ray source housing; and

(2) CAUTION RADIATION -- THIS EQUIPMENT PRODUCES RADIATION WHEN ENERGIZED , or words having a similar intent, near any switch that energizes an X-ray tube if the radiation source is an X-ray tube.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
