## Full text

333-122-0120 General Requirements for All RGD: Posting.

(1) Each area or room containing X-ray equipment shall be conspicuously posted with a sign or signs bearing the radiation symbol and the words CAUTION -- X-RAY EQUIPMENT , or words having a similar intent in accordance with OAR 333-120-0400 .

(2) All areas in which X-ray machines are operated must be conspicuously posted as required by 333-120-0410 .

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
