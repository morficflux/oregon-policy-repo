## Full text

333-122-0310 X-ray and Hybrid Gauges: Surveillance During each radiographic operation, the X-ray machine operator must ensure continuous direct visual surveillance of the operation to protect against unauthorized entry into a radiation area, as defined in OAR 333-100-0005(106), except where all entryways are locked and inaccessible during X-ray operations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
