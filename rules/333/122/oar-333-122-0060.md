## Full text

333-122-0060 General Requirements for All RGD: Shutters

(1) On open-beam configurations installed after January 1, 1978, each port on the radiation source housing shall be equipped with a shutter that cannot be opened unless a collimator or a coupling has been connected to the port.

(2) A visible indication of the status of the shutter shall be provided, example being a red-light indicating beam "on" or a green light indicating beam "off". (a) The device shall be tested, no less than every six months, to ensure operations will not continue without a properly functioning warning device. (b) On equipment installed after January 1, 1978, this device shall be of fail-safe characteristics.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.605 – 453.807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.605 – 453.807
