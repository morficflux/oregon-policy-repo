## Full text

333-035-0270 Formal Enforcement

(1) If during an investigation or survey Authority staff document a substantial failure to comply with hospice program licensing laws or conditions of participation, or if a hospice program fails to pay a civil penalty imposed under ORS 443.869 and these rules, the Authority may issue a Notice of Proposed Suspension or Notice of Proposed Revocation in accordance with ORS 183.411 through 183.471.

(2) The Authority may issue a Notice of Imposition of Civil Penalty for violations of hospice program licensing laws.

(3) At any time the Authority may issue a Notice of Emergency License Suspension under ORS 183.430.

(4) If the Authority revokes a hospice program license, the order shall specify when, if ever, the hospice program may reapply for a license.

(5) The Authority may reinstate a hospice program license that has been suspended after the Authority determines that compliance with these rules have been achieved.
