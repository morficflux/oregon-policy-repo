## Full text

257-035-0005 Use of Crime Detection Laboratories Equipment “Services” as used in ORS 181.080 means the operation of the equipment by authorized Department personnel and excludes the private use of laboratory facilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.080

History: OSP 2-1985, f. & ef. 10-1-85
