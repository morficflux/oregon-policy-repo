## Full text

257-070-0005 Statement of Purpose The purpose of these rules is to implement and interpret ORS 163A.005 to 163A.235. The Oregon State Police is authorized to adopt rules interpreting and implementing the described statutes. These rules establish standards and procedures for victim and public access to sex offender information.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 & ORS 192.318 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 163A.005-163A.235
