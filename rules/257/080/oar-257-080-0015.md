## Full text

257-080-0015 Grounds for Removal Because members subject to these rules hold their position as “at-will” employees, the member’s employment is not subject to any cause standard and the Department legally retains discretion in making a decision on removal.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.280 & 181.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.290 - 181.340
