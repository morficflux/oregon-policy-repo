## Full text

257-050-0130 Appeal A tow business or qualified tow business aggrieved by the decision of the Hearings Program officer denying, suspending, or revoking a letter of appointment must make any further appeal of such decision to the Oregon Court of Appeals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.440 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.440
