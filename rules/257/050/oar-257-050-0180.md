## Full text

257-050-0180 Judicial Review A tow business or qualified tow business aggrieved by a final order of the Department denying, suspending, or revoking a letter of appointment may seek judicial review of such decision in the Oregon Court of Appeals. The denial, suspension or revocation shall remain in effect during the appeal.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.440 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.440
