## Full text

257-050-0095 Letter of Appointment The letter of appointment shall be completed by the Department establishing that the tow business has met the requirements for a letter of appointment. The designated tow zone(s), class of tow vehicle(s), specific vehicles authorized to operate in each zone, and any waivers will be listed in the letter.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.440 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.440
