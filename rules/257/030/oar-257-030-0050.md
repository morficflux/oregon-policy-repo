## Full text

257-030-0050 Definitions As used in these rules, “Chemical Test” and “Chemical Analysis” both mean a quantitative analysis for alcohol by means of direct or indirect measurement of physicochemical technique performed on a sample of breath.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 & 813.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 813.160
