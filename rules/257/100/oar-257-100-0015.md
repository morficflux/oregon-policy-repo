## Full text

257-100-0015 Product Liability for Ignition Interlock Devices

(1) Each device manufacturer or manufacturer’s representative must have product liability insurance with minimum liability limits of $1,000,000 per occurrence and $3,000,000 in the aggregate throughout the certification period. A manufacturer or manufacturer’s representative may not cancel, materially change, or fail to renew the coverage of the insurance company.

(2) Failure to correct any discrepancies with product liability insurance within 60 calendar days from written notification from OSP, shall result in OSP removing the device from the Oregon list of qualified devices.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 813.600 & ORS 813.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 813.599 – 813.680
