## Full text

257-015-0070 System Security and Privacy The data stored in the LEDS, NCIC, and other criminal justice information system files is documented criminal justice information. This information must be protected to ensure its integrity and its correct, legal and efficient storage, dissemination and use. It is incumbent upon and agency operating a LEDS terminal, or a terminal on another system which has access to the LEDS network, to implement the procedures necessary to make the terminal secure from any unauthorized use. All agency personnel authorized to access the LEDS network must be instructed in the proper use and dissemination of the information.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181A.280(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181A.280
