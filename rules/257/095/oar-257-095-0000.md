## Full text

257-095-0000 Purpose of Rules Rules adopted herein prescribe the policies and procedures for operation and use of the statewide School Safety Tip Line Program (SSTL). The SSTL is established to facilitate the safety and health of students.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 165.570(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 165.570
