# Anonymity (OAR 257-095-0090)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

257-095-0090 Anonymity Persons submitting a tip via the SSTL may choose to identify themselves or to remain anonymous. The identity of persons making reports who choose to identify themselves shall be protected and disclosed only as set out in OAR 257-095-0080(2).

Statutory/Other Authority: OL 2016, ch 74 & section 1(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 2016 & ch 74
