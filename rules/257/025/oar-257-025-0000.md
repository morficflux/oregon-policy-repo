## Full text

257-025-0000 Statement of Purpose The purpose of these rules is to implement and interpret ORS Chapters 801 and 813 with regard to field sobriety tests. The Department of State Police is empowered to make rules interpreting and implementing the described statutes. It is intended by these rules to standardize field sobriety tests throughout the state.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 801.272 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 801.272 & 813.135
