# Casual (OAR 656-010-0025)

## At a glance

## Full text

656-010-0025 Casual Sales Casual sales by a producer of fish and shellfish taken (produced) by ocean trawling shall not be subject to the assessment due and payable to the Oregon Trawl Commission. "Casual Sales," as referred to in ORS 576.325 is defined as sale or sales of fish and shellfish sold by the producer direct to a consumer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576
