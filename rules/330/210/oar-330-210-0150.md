## Full text

330-210-0150 Compliance and Pass-through

(1) All participants in this program are subject to OAR 330-230. If the tax credit is subject to recertification under OAR 330-210-0110, only that portion of the tax credit that has been certified or recertified may be transferred at the five-year present value rate in OAR 330-230-0130.

(2) The department may periodically inspect energy conservation projects and related documents during the five-year term of the tax credit.
