## Full text

330-060-0070 Oil Audit Standards: Calculation Procedures

(1) The energy savings from energy conservation measures shall be calculated by the Oregon Department of Energy.

(2) The Oregon Department of Energy may approve use of alternate energy savings calculations, if the calculations and the methodology supporting those calculations are submitted to the Oregon Department of Energy for its review and approval.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.040 & 469.165 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.675
