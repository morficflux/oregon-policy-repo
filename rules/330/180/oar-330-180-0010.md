## Full text

330-180-0010 Purpose and Scope These rules provide procedures to assist the governing boards of consumer-owned utilities to comply with the greenhouse gas emissions performance standard for electricity established in ORS 757.522 to 757.536.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 757.522 - 757.538 & 469.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 757.522 - 757.538
