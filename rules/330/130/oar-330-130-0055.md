# Procedure for (OAR 330-130-0055)

## At a glance

## Full text

330-130-0055 Procedure for Leased Buildings The department, in consultation with the agencies, shall establish guidelines for incorporating energy efficiency requirements into lease agreements of 10 years or more to be phased in as current leases expire or as agencies enter into new agreements.

Statutory/Other Authority: Ch. 26 & OL 2008 HB 3612 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 276.900 - 276.915
