## Full text

330-170-0030 Applicant Eligibility To be eligible for certification, the applicant must:

(1) Be subject to taxation under ORS 316, 317 or 318;

(2) Be an agricultural producer or biomass collector;

(3) Have title to the biomass at the time the biomass is delivered to a biofuel producer;

(4) Produce or collect the biomass in Oregon; and

(5) Deliver or cause the delivery of the biomass to be: (a) Used as biofuel in Oregon; or (b) Used to produce biofuel in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 351.141 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 315.141 & 469B.403
