# Minimum Discount Value (OAR 330-170-0070)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

330-170-0070 Minimum Discount Value The minimum discounted value of a tax credit issued under ORS 315.141 is 90 percent of the amount of the tax credit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 351.141 & 469.791 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 351.141 & 469.791

History: DOE 8-2011, f. 11-4-11, cert. ef. 1-1-12 DOE 13-2010, f. & cert. ef. 11-2-10 DOE 9-2010(Temp), f. & cert. ef. 7-1-10 thru 12-28-10
