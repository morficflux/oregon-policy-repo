## Full text

330-160-0038 Expiration of Oregon RPS Certification Except as otherwise indicated in these rules, a facility’s Oregon RPS certification will not have an expiration date.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469A.130 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469A.130 - 469A.145
