## Full text

330-068-0040 Other Sources of Payment The Department shall not pay for measures funded by a cost sharing grant under the U.S. Department of Energy Institutional Buildings Grants Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469

History: DOE 3-1985, f. & ef. 3-20-85 DOE 5-1984, f. & ef. 4-3-84 DOE 11-1982, f. & ef. 12-17-82
