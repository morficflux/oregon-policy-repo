# Purpose (OAR 330-068-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 330-068-0005 — Purpose. Chapter 330, Division 068. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

330-068-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards and criteria for administration by the Oregon Department of Energy of a contract agreement with the Bonneville Power Administration to acquire electricity energy savings through subcontracts with schools, hospitals, public care, government buildings and eligible facilities other than buildings.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469

History: DOE 3-1985, f. & ef. 3-20-85 DOE 5-1984, f. & ef. 4-3-84 DOE 11-1982, f. & ef. 12-17-82
