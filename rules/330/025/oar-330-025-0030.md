## Full text

330-025-0030 Rejection of Deficient Submission If the resource plan submitted by an electric Utility fails to satisfy requirements of OAR 330-025-0020, the Director shall reject the submission and require the Utility to submit a new plan which remedies the deficiencies. The Director may specify the date by which the resubmission shall be made.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.080

History: DOE 4-1982, f. & ef. 3-12-82
