## Full text

330-025-0025 Supplemental Information The Director may, by letter, require a utility to submit as part of the resource plan supplemental information assessing the effectiveness of resource development efforts, including conservation and renewable resources programs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.080
