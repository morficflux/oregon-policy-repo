## Full text

330-112-0000 Purpose and Objectives These rules carry out provisions of ORS Chapter 470 as they pertain to the administration by the Oregon Department of Energy of the Energy Efficiency and Sustainable Technology Act of 2009. Oregon Administrative Rule, chapter 330, division 112 sets out the rules governing the department’s energy efficiency and sustainable technology loan program. The purpose of the program is to provide financing for energy efficiency upgrades of residential and commercial buildings in the State of Oregon.
