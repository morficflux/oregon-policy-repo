## Full text

330-112-0070 Standards for Contractors during Pilot Programs Contractors without certification may work on projects under pilot programs if no certified contractor is available, and the PPFA or sustainable energy project manager has approved the contractor. The contractor must pay wages to employees used for pilot projects at a rate equal to at least 180 percent of the state minimum wage or, if the project is for a commercial structure or is subject to prevailing wage laws, the prevailing wage for each trade or occupation employed.
