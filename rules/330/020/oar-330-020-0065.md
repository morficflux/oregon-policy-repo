# Substitution of Data (OAR 330-020-0065)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

330-020-0065 Substitution of Data Any energy supplier may, in lieu of submitting a form required by OAR 330-020-0035, submit a report it made to another governmental agency, if the alternative report submitted contains all of the information required by, and is clearly cross-indexed to, the form for which it is substituted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.080
