## Full text

330-020-0070 Request for Confidential Treatment If any form described in OAR 330-020-0035 requests proprietary information, the energy supplier may request that the information be received and maintained on a confidential basis (see the procedures in Department of Energy rule 330-010-0090). A request for confidential treatment must be made by the energy supplier no later than the date on which the information is submitted in accordance with the submission deadline contained in 330-020-0050.
