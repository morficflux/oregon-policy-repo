## Full text

330-020-0030 Forms — General With the exception of the provisions of OAR 330-020-0065, all information required to be submitted by ORS 469.080(1), or by these rules shall be submitted on forms provided by the Department and described in these rules. The information required by each form shall be determined by the provisions of OAR 330-020-0035. The actual forms provided by the Department may vary as to format and phrasing from the descriptions provided in 330-020-0035 in any manner consistent with these rules, and may contain such notation as may facilitate computer data entry.
