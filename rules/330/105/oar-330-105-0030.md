## Full text

330-105-0030 Procedure for Appeal Introduction The Director will decide the amount of any loan. If the decision is adverse, the Committee may appeal to the Governor.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 & 470.140 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 470.090 - 470.100
