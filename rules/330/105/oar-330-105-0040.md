## Full text

330-105-0040 Appeal to the Committee An applicant may ask the Committee to review an adverse decision. The request must be made in writing to the Secretary within 10 working days after the date of the notice of the Director's decision. It must give the grounds for review in detail. The Secretary will send the request promptly to the Committee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 & 470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 470.100
