## Full text

330-105-0005 Definitions Where context permits, these definitions apply to ORS Chapter 470 and these rules:

(1) "Application" or "Request" means an application submitted on a Department-approved form and its supporting papers.

(2) "Adverse Decision" means a decision to deny a loan application or to make a loan in an amount contrary to advice of the Committee.

(3) "Committee" means the Small Scale Local Energy Project Advisory Committee created under ORS 470.070.

(4) "Secretary" means the Secretary to the Committee. He or she will be an employee chosen by the Director.

(5) The definitions contained in OAR 330-110 and ORS 470.050, as amended, apply to these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 & 470.140 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 470.050 - 470.100
