# Compliance with Land Use Laws (OAR 330-125-0045)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

330-125-0045 Compliance with Land Use Laws Any party receiving an incentive shall agree to comply with all local land use ordinances and building codes and to obtain all necessary permits for construction of a residence. A portion or all of the incentive payment may be withheld until an occupancy permit is issued by the local jurisdiction.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469

History: DOE 3-1984, f. & ef. 1-30-84
