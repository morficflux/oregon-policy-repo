## Full text

330-030-0010 Legislative Authority These rules are promulgated concurrently by the Council and the Director pursuant to their respective rulemaking authorities contained in ORS Chapter 183, 469.040, 469.470, 469.501 to 469.507, 469.530, 469.560 and 192.500, in order to implement their joint responsibility under 469.530.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.530
