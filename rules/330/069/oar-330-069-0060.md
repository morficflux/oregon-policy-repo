## Full text

330-069-0060 Extension of Time for Obligated Measures The owner may request to extend the completion date of a project contracted in accord with OAR 330-069-0030. A written request must be received by ODOE no less than 30 days before the completion date stated in the contract. The request shall include the new date to complete the project and the reason to extend the date. ODOE shall notify the owner in writing, if all or part of the request has been granted or denied. Requests for extension will be judged on a case by case basis. In no case will more than two extensions be granted for a single contract. In no case will an extension be granted that would prevent completion of the project in accord with OAR 330-069-0015(20).
