# Operating Area (OAR 330-069-0075)

## At a glance

## Full text

330-069-0075 Operating Area ODOE shall run this program in the Oregon service areas of these IOUs:

(1) CP National;

(2) Idaho Power Company;

(3) Pacific Power and Light Company;

(4) Portland General Electric Company.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469
