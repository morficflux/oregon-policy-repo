# Termination (OAR 330-069-0070)

## At a glance

## Full text

330-069-0070 Termination If the IOU has made progress payments for a project under OAR 330-069-0025, and such project is not completed by the owner in accord with these rules, the owner shall be liable for excess costs paid by the funding IOU to complete such project. The owner shall be liable for refund to the IOU all or part of any payments received, as ODOE may decide.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469

History: DOE 5-1985, f. & ef. 6-17-85
