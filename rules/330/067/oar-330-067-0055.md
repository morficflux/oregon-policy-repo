# Requests for TA Grants (OAR 330-067-0055)

## At a glance

OAR 330-067-0055 — Requests for TA Grants. Chapter 330, Division 067. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

330-067-0055 Requests for TA Grants Institutions shall submit a completed Request for Services Form. If ODOE determines the institution is eligible for a TA grant, the institution shall:

(1) Complete and submit an EUE for each building for which a TA grant is requested;

(2) Complete and submit an ODOE Partnership Agreement; and

(3) Any other information required by ODOE or which the applicant wants considered, such as a hardship request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.040 & 469.165 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469
