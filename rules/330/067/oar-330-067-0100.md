## Full text

330-067-0100 TA Grant Awards

(1) ODOE may award grants to schools and hospitals for up to 50 percent of the cost of doing TASs for eligible buildings.

(2) ODOE may award up to ten percent of the state’s total allocation to schools and hospitals in cases of severe hardship.

(3) TA grant awards will be made by ODOE in accord with Subpart M, 10 CFR 455.144, in the February 19, 1993, Federal Register and approved State Plan.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.040 & 469.165 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469
