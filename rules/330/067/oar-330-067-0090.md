## Full text

330-067-0090 State Liaison, Monitoring and Reporting ODOE shall:

(1) Notify institutions and coordinating agencies of any changes to the State Plan;

(2) Notify each applicant if its application will receive funding. If the application will not be funded, ODOE will tell the applicant why;

(3) Monitor projects funded by ICP grants. ODOE will notify US DOE of any failure by grantees to comply with ICP rules and laws; and

(4) Report as required by Subparts J, K, and L of 10 CFR 455, in the February 19, 1993, Federal Register.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.040 & 469.165 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469
