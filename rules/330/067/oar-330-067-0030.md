# Recordkeeping (OAR 330-067-0030)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

330-067-0030 Recordkeeping TA grantees that receive federal dollars under this program shall make and retain records required by US DOE and ODOE. These include records which fully disclose:

(1) The amount of the grant and how it was spent;

(2) The total cost of the activities done under the grant;

(3) The source and amount of any funds not supplied by US DOE;

(4) Three years of utility data after the TAS is completed; and

(5) Any information which US DOE and ODOE needs to protect the interest of the United States and the state. US DOE and ODOE shall have access to any documents or records which are related to any grant given under this program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469.040 & 469.165 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469
