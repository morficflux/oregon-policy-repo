## Full text

330-066-0005 Purpose, Statutory Authorization, Policy The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures and standards for publicly-owned electric utilities to present energy conservation services programs for commercial buildings as required by Chapter 708, Oregon Laws 1981.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.030
