## Full text

330-066-0045 Coordination with Federal Statutes and Regulations The Director shall review each commercial energy audit conservation services program to ensure that it does not conflict with federal statutes and regulations applicable to energy conservation in commercial buildings.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469

History: DOE 10-1982, f. & ef. 11-1-82
