# Purpose (OAR 330-050-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 330-050-0005 — Purpose. Chapter 330, Division 050. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

330-050-0005 Purpose The purpose of this rule is to prescribe the rate which shall be used by petroleum suppliers and by the Department of Energy (DOE) in converting barrels of petroleum products into British thermal units (Btus) of energy in connection with the assessments imposed upon petroleum suppliers by Section 4 of Chapter 813, Oregon Laws 1977.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 469 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 469.030

History: DOE 13, f. & ef. 12-6-77 DOE 11(Temp), f. & ef. 9-21-77 DOE 9(Temp), f. & ef. 8-5-77
