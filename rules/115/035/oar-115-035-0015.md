## Full text

115-035-0015 Withdrawal of Complaint Unless the respondent agrees, a complainant may not withdraw a complaint after issuance of a Recommended Order without a showing of good cause.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243
