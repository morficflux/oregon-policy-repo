## Full text

115-035-0020 Dismissal of Complaint If an investigation reveals that no issue of fact or law exists that warrants a hearing, the Board may dismiss the complaint.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243
