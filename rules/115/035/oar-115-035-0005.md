# Investigation of Complaint (OAR 115-035-0005)

## Full text

115-035-0005 Investigation of Complaint A Board Agent shall investigate the complaint to determine if an issue of fact or law exists that warrants a hearing. Information submitted by a party as part of the investigatory process is confidential.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243
