## Full text

115-010-0020 Board Meetings and Contested Case Hearings Ordinarily, contested case hearings shall be open to the public. When circumstances warrant, the Board may close contested case hearings. The Board shall meet at such times and places as specified by the chair or at the request of two members of the Board. Advance notice of the time and place of each meeting shall be given to each Board member. Two members shall constitute a quorum.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.610 - 192.690 & 240.080
