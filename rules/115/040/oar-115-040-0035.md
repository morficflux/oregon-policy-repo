## Full text

115-040-0035 Filing of Arbitrator and Factfinder Decisions All arbitrators and factfinders listed on the State Conciliation Panel pursuant to OAR 115-040-0030 shall provide the State Conciliation Service with one copy of all written decisions or recommendations issued concerning a labor dispute involving public employees, public employers or labor organizations as defined by ORS 243.650.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243

History: ERB 9-2016, f. 11-9-16, cert. ef. 2-1-17 ERB 11-1985, f. 10-29-85, ef. 10-31-85
