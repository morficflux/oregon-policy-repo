## Full text

115-040-0017 Interest Arbitration Enforcement Pursuant to ORS 243.752, a party alleging that another party is refusing or failing to comply with an interest arbitration award may seek enforcement of the award by filing an unfair labor practice complaint with the Board charging a violation of 243.672(1)(f) or (2)(c). The Board generally will hold an expedited hearing on the matter, if requested to do so, under the procedures provided by OAR 115-035-0060.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243
