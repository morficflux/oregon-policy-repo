## Full text

115-025-0074 Runoff Election A runoff election will be conducted in any representation election where there are more than two choices on the ballot and no choice receives a majority of the valid votes cast. The Board will follow the procedures for conducting an election. The ballot in a runoff election will be the two choices that received the largest number of votes on the original ballot. To be eligible to vote in the runoff election, an employee must have been eligible to vote in the original election and must still be employed on the date of the runoff election.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243
