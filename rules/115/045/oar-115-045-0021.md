## Full text

115-045-0021 Dismissal Appeals by Management Service Employees with Immediate Prior Regular Classified Service An appeal under ORS 240.570 shall comply with OAR 115-045-0005 and must contain a detailed statement specifying:

(1) The action being appealed;

(2) The reason(s) why the employee believes that the action violated ORS 240.570 (and any related statute); and

(3) The corrective action being requested.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 240
