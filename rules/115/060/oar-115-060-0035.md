## Full text

115-060-0035 Consent Election Agreement The parties may waive a hearing and enter into a consent election agreement. Such agreement shall include a description of the bargaining unit, time and place of the election and the payroll period to be used in determining the employees eligible to vote. The bargaining unit set out in the consent agreement shall be deemed an appropriate bargaining unit when the consent agreement is approved by the Board or its agent. The parties may agree to a mail ballot election with the approval of the Board or its agent.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 243 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 663.025
