## Full text

115-060-0020 Validity of Showing of Interest The showing of interest submitted pursuant to OAR 115-060-0010(1) and

(3) shall not be furnished to any of the parties. The Board or its agents shall determine the adequacy of the showing of interest and such decision shall not be subject to collateral attack.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 243 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 663.025

History: ERB 1-1980, f. & ef. 1-9-80
