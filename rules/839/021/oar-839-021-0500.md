## Full text

839-021-0500 Exemptions The provisions of ORS 653.305 to 653.370 and OAR 839-021-0001 to 839-021-0500 do not apply to employers employing minors in the following work or occupations:

(1) Domestic work or chores in or about a private residence.

(2) Newspaper carrier.

(3) Newspaper vendor.

(4) Persons under 18 years of age serving as a referee or assistant referee in a youth or adult recreational soccer match.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 653 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 653.315 & 653.355
