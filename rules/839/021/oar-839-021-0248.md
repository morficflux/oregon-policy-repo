## Full text

839-021-0248 Reports from Employers Employing Minors Employers employing minors shall file reports that may be required by the Bureau.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651.060(4) & 653.261 Statutes/Other Implemented: S.B. 135, 77th Leg., Reg. Ses. (Or.2013) & ORS 653
