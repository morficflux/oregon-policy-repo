## Full text

839-021-0360 Refusal of Special Permits, Hours Variances or Employment Certificates in the Entertainment Industry When it appears to the Bureau that the proposed employment would be prohibited by OAR 839-021-0355, the Bureau may refuse to issue any special permit, hours variance or employment certificate.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651.060(4) & 653.261 Statutes/Other Implemented: S.B. 135, 77th Leg., Reg. Ses. (Or.2013) & ORS 653
