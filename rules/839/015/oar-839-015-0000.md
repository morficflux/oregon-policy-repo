## Full text

839-015-0000 Notice of Proposed Rule Prior to the permanent adoption, amendment or repeal of any rule relating to labor contractors, the Bureau of Labor and Industries shall give notice of the intended action as required in OAR 839-002-0002.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 651.060(4), 658.407(3) & 658.405 - 658.503 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.405 - 658.503
