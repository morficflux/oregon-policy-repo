## Full text

839-015-0178 Grading of Examinations/Notice of Score

(1) The passing score for each examination shall be 75 percent.

(2) The License Unit shall notify applicants in writing of their examination scores.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651 & 658 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.405 - 658.503
