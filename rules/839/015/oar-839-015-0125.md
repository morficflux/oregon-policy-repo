## Full text

839-015-0125 Contractors Must Obtain a License; Indorsement No person may perform the activities of a labor contractor without first obtaining a temporary permit or license issued by the bureau. No person may perform the activities of a forest labor contractor or operate a farmworker camp without first obtaining a special indorsement from the bureau authorizing such performance. Unless otherwise specifically exempt, and except for cooperative corporations, no person may perform the duties of a labor contractor or operate a farmworker camp under a license issued to a corporation unless the person is also licensed to perform such duties.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651 & ORS 658 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.405 - 658.503 & OL Ch. 676 (2017)
