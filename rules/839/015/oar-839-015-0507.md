## Full text

839-015-0507 Violations Separate and Distinct Each violation is a separate and distinct offense. In the case of continuing violations, each day’s continuance is a separate and distinct violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651 & 658.407(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.405 - 658.503
