# Records (OAR 839-015-0410)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

839-015-0410 Records Availability Labor Contractors will make available to representatives of the Wage and Hour Division records necessary to determine compliance with ORS 658.405 to 658.475 and these rules. Records will be made available upon request of such representatives.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651 & 658.407(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.405 - 658.503

History: BLI 13-2014, f. 11-14-14, cert. ef. 7-1-15 BL 6-1984, f. & ef. 4-27-84
