## Full text

839-015-0250 Protesting the Issuance of a License Any individual may protest the issuance of a labor contractor’s license to any applicant for such license. The protest may be made at any time after the license is actually issued as well as at any time prior to the issuance of the license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651 & 658.415(14) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.420(3)
