## Full text

839-015-0160 Procedure for Obtaining a Duplicate License In the event a license is lost or stolen, the licensee shall submit a written request for a duplicate license. The licensee shall state the reasons for the request and the circumstances of the loss or theft. The new license will indicate the word "DUPLICATE" on the license above the number. The written request shall be made to the Bureau of Labor and Industries, Wage and Hour Division, License Unit, 3865 Wolverine Street, NE, Bldg. E-1, Salem, OR 97305.
