## Full text

839-003-0210 Withdrawal of a Housing Discrimination Complaint An aggrieved person may voluntarily withdraw a complaint at any time by giving the division written notice of the aggrieved person’s decision to withdraw. The division will then close the case.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 659A.805 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 659A.145 & 659A.421
