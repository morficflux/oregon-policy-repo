## Full text

839-019-0000 Notice of Proposed Rules Prior to the permanent adoption, amendment or repeal of any rule relating to civil penalties for child labor violation matters, the Wage and Hour Division of the Bureau of Labor and Industries shall give notice of intended action as required in OAR 839-002-0002.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651.060(4) & 653.305 - 653.545 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.335
