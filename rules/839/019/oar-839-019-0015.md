# Violations (OAR 839-019-0015)

## At a glance

## Full text

839-019-0015 Violations Separate and Distinct Each violation is a separate and distinct offense. In the case of continuing violations, each day’s continuance is a separate and distinct violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651 & 653 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 653.370

History: BL 5-1988, f. & cert. ef. 4-12-88
