## Full text

839-014-0410 Violations Separate and Distinct Each violation is a separate and distinct offense. In the case of continuing violations, each day’s continuance is a separate and distinct violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 164, 165, 651, 658.820(1) & 962

History: BL 2-1990, f. & cert. ef. 3-1-90
