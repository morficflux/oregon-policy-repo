## Full text

839-014-0320 Records Availability A farmworker camp operator shall make available to representatives of the Wage and Hour Division records necessary to determine compliance with ORS 658.405 to 658.475, 658.705 to 658.850 and these rules. Records shall be made available upon the request of such representatives.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 164, 165, 651, 658 & 962 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.705 - 658.850
