## Full text

839-014-0050 Camp Operators Must Obtain Indorsement Farm-worker camp operators must obtain a farm labor contractor’s license pursuant to ORS 658.405 to 658.475 and the rules adopted thereunder, unless otherwise exempt pursuant to OAR 839-014-0060. Additionally, farmworker camp operators must obtain a special indorsement from the Bureau authorizing the operator to act as such.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 164, 165, 651, 658 & 962 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.705 - 658.850
