## Full text

839-014-0250 Protesting the Issuance of an Indorsement Any individual may protest the issuance of an indorsement to any applicant for such an indorsement. The protest may be made at any time after the indorsement is actually issued as well as at any time prior to the issuance of the indorsement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 164, 165, 651, 658.730(1) & 962 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.730(1)
