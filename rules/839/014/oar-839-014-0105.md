## Full text

839-014-0105 Procedure for Renewing Indorsement

(1) Indorsement shall expire with the farm/forest contractor's license each year unless sooner revoked.

(2) Applications for renewal shall be made 30 days before the expiration date of the license by filing a renewal application, paying the appropriate fees, and filing proof of financial ability to pay wages and certain advances.

(3) Applications, fees and other forms and documents must be filed with the Bureau of Labor and Industries, Wage and Hour Division, Farm Labor Unit, 3865 Wolverine Street, NE, Bldg. E-1, Salem, OR 97305.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 164, 165, 651, 658 & 962 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.705 - 658.850
