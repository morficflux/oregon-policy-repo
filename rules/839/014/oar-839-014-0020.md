## Full text

839-014-0020 Notice of Proposed Rules Prior to the permanent adoption, amendment or repeal of any rule relating to farm-worker camp operations and registration of farm worker camps, the Bureau of Labor and Industries shall give notice of the intended action as required in OAR 839-002-0002.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 651.060(4) & 658.705 - 658.850 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.705 - 658.850
