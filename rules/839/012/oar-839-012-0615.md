## Full text

839-012-0615 Applications and Eligibility

(1) Applications must be submitted in the form required, and by a deadline set, in the solicitation.

(2) Applicants for Trade Outreach Veterans Grants must be an Oregon Trade Union or Trade Association.

(3) Applicants shall be permitted to partner with Community Based Organizations.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL CH. 528 (2019) Statutes/Other Implemented: OL CH. 528 (2019)
