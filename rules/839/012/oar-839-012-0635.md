## Full text

839-012-0635 Evaluation Criteria Trade Outreach Veterans Grant applications are evaluated according to how they address the themes described in OAR 839-011-0610(1). Additional evaluation criteria include:

(1) Significance of the activity or the project, including the quality of the organization(s), structures, apprenticeship and training programs, or services that will be involved;

(2) The potential to broaden or deepen veteran knowledge, understanding, appreciation of, and access to Apprenticeship Opportunities;

(3) The appropriateness of the budget, the quality and clarity of the project goals and design, the resources involved, and the qualifications of the project's personnel;

(4) Evidence of sound fiscal management;

(5) Evidence of public or private matching funds or in-kind services;

(6) The process by which the project will be evaluated and plans in place to sustain or maintain the activity or project, if appropriate, following the period of the grant.
