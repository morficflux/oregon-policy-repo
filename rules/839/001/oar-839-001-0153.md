## Full text

839-001-0153 Violations Separate and Distinct Each violation of ORS 652.020 or any rule adopted pursuant thereto is a separate and distinct offense. In the case of continuing violations, each day's continuance is a separate and distinct violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651.060(4) & 652 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 652.020
