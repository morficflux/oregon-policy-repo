## Full text

839-001-0525 Priority of Payments from the Wage Security Fund The Bureau shall pay from or encumber the Fund, on a monthly basis, the administrative and operating expenses incurred while carrying out the provisions of ORS 652.414, and these rules before paying wage claims.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 652.414(6) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 652.414
