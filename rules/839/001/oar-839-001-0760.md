## Full text

839-001-0760 Violations Separate and Distinct Each violation of ORS 652.710(1) and

(3) is a separate and distinct offense. In the case of continuing violations, each day's continuance is a separate and distinct violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 652.710(7) & 652.710(11) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.090 & 652.710

History: BLI 37-2007, f. 12-28-07, cert. ef. 1-1-08 BL 10-1992, f. & cert. ef. 7-1-92
