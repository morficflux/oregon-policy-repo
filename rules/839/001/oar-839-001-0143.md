# Failure to Establish a Workweek (OAR 839-001-0143)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

839-001-0143 Failure to Establish a Workweek If an employer fails to establish the beginning day of the workweek as provided for in ORS 652.020(1)(b) and OAR 839-001-0100(19), the Bureau shall consider the workweek to begin on the day the individual commences work and to end seven consecutive days after the work began, for the period of time in which the employer failed to establish a workweek.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651.060(4) & 652 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 652.020

History: BLI 8-2017, amend filed 12/27/2017, effective 01/01/2018 BL 8-1990, f. & cert. ef. 5-11-90
