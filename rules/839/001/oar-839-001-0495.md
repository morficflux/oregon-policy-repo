## Full text

839-001-0495 Violations Separate and Distinct Each violation of ORS 652.110, 652.140, 652.145, 652.260, 652.610(4), 652.750 or any rule adopted pursuant thereto is a separate and distinct offense. In the case of continuing violations, each day's continuance is a separate and distinct violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651.060(4), 652.165 & 652.900 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 652.010–652.900
