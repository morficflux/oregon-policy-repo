## Full text

839-025-0300 Actions that Circumvent Payment of Prevailing Wages Prohibited No person shall take any action which circumvents the payment of the prevailing wage rate to workers on public works projects.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279 & 651 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279.348 - 279.380
