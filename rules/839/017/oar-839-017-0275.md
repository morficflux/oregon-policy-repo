## Full text

839-017-0275 Violations Separate and Distinct Each violation is a separate and distinct offense. In the case of continuing violations, each day’s continuance is a separate and distinct violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 658.210 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.115
