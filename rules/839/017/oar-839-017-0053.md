## Full text

839-017-0053 When an Exploratory Job Order/Interview Is Considered to Be Given An Exploratory Job Order/Interview shall be considered to have been given if, as the result of the agency’s bringing the qualifications of an applicant for employment to the attention of an employer, the employer’s interest in exploring the possibility of employing the applicant is evidenced by the employer’s agreement to interview the applicant.
