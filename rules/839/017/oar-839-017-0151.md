## Full text

839-017-0151 Interest, Fees, or Other Charges Allowed as Credits for Permanent Employment Lasting Less Than 90 Days In all cases where employment lasts less than 90 full calendar days, all interest, fees, or other charges paid or required to be paid to any person or organization in order to procure the funds to pay an employment agency’s charge for service shall be allowed as a credit against the charge. This includes all carrying and service charges and all related charges in obtaining funds for payment of the agency’s charge.
