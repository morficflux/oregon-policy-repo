## Full text

839-017-0031 Oral Agreements or Additions to Contracts Prohibited There shall be no oral agreements or oral additions to any contract between the employment agency and the applicant for employment. Any terms, conditions, or understandings between the employment agency and the applicant for employment shall be in writing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 658.210 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 658.168
