## Full text

839-017-0153 Agencies Required to Make Effort to Procure Necessary Information for Making Rebates A reasonable effort shall be made by the agency, within the ten day specified time for making the rebate, to procure all information necessary regarding the interest, fees, and other charges required to be paid by the applicant for employment in procuring the funds to pay the agency’s charge for service.
