## Full text

839-006-0250 Disability and Employment Rights: Customer or Co-Worker Preference An employer may not consider the attitude or preference of employers, managers, supervisors, co-workers, customers, clients or the general public toward an individual’s perceived or actual impairment in determining whether an individual is qualified for a position.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 659A.805 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 659A.103 - 659A.142
