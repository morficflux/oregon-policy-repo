## Full text

839-006-0265 Disability and Employment Rights: Subterfuge An employer may not use the provisions of these rules as a subterfuge to avoid the employer's duty not to discriminate under ORS 659A.103 to 659A.142.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 659A.805 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 659A.103 - 659A.142
