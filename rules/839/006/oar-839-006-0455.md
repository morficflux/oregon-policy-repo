## Full text

839-006-0455 Veterans’ Preference in Public Employment: Employment Preference for Promotions A public employer must grant a preference to a person seeking promotion in the manner described in ORS 408.225 to 408.237.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 659A.805 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 408.225 - 408.237
