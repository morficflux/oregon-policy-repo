## Full text

839-006-0202 Disability and Employment Rights: Determining Disability under ORS 659A.103 to 659A.142 The determination of whether an individual has a disability shall be construed in favor of broad coverage of individuals under ORS 659A.103 to 659A.142, to the maximum extent permitted by the terms of ORS 659A.103 to 659A.142.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 659A.805 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 659A.103 - 659A.142
