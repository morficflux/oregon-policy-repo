## Full text

839-010-0300 Disclosures Regarding Violations of Election Laws: Application These rules apply to a person who pays money or offers other valuable consideration for obtaining signatures of electors on a state initiative, referendum, or recall petition or on a prospective petition of a state measure to be initiated.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 659A.805 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2013 OL Ch. 519 & Sec. 7
