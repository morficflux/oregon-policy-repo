# Violations (OAR 839-020-1000)

## At a glance

## Full text

839-020-1000 Violations Separate and Distinct Each violation is a separate and distinct offense. In the case of continuing violations, each day’s continuance is a separate and distinct violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 653.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: Ch. 314 & 1997 OL

History: BL 9-1997, f. & cert. ef. 11-13-97
