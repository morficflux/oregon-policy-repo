## Full text

839-020-0300 Authority of Labor Commissioner to Define Terms Relating to Executive, Administrative, Supervisory and Professional Employees of a Public Agency ORS 653.269(5)(a) requires the Labor Commissioner to define certain terms relating to executive, administrative, supervisory or professional employees of a public agency who are exempt from the overtime provisions of ORS 653.268 because of the nature of their employment.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279.342(5)(a) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279.342
