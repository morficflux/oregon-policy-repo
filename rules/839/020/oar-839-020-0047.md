## Full text

839-020-0047 Application of OAR 839-020-0040 to 839-020-0047 to Agricultural Employees The provisions of OAR 839-020-0040 to 839-020-0047 apply to all persons employed in Agriculture who are required to be paid minimum wages pursuant to ORS 653.025.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 653 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 653.025
