# Failure to Establish a Workweek (OAR 839-020-0415)

## Full text

839-020-0415 Failure to Establish a Workweek If an employer fails to establish the beginning day of the workweek as provided for in ORS 653.265(1)(c), the Bureau shall consider the workweek to begin on the day the individual employee commences work and to end seven

(7) consecutive days after the work began, for the period of time in which the employer failed to establish a workweek.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL Ch. 685 (2017) & ORS 651 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL Ch. 685 (2017) & ORS 653

History: BLI 8-2017, adopt filed 12/27/2017, effective 01/01/2018
