## Full text

839-020-0000 Notice of Proposed Rule Prior to the adoption, amendment, or repeal of any rule relating to minimum wages, overtime and working conditions matters, the Bureau of Labor and Industries will give notice of the proposed adoption, amendment, or repeal as required in OAR 839-002-0002.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 653 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183
