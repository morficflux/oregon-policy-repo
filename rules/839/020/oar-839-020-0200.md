## Full text

839-020-0200 Authority of Labor Commissioner to Define Terms Relating to Persons Employed by a Public Agency in Fire Protection and Law Enforcement Activities ORS 653.269(3) requires the Labor Commissioner to define certain terms relating to persons employed by a public employer in fire protection and law enforcement activities (including security personnel in corrections institutions), who are exempt from the overtime provisions of ORS 653.268.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279.342(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279.342
