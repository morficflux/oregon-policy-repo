## Full text

839-002-0001 Rulemaking: Purpose of Rules The Bureau of Labor and Industries is authorized to develop administrative rules necessary for enforcement of statutes for which it is responsible. The purpose of these rules is to guide the bureau in the rule-making process.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.335
