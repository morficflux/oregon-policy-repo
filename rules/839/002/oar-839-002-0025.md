## Full text

839-002-0025 Investigative Subpoenas for Civil Rights Division and Wage and Hour Division: Who and What May Be Subpoenaed The commissioner may issue subpoenas to persons to compel testimony and the production of documents or other things that are relevant to the commissioner’s lawful investigative purpose and reasonable in scope under matters relating to the duties required under ORS 279C.800 to 279C.870, 651.030, 651.050, 651.120 and 651.170, and chapters 652, 653, 658, and 659A.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651.060, 658.220 & 659A.800 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279C, 651, 652, 653, 658 & 659A
