## Full text

839-005-0325 Discrimination by Career Schools: Retaliation or Discrimination Pursuant to, ORS 659.852(1)(b), it is an unlawful practice for a career school or its agent to retaliate or discriminate against any individual because the individual has in good faith reported information that the student believes is evidence of a violation of a state or federal law, rule or regulation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651.060 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 345.240 & 659.850
