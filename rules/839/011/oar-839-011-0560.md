## Full text

839-011-0560 Apprenticeship and Training Council Subpoenas — Failure to Appear If a person served with a subpoena fails to appear and has not filed any prior objections, the Council will conclude that the person has refused, without reasonable cause, to answer any question or to produce any document or other thing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 660.120(1)
