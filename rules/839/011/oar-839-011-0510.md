## Full text

839-011-0510 Apprenticeship and Training Council Subpoenas — Who and What May Be Subpoenaed The Council may issue subpoenas to persons to compel testimony and the production of documents or other things that are relevant to the Council’s lawful investigative purpose and reasonable in scope under matters relating to the duties required under ORS Chapter 660.002 to 660.210.
