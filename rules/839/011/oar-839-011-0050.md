## Full text

839-011-0050 Apprenticeship and Training Council — Certificate of Meritorious Service Upon the recommendation of a local committee or the motion of a Council member, the Council may award a certificate of meritorious service to any individual who has devoted a minimum of three years of service to a registered apprenticeship program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 660.120
