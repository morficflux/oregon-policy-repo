## Full text

839-011-0401 Youth Apprenticeship — Program Approval

(1) Council approval of the youth program is required prior to implementation.

(2) Youth apprenticeship committees and standards must meet the requirements outlined in these rules for adult apprenticeship programs.

(3) Youth standards must directly relate to an apprenticeable occupation recognized by federal Office of Apprenticeship.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 344.745 & 344.750 & 660.120 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 344.745 & 344.750 & 660.120
