## Full text

839-011-0530 Apprenticeship and Training Council Subpoenas — Subpoena Ad Testificandum

(1) A subpoena ad testificandum may be issued to any person when the conditions set out in 839-011-0105(1) or 839-011-0515(2) have been met.

(2) The subpoena ad testificandum must give the person a reasonable time for preparation and travel to the place of attendance and the place of attendance must be suitable place in the vicinity to which testimony is applicable.
