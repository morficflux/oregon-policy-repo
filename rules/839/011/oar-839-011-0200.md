## Full text

839-011-0200 Apprenticeship and Training Committees — Equal Employment Opportunity in Apprenticeship The Oregon Plan Equal Employment Opportunity in Registered Apprenticeship Programs (the Plan), pursuant to 29 CFR Part 30, is adopted and incorporated by reference as if fully set forth in these rules. The Plan is effective November 1, 2021. [Attachment: Oregon Plan for Equal Employment Opportunity in Registered Apprenticeship Programs] [ED.
