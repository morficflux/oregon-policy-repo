## Full text

839-011-0073 Apprenticeship and Training Committees — Policies and Procedures

(1) All local committees shall develop and administer operating policies and procedures to govern program operations as directed by the Council and administer such policies and procedures in a consistent manner. Policies and procedures will be approved by the committee and recorded in the meeting minutes.

(2) When adopted or revised, these policies and procedures will be submitted to the local committee’s assigned Apprenticeship Representative who will review and approve the policies and procedures if they are in conformity with apprenticeship laws, rules and Council guidelines.
