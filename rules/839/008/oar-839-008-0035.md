## Full text

839-008-0035 Individual Acts of Remuneration as Distinct Violations An employer commits an unlawful compensation practice each time an employee is remunerated in violation of ORS 652.220.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 659A.805 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 652.220 & ORS 659A.875
