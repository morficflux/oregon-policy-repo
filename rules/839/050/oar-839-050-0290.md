# Witnesses (OAR 839-050-0290)

## At a glance

## Full text

839-050-0290 Witnesses All testimony to be taken at the hearing, except matters officially noticed or entered by stipulation, will be sworn or affirmed. This may include testimony given on deposition, by declaration, or in answers to interrogatories.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS chapter 183 & ORS 651.060(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279C.860, 279C.865, 652.332(3), 653.065(1), 658.115, 658.407(3), 658.820, 659A.845 & 659A.850
