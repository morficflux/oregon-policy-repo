## Full text

859-550-0050 Testimony Given on Oath The Board shall take testimony of a witness upon oath or affirmation of the witness administered by the chairperson or acting chairperson at the hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.385, 161.387; OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.346, 161.385 & 419C.532
