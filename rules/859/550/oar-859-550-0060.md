## Full text

859-550-0060 Burden of Going Forward The party that has the burden of proof shall also have the burden of going forward with the evidence by calling and examining witnesses, proposing conditions of release or bringing other matters before the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387, OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.346, 419C.532, 419C.538, 419C.540 & 419C.542
