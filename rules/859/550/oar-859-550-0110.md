# Records of (OAR 859-550-0110)

## Full text

859-550-0110 Records of Hearings All Juvenile Panel hearings, except Board deliberations, will be recorded by electronic means which can be transcribed. All documents considered will be included in the file and kept as part of the record:

(1) Audio recordings capable of being transcribed will be kept by the Board for a minimum period of five years from the hearing date;

(2) Any material to which an objection is sustained will be removed from the record; the objection and ruling of the Board will be noted on the record.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.295-161.400
