## Full text

859-550-0020 Chairperson Conducting Hearing During hearings of the Board, the chairperson or acting chairperson shall preside. The chairperson shall designate the order of presentation and the order of questioning and scope of questioning. The chairperson may set time limits and interrupt or terminate irrelevant questions or unresponsive answers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387, OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.385(1)-(7)
