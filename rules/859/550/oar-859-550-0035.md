# Motion Practice (OAR 859-550-0035)

## At a glance

OAR 859-550-0035 — Motion Practice. Chapter 859, Division 550. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

859-550-0035 Motion Practice Any party bringing a motion before the Board shall submit five copies of the motion and supporting memorandum of law to the Board and one to the opposing party no later than 10 days prior to the hearing date in which the motion will be heard.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387, OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.346 & 419C.532

History: PSRB 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 9-28-10
