## Full text

859-550-0085 Notification of Right to Appeal At the conclusion of a Board hearing, the chair or acting chair shall provide the youth and attorney with written notification of the right to appeal an adverse decision within 60 days from the date an order is signed and the right to an attorney for that purpose, if indigent.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387, OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.385

History: PSRB 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 9-28-10
