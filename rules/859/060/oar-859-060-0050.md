## Full text

859-060-0050 Status Hearing The Board may hold a hearing at any time to review the status of the patient to determine whether modification of a conditional release order is appropriate, whether a revocation is appropriate or any other change in status as allowed by law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.336

History: PSRB 1-1985, f. 1-3-85, ef. 1-15-85
