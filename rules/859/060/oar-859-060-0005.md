## Full text

859-060-0005 Issues At any hearing before the Board issues considered shall be limited to those relevant to the purposes of the hearing. Notice of intent to raise new issues shall be given to the Board in writing prior to the hearing. If new issues are raised, the Board may continue the hearing to consider the issues and give the parties an opportunity to submit additional evidence.
