## Full text

859-300-0060 Time Limits; Applying for Relief Subject to OAR 859-300-0080(3), a petition for relief may not be filed with the Board more than once every two years.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1), OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1), OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853))
