## Full text

859-300-0190 Testimony Given on Oath The Panel shall take testimony of a witness upon oath or affirmation of the witness administered by the presiding officer at the relief hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1); OL2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1); OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853)
