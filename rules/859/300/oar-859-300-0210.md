## Full text

859-300-0210 Mandatory Personal Appearance Personal appearance by petitioner is required at all stages of a relief hearing. An appearance by an attorney or personal representative on behalf of a petitioner shall not constitute personal appearance. The petitioner may appear byremote means.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387, ORS 419C.533 & ORS 166.273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387, ORS 419C.533 & ORS 166.273
