## Full text

859-300-0230 Submission of Final Order of Relief to Oregon State Police No later than ten days after the date the Board issues a written final order granting relief from the firearms prohibition, the PSRB shall notify the Department of State Police to either transmit the record of the relief granted by the Board to the federal government as required under federal law, or to update or modify its state database(s), as provided by the Department of State Police’s administrative rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161, 183 & 419C Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1), OL 2007 & Ch.r 889 § 6 (SB 328)

History: PSRB 1-2011, f. 2-2-11, cert. ef. 2-15-11 PSRB 3-2010(Temp), f. 10-5-10, cert. ef. 10-8-10 thru 4-6-11
