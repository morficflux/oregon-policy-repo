# Motion Practice (OAR 859-300-0150)

## At a glance

## Full text

859-300-0150 Motion Practice Any party bringing a motion before the Panel shall submit five copies of the motion and memorandum of law to the Panel at least 10 days prior to the date of the hearing in which the motion will be heard.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1), OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1), OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853))
