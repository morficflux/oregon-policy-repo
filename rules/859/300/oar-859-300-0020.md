## Full text

859-300-0020 Cost of Administrative Rules State employees may receive a printed copy of Oregon Administrative Rules on request at no cost. Any person not employed by the State of Oregon shall pay $0.25 per page for a printed copy of the Gun Relief Program Administrative Rules. Oregon Administrative Rules are available electronically at no charge from the Internet Web site of the Oregon Secretary of State.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1), OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1), OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853))
