## Full text

859-100-0025 Discharge and Conditional Release Plans The Legislature has provided that the Mental Health and Developmental Disability Services Division is responsible for preparation of discharge or conditional release plans.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.336 & 161.390
