## Full text

859-100-0015 Temporary Custody of Patient in State Hospital At any time, the Board may appoint a psychiatrist or licensed psychologist to examine the person and submit a report to the Board. In order to facilitate the examination, the person may be placed in the temporary custody of any state hospital or other suitable facility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137, 161, 192 & 428 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.346

History: PSRB 1-1985, f. 1-3-85, ef. 1-15-85
