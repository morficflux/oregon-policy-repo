## Full text

859-200-0200 Court Conditional Release

(1) If a court orders the conditional release of a person it is called a court conditional release.

(2) The Board may review a Court Conditional Release Order at an administrative review. The possible results of the hearing are as follows: (a) The Board may issue an order continuing the Court Order of Conditional Release; (b) The Board may issue a modification of the Court Order of Conditional Release when, upon review, elements of the plan have changed, have not been set out in sufficient detail or additional conditions are needed; (c) The Board may issue an Order of Revocation in accordance with provisions set forth in the revocation rules subsequent hereto; or (d) The Board may order the person to appear at a full hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421)
