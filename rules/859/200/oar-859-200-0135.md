## Full text

859-200-0135 Notification of Right to Appeal At the conclusion of a Board hearing, the chairperson or acting chairperson shall provide the person and attorney with written notification advising of the right to appeal on an adverse decision within 60 days from the date an order is signed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421)
