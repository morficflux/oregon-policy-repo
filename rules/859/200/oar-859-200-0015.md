## Full text

859-200-0015 Copies; Cost of Administrative Rules State employees may receive a printed copy of Oregon Administrative Rules on request at no cost. Any person not employed by the State of Oregon shall pay $0.25 per page for a printed copy of the Extremely Dangerous Civil Commitments Program Administrative Rules. Oregon Administrative Rules are available electronically at no charge from the Oregon Secretary of State website or from the Psychiatric Security Review Board’s website.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421)
