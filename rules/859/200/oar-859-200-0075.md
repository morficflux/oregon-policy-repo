## Full text

859-200-0075 Chairperson Conducting Hearing During all hearings of the Board, the chairperson or acting chairperson shall preside. The chairperson shall designate the order of presentation and questioning. The chairperson shall also determine the scope of questioning and may set time limits and cut off irrelevant questions and irrelevant or nonresponsive answers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421)
