## Full text

859-200-0230 Conditional Release Evaluation and Reports

(1) All reports and evaluations received on the person’s fitness for conditional release, modification of conditional release or revocation, and monthly progress shall be made a part of the record in the hearing.

(2) All serious incidents must be communicated through an immediate phone call to the PSRB executive director or designee and followed-up with a written incident report. A serious incident includes, but is not limited to: (a) Unauthorized departure; (b) Arrest; (c) Positive urine or oral drug screen or other substance use testing; (d) Repeated refusals to take psychotropic medications or other medications prescribed to manage the person’s mental health; (e) Psychiatric or medical hospitalization; (f) Violent or dangerous behaviors; or (g) Other circumstances deemed to be serious by the reporting party.
