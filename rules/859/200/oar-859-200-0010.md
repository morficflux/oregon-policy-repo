## Full text

859-200-0010 Rules Applicability OAR 859-200-0005 through 859-200-0320 apply to a person who a court has civilly committed as an extremely dangerous person with a qualifying mental disorder under ORS 426.701 and who is placed under the jurisdiction of the Psychiatric Security Review Board (PSRB).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 426.701 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 426.701 & ORS 426.702
