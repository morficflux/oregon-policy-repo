## Full text

859-200-0100 Testimony Given under Oath The Board shall take testimony of a witness upon oath or affirmation of the witness administered by the chairperson or acting chairperson at the hearing. Victims who wish to give a victim impact statement at a hearing do not need to be sworn in.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1) & 2013 OL Ch. 715 (SB 421)
