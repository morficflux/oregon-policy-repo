## Full text

859-040-0025 Public Participation

(1) The Adult Panel of the Board will allocate a public comment period during its Administrative meetings. The Adult Panel Chairperson or Acting Chairperson may set a time limit for participants electing to speak during that period.

(2) The public may not participate in the discussion during Administrative meetings unless invited by the Board Chairperson.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.385, ORS 192.630, ORS 192.690 & ORS 192.672
