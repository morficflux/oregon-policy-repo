# Public (OAR 859-040-0015)

## Full text

859-040-0015 Public Meetings Law All meetings of the PSRB are open to the public in accordance with the Public Meetings Law; the deliberations of the Board are not open to the public. For the purposes of this rule, the term “public” does not include employees of the PSRB.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387
