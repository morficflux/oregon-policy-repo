## Full text

859-050-0020 Chairperson Conducting Hearing During Board hearings, the Chairperson or acting Chairperson will preside. The Chairperson will designate the order of presentation and questioning. The Chairperson will also determine the scope of questioning and may set time limits and cut off irrelevant questions and irrelevant or unresponsive answers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.385
