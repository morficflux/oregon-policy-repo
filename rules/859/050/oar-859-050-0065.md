## Full text

859-050-0065 Continuance of Hearing Upon the request of any party or on its own motion, the Board may, for good cause, continue a hearing for a reasonable period, up to 60 days to obtain additional information, psychiatric reports or testimony or to accommodate a witness’s schedule. The patient may waive the 60-day time period for good cause.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.346
