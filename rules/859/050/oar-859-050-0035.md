## Full text

859-050-0035 Motion Practice Any party bringing a motion before the Board will submit it to the Board and the opposing party one week prior to the scheduled hearing date. This rule does not apply to requests for set-overs for good cause, or to requests for continuation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.346
