# Deliberations (OAR 859-050-0060)

## Full text

859-050-0060 Deliberations Board deliberations are confidential and not open to the public. For purposes of this rule, “the public” does not include PSRB employees. PSRB staff may attend Board deliberations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.346
