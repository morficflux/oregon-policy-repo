## Full text

859-520-0010 Chair The Board shall elect one of its members as chairperson to serve for a term of one year, with the possibility of re-election.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.385, 161.387; OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.385(1)-(7) & 161.387(1)
