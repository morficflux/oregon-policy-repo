## Full text

859-520-0005 Membership and Terms The Juvenile panel of the Psychiatric Security Review Board shall consist of five members as constituted in ORS chapter 419C.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.385, 161.387; OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.385(1)-(7) & 161.387(1)
