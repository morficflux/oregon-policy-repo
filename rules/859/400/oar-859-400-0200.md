## Full text

859-400-0200 Standard and Burden of Proof

(1) The standard of proof at the relief hearing shall be whether petitioner has demonstrated by clear and convincing evidence that the petitioner: (a) is statistically unlikely to reoffend; and (b) does not pose a threat to the safety of the public.

(2) The burden of proof shall be on the petitioner.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1) & ORS 163A.125 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1) & ORS 163A.125
