## Full text

859-400-0210 Mandatory Personal Appearance Personal appearance by petitioner is required at all stages of a relief hearing. An appearance by an attorney or personal representative on behalf of a petitioner shall not constitute personal appearance. The petitioner may appear by remote means.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 163A.105, ORS 161.387 & ORS 419C.533 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 163A.105, ORS 161.387 & ORS 419C.533
