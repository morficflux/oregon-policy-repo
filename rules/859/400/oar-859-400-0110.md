## Full text

859-400-0110 Public Meetings Law

(1) All sex offender relief hearings shall be open to the public in accordance with the Public Meetings Law.

(2) Deliberations of the Panel shall not be open to the public. For the purposes of this subsection, the term “public” does not include PSRB employees or staff.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1) & ORS 163A.125 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1) & ORS 163A.125
