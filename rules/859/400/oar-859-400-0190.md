## Full text

859-400-0190 Testimony Given on Oath The Panel shall take testimony of a witness upon oath or affirmation of the witness administered by the presiding officer at the relief hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 161.387(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 161.387(1) & ORS 163A.125
