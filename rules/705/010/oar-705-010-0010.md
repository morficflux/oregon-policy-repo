## Full text

705-010-0010 Parties to Achievement Compacts Prior to the beginning of each fiscal year, the governing body of each education entity must enter into an achievement compact with the Oregon Education Investment Board for the fiscal year.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2012 OL Ch. 36 Sec. 14 (Enrolled SB 1581) Statutes/Other Implemented: 2012 OL Ch. 36 Sec. 14 (Enrolled SB 1581)
