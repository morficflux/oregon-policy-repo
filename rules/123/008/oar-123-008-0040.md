## Full text

123-008-0040 Consistency with Local Economic Development Plans Applicable adopted local economic development plans under ORS 285A.055(5) include, but are not limited to, relevant parts of the comprehensive (land use) plans of cities and counties, such as those related to Planning Goal 9.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285B.075(5) & 285A.110(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.180, 285A & 285B

History: EDD 4-2003, f. & cert. ef. 3-26-03 EDD 9-1990, f. & cert. ef. 5-23-90
