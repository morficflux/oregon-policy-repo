## Full text

123-006-0040 Contracts Must be Authorized Prior to Performance All Contracts, including any amendments, must be in writing and fully executed before any Work or Services may be performed or payment made. Contractors are not entitled to payment for any Work or Services performed prior to such execution.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.075 & 279.070
