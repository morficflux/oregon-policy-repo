# Purpose (OAR 123-022-0060)

## Full text

123-022-0060 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards, and criteria for making grants under ORS 285B.168 and 285A.340 to 285A.349 to community colleges, community college service districts, and other allowed entities to assist in the improvement and formation of small business development centers.

Statutory/Other Authority: 285B.165 - 285B.180 & ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.340 - 285A.349 & 285B.165 - 285B.180

History: OBDD 1-2018, amend filed 01/18/2018, effective 01/18/2018 EDD 6-1998, f. & cert. ef. 4-22-98
