# Waivers (OAR 123-090-0060)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

123-090-0060 Waivers The Director may waive non-statutory requirements of this division of administrative rules if the project demonstrates a contribution to state job strategies consistent with the purpose of the Strategic Reserve Fund. Projects that improve the economic condition of a Rural or Distressed Area, or further global competitiveness of Oregon firms may receive special consideration by the Director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075(5) & 285A.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.266

History: EDD 29-2009, f. 12-31-09, cert. ef. 1-1-10 EDD 7-2000, f. & cert. ef. 4-11-00
