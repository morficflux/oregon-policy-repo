# Additional Conditions with Deferral (OAR 123-674-6885)

## Full text

123-674-6885 Additional Conditions with Deferral In order for an authorized or qualified business firm to have its exemption under ORS 285C.175 suspended and then resume according to OAR 123-674-6880, though not directly provided under ORS 285C.203, a zone sponsor might impose conditions on the firm according to OAR 123-668-2000(2)(d):

(1) As specified in the resolution(s) granting the suspension in terms of the obligations on the firm and respective contingencies.

(2) That are enforced primarily through the sponsor’s revocation of the suspension or decisions described in OAR 123-674-6880(10).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285C.060(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285C.105(1)(i), 285C.203, 285C.225(1), 285C.230(2), 285C.235(2) & 285C.240(1)
