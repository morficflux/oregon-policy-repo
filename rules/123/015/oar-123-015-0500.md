# Reporting (OAR 123-015-0500)

## At a glance

## Full text

123-015-0500 Reporting Grantees are required to report to the department on a quarterly basis on progress made in meeting all obligations set forth in the performance based contracts and agreements.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL 2013 & ch. 762 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 2013 & ch. 762

History: OBDD 4-2014, f. & cert. ef. 4-1-14
