## Full text

123-025-0030 Remedies The authority may seek legal remedies against ports that fail to comply with the requirements governing the fund. Remedies will not be imposed by the authority until the port has been notified in writing of deficiencies and has been given a reasonable time to respond and correct the deficiencies noted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.654 - 285A.660
