## Full text

123-025-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards and criteria for operation of the Port Planning and Marketing Fund program authorized by ORS 285A.654 through 285A.660.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.654 - 285A.660
