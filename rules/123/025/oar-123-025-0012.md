# Annual (OAR 123-025-0012)

## At a glance

## Full text

123-025-0012 Annual Funding of Program The authority will transfer up to 5.00% of the assets of the Port Revolving Fund, not to exceed the annual accrued net income from the Port Revolving Fund into the Port Planning and Marketing Fund annually as calculated on receipt of the Fund Audit each year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.654 - 285A.660
