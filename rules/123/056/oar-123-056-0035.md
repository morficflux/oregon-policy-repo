# Distribution of (OAR 123-056-0035)

## Full text

123-056-0035 Distribution of Funds The Department, in its sole discretion, shall determine grants awarded from the Fund. The grant must support implementation of a project included in an Approved Strategic Plan.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285B.230 - 285B.266 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.230 - 285B.266
