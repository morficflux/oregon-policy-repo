## Full text

123-061-0010 Purpose These rules establish the criteria and process for selecting Projects to be funded by the Regional Infrastructure Fund. The Oregon Business Development Department administers the Regional Infrastructure Fund for the purpose of providing Grants and Loans to Local Governments and Tribes for infrastructure Projects including long-range planning, research and design.
