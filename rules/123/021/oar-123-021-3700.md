## Full text

123-021-3700 Administrative Costs of the Program The Department may charge the fund actual and necessary administrative expenses in operating the Fund and its programs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285B.200 - 285B.218 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285.476(2),
