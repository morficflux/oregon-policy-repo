## Full text

123-021-2200 Delegation With the exception of appeals, the Department may authorize and approve loan insurance authorizations and require execution of any document necessary or convenient to make effective such insurance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285B.200 - 285B.218 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.200 - 285B.218
