## Full text

123-051-1000 Grant Awards and Match

(1) Grants will be awarded only when there are sufficient funds available in the Seismic Rehabilitation Grants program.

(2) Grant funds shall be distributed to public education facilities and emergency services facilities as allocated by the Legislative Assembly.

(3) The maximum grant award is $2.5 million.

(4) There is no required match for this program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.093, 285A.098, 401.910 & 2013 OL Ch. 782 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.093, 285A.098 & 401.910
