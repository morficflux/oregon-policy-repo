## Full text

123-016-0060 Confidential Records For applicants and recipients of business retention services, ORS 192.502(17) lists certain types of business information that are exempted from public records requests.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.224 & 192.502(17)
