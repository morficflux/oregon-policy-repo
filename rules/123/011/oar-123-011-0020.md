## Full text

123-011-0020 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards and criteria for operation of the Oregon Economic Development Revenue Bond program authorized by ORS 285B.320 to 285B.371.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075(5) & 285A.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.320 - 285B.371
