## Full text

123-046-0040 Loan and Grant Applicant Eligibility

(1) Parties eligible for a loan or grant under these rules include municipalities, Persons, and non-profit organizations.

(2) The Department may limit the number of open projects an applicant may have at any one time.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & Ch 10, OL 2020 Statutes/Other Implemented: Ch 10, OL 2020
