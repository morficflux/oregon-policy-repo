# Ineligible Project Costs (OAR 123-046-0030)

## Full text

123-046-0030 Ineligible Project Costs Tide gate project expenses and costs expressly allowed by OAR 123-046-0020 are eligible for reimbursement from the fund. All other project costs, including but not limited to those listed below, are ineligible for reimbursement:

(1) Cost of purchase of general purpose motor vehicles and other equipment not directly related to the project;

(2) Indirect costs incurred by the applicant or a contractor; and

(3) Project operating or maintenance expenses.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & Ch 10, OL 2020 Statutes/Other Implemented: Ch 10, OL 2020
