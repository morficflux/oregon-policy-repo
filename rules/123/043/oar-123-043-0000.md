## Full text

123-043-0000 Purpose and Objectives Pursuant to ORS 285B.563, the Oregon Business Development Department is required to adopt rules that provide procedures, standards and criteria for the Water/Wastewater Financing Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285B.563 & 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.560 - 285B.599
