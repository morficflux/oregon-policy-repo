## Full text

123-623-1800 State Application and Approval Fees With respect to ORS 285C.612 and the fees payable to and collected by the Department:

(1) The following (non-refundable) amount must accompany the Application: (a) $5,000; or (b) $10,000 for a proposed Urban Project.

(2) After the Commission decides to approve the Application, but pending formal authorization as such through the Department, the Applicant must pay the following amount (of which the Department shall transfer 50 percent to the Department of Revenue to administer ORS 307.123): (a) $10,000; or (b) $50,000 for a proposed Urban Project.

(3) The Commission or Department will allocate payments collected and retained consistent with relevant provisions in OAR 123-009.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285C.615(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285C.612
