## Full text

123-030-0000 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards and criteria for operation of the Port Revolving Fund program authorized by ORS 285A.669 through 285A.732.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.669 - 285A.732
