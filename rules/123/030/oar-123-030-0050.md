## Full text

123-030-0050 Sanctions, Exceptions and Appeals The Department may seek legal remedies against ports that fail to comply with the requirements governing the fund. The Department will not impose remedies until the port has been notified in writing of deficiencies and has been given a reasonable time to respond and correct the deficiencies noted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.669 - 285A.732 & 285A.690
