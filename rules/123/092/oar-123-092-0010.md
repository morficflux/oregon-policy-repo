## Full text

123-092-0010 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards and criteria for the making of loans from the Small Business Expansion Loan Fund, also referred to as the Oregon Royalty Fund (ORF).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285B.092 & OL Ch 71 2018 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.092 & OL Ch 71 2018
