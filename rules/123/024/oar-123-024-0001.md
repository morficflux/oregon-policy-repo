## Full text

123-024-0001 Scope and Purpose In accordance with ORS 285A.020(5), the department shall give priority when providing funding for a project, a program or activity, to counties, cities, communities or other geographic areas that are designated as distressed by the department. The designation of distressed areas must be based on indicators of economic distress, including but not limited to unemployment, poverty and job loss.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.020, 285A.075, 285B.062 & 285B.065
