# Purpose (OAR 123-500-0150)

## At a glance

## Full text

123-500-0150 Purpose The Arts Program, under the direction of the Cultural Trust Board, awards grant funds to five statewide cultural entities to promote arts, heritage and humanities programs across Oregon and leverage existing resources to benefit more Oregonians.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 359.416 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 359.400 - 359.444
