# Purpose (OAR 123-500-0000)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

123-500-0000 Purpose The Arts Program, under the direction of the Cultural Trust Board, awards grants for the Oregon Cultural Trust through three grant programs: Cultural Development Grants to cultural organizations, Community Cultural Participation Grants to county and tribal cultural coalitions and Cultural Partner Grants to statewide cultural partner entities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 359.416 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 359.400 - 359.444
