## Full text

123-165-0050 Rule Waiver The director may waive any non-statutory requirements of OAR chapter 123, division 165 if it is demonstrated such a waiver will further the goals and objectives of the program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 286A.630 & 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.075
