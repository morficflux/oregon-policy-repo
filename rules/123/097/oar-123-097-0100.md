# Purpose (OAR 123-097-0100)

## Full text

123-097-0100 Purpose This division of administrative rules establishes procedures and standards for the designation of regionally significant industrial sites and the qualification of sponsors, which become the basis of tax reimbursement arrangements to assist public entities to develop such sites.

Statutory/Other Authority: 285B.626(1) & (9), 285B.627 (2), (3), (10), 285B.630(7) & ORS 285A.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.625 - 285B.632
