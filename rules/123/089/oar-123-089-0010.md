## Full text

123-089-0010 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to describe objectives, programs, processes and criteria for funding projects from the Oregon Innovation Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 284.706, ORS 284.720 & ORS 284.742 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 284.720 & ORS 284.742
