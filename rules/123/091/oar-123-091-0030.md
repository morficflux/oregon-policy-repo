## Full text

123-091-0030 Waivers The Director, or his designee, may waive non-statutory requirements of this division of administrative rule if such a waiver will serve to further the goals of the Oregon Business Retention and Expansion Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075, 285B & OL Ch. 549, Sec. 1-8 & 10-11 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B, OL Ch. 549, Sec. 1-8 & 10-11
