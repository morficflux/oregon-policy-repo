## Full text

123-656-0001 Purpose and Scope This division of administrative rules addresses the existence of enterprise zones in addition to OAR 123-650, as provided under:

(1) ORS 285C.300 to 285C.320 respective to Oregon-based Tribes; and

(2) ORS 285C.085 based on special federal designations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285C.060(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285C.085 & 285C.300 – 285C.320
