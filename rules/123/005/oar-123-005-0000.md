## Full text

123-005-0000 Applicability of Rules and Definitions This division of administrative rules applies to all public records for which the Department is custodian of. For the purposes of these rules definitions may be found in Procedural Rules, OAR 123-001.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075(A) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.410 - 192.505, 285A & 285B
