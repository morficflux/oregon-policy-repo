## Full text

123-098-0060 Uncommitted Annual Program Allocation Uncommitted funds from annual allocations will be added to the following annual allocation and made available in procedures defined within these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285B.635-285B.642 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.635-285B.642
