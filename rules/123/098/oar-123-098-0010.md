## Full text

123-098-0010 Purpose This division of administrative rules establishes standards and criteria for the approval of grants related to the Industrial Site Readiness Assessment Program (“Program”) to evaluate regional industrial land inventories.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285B.635-285B.642 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.635-285B.642
