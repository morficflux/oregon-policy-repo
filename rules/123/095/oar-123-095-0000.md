## Full text

123-095-0000 Purpose and Objectives The purpose of this division of administrative rules is to govern the use of funds in the Industry Competitiveness Fund established by ORS 285B.290.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075(5) & 285A.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.286 & 285B.290
