## Full text

123-018-0200 Administrative Costs of Program The Department may charge actual and necessary administrative expenses in operating the Program to the Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285B.115(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.115 & 285B.119
