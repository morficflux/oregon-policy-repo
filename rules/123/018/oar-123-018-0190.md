## Full text

123-018-0190 Reports of Regulatory Agencies The Department may require from the Participating Financial Institution information relating to the Participating Financial Institution’s status and performance, as developed by or for applicable state or federal regulatory bodies, and relevant to the Participating Financial Institution’s participation in the Program or the financial health of institution, or the Department may obtain public information from state or federal regulatory bodies such as the Oregon State Department of Consumer and Business Services, Division of Finance and Corporate Securities. The Department shall not disclose any confidential information obtained as described in thus rule, to the extent permitted by law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285B.115(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.113
