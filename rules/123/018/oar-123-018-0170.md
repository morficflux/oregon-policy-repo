## Full text

123-018-0170 Disposition of Funds Withdrawals from Loss Reserve Accounts The Department shall deposit moneys withdrawn by the Department from Loss Reserve Accounts as described in OAR 123-018-0040, 123-018-0150 and 123-018-0160 into the Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285B.115(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.115 & 285B.118
