123-017-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards and criteria for the making of loans from the Oregon Business Development Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285B.050 - 285B.098
