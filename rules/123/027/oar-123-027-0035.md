## Full text

123-027-0035 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards and criteria for operation of the Marine Navigation Improvement Fund authorized by ORS 777.262 to 777.267.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075(5) & 285A.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 777.262 - 777.267
