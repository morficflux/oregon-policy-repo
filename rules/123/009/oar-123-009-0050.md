# Purpose (OAR 123-009-0050)

## At a glance

## Full text

123-009-0050 Purpose The Oregon Business, Innovation and Trade Fund is established by ORS 285A.227 as a means to provide the Oregon Business Development Department with flexibility in funding and decision-making to respond to economic development needs on a statewide and regional basis.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285A.227(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.227
