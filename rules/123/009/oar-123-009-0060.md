## Full text

123-009-0060 Definitions For the purposes of these rules additional definitions may be found in Procedural Rules, OAR 123-001. As used in this division of administrative rules, unless the context requires otherwise the following definitions apply:

(1) Fund means the Oregon Business, Innovation and Trade Fund established in ORS 285A.227, which includes lottery funding for grant and loan programs and contracted services and all interest earnings that accrue to the Fund.

(2) "Allocation Plan" means the distribution plan of the legislatively authorized Fund biennial budget.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075 & 285A.227(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.227
