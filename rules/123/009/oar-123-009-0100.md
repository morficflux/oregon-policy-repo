## Full text

123-009-0100 Director’s Review and Approval of Awards Under Allocation Plan Once allocations from the Fund have been made by the Commission, the Director shall have final project funding approval unless otherwise delegated. All project funding shall be consistent with Commission allocations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 285A.075(5), 285A.110 & 285A.227(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 285A.227
