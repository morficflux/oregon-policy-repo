# Backtagging (OAR 603-012-0021)

## At a glance

## Full text

603-012-0021 Backtagging Eligible cattle destined for slaughter at all livestock auction markets must be identified by a Department approved backtag applied by auction market personnel under the supervision of a brand inspector or other authorized Department representative before such cattle leave the market zone. Backtags shall be applied in such a manner and location as specified by the Department. The backtags and glue for application may be furnished by the Department at no cost to the livestock auction market.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 596, 597 & 599 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 599.490

History: AD 850(22-67), f. 9-5-67, ef. 9-13-67
