## Full text

603-009-0020 Eligible Applicants Eligible applicants shall include:

(1) Cities, counties, councils of government, and state agencies.

(2) Private nonprofit organizations.

(3) Consortia and combinations of the above.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 576 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.013
