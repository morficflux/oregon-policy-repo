# Water (OAR 603-022-0520)

## At a glance

## Full text

603-022-0520 Water Supply Water under pressure shall be available to grading and candling area or room for cleaning purposes. Such water shall be from a supply properly located, protected, and operated, and shall be easily accessible, adequate, and of a safe, sanitary quality.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 632.811 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.811

History: AD 811(18-65), f. 12-21-65, ef. 1-1-66
