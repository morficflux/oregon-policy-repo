# Holding or Cold Room (OAR 603-022-0515)

## Full text

603-022-0515 Holding or Cold Room Graded and ungraded eggs and shall be stored in a manner to that they prevent cross-contamination and shall be kept clean, free of mold and objectionable odors, and properly lighted. The room shall be so constructed and equipped so that a temperature not to exceed 45 degrees F. can be maintained.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 632.811 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.811
