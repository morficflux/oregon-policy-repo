## Full text

603-022-0010 Oregon State Egg Fee

(1) Except as provided in OAR 603-022-0010(2), a fee of 2 ½ mills per dozen eggs is hereby established for eggs first sold in Oregon.

(2) Firms producing or distributing fewer than 101 dozen eggs per week are exempt from Oregon State Egg Fees.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 632 & ORS 561 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.811
