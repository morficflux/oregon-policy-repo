## Full text

603-022-0101 Eggs and Egg Products As provided in ORS 632.811, the regulations governing this subject matter, adopted by the Food and Drug Administration of the United States Department of Health, Education, and Welfare and in effect as of April 1, 2001, are hereby adopted as the rules governing this subject matter in Oregon. Such federal regulations are contained in Title 21, Chapter 1, Part 160 of the Code of Federal Regulations and entitled "Eggs and Egg Products.”

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 632 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.811
