## Full text

603-022-0340 Weight Classes

(1) The weight classes for Oregon Consumer Grades for Shell Eggs shall be as indicated in Table 5 and shall apply to all consumer grades.

(2) A lot average tolerance of 3.3 percent for individual eggs in the next lower weight class is permitted as long as no individual case within the lot exceeds five percent. [ED NOTE: Tables referenced are available from the agency.]
