## Full text

603-022-0605 Plant Requirements The sanitation standards for egg candling and grading facilities and establishments shall be those established in OAR 603-025-0020 and by the USDA Egg Products Inspection Act, 21 U.S.C. 1031, et seq.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 632.811 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.811
