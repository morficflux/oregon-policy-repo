## Full text

603-022-1000 Egg Establishment License Fees and Categories Except as provided in ORS 632.715, a person may not sell or distribute within this state any eggs to consumers or retailers without first obtaining a license from the department. As provided in ORS 632.730, a person may not engage in the commercial breaking of eggs for the purpose of recovering therefrom, for human food, the whites, yolks or whole egg meats, or any part thereof, without first obtaining a license from the department. The annual license fees for egg establishments, as provided for in ORS 632.720 and 632.730, are as follows:

(1) Egg handler: $30

(2) Egg breaker: $80

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561, ORS 616 & ORS 632 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 616.706, OL 2021, Ch. 564, ORS 632.720 & ORS 632.730
