## Full text

603-053-0440 Meetings The Committee will meet at the call of the Director or an official representative at least once annually. A majority of the members constitutes a quorum (7 members constitutes a quorum), a majority vote of the quorum at any meeting shall constitute an official action (a minimum of 4 members).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, 632.940 & 632.945 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.940 & 632.945
