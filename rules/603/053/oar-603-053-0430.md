## Full text

603-053-0430 Officers and Ex Officio The Director or an official representative shall serve as ex-officio members without the right to vote.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, 632.940 & 632.945 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.940 & 632.945
