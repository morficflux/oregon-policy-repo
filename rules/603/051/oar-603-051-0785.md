## Full text

603-051-0785 Prohibition of Imitation Olive Oil A person may not manufacture, produce, process, pack, expose, sell, offer for sale, possess, dispense, supply, or give away imitation olive oil.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, 616.230 & 616.761 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 616.761
