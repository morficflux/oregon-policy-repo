## Full text

603-051-0150 Official Standards The United States Standards for Bunched Carrots adopted by the United States Department of Agriculture effective September 18, 1954, and in effect on January 1, l963, hereby are adopted as the standards of the State of Oregon for bunched carrots. Such federal standards are contained in Title 7, Chapter 1, Part 51 of the Code of Federal Regulations in Sections 51.2455 to 51.2471, a copy of which is filed herewith and by this reference made a part hereof. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]
