## Full text

603-051-1020 Application of Tolerances The contents of individual containers in the lot, based on sample inspection, are subject to the following limitations. Individual samples shall consist of not less than 50 fruit, and shall not have more than double a specified tolerance except that at least two defective specimens may be permitted in any sample, provided that not more than one fruit affected by internal breakdown, freezing or decay be permitted in any sample and that the average for the entire lot is within the tolerances specified for the grade.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 632.905 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.905
