## Full text

603-051-0410 Official Standards; Lettuce The United States Standards For Grades of Lettuce adopted by the United States Department of Agriculture effective December 1, 1975, are hereby adopted as the standards for lettuce in the State of Oregon. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]
