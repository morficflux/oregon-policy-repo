## Full text

603-051-0515 Official Standards; Parsnips The United States Standards for Grades of Fresh Parsnips adopted by the United States Department of Agriculture effective March 23, 1954, are hereby adopted as the standards for fresh parsnips in the State of Oregon. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]
