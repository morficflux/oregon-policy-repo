## Full text

603-051-0483 Definitions For the purposes specified in ORS 632.206–632.260, “lot” means a continuous identifiable amount of fresh packed onions that does not exceed one day’s run.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 632.905 & 632.211 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.900 - 632.980 & 632.206 - 632.260
