## Full text

603-051-0695 Official Standards The United States Standards for Fall and Winter Type Squash adopted by the United States Department of Agriculture effective November 15, 1944, and in effect on January 1, 1963, hereby are adopted as the standards of the State of Oregon for fall and winter type squash. A copy of such federal standards is filed herewith and by this reference made a part hereof.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 632.900 - 632.980 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.900 - 632.980

History: AD 756, f. 7-2-63
