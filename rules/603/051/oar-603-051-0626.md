## Full text

603-051-0626 Official Standards The United States Standards for Grades of Potatoes adopted by the United States Department of Agriculture effective September 1, 1971, are hereby adopted as the official standards for the State of Oregon. A copy of the standards is filed herewith and by this reference made a part hereof. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]
