## Full text

603-051-0725 Official Standards; Fresh Tomatoes The United States Standards for Grades of Fresh Tomatoes adopted by the United States Department of Agriculture effective December 1, 1973, as amended February 1, 1975, and April 15, 1976, are hereby adopted as the standards for fresh tomatoes in the State of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 632 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.900 - 632.980
