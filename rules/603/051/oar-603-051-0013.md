## Full text

603-051-0013 Condition After Storage or Transit As applied to Oregon grades (OAR 603-051-0005), decay, scald, or any other deterioration which may have developed on apples after they have been in storage or transit shall be considered as affecting condition and not the grade.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 632.900 - 632.980 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.900 - 632.980
