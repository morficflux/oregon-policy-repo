## Full text

603-051-0305 Application of Standards The grade of a lot of filbert kernels shall be determined on the basis of a composite sample drawn from containers in various locations in the lot. However, any container or group of containers in which the filberts are obviously of a quality, type, or size materially different from that in the majority of containers shall be considered a separate lot, and shall be sampled separately.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 632 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.900 - 632.980
