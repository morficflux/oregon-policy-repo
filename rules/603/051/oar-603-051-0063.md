# Unclassified (OAR 603-051-0063)

## At a glance

## Full text

603-051-0063 Unclassified “Unclassified” consists of stalks of asparagus which have not been classified in accordance with any of the foregoing grades. The term “unclassified” is not a grade within the meaning of these standards but is provided as a designation to show that no grade has been applied to the lot.
