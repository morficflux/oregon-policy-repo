## Full text

603-050-0200 Declaration of Policy Providing and maintaining a voluntary program for the identification and standards relating to controlled atmosphere storage of produce are functions of state government, the responsibilities of which are vested in the Department in keeping with ORS 632.460 and 632.905.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 632 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 632.460 & 632.905
