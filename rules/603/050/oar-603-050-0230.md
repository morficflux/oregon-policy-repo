## Full text

603-050-0230 Penalties Upon a determination that a person has willfully violated any of the provisions of OAR 603-050-0210 to 603-050-0225, the Department may suspend, revoke, or refuse to nenew the registration of the official identification number assigned under OAR 603-050-0210(1).
