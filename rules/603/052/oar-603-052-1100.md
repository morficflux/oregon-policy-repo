603-052-1100 Purpose These rules guide the Oregon State Department of Agriculture, Plant Division, in administering its Imported Timber Products Inspection Program, as provided in ORS 570.700 to 570.710.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, 570.305 & 570.700 - 570.710 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 570.705 & 570.710
