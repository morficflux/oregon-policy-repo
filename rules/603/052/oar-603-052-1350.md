## Full text

603-052-1350 Premises and Permit Accessible to Department Officials Department officials shall have access to the premises where the invertebrates are housed and to the permit during normal business hours. The permit holder must keep a copy of the permit until it expires, the invertebrates are no longer alive, or for one year after release if release is allowable under the permit.

Statutory/Other Authority: 570.210 & 570.215 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 570.215
