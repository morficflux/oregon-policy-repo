## Full text

603-052-0260 Hood River Control Area: Insect Pests and Diseases in Fruit Trees A control area as authorized by ORS 570.405 to 570.415 is hereby created for the control of insects, pests and diseases related to, but not limited to, Apple scab, Pseudomonas blight, Coryneum blight, Fire blight, Codling Moth, Pear psylla, San Jose scale, Peach leaf curl, Twig borer, Shot hole borer, Apple Maggot and Oriental fruit moth within the boundaries of Hood River County, Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 570 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 561.190, 561.510 - 561.600, 570.305, 570.405 & 570.410 - 570.415
