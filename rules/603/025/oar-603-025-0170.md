# Bulk Placard Requirements (OAR 603-025-0170)

## Full text

603-025-0170 Bulk Placard Requirements If in bulk display form for wholesale or retail sale (rather than packaged form) any food that has been salvaged, as defined in subsection

(16) of ORS 616.250, shall be conspicuously and prominently displayed immediately adjacent to such bulk display. Such placard shall be of easily legible bold face print or type, of such color contrast that it may be easily read, and shall contain the statements required by OAR 603-025-0180.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, 616.230 & 616.700 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 616.230 & 616.700
