## Full text

603-025-0130 Label Pull Date Information Upon the request of the department, a person required to label a perishable food with a pull date shall obtain from the food manufacturer, processor or packager recommending the pull date, data from recognized scientific sources substantiating the establishment and use of the recommended date.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 616 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 616.835
