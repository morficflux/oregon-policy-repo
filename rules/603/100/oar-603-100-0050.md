## Full text

603-100-0050 Shellstock Harvesting and Distribution from Non-Interstate Approved Harvest Areas

(1) Each tag of any shellstock harvested from a non-interstate approved harvest area shall include the following statement in bold capitalized type, “OREGON DISTRIBUTION ONLY.” (a) This requirement is in addition to the Shellstock Identification requirements found in the 2009 Version of the National Shellfish Sanitation Program Guide for the Control of Molluscan Shellfish.

(2) Any person listed in the Interstate Certified Shellfish Shipper List (ICSSL) shall not sell or possess shellstock harvested from a non-interstate approved harvest area.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 622.180 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 622.180

History: DOA 13-2012, f. 5-29-12, cert. ef. 1-1-13
