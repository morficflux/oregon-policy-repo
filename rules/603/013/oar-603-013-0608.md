## Full text

603-013-0608 Separation of Facilities In a slaughterhouse wherein meat is slaughtered and sold for human consumption and one which also processes, handles, offers, or sells pet or animal food; then such facilities and equipment relating to the pet or animal food operation must be all located in a separate room from all the operations of the edible food or meat operations, as approved by the Department. Such pet or animal food procedures and operation shall not interfere with the proper required functions of the operation of the edible food operations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 603 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 603

History: AD 636, f. 3-15-60
