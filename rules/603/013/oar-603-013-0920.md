## Full text

603-013-0920 Schedule of Civil Penalties

(1) Operating an animal food slaughtering establishment or processing establishment without first obtaining a license therfor from the Department as required in ORS 619.031. Penalty — $5,000 to $10,000.

(2) Violation of ORS 619.031(7) by a person licensed under ORS 619.031(1)–(5). Penalties: (a) Minor — $1,000 to $4,000; (b) Moderate — $4,001 to $7,000; (c) Major — $7,000 to $10,000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, 603.995 & 619.996 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 603.995 & 619.996

History: DOA 2-2013, f. & cert. ef. 2-7-13
