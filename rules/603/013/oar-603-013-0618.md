## Full text

603-013-0618 Definitions In addition to the definitions set forth in ORS 601.010, the following shall apply:

(1) “Conduct of the business” means the disposal of raw material according to a license issued pursuant to ORS 601.040.

(2) “Raw material” means the body, carcass or all parts, including viscera, of an animal.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 601.120 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 601.120

History: AD 12-1995, f. & cert. ef. 6-30-95
