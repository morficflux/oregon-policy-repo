## Full text

603-058-0170 Labeling as to Directions for Use and Precautionary Statements

(1) Directions for use and precautionary statements on the labeling of all commercial feeds and custom mixed feeds containing additives (including drugs, special purpose additives, or non-nutritive additives) shall: (a) Be adequate to enable safe and effective use for the intended purposes by users with no special knowledge of the purpose and use of such articles; and (b) Include, but not be limited to, all information described by all applicable regulations under the Federal Food, Drug and Cosmetic Act effective as of the date these rules are promulgated.

(2) Adequate directions for use and precautionary statements are required for feeds containing non-protein nitrogen as specified in OAR 603-058-0180.

(3) Adequate directions for use and precautionary statements necessary for safe and effective use are required on commercial feeds distributed to supply particular dietary needs or for supplementing or fortifying the usual diet or ration with any vitamin, mineral, or other dietary nutrient or compound.
