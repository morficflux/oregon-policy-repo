## Full text

603-048-2305 Purpose

(1) The purpose of OAR 603-048-2300 to 603-048-2500 is to establish minimum testing standards for industrial hemp for human consumption and hemp items.

(2) All references to OAR Chapter 333, Division 7 refer to the rules in effect as of November 19, 2025.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, ORS 571.263, ORS 571.281, ORS 571.285, ORS 571.322, ORS 571.330 & ORS 571.337 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 571.322, ORS 571.330, ORS 571.337, ORS 571.333, ORS 571.336, ORS 571.339 & ORS 571.348
