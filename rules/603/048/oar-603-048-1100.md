## Full text

603-048-1100 Licensees Certified or Licensed by OLCC

(1) Licensees certified by OLCC under ORS 571.336 must comply with all applicable OLCC rules.

(2) Licensees licensed by OLCC under ORS 475C.065, 475C.085, 475C.093, or 475C.097 must comply with all applicable OLCC rules.
