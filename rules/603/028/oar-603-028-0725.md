## Full text

603-028-0725 Sanitation Standards A poultry or rabbit business claiming the exemption of OAR 603-028-0720 or OAR 603-028-0722 must slaughter the poultry and rabbits and conduct activities in accordance with ORS 619.026 and OAR 603-028-0100 in the establishment of the rabbit or poultry business.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561, 616 & 619 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 603.085, 619.046 & 603.038
