## Full text

603-028-0212 General Controls The operator of a slaughtering facility shall ensure that required blood and tissue samples, and any means of identification of domesticated elk slaughtered at that facility be collected and submitted to the Oregon Department of Agriculture.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 603.085

History: DOA 13-2002, f. & cert. ef. 4-11-02
