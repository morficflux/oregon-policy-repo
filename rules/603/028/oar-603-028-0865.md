## Full text

603-028-0865 Game Meat Inspection Reports On or before the 10th day of each month, a copy of each completed official inspection tag for the prior month shall be submitted to the Department by the agencies employing inspectors.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 619 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 603.085, 619.031 & 619.046
