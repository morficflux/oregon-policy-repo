603-001-0130 Procedures for Obtaining General Information Except as otherwise provided in OAR 603-001-0125 to 603-001-0170, requests for information shall be directed to the department’s Information Officer at the Agriculture Building, 635 Capitol Street, N.E., Salem, OR 97301.
