# Purpose (OAR 603-075-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

603-075-0005 Purpose These rules implement the agricultural mediation programs provided in ORS 36.252 – 268 (agricultural mediation services) and ORS 36.280 – 283 (mediation of disputes related to farming practices) and describe the process for the mediation programs offered by the Department, including but not limited to: eligibility for programs, application process, selection of mediation service providers for the programs, fees, methods of advertising the availability of mediation services and data collection.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & ORS 36.280-283 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 36.210 & ORS 36.252-268

History: DOA 22-2019, amend filed 11/26/2019, effective 11/26/2019 DOA 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-17-17 AD 14-1990, f. & cert. ef. 7-3-90
