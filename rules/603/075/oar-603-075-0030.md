## Full text

603-075-0030 Application for Mediation

(1) A person involved in a dispute may request mediation services by submitting a request to the Department on a form provided by the Department.

(2) The Department shall, within ten business days of the receipt of a request for mediation services, give written notice of receipt of the request to the parties or representative of the parties, to the dispute.

(3) Process for assigning a mediator is as described in 603-075-0045 and 603-075-0050.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 36.280 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 36.252 & 36.256
