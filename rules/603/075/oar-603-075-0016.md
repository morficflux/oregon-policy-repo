## Full text

603-075-0016 Eligibility for Mediation Programs The program coordinator will determine the eligibility of an applicant for the mediation programs consistent with ORS 36.256 (agricultural mediation services) or ORS 36.280 (disputes related to interference with farming practices), after review of an application for mediation services to determine whether mediation is appropriate as provided by the requirements for each program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 36.280 & ORS 36.256
