## Full text

603-024-0551 License Fees for Frozen Dessert Wholesalers As provided, in ORS 621.335, a person engaged in the business of freezing or making frozen desserts and then selling those frozen desserts at wholesale must obtain a license from the department. The annual license fee for a frozen dessert wholesaler, as defined in ORS 621.003(12), is $60.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, 616 & 621 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 621.335
