## Full text

603-024-0641 Transportation of Milk and Milk Products and Surroundings All producer Grade A raw milk for pasteurization must be picked up at least once each 72 hour period. In addition, to facilitate cleaning of the farm bulk tank, the tank shall be completely emptied once each 72 hour period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 621 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 621.060 & 621.261
