## Full text

603-024-0581 Standards for Licensed Milk Samplers and Graders Tank truck drivers and milk plant personnel licensed to officially sample milk from farm bulk milk tanks or producer containers at receiving plants shall use the procedures described in OAR 603-024-0211.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 621 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 621.060 & 621.261
