# Laboratory (OAR 603-024-0554)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

603-024-0554 Laboratory Certification Certificate to perform specific tests or analyses on specific products shall be issued on receipt of application and $50 fee for the first test and $10 each for additional tests and following an approved survey or split sampling for such tests. The total fee shall not exceed $100. Laboratories must apply for a license with the Department on the specified form in order to be certified. A certified laboratory may be limited to a specific test, to a specific method, and to specific products by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 621.072 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 621.072
