## Full text

603-024-0796 Nondairy Frozen Dessert Mix: Identity Nondairy Frozen Dessert Mix is the food, in a dry or concentrated form, used in the manufacture of a nondairy frozen dessert, which is prepared in accordance with the requirements of OAR 603-024-0795.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 621 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 621.311
