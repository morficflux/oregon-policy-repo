# Sampling Standard (OAR 603-024-0572)

## At a glance

## Full text

603-024-0572 Sampling Standard Sampling standard means during any consecutive six months the collection and examination or testing of at least four samples of milk and milk products in at least four separate months. Except that when 3 months show a month containing 2 sampling dates separated by at least 20 days.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 621 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 621.060 & 621.261
