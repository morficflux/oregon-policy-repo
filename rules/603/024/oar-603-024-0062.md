## Full text

603-024-0062 Sterilized Fluid Milk and Milk Products; Standards of Identity The standard of identity for sterilized milk and milk products shall be the standards for fluid milk and fluid milk products as set forth in OAR 603-024-0017.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561, 616 & 621 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 621.060 & 621.405
