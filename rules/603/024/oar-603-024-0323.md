## Full text

603-024-0323 Testing Procedures and Equipment All tests of milk and milk products shall be performed in accordance with standards established in Association of Agricultural Chemists (AOAC) or the 1992, 16th Edition of Standard Methods for the Examinations of Dairy Products, Chapter 5, 6, 10 & 12. Procedures or tests not found in AOAC or Standard Methods for the Examination of Dairy Products may only be used if approved by the Department. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651 & 621
