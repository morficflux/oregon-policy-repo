## Full text

603-024-0031 Standards of Multi-Vitamin Fortified or Multi-Mineral Fortified Milk or Milk Products The label of the milk or milk product shall include the specific vitamins or minerals added. The label of the milk or milk product shall bear the statement “mineral fortified” or similar statement approved by the Department. All additives shall be listed in the ingredient statement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 651 & 621

History: DOA 6-2002, f. & cert. ef. 1-28-02
