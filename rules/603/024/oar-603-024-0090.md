# Standard of Quality (OAR 603-024-0090)

## At a glance

## Full text

603-024-0090 Standard of Quality Imitation milk products when sold or offered for sale in the final delivery container shall not contain more than 20,000 bacteria per milliliter, 10 coliform per milliliter, and the temperature shall not exceed 45°F

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 621.418 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 621.418

History: AD 910(16-69), f. 12-5-69, ef. 1-1-70
