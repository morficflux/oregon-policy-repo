## Full text

603-074-0030 Multiple Violations; Consolidation of Enforcement Proceedings Notwithstanding that each and every violation is a separate and distinct offense, and in cases of continuing violations, that each day’s continuance is a separate and distinct violation unless otherwise determined by the Department, proceedings for the assessment of multiple civil penalties for multiple violations against the same person may be consolidated into a single proceeding.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468B.217, 468B.230, 561.190 & 561.191 Statutes/Other Implemented: 468B.230
