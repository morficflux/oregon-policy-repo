## Full text

603-054-0075 Mitigating Circumstances During the process of determining the amount of civil penalty to be assessed, the Director may take into consideration any factors which may have an influence on the particular case. Such factors may include whether the violation appears to be accidental or willful and repeated, whether the violation is a first time offense or if there is a history of violations and the degree of cooperation shown by the person involved in the violation.
