## Full text

603-054-0024 Fees for Issuance of Phytosanitary and Other Certificates The following fees and charges are established for inspections requested by nurseries in order to issue state or federal phytosanitary certificates and any other certificate that requires inspection prior to issuance of such certificates. The base charge for certificates will be $25 each. The USDA administrative charge for federal phytosanitary certificates will be $12 for federal phytosanitary certificates completed by ODA personnel or $6 for federal phytosanitary certificates issued through PCIT.
