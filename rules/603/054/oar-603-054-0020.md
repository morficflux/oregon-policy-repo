## Full text

603-054-0020 Fees for European Pine Shoot Moth Survey The fee for licensed nurseries or Christmas tree growers requesting European pine shoot moth survey is established at $85 per location (field) per year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 571 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 571.145

History: DOA 2-2003, f. & cert. ef. 1-7-03
