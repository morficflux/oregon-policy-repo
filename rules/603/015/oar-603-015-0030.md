## Full text

603-015-0030 Intent and Purpose It is the intent and purpose of the provisions of OAR 603-015-0025 to 603-015-0060 to verify and require that all measures and procedures are maintained and taken to eradicate and control diseases in pet animals, and/or the diseases in pet animals which may be transferable or transmissible from pet animals to other animals or persons.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 596 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 596.020

History: AD 8-1984, f. & ef. 5-23-84
