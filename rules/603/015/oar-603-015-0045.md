# Facilities — (OAR 603-015-0045)

## At a glance

## Full text

603-015-0045 Facilities — Outdoors Shall offer shade against sun, shelter against rain, snow, and wind; sufficient protection for pet animals against cold and inclement weather, and adequate drainage to eliminate excessive water in the runs. Adequate space should be allowed in outside houses and cages to permit the animal to stand, turn around, and lie down, without obstructing freedom of movement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 596 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 596.020

History: AD 8-1984, f. & ef. 5-23-84
