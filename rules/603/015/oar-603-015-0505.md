## Full text

603-015-0505 Hybrid Crossed Animals For the purposes of OAR 603-011-0705–603-011-0725, any hybrid animal that is produced by crossing a domestic animal and an exotic animal shall be regulated according to the laws governing domestic animals and not subject to exotic animal permit by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 609 Statutes/Other Implemented: 609.335 & ORS 609.305
