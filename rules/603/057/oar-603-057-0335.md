## Full text

603-057-0335 Sodium Cyanide and M-44 Device; Restrictions on Use Only employees of the United States Department of Agriculture, Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Animal Damage Control, who are licensed as public applicators in the category specified in OAR 603-057-0115(4)(a), shall be entitled to use sodium cyanide and the M-44 device for the control of coyotes and certain other wild canids.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 634 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 634.306(13) & 634.322(6)
