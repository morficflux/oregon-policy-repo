## Full text

603-057-0200 Limitations on Restricted Use Pesticides As provided in ORS 634.306 and ORS 634.322 and in addition to other provisions of ORS Chapter 634, the following limitations and procedures shall apply to the distribution and use of restricted use pesticides identified in OAR 603-057-0205:

(1) A restricted use pesticide shall only be: (a) Distributed for use by and used by an appropriately licensed commercial, noncommercial, public or private applicator; and (b) Distributed or sold to users by an appropriately licensed pesticide dealer.

(2) A private applicator, a pesticide operator, a commercial applicator, a noncommercial applicator, a public applicator, or their designated agent shall display their appropriate certificate or license to the pesticide dealer at the time of purchase of a restricted use pesticide, so that the number thereof may be recorded by the pesticide dealer pursuant to OAR 603-057-0140.
