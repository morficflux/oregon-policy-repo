## Full text

603-057-0365 Sodium Fluoroacetate (Compound 1080) and the Livestock Protection Collar; Registrant of Livestock Protection Collars Only the United States Department of Agriculture, Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service, Animal Damage Control may be the registrant of the livestock protection collar containing the active ingredient sodium fluoroacetate (Compound 1080).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 634 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 634.306(13) & 634.322(6)
