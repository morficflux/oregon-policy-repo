## Full text

603-057-0505 Consolidation of Proceedings Notwithstanding that each and every violation is a separate and distinct act and in cases of continuing violations, each day’s continuance is a separate and distinct violation, proceedings for the assessment of multiple civil penalties for multiple violations against a person may be consolidated into a single proceeding.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 634 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 634.306

History: AD 4-1990, f. & cert. ef. 3-16-90
