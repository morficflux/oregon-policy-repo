# Areas (OAR 603-057-0305)

## At a glance

## Full text

603-057-0305 Areas Affected All lands situated north of Interstate Highway 80N within the Counties of Morrow and Umatilla, excluding therefrom land presently subject to the East Umatilla Chemical Control District Order, shall be subject to the restrictions and limitations set forth in OAR 603-057-0305 to 603-057-0320.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190, 634.306 & 634.322 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 634.306 & 634.322

History: AD 1072(18-75), f. & ef. 12-5-75
