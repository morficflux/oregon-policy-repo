## Full text

603-055-0200 Definition As used in this rule, unless the context or a specially applicable definition requires otherwise: As defined in ORS 602.010, “Colony” or “colonies of bees” refers to any hive occupied by bees and “hive” means any receptacle or container made or prepared for use of bees, or box or similar container taken possession of by bees, except for nucleus hives.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 602.090 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 602.090
