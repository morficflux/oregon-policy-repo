## Full text

603-014-0010 Size of Brand and One Impression Limitation All brands shall be a single stamp impression of such size that the entire design can be placed within a circle one foot in diameter.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 604.027

History: AD 22-1981, f. & ef. 10-7-81 AD 585, f. 7-15-58, ef. 7-11-58
