## Full text

603-014-0030 No Ear or Flesh Marks Not Entitled to Be Recorded No ear or flesh marks shall be recorded for any species of livestock. However, the Department encourages the owner of recorded brands to submit such marks with the application for recording of brand.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 561.190
