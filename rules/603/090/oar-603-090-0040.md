## Full text

603-090-0040 Specific Action Requirements — Appeals

(1) Pursuant to ORS 568.912, a landowner subject to agricultural water quality management area rules may be required to undertake certain specific actions. The required specific actions may but need not be incorporated into an individual water quality management plan. A landowner may appeal a specific action requirement by filing a formal request for alternate measures as provided in OAR 603-090-0050.

(2) Prior to filing a formal request for alternate measures, a landowner may informally consult with the department regarding the specific actions required to comply with the agricultural water quality management area rules. Such consultation, however, shall not extend the time periods required for filing a formal request.

(3) A general requirement for an individual water quality management plan may not be appealed under this provision.
