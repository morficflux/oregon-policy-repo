## Full text

603-010-0053 Definition As used in ORS Chapter 607, “swine” means a breed of domestic pig that is used for food production, but does not include any breed used primarily as a pet (e.g., pot-bellied pig).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 607.005
