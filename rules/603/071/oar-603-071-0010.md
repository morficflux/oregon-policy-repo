## Full text

603-071-0010 Referendum on the Consolidation of Districts The administrative procedures applicable to a referendum on the consolidation of districts, as provided for in ORS 568.450 and 568.460, shall be those specified in OAR 603-071-0005, except the ballots shall provide for approval or disapproval of consolidation.
