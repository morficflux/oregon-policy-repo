## Full text

603-072-0015 Eligibility Any soil and water conservation district may apply to the department for implementation of a streambank erosion control or stream corridor management project so as to be eligible for partial state or federal funding of such activity. Another soil and water conservation district, or other local governmental agencies, may be co-participants with the applicant without jeopardizing the applicant’s eligibility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 568 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 568.912

History: AD 14-1982, f. & ef. 11-1-82
