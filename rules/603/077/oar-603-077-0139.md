## Full text

603-077-0139 Emergency Open Burning The OARs regarding Emergency Opening Burning are defined by the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality in OAR 340-266-0065. If approved, the open field burning of the emergency burn will be conducted by the Department in accordance with these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.585
