## Full text

603-077-0188 Stipulated Penalties Nothing in OAR chapter 603, division 077 shall affect the ability of the Director to include stipulated penalties in a Stipulation and Final Order, Consent Order, Consent Decree or any other agreement issued under ORS Chapters 183, 468, or 468A.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.585
