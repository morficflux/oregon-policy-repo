## Full text

603-077-0170 Consolidation of Proceedings Notwithstanding that each and every violation is a separate and distinct offense, and in cases of continuing violations, that each day’s continuance is a separate and distinct violation, proceedings for the assessment of multiple civil penalties for multiple violations may be consolidated into a single proceeding.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.585

History: DOA 2-1998, f. & cert. ef. 3-3-98
