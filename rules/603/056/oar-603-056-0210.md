## Full text

603-056-0210 Embargo, Seizure, Detention or Quarantine of Seed Any embargo or seizure of agricultural seed or vegetable seed under the authority of ORS 633.670(3), and any quarantine or detention of agricultural seed or vegetable seed under the authority of 633.690, shall conform to the requirements of 561.605 to 561.630 relating to the exercise of the Department’s seizure, detention and embargo powers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 633 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 633.680

History: AD 8-1983, f. & ef. 7-19-83
