## Full text

603-027-0665 Participating Laboratory The Director is authorize to operate a Participating Laboratory as part of the National Type Evaluation Program. In this regard, the Director is authorized to charge and collect fees for type evaluation services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 618 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 618.156
