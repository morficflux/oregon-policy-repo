## Full text

603-027-0660 Certificate of Conformance The Director may require any weight or measure, or any weighing or measuring instrument or device to be issued a Certificate of Conformance prior to use for commercial or law enforcement purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561 & 618 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 618.156
