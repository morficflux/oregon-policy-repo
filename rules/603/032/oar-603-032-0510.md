# Posting of License Number (OAR 603-032-0510)

## Full text

603-032-0510 Posting of License Number Each warehouseman shall post the warehouse license number assigned to the facility by the Department, on all grain warehouses and elevators, just below the “Oregon Bonded Grain Warehouse” sign, in letters and numerals not less than eight

(8) inches high and as follows: “LICENSE NO. _______” (Also see OAR 603-032-0110(6)).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 586.225 - 586.561 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 586.225 - 586.561
