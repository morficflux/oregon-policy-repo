# Other Regulations Also Apply (OAR 603-021-0760)

## At a glance

OAR 603-021-0760 — Other Regulations Also Apply. Chapter 603, Division 021. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

603-021-0760 Other Regulations Also Apply OAR 603-021-0709 to 603-021-0755, relating to nonalcoholic beverages, are in addition to, and not in lieu of, other regulations relating to food and such beverages, and unless in conflict herewith, continue to apply to such beverages and the plants in which they are handled and manufactured.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 635.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 635.045

History: AD 851(23-67), f. & ef. 9-13-67
