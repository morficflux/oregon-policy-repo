## Full text

603-021-0735 Control of Insects and Animals The soft drink plant shall be free of rodents, rodent harborages, insects, and insect-breeding places. Effective measures shall be used to control and eliminate insects, vermin, rodents, and domesticated animals. Insecticides and rodenticides shall be properly identified, used, and stored in a safe and acceptable manner.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561.190 & 635.045 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 635.045
