## Full text

603-021-0030 Unwrapped Bread Labeling Exemption The provisions of ORS 625.220 and these regulations do not apply in a bakery where unwrapped bread is on display and available for inspection by the prospective purchaser and where, after purchase, each loaf is immediately placed in a bag by bakery personnel for the convenience of such purchaser.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561, 616 & 625 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 625.150
