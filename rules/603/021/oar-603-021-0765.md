## Full text

603-021-0765 Nonalcoholic Beverage Manufacturer License Fees As provided in ORS 635.027, no person shall operate or engage in the business of a nonalcoholic beverage manufacturer without first obtaining and thereafter maintaining a license. Pursuant to ORS 635.030, the department may establish license fees for nonalcoholic beverage manufacturers based on the license applicant’s annual gross sales in Oregon. The annual fees for non-alcoholic beverage manufacturing businesses, as defined in ORS 635.010(2), are as follows:

(1) $0-$5,000 - $191

(2) $5,001 - $50,000 - $191

(3) $50,001 - $500,000 - $317

(4) $500,001 - $1,000,000 - $445

(5) $1,000,001 - $5,000,000 - $696

(6) $5,000,001 to $10,000,000 - $824

(7) greater than $10,000,000 - $1,077

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561, ORS 616 & ORS 635 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 635.027, OL 2021, Ch. 564, ORS 635 & ORS 635.030
