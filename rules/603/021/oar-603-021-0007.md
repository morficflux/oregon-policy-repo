## Full text

603-021-0007 Domestic Kitchen Bakeries In addition to the requirements of OAR 603-021-0010, a bakery in an area which is part of a domestic kitchen shall comply with the provisions of OAR 603-025-0200.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 561, 616 & 625 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 625.150
