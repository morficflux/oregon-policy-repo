603-017-0010 Statement of Purpose Pursuant to authority vested in the Department of Agriculture under ORS 628.350, the following rules and regulation for the purpose of OAR 603-025-0020 to 603-025-0050 are for the purpose of regulating and licensing the operation of refrigerated locker plants.
