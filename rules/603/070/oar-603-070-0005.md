## Full text

603-070-0005 Hearing Upon a Petition for the Formation of a District The administrative procedures applicable to a hearing upon a petition for the formation of a district, as provided for in ORS 568.310, and the department’s determination resulting therefrom, as provided for in ORS 568.330 and 568.340, shall be those specified in OAR 603-001-0005.
