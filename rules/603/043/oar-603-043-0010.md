## Full text

603-043-0010 Scope and Purpose These rules establish a challenge process for those producers who dispute the amount of a commodity assessment levied against the producer pursuant to ORS 576.325(3)(a) and procedures for the affected commission to process the challenge. These rules also establish guidelines for the commission in setting their industry average unit price.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576.044 & 576.370 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.320
