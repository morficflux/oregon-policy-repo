## Full text

603-042-0066 Types and subtypes of commodities to be assessed by Oregon Tall Fescue Commission For the purposes of implementing section 5 of HB 3401 (2019) and ORS 576.051(3), the Director identifies all common or named varieties of (bot.) Dactylis glomerata as subject to assessment, support and promotion by the Oregon Tall Fescue Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576.066 (1)(e) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.051
