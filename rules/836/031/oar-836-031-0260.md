## Full text

836-031-0260 Reinsurance Increases to or credits against reserves held that arise because of reinsurance assumed or reinsurance ceded must be determined in a manner consistent with OAR 836-031-0200 to 836-031-0300 and also with all applicable provisions of the reinsurance contracts that affect the liabilities of the insurer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 733.080 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 733.080
