## Full text

836-028-0016 Amendments to Registration by Purchasing Group

(1) A purchasing group shall amend its registration in this state when the purchasing group changes any of the following. (a) The principal place of business of the purchasing group; (b) The insurer or risk retention group from whom coverage is purchased; (c) The lines or classifications of liability insurance that the purchasing group purchases.

(2) When a purchasing group changes its principal place of business, the purchasing group shall include the following in the amended registration: (a) The street address, including city and state; (b) The mailing address, if different; (c) The telephone number.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731, 735 & 746 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 735.330(1)(e)
