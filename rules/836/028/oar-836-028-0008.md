## Full text

836-028-0008 Unfair Trade Practice Failure of a purchasing group to comply with OAR 836-028-0013 or 836-028-0020 is an unfair trade practice under ORS 746.240.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731, 731 & 746 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.240
