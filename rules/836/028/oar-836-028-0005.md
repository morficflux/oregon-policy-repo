## Full text

836-028-0005 Statutory Authority, Purpose OAR 836-028-0005 to 836-028-0045 are adopted pursuant to ORS 731.244 and 735.360 in order to carry out the Oregon Liability Risk Retention Law and to protect the insurance-buying public.
