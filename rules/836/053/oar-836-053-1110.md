## Full text

836-053-1110 Notice of Complaint Filing with Director A written decision by an insurer in response to a grievance must prominently disclose the following information:

(1) That the enrollee has a right to file a complaint or seek other assistance from the Division of Financial Regulation of the Department of Consumer and Business Services; and

(2) The contact information for the director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services described in OAR 836-053-1030(6).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743.804
