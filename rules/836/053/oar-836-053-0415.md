## Full text

836-053-0415 Cancellation of an Individual Health Benefit Plan Coverage The notice requirements of ORS 743.499 and 743.894 are triggered at the time an insurer takes administrative action to terminate coverage.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 743.499, 743.769 & 743.894 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743.499, 743.766–743.769 & 743.894
