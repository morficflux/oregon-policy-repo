## Full text

836-053-0900 Purpose; Statutory Authority OAR 836-053-0900 and 836-053-0910 are adopted under the authority of ORS 731.244 for the purpose of carrying out ORS 743.730 to 743.773 and providing rate filing requirements and procedures for small employer and individual health benefit plans.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743.730 - 743.773
