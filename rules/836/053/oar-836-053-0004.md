## Full text

836-053-0004 Compliance with Federal and State Law Upon contract issuance or renewal, any insurer offering a health benefit plan must update the plans of the insurer as necessary to comply with state and federal law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 742.005
