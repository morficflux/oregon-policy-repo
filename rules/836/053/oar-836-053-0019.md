## Full text

836-053-0019 Purpose; Statutory Authority; Enforcement

(1) OAR 836-053-0010 to 836-053-0070 are adopted for the purpose of implementing ORS 743B.003 to 743B.013 and 743B.100, pursuant to the authority of ORS 731.244, 743B.003 to 743B.013 and 743B.100.

(2) Violation of any provision of OAR 836-053-0021 to 836-053-0065 is an unfair trade practice under ORS 746.240.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 743B.003 & 746.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743B.003 to 743B.013 & 743B.100.
