## Full text

836-012-0090 Other Security A ceding insurer may take credit for unencumbered funds withheld by the ceding insurer in the United States subject to withdrawal solely by the ceding insurer and under its exclusive control.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.508 - 731.511 & 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 731.508 - 731.511 & Or Laws 2019, ch 151
