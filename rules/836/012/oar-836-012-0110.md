# Contracts Affected (OAR 836-012-0110)

## At a glance

## Full text

836-012-0110 Contracts Affected All new and renewal reinsurance transactions entered into on and after January 1, 2019, shall conform to the requirements of ORS 731.509 to 731.511 and OAR 836-012-0000 to 836-012-0110 if credit is to be given to the ceding insurer for such reinsurance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.508 - 731.511 & 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 731.508 - 731.511 & Or Laws 2019, ch 151
