## Full text

836-012-0011 Credit for Reinsurance — Reinsurer Authorized in this State Pursuant to ORS 731.509(4), the director shall allow credit for reinsurance ceded by a domestic insurer to an assuming insurer that was authorized in this state as of any date on which statutory financial statement credit for reinsurance is claimed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.508 - 731.511 & 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 731.508 - 731.511 & Or Laws 2019, ch 151
