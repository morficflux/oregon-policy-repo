## Full text

836-042-0115 Trade Practices Found Injurious to the Insurance Buying Public Failure of an insurer to comply with OAR 836-042-0110 constitutes an unfair claims practice under ORS 746.240.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 737.325(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.240
