## Full text

836-042-0501 Statutory Authority; Purpose; Applicability; Effective Date

(1) OAR 836-042-0501 to 836-042-0520 are adopted pursuant to: (a) The general rulemaking authority of the Director in ORS 731.244 as an aid to the effectuation of 737.320(6) which enables the Director to require that filed rates, rating plans and rating systems be submitted to the Director for review prior to becoming effective; and (b) The specific authority of the Director under ORS 737.207 to specify the markets of commercial liability insurance in which insurers and rating organizations must submit rate filings before they become effective if the average annual rate level increase or decrease for each market exceeds 15 percent.

(2) The purpose of OAR 836-042-0501 to 836-042-0520 is to moderate the sharp cyclical swings in the availability and affordability of commercial liability insurance. Such moderation is accomplished by requiring that insurers and rating organizations submit to the Director their filings of rates, rating plans and rating systems for a specified commercial liability insurance market for review before such filings become effective, if the average annual rate level increase or decrease for that market exceeds 15 percent.

(3) OAR 836-042-0501 to 836-042-0520 apply to all commercial liability insurance filings.
