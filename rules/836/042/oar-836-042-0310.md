## Full text

836-042-0310 Producers A person shall not act as an insurance producer in connection with a mass marketing plan for any kind of insurance unless such person is licensed as an insurance producer to transact casualty insurance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 & 744 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 737.600(3)(d)
