## Full text

836-042-0430 Disclosure Required for Day Care Facilities Every insurer selling insurance under a group policy for day care facilities shall make full and fair disclosure to prospective insureds, prior to sale, of all features of the plan, whether favorable or unfavorable, including but not limited to pricing and policyholder services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 & 737 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 737.600(3)(g)
