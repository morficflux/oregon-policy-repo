## Full text

836-011-0260 Distribution of Annual Financial Statement A public body or the administrator of a self-insurance program must make the annual financial statement available to program participants and to the director not later than six months after the close of the program’s fiscal year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 30.282 & 731.036
