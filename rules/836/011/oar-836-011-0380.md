## Full text

836-011-0380 Supplemental Provisions; Exemption

(1) 836-011-0300 to 836-011-0390 are supplemental to any other provisions of the laws of this state, and do not preclude or limit any other powers or duties of the Director under such laws, including, but not limited to, OAR 836-011-0100 to 836-011-0120.

(2) OAR 836-011-0300 to 836-011-0390 do not apply to any domestic insurer transacting property and casualty insurance that: (a) Writes direct business only in this state; (b) Writes direct annual premiums of $2 million or less; and (c) Assumes no reinsurance in excess of five percent of direct premium written.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 731.554 & 733.210 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 731.554 & 731.574
