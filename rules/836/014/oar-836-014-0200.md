## Full text

836-014-0200 Statutory Authority and Implementation

(1) OAR 836-014-0200 to 836-014-0330 are adopted under the authority of ORS 731.244, 731.804, 744.331, 744.358 and 746.240 and section 20, chapter 711, Oregon Laws 2009 (Enrolled Senate Bill 973) for the purpose of implementing 744.326, 744.328, 744.331, 744.341 and 744.358 and sections 2, 5, 11 to 19 and 22, chapter 711, Oregon Laws 2009 (Enrolled Senate Bill 973).

(2) OAR 836-014-0205, 836-014-0226, 836-014-0263, 836-014-0285 and 836-014-0325, and the amendments to 836-014-0200 to 836-014-0325 are operative on and after the date that they are filed with the Secretary of State.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 744.331 & 744.358 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 744.326, 744.328, 744.331, 744.341, 744.358, Sec. 2, 5, 11–19 & 22, Ch. 711 & OL 2009
