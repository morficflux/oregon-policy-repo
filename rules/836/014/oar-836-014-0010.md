# Required (OAR 836-014-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

836-014-0010 Required Capitalization A legal expense organization shall possess and thereafter maintain capital or surplus, or any combination thereof, of not less than $25,000 or an amount equal to 25 percent of the gross written prepaid fees collected from plan members in the preceding calendar year, whichever is greater, but in no case shall the required amount be more than $300,000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 & 750 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 750.535(3)(a)

History: ID 4-1990, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-90
