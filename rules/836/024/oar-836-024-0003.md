## Full text

836-024-0003 Statutory Authority; Purpose OAR 836-024-0003 to 836-024-0055 are authorized by ORS 732.415(4). These rules are adopted to carry out the purposes of 732.415, to prevent fraud or deception in connection with the solicitation of proxies, consents, and other authorizations, and to protect the insurance-buying public in accordance with the purpose of the Insurance Code.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 & 732 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.415(4)
