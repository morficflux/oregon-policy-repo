## Full text

836-024-0205 Exemption of Certain Securities from ORS 732.440 A security is exempt from ORS 732.440 to the extent necessary to render lawful under that statute the broker’s execution of an order for an account in which the broker has no direct or indirect interest.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 732.420, 732.430, 732.435, 732.445, 732.450 & 732.455 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.420 - 732.455
