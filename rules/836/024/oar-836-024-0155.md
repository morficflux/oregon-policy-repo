## Full text

836-024-0155 Exemption from ORS 732.435 That Need not be Reported Under ORS 732.430 A transaction that has been or is exempted from the requirements of ORS 732.430 is also exempted from 732.435 to the extent that the transaction would otherwise be subject to 732.435.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 732.420, 732.430, 732.435, 732.445, 732.450 & 732.455 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.420 - 732.455
