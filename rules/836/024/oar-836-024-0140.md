## Full text

836-024-0140 Certain Transactions Subject to ORS 732.430 Acquisition or disposition of the privilege of a transferable option, put, call, spread or straddle is a change in the beneficial ownership of the security to which the privilege relates. A person who acquires or disposes of such a privilege shall file a statement relating to the acquisition or disposition. This rule does not exempt a person, however, from filing the statements required when the option, put, call, spread or straddle is exercised.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 732.420, 732.430, 732.435, 732.445, 732.450 & 732.455 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.420 - 732.455
