## Full text

836-033-0105 Statutory Authority; Purpose OAR 836-033-0110 is adopted pursuant to ORS 733.550(3), which grants the Commissioner authority to prescribe standards to be applied to determine if obligations invested in by an insurer are “amply secured obligations” under the statutes regulating investments of insurers. The purpose of the section is to prescribe such standards.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 733 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 733.550(3)

History: IC 66, f. & ef. 6-4-76
