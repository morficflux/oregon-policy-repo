## Full text

836-052-0840 Termination of Coverage For purposes of ORS 743.560 and 743.562, termination of coverage under a group health insurance policy includes the amendment or reissuance of a policy to delete one or more classes of certificate holders from coverage.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 743.526, 743.560 & 743.562 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743.560
