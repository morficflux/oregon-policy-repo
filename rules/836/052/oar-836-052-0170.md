## Full text

836-052-0170 Filing Requirements for Advertising An issuer shall provide to the Director a copy of any Medicare supplement advertisement intended for use in this state, whether through the written, radio or television medium, for review or approval by the Director to the extent it may be required under ORS 742.009 and other state law. Each advertisement shall comply with all applicable laws and rules of this state.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 743.010, 743.013, 743.680 - 743.689 & 746.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743.687
