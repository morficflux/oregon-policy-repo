## Full text

836-052-0606 Use and Definition of “Home” or Similar Wording A long-term care insurance policy that defines “home” or uses similar wording to refer to the residence of the insured shall define or use wording that means or refers to the principal place of residence for the insured, whether a private home, a foster home, congregate care or assisted living facility or other place in a community setting, other than a licensed nursing facility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731, 742 & 743 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743.655(1)(a) & 743.656
