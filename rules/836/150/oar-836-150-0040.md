## Full text

836-150-0040 Reinsurance Parameters

(1) For the benefit year beginning on January 1, 2022 the parameters for the Oregon Reinsurance Program are: (a) An attachment point of $92,000; (b) A reinsurance cap of $1,000,000; and (c) A coinsurance rate of fifty percent.

(2) For the benefit year beginning on January 1, 2023 the parameters for the Oregon Reinsurance Program are: (a) An attachment point of $95,000; (b) A reinsurance cap of $1,000,000; and (c) A coinsurance rate of fifty percent.

(3) For the benefit year beginning on January 1, 2024 the parameters for the Oregon Reinsurance Program are: (a) An attachment point of $95,000; (b) A reinsurance cap of $1,000,000; and (c) A coinsurance rate of fifty percent.

(4) For the benefit year beginning on January 1, 2025 the parameters for the Oregon Reinsurance Program are: (a) An attachment point of $103,000; (b) A reinsurance cap of $1,000,000; and (c) A coinsurance rate of fifty percent.

(5) For the benefit year beginning on January 1, 2026 the parameters for the Oregon Reinsurance Program are: (a) An attachment point of $108,000; (b) A reinsurance cap of $1,000,000; and (c) A coinsurance rate of fifty percent.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & Or Laws 2017, ch 538, sec 19 Statutes/Other Implemented: Or Laws 2017, ch 538, sec 18-21
