## Full text

836-150-0010 Purpose and Statutory Authority The purpose of OAR 836-150-0010 to 836-150-0060 is to administer the Oregon Reinsurance Program established in the Department of Consumer and Business Services for the purposes of stabilizing the rates and premiums for individual health benefit plans and providing greater financial certainty to consumers of health insurance in this state.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 2017 Or Laws ch 538 sec 19 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2017 Or Laws ch 538 sec 18-21
