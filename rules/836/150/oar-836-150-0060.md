## Full text

836-150-0060 Duties of the Administrator As administrator of the Oregon Reinsurance program, the department will:

(1) Conduct an audit of the information submitted under 836-150-0030.

(2) Notify reinsurance eligible issuers of the results of the calculation described under 836-150-0050, including any modifications of the coinsurance rate.

(3) Issue reinsurance payments to each reinsurance eligible issuer in accordance with OAR 836-150-0050.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 2017 Or Laws ch 538 sec 19 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2017 Or Laws ch 538 sec 18-21
