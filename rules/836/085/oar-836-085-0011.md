## Full text

836-085-0011 Hearing on Cancellation Within 30 days after receiving a notice of cancellation under OAR 836-085-0010, an insured may request a hearing before the Director. The purpose of this hearing shall be limited to establishing the existence of the proof or evidence given by the insurer in its notice of cancellation. The burden of proving the reason for cancellation shall be upon the insurer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.045, 746.055, 746.160(3) & 746.240
