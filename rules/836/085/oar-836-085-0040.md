## Full text

836-085-0040 Cancellation for Nonpayment of Premium OAR 836-085-0035 does not apply if the ground for cancellation or nonrenewal is nonpayment of the premium and if the notice so states.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.045, 746.055, 746.160(3) & 746.240
