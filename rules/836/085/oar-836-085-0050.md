## Full text

836-085-0050 Proof of Notice A post office certificate of mailing to the named insured at the named insured's last-known address constitutes conclusive proof that the named insured received the notice of cancellation or nonrenewal on the third calendar day after the date of the certificate of mailing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 746.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.045, 746.055, 746.160(3) & 746.240
