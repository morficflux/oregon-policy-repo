## Full text

836-080-0230 Standard for Prompt Claim Investigation An insurer shall complete its claim investigation not later than the 45th day after its receipt of notification of claim, unless the investigation cannot reasonably be completed within that time.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.230
