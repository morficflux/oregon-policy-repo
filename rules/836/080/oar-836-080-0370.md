## Full text

836-080-0370 Instruction of Title Company Employees About Rules Required

(1) A title company shall give a copy of OAR 836-080-0305 to 836-080-0370 to each employee who markets or performs any service offered by the title company.

(2) A title company shall instruct each employee described in section

(1) of this rule about the content of the rules as follows: (a) In the case of each employee employed on the effective date of OAR 836-080-0305 to 836-080-0370, within 60 calendar days after that date; and (b) In the case of each employee employed after the effective date of OAR 836-080-0305 to 836-080-0370, within 30 calendar days after the date the employee commences work.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.045, 746.055, 746.160 & 746.240
