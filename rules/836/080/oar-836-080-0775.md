# Severability (OAR 836-080-0775)

## Full text

836-080-0775 Severability If any provisions of OAR 836-080-0750 to 836-080-0775 or their application to any person or circumstance is held invalid for any reason, the invalidity shall not affect the other provisions or any other application of 836-080-0750 to 836-080-0775 that can be given effect without the invalid provisions or applicant. To this end all provisions of 836-080-0750 to 836-080-0775 are declared to be severable.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.075, 746.110 & 746.240

History: ID 7-2007, f. 9-14-07, cert. ef. 1-1-08
