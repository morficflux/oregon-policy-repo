## Full text

836-080-0440 Unfair Insurance Trade Practice Violation of any provision of OAR 836-080-0425 to 836-080-0440 is an unfair trade practice for purposes of ORS 746.240.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.240
