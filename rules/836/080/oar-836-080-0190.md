## Full text

836-080-0190 Annuity Sales; Disclosure Not a Defense The fact that a person made a disclosure to a consumer about the nature of an annuity in connection with the sale of the annuity to the consumer is not a defense by itself to a determination by the director under OAR 836-080-0170 to 836-080-0188 that the annuity sale violated the care requirements under ORS 743.262.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 743.262 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.100, 746.110, 746.240 & 743.262
