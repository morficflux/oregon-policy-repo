# False or Deceptive Publications by Insurer Prohibited (OAR 836-080-0155)

## Full text

836-080-0155 False or Deceptive Publications by Insurer Prohibited An insurer shall not knowingly adopt, or cause or permit to be issued, circulated or used, any representation, plan, schedule, letter, or advertising material of any kind stating or implying that the insurer has acted or will in the future act in any manner at variance with OAR 836-080-0105 to 836-080-0155.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 & 746 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.015(1), 746.075, 746.160(3) & 746.240

History: IC 69, f. & ef. 7-20-76
