## Full text

836-080-0800 Definitions As used in OAR 836-080-0800 to 836-080-0810, “loss runs” means information about a current or prior commercial policyholder’s reported losses, including paid amounts on all reported claims. Such losses shall be valued as of the insurer’s most current valuation date, but not more than 90 days prior to the date of the request for loss runs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.160 & 746.240
