## Full text

836-200-0215 One-time processing fee, cancelled application A discount medical plan organization may retain a one-time processing fee that is not more than $30 when the organization refunds fees, service or subscription charges, dues or other consideration paid by a member who cancels a discount medical plan as provided in section 8, chapter 272, Oregon Laws 2007.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 742.432 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 742.432

History: ID 5-2008, f. & cert. ef. 4-14-08
