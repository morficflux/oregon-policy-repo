## Full text

836-200-0060 Service on Registrant The Director may make service on a registrant at the address shown on the current registration of the registrant on file with the Director, in the manner provided in ORS 183.310 to 183.550.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 646.267 & 646.285 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 646.263 - 646.285
