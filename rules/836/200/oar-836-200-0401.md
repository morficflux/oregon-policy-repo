## Full text

836-200-0401 Statement of Purpose; Authority; Applicability Under the authority of Oregon Laws 2013, chapter 570, section 1, ORS 735.530 to 735.552 shall be administered and enforced in accordance with the Insurance Code. The rules promulgated under ORS 735.530 to 735.552 are authorized and reasonably necessary for, or as an aid to, the effectuation of the Insurance Code.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 735.532 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 735.530 - 735.552
