## Full text

836-200-0500 Purpose and Statutory Authority The purpose of OAR 836-200-0500 to 836-200-0560 is to administer the Oregon Prescription Drug Price Transparency Program established in the Department of Consumer and Business Services for the purposes of providing notice and disclosure of information relating to the cost and pricing of prescription drugs in order to provide accountability for prescription drug pricing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 646A.689 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 646A.689 & 646A.692
