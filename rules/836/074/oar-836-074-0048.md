# Other Trust Account Requirements (OAR 836-074-0048)

## At a glance

OAR 836-074-0048 — Other Trust Account Requirements. Chapter 836, Division 074. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

836-074-0048 Other Trust Account Requirements OAR 836-074-0005 to 836-074-0050 do not prohibit an insurer from establishing requirements applicable to trust accounts of insurance producers of the insurer, if the requirements are more restrictive than the provisions of 836-074-0005 to 836-074-0050.
