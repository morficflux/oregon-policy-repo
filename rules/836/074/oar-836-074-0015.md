## Full text

836-074-0015 Director’s Enforcement Authority The Director may apply any sanction for violation of ORS 744.225 that the Director may apply for any other violation of the Insurance Code or rules adopted thereunder.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 744.225

History: ID 9-1987, f. 12-22-87, cert. ef. 1-1-88
