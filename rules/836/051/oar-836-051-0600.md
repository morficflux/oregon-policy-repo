## Full text

836-051-0600 Trade Practice Regulation Violation of any provision of OAR 836-051-0500 to 836-051-0600 is an unfair trade practice under ORS 746.240.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 746.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.075, 746.085, 746.100, 746.110 & 746.240
