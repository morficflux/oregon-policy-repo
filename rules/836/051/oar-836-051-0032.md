## Full text

836-051-0032 Definition "Small face amount policy" means a life insurance policy or certificate with an initial face amount of $15,000 or less.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 746.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.075, 743.218, 746.100, 746.110 & 746.240
