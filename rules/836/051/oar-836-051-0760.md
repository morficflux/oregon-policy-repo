## Full text

836-051-0760 Minimum Valuation Mortality Standards For preneed insurance and to similar policies and certificates used to fund funeral services and expenses, the minimum mortality standard for determining reserve liabilities and nonforfeiture values for both male and female insureds shall be the Ultimate 1980 CSO.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 733.306 & 743.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 733.306, 733.310, 743.215, 743.216 & 743.221
