## Full text

836-051-0310 Acknowledgement of Concurrence for Payout from Assignee or Beneficiary Prior to payment of an accelerated benefit, an insurer shall obtain a signed acknowledgement of concurrence for payout as follows:

(1) From an assignee, when the amount of benefit that is accelerated exceeds the unassigned portion of the death benefit, unless the assignee is the insurer.

(2) From an irrevocable beneficiary, if there is an irrevocable beneficiary.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 743.154 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743.154
