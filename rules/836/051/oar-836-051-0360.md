## Full text

836-051-0360 Discrimination In the payment of accelerated benefits, an insurer shall not unfairly discriminate among insureds with differing qualifying events covered under the policy or among insureds with similar qualifying events covered under the policy. An insurer shall not apply further conditions on the payment of the accelerated benefits other than those conditions specified in the policy or rider. An insurer who provides that its accelerated benefit provision is exercisable in the event of a medical condition to which ORS 743.154(2)(d) applies shall not limit exercise of the benefit to specific medical conditions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 743.154 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743.154
