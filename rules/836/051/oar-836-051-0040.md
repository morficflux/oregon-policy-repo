## Full text

836-051-0040 Trade Practice Regulation Violation of any provision of OAR 836-051-0030 to 836-051-0040 is an unfair trade practice under ORS 746.240.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 746.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 746.075, 743.218, 746.100, 746.110 & 746.240
