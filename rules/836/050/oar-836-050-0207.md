## Full text

836-050-0207 Unfair Trade Practices Failure of an insurer to comply with OAR 836-050-0210 and 836-050-0215 is an unfair trade practice under ORS 746.240.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 433, 731, 743 & 746 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 742.005 & 746.240
