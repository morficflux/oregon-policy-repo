## Full text

836-050-0237 Unfair Trade Practices Failure of an insurer to comply with any provision of OAR 836-050-0240 or 836-050-0245, or the requirement in 836-050-0250(2)(a) that testing for HIV infection be done only with the informed consent of the applicant for insurance, is an unfair trade practice under ORS 746.240.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 433, 731, 743 & 746 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 433.045(7), 742.005 & 746.240
