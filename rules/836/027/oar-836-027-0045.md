## Full text

836-027-0045 Forms; Additional Information and Exhibits In addition to the information expressly required to be included in Forms A, B, C, D, E and F there shall be included further material information, if any, as may be necessary to make the information contained in the form not misleading. The person filing may also file exhibits in addition to those expressly required by the statement. Such exhibits shall be marked to indicate clearly the subject matters to which they refer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 & 732.705 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.523, 732.552, 732.553 & 732.574
