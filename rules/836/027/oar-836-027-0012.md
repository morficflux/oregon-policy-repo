## Full text

836-027-0012 Summary of Registration — Statement Filing. An insurer required to file an annual registration statement pursuant to ORS 732.517 to 732.592 is also required to furnish information required on Form C. Form C is set forth on the website of the Department of Consumer and Business Services at dfr.oregon.gov. An insurer shall file a copy of Form C in each state in which the insurer is authorized to do business, if requested by the Insurance Commissioner of that state.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 732.572 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.517 - 732.592
