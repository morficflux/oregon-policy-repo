## Full text

836-027-0001 Statutory Authority and Purpose of OAR 836-027-0005 to 836-027-0180 OAR 836-027-0005 to 836-027-0180 are adopted pursuant to authority in ORS 732.572. They are adopted to carry out 732.517 to 732.592. The information required by OAR 836-027-0005 to 836-027-0180 is declared to be necessary and appropriate in the public interest and for the protection of the policyholders in this state.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 732.572 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.517 - 732.592
