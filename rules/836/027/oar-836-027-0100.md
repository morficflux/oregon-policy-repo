## Full text

836-027-0100 Acquisition of Control — Statement Filing A person required to file a statement pursuant to ORS 732.517 to 732.592 shall furnish the required information on Form A, which is incorporated in and made a part of this rule as Exhibit 1. The person also shall furnish the required information on Form E, which is described in OAR 836-027-0125. Form E is set forth on the website of the Department of Consumer and Business Services at dfr.oregon.gov.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 732.705 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.517 - 732.592
