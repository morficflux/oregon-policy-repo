## Full text

836-027-0110 Amendments to Form A An applicant who has filed a statement pursuant to ORS 732.517 to 732.592 shall promptly advise the Director of any changes in the information so furnished on Form A arising subsequent to the date upon which the information was furnished but prior to disposition of the application by the Director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 732.705 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 732.517 - 732.592
