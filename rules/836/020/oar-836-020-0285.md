## Full text

836-020-0285 Prior Approval No advertising which includes an application or enrollment form may be used without its prior approval by the Insurance Commissioner.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731, 743 & 746 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 742.009 & 746.075
