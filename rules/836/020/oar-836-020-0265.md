## Full text

836-020-0265 Group or Quasi-Group Implication An advertisement of a particular policy shall not state or imply that prospective insureds become group or quasi-group members covered under a group policy and as such enjoy special rates or underwriting privileges, unless such is the fact.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731, 743 & 746 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 742.009 & 746.075
