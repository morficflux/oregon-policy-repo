## Full text

836-071-0115 Satisfaction of Qualifications for Classes of Insurance An applicant must satisfy all applicable qualifications for each class of insurance applied for within a license category before the applicant may be issued a license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 & 744

History: ID 3-1990, f. & cert. ef. 1-19-90
