## Full text

836-071-0185 Qualification of Agents Selling Variable Life Insurance, Including Variable Annuities A person shall not solicit, place or procure variable life insurance contracts or policies, including variable annuities, unless the person is licensed as authorized by OAR 441-175-0120 or by OAR 441-175-0130.
