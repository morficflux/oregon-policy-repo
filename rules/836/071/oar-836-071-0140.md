## Full text

836-071-0140 License Amendment An applicant for an amendment to an adjuster or insurance consultant license shall apply electronically in the manner provided for application for the initial license under Oregon Laws 2019, chapter 151.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244, 731.804, ORS 744.619, 744.621 & Or Laws 2019, ch 151 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 744.619, 744.621 & Or Laws 2019, ch 151
