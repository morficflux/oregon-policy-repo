## Full text

836-071-0300 Requirement of Contract with or Employment of Licensee A firm or corporate adjuster or insurance consultant may engage in a category or class of insurance through an individual licensee as authorized under ORS 744.022 only if the firm or corporate licensee employs or has entered a contract with the individual licensee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 744.022
