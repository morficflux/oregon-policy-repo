# Fingerprints (OAR 836-071-0110)

## Full text

836-071-0110 Fingerprints All applicants for a license shall furnish fingerprints required by the Director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services to the examination administrator who will perform the duties of obtaining electronically the fingerprints of applicants and submitting the fingerprints for Oregon or nationwide criminal history checks. The applicant shall submit the fingerprints according to the requirements and instructions of the examination administrator.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.534, 705.135 & 731.244 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.534, 705.141, 744.001 & 744.059
