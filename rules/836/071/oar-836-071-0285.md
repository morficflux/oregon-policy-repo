## Full text

836-071-0285 Insurance Producer Review of Applications An insurance producer who permits an unlicensed person to fill out an application for insurance as authorized under OAR 836-071-0280 must indicate on the office copy of the application that the insurance producer reviewed the application, giving the date of the review.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731 & 744

History: ID 8-2005, f. 5-18-05, cert. ef. 8-1-05 ID 3-1990, f. & cert. ef. 1-19-90
