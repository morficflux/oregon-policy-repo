## Full text

734-078-0005 Scope These rules shall apply to and govern the issuance of permits by the Department of Transportation, for the movement of vehicles transporting logs, poles, piling and structural members which, when loaded, have overall lengths in excess of those permitted by Oregon Revised Statutes or by OAR Chapter 734, Division 71.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184, 366 & 818 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.220
