## Full text

734-082-0006 Acceptance of Permit The receipt of a permit issued under these Division 82 rules by the permittee and initiation of any movement authorized by the permit is considered acceptance of all descriptions, terms and conditions contained in the permit provisions and requirements.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 818.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.220 & 818.225
