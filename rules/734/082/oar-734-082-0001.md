## Full text

734-082-0001 Scope OAR Chapter 734, Division 82 governs the operation of vehicles transporting loads that exceed legal limits and variance permits issued for vehicles or loads having weight or dimension greater than that allowed by statute.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 810.050 & 818.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.220 & 818.225
