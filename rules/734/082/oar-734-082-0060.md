## Full text

734-082-0060 Chief Engineer’s Authority Some extraordinary movements may exceed the limits established by these rules. The Chief Engineer may vary from these rules and issue single-trip permits if the movement would be in the public interest. Any such deviations may be considered on an individual basis and not be construed as a change in policy. The Chief Engineer will consider potential damage to the highway and the potential hazard to the motoring public by allowing such loads.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 810, 183.335(5) & 818.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.220 & 818.225
