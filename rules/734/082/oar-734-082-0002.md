# Duration (OAR 734-082-0002)

## At a glance

OAR 734-082-0002 — Duration. Chapter 734, Division 082. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

734-082-0002 Duration Permits issued under Division 82 are valid from the effective date for a single trip within a ten consecutive day period, continuous trips within a 30 consecutive day period or continuous trips for one year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 810.050 & 818.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.220 & 818.225
