## Full text

734-082-0004 Permits Issued Permits authorized by Division 82 are issued to the transporting motor vehicle. The vehicle or combination of vehicles must be of legal size and weight except when operating under the terms of a permit. A permit may be issued for a load consisting of multiple items that are non-divisible.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 810.050, 818.060 & 818.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.220 & 818.225
