## Full text

734-072-0030 Cancellation of Permits The cancellation authority granted under ORS 818.220 will apply to and govern the cancellation of single-trip variance permits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200 & 818.220
