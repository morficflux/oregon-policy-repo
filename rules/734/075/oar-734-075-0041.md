## Full text

734-075-0041 Warning Lights for Transported Over Width Mobile Homes In addition to any other lighting required by law, a transported over width mobile home must be equipped with two flashing type amber lights mounted on the rear of the towed unit. The lights must be:

(1) On a horizontal plane at least eight feet above the road surface and separated as far as practical; and

(2) Clearly visible from 500 feet.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 810.060, 184.619 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200 & 818.220
