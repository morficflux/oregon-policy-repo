## Full text

734-073-0085 Statutory Weight Exemptions not Applicable to Interstate Highways Except when operating under the terms of a variance permit, the weight exemptions authorized by ORS 818.030(8) and

(9) do not apply to vehicles operating on any interstate highway.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 810.050, 818.030 & Ch. 510 & OL 1993 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.030
