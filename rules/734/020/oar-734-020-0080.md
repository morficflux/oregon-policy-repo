## Full text

734-020-0080 State Highway Right of Way Parking; General Policy It is the policy of the Oregon Transportation Commission to permit the Chief Engineer to define areas within the state highway rights-of-way in which overnight parking of any motor vehicle shall be prohibited. Accessible areas are provided and motorist usage will be permitted for reasons of safety and rest by drivers in need thereof and to permit viewing of scenic vistas.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 366, 390 & 810 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 810.030 & 810.160
