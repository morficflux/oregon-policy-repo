## Full text

734-020-0200 Yield Signs Attached to Transit Buses OAR 734-020-0200 through 734-020-0220 establish specifications for a yield sign to be attached to the back of transit buses as directed by ORS 811.167. The yield sign shall warn a person operating a vehicle approaching the rear of the transit bus that the person must yield when the transit bus in entering traffic.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 811.167 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 811.167
