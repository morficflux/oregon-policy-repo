## Full text

734-020-0070 Fee for Issuance of Winter Recreation Parking Sno-Park Permits

(1) The fee for parking permits in winter recreation parking areas (Sno-Parks) shall be as follows: (a) One day — $4; (b) Three consecutive days — $9; (c) Annual, beginning each November — $25.

(2) Sno-Park permits may be issued by the Department or persons appointed by the Department as provided in ORS 811.595.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 811.595 & 811.600 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 811.600
