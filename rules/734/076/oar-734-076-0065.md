## Full text

734-076-0065 Payment of Appropriate Taxes and Fees The motor carrier towing a disabled vehicle(s) is responsible for paying the appropriate road use taxes when operating under Division 76 rules. When the gross weight exceeds 98,000 pounds, road use fees described in ORS 818.225 must be paid. When the gross weight is 98,000 pounds or less, weight-mile tax must be paid. All fees must be paid to the CCD.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200 & 818.220
