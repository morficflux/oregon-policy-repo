## Full text

734-035-0060 Determination that Property is Surplus The following criteria may be used to determine real property is surplus: The property is no longer needed by the agency for public purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184, 273 & 366 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 270.110
