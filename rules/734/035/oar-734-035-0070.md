## Full text

734-035-0070 Notice to Department of Administrative Services Upon declaring property to be surplus to its needs, the Department of Transportation shall notify the Department of Administrative Services of the intent to sell the property.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 273 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 270.100

History: HWY 9-1990, f. & cert. ef. 5-24-90
