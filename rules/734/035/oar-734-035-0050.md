## Full text

734-035-0050 Disposition of Surplus Property; General Policy It is the general policy of the Department of Transportation to efficiently and economically dispose of real property that is determined by the agency to be surplus.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 366 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 270.100
