## Full text

734-035-0010 Removing Personal Property from Illegal Camping on State Highway Right of Way — Purpose The purpose of OAR 734-035-0010 through 734-035-0040 is to outline a process for removing personal property from state highway rights of way by the Department of Transportation under ORS 377.650 and 377.653 where the personal property is reasonably believed to be the result of illegal camping.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 377.653 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 377.650 & 377.653
