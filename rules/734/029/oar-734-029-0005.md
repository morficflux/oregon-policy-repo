# Purpose (OAR 734-029-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

734-029-0005 Purpose The purpose of the Adopt-A-Highway program is to provide citizens of Oregon an opportunity to pick up litter, remove noxious weeds, and improve the appearance of the State Highway System in accordance with ORS 366.158.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 366.158 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 366.158
