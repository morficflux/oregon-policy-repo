# Scope (OAR 734-029-0010)

## At a glance

OAR 734-029-0010 — Scope. Chapter 734, Division 029. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

734-029-0010 Scope Any volunteer group, identified as “Applicant” in Division 29 rules, may adopt a section of highway on the State Highway System for the purpose of picking up litter, removing noxious weeds, or both. Work activities, when approved, may also include graffiti removal or maintenance of landscaped areas.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 366.158 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 366.158

History: HWD 5-2010, f. 6-16-10, cert. ef. 7-1-10 HWY 6-1992, f. & cert. ef. 3-26-92
