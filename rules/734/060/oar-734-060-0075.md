## Full text

734-060-0075 Portable Signs on State Highway Right of Way — Removal Provisions Signs subject to OAR 734-027-0005 through 734-027-0050 are subject to the removal provisions of those rules and ORS 377.775.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 377 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 377.650
