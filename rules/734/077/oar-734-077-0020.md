# Authorized (OAR 734-077-0020)

## Full text

734-077-0020 Authorized Vehicles Notwithstanding any limitation imposed under OAR 734-077-0040 a permit may be issued under these rules for the operation of any vehicle or combination of vehicles meeting the requirements of ORS 818.080, 818.110 or rules adopted pursuant to ORS 810.060, provided, however, that combinations of vehicles as described in OAR 734-074-0005 exceeding 75 feet in overall length are not authorized.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 366 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.230
