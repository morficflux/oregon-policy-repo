# Authorized (OAR 734-077-0007)

## At a glance

## Full text

734-077-0007 Authorized Commodities Permits shall be issued only for the following:

(1) Vehicles transporting food processing plant by-products to be used for livestock feed or fertilizer from which there is a fluid leakage; or

(2) Vehicles transporting agricultural products from which there is fluid leakage, while the vehicles are enroute from the place of harvest to a place where the products will be processed, stored or sold.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184, 366 & 818 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.230

History: 2HD 5-1985, f. & ef. 11-22-85
