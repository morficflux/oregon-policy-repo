## Full text

734-051-5100 Effective Period of Permit to Operate, Maintain and Use an Approach

(1) General Provisions. A permit to operate, maintain and use (“permit to operate”) an approach runs with the land. Except as otherwise provided, a permit to operate is effective until: (a) Revoked by mutual consent; (b) Revoked for failure to abide by the terms and conditions; (c) The approach is subject to a change of use as set forth in OAR 734-051-3020; (d) The development of safety or operational concerns as set forth in OAR 734-051-4020(3); (e) The approach is modified, mitigated, or removed in accordance with OAR 734-051-5120 Project Delivery; or (f) By other operation of law.

(2) Successors and Assignees. The permit to operate is binding on successors and assignees including successors in interest to the property being served by the approach.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 374.310–374.314, 374.345 & 374.355 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 374.300–374.360, §27, ch. 330 & OL 2011
