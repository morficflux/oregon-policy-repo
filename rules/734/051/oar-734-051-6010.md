734-051-6010 Remedies in Closure of Approaches — Authority and Purpose of OAR 734-051-6010 through 734-051-6060

(1) Pursuant to ORS 374.313, a person holding an interest in real property which is or would be served by an approach may file a claim for relief when: (a) The department closes an approach for which a permit was issued under ORS 374.310 or that was allowed by law prior to enactment of statutory permit requirements for approaches, or denies an application for an approach at the location of a grant or reservation of access; and (b) Such closure or denial is not the result of conditions contained in a contract, condemnation judgment, recorded deed or permit.

(2) The department may offer administrative remedies upon such closure or denial to address issues related to real property, value, utility and use; and provide a simplified procedure for resolving the claim.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 374.310–374.314, 374.345 & 374.355 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 374.300–374.360, §27, ch. 330 & OL 2011
