# Purpose (OAR 734-005-0005)

## Full text

734-005-0005 Purpose The purpose of this administrative rule is to define the Oregon Coordinate System, consisting of three mapping projection coordinate systems that are authorized for use in the State of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & Ch.179 OL 2011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 209.130, 209.155, 209.250, 390.770 & Ch.179 OL 2011
