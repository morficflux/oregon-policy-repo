## Full text

734-079-0005 Scope and Purpose A combination of vehicles consisting of a log truck and pole trailer equipped for self loading and transporting logs may operate with a permit allowing the weight provisions of ORS 818.210(3) only if:

(1) The combination does not exceed the maximum allowable length limitations established in OAR Chapter 734, Division 71; and

(2) The combination meets any other restrictions that may be imposed pursuant to ORS 818.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200 & 818.210
