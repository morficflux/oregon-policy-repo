## Full text

734-074-0029 Insurance Requirements At the discretion of the Chief Engineer, permit applicants may be required to furnish liability and indemnity insurance as provided for under ORS 818.220(1)(d) and (e).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 184.617, 184.619, 366.205 & 818.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200 & 818.220
