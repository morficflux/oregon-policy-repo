## Full text

734-074-0051 Splash and Spray Suppressant Devices

(1) The Chief Engineer requires combinations of vehicles operating under OAR Chapter 734, Division 74 when highways are wet, including those surfaces that have rain, frost, ice, sleet or snow to be equipped with devices designed to suppress water splash and spray.

(2) The Chief Engineer is hereby granted authority to approve and require by written order the type, style, design, and installation details of splash and spray devices. These devices may consist of but are not limited to the following: (a) Air deflectors mounted on the vehicles; (b) Fender flaps behind wheels; (c) Side flaps over wheels; and (d) Water collection type fenders.

(3) Minimum splash and spray requirements are shown on Attachment 74A, revised January 2023, and Attachment 24, revised January 2023. Attachment 74A and Attachment 24 are by reference made part of Division 74 rules and are available from the CCD Over-Dimension Permit Unit.

(4) The headlights of a triple trailer combination and the combination described in OAR 734-074-0010 (1)(h) must be illuminated any time windshield wipers are used.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 818.220 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200 & 818.220
