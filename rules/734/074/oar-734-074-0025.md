# Permit (OAR 734-074-0025)

## At a glance

## Full text

734-074-0025 Permit Duration Permits issued under Division 74 rules are for a single trip within a period of time not to exceed 10 days, or continuous trips for up to one year from the effective date.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 818.220 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200 & 818.220
