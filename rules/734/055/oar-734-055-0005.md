## Full text

734-055-0005 Scope This rule shall apply to and govern the location, installation, construction, maintenance and use of pole lines, buried cables, pipe lines, signs, miscellaneous operations upon State Highway right-of-way and properties under the jurisdiction of the Department of Transportation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 374 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 374.305

History: 2HD 2-1981, f. & ef. 2-25-81 HC 1176, f. 9-24-68
