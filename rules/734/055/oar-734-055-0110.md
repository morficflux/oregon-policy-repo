# Special "X" (OAR 734-055-0110)

## Full text

734-055-0110 Special “X” Permits Utility facilities that originally occupied a portion of the right-of-way in which the owner had a compensable interest or a prior right to occupy said right-of-way, will be issued a permit titled “X” Permit. Applicant will be responsible for furnishing the DM with the documentation of the prior rights. The special provisions of this permit will identify the obligations and responsibilities of applicant and the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 374 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 374.305

History: HWY 6-1989, f. & cert. ef. 10-25-89
