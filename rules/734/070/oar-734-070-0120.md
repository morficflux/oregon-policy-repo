## Full text

734-070-0120 Participation Each road authority choosing to participate in the COVP Program shall enter into an agreement with the Department indicating the road authority’s level of participation. Further, each participant shall:

(1) Provide CCD with an electronic mail address;

(2) Provide CCD with an authorized permit fee schedule;

(3) Provide CCD with at least 30 days notice to terminate participation or to change the level of participation; and

(4) Provide CCD with updated road restrictions as conditions dictate, including emergency road restriction notices.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 818.205 & 818.220 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200, 818.205 & 818.220
