## Full text

734-070-0025 Permits Issued by a Third Party Agent The Department may enter into an agreement with any third party for the purpose of issuing approved overweight and over-dimensional permits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 818.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 818.200 & 818.220
