## Full text

415-054-0510 Monitoring

(1) The ADSS shall monitor the progress of individuals under a DUII Diversion Agreement or convicted of a DUII by documenting: (a) Enrollment or failure to enroll in the DUII services program; and (b) Successful completion of the DUII services program.

(2) Failure to complete all or any part of the screening interview or DUII services program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 430.256 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 430.256
