## Full text

415-052-0100 Purpose These rules prescribe standards for providing financial assistance in the form of loans under 42 U.S.C. 300x-25 to support the establishment of recovery homes for people in recovery from alcohol and drug abuse or dependency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 430.256 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 90.100 - 90.459, 105.105 - 105.168, 430.265 - 430.920 & 279B
