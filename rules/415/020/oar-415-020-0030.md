## Full text

415-020-0030 Diagnostic Assessment

(1) Written Procedure: The Opioid Treatment Program shall develop and implement a written procedure for assessing each patient's treatment needs consistent with The ASAM Criteria .

(2) The diagnostic assessment shall be documented in the permanent patient record. It shall consist of the elements described in The ASAM Criteria and documentation of the patient's self-identified cultural background. Cultural information documented should include level of acculturation, knowledge of own culture, primary language, spiritual or religious interests, and cultural attitudes toward alcohol and other drug use.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 430.256 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 430.010(4)(b) & 430.560 - 430.590
