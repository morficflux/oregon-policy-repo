## Full text

413-010-0714 Notice and Review when the Perpetrator is a Department Employee When the perpetrator is a Department employee, the Department will follow the Child Welfare Procedure Manual for notice and review outlined in “CPS Assessment and Founded CPS Disposition Review for Department Employees".

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005
