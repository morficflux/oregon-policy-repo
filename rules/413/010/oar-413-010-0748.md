# Review (OAR 413-010-0748)

## Full text

413-010-0748 Review Initiated by the Department The Child Safety Program Manager or their designee may direct that either the local Child Welfare office or Central Office review a founded disposition if there is good cause to do so, such as a determination that there is a legal finding that contradicts the CPS founded disposition.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005
