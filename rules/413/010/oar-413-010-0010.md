## Full text

413-010-0010 Purpose The purpose of OAR 413-010-0010 to 413-010-0075 is to describe circumstances in which the Department may and may not disclose client information without a court order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.225, 419A.170 & 419A.255
