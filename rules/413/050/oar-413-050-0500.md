## Full text

413-050-0500 Policy These rules (OAR 413-050-0500 to 413-050-0590) provide procedures for the Department of Human Services (Department) to fund programs from the Domestic Violence Fund. The 1981 Oregon Legislature established this fund to provide intervention on behalf of and support for victims of domestic violence. The Department is authorized to enter into grant contracts with public agencies or private non-profit organizations to support operation by those organizations and agencies of programs designed to prevent, identify, and treat family violence
