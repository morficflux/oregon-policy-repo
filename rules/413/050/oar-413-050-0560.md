## Full text

413-050-0560 Allocation of Funds The Department uses an equitable distribution method for funding programs throughout the state. The distribution method must be reviewed by the Domestic and Sexual Violence Fund Advisory Committee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 108.610 - 108.660
