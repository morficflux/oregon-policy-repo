## Full text

413-050-0440 Medical Services “Permanent Planning” In cases designated permanent planning, the Department will make payments for necessary evaluations of parents, stepparents and/or live-in companions. This includes psychological, psychiatric and neurological evaluations, drug and alcohol assessments and parent-child interaction assessments. These services must be authorized in writing by the Adoption Services Unit prior to the services being provided.
