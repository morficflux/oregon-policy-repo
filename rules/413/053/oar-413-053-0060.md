## Full text

413-053-0060 Outcome Measures

(1) All SPRF services have client-focused functional outcome measures.

(2) SPRF outcomes are delineated by service category and are in the SPRF Outcomes table, dated November 2017, which is adopted as Exhibit 1 and incorporated by reference into this rule. A printed copy may be obtained from the Department. [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
