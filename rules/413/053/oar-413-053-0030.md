## Full text

413-053-0030 Referral for a SPRF Service To receive SPRF services, the Department must submit to the provider:

(1) A written service referral approved by a supervisor; and

(2) A completed DHS form 2099.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & ORS 418.575-418.598 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.575-418.598
