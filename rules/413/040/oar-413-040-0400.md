## Full text

413-040-0400 Policy HIV testing is an intrusive medical procedure which can have serious social consequences. The Department will subject children in its custody to this procedure only if it is medically indicated. When a child in Department custody is tested for HIV, informed consent procedures shall be followed and the results of the test held in strictest confidence.
