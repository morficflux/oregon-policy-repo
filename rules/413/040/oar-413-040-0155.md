## Full text

413-040-0155 Participants in Administrative Reviews and Permanency Hearings

(1) All legal custodians and parents must be invited and encouraged to participate in Administrative Reviews and Permanency Hearings.

(2) Other individuals to invite are: (a) Substitute care providers; (b) Children, when it is determined that the child's attendance would be appropriate and the child wishes to attend; (c) Attorneys and Court Appointed Special Advocates (CASA); (d) Indian tribe (if applicable).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 419A.090 – 419A.122, ORS 419B.440 – 419B.476 & ORS 419C.623 – 419C.656
