# Purpose (OAR 413-040-0005)

## Full text

413-040-0005 Purpose The purpose of OAR 413-040-0000 to 413-040-0032 is to describe the activities required to:

(1) Complete a protective capacity assessment;

(2) Use the Family Engagement Meeting;

(3) Develop a case plan;

(4) Develop an action agreement;

(5) Monitor the case plan;

(6) Review and revise the case plan;

(7) Determine when conditions for return have been met; and

(8) Close the ongoing safety plan and close the case.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 & ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.010 & ORS 418.005
