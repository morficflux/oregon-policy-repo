## Full text

413-015-0215 Information for which OTIS is Responsible

(1) When the screener receives information involving any of the following settings or individual representing the setting, the screener must immediately forward the information to the OTIS and notify the OTIS the information was forwarded: (a) Child care; (b) Child-caring agency; (c) Educational provider; (d) ODDS licensed group home; (e) ODDS licensed host home; (f) ODDS certified foster home; (g) OYA certified foster home; or (h) Proctor foster home.

(2) When the screener receives information involving third party abuse that is not the responsibility of Child Welfare to assess, the screener must immediately forward the information to OTIS and notify OTIS the information was forwarded.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005, ORS 419B.017 & ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.205-418.327, ORS 419B.035, ORS 418.005, ORS 419B.017 & ORS 419B.015
