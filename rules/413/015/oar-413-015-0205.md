## Full text

413-015-0205 Information Received All information received at screening must be reviewed by a screener to determine the type of information:

(1) Report of abuse;

(2) Request for family support services;

(3) Notification;

(4) Information the OTIS is responsible for screening;

(5) Case management related information; or

(6) Information or referral.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005, ORS 418.205-418.327 & ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005, ORS 418.205-418.327 & ORS 419B.020
