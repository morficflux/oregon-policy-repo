## Full text

413-015-0460 Visitation

(1) If an out-of-home ongoing safety plan is developed, the CPS worker must arrange for visitation between the child and the child's family.

(2) The CPS worker must refer to "Visits and Other Types of Child and Family Contact", OAR 413-070-0800 to 413-070-0880.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.185, ORS 418.005, ORS 418.015 & ORS 419B.005 - 419B.050
