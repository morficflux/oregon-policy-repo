## Full text

413-310-0440 Special Account Money received for a child or young adult that has been identified for a specific purpose other than maintenance must be deposited into a Special Account.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 416.820, ORS 418.032, ORS 419B.400 - 419B.406 & ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 416.820, ORS 418.032 & ORS 419B.400 - 419B.406
