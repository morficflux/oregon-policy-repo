## Full text

413-310-0350 Recovery of Overpayments — General These rules describe SOSCF’s procedures for handling the collection of overpayments to contracted providers of child care and treatment services, and for granting Administrative Reviews and hearings in contested cases. Contested case hearings of SOSCF are conducted in accordance with the Attorney General’s Model Rules OAR 137-003-0501 and the following, entitled “Model Rules of Procedure for Contested Cases”; Hearing Officer Panel Rules.
