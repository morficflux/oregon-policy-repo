## Full text

413-310-0220 Unintentional Error In instances of unintentional error, SOSCF will report the details of the inappropriate payment to SOSCF Collections Unit for review. The Collections Unit will establish an account receivable and collect from future payments or prepare a billing invoice.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 293.235 - 293.245 & 293.265
