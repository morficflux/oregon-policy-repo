# Purpose (OAR 413-310-0200)

## At a glance

## Full text

413-310-0200 Purpose These rules describe SOSCF’s procedures for handling payments made by SOSCF which are in error or result from fraudulent acts committed by service providers and subcontractors of service providers contracting with SOSCF, persons working under personal service contracts with SOSCF, or SOSCF employees.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 293.235 - 293.245
