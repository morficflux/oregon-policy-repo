## Full text

413-020-0233 When a Supervision Plan is Required

(1) The caseworker must develop a supervision plan with the certified family with whom the child or young adult lives within 30 days of: (a) The receipt of the CANS screening results that indicates enhanced supervision is necessary to maintain the safety and support the well-being of the child or young adult and the child or young adult qualifies for a level of care payment ; or (b) When a child or young adult who has enhanced supervision needs and is receiving a level of care moves from one certified family to another certified family .

(2) A supervision plan is not required for a level of care payment determined for the purpose of negotiating an adoption assistance or guardianship assistance payment .

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005
