## Full text

413-020-0005 Purpose The purpose of OAR 413-020-0005 to 413-020-0050 is to describe:

(1) The circumstances in which parents, guardians, or Indian custodians and the Department may enter into a Voluntary Custody Agreement concerning a child who is in the legal custody of the parents or guardians; and

(2) The responsibilities of the parents, guardians, or Indian custodians and the Department in connection with these agreements.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 109.056, 418.015, 418.270, 418.275, 418.280 & 418.285
