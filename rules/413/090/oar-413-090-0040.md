## Full text

413-090-0040 Payments During Adoptive Supervision When a child is free for adoption and placed in an approved or certified family's home designated by the Department's Adoption Program Manager as the child's pre-adoptive family, the Department pays base rate payment and any level of care payment to the pre-adoptive family until the adoption assistance payment commences. See OAR 413-130-0000 to 413-130-0130 for the adoption assistance eligibility requirements of the Adoption Assistance Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & 418.340 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005, 418.330, 418.335 & 418.340
