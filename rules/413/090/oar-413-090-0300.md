# Purpose (OAR 413-090-0300)

## At a glance

OAR 413-090-0300 — Purpose. Chapter 413, Division 090. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

413-090-0300 Purpose The purpose of OAR 413-090-0300 to 413-090-0380 is to describe how payments for special and/or extraordinary needs may be used to benefit a child or young adult in the custody of the Department in foster care, family and professional shelter care, residential group care, or non-reimbursed placement such as SAIP and SCIP.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005
