# Purpose (OAR 413-090-0100)

## At a glance

## Full text

413-090-0100 Purpose The purpose of these rules, OAR 413-090-0100 to 413-090-0210, is to describe the requirements for eligibility and receipt of personal care services when a child or young adult is placed with a foster parent or relative caregiver by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005
