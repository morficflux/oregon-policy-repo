413-090-0365 Education Costs Not Eligible for Title IV-E

(1) Basic school costs are to be paid by local school districts.

(2) The Department‘s maximum payments for educational cost are outlined in I-E.5.2 Information Letter.

(3) The educational costs on the list below are not Title IV-E eligible. The Department may authorize the following allowable school costs if sufficient funds are available: [Table not included. See ED. NOTE.] [ED. NOTE: Tables referenced are available from the agency.]
