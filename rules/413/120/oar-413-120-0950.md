## Full text

413-120-0950 Adoption Assistance To be eligible for adoption assistance a child who is the subject of a Convention adoption must be a United States citizen and meet all other eligibility requirements under Child Welfare Policy I-G.3.1 “Adoption Assistance”, OAR 413-130-0000 to 413-130-0130.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 417.262, 417.265 & 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.262, 417.265 & 418.005
