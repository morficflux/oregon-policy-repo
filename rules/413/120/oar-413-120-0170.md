## Full text

413-120-0170 Attorney Fees If Adoption Assistance has been approved, and the adoptive resource elect to use an attorney to assist with legalization, the adoptive resource can request reimbursement for reasonable and necessary attorney fees through the Adoption Assistance Program pursuant to OAR 413-130-0080(1), (4), 413-130-0090, and 413-120-0115(1) and (2).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 419B.529
