## Full text

413-120-0110 Values

(1) Creating a legal child-parent relationship is the final goal of adoption.

(2) Achieving permanency for the child is important.

Statutory/Other Authority: HB 2004 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 109.307, 109.309 & 109.316
