# Purpose ORS 419B (OAR 413-120-0142)

## At a glance

## Full text

413-120-0142 Purpose ORS 419B.529 sets forth conditions under which an adoptive resource is not required to file a petition for adoption. These rules establish the Department’s responsibilities in the process for legalizing an adoption through the juvenile court where a petition for adoption is not required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 419B.529
