# Full Faith and Credit (OAR 413-115-0150)

## Full text

413-115-0150 Full Faith and Credit The Department must give full faith and credit to the public acts, records and judicial proceedings of any Indian tribe regarding any Indian child -custody proceeding to the same extent the Department gives full faith and credit to Indian tribe s.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & ORS 409.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005 & ORS 419B.100
