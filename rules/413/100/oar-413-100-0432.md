## Full text

413-100-0432 Payments for Services Provided in Emergency Situations When a Child or Young Adult is Not Enrolled in Title XIX Medicaid Payment may be made for emergency medical services only for a child or young adult in the Department’s care or custody. The payment is made from the local office’s “Other Medical” budget.
