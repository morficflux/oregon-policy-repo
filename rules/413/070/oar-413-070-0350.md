## Full text

413-070-0350 Judicial Determination As required by ORS 418.933(1), a refugee child may not remain out of the child's home for longer than five days unless there has been a judicial determination supported by clear and convincing evidence that:

(1) Preventive or remedial services provided by the Department have failed to alleviate the need for removal; and

(2) Return to the home will likely result in psychological or physical harm to the child.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & 418.945 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005 & 418.925 - 418.945
