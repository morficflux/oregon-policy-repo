## Full text

413-070-0944 Legal Expenses of a Guardian The Department may not authorize payment for legal services provided:

(1) For the potential guardian, guardian, or successor legal guardian in connection with a contested case hearing; or

(2) To defend or retain a guardianship upon challenge by another party once a guardianship is established.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.141 & 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.141 & 418.005
