## Full text

413-070-0970 Guardianship Social Support Services The guardian or child in an assisted guardianship may request family support services from the Department as described in OAR 413-030-0000 to 413-030-0030.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 411.141 & 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 411.141 & 418.005
