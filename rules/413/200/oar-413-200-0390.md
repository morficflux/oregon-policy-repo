## Full text

413-200-0390 Requirements Regarding Renewal of the Certificate of Approval

(1) To renew a Certificate of Approval or Child-Specific Non-Relative Certificate of Approval for up to two additional years, the resource parent must submit a completed Application for Renewal or Change of Status, or an application for Adoption, whichever is applicable. Upon receiving an application, the Department will assess the application under OAR 413-200-0287 .

(2) When the resource parent has submitted an application for renewal, the current certificate does not expire, despite any expiration date, until the Department has renewed the certification, the renewal application is withdrawn, or there is a proposed and final order denying the application.
