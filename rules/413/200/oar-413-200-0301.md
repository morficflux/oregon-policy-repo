## Full text

413-200-0301 Purpose and Applicability of Certification Standards

(1) The purpose of OAR 413-200-0301 to 413-200-0396 is to describe the criteria for approval as: (a) A resource parent; or (b) A potential adoptive resource.

(2) These rules apply to any individual who: (a) Has applied to become a resource parent or potential adoptive resource; (b) Is currently a resource parent ; or (c) Has applied to renew certification.
