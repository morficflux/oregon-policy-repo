## Full text

413-110-0050 Effect on a Release and Surrender A Legal Risk Placement shall be considered a placement for the “purpose of adoption” in accordance with ORS 418.270, subsection (4), which specifies when a release and surrender given by a parent or guardian shall be considered irrevocable. These rules shall apply to any release and surrender accepted prior to, concurrent with or subsequent to the Legal Risk Placement.
