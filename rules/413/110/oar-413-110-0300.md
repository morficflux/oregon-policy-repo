# Purpose (OAR 413-110-0300)

## Full text

413-110-0300 Purpose The purpose of OAR 413-110-0300 to 413-110-0360 is to establish the Department's policies for determining whether adoption is an appropriate plan for a child. In the case of an Indian child, the Department follows OAR chapter 413, division 115.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005
