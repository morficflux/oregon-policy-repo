## Full text

413-110-0015 Values

(1) Any child whose parent or parents are unable to provide a safe and healthy home for the child should be assured the opportunity to become a valued and permanent member of another safe family that understands and responds to the child's needs.

(2) Early identification and development of an alternative to placement with a parent increases the likelihood that the alternate permanency plan can be achieved in a timely manner.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005(1)(a) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005
