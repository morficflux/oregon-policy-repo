## Full text

413-110-0060 Termination of Legal Risk Placement Status When the child is legally free to be adopted, the Central Office Adoption Services Unit will change the child's placement status from Legal Risk Placement to Adoptive Placement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005(1)(a) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005
