# Minimum Credits Required to Obtain a Diploma (OAR 413-105-0090)

## Full text

413-105-0090 Minimum Credits Required to Obtain a Diploma If at any time from grade nine to 12 a child or young adult was a foster child, as defined in ORS 30.297, the child or young adult cannot be required to complete more than the minimum state credit requirements specified in ORS 329.451(2)(a) to receive a high school diploma.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 409.050 & ORS 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 409.050, ORS 418.005 & ORS 329.451
