## Full text

413-130-0090 Special Payments

(1) A request for a special payment: (a) May be made after finalization of the adoption by an adoptive family who has an existing adoption assistance agreement with the Department; and (b) Must include documentation from the adoptive family when requested by the Department.

(2) The Department may authorize a special payment for a limited duration, on a case-by-case basis, subject to the availability of resources.

(3) An approved special payment may only be issued to the adoptive family.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 418.005 & 418.340 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 418.005, 418.330, 418.335 & 418.340
