## Full text

413-130-0360 Access to Registry Records

(1) A licensed adoption agency may examine adoption records maintained by the Department as part of an assisted search.

(2) The Department may examine adoption records maintained by a licensed adoption agency as part of an assisted search if the licensed adoption agency so allows.

(3) Original file contents or copies of confidential documents may not be removed from a registry.

(4) A searcher may not inspect any other files of another registry except those directly related to the file of the requester.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 109.506 & 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 109.425 - 109.507
