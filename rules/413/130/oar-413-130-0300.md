# Purpose (OAR 413-130-0300)

## Full text

413-130-0300 Purpose The purpose of OAR 413-130-0300 to 413-130-0365 is to describe the responsibilities of the Adoption Search and Registry Program for adoptions that finalized in Oregon and the eligibility requirements that must be met in order to:

(1) Receive non-identifying information from the registry;

(2) Register to allow the release of identifying information; and

(3) Request an assisted search for certain members of an individual’s birth family or the county in which the adoption was finalized.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 109.506 & 418.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 109.425 - 109.507
