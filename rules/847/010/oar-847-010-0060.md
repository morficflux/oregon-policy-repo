## Full text

847-010-0060 Limited License, SPEX/COMVEX, and Limited License, Postgraduate A physician who is granted a Limited License, SPEX/COMVEX, or Limited License, Postgraduate in the State of Oregon is entitled to apply for and obtain a federal narcotic stamp.
