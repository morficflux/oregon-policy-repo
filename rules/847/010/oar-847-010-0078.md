# Agreement Prohibited between Physician and Patient that Limits a Patient's Rights (OAR 847-010-0078)

## At a glance

## Full text

847-010-0078 Agreement Prohibited between Physician and Patient that Limits a Patient’s Rights Licensees and applicants shall not make an agreement with a patient or person, or any person or entity representing patients, nor provide any form of consideration, that would prohibit, restrict, discourage or otherwise limit a person's ability to file a complaint with the Oregon Medical Board, to truthfully and fully answer any questions posed by an agent or representative of the Board, or to participate as a witness in a Board proceeding. Statutory Auth.: ORS 677.265 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 677.132
