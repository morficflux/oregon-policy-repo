## Full text

847-010-0081 Physician- Assisted Death with Dignity A licensee's compliance with ORS 127.800 et seq shall not be considered a violation of 677.190(1), unprofessional or dishonorable conduct, as defined in 677.188(4)(a), (b), or (c).
