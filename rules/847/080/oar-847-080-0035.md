# Approved (OAR 847-080-0035)

## At a glance

## Full text

847-080-0035 Approved Colleges of Podiatric Medicine Colleges of podiatric medicine approved by the Board are only those approved by the American Podiatric Medical Association Council on Podiatric Medical Education.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 677.265 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 677.265 & 677.820
