## Full text

847-026-0005 Application The applicant must submit a completed application to the board on a form furnished by the Board with the required non-refundable application fee. The applicant must attest that all questions have been answered completely and all answers and statements are true and correct. Any false information is grounds for denial, limitation, suspension or revocation of licensure.
