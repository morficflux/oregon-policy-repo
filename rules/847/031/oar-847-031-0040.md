## Full text

847-031-0040 Approval of Foreign Schools of Medicine by Other States The Oregon Medical Board may accept any foreign school of medicine which has been approved by another state using criteria substantially similar to Oregon's.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 677.265 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 677.265

History: BME 9-2001, f. & cert. ef. 7-24-01
