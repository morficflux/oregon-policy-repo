## Full text

847-070-0025 Disciplinary Proceedings The Board may suspend or revoke the authority of an acupuncturist to engage in the practice of acupuncture and any disciplinary proceedings against an acupuncturist or any individual charged with the unlawful practice of acupuncture shall be in accordance with ORS Chapter 183.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 677 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 677.190
