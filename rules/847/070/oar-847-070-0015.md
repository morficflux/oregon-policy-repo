## Full text

847-070-0015 Application

(1) Every applicant must satisfactorily complete an application and document evidence of qualifications listed in OAR 847-070-0016 to the satisfaction of the Board. Such application and documentation must be complete before an applicant may be considered eligible for licensure.

(2) When applying for licensure, the applicant must submit to the Board the completed application, fees, documents, letters, and any civil penalties or hearing costs that may be due.

(3) False documentation is grounds for denial of licensure or disciplinary action by the Board.

(4) An applicant applying for licensure under these rules who has not completed the licensure process within a 6 month consecutive period must file a new application, documents, letters and pay a full filing fee as if filing for the first time.

(5) No applicant is entitled to licensure who: (a) Has had his/her license or certificate revoked or suspended in this or any other state unless the said license or certificate has been restored or reinstated and the applicant’s license or certificate is in good standing in the state which had revoked the same; (b) Has been refused a license or certificate in any other state on any grounds other than failure in an acupuncture licensure examination; or (c) Has been guilty of conduct similar to that which would be prohibited by or to which ORS 677.190 would apply.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 677.265 & 677.759 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 677.759
