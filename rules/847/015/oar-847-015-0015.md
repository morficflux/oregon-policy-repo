# Maintenance of Controlled Substances Log by Prescribing Practitioners (OAR 847-015-0015)

## Full text

847-015-0015 Maintenance of Controlled Substances Log by Prescribing Practitioners Any practitioner dispensing or administering controlled substances from the practitioner’s office must have a Drug Enforcement Administration registration indicating the address of that office. The practitioner shall maintain an inventory log showing all controlled substances received, and administered or dispensed. This log shall also list for each controlled substance, the patient’s name, amounts used, and date administered or dispensed. This log shall be available for inspection on request by the Oregon Medical Board or its authorized agents. Controlled substances samples are included in this rule.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 677 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 475.165

History: ME 15-1987, f. & ef. 8-3-87
