## Full text

847-020-0200 Required School Subjects Subjects covered in schools of medicine that grant degrees of Doctor of Medicine or Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine as set forth in ORS 677.110 are basic sciences, clinical sciences, clinical competence and/or other subjects that may be specified by the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 677.265 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 677.110
