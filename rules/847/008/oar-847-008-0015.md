## Full text

847-008-0015 Active Registration

(1) Each licensee of the Board who practices within the State of Oregon shall register and pay a biennial active registration fee prior to the last day of the registration period, except where the licensee is a physician in a qualified training program and elects to register on an annual basis.

(2) Each licensee of the Board whose practice address of record with the Board is within 100 miles of the border of the State of Oregon and who intends to practice within Oregon shall qualify for active registration status. Such licensee shall submit a statement to the Board attesting to practice in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 677.265 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 677.228 & ORS 677.172
