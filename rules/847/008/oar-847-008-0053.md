## Full text

847-008-0053 Restoration of License from Revoked Status

(1) A licensee whose license has been revoked may request restoration of the licensure two years after the date of revocation of his license, and must apply as a new applicant.

(2) The applicant must meet all current licensing requirements, and pay all applicable fees.

(3) Prior to the Board reviewing the request for restoration of a revoked license the applicant shall provide the Board with: (a) All relevant disciplinary actions in the applicant’s history; and (b) Professional history since the date of revocation, including continuing medical education, and professional or personal rehabilitation.
