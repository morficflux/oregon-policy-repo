## Full text

847-050-0046 Emeritus Status A physician associate with Emeritus status pursuant OAR 847-008-0030 must enter into a collaboration agreement, prior to starting any temporary or volunteer assignments.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 677.265 & 677.545 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 677.265, 677.510 & 677.515
