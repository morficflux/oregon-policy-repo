## Full text

335-001-0009 Filing Exceptions and Argument to the Board A party adversely affected by a proposed order may file written exceptions and argument with the Board. To be considered by the Board, any exceptions and argument submitted by a party must be received by the Board within 21 days of the mailing date of the proposed order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 681.420(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
