## Full text

335-001-0007 Time for Requesting a Contested Case Hearing A request for a contested case hearing must be in writing and must be received by the Board within twenty-one days from the date the contested case notice was served.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 681.420(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
