# Pharmacy Point of Sale Access (OAR 943-120-0165)

## Full text

943-120-0165 Pharmacy Point of Sale Access Pharmacy providers who electronically bill pharmaceutical claims must participate in and submit claims using the POS system, except as provided in OAR 410-121-0150.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 414.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 413.042 & 414.065
