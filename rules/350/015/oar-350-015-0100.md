## Full text

350-015-0100 Public Contracting For reasons of efficiency, and to avoid a piecemeal approach to public contracting administration, the Commission shall conduct public contracting in accordance with the public contracting law of the State of Washington. The Commission deems the public contracting law of the State of Washington as a total package to be equivalent in its degree of restrictiveness to the public contracting law of the State of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.016 & 16 USC 544c(b)

History: CRGC 2-2008, f. 11-4-08, cert. ef. 12-15-08
