# Prima Facie Evidence of Violation Required of Plaintiff (OAR 350-011-0009)

## Full text

350-011-0009 Prima Facie Evidence of Violation Required of Plaintiff In any suit commenced under 350-011-0008(1), the plaintiff shall be required to present prima facie evidence of a violation of 350-011-0001 to 350-011-0007 before the commission shall be required to prove that its acts in deliberating toward a decision complied with the law. When a plaintiff presents prima facie evidence of a violation of the open meeting law, the burden to prove that the provisions of 350-011-0001 to 350-011-0007 were complied with shall be on the commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.160 & RCW 43.97.015 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.160, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. 544 et seq.
