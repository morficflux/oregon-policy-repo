## Full text

350-040-0070 Report of the Executive Director The Executive Director shall prepare a report analyzing the proposed Urban Area boundary revision, and which may include recommendations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 & RCW 43.97.015 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.
