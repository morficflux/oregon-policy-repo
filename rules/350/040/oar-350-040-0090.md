# Consultation (OAR 350-040-0090)

## At a glance

## Full text

350-040-0090 Consultation In considering amendments to urban area boundaries, the Gorge Commission shall consult with the Forest Service, both states, the six Gorge counties, all four Indian tribal governments and agencies or organizations that have a specific interest.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 & RCW 43.97.015 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.
