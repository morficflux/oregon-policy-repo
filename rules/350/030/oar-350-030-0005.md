# Purpose (OAR 350-030-0005)

## Full text

350-030-0005 Purpose The purpose of this division is to establish procedures and criteria for enforcement of P.L. 99-663 by the Commission as set forth in section 15 of the Scenic Area Act.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.
