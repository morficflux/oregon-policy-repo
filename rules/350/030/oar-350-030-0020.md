## Full text

350-030-0020 Investigation

(1) The Director shall investigate alleged violations of the measures listed in subsection 1 of 350-30-015 of this Division.

(2) The Director may inspect the subject property if necessary to conduct an investigation under subsection

(1) of this section.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.
