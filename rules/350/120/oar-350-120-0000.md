# Purpose (OAR 350-120-0000)

## At a glance

OAR 350-120-0000 — Purpose. Chapter 350, Division 120. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

350-120-0000 Purpose This division specifies the process of the Columbia River Gorge Commission (Commission) to certify activities undertaken under a National Scenic Area economic development grant and/or loan are consistent with the purposes of the Scenic Area Act, the management plan, and land use ordinances adopted pursuant to the Act.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.

History: CRGC 3-1994, f. 10-3-94, cert. ef. 10-31-94 CRGC 1-1994(Temp), f. & cert. ef. 5-4-94
