## Full text

350-016-0001 Commission Required to Prepare Public Writings in Readable Form

(1) The commission shall when reasonable prepare its public writings in simple language with short, precise, affirmative, active-voice sentences.

(2) As used in this section, “public writing” means any rule, form, license or notice prepared by the commission.
