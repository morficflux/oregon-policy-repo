## Full text

350-016-0022 Service of Process A petition for judicial review of a final order in a contested case shall be served upon the commission by delivery of a copy of the petition to the office of the executive director or chairperson of the commission at the principal office of the commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 - 196.165 & 390.500 - 390.515
