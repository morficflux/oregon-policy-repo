## Full text

350-016-0025 Appearance of Fairness Members of the Commission shall comply with Washington’s appearance of fairness doctrine in appeals and proceedings under this rule and under Rules 350-060 et seq. and Rules 350-070 et seq.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197.150 & RCW 43.97 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.150, RCW 43.97 & 16 U.S.C. 544 et seq.
