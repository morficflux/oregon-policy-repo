# Right to Inspect Public Records (OAR 350-012-0002)

## Full text

350-012-0002 Right to Inspect Public Records Every person has a right to inspect any public record of the commission, except as otherwise expressly provided by 350-012-0008.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197.150 & RCW 43.97 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.150, RCW 43.97 & 16 U.S.C. 544 et seq.
