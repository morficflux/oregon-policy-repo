## Full text

350-050-0090 Report of the Executive Director

(1) The Executive Director shall prepare a staff report, which may include recommendations. The report will analyze the proposed amendment based on the criteria of the Scenic Area Act and Rule 350-50-030.

(2) For legislative amendments, the Executive Director shall include recommended plan amendment language in the staff report.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 & RCW 43.97.015 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.
