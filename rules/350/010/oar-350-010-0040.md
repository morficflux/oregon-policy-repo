## Full text

350-010-0040 Resolving Prior Interpretations The Commission, Commission staff, U.S. Forest Service staff, and surveyors have made prior interpretations of the maps that Congress enacted. These prior interpretations may differ from the Legal Boundary Descriptions. The Legal Boundary Descriptions shall prevail in the event of a difference. Land use claims involving any difference shall be resolved in accordance with the Existing Uses provisions in the applicable county or Commission land use ordinance corresponding to the Existing Uses provisions in Section 7 of the Management Plan.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150; RCW 34.97.015; 16 U.S.C. 544b(e) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 34.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. 544b(e)
