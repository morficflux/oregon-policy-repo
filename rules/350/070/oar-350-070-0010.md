# Authority (OAR 350-070-0010)

## At a glance

OAR 350-070-0010 — Authority. Chapter 350, Division 070. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

350-070-0010 Authority The National Scenic Act authorizes appeals to the Gorge Commission by a person or entity adversely affected by a final action or order, and this includes decisions by the Executive Director under a land use ordinance for a county adopted by the Gorge Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.
