## Full text

350-060-0020 Scope Scope of Rules: All proceedings commenced by Notice of Appeal shall be governed by these rules. Where this division is silent, divisions 11, 12, 14, and 16 of the Commission’s rules shall be applicable provided that the specific provisions are applied in a manner that does not conflict with the provisions of this division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015, 16 U.S.C. § 544(b) et seq.16 & U.S.C. § 544m(a)(2)
