## Full text

350-060-0010 Authority The National Scenic Act authorizes appeals to the Gorge Commission by a person or entity adversely affected by a final action or order of a county.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.
