## Full text

643-010-0010 Assessments Any person who is first purchaser as defined in ORS Chapter 576, shall deduct and withhold two cents per pound (002.0) from the price to the producer, on and after September 1, 2024, on a dried weight basis, for all purchases from the producer of hops produced in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.325, 576.305 & 576.325 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.325
