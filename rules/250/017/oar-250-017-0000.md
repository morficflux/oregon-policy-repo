## Full text

250-017-0000 Purpose The purpose of this program is to provide volunteers an opportunity to remove litter and invasive species to improve the quality of the waters of this state in accordance with ORS 830.055.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.055
