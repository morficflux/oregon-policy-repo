## Full text

250-010-0230 Transfer of Ownership of Waterway Markers When any waterway marker(s) authorized under permit by the Marine Board, or the facility with which the marker(s) are associated, is sold or transferred, the Marine Board must be notified within 30 days.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: 830.110

History: OSMB 3-2015, f. 4-30-15, cert. ef. 5-1-15
