# Standards for (OAR 250-010-0260)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

250-010-0260 Standards for Letter or Numeral Elements of Markers Letters, numerals, or figures used with the marker symbol shall be black, and in block characters of good proportion, and spaced in a manner which will provide maximum legibility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.110
