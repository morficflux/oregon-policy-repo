## Full text

250-030-0100 Restrictions for the Chetco River A person must not use a motor for propelling a boat for any purpose above the Rogue-Siskiyou National Forest Boundary, as marked.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110 & 830.175 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.110

History: OSMB 1-2015, f. 1-11-16, cert. ef. 2-1-16
