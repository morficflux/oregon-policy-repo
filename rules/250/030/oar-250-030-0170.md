## Full text

250-030-0170 Restrictions for the Salmon River in Clackamas County A person must not use a motor for propelling a boat for any purpose on the Salmon River in Clackamas County.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110 & 830.175 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.110
