## Full text

250-015-0026 Light/Smoke Flares Vessels operating in ocean or coastal waters and bays/rivers, with an opening to the seas of 2 miles or more, are required to carry light and or smoke flares as follows:

(1) One electronic distress light, or 3 approved flares; and

(2) One distress flag or 3 approved flares or 3 approved smoke signals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.430 - 830.460

History: OSMB 5-2014, f. & cert. ef. 1-15-14 OSMB 3-2005, f. & cert, ef, 1-24-05
