# Navigation Equipment (OAR 250-015-0022)

## At a glance

OAR 250-015-0022 — Navigation Equipment. Chapter 250, Division 015. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

250-015-0022 Navigation Equipment Each vessel shall have installed a RADAR or a GLOBAL POSITIONING SYSTEM (GPS) navigation system. It shall be capable of providing the operator with rapid, reliable vessel positioning information. Portable or hand-held GPS is allowable on uninspected vessels less than 26 feet of open construction as an alternative means of compliance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110 & 830.175 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.450
