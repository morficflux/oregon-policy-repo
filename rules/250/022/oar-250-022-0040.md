# Title and Registration Requirements (OAR 250-022-0040)

## Full text

250-022-0040 Title and Registration Requirements Unless documented with the U.S. Coast Guard, all vessels engaged in plankton harvest that are powered by machinery must be titled and numbered in accordance with ORS Chapter 830, and must comply with proper display of number as provided in ORS 830.780.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110, 830.210 & 830.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.990

History: OSMB 4-1998, f. & cert. ef. 4-3-98
