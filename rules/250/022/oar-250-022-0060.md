## Full text

250-022-0060 Operating Requirements The provisions of ORS 830.300 to 830.370, and 830.505 to 830.550 and any related administrative rules do apply to all vessels engaged in the harvest of plankton on Klamath Lake.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110, 830.210 & 830.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.990

History: OSMB 4-1998, f. & cert. ef. 4-3-98
