## Full text

250-016-0055 Contested Case Hearings The Marine Board will accord opportunity for a hearing as provided in ORS 183.310 to 183.550. The Marine Board has adopted the current edition of the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure, as referenced in OAR 250-001-0005.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 704.500 & 704.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 704.035 & 704.040
