## Full text

122-085-0160 State Agency MCAT Assignments to PCFs

(1) State agency MCAT accounts assigned to a PCF prior to July 1, 2018 are not subject to OAR 122-085-0120. These accounts may continue through the collection cycle as per the purchase order agreement between the state agency and the PCF. Once the account is returned to the assigning state agency from the PCF, the state agency may evaluate the account for write-off or assign the account to DOR-OAA for additional collection services.

(2) State agency MCAT accounts subject to assignment after July 1, 2018 are subject to OAR 122-085-0120.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 293.231 & 293.233 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 293.231(5), 293.231(6)(c), 293.233(1) & 293.233(2)
