## Full text

122-085-0100 Statement of purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide criteria to determine when Mandatory Collection Agency Transfer (MCAT) accounts are subject to assignment or exempt from assignment to DOR-OAA under Oregon Revised Statute (ORS) 293.231. This rule also provides information on how a state agency may request an exemption from the statutory assignment timeframe referenced in ORS 293.231.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 293.231 & 293.233 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 293.231(5), 293.231(6)(c), 293.233(1) & 293.233(2)
