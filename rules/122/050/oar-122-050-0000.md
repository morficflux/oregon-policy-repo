# Purpose (OAR 122-050-0000)

## At a glance

## Full text

122-050-0000 Purpose The Oregon Agricultural Commodity Commissions (OACC) occasionally require the services of an outside party to accomplish all or part of a project. The purpose of these rules is to specify the screening and selection procedures which will be used for personal service contracts.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335(5)
