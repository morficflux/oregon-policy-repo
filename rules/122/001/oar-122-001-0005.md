122-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure The Department of Administration Services and the Chief Financial Office adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, as amended and effective January 1, 2024. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the agency.]
