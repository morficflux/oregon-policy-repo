# Authority (OAR 122-070-0100)

## At a glance

## Full text

122-070-0100 Authority Only the Director of the Department of Administrative Services is authorized by ORS 283.085 to 283.092 to enter into Financing Agreements to acquire real property or personal property for State Agencies. ORS 184.340 authorizes the Department of Administrative Services, with the approval of the Governor, to make reasonable rules and regulations that are necessary or proper for the administration of the law that the Department is charged with administering.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.340 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 283.085 - 283.092

History: BMD 1-2012, f. 1-26-12, cert. ef. 2-1-12
