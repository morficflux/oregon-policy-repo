## Full text

837-090-1010 Effective Dates

(1) OAR 837-090-1000 through 837-090-1045 are effective upon date of filing.

(2) Local government Hazardous Substance Possession Fee programs shall be in compliance with these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.408 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.396 - 453.414
