## Full text

837-085-0320 Payment of Penalties

(1) All civil penalties become due and owing ten days after the Notice of Noncompliance and Proposed/Final Penalty Assessment Order becomes final.

(2) If payment is not received within ten days after the order becomes final, it may be docketed as a judgment as provided by law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.367 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 435.357
