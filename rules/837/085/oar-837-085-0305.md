## Full text

837-085-0305 Penalties for Class V Noncompliance A covered employer, owner, or operator identified as being in Class V Noncompliance shall be assessed a $1000 penalty for each day they are in noncompliance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.367 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 435.357
