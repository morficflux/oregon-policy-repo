## Full text

837-085-0050 Hazardous Substance Information Survey — General

(1) The State Fire Marshal shall develop and distribute a Hazardous Substance Information Survey to covered employers, owners, and operators. The information received shall be used to establish and maintain the hazardous substance information program required by the Community Right-to-Know and Protection Act.

(2) A covered employer, owner, or operator receiving a Hazardous Substance Information Survey shall complete and return to the State Fire Marshal within 60 days following the date the State Fire Marshal mailed it.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.367 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.307 - 453.372
