## Full text

837-085-0010 Authority and Application

(1) These rules are promulgated under the State Fire Marshal’s authority contained in ORS 453.367 and 453.402(2).

(2) OAR 837, division 085 applies to a covered employer, owner, and operator of a fixed facility where hazardous substances or wastes are likely to be manufactured, generated, used, stored, possessed or disposed of.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.367 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.307 - 453.372
