## Full text

837-085-0390 Annual Summaries The State Fire Marshal shall annually summarize all incidents reported and information received from the Hazardous Substance Information Survey. Copies of these summaries will be distributed at no charge to:

(1) Governor;

(2) Legislative Assembly;

(3) Department of Environmental Quality;

(4) Workers' Compensation Division;

(5) Department of Transportation;

(6) Health Division of the Department of Human Resources;

(7) Environmental Health Sciences Center at Oregon State University;

(8) Poison Control Center at Oregon Health Sciences University;

(9) Oregon Emergency Management Division of the Department of State Police;

(10) Oregon State Police;

(11) Every public library as defined in ORS 357.400;

(12) Public fire departments;

(13) Local law enforcement agencies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.367 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 435.357
