## Full text

837-120-0360 Grounds for Revocation of Approval The State Fire Marshal may revoke the approval of a training program after written notice and hearing if the State Fire Marshal finds that the applicant of the approved program has failed to continue to satisfy the requirements of OAR 837-120-0340 or 837-120-0350 or the State Fire Marshal’s letter of approval, or has misrepresented itself in its application.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.367 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.374 - 453.390
