## Full text

837-120-0530 Hazardous Materials Response Resource Management The Department of the State Fire Marshal (OSFM) shall:

(1) Inventory oil and hazardous materials emergency response resources available in Oregon by identifying their locations, means to access them, whether they are publicly or privately maintained, and uploading them to the Western Response Resource List;

(2) Identify the need for additional Hazardous Materials Transportation by Rail emergency response resources for spill or release of hazardous materials;

(3) Facilitate the acquisition and strategic deployment of oil and hazardous materials spill response resources to support emergency response to hazardous materials by rail incidents.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.392 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.392 – 453.394
