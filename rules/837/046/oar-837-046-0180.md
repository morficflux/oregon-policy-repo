## Full text

837-046-0180 Procedures, Hearings and Judicial Review

(1) Hearings are conducted according to ORS 183.413 through 183.470.

(2) The Attorney General may bring action for the OSFM to: (a) Seek injunctive relief to prevent or end a violation; (b) Recover civil penalties; (c) Obtain access for inspections; or (d) Recover attorney fees and other enforcement costs and disbursements.
