## Full text

837-012-0530 Permit Fees

(1) Permit fees shall be paid to the Department of the State Fire Marshal and must accompany the permit application.

(2) Payment may be made by personal check, business check, cashier’s check, money order, online, or other approved OSFM method, and made payable to the Department of the State Fire Marshal.

(3) The permit fee is $3,000.

(4) Permit fees are non-refundable. Exception: The State Fire Marshal may refund all or part of the permit fee if it is determined the application is not appropriate or the permit is denied.

(5) Permit fees are non-transferable to any other individual or business.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476, 478 & 480
