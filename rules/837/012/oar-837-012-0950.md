## Full text

837-012-0950 Insurance Requirements The governing body of any municipality or of any county, may require liability insurance or other form of indemnity deemed adequate by the municipality, or the county, from any person, in a sum not less than $500, conditioned for payment of all damages which may be caused either to a person or property by reason of the authorized fireworks display and arising from any acts of any person or agents, employees or subcontractors of the person. NOTE: Local Fire Authorities often require the industry standard amount of liability insurance of $1 million.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 480.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 480.110 - 480.165
