## Full text

837-012-0600 Purpose and Scope—Retail Sales and Storage of Pyrotechnics (Allowed Fireworks) in Oregon These rules establish permit and other requirements for persons who sell or otherwise provide, intend to sell or otherwise provide, retail fireworks to individual members of the general public.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476 & 480 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 480.110, 480.120, 480.127, 480.130, 480.150, 480.152, 480.154, 480.156, 480.158, 480.160 & 480.165
