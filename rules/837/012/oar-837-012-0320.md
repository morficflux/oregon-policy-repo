## Full text

837-012-0320 Storage of Pest Control Fireworks Pest control fireworks must be stored in accordance with the requirements of these rules, the OFC, and the OSSC.

Statutory/Other Authority: HB 2432 (2015 OL Ch. 57) & 480.124 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 480.110 - 480.165
