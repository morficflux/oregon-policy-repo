## Full text

837-047-0135 Exemption A landlord who rents a space for a manufactured dwelling or who rents moorage space for a floating home as defined in ORS 830.700, but does not rent the manufactured dwelling home or floating home is exempt from these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476.725 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.725
