## Full text

837-047-0140 Power Source Carbon monoxide alarms must be battery operated or receive their primary power source from the building wiring with a battery back-up. Plug in devices must have a battery back-up.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476.725 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.725
