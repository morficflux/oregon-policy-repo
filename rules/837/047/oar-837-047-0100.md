## Full text

837-047-0100 Purpose and Scope The purpose of these rules is to establish minimum standards for the design, inspection, testing, placement and location and maintenance of carbon monoxide alarms in one and two family dwellings, manufactured dwellings, and multifamily housing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476.725 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.725
