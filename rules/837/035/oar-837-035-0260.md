## Full text

837-035-0260 Inspections The OSFM or an authorized representative may inspect Oregon wholesale dealers, agents, and retailers for compliance with ORS 476.760. Inspections include packaging, certification, cigarettes, and any other documents to determine compliance with ORS 476.760, 476.770 and 476.785.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.755 - 476.806 & 476.995
