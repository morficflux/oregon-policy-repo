## Full text

837-130-0010 Definitions

(1) “Unprotected Area” shall mean an area outside the boundaries of recognized public or private fire protection.

(2) “Transportation Route” shall mean a roadway, waterway, railroad right-of-way or airplane route against which no taxes or assessments for fire protection are levied by the municipality, fire district, or fire protection agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.280 & 478.310
