## Full text

837-110-0050 Prohibited Use The use of pitch forks, harrows, or the dragging of burning tires to ignite the fire is prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476 & 478 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.380 & 478.960

History: FM 1-1989, f. & cert. ef. 2-7-89 FM 8-1988(Temp), f. & cert. ef. 8-15-88 FM 7-1988(Temp), f. & cert. ef. 8-12-88
