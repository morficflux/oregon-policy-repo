## Full text

837-110-0120 Firefighting Water Supplies When propaning acreage, the following safety measures shall apply:

(1) At least one firefighting water tank vehicle meeting the equipment requirements of OAR 837-110-0120 through 837-110-0140 and which has a minimum water tank capacity of 200 gallons shall be on site.

(2) If additional firefighting assistance is more than five minutes from a burn site within a fire safety buffer zone, or ten minutes otherwise, then water tank capacity mentioned in section

(1) of this rule shall be raised to 500 gallons.

(3) A means to refill the tanks mentioned in sections

(1) and

(2) of this rule shall be provided within a ten minute turn around time. EXCEPTION: Water tank vehicles of smaller capacity may be used provided the total gallonage capacity complies with the above.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476.030, 476.380 & 478.960 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.380 & 478.960
