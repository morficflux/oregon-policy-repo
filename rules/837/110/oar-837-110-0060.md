# Communication (OAR 837-110-0060)

## At a glance

OAR 837-110-0060 — Communication. Chapter 837, Division 110. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

837-110-0060 Communication The ability to call assistance and summon help from an appropriate emergency response agency shall be maintained at all times by phone, or by radio communications between all firefighting equipment used in the burning of the field and a constantly staffed base station or home with phone access.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476 & 478 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.380 & 478.960
