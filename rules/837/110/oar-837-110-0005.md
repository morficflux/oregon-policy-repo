## Full text

837-110-0005 Purpose and Scope The purpose of these rules is to increase the degree of public safety by preventing unwanted wild fires and smoke from field burning and propaning near highways and freeways within the State of Oregon. These rules shall apply to that area west of the crest of the Cascade Range and south to the Douglas/Lane County lines.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 476.030, 476.380 & 478.960 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 476.380 & 478.960
