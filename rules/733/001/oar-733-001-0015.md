## Full text

733-001-0015 Assumed Business Name Pursuant to ORS 377.835(5) and 648.005, the Travel Information Council adopts the Assumed Business Name of the Oregon Travel Experience to conduct or transact business in the State of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 377.835(5) & 648.005 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 648.005
