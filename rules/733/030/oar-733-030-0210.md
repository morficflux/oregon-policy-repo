## Full text

733-030-0210 Temporary Removal and Reinstallation

(1) Upon request, the Council and Committee may authorize a relocation of an existing marker.

(2) All costs including site preparation and advance signing, associated with moving the marker must be borne by the party requesting the relocation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 377.787 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 377.710, 377.790-377.830, 377.833-377.836 & 377.838 – 377.845
