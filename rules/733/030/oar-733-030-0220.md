## Full text

733-030-0220 Maintenance

(1) Markers are the property of the Council. The Council encourages cooperative maintenance agreements with any sponsoring group.

(2) Any physical changes to the marker must be approved in advance by the Committee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 377.787 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 377.710, 377.790-377.830, 377.833-377.836 & 377.838 – 377.845
