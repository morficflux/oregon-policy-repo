## Full text

733-030-0400 Applicability and Purpose

(1) The purpose of these rules is to establish standards for Interstate Oasis signing erected within highway rights-of-way to provide directional information to qualified businesses that provide products and services to the public.

(2) These rules are applicable to the Interstate, Freeway and Expressway systems.

(3) The authority for the issuance of these rules is Oregon Laws 1979, Chapter 478, Section 7 and 23 U.S.C. 109(d), 131(f), 315 and 49 CFR 1.48(b).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 377.787 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 377.710, 377.790-377.830, 377.833-377.836 & 377.838 – 377.845
