## Full text

409-060-0140 Evidence-based Guidelines The EbGS shall develop evidence-based guidelines based on one or more existing guidelines which may involve the consideration of additional research. Evidence-based guidelines shall be developed according to the process described in OAR 409-060-0120 except as described in this section.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 414.695 & 413.042 Statutes/Other Implemented: 414.695 & 414.698
