## Full text

409-015-0012 Filing Date The date of filing for the Databank Monthly Data electronically, is the date of receipt by the Oregon Association of Hospitals and Health Systems. The date of filing for the Patient Revenue and Unreimbursed Care (Form FR-3) is the postmark date. [ED. NOTE: Forms referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 442.405, 442.420 & 442.425 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 442.425
