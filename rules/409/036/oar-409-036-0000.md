## Full text

409-036-0000 Purpose and Scope These rules (OAR 409-036-0000 to 409-036-0150) establish the Health Care Provider Incentive Program within the Oregon Health Authority. The program promotes culturally responsive care by ensuring equitable incentives for qualified health providers who commit to serving patients in both rural and non-rural underserved areas across Oregon, thereby improving access to care for all residents.
