## Full text

409-030-0200 Tuberculosis Screening

(1) A student must obtain and provide documentation for TB screening consistent with the requirements for immunization in OAR 409-030-0180.

(2) TB screening must be conducted in a manner consistent with the CDC guidelines available at https://www.cdc.gov/tb-healthcare-settings/ or other Oregon or federal health authority guidelines prior to the start date of the initial clinical placement.
