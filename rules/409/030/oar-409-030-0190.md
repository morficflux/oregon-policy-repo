# Screening Standards (OAR 409-030-0190)

## At a glance

OAR 409-030-0190 — Screening Standards. Chapter 409, Division 030. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

409-030-0190 Screening Standards Table 1 in OAR 409-030-0150 provides detailed information related to required screenings for students’ clinical placements. Required screenings consist of:

(1) Tuberculosis (OAR 409-030-0200);

(2) Substance abuse or misuse (OAR 409-030-0210); and

(3) State and nationwide criminal background check (OAR 409-030-0220). [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
