## Full text

806-010-0075 The Practice of Architecture

(1) The "Practice of Architecture" is defined in ORS 671.010 and relates to the professional activities of the registered architect. These activities include all analysis, calculations, research, graphic presentation, literary expression, and advice related to the preparation of documents for the design and construction of buildings, structures, and their related environment whether interior or exterior.

(2) The permission granted in ORS 671.065(2) that permits an architect who is not registered in Oregon to offer to render services does not allow that non-registered architect to engage in the Practice of Architecture.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670, 671, 670.310 & 671.125 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.010 – 671.220
