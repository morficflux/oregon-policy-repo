## Full text

806-010-0115 Construction Document Modification Modifications to technical submissions for buildings/structures are an integral part of the practice of architecture; and as such, modifications which become part of change orders and/or addenda to alter those documents must bear the stamp and signature, as required, of the registered architect responsible for the modifications.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, 671.020 & 671.125 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.010 – 671.220
