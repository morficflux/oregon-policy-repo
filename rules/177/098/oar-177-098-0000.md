# Purpose (OAR 177-098-0000)

## Full text

177-098-0000 Purpose These rules establish the requirements for playing Mega Millions, a lotto game operated through a Cross-Sell Agreement for the selling of the Mega Millions Game by the Multi-State Lottery Association (hereinafter referred to as “MUSL”), of which the Oregon State Lottery is a member.

Statutory/Other Authority: Or Const, Art XV, § 4(4)(a), ORS chapter 190, ORS 461.120, 461.210, 461.220, 461.230, 461.240, 461.250, 461.260 & 461.300 Statutes/Other Implemented: Or Const, Art XV, § 4, ORS chapter 190, ORS 461.120, 461.210, 461.220, 461.230, 461.240, 461.250, 461.260 & 461.300
