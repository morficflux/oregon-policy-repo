## Full text

177-099-0040 Cancellation of Tickets A player may cancel a Keno ticket. To cancel a ticket, a player must follow the procedure in OAR 177-046-0060.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.200
