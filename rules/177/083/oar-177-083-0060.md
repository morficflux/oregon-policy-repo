## Full text

177-083-0060 Ticket Validation Requirements

(1) General: A Lucky Lines ticket must meet the requirements in OAR chapter 177 to be valid and eligible to receive prize payment.

(2) Validation of Consecutive Play Tickets: A game ticket containing a winning game play and purchased for play in consecutive drawings may be validated prior to the occurrence of future drawings for which the game ticket was purchased. At the time the original game ticket is validated, an exchange ticket shall be issued for the remainder of the drawings appearing on the validated game ticket.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 461 & OR Const., Art. XV & §4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.210
