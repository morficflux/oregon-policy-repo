# Purpose (OAR 177-085-0000)

## Full text

177-085-0000 Purpose These rules, OAR 177-085-0005 to 177-085-0065, establish the procedures and requirements for playing Powerball®, a lotto game operated by the Multi-State Lottery (hereinafter referred to as “MUSL”), of which the Oregon State Lottery is a member.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.210
