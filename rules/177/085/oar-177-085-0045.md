## Full text

177-085-0045 Ticket Responsibility Until such time as a signature is placed in the area designated for signature, a ticket is owned by the bearer of the ticket. MUSL, the Product Group, and the Lottery are not responsible for lost or stolen tickets.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 461.250 & OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.250
