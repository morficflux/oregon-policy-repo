## Full text

177-200-0015 Game Play Price The price of a game play for a Video LotterySM game shall be clearly displayed on the terminal screen during play.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4), ORS 461.210, 461.220, 461.230, 461.240, 461.250, 461.260 & 962 & OL 1991 (enrolled HB 3151) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.240
