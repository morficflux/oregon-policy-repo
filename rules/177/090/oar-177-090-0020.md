## Full text

177-090-0020 Cost of a Ticket The cost of a ticket is determined by the amount of money a player chooses to wager. The minimum wager is $2 per ticket. A player may choose to wager $2, $3, $4, $5, $10, or $20 per ticket.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.240
