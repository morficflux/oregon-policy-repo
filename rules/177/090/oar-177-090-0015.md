## Full text

177-090-0015 Play Dates and Times For a given play week, a player may purchase a ticket for an event starting at the beginning of the play week. After the scheduled start time of a game as listed on the official program, a ticket for that game and any special plays in that game can no longer be purchased. A ticket for a specific play week can not be purchased when there are less than three events remaining in that play week.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.213
