## Full text

177-090-0050 Odds of Winning The number of prizes for Sports Action is not pre-determined by the Lottery. The overall prize percentage payout for Sports Action prizes is estimated at sixty percent over time but will vary week to week, as well as year to year, due to factors including, but not limited to, the number of players participating each week and the number of winning wagers.
