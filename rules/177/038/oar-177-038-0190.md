# Cancellation of a Solicitation Document, Offer, or Award (OAR 177-038-0190)

## Full text

177-038-0190 Cancellation of a Solicitation Document, Offer, or Award Lottery may cancel a procurement, or may reject all Offers when the cancellation or rejection is in the best interest of the Lottery as determined by the Lottery. The reasons for the cancellation or rejection must be made a part of the procurement file. The Lottery is not liable to any Offeror for any loss or expense caused by or resulting from the cancellation of a Solicitation Document, or the rejection of any or all Offers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS Chapter 461 & Oregon Constitution, Article XV, §4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.400, ORS 461.410, ORS 461.420, ORS 461.430 & ORS 461.440
