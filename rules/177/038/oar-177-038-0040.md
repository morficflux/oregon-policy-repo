## Full text

177-038-0040 Environmentally Preferable Procurement General Policy In developing work statements, Specifications, or other product descriptions, the Lottery shall consider the purchase of environmentally preferable products or services that comply with the Lottery's corporate social responsibility principles. This includes, but is not limited to, acquiring Goods that are durable, recyclable, reusable, readily biodegradable, energy efficient, made from recycled materials, and nontoxic. In doing so, the Lottery shall purchase Goods and Services based on long-term environmental impacts and operating costs. Furthermore, the Lottery shall first seek to reuse, repair, or refurbish existing equipment and products prior to purchasing new, to the extent reuse is fiscally sound and complements other Lottery safety and sustainability policies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS Chapter 461 & Oregon Constitution, Article XV, §4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.400, ORS 461.410, ORS 461.420, ORS 461.430 & ORS 461.440
