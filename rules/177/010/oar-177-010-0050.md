## Full text

177-010-0050 Merchandise Prizes In the exercise of the Director’s discretion, the Director may pay the cash value of any merchandise prize in lieu of that prize. The cash value of a merchandise prize is the amount that the Lottery paid for the merchandise.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.230
