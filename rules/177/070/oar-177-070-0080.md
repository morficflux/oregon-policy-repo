## Full text

177-070-0080 Unauthorized Use of Play Slips General: A person shall not insert into a Draw game terminal’s play slip reader any material that is not a play slip printed and approved for use by the Oregon Lottery, including but not limited to facsimiles or copies of play slips. No device shall be connected to a Lottery terminal to enter plays, except as may be approved by the Lottery.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.210
