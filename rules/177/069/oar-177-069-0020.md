## Full text

177-069-0020 Cancellation of Lottery Raffle Tickets A Lottery raffle ticket once issued cannot be cancelled by a player or a retailer.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.210, 461.220, 461.240, 461.250 & 461.260
