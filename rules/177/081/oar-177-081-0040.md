## Full text

177-081-0040 Cancellation of Tickets A player may cancel a Pick 4 ticket for a single drawing or consecutive drawings. To cancel a ticket, a player must follow the procedure in OAR 177-046-0060.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV Sec.

(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.210, 461. 220, 461.240 & 461. 250
