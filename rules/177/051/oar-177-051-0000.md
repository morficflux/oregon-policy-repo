## Full text

177-051-0000 Purpose The purpose of this division of OAR chapter 177 is to authorize and set forth the provisions for promotions, giveaways that may be conducted from time to time, and Player Loyalty Programs of the Oregon State Lottery. The rules in this division do not apply to promotions conducted by Lottery retailers or incentive programs that the Lottery may conduct for Lottery retailers.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.200
