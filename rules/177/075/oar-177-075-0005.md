# Price (OAR 177-075-0005)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

177-075-0005 Price Game plays on a Megabucks tickets sell for $0.50 each with the minimum wager being two plays for $1. All plays must be made in $1 increments, hence only an even number of selections may be made, up to a maximum of ten selections for $5.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.240
