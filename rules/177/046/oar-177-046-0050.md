## Full text

177-046-0050 Accuracy of Tickets and Shares It is the sole responsibility of a player to verify the accuracy of a Lottery ticket or share purchased by the player. The Lottery is not liable for any Lottery ticket or share purchased or printed in error. Specific Lottery game rules may provide for cancellation of a Lottery ticket or share under certain circumstances.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 461 & OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.020, 461.210, 461.220, 461.230, 461.240, 461.250 & 461.260
