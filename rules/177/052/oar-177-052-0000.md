# Purpose (OAR 177-052-0000)

## Full text

177-052-0000 Purpose The purpose of this division of OAR chapter 177 is to authorize and set forth the provisions for second chance drawings that the Oregon State Lottery, or a drawing agent, may conduct from time to time.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 461 & OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.220, 461.230 & 461.250
