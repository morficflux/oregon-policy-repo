# Subsequent Application (OAR 177-040-0120)

## Full text

177-040-0120 Subsequent Application When a retailer contract is terminated for any reason, the retailer, or an applicant that is similar to the retailer whose contract was terminated, will be required to wait one year to reapply for a retailer contract. In the Director’s sole discretion, the Director may waive all or a portion of the one-year requirement based upon a showing of good cause by the applicant.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.300
