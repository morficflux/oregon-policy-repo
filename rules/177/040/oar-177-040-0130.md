## Full text

177-040-0130 Adding a Location An existing Lottery retailer may apply for and may receive approval for a contract for an additional location prior to that site being open to the public.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.300
