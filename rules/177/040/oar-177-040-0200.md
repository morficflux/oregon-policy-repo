## Full text

177-040-0200 Lottery Retailer Second Chance Drawings Lottery retailers are prohibited from conducting second chance drawings for prizes which require the use of a non-winning Oregon Lottery® ticket or share as an entry into a drawing conducted or operated by the retailer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 461 & OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.200
