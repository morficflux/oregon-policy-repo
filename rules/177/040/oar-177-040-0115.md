## Full text

177-040-0115 Underage Seller No one under the age of 18 may sell Lottery tickets or shares.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.300
