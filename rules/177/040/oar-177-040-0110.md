## Full text

177-040-0110 Contract Non-Transferable A Lottery retailer contract does not have value, and cannot be sold, transferred, or assigned.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.300
