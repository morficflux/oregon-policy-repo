# Notice (OAR 177-040-0180)

## Full text

177-040-0180 Notice Requirement Lottery retailers who offer video Lottery games must post in a conspicuous place on the retailer’s premises at least one copy of a notice containing information concerning compulsive gambling.

Statutory/Other Authority: OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.300
