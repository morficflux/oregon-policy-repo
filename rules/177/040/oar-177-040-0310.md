## Full text

177-040-0310 Alternative Dispute Resolution Policy The Lottery encourages the use of an alternative dispute resolution process, such as mediation, in disputes arising between a retailer and the Lottery, when such process is appropriate and provides for a more efficient and effective dispute resolution.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 461 & OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & HB 2237 (2005) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 461.300
