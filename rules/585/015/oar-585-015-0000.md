## Full text

585-015-0000 Introduction These rules govern the Business Enterprise Program of the Oregon Commission for the Blind. They are intended to supplement the governing law of the Randolph-Sheppard Act, 20 USC sections 107 to 107f and ORS 346.510 to 346.570.

Statutory/Other Authority: HB 3253 (2017) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 346.510 to 346.570 & Randolph-Sheppard Act, 20 USC sections 107 to 107f
