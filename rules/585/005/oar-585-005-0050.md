## Full text

585-005-0050 Crimes Relevant to a Fitness Determination The following are crimes relevant to a fitness determination made under OAR Chapter 585, Division 5.

(1) Active Warrants.

(2) Restraining Orders.

(3) Sex Offender Registration.

(4) Any Felony Conviction.

(5) Any Sex Offense Conviction.

(6) Any Controlled Substance Conviction within the past five years.

(7) Any Violent Crimes against Persons within the past ten years.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 346.300 & ORS 181A.195 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 346.300 & ORS 181A.195
