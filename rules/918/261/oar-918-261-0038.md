## Full text

918-261-0038 Board Exemption for Manufactured Construction Services An exemption from licensing is created under ORS 479.540 for the placement of manufactured construction services of 100 amperes, 240 volts single-phase or less. This exemption applies only to manufactured services used during construction. Repairs or alterations to the manufactured construction service require permit, inspection and appropriately licensed person(s). The word “manufactured” as used in this rule applies to a construction service built by and supplied from an electrical contractor or manufacturer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.540
