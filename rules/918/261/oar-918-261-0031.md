# Board (OAR 918-261-0031)

## Full text

918-261-0031 Board Exemptions for Industrial Electrical Equipment Industrial electrical equipment designed for and used directly in the production of a product; which due to the equipment design or use requires specialized and specific training in the process, function, design, modification, repair, or maintenance of the equipment is exempt from permits specifically for repair, modification or maintenance. Individuals performing work under this rule are also exempt from licensing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.540 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.540

History: BCD 13-2005, f. 6-20-05, cert. ef. 7-1-05
