## Full text

918-311-0000 Design, Planning and Lay Out of Electrical Installations by Supervising Electricians A licensed supervising electrician may design, plan and lay out electrical installations for:

(1) The customers of the electrical contractor for whom the supervising electrician is employed; or

(2) The industrial plant that employs the supervising electrician.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.730 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.730
