## Full text

918-690-0410 Cross Reference to Statutes Statutory plumbing definitions are generally in ORS 447.010 and 693.010. The following are among words defined by statute:

(1) “Board” is defined in ORS 447.010;

(2) “Building sewer” is defined in ORS 701.348;

(3) “Journeyman Plumber” is defined in ORS 693.010;

(4) “Limited Specialty Plumber” is defined by ORS 693.103;

(5) “Plumbing” is defined in ORS 447.010.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 447.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 447.020
