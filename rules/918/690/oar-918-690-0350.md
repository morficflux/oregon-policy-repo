## Full text

918-690-0350 Plumbing Specialty Code Appeals, ORS 455.690 All code-related appeals from a municipal appeals board or from a subordinate officer of the division are under ORS 455.690.

(1) The person appealing shall prepare a statement showing: (a) Parties involved, addresses, telephone and FAX numbers, if available; (b) Code section involved; (c) An explanation of why the ruling should be reversed; and (d) Whether the inspecting jurisdiction issued a “stop work” order.

(2) The person being appealed shall file a copy of the written interpretation or decision, advise if a stop work order was issued and detail the immediate hazards involved.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 447.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 455.690
