# Standards for Electrical Plan Submissions (OAR 918-305-0500)

## Full text

918-305-0500 Standards for Electrical Plan Submissions A plan review shall be performed prior to the issuance of a permit. Fees shall be based on an hourly rate of $69 per hour. The plan review under these rules shall be limited to the review of the electrified fixed guideway railway system(s) being installed. Other permits and plan reviews may be required by the jurisdiction administering the state building code as defined in ORS 455.010.
