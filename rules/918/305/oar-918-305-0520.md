## Full text

918-305-0520 Time Limit for Plan Review

(1) Plan reviews shall be approved or denied within ten working days from receipt of the complete electrified fixed guideway railway system plans by the jurisdiction requiring plan review. A reviewed set of plans shall be returned to the plan applicant: (a) If a plan is rejected, the deficiencies must be set out in writing. Upon resubmission of the necessary items, the ten-day requirement starts again; (b) If, because of the complexity or size of the installation, additional time beyond the ten working days is required to complete the review, the jurisdiction shall notify the person taking out the electrical permit of the reasons for the delay and the date the review will be completed.

(2) Nothing in this rule shall prohibit a jurisdiction from issuing a complete or partial permit before the entire plans and specifications are submitted or approved, provided adequate information is provided showing compliance with pertinent portions of the code. The permittee proceeds at risk, without assurance that the permit for the entire installation will be granted, or that corrections will not be required including those portions permitted.
