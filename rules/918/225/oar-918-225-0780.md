## Full text

918-225-0780 Filing of Insurance Notices Insurance companies shall notify the Division within 30 days of providing insurance, cancellation or suspension of insurance or non-renewal of insurance regarding boilers or pressure vessels. This notice shall be on a Division-approved form and shall use Oregon State Numbers to designate the boilers or pressure vessels involved.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 480.545 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 480.545
