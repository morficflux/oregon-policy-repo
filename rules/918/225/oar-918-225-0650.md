## Full text

918-225-0650 Practical Experience Requirement Inspectors shall have at least the following practical experience in activities listed by ORS 480.565(1):

(1) Chief Inspector, ten years experience;

(2) Deputy or Special Inspector, experience required by the bylaws of the National Board for a commission as a National Board Inspector.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 480.565 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 480.565

History: BCA 36-1993, f. 12-30-93, cert. ef. 1-1-94
