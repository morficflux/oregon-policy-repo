# Reporting of (OAR 918-225-0565)

## At a glance

OAR 918-225-0565 — Reporting of. Chapter 918, Division 225. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

918-225-0565 Reporting of Immediate Hazards to Health or Safety Deputy and special inspectors shall report immediate hazards to health or safety concerning boilers or pressure vessels to the chief inspector immediately by telephone and also that a notice of defective condition was issued and that the hazardous operations were stopped.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 480.545 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 480.545
