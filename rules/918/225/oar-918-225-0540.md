## Full text

918-225-0540 Scope of Special Inspector’s Authority

(1) General inspection requirements are in ORS 480.560.

(2) The type of inspections a special inspector can do is set out in ORS 480.570.

(3) The special inspector’s enforcement powers are in ORS 480.660.

(4) Appeals from special inspector actions are under ORS 480.660(4).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 480.560, 480.570 & 480.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 480.560, 480.570 & 480.660

History: BCA 36-1993, f. 12-30-93, cert. ef. 1-1-94
