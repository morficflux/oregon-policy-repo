## Full text

918-225-0435 Amendments to the Oregon Boiler and Pressure Vessel Specialty Code The Oregon Boiler and Pressure Vessel Specialty Code is amended pursuant to OAR chapter 918, division 8. Amendments adopted for inclusion into the Oregon Boiler and Pressure Vessel Specialty Code are placed in this rule. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 455.020, ORS 480.545 & ORS 480.550 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 480.545 & ORS 480.550
