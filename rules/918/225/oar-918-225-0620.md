## Full text

918-225-0620 Bulk Rate Permits

(1) Bulk rate permits may be granted if there is a quantity of either six

(6) or more boilers or six

(6) or more pressure vessels at the same location.

(2) To obtain bulk rate permits, the owner or user must make a written request to the division at least 30 days before the expiration of existing operating permits.

(3) If a bulk rate site requires two or more inspections during an inspection cycle, the division may rescind bulk rate permits and charge the full operating permit fees, as provided in Table 3-B. Inspection cycles are listed in OAR 918-225-0570 Table 1-B. [NOTE: Table referenced is not included in rule text.] [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
