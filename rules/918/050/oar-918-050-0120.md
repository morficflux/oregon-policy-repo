## Full text

918-050-0120 Statewide Fee Methodologies for Electrical Permits An electrical permit fee shall be calculated based on the categories, procedures, and requirements established in OAR 918-309-0020 to 918-309-0070. A set minimum fee may be established.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 455.048, 455.055 & 479.870 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 455.046, 455.055 & 479.870
