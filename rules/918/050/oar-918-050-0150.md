## Full text

918-050-0150 Statewide Fee Methodology for Medical Gas Installations

(1) A plumbing permit fee for the installation of a medical gas system shall be determined based on the value of installation costs and the system equipment, including but not limited to, inlets, outlets, fixtures, and appliances and applied to the municipality’s fee schedule, with a set minimum fee.

(2) The plan review fee shall be based on a predetermined percentage of the permit fee as set by the municipality.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 455.048 & 455.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 455.046 & 455.055
