## Full text

918-035-0000 Purpose and Scope OAR 918, division 35 establishes standards and procedures for approval of continuing education courses by the appropriate board or division. These rules apply to continuing education providers and instructors for purposes of gaining and maintaining approval of continuing education courses and instructors.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 455.117 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 455.117
