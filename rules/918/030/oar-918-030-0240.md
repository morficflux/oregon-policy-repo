## Full text

918-030-0240 Renewal — Extension for Hardship or Illness

(1) The appropriate board or the division may extend the period for complying with continuing education requirements or for complying with renewal requirements in cases of hardship or illness.

(2) Requests for extension must: (a) Be in writing; (b) Describe the hardship or illness; (c) Describe why the applicant is unable to comply; and (d) State when the person will complete the continuing education requirements.

(3) A hardship or illness extension will not be granted for: (a) Failure or inability to pay renewal fees; or (b) Renewal applications that are lost or otherwise are not delivered to the applicant.
