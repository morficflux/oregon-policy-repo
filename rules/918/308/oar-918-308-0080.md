## Full text

918-308-0080 Updating by Existing Municipalities

(1) If a municipality is unable to implement the program following delegation, the municipality shall notify the division explaining why and outlining how enforcement will be accomplished.

(2) Municipalities with ongoing electrical programs shall update their ordinances and operating plans, to reflect current operations according to the Electrical Delegation Rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.855 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.855
