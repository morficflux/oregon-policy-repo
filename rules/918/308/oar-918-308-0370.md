## Full text

918-308-0370 Exit Interview

(1) Immediately following completion of a comprehensive review, the division shall conduct an exit interview with the building official or designated representative.

(2) The exit interviewer shall provide: (a) The general result of the review; (b) Formal notification of any items requiring immediate attention; and (c) Specific information to the extent such is available.
