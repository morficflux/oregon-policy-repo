## Full text

918-308-0410 Appeal of Deficiency Citations A municipality aggrieved by a notice of deficiency or the denial of proposed correction procedures may ask for a contested case hearing under ORS Chapter 183 and the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.855 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.855
