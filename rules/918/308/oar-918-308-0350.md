## Full text

918-308-0350 Inspector Performance Ratings

(1) During a comprehensive review, the division shall review selected electrical inspections.

(2) The reviewer shall assess points for any electrical violation not noted by the inspector on the following basis: (a) Ten points for each mandatory item in OAR 918, division 271; (b) One-tenth point for each secondary item in OAR 918, division 271.

(3) An individual performance rating for selected municipality inspectors shall be determined by dividing the total points assessed for all inspection items missed by the number of inspections reviewed.
