# Program (OAR 918-308-0380)

## At a glance

## Full text

918-308-0380 Program Report The division shall provide the municipality a written report of its findings within 60 days of completion of a comprehensive review or within 15 days of completion of a single-purpose review.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.855 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.855
