## Full text

918-308-0360 Program Inspection Rating An inspection rating for the municipality program shall be determined by dividing the combined points assigned for all inspections reviewed pursuant to these rules by the total number of inspections reviewed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.855 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.855
