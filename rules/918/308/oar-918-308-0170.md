## Full text

918-308-0170 Accounting Plan The municipality shall have an accounting system which segregates electrical revenues, shows the source of electrical income including interest earned on held funds, shows charges, and where electrical revenues were spent. If overhead charges to the inspecting organization are based on allocations, the allocations must be supportable under general accounting principles.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.730 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.855, 455.148 & 455.150
