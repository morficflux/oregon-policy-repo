# Permit (OAR 918-308-0120)

## Full text

918-308-0120 Permit Sales Office A municipality shall establish at least one office within each noncontiguous service area where electrical permits can be obtained directly or have self-issuing or temporary permit procedures for all regular work days, which excludes weekends and holidays.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.855 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.855
