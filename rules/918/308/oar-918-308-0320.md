## Full text

918-308-0320 Initial Interview

(1) The division shall conduct an initial interview with the building official or designated representative as the first step in the comprehensive review process.

(2) The initial interview shall give the building official or representative an understanding of the manner in which the review will be performed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.855 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.855
