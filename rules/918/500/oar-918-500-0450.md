## Full text

918-500-0450 Insignia and Label Requirements All manufactured dwellings constructed on or after June 15, 1976, must bear an insignia of compliance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.176 & 446.230 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.170
