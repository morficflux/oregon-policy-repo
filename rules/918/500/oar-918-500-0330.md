## Full text

918-500-0330 Change of Name, Address or Location When a manufacturer changes its name, address, or location, the manufacturer must notify the division in writing within ten days of the date of change. The notice must be accompanied by an Application for Manufacturer Registration, together with the fee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.176 & 446.230 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.230
