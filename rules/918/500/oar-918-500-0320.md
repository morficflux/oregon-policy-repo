## Full text

918-500-0320 Change of Ownership If there is a change of ownership of a manufactured dwelling manufacturer, the new owner must, within ten days of the date of change, register with the division pursuant to OAR 918-500-0300..

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.176 & 446.230 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.230
