# Warranty (OAR 918-500-0550)

## At a glance

## Full text

918-500-0550 Warranty Work Permits are not required for manufacturer’s warranty work on manufactured dwellings as per the Manufactured Home Procedural and Enforcement Regulations (24 CFR 3282) when completed by the manufacturer or the manufacturer’s representative. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.185 & 446.225

History: BCD 1-2010, f. 3-1-10, cert. ef. 4-1-10
