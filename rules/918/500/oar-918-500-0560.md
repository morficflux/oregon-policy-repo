## Full text

918-500-0560 Siting Without Insignia Insignias of compliance are not required to site a manufactured dwelling, unless the requirement is provided by the municipality.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.170

History: BCD 1-2010, f. 3-1-10, cert. ef. 4-1-10
