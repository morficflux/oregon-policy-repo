## Full text

918-500-0430 Field Technical Service Any person may request field technical service by submitting the request in writing to the division; however, the division may deny the request if budget or staffing levels are not sufficient to provide the service.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.176 & 446.230 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.230
