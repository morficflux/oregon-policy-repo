## Full text

918-500-0340 Production Discontinuance When a manufactured dwelling manufacturer discontinues production, the manufacturer must return all unused insignia of compliance to the division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.176 & 446.230 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.230
