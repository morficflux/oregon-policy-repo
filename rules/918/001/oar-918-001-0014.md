## Full text

918-001-0014 Written Exceptions Written exceptions to proposed orders must be received by the Division or appropriate advisory board within 20 calendar days after the service date of the proposed order. Written exceptions shall be sent to: Building Codes Division, Enforcement and Licensing, PO Box 14470, Salem, Oregon 97309-0404.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
