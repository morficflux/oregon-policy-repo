## Full text

918-001-0010 Model Rules of Procedure The Director adopts by reference the Attorney General's Model Rules for rulemaking, OAR 137-001-0005 through 137-001-0100, effective January 1, 2012. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Building Codes Division.]
