## Full text

918-001-0240 Refunds

(1) As required by ORS 293.445 moneys received in excess of the amount legally due and payable to the division, or in which the division determines it has no legal interest, shall be refunded. However, as authorized by 293.445, for sums of $25.00 or less the division will only refund the money upon request by the person who paid the money or the person’s legal representative. Refund requests must be made within three years of the date that the money was paid to the division.

(2) “Amounts legally due and payable” includes but is not limited to, fees for license, renewal license, permit or plan review applications, regardless of whether or not application review results in the issuance of a license or permit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 293.445 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 293.445
