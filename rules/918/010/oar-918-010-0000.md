## Full text

918-010-0000 Purpose and Scope of Rule This rule carries out the division’s responsibilities under ORS 453.879 and 453.906. It is intended for use in connection with OAR 333, division 040, rules of the Health Division relating to cleanup of toxic contamination from illegal drug manufacturing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 453.864 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 453.864
