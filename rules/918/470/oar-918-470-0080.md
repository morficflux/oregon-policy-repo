## Full text

918-470-0080 Appeals

(1) Appeals from a decision of a person acting under authority of the division shall be heard as a contested case under the provisions of ORS 183.413 through 183.464.

(2) Appeals from an order of an authority having jurisdiction shall be heard by the local appeals board and any appeal from a decision of a local appeals board shall proceed under the provisions of ORS 455.690.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 455.020 & 455.449 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 455.449
