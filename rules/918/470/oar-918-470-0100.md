## Full text

918-470-0100 Staying of Order Under Appeal Except for vacation orders according to OAR 918-470-0070, enforcement of any order issued under these rules shall be stayed while the appeal is pending.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 401.539 & 455.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 401.539
