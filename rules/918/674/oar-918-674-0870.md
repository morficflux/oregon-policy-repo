## Full text

918-674-0870 Prefabricated Third-Party Certification Renewal

(1) A prefabricated third-party agency must renew its prefabricated third-party certification by April 1, 2024, and every three years thereafter.

(2) Renewal notices are mailed to the mailing address on file with the division approximately 60 days from the expiration date. Renewal notices must be received by the division prior to the expiration date along with the renewal fee specified OAR 918-674-0730.

(3) When a prefabricated third-party agency certification expires as a result of failure to renew with the division, the prefabricated third-party agency may not accept any new plan submittals or plan revisions, perform any plan reviews or inspections, or apply for or issue any Oregon insignia of compliance.
