# Bungee (OAR 918-200-0100)

## Full text

918-200-0100 Bungee Jumping Except for jumps from towers or devices specifically engineered and designed for reverse jumping or catapulting, “bungee catapulting,” “negative jumping,” “reverse jumping,” “launching,” “tandem, double or multiple jumping,” or “sandbagging” shall not be permitted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 460.355 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 460.355
