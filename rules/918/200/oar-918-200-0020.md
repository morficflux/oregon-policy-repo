# Uniformity (OAR 918-200-0020)

## At a glance

## Full text

918-200-0020 Uniformity This code shall be applicable and uniform throughout this state and in all municipalities. No municipality shall enact or enforce any ordinance, rule or regulation relating to the same matters encompassed by these rules unless authorized by the Administrator.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 460.355 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 460.355
