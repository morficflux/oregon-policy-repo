## Full text

918-200-0040 Authority of Inspector An inspector not employed by the Division may inspect only amusement rides and devices insured or to be insured by the inspector’s employer or principle.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 460.355 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 460.355
