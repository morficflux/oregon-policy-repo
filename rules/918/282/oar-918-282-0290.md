## Full text

918-282-0290 Elevator Apprenticeship Program The National Elevator Industry Educational Program (NEIEP) approved by the Elevator Safety Board, now the Electrical and Elevator Board, is approved as an elevator apprenticeship program referred to in ORS 479.630 as a prerequisite for a limited journeyman elevator license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 460.085 & 479.730 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 460.085
