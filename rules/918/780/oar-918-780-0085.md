# License (OAR 918-780-0085)

## At a glance

OAR 918-780-0085 — License. Chapter 918, Division 780. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

918-780-0085 License Required Whenever any plumbing installation is being made by an unlicensed person contrary to the provisions of the Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code or OAR chapter 918, division 695, the building official must stop the installation through written notice. The building official may serve the notice on any person engaged in such an installation, or by posting a copy of the written notice at the site of the installation. No person may proceed with the installation until authorized by the building official. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 447.010 & 447.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 447.010 & 447.020
