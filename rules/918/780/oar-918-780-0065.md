# Permits Required (OAR 918-780-0065)

## Full text

918-780-0065 Permits Required No person, firm, or corporation shall do plumbing or medical gas systems work in the State of Oregon without first obtaining a plumbing permit or minor label when required and paying the appropriate fees to the authorized permit issuing agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 447.010 & 447.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 447.010 & 447.020
