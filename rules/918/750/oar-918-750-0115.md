## Full text

918-750-0115 Amendments to the Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code The Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code is amended pursuant to OAR chapter 918, division 8. Amendments adopted during the code-cycle for inclusion into the Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code are placed in this rule, showing the section reference, a descriptive caption, and a short description of the amendment. [Publications: Publications are available for review at the division.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 447.020, ORS 455.020, ORS 455.030 & ORS 455.110 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 447.020, ORS 455.020, ORS 455.030 & ORS 455.110
