## Full text

918-090-0100 General Business Registration Requirements A registration as a plan review and inspection business shall be held by any person who engages in or follows the business or occupation of, or advertises or purports to engage in or perform plan reviews or inspections other than as an exclusive employee of a municipality, the division or a registered plan review and inspection business. Individuals who have a limited license to perform specialty code inspections or plan reviews who work under the authority of a designated state certified building official employed by a municipality or the division are not required to hold a business registration.
