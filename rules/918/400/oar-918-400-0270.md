## Full text

918-400-0270 Definitions, Cross Reference

(1) Statutory definitions for the elevator laws are in ORS 460.005.

(2) Electrical definitions are in ORS 479.530.

(3) Code definitions are in Section 1.3 of the adopted Oregon Elevator Specialty Code (ASME A17.1).
