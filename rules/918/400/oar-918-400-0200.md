# Notice of Rule Making (OAR 918-400-0200)

## At a glance

## Full text

918-400-0200 Notice of Rule Making The following persons shall be provided all elevator-related rulemaking notices at no charge:

(1) All active Oregon licensed elevator contractors;

(2) All Oregon state elevator inspectors; and

(3) International Union of Elevator Constructors, Locals 19 (Seattle), 23 (Portland) and 38 (Salt Lake City).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.335
