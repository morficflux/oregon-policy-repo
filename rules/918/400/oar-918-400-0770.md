## Full text

918-400-0770 Withdrawal of Exceptions

(1) Exceptions granted under ORS 460.085(3) can be withdrawn if reasonable safety can no longer be maintained: (a) Because of changes of facts, use or code requirements; or (b) If the elevator is placed out of service for longer than one year.

(2) The elevator owner or operator shall be notified of an intent to withdraw an exception and of a right to a contested case hearing upon request.
