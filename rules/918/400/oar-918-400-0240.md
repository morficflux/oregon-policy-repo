## Full text

918-400-0240 Appeals under ORS 460.155 When an appeal is filed under ORS 460.155, formal contested case proceedings will be provided to the appellant.

(1) A hearings officer will hear the appeal and provide a report and recommendations to the board and the administrator.

(2) The board considers the report and exceptions, if any are filed, and provides recommendations to the administrator.

(3) The administrator enters a final order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 460.155 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 460.155
