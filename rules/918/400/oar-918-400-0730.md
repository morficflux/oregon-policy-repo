## Full text

918-400-0730 Reclassification of Elevators Elevators shall be reclassified only if the elevator meets, exceeds or is altered to at least the current requirements for the new classification and is so used.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 460.085 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 460.085
