918-400-0458 Amendments to the Oregon Elevator Specialty Code

(1) The Oregon Elevator Specialty Code, adopted at OAR 918-400-0455, is amended pursuant to OAR chapter 918, division 8. Amendments to the Oregon Elevator Specialty Code are provided in this rule, along with the title of the amended code and a descriptive caption of the amendment.

(2) Effective January 1, 2024, the Safety Code for Elevators and Escalators, ASME A17.1 2019, is amended by the adoption of Oregon specific amendments. The Oregon specific amendments are published in their entirety at Table 2-L.
