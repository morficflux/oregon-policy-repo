## Full text

918-515-0400 Limited Installer License Minimum Requirements To be licensed as a manufactured dwelling limited installer, an applicant shall:

(1) Provide personal identification;

(2) Submit to the Division a completed application, together with the required fees; and

(3) Attend a Division-approved manufactured dwelling installation education program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.176, 446.400 & 446.405 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.395
