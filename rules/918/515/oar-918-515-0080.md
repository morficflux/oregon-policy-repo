# Revocation or (OAR 918-515-0080)

## At a glance

## Full text

918-515-0080 Revocation or Suspension of Provider Approval Approval as an educational provider may be suspended or revoked for misuse of a Division examination, providing false records or information to the Division, helping or allowing a person to cheat or failure to comply with the requirements of ORS Chapter 446, these rules or any other applicable State laws.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.176, 446.400 & 446.405 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.395
