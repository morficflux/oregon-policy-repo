## Full text

918-515-0005 General

(1) Any person who engages in the business of installing manufactured dwellings, cabanas, skirting, tie-downs, earthquake-resistant bracing systems (ERBs); who directs, supervises or controls installations or performs repairs to an existing installation shall have an appropriate, valid Oregon manufactured dwelling installer’s license issued by the Division.

(2) Persons who have had a license or approval issued by this Division suspended or revoked, may not apply for approval as an educational provider, or a licensed installer, a limited installer or a limited skirting installer within one year of the date of suspension or revocation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.176, 446.400 & 446.405 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.395
