## Full text

918-515-0480 Limited Skirting Installer License Requirements To be licensed as a limited skirting installer, an applicant must:

(1) Provide personal identification;

(2) Submit a completed application, together with the fee required in OAR 918-500-0100; and

(3) Attend a division-approved manufactured dwelling installation education program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.325 - 410, 446.400, 446.405, 455.130 & 455.610 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.400 & 446.405
