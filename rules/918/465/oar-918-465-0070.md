## Full text

918-465-0070 Fees for Plan Review and Permits Fees for plan review and permits issued by the Division for construction, alteration and repair of prefabricated structures and of buildings and other structures as established by these rules and authorized by ORS 455.210, shall be determined in accordance with 918-460-0030, 918-440-0050, 918-480-0020 and 918-309-0030 through 918-309-0070 as appropriate.
