## Full text

332-010-0002 Election The chairperson of the Board must be elected annually. Elections must be held during the last regularly scheduled Board meeting of the year or if canceled the next regularly scheduled meeting. Terms of office run from January 1st to December 31st.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.615 & 687.475 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 687.425, 687.475 & 687.493
