# Education (OAR 332-015-0040)

## At a glance

## Full text

332-015-0040 Education Applicant’s education must incorporate the general educational requirements listed in the NARM CPM candidate information bulletin, including:

(1) Core competencies developed by MANA;

(2) NARM written test specifications;

(3) NARM skills assessment test specifications;

(4) NARM written examination primary reference list; and

(5) NARM skills assessment reference list.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 687.420, 687.480 & 687.485 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183, 687.420, 687.480 & 687.485
