## Full text

332-015-0050 NARM Midwifery Examination The qualifying examination is the NARM examination. An applicant is responsible for payment of all fees for NARM applications, examinations, and any other fees paid directly to NARM.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.615, 687.480 & 687.485 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.615, 687.480 & 687.485
