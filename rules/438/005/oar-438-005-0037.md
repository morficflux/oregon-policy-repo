## Full text

438-005-0037 Penalty for Failure to Comply with Rules If failure to comply with these rules by an insurer or self-insured employer results in delay in compensation ultimately ordered paid to a claimant by an Administrative Law Judge, an additional amount may be assessed on such compensation for unreasonable delay under the provisions of ORS 656.262(11).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.307, 656.388, 656.593 & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.262(10)
