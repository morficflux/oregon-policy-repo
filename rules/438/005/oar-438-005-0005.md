## Full text

438-005-0005 Statutory Authority These rules are adopted under the Board's general rulemaking authority of ORS 656.726(5) to provide rules of practice and procedure for hearing and review proceedings under 656.001 to 656.990, for exercising its continuing authority under 656.278 and providing for the payment of attorney fees in cases under the Workers' Compensation Law pursuant to 656.388.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.726(5)
