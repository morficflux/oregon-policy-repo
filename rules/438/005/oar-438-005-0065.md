## Full text

438-005-0065 Manner of Giving Notice Notice of denial or other notice from which statutory time runs against a claimant shall be in writing and shall be delivered by registered or certified mail with return receipt requested or by personal service meeting the requirements for service of a summons.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.307, 656.388, 656.543 & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.262(6)
