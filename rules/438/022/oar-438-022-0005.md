## Full text

438-022-0005 Adoption of Attorney General’s Model Rules To the extent that the following rules are applicable to the Workers’ Compensation Law (ORS chapter 656), the Board adopts by reference OAR 137-001-0005 through 137-001-0100 (Attorney General’s Model Rules for Rulemaking), as adopted by the Department of Justice effective January 1, 2008. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Model Rules is available from the Department of Justice, the Workers’ Compensation Board, or the Oregon State Archives website at https://secure.sos.state.or.us/oard/displayDivisionRules.action?selectedDivision=281.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(5) & ORS 654.025(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341(4)
