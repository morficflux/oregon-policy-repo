## Full text

438-010-0010 Effective Date for Applying Disability Standards In applying the disability rating standards at hearing and on review, the Administrative Law Judge and the Board shall apply the disability rating standards as required by OAR 436-035-0003.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.283(7) & 656.295(5)
