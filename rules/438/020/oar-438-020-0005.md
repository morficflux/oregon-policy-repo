## Full text

438-020-0005 ALJ Designation Pursuant to ORS 45.275(1), an Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) is empowered to appoint qualified interpreters in hearings over which they preside. The ALJ may designate the Hearings Division's Interpreter Services Coordinator (ISC) or another designee to appoint the interpreter.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(5) & 183.310 - 183.400 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.726(5), 45.273, 45.275, 45.285 & 45.288
