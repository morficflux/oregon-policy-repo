## Full text

438-020-0020 Cancellation When a party or a party's attorney becomes aware that interpreter services are no longer needed, the attorney for the party, or the unrepresented party, shall immediately notify the assigned ALJ.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(5) & 183.310 - 183.400 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.726(5), 45.273, 45.275, 45.285 & 45.288
