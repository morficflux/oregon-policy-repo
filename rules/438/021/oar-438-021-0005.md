## Full text

438-021-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish a standard procedure for regular open meetings of the Workers’ Compensation Board. With the exception of its review of contested cases and any other confidential or privileged matter, it is the specific intent of the Board to make the public aware of its deliberations, decisions, and the information upon which such decisions are made.
