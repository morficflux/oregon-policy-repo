## Full text

438-007-0024 Offers of Proof Whenever the Administrative Law Judge excludes a document or the testimony of a witness, the party adversely affected may make an offer of proof for the record in a form determined by the Administrative Law Judge.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.726(5)
