## Full text

438-012-0016 Communication with Board and Parties in Own Motion Cases A copy of any document in an Own Motion proceeding, including correspondence, directed to the Board or to a party in the claim shall be simultaneously mailed or delivered to all other parties involved in the claim or, if a party is currently represented by an attorney, to the party's attorney.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.278(1) & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.278(1) & 656.726(4)
