## Full text

438-012-0062 Referral of Request for Enforcement of Board's Own Motion Order to Hearings Division

(1) The Board may refer a request to enforce an Own Motion order to the Hearings Division for an evidentiary hearing and recommended findings of fact and conclusions.

(2) The Board shall issue its order within a reasonable time after receipt of all evidence and argument from the parties and any recommendations from the Hearings Division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: 656.278(1) & 656.726(5)
