## Full text

438-012-0031 Notification of Pending Proceedings Parties to an Own Motion proceeding shall notify the Board of any pending proceeding involving a contested case under ORS 656.283 through 656.298, 656.307, or 656.308, an arbitration or mediation proceeding under ORS 656.307, a managed care dispute resolution review process, or a Director's medical review under ORS 656.245, 656.260, or 656.327. The parties shall also specify the issues raised in that proceeding.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.278(1) & 656.726(5)
