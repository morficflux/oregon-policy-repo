## Full text

438-085-0616 Withdrawal by OR-OSHA OR-OSHA may withdraw its contested citation, notice or order at any time before an order issued by an Administrative Law Judge becomes final. Upon such withdrawal, the Administrative Law Judge shall issue an order vacating the citation, notice or order and dismissing the request for hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 654.025(2) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.025 & 654.078
