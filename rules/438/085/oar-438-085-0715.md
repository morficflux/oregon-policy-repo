# Place of Hearing (OAR 438-085-0715)

## Full text

438-085-0715 Place of Hearing The hearing shall be held in Oregon at a place that is reasonably convenient for the party requesting the hearing and affected employees, or at such other place as may be selected by the Administrative Law Judge.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 & 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.025 & 654.078
