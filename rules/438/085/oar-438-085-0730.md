## Full text

438-085-0730 Consolidation or Separation If no substantial right of any party will be prejudiced, the Administrative Law Judge may:

(1) Consolidate cases in which there are common parties and common questions of law or fact; or

(2) Divide the issues of a case into separate cases.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 & 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.025 & 654.078
