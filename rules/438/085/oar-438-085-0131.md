## Full text

438-085-0131 Computing Time Periods Time periods required or allowed by the Act or these rules shall be computed in calendar days. The first full day after the time begins to run is counted as the first day. If the last day is a Saturday, Sunday, or State holiday, the period runs until the end of the next business day.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 & 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.025(2)
