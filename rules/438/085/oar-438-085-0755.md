## Full text

438-085-0755 Extensions of Time Requests for extensions of time for filing of any documents shall be received in the office of the assigned Administrative Law Judge in advance of the day on which the document is due to be filed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 & 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 654.025 & 654.078
