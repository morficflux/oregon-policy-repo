## Full text

438-009-0023 Notice to Claimant Required Each claimant shall, at the time of execution of a Claim Disposition Agreement, be given a written notice, separate from the agreement, in the form prescribed by the Board pursuant to OAR 438-009-0022.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.236
