## Full text

438-006-0037 Waiver of Notice A party may waive objection to lack of notice, or a defect in the form of notice, of any issue raised at a hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.307, 656.388, 656.593 & 656.726(4)
