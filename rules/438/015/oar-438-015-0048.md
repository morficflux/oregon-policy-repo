## Full text

438-015-0048 Attorney Fees When a Claimant Requests a Hearing on a Claim Reclassification If a claimant requests a hearing regarding a claim reclassification order from the Workers’ Compensation Division, and the Administrative Law Judge finally determines that the claim should be classified as disabling, the Administrative Law Judge may award a reasonable assessed fee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.386(3)

History: WCB 1-2015, f. 12-16-15, cert. ef. 1-1-16
