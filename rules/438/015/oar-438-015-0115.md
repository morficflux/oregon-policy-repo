## Full text

438-015-0115 Fees for Attorneys Representing Insurers and Self-Insured Employers A requested client paid fee shall:

(1) Be reasonable, considering the factors prescribed in OAR 438-015-0010(4), to the extent such factors are relevant to a client-paid fee; and

(2) Not exceed that agreed upon in the retainer agreement.
