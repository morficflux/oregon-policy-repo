## Full text

438-015-0038 Attorney Fees When a Claimant Requests a Hearing on a Responsibility Denial If the claimant’s attorney appears in any proceeding regarding a responsibility denial issued under ORS 656.308(2), and actively and meaningfully participates, and finally prevails against that responsibility denial, the Administrative Law Judge shall award a reasonable assessed fee to be paid by the insurer or self-insured employer who issued the responsibility denial. Absent a showing of extraordinary circumstances, the assessed attorney fee shall not exceed $2,500. The maximum attorney fee awarded under this rule is subject to an annual adjustment on July 1 as calculated by the Workers’ Compensation Division (on behalf of the Director) by the same percentage increase as made to the average weekly wage defined in ORS 656.211, if any. Before July 1 of each year, the Board, by bulletin, will publish the maximum fee, after adjusting the fee by the same percentage increase, if any, to the average weekly wage. Dollar amounts will be rounded to the nearest whole number.
