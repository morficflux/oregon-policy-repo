## Full text

819-005-0015 ELECTION OF CHAIRPERSON AND VICE CHAIRPERSON The chairperson and vice chairperson may be elected at the request of the Board. The elected Board chairperson and vice chairperson may serve continuously until the Board elects another chairperson and vice chairperson.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.615, ORS 676.650 & ORS 676.655 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.650 & ORS 676.655
