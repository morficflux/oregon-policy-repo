## Full text

737-025-0000 Purpose of the Rules ORS 184.740 establishes the Safe Routes to School Fund to assist communities in identifying and reducing barriers and hazards to children walking or bicycling to and from school. The purpose of the rules in division 25 is to establish the criteria used in awarding grants from the Safe Routes to School Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 184.740, 184.741 & 184.742 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 184.741 & 184.742
