## Full text

737-025-0040 Eligible Projects and Activities for Education Grants Eligible Projects and Activities for Education Grants

(1) Grants awarded through the Safe Routes to School Education Program may be used for projects or activities in: (a) Education; (b) Enforcement; (c) Safe Routes to School Infrastructure Plan Development; or (d) Any combination of the above.

(2) All projects and activities must directly benefit a qualifying school.

(3) All projects and activities must meet the eligibility criteria applicable and match requirements to the specific funds being dispensed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 184.740, 184.741 & 184.742 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 184.741 & 184.742
