# Purpose (OAR 731-012-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

731-012-0010 Purpose This division implements ORS 366.215. The purpose of this division is to define terms, identify a review process and facilitate communication and development of consensus during this review process.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 366.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 366.215
