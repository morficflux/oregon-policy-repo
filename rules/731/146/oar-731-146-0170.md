## Full text

731-146-0170 Late Offers, Late Withdrawals and Late Modifications Any Offer received after Closing is late. An Offeror's request for withdrawal or modification of an Offer received after Closing is late. ODOT shall not consider late Offers. ODOT shall not consider withdrawals or modifications except as permitted in OAR 731-146-0180 or 137-047-0261.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 279A.065 & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.050(3), 279A.065(6) & 279A.070
