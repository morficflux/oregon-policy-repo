## Full text

731-146-0130 Fragmentation A procurement may not be artificially divided or fragmented for the purpose of restricting competition or circumventing a legal requirement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 279A.065(6)(a) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070
