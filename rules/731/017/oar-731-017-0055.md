# Review and Update (OAR 731-017-0055)

## At a glance

## Full text

731-017-0055 Review and Update Beginning two years following the adoption of this division, the Commission shall commence a review to evaluate implementation of and consider possible modifications to this division. This evaluation shall include considerations of the cumulative effects from applications that have been approved.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 367.850 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 367.850
