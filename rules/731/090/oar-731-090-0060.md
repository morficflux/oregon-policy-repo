## Full text

731-090-0060 RUC Payer: Fraud RUC Payers who are suspected of tampering with their Mileage Reporting Device will be subjected to a non-compliance investigation by the Department, and depending on the results of the investigation, may be subject to a penalty of 10 percent of delinquent payments in addition to actual damages and a Class A traffic violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 319.883 - 319.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 319.915

History: DOT 1-2015, f. 4-21-15, cert. ef. 7-1-15
