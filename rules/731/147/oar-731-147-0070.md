# Mandatory (OAR 731-147-0070)

## At a glance

## Full text

731-147-0070 Mandatory Use Contracts For Contracts entered into pursuant to ORS 279A.050(3), ODOT is exempt from Mandatory Use Contracts entered into pursuant to OAR 125-247-0296 when purchasing Goods or Services through federal programs pursuant to ORS 279A.180.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 279A.065(6) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.050(3) & 279A.180

History: DOT 3-2020, adopt filed 07/17/2020, effective 07/17/2020
