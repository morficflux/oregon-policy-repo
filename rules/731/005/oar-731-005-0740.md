## Full text

731-005-0740 Documentation of Award

(1) Basis of Award. After award, ODOT shall make a record showing the basis for determining the successful Offeror part of ODOT's solicitation file.

(2) Contents of Award Record. ODOT's record shall include: (a) Bids. (b) Completed Bid tabulation sheet; and (c) Written justification for any rejection of lower Bids.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 279A.050 & 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279C.430
