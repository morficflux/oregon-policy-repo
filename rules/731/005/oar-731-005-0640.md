## Full text

731-005-0640 Extension of Time for Acceptance of Offer ODOT may request, orally or in Writing, that Offerors extend, in Writing, the time during which ODOT may consider their Offer(s). If an Offeror agrees to such extension, the Offer shall continue as a Firm Offer, irrevocable, valid and binding on the Offeror for the agreed-upon extension period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 279A.050 & 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279C.375

History: DOT 2-2005, f. 2-16-05, cert. ef. 3-1-05
