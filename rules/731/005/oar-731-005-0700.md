# Negotiation with (OAR 731-005-0700)

## At a glance

## Full text

731-005-0700 Negotiation with Bidders ODOT shall not negotiate with any Bidder prior to award of Contract. After award of the Contract, ODOT and Contractor may only modify the Contract as specified in the Contract.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 279A.050 & 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279C.300, 279C.305, 279C.335, 279C.365 & 279C.375
