## Full text

731-030-0010 Purpose of the Rules OAR 731-030-0010 to 731-030-0170 establish the procedures and requirements for the administration of the Oregon Transportation Infrastructure Fund and for the creation and operation of the Oregon Transportation Infrastructure Bank.
