731-030-0020 Statutory Authority Oregon Revised Statutes (ORS) 367.010 to 367.060 provide authority for the Department to operate the Oregon Transportation Infrastructure Fund.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 367.015 & 367.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 367.010 - 367.060 & US Code, PL 104-59 & Sect. 350
