## Full text

731-070-0220 Legal Sufficiency Review of Final Agreement On completion of a final agreement, the Attorney General will review it for legal sufficiency under ORS 291.047 and OAR chapter 137, division 045. When conducting that review, the Attorney General shall:

(1) Recognize that the agreement is the product of a partnership; and

(2) Defer to the business judgment of the Department and the Oregon Transportation Commission concerning the assignment of risks and the incentives provided within the agreement.
