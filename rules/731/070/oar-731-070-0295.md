## Full text

731-070-0295 Consultation with Local Government, Metropolitan Planning Organization or Area Commission on Transportation As part of its evaluation of a proposal submitted under these rules, ODOT will consult with appropriate local governments, metropolitan planning organizations and area commissions on transportation. Consultation under this rule will occur in such manner and at such time as ODOT considers appropriate in the particular circumstance.
