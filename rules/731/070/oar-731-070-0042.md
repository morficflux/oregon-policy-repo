## Full text

731-070-0042 Solicitation of Proposals for OIPP Projects ODOT may solicit proposals or enter into negotiations with one or more legal entities, as described in OAR 731-070-0044, to develop a public-private partnership approach to planning, acquiring, financing, developing, designing, managing, constructing, reconstructing, replacing, improving, maintaining, repairing, leasing or operating a Transportation Project.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 367.824 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 367.800 - 367.824
