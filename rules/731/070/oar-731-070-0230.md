## Full text

731-070-0230 Commission Review of Final Agreement On completion of the Attorney General’s legal sufficiency review of the final agreement, the Commission shall:

(1) Approve the final agreement;

(2) Reject the final agreement; or

(3) Return the final agreement to the working group for further negotiation on issues the Commission specifies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 367.824 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 367.800 - 367.824
