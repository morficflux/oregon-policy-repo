## Full text

731-015-0125 Referral of Compatibility Disputes to the Land Conservation and Development Commission If a compatibility conflict persists after pursuing the compatibility procedures listed in OAR 731-015-0045 through 731-015-0115, the Department shall request that the Land Conservation and Development Commission make a compatibility determination in accordance with OAR 660-030-0070(7) through (12).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 197 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.180
