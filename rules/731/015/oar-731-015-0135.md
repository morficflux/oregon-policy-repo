## Full text

731-015-0135 Statewide Goal Compliance and Acknowledged Plan Com-paibility for New or Amended Rules and Programs Significantly Affecting Land Use

(1) The Commission and Department shall follow the procedures in OAR 660-030-0075 to assure that new or amended rules and programs comply with the requirements of ORS 197.180 and OAR chapter 660, division 30.

(2) The Department shall determine whether new or amended rules and programs affect land use pursuant to OAR 660-030-0005(2) and 731-015-0035.

(3) This section shall not apply to the adoption of temporary rules or programs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184 & 197 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.180
