## Full text

340-245-9000 Colored Art Glass Manufacturing Facility Rules; Applicability and Jurisdiction Notwithstanding OAR chapter 340, division 246, OAR 340-245-9000 through 340-245-9080 apply to all facilities in the state of Oregon that:

(1) Manufacture glass from raw materials, or a combination of raw materials and cullet, for: (a) Use in art, architecture, interior design and other similar decorative applications, or (b) Use by glass manufacturers for use in art, architecture, interior design and other similar decorative applications; and

(2) Manufacture 5 tons per year or more of glass using raw materials that contain glassmaking HAPs.

(3) Subject to the requirements in this division and OAR 340-200-0010(3), LRAPA is designated by the EQC to implement OAR 340-245-9000 through 9095 within its area of jurisdiction. NOTE: This rule was moved verbatim from OAR 340-244-9000 and renumbered and amended.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468A.025 & 468A.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: 468A.025 & 468A.040
