# Purpose (OAR 340-082-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 340-082-0005 — Purpose. Chapter 340, Division 082. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-082-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to prescribe requirements and procedures for obtaining state financial assistance for planning and construction of pollution control facilities for the disposal of solid waste pursuant to Article XI-H of the Oregon Constitution, and to provide for pass-through of federal funds to designated agencies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.195 - 468.272
