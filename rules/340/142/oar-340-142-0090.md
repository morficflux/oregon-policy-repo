## Full text

340-142-0090 Cleanup Report The Department may require the responsible person to submit a written report to the Department describing all aspects of the spill and cleanup, and steps taken to prevent a recurrence.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 466 & 468B Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.610, 466.625, 466.645 & 468B.305 - 315
