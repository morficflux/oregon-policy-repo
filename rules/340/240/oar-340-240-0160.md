## Full text

340-240-0160 Medford-Ashland Air Quality Maintenance Area and Grants Pass Urban Growth Area: Wigwam Waste Burners No person owning or controlling any wigwam waste burner is allowed to cause or permit the operation of the wigwam waste burner. NOTE: These rules are included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
