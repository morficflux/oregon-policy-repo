# Emission Limitations (OAR 340-240-0020)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-240-0020 Emission Limitations Emission limitations established herein and stated in terms of pounds per 1,000 square feet of production are to be computed on an hourly basis using the maximum 8 hour production capacity of the plant. NOTE: These rules are included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468A.025 & 468A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025 & 468A.070
