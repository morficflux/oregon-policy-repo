## Full text

340-240-0440 Lakeview Urban Growth Area: Open Burning No open burning of domestic waste is allowed to be initiated on any day or at any time when the local air stagnation advisory forecasts adverse meteorological or air quality conditions. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468A.025 & 468A.085 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025 & 468A.085
