340-240-0300 La Grande Urban Growth Area: Applicability OAR 340-240-0300 through 340-240-0360 apply in the La Grande Urban Growth Area. NOTE: These rules are included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.
