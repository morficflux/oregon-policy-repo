## Full text

340-240-0570 Klamath Falls Nonattainment Area Contingency Measures: Applicability OAR 340-240-0570 through 340-240-0630 apply to the Klamath Falls Nonattainment Area for PM2.5 should the area not achieve attainment by the applicable attainment date established pursuant to 42 U.S.C. 7502(a)(2).
