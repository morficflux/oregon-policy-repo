340-240-0620 Klamath Falls Nonattainment Area Contingency Measures: Contingency Measures: New Industrial Sources New industrial sources must comply with OAR 340-240-0570 through 340-240-0610 immediately upon receiving an Air Contaminant Discharge Permit or an Oregon Title V Operating Permit.
