## Full text

340-204-0030 Designation of Nonattainment Areas There are not currently any areas designated as Nonattainment Areas in the State of Oregon. [NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025 & 468A.035
