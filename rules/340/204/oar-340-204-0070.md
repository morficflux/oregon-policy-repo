## Full text

340-204-0070 Special Control Areas The following areas are designated as Special Control Areas:

(1) The counties within the Willamette Valley, including Benton, Clackamas, Columbia, Lane, Linn, Marion, Multnomah, Polk, Washington and Yamhill Counties;

(2) Umpqua Basin;

(3) Rogue Basin;

(4) Within incorporated cities having a population of 4,000 or more, and within three miles of the corporate limits of any such city. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
