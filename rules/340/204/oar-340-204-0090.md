## Full text

340-204-0090 Oxygenated Gasoline Control Areas The EQC may adopt or amend a CO maintenance plan that includes contingency plan provisions that require use of oxygenated fuel. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.035 & 468A.420
