## Full text

340-178-0080 Appeal Procedures An applicant may appeal determinations by the Department under these rules, in accordance with OAR 340-172-0110, by sending a written request for a formal contested case hearing within 20 days after the Department issues a reduced interest rate certificate or denies a reduced interest rate application.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1991, Ch. 863, Sec. 6 & 6a
