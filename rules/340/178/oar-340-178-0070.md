## Full text

340-178-0070 Notice of Default or Termination Any commercial lending institution administering a reduced interest rate loan under this Division shall notify the Department in writing within 30 days after loan default or termination.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1991, Ch. 863, Sec. 6 & 6a
