## Full text

340-228-0600 Utility Mercury Rule, General Provisions: Purpose This rule establishes the mandatory reduction levels and monitoring provisions for the Utility Mercury Rule, as a means of reducing mercury (Hg) emissions in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
