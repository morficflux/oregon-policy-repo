## Full text

340-228-0100 Sulfur Content of Fuels: Residual Fuel Oils No person may sell, distribute, use, or make available for use, any residual fuel oil containing more than 1.75 percent sulfur by weight. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan as adopted by the Environmental Quality Commission under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
