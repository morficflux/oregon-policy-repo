## Full text

340-228-0110 Sulfur Content of Fuels: Distillate Fuel Oils No person shall sell, distribute, use, or make available for use, any distillate fuel oil containing more than the following percentages of sulfur:

(1) ASTM Grade 1 fuel oil — 0.3 percent by weight.

(2) ASTM Grade 2 fuel oil — 0.5 percent by weight. [NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.] [NOTE: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
