## Full text

340-081-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to prescribe procedures and requirements for obtaining state financial assistance for the construction of water pollution control facilities pursuant to Article XI-H of the Oregon Constitution and ORS 468.195 et seq.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.423 - 468.440
