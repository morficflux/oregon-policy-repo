## Full text

340-011-0370 Public Records Access and Reproduction: Certification of Copies of Records Certification of both hard and electronic copies of records will be provided. DEQ will only certify that on the date copied, the copy was a true and correct copy of the original record. DEQ cannot certify as to any subsequent changes or manipulation of the record.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.410 - 192.505 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.440
