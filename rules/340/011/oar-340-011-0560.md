## Full text

340-011-0560 Contested Cases: Public Attendance at Contested Case Hearing An administrative law judge may close a contested case hearing to the public upon the request of a participant in the contested case hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
