## Full text

340-011-0061 Rulemaking: Declaratory Ruling: Institution of Proceedings, Consideration of Petition and Disposition of Petition The declaratory ruling process shall be governed by the Attorney General's Uniform Rules of Procedure, OAR 137-002-0010 through 137-002-0060. As used in those rules, the terms "agency," "governing body, and "decision maker" generally should be interpreted to mean "Commission." The term "agency" may also be interpreted to be the "DEQ" where context requires.
