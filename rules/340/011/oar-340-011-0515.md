## Full text

340-011-0515 Contested Cases: Authorized Representative of a Participant other than a Natural Person in a Contested Case Hearing A corporation, partnership, limited liability company, unincorporated association, trust and government body may be represented by either an attorney or an authorized representative in a contested case hearing before an administrative law judge or the commission to the extent allowed by OAR 137-003-0555.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.457
