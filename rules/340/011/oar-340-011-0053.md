## Full text

340-011-0053 Rulemaking: Periodic Rule Review Periodic review of agency rules shall be accomplished once every five years in accordance with ORS 183.405 and the Attorney General's Model Rule OAR 137-001-0100.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.405
