## Full text

340-011-0009 Rules of Practice and Procedure: Incorporation of Attorney General’s Uniform and Model Rules The following Attorney General’s Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure are adopted and incorporated into this Division, except as otherwise provided in this Chapter: OAR chapter 137, division 001 (excluding 137-001-0008 through 137-001-0009), OAR chapter 137, divisions 003 and 004, as in effect on January 1, 2006.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 183.341 & 183.452 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.020, 468.070, 468.090 - 0140, 183.341 & 183.452
