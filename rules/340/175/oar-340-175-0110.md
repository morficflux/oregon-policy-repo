# Appeal (OAR 340-175-0110)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-175-0110 Appeal Procedures An applicant may appeal determinations by the Department under these rules, in accordance with OAR 340-172-0110, by sending a written request for a formal contested case hearing within 20 days after the Department awards or denies financial assistance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1991, Ch. 863 & Sec. 7
