## Full text

340-175-0050 Additional Information Required on the Financial Assistance Application In addition to the requirements of OAR 340-172-0040, the financial assistance application shall include:

(1) Where the UST project work is partially or fully completed: (a) A description of the completed UST project work including the date the UST was placed into service; (b) Cost of completed UST project work; and (c) A description of all UST project work still to be done to meet new UST standards, including estimated cost and schedule of work.

(2) A signed agreement meeting the requirements of OAR 340-175-0055.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL 1993 & Ch. 661 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1991, Ch. 863 & Sec. 7

History: DEQ 8-1994, f. & cert. ef. 3-22-94 DEQ 14-1992, f. & cert. ef. 6-11-92 DEQ 29-1991(Temp), f. 12-18-91, cert. ef. 12-20-91
