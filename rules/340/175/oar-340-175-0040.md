## Full text

340-175-0040 Application for Grant Any person wishing to apply for a grant under these rules shall submit a written application in accordance with OAR 340, division 172.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1991, Ch. 863 & Sec. 7
