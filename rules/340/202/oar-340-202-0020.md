## Full text

340-202-0020 Applicability and Jurisdiction Subject to the requirements in this division and OAR 340-200-0010(3), LRAPA is designated by the EQC to implement the rules in this division within its area of jurisdiction.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468A.025 & 468A.035 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025, 468A.035 & 468A.135
