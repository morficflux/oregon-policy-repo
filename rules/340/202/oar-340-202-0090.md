## Full text

340-202-0090 Ambient Air Quality Standards: Ozone Concentrations of ozone in ambient air as measured by an approved method must not exceed 0.070 ppm as a daily maximum eight-hour average concentration. This standard is attained when, at any site the average of the annual fourth-highest daily maximum eight-hour average ozone concentration is equal to or less than 0.070 ppm as determined by the method of Appendix I, 40 C.F.R. 50.
