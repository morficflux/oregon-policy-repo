# Requirements for (OAR 340-124-0055)

## At a glance

OAR 340-124-0055 — Requirements for. Chapter 340, Division 124. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-124-0055 Requirements for Dry Stores Any dry cleaning machinery at a dry store location shall have dry cleaning solvent and solvent-containing residue removed from the dry cleaning machinery in accordance with 40 CFR 261.4(c) and the dry cleaning machine shall not be connected to any utilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 465.505(5) & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 465.505

History: DEQ 13-2002, f. & cert. ef. 10-9-02
