# Identification (OAR 340-103-0011)

## At a glance

OAR 340-103-0011 — Identification. Chapter 340, Division 103. Statutory authority and AON

## Full text

340-103-0011 Identification Number In addition to the requirements of 40 CFR 263.11, as a matter of policy, the Department will accept EPA identification numbers already assigned and use a modified EPA registration form and EPA’s identification numbering system (Dun and Bradstreet) for transporters who register in the future.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 466 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.020

History: DEQ 8-1985, f. & ef. 7-25-85
