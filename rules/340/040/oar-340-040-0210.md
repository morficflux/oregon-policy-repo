## Full text

340-040-0210 Wellhead Protection: Appeal Procedure Final Department decisions regarding certification, recertification, or decertification are not subject to contested case hearing rights but are reviewable under ORS 183.484.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468B Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468 & 468B
