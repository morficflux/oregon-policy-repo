## Full text

340-230-0100 Solid and Infectious Waste Incinerators: Best Available Control Technology

(1) Notwithstanding the specific emission limits set forth in OAR 340-230-0110, in order to maintain overall air quality at the highest possible levels, all solid waste facilities and infectious waste facilities are required to use Best Available Control Technology (BACT). In no event shall the application of BACT result in emissions of any air contaminant which would exceed the emission limits set forth in 340-230-0100 through 340-230-0150.

(2) All installed equipment shall be operated and maintained in such a manner that emissions of air contaminants are kept at lowest possible levels.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 468 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
