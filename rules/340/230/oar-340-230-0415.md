## Full text

340-230-0415 Adoption of Federal Plan by Reference The federal plan for hospital, medical, and infectious waste incineration units constructed on or before December 1, 2008, in 40 CFR Part 62 Subpart HHH, is by this reference adopted and incorporated herein.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
