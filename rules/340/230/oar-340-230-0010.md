# Purpose (OAR 340-230-0010)

## At a glance

OAR 340-230-0010 — Purpose. Chapter 340, Division 230. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-230-0010 Purpose The purpose of this division is to establish state of the art emission standards, design requirements, and performance standards for solid and infectious waste incinerators, hospital/medical/infectious waste incinerators, crematory incinerators, municipal waste combustors, and commercial and industrial solid waste incineration units in order to minimize air contaminant emissions and provide adequate protection of public health.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
