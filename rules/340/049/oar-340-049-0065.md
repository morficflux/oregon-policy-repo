340-049-0065 Fee Schedules for Wastewater System Operator Certification and Operator Certification Program Support

(1) Operator Certification Fee Schedule. Fees for new applications for certification, fees related to taking the exam, fees for evaluating reciprocity, fees for renewal of certification, and fees for reinstating certificates are found in Table B.
