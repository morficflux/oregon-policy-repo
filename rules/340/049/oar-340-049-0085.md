## Full text

340-049-0085 Advisory Committee

(1) The Department will maintain an Advisory Committee to: (a) Evaluate the effectiveness of the program; and (b) Recommend needs of the program.

(2) The Advisory Committee will convene at least twice a year at time and place set by the Department.

(3) The composition of the Committee will include, representatives of system owners, operators of small and large wastewater systems, industry associations, and the educational community.
