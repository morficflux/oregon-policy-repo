## Full text

340-218-0030 Definitions The definitions in OAR 340-200-0020, 340-204-0010, 340-245-0020, and this rule apply to this division. If the same term is defined in this rule and 340-200-0020, 340-204-0010 or 340-245-0020, the definition in this rule applies to this division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468A.025, 468A.040 & 468A.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A
