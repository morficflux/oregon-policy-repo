# Emergency (OAR 340-266-0110)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-266-0110 Emergency Burning Cessation Pursuant to ORS 468A.610 and upon finding of danger to public health or safety, the Commission may order temporary emergency cessation of all open field burning in any area of the Willamette Valley.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.610
