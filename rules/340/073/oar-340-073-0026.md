# Septic Tanks (OAR 340-073-0026)

## Full text

340-073-0026 Septic Tanks Septic tanks must be constructed of concrete, fiberglass, or other non-corrosive materials approved by DEQ.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 454.625 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 454.615
