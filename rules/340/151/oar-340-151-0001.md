# Purpose (OAR 340-151-0001)

## At a glance

OAR 340-151-0001 — Purpose. Chapter 340, Division 151. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-151-0001 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to protect public health, safety, welfare and the environment from the potential harmful effects of spills and releases of petroleum from USTs by requiring UST owners and permittees to maintain sufficient financial resources in the event that corrective action or compensation for bodily injury or property damage is required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.746 & 466.815 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.815

History: DEQ 6-2003, f. & cert. ef. 2-14-03
