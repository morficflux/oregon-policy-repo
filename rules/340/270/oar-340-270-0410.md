## Full text

340-270-0410 Vehicles Eligible for the Charge Ahead Program To qualify for a Charge Ahead rebate, a motor vehicle must be either a new eligible vehicle or a used electric vehicle with an electrochemical storage capacity and have a purchase or lease date on or after January 1, 2018, or a new or used plug-in hybrid electric vehicle and have a purchase or lease date on or after September 29, 2019. Vehicles receiving a Standard Rebate or rebated by the Zero Emission Rebates for Oregon Fleets program, as established in OAR 340-270-0030 and OAR 340-270-0600 through OAR 340-270-0700, are not eligible for a Charge Ahead rebate.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 2017 Or. Law Ch. 750 Sec. 148-157, House Bill 4059 (2018), Sec. 18-21 & House Bill 2592 (2019), Sec. 35-37 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2017 Or. Law Ch. 750 Sec. 148-157, House Bill 4059 (2018), Sec. 18-21 & House Bill 2592 (2019), Sec. 35-37
