## Full text

340-270-0500 Allocation of Rebate Funding DEQ will allocate at least 20 percent of available moneys deposited per biennium into the Zero-Emission Incentive Fund for rebates under the Charge Ahead Program. The amount required to be allocated for the Charge Ahead Program rebates from the Zero-Emission Incentive Fund shall be reduced, but not below zero, by the amount deposited from any other source in the Charge Ahead Zero-Emission Incentive Fund.
