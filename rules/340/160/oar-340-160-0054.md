## Full text

340-160-0054 Reciprocity with Other Jurisdictions The department may develop agreements with other jurisdictions for the purposes of establishing reciprocity in training, licensing, and certification if the department finds that the training, licensing and certification standards of the other jurisdictions are at least as stringent as those required by this division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.746 & 466.750 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.750
