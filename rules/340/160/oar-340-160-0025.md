## Full text

340-160-0025 Types of Licenses

(1) The department may issue the following types of licenses: (a) Service provider; (b) Installation supervisor; (c) Decommissioning supervisor; (d) UST testing supervisor; and (e) Cathodic protection system test supervisor.

(2) Licenses will be issued in accordance with the requirements of OAR 340-160-0030 for service providers and 340-160-0035 for supervisors.
