## Full text

340-160-0020 General Provisions

(1) A person may not perform or offer to perform UST services without first obtaining a license from DEQ.

(2) A service provider is prohibited from offering or performing UST services on an UST system defined by OAR 340-150-0010(84) (except as exempted or deferred by OAR 340-150-0008), unless the owner and permittee of the UST system have been issued a general permit registration certificate by DEQ.
