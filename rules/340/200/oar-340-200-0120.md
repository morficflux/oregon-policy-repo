## Full text

340-200-0120 Conflicts of Interest: Disclosure of Potential Conflicts of Interest Each member of the EQC and the Director must disclose any potential conflict of interest. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.035 & 468A.310
