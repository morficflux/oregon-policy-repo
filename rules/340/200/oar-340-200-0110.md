## Full text

340-200-0110 Conflicts of Interest: Public Interest Representation At least a majority of the members of the EQC and the Director must represent the public interest and may not derive any significant portion of their respective incomes directly from persons subject in Oregon to permits or enforcement orders under the FCAA. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: 468A.035 & 468A.310
