# Appeal Procedures (OAR 340-176-0090)

## At a glance

OAR 340-176-0090 — Appeal Procedures. Chapter 340, Division 176. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-176-0090 Appeal Procedures An applicant may appeal determinations by the Department under these rules, in accordance with OAR 340-172-0110, by sending a written request for a formal contested case hearing within 20 days after the Department awards or denies financial assistance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1989 & Ch. 1071
