## Full text

340-258-0100 Oxygenated Gasoline: Policy The Environmental Quality Commission finds and determines that control area responsible parties, distributors and retail outlets are “Indirect Sources” as defined in OAR 340-254-0030.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
