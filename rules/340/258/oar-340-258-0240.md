## Full text

340-258-0240 Oxygenated Gasoline: Inspection and Sampling With consent of the owner or operator, the Department will, at any reasonable time, enter the premises of any person subject to the requirements of OAR 340-258-0110 through 340-258-0310 to determine compliance. The Department will inspect all relevant records and equipment, and will, in its discretion, purchase gasoline samples for testing by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.420
