## Full text

340-258-0130 Oxygenated Gasoline: Compliance Options Each CAR or blender CAR must comply with applicable oxygen content standards set out in OAR 340-258-0140(1), 340-258-0150(1), and 340-258-0170 by means of either the per gallon compliance option established in 340-258-0140 or the averaging method compliance option established in 340-258-0150.
