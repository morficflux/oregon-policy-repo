## Full text

340-258-0280 Oxygenated Gasoline: Quality Assurance Program To demonstrate an acceptable quality assurance program under this rule, a person shall conduct periodic sampling and testing to determine if the oxygenated gasoline has oxygen content that is consistent with the product transfer documentation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.420
