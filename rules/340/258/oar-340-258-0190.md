## Full text

340-258-0190 Oxygenated Gasoline: CAR, Distributor and Retail Outlet Operating Permits Each CAR, distributor and retail outlet supplying gasoline to a control area during a control period shall apply for and receive a permit as specified by OAR 340-258-0200.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.420
