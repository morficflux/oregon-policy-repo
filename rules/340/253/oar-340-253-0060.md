## Full text

340-253-0060 Acronyms The following acronyms apply to this division:

(1) “AFP” means Alternative Fuel Portal.

(2) “ASTM” means ASTM International (formerly American Society for Testing and Materials).

(3) “BEV” means battery electric vehicle.

(4) “CARB” means the California Air Resources Board.

(5) “CA-GREET” means the California Air Resources Board adopted version of the GREET model, including simplified calculators.

(6) “CCS” means carbon capture and sequestration.

(7) “CFP” means the Clean Fuels Program established under OAR chapter 340, division 253.

(8) “CI” means carbon intensity.

(9) “CNG” means compressed natural gas.

(10) “CO2e” means carbon dioxide equivalents.

(11) “DEQ” means Oregon Department of Environmental Quality.

(12) “DCO” means distillers technical corn oil.

(13) “DGS” means distillers grains and solubles.

(14) “DDGS” means dry DGS.

(15) “DSO” means distillers technical sorghum oil.

(16) “eCHE” means electric cargo handling equipment.

(17) “EER” means energy economy ratio.

(18) “EN” means a European Standard adopted by one of the three European Standardization Organizations.

(19) “eOGV” means electric ocean-going vessels.

(20) “EQC” means Oregon Environmental Quality Commission.

(21) “eTRU” means electric transport refrigeration unit.

(22) “EV” means electric vehicle.

(23) “FEIN” means federal employer identification number.

(24) “FFV” means flex fuel vehicle.

(25) “FPC” means fuel pathway code.

(26) “FSE” means fuel supply equipment.

(27) “gCO2e/MJ” means grams of carbon dioxide equivalent per megajoule of energy.

(28) “HDV” means heavy-duty vehicle.

(29) “HDV-CIE” means a heavy-duty vehicle compression ignition engine.

(30) “HDV-SIE” means a heavy-duty vehicle spark ignition engine.

(31) “HEFA” means hydroprocessed ester and fatty acid.

(32) “L-CNG” means liquefied-compressed natural gas.

(33) “LDV” means light-duty vehicle.

(34) “LNG” means liquefied natural gas.

(35) “LPG” means liquefied petroleum gas.

(36) “LPGV” means liquefied petroleum gas vehicle.

(37) “MDGS” means modified DGS.

(38) “MDV” means medium-duty vehicle.

(39) “mmBtu” means million British Thermal Units.

(40) “NERC” means the North American Electric Reliability Corporation.

(41) “NGV” means natural gas vehicle.

(42) “OFRS” means the Oregon Fuels Reporting System, the electronic reporting, trading, and compliance platform for the Clean Fuels Program and the Greenhouse Gas Reporting Program.

(43) “OPGEE” means Oil Production Greenhouse Gas Emissions Estimator.

(44) “PHEV” means partial hybrid electric vehicle.

(45) “PTD” means product transfer document.

(46) “REC” means Renewable Energy Certificate.

(47) “RFS” means the Renewable Fuel Standard implemented by the US Environmental Protection Agency.

(48) “RTC” means Renewable Thermal Certificate.

(49) “scf” means standard cubic foot.

(50) “UCO” means used cooking oil.

(51) “ULSD” means ultra-low sulfur diesel.

(52) “WA-GREET” means the Washington Department of Ecology adopted version of the GREET model, including simplified calculators.

(53) “WDGS” means wet DGS.

(54) “WECC” means the Western Electricity Coordinating Council.

(55) “WREGIS” means the Western Renewable Energy Generation Information System run by the Western Electricity Coordinating Council.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468A.266, 468A.268 & 468A.277 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.020 & ORS 468A.265 - 468A.277
