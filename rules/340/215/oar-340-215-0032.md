## Full text

340-215-0032 Deferrals and Exemptions DEQ may defer or exempt specific processes, categories of sources, or specific types of greenhouse gas emissions, from this division’s requirements if DEQ determines that adequate reporting protocols are not available or that other extenuating circumstances make reporting unfeasible.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468A.050 & 468A.280 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468 & 468A

History: DEQ 14-2020, adopt filed 05/07/2020, effective 05/07/2020
