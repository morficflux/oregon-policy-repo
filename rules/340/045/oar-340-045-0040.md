## Full text

340-045-0040 Renewal of NPDES or WPCF Permits

(1) The procedures for issuing NPDES and WPCF permits apply to renewing these permits.

(2) If a completed application for renewal of a permit is filed with DEQ under OAR 340-045-0030, the permit will not expire until final action has been taken on the renewal application.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468B.020 & 468B.035 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.065, 468B.015, 468B.035 & 468B.050
