# Purpose (OAR 340-045-0005)

## Full text

340-045-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to prescribe limitations on discharge of wastes and the requirements and procedures for obtaining NPDES and WPCF permits from the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468B Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.065, 468B.015 & 468B.030
