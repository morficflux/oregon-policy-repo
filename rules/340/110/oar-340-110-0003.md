## Full text

340-110-0003 Definitions

(1) The definitions of the following sections are added to 40 CFR 761.3.

(2) The definitions of OAR 340-100-0010.

(3) For the purpose of this Division: (a) “Agency’s Regional Administrator in the EPA Region in Which the PCBs are Located” means the Department; (b) “Agency” means the Department; (c) “Approve” means permit; (d) “Approved” means permitted; (e) “Approval” means permit; (f) “Assistant Administrator for Pesticides and Toxic Substances” means the Department; (g) “Appropriate Regional Administrator” means the Department; (h) “Chemical Waste Landfill” means PCB landfill; (i) “Environmental Protection Agency” and “EPA” means the Department; (j) “Initial Report” means application; (k) “Receive Written Approval” means obtain a permit; (l) “Regional Administrator” means the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 459 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.005

History: DEQ 12-1986, f. & ef. 5-20-86 DEQ 7-1984, f. & ef. 4-26-84
