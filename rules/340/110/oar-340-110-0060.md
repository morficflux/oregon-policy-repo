## Full text

340-110-0060 Treatment and Disposal

(1) Sections (2)–(4) of this rule are added to the provisions of 40 CFR 761.60(a).

(2) “PCB disposal facility” includes a facility for treatment or disposal of PCB or PCB items.

(3) No person shall treat or dispose of PCB or PCB items except at a PCB disposal facility permitted by the Department.

(4) No person shall establish, construct or operate a PCB disposal facility without a permit issued by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 459 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.025, 466.030 & 466.255

History: DEQ 12-1986, f. & ef. 5-20-86 DEQ 7-1984, f. & ef. 4-26-84
