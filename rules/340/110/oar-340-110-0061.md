## Full text

340-110-0061 Additional Disposal Requirements

(1) Section

(2) of this rule is added to the provisions of 40 CFR 761(a).

(2) Spills, leaks and other uncontrolled discharges of PCB constitute disposal of PCB and shall be reported and managed in accordance with division 142.

(3) Section

(4) of this rule is added to the provisions of 40 CFR 761.60(e).

(4) The permit shall be issued in accordance with divisions 106 and 120 and may contain conditions and provisions as the Department deems appropriate.

(5) Section

(6) of this rule is added to 40 CFR 761.60.

(6) Waste Oil. The use of waste oil that contains any detectable concentration of PCB as a sealant coating or dust control agent is prohibited. Prohibited uses include, but are not limited to, road oiling, general dust control, use as a pesticide carrier and use as a rust preventative on pipes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 459A.595 & 466.635
