## Full text

340-017-0045 Procedures for Transfer of a Tax Credit Certificate To transfer a tax credit certificate from one holder to another, the Commission shall revoke the certificate and grant a new one to the new holder for the balance of the available tax credit following the procedure set forth in ORS 315.324.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.150 - 468.190
