## Full text

340-012-0073 Environmental Cleanup Classification of Violations

(1) Violating any otherwise unclassified environmental cleanup-related requirements is addressed under OAR 340-012-0053.

(2) Class II: Failing to provide information under ORS 465.250.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 465.280, 465.400 - 465.410, 465.435 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 465.210 & 468.090 - 468.140
