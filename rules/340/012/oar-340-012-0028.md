# Scope of (OAR 340-012-0028)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-012-0028 Scope of Applicability Amendments to OAR 340-012-0026 to 340-012-0170 shall only apply to formal enforcement actions issued by DEQ on or after the effective date of such amendments and not to any contested cases pending or formal enforcement actions issued prior to the effective date of such amendments.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 454, 459.995, 466, 467, 468.020 & 468.996 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.090, 454.635, 454.645, 459.376, 459.995, 465.900, 466.210, 466.880 - 895, 468.090 - 140, 468A.990, 468.992, 468B.025, 468B.220 & 468B.450
