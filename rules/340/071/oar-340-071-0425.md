## Full text

340-071-0425 Variance Officers

(1) To qualify for appointment as a variance officer, an individual must: (a) Have the equivalent of five years of full time experience in onsite wastewater treatment methods since January 1, 1974; three years must have been in Oregon; and (b) Have attended a seminar, workshop, or short course pertaining to soils and their relationship to onsite wastewater treatment.

(2) Contract counties may request appointment of county staff as variance officers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 454.625 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 454.660
