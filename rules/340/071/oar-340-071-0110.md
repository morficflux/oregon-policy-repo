# Purpose (OAR 340-071-0110)

## Full text

340-071-0110 Purpose These rules establish requirements for constructing, altering, repairing, operating, and maintaining onsite wastewater treatment systems. Their purpose is to restore and maintain the quality of public waters and to protect the public health and general welfare of the people of the State of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 454.625 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 454.605 - 454.779
