## Full text

340-071-0200 Prior Construction Permits or Approvals All construction-installation permits and written approvals issued before January 1, 1974, expired on July 1, 1976.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 454.625 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 454.655

History: DEQ 11-2004, f. 12-22-04, cert. ef. 3-1-05 DEQ 10-1981, f. & ef. 3-20-81
