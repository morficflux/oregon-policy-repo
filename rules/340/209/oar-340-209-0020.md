# Applicability (OAR 340-209-0020)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-209-0020 Applicability This division applies to permit actions requiring public notice as specified in OAR 340, divisions 216, 218 and 245. NOTE : This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040 with the exception of all references to toxic air contaminants and OAR chapter 340, division 245.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468.065 & 468A.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.065, 468A.035, 468A.040 & 468A.310

History: DEQ 197-2018, amend filed 11/16/2018, effective 11/16/2018 DEQ 7-2015, f. & cert. ef. 4-16-15 DEQ 6-2001, f. 6-18-01, cert. ef. 7-1-01
