# Purpose (OAR 340-209-0010)

## At a glance

OAR 340-209-0010 — Purpose. Chapter 340, Division 209. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-209-0010 Purpose The purpose of this division is to specify the requirements for notifying the public of certain permit actions and providing an opportunity for the public to participate in those permit actions. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468.065 & 468A.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.065, 468A.035, 468A.040 & ORS 468A.310
