## Full text

340-090-0310 Rigid Plastic Containers: Purpose The following administrative rules, OAR 340-090-0320 - 0430, are intended to establish the minimum requirements for implementing the Oregon Rigid Plastic Container Recycling Law, ORS 459A.650 through 680. The Commission's purposes in adopting these rules are to:

(1) Reduce the amount of rigid plastic containers being disposed of in Oregon;

(2) Increase the reuse or recycling of rigid plastic containers that would otherwise be disposed of;

(3) Increase the use of recycled material in the manufacture of rigid plastic containers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 459A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 459A.650 - 459A.665
