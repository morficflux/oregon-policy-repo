## Full text

340-208-0450 Nuisance Control Requirements: Particle Fallout Limitation No person may cause or permit the emission of particulate matter larger than 250 microns in size at sufficient duration or quantity as to create an observable deposition upon the real property of another person.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468, 468A.010 & 468A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.010 & 468A.025
