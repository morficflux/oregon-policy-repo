# Identification Number (OAR 340-104-0011)

## At a glance

OAR 340-104-0011 — Identification Number. Chapter 340, Division 104. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-104-0011 Identification Number In addition to the provisions of 40 CFR 264.11, as a matter of policy, the Department will accept EPA identification numbers already assigned, use an amended EPA registration form, and use EPA’s identification numbering system (Dun and Bradstreet) for owners and operators who register in the future.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 459 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.020 & 466.115

History: DEQ 8-1985, f. & ef. 7-25-85
