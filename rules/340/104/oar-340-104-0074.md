## Full text

340-104-0074 Availability of Records

(1) The provisions of 40 CFR 264.74(a) are replaced with section

(2) of this rule.

(2) All records, including plans, required under this Division must be furnished upon request, and made available at all reasonable times for inspection, by any officer, employee, or representative of the Department as authorized by ORS 466.185.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 459, 466.020, 466.075, 466.105, 466.195 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.150 & 466.195

History: DEQ 4-1991, f. & cert. ef. 3-15-91 (and corrected 6-20-91) DEQ 8-1985, f. & ef. 7-25-85
