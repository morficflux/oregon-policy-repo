## Full text

340-104-0276 Food-Chain Crops

(1) In 40 CFR 264.276, the term “animal feed crops” is substituted for the term “food chain crops.”

(2) The provisions of 40 CFR 264.276(b)(1) are deleted. NOTE: The state program is more stringent than the federal program in that it does not permit crops intended for human consumption to be grown on a land treatment facility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 459 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.015 & 466.020

History: DEQ 8-1985, f. & ef. 7-25-85
