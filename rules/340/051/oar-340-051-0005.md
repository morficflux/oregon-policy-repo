# Purpose (OAR 340-051-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 340-051-0005 — Purpose. Chapter 340, Division 051. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-051-0005 Purpose It is the purpose of these rules to protect the quality of the environment and public health in Oregon by requiring compliance with federal requirements in 40 CFR §122, 123, and 412 (68 FR 7176 (February 12, 2003)) and application of applicable waste control technology relative to location, construction, operations and maintenance of confined animal feeding operations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468B.200 - 468B.230 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468B.200 - 468B.300

History: DEQ 12-2003, f. & cert. ef. 9-2-03 DEQ 34, f. 2-3-72, ef. 2-15-72
