# New (OAR 340-013-0035)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-013-0035 New Wilderness Areas This Division shall not apply to any wilderness area established after January 1, 1972, by the United States until a public hearing on the possible application of this Division or other rules thereto shall have first been held by the Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.035
