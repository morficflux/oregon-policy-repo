## Full text

340-013-0025 Penalties In addition to and not in lieu of any other judicial redress, a person violating this Division shall be subject to criminal prosecution as provided by Oregon Law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.090 - 468.140
