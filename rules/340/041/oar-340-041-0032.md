# Total (OAR 340-041-0032)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-041-0032 Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) Total Dissolved Solids: Total Dissolved Solids: The concentrations listed in the basin specific criteria found in OAR 340-041-0101 through 340-041-0350, may not be exceeded unless otherwise specifically authorized by DEQ upon such conditions as it may deem necessary.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468B.030, 468B.035 & 468B.048 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468B.030, 468B.035 & 468B.048
