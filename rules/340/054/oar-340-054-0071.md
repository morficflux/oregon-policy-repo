## Full text

340-054-0071 Purchase and Refinancing a Public Agency’s Debt Obligation: Debt Obligation Purchase DEQ may use the CWSRF to buy a public agency’s debt obligation subject to all of the following limitations:

(1) The debt was incurred after March 7, 1985.

(2) The debt obligation does not exceed 30 years.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468.440 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.423 - 468.440
