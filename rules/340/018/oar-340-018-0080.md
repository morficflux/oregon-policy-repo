## Full text

340-018-0080 Compliance with DLCD Permit Compliance and Compatibility Rule The Department’s Waste Tire Storage Permit is classified a Class B permit pursuant to OAR 660 division 31. This permit is subject to the procedures of OAR 340-018-0040 and 340-018-0050 to assure statewide goal compliance and acknowledged plan compatibility.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.180
