## Full text

340-122-0510 Solid Waste Orphan Site Account: Purpose These rules establish eligibility, selection criteria, and conditions for use of solid waste Orphan Site Account funds. Solid waste Orphan Site Account funds are to be used for investigation and cleanup of hazardous substance releases from solid waste disposal facilities, in accordance with ORS 459.236.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 459.045, 459.236 & 459.311 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 459.005, 459.236, 459.311, 465.200-455 & 465.900
