## Full text

340-122-0205 Cleanup Rules for Leaking Petroleum UST Systems: Purpose These rules establish the standards and process for the investigation, monitoring, and remedial activities necessary to protect public health, safety, and welfare and the environment in the event of a release or threat of a release from a petroleum UST system subject to ORS 466.705 through 466.835, 466.994, 465.200 through 465.455, and 465.900.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 465.400 & 466.746 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 465.200 - 465.455 & 466.706 - 466.835

History: DEQ 23-1998, f. & cert. ef. 11-2-98 DEQ 15-1991, f. & cert. ef. 8-14-91 DEQ 29-1988, f. & cert. ef. 11-9-88
