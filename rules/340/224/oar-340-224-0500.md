## Full text

340-224-0500 Net Air Quality Benefit Emission Offsets: Net Air Quality Benefit for Sources Locating Within or Impacting Designated Areas OAR 340-224-0510 through 340-224-0540 are the requirements for demonstrating net air quality benefit using offsets. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-020-0040.
