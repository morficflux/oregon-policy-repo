## Full text

340-224-0255 State New Source Review: Requirements for Sources in Reattainment Areas Within a designated reattainment area, a source subject to State NSR must comply with the requirements in OAR 340-224-0260 for each reattainment pollutant treating the reattainment pollutant as a maintenance pollutant for that rule, except that 340-224-0260(2)(b)(C) and

(4) are not applicable unless DEQ has approved a contingency plan for the reattainment area. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468A.025, 468A.035, 468A.040, 468A.050, 468A.055 & 468A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A

History: DEQ 7-2015, f. & cert. ef. 4-16-15
