# Employee (OAR 340-242-0280)

## Full text

340-242-0280 Employee Commute Options Program: Participation in the Industrial Emission Management Program Employers that donate unused Plant Site Emission Limit (PSEL) to the Department's Industrial Emission Management program (see OAR 340-242-0400 through 340-242-0440) are exempt from the ECO rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.363
