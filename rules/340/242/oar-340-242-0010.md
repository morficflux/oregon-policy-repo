## Full text

340-242-0010 Employee Commute Options Program: What is the Employee Commute Options Program?

(1) The Employee Commute Options or "ECO" Program requires larger employers to provide commute options to encourage employees to reduce auto trips to the work site.

(2) ECO is one of several strategies included in the Ozone Maintenance Plan for the Portland Air Quality Maintenance Area. The Ozone Maintenance Plan will keep the area in compliance with the federal ozone standard.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.363
