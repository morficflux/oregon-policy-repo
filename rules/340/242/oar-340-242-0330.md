## Full text

340-242-0330 Voluntary Maximum Parking Ratio Program: How Does a Property Owner Comply with the Voluntary Parking Ratio Program? A property owner complies by building no more than the number of parking spaces specified by maximum parking ratios in OAR 340-242-0390 and obtaining a Parking Ratio Permit from the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.363
