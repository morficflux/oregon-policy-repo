## Full text

340-242-0300 Voluntary Maximum Parking Ratio Program: What is the Voluntary Parking Ratio Program? The Voluntary Parking Ratio Program encourages property owners to voluntarily locate and design facilities that need less parking by building in a more pedestrian, bicycle and transit friendly manner.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.363
