## Full text

340-242-0030 Employee Commute Options Program: What Does ECO Require? Employers must provide commute options that have the potential to reduce employee commute auto trips by ten percent within three years of its baseline survey. Employers must continue to provide commute options that have the potential to achieve and maintain the reduced auto trip rate. Options are available for alternative emission reduction measures, credits for past actions, and exemptions.
