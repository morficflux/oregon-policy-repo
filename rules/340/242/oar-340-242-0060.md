## Full text

340-242-0060 Employee Commute Options Program: Should All Employees at a Work Site Be Counted? The count of employees at a work site must include:

(1) Employees from all shifts, Monday through Friday, during a 24-hour period, averaged over a 12-month period;

(2) Employees on the employer’s payroll for at least six consecutive months at one work site; and

(3) Part-time employees assigned to a work site 80 or more hours per 28-day-period; but

(4) Excludes volunteers, disabled employees (as defined under the Americans with Disabilities Act), employees working on a non-scheduled work week, and employees required to use a personal vehicle as a condition of employment.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.363
