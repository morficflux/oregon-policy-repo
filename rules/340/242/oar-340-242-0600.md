## Full text

340-242-0600 Motor Vehicle Refinishing: Applicability OAR 340-242-0600 through 340-242-0630 apply to any person who owns, leases, operates or controls a motor vehicle refinishing facility in the Portland AQMA. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025 & 468A.035
