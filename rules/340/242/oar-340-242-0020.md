## Full text

340-242-0020 Employee Commute Options Program: Who is Subject to ECO? ECO applies to employers within the Portland Air Quality Maintenance Area (AQMA) with more than 100 employees at a work site. The Portland Air Quality Maintenance Area is defined in Oregon Administrative Rules (OAR) 340-204-0010 and is illustrated in Figure 1.
