## Full text

340-242-0380 Voluntary Maximum Parking Ratio Program: When Will the Department Act on a Submitted Permit Application?

(1) The Department will notify the applicant within 15 days of filing an application if further information is needed or if the application is complete.

(2) The Department will grant or deny a letter permit within 45 days of receiving a complete application.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.363
