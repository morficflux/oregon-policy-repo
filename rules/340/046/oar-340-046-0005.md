# Purpose (OAR 340-046-0005)

## At a glance

OAR 340-046-0005 — Purpose. Chapter 340, Division 046. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-046-0005 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to regulate the beneficial use of motor vehicle bodies, chassis, parts, and accessories in the waters of the state or in locations where they may be likely to escape or be carried into said waters by any means, and the means and method for their deposit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468B Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468B.065

History: DEQ 7, f. 3-31-70, ef. 4-25-70
