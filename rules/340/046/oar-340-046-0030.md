# Other Governmental Requirements (OAR 340-046-0030)

## At a glance

OAR 340-046-0030 — Other Governmental Requirements. Chapter 340, Division 046. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-046-0030 Other Governmental Requirements Any permit or approval granted by the Department for the deposit of motor vehicle bodies or parts thereof into the waters of the state or in areas adjacent thereto does not supersede or obviate any municipal, county, state, or federal ordinance, regulation, or statutory requirements to the contrary.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468B Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468B.065

History: DEQ 7, f. 3-31-70, ef. 4-25-70
