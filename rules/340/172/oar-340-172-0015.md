# Interim (OAR 340-172-0015)

## Full text

340-172-0015 Interim Program Benefits Financial Assistance Program applications approved and confirmed for funding between July 1, 1993 and July 1, 1995 can only qualify for an essential services grant.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL 1993 & Ch. 661 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1991, Ch. 863 & OL 1993 & Ch. 661
