# Financial (OAR 340-172-0080)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-172-0080 Financial Assistance Priority Monies, other than monies necessary to pay the Department’s program administration expenses, will be allocated in the following priority order:

(1) First to satisfy the 1993–1995 biennial obligations for the financial assistance commitments made prior to July 1, 1993 under ORS 466.706 through 466.835 and 466.895 through 466.995, as amended by Chapter 1071, Oregon Laws, 1989. (House Bill 3080) and Chapter 863, Oregon Laws 1991 (Senate Bill 1215);

(2) Second to fund Tier 4 UST project work allowed under these rules on a first come, first serve basis, based upon the date of Department determination of a complete application.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL 1993 & Ch. 661 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1991, Ch. 863 & OL 1993 & Ch. 661
