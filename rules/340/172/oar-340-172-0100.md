# Records (OAR 340-172-0100)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-172-0100 Records The Department and commercial lending institutions shall have access to books, documents, papers and records of the applicant which are directly pertinent to qualifying for financial assistance for the purpose of making audit, examination, excerpts and transcripts. The applicant shall maintain these records for three years after upgrading the UST to new tank standards, replacement of the UST, or completion of the AST project.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 1991 & Ch. 863
