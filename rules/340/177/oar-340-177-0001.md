## Full text

340-177-0001 Purpose and Scope This Division specifies requirements for the cleanup of releases of petroleum from underground heating oil tanks, technical standards for the voluntary decommissioning of underground heating oil tanks, and requirements for submittal of technical reports that have been certified by licensed service providers.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 465.200-320, 466.706 & 466.850-870 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 465.400, 465.405, 466.706, 466.855 & 466.870
