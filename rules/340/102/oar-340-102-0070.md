## Full text

340-102-0070 Farmers In addition to the provisions of 40 C.F.R. § 262.70, a farmer disposing of waste pesticides from the farmer’s own use which are hazardous wastes must comply with the requirements of division 109 of these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 459, 466.020, 466.075, 466.105, 466.195 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.020 & 466.075
