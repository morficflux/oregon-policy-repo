## Full text

340-220-0180 Late and Underpayment of Fees

(1) Notwithstanding any enforcement action, the owner or operator will be subject to a late payment fee of: (a) Two hundred dollars for payments received more than seven and less than 30 days late; and (b) Four hundred dollars for payments received on or after 30 days late.

(2) Notwithstanding any enforcement action, DEQ may assess an additional fee of the greater of $400 or 20 percent of the amount underpaid for substantial underpayment.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468.065, 468A.310 & 468A.315 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468 & 468A
