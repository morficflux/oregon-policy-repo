## Full text

340-256-0150 Visible Emissions: Opacity Method of Measurement Only a trained person may make the opacity observation for purposes of OAR 340-256-0100 through 340-256-0140. A trained person may use a Ringelmann Smoke Chart or DEQ approved opacity equipment to measure emission opacity. [NOTE: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.360
