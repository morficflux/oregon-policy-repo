# Compliance (OAR 340-256-0220)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-256-0220 Compliance With Oregon Low Emission Vehicle Program Model years 2009 and newer vehicles that have 7,500 or fewer miles must meet the requirements of Chapter 340, Division 257 to qualify for a Certificate of Compliance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020, 468A.010, 468A.015, 468A.025, 468A.360 & 468A.380 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.020 & 468A.365

History: DEQ 21-2020, amend filed 11/19/2020, effective 11/19/2020 DEQ 6-2006, f. & cert. ef. 6-29-06
