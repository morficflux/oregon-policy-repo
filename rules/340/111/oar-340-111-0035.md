## Full text

340-111-0035 On-Site Burning in Space Heaters In addition to the provisions in 40 CFR 279.23, used oil generators shall comply with the following: The on-site space heater is operated according to manufacturers specifications.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192, 465.009, 466.015, 466.020, 466.075, 466.090, 468.020 & 646 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 459A.590
