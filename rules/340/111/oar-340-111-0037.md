# Off-Site Shipments (OAR 340-111-0037)

## Full text

340-111-0037 Off-Site Shipments The provisions in 40 CFR 279.24(a)(1) and (b)(1) are replaced with the following: The generator transports the used oil in a vehicle owned or leased by the generator or owned by an employee of the generator.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192, 465.009, 466.015, 466.020, 466.075, 466.090, 468.020 & 646 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 459A.590 & 466.020
