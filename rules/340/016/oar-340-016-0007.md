## Full text

340-016-0007 Facilities certified under the 1999 Edition For the purposes of ORS 468.173(1), a facility may be certified under the 1999 edition of 468.155 to 468.190 if the facility was substantially completed on or before December 31, 2001, and an application was filed with the Department within two years after the date of substantial completion.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 483.335(5) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.155 - 468.190

History: DEQ 16-2002, f. 10-28-02 cert. ef. 11-1-02
