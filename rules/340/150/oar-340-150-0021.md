# Termination of (OAR 340-150-0021)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-150-0021 Termination of Temporary Permits Any owner or permittee holding a temporary permit to operate an UST on or before December 22, 1998, who was not issued an operation certificate by the department by December 23, 1998, must apply for a general permit for temporary closure pursuant to OAR 340-150-0167 or decommission the UST under a general permit for permanent closure or change-in-service pursuant to OAR 340-150-0166 and 340-150-0168.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.706 - 466.835, 466.994 & 466.995 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.746, 466.750, 466.760 & 466.765

History: DEQ 3-2008, f. 2-29-08, cert. ef. 3-10-08 DEQ 6-2003, f. & cert. ef. 2-14-03 DEQ 24-1998, f. & cert. ef. 11-2-98
