# Reporting: Applicability (OAR 340-214-0100)

## Full text

340-214-0100 Reporting: Applicability OAR 340-214-0100 through 340-214-0130 apply to all stationary sources in the state. Stationary source includes portable sources that are required to have permits under division 216. NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025 & 468A.050
