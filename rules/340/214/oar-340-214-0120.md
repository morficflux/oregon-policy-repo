## Full text

340-214-0120 Reporting: Enforcement Notwithstanding any other provisions contained in any applicable requirement, any credible evidence may be used for the purpose of establishing whether a person has violated or is in violation of any such applicable requirements.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.035 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468.100
