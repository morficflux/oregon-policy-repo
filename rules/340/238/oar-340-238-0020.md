# Statement of Policy (OAR 340-238-0020)

## At a glance

OAR 340-238-0020 — Statement of Policy. Chapter 340, Division 238. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-238-0020 Statement of Policy It is the policy of the Commission to consider the performance standards for new and existing stationary sources contained in this division to be minimum standards; and, as technology advances, conditions warrant, and Commission or regional authority rules require or permit, additional rules may be adopted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
