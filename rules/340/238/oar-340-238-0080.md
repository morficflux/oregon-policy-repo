## Full text

340-238-0080 More Restrictive Regulations If at any time there is a direct conflict between this division or regional authority rules and the Federal Regulation (40 CFR, Part 60), the federal regulation applies. Direct conflict means that compliance with the state rule creates noncompliance with a federal regulation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468 & 468A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
