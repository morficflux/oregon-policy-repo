## Full text

340-238-0010 Statement of Purpose The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency has adopted in Title 40, Code of Federal Regulations, Part 60, Standards of Performance for certain new stationary sources. It is the intent of this division to specify requirements and procedures necessary for the Department to implement and enforce the aforementioned Federal Regulation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 468.020 & 468A.025 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 468A.025
