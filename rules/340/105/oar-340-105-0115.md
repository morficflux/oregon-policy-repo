# Interim (OAR 340-105-0115)

## At a glance

## Full text

340-105-0115 Interim Status The provisions of 40 CFR 270.70 to 270.73, pertaining to interim status, are not included in the State’s hazardous waste management program. NOTE: State requirements applicable to existing hazardous waste management facilities are identified in rule 340-105-0010 and include provisions analogous to those of 40 CFR 270.71 and 270.72.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 459 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.030

History: DEQ 8-1985, f. & ef. 7-25-85
