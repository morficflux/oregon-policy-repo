340-105-0003 Considerations Under Federal Law The provisions of 40 CFR 270.3, and the Remedial Action Plan provisions under 40 CFR 270.2, 270.11(d), 270.42, 270.68, 270.73(a) and 270.79–270.230 are deleted.
