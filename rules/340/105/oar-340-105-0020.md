## Full text

340-105-0020 Specific Part B Information Requirements for Land Treatment Facilities The requirements of 40 CFR 270.20(d) and (e) are applicable to animal feed crops. NOTE: The Department does not allow food-chain crops to be grown in or on the treatment zone of a hazardous waste land treatment unit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 459 & 468 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.020, 466.115 & 466.120
