## Full text

340-105-0021 Specific Part B Information Requirements for Landfills In addition to the information required by 40 CFR 270.21 the following additional information shall be submitted in a Part B application. A detailed report with supporting information justifying the need for the landfill as proposed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 466.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.020, 466.115 & 466.120

History: DEQ 4-1991, f. & cert. ef. 3-15-91 (and corrected 6-20-91) DEQ 8-1985, f. & ef. 7-25-85
