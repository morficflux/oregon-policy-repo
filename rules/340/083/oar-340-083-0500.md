## Full text

340-083-0500 Rebates to Distressed Counties

(1) By September 30 of each year beginning in 2016, DEQ shall provide a partial rebate of the fees authorized under ORS 459A.110 to the nine most economically distressed counties in Oregon.

(2) DEQ shall annually identify the counties that will receive the rebate provided for under section

(1) and, no later than January 31 of each year, provide notice to: (a) Each of the economically distressed counties DEQ identifies that will receive a rebate under section (1), and, (b) Each of the counties that received a rebate during the previous calendar year.

Statutory/Other Authority: Sec. 6a, Ch. 662 & OL 2015 Statutes/Other Implemented: Sec. 6a, Ch. 662 & OL 2015
