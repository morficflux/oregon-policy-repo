# Applicability (OAR 340-113-0010)

## At a glance

OAR 340-113-0010 — Applicability. Chapter 340, Division 113. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

340-113-0010 Applicability In addition to provisions under 40 CFR 273.1, the following wastes are subject to universal waste management standards but are not “Universal Wastes” as defined in OAR 340-113-0020(4): Waste pesticides as defined in OAR 340-109-0010(2)(a), and pesticide residues as defined in OAR 340-100-0010, that are collected and managed as part of any pesticide collection program that has notified the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.325 - 183.335, 465.009, 466.020 & 468.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 466.015, 466.020 & 466.075

History: DEQ 13-2003, f. & cert. ef. 10-24-03 DEQ 10-2000, f. & cert. ef. 7-21-00 DEQ 12-1996, f. & cert. ef. 7-31-96
