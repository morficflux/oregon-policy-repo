## Full text

337-021-0020 Fee for a Delinquent Renewal An additional $25 delinquent fee must accompany license or permit renewals not postmarked or electronically received at the OBMI office on or before the first day of the renewal month.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.555(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.445(4) & 688.515(4)
