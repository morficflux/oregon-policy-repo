## Full text

337-021-0040 Fee for a LXMO Permit — Permanent The fee for an initial or renewal LXMO permit is $9 per month.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.555(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.515 & ORS 688.515(3)
