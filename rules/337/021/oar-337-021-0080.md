## Full text

337-021-0080 Refunds When the Board determines that a licensee or permit holder has overpaid fees legally due in the amount of $25 or less, the Board will refund the overpaid amount upon receipt of a written request from the person or the legal representative of that person who paid the fee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.555(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 293.445(4)

History: BMI 7-2020, amend filed 07/28/2020, effective 07/28/2020 BRT 2-2002, f. & cert. ef. 11-18-02
