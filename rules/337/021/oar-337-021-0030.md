## Full text

337-021-0030 Fee for a Permanent Medical Imaging License The fee for an initial or renewal Medical Imaging License is $9 per month.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.555(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.445(5), ORS 688.445(1) & ORS 688.455(1)
