## Full text

337-021-0070 Application Fees Non-Refundable All fees associated with obtaining and maintaining a license or permit are nonrefundable.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.555(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.455 & 688.515(3)
