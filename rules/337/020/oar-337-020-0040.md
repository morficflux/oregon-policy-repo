# Requirements for (OAR 337-020-0040)

## At a glance

## Full text

337-020-0040 Requirements for Prorating Fees License and permit renewal fees will be prorated only if the applicant can demonstrate to the Board that the applicant did not practice medical imaging in Oregon during the time the license or permit was expired.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688 & ORS 688.555(1)
