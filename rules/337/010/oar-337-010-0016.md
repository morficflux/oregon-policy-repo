## Full text

337-010-0016 Qualifications of Nuclear Medicine Technologist An individual who operates nuclear medicine equipment must submit evidence of current credentialing by the Nuclear Medicine Technology Certification Board (NMTCB) or the American Registry of Radiologic Technologists (ARRT) in Nuclear Medicine. The Board will verify current credential status through review of the NMTCB or ARRT directory. If the applicant is not listed in the current applicable directory, the applicant must request written verification of current credential and have the current credential submitted directly to the Board by the credentialing organization.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.555(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.455
