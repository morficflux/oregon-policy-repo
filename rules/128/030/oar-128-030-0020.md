# State Information Security (OAR 128-030-0020)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** The operative state information security rule: assigns DAS the role of
primary point of accountability and coordination for information security in state
government, with central leadership duties. Renumbered from OAR 125-800-0020 effective
5/1/2026 (chapter 128 = Office of the State Chief Information Officer). DAS policy
107-004-052 and its cluster operationalize this rule; older policies still cite the
125-800 number.

## Rule text (selected)

### DAS duties

> **[VERBATIM]** "The Department shall serve as the primary point of accountability and
> coordination for information security in state government except for elected offices as
> identified in section 4, Elected Offices Exception."

> **[VERBATIM]** "The Department shall provide central leadership for state
> government-wide information security including, but not limited to: centrally directing
> and coordinating all enterprise information security activities; determining security
> risks to the state's Information assets"

**[SUMMARY]** DAS, in collaboration with agencies, routinely takes proactive and reactive
protective actions for the state's shared computing and network infrastructure: scanning
and monitoring, intrusion prevention/detection, security reviews and compliance audits,
containment and mitigation, connection termination or filtering, and quarantine of
infected systems for forensics. (Source: section (1)(a).)
