# Purpose, Application, and Authority (OAR 128-030-0005)

## At a glance

The purpose rule for Oregon's state-government-wide information security
rules. **Renumbering note:** effective 5/1/2026 this rule moved from DAS chapter 125,
Division 800 (OAR 125-800-0005) to chapter 128 (Office of the State Chief Information
Officer), Division 30. Older documents — including DAS policy 107-004-052 (effective
2/17/2026) — still cite "OAR 125-800"; those citations now resolve here.

## Full text

128-030-0005 Purpose, Application, and Authority These rules are adopted under 2005 Oregon Laws Chapter 739. These rules set forth the policies for state government-wide information security.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 182.122 & 291.038 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 182.122
