# Definitions (OAR 128-030-0010)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Definitions for the state information security rules (renumbered from OAR
125-800-0010 effective 5/1/2026). Defines: incident, information resources, material
adverse event, ordinary public access, publicly/privately addressable interfaces, and
State Information Security Plan.

## Rule text (selected definitions)

> **[VERBATIM]** "“Incident” means any material adverse event that impairs the
> confidentiality, integrity or availability of information resources."

> **[VERBATIM]** "“Information Resources” means all categories of automated or
> non-automated systems and data, including but not limited to, records, files, and
> databases, information technology equipment, facilities, and software owned or leased by
> the state."

> **[VERBATIM]** "“State Information Security Plan” means a compilation of documents
> including, but not limited to, statutes, administrative rules, policies, and plans,
> prescribing the information security practices of the State of Oregon."

**[SUMMARY]** The State Information Security Plan definition ties the rule tier to the
plan/standards documents in `agencies/das/standards/`. Remaining definitions (material
adverse event; ordinary public access; publicly and privately addressable interfaces) are
in the source. (Source: paragraphs (3)–(6).)
