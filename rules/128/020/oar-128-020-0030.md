## Full text

128-020-0030 De-Designation Criteria The State Chief Information Officer will consider one or more of the designation criteria in OAR 128-020-0020 when de-designating or re-evaluating a corporate entity’s status as a national security threat.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 276A.300 Statutes/Other Implemented: Or Laws 2023, ch 256 (HB 3127)
