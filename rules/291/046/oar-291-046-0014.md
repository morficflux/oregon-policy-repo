## Full text

291-046-0014 Administrative Hold An inmate may be voluntarily or involuntarily assigned to administrative housing for a period not to exceed 30 days without a hearing. Any assignment exceeding 30 days must follow the procedures outlined under OAR 291-046-0020 (Voluntary Administrative Housing) or 291-046-0025 (Involuntary Administrative Housing).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
