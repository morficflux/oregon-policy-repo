## Full text

291-158-0035 Interest Accruals Any interest accrued on funds in an adult in custody’s protected money’s reserve account will be credited into that account monthly. Interest accrued on funds in any other account or sub-account will be credited monthly into the general spending trust account. Adults in custody may request, in writing, the exclusion of interest on their trust account.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 30.643, 34.365, 34.370, 138.590, 144.335, 179.040, 179.510, 421.068, 421.125, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 30.643, 34.365, 34.370, 138.590, 144.335, 179.040, 421.068, 421.125, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
