# Definitions (OAR 291-038-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

291-038-0010 Definitions Victim: ORS 137.530 defines a victim as “the person or persons who have suffered financial, social, psychological or physical harm as a result of an offense, and includes, in the case of any homicide or abuse of corpse in any degree, an appropriate member of the immediate family of the decedent."

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.077, 137.079, 144.120, 144.185, 144.791, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.077, 137.079, 144.120, 144.185, 144.791, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
