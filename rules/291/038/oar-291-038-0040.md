# Post-Sentence (OAR 291-038-0040)

## Full text

291-038-0040 Post-Sentence Reports If the defendant is committed to the custody of the Department of Corrections without a presentence report, the Department shall prepare a report of similar content to the presentence report at the request of and for submission to the Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.077, 137.079, 144.120, 144.185, 144.791, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.077, 137.079, 144.120, 144.185, 144.791, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
