## Full text

291-022-0180 Blood Borne Pathogens When a person has been exposed to a blood or body fluid resulting from the use of force, standard universal precautions shall be implemented.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
