## Full text

291-061-0240 Doors and Windows — Vermin, Rodent and Insect Control Flies shall not be present in food preparation storage, dining or serving areas. All openings into the outer air shall be effectively screened and doors shall be self-closing unless other effective means are provided to prevent the entrance of flies, rodents, or other pests.
