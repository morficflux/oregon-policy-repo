## Full text

291-066-0170 Revocation Hearings Process

(1) A violation of a condition of leave that warrants a revocation of leave may only be found upon proof by a preponderance of the evidence.

(2) Unless the Hearings Officer finds good cause on the record, the supervising officer shall present information and evidence at the revocation hearing and arrange for the presence of witnesses for the state. The supervising officer shall make dispositional recommendations.

(3) The Hearings Officer shall make a recording of the revocation hearing.
