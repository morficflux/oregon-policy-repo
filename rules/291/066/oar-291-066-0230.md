## Full text

291-066-0230 Reopening Revocation Hearings

(1) After the completion of a revocation hearing, the Hearings Officer may reopen a hearing if substantial new information is discovered which was not known or could not be anticipated at the time of the hearing and which would significantly affect the outcome of the hearing.

(2) The Hearings Officer shall send the AIC notice of the decision to reopen the revocation hearing and the new information to be considered. The reopened hearing shall conform to the procedures of these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075, 421.168 & 421.500 to 421.512 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075, 421.168 & 421.500 to 421.512
