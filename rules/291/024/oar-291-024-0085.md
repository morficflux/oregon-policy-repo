## Full text

291-024-0085 Stay of Execution If, during any stage of the execution prior to infusion of the lethal injection, the Superintendent is notified that a stay of execution has been ordered, execution procedures shall be halted and the witnesses shall be removed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 137.463, 137.473, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 137.463, 137.473, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
