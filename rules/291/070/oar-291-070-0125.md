## Full text

291-070-0125 Transporting Working Files

(1) The department will use proper security controls for transportation of adult in custody and adult on supervision working files during transit between institutions, functional units, agencies, or customers.

(2) Each functional unit that sends, receives, or transports confidential or sensitive information is responsible to assure that the information is protected appropriately during transit from loss, destruction, or unauthorized access in accordance with the Department of Administrative Services Statewide Policy on Transporting Information Assets (DAS policy 107-004-100).
