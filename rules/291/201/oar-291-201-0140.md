## Full text

291-201-0140 Assignment Changes In accordance with the Department’s rules on Performance Recognition and Award System (OAR 291-077), designated staff may remove an AIC from an approved program assignment for reasons including, but not limited to, unsatisfactory performance, misconduct or behavior issues , program needs, safety and security issues, and schedule conflicts.

(1) If an AIC is removed from an approved program assignment , staff shall reassign the AIC appropriately, and ensure the AIC is notified of the assignment change.

(2) If an AIC is failed from an approved AIC program assignment (or given a daily fail), staff shall fill out an Adult in Custody Performance Failure Record (CD118aD) and distribute all necessary copies.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
