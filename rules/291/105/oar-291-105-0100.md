## Full text

291-105-0100 Vacating or Withdrawing the Final Order in the Interest of Justice The Inspector General, Assistant Director for Operations, or their designees may, in the interest of justice, vacate all or part of a final disciplinary order or withdraw the order and direct that a disciplinary hearing be reopened for consideration of new evidence.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 421.068, 421.180, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 421.068, 421.180, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
