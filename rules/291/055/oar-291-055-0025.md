## Full text

291-055-0025 Situational Reviews Individual inmate assessments may be requested by the Assistant Superintendent of Correctional Rehabilitation or designee or the IMU Inmate Program Committee at any time. Inmates requesting mental health services may be referred to qualified Behavioral Health Services personnel pursuant to recommendation of Behavioral Health Services program staff or the Assistant Superintendent of Correctional Rehabilitation or designee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
