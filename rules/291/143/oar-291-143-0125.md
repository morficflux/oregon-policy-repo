## Full text

291-143-0125 Religious Grooming Requirements Inmates may comply with religious practices relating to head or facial hair if the observance remains consistent with the department's rules on Hygiene and Grooming (OAR 291-123).

Statutory/Other Authority: 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
