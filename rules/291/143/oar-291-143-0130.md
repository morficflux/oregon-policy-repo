## Full text

291-143-0130 Restriction of Religious Activity/Items An inmate's participation in approved religious activities and possession of approved religious items may be restricted by the Department of Corrections when deemed necessary to maintain facility security, safety, health and order, or to further inmate rehabilitation or other penological interest, consistent with applicable legal standards.

Statutory/Other Authority: 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
