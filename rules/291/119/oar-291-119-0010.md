## Full text

291-119-0010 Definitions “Inmate”: Any person under the supervision of the Department of Corrections who is not on parole, post-prison supervision, or probation status.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 421.095, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 147.275, 179.040, 421.095, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
