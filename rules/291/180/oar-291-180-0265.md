## Full text

291-180-0265 Fugitives From Justice Transfer investigations shall not be requested or accepted for adults on supervision with outstanding warrants who are non-residents of the receiving state. Fugitive status is inconsistent with a proper “valid plan of supervision” and compliance with conditions of supervision. Exceptions may be granted by the Compact Administrator or designee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.600, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.600, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
