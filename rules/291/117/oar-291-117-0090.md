## Full text

291-117-0090 Authorized Inmate Property at Initial Intake

(1) Each inmate received at intake shall possess only the following personal property items while awaiting assignment to an appropriate facility within the state system: (a) Medical prosthesis as approved by Health Services (includes prescription eyeglasses); (b) One plain smooth wedding band, without stones or protrusions (must be married to possess); and (c) Authorized legal material.

(2) All property received will be inspected and inventoried. Unauthorized property or excess property will be handled in accordance with procedures set forth in Disposition of Property (OAR 291-117-0140)

(3) Money in the inmate’s possession shall be received and processed in accordance with the Department of Corrections rule on Trust Accounts (Inmate).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
