## Full text

291-130-0080 Remaining Telephone Fund Balances Upon Release Remaining funds balances received from the inmate telephone service provider will be deposited to individual inmate trust accounts as per the department’s rule on Trust Accounts (OAR 291-158-0045) less a processing fee imposed by the department. Funds deposited on behalf of inmates who are indebted to the department are subject to collection as per 291-158-0065. Any remaining funds are disbursed to the inmate through the Oregon Trail card or by check.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
