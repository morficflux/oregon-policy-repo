## Full text

291-013-0110 Bloodborne Pathogens When a person has been exposed to blood or body fluid resulting from the use of force, standard universal precautions shall be implemented.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
