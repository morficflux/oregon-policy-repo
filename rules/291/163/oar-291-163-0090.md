## Full text

291-163-0090 Application of Federal Law Notwithstanding any provisions in these rules, the applicable federal laws, rules, and regulations shall govern if federal funds are used in the transaction and such laws, rules, or regulations conflict with these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 179.040, 179.460, 421.305(1), 421.310(1), 421.440, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 179.040, 179.460, 421.305(1), 421.310(1), 421.440, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
