# Authority (OAR 125-035-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

125-035-0005 Authority The Department of Administrative Services, State of Oregon, is authorized by ORS 279.820 to acquire, warehouse and distribute federal surplus property from the Government of the United States or any surplus property disposal agency thereof (all such property is hereinafter referred to as “Federal surplus property”) to all eligible donees in the state and to enter into cooperative agreements pursuant to the provisions of 41 CFR Ch. 101-44.206. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279 & 283

History: GS 1-1994, f. & cert. ef. 11-1-94 GS 3-1984, f. 6-29-84, ef. 6-30-84
