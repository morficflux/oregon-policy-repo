# Policies (OAR 125-249-0110)

## Full text

125-249-0110 Policies In addition to the policies of the Code as set forth in ORS 279A.015, the policy on competition as provided in 279C.300 applies to this division, except as provided in 279C.335. The policy on least-cost for Public Improvements applies as described within 279C.305.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279C.300 & 279C.305

History: DAS 4-2004, f. 11-23-04, cert. ef. 3-1-05
