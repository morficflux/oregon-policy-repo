## Full text

125-020-0020 Applicability of Rules The Administrative Rules set forth in Chapter 125, Division 20 shall apply to all public records for which the Department is custodian, except as otherwise administered under OAR 105-010-0011 and 105-010-0016 of the Department's Human Resource Services Division.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.340 & 192.430 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.410 - 192.505
