# Development (OAR 125-125-0522)

## At a glance

## Full text

125-125-0522 Development Polices and Design Standards Contained in Area Plans Development policies and design standards applicable to each Area Plan shall be included in the Area Plans. The Department shall provide model development policy and design standards guidelines in accordance with ORS 276.098 and shall be included in the Statewide Facility Planning Process Manual.

Statutory/Other Authority: 276.098 & ORS 276.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 276.056
