## Full text

125-040-0010 Delivery Disclaimer The department is not responsible for timely delivery of time critical mail. Mail senders assume all risk of delay, loss or destruction of mail submitted to the state mail system.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 283

History: GS 6-1988, f. 12-1-88, cert. ef. 1-1-89
