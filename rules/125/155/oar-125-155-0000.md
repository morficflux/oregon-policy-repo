## Full text

125-155-0000 Purpose These rules are written under the authority of ORS 283.305 through ORS 283.395 and ORS 278.200 through ORS 278.215. These rules set standards for use, operation and access to state vehicles, including private vehicles in use for state business.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.305, 184.340 & 278.405 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 283.310, 283.340, 283.345 & 283.395
