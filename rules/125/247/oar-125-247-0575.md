## Full text

125-247-0575 Debarment of Prospective Offerors

(1) See OAR 137-047-0575.

(2) Regardless of OAR 137-047-0575, the State Chief Procurement Officer is the Contracting Agency and acts on behalf of the Director of the Department under ORS 279B.130 and 279B.425.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279B.050-279B.085 & 279B.130
