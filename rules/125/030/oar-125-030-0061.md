## Full text

125-030-0061 Charitable Fund Drive Management Organization

(1) The Charitable Fund Drive Committee will select a person or organization as the Charitable Fund Drive Management Organization through an open competitive process.

(2) The selection process will consider cost, experience, and ability to conduct a statewide fund drive.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.340, 292.045 & 184.305 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 292.043 & 292.045
