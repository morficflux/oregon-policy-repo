## Full text

125-120-0030 Allotment of Space in Office Quarters (ORS 270.410) Allotment of space in all leased office quarters shall be guided by the Department’s Office Space Standards (Policy #125-6-100).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 276.428 & 283.060
