# Locating Office Quarters (OAR 125-120-0120)

## At a glance

OAR 125-120-0120 — Locating Office Quarters. Chapter 125, Division 120. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

125-120-0120 Locating Office Quarters Division has the statutory authority to search, select and negotiate for office quarters to rent or lease in any manner necessary to best serve the interests of the state. Division also reserves the right to reject, in the best interest of the state, any and all offers received while conducting solicitation for offers on leasable properties or facilities. Unless exempted by the Administrator, Division will search for available office space in a manner consistent with applicable executive orders, policies and in the best judgment of Division.
