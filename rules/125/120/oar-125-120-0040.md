## Full text

125-120-0040 Measuring Office Quarters The Division will use the standards of the American National Standards Institute (ANSI) as adopted by the Building Owners and Managers Association (BOMA) as a guide in measuring office space.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.340 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 276.410
