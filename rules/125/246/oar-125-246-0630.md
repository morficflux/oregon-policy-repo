## Full text

125-246-0630 Fragmentation A Procurement may not be artificially divided or fragmented so as to constitute a Small Procurement, pursuant to ORS 279B.065, or an Intermediate Procurement, pursuant to ORS 279B.070.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.015, 279A.065(5)(a), 279A.070 & 279B.065

History: DAS 4-2004, f. 11-23-04, cert. ef. 3-1-05
