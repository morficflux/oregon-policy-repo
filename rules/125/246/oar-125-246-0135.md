## Full text

125-246-0135 Solicitation Templates; Contract Forms and Templates; Accountability for Advice (Director Verification)

(1) See OAR 137-046-0140.

(2) Regardless of OAR 137-046-0140, a State Contracting Agency, in Solicitation documents, must comply with SB 471 (Chapter 294, 2019) regarding conflict minerals.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.157, 279A.161 & SB 471 (Chapter 294, 2019)
