# Sole-Source (OAR 125-246-0625)

## At a glance

OAR 125-246-0625 — Sole-Source. Chapter 125, Division 246. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

125-246-0625 Sole-Source Authorized Agencies may not select a Sole-Source Procurement pursuant to ORS 279B.075 and avoid a competitive Procurement if the purpose of the selection is to primarily benefit the Provider, directly or indirectly, to the detriment of an Authorized Agency or the best interest of the State.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.015, 279A.065(5)(a), 279A.070 & 279A.140

History: DAS 4-2004, f. 11-23-04, cert. ef. 3-1-05
