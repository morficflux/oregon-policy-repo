## Full text

877-040-0010 Form of Complaints Any person may file a complaint alleging a violation of ORS 675.510 to 675.600 or of the rules of the board or an impairment. A complaint must identify the complainant and the respondent.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.510 - 675.600, 675.900 & 675.990 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.595(11)
