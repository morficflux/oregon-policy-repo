# Ethical Responsibility (OAR 877-030-0030)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

877-030-0030 Ethical Responsibility Regulated social workers' ethical responsibilities to their clients begin when the client-provider professional relationship is entered into by the regulated social worker and the client and remain in effect until the relationship is terminated by either party, except as provided in OAR 877-030-0070.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.510 - 675.600 & 675.900 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.595
