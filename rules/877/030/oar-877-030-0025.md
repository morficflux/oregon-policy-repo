## Full text

877-030-0025 Applicability The following rules in this division of rules provide a standard of ethics to be followed by regulated social workers and applicants for licensure or certification to practice as a regulated social worker.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.510 - 675.600 & 675.900 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.595
