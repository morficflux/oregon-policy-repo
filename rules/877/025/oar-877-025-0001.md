## Full text

877-025-0001 General A regulated social worker must complete and report continuing education according to the requirements of OAR Chapter 877, Division 025. A certified social work associate is not required to complete or report continuing education.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675-510-675.600 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.510 - 675.600
