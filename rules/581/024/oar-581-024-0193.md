## Full text

581-024-0193 Election of Board of New ESD Within 90 days following the establishment of the zones as required in OAR 581-024-0192, the joint board must call for a special election in the new ESD to elect directors representing each of the newly established zones.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.051 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 334.710 - 334.770 (Ch. 518 OL 2001)
