## Full text

581-055-0011 Appeal of a Substantiated Final Determination

(1) After issuing a substantiated final determination under OAR 581-055-0007, ODE shall send to the respondent notice of substantiated determination that describes a right to a contested case hearing under OAR 137-003-0501 to 137-003-0700. The notice shall be sent to the respondent by certified mail with return receipt requested.

(2) The respondent has twenty-one days from the mailing date of the notice to request from ODE a contested case hearing to appeal the substantiated final determination. Hearings requested under this section are subject to the provisions established for contested case hearings under OAR 137-003-0501 to 137-003-0700.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.051 & ORS 339.391 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 339.391 & ORS 419B.019
