## Full text

581-015-2385 Hearing Costs

(1) Costs of the Proceedings: (a) The school district must reimburse the Department for the costs related to conducting the hearing, including pre-hearing conferences, scheduling arrangements, and other related matters. (b) The school district must provide the parent with a written, or at the option of the parent, an electronic verbatim recording of the hearing, within a reasonable time of the close of the hearing.

(2) Attorney Fees: Funds under Part B of the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act may not be used to pay attorney fees or costs of a party related to an action or proceeding under this rule.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343.041, 343.045, 343.055 & 343.167; Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 343.165, 343.167, 34 CFR 300.512 & 300.517(b)
