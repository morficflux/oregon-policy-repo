## Full text

581-015-2605 Plans to Serve Students in Local or Regional Correctional Facilities A school district must have a plan, approved by the local school board, to provide or cause to be provided appropriate education for children placed in a local or regional correctional facility located in the school district.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 339.129
