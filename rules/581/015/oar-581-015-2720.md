## Full text

581-015-2720 Census and Data Reporting – EI/ECSE Program Contractors and subcontractors must follow the rules and procedures in OAR 581-015-2010 for reporting to the Department all resident preschool children with disabilities with the following definition exception: "School district" means contractors or subcontractors.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343.475 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 343.475, 343.495 & 343.513 - 343.533
