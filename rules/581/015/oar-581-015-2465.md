## Full text

581-015-2465 Documentation Requirements Each public agency must maintain in its records and provide annually to the Department the following information for parentally-placed private school children:

(1) The number of parentally-placed private school children evaluated;

(2) The number determined to be children with disabilities, and

(3) The number of children served.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343.041, 343.045 & 343.055 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 343.155 & 34 CFR 300.132

History: ODE 10-2007, f. & cert. ef. 4-25-07
