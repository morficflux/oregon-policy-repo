## Full text

581-015-2770 Confidentiality of Records for Preschool Children with Disabilities Contractors and subcontractors must follow the rules and procedures in OAR 581-015-2030 and 34 CFR 303.400 through 303.416 for confidentiality of records for preschool children with disabilities with the following exception: "School district" means contractors or subcontractors.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343.475 & 343.485 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 343.485 & 34 CFR 303.400–303.417
