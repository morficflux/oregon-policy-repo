## Full text

581-015-2500 Use of Personnel

(1) A public agency may use IDEA funds to make public agency personnel available in other than public facilities: (a) To the extent necessary to implement any of the requirements related to private school children with disabilities; and (b) If those services are not normally provided by the private school.

(2) A public agency may use IDEA funds to pay for the services of an employee of a private school to provide services to private school children if: (a) The employee performs the services outside of his or her regular hours of duty; and (b) The employee performs the services under public supervision and control.
