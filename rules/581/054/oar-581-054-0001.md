## Full text

581-054-0001 Definitions

(1) ADMr means average daily membership, as defined and calculated by ORS 327.006(3).

(2) ADMw means extended weighted average daily membership as calculated in ORS 327.013.

Statutory/Other Authority: Sec. 2, Ch. 62, OL 2018 Statutes/Other Implemented: Sec. 2, Ch. 62, OL 2018
