## Full text

581-018-0020 Timelines and Performance Measures Recipients of network grant or contract funds shall meet timelines, performance measures and other requirements related to the accumulation and evaluation of data collected as required by the Oregon Department of Education.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2013 OL Ch. 661 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3233) Statutes/Other Implemented: 2013 OL Ch. 661 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3233)
