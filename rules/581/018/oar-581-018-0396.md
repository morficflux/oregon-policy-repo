581-018-0396 Funding: Oregon Minority Educator Retention Grant

(1) Up to five school district grant awards will be given as follows: $10,000 in Year One and $25,000 in year two which shall be awarded during the following phases based on a detailed budget narrative and budget template: (a) Planning phase. (b) Implementation phase.

(2) Grantees shall use $10,000 awards for design grants and $25,000 awards to plan, develop, and pilot district models.

(3) Grantees must be able to expend the funds for allowable purposes specified in the request for proposal within the grant timeline according to acceptable accounting procedures.
