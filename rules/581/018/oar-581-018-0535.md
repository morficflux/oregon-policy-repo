## Full text

581-018-0535 Reporting: American Indian/Alaskan Native Culturally Relevant Teaching, Learning, and Pedagogy Grant Program The Oregon Department of Education shall provide to grant recipients a template for an interim and final grant report. Grantees are required to submit a final report prior to receiving their final request for funds.
