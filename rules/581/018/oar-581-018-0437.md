## Full text

581-018-0437 Eligibility: Proficiency-Based Teaching and Learning Grants

(1) The following entities shall be eligible to receive Proficiency-Based Teaching and Learning Grants: (a) School districts. (b) Non-profit organizations working with at least one school district.

(2) A single grant proposal may include more than one eligible applicant and may also include other entities such as education service districts or post-secondary institutions, but the fiscal agent must be one of the eligible applicants identified in subsection

(1) of this rule.
