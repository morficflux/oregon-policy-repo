## Full text

581-018-0425 Reporting: Oregon Minority Educator Pipeline Models Grant The Oregon Department of Education shall provide to grant recipients a template for an interim and final grant report. Grantees are required to submit a final report prior to receiving their final request for funds.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2013 OL Ch. 661 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB3233) Statutes/Other Implemented: 2013 OL Ch. 661 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB3233)
