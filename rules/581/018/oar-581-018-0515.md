## Full text

581-018-0515 Reporting: Culturally Responsive Pedagogy and Practices Grant The Oregon Department of Education shall provide to grant recipients a template for an interim and final grant report. Grantees are required to submit a final report prior to receiving their final request for funds.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 342.950 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 342.950
