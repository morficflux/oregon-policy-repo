## Full text

581-018-0411 Eligibility: Oregon Minority Educator Pipeline Models Grant

(1) To be eligible to receive the Oregon Minority Educator Pipeline Models Grant an applicant must be a: (a) School district; (b) Post-secondary institution; (c) Consortium of school districts, post-secondary institutions and other entities (each consortium must include at least one school district or post-secondary institution as a member).

(2) A single grant proposal may include more than one eligible applicant, but the fiscal agent must be one of the eligible applicants identified in subsection

(1) of this rule.
