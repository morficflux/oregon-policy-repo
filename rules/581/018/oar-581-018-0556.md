## Full text

581-018-0556 Reporting: English Language Proficiency Standards Professional Learning Grant The Oregon Department of Education will provide a template for an interim and final grant report. Grantees are required to submit a final report prior to receiving their final request for funds.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 342.950 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 342.950
