## Full text

581-018-0398 Reporting: Oregon Minority Educator Retention Grant The Oregon Department of Education shall provide to grant recipients a template for an interim and final grant report. Grantees are required to submit a final report prior to receiving their final request for funds.

Statutory/Other Authority: OL Ch. 661 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3233) Statutes/Other Implemented: OL Ch. 661 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3233)
