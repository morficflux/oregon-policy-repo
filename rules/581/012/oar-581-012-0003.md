## Full text

581-012-0003 Equity Lens: Regional Educator Networks The Educator Advancement Council, the Oregon Department of Education, and the Regional Educator Networks will apply the Oregon equity lens in all work relating to the Educator Advancement Fund and the Regional Educator Networks.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 342.940 & ORS 324.943 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 342.940 & ORS 324.943
