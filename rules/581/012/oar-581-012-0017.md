## Full text

581-012-0017 Other Investments

(1) The Educator Advancement Council may make other investments at the direction of the legislature or at the discretion of the Educator Advancement Council.

(2) The Oregon Department of Education will distribute funds according to the direction of the Educator Advancement Council.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 342.940 & ORS 324.943 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 342.940 & ORS 324.943
