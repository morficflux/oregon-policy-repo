## Full text

581-012-0019 Reporting: Regional Educator Network Prior to the end of each fiscal year, a Regional Educator Network receiving funds from the Educator Advancement Fund must submit to the Educator Advancement Council:

(1) An expenditure report; and

(2) Audited financial statements.

(3) A report on progress toward goals and locally identified metrics in the regional plan and other metrics identified by the Educator Advancement Council.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 342.940 & ORS 324.943 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 342.940 & ORS 324.943
