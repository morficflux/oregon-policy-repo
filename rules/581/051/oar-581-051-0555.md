## Full text

581-051-0555 Contract Renewal The sponsor shall renew its contract with FSMC only if all of the following conditions are satisfied:

(1) The original contract allows for annual renewal and the total contract term does not exceed five years, original contract year plus four additional renewal years;

(2) The Sponsor consents to the renewal by action of the board;

(3) There is no change in financial terms, unless so designated in the original agreement. Contracts may not allow for an unlimited or discretionary change to the meal fee at renewal. Contracts may allow for an annual change to the meal fee not to exceed Consumer Price Index for All Urban Consumer Food Away from Home for the 12 months ending in March.
