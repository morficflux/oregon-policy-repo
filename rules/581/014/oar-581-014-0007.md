## Full text

581-014-0007 Charter School Eligibility for Student Investment Account Before each application cycle, the Department of Education will publish a list of public charter schools that meet the eligibility requirements of ORS 327.185. The Department will make the determination of eligibility based on the most recent finalized Spring Membership report.
