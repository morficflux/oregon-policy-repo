## Full text

581-014-9999 Definitions for Student Investment Account: Third Grade Reading Proficiency “Third-grade reading proficiency rate” means the percentage of students in the third grade who are determined to be proficient or above based on Oregon’s statewide summative assessments in English Language Arts (ELA).

Statutory/Other Authority: Sec. 10, Ch. 122, OL 2019 Statutes/Other Implemented: Sec. 10, Ch. 122, OL 2019
