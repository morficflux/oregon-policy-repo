## Full text

581-021-0240 The Rights of Eligible Students When a student becomes an eligible student, the rights accorded to, and consent required of, parents under OAR 581-021-0220 through 581-021-0420 transfer from the parents to the student. Nothing prevents educational agencies or institutions from giving students rights in addition to those given to parents.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.565 & 34 CFR § 99.5 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 326.565
