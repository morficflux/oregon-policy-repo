## Full text

581-021-0290 Limitations on the Right to Inspect and Review Records If the education records of a student contain information on more than one student, the parent or eligible student may inspect, review, or be informed of only the specific information about that student.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.565 & 34 CFR § 99.12 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 326.565
