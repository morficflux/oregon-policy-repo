## Full text

581-021-0566 Required Use of Approved Restraint and Seclusion Programs On or after July 1, 2012, a Public Education Program may only use training programs on restraint and seclusion that are approved by the Department of Education under OAR 581-021-0563. The Department of Education shall make the approved training list available to all Public Education Programs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS. 326.051 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 339.285 - 339.303
