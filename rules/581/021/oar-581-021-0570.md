## Full text

581-021-0570 Complaint Procedures

(1) An organization or an individual may file a complaint alleging that a public education program is violating or has violated a provision of ORS 339.285 to 339.303 or OAR 581-021-0550 to 581-021-0566. If the public education program is a school district, the organization or individual and the school district shall follow the school district’s complaint procedure established under OAR 581-022-2370.

(2) A complainant may appeal a final decision by a school district to the Department of Education as provided in OAR 581-075-0001 to OAR 581-075-0045 by: (a) Mailing a complaint appeal to the Department of Education, or (b) Submitting a complaint appeal through the Department of Education's website.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 339.303 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 339.285 - 339.303
