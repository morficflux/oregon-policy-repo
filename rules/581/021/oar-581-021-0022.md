## Full text

581-021-0022 Student Evaluation The district shall develop policies to assure that a students’ academic grade reflects his/her academic achievement in that course. Absenteeism or misconduct shall not be a sole criterion for the reduction of grades.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.051(1)(b) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 339.280
