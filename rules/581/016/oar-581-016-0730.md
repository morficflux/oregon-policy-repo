## Full text

581-016-0730 Career and Vocational Education The Oregon School for the Deaf shall provide a continuum of career and technical instructional program to include career awareness, career exploration, career guidance, job sampling, and work experience.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343 & 346 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 346.010
