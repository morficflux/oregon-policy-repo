## Full text

581-016-0760 Required Days of Instruction The Oregon School for the Deaf shall provide a program of instructional hours or days equivalent to the requirements for public schools as set forth in OAR chapter 22.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343 & 346 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 346.010

History: EB 31-1989, f. & cert. ef. 11-2-89
