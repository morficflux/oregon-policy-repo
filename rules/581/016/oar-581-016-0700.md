## Full text

581-016-0700 Goals for Education To the extent appropriate, each student at the Oregon School for the Deaf shall have the opportunity to function as effectively as possible in six life roles as set forth in OAR 581-022-0201.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343 & 346 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 346.010
