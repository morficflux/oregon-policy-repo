# General (OAR 581-016-0537)

## At a glance

## Full text

581-016-0537 General Obligations OSD is subject to the rules contained in division 15 of this chapter including, but not limited to, the requirements for identifying and determining eligibility of students with handicapping conditions, development of IEPs, placement of students and the provision of a free, appropriate education.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 346 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 346.010
