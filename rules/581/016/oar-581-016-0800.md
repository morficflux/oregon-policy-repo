# Educational (OAR 581-016-0800)

## Full text

581-016-0800 Educational Materials The Oregon School for the Deaf shall provide textbooks and other instructional materials and equipment which contribute to the attainment of general and Individualized Educational Program (IEP) goals and which are appropriate to each child’s capabilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 343 & 346 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 346.010

History: EB 31-1989, f. & cert. ef. 11-2-89
