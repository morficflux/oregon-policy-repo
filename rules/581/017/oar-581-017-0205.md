## Full text

581-017-0205 Establishment

(1) The Mentoring, Monitoring, Acceleration Grant is established as part of the Guidance and Support for Post-Secondary Aspirations Strategic Investment.

(2) The purposes of the grant is to: (a) Extend or expand a students’ ability to achieve a “C” or higher within the current academic year in all core subjects; (b) Increase the academic achievement for underserved and at-risk students in addition to the cultural competence within academic communities; and (c) Create, expand or replicate programs that achieve the purposes of the grant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 327.800 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 327.815
