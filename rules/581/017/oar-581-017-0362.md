## Full text

581-017-0362 Timelines and Performance Measures The Regional Promise program shall provide award recipients a template for an interim, legislative, and final grant report. Recipients are required to submit the all reports and teacher and student data prior to their final request for funds.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 327.800 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 327.820
