## Full text

581-017-0395 Reporting The Oregon Department of Education shall provide to grant recipients a template for an interim and final grant report. Grantees are required to submit a final report prior to receiving their final request for funds.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.051 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 336.079
