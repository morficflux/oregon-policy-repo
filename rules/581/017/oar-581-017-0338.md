## Full text

581-017-0338 Eligibility of STEM Lab Schools

(1) The Oregon Department of Education may allocate funds for STEM Lab Schools to the following entities: (a) Public Schools; (b) School districts; (c) Student-focused nonprofit organizations; or (d) Postsecondary institutions for the purpose of supporting STEM education.

(2) A single grant proposal may include more than one eligible applicant and other entities but the lead agency for the proposal must be one of entities listed in subsection

(1) of this rule.
