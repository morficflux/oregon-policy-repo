## Full text

581-017-0714 Competitive Oregon Farm-to-School Grant for Reimbursement of Oregon Foods: Establishment The purpose of the competitive Oregon Farm-to-School grant for reimbursement of Oregon Foods is to encourage and sustain successful efforts by eligible entities to purchase or promote foods that were:

(1) Produced in the State of Oregon;

(2) More than minimally processed in the State of Oregon; or

(3) Both produced and processed in the State of Oregon.
