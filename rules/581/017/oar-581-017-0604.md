## Full text

581-017-0604 Purpose: FFA Summer Duty Extended Contract Grant The purpose of the FFA Summer Duty Extended Contract Grant is to fund extended duty contracts in school districts to allow school district personnel to manage approved Agriculture Science and Technology Programs of Study during the summer months between school years.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2019 OL Ch. 588, Sec. 3 (Enrolled HB 2444) Statutes/Other Implemented: 2019 OL Ch. 588, Sec. 3 (Enrolled HB 2444)
