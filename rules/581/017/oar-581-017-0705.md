## Full text

581-017-0705 Latino/a/x and Indigenous Student Success Plan Grant Program: Funding

(1) Applicants must submit a detailed budget that explains how Grant funds will be used.

(2) Administrative costs, which include indirect costs, will be allowed as a percentage of the Grant funds disbursed as follows: (a) Up to five percent for school districts, educational service districts, early learning hubs, or post-secondary institutions of education; or (b) Up to fifteen percent for Community-Based Organizations, Tribes, or providers of early learning services.
