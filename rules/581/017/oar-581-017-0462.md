## Full text

581-017-0462 Reporting Recipients of the CTE Teacher Preparation Program grant must report on the grant to the Department of Education. The report must include metrics developed by the Department of Education.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2015 OL Ch. 763 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3072) Statutes/Other Implemented: 2015 OL Ch. 763 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3072)
