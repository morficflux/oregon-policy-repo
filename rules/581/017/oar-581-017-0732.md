## Full text

581-017-0732 Intensive Program & Student Success Teams: Purpose

(1) The purpose of the intensive program is to assist school districts with the highest needs.

(2) For the purpose of assisting school districts participating in the intensive program, a Leadership Steward, a Teaching and Learning Steward, and a Community Steward will work with local personnel as part of the Stewarding Body of the Student Success Team.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 327.222 & ORS 327.175 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 327.222
