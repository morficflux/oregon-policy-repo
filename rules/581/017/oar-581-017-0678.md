## Full text

581-017-0678 American Indian/Alaska Native Student Success Plan Grant Program: Establishment

(1) There is established the American Indian/ Alaska Native Student Success Plan Grant Program to support early learning hubs, providers of early learning services, school districts, education service districts, post-secondary institutions of education, Community-Based Organizations, and Tribes that are working to design, implement, improve, expand, or otherwise revise programs and services for American Indian/ Alaska Native students.

(2) Programs and services provided under a Grant must address one or more of the strategies or objectives included in the American Indian/Alaska Native Student Success Plan.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 329.843 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329.843
