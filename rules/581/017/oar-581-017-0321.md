## Full text

581-017-0321 Establishment of the STEM Innovation Grants The purpose of the STEM Innovation Grant Program is to award grants that expand the implementation of effective programs relating to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, that:

(1) Propose innovative approaches to STEM-based education; or

(2) Provide professional development relating to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
