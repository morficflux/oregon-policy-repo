## Full text

581-017-0333 Reporting of STEM Innovation Grant Recipients of the STEM Innovation Grant must report on the grant to the Department of Education. The report must include metrics developed by the Department of Education, in collaboration with the STEM Council.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2013 OL Ch. 661 & Sec. 4 (Enrolled HB 3232) Statutes/Other Implemented: 2013 OL Ch. 661 & Sec. 4 (Enrolled HB 3232)
