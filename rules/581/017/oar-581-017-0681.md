## Full text

581-017-0681 American Indian/Alaska Native Student Success Plan Grant Program: Eligibility To be eligible for an American Indian/Alaska Native Student Success Grant, an entity must:

(1) Be an early learning hub, a provider of early learning services, a school district; an education service district; a post-secondary institution of education, Tribes, a Community-Based Organization or a Culturally Specific Organization; and

(2) Serve a percentage or number of American Indian/ Alaska Native student and youth populations as determined by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 329.843 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329.843
