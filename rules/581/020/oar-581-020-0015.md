## Full text

581-020-0015 Eligibility: School Improvement and Professional Development Grant A school district, an education service district, a state-operated school, or a combination of such entities may submit an application to the Department of Education to receive a School Improvement and Professional Development grant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 336 & 896 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329.695
