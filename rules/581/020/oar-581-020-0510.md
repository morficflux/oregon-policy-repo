# Oregon Teacher (OAR 581-020-0510)

## At a glance

## Full text

581-020-0510 Oregon Teacher Teaching CORE classes in Oregon requires State licensure as well as teaching in the content area of approval as outlined by NCLB. This applies to all public schools and is defined in OAR 584-005-0005 #74, 584-005-0005 #21 and ORS 342.120 for Charter School instruction. All instructors must be licensed by the Teachers Standards and Practices Commission and highly qualified as defined by TSPC.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 319.840 & 326.051 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 319.840
