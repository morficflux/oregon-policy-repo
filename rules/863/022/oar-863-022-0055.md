## Full text

863-022-0055 Board Approval of Law and Rule Required Course

(1) The Board will develop the course content required for the course on recent changes in real estate rule and law required by ORS 696.174, and the Agency will make the course content available to the public.

(2) The Board will update the course at least biannually.

(3) To be eligible for continuing education credit required under OAR 863-020-0010, the Board-approved course must be provided by a certified continuing education provider.

(4) This course is referred to in these rules as the "Law and Rule Required Course".

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 696.385 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 696.174, 696.182 & 696.184
