## Full text

863-001-0005 Rules of Procedure The Real Estate Agency adopts by reference the Attorney General's Uniform and Model Rules OAR 137-001-0005 through 137-005-0070 bearing the effective date of January 1, 2008. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Real Estate Agency.]
