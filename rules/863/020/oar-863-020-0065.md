## Full text

863-020-0065 Certification Revocation The Agency may revoke the continuing education provider’s certification if the provider disregards or violates any applicable provision of ORS Chapter 696 or this rule division. Such revocation is an agency order subject to the contested case provisions contained in ORS Chapter 183.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 696.385 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 696.182
