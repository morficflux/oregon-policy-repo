## Full text

863-020-0015 Licensee Records

(1) A real estate licensee must maintain the licensee’s certificates of completion the licensee received from continuing education providers for three years after the renewal date for which the continuing education credit applies.

(2) A n authorized principal broker is not required to maintain the continuing education records of a real estate broker , principal real estate broker, or property manager under the supervision of the authorized principal broker.

(3) Upon request by the Agency, a licensee must produce a copy of the records required under this rule.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 696.385 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 696.174
