# Condominium Onsite Inspection Expenses (OAR 863-060-0015)

## Full text

863-060-0015 Condominium Onsite Inspection Expenses Within a reasonable time after completion of the examination of a condominium under ORS 100.710 for which a developer has advanced a deposit, the Commissioner shall account to the developer for the Commissioner’s onsite inspection expenses. If the expenses incurred by the Commissioner exceed the funds deposited by the developer, the Commissioner shall bill the developer for the balance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 91, 94, 100 & 696 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 100.700
