## Full text

863-014-0000 Applicability and Purpose

(1) This division sets forth the requirements and process for licensing real estate brokers and principal real estate brokers, as those terms are defined in ORS 696.010.

(2) The purpose of this division is to specify the requirements for obtaining the desired real estate license.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 696.385 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 696.020 & 696.022
