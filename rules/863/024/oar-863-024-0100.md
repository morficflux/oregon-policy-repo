## Full text

863-024-0100 Branch Office Registration

(1) Before a real estate property manager may engage in the management of rental real estate from a branch office, the real estate property manager must provide to the Commissioner an online application available through the Agency’s website the branch office street and mailing addresses and the fee authorized by ORS 696.270.

(2) For the purposes of ORS 696.270, a branch office registration does not require renewal.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 696.385 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 696.026 & ORS 696.200
