## Full text

863-050-0015 Documents or Property Held in Escrow Except as otherwise provided in ORS 696.581, an escrow agent must use documents or other property deposited in escrow only in accordance with the dated written instructions of the principals to the escrow transactions or pursuant to court order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 696.385 & 696.541 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 696.581
