# Attorney's (OAR 863-015-0155)

## Full text

863-015-0155 Attorney’s Advice A real estate licensee must not discourage any party to a real estate transaction from seeking the advice of an attorney concerning any matter involving real estate activity in which such licensee is involved.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 696.385 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 696.805, 696.810 & 696.815
