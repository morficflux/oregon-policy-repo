## Full text

543-010-0017 Appearances Before the Board The policy of the Board is to hold public meetings and to permit all persons, groups and organizations having relevant business or opinions to appear before them and be heard.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 357.026(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.630
