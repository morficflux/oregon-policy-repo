## Full text

543-010-0034 Hours of Service The Oregon State Library Building will be open from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday, excepting legal holidays, and may be open at other times by special arrangement with the State Librarian. The State Library Board authorizes the State Librarian to set times for public access to materials and services. Schedules will be available to the public in a State Library policy.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 357.026(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 357.026(1)
