## Full text

543-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure Pursuant to provisions of ORS 183.341, the State Library adopts the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure as amended effective July 2019. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or State Library. ]
