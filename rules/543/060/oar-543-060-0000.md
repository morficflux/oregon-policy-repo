## Full text

543-060-0000 Scope OAR chapter 543, division 60, applies to programs that assist libraries to provide reference services, including statewide licensing of electronic databases in public, school, academic, and tribal libraries and statewide cooperative reference services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 357.008(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 357.206, 357.209 & 357.212
