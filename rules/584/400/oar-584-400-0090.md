## Full text

584-400-0090 Elimination of Programs

(1) The Educator Preparation Provider (EPP) must notify the Commission of the elimination of a state recognized licensure, endorsement, or specialization program. The notification must include: (a) The date the licensure, endorsement, or specialization program will be eliminated (for active programs), or was eliminated (for inactive programs); (b) The reason(s) for the elimination of the program; and (c) Teach-out plans, to assure enrolled candidates are able to complete the program.

(2) If an EPP has not recommended any candidates from a state recognized program for five continuous years, the Commission may review the state recognition of the program. (a) The Commission must notify the EPP if a program will be reviewed due to inactivity. (b) If the EPP would like to continue to offer the inactive program, the EPP must submit documentation on the inactive program to the Commission, as provided in the Program Review and Standards Handbook.
