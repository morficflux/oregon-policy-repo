## Full text

584-023-0015 Standards of Professional Practices and Ethics for Charter School Registrants The provisions of ORS 342.120 to 342.430 and the administrative rules in OAR chapter 584 relating to the issuance, denial, continuation, renewal, lapse, revocation, suspension or reinstatements of licenses shall be applicable to all teachers and administrators holding a charter school registration.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 342 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 342.125 & 338.135
