# Model Rules of Procedure (OAR 584-001-0005)

## At a glance

## Full text

584-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure Pursuant to ORS 183.341, the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure for Contested Cases, effective in December 2024; the Collaborative Dispute Resolution Model Rules, effective in December 2024 and the Model Rules For Rulemaking, effective in December 2024, are by this reference adopted as the rules of procedure for the Teacher Standards and Practices Commission and shall be controlling except as otherwise required by statute or rule. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Teacher Standards and Practices Commission.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
