## Full text

167-050-0100 Statement of Purpose Purpose. These rules control the Secretary of State’s acquisition of information about a subject individual’s criminal history through criminal records checks and its use of that information to determine whether the subject individual is fit to provide services to the Secretary of State as an employee or contractor in a position covered by OAR 167-050-0120(2)(a)–(e). The fact that the Secretary of State approves a subject individual as fit does not guarantee the individual a position as a Secretary of State employee or contractor.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.534 & ORS 177.075 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.534(9)
