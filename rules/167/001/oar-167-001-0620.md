## Full text

167-001-0620 Specifications Secretary staff and providers may not develop specifications that primarily benefit only one provider, directly or indirectly.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065(5)(a), 279A.070 & 279A.140
