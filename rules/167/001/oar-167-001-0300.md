## Full text

167-001-0300 Electronic Public Notice The Secretary may publish the advertisement (notice) for Offers on the Department of Administrative Services ORPIN Electronic Procurement System, or subsequent system, instead of publishing notice in a newspaper of general circulation as required by ORS 279B.055(4)(b). In addition, the Secretary may publish the advertisement on the Secretary's web-page.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065, 279A.070 & 279B.055
