## Full text

167-001-0020 Purpose The Divisions of the Oregon Secretary of State (Secretary) occasionally require the services of an outside party to accomplish all or part of a project. The Secretary intends that the Attorney General's Model Public Contracting Rules, Chapter 137, will govern all Secretary of State public contracting. It is the intent of the Secretary that these Model Rules will be accepted by default in accordance with the provisions of ORS 279A.065(4). These rules supplement and do not replace the Attorney General’s Model Public Contract Rules.
