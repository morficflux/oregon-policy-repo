## Full text

167-001-0630 Fragmentation A procurement may not be artificially divided or fragmented so as to constitute a small procurement, pursuant to ORS 279B.065, or an intermediate procurement, pursuant to 279B.070, in order to avoid competition.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065(5)(a), 279A.070 & 279B.065
