## Full text

167-001-0007 Application of Rules These OAR chapter 167, division 001 rules apply to public contracts of the Secretary of State first advertised, but if not advertised then entered into, on or after March 1, 2014.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.020, 279A.030 & 279A.065
