# Basic (OAR 167-001-0030)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

167-001-0030 Basic Policy The Secretary will contract for services when the specialized skills, knowledge or resources are not available within the Secretary of State's office; when the work cannot be done in a reasonable time with the Secretary's own work force; when it will be less expensive to contract for the work; or when an independent and impartial evaluation of a situation by recognized professionals is required. Contracts will be granted only after approval of the Secretary or his/her designee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 & 279A.070 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.015
