## Full text

848-010-0020 Endorsement of Out-of-State Physical Therapists and Physical Therapist Assistants Physical therapists and physical therapist assistants not licensed in the State of Oregon may be licensed by endorsement if they comply with all of the following:

(1) File a completed application form, pay a non-refunable application fee and submit required documents. All applications are valid for six months from the date first received by the Board.

(2) Are at least 18 years of age.

(3) Are graduates of an approved school for physical therapists or physical therapist assistants as provided in OAR 848-010-0010 and 848-010-0015(3).

(4) At the time of application, applicant holds a valid, unrestricted license in any other state or territory of the United States.

(5) Have passed the physical therapist or physical therapist assistant examination provided by a Board-approved examination service.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.080
