## Full text

848-020-0010 Eligibility Standards A physical therapist aide shall:

(1) Be at least 18 years of age.

(2) Have received a high school diploma or equivalent.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.160 & 688.210
