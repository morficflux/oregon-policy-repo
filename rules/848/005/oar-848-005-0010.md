## Full text

848-005-0010 Establish Physical Therapist Licensing Board Budget for 25-27 Biennium. The Physical Therapist Licensing Board hereby adopts by reference the Oregon Board of Physical Therapy 2025-2027 Biennium Budget of $2,245 ,651 covering the period from July 1, 2025 through June 30, 2027. The Executive Director of the Board will amend budgeted accounts as necessary within the approved budget of $2,245,651 for the effective operation of the Board. The Board will not exceed the approved 2025-2027 Biennium Budget expenditures without amending this rule, notifying holders of licenses, and holding a public hearing thereon as required, by ORS Chapter 182.462(1) and (2). Copies of the budget are available from the Board's office and posted on the Board’s website.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.210 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.160(5)(c)
