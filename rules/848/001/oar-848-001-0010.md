## Full text

848-001-0010 Time for Requesting a Contested Case Hearing A request for a contested case hearing must be in writing and must be received by the Board within thirty

(30) days from the date the proposed notice of disciplinary action was served.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 688.160
