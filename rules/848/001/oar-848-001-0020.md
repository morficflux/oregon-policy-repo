## Full text

848-001-0020 Filing Exceptions to the Board After a proposed order has been served on a party, the Board shall notify the party when written exceptions must be filed to be considered by the Board. A licensee may request to appear before the Board to discuss the proposed sanction before the Board issues a Final Order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 688.160
