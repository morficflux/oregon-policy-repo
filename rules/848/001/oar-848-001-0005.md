## Full text

848-001-0005 Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure The following Model Rules of Procedure promulgated by the Attorney General of the State of Oregon, in effect January 01, 2018, are adopted by the Board by reference. These rules apply to rulemaking; OAR 137-001-0005 through 137-001-0100. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Physical Therapist Licensing Board.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 688.160
