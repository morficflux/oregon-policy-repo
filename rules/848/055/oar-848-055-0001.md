## Full text

848-055-0001 Compact Commission Rules

(1) Pursuant to ORS 688.240 Section 9.A.2, the Oregon Board of Physical Therapy hereby adopts as rule the Physical Therapy Compact Commission Rules as adopted by the Commission effective December 2025, with the following additions: (a) These rules are adopted only to the extent that the Compact Commission Rules are consistent with Oregon and Federal law, as determined by the Board. (b) Pursuant to Article XI, 7 of the Oregon Constitution and per ORS 688.240, any monetary liabilities incurred as a result of arbitration or litigation are subject to the limitations in Oregon law. (c) The Oregon Board of Physical Therapy will comply with Commission Rule 6.1(C) to the extent that the Board determines that compliance is within the requirements and limitations of law.

(2) The full text of the Commission Rules will be made available for review via the Oregon PT Board website at www.oregon.gov/pt.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.160(6)(c) & ORS 688.240 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.240
