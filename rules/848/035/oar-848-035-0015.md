## Full text

848-035-0015 Definitions As used in this Division:

(1) “Continuing Competence” means continuing education mandatory requirements, course work or other professional activities as provided in OAR 848-035-0030(1),

(2) and (3).

(2) "Certification period" means a 24 month continuing competence certification period which runs from April 1st of each even numbered year, through March 31st of the next even numbered year, (e.g. April 1, 2018 through March 31, 2020).

(3) "Hours" means contact hours.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.160(6)(g)
