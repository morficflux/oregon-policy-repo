## Full text

848-040-0160 Standards For The Documentation of a Reexamination

(1) When a physical therapist is required to perform a reexamination under OAR 848-040-0155, the documentation of the reexamination shall include at a minimum: (a) Subjective status of patient; and, (b) Any change in physical therapy goals (including patient goals) or the plan of care.

(2) After a physical therapist reexamines the patient and documents the reexamination, either the physical therapist or a physical therapist assistant may prepare the documentation of the patient’s current status.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.160 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 688.160, 688.010 & 688.210
