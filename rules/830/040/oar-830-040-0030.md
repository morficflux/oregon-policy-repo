## Full text

830-040-0030 Name Registered with Board Each licensed facility shall be registered with the State Mortuary and Cemetery Board by its true corporate, firm or individual name. In addition, one assumed business name, as registered with the Secretary of State Corporation Division, may be used by such licensed facility and shall be promptly reported to the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 692.320 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 692.025
