# Rental Cover (OAR 830-040-0080)

## Full text

830-040-0080 Rental Cover Where an establishment offers an outside cover for reuse which is to be used in conjunction with a burial or cremation container, the items shall be represented as separate and distinct items. The “cover” is defined as a partial enclosure which appears similar to a casket.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 692.320 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 692.320
