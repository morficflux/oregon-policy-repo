## Full text

830-040-0095 Lists Maintained by the Oregon Mortuary & Cemetery Board

(1) Pursuant to ORS 97.190, the Board will maintain a list of person or persons authorized to grant consent for an exception to the moratorium on post mortem examination of bodies under ORS 97.170, as well as instructions for requesting the exception.

(2) Pursuit to ORS 97.170(2) and (3)(G)(b), the Board will maintain a list of institutions that may accept or process bodies for education or research purposes. The Board will also provide information on how to contact these institutions. The Board may alternately name a person or persons to whom maintaining this list is delegated.
