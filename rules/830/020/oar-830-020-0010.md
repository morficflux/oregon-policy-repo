## Full text

830-020-0010 License Fee Due After Examination A license fee which is not received at the office of the Board within 30 days following receipt of notice of successful completion of the examination and completion of the apprenticeship for funeral service practitioner or embalmer shall be considered delinquent and shall be subject to the reinstatement provisions of ORS 692.170 for lapsed licenses.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 692.320 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 692.148 & 692.160
