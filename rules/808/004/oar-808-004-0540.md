## Full text

808-004-0540 Establishing Monetary Damages, Issuing Proposed Default Order or Referring Claim for Hearing If the claimant seeks monetary damages or the agency so requests, the claimant shall file a declaration of damages stating the amount the claimant alleges the respondent owes the claimant, limited to claim items listed in the Statement of Claim. The agency may require the claimant to submit, in support of the amount alleged:

(1) One or more estimates from licensed landscape contracting businesses for the cost of correction of each of the claim items; or

(2) Other basis for monetary award.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.415, 183.460, 183.470 & 671.703
