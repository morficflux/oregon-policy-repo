## Full text

808-004-0120 Liability of Landscape Contracting Business A licensed landscape contracting business participating in a corporation wholly-owned by the landscape contracting business, or a limited liability partnership, limited liability company, joint venture, limited partnership or partnership, may be held liable for claim actions brought under ORS 671.690 to 671.710, whether or not the corporation, limited liability partnership, limited liability company, joint venture, limited partnership or partnership was licensed as required by ORS chapter 671.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310 - 183.500, 670.310 & 671 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.
