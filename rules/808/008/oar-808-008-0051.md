## Full text

808-008-0051 Application for Judicial Relief An application to the court for judicial relief under the rules in division 8 of this chapter or under ORS 36.600 to 36.740 shall be subject to ORS 36.615.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: Ch. 598 & OL 2003
