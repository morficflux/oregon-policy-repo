## Full text

808-008-0060 Appointment of Arbitrator Appointment of arbitrator shall be as provided in ORS 671.703 and shall be subject to a request for a different administrative law judge to act as arbitrator under ORS 183.645 and OAR 471-060-0005.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.703 & 183.645
