## Full text

808-008-0460 Filing with Court; Exceptions

(1) A party may petition the court to confirm an award under ORS 36.700. The petitioning party shall serve the agency with a copy of a petition filed under this section.

(2) A party may petition the court to vacate, modify or correct an award under ORS 36.705 or 36.710. The petitioning party shall serve the agency with a copy of the petition filed under this section.

(3) Failure of a party to serve the agency under section

(2) and

(3) of this rule constitutes a waiver of any objection to transmittal of the award to respondent’s surety company for payment under OAR 808-004-0600.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310 - 183.500, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 671.703
