## Full text

808-008-0180 Representation by Counsel Any party may be represented at an arbitration by an attorney. A corporation, partnership or other business entity may appear or be represented at an arbitration as provided under ORS 183.457.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310 - 183.500, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 671.703(3)
