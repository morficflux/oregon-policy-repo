## Full text

808-008-0220 Postponement, Recess and Continuance An arbitration may postpone or recess and later continued by the arbitrator. A party requesting a postponement or continuance must show good cause. The arbitrator will determine whether to grant a postponement or continuance. The arbitrator’s determination shall be final.
