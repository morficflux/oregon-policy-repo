## Full text

808-008-0360 Close of Hearings When satisfied that the parties have completed their presentations, the arbitrator shall close the hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310 - 183.500, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 671.703(3)
