## Full text

808-001-0015 Election of Officers The Board may elect a Board Chairman and Vice Chairman at the first board meeting held after January 1 of each year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 671 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 670.300 & 671.630
