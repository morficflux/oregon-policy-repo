## Full text

808-040-0070 Waivers

(1) CEH waivers. The Board, in its discretion, may waive CEH requirements for: (a) Reasons of health, certified by a medical doctor, that prevents the licensee from complying with CEH requirements; (b) A licensee who is on active military duty and who provides a copy of orders to active military duty; or (c) Other good cause, to be demonstrated as the Board requests.

(2) Requests for waivers. A request for waiver of CEH requirements must be submitted in writing for each renewal period during which the conditions supporting the waiver exist.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: Ch. 550 OL 2007
