## Full text

808-040-0080 CEH Requirement for Reinstatement to Active Status

(1) Any licensee that reinstates an inactive or expired landscape construction professional license to active status more than 14 days after the expiration of the license must: (a) Comply with OAR 808-003-0255; (b) Complete the CEH required in OAR 808-040-0020 and submit documentation as per the audit requirements of OAR 808-040-0060 for the required CEH obtained within the two years immediately preceding the reinstatement date of the landscape construction professional license; and (c) Meet the CEH requirement for each subsequent renewal period.

(2) Upon honorable discharge to convert a license form inactive military status to active status, the servicemember must submit a written request within 60 days from the date of discharge. No renewal or late fee will be charged until the first renewal date after conversion of that license. No CEH will be required until the first renewal date after conversion of that license

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & ORS 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.676
