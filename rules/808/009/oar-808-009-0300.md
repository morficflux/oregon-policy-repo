## Full text

808-009-0300 Hearing Notice and Limitation on Final Civil Penalty Order A notice of hearing shall contain the amount of the civil penalty sought by the agency. The administrative law judge shall not issue a final civil penalty order in an amount greater than that shown in the notice of hearing.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.415
