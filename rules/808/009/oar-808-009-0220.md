## Full text

808-009-0220 Petition for Reconsideration or Rehearing; Request for Stay A petition for reconsideration or rehearing under OAR 137-003-0675 or a request for a stay under OAR 137-003-0690 of a final order on a claim issued by an administrative law judge under this division must be filed with the agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.415 & 671.703
