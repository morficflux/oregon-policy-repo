## Full text

808-009-0320 Administrative Law Judge; Entry of Agency Evidence Contested case enforcement hearings may be held before an administrative law judge of the agency. The agency’s evidence may be entered into the record by the administrative law judge or by another representative of the agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.415
