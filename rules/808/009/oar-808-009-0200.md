# Final Order without a Proposed Order (OAR 808-009-0200)

## Full text

808-009-0200 Final Order without a Proposed Order If the parties voluntarily agree to a settlement of a claim in accordance with ORS 183.415(5) and the settlement agreement includes an agreement for future performance, the administrative law judge shall issue an intermediate order containing any necessary findings of fact and return the claim to the agency for further processing and issuance of the final order.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183 & 671
