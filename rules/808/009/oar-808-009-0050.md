## Full text

808-009-0050 Providing Required Information to Parties The agency delegates to the Office of Administrative Hearings or the law judge assigned to hear a claim the responsibility to provide the information required to be given to each party under ORS 183.413(2) and 137-003-0510(1).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.415 & 671.703
