## Full text

808-030-0050 Testing Requirements

(1) The test required in ORS 671.595 shall cover the subjects listed in OAR 808-030-0040.

(2) A person seeking to take the test shall: (a) Apply on a form provided by the agency; (b) Pay any fees required by the agency; and (c) Comply with OAR 808-003-0730, 808-003-0760, 808-003-0810, 808-003-0830, 808-003-0840, and 808-003-0850.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & ORS 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.595
