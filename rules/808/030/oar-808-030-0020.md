## Full text

808-030-0020 Course Requirements

(1) The course required in ORS 671.595(4) shall cover the subjects listed in OAR 808-030-0040.

(2) The course must be offered by a provider approved by the agency as provided in OAR 808-030-0030.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & ORS 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.595
