# Legal Entity (OAR 808-002-0560)

## At a glance

## Full text

808-002-0560 Legal Entity “Legal Entity” includes, but is not limited to, an individual, a partnership, a limited liability partnership, a corporation, a limited liability company, an association, or a firm.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.325 - 183.410, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.510 - 671.720
