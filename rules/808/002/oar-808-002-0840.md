# Residential (OAR 808-002-0840)

## Full text

808-002-0840 Residential Property "Residential Property" as used in ORS 671.540(1)(h) and (i) means property upon which one or more "residential dwellings," as defined in 808-002-0820 are constructed or will be constructed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.540
