# Employ (OAR 808-002-0340)

## At a glance

OAR 808-002-0340 — Employ. Chapter 808, Division 002. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

808-002-0340 Employ "Employ," as used in ORS 671.530(6), 671.565(1)(b) and 671.610(2)(f), means to hire an employee, as defined in OAR 808-002-0360, and thereafter be subject to ORS Chapters 654, 656, 657 and state and federal wage and hour laws.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.610(1)(o)
