## Full text

808-002-0625 Managing Employee The term “Managing Employee” is defined as any individual, including a general manager, business manager, or administrator employed by a landscape contracting business who exercises operational or managerial control over the business activities of the landscape contracting business. An individual can only be a managing employee of one landscaping business at a time.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: Ch. 249 OL 2007
