## Full text

808-002-0490 Landscape Edging "Landscape edging," as used in ORS 671.520(1)(f), means concrete, metal, plastic, wood or other material that is used to separate different elements of a landscape from each other.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.520
