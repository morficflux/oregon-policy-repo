## Full text

808-002-0680 Nature and Complexity “Nature or Complexity” as used in ORS 671.703 includes, but is not limited to the following meaning:

(1) Involves issues requiring legal interpretation of statutes and case law that are not normally part of a landscaping claim;

(2) In the interest of fairness and equity, requires rulings against persons or entities outside the jurisdiction of the agency; or

(3) Involves issues and fact determinations that are outside the expertise of the agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.325 - 183.410, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.703
