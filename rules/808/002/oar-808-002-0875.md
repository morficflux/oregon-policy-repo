# Rough Grading (OAR 808-002-0875)

## At a glance

## Full text

808-002-0875 Rough Grading “Rough grading,” for purposes of ORS 671.540(1)(e), means the movement of earth by cutting and/or filling of a site to establish proper sub-grade elevations prior to the preparation and establishment of the final grade for seed beds or tree or shrub planting. Rough grading does not include grading done by raking or other mechanical means to establish a grade that is suitable for planting.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.540(5)

History: LCB 6-2005, f. 12-30-05, cert. ef. 1-1-06
