## Full text

808-002-0882 Under Contract for the Construction of a New Dwelling “Under contract for the construction of a new dwelling” as used in ORS 671.540(1)(h) means a residential general contractor has agreed, pursuant to a written contract, to construct a new residential dwelling for the owner in a location on the property where a structure did not previously exist or the existing structure is completely demolished, including the foundation, and removed prior to the new construction. For the purposes of this rule, remodeling or modifying an existing structure is not considered construction of a new dwelling.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310, 671.670 & 2009 OL Ch. 483 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.520 & 671.540

History: LCB 13-2009, f. 12-23-09, cert. ef. 1-1-10
