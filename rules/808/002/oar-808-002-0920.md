# Work (OAR 808-002-0920)

## At a glance

OAR 808-002-0920 — Work. Chapter 808, Division 002. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

808-002-0920 Work Period "Work Period" as used in OAR 808-004-0320 and 808-004-0600 means the time period from the date a landscape contracting business accepts a payment, offers a written or verbal proposal which is later accepted as a contract or enters into a contract or begins construction, whichever occurs first, until the date the work is completed as defined in OAR 808-002-0280 or the landscape contracting business ceases work.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.325 - 183.410, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.710
