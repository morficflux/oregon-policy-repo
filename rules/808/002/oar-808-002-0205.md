# Charges (OAR 808-002-0205)

## At a glance

## Full text

808-002-0205 Charges “Charges” as used in ORS 671.690 means any compensation that a landscape contracting business asks for to perform landscaping work.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.690

History: LCB 7-2009, f. & cert. ef. 10-28-09
