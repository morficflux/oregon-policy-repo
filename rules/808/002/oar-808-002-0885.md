# Valid "Valid," as used in ORS 671 (OAR 808-002-0885)

## At a glance

OAR 808-002-0885 — Valid "Valid," as used in ORS 671. Chapter 808, Division 002. Statutory authority and AON

## Full text

808-002-0885 Valid "Valid," as used in ORS 671.530 and OAR Chapter 808, means a landscape contracting business license or a landscape construction professional license that is active.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.530(1)
