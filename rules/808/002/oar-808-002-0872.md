# Retaining (OAR 808-002-0872)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

808-002-0872 Retaining Walls Landscape "retaining walls" as used in ORS 671 and this chapter is a structure designed to a height exceeding six

(6) inches from the lowest finish grade and constructed to resist the lateral pressure of soil, when there is an actual, or desired change in ground elevation that exceeds the angle of repose of the soil.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 671.760 & ORS 670.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.520 & ORS 671.540

History: LCB 5-2019, adopt filed 09/24/2019, effective 09/24/2019
