## Full text

808-002-0870 Respondent “Respondent” as used in this chapter, means a landscape contracting business that a claim is filed against under ORS 671.690 to 671.710 or that the board proposes to impose a penalty against under 671.510 to 671.625 and 671.950 to 671.992.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.325 - 183.410, 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.690 - 671.710
