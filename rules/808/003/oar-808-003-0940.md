## Full text

808-003-0940 Practical Skills Exam Retakes

(1) An applicant wanting to retake any practical skills exam sections must schedule testing by registering with the exam provided as stated in OAR 808-003-0910.

(2) An applicant must pay the exam fee as required in OAR 808-003-0710 to retake any sections of the exam.
