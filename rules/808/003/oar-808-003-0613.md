808-003-0613 Bond, Letter of Credit or Cash Deposit Amounts

(1) Except as provided in subsection

(2) of this rule, a surety bond, letter of credit or cash deposit required under ORS 671.690 must be in the amount of $ 2,000.

(2) A surety bond, letter of credit or cash deposit required under ORS 671.571 for a Probationary All Phase Plus Backflow license phase must in the amoutn of not less than $15,000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & ORS 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.690
