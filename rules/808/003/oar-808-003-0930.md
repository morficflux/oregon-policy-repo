## Full text

808-003-0930 Scoring of Practical Skills Exam Sections

(1) Each practical skills exam section will be scored separately.

(2) Pursuant to ORS 671.561, the passing score for a practical skills exam shall be determined by the National Association of Landscape Professionals (NALP).

(3) Except as provided in OAR 808-003-0730 a passing score for a practical skills exam section shall be valid until the third December 31st following passage of that exam section. An applicant who fails to pass all the sections required for a particular license phase and obtain that license phase by the third December 31st following passage of that section shall retake that section.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.561
