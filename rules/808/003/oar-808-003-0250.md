## Full text

808-003-0250 Renewal of Inactive Status To renew an inactive license in an inactive status:

(1) The licensee must submit the request to renew the license in inactive status on forms provided by the board;

(2) The licensee must submit the fees required under OAR 808-003-0130.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.568 & 671.574
