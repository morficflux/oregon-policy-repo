## Full text

808-003-0440 Notification of Conviction of a Crime A sole proprietor or partner in a partnership or individual landscape construction professional licensed by the agency who is convicted of a crime listed in ORS 671.610(1)(q) must notify the agency in writing within 30 days from the date of the entry of the judgment of conviction.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310 & 671.670 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 671.610

History: LCB 4-2007, f. 12-19-07, cert. ef. 1-1-08 LCCB 3-2007, f. & cert. ef. 8-1-07
