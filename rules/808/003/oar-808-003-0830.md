## Full text

808-003-0830 Conventional Examination and LCB Prep Course & Exam Retakes

(1) An applicant wanting to retake any conventional exam section or LCB Prep Course & Exam Module must schedule testing at an approved testing center in Oregon or in another jurisdiction as stated in OAR 808-003-0810.

(2) An applicant must pay the exam fee directly to the approved testing center.
