## Full text

851-070-0075 Approval of Worksite Monitors Board approved worksite monitors must:

(1) Be a nurse with an unencumbered license or other identified health professional who holds an unencumbered license, as approved by the Board.

(2) Conduct routine observation and monitoring of certificate holder or licensee’s performance. The worksite monitor may serve as the supervisor if the observation requirements are delegated to another licensed individual who meets the requirements.

(3) Provide evidence of relevant specialized education as approved by the Board.

(4) Agree in writing to perform the worksite monitor role.

(5) Complete a written report with input from worksite monitors.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.200 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.200
