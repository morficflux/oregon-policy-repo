## Full text

851-001-0000 Notice of Proposed Rulemaking Prior to adoption, amendment or repeal of any rule, the Board of Nursing shall give notice of the intended action per ORS 183.335 and ORS 183.360.

(1) In the Secretary of State’s Bulletin at least 21 days before the effective date of the intended action.

(2) Mail a copy of the rule hearing notice to persons on the Board of Nursing established mailing list(s) at least 28 days before the effective date of the rule.

(3) If applicable, at least 49 days before the effective date of the rule, the Board shall provide notice to the legislative committee identified in ORS 183.335(15).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 678.150, ORS 183. 355 & ORS 183.360 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 678.150 & ORS 678.335
