## Full text

851-002-0020 Nurse Practitioner Schedule of Fees

(1) Initial License — $250.

(2) Renewal — $205.

(3) Surcharge to Support the Prescription Monitoring Fund — $70.

(4) Additional Population Focus: Initial and Renewal — $50.

(5) Delinquent Fee — $100.

(6) Prescriptive Authority Initial Application — $75.

(7) Temporary License for Active Armed Forces Military Spouse or Domestic Partner — $50 .

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 678.150 & ORS 678.410 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 678.410 & ORS 431A.880
