## Full text

851-045-0030 Purpose of Standards and Scope of Practice

(1) To identify licensed practical nurse (LPN) scope in the practice of nursing.

(2) To identify registered nurse (RN) scope in the practice of nursing.

(3) To establish standards of nursing practice.

(4) All standards of nursing practice and RN scope of practice standards apply to the advanced practice registered nurse (APRN).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 678.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 678.150 & 678.010
