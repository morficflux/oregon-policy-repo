## Full text

851-062-0080 Oregon CMA Certification A CMA must have a current Oregon CMA certificate and be listed on the Oregon CNA Registry prior to performing medication aide duties.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 678.442 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 678.442
