# Purpose (OAR 660-033-0010)

## At a glance

## Full text

660-033-0010 Purpose The purpose of this division is to preserve and maintain agricultural lands as defined by Goal 3 for farm use, and to implement ORS 215.203 through 215.327 and 215.438 through 215.459 and 215.700 through 215.799.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 197.040, 197.230 & 197.245 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.015, 197.040, 197.230, 197.245, 215.203, 215.243 & 215.700
