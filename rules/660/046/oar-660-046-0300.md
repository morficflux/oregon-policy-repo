## Full text

660-046-0300 Purpose of Infrastructure-Based Time Extension Request Process OAR 660-046-0300 to OAR 660-046-0370 establish the form and substance of the IBTER application and review process. The purpose of these rules is to provide submittal requirements, including required data and analyses that a local government must submit with an IBTER, prescribe when a local government is eligible for a time extension in response to an IBTER, and to provide the evaluation process and criteria that the department will use to review IBTERs and issue Time Extensions.
