## Full text

660-013-0110 Other Uses Within the Airport Boundary Notwithstanding the provisions of OAR 660-013-0100, a local government may authorize commercial, industrial, manufacturing and other uses in addition to those listed in OAR 660-013-0100 within the airport boundary where such uses are consistent with applicable provisions of the acknowledged comprehensive plan, statewide planning goals and LCDC administrative rules and where the uses do not create a safety hazard or otherwise limit approved airport uses.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 197 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 836.600 - 836.630 & 1997 OL & Ch. 859
