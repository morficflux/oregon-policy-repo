## Full text

660-006-0020 Plan Designation Within an Urban Growth Boundary Goal 4 does not apply within urban growth boundaries and therefore, the designation of forest lands is not required.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.040, 197.230, 197.245 & Ch. 792 & 1993 OL
