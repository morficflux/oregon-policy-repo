# Rezoning Land to an Agriculture/Forest Zone (OAR 660-006-0057)

## Full text

660-006-0057 Rezoning Land to an Agriculture/Forest Zone Any rezoning or plan map amendment of lands from an acknowledged zone or plan designation to an agriculture/forest zone requires a demonstration that each area being rezoned or replanned contains such a mixture of agriculture and forest uses that neither Goal 3 nor 4 can be applied alone.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197.040, 197.230 & 197.245 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.040, 197.230, 197.245, 215.213, 215.283, 215.700, 215.705, 215.720, 215.740, 215.750, 215.780 & Ch. 792 & 1993 OL
