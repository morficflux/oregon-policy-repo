## Full text

660-014-0060 Annexations of Lands Subject to an Acknowledged Comprehensive Plan A city annexation made in compliance with a comprehensive plan acknowledged pursuant to ORS 197.251(1) or 197.625 shall be considered by the commission to have been made in accordance with the goals unless the acknowledged comprehensive plan and implementing ordinances do not control the annexation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 196 & 197 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 195, 196 & 197
