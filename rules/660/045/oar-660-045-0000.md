## Full text

660-045-0000 Purpose The purpose of this division is to establish uniform procedures for citizen-initiated enforcement orders, as provided in ORS 197.319 to 197.335 and 197.646.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 197 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.319 - 197.335 & 197.646
