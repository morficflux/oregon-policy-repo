660-036-0003 Territorial Sea Plan: Ocean Policies The Land Conservation and Development Commission adopts as part of the Oregon Coastal Management Program, and herein incorporates by reference, an amendment to the Territorial Sea Plan entitled Ocean Management Goals and Policies that was approved by the Ocean Policy Advisory Council on June 4, 1999.
