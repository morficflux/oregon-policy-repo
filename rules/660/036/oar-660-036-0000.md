## Full text

660-036-0000 Territorial Sea Plan The Land Conservation and Development Commission adopts and herein incorporates by reference as part of the Oregon Coastal Management Program, the Territorial Sea Plan:

(1) Part One (Ocean Management Framework) approved by the Ocean Policy Advisory Council on August 12, 1994, and as amended May 4, 2001.

(2) Part Two (Making Resource Use Decisions) approved by the Ocean Policy Advisory Council on August 12, 1994.

(3) Part Three (Rocky Habitat Management Strategy) approved by the Ocean Policy Advisory Council on November 4, 2021 and as amended on December 9, 2022.
