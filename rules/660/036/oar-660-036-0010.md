660-036-0010 Ocean Resources Management Plan The Land Conservation and Development Commission adopts and herein incorporates by reference the Ocean Resources Management Plan adopted by Commission Order #90-OCEAN-699, December 12, 1990, and amendments to the Ocean Resources Management Plan as approved by the Ocean Policy Advisory Council on March 11, 1994 and June 10, 1994.
