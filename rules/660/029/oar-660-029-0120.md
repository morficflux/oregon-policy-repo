## Full text

660-029-0120 Amending or Abolishing a TDC System If a county amends or abolishes a TDC system, the county shall notify the owners of all TDCs that have not been used. The county must allow at least 12 months for an owner of TDCs to use them under the prior system.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 195.300-195.336; 2007 OL & ch. 424
