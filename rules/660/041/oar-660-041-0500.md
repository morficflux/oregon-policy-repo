## Full text

660-041-0500 Purpose and Applicability The purpose of OAR 660-041-0500 to 660-041-0530 is to clarify and implement ORS 195.300 to 195.336 (2007 Oregon Ballot Measure 49) in terms of the requirements and procedures for filing and reviewing Measure 49 Claims. These rules apply to Measure 49 Claims filed with the State of Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 195.300 - 195.336, 197.040 & 197.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 195.300 - 195.336, 197.015, 197.040, 197.065 & 197.353
