# Groundwater Restricted Areas (OAR 660-041-0140)

## At a glance

## Full text

660-041-0140 Groundwater Restricted Areas Measure 37 Claim Property is in a Ground Water Restricted Area if the Measure 37 Claim Property is located entirely within the boundaries of a Ground Water Limited Area or Critical Ground Water Area, or both.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197.040, 197.065 & 2007 OL Ch. 424 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 195.300 - 195.336, 197.015, 197.040, 197.065, 197.353 & 2007 OL Ch. 424
