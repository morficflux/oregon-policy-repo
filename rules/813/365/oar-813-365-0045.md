## Full text

813-365-0045 Use of Funds

(1) RGP funds will be used for RGP services and activities as further defined in the contract and RGP Manual.

(2) Prior to approving an RGP application, OHCS will determine if the application is complete and satisfies the criteria necessary to be a qualifying application for RGP funds.

(3) OHCS will review RGP applications and make awards of RGP funds to program providers within 14 days of its receipt of all required information. OHCS may choose to require the submittal of additional or clarifying information.
