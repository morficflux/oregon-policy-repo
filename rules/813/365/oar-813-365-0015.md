## Full text

813-365-0015 Manual The Rent Guarantee Program Operations Manual (RGP Manual) with the requirements and standards therein, dated May 24, 2023, is incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules, by reference. The RGP Manual may be accessed online at the OHCS website.
