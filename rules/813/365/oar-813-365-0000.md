## Full text

813-365-0000 Purpose and Objectives The purpose of OAR chapter 813, division 365, of the administrative rules is to implement the Rent Guarantee Program (RGP) as provided by ORS 456.607 to 456.609. Oregon Housing and Community Services (OHCS) is designated as the state agency responsible for administering the program. The purpose of the RGP is to provide incentives and financial assistance to eligible landlords that rent or lease to low income tenants by mitigating unpaid rent and property damages.
