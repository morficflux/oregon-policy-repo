## Full text

813-004-0300 Recordkeeping and Confidentiality Any information obtained in the criminal records check is confidential. The department must restrict the dissemination of information obtained in the criminal records check. Only those persons, as identified by the department, with a demonstrated and legitimate need to know the information, may have access to criminal records check records.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181.534 & 456.135 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181.534 & 456.569
