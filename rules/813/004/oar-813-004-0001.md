## Full text

813-004-0001 Confidentiality and Inadmissibility of Mediation Communications The department is a party or is mediating a dispute as to which the department has regulatory authority. This rule does not apply when the department is acting as the “mediator” in a matter in which the department also is a party as defined in ORS 36.234.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 36.224 & 36.234 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 36.224, 36.228, 36.230, 36.232 & 36.234
