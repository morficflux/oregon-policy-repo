## Full text

813-350-0005 Purpose and Objectives OAR 813, division 350 is promulgated to accomplish the general purposes of ORS 456.515 to 456.725, and more specifically, of 456.625(12) and 456.625(16), which respectively authorize the Department to implement actions that the Department deems necessary or appropriate to carry out housing and community services programs, and to employ certain funds, inter alia, for loan guarantees. The General Guarantee Program as set forth in this Division establishes the process by which the Department may provide such loan guarantees to lenders, and lease guarantees to sponsors and leasing companies for the development of suitable housing for low and very-low income families and individuals including, but not limited to, disabled persons, farmworkers, and ex-offenders.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 456.555(2) & 456.625(12)(16) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.625(12)(16)

History: OHCS 2-2003, f. & cert. ef. 5-1-03
