## Full text

813-350-0070 Waiver The Director may waive or modify any requirement of OAR 813, division 350, unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable state or federal statutes or regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 456.555(2) & 456.625(12)(16) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.515 - 456.725
