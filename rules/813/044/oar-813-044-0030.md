## Full text

813-044-0030 Distribution of Funds The maximum amount of an award to an organization under the Home Ownership Assistance Program is subject to determination by the Housing Stability Council.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 456.555 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 458.620 & 458.655
