## Full text

813-045-0035 Waiver for Other Sources If the Department has applied for and received funds on deposit in the Housing Development Account from other sources or on behalf of another person, the provisions of chapter 813, division 45 may be waived by the Director as necessary or convenient to comply with the rules, regulations or procedures prescribed by the source of funds, unless such waiver would violate applicable statutes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 90.800 - 90.840, 91.886, 183, 456.515 - 456.723 & 458.210 - 458.650 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.515 - 456.720
