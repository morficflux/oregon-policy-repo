813-042-0110 Waiver The Director may waive or modify any requirements of these Program rules, unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable federal or state statutes or regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 456.515 - 456.720 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.555
