## Full text

813-207-0090 Remedies and Repayment Applicants who knowingly make false statements or misrepresentations of any factor with respect to eligibility or who falsify application materials must repay all SHOW Program cash payments paid to them under the false information. The remedies provided for in this rule are in addition to any civil or criminal fines or penalties applicable under law, including any applicable provisions of federal, state, or local law. Failure to follow these rules will make the applicants ineligible for future eligibility under the SHOW Program.
