## Full text

813-207-0015 Manuals The State Home Oil Weatherization Program Manual (SHOW Program Manual) with the requirements and standards therein, dated December 1, 2024, is incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules, by reference. The SHOW Program Manual may be accessed online at the OHCS website. [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
