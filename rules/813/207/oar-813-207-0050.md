## Full text

813-207-0050 Application Procedure

(1) All applicants must submit a complete SHOW Program Cash Payment Application to OHCS to be considered for eligibility for SHOW Program cash payments. OHCS will make applications available for public access. Documentation for proof of eligibility is indicated in the Manual.

(2) Complete applications for SHOW Program cash payments must include: (a) The SHOW Program Cash Payment Application, completed in full. (b) Proof of purchase from or delivery by a fuel oil dealer for primary heating fuel for the eligible dwelling. (c) Proof of energy conservation measures installed: (A) For dwelling owners as the applicant, receipts of all eligible energy conservation measures completed must be marked paid in full on or before the date of the application. (B) For contractors as the applicant, invoices for all eligible energy conservation measures must be completed on or before the date of the application. (d) Proof of low-income status (for Tier 1 cash payments only if eligible): (A) For dwelling owners or their tenants, documentation of income for the household occupying the eligible dwelling to verify proof of low-income status. (B) For contractors, verification of income for the household occupying the eligible dwelling to verify proof of low-income status.

(3) OHCS will review applications for eligibility on a first-come, first-served basis and award cash payments subject to availability of funding.
