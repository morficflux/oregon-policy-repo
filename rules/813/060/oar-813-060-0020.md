813-060-0020 Older Adult Housing Program Manual

(1) Additional guidance, including eligibility, affordability and funding terms, application process, and program requirements, is outlined in the OAHP Manual dated July13, 2026, incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules by reference.

(2) The manual may be accessed online at OHCS’s website. [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
