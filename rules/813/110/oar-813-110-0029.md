# Supplemental Application Charge (OAR 813-110-0029)

## Full text

813-110-0029 Supplemental Application Charge A sponsoring entity of a project under the OAHTC program shall pay a supplemental application charge, as established by OHCS from time to time, when the sponsoring entity requests additional resources for a project that has already been funded under the OAHTC program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 317.097 & ORS 456.515-456.725 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 317.097
