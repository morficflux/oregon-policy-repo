## Full text

813-110-0026 Reservations, Certifications and Other Commitments Subject to Housing Stability Council Approval Applications for OAHTC program credits follow the approval process set forth in OAR 813-001-0007(1)(b) for review and approval by the Housing Stability Council when combined with OHCS resources subject to Council approval. Based upon any relevant council determination, including with respect to complementary funding, any subject reservation, certification or other commitment may be deemed revoked, be modified, or be further conditioned.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 317.097 & ORS 456.515-456.725 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 317.097
