## Full text

813-140-0120 Waiver The Department may waive or modify any requirements of OAR 813, division 140, unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable federal or state statutes or regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 90.800 - 90.840, 90.630, 183, 446, 456.515 - 456.723 & 458.210 - 458.650 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.579 - 456.581
