## Full text

813-210-0021 Applicant Eligibility CSBG services may be available to low-income households who are in need of assistance to meet basic needs and achieve self-sufficiency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 456.555 Statutes/Other Implemented: 42 USC 106 & ORS 458.505
