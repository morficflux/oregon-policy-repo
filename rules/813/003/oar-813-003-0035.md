## Full text

813-003-0035 Waiver The Department may waive or modify any requirements of OAR 813, division 3, unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable federal or state statutes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 456.555 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 291.055 & 456.562
