## Full text

813-038-0040 Waiver Subject to applicable law, the Department may waive or modify any requirements of OAR 813, division 038, as it deems appropriate for the public interest.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341, 456.555(2) & 456.625(12) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.561, 456.574, 456.620 & 456.625
