## Full text

813-039-0040 Waiver The Department may waive or modify any requirements of OAR 813, division 039, unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable federal or state statutes or regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 456.515, 456.547, 456.550, 456.555, 456.559, 456.571, 456.625, 458.620 & 458.660 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.555, 456.625, 458.620 & 458.660
