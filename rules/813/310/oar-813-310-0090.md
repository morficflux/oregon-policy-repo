# Good Faith (OAR 813-310-0090)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

813-310-0090 Good Faith An applicant who requests an exemption from the requirements of these rules must act in good faith in making the request and in performing any action prior to and related to the request, including the creation of the conditions that support the need or justification for an exemption.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 456.514 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.506 - 456.514

History: OHCS 3-2004, f. & cert. ef. 6-28-04
