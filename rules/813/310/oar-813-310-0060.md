# Group Home Visitability Requirements (OAR 813-310-0060)

## Full text

813-310-0060 Group Home Visitability Requirements Group homes are generally considered to be a single unit. Consequently, as with a single unit, these rules require only one visitable exterior route, one visitable entrance, and one powder room with a visitable interior route and a visitable doorway for a group home. The shared space in a group home is not considered to be a shared community room as defined in OAR 813-310-0020(15) and is not subject to the visitability and availability requirements outlined in 813-310-0025(1)(b) and 813-310-0030 for shared community rooms.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 456.514 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.506 - 456.514
