## Full text

813-310-0110 Waiver The Department may waive or modify any requirement of OAR 813-310, unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable state or federal statutes or regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 456.514 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.506 - 456.514
