## Full text

813-310-0055 Light Switches, Electrical Outlets and Environmental Control Requirements Light switches, electrical outlets and environmental controls in the dwelling units shall be installed no lower than 15 inches, or any higher than 48 inches, above the adjacent floor level.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 456.514 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.506 - 456.514

History: OHCS 3-2004, f. & cert. ef. 6-28-04
