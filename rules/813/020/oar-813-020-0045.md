## Full text

813-020-0045 Manual The Oregon Bond and Flex Lending FirstHome Guideline Manual with the requirements and standards therein, is incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules, by reference. The program manual may be accessed online at the OHCS website. The manual is dated August 29, 2025. [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
