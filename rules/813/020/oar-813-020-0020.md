## Full text

813-020-0020 Approved Lenders All Mortgage Loan(s) will be originated and closed using Approved Lenders who apply for and meet the requirements of a solicitation issued by the Department. Mortgage Loans delivered to the Department by Approved Lenders must also meet the requirements of the Department’s Servicer.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 456.555 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.625
