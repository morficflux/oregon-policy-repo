## Full text

813-090-0040 Qualified Basis Limited by Application The qualified basis established for the project at the time the LIHTCs are allocated cannot be increased without the applicant submitting another application for an additional LIHTC allocation in accordance with OAR 813-090-0031 and OHCS’s ORCA process and procedures.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 456.555 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.508, 456.510, 456.513, 456.559, 456.605, 456.625, 456.722 & 26 U.S.C. § 42
