## Full text

813-202-0007 Manuals The LIHEAP & OEAP Intake Operations and Policy Manual (program manual) with the requirements and standards therein, dated October 1, 2025, is incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules, by reference. The program manual may be accessed online at the OHCS website. [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]
