## Full text

813-202-0050 Coordination with Electric Utilities

(1) Subgrantees must execute a contract with an electric utility in order for the electric utility to receive an energy assistance payment under OEAP.

(2) Subgrantees must use a contract template provided or approved by OHCS in fulfillment of its obligation under subsection

(1) above. .
