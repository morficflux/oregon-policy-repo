## Full text

813-030-0062 Loan Servicing The department may service program loans internally or utilize a third party to provide loan servicing for any or all program loans. When utilizing a third-party servicer, the Department will specify the loan servicing process through a servicing agreement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 456.555 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.555 & 456.625
