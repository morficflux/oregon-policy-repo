# Equal Opportunity (OAR 813-040-0050)

## At a glance

OAR 813-040-0050 — Equal Opportunity. Chapter 813, Division 040. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

813-040-0050 Equal Opportunity No person receiving a Seed Money Advance under these rules shall make any distinction, discrimination, or restriction against any purchaser, occupant lessee, or prospective purchaser, occupant or lessee, relating to the sale, rental, lease or occupancy of real property, because of race, color, creed, sex, marital status or national origin.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 90.800 - 90.840, 91.886, 456.515 - 456.723 & 458.210 - 458.650 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.550, 456.559, 456.574 & 456.710
