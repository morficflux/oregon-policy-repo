## Full text

813-040-0015 Eligibility for the Seed Money Advance The prospective Nonprofit Borrower or other Borrower will demonstrate the need of a Seed Money Advance to pay Predevelopment Costs as set forth in the Proposal and will satisfy the standards of the Department for financial responsibility and stability.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 90.800 - 90.840, 91.886, 183, 456.515 - 456.723 & 458.210 - 458.650 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 456.550, 456.559, 456.574 & 456.710
