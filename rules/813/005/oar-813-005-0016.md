## Full text

813-005-0016 Waiver The director may waive, suspend or modify any term or provision of OAR 813, unless such waiver, suspension or modification would violate applicable federal or state law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 91.886, 183 & 456.555 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 90.800 - 90.840, 91.886, 456.515, 456.725 & 458.005 - 458.740
