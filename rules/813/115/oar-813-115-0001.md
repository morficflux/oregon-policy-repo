## Full text

813-115-0001 Purpose and Objectives The rules of OAR chapter 813, Division 115, describe a required process for Publicly Supported Housing Contract Preservation. This process is defined in ORS 456.766 through 456.813 and 456.828 which provides a framework to help preserve the affordability restrictions of properties that entered into these restrictions to receive federal, state, or local government assistance. This process is also subject to OHCS’ general rules (OAR 813-005).
