## Full text

813-070-0015 Allocation of Funds to Approved Lenders Under the Home Improvement Loan Program, the Department may rely on a commitment system or a first-come first-served reservation system to solicit and monitor participation by Approved Lenders when funds become available from the proceeds of Bonds or other sources. The Department solicits commitment requests or participation in the Program by Approved Lenders when the Department determines that the Home Improvement Loan Program will serve to carry out the purposes of the Act and funds may be available.
