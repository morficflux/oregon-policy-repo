## Full text

642-010-0010 Assessments Any first purchaser shall deduct and withhold an assessment of twelve cents ($.12) per pound for all mint oil purchased after June 30, 2020. All mint oil produced in the State of Oregon is to be included.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 576.325 - 576.365 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 576.325 - 576.365
