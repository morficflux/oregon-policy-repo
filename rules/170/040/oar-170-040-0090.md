## Full text

170-040-0090 Weekly Reporting Requirement for Depositories at 110% Collateralization Depositories ordered to collateralize their public funds deposits at 110% by the State Treasurer are required to submit a new Treasurer Report weekly. The weekly reporting requirement shall remain in effect until such time as the depository no longer holds public funds deposits over the FDIC limit or the State Treasurer removes the 110% collateralization requirement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 295.018(1) & 295.061(3) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 295
