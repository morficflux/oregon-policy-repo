## Full text

170-002-0165 Late Offers, Late Withdrawals, Late Modifications The Treasurer will not consider late Offers, late withdrawals or late modifications of an Offer received after the Closing date and time, unless otherwise provided in these rules or the Solicitation Document.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065

History: OST 1-2018, adopt filed 02/27/2018, effective 03/02/2018
