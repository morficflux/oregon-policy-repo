# Emergency (OAR 170-002-0135)

## At a glance

## Full text

170-002-0135 Emergency Procurements The Treasurer may award a Contract as an emergency procurement pursuant to the requirements of ORS 279B.080. The Treasurer will competitively procure Goods and Services in an emergency using a process that is reasonable and appropriate under the circumstances.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279B.080 & ORS 279A.050(5)

History: OST 1-2018, adopt filed 02/27/2018, effective 03/02/2018
