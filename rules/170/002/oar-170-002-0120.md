## Full text

170-002-0120 Small Procurements

(1) Generally. For Procurements less than or equal to $25,000, the Treasurer may award a Contract as a small procurement under ORS 279B.065.

(2) Amendments. The Treasurer may amend a Contract awarded as a small procurement. However, cumulative amendments to one Contract may not increase the total Contract price to greater than one hundred twenty-five percent (125%) of the dollar amount stated in ORS 279B.065.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279B.065 & 279A.050(5)
