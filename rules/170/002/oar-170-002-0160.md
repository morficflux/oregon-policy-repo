# Offer Preparation (OAR 170-002-0160)

## At a glance

## Full text

170-002-0160 Offer Preparation Any Offeror submitting an Offer must sign the Offer, and include any information, forms or other documents required by the Solicitation Document. An Offeror must sign any correction or erasure in its Offer prior to the Closing date and time as permitted by the Solicitation Document.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 279A.065 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279A.065
