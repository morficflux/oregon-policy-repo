## Full text

170-080-0005 Inspection, Certification or Copying Public Records The policies and procedures of the Oregon State Treasurer set forth in OAR 170, Division 2 in regard to inspection, certification or copying of public records, to the extent not inconsistent with the Act or ORS Chapter 178, are adopted as the policies and procedures of the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: 178.050 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS Ch 183 & 192.410–192.505
