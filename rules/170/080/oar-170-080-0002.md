## Full text

170-080-0002 Confidentiality and Inadmissibility of Mediation Communication The policies and procedures of the Oregon State Treasurer set forth in OAR 170 in regard to confidentiality and inadmissibility of mediation communication, to the extent not inconsistent with the Act or the Code, are adopted as the policies and procedures of the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 36.224, 183.335, 183.341 & 183.502 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 36.224, 36.228, 36.230 & 36.232
