## Full text

170-080-0062 Abandoned Accounts A Program IRA will be presumed abandoned according to the unclaimed property law of the state of the last known address of the Participating Employee or Participating Individual. If the last known address of the Participating Employee or Participating Individual is in Oregon, the provisions of the Uniform Disposition of Unclaimed Property shall apply (ORS 98.302 to 98.436). If there is no last known address of the Participating Employee or Participating Individual in the Program records, federal common law shall determine the state with the first priority claim.
