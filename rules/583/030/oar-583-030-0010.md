## Full text

583-030-0010 Exemptions The standards and procedures in these rules shall not apply to schools exempt under ORS 348.597 or those entities listed in 348.594 (3)(b).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.604 & 348.606 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.604, ORS 348.597, 348.605 & ORS 348.594
