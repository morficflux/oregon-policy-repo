## Full text

583-030-0016 Exclusive Use of Term “University” Effective January 13, 2015. A school may not use the term "university” to describe itself unless the school is an accredited institution authorized by the Commission to offer bachelor's degrees together with graduate or first professional degrees, or to an organization that constitutes a formal consortium of schools so authorized.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.606 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.606, ORS 348.596 & 348.603
