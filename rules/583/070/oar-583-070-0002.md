## Full text

583-070-0002 Purpose and Scope Effective January 13, 2015. This rule implements ORS 348.603 in establishing standards and procedures for the evaluation and approval of postsecondary accrediting bodies seeking to operate in or from Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.603 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.603
