## Full text

575-075-0045 Voucher Disbursement to Students An approved social service agency may authorize the Commission to award a VISTO voucher to a VISTO volunteer for each eight hours of qualifying service to the agency.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.405 - 348.425
