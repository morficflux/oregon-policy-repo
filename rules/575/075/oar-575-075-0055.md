## Full text

575-075-0055 Appeals A County VISTO Agency or approved social service agency may appeal to the Commission any disagreement it has with a policy or procedure of the Commission or its staff. In such a case, the decision of the Commission is final unless a court of competent jurisdiction orders to the contrary.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.405 - 348.425
