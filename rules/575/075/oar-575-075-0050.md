## Full text

575-075-0050 Record Keeping Each approved social service agency shall:

(1) Maintain records sufficient to document its activities relating to the VISTO program.

(2) Make its financial and work performance records available to the Commission at reasonable times for the purpose of assuring that the social service agency is complying with the rules and guidelines relating to the administration of the VISTO program.

(3) Retain the financial and work performance records relevant to the disbursement of VISTO vouchers for not less than five years following the end of the fiscal year in which that disbursement occurs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.405 - 348.425
