## Full text

575-075-0010 VISTO Vouchers A VISTO voucher shall:

(1) Be provided to a volunteer by the Commission upon notification by the approved social service agency of receipt of volunteer services;

(2) Be issued with a redemption value of $25, or multiple thereof, with an issue date;

(3) Be non-transferable; and

(4) Expire five years after the date of issue.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.405 - 348.425
