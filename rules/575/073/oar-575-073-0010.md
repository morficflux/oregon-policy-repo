## Full text

575-073-0010 Purpose of the Program The purpose of this program is to provide student loan repayments on behalf of nurses who enter into agreements to practice in "nursing critical shortage areas" in Oregon, and who fulfill minimum terms of such practice specified in OAR 575-073-0040(2).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: SB 331 & 2001 OLA

History: OSAC 2-2002, f. & cert. ef. 2-6-02
