## Full text

575-070-0045 Renewal of Awards Awards made under this program are renewable in subsequent academic years provided that:

(1) The awardee continues to be enrolled as at least a half-time student in an approved teacher education program at an eligible institution;

(2) The awardee is making satisfactory academic progress, as defined by the institution; and

(3) The awardee arranges for the Commission to receive a transcript of the academic work completed in the prior year of award.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329.757 - 329.780
