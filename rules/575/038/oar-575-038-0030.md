## Full text

575-038-0030 Grant Amount State Grant Supplemental Award amounts will be set annually by the Higher Education Coordinating Commission in consultation with institutions participating in the State Grant Supplemental Award Program.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: HB 5030(6) & 1995
