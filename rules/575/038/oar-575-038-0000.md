# Purpose (OAR 575-038-0000)

## Full text

575-038-0000 Purpose The purpose of this program is to carry out provisions of House Bill 5030, Section 6, enacted by the Legislative Assembly during the regular 1995 session. That measure calls for supplemental grants to be made to students receiving Oregon Opportunity Grants and who are enrolled in private, nonprofit colleges in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: HB 5030(6) & 1995
