## Full text

575-038-0010 Student Eligibility Students eligible for State Grant Supplemental Awards are those who have been designated by the Commission to receive Opportunity Grants for attendance at private, nonprofit colleges in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: HB 5030(6) & 1995

History: OSAC 1-2015, f. & cert. ef. 12-18-15 SSC 1-1995, f. & cert. ef. 8-24-95
