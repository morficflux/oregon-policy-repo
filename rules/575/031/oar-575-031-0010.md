## Full text

575-031-0010 Financial Need A student’s financial need for purposes of determining eligibility for an Oregon Opportunity Grant is based upon the need-analysis calculation from the U.S. Department of Education, regardless of the need analysis application used by the student.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.205 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.205
