# Appeals (OAR 575-031-0055)

## Full text

575-031-0055 Appeals An institution shall have the right to appeal to the Commission in the instance of a disagreement with a policy or procedure of the Commission or its staff. In such a case, the decision of the Commission is final unless a court of competent jurisdiction orders to the contrary.
