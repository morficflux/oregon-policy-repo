## Full text

575-095-0020 Student Eligibility Criteria To receive financial aid from this program an applicant must:

(1) Be an Oregon resident with a child;

(2) Be enrolled or accepted for enrollment as an undergraduate at an Oregon postsecondary institution;

(3) Maintain satisfactory academic progress; and

(4) Not be in default on any federal Title IV loan or owing a refund on federal Title IV funds previously disbursed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.520-348.530
