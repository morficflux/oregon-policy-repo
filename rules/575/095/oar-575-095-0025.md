## Full text

575-095-0025 Application Process

(1) All applicants will complete an application that is specified by the Commission.

(2) All applicants must complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) or Oregon Student Aid Application (ORSAA).

(3) The Commission will process all applications and award students annually.
