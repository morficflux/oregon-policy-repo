## Full text

575-076-0005 Purpose and Scope This rule implements the Access to Student assistance Programs In Reach of Everyone (ASPIRE) Program. The program encourages all Oregon students to see education, training, and career pathways beyond high school as an option. The program provides information about financial aid, education and training, and career pathway options to students, ASPIRE site staff, community volunteers and families for this purpose.
