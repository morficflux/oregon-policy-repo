## Full text

575-090-0050 IEA Redemption

(1) The school or program shall submit payment authorization vouchers to the Commission for redemption.

(2) OSAC shall make the requested payment from the Recipient's IEA to the appropriate redeemer upon submission of the payment authorization voucher to the Commission.

(3) Payment is not made directly to the IEA Recipient under any circumstance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: HB 3050 & 1995
