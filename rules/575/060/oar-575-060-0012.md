## Full text

575-060-0012 Eligible Institutions A donor must specify which institutions are eligible to receive award recipients. However, only institutions located in a state and recognized by the U.S. Department of Education as eligible to participate in federal Title IV student financial aid programs may be so designated and at least two institutions must be designated.
