## Full text

575-060-0045 Administrative Fees The Commission may charge an annual fee for servicing an award program. The Commission shall set the amount of the fee, based on a percentage of the amount of scholarships awarded annually.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.520 - 348.530 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.520 - 348.530
