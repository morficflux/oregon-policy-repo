## Full text

575-065-0055 Award Amounts

(1) Scholarships awarded to eligible applicants shall equal the amount of tuition and fees levied by the eligible institution for full-time students.

(2) Eligible students attending eligible Oregon independent institutions of postsecondary education may not receive awards in excess of the amount of tuition and fees levied on resident undergraduate students by the University of Oregon. The source of the relevant tuition and fee information shall be the official Fee Schedule published annually by the governing boards of trustees of the University of Oregon, Oregon State Universtiy, and Portland State University and the Office of the Chancellor for regional and technical public universities.

(3) Receipt of this award does not impact a student's eligibility for the Oregon Opportunity Grant.

(4) For concurrently enrolled students, the amount of the award will be based on the school disbursing the funds.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: HB 2431 & 2001 Leg. Assembly
