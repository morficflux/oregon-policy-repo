## Full text

575-035-0005 Residency For a student to be eligible for a Barbers and Hairdressers (B & H) Grant, the student must be a resident of the State of Oregon as defined in OAR 575-030-0005(1).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.290 & ORS 348.505
