## Full text

575-035-0015 Institutional Eligibility Eligible institutions are any schools of Barbering, Hair Design, Cosmetology and Manicure, that are located in and licensed by the State of Oregon and have signed an agreement with the Commission to participate.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.290 & ORS 348.505
