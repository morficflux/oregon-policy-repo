# Appeals (OAR 575-035-0055)

## Full text

575-035-0055 Appeals An institution shall have the right to appeal to the Commission in the instance of a disagreement with a policy or procedure of the Commission or its staff. In such a case, the decision of the Commission is final unless a court of competent jurisdiction orders to the contrary.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.290 & ORS 348.505
