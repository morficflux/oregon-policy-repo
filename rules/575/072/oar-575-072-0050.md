# Maximum (OAR 575-072-0050)

## At a glance

## Full text

575-072-0050 Maximum Awards Annual awards made under this program shall not exceed 50 percent of the sum of tuition, fees, and room and board expenses for any participating student.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348.115 & 348.117 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.105 - 348.117

History: OSAC 1-2015, f. & cert. ef. 12-18-15 SSC 3-1992, f. & cert. ef. 2-28-92
