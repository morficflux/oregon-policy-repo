## Full text

575-072-0020 Purpose

(1) This program is to provide student loans for Oregon residents attending Oregon institutions who are enrolled in programs leading to the following degrees: (a) Associate Degree in Nursing; (b) Bachelor of Science in Nursing; and (c) Prelicensure Masters Program.

(2) Loans made to students under this program may be forgiven (cancelled), in part or in toto, upon fulfillment of the requirements related to the practice of nursing in the State of Oregon set forth in OAR 575-072-0070(8).
