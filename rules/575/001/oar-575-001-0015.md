## Full text

575-001-0015 Right of Appeal A student who is denied participation in any student assistance program administered by the Higher Education Coordinating Commission may appeal the denial to the Commission. The appeal shall be submitted in writing a minimum of ten days before the meeting at which the appeal is to be considered.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 348 Statutes/Other Implemented: HB 2249 & the Higher Education Act of 1965 as amended
