## Full text

575-039-0050 Enrollment A student must be enrolled or accepted for enrollment as at least a half-time undergraduate student at an eligible institution to receive Oregon Promise grant funds.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 341.522 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 341.522
