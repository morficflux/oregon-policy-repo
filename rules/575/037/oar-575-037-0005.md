## Full text

575-037-0005 Definitions

(1) “Public Safety Officer” includes the following public safety categories as defined in ORS181A.355: (a) C orrections officer (b) F ire service professional (c) P arole and probation officer (d) P olice officer (e) R eserve officer (f) Y outh correction officer

(2) “Child of a public safety officer” is defined as: (a) Natural child (b) Adopted child (c) Stepchild

(3) “Death or permanent total disability suffered by a public safety officer while on or off duty” is defined by ORS 243.954.

(4) “Eligible institution of higher education” is any Oregon community college, career school as defined in ORS 341.005, Oregon public university, Oregon Health and Science University, or Oregon-based private non-profit institution of higher education as defined in ORS 348.582.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.325 - 183.355, ORS 348.270 & ORS 348.280 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 348.270 & ORS 348.280
