165-022-0080 Billing Electoral Jurisdictions for County Voters’ Pamphlet The formula used to determine the apportioned expenditure and revenue allocations for the county voters’ pamphlet as related to election costs is outlined in OAR 165-020-0050, 165-020-0055, and 165-020-0060, Billing for Local Elections.
