## Full text

165-016-0000 Designating the State Voters’ Pamphlet Manual and Forms The Secretary of State designates the State Voters’ Pamphlet Manual, revised 1/2026 and associated forms as the procedures and forms to be used to submit candidate statements, measure arguments, statements of arguments by any political party or assembly of electors, arguments in support of a legislative referral, and filer submitted translations allowed by statute, explanatory statements, financial estimates and statements, statements prepared by the Legislative Counsel Committee under ORS 251.225, and a community college district measure relating to bonding, a county measure, or a metropolitan service district measure submitted under ORS 251.285.
