## Full text

165-004-0005 Forms to Petition for Incorporation of a City The Secretary of State hereby adopts by reference and designates the following forms for filing a petition for incorporation of unincorporated territory as a city:

(1) SEL 701, Prospective Petition for Incorporation, which designates not more than three chief petitioners;

(2) SEL 702, Petition for Incorporation of a City Signature Sheet;

(3) SEL 702a, Petition for Incorporation of a City Signature Sheet, to be used when the territory proposed to be incorporated is within the jurisdiction of a local government boundary commission. [ED. NOTE: Forms referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 221.031 & 246.150 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 221.031 & 221.040
