# Special (OAR 629-170-0040)

## At a glance

OAR 629-170-0040 — Special. Chapter 629, Division 170. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

629-170-0040 Special Case If the firefighter seeking reimbursement of legal costs under this division is the State Forester, all authorities and responsibilities of the State Forester under ORS 477.128 and this division must be delegated to a management service employee of the department, knowledgeable in firefighting organization, tactics and procedures, to be carried out under the supervision of an ad hoc committee of the Board of Forestry.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526.041 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 477.128

History: DOF 2-2016, f. 6-10-16, cert. ef. 7-1-16
