## Full text

629-170-0030 Funding Prior to authorizing expenditures for costs and reasonable attorney fees pursuant to ORS 477.128, the State Forester must determine the source of funding to be used and the amount that may be available for such expenditures.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526.041 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 477.128
