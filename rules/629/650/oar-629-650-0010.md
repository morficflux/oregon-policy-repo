## Full text

629-650-0010 Live Tree Retention for Lakes

(1) Operators shall retain in the riparian management areas of lakes approximately 50 percent of the original live trees, by species, in each of the following diameter classes (DBH): (a) 6 to 10 inches; (b) 11 to 20 inches; (c) 21 to 30 inches; and (d) larger than 30 inches.

(2) As part of the live trees in subsection

(1) above, trees on the edge of lakes shall be retained.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 527.710 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 527.710 & 527.765
