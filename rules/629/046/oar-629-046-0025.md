# Forest (OAR 629-046-0025)

## Full text

629-046-0025 Forest Products; Shingle Bolts, Shake Bolts, Stud Logs Shingle bolts, shake bolts, eight-foot stud logs, and other such forest products shall each be branded in accordance with the provisions of ORS 532.010 to 532.140; excepting, however, when being transported directly to the place of manufacturing, then, at the option of the owner, such may be branded by marking at least four outside units of such bolts, logs, or other forest products.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 530.059

History: FB 9, f. 9-20-61
