# Fire (OAR 629-047-0060)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

629-047-0060 Fire Prevention Enforcement Limitations Only authorized fire wardens who have been designated a Fire Warden — Class A, by the forester on or after the original effective date of this rule, may issue a Fire Prevention Warning or a Fire Prevention Citation for violations involving the fire prevention requirements of ORS 477.510, 477.565, 477.615, 477.625, 477.635, 477.650, 477.655, 477.660, 477.665, 477.670, and 477.695.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 477.980 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 477.980 - 477.993
