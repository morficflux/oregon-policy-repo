# Purpose (OAR 629-047-0030)

## At a glance

## Full text

629-047-0030 Purpose The purpose of OAR 629, division 047 is to ensure a uniform response by authorized fire wardens to violations. Exceptions to these enforcement policies are intended to be few in number and shall be approved in writing by the district forester on forms provided by the State Forester for that purpose.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 477 & 526 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 477.005
