## Full text

629-035-0110 Management of Common School Fund Lands Common School Fund Lands managed by the State Forester under an agreement with the State Land Board shall be managed consistent with OAR 629-035-0030 through 629-035-0100 if the Agreement or the State Land Board so directs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526.016(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 530.490 - 530.500
