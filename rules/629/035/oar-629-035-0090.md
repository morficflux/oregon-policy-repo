## Full text

629-035-0090 Consultation with Forest Trust Land Advisory Committee As provided in ORS 526.156(3), the Forest Trust Land Advisory Committee shall advise the Board and the State Forester on the management of lands subject to the provisions of 530.010 to 530.170, and on other matters in which counties may have a responsibility pertaining to forest land. The Board and the State Forester shall consult with the committee with regard to such matters.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526.016(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 526.156(3)

History: DOF 2-1998, f. 1-15-98, cert. ef. 3-1-98
