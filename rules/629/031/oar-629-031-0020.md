## Full text

629-031-0020 Applicable State Timber All unprocessed timber, as defined in OAR 629-031-0005, which originates from state lands is prohibited from export.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 530 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 526.806, 530.040 & 530.050

History: FB 10-1990, f. 12-18-90, cert. ef. 12-20-90
