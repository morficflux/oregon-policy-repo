## Full text

629-031-0025 Surplus Timber The prohibitions against export contained in OAR 629-031-0005 to 629-031-0045 shall not apply to specific quantities of grades and species of unprocessed timber originating from state land which the United States Secretary of Agriculture or Interior has determined by rule to be surplus to the needs of timber manufacturing facilities in the United States.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 530 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 526.811, 530.040 & 530.050

History: FB 10-1990, f. 12-18-90, cert. ef. 12-20-90
