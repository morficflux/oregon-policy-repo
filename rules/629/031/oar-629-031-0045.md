# Enforcement (OAR 629-031-0045)

## At a glance

OAR 629-031-0045 — Enforcement. Chapter 629, Division 031. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

629-031-0045 Enforcement Investigation of suspected violations of these rules and/or surveillance of unprocessed timber in transit and at port facilities may be conducted by the State Forester, or contracted by the State Forester to other state or federal agencies. Any alleged violations of the export prohibition provisions of this section will be referred by the State Forester to the appropriate federal or state agency for prosecution or other legal action.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 530 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 526.801 - 526.992, 530.010 - 530.520 & 532.010 - 532.100

History: FB 10-1990, f. 12-18-90, cert. ef. 12-20-90
