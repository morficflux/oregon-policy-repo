## Full text

629-680-0310 Significant Wetland Types The following wetland types are determined by the Board to be significant:

(1) Wetlands that are larger than eight acres;

(2) Estuaries;

(3) Bogs; and

(4) Important springs in eastern Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 527.710 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 527.715
