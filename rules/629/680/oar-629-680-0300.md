# Purpose (OAR 629-680-0300)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

629-680-0300 Purpose ORS 527.710(3)(a)(D) requires the Board to identify and protect significant wetlands. The purpose of these rules is to identify significant wetlands types on forestlands. Significant wetlands require site-specific protection and written plans. Protection rules for significant wetlands are in OAR 629-645-0000 through 629-645-0050.
