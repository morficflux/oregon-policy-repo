## Full text

629-620-0500 Disposal of Chemical Containers Operators shall dispose of chemical containers in accordance with the Department of Environmental Quality’s disposal requirements. Operators may apply flushing solution resulting from cleaning of chemical containers to the operation area.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 527.710 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 527.715 & 527.765
