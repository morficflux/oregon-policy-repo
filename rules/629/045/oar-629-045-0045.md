## Full text

629-045-0045 Community Meetings

(1) Prior to holding the public hearing required by ORS 526.328(1), a committee shall hold one or more community meetings in each county of its jurisdiction.

(2) At a community meeting required by section

(1) of this rule, a committee shall: (a) Present to any interested persons information about its: (A) Forestland identification process, completed pursuant to ORS 526.320 and OAR 629-045-0025; and (B) Preliminary classification decision process, completed pursuant to ORS 526.324 and OAR 629-045-0030 and 629-045-0035. (b) Make available for inspection the preliminary classification maps required by ORS 526.324.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526.041, Sec. 11 & Ch. 69 OL 2009 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 477.205, 477.230 & 526.305 - 526.370

History: DOF 1-2010, f. & cert. ef. 5-19-10
