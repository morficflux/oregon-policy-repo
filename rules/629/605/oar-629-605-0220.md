## Full text

629-605-0220 Judicial Determinations of Rule Validity It is the intent of the Board that if any section of a rule is declared invalid or is remanded by the Court of Appeals under ORS 183.400, the remaining sections of the rule will remain valid.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 527.710 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 527.715
