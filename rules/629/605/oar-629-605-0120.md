## Full text

629-605-0120 Consultation Department personnel shall consult with personnel of other state agencies concerned with the forest environment situations where expertise from such agencies is desirable or necessary.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 527.710 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 527.721
