## Full text

629-605-0110 Annual Review The State Forester shall, at least once each year, meet with other state agencies concerned with the forest environment to review the Forest Practice Rules relative to sufficiency. The State Forester shall then report to the Board of Forestry a summary of such meeting or meetings together with recommendations for amendments to rules, new rules, or repeal of rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 527.710 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 527.721
