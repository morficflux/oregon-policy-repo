## Full text

629-605-0105 Notice of Federal Endangered Species Act Compliance with the forest practices rules does not substitute for or ensure compliance with the federal Endangered Species Act. Nothing in these rules imposes any state requirement to comply with the federal Endangered Species Act. Landowners and operators are advised that federal law prohibits a person from taking certain threatened or endangered species which are protected under the Endangered Species Act.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 527.710 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 527.710 & 527.715
