## Full text

629-010-0050 Quorum A majority of the members of the Board or a committee constitutes a quorum to do business.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310(1), 192.610 - 192.710, 526.016(4) & 526.041(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.610 - 192.710 & 526.016(4)
