## Full text

629-010-0090 Attendance In accordance with ORS 182.020, the Secretary shall submit a copy of the minutes of each Board meeting to the Governor or the Governor’s Assistant including members present and absent with an attachment indicating the members’ reason for absence.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.310(1), 192.610 - 192.710, 526.016(4) & 526.041(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.610 - 192.710 & 526.016(4)
