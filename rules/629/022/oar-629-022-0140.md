## Full text

629-022-0140 Approval of a Proposed Project

(1) The State Forester must use the information in the Landowner's Project plan, described in OAR 629-022-0130, in a point rating system based on the Project criteria in 629-022-0050, to select Projects for funding, subject to the requirements of 629-022-0150.

(2) When a Project is selected for funding, the State Forester must approve all Practices, Environmental Restoration, consulting services and Budgeted Costs described in the Project plan in OAR 629-022-0130(3),

(4) and (5). Budgeted Costs and fees are subject to the limitations in 629-022-0220 and 629-022-0230.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526.700 - 526.730 & 526.745 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 526.695 - 526.775
