# Adequately Stocked Forest Stand (OAR 629-022-0390)

## Full text

629-022-0390 Adequately Stocked Forest Stand The Landowner must manage Forest Stands established with Trust funds at or above the reforestation stocking standards in the Forest Practices Act and in OAR 629-610-020 or as otherwise specified in the contract, until the conditions in 629-022-0250, Life of the Contract, are met.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526.700 - 526.730 & 526.745 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 526.695 - 526.775
