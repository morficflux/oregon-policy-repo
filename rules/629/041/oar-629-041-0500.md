## Full text

629-041-0500 Purpose The purpose of OAR 629-041-0500 to 629-041-0575 is to set forth the boundaries of Forest Protection Districts, pursuant to ORS 477.225, and thereby identify the lands to which the applicable duties, requirements and penalties of ORS Chapter 477 and 526 shall apply.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 477.225 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 477.225
