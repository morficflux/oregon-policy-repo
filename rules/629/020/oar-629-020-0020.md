## Full text

629-020-0020 Compliance with the Statewide Planning Goals and Compatibility with Acknowledged Comprehensive Plans and Land Use Regulations Prior to undertaking any action or program listed in OAR 629-020-0010, the Department shall find that the program or action complies with the statewide planning goals and is compatible with acknowledged comprehensive plans and land use regulations. The Department shall make its goal compliance and plan compatibility findings in accordance with OAR 629-020-0000 through 629-020-0080.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.180
