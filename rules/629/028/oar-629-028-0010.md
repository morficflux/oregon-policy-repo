# Purpose of Woodcutting Permitting on State Forest Lands (OAR 629-028-0010)

## Full text

629-028-0010 Purpose of Woodcutting Permitting on State Forest Lands As set forth in ORS 530.050 the Forester in exercising legal authority for the management of State forest lands shall manage the land to secure the greatest permanent value to the State. The Forester, in the management, protection, utilization, and conservation of the lands may, in addition to selling forest products from the lands, permit the use of the lands for other purposes not detrimental to the State. The Board and the Forester have determined that it is in the interest of the State to permit the people of the State to utilize wood fiber that has a marginal economic or commercial value as firewood.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 530 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 530.050 & 530.500
