## Full text

607-030-0010 Number of Commissioners, Terms The Oregon Tall Fescue Commission will consist of nine

(9) members appointed by the Director of the Oregon Department of Agriculture for a term of three

(3) years or, if for a term following a prior term for that person, ending three

(3) years from the date of expiration of any prior term. The initial term of office for producer 7 will end June 30, 2020; thereafter, the length of term will be three

(3) years. No commissioner will serve for more than 2 consecutive, full terms of office.
