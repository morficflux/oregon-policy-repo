## Full text

607-010-0015 Definitions

(1) "Person" means any individual, corporation, partnership, or joint stock company.

(2) "Commission" means the Oregon Tall Fescue Commission.

(3) "Casual Sale" means any sale or sales of Tall Fescue Seed or Orchardgrass Seed made by the producer direct to the consumer where the total accumulated sales during a calendar year is not more than 100 pounds.

(4) "First Purchaser" means any person who buys Tall Fescue Seed or Orchardgrass Seed from the producer in the first instance, or handler who receives the Tall Fescue Seed or Orchardgrass Seed in the first instance from the producer for resale or processing.

(5) "Producer" means a person or other legal entity producing Tall Fescue Seed or Orchardgrass Seed in Oregon for market, whether as landowner, landlord, tenant, sharecropper, or otherwise.

(6) "Handler" means any producer, processor, or distributor, or other person engaged in handling or marketing of or dealing in Tall Fescue Seed or Orchardgrass Seed, whether as owner, agent, employee, broker, or otherwise.

(7) Tall Fescue Seed (Festuca arundinacea) includes all varieties presently being produced in the state and any new varieties that may be released in the future with the exception of Alta and Goar.

(8) Orchardgrass Seed (Dactylis glomerata) includes seed of all common or named varieties presently being produced in the state and any new varieties that may be released in the future.
