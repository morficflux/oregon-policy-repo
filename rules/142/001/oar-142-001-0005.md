## Full text

142-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure Pursuant to ORS 183.341, the Division of State Lands and the State Land Board adopt the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act as amended October 3, 2001. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Division of State Lands, South Slough National Estuarine Research Reserve.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 183.341 & 273.554 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.553 - 273.558
