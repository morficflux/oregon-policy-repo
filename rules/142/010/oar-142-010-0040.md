## Full text

142-010-0040 Leases and Rentals Any fee for a lease or rental approved by the Commission for any use of the Reserve lands or buildings shall be at fair market value.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.554 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.553 - 273.558
