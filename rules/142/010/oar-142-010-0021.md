## Full text

142-010-0021 Prohibited Activities The following uses of the Reserve are prohibited activities as determined by the Commission, by authority of ORS 273.553(3):

(1) Commercial bait gathering;

(2) Commercial timber harvest;

(3) Commercial mineral removal;

(4) Discharge of firearms for pleasure shooting or for target practice;

(5) Picking of forest greenery and cascara bark gathering for commercial purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.554 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.553 - 273.558
