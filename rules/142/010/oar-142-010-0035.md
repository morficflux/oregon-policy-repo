## Full text

142-010-0035 Roads and Trails Roads and trails other than those authorized by the Commission will be closed. No roads or trails will be constructed without approval of the Commission. Access to Reserve lands shall be on authorized roads and trails only, and not through private property unless the state has acquired an easement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.554 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.553 - 273.558
