## Full text

142-015-0000 Purpose OAR 142-015-0000 to 142-015-0050 identify the facilities, equipment and educational and technical services provided by the South Slough National Estuarine Research Reserve for which fees are required, and establish the process that will be used to establish and revise those fees.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.554 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.554(2)
