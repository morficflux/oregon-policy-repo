## Full text

142-015-0050 Addition of Fee for New Category In the event the Reserve is requested to allow a usage or activity not anticipated at the time the Commission approves the schedule under OAR 142-015-0040, the Manager or the Operations Manager may establish a proposed fee and submit the fee to the Commission at its next regular meeting. The fee shall be based on the costs to the Reserve in sponsoring or carrying out the activity or usage and shall take into consideration the criteria set forth in 142-015-0010(1). Upon approval by the Commission the fee shall be incorporated into the fee schedule.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 273.554 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 273.554(2)
