## Full text

259-008-0200 Civil Penalties

(1) For purposes of this rule, “agency” means a law enforcement unit or public or private safety agency as defined in OAR 259-008-0005.

(2) The Department may impose a civil penalty on any agency for any violation of ORS 181A.490, 181A.520, 181A.530 or 181A.560. All civil penalties will be imposed in the manner provided by 183.745.

(3) The amount of any civil penalty imposed under subsection

(1) of this section may not exceed $1,500 for any single violation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181A.655 & ORS 183.745 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181A.655 & ORS 183.745
