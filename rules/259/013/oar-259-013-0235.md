## Full text

259-013-0235 Refusal to Consent to a Criminal records Check If any subject individual refuses to consent to a criminal records check, or refuses to be fingerprinted when required, the Department may revoke or deny any application, license or certificate issued by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181A.195 & ORS 181A.400 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181A.400
