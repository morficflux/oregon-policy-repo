## Full text

259-009-0035 Certificates Are Property of Department Certificates and awards are the property of the Department. The Department has the power to revoke or suspend any certificate or award as provided in the Act.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181A.410 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181A.410, ORS 181A.570, ORS 181A.590, ORS 181A.630, ORS 181A.640 & ORS 181A.650
