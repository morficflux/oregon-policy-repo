## Full text

259-003-0015 Rules of Procedure Pursuant to the provisions of ORS 183.341, the Board and Department adopt the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure applicable to proceedings for agency declaratory rulings under the Administrative Procedures Act as amended and in effect July, 2014.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 181A.410, ORS 703.230, ORS 703.465 & ORS 181A.870 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 181A.410, ORS 703.230, ORS 703.470, ORS 181A.630, ORS 181A.640, ORS 181A.650, ORS 181A.870 & ORS 181A.880
