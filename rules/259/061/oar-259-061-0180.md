## Full text

259-061-0180 Maintaining Current Information Within 10 days of a change, a licensed investigator or applicant for licensure must notify the Department in writing of any changes to name, home address, home phone number, mailing address, business name, business address, or business phone number.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 703.415, 703.425, 703.430, 703.435, 703.445, 703.450, 703.460, 703.465 & 703.480 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 703.401 - 703.995
