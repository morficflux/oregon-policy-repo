## Full text

698-010-0030 Eligible Applicants

(1) Eligible applicants for Conservation Management Plan Grants are: (a) Entities eligible to hold a conservation easement as defined in ORS 271.715, other than a state agency; (b) Watershed councils; and (c) Tax exempt organizations under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.

(2) Individual agricultural landowners or operators are not eligible to apply for a Conservation Management Plan Grant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.984, ORS 541.988 & ORS 541.989 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.977-541.989
