# Definitions "Technical (OAR 698-020-0020)

## At a glance

## Full text

698-020-0020 Definitions "Technical Assistance" means supporting the development of working land projects or programs as described in ORS 541-981 and Division 10 (Conservation Management Plans) and ORS 541-982 and Division 15 (Working Land Conservation Covenants and Easements).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.984, ORS 541.988 & ORS 541.989 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.977-541.989
