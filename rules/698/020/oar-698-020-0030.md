## Full text

698-020-0030 Eligible Applicants

(1) Eligible applicants for Technical Assistance Grants are entities that are eligible to enter into agreements resulting in a Conservation Management Plan under Division 10 or acquire a Working Land Conservation Covenant or Easement under Division 15.

(2) Individual Agricultural Landowners or Operators are not eligible to apply for a Technical Assistance Grant.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.984, ORS 541.988 & ORS 541.989 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.977-541.989
