## Full text

698-015-0150 Grant Reporting Requirements for Working Land Conservation Covenants Upon completion of the term of a Working Land Conservation Covenant, the Grantee will provide OWEB with a copy of the project completion report in the manner prescribed by OWEB.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 541.984, ORS 541.988 & ORS 541.989 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 541.977-541.989
