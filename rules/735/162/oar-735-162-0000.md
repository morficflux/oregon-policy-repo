# Qualifications (OAR 735-162-0000)

## Full text

735-162-0000 Qualifications Any person who does not have an operator’s license issued under ORS Chapter 807 may be issued a certificate to operate a snowmobile if they have taken a Snowmobile Safety Education Course that has been approved by the Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division of the Department of Transportation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 802.010, 821.150, 821.160 & 821.190 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 821.160

History: MV 8-1981, f. & ef. 7-1-81, Renumbered from 735-061-0010 MV 46, f. 9-10-71, ef. 10-1-71 Repealed by DMV 1-2004, f. & cert. ef. 1-15-04 DMV 3-1999, f. & cert. ef. 9-3-99
