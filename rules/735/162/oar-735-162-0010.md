# Certification Application (OAR 735-162-0010)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

735-162-0010 Certification Application Each applicant for a certificate to operate a snowmobile who has completed the Snowmobile Safety Education Course must make application on a form provided by the Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division of the Department of Transportation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 802.010, 821.150, 821.160 & 821.190 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 821.160
