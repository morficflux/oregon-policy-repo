## Full text

735-128-0020 Hauling of Explosives Prohibited No explosives shall be hauled on any vehicle while it is engaged in transporting workers. This rule shall not prohibit the driver and one qualified powder man from riding in a vehicle in which explosives are being hauled.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616 & 820.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 820.030 & 820.070
