## Full text

735-164-0020 Placement of Snowmobile Registration Decal Registration numbers assigned to snowmobiles are issued by DMV in the form of a registration decal. Snowmobile registration decals must be affixed on the left side of the snowmobile in an area that is visible during operation.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184. 616, 184.619, 802.010 & 821.080 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 821.080
