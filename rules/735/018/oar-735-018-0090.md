## Full text

735-018-0090 Electronic Records DMV records pertaining to electronic transactions conducted at DMV's website are subject to all of the requirements for processing and records maintenance as described in OAR chapter 735, division 10.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.012 & 803.460 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 802.012, 802.560, 803.200, 803.220, 803.360, 803.370, 803.450, 803.460, 807.420 & 807.560
