# Signature (OAR 735-018-0060)

## At a glance

## Full text

735-018-0060 Signature For any transaction that is required under Oregon law to be in writing and have a signature, an electronic signature shall have the same legal force and effect as if the person or organization had submitted the transaction in hardcopy with a handwritten signature in ink on paper.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.012 & 803.460 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 802.012, 802.560, 803.200, 803.220, 803.360, 803.370, 803.450, 803.460, 807.420 & 807.560
