## Full text

735-042-0010 Fleet Operator Responsibilities

(1) Fleet registration plates shall be surrendered to the Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division of the Department of Transportation (DMV) within 30 days of the date: (a) A vehicle becomes ineligible for fleet registration; or (b) A vehicle is withdrawn from a fleet.

(2) The fleet operator may certify that a plate has been lost or destroyed instead of surrendering a plate under section

(1) of this rule.

(3) The fleet operator is responsible for registration fees until a vehicle is ineligible or withdrawn from the fleet and: (a) The registration plate is received by DMV; or (b) DMV receives a certification that the registration plate is lost or destroyed.

(4) When a new fleet qualifies for this program, the fleet operator shall: (a) Turn in the plates currently on the vehicles to DMV; or (b) Destroy the plates and provide DMV with a certification listing the plates that were destroyed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.010, 803.415, 803.420 & 805.120 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 805.120
