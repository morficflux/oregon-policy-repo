## Full text

735-090-0125 Original Hearing The date of the original hearing, as used in ORS 813.440(1)(f), is the initial date that OAH scheduled a hearing to be held for a particular case, regardless of whether a hearing actually took place on that date.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341, 184.616, 813.410 & 813.440 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 813.410 & 813.440

History: DMV 30-2009, f. 12-22-09, cert. ef. 1-1-10
