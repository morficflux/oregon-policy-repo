## Full text

735-070-0035 Effect of Commercial Driving Privilege Withdrawal on Non-Commercial Driving Privileges

(1) DMV may withdraw a person’s commercial driving privileges without withdrawing the person’s non-commercial driving privileges

(2) DMV may not issue a hardship permit to operate a commercial motor vehicle.

(3) When DMV withdraws a person’s commercial driving privileges without withdrawing a person’s non-commercial driving privileges, the person shall surrender the person’s commercial driver license and apply for a Class C driver license.

(4) For the purpose of this rule, “withdraw” or “withdrawal” means cancellation, suspension, revocation, or disqualification of commercial driving privileges by this state or by another jurisdiction.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 802.010, 802.200 & 807 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 809.240 & 49 CFR 384.210
