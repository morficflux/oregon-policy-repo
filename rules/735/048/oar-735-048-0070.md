## Full text

735-048-0070 Complaints Complaints about improper farm or proportional farm registration or misuse of vehicles registered may be filed with the Commerce and Compliance Division (CCD) Salem Office: 455 Airport Road SE, Building A, Salem, OR 97301.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 805.200 & 805.300 - 805.410 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 805.380 & 805.410
