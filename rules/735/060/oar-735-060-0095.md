## Full text

735-060-0095 CDL Third Party Examiner Training

(1) An individual wishing to obtain a CDL Third Party Examiner Certificate must successfully complete the CDL Third Party Examiner initial training course as required by DMV as part of the approval process. This initial training course includes the CSTIMS Privacy Awareness Training.

(2) Each Examiner must successfully complete a refresher training course and examination at least every four years. DMV may require an Examiner to successfully complete the refresher training course and examination more frequently than every four years.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 802.010 & 807.080 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 807.040 & 807.070
