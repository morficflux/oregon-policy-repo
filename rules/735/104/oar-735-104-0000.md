## Full text

735-104-0000 Glazing Materials The Transportation Safety Office of the Oregon Department of Transportation (Transportation Safety) adopts Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) Number 205, Glazing Materials, regarding glazing materials that may be used in vehicle windows and windshields. This standard is as found in the Code of Federal Regulations, Title 49, Part 571.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 815.030 & 815.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 815.040
