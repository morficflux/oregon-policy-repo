## Full text

735-062-0002 Definitions As used in these OAR 735, division 62, rules the following definitions apply:

(1) “AAMVA” means the American Association of Motor Vehicle Administrators.

(2) “Biometric data” means measurements of the physical characteristics of person’s face that can be used to authenticate the identity of the person.

(3) “Caregiver” means an individual who has any level of responsibility for the safety and well-being of a minor.

(4) “CDLIS” means the Commercial Driver’s License Information System operated by AAMVA for the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration.

(5) “DMV” means the Oregon Department of Transportation, Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division.

(6) “First-time Real ID” means the first time DMV issues an Oregon Real ID to an applicant and may be issued as an original, renewal or replacement driver license, driver permit or identification card, depending on the privilege that the applicant possesses.

(7) “Lawful status,” for the purpose of applying for a Real ID driver license, driver permit or identification card, has the meaning set forth in 6 CFR §37.3.

(8) “LexisNexis Database” means a database of personal information provided by the LexisNexis corporation that is used to verify the identification of an individual.

(9) “NDR” means National Driver Register, a division in the National Center for Statistics and Analysis under the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration.

(10) “PDPS” means the Problem Driver Pointer System, a database operated by NDR.

(11) “Proctor” means a caregiver of the applicant who is at least 21 years of age and whose identity (name, date of birth and address) has been verified through the LexisNexis database.

(12) “Real ID” has the meaning set forth in ORS 807.450, and is marked with the distinguishing feature of a star in the upper right corner.

(13) “S2S” means State to State Verification Services operated by AAMVA.

(14) “SAVE” means Systemic Alien Verification for Entitlements program, administered by the United States Citizenship and Immigration Services, a component of the Department of Homeland Security.

(15) “SPEXS” means the State Pointer Exchange Services, the platform that supports S2S and CDLIS.

(16) “SSA” means the Social Security Administration.

(17) “SSN” means Social Security number.

(18) “USPVS” means United States Passport Verification Services which allows licensing agencies to verify US Passport document data against the Department of Homeland Security’s backend systems and is operated by AAMVA.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 802.010, 807.021, 807.050, 807.450, 807.460 & 6 CFR §37.3 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 801.163, 802.200, 807.021, 807.024, 807.050 & 807.450
