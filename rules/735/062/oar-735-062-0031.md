735-062-0031 Proof of Residence Address For Real ID

(1) DMV requires all applicants for a first-time Real ID to present proof to establish the applicant’s current residence address. Current residence address is the address where the applicant actually lives. DMV will include this address on the Real ID driver license, driver permit, or identification card. Acceptable proof to establish current residence address includes any two items listed in section

(2) of this rule, which must be presented in hardcopy format. Proof may be of the same type, but may not be issued from the same institution.

(2) Proof of residence address must contain the applicant’s first name, last name, and current residence address and includes any of the following: (a) Any proof of lawful status, identity and date of birth document listed in OAR 735-062-0022 or 735-062-0020. (b) Mortgage documents or a rental or lease agreement. (c) Monthly rental receipt or letter from a hotel, motel, campground or recreational vehicle park that permits extended stays. (d) DMV Form 735-7480, Affidavit of Shared Residence. The person certifying the applicant’s address must accompany the applicant at the time of application and present one acceptable proof of address document as set forth in this rule. (e) Utility hook-up order or account statement issued by the service provider. (f) A loan agreement, payment booklet, voucher, statement or other financial record. (g) Any document delivered to the current resident address by the United States Postal Service, FedEx, United Parcel Service, or other delivery service. DMV will accept a document with or without the envelope or packaging as long as the residence address is printed on the document. (h) Oregon vehicle title or registration documents. (i) Oregon manufactured structure ownership documents. (j) Oregon voter notification card. (k) Selective Service card. (L) Insurance card, insurance policy, medical records or health benefits card. (m) Educational institution transcript forms or other school documents showing enrollment for the current school year. (n) An unexpired professional license issued by an agency in the United States. (o) Form DS2019, Certificate of Eligibility for Exchange Visitor (J-1) Status. (p) A pay stub, W2, 1099 or an income tax return. (q) A letter approved by DMV. The letter must certify the applicant’s residence address and be from one of the following organizations: (A) The United States Postal Service; (B) The Oregon State Hospital; (C) An Oregon VA Rehabilitation Center; (D) A homeless shelter; (E) A transitional service provider; (F) A nursing home; (G) An assisted living care facility; (H) An adult care service provider; (I) A skilled nursing care facility; (J) A halfway house; or (K) A group home. (r) A letter from a corporation or business that provides corporate housing to employees. (s) Form 735-6438A, Request for Police or Public Agency Address on DMV Records, if the applicant is authorized under ORS 802.250 to use an agency address in lieu of residence address.

(3) If the applicant does not have a residence address, DMV may accept a descriptive address with a mailing address. DMV may require the applicant to provide proof that no residence address has been assigned to the property. Such proof may include, but is not limited to, a statement from the United States Postal Service or from the Assessor’s Office in the county where the property is located.

(4) An applicant who is homeless may use a descriptive address of the location where the applicant actually resides, e.g., “under the west end of Burnside Bridge.” The applicant must prove that the applicant is a resident or domiciled in Oregon pursuant to OAR 735-016-0040. In addition to the descriptive address, the applicant must also provide a mailing address.

(5) Notwithstanding OAR 735-062-0030, an applicant may not use a residence address of “continuous traveler” or “temporary residence” for a first-time, renewal or replacement Real ID driver license, permit, or identification card.

(6) An applicant applying for a renewal Real ID must provide proof of residence address as provided in OAR 735-062-0030.
