## Full text

735-062-0295 Fee for Real ID Credentials In addition to any fee imposed by ORS 807.370 or 807.410, DMV shall impose a fee of $30 for each original, renewed or replacement Real ID credential to cover the costs related to issuing Real IDs.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 802.010 & 807.460 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 807.460

History: DMV 25-2020, adopt filed 06/25/2020, effective 07/06/2020
