## Full text

735-076-0060 Hearing Provisions A person issued a notice of suspension or cancellation under these rules has the right to request a contested case hearing. To request a hearing, the person must submit a hearing request that meets the requirements of OAR 735-074-0220.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 807.340 & 809.410 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 807.340
