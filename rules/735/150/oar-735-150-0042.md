## Full text

735-150-0042 Vehicle Consignments For purposes of ORS 822.015 and 822.060, only a certified dealer who holds a vehicle dealer certificate issued or renewed under ORS 822.020 may take and sell vehicles on consignment from other jurisdictions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 802.010, 822.015, 822.060 & 822.094 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 822.015, 822.060 & 822.094
