## Full text

735-150-0000 Authority and Purpose Division 150 rules are adopted under the authority of ORS 822.035. These rules establish procedures and requirements for the administration and enforcement of laws relating to the regulation of Oregon vehicle dealers. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.010 & 822.035 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 822.035
