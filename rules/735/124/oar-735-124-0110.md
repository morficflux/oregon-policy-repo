# Mirrors (OAR 735-124-0110)

## At a glance

OAR 735-124-0110 — Mirrors. Chapter 735, Division 124. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

735-124-0110 Mirrors All Class “A” and “B” vehicles shall also be equipped with two side view mirrors; one installed on each side in such a manner as to give the driver a clear view to the rear in all lanes of traffic.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616 & 820.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 820.010 - 820.030

History: MV 57, f. 7-19-74, ef. 8-11-74, Renumbered from 735-081-0032 MV 49(Temp), f. & ef. 7-11-73 thru 11-8-73
