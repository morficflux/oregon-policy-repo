## Full text

735-124-0000 Use of Trailer Prohibited The use of a trailer for transporting workers except a bus trailer attached to a truck tractor by means of a fifth-wheel mounting is prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616 & 820.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 820.010 - 820.030
