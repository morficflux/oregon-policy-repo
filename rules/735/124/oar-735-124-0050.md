# Overhead Protection Required (OAR 735-124-0050)

## At a glance

OAR 735-124-0050 — Overhead Protection Required. Chapter 735, Division 124. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

735-124-0050 Overhead Protection Required Class “A” and “B” vehicles shall be provided with bodies and tops of sufficient strength to support the entire weight of the fully loaded vehicle on its top or side if overturned. Adequate means of escape and proper ventilation shall be provided.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616 & 820.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 820.010 - 820.030
