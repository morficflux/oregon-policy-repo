# Horn (OAR 735-124-0190)

## At a glance

OAR 735-124-0190 — Horn. Chapter 735, Division 124. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

735-124-0190 Horn Every vehicle shall be equipped with a horn or horns in good working order and capable of emitting sounds audible under normal conditions from a distance of not less than 200 feet.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 815.230 & 820.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 820.010 - 820.030

History: MV 57, f. 7-19-74, ef. 8-11-74, Renumbered from 735-081-0048 MV 49(Temp), f. & ef. 7-11-73 thru 11-8-73
