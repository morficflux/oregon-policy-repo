## Full text

735-124-0010 School Bus Identification Removal Whenever a school bus is used in any service other than the actual transportation of children to and from school or authorized activities, the word “school bus” shall be covered or concealed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616 & 820.030 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 820.010 - 820.030
