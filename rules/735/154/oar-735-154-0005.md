## Full text

735-154-0005 Authority of Towing Business to Issue 10-day Trip Permits

(1) A towing business may issue 10-day trip permits as provided in ORS 803.600, if the towing business: (a) Submits a completed and signed agreement to issue trip permits to DMV; and (b) Agrees to abide by the Oregon statutes and administrative rules relating to the issuance and sale of 10-day trip permits, including but not limited to ORS 803.600 and OAR 735-034-0010.

(2) DMV may revoke the authority of a towing business to issue 10-day trip permits if the towing business fails to comply with the requirements of ORS 803.565 and OAR 735-034-0010.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.010, 803.600, 822.205 & 822.215 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 803.600 & 822.215
