## Full text

735-030-0105 Registration Card; Contents This rule is adopted under the authority of ORS 803.500(8). The purpose of the rule is to specify the information required by DMV to be shown on an Oregon registration card. In addition to the information specified under ORS 803.500(1) to (7), the following information is required to be shown on an Oregon vehicle registration card:

(1) Vehicle registration plate number;

(2) Vehicle title number;

(3) Registration card issue date;

(4) Registration expiration date;

(5) Vehicle fuel type (i.e., gas, diesel, electric, hybrid, etc.);

(6) Equipment number, if applicable;

(7) Vehicle style (i.e., pickup, 4-door, 2-door, etc.);

(8) Vehicle weight and length, if applicable;

(9) Title brand(s), if issued pursuant to OAR 735-024-0025;

(10) Odometer message, if required under OAR 735-028-0040;

(11) Oregon county of residence;

(12) Oregon county of use, if applicable;

(13) If the registered owner is an individual, the person's current residence address;

(14) If the registered owner is a business, the business address as defined in OAR 735-010-0008; and

(15) If the registered owner is an individual or business and the vehicle is primarily housed or dispatched from a location other than the actual residence or business address of the owner, the vehicle address as defined in OAR 735-010-0008.
