735-080-0046 Expiration and Renewal of Disabled Person Parking Permits

(1) Permits and their placards or decals expire as follows: (a) Temporary Disability Permit - at 11:59 pm on the date not later than six months from the date of issuance. When a certificate specifies an ending date of the disability that is less than six months, the ending date of the disability is used as the expiration date. (b) Foreign Visitor Permit - at 11:59 pm on the 30th day from the date of issuance. (c) Individual , Oregon Wounded Warrior , Wheelchair User , Family , and Program Disabled Person Parking - at 11:59 pm on the last day of the eighth year from the date of issuance, unless DMV invalidates the permit in accordance with ORS 811.628.

(2) To renew any permit placard or decal described in section

(1) of this rule, the holder must submit the appropriate completed renewal reminder or application as follows: (a) Parking Placard and Driver license or ID card Renewal Reminder (DMV form 735-7353) that includes the Certificate of Disability completed by the holder’s licensed physician, licensed nurse practitioner or licensed physician assistant and signed and dated no more than six months prior to the receipt of the application by DMV. (b) Disabled Person Parking Permit Application/Renewal: Individuals (DMV form 735-265) as described in OAR 735-080-0020, that includes the Certificate of Disability included on the application completed by the holder’s licensed physician, licensed nurse practitioner or licensed physician assistant and dated no more than six months prior to the receipt of the application by DMV. (c) Disabled Person Parking Family Placard Application (DMV form 735-265 FPP) that includes the Certificate of Disability, included on the Application, completed by the licensed physician treating the disabled individual, and signed and dated no more than six months prior to the receipt of the application by DMV. (d) Application for Program Disabled Person Parking Permit Placard (DMV form 735-265PP). (e) Parking Identification Card and Disabled Person Parking Permit Placard Application (DMV form 735-265PIP) that meets requirements described in OAR 735-080-0060 including the Certificate of Disability, included on the application, completed by the holder’s licensed physician, licensed nurse practitioner or licensed physician assistant.

(3) Expired permits must be destroyed. Invalidated permits must be returned immediately to DMV.
