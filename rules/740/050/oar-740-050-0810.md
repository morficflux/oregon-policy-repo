## Full text

740-050-0810 Storage of Household Goods Carriers of household goods are prohibited from granting free storage of household goods except as provided by tariff.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823.011, 825.202 & 825.224 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.202 & 825.224
