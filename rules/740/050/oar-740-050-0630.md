## Full text

740-050-0630 Rates Orders Unless otherwise provided by rule or specific order of the Department, rate orders related the transportation of household goods shall be mandatory upon all carriers enjoying operating authority to transport the affected commodities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 184.619, 823.011 & 825.232 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.200, 825.202 & 825.224
