## Full text

740-050-0600 New Rates Effective on Granting of Authority The initial rates of a household goods carrier shall be effective with the granting of authority to operate as a motor carrier.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 825.232 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.110, 825.202 & 825.224
