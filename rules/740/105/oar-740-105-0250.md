## Full text

740-105-0250 Welder Qualification

(1) Welders performing work under this procedure shall be qualified in accordance with AWS D1.1-92.

(2) Certificate of Welder Qualifications can be arranged for at community colleges, unions, and testing laboratories.

(3) Record of Certification. A record of welder certification shall be maintained by motor carriers employing welders completing welded repairs on LTRs. Such records shall be maintained at the motor carrier’s or repair facility’s address where such repairs are completed. The record of certification shall include at a minimum: (a) Welder’s name; (b) Certification number; (c) Date of certification; (d) Name of facility where certification was completed; (e) Name and title of person who issued the certification; and (f) Dates this certification was used. [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]
