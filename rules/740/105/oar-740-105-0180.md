# Arc (OAR 740-105-0180)

## At a glance

## Full text

740-105-0180 Arc Strikes Arc strikes shall be restricted to the area that will be covered by weld metal in the completed production weld. Arc strikes visible on post weld inspection shall be removed in such a manner as to restore the original surface.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 823.011, 825.210 & 825.252 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.210 & 825.252

History: MCT 3-1996, f. & cert. ef. 3-14-96, Renumbered from 860-066-0108 PUC 16-1993, f. & cert. ef. 8-19-93 (Order No. 93-1157)
