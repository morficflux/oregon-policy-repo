## Full text

740-105-0240 Safety Precautions As a minimum, safety precautions as published by the American Welding Society shall be used when welding and cutting.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 823.011, 825.210 & 825.252 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.210 & 825.252
