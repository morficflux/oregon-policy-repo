## Full text

740-105-0220 Inclement Weather Protection Welding will not be permitted in rain or high winds without adequate protection such as tents, etc.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 823.011, 825.210 & 825.252 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.210 & 825.252
