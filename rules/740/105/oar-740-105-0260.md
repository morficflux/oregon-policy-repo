# Records of (OAR 740-105-0260)

## Full text

740-105-0260 Records of Repair Each record of repair shall be maintained at the motor carrier’s official physical address of record and shall include at a minimum:

(1) Date of repair;

(2) Welder’s name and certification number that effected the repair; and

(3) Reason repair was needed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 823.011, 825.210 & 825.252 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.210 & 825.252

History: MCT 3-1996, f. & cert. ef. 3-14-96, Renumbered from 860-066-0116 PUC 16-1993, f. & cert. ef. 8-19-93 (Order No. 93-1157)
