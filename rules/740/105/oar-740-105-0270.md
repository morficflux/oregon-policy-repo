## Full text

740-105-0270 Reach Repair Identification The repaired reach shall be stamped on the side of the reach within 24 inches of the compensator with:

(1) The welder’s six-digit certification number; and

(2) The month and year of the completed repair.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 823.011, 825.210 & 825.252 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.210 & 825.252

History: MCT 3-1996, f. & cert. ef. 3-14-96, Renumbered from 860-066-0117 PUC 16-1993, f. & cert. ef. 8-19-93 (Order No. 93-1157)
