## Full text

740-105-0170 Groove Welds and Weld Profiles

(1) Groove Welds. Complete penetration groove welds shall be welded with a backing strip.

(2) Weld Profiles. All single bevel groove welds shall be ground flush with the base material.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 823.011, 825.210 & 825.252 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.210 & 825.252

History: MCT 3-1996, f. & cert. ef. 3-14-96, Renumbered from 860-066-0107 PUC 16-1993, f. & cert. ef. 8-19-93 (Order No. 93-1157)
