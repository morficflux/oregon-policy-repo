# General Information (OAR 740-105-0010)

## At a glance

OAR 740-105-0010 — General Information. Chapter 740, Division 105. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

740-105-0010 General Information Any empty trailer placed upon any truck or any empty pole trailer loaded upon any truck-tractor shall be fastened by not less than one chain, wire rope or synthetic webbing and one tensioning or locking device in such manner as to prevent it from falling, rolling or sliding off the truck or truck-tractor while in transit.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 469, 757, 823, 824 & 825 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.252
