# Holding (OAR 740-035-0050)

## At a glance

## Full text

740-035-0050 Holding Out as For-Hire Carrier No person shall hold himself or herself out to the public as willing to transport persons or property for hire by motor vehicle without holding operating authority from the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823.011 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.100
