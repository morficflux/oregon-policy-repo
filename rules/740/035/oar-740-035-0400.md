## Full text

740-035-0400 Commissions Paid to Agents Motor carriers are forbidden to pay commissions or grant consideration to their agents upon charges for motor carrier services performed for such agents.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823 & 825 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.224
