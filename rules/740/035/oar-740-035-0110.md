## Full text

740-035-0110 Classification of Motor Carriers The issuance of certificates or permits to for-hire and private carriers will be classified as follows: (1)(a) 1A Intrastate Carrier — Property, except Household Goods; (b) 1G Intrastate For-Hire Carrier — Other than Local Cartage Household Goods; (c) 1C Intrastate For-Hire Carrier — Local Cartage Household Goods; (d) 1B Intrastate For-Hire Carrier — Local Cartage Household Goods under ORS 825.240; (e) 1P Intrastate For-Hire, Regular Route Full-Service Scheduled Carrier — Passengers; and (f) 1R Intrastate, For-Hire, Other Than Regular Route Full-Service Scheduled Carrier — Passengers. (2)(a) 4A Interstate For-Hire Carrier — Regulated Commodities; and (b) 4E Interstate For-Hire Carrier — Exempt Commodities. (3)(a) 3A Oregon-Based Private Carrier; and (b) 6A Foreign-Based Private Carrier.

(4) 7W Carriers Operating Under ORS 825.020.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823.011 & 825.232 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.230 & 825.234
