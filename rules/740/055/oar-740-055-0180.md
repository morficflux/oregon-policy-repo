## Full text

740-055-0180 Certified Scale Weights Required Actual weight of shipments of household goods must be obtained by having such shipments weighed over a certified scale whenever scale is available at point of origin, destination or enroute. The scale ticket shall be attached to the carrier’s copy of the freight bill and be retained as a part of the carrier’s records.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823.011, 825.202 & 825.224 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.202 & 825.224
