## Full text

740-055-0500 Motor Carriers of Property Except as modified by ORS 825.515, “Preservation of Records,” contained in 49 CFR, Parts 1220-1239, revised as of October 1, 2015, a copy of which is available from ODOT Commerce and Compliance Division, is adopted and prescribed by the Department for all for-hire motor carriers of property.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 823.011, 825.202, 825.232 & 825.515 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.202, 825.232 & 825.515
