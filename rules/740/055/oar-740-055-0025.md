## Full text

740-055-0025 Temporary Pass Fees The department may issue a temporary pass as described in ORS 825.470 for a single trip or short-time operation not exceeding 10 days. A pass fee of $9 will be collected for each pass issued.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 825.470 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.470
