## Full text

740-010-0020 Emergency Suspension of Statutes — Delegation The Director of the Oregon Department of Transportation authorizes the following line of succession to temporarily suspend operation of statutes involving motor carriers as described in ORS 823.012 in the event the Director is not available:

(1) Administrator of the Commerce and Compliance Division (CCD);

(2) Manager of the Salem Motor Carrier Services Section of CCD;

(3) Manager of Investigations/Safety/Federal Programs Section of CCD;

(4) Manager of Field Motor Carrier Services Section of CCD.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619, 823.011 & 823.012 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 823.012
