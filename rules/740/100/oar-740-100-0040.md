## Full text

740-100-0040 Chains/Traction Devices All motor carriers are required to carry and use chains and traction tires as provided in OAR chapter 734 division 17 rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823.011 & 825.252 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 815.140
