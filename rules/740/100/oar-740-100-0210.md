## Full text

740-100-0210 Smoking Prohibited The use of lighted tobacco in any form in any vehicle engaged in transporting passengers in regular route scheduled service is prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823 & 825 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.210 & 825.252
