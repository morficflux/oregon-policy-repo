## Full text

740-100-0055 Commercial Vehicle Inspection Reciprocity In general, vehicles displaying a valid Oregon Commercial Vehicle Inspection Decal are not subject to re-inspection. However, if an obvious defect is noticed on a vehicle with a current decal, nothing prevents a party from re-inspecting that vehicle. Maximum reciprocity will be granted to inspections conducted pursuant to the North American Standard Inspection procedures by other jurisdictions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 825.232 & 825.252 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 810.560, 825.210 & 825.250
