# Electronic Records (OAR 740-015-0090)

## At a glance

## Full text

740-015-0090 Electronic Records CCD records pertaining to electronic transactions conducted via an authorized system site are subject to all requirements for processing and records maintenance as described in OAR 740, division 55.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.619 & 802.012 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 802.012 & 825.515
