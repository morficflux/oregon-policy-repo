# Signature (OAR 740-015-0070)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

740-015-0070 Signature For any transaction that is required under Oregon law to be in writing and have a signature, an electronic signature shall have the same legal force and effect as if the person or organization had submitted the transaction in hardcopy with a handwritten signature in ink on paper.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 802.012 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 802.012

History: MCTD 5-2003, f. & cert. ef. 10-24-03
