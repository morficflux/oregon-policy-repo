## Full text

740-040-0020 Liability Insurance Carriers who are required to file liability insurance shall file evidence of such insurance for a minimum single limit of $750,000 per collision.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823 & 825 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.160
