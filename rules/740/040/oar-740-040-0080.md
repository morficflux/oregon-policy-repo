## Full text

740-040-0080 Instruments Filed Become Permanent Records All bonds, except those filed pursuant to ORS 825.166, policies or certificates of insurance filed with the Department as required by statute are permanent records and cannot be returned to insurer or removed from the custody of the Department as long as the insured is subject to regulation under ORS Chapter 825.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823 & 825 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.506
