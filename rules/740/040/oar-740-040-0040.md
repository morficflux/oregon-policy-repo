# Sanctions for Insurance Cancellation (OAR 740-040-0040)

## Full text

740-040-0040 Sanctions for Insurance Cancellation If a carrier’s policy required pursuant to ORS 825.160 or 825.162 is cancelled, the motor vehicles assigned to that carrier shall not be operated on the highways of this state and the carrier’s certificate or permit may be suspended by the Department.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 823.011, 825.160 & 825.164 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.164
