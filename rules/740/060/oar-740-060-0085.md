## Full text

740-060-0085 Hourly Rate Local Moves A carrier must apply a local hourly rate when a move is wholly within a city limit or commercial zone specified in the carrier’s ODOT approved local cartage authority. The Department may approve an extension to the radius of mileage for a commercial zone when approving a tariff.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 825.232 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.202
