## Full text

740-045-0130 Leased Vehicles in Interstate Commerce With the exception of buses, all vehicles operating under lease within the state of Oregon in interstate commerce must carry a copy of the lease, to be produced on request of the Department or its authorized representative.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.335, 823.011 & 825.232 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 825.100, 825.104 & 825.210
