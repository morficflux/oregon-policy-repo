## Full text

459-013-0110 Eligibility for Early Benefits A member who reaches earliest retirement age under ORS 238.280 becomes eligible for a service retirement benefit that will be paid upon written application to the Board. After becoming eligible under that section, a member may not withdraw their account under ORS 238.265 nor will their membership terminate by operation of ORS 238.095(2).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 238.650 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 238.280 & 238.095
