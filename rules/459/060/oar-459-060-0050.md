## Full text

459-060-0050 Request for Benefit Estimate Unless otherwise required by a judicial order or valid subpoena, or expressly authorized in writing by the affected active or inactive member, as defined in ORS 238.005(7), PERS shall not provide an estimate of a potential service or disability retirement allowance to anyone other than the member.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.355 & 238.650 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.311 - 192.431, 237.410 - 237.520, 237.610 - 237.620, 237.950 - 37.980 & 238
