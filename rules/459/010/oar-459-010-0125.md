## Full text

459-010-0125 Eligibility of Volunteer Firefighters Upon proper application by the employer, volunteer firefighters as a unit are eligible for membership even though all other employees of that employer are excluded.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 238.650 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 238.015
