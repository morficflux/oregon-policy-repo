## Full text

459-007-0340 Crediting Earnings for an OPSRP Pension Program Cash Out of Small Benefits No earnings or distribution interest from the effective date of retirement to the date of distribution shall be credited to the amount of a cash out of small benefits under ORS 238A.195.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 238A.450 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 238A.120 & 238A.195

History: PERS 8-2023, amend filed 06/02/2023, effective 06/02/2023 PERS 5-2009, f. & cert. ef. 4-6-09
