## Full text

459-007-0020 Statutory Limitation on the Crediting of Earnings No earnings shall be credited to any payment from the Fund unless specifically provided for in ORS Chapters 238, 238A and OAR chapter 459, divisions 007 and 009.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 238.650 & 238A Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 238 & 238A
