## Full text

459-005-0260 Accounts Receivable — Fraud

(1) PERS will investigate all suspected fraudulent activities in order to maintain the integrity and proper distribution of benefits.

(2) PERS may pursue all available legal and administrative actions in fraud cases discovered under section

(1) of this rule, including but not limited to: (a) Criminal prosecution under ORS Chapters 162, 164 and 165; or (b) Civil sanctions under ORS Chapter 180.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 238.650 & 238A.450 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS Ch. 238 & 238A
