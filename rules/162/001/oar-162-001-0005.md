## Full text

162-001-0005 Model Rules of Procedure The Model Rules of Procedure as promulgated by the Attorney General of the State of Oregon under the Administrative Procedures Act effective January 1, 2006, are adopted as the rules of procedure for the Audits Division of the Secretary of State's Office. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Audits Division.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 297.210, 297.465, 128.435 & 692.285
