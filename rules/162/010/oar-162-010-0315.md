## Full text

162-010-0315 State School Fund There shall be comments regarding the school district’s compliance with legal requirements as stated in ORS 327.013 and as further defined by the Oregon State Department of Education pertaining to the district’s calculation and reporting of the factors used to compute the State School Fund distribution.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 297 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 328.465

History: AUDIT 1-1998, f. 2-2-98, cert. ef. 2-15-98
