## Full text

162-010-0140 Schedule of Accountability for Independently Elected Officials A schedule of accountability must be prepared for each independently elected official collecting or receiving money on behalf of the municipal corporation. This schedule is to present, at a minimum, beginning balances, receipts, disbursements, refunds, and turnovers to the appropriate governmental official, reconciled to cash on hand at the beginning and end of the audit period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 297 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 297.465
