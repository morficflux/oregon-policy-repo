## Full text

162-010-0270 Insurance and Fidelity Bonds Compliance with legal requirements and the government’s policies relating to insurance and fidelity bond coverage shall be disclosed. Recommendations for further review of coverage by qualified individuals may be included, if appropriate.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 297 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 297.465
