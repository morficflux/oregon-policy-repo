## Full text

162-010-0260 Budget Compliance with legal requirements relating to the preparation, adoption, and execution of the annual or biennial budget for the year being audited, and the preparation and adoption of the budget for the next succeeding year shall be disclosed. The auditor shall also disclose the financial or organizational level at which the governing body makes the annual appropriations. The minimum levels of legal appropriation are established in ORS 294.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 297 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 297.465
