## Full text

162-010-0316 Public Charter Schools Public charter schools created under ORS Chapter 338 are subject to review and compliance with the following:

(1) Insurance and Fidelity Bonds — OAR 162-010-0270;

(2) Programs Funded from Outside Sources — OAR 162-010-0280; and

(3) Public Contracts and Purchasing — OAR 162-010-0310.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 297 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 328.465

History: AUDIT 1-2010, f. 3-23-10, cert. ef. 4-1-10
