## Full text

162-040-0095 Officers and Registered Agent of the Municipal Corporation Immediately inside the front cover, each financial report shall contain the names and addresses of officers of the municipal corporation and members of its governing body. In addition, financial reports of special districts, as defined by law (ORS Chapter 198), shall contain the name of the district's registered agent and its registered address. If a special district has not designated a registered agent or registered address, the report shall so indicate.
