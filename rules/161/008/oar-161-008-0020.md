## Full text

161-008-0020 Requests to Inspect or Obtain Copies of Public Records A request to inspect or obtain copies of a public record or information from public records shall be made in writing and shall include:

(1) The name of the requester;

(2) Identification of the specific public record being requested, and the type and format of needed public record information, if known to the requester; and

(3) The number of copies requested, if applicable.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 674.305 & 674.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.420 & 674.310
