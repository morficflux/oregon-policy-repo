## Full text

161-020-0065 Denial of Approval If an application for course approval is accepted for consideration, but the course is subsequently not approved by the Board, the course owner/affiliated entity shall be notified in writing. The Board will also notify the course owner/affiliated entity of the reasons for the denial of the application and also inform the course owner/affiliated entity of its right to appeal the decision to deny the application by requesting a contested case hearing in accordance with ORS Chapter 183 within sixty days of the notice of denial.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 674.305 & 674.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 674.310
