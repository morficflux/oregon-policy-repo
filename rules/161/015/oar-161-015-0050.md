## Full text

161-015-0050 Qualifications to Take Examination To qualify to take any examination, an applicant must meet the education and experience requirements set forth in these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 674.305(8) & 674.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 674.310
