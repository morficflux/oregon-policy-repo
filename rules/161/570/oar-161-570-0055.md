## Full text

161-570-0055 Enforcement

(1) The Board may issue a notice of proposed disciplinary action for violation(s) of ORS 674.200 to 674.250, or of rules adopted by the Board.

(2) The Administrator or the Administrator’s designee shall have the authority to negotiate and approve a stipulated settlement at any time prior to issuance of a Final Order by the Board.

(3) In the event of second or subsequent violations of ORS 674.200 to 674.250, or of rules adopted by the Board, the Administrator shall not consider a prior Final Order that was issued more than five

(5) years preceding the date of the second or subsequent notice of proposed sanctions.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.355, 674.305 & 674.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 674

History: ACLB 1-2013, f. 1-30-13, cert. ef. 1-31-13
