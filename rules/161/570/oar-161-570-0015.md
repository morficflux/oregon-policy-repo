## Full text

161-570-0015 Appraisal Review Any employee or independent contractor appraiser of an appraisal management company that performs the act or process of developing and communicating a reviewer’s own opinion of value as part of an appraisal review for a property located in this state:

(1) Must be licensed or certified in this state pursuant to ORS 674.100; and

(2) Must comply with the appraisal review provisions of the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.355, 674.305 & 674.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 674.305(7), 674.310(2), 674.205, 674.215, 674.230, 674.245 & 674.250
