# Display of Certificate or License (OAR 161-050-0030)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

161-050-0030 Display of Certificate or License Each licensee, and each appraiser assistant registered in this state (other than those of inactive status), shall display prominently, his or her license or certificate or registration in their principal place of business.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 674.305(8) & 674.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 674.310
