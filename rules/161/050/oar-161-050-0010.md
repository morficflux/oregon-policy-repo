## Full text

161-050-0010 Grandfathering Not Permitted No applicant shall be deemed exempt from meeting the criteria established for licensing or certification, or be otherwise “grandfathered” into the Oregon appraiser licensing and certification system.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 674.305(8) & 674.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 674.310

History: ACLB 1-1994, f. & cert. ef. 2-1-94 ACLB 2-1992, f. & cert. ef. 4-30-92 ACLB 1-1992(Temp), f. & cert. ef. 1-23-92
