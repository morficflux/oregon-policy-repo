## Full text

161-003-0020 Fees The Board shall charge and collect the following fees:

(1) Examination Fee — Actual Fee;

(2) Application Fee — $150;

(3) Fee for Certificate or License Issued (two years) — $675;

(4) Fee for Certificate or License Renewed (two years) — $715;

(5) Fee for Duplicate Certificate/License — $40;

(6) Fee for Inactive Certificate or License (two years) — $300;

(7) Fee for Renewal of Inactive Certificate or License (two years) — $300;

(8) Fee for Reactivation of Inactive Certificate or License — $200;

(9) Fee for Late License/Certificate Renewal (in addition to renewal fee): (a) Within 90 days after license expiration – $150; (b) Within 91-180 days after license expiration – $300; (c) 181 days or more after license expiration - $500.

(10) Fee for Temporary Registration — $275;

(11) Annual Federal Registry Fee (set by the ASC of the FFIEC) — Actual Fee;

(12) Appraiser Assistant Registration — $175;

(13) Appraiser Assistant Registration Renewal — $175;

(14) Supervising Appraiser Endorsement — $125;

(15) FBI Criminal Background Check — Actual Fee;

(16) Fee for License History — $50;

(17) Qualifying Education Course — $250;

(18) Continuing Education Course — $200;

(19) Continuing Education Course - 1-2 day seminar — $75;

(20) Education Course Provider Application — $100.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 674.305 & 674.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 674
