## Full text

101-005-0100 Extension of Time for Acceptance of Proposal The Board may request, orally or in writing that Proposers extend, in writing, the time during which the Board may consider their Proposal. If a Proposer agrees to such extension, the Proposal shall continue as irrevocable, valid and binding on the Proposer for the agreed-upon extension period.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 243.061 - 243.302 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS.243.125(1)
