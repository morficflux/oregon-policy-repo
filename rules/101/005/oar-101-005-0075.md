## Full text

101-005-0075 Pre-Proposal Conference

(1) Unless identified in the procurement as required, the Pre-Proposal Conference will: (a) Include voluntary attendance; (b) Will be held in Salem, Oregon or will be conducted via conference call or by other electronic means; and (c) Will identify attendees by name and company represented;

(2) If the Pre-Proposal Conference requires mandatory attendance by prospective proposers, no remuneration will be offered to prospective proposers for attendance, travel, document preparation, etc.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 243.061 - 243.302 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243.135 & 243.125
