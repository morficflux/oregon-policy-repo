# Policy (OAR 101-005-0020)

## At a glance

OAR 101-005-0020 — Policy. Chapter 101, Division 005. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

101-005-0020 Policy The policy of the Board is to select Contractors in an expeditious and efficient a manner that is consistent with the goal of delivering high quality Benefits and other services at a cost that is affordable to both the employees and the state, consistent with the requirements of ORS 242.135 and OAR 101-002-0005. The Board may enter into more than one contract for each type of Benefit Plan or other service sought.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 243.061 - 243.302 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243.125 & 243.135(2)
