## Full text

101-030-0026 Employer Designated Furlough Leaves The State of Oregon as the employer may designate furlough leave without pay. Furlough leave does not affect an employee’s eligibility or current enrollment in medical, dental, and employee basic life insurance.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 243.061 - 302 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 243.061-302 & 292.05
