## Full text

951-004-0002 Application for Certification A person proposing to produce a Qualifying Film Production and wishing to receive a labor rebate with respect to the production shall submit an application to the Oregon Film and Video Office for certification. The application must be in a form designated by OFVO and submitted within 10 business days of commencement of pre-production in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 284.335 & OL 2005 & ch 559 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 2005 & ch 559

History: FVO 2-2006, f. & cert. ef. 11-17-06
