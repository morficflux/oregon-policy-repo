# Purpose (OAR 951-007-0000)

## At a glance

## Full text

951-007-0000 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide guidance for the administration of the portion of the Oregon Production Investment Fund that is to be used for film and television production expense reimbursement for regional production work as authorized by ORS284.368(4).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS284.368(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS284.368(4)
