# Purpose (OAR 951-005-0000)

## At a glance

## Full text

951-005-0000 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to provide guidance for the payment of rebates from the Greenlight Oregon Labor Rebate Fund as authorized by Oregon Laws 2005, chapter 559. The Greenlight Oregon Labor Rebate Fund was authorized by the 2005 legislature to encourage film and video production in Oregon, thereby increasing job opportunities for Oregonians in the film and video industry and bringing additional production spending into the state.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 284.335 & OL 2005 & ch 559 Statutes/Other Implemented: OL 2005 & ch 559

History: FVO 1-2006, f. & cert. ef. 11-17-06
