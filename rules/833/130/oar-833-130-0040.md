## Full text

833-130-0040 Supervisor Candidates

(1) Supervisor Candidates must work toward meeting the requirements of an Approved Supervisor.

(2) To qualify as a Supervisor Candidate, a licensee must meet the following requirements: (a) Hold an active Oregon license as a professional counselor or as a marriage and family therapist; (b) Complete 30 clock hours of post-master’s degree supervision training; (c) Successfully pass the Board’s law and rules exam; and (d) For supervisors of marriage and family therapist associates only: supervision training that includes systems components.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.705 - 675.835 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.705 - 675.835
