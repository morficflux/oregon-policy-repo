## Full text

833-070-0021 Fee Refunds Fees are nonrefundable, except that overpayment of fees or fees submitted as part of application before required will be refunded.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.785 - 675.835 & 676.160 - 676.180 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.785 - 675.835
