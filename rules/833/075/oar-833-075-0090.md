## Full text

833-075-0090 Representation of Credentials A person may not use the title of “licensed professional counselor” or “licensed marriage and family therapist,” including the abbreviations “LPC” and “LMFT,” unless the person holds a current license issued by the Board.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.705 - 675.835 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.705 - 675.835
