## Full text

833-050-0141 Placing Registration on Hold

(1) Registered associates may request to place associate registration on hold for up to 90 days by submitting a written request to the board with explanation of need for being on hold.

(2) Registered associates must notify the Board within 10 days, in writing, of a plan to return to practice.

(3) If after 90 days, the registered associate does not submit a plan to return to practice, the associate registration will expire.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.785 - 675.835 & 676.160 - 676.180 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.785 - 675.835
