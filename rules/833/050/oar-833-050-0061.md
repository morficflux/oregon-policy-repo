## Full text

833-050-0061 Effective Dates of Associate Registration

(1) Initial registration as an associate will be effective when applicant completes the registration process that includes approval of completed request and associate registration plan.

(2) A registered associate must practice under supervision until all license requirements are met and the license is issued.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.785 - 675.835 & 676.160 - 676.180 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.785 - 675.835
