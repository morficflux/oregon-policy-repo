## Full text

833-050-0151 Termination of Associate Registration Plan A registered associate may voluntarily resign registration. A resigned registration will constitute withdrawal of application for licensure.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 675.785 - 675.835 & 676.160 - 676.180 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 675.785 - 675.835
