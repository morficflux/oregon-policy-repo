# Review of (OAR 809-040-0020)

## Full text

809-040-0020 Review of Examination Test questions, scoring keys, and other data used to administer the national examination and the engineering geologist examination are confidential and may not be disclosed to candidates unless otherwise required by law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 192.345(4), 670.310(1) & ORS 672.575 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.575
