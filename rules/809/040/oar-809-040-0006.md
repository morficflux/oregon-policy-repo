## Full text

809-040-0006 Scope of Geologist Examination The national examination consists of:

(1) The Fundamentals of Geology section testing knowledge of geology obtained during education in the geosciences; and

(2) The Practice of Geology section testing knowledge, skills, and abilities gained by experience in the field and the ability to solve geologic problems.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310(1), 672.555 & ORS 672.575 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.575 & ORS 672.555(2)(c)
