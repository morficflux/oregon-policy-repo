## Full text

809-040-0008 Scope of Engineering Geologist Examination The engineering geologist examination shall be a written examination testing knowledge of engineering geology gained by the required experience in that specialty.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310(1), 672.565 & ORS 672.575 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.575 & ORS 672.565(2)
