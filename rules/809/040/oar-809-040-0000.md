## Full text

809-040-0000 Examination Application

(1) Application for an examination or examination section shall be on forms provided by the Board and must be accompanied by the required fee(s), official transcript(s) of education, and, when applicable or requested by the Board, work experience verification(s).

(2) The Board shall evaluate the candidate’s qualifications to determine that the prerequisites as prescribed by ORS 672.555–672.565 and applicable Board rules in OAR Chapter 809 Division 30 have been met before the candidate is allowed to take the requested examination or examination section.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 182.466(4), 670.310, 672.575 & 672.705 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.555 to 672.595
