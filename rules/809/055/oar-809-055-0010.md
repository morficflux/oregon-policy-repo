## Full text

809-055-0010 Contested Case Proceedings The State Board of Geologist Examiners adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules for Contested Case Proceedings, OAR 137-003-0501 to 137-003-0700, as the rules of procedure for contested cases.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341 & 670.310(1) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.310 - 183.550 & 670.325
