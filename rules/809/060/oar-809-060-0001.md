## Full text

809-060-0001 Appointment of Board Members

(1) The Public Member shall be appointed by the Governor.

(2) The Governor may select and appoint any qualified Registered Geologist to fill a Board geologist position.

(3) If the Governor does not appoint a licensee to fill the expired term of an existing Board member, those members shall continue to serve until replaced.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, SB 546 & SB 1126 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 672.505 - 672.705
