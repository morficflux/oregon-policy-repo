## Full text

945-020-0010 Purpose; Applicability

(1) The purpose of OAR chapter 945, division 20 is to establish the process for certification of: (a) Health plans as qualified health plans (QHPs); and (b) Standalone dental plans (SADPs) as providing pediatric dental benefits.

(2) Except for multistate plans, as defined in 45 CFR 800.20, OAR chapter 945, division 20 applies to: (a) All QHPs offered through the Marketplace; and (b) All SADPs marketed through or outside the Marketplace as providing pediatric dental benefits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 741.002 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 741.310
