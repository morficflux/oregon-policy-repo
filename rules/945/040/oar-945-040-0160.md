## Full text

945-040-0160 Appeal to the United States Department of Health and Human Services If an appellant disagrees with the appeal decision of the Exchange, he or she may make an appeal request to HHS within 30 days of the date of the notice of appeal decision through any of the methods described in OAR 945-040-0100(5).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 741.002 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 741.500
