## Full text

845-020-0030 Standards of Cleanliness for Redemption Centers All persons responsible for the establishment and operation of the redemption center shall at all times keep the redemption center premises, including the parking facilities serving the redemption center, in full compliance with the law. Such persons shall keep such redemption center premises in good repair, painted, clean, well-lighted, free of litter and trash, and free of rodents, vermin, infestations of insects, and their harborages or breeding places.
