## Full text

845-004-0031 Refunds

(1) The Commission shall not refund any amount of $25.00 or less, except if a written request for a refund is filed by the person who paid the money.

(2) Refund requests must be filed within 18 months from the date of payment.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 471.040 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 293.445

History: OLCC 13-2016, f. 8-23-16, cert. ef. 9-3-16
