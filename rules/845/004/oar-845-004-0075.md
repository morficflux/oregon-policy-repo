## Full text

845-004-0075 Public Contracting The Commission adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules for Public Contracting effective August, 1990, by reference as a permanent rule of the Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 471 & 472, including 471.030, 471.730(1) & (5), 472.030, 472.060(1) & (2)(d) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 279.049
