## Full text

845-025-2110 Medical Marijuana Registrant CTS Registration A grow site administrator approved by the Authority for a grow site that is subject to tracking under OAR 333, Division 8 and a processing site or dispensary registered with the Authority under 475C.815 or 475C.833 must:

(1) Activate the assigned CTS account within 10 calendar days of notification; and

(2) Use CTS to track inventory as required by these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 475C.017 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 475C.871
