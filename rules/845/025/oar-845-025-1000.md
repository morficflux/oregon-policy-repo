## Full text

845-025-1000 Applicability

(1) A person may not produce, process, store, transport, sell, sample, test, or deliver marijuana for commercial recreational use without a license from the Commission or as otherwise authorized under these rules.

(2) Nothing in these rules exempts a licensee or licensee representative from complying with any other applicable state or local laws.

(3) Licensure under these rules does not protect a person from possible criminal prosecution under federal law.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 475C.017 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 475C.013, 475C.065, 475C.085, 475C.093, 475C.097, 475C.449 & 475C.548
