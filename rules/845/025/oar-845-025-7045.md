## Full text

845-025-7045 Marijuana Plant Labeling Requirements Prior to an immature marijuana plant being sold or transferred to a consumer, patient or designated primary caregiver a tag or label must be affixed to the plant or plant container that has the following information:

(1) Producer’s business or trade name and license number;

(2) Business or trade name of licensee that packaged the product, if different from the producer;

(3) Name of the strain;

(4) Product identity;

(5) UID number; and

(6) Universal symbol.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 475C.604 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 475C.604
