## Full text

845-006-0435 Maintaining Records: Retail Licensees Each retail licensee must keep an invoice of all alcoholic beverage deliveries to the licensed premises, together with the purchase particulars thereof, and any discount, rebate or allowance given by any manufacturer, importer or wholesaler of alcoholic beverages. Licensees will keep these records for a period of two years. Licensee will have these records available for inspection by the Commission at all times during business hours.
