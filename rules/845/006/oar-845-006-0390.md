## Full text

845-006-0390 Transportation by Licensed Retailer from Licensed Wholesaler Premises A licensed malt beverage or wine retailer may transport the malt beverages, cider, or wine the retailer purchases from a licensed wholesaler from the wholesaler’s premises to the retailer’s premises. The purchase price of such malt beverages, cider, or wine shall be the price listed pursuant to OAR 845-010-0210.
