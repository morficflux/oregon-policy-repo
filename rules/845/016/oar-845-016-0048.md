## Full text

845-016-0048 Restrictions

(1) The Commission may restrict a provider’s or instructor’s certificate when: (a) In the absence of a restriction, the Commission has a basis to cancel, suspend/fine or deny the certification; or (b) In addition to all or part of a suspension or fine, a restriction may prevent the recurrence of the problem(s) that caused the violation(s).

(2) Violation of a restriction is a Category I violation (see OAR 845-016-0080, Sanctions).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 471.030, 471.730(1) & (5), 472.030, 472.060(1) & (2)(d) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 471.542 & 471.547
