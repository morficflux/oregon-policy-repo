## Full text

845-015-0168 Full On-Premises Sales Licensee Refund A Full On-Premises Sales licensee who is going out of business may make a written request to the Commission to return resalable merchandise for a refund. If the Commission approves the request, the Commission will issue a refund after it determines that the merchandise is resalable.
