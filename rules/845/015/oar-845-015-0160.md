## Full text

845-015-0160 Sale of Lottery Tickets Despite OAR 845-015-0143(1) (sale of related items), retail sales agents may contract with the Oregon State Lottery Commission to sell Oregon State Lottery Tickets in retail liquor stores.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 471, 471.030, 471.730(1) & (5), 472.030, 472.060(1) & (2)(d) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 471.750(1)

History: OLCC 2-2003, f. 1-27-03, cert. ef. 2-1-03, Renumbered from 845-015-0065 LCC 5-1985, f. 3-28-85, ef. 4-1-85
