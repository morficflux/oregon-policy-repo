## Full text

845-015-0150 Service Refusal in a Retail Liquor Store

(1) A customer who meets the age and identification requirements in ORS 471.130 has the right to purchase alcoholic liquor in a retail liquor store.

(2) Despite section

(1) of this rule, a retail sales agent must refuse to sell alcoholic liquor to anyone who is visibly intoxicated, and may refuse service to anyone who is disruptive or abusive in a retail liquor store.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 471, 471.030, 471.730(1) & (5), 472.030, 472.060(1) & (2)(d) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 471.750(1)
