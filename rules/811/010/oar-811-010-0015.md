## Full text

811-010-0015 Filing Addresses Each licensee and certificate holder shall provide their current business and mailing addresses, including all practice locations, with the OBCE. Each licensee and certificate holder shall also provide their current electronic mail address. Licensees and certificate holders shall notify the OBCE in writing, giving the new mailing and electronic mail addresses within 30 calendar days of any change.
