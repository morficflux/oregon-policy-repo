## Full text

811-010-0080 Attendance at Board Meetings

(1) The Board is composed of seven members appointed by the Governor of the State of Oregon and subject to confirmation by the Senate.

(2) Four members present at any meeting shall constitute a quorum and therefore the regular or special business of the Board may be conducted.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 684 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 684.130 & 684.140
