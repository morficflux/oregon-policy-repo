## Full text

811-001-0060 Purchase of Board-Provided Materials For the following materials and services available to the public and licensees, the OBCE may charge as follows:

(1) List of licensees: $25.00.

(2) Ornamental wall certificate: $50.00.

(3) Requests requiring records search: Labor — $25.00 per hour or partial hour.

(4) The OBCE may charge for pamphlets and/or forms developed by the agency. Costs will be determined by the actual costs of printing and handling incurred by the OBCE.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183, 192 & 684 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 192.440 & 684.155(4)
