# Procedure in Disciplinary Proceedings (OAR 811-001-0025)

## At a glance

history are in the full text below.

## Full text

811-001-0025 Procedure in Disciplinary Proceedings All proceedings relating to the refusal to grant, suspension, or revocation of a license to practice chiropractic or for the reissuance or reinstatement of a license which has been suspended or revoked or for the disciplining of licensing in any manner shall be conducted in accordance with the provisions of the Rules of Administrative Procedure which are filed with the Secretary of State in accordance with the OAR 811-001-0005.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183 & 684 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341 & 684.155(8)
