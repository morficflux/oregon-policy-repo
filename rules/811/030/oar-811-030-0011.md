## At a glance

## Full text

811-030-0011 X-Rays and Other Imaging Staff of a chiropractic physician may be directed to take X-rays or other types of imaging of a patient if they are in possession of a permit or license issued by the Oregon Board of Medical Imaging.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 684 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 684.155
