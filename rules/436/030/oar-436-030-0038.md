## Full text

436-030-0038 Permanent Partial Disability The standards developed under ORS 656.726(4) and contained in OAR 436-035 must be applied when evaluating a worker’s permanent partial disability.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.268, 656.726, OL Ch. 332 1995 & Ch. 313 1999 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.214, 656.268, 656.726, OL Ch. 332 1995 & Ch. 313 1999
