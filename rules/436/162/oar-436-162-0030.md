## Full text

436-162-0030 Retention of Electronic Records Insurers and self-insured employers must retain workers' compensation records under OAR 436-050-0120 and OAR 436-050-0220. Records may be retained in electronic format if the records can be reproduced.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.455 & 731.475

History: WCD 7-2010, f. 10-1-10, cert. ef. 1-1-11
