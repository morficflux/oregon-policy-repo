## Full text

436-060-0160 Use of Sight Draft to Pay Compensation Prohibited Insurers may not use a sight draft to pay any benefits or payments due a worker or beneficiary under ORS chapter 656.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.726(4)
