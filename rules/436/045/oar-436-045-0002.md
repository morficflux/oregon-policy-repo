# Purpose (OAR 436-045-0002)

## At a glance

## Full text

436-045-0002 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish guidelines for administering disbursements made from the Reopened Claims Program established to reimburse compensation paid as a result of awards made by the Board or voluntary claim reopenings pursuant to ORS 656.278.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.625 & 656.726 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.625 & 656.726
