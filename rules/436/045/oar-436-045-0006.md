## Full text

436-045-0006 Administration of Rules Any orders issued by the divisions in carrying out the director’s authority to enforce ORS Chapter 656 and these rules are considered orders of the director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.704 & 656.726 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.704 & 656.726
