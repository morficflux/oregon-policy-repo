## Full text

436-110-0150 Pilot Projects

(1) The director may develop one or more pilot projects to test alternatives to the current system of re-employing preferred workers.

(2) Notwithstanding any other provision of these rules, the director and others participating in pilot projects are bound by the terms of the pilot project.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.622 & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.622
