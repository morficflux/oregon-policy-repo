## Full text

436-055-0110 Assessment of Civil Penalties

(1) Penalties for failure to comply with statutes, rules, and orders. The director may assess a civil penalty under ORS 656.745(2) against an insurer that violates ORS chapter 656, OAR 436-055, or an order of the director.

(2) Penalties for failure to comply with ORS 656.780. The director may assess a civil penalty against an insurer that fails to maintain or produce certification and training records as required by these rules, or that employs anyone other than an Oregon certified claims examiner to process claims.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726 & ORS 656.780 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.780 & 656.745
