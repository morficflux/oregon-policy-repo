## Full text

436-100-0002 Purpose of Rules The purpose of these rules is to establish requirements and procedures for offsetting permanent total disability benefits against social security disability benefits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.209 & 656.727 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.209
