## Full text

436-100-0008 Administrative Review

(1) Any worker aggrieved by any offset authorization of the division may apply to the Workers’ Compensation Division for a reconsideration of that authorization before requesting a hearing.

(2) Any party aggrieved may request a hearing under ORS 656.283.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726 & 656.727 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.209
