## Full text

436-085-0060 Assessment of Civil Penalties The director may assess a civil penalty under ORS 656.745(2) against an insurer, self-insured employer, or self-insured employer group that violates ORS chapter 656, OAR 436-085, or an order of the director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.745
