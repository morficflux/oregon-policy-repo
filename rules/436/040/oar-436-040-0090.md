## Full text

436-040-0090 Assessment of Civil Penalties The director, through the division and pursuant to ORS 656.745, may assess a civil penalty against an insurer. When the division imposes a penalty under this section, the order shall be issued in accordance with ORS 656.447, 656.704 and the contested case provisions of the Administrative Procedures Act (ORS chapter 183).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.628
