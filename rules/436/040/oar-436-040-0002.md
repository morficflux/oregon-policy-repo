# Purpose (OAR 436-040-0002)

## Full text

436-040-0002 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to establish guidelines for the administration of the Workers with Disabilities Program established to encourage the employment or re-employment of workers with disabilities.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.628, §286, ch. 70 & OL 2007
