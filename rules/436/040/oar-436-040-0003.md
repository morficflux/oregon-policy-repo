## Full text

436-040-0003 Applicability of Rules

(1) These rules are effective July 1, 2008, and apply to all applications for relief submitted prior to May 1, 1990 and all requests for reimbursement from the Workers with Disabilities Program filed with the director on or after July 1, 2008 for injuries occurring on or after November 1, 1981.

(2) These rules carry out the provisions of ORS 656.628.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.236, §286, ch. 70 & OL 2007
