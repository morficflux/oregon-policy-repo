## Full text

436-040-0006 Administration of Rules For the purpose of administration of the Workers with Disabilities Program, orders of the division are deemed orders of the director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.628, §286, ch. 70 & OL 2007
