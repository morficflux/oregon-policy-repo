## Full text

436-015-0075 Worker Exams When the MCO schedules a worker exam that includes a psychological evaluation, the appointment letter must:

(1) Inform the worker that a psychological evaluation is part of the exam; and

(2) State the reason for the psychological exam.

Statutory/Other Authority: 656.726(4) & ORS 656.260 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.260
