## Full text

436-150-0006 Administration of Rules Any orders issued by the division in carrying out the director’s authority to enforce ORS Chapter 656 and these rules are considered orders of the director.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.445 & 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.445
