# Purpose (OAR 436-140-0002)

## Full text

436-140-0002 Purpose The purpose of these rules is to implement ORS 656.170 and 656.172, and to establish and provide procedures and requirements for the administration and enforcement of programs entered into under ORS 656.170 and 656.172.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4) & 656.174 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.170, 656.172 & 656.174
