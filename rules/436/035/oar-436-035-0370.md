## Full text

436-035-0370 Pelvis

(1) The following ratings are for a fractured pelvis which heals with displacement and deformity: [Tables not included. See ED. NOTE.] In the acetabulum — Rate only loss of hip motion as in OAR 436-035-0340

(2) A hemipelvectomy receives 25% for the pelvis, and the accompanying loss of the leg is determined under OAR 436-035-0140(1). [ED. NOTE: Ratings referenced are available from the agency.] [ED. NOTE: To view attachments referenced in rule text, click here for PDF copy.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726 Statutes/Other Implemented: 656.005, 656.214, 656.268 & 656.726
