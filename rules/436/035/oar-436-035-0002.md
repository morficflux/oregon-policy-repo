# Purpose of Rules (OAR 436-035-0002)

## At a glance

## Full text

436-035-0002 Purpose of Rules These rules establish standards for rating permanent disability under the Workers’ Compensation Act. These standards are written to reflect the criteria for rating outlined in ORS chapter 656 and assign values for disabilities that are applied consistently at all levels of the workers’ compensation award and appeal process.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.005, 656.012, 656.210, 656.212, 656.214, 656.222, 656.225, 656.245, 656.262, 656.267, 656.268, 656. 273, 656.726 & 656.790
