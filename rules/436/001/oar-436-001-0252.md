## Full text

436-001-0252 Stay of Director and Administrative Review The director may stay director review, administrative review, or referral of a matter if there is another pending matter concerning a claim that may affect the outcome of the matter within the director’s jurisdiction.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.704
