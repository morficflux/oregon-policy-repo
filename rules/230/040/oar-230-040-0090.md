# Seats to (OAR 230-040-0090)

## At a glance

## Full text

230-040-0090 Seats to Correspond with Tickets Ushers shall see that spectators occupy seats corresponding with their ticket stubs, and that anyone occupying such seat unlawfully be asked to vacate, and if necessary be ejected.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
