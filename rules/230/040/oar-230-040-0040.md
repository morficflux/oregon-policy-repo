# Color of (OAR 230-040-0040)

## Full text

230-040-0040 Color of Tickets Tickets of different prices shall be printed on different colored ticket stock. If a promoter is using a computerized and bonded ticket service, this requirement may be waived by the Administrator. Use of passout tickets is permitted for purposes of upgrading tickets only. Any other use of passout tickets is prohibited unless the promoter receives permission from the Administrator to use them.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
