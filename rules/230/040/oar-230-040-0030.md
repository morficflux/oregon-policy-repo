# Printing of (OAR 230-040-0030)

## At a glance

## Full text

230-040-0030 Printing of Tickets All tickets shall have the price, the name of the promoter and event date printed or date stamped plainly thereon. Complimentary tickets must adhere to guidelines in OAR 230-040-0010.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
