# Destruction of Tickets (OAR 230-040-0110)

## At a glance

OAR 230-040-0110 — Destruction of Tickets. Chapter 230, Division 040. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

230-040-0110 Destruction of Tickets Tickets and stubs of every description sold or unsold used for an unarmed combat sports or entertainment wrestling event, shall be retained by the promoter for a period of at least six months. During the required period of retention, the promoter shall store the tickets for each show separately and make them available to the Administrator upon request.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
