## Full text

230-140-0580 When a Competitor is Deemed Down A competitor is deemed down or grounded whenever any part of the body other than the hands and sole of the feet are in contact with the floor .

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113 & ORS 463.025
