## Full text

230-100-0040 Advertising Appearance of Champion or Contender No person shall advertise a boxer in Oregon as a champion or contender in any manner which is false or misleading.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113

History: BWC 1-1988, f. 3-22-88, cert. ef. 3-29-88
