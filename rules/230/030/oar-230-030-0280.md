## Full text

230-030-0280 Rules Meeting

(1) The head referee, lead inspector or an authorized representative of the superintendent for the event shall conduct a rules meeting before any unarmed combat sports event to review rules and expectations of the competitors and seconds.

(2) All competitors are required to attend the rules meeting unless excused by an authorized representative of the superintendent.
