## Full text

230-030-0020 Staging Area

(1) The promoter must provide two separate staging areas for unarmed combat sports competitors at the event location.

(2) On the day of an event, only the following people are allowed in the staging areas: (a) The manager of the competitor; (b) The seconds of the competitor; (c) Any representatives of the promoter; (d) Any representatives of the Commission; (e) Working press or other media representatives if approved by the Commission.

(3) Mats must be provided to allow competitors an area to warm up.
