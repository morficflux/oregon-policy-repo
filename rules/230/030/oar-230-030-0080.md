## Full text

230-030-0080 Substitutions Substitution shall not be permitted in an unarmed combat sports main event except in cases of emergency where the Administrator finds such action is justified and then only where the substitute has been approved by the Administrator in accordance with these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
