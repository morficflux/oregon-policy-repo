## Full text

230-030-0090 Solicitation in Venue No soliciting of any kind by any individual or organization shall be allowed in any unarmed combat sports venue without the approval of the Administrator.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
