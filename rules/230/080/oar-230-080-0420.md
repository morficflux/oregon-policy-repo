# Approved Beverages (OAR 230-080-0420)

## At a glance

OAR 230-080-0420 — Approved Beverages. Chapter 230, Division 080. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

230-080-0420 Approved Beverages Only unopened, factory-sealed containers of bottled water are permitted for the treatment of dehydration of an unarmed combat sports competitor between rounds. Honey, electrolyte solutions, glucose, sugar, sports drinks or any other substance mixed with the water or sports drink is prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
