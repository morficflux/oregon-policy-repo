## Full text

230-080-0450 Persons Allowed in the Ring

(1) No persons other than the competitors and the referee may be in the ring area during the progress of a round.

(2) A referee may call a halt to a bout to request medical personnel or the Administrator to enter the ring at the discretion of the referee.

(3) The referee may at their discretion; stop a bout if an unauthorized person enters the ring during a round.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
