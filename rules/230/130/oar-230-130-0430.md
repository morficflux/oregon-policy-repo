## Full text

230-130-0430 Competitors Appearance All unarmed combat sports combat competitors shall be clean and present a tidy appearance. It shall be at the discretion of medical personnel or the referee to determine whether facial hair, piercings, or other adornments, and length of hair present any potential hazard to the safety of the competitor or the opponent, or will interfere with the supervision and conduct of the bout.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
