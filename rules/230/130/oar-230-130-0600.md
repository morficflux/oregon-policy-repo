## Full text

230-130-0600 Wiping Gloves Before a competitor resumes competition after having been knocked or having fallen or slipped to the floor, the referee shall wipe any accumulated resin from the competitor’s gloves with a damp towel or on the referee's shirt.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
