## Full text

230-020-0002 Licensing Year

(1) Licenses, other than Officials licenses, issued by the Superintendent shall be issued on an annual basis, and expire one year from the date of issuance.

(2) Officials’ licenses shall be issued on an annual basis and expire the 31st of December each calendar year.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.025
