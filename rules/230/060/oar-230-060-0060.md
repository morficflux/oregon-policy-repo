## Full text

230-060-0060 Expiration of Contract No manager shall be allowed to contract for their services with a professional unarmed combat sports competitor for a bout to take place after the contract’s expiration date.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 463.113 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 463.113
