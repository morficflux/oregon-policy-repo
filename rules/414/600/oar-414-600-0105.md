## Full text

414-600-0105 Reporting

(1) A Relief Nursery shall provide information and data demonstrating program compliance to the Department upon request and in a manner provided by the Department.

(2) A Relief Nursery must develop and record individual child goals in the child’s records.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.425(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.788 & ORS 417.786
