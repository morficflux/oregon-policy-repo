# Mandatory Reporters (OAR 414-600-0115)

## At a glance

OAR 414-600-0115 — Mandatory Reporters. Chapter 414, Division 600. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

414-600-0115 Mandatory Reporters A Relief Nursery, Relief Nursery staff and other providers having reasonable cause to believe that any child with whom the provider or program staff comes into contact has suffered or is suffering from abuse or neglect shall report or cause a report to be made in the manner described in ORS 419B.005 to 419B.015.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.425(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.788 & ORS 417.786
