## Full text

414-600-0005 Purpose Statement These rules, OAR 414-600-0005 to 414-600-0120, prescribe the process for determining eligibility and eligibility requirements for Relief Nurseries to receive state funds to provide services to children and families in accordance with ORS 417.788.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.425(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.788 & ORS 417.786
