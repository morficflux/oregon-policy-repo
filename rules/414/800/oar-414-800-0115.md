## Full text

414-800-0115 Eligibility The following types of organizations may apply for funding:

(1) Early Learning Hubs

(2) Education Service Districts;

(3) K-12 school districts;

(4) Non-profit organizations;

(5) Post-Secondary institutions; or

(6) A collaboration of any of the above.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 336.101 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 336.104
