## Full text

414-061-0065 Requirements for Reciprocal Agreement Programs A reciprocal agreement program must enter into a reciprocal agreement with the Department that provides for sharing information on enrollment status of the individuals described in OAR 414-061-0020(16)(a) and (b) respectively, and for the recovery of administrative, including direct and indirect costs incurred by the Department from the participation in the agreement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 329A.030(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329A.030
