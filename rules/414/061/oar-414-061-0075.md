## Full text

414-061-0075 Duty of Subject Individuals A subject individual who is conditionally enrolled or enrolled in the CBR shall inform the Department within 30 days of the date of any change in their mailing address, phone number or email listed on their application materials.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 329A.030(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329A.030, HB 2259 (2017)
