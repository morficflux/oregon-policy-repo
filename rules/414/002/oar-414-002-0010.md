## Full text

414-002-0010 Model Rules of Procedure Pursuant to the provisions of ORS 183.341, the Early Learning Council adopts the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedure Act in effect on January 1, 2012. [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Oregon Education Investment Council.]
