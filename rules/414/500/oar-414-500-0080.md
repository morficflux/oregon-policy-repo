## Full text

414-500-0080 Records of Requests for Services

(1) The R & R shall maintain records of requests for services.

(2) Documentation of requests for services shall consist of: (a) Number and time of day and date of calls and contacts to the R & R; (b) Ages of children for whom care is requested; and (c) Time of day or night for which child care is requested, identifying special times including before and after school, nights, weekends, and swing shift.

(3) Information on services requested shall be maintained in such a manner that it is easily accessible for dissemination and evaluation purposes.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 329A.120, ORS 329A.120 & ORS 329A.135 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329A.100, ORS 329A.110, ORS 329A.120 & ORS 329A.135
