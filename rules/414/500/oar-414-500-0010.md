## Full text

414-500-0010 Applicability of Rules

(1) The purpose of division 500 is to establish the Department of Early Learning and Care's requirements for contracts concerning child care resource and referral funding and activities that are subject to Oregon laws governing child care resource and referral agencies, ORS 329A.100 through 329A.135.

(2) If any court of law finds that any clause, phrase, or provision of these rules is unconstitutional or invalid for any reason whatsoever, this finding shall not affect the validity of the remaining portion of these rules.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 329A.100, ORS 329A.135 & ORS 329A.120 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329A.135, ORS 329A.110, ORS 329A.120 & ORS 329A.130
