## Full text

414-470-0025 Preschool Promise Provider Eligibility The following entities are eligible to apply to be a Preschool Promise Provider:

(1) Child Care Provider;

(2) Community-based organization;

(3) Culturally specific organizations;

(4) Early Learning Hub as designated in ORS 417.827

(5) Education service district,

(6) Federal Head Start program;

(7) Federally Recognized Oregon Indian Tribes

(8) Oregon Prekindergarten;

(9) Private preschool;

(10) Public school;

(11) Public charter school, or:

(12) Relief Nursery.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.425(7) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329.172
