## Full text

414-180-0035 Nutrition

(1) Meals and snacks must be based on the guidelines of the USDA Child and Adult Care Food Program.

(2) Foods must be stored and maintained at the proper temperature.

(3) Infants must be held or sitting up for bottle feeding. Propping bottles is prohibited.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 326.425 & ORS 329.020 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 329A.505, ORS 329A.010, ORS 329.020 & ORS 329A.500
