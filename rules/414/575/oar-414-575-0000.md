# Purpose (OAR 414-575-0000)

## At a glance

OAR 414-575-0000 — Purpose. Chapter 414, Division 575. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

414-575-0000 Purpose The purpose of this division is to establish a process for making grants from the Early Childhood Equity Fund for culturally specific early learning, early childhood and parent support programs, to promote the capacity of culturally specific organizations to deliver these programs, to monitor capacity needs and provide technical assistance to grantees .

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 417.781 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 417.781
