# Annual Report (OAR 441-930-0068)

## At a glance

## Full text

441-930-0068 Annual Report Each master trustee is responsible for and shall file an annual report with the director by April 1 of each year on forms provided by the director. The report shall cover the preceding calendar year and shall include information on trusted funds:

(1) The actual value at the beginning and end of the calendar year;

(2) Deposits and withdrawals;

(3) Income earned and fees paid;

(4) Taxes paid for beneficiaries;

(5) Gains and losses; and

(6) The balance of all trust accounts as of December 31.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 97.926 & 97.935 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 97.935

History: FCS 14-2010, f. 12-30-10, cert. ef. 1-1-11
