## Full text

441-930-0220 Renewal Procedure Submission of the annual report pursuant to OAR 441-930-0250 and the fees pursuant to 441-930-0270 shall constitute renewal of the application for certification.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 97.926 & 97.933 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 97.933
