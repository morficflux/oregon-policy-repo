## Full text

441-505-2010 Dividend Excess of Retained Earnings An institution may not pay dividends in an amount that exceeds the institution’s retained earnings except with the prior written approval of the director of the Department of Consumer and Business Services.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 706.795 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 707.410 & 707.415
