## Full text

441-505-3040 Real Estate Sales Contracts Property sold under a contract of sale shall be carried as Real Estate Sold Under Contract until the contract is paid out or sold.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 706.790 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 708A.195
