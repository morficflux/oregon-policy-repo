## Full text

441-505-4020 Exemption for Out of State Trust Company An exemption from the trust company certificate of authority and deposit requirements is created under ORS 709.030(4) for an out-of-state trust company that:

(1) Has no office in this state;

(2) Is regulated in its home state by an authority comparable to the Division of Financial Regulation; and

(3) Does not engage in soliciting trust business in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 709.030(4)(g) Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 709.030(4)(g)
