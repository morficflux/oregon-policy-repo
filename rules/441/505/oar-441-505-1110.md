## Full text

441-505-1110 References to Federal Statutes and Regulations Pursuant to ORS 706.015, references in the Bank Act to Federal statutes and regulations shall be construed to refer to statutes or regulations in effect on June 30, 2007, unless specifically provided otherwise in the Bank Act.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 706.015 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 706.015
