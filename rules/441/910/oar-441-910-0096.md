# Business (OAR 441-910-0096)

## Full text

441-910-0096 Business Activities Fees for services shall be construed as all fees charged in connection with the debt management service provider transaction or activity, including but not limited to “escrow” fees.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 697.840Implemented: ORS 697.692
