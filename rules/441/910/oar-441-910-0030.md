# Renewal A debt management service provider shall renew a registration by completing the following on or before (OAR 441-910-0030)

## Full text

441-910-0030 Renewal A debt management service provider shall renew a registration by completing the following on or before December 1 of the year the debt management service provider’s registration expires:

(1) Submitting a renewal request through the Nationwide Mortgage Licensing System; and

(2) Paying any required fee for renewal under OAR 441-910-0055.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 697.840 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 697.632
