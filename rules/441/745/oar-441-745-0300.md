## Full text

441-745-0300 Application Procedure

(1) Each person desiring to obtain a money transmitters license shall apply to the Director by submitting all information required pursuant to ORS 717.220 plus a list of permissible investments pursuant to 717.215(3), if required.

(2) A person seeking a license must submit an application attested to by an authorized owner or officer of the applicant through the NMLS.

(3) Applicants and licensees must comply with the registration, application, licensing, and renewal processes set forth in the NMLS.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 717.310 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 717.215 & 717.220

History: FSR 7-2017, f. & cert. ef. 4-14-17 FCS 8-2000, f. & cert. ef. 6-27-00
