# Daily-Interest (OAR 441-740-0016)

## At a glance

OAR 441-740-0016 — Daily-Interest. Chapter 441, Division 740. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

441-740-0016 Daily-Interest Computation Interest on a loan must be computed on a daily basis using a 365-day year. The maximum charge for each day shall be 1/365th of the annual rate when calculating the elapsed time of the loan.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 726.260 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 726.390

History: FCS 11-2008, f. & cert. ef. 11-13-08
