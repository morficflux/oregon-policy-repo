## Full text

441-740-0040 Loan Agreement A pawnbroker may not capture a pledgor’s race or sex on any copy of a loan agreement other than the copy to be provided to law enforcement.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 726.260 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 726.280 and 726.285
