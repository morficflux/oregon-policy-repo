## Full text

441-025-0020 Manual Exemption Securities maintaining the following ratings by publishers of securities manuals are approved for the purpose of the exemption from registration in subsection

(5) of ORS 59.025:

(1) Ratings of BBB or better for debt securities, and ratings of F-3 or better for commercial paper by Fitch Investors Service, Inc.

(2) Ratings of Baa or better for debt securities and ratings of P-3 or better for commercial paper by Moody's Investors Service.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 59.285 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 59.025
