## Full text

441-865-0090 Trust Account and Escrow Depository In addition to the books and records required pursuant to the provision of OAR 441-865-0010 to 441-865-0080, a mortgage banker or a mortgage broker is required to maintain the books and records required under the provisions of OAR chapter 441, division 875.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 86A.112 & 86A.136 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 86A.157

History: FCS 14-2009, f. 12-30-09, cert. ef. 1-4-10 FCS 3-1993, f. & cert. ef. 11-15-93
