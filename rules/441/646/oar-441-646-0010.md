## Full text

441-646-0010 Definitions As used in ORS 646A.600 to 646A.628 and this division 646, unless the context requires otherwise:

(1) “Continuing violation” means the same activity extending longer than one day that violates the Oregon Consumer Identity Theft Protection Act.

(2) “Harm,” for purposes of the notification requirement, is limited to monetary loss.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 646A.626 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 646A.604 & 646A.624

History: FCS 10-2008, f. & cert. ef. 11-5-08
