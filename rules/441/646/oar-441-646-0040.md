## Full text

441-646-0040 Safeguarding of Personal Information Protecting the security, confidentiality and integrity of personal information extends to:

(1) Electronic and paper methods of storing personal information;

(2) Electronic, paper and verbal methods of transmitting personal information; and

(3) All methods of disposing of personal information.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 646A.626 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 646A.622
