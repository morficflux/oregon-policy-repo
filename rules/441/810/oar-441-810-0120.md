## Full text

441-810-0120 Claims

(1) Persons having a claim against a collection agency may notify the Director by mail. The information shall include: (a) Name, address and telephone number of the claimant; (b) Name, address and telephone number of the collection agency; (c) The amount of money involved; (d) A brief statement of the nature of the claim; (e) A copy of the contract with relevant documents attached.

(2) If a court judgment is the basis for the claim, a copy of the judgment shall be attached.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 697.085 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 697.058(5)
