## Full text

441-810-0260 Compliance with Fair Debt Collection Practices Act A collection agency that is subject to and in compliance with the requirements of the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (Public Law 95-109, 15 U.S.C. 1692 et seq.) shall also be considered to be in compliance with the requirements of OAR 441-810-0200 through 441-810-0250.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 697.085 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 25.020, 697.085 & Ch. 421 Sec. 4 & 2003 OL
