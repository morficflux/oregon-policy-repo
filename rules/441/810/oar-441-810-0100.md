# Audits (OAR 441-810-0100)

## At a glance

## Full text

441-810-0100 Audits Collection agencies being audited shall provide working space in their office for the auditor or, if office space is not available, submit the records being audited to the Director within ten working days of the request for records.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 697.085 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 697.632
