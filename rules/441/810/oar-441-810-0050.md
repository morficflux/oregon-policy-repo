## Full text

441-810-0050 Update Filings At least ten days prior to a change of ownership, business address, business manager, or change in information relating to the trust account, the collection agency business shall notify the Director of the changes by filing the appropriate forms through the Nationwide Multistate Licensing System.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 697.085 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 697.031(3)
