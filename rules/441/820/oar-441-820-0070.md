## Full text

441-820-0070 Fees In addition to any fees required to participate in the NMLS, a debt buyer must pay the following fees to the director at the time of application or renewal:

(1) A non-refundable application fee of $450; or

(2) A non-refundable renewal fee of $450.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2017 Or Laws ch 625 §§ 8, 13, 15 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2017 Or Laws ch 625 §§ 4 - 10
