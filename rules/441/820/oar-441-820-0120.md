## Full text

441-820-0120 Display of NMLS number

(1) A debt buyer licensed under these rules must display its NMLS number on: (a) All advertising materials; (b) Written communications, or communications that are otherwise reduced to writing, with debtors and otherwise used in the licensee’s business; (c) Promotions; and (d) Websites and social media.

(2) The NMLS number must be displayed in a reasonable location and font size.

Statutory/Other Authority: 2017 Or Laws ch 625 §§ 8, 13, 15 Statutes/Other Implemented: 2017 Or Laws ch 625 §§ 4 - 10
