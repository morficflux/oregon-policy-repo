## Full text

441-065-0224 Retroactive Registration of SOAR Securities that Exceed the Amount Registered If a person sells securities that are registered as a SOAR Offering that are in excess of the amount stated on the Form 440-2013 and the Order of Registration, the seller may seek to retroactively register the unregistered shares by paying three times the difference between the initial fee paid and the fee required under OAR 441-065-0001 for the securities sold in Oregon. The additional fee may not be less than $100. Registration of the excess securities shall be effective retroactively to the date of sale.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 59.065, 59.125 & 59.285 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 59.065 & 59.125
