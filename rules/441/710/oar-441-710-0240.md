## Full text

441-710-0240 Limitations on Ownership of Fixed Assets Under ORS 723.152(4), a credit union has the power to acquire, lease, hold and dispose of property necessary or incidental to its operations. A credit union’s ownership of fixed assets is limited as described by OAR 441-710-0240 to 441-710-0270.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 723.102 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 723.152
