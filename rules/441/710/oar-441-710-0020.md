## Full text

441-710-0020 Membership in General The rules in this section create procedures for establishing and expanding the membership of a credit union. These rules do not invalidate or change the field of membership of any credit union approved prior to March 31, 2000.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 723.102 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 723.172
