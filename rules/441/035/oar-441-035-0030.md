## Full text

441-035-0030 Manual Exemption The following are approved for the purposes of the exemption granted under ORS 59.035(10)(c):

(1) The Mergent securities manual;

(2) Fitch Investors Service securities manual; and

(2) The OTCQX and OTCQB markets.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 59.285 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 59.035(10)
