## Full text

441-035-0180 Prohibition on Commissions, Fees and Other Remuneration No person may receive a commission, fee, or other remuneration for offering, soliciting or selling any OIO security.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 59.035 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 59.035
