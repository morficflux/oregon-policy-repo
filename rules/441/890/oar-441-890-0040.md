## Full text

441-890-0040 Fees Payable to the Director In addition to any fees required to participate in the NMLS, a mortgage servicer shall pay to the director the following fees at the time of application or renewal:

(1) A nonrefundable application fee for a mortgage servicer license of $960 plus a $330 nonrefundable application fee for each branch the mortgage servicer establishes in Oregon.

(2) A nonrefundable renewal application fee for a mortgage servicer license of $480 plus a $165 nonrefundable renewal application fee for each branch the mortgage servicer maintains in Oregon.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 86A.309 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 86A.309
