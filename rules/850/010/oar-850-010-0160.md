## Full text

850-010-0160 State Industrial Accident Cases Naturopathic physicians may accept injured workers who are employed under the provisions of the State Industrial Accident Commission, in conformance with the Workers’ Compensation Law and the rules of committee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 685

History: NE 2, f. 6-7-59
