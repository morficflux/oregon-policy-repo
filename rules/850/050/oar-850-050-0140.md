# Advertising (OAR 850-050-0140)

## At a glance

OAR 850-050-0140 — Advertising. Chapter 850, Division 050. Statutory authority and AON
history are in the full text below.

## Full text

850-050-0140 Advertising While constructive educational publicity shall be encouraged, licentiates of the Board shall refrain from using or causing to be used advertising matter which contains misstatements, falsehoods, misrepresentations, distorted, or fabulous statements as to cures.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 685
