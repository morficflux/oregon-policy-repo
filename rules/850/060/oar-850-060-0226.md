# Formulary Compendium Classifications (OAR 850-060-0226)

## Full text

850-060-0226 Formulary Compendium Classifications The Formulary Council has approved the current American Hospital Formulary Service Pharmacologic-Therapeutic Classification. This listing does not supersede the education and training requirement established in 850-060-0212 for administration of IV agents or the exclusions listed in 850-060-0223 The Formulary Council may consider new agents, substances and pharmacologic-therapeutic classifications for addition to this list.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 685.125 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 685.145
