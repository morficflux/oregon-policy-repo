# Revocation of (OAR 850-020-0030)

## Full text

850-020-0030 Revocation of Approval Approval obtained under ORS 685.060 may be revoked for proper cause by the Board at its discretion, after a hearing. Such hearing shall be held in accordance to Model Rules of Procedure applicable to contested cases.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 685.125 Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 685.010
