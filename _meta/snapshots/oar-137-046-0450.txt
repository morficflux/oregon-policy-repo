 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 46 MODEL RULES GENERAL PROVISIONS RELATED TO PUBLIC CONTRACTING 
 137-046-0450 
 Interstate Cooperative Procurements 
 
 A Contracting Agency that chooses to participate in, sponsor, conduct or administer an Interstate Cooperative Procurement may do so only in accordance with ORS 279A.220. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 279A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279A.065 & 279A.220 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOJ 11-2004, f. 9-1-04, cert. ef. 3-1-05
