 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 USE OF FORCE (COMMUNITY CORRECTIONS) 
 291-022-0150 
 Deadly Use of Force 
 
 (1) Officers should consider other reasonable means of control before resorting to the use of deadly force as time and circumstances safely permit.  (2) Deadly force may be used upon the reasonable belief that an officer’s life or safety, or the life or safety of another, is in imminent danger of death or serious bodily injury, given the totality of the circumstances known to the officer at the time of his/her action. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
