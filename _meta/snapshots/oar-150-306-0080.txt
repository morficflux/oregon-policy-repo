 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 306 PROPERTY TAX GENERALLY 
 150-306-0080 
 Electronic Data Transfer
 
 
 Any data required to be delivered in compliance with property taxation law may be sent and received electronically by agreement of the parties. All required data fields must be included in the transmission. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 306.125
