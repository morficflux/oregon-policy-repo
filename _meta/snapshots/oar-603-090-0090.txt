 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 AGRICULTURAL WATER QUALITY  MANAGEMENT PROGRAM 
 603-090-0090 
 Hearing Procedures
 
 
 All formal hearings requested by the respondent concerning a request for alternate measures, Notice of Noncompliance, Plan of Correction, or Civil Penalty assessment shall be conducted in accordance with applicable contested case procedures as outlined in ORS chapter 183 and OAR chapter 137. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & ORS 568.900 - 568.933 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 568.900 - 568.933
