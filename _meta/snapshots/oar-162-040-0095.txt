 Audits Division - Chapter 162 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 MINIMUM STANDARDS FOR AGREED-UPON PROCEDURES OF OREGON MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS 
 162-040-0095 
 Officers and Registered Agent of the Municipal Corporation
 
 
 Immediately inside the front cover, each financial report shall contain the names and addresses of officers of the municipal corporation and members of its governing body. In addition, financial reports of special districts, as defined by law (ORS Chapter 198), shall contain the name of the district's registered agent and its registered address. If a special district has not designated a registered agent or registered address, the report shall so indicate. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 297.465 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 297.465
