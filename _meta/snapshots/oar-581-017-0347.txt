 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0347 
 Reporting of STEM Lab Schools
 
 
 The Department of Education shall develop partnership-reporting requirements for allocation of funds for implementation of STEM Lab Schools. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 327.800 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 327.820
