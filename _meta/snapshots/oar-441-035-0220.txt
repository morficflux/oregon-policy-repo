 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 SECURITIES TRANSACTIONS EXEMPT FROM REGISTRATION 
 441-035-0220 
 Records
 
 
 The issuer shall maintain the following records for inspection by the Director for four (4) years from the date the OIO is completed. 

 (1) Any information the issuer, broker dealer, or salesperson collected and relied upon to determine the residency and income of an investor; 

 (2) Records relating to sales of securities following the close of the offering that are considered as part of the offering; and 

 (3) All disclosures, advertising, and materials used in connection with the sale of OIOs. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 59.035 & 17 CFR § 230.147A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 59.035
