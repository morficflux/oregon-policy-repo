 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 240 RULES FOR AREAS WITH UNIQUE AIR QUALITY NEEDS 
 340-240-0570 
 Klamath Falls Nonattainment Area Contingency Measures: Applicability 
 
 OAR 340-240-0570 through 340-240-0630 apply to the Klamath Falls Nonattainment Area for PM2.5 should the area not achieve attainment by the applicable attainment date established pursuant to 42 U.S.C. 7502(a)(2). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.480 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 10-2012, f. & cert. ef. 12-11-12
