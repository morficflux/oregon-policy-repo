 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 47 MENTAL HEALTH TREATMENT PROGRAM (AIC ASSIGNMENT AND TRANSFER) 
 291-047-0090 
 Postponement 
 
 (1) A hearing may be postponed by the hearings officer for good cause and for reasonable periods of time.  (2) Good cause includes, but is not limited to:  (a) Illness or unavailability of the inmate;  (b) Gathering of additional evidence; or  (c) Gathering of additional documentation.  (3) The reason(s) for the postponement shall be made part of the record. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 179.473, 179.475, 179.477, 179.478, 179.479, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075, OL 2005 & Ch. 439 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 179.473, 179.475, 179.477, 179.478, 179.479, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075, OL 2005 & Ch. 439
