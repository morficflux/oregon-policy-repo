 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 ENERGY SUPPLIER REPORTING REQUIREMENTS 
 330-020-0065 
 Substitution of Data 
 
 Any energy supplier may, in lieu of submitting a form required by OAR 330-020-0035, submit a report it made to another governmental agency, if the alternative report submitted contains all of the information required by, and is clearly cross-indexed to, the form for which it is substituted. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.080
