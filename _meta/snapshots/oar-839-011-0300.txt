 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 APPRENTICESHIP AND TRAINING COUNCIL 
 839-011-0300 
 Apprenticeship and Training Committees — Effective Date of Actions 
 
 All Council actions, other than rulemaking, unless otherwise specified by the Council, shall be effective on the first day of the month following the Council meeting at which such action is taken. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 660.120(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 660.120
