Department of Consumer and Business Services

Building Codes Division - Chapter 918

Division 305
ELECTRICAL CODES AND STANDARDS

918-305-0005
Interpretations

All electrical interpretations dated prior to October 1, 2023, issued by the Building Codes Division are withdrawn.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 479.730, ORS 455.020, ORS 455.110, ORS 479.680 & ORS 455.030
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 479.730 & ORS 455.610
