 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 LANDS: DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE LANDS 
 635-008-0015 
 Agreements to Restrict Use of Motor-Propelled Vehicles
 
 
 No person shall violate posted motor-propelled vehicle use restrictions established by agreements entered into pursuant to ORS 498.152. 

 [ED. NOTE: This rule was Renumbered from 630-010-0515, 635-015-0015] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 498.152 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 498.152
