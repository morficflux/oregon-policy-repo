 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR RADIOACTIVE TAILINGS AND PONDS 
 333-110-0025 
 Waiver 
 
 Upon application to the Authority, certain requirements of this Division may be waived or modified if it can be shown that the requirements are unnecessary or impractical in specific cases. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.745 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
