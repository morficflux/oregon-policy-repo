 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 OREGON AFFORDABLE HOUSING TAX CREDITS: AFFORDABLE HOUSING PROJECT CERTIFICATION 
 813-110-0031 
 Charges
 
 
 (1) A lending institution claiming OAHTC program tax credits under ORS 317.097 shall pay a base charge of 5 percent of the annual tax credits claimed to OHCS annually. 

 (2) The Annual Report for the preceding year is due by May 31st. 

 (3) OHCS may assess a $100 per month charge for each full month the annual report is delayed after the due date. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 317.097 & ORS 456.515 - 456.725 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 317.097
