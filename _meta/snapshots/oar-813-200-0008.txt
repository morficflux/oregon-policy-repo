 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 LOW-INCOME HOME ENERGY ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (LIHEAP) 
 813-200-0008 
 Manuals 
 
 The LIHEAP & OEAP Intake Operations and Policy Manual (program manual) with the requirements and standards therein, dated October 1, 2025, is incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules, by reference. The program manual may be accessed online at the OHCS website.
