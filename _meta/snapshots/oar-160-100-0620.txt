 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 NOTARIES PUBLIC 
 160-100-0620 
 Appeal Process 
 
 In the event the Secretary of State sanctions a notary public for official misconduct or proposes to refuse to appoint or proposes to revoke or suspend a commission, an opportunity for a public hearing will be provided pursuant to ORS 194.340, the contested case procedures set out in ORS 183.413 through 183.500 and the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure for Contested Cases. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 194.360 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 194.340
