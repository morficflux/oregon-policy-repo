 Oregon Medical Board 
 Chapter 847 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 BOARD APPROVAL OF FOREIGN SCHOOLS OF MEDICINE 
 847-031-0040 
 Approval of Foreign Schools of Medicine by Other States 
 
 The Oregon Medical Board may accept any foreign school of medicine which has been approved by another state using criteria substantially similar to Oregon's. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 677.265 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 677.265 
 
 History:
