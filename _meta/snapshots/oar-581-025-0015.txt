 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 DISTRICT BOUNDARY BOARD HEARINGS 
 581-025-0015 
 Submission of Record to the State Board of Education 
 
 Upon receipt of a copy of the notice of appeal to the State Board of Education, the boundary board shall forward the complete record of its consideration of the request or petition to the State Board within 30 days. The complete record shall include the original request or petition, affidavits of publication of notice of the meeting at which the request or petition was considered and a copy of the notice, and the resolution of final decision. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 330.080, 330.090(5), 330.107 & 330.123 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 330.080, 330.090, 330.107 & 330.125
