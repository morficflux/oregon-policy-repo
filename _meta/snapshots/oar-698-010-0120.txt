 Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board 
 Oregon Agricultural Heritage Program - Chapter 698 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 Conservation Management Plans 
 698-010-0120 
 Conservation Management Plan Mutual Modification 
 
 If funding is provided for Conservation Management Plan implementation: 

 (1) The Conservation Management Plan holder and the Agricultural Landowner or Operator must review the Conservation Management Plan at least annually and may request to mutually modify the Conservation Management Plan if necessary. 

 (2) Any changes to Conservation Management Plans must achieve the same or greater level of benefits as the original plan and be consistent with OAR 698-010-0090. 

 (3) Any change in the Conservation Management Plan must be mutually agreed to by both the Agricultural Landowner or Operator, the Conservation Management Plan holder, and OWEB. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 541.984, ORS 541.988 & ORS 541.989 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541.977-541.989
