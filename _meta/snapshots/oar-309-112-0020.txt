 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 112 USE OF RESTRAINT AND SECLUSION FOR PATIENTS IN STATE INSTITUTIONS 
 309-112-0020 
 Use of Security Transportation 
 
 The chief medical officer or designee may authorize the use of secure transportation for patients of a secure program when outside the security area. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 426.385 & 427.031
