 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 53 HEALTH BENEFIT PLANS 
 836-053-0605 
 Definitions for OAR 836-053-0600 to 836-053-0615 
 
 As used in OAR 836-053-0600 to 836-053-0615:  (1) The definitions set forth in Section 2, chapter 470; Oregon Laws 2015 apply to the use of those terms in these rules.  (2) “Enrollee” includes a person covered under a health benefit plan and a dependent of a person covered under a health benefit plan. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & OL 2015, Ch 470 Sections 2 & 3 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Ch 470, OL 2015 Ch 470 Sections 2, 3 & 12
