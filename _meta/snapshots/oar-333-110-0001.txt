 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR RADIOACTIVE TAILINGS AND PONDS 
 333-110-0001 
 Scope 
 
 The rules in this division establish requirements for radioactive tailings and ponds containing radioactive material from industrial processes. The provisions of this division are in addition to, and not in substitution for, other applicable provisions of these rules and any specific license issued pursuant to OAR 333-102-0300. The rules in this division do not apply to activities regulated by the Uranium Mill Tailings Radiation Control Act of 1978. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.745 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
