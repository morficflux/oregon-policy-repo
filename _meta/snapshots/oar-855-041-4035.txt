 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 OPERATION OF PHARMACIES 
 855-041-4035 
 Home Dialysis - Definitions
 
 
 “Dialysis solutions” means peritoneal dialysis solutions, dialysate solutions, and legend devices including hardware, bloodlines and dialysis tubing and connectors. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 689.305
