 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 TRADE PRACTICES 
 836-080-0215 
 Claim Files 
 
 An insurer’s claim files shall contain the information pertaining to each claim in sufficient detail that pertinent events and their dates can be reconstructed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 746.230(1)(d) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 IC 2-1980, f. 5-8-80, ef. 6-1-80
