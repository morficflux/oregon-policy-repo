 Oregon Medical Board 
 Chapter 847 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 GENERAL 
 847-010-0012 
 Certification of Examination Scores and Verification of Oregon Licensure 
 
 (1) Certification of examination scores will be furnished provided that:  (a) The licensee submits a written request, fee and proper form for certification;  (b) The license was issued on the basis of written examination taken in this state.  (2) Verification of Oregon license number, date issued and current status will be furnished regardless of the status of the license (revoked/suspended/lapsed) provided the licensee submits a written request and fee. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 677 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 677.110
