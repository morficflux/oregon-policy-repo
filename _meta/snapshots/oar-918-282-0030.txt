 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 282 ELECTRICAL AND ELEVATOR LICENSING 
 918-282-0030 
 Limited Energy Contractor License 
 
 A limited energy contractor:  (1) Continuously employs at least one full-time Class "A" limited energy technician, Class "B" limited energy technician or general journeyman to act as a signing supervising electrician to obtain and sign permits;  (2) Is limited to electrical work on limited energy systems and the scope of work authorized under the employed signing supervisor’s license; and  (3) Is authorized to make, direct, supervise or control the making of an electrical installation, only if properly licensed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.730 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.730
