 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 RETAIL SALES AGENTS 
 845-015-0160 
 Sale of Lottery Tickets 
 
 Despite OAR 845-015-0143(1) (sale of related items), retail sales agents may contract with the Oregon State Lottery Commission to sell Oregon State Lottery Tickets in retail liquor stores. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 471, 471.030, 471.730(1) & (5), 472.030, 472.060(1) & (2)(d) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 471.750(1)
