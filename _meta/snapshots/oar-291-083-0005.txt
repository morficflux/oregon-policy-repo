 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 83 CONTROLLED FEEDING 
 291-083-0005 
 Authority and Purpose 
 
 (1) Authority: The authority for this rule is granted to the Director of the Department of Corrections in accordance with ORS 423.020 and 423.075.  (2) Purpose: To establish criteria and procedure for placement of an inmate on controlled feeding status. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 423 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
