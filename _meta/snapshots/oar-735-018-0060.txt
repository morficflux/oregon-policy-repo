 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 ACCEPTANCE AND VERIFICATION OF INFORMATION SUBMITTED BY ELECTRONIC MEANS 
 735-018-0060 
 Signature 
 
 For any transaction that is required under Oregon law to be in writing and have a signature, an electronic signature shall have the same legal force and effect as if the person or organization had submitted the transaction in hardcopy with a handwritten signature in ink on paper. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.012 & 803.460 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 802.012, 802.560, 803.200, 803.220, 803.360, 803.370, 803.450, 803.460, 807.420 & 807.560 
 
 History:
