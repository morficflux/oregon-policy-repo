 Oregon Board of Maritime Pilots - Chapter 856 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 Training and Licensing 
 856-015-0050 
 Reduced License Fees
 
 
 Individuals licensed by this Board as pilots for Yaquina Bay bar pilotage ground, who anticipate less than full-time employment as pilots, may pay a reduced annual license fee of $50. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 776 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 776.355
