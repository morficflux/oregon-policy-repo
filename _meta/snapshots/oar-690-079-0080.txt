 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 79 RESERVATIONS OF WATER FOR FUTURE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT 
 690-079-0080 
 Copies of Reservation Request 
 
 Upon request from any governmental entity or other party, the Department shall provide a complete copy of the request for reservation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025, 536.027, 536.220, 536.300, 536.310, 537.338 & 537.356 - 537.358 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 9-1992, f. & cert. ef. 7-1-92
