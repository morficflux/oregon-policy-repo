 Board of Chiropractic Examiners 
 Chapter 811 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 GENERAL RULES AND LICENSING 
 811-010-0025 
 Display of Renewal Certificate
 
 
 Licensees are not required to display a current annual Certificate of Registration. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 684 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 684.155
