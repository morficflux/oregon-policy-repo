 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 123 THERAPEUTIC RADIATION MACHINES 
 333-123-0070 
 Control Panel Functions 
 
 A control panel must be designed to provide:  (1) An indication of whether electrical power is available at the control panel and if activation of the electronic brachytherapy source is possible;  (2) An indication of whether X-rays are being produced;  (3) A means for displaying electronic brachytherapy source accelerating voltage(kV), beam current(µA), pre-set value of radiation dose(cGy) and real time display of dose delivered;  (4) The means for terminating an exposure at any time; and  (5) An access locking control device that can prevent unauthorized use of the electronic brachytherapy device. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
