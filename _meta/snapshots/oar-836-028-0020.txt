 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 PURCHASING GROUPS AND RISK RETENTION GROUPS 
 836-028-0020 
 Use of Insurance Producers by Purchasing Groups
 
 
 A purchasing group shall employ an insurance producer to procure insurance for its members in Oregon. Such an insurance producer: 

 (1) Must hold a current insurance producer’s license issued by the Division of Financial Regulation, in order to transact insurance in Oregon; or 

 (2) Must hold a current surplus line licensee's license issued under the Oregon Surplus Lines Law, in order to place business with a surplus lines insurer. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731, 735 & 746 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 735.350
