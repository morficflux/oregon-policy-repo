 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 SERVICE PERMITS 
 845-009-0110 
 Oral Option 
 
 An applicant or permittee may take the exam either in writing or orally at the completion of the course. He/she may also take the retake exams either in writing or orally. The applicant or permittee must make arrangements with the provider or field office for an oral exam. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 471, including 471.030, 471.730(1) & (5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 471.542
