 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 105 MANAGEMENT FACILITY PERMITS 
 340-105-0061 
 Emergency Permits 
 
 (1) The provisions of 40 CFR 270.61(b)(4) are deleted and replaced with section (2) of this rule.  (2) May be suspended or renewal refused by the Department at any time without prior hearing if it finds a serious danger to the public health, safety or the environment and sets forth specific reasons for such findings. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 459 & 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.095 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 8-1985, f. & ef. 7-25-85
