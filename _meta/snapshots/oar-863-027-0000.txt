 Real Estate Agency 
 Chapter 863 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 27 INVESTIGATIONS AND DISCIPLINE 
 863-027-0000 
 Applicability and Purpose
 
 
 This division applies to real estate licensees. It describes the methods and procedures the Agency will use to investigate complaints and the process the Agency will follow when taking disciplinary action. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 696.385 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 696.396
