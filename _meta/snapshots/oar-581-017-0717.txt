 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0717 
 Competitive Oregon Farm-to-School Grant for Reimbursement of Oregon Foods: Eligibility 
 
 (1) The following entities are eligible for this grant: 

 (a) School district, 

 (b) Public charter school, 

 (c) A provider of center-based programs for children in the Child and Adult Care Food Program, or 

 (d) An entity that provides meals through the Summer Food Service Program 

 (2) All eligible entities shall: 

 (a) Be the recipient of a noncompetitive Oregon Farm-to-School grant for reimbursement of Oregon foods; and 

 (b) Have a zero balance of its initial noncompetitive Oregon Farm-to-School grant for reimbursement of Oregon foods award. 

 (3)(a) Eligible entities must submit their grant applications on the most current form prescribed by the Oregon Department of Education. The department shall publish the current request for applications form on the department’s website. 

 (b) If an eligible entity that has applied for a competitive Oregon Farm-to-School grant for reimbursement of Oregon foods is unable to provide the information requested in the application form, then the grant applicant must provide an explanation why the information cannot be provided. Grant applicants may submit additional information that will aid the department in evaluating their grant applications. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 336.431
