 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 OREGON CHILD SUPPORT PROGRAM 
 137-055-3040 
 Temporary Order for Support
 
 
 (1) When a party to an order to establish parentage objects to the entry of such order, and genetic testing results in a probability of parentage of at least 99% and a combined relationship index of 100 to 1, the administrator may request the court issue a temporary support order. 

 (2) A party other than the state may request an order establishing temporary support. 

 (3) The temporary order entered by the court shall have the same force and effect as any other order entered by the administrator, court, or other tribunal. 

 (4) If the court makes a determination of nonparentage, the obligor may request that the court order the return of any monies collected because of the temporary order. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 180.345 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 25.550, 109.154 & 109.259
