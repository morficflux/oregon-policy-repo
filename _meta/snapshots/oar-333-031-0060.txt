 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF RECREATION PARKS 
 333-031-0060 
 All Recreation Parksites 
 
 Condition of soil, groundwater level, drainage, and topography shall be considered in the design of the park and in the selection of the type and location of water supply and sewage disposal systems so that a health hazard is not created. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 466.330 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.330, 446.340 & 446.345 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 HB 231, f. 12-22-69
