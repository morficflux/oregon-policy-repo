 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 STANDARDS FOR EDUCATION SERVICE DISTRICTS 
 581-024-0290 
 Advisors to the Board 
 
 Each district board may appoint nonvoting advisory members to the board as the board deems important. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 334.025(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 334.025
