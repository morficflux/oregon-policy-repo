 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 FIELD BURNING AND PROPANING RULES 
 837-110-0050 
 Prohibited Use 
 
 The use of pitch forks, harrows, or the dragging of burning tires to ignite the fire is prohibited. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476 & 478 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 476.380 & 478.960
