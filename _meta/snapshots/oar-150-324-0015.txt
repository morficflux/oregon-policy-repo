 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 324 OIL AND GAS TAX 
 150-324-0015 
 Production Unit for Calculation and Reporting 
 
 (1) The “net gas produced and saved” from an individual well is the total gas produced and saved from that well less that gas used for purposes enumerated in ORS 324.110(3).  (2) Production of natural gas shall be measured and reported in therms. One therm equals 100,000 British Thermal Units (BTUs). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 324.050
