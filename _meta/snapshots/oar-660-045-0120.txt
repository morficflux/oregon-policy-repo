 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 CITIZEN-INITIATED ENFORCEMENT ORDERS 
 660-045-0120 
 Participation of Parties or Limited Parties in the Contested-Case Hearing 
 
 (1) Persons interested in the enforcement proceeding may request status as a party or limited party in accordance with OAR 137-003-0005 (“Participation as a Party or Limited Party”). Any such request must be filed with the department at least 21 days before the date of the contested case hearing. The department shall respond to such requests as is specified in OAR 137-003-0005.  (2) In accordance with OAR 137-003-0007 (“Agency Participation as Interested Agency or Party”), the department may designate another agency as an interested agency or party. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 197 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.319 - 197.335 & 197.646
