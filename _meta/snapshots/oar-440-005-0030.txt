 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Director's Office - Chapter 440 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 ACCESS OF PUBLIC RECORDS, FEES FOR RECORD SEARCH AND COPIES OF PUBLIC RECORDS 
 440-005-0030 
 Fees and Miscellaneous Charges 
 
 The director establishes fees and miscellaneous charges in agency policy, reviewed periodically to ensure all charges reflect no more than the actual cost of producing and processing. The department posts current fees and charges on its Web site: http://egov.oregon.gov/DCBS/records_request.shtml. This information is also available by calling or writing the department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192.430, 192.440, 656.726, 697.732, 731.244, 731.282, 731.284 & 731.804 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.440, 731.282, 731.284 & 731.804
