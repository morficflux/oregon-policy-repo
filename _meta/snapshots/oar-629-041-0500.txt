 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 PROTECTION ADMINISTRATION 
 629-041-0500 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 629-041-0500 to 629-041-0575 is to set forth the boundaries of Forest Protection Districts, pursuant to ORS 477.225, and thereby identify the lands to which the applicable duties, requirements and penalties of ORS Chapter 477 and 526 shall apply. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 477.225 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 477.225 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOF 10-1998, f. & cert. ef. 8-13-98
