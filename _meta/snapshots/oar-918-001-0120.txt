 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 918-001-0120 
 Appointment of Chiefs 
 
 The division shall appoint individuals as specialty code chief inspectors knowledgeable to carry out the provisions of ORS 455.475. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.475 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.475
