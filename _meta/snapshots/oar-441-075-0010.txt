 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 RENEWAL OF SECURITIES REGISTRATIONS 
 441-075-0010 
 General Renewal Procedure 
 
 (1) Except for orders issued pursuant to OAR 441-065-0270(5), every order of registration or renewal automatically expires no later than 12 months from its effective date unless otherwise renewed pursuant to the rules contained in this division.  (2) Generally, the renewal process shall be accomplished by following all requirements and procedures necessary for initial registration, as supplemented or modified by the requirements set forth in this division. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 59.075 & 59.285 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 59.075
