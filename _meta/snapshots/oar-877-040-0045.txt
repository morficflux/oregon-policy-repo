 Board of Licensed Social Workers 
 Chapter 877 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 PROCEDURES FOR HANDLING COMPLAINTS TO THE BOARD 
 877-040-0045 
 Stipulated Agreement 
 
 In the event the Consumer Protection Committee submits a proposed stipulated agreement to the board for consideration, the board may:  (1) Determine that approval is warranted and authorize the Chair or the board's designee to sign the agreement on behalf of the board;  (2) Determine that approval is not warranted;  (3) Direct the Consumer Protection Committee to renegotiate the agreement; or  (4) Take any other action authorized by law. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.510 - 675.600, 675.900 & 675.990 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.595
