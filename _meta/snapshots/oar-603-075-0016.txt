 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 FARM MEDIATION 
 603-075-0016 
 Eligibility for Mediation Programs 
 
 The program coordinator will determine the eligibility of an applicant for the mediation programs consistent with ORS 36.256 (agricultural mediation services) or ORS 36.280 (disputes related to interference with farming practices), after review of an application for mediation services to determine whether mediation is appropriate as provided by the requirements for each program. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 36.280 & ORS 36.256 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOA 22-2019, adopt filed 11/26/2019, effective 11/26/2019
