 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 19 OUTPATIENT BEHAVIORAL HEALTH SERVICES 
 309-019-0310 
 Minimum Staffing Requirements
 
 
 (1) At least one QMHP shall be available by phone or face-to-face 24/7 for consultation. 

 (2) At least one QMHP shall provide regular clinical supervision to staff. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & 430.640 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 430.630, 430.640 & 430.644 - 430.646
