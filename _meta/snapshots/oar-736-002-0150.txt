 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 ADMINISTRATIVE ACTIVITIES 
 736-002-0150 
 Recordkeeping, Confidentiality, and Retention
 
 
 (1) Recordkeeping: 

 (a) An authorized designee shall document a preliminary or final fitness determination, or the closing of a fitness determination file in writing. 

 (b) Written documentation of preliminary or final fitness determination and closing of a fitness determination file shall be retained in the subject individual file for two years after the date of notice. 

 (2) Confidentiality: the Department will keep information obtained in a criminal records check confidential and disseminate only as provided in OAR 125-007-0310 and ORS 181A.195(11). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.124, 390.131, 390.140, ORS 181A.195 & 390.200 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 181A.195
