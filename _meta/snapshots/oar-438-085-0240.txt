 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE FOR CONTESTED CASES UNDER THE OREGON SAFE EMPLOYMENT ACT 
 438-085-0240 
 Standards of Conduct for Representatives and Parties 
 
 (1) A party may attend a mediation personally or through a representative.  (2) A party or its representative shall come to the mediation with authority to settle the issues, or bring with them or otherwise make available that person or those persons who have such authority.  (3) By consenting to mediation, all parties and their representatives agree to bargain in good faith. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 654.078 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.502 
 
 History:
