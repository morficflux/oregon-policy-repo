 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 CIVIL PENALTIES 
 738-140-0010 
 Definitions 
 
 For purposes of 738-140-0005 through 738-140-0050, the following definitions apply:  (1) “Aircraft” is a device that is used or intended to be used for flight in the air.  (2) “Class A Violation” is an action or non-action in breach of a statute or rule that incurs a maximum fine of $2,000 for an individual and $4,000 for a corporation in accordance with ORS 153.012. See also: ORS 153.018, 153.019, 153.021.  (3) “Department” is the Oregon Department of Aviation.  (4) “Director” is the Director of the Oregon Department of Aviation.  (5) “Director’s delegate” is any person acting with sanction from and on behalf of the Director of the Department of Aviation.  (6) “Intentional” is the deliberate or willful action or non-action of a person or entity.  (7) “OAR” is the Oregon Administrative Rules.  (8) “ORS” is the Oregon Revised Statutes.  (9) “Violation” is a breach of a statute, rule, standard, or permit condition.  (10) “Violator” is any person, entity, or public department that has breached a statute, rule, standard, or permit condition. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 153.022, 183.745, 835.035, 835.112 & 837.998 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 837.005 - 837.998
