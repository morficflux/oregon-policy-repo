 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 ANNUAL STATEMENTS AND REPORTS BY INSURERS 
 836-011-0300 
 Statutory Authority; Statutes Implemented 
 
 (1) OAR 836-011-0300 to 836-011-0390 apply to insurers that are subject to the capital and surplus requirements of ORS 731.554 and insurers that are subject to the capital and surplus requirements of 731.566.  (2) OAR 836-011-0300 to 836-011-0390 are adopted pursuant to the authority of ORS 731.244, 731.554, 731.574 and 733.210 for the purpose of implementing 731.554 and 731.574. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244, ORS 731.554 & ORS 733.210 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 731.554 & ORS 731.574
