 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 536 IN-HOME CARE AGENCIES 
 333-536-0105 
 Operating Without a License
 
 
 If an agency is found to be operating without a valid license, the agency must, within 14 days of the receipt of an injunction obtained by the Authority pursuant to ORS 443.327: 

 (1) Inform its clients that the agency can no longer provide services; 

 (2) Refund all fees collected from the clients for services not rendered; and 

 (3) Cease providing services to clients. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 443.340 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 443.315, 443.325, 443.327 & 443.340
