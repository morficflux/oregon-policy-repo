 Board of Massage Therapists 
 Chapter 334 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 334-001-0045 
 Personnel Policies 
 
 The Oregon Board of Massage Therapists hereby adopts its own personnel policies which are controlling.
