 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 29 TRAVELERS’ ACCOMMODATION RULES 
 333-029-0070 
 Spa and Swimming Pools 
 
 Any spa or swimming pool located at or operated in connection with a travelers' accommodation or hostel must comply with the respective Oregon Public Health Division rules:  (1) For Public Spa Pools, OAR 333-062-0005 through 333-062-0185; and  (2) For Public Swimming Pools, OAR 333-060-0005 through 333-060-0515. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 448.035 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 448.005 - 448.090
