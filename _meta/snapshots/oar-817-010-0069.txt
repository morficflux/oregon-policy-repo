 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office, Board of Cosmetology - Chapter 817 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 SAFETY AND INFECTION CONTROL 
 817-010-0069 
 Disinfecting Electrical Tools and Implements 
 
 (1) All electrical tools and implements, with the exception of clipper blades, must be disinfected before each use. To disinfect electrical tools and implements:  (a) Remove all hair and all foreign material;  (b) Disinfect with a low-level disinfectant used according to the manufacturer's instructions.  (2) Electrical clipper blades must be disinfected before each use. The method is as follows:  (a) Remove all hair and all foreign material;  (b) Completely saturate clipper blade with a high-level disinfectant solution, spray, or foam used according to the manufacturer's instructions.  (3) Electrical clipper blades may be sterilized, using one of the approved methods listed in OAR 817-010-0065(5)(b) or (c). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.605, 690.165 & 690.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.605, 690.165 & 690.205
