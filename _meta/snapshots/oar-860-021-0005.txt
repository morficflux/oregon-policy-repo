 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 UTILITY REGULATION 
 860-021-0005 
 Scope of the Rules 
 
 Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any of the Division 021 rules for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 756 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040
