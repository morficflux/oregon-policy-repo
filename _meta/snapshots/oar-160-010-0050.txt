 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 BUSINESS REGISTRY SECTION 
 160-010-0050 
 E-mail Address 
 
 (1) Documents electronically delivered to the office of the Secretary of State for filing under ORS chapters 58, 60, 62, 63, 65, 67, 70, 554, 647, 648 and 649 and ORS 128.560 to 128.600 and 661.210 to 661.280 shall include an e-mail address for delivery of official notices and filing acknowledgments.  (2) For the purposes of this rule, “electronically delivered” shall not include electronic facsimile transmission. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 56.022 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 56.016 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CORP 4-2012, f. & cert. ef. 6-1-12
