 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 RULEMAKING PROCEDURE 
 255-001-0020 
 Draft to Legislative Counsel 
 
 Prior to a proposed change in the rules, including temporary rules, the Board may submit a draft of the proposed action to Legislative Counsel. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144.140 & 183.310 - 183.550 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183
