 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 150 RISK MANAGEMENT 
 125-150-0010 
 Purchase of Professional Services
 
 
 (1) The Department is authorized under ORS 278.405(4) to purchase necessary professional services. 

 (2) Services will acquired following guidelines established by the Public Contracting code based on; ORS 279A and 279B et. seq. as codified in OAR Chapter 125, Division 247. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 278.405(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279A and 279B
