 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 142 OIL AND HAZARDOUS MATERIALS EMERGENCY RESPONSE REQUIREMENTS 
 340-142-0090 
 Cleanup Report 
 
 The Department may require the responsible person to submit a written report to the Department describing all aspects of the spill and cleanup, and steps taken to prevent a recurrence. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 466 & 468B 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.610, 466.625, 466.645 & 468B.305 - 315 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 3-2003, f. & cert. ef. 1-31-03
