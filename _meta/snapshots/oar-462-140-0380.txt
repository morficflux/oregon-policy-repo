 Oregon Racing Commission 
 Chapter 462 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 DUTIES 
 462-140-0380 
 Groom 
 
 (1) A groom assists the trainer or assistant trainer in the care of a racehorse.  (2) A groom may assist the trainer or assistant trainer in the paddock.  (3) A groom may be the trainer’s or assistant trainer’s authorized representative in the test barn.  (4) A groom must be employed and on a trainer’s list of employees. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 462.270(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 462.270 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
