 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 111 USED OIL MANAGEMENT 
 340-111-0030 
 Prohibitions 
 
 In addition to the provisions in 40 CFR 279.12, the following provisions shall apply:  (1) The use of used oil as a pesticide is prohibited.  (2) Disposal at a solid waste disposal facility of liquid used oil or used oil purposely mixed with other materials for the purpose of disposal but not including cleanup materials from incidental or accidental spills where the used oil spilled cannot be feasibly recovered as liquid oil is prohibited. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192, 465.009, 466.015, 466.020, 466.075, 466.090, 468.020 & 646 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 459A.590, 459A.595 & 466.020
