 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 755 CHECK-CASHER RULES 
 441-755-0130 
 Renewal of License 
 
 (1) A licensee may renew its license or licenses by identifying any principal place of business and licensed locations being renewed, and paying the renewal fees specified in OAR 441-755-0010. New locations may be added during this process.  (2) Renewal fees must be actually received by the Director on or before the last business day in December. Fees submitted but subsequently not honored by the licensee’s financial institution or credit card company are considered not received.  (3) If not timely renewed, a license will automatically expire and become void without further action of the Director. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 697.550 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 697.514
