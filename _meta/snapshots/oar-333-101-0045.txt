 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 101 REGISTRATION OF RADIATION MACHINES, GENERAL LICENSE RADIOACTIVE MATERIALS, LICENSING OF RADIATION SERVICES, AND ACCREDITATION OF HOSPITAL RADIOLOGY INSPECTORS 
 333-101-0045 
 Renewal of Notice of Registration 
 
 (1) Application for renewal of registration must be filed in accordance with OAR 333-101-0005 or 333-101-0020.  (2) In any case in which a registrant has filed an application in proper form for renewal, not less than 30 days prior to the expiration of his existing notice of registration, such existing notice of registration shall not expire until the application status has been determined by the Authority. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - ORS 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - ORS 453.807
