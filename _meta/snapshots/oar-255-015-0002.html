Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision

Chapter 255

Division 15
REQUEST FOR BOARD RECORDS OR FILES

255-015-0002
Board Records

The Board shall maintain a separate file on each person under its jurisdiction which shall contain the materials obtained pursuant to ORS 144.185.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 144.185, ORS 183.335, ORS 192.311 - 192.431, ORS 144.025, ORS 144.050 & ORS 192.001
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 144.185, ORS 144.130 & ORS 192.314
