 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 43 CHEMICAL MINING 
 340-043-0110 
 Guidelines for the Design, Construction, Operation and Closure of Chemical Mining Operations: Protection of Wildlife 
 
 (1) Wildlife shall be positively excluded from contact with chemical processing solutions and wastewaters containing chemicals.  (2) The Department may waive the positive exclusion requirement if the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife (ODF&W) certifies to the Department that the project is designed such that it will adequately protect wildlife. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468.020 & 498.046 
 
 History:
