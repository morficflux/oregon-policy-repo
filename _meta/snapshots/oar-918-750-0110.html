Department of Consumer and Business Services

Building Codes Division - Chapter 918

Division 750
PLUMBING CODES

918-750-0110
Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code

(1) The Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code is adopted pursuant to OAR chapter 918, division 8.
(2) Effective October 1, 2023, the 2023 Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code is based upon the following:  
(a) The 2021 Edition of the Uniform Plumbing Code, First Printing;
(b) Chapters 1–11 and 13–17;
(c) Appendices A, B, C, D, E, K, and M; and
(d) As further amended by the division.
(3) Chapter 12, and Appendices F, G, H, I, J, L, and N of the 2021 Uniform Plumbing Code are not adopted as part of the Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code.
(4) Appendices L and N are available to be adopted by local jurisdictions.
[Publications: Publications are available for review at the division. See division website for information on where to purchase publications.]

  

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 447.020, ORS 455.020, ORS 455.030 & ORS 455.110
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 447.020, ORS 455.020, ORS 455.030, ORS 455.110 & ORS 455.610
