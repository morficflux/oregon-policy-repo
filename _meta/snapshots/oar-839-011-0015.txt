 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 APPRENTICESHIP AND TRAINING COUNCIL 
 839-011-0015 
 Apprenticeship and Training Council — Rules of Order 
 
 The Council adopts Roberts Rules of Order for the conduct of meetings; provided, however, that specific Administrative Rules of the Council will take precedence over Roberts Rules of Order. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 660.120
