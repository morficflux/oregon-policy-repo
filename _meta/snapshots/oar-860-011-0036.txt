 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 GENERAL 
 860-011-0036 
 Personal Services Contracts 
 
 (1) The Commission may enter into personal services contracts as required or appropriate for the discharge of its duties.  (2) The model rules adopted by the Attorney General do not apply to the Commission.  (3) Department of Administrative Services Public Contracting Rules for Personal Services Contracts OAR chapter 125, divisions 246, 247 and 248 apply to all personal services contracts, except as specified in section (4) of this rule.  (4) The following public contracting rules do not apply to personal services contracts used by the Commission: OARs 125-246-0345(2), 125-246-0350, 125-246-0351, and 125-246-0352. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 756.036 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.036 & 279A.050 
 
 History:
