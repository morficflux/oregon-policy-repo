 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 PRODUCE STANDARDS 
 603-051-0435 
 Tolerance: Sizes 
 
 (1) In order to allow for variations incident to proper grading and handling, not more than 10 percent of the bulbs, by count, of any lot may be below the requirements of the grade. Slight imperfections which are not discernible in good commercial sorting practice shall not be considered defects of grade.  (2)(a) Unless otherwise specified, the size stated by inches in circumference shall be as follows:  (A) 6–7, 8–9, 9–10, etc.; or  (B) 6–8, 7–9, 8–10, etc.  (b) The above sizes shall be determined as follows: A bulb that will caliper between six and seven inches in circumference is a 6–7, a bulb that will caliper between seven and eight inches is a 7–8, etc. A bulb that will caliper between 6–8 inches in circumference is a 6–8, a bulb that will caliper between 7–9 is a 7–9, etc. Bulbs that are not of a circular shape shall be so measured that the size in circumference will be comparable to that if the bulb was round. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & 632.900 - 632.980 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 632.900 - 632.980
