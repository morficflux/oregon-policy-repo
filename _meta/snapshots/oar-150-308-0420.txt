 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ASSESSMENT OF PROPERTY FOR TAXATION 
 150-308-0420 
 Exemption of Watercraft Undergoing Repairs 
 
 (1) Watercraft owned or operated by centrally assessed water transportation companies and undergoing “major” repairs as defined in ORS 308.256(4), shall be deemed exempt from taxation if such repairs are in progress as of January 1, of the assessment year, but only upon receipt by the Department of Revenue of documentation included in the annual filing stating the nature, extent, and location of such repairs.  (2) All other assessable Watercraft undergoing “major” repairs as defined in ORS 308.256(4), shall be deemed exempt from taxation if such repairs are in progress as of January 1, of the assessment year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 308.256
