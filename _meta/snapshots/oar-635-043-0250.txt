 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 43 OTHER REGULATIONS: MISCELLANEOUS PERMITS AND RECORDS 
 635-043-0250 
 Purpose of the Urban Deer and Elk Population Control Pilot Program 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to describe the procedures and requirements for issuing kill permits to take deer and elk within urban boundaries, thereby allowing cities the ability to reduce deer or elk populations in response to public nuisance as defined in OAR 498.012, health or safety concerns, and/or property damage. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.162, 498.002, 498.006, 498.012, Or Laws 2017, ch 331, § 1-3, compiled as a note after ORS 498.012 & Senate Bill 761 (2021) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.162, 498.002, 498.006, 498.012, Or Laws 2017, ch 331, § 1-3, compiled as a note after ORS 498.012 & Senate Bill 761 (2021)
