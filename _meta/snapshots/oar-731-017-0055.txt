 Department of Transportation 
 Chapter 731 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS UNABLE TO MEET TPR REQUIREMENTS FOR STATE HIGHWAYS 
 731-017-0055 
 Review and Update 
 
 Beginning two years following the adoption of this division, the Commission shall commence a review to evaluate implementation of and consider possible modifications to this division. This evaluation shall include considerations of the cumulative effects from applications that have been approved. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 367.850 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 367.850 
 
 History:
