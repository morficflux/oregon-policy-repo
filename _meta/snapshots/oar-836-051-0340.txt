 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 LIFE, INDIVIDUAL AND GROUP; ANNUITIES 
 836-051-0340 
 Exercise of the Accelerated Benefit 
 
 (1) For conditions of eligibility resulting from accident, an accelerated benefit provision must be exercisable on or after the issue date of the accelerated benefit provision.  (2) For conditions of eligibility resulting from illness, an accelerated benefit provision must be exercisable not later than the 30th day after the issued date of the accelerated benefit provision.  (3) For the purpose of defining the qualifying event described in ORS 743.154(2)(c), an insurer shall not require a period of continuous confinement that is greater than 180 days. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & 743.154 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 743.154
