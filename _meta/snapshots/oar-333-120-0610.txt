 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION 
 333-120-0610 
 Records of Radiation Protection Programs 
 
 (1) Each licensee must maintain records of the radiation protection program, including:  (a) The provisions of the program; and  (b) Audits and other reviews of program content and implementation.  (2) The licensee must retain the records required by subsection (1)(a) of this rule until the Authority terminates each pertinent license or registration requiring the record. The licensee must retain the records required by subsection (1)(b) of this rule for five years or until inspected by the Authority. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
