 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 180 SHARED SERVICES FUND 
 125-180-1060 
 Funds Distribution 
 
 (1) The department shall distribute moneys from the Shared Services Fund to the eligible county annually.  (2) Counties shall transfer funds to local taxing districts according to the percentages derived from the personal income tax estimate provided in rule 125-180-1040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.340, 285C.615(7) & 285C.635(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285C.600 - 285C.626, 285C.635 & 285C.639 
 
 History:
