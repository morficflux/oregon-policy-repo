 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION 
 333-120-0160 
 Radiation Dose Limits: Occupational Dose Limits for Minors 
 
 The annual occupational dose limits for minors are 10 percent of the annual dose limits specified for adult workers in OAR 333-120-0100. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
