 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 PROCEDURES FOR RESPONSES TO PAROLE AND POST-PRISON SUPERVISION CONDITIONS VIOLATIONS FOR OFFENDERS UNDER THE JURISDICTION OF THE BOARD OF PAROLE AND POST-PRISON SUPERVISION OR LOCAL SUPERVISORY AUTHORITY 
 255-075-0097 
 Time for Future Disposition Hearing 
 
 When the Board holds a future disposition hearing pursuant to OAR 255-075-0072(2) or 255-075-0096, the following timelines shall apply:  (1) If the offender has a new conviction, the Board may schedule the future disposition hearing at the same time as the new prison term hearing.  (2) If the offender has no new conviction or has no prison term hearing, the Board shall schedule the future disposition hearing within 60 days of return to the institution. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144.050, 144.140 & 144.395 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 144.050, 144.140 & 144.395
