 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 105 RIGHTS AND RESPONSIBILITIES 
 461-105-0150 
 Requests and Fees for Written Public Records 
 
 (1) A request for written public records from the Office of Self-Sufficiency Programs must be in writing and must specifically identify the record being requested, the number of copies requested, and the name and address of the individual or entity making the request.  (2) The topic of fees for public records is covered in OAR 407-003-0010 and 461-105-0060. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192.430, 409.050, 411.060, 411.816 & 412.049 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.430, 192.440, 409.010, 411.060, 411.816 & 412.049
