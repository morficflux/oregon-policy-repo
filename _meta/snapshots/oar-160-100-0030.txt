 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 NOTARIES PUBLIC 
 160-100-0030 
 Signature of Notary Public 
 
 (1) Whenever ORS 194 and OAR 160-100-0000 to 160-100-1150 require or permit a notary public to sign his or her name, the notary public shall use the signature that is evidenced on the notary public’s current oath of office, or, for electronic records, the electronic signature on file with the Secretary of State.  (2) For the purposes of this chapter, the legal signature on the notary public’s oath of office must be proven with satisfactory evidence per ORS 194.240(2). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 194.240 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 194.360
