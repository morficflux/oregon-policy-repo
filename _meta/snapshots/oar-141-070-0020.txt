 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 LEASING OF ONSHORE STATE-OWNED OIL AND GAS RIGHTS 
 141-070-0020 
 Qualification Requirements 
 
 (1) To qualify for a State of Oregon geophysical permit or oil and gas lease, an applicant must be:  (a) A citizen of the United States of legal age;  (b) A partnership conducting business under an assumed business name or a corporation registered with the State Corporation Division; or  (c) A domestic governmental body.  (2) Members of the State Land Board and employees of the Division of State Lands shall not take or hold leasehold interests in state-owned oil and gas rights. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 273 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.780 - 273.790 
 
 History:
