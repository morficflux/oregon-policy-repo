 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 ATTORNEY FEES 
 438-015-0115 
 Fees for Attorneys Representing Insurers and Self-Insured Employers 
 
  A requested client paid fee shall: 

 (1) Be reasonable, considering the factors prescribed in OAR 438-015-0010(4), to the extent such factors are relevant to a client-paid fee; and 

 (2) Not exceed that agreed upon in the retainer agreement. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.388(4), (5) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WCB 1-2020, adopt filed 03/06/2020, effective 06/01/2020
