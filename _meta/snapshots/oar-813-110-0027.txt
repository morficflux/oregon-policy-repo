 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 OREGON AFFORDABLE HOUSING TAX CREDITS: AFFORDABLE HOUSING PROJECT CERTIFICATION 
 813-110-0027 
 Certification Request by Lending Institution
 
 
 (1)  The sponsoring entity shall submit on behalf of the lending institution a separate request for each certification of a sponsoring entity requested under the OAHTC program. 

 (2) The sponsoring entity shall pay a charge as assessed by OHCS for each request for a certification. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 317.097 & ORS 456.515-456.725 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 317.097
