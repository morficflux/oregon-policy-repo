 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 175 UNDERGROUND STORAGE TANK GRANT PROGRAM 
 340-175-0020 
 Grant Benefits 
 
 (1) A pollution prevention grant will fund up to 50 percent not to exceed $50,000 of UST project work.  (2) An essential services grant will fund up to 75 percent not to exceed $75,000 of UST project work. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OL 1993 & Ch. 661 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  OL 1991, Ch. 863 & Sec. 6
