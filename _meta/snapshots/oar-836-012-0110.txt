 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 CREDIT FOR REINSURANCE 
 836-012-0110 
 Contracts Affected
 
 
 All new and renewal reinsurance transactions entered into on and after January 1, 2019, shall conform to the requirements of ORS 731.509 to 731.511 and OAR 836-012-0000 to 836-012-0110 if credit is to be given to the ceding insurer for such reinsurance. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.508 - 731.511 & 731.244 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 731.508 - 731.511 & Or Laws 2019, ch 151
