 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 210 WELL CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS 
 690-210-0300 
 Drill Cuttings or Chips 
 
 In no case shall drill cuttings or drill chips be used or allowed to fill, partially fill, or fall into the required sealing interval of a well during the construction or the completion of a well. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
