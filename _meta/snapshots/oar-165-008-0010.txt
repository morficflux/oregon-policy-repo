 Secretary of State 
 Elections Division - Chapter 165 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 REAPPORTIONMENT 
 165-008-0010 
 Schedule if Legislature Does Not Complete Reapportionment by July 1 
 
 (1) Subsection (3) of Section 6, Article IV of the Oregon Constitution requires the Secretary of State to make the reapportionment if the legislature fails to enact a reapportionment by July 1 of the year following a federal census.  (2) In this event, the Secretary of State will follow this schedule:  (a) July 16 — Publish a draft reapportionment plan; distribute to the public;  (b) July 16–August 3 — Accept written comments from the public;  (c) July 20–August 3 — Conduct one or more public hearings at locations to be announced;  (d) August 4–15 — Complete transcription of hearing or hearings. Review "evidence, views and argument" submitted by the public. Prepare reapportionment plan;  (e) August 15 — Submit reapportionment plan, together with transcript and evidence, to the Supreme Court. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 188.015 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 188.015
