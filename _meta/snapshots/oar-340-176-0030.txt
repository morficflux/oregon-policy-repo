 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 176 UNDERGROUND STORAGE TANK LOAN GUARANTEE 
 340-176-0030 
 General Provisions, Guaranteed Underground Storage Tank Facility Loan 
 
 (1) The guaranteed loan must be issued by a commercial lending institution as defined in OAR 340, division 172.  (2) Grant funds, described in OAR 340, division 175, may not be used to pay either the principal or interest portion of the guaranteed loan. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  OL 1989 & Ch. 1071 
 
 History:
