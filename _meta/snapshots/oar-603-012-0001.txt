 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 LIVESTOCK AUCTION MARKETS 
 603-012-0001 
 Definitions 
 
 As used in OAR 603-012-0003 to 603-012-0027:  (1) “Form 1-27” is a document prescribed by the Department which includes the description and identification of livestock, the name of the buyer of the livestock, the destination of the livestock for slaughter, and an acknowledgment from the designated slaughtering establishment that the described livestock had been received.  (2) “Slaughtering establishment” means an establishment as defined in ORS 603.010(5), required to be licensed under ORS 603.025(4)(c) or (d). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 596 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 596.331
