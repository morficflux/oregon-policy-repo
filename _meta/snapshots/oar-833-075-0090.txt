 Mental Health Regulatory Agency 
 Oregon Board of Licensed Professional Counselors and Therapists - Chapter 833 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 GENERAL LICENSURE PROVISIONS 
 833-075-0090 
 Representation of Credentials 
 
 A person may not use the title of “licensed professional counselor” or “licensed marriage and family therapist,” including the abbreviations “LPC” and “LMFT,” unless the person holds a current license issued by the Board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.705 - 675.835 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.705 - 675.835 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BLPCT 4-2016, f. & cert. ef. 10-10-16
