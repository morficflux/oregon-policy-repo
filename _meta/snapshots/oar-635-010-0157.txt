 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 LICENSING - PROCEDURES FOR ESTABLISHING, OPERATING, DISCONTINUING AND SUSPENDING LICENSES AND AGENTS 
 635-010-0157 
 Free Resident Licenses for Disabled Veterans 
 
 (1) Pursuant to ORS 497.102 and 497.121, a disabled veteran is entitled to a free hunting license, angling license and shellfish license if the veteran meets the following requirements. The license remains valid so long as the licensee remains an Oregon resident.  (a) The veteran has been an Oregon resident for at least six months; and  (b) The veteran provides to the Department written certification from the U.S. Veteran's Administration or any branch of the U.S. Armed Forces that the veteran is at least 25 percent disabled.  (2) Any valid permanent angling license issued before January 1, 2000, to a person permanently confined to a wheelchair is valid for as long as the licensee is a resident of Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496, 497 & Ch. 25 & OL 1999 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496 & 497, Ch. 25 & OL 1999
