 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 FORENSIC MENTAL HEALTH EVALUATORS AND EVALUATIONS 
 309-090-0050 
 Confidentiality
 
 
 Except for the names of certified evaluators, all records provided to the Authority under these rules are confidential and privileged and may not be released or utilized for any purpose outside these rules. Any practitioner who in good faith complies with these rules, including providing sample evaluations for review in order to maintain certification, is not responsible for any failure by another individual or agency to maintain confidentiality, in regard to these rules. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.392, 413.042 & 419C.382 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.295-325, 419C.378-386, 161.360-370, 161.392, 419C.411 & 419C.520-529
