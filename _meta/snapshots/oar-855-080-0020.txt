 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 SCHEDULE OF CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES 
 855-080-0020 
 Schedules
 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 475.005(6) those drugs and their immediate precursors classified in Schedules I through V under the Federal Controlled Substances Act, 21 USC 811 (v. 02/26/2026), 21 USC 812 (v. 02/26/2026) and as amended by the board pursuant to ORS 475.035 are the controlled substances for purposes of regulation and control under the Act. Those schedules are set out in OAR 855-080-0021 through 855-080-0026. 

 [Publications: Publications referenced are available for review at the agency.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475.035
