 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 INDOOR CLEAN AIR ACT, NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION AT CHAIN RESTAURANTS AND RETAIL SALE OF TOBACCO PRODUCTS AND INHALANT DELIVERY SYSTEMS 
 333-015-0062 
 Oregon Indoor Clean Air Act: Vehicles 
 
 (1) An employer may allow smoking in vehicles only when the vehicle is permanently assigned to a single employee and no other employees, clients, or members of the public are required or compelled to operate or otherwise occupy the vehicle.  (2) Nothing in these rules shall prevent an employer from designating all vehicles as smokefree. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 433.855 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 433.835 - 433.870
