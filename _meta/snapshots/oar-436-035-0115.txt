 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 DISABILITY RATING STANDARDS 
 436-035-0115 
 Conversion of Upper Extremity Values to Whole Person Values 
 
 (1) The tables in this rule are used to convert losses in the upper extremity to a whole person (WP) value for claims with a date of injury on or after January 1, 2005.  (2) The following table is used to convert losses in the thumb and fingers to a whole person (WP) value.  (3) The following table is used to convert a loss in a hand/forearm to a whole person (WP) value.  (4) The following table is used to convert a loss in the arm to a whole person (WP) value. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656 
 
 History:
