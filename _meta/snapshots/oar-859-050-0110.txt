 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD HEARING PROCEDURES 
 859-050-0110 
 Records of Hearings 
 
 All Board hearings, except Board deliberations, will be recorded by electronic means which can be transcribed. All documents considered will be included in the file and kept as part of the record: 

 (1) Audio recordings capable of being transcribed will be kept by the Board for a minimum period of five years from the hearing date; 

 (2) Any material to which an objection is sustained will be removed from the exhibit file and will not be considered in future Board hearings; the objection and ruling of the Board will be noted on the record. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.295-161.400 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PSRB 5-2017, adopt filed 12/07/2017, effective 12/07/2017
