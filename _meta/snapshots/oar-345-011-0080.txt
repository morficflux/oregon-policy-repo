 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 COUNCIL MEETINGS AND COMMUNICATIONS 
 345-011-0080 
 Unacceptable Conduct 
 
 The chair may expel any person who engages in conduct that disrupts any Council proceeding. In any proceeding of a Council committee, the chairman or chairwoman of the committee may expel any person who engages in conduct that disrupts the proceeding. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.460
