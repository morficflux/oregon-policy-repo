 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 316 PERSONAL INCOME TAX GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 150-316-0380 
 Withholding Penalties
 
 
 See ORS 305.228, 305.990, 305.992, 314.400, 314.410, 314.440, 314.991, 316.992 and the corresponding rules for provisions regarding penalties, misdemeanors, and jeopardy assessments applicable to income tax withholding, statewide transit tax and associated reports. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 316.212
