 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 271 ELECTRICAL INSPECTIONS 
 918-271-0010 
 Calls for Inspection 
 
 (1) All persons who take out an electrical permit, homeowners as well as electrical contractors, shall request an inspection within three working days of:  (a) The completion of any electrical installation intended to be covered or concealed or that is intended to be placed into service before the final electrical inspection; and  (b) The completion of all electrical installations for the job site covered by a particular permit.  (2) Transactions under a master inspection permit are covered by separate requirements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.730 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.730
