 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0022 
 Dixon Unit 
 
 The Dixon Unit, number 22, is that area beginning at Shady Cove; northwest on State Highway 227 to Dixonville-Hanna Nickel main power transmission line at a point approximately two miles west of Days Creek; north along main power transmission line to North Umpqua River; east along North Umpqua River to Toketee; south on Toketee Road to State Highway 138; east and south on State Highway 138 to State Highway 230; southwest on State Highway 230 to State Highway 62; southwest on State Highway 62 to Shady Cove, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
