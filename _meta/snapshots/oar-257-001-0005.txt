 Department of State Police 
 Chapter 257 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 257-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Practice and Procedures 
 
 Except as provided in OAR 257-001-0020, 257-001-025 and 257-045-0010 to 257-045-0050 with respect to the rules for impoundment hearings under ORS 806.016 and hearings for vehicles taken into custody and towed under 819.110 or 819.120, the Model Rules of Procedure as promulgated by the Attorney General of the State of Oregon under the Administrative Procedure Act effective January 1, 2010, are adopted as the Rules of Procedure of the Department of State Police and shall be controlling except as otherwise required by statute or rule.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Oregon State Police.]  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 181, 181.040, 181.280, 183, 806, 806.014, 806.016 & 814, OL 2009 & ch. 371 (HB 2739) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341(4), OL 2009 & ch. 371 (HB 2739)
