 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 215 MAINTENANCE, REPAIR AND DEEPENING OF WATER SUPPLY WELLS 
 690-215-0090 
 Conversion to an Artesian Well 
 
 If a water supply well becomes artesian upon deepening, the well shall be cased, sealed and completed in accordance with OAR 690-210-0155. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
