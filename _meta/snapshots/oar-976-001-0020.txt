 Oregon Tourism Commission 
 Chapter 976 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 976-001-0020 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, in effect on November 1, 2014, are adopted as the rules of procedure for the Oregon Tourism Commission and shall be followed in all matters except where a different procedure is prescribed by statute. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 284.111(6) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 284.111(6) & 183.341 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ORTC 1-2014, f. & cert. ef. 12-17-14
