 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 646 IDENTITY THEFT 
 441-646-0010 
 Definitions 
 
 As used in ORS 646A.600 to 646A.628 and this division 646, unless the context requires otherwise:  (1) “Continuing violation” means the same activity extending longer than one day that violates the Oregon Consumer Identity Theft Protection Act.  (2) “Harm,” for purposes of the notification requirement, is limited to monetary loss. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 646A.626 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 646A.604 & 646A.624 
 
 History:
