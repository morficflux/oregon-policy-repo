 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 OPERATION OF OREGON CREDIT ENHANCEMENT FUND 
 123-021-3700 
 Administrative Costs of the Program
 
 
 The Department may charge the fund actual and necessary administrative expenses in operating the Fund and its programs. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 & 285B.200 - 285B.218 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285.476(2), (5) & 285.471(1)(b)
