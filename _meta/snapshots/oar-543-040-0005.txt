 State Library 
 Chapter 543 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 READY TO READ GRANTS 
 543-040-0005 
 Scope
 
 
 OAR chapter 543, division 40 carries out the provisions of ORS 357.740 to 357.780: Financial Assistance for Public Library Services to Children. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 357.026 & ORS 357.760 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 357.740 - 780
