 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 266 FIELD BURNING RULES 
 340-266-0140 
 Burning Fees Outside Willamette Valley 
 
 In accordance with ORS 468A.615(1)(b), each person open field burning perennial or annual grass seed crops in counties outside the Willamette Valley, shall pay the Department $8.00 for each acre burned. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.615 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 8-2010, f. & cert . ef. 8-27-10
