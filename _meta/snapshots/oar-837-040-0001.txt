 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 FIRE AND LIFE SAFETY REGULATIONS 
 837-040-0001 
 Scope 
 
 (1) The International Fire Code and the Oregon amendments represent a total scope of regulation.  (2) None of the individual chapters in the International Fire Code and Oregon amendments are stand alone requirements. (Referenced publications are available for review at the agency. See agency web site for information on where to purchase publications.) 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 476.030
