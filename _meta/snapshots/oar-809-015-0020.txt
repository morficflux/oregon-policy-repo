 Board of Geologist Examiners 
 Chapter 809 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 RENEWALS, RESTORATIONS 
 809-015-0020 
 Reissuance of Revoked Registration 
 
 At its discretion, the Board may reissue a revoked certificate of registration according to ORS 672.685 under the following conditions:  (1) Submission of a written request for reissuance by the revoked registrant;  (2) Receipt of written documentation demonstrating good cause to justify the reissuance;  (3) Submission of all application materials meeting the requirements for first-time registrants as prescribed in Qualification Standards and Experience, OAR 809-30-0000 through 809-30-0025, and all relevant registration and application fees as prescribed in Fees, OAR 809-010-0001;  (4) Take or retake and pass the ASBOG fundamental and practice examinations, and specialty examinations as applicable. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 672.515 & 672.685 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.505-672.705 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BGE 1-2012, f. & cert. ef. 6-15-12
