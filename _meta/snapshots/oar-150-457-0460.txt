 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 457 URBAN RENEWAL 
 150-457-0460 
 Notice of Plan Adoption or Area Change 
 
 An urban renewal agency’s notice to the assessor of a plan adoption or amendment to a plan area must provide the following information:  (1) A legal description of the plan area boundary, or the boundary of the amended area of the plan, that includes the information required by ORS 308.225(2)(b);  (2) An accurate map showing the boundary line of the plan area or the boundary of the amended area of the plan;  (3) The date the plan or plan amendment was approved; and  (4) The name of the plan area. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100. 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 457.450(1).
