Land Use Board of Appeals

Chapter 661

Division 10
RULES OF PROCEDURE FOR APPEALS

661-010-0000
Introduction

Scope of Rules and Effective Date: All proceedings commenced by a notice of intent to appeal filed on or after January 1, 2026, shall be governed by these rules. Proceedings commenced before January 1, 2026, shall be governed by OAR 661-010-0005 through OAR 661-010-0075 as effective on the date the notice of intent to appeal was filed.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 197.820(4)
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 197.805
