 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 29 SALE OF FOREST PRODUCTS FROM LANDS OWNED OR MANAGED BY THE STATE BOARD OF FORESTRY 
 629-029-0000 
 Definitions 
 
 These rules apply only to sales of forest products with a value in excess of $25,000 or as otherwise allowed by ORS 530.059; 273.521; or 273.525. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.016 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 630.050 
 
 History:
