 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 246 GENERAL PROVISIONS FOR PUBLIC CONTRACTING 
 125-246-0610 
 Appointments to Advisory Committees
 
 
 The Director, State Chief Procurement Officer, Designated Procurement Officer or a delegatee may appoint procurement advisory committees to assist with Specifications, procurement decisions, and structural change that can take full advantage of evolving procurement methods as they emerge within various industries, while preserving competition pursuant to ORS 279A.015. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279A.015, 279A.065(5)(a), 279A.070 & 279A.140
