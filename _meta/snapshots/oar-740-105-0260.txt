 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 105 WELDED REPAIR OF LOG TRUCK REACHES; EQUIPMENT: LOGS, POLES OR PILING 
 740-105-0260 
 Records of Repair 
 
 Each record of repair shall be maintained at the motor carrier’s official physical address of record and shall include at a minimum:  (1) Date of repair;  (2) Welder’s name and certification number that effected the repair; and  (3) Reason repair was needed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335, 823.011, 825.210 & 825.252 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.210 & 825.252
