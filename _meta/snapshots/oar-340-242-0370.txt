 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 242 RULES APPLICABLE TO THE PORTLAND AREA 
 340-242-0370 
 Voluntary Maximum Parking Ratio Program: How is the Parking Ratio Program Enforced? 
 
 (1) A Parking Ratio Permit is a written permit in letter form issued by the Department bearing the signature of the Director or his/her designee.  (2) The general permitting provisions of Oregon Administrative Rules, chapter 340, division 14 apply (issuance, renewal, denial, suspension), except that OAR 340-014-0025 (public notice requirement) does not apply.  (3) An employer is no longer exempt from the ECO rule requirements if the property owner fails to comply with the terms of the Parking Ratio letter permit. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.363
