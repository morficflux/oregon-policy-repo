 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 STANDARD FOR THE OPERATION BY CLASS I RAILROADS OF FREIGHT TRAINS WITHOUT AN OCCUPIED CABOOSE AS THE REAR CAR 
 741-400-0010 
 Standards for Cabooseless Train Operations 
 
 The Public Utility Commission adopts the following orders as standards for the operation by Class I railroads of freight trains without an occupied caboose as the rear cars:  Case Order  Railroad Number Number  Southern Pacific RC-47 84-904  86-787  Union Pacific RC-58 86-1119  87-039  Burlington Northern RC-59 85-1214  RC-78 86-764 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756 & 761 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 761.612
