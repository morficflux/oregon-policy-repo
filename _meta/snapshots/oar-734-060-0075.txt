 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 SIGNS 
 734-060-0075 
 Portable Signs on State Highway Right of Way — Removal Provisions 
 
 Signs subject to OAR 734-027-0005 through 734-027-0050 are subject to the removal provisions of those rules and ORS 377.775. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184 & 377 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 377.650 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 2HD 4-1985, f. & ef. 11-22-85
