 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 SOIL AND WATER CONSERVATION — HEARING PROCEDURES 
 603-070-0005 
 Hearing Upon a Petition for the Formation of a District 
 
 The administrative procedures applicable to a hearing upon a petition for the formation of a district, as provided for in ORS 568.310, and the department’s determination resulting therefrom, as provided for in ORS 568.330 and 568.340, shall be those specified in OAR 603-001-0005. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 561 & 568
