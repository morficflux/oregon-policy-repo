 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 605 PLANNING FOREST OPERATIONS 
 629-605-0190 
 Written Plans for Operations Near Critical, Threatened, or Endangered Wildlife Habitat Sites 
 
 Operators must submit a written plan to the State Forester before operating near or within:  (1) Critical wildlife or aquatic habitat sites that are listed in a 1984 cooperative agreement between the Board of Forestry and the Fish and Wildlife Commission or sites designated by the State Forester; or  (2) Habitat sites of any wildlife or aquatic species classified by the Department of Fish and Wildlife as threatened or endangered. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 527.710 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 527.710
