 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 DEPARTMENT REGULATORY PROGRAMS 
 836-200-0510 
 Account Generation Requirement 
 
 (1) Beginning in 2020 and for any subsequent year, reporting manufacturers must create an online account with the department no later than 30 days after becoming a reporting manufacturer or 10 days prior to a required reporting deadline, whichever is earlier. 

 (2) Reporting manufacturers are responsible for ensuring that they designate at least one contact person, with a valid email address, mailing address, and phone number, in the department’s reporting system for purposes of communications and notices by the department. At least one contact person must be a reporting manufacturer employee who manages access to the account and receipt of trade secret determinations. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 646A.689 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 646A.689
