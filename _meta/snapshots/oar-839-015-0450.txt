 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 RULES REGULATING LABOR CONTRACTORS 
 839-015-0450 
 Notice of Compliance with Bond Requirements
 
 
 (1) Every labor contractor is required to post a notice of compliance with bond requirements in English and in any language used by the contractor to communicate with the contractor's workers. The notice must be posted in a conspicuous place on the job site where the contractor's employees are working and must be easily accessible to them. 

 (2) The notice shall state that the contractor is either exempt from bond requirements or has complied with ORS 658.415 by obtaining a corporate surety bond or by making a deposit with the commissioner. The notice shall also indicate the amount of the surety bond or deposit and the expiration date of the bond or deposit, and that the surety bond or deposits are conditioned on the payment of wages and advances made by construction property owners, farmers or owners or lessees of land intended to be used for the production of timber. 

 (3) The notice shall contain the name and Oregon address of the surety on the bond, if applicable, or the address of the bureau when a deposit is made with the commissioner. 

 (4) The commissioner has prepared a notice (WH-155) in English and Spanish which complies with this rule. Contractors may use any form or notice so long as it contains all the elements of Form WH-155. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 164, 165, 651 & 658 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 658.405 - 658.503
