 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 73 102"-WIDE COMMERCIAL VEHICLES — COMBINATIONS NOT SUBJECT TO OVERALL LENGTH RESTRICTIONS 
 734-073-0130 
 Specialized Equipment — Drive-Away Operations 
 
 The Federal Highway Administration determines Drive-away saddlemount vehicle transporter combinations are Specialized Equipment as provided by 23 Code of Federal Regulations 658.13(e)(iii). 

 (1) A Drive-away saddlemount vehicle transporter combination or a Drive-away saddlemount with fullmount vehicle transporter may operate without permit on National Network Highways with an overall length limit of 97 feet. 

 (2) All Drive-away saddlemount vehicle transporter combinations must comply with all applicable safety regulations of 49 Code of Federal Regulations 393.71. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 810.050, 810.060, 184.619, 818.200 & 818.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.030, 818.200 & 818.220
