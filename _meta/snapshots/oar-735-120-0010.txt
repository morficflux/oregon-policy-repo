 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 DEFINITIONS/RIGHTS OF TRANSPORTATION SAFETY 
 735-120-0010 
 Definitions 
 
 The following definitions shall be used for the purpose of this Code:  (1) “Class ‘A’ vehicle”: A vehicle of the bus type designed to carry 12 or more workers; or of the “work crew” type especially built or accommodated for carrying passengers.  (2) “Class ‘B’ vehicle”: A vehicle especially built or accommodated for transporting work crews in compartments separate from space used to transport supplies, tools, and equipment such as vehicles commonly used by public utilities.  (3) “Class ‘C’ vehicle”: A vehicle of the flatbed, pickup body, or dump truck body type, or of similar open body construction.  (4) “Class ‘D’ vehicle”: A vehicle of the passenger car or station wagon type. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616 & 820.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 820.010 - 820.030
