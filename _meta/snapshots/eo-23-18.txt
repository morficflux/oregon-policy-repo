    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23-18


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    LOOKOUT FIRE IN LANE COUNTY


    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Lookout Fire” is burning in Lane County.


    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Lookout Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by the Lane County Fire Defense Board Chief, and the State Fire
    Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Lookout Fire in Lane
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 6:17 p.m. on August 13, 2023. The Oregon State Fire
    Marshal is mobilizing resources and the Blue Incident Management Team to the
    command of the local Fire Defense Board Chief for the Lookout Fire. The Blue
    Incident Management Team will enter into unified command on the morning of
    August 14, 2023. I now confirm these findings with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    ike    The Oregon Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources statewide and
           coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for the use of
           personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency Conflagration
           Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to the Lookout
           Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

    De     This conflagration is declared only for the Lookout Fire threatening
           structures in Lane County.


    3,     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 6:17 p.m. on August 13,
           2023, and signed on August 16'", 2023, in Josephine County, Oregon.
