 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 RIGHT OF WAY AND REAL PROPERTY 
 734-035-0050 
 Disposition of Surplus Property; General Policy 
 
 It is the general policy of the Department of Transportation to efficiently and economically dispose of real property that is determined by the agency to be surplus. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184 & 366 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 270.100 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 HWY 9-1990, f. & cert. ef. 5-24-90
