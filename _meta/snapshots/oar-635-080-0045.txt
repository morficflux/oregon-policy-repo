 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0045 
 Fossil Unit 
 
 The Fossil Unit, Number 45, is that area beginning at Fossil, north on State Highway 19 to Condon; east on State Highway 206 to State Highway 207; south on State Highway 207 to State Highway 19; southeast on State Highway 19 to U.S. Highway 26; west on U.S. Highway 26 to Bridge Creek near Mitchell; northwest along Bridge Creek to John Day River; northwest along John Day River to State Highway 218 at Clarno; northeast on State Highway 218 to Fossil, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496
