 Oregon Board of Physical Therapy 
 Chapter 848 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 CONTINUING COMPETENCE 
 848-035-0010 
 Purpose
 
 
 The 2005 Oregon Legislation gave new authority to the Oregon Board of Physical Therapy, effective January 1, 2006, to adopt rules establishing minimum continuing competence requirements for all licensees. The following rules set out the requirements. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 688.160 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 688.160(6)(g)
