 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 PUBLIC REAL PROPERTY NEAR RAIL INFRASTRUCTURE NOTIFICATION 
 741-040-0040 
 Exempt from Notification 
 
 The requirement for notification does not apply:  (1) To listing or placing any Real Property for sale, exchange or conveyance within a Light Rail Corridor;  (2) If the proposed sale, exchange or conveyance of the real property is to a provider of rail service; or  (3) To the proposed sale, exchange or conveyance of easements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 271.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 271
