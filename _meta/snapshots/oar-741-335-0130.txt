 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 335 RAILROAD CABOOSE AND LOCOMOTIVE SANITATION FACILITY AND WATER STANDARDS 
 741-335-0130 
 Drinking Water 
 
 (1) Sanitary drinking water facilities shall be provided and maintained on locomotives and cabooses in service so as to provide an adequate supply of safe drinking water.  (2) When ice is used for water cooling purposes, the containers shall be so arranged that the drinking water will not come in contact with the ice. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 824.068
