 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 MOBILE HOME PARK PURCHASE PROGRAM 
 813-009-0035 
 Waiver 
 
 The Director, with the concurrence of the Council, may waive or modify any requirements of OAR 813, division 009, unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable state or federal law. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 90.800–90.840, 90.630, 813, 446, 456.515–456.723 & 458.210–458.650 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.579–456.581 
 
 History:
