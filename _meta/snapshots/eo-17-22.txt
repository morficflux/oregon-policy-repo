    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 17-22


                      AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 17-06


    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    Executive Order 17-06 was issued on April 13, 2017, and declared an emergency in
    eighteen Oregon counties due to a severe winter storm. Specifically, the storms
    caused significant damage to state roads on the federal-aid highway system.

    Since the time of the initial damage assessments, three additional counties have
    been identified as having sustained damage requiring assistance from the Oregon
    Department of Transportation.


    Executive Order 17-06 is amended to add the following counties:


           Polk, Union, and Wheeler.
