    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-57


    EXTENDING THE DURATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-42
    (DECLARATION OF ABNORMAL DISRUPTION OF THE MARKET DUE
    TO WILDFIRES)


    Executive Order 20-42, declaring an abnormal disruption of the market due to
    wildfires, was issued on September 10, 2020. On page 2 of Executive Order 20-42,
    paragraph 3 is amended to provide as follows:


           This Executive Order remains in effect until terminated by the Governor,
           provided that the Governor periodically notifies the Attorney General that
           the abnormal disruption of the market continues to exist, pursuant to
           ORS 401.965(6)(d).


    Done at Salem, Oregon, this 6" day of October, 2020.
