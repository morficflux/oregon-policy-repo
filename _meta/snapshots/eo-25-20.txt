         EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25-20


         INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
         FLAT FIRE IN JEFFERSON AND DESCHUTES COUNTIES


         Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, | find that:


         The fire known as the “Flat Fire” is burning in Jefferson County and threatening
         structures in Deschutes County.


         The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Flat Fire are
         beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
|        was requested by the Deschutes and Jefferson Counties Fire Defense Board Chiefs
|        and the State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

         In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
         safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Flat Fire in Jefferson
         County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
         personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
         Conflagration Act.


         These findings were made at 8:25 am on August 22, 2025, with the Department of
         the State Fire Marshal. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management
         Team will be in unified command with Central Oregon Fire Management Services
         Type 3 team on August 22, 2025. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Red Incident
         Management Team will be briefed at 3:00 p.m. on August 22, 2025, and I now
         confirm them with this Executive Order.


         NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


         Ih.    The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
                statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
                the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
                Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
                the Flat Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.


         De     This conflagration is declared only for the Flat Fire threatening structures in
                Jefferson and Deschutes Counties.


         3);    This order was made by verbal proclamation at 8:25 a.m. on the 22" day of
                August 2025 and signed this 22"4 day of August 2025, in Salem, Oregon.
