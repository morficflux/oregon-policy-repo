 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 67 IMPLEMENTING THE INSTITUTIONAL CONSERVATION PROGRAM 
 330-067-0023 
 Program for Technical and Program Assistance 
 
 As allowed under Subparts H and K, 10 CFR 455 in the February 19, 1993, Federal Register, ODOE will operate a program to provide technical and program assistance through the following services:  (1) Resources accounting program and training;  (2) On-site assessments;  (3) Building operator training, including trouble shooting and repair;  (4) Detailed energy studies;  (5) Assistance to secure financing of identified energy conservation measures; and  (6) Training on how to operate and maintain installed measures. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.040 & 469.165 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOE 3-1993, f. & cert. ef. 8-23-93
