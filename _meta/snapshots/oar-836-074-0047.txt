 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 74 INSURANCE DIVISION TRUST ACCOUNTS 
 836-074-0047 
 Examinations and Audits 
 
 (1) The Director may examine or audit any trust account and any accounting records of premium funds as the Director determines necessary.  (2) The examination or audit shall be performed at the expense of the insurance producer, when the trust account or accounting records are located outside this state. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 744.083
