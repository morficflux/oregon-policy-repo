 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 REGISTRATION OF SECURITIES 
 441-065-0190 
 Filings and Reports
 
 
 (1) For offerings registered as a SOAR Offering, the Director may, as a condition of registration, require the issuer to file one or more sales reports. 

 (2) For offerings registered as a SCOR Offering, the issuer shall file the following reports with the Director: 

 (a) If the offering is made in reliance on Securities Act Rule 504(17 CFR §230.504), a copy of any completed Form D that is filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission; or 

 (b) If the offering is not made in reliance on to Securities Act Rule 504(17 CFR §230.504), a report on a form and at times specified by the Director as a condition of registration. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 59.085 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 59.085
