 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 PHARMACEUTICAL MANUFACTURERS 
 855-060-0025 
 Labeling 
 
 (1) All stocks and materials, as well as products produced, shall be labeled and conform to the strength and purity as required by law.  (2) A sample label of each product manufactured shall be supplied to the State Board of Pharmacy upon request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689
