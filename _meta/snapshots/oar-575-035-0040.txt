 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 BARBERS AND HAIRDRESSERS GRANT PROGRAM 
 575-035-0040 
 Award Priority 
 
 When considering application for award, the Commission shall give first priority to the funding of grants to full-time students. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.290 & 348.505
