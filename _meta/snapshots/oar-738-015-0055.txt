 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 LEASING FOR AERONAUTICAL ACTIVITIES AT STATE-OWNED AIRPORTS 
 738-015-0055 
 Access Permits 
 
 Effective August 15, 2001, the date of approval of the Leasing Policy for Commercial Property by the State Aviation Board, the Department shall not issue any new access permits until all State-owned airport property has been developed, except at those airports where access permits already exist. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 835.035, 835.040 & 835.112 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 935.035, 935.040, 835.112 & 836.055 
 
 History:
