 Board of Optometry 
 Chapter 852 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 CONTINUING OPTOMETRIC EDUCATION 
 852-070-0005 
 Definitions 
 
 “Hour” means clock hour of 60 minutes of instruction time, plus or minus 10 minutes. “Half-hour” means 30 minutes of instruction time, plus or minus five minutes. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 683 & 182 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 683.210 & 182.466
