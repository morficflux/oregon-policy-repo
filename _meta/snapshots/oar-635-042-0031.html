Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 42
COLUMBIA RIVER SYSTEM COMMERCIAL FISHERIES

635-042-0031
Early Fall Salmon Season

The early fall commercial salmon season in the Columbia River downstream of Bonneville Dam is assessed annually based on stock status and allocation policies.  Harvest opportunity and gear regulations will be established by inseason modification.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.118, ORS 506.109 & ORS 506.129
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 506.119 & ORS 507.030
