 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 52 INSURANCE POLICIES 
 836-052-0185 
 Reporting of Multiple Policies 
 
 (1) On or before March 1 of each year, each issuer shall report to the Director the following information for every individual resident of this state for which the issuer has in force more than one Medicare supplement insurance policy or certificate:  (a) Policy and certificate number; and  (b) Date of issuance.  (2) The information required under section (1) of this rule must be grouped by individual policyholder.  (3) Each issuer shall report the information required under this rule on the reporting form prescribed in Exhibit 1 to this rule.  [ED. NOTE: Exhibits referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244, 743.010, 743.013, 743.680 - 743.689 & 746.240 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 743.010(1)(c) & 743.010(2)
