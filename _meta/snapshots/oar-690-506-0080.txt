 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 506 JOHN DAY BASIN PROGRAM 
 690-506-0080 
 Storage of Water 
 
 (1) An application for a permit to store water on a stream with uses classified as conditional shall be subject to the standards and review process in OAR 690-506-0010(3).  (2) The Department shall not accept an application to store or impound water within the John Day Scenic Waterway as defined in OAR 736, division 40. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536 & 537
