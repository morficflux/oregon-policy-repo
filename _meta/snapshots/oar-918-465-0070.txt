 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 465 REACH CODE 
 918-465-0070 
 Fees for Plan Review and Permits 
 
 Fees for plan review and permits issued by the Division for construction, alteration and repair of prefabricated structures and of buildings and other structures as established by these rules and authorized by ORS 455.210, shall be determined in accordance with 918-460-0030, 918-440-0050, 918-480-0020 and 918-309-0030 through 918-309-0070 as appropriate.  [ED. NOTE: Publications referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.210 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.210
