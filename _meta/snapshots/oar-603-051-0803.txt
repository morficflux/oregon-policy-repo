 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 PRODUCE STANDARDS 
 603-051-0803 
 Reference 
 
 The book, Modern Roses, most recent edition, and the American Rose Annual, published by the American Rose Society, may be used as references to determine the classification of a variety.  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & 633.620 - 633.660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 633.620 - 633.660 
 
 History:
