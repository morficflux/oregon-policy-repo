 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 OFFENSE SUBCATEGORIES 
 213-018-0068 
 Subjecting Another Person to Involuntary Servitude I (2007 Oregon Laws Ch 811) 
 
 (1) CRIME CATEGORY 9: Subjecting Another Person to Involuntary Servitude I shall be ranked at Crime Category 9 if the offender caused or threatened to cause death or serious physical injury to a person.  (2) CRIME CATEGORY 6: Subjecting Another Person to Involuntary Servitude I shall be ranked at Crime Category 6 if the offender restrained or threatened to physically restrain a person. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667-137.669 & 2007 OL Ch. 811
