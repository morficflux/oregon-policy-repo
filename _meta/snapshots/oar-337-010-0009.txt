 Board of Medical Imaging 
 Chapter 337 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 LICENSURE 
 337-010-0009 
 Change of Credentialing or License Status
 
 
 Every licensee or permit holder must: 

 (1) Maintain credentialing in their licensed specialty throughout the licensure period. 

 (2) Notify the Board within 30 days of any issuance of any pending or final actions against the licensee or permit holder by the credentialing organization. 

 (3) Notify the Board within 30 days of lapse or suspension of any professional license. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 688.555(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 688.455
