 Oregon Public Employees Retirement System 
 Chapter 459 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 EARNINGS AND INTEREST DISTRIBUTION 
 459-007-0003 
 Determination of Tier One Year-to-Date Calculation 
 
 (1) Any year-to-date calculation ("factor") used to credit earnings to Tier One member regular accounts shall be a pro-rate of the assumed interest rate and cannot be greater unless and until the conditions in ORS 238.255 have been met.  (2) The provisions of this rule shall be applied retroactively to July 1, 2003. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 238.650 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 238
