 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 AIRPORT LISTINGS 
 738-090-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of this division is to carry out the requirements of ORS 836.610. This division:  (1) Defines terminology used in the division;  (2) Identifies publicly owned airports registered, licensed or otherwise recognized by the Oregon Department of Aviation on or before December 31, 1994, that in 1994, were the base for three or more aircraft;  (3) Identifies privately owned public-use airports that:  (a) Provide important links in air traffic in this state;  (b) Provide essential safety or emergency services; or  (c) Are of economic importance to the county where the airport is located; and  (4) Establishes a process for adding an airport to or deleting an airport from the listings of airports established in OAR 738-090-0030. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 836.610 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 836.610 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AERO 1-1999, f. & cert. ef. 3-25-99
