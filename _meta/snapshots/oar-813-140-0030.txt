 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT INCENTIVE PROJECT FUND 
 813-140-0030 
 Fund Administration 
 
 (1) The Board will develop program guidelines, including specific project criteria and financing mechanisms which can be found in the Program Overview and application materials.  (2) The Department will administer the Fund in accordance with ORS 458.735. The Department's Administration of the Fund is not subject to Housing Stability Council policy, rules or standards. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 458.705 - 458.740 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 458.705 - 458.740
