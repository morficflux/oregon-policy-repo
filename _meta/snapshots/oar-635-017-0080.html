Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 17
WILLAMETTE ZONE SPORT ANGLING REGULATIONS

635-017-0080
Purpose and Scope

(1) The purpose of Division 017 is to provide for management of sport fisheries in the Willamette Zone over which the State has jurisdiction.

(2) Division 017 incorporates by reference the most current version of the annual 2026 or 2027 Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations. Therefore, persons must consult the current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations in addition to Division 011 and Division 017 to determine all applicable sport fishing requirements for the Willamette Zone.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138 & ORS 496.146
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.162
