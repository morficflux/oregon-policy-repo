 Columbia River Gorge Commission 
 Chapter 350 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 16 ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURES 
 350-016-0025 
 Appearance of Fairness 
 
 Members of the Commission shall comply with Washington’s appearance of fairness doctrine in appeals and proceedings under this rule and under Rules 350-060 et seq. and Rules 350-070 et seq. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.150 & RCW 43.97 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.150, RCW 43.97 & 16 U.S.C. 544 et seq. 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CRGC 1-2002, f. 10-16-02 cert. ef. 12-2-02
