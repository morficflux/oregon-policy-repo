 Business Services Division - Chapter 167 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 167-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 183.341, the Secretary of State adopts for use by its Business Services Division the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure as amended and effective on January 1, 2010.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the Attorney General or Business Services Division.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183
