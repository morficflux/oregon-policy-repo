 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 RULES OF GENERAL APPLICABILITY AND ORGANIZATION 
 340-011-0505 
 Contested Cases: Powers of the Director 
 
 The director, on behalf of the Commission, may execute  (1) Any written order which has been consented to in writing by the participants;  (2) Formal enforcement actions;  (3) Orders upon default; and  (4) Any other final order implementing any action taken by the Commission on any matter. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468.045 & 468.130
