 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 SUBSTITUTE CARE — TYPES OF SERVICES 
 413-080-0065 
 Substance Affected Infant 
 
 When a child on an open case is identified as a substance affected infant , the caseworker must: 

 (1) Ensure a plan of care is developed; 

 (2) Ensure the substance affected infant and family are referred to services identified in the plan of care ; and 

 (3) Document the plan of care and referrals made in the Department’s electronic information system. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005 & 42 U.S.C. 67
