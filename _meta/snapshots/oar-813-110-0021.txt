 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 OREGON AFFORDABLE HOUSING TAX CREDITS: AFFORDABLE HOUSING PROJECT CERTIFICATION 
 813-110-0021 
 Reservation in Lieu of Certification
 
 
 (1) For a reservation granted under OAR 813-110-0025: 

 (a) The reservation is valid for 240 days and is subject to extension by OHCS at its sole discretion; and 

 (b) Is a confirmed reservation unless the lending institution modifies the original letter of intent or there is a failure to comply with material terms of the reservation. 

 (2) A sponsor that furnishes OHCS a firm commitment of financing prior to the expiration of a reservation is eligible, subject to other program requirements, for issuance of a certification. 

 (3) A sponsor that has a reservation issued shall notify OHCS of any change in the lending institution as well as any failure to comply with a material term of the reservation. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 317.097 & ORS 456.515-456.725 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 317.097
