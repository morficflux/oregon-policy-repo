 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 770 PLUMBING PRODUCT APPROVALS 
 918-770-0330 
 Exempt Plumbing Products 
 
 The following plumbing products are exempt from product approval by the board:  (1) Lead roof flashings;  (2) Pipe hanger hooks; and  (3) Nail plates. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 447.154 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 447.154
