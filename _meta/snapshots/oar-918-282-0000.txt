 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 282 ELECTRICAL AND ELEVATOR LICENSING 
 918-282-0000 
 Electrical Contractors in General 
 
 (1) An electrical contractor license is a specialized license allowing a company to engage in the business of making electrical installations. This license is in addition to the licensing and bonding required by the Construction Contractors Board.  (2)(a) Generally, the contractor is required to have a full-time general supervising electrician to supervise the electrical work and sign permits; and  (b) Generally, the electrical installations are required to be made by individuals holding an appropriate electrical license.  (3) Exceptions to Sections (1) and (2). Certain statutory exemptions are in ORS 479.540. Different electrical contractor categories and requirements are in ORS 479.630 and this division of rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.730 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.730
