 State Board of Architect Examiners 
 Chapter 806 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 806-001-0004 
 Rules of Procedure
 
 
 The Board adopts the Attorney General's Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act in effect July 1, 2019, as the Board's general administrative procedural rules and to govern the Board's rulemaking and contested cases or equivalent proceedings. 

 [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Board of Architect Examiners.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341, 670.310 & 671.125 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.010 – 671.220
