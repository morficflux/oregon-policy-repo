 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 SERVICE PERMITS 
 845-009-0105 
 The Examination (Service Permittees and Service Permit Applicants)
 
 
 (1) A passing grade on the Commission-issued exam is 70 percent. 

 (2) After the service permit applicant or permittee takes the course and exam, the Commission will give him/her the examination results. 

 (3) A service permit applicant, who does not pass this exam, may retake the exam. 

 (4) If the applicant does not take and pass the exam within 45 days of the date their application was received, the Commission will deem the application incomplete under OAR 845-009-0005. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 471.030, 471.730 (1), 471.730 (5), 472.030 & 472.060 (1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 471.542 & 2024 OL Ch. 40 Sec. 15
