 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 SALE, PURCHASE OR EXCHANGE OF WILDLIFE PARTS (EXCLUDING MARINE AND COMMERCIAL FISH, SHELLFISH AND MARINE INVERTEBRATES) 
 635-200-0060 
 Fallow Deer, Reindeer, and Sika Deer 
 
 (1) Any person may sell, purchase or exchange the meat, antlers, or other parts of a fallow Deer (Dama dama), reindeer (Rangifer tarandus) or sika deer (Cervus nippon).  (2) Any person may sell, purchase or exchange the meat, antlers, or other parts of any other nonnative cervid. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 498.019, 498.022 & 498.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 498.019, 498.022 & 498.042
