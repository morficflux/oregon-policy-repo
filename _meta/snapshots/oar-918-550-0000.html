Department of Consumer and Business Services

Building Codes Division - Chapter 918

Division 550
MANUFACTURED STRUCTURES OWNERSHIP DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS

918-550-0000
Purpose and Scope

(1) OAR 918-550-0000 through 918-550-0600 establish requirements and procedures to obtain an ownership document for a manufactured structure, or to obtain an ownership document for a manufactured structure that previously did not require a title or ownership document under ORS 820.510, or 446.626 after May 1, 2005.

(2) These rules also describe the requirements for issuing a manufactured structure trip permit for the movement of a manufactured structure from one site to another.

(3) Nothing in these rules shall change any rights and liabilities of the various parties governed by the Uniform Commercial Code, ORS Chapter 79, and any applicable Oregon tax laws.

(4) These rules are intended to repeal all Department of Motor Vehicle rules, located in OAR chapter 735, to the extent that they apply to the titling and registration of manufactured structures and manufactured structure trip permits.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 446.571, 446.631 & 446.646
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 446.566, 446.571, 446.576, 446.621, 446.631 & 446.636
