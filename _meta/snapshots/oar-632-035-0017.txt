 Department of Geology and Mineral Industries 
 Chapter 632 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 OREGON MINED LAND RECLAMATION ACT — PLACER MINES, COAL MINES, AND METAL MINES USING ONLY GRAVITY SEPARATION 
 632-035-0017 
 Limited Exemptions 
 
 (1) Limited exempt status is available only for operations that have received and maintained a certificate of limit exemption as provided in ORS 517.770 and 517.775.  (2) Operations under a certificate of limited exemption are subject to the provisions in OAR 632-030-0017. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 517 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 517.770 & 517.775
