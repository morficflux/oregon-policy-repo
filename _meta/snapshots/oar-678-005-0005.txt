 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Wheat Commission - Chapter 678 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 SCREENING AND SELECTION PROCEDURES FOR PERSONAL SERVICE CONTRACTS ENTERED INTO BY THE OREGON WHEAT COMMISSION 
 678-005-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 The Oregon Wheat Commission (OWC) occasionally requires the services of an outside party to accomplish all or part of a project. The purpose of these rules is to specify the screening and selection procedures which will be used for personal service contracts. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 578 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WC 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 7-11-91
