 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 316 PERSONAL INCOME TAX GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 150-316-0239 
 Withholding on Fringe Benefits 
 
 (1) A fringe benefit is not subject to withholding for Oregon purposes if it is not subject to Oregon income tax.  (2) When a fringe benefit is subject to withholding, the rate of withholding is determined by Oregon withholding tax tables considering total income for the payroll period. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 316.162
