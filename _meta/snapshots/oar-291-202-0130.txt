 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 202 SEX OFFENDERS, SPECIAL PROVISIONS 
 291-202-0130 
 Sexually Violent Dangerous Offender Community Supervision
 
 
 The community corrections agency supervising an AIC or offender found to be a sexually violent dangerous offender shall subject the AIC or offender to intensive supervision as defined in OAR 255-005-0005(26). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 144.635, 144.637, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 144.635, 144.637, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
