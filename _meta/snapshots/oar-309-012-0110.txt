 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 ADMINISTRATIVE PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE 
 309-012-0110 
 Earned Income in Calculation of Ability-to-Pay 
 
 The Division includes earned income as income in the calculation of ability-to-pay, as described in OAR 309-012-0030. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.770 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.610 – 179.770 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 MHD 11-1985, f. & ef. 6-19-85
