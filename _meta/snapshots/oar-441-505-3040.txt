 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 505 BANKING SECTION (IN GENERAL) 
 441-505-3040 
 Real Estate Sales Contracts 
 
 Property sold under a contract of sale shall be carried as Real Estate Sold Under Contract until the contract is paid out or sold. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 706.790 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 708A.195
