 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 248 ASBESTOS REQUIREMENTS 
 340-248-0005 
 Applicability
 
 
 OAR 340-248-0010 through 340-248-0280 apply to asbestos milling, manufacturing, fabricating, abatement, and disposal, or any situation where a potential for exposure to asbestos fibers exists. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 468A.025, 468A.135 & 468A.745 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.700 - 468A.760
