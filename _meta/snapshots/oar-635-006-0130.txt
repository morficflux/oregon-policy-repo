 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 COMMERCIAL FISHERIES: GEAR, LICENSES, POUNDAGE FEES, RECORDS AND REPORTS 
 635-006-0130 
 Wholesale Fish Bait Dealer License 
 
 The sale of food fish or shellfish under a wholesale fish bait dealer’s license for any other purpose than use as bait, for scientific or educational purposes, or for live public display is prohibited. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 508 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 508
