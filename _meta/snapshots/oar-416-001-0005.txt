 Oregon Youth Authority 
 Chapter 416 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 Rule Development 
 416-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure
 
 
 Under the provisions of ORS 183.341, the Oregon Youth Authority adopts the 2024 Attorney General's Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 420A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.335 & ORS 183.341
