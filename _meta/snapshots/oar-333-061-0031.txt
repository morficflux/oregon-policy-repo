 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 61 DRINKING WATER 
 333-061-0031 
 Maximum Residual Disinfectant Levels
 
 
 MRDLs are enforceable in the same manner as maximum contaminant levels and are specified in Table 7. The MRDL for chlorine and chloramines shall be calculated as a running annual average according to OAR 333-061-0036(4)(i). The MRDL for chlorine dioxide is determined by follow-up monitoring according to OAR 333-061-0036(4)(i).
