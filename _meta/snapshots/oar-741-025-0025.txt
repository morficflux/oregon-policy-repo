 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 STANDARDS TO DETERMINE PROJECT ELIGIBILITY AND APPLICATION PROCEDURES FOR GRANTS OR LOANS FOR SHORT LINE RAILROAD RAIL INFRASTRUCTURE 
 741-025-0025 
 Application Submission Periods 
 
 (1) The Department will announce periods for submitting applications for funding from the Short Line Credit Premium Account, as funding is available.  (2) Project applications will be reviewed for compliance with the requirements in OAR 741-025-0040 and as prescribed in 741-025-0050.  (3) Projects not funded may be resubmitted during application submission periods prescribed by the Board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 367.066 - 367.067 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 367.067(2) 
 
 History:
