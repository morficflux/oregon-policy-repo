 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 SAFETY CODE FOR ELEVATORS 
 918-400-0270 
 Definitions, Cross Reference 
 
 (1) Statutory definitions for the elevator laws are in ORS 460.005.  (2) Electrical definitions are in ORS 479.530.  (3) Code definitions are in Section 1.3 of the adopted Oregon Elevator Specialty Code (ASME A17.1). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 460.085 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 460.085
