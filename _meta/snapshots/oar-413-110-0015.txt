 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 PRE-ADOPTION SERVICES 
 413-110-0015 
 Values 
 
 (1) Any child whose parent or parents are unable to provide a safe and healthy home for the child should be assured the opportunity to become a valued and permanent member of another safe family that understands and responds to the child's needs.  (2) Early identification and development of an alternative to placement with a parent increases the likelihood that the alternate permanency plan can be achieved in a timely manner. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005(1)(a) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SOSCF 18-2001, f. 6-29-01, cert. ef. 7-1-01
