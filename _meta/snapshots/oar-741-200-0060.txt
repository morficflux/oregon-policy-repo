 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 APPLICATIONS 
 741-200-0060 
 Application Alternative 
 
 Upon approval of Rail and Public Transit Division management staff, certain requirements of OAR 741-200-0050 may be waived. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 824.202 & 824.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 824.202
