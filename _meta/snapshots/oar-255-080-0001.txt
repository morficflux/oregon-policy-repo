 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 ADMINISTRATIVE APPEAL 
 255-080-0001 
 Exhaustion of Remedies 
 
 (1) A Board order is final and effective the date it is signed, however it is not final for purposes of the time period within which to appeal to the Court of Appeals until the inmate/offender exhausts his or her administrative review remedies.  (2) An inmate/offender has exhausted his or her administrative remedies after complying with OAR 255-080-0005 and 255-080-0008, and after the Board denies review, or grants review and either denies or grants relief. The Board shall notify the inmate/offender that exhaustion has occurred and the time for judicial appeal of appealable orders shall run from the mailing date of the notice. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144.335 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 144.335
