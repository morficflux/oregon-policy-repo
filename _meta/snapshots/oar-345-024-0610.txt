 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 SPECIFIC STANDARDS FOR SITING FACILITIES 
 345-024-0610 
 Modification of the Standard for Non-Base Load Power Plants 
 
 The Council may by rule modify the carbon dioxide emissions standard for non-base load power plants in OAR 345-024-0590 so that the standard remains equivalent to the standard for the net carbon dioxide emissions rate of a base load gas plant, subject to the principles described in OAR 345-024-0510. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 & 469.503 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.501 & 469.503
