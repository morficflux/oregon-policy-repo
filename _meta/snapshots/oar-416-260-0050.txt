 Oregon Youth Authority 
 Chapter 416 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 260 Youth Welfare and Trust Accounts 
 416-260-0050 
 Financial Accounting
 
 
 OYA must complete financial accountings as required for legal proceedings, audits, and when a maintenance account balance exists and the youth in custody's OYA custody has been terminated. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 420A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 420A.030 & 420A.032
