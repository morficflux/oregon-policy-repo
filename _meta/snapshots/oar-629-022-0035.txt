 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 FOREST RESOURCE TRUST 
 629-022-0035 
 Applicability 
 
 (1) Provisions in OAR 629-022-0030 through 629-022-0120 apply to both the loan and cost share programs of the Forest Resource Trust.  (2) Provisions in OAR 629-022-00130 through 629-022-0410 apply only to the loan program of the Forest Resource Trust.  (3) Provisions in OAR 629-022-0800 through 629-022-0850 apply only to the cost share program of the Forest Resource Trust. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.700 - 526.730 & 526.745 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 526.695 - 526.775 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOF 1-2009, f. 1-12-09, cert. ef 2-1-09
