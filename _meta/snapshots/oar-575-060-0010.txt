 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 PRIVATELY FUNDED AWARD PROGRAMS 
 575-060-0010 
 Eligibility 
 
 Unless otherwise specified by a donor, an applicant for an award must:  (1) Be a resident of the State of Oregon; and  (2) Be a U.S. citizen or be in the United States for other than a temporary purpose and intend to become a permanent resident of the United States. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.520 - 348.530 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.520 - 348.530
