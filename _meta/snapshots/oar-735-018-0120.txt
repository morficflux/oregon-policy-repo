 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 ACCEPTANCE AND VERIFICATION OF INFORMATION SUBMITTED BY ELECTRONIC MEANS 
 735-018-0120 
 Notification of Transfer or Sale of Vehicle by Electronic Means 
 
 Notice required under ORS 803.112 and 803.117 that a person has transferred interest in a vehicle covered by an Oregon title may be submitted to DMV by means of an electronic transaction through DMV’s website. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 814.616, 184.619, 802.012, 803.112 & 803.112 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 802.012, 803.112 & 803.117
