 Mental Health Regulatory Agency 
 Oregon Board of Licensed Professional Counselors and Therapists - Chapter 833 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL 
 833-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure
 
 
 The Board adopts the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure, OAR Chapter 137, Divisions 1 through 5, as its rules of procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.785, ORS 36.224, ORS 183.341 & ORS 183.502 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
