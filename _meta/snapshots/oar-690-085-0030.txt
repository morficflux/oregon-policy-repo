 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 ANNUAL REPORTS AND SERIOUS WATER MANAGEMENT PROBLEM AREAS 
 690-085-0030 
 Failure to Report 
 
 (1) The Director may order regulation or termination of deliveries of water to and use of water by any water right holder who fails to satisfactorily install or maintain a measuring device or to submit a report required by OAR 690-085-0020. Notice of intended regulation shall be served on the person by certified mail, return receipt requested. Regulation shall become effective not earlier than 30 days after the date of mailing of the notice. Regulation shall not become effective if the person satisfactorily complies within 30 days from the date of mailing of the notice or if the person is granted an extension of time under section (2) of this rule.  (2) A water right holder required under OAR 690-085-0010 or 690-085-0020 to submit an annual water use report may request in writing a 60-day extension of time to submit the report. The Director may grant the extension upon finding that the additional time is needed by the person or government entity to compile and submit accurate and complete information. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 537 & 540 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 13-1988, f. & cert. ef. 8-10-88
