 Oregon Board of Dentistry 
 Chapter 818 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 EXAMINATION AND LICENSING 
 818-021-0095 
 Resignation of License
 
 
 (1) The Board may allow a licensee  who no longer practices in Oregon to resign their license, unless the Board determines the license should be revoked. 

 (2) Licenses that are resigned under this rule may not be reinstated. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 679 & 680 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 679.090, 679.120, 680.072 & 680.075
