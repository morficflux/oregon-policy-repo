 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 STRATEGIC RESERVE FUND 
 123-090-0060 
 Waivers 
 
 The Director may waive non-statutory requirements of this division of administrative rules if the project demonstrates a contribution to state job strategies consistent with the purpose of the Strategic Reserve Fund. Projects that improve the economic condition of a Rural or Distressed Area, or further global competitiveness of Oregon firms may receive special consideration by the Director. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075(5) & 285A.110 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285B.266
