 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 PATIENT TRANSFERS 
 309-120-0230 
 Investigation
 
 
 (1) The adult in custody may request that an investigation be conducted. If an investigation is ordered, a designee of the hearings officer shall conduct the investigation. No person shall serve as an investigator who has participated in any previous way in the process. 

 (2) An investigation shall be conducted upon the adult in custody’s request, if an investigation will assist in the resolution of the proceedings and the information sought is within the ability of the facility to procure or the adult in custody to provide with his or her own resources. 

 (3) The hearings officer may order an investigation on his or her own motion. 

 (4) The hearings officer shall allow the adult in custody access to the results of the investigation unless disclosure of the investigative results would constitute a threat to the safety and security of the facility, its staff or others, or to the orderly operation of the facility. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 179.473, 179.479 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.471-179.486 & 179.495-179.508
