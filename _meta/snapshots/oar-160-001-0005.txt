 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 160-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Model Rules of Procedure as promulgated by the Attorney General of the State of Oregon under the Administrative Procedures Act effective January 1, 2010, are adopted as the rules of procedure for the Corporation Division of the Secretary of State's office.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Corporation Division.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
