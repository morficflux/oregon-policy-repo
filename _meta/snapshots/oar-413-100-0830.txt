 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 SUBSTITUTE CARE — FUNDING ELIGIBILITY 
 413-100-0830 
 Paternity and Parentage Establishment 
 
 The Department will refer substitute care cases to DCS for establishment of parentage under any of the following circumstances:  (1) Only one parent is listed on the child’s birth record; or  (2) The Department has not begun the parentage establishment process through genetic testing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  PL93.647, ORS 25.010 - 120 & 180.320 - 370
