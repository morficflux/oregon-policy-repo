 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 118 INHERITANCE TAX 
 150-118-0190 
 Application for Determination of Estate Tax and Discharge from Personal Liability 
 
 (1) The executor may apply to the department for a determination of tax due and discharge from personal liability of estate tax.  (2) The written application must include the following information:  (a) The name and date of death of the decedent;  (b) The decedent's Social Security Number;  (c) If the executor applies before filing the estate tax return, a copy of the decedent’s will, the decedent’s trust, or other document indicating the person is authorized to act on behalf of the estate.  (3) The discharge does not apply to tax liability resulting from assets of the decedent's estate that are still in the possession or control of the executor. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 118.265
