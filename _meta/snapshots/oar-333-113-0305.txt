 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 113 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR WIRELINE SERVICE OPERATIONS AND SUB-SURFACE TRACER STUDIES 
 333-113-0305 
 Precautionary Procedures in Logging and Subsurface Tracer Studies: Handling Tools 
 
 The licensee must provide and require the use of tools that will assure remote handling of sealed sources other than low activity calibration sources. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
