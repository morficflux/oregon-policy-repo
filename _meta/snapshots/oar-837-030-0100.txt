 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 LIQUEFIED PETROLEUM GAS 
 837-030-0100 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 (1) The purpose of these rules is to implement the standards, policies and procedures for liquefied petroleum gas for the protection and safety of the public and persons using or handling liquefied petroleum gas, pursuant to the authority vested in the State Fire Marshal by ORS 476.030 and 480.320.  (2) The scope of these rules apply to the implementation of the statutes in ORS 480.410 through 480.460 relating to liquefied petroleum gas.  (3) These rules establish application, examination, reporting inspection criteria, and other requirements for liquefied petroleum gas. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476 & 480 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.410 - 480.460
