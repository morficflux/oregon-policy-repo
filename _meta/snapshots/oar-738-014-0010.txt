 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 THROUGH THE FENCE PILOT PROGRAM 
 738-014-0010 
 Through the Fence Pilot Program: Purpose and Policy 
 
 OAR 738-014-0010 through 738-014-0060 implement ORS 836.640 and 836.642 (Or Laws 2005, ch 820). The policy of the State of Oregon is to encourage and support the continued operation and vitality of Oregon’s airports. These rules establish a pilot program at up to three rural airports to encourage development of through the fence operations designed to promote economic development by creating family wage jobs, by increasing local tax bases and by increasing financial support for rural airports. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 835.035, 836.642, sec. 4, ch. 820 & OL 2005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 836.640, 836.642 & ch. 820 & OL 2005.
