 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 124 STANDARDS APPLICABLE TO DRY CLEANING FACILITIES AND DRY STORES 
 340-124-0015 
 Applicability 
 
 These rules are applicable to:  (1) Persons operating a dry cleaning facility or dry store.  (2) Persons delivering perchloroethylene to a dry cleaning facility in Oregon.  (3) Persons who own real property underlying active and inactive dry cleaning facilities.  (4) Persons generating hazardous waste at a dry cleaning facility. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 465.505(5) & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 465.200 & 465.500 - 465.548
