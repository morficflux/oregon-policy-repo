 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 69 INVESTOR OWNED UTILITY INSTITUTIONAL BUILDINGS PROGRAM 
 330-069-0035 
 Payment Procedures 
 
 (1) ODOE shall approve TAS and ECM payments according to OAR 330-069-0025 and 330-069-0085. ODOE shall request the funding IOU to make payments.  (2) All payout of program funds shall be by the IOU as per ODOE’s approved requests. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOE 5-1985, f. & ef. 6-17-85
