 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 RATES AND RATEMAKING 
 836-042-0302 
 Definitions 
 
 As used in OAR 836-042-0300 to 836-042-0322 unless the context requires otherwise:  (1) “Mass marketing plan” means a method of selling property-casualty personal lines insurance to members of a particular association which has agreed to or otherwise endorsed the sale of the insurance to its members.  (2) “Personal lines” means property and casualty insurance policies for personal, family or household purposes, and not for commercial or business purposes.  (3) “Property-casualty insurance” means insurance to which ORS 731.158 and 731.182 apply. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & 737.600 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 737.600
