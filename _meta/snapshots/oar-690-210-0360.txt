 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 210 WELL CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS 
 690-210-0360 
 Movement of Casing after Cement Grouting 
 
 In no case shall the permanent well casing be moved or driven following the placement and initial set of the cement grout. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 
 
 
 History:
