 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 CASE MANAGEMENT SERVICES FOR OLDER ADULTS AND ADULTS WITH DISABILITIES 
 411-028-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 (1) The rules in OAR chapter 411, division 028 ensure case management services support the independence, empowerment, dignity, and human potential of older adult individuals and adult individuals with disabilities with the purpose of helping the individuals reside in their own home or in a community-based setting.  (2) Case management services are a component of an individual's comprehensive, person-centered plan for services. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.070
