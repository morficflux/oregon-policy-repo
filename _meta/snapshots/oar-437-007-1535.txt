 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 FOREST ACTIVITIES 
 437-007-1535 
 Storage and Transportation of Climbing Equipment General Requirements 
 
 Do not store or transport climbing equipment:  (1) Near extreme heat sources.  (2) In contact with gas, oil, chemicals, chemical vapors, battery and other acids.  (3) In contact with sharp or pointed objects and other items that can damage the climbing gear. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295 
 
 History:
