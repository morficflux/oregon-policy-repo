 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE — ARTICLE 9 
 160-040-0308 
 Assignment of Powers of Secured Party of Record 
 
 (1) Status of the parties. An assignment shall have no effect on the status of the parties to the financing statement, except that each assignee named in the assignment shall become a secured party of record.  (2) Status of financing statement. An assignment shall have no effect upon the status of the financing statement. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 79.0526 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 79.0514
