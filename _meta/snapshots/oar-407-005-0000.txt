 Department of Human Services 
 Chapter 407 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 CLIENT RIGHTS 
 407-005-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules (407-005-0000 through 407-005-0030) establish a Department policy of non-discrimination on the basis of disability in accordance with the Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 (ADA) and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.050 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DHSD 1-2006, f. & cert. ef. 3-1-06
