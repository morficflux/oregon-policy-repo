 Economic Recovery Review Council 
 Chapter 966 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 REGIONALLY SIGNIFICANT INDUSTRIAL AREAS 
 966-100-0700 
 North Coast Business Park 
 
 (1) The North Coast Business Park Industrial Area was approved by the ERRC as a designated RSIA on January 28, 2015.  (2) The North Coast Business Park Industrial Area located in the City of Warrenton, consists of 162 acres and is comprised of the following parcel acres: The 2 parcels at the designated area are as follows:  (a) Tax Lot — 206.  (A) Acres — 10.5.  (B) Address —Township: 8, Range: 10, Section: 27, Tax Lot: 206.  (b) Tax Lot — 207.  (A) Acres — 151.5.  (B) Address —Township: 8, Range: 10, Section: 27, Tax Lot: 207. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.723 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.723
