 Real Estate Agency 
 Chapter 863 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 27 INVESTIGATIONS AND DISCIPLINE 
 863-027-0005 
 Definitions 
 
 As used in this division, unless the context requires otherwise, the following definitions apply:  (1) "Agency" is defined in ORS 696.010.  (2) "Commissioner" is defined in ORS 696.010. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 696.385 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 696.010
