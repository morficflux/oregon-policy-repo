 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 380 WATER RIGHT TRANSFERS 
 690-380-5110 
 Original Right Terminated 
 
 Approval of a change in use or place of use terminates the right to use water for the existing character of use or place of use under the original water right as described in the transfer application form required under OAR 690-380-3000. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025 & 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 540.510 - 540.532
