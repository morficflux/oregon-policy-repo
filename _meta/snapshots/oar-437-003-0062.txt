 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 CONSTRUCTION 
 437-003-0062 
 Lead Respiratory Protection Program 
 
 The employer must implement a respiratory protection program in accordance with Division 2/I, 1910.134(b) through (d) (except (d)(1)(iii)), and (e) through (m) and (o), which covers each employee required by Division 3/D, 1926.62 Lead, to use a respirator.  NOTE: This is in addition to other respiratory protection and medical suveillance requirements specified in these Lead rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295 
 
 History:
