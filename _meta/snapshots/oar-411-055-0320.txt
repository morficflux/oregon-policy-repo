 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 INTENSIVE INTERVENTION COMMUNITIES (IIC) 
 411-055-0320 
 Service Provider Requirements 
 
 The service provider shall be a Residential Care Facility that must: 

 (1) Have a history of an unencumbered license from the Department and meet the requirements of OAR 411-054-0016. 

 (2) Meet all Home and Community-Based Service requirements set forth under OAR chapter 411, division 4. 

 (3) Meet the staffing requirements described in OAR 411-055-0330. 

 (4) Comply with all the rules in OAR chapter 411, division 54. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, 410.070, 443.450 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.050, 410.070, 443.400 - 443.455, 443.991 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 APD 20-2019, adopt filed 06/26/2019, effective 06/28/2019
