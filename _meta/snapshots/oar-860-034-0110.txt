 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 34 SMALL TELECOMMUNICATIONS UTILITIES AND COOPERATIVES 
 860-034-0110 
 Due and Payable Period 
 
 (1) Each small telecommunications utility shall establish procedures to ensure that the period from the billing transmittal for all current charges, including payment of final bills, to the due date, is not less than 15 days.  (2) If the bill is delivered by US mail, the due and payable period begins the day after the US Postal Service postmark or the day after the date of postage metering. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756 & 759 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 759.045
