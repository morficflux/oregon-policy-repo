 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 STEERING AND SAILING RULES 
 250-011-0170 
 Law Enforcement Vessels 
 
 (1) Law enforcement vessels may display a flashing blue light when engaged in direct law enforcement or public safety activities. This light must be located so that it does not interfere with the visibility of the vessel's navigation lights.  (2) The blue light described in this section may be displayed only by law enforcement vessels of the United States, Oregon and its political subdivisions. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830.110 & ORS 830.250 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.035
