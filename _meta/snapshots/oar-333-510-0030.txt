 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 510 PATIENT CARE AND NURSING SERVICES IN HOSPITALS 
 333-510-0030 
 Nursing Services 
 
 (1) The hospital shall provide a nursing service department, which provides 24-hour onsite registered nursing care, 7 days per week.  (2) The nursing services department shall be under the direction of a nurse executive who is a registered nurse, licensed to practice in Oregon.  (3) All nursing personnel shall maintain current certification in cardiopulmonary resuscitation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & 441.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 441.160 - 441.192
