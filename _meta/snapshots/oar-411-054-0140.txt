 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 54 RESIDENTIAL CARE AND ASSISTED LIVING FACILITIES 
 411-054-0140 
 Additional Authority 
 
 SPD may commence a suit in equity to enjoin operation of a facility when:  (1) A facility is operated without a valid license; or  (2) Notice of revocation has been given and a reasonable time has been allowed for placement of individuals in other facilities. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 & 443.450 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 443.400 - 443.455 & 443.991 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SPD 14-2007, f. 8-31-07, cert. ef. 11-1-07
