 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 205 TRAUMA SYSTEM HOSPITAL DESIGNATION IN TRAUMA AREA # 1 
 333-205-0050 
 Hospital Designation Criteria
 
 
 (1) The Oregon Health Authority (Authority) shall utilize criteria as set forth in OAR 333-200-0090(5) and may, in addition, utilize the following criteria for selecting trauma system hospitals: 

 (a) Locations of major trauma incidents; and 

 (b) Geographical barriers which impede air or ground transportation. 

 (2) The Authority shall consider the information contained in Resources for Optimal Care of the Injured Patient 2022 Standards, Revised December 2023; Verification, Review and Consultation Program, American College of Surgeons when interpreting the standards for the purpose of designating trauma system hospitals. This publication is not adopted as part of these rules. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431A.060, ORS 431A.065 & ORS 431A.090
