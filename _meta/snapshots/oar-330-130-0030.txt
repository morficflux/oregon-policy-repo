 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 130 ENERGY EFFICIENT DESIGN AND OPERATION FOR STATE AGENCY FACILITIES 
 330-130-0030 
 Notification 
 
 When the building class has been determined during the pre-design or programming phase of a building project, the following procedures shall be followed:  (1) Class 1 Buildings. The agency shall notify the department of a building project prior to the release of the Request for Proposals (RFP) for contracting/design services. The agency contact shall coordinate with the department to set-up the initial meeting early in the pre-design or programming phase of a building project.  (2) Class 2 Buildings. The agency shall notify the department and may request a list of recommended ECMs and services applicable to the building. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 276.900 - 276.915 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469
