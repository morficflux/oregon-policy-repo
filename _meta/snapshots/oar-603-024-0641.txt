 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS OF IDENTITY, LABELING AND OTHER REGULATIONS RELATING TO FLUID MILK AND DAIRY PRODUCTS 
 603-024-0641 
 Transportation of Milk and Milk Products and Surroundings 
 
 All producer Grade A raw milk for pasteurization must be picked up at least once each 72 hour period. In addition, to facilitate cleaning of the farm bulk tank, the tank shall be completely emptied once each 72 hour period. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 621 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 621.060 & 621.261
