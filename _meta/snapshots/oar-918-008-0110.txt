 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 DIVISION CODE DEVELOPMENT RULES, IN GENERAL 
 918-008-0110 
 Enforcement 
 
 All jurisdictions administering and enforcing the state building code must enforce statewide code interpretations and allow the use of alternate method rulings consistent with the original scope of the ruling. Failure to enforce statewide code interpretations or allow statewide alternate method rulings may subject building officials, plans examiners, and inspectors to revocation or suspension of certifications. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.144, 455.148, 455.150 & 455.740 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.148, 455.150 & 455.740
