 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 210 WELL CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS 
 690-210-0220 
 Plastic Casing Joints
 
 
 All plastic casing joints shall be watertight. Either "bell" type, threaded, or coupling hubs are approved. Hub couplings shall be of material meeting the specifications for plastic casings as set forth in OAR 690-210-0210. Joints shall be made by solvent cement in accordance with manufacturer's directions. Newly assembled joints require careful handling until the initial set has taken place, which varies with the temperature and the pipe size. The recommended initial set times are from manufacturer's recommendations (See Table 210-4).
