 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 76 CRIME VICTIMS’ COMPENSATION 
 137-076-0040 
 Payment of Benefits
 
 
 No compensation will be paid to an applicant until expenses are accrued, treatment has been completed and all required documentation is received by the Department. Pursuant to ORS 147.125(1)(c) benefits, payments or awards received by the victim, or survivors or dependents of the victim, from any source are considered a p rior resource. A prior resource may include but is not limited to: private or public health insurance, automobile insurance, workers’ compensation, disability insurance, homeowner’s insurance, Paid Leave Oregon or equivalent plan, unemployment benefits, restitution, civil settlements, and tribal per capita payments. Indian Health Services insurance is not considered a prior resource. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 147.205(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 147.035
