 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 RETAILER CONTRACT 
 177-040-0310 
 Alternative Dispute Resolution Policy 
 
 The Lottery encourages the use of an alternative dispute resolution process, such as mediation, in disputes arising between a retailer and the Lottery, when such process is appropriate and provides for a more efficient and effective dispute resolution. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 461 & OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & HB 2237 (2005) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.300 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LOTT 3-2006, f. 2-16-06, cert. ef. 3-1-06
