 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 BINGO/RAFFLES/MONTE CARLO 
 137-025-0455 
 Monte Carlo Event Prizes 
 
 (1) No cash prize shall be offered or awarded. Once purchased, imitation money cannot be redeemed for cash or cash equivalent.  (2) No prize shall be offered or awarded with a retail market value in excess of $50,000 and the retail market value of prizes offered or awarded to Monte Carlo players shall not exceed $100,000 per event. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 914 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  HB 3009 & 1997
