 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 CONTINUING EDUCATION PROVIDERS 
 918-035-0080 
 Recording Continuing Education Credits 
 
 (1) Providers must submit a list of course attendees in a method specified by the division.  (2) Providers must document course completion and give proof of course completion to each attendee.  (3) Providers shall retain records of attendees for each course for at least 5 years from the date of the course. Providers shall provide a copy of the record to the division at the division’s request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.117 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.117
