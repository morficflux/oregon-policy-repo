 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-1270 
 Angler Access 
 
 (1) The following operating principles apply to the Main Stem Willamette Subbasin:  (a) The Department shall seek to provide access for boat and bank angling that will satisfy public need for a variety of angling opportunities and a dispersion of angling effort along the Main Stem Willamette River;  (b) Acquisition and development of angler access sites shall be consistent with guidelines and objectives for management of fish species and habitat.  (2) In accordance with these operating principles, it is the objective of the Department to:  (a) Provide and maintain 49 permanent boat access sites on the Main Stem Willamette River;  (b) Increase bank angling access along the Willamette River. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720
