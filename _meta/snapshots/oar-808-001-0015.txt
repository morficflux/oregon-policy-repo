 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 ADMINISTRATION 
 808-001-0015 
 Election of Officers 
 
 The Board may elect a Board Chairman and Vice Chairman at the first board meeting held after January 1 of each year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 671 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 670.300 & 671.630 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LC 1-1984, f. & ef. 7-17-84
