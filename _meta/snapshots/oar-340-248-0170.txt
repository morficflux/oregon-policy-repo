 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 248 ASBESTOS REQUIREMENTS 
 340-248-0170 
 Licensing and Certification Requirements: Reciprocity
 
 
 DEQ may develop reciprocity agreements with other jurisdictions regarding all activities under this division. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.745
