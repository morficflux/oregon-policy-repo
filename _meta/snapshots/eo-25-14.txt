    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25-14


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    HIGHLAND FIRE IN CROOK COUNTY


    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:


   The fire known as the “Highland Fire” is burning in Crook County.


   The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Highland Fire are
   beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
   was requested by the Crook County Fire Defense Board chief and the State Fire
    Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Highland Fire in Crook
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 7:00 pm on July 12, 2025, with the Department of the
    State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Blue Incident Management
    Team will be entering into unified command with the Central Oregon Fire
    Management Service Type 3 Incident Management Team. The Oregon State Fire
    Marshal Blue Incident Management Team will be briefed at 9:30 am on July 13,
    2025, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    L      The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
           statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
           the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
           Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding
           to the Highland Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

    2,     This conflagration is declared only for the Highland Fire threatening
           structures in Crook County.


    3,     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 7:00 pm on the 12" day of
           July 2025 and signed this 14!" day of July 2025, in Salem, Oregon.
