 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 660-001-0007 
 Request for Stay — Agency Determination 
 
 Except as provided in OAR 660-001-0005(1) with regard to the number of calendar days allowed to act on a stay request, agency determinations concerning a request for a stay will be processed under OAR 137-003-0090 to 137-003-0092. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.040 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 195, 196, 197 & OAR Ch. 137
