 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 106 X-RAYS IN THE HEALING ARTS 
 333-106-0230 
 Fluoroscopic X-ray Systems Requirements: Fluoroscopic Timer 
 
 (1) Means shall be provided to present the cumulative on-time of the fluoroscopic tube.  (2) The maximum cumulative time of the timing device shall not exceed five minutes without resetting.  (3) A signal audible to the fluoroscopist, or the appropriate operator, shall indicate the completion of any preset cumulative on-time; or if no audible signal is provided, the exposure shall terminate. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.752 - 453.775 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.752 - 453.775
