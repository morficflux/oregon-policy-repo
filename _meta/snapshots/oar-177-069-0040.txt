 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 69 LOTTERY RAFFLE GAME RULES 
 177-069-0040 
 Ticket Validation Requirements 
 
 (1) General: For a Lottery raffle ticket to be valid and eligible to receive prize payment, the ticket must be validated in accordance with the provisions of OAR chapter 177, including but not limited to 177-070-0035. A Lottery raffle ticket may be validated only after the winning raffle ticket numbers for a Lottery raffle drawing become official as provided in 177-069-0030(2).  (2) Claiming a Prize: A Lottery raffle ticket is the only proof of a game play and the submission of a winning Lottery raffle ticket to the Lottery is the sole method of claiming a prize. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.210, 461.220, 461.240, 461.250 & 461.260
