 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 311 MISCELLANEOUS ELECTRICAL RULES 
 918-311-0020 
 Time Limit for Electrical Plan Review 
 
 (1) Electrical plan reviews shall be approved or denied within ten working days from receipt of the complete electrical plans by the jurisdiction requiring plan review. A reviewed set of plans shall be returned to the plan applicant:  (a) If a plan is rejected, the deficiencies must be set out in writing. Upon resubmission of the necessary items, the ten-day requirement starts again;  (b) If, because of the complexity or size of the installation, additional time beyond the ten working days is required to complete the review, the jurisdiction shall notify the person taking out the electrical permit of the reasons for the delay and the date the review will be completed.  (2) Phased permits may be issued as described in OAR 918-309-0025. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.870 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.870
