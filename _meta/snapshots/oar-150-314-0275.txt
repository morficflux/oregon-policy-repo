 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 314 INCOME TAXATION GENERALLY  GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 150-314-0275 
 Definition: Collection Charge 
 
 As used in ORS 314.430(1) “collection charge” includes:  (1) The fees and costs listed in ORS 18.999(4), related to recovery of expenses incurred in enforcing judgments, and  (2) All fees or charges of the Secretary of State imposed under ORS 305.184 incurred in filing, releasing, cancelling, or satisfying a warrant filed with the Secretary of State under ORS 305.182. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 314.430
