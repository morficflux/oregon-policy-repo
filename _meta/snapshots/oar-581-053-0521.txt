 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 53 SCHOOL BUS DRIVER TRAINING, LICENSING, AND SCHOOL BUS STANDARDS 
 581-053-0521 
 Type 21 Driver Training and Certification 
 
 (1) No person shall transport pupils in a type 21 activity vehicle unless such person has completed all requirements for a School Bus Permit or Certificate as outlined in OAR 581-053-0220, except that a restricted “Type 21 Only” School Bus Permit or Certificate will be issued to a person who does not have the proper CDL for, or does not test in, a school bus.  (2) A person may drive a type 21 activity vehicle as a temporary driver as listed in OAR 581-053-0220, except that a restricted “Type 21 Only” temporary driver may be approved. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 327.013 & 820.100 - 820.120 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 327.013, 820.100, 820.105, 820.110 & 820.120 
 
 History:
