 Department of Transportation 
 Chapter 731 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 PUBLIC CONTRACT RULES; HIGHWAY AND BRIDGE PROJECTS 
 731-005-0700 
 Negotiation with Bidders
 
 
 ODOT shall not negotiate with any Bidder prior to award of Contract. After award of the Contract, ODOT and Contractor may only modify the Contract as specified in the Contract. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 279A.050 & 279A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279C.300, 279C.305, 279C.335, 279C.365 & 279C.375
