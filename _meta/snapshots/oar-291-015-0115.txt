 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 VOLUNTEER SERVICES/STUDENT INTERNS 
 291-015-0115 
 Recruitment 
 
 (1) Prospective volunteers who best meet program needs will be recruited from all ethnic, cultural, gender and economic segments of the community.  (2) Recruitment will be based on the needs of inmates, offenders, functional units, and the availability of staff to supervise volunteers. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
