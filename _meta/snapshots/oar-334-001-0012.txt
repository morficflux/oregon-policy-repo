 Board of Massage Therapists 
 Chapter 334 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 334-001-0012 
 Budget
 
 
 The Oregon Board of Massage Therapists hereby adopts, and fully incorporates herein, the Oregon Board of Massage Therapists' 2025-2027 Biennium budget of $2,967,500.00. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 182.462 & ORS 687.121 (10) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 182.462
