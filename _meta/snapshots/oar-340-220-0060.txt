 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 220 OREGON TITLE V OPERATING PERMIT FEES 
 340-220-0060 
 Pollutants Subject to Emission Fees 
 
 (1) DEQ will assess emission fees on emissions of regulated pollutants up to and including 7,000 tons per year of all regulated pollutants for each source each calendar year thereafter.  (2) The owner or operator must pay emission fees for all regulated pollutants emitted from the source, except as limited in section (1). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 468.065, 468A.310 & 468A.315 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468.065 & 468A.315
