 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 APPRENTICESHIP AND TRAINING COUNCIL 
 839-011-0405 
 Youth Apprenticeship — Supervision 
 
 All youth apprentices shall be under direct line of sight supervision of a journey person while engaged in on-the-job training within hazardous occupations as defined by OAR 839-021-0104. Youth apprentices shall be under direct supervision at all other times to ensure optimal safety while on the job. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 344.750 & 660.120 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 660.120
