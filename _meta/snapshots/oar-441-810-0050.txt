 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 810 GENERAL 
 441-810-0050 
 Update Filings 
 
 At least ten days prior to a change of ownership, business address, business manager, or change in information relating to the trust account, the collection agency business shall notify the Director of the changes by filing the appropriate forms through the Nationwide Multistate Licensing System. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 697.085 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 697.031(3)
