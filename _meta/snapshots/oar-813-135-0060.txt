 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 135 LOCAL INNOVATION AND FAST TRACK (LIFT) 
 813-135-0060 
 Strategies for Serving Underserved Communities
 
 
 In order to reach historically underserved communities, all Applicants must include detailed information on how the project will provide service to communities of color, as instructed in the LIFT Manuals (See OAR 813-135-0025 and 813-135-0026). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 458.485 – 458.490 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.559(1)(f)
