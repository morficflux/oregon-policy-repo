 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 309 EQUALIZATION OF PROPERTY TAXES 
 150-309-0260 
 Administration of Appeals Process
 
 
 (1) The Property Value Appeals Board clerk shall summarize orders on a form provided by the Department of Revenue. 

 (2) The completed form shall be sent to the Department of Revenue within 45 days after adjournment. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 309.360
