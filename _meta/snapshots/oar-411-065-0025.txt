 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 SPECIALIZED LIVING SERVICES CONTRACTS 
 411-065-0025 
 Staffing Requirements 
 
 (1) The provider must have staff of sufficient number and qualifications to meet the specialized needs of the target group.  (2) The number and quality of staff will be specified by the Department contract at the time of approval of the contract. At least one staff person must be in attendance at all times when a resident is on the premises. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.070
