 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 736-001-0000 
 Notice of Proposed Rules 
 
 Prior to the adoption, amendment or repeal of any permanent rule, the Oregon Parks and Recreation Commission shall give notice of the intended action:  (1) In the Secretary of State's Bulletin referred to in ORS 183.360 at least 21 days before the effective date of the rule;  (2) By mailing a copy of the notice to persons on the Oregon Parks and Recreation Commission's mailing list established pursuant to ORS 183.335(8) at least 28 days before the effective date of the rule; and  (3) By mailing or furnishing a copy of the notice to:  (a) The Associated Press;  (b) Associated Oregon Industries; and  (c) Associated General Contractors. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341(4)
