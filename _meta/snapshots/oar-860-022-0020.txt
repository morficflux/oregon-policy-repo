 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 RATES 
 860-022-0020 
 Applications to Make Tariffs or Rate Schedules Effective on Less Than Statutory Notice 
 
 An energy or large telecommunications utility seeking authority to make tariffs or rate schedules effective on less than statutory notice must use application forms approved by the Commission.  [ED. NOTE: Forms referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 757.220 & 759.190
