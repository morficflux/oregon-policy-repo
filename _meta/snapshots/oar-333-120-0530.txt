 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION 
 333-120-0530 
 Waste Disposal: Treatment of Disposal by Incineration 
 
 A licensee may treat or dispose of licensed material by incineration only in the amounts and forms specified in OAR 333-120-0540 or as specifically approved by the Authority pursuant to OAR 333-120-0510. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
