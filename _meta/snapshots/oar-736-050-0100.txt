 Division 50 HISTORIC PRESERVATION OFFICER 
 736-050-0100 
 Special Assessment of Historic Property
 
 
 ORS 358.480 to 358.545 provides the process for applying for a special assessment of historic property. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 358.545 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 358.487
