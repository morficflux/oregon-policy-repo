 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD ADMINISTRATIVE MEETINGS 
 859-040-0020 
 Records 
 
 Written minutes will be kept of Board action taken at administrative meetings and adopted at the subsequent administrative meeting. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.345 & 161.346
