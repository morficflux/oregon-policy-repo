 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 COLUMBIA RIVER SYSTEM TREATY INDIAN FISHERIES 
 635-041-0072 
 Carp, Shad and Other Nongame Fish 
 
 It is unlawful to fish for or possess carp and other nongame fish from the Columbia River for commercial purposes except:  (1) As authorized by ORS 508.106 (Permit to take carp or other foodfish).  (2) During open commercial fishing periods with gear authorized for those open commercial fishing periods. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.325 & 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.129 & 507.030
