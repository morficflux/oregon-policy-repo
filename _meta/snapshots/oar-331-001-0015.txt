 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 331-001-0015 
 Applicability of Office Rules 
 
 The provisions of OAR 331-001-0000 through 331-030-0040 shall apply in the administration and regulation of all programs administered by the Office pursuant to ORS 676.565 and ORS 681.700 to 676.730 except as otherwise provided. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.615, ORS 681.700-676.730 & ORS 676.565 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.615, ORS 681.700-676.730 & ORS 676.565 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 HLO 71-2018, adopt filed 04/30/2018, effective 05/01/2018
