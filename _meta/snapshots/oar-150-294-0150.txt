 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 294 LOCAL BUDGET 
 150-294-0150 
 Calculation of Interest on Late Payments 
 
 The interest rate calculation must be carried out a minimum of seven places to the right of the decimal point and rounded back to six places to the right of the decimal point. When rounding back to the sixth place, all numbers five and above increase the sixth place by one; all numbers four or less have no effect on the sixth place number. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 294.187
