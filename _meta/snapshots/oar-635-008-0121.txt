 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 LANDS: DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE LANDS 
 635-008-0121 
 Little Sheep Wildlife Area (Wallowa County)
 
 
 The Little Sheep Wildlife Area is open to wildlife-oriented public use unless otherwise excluded or restricted. 

 (1) Discharging of firearms is prohibited except while hunting during authorized game bird and game mammal seasons. 

 (2) Camping is prohibited. 

 (3) Open fires are prohibited. 

 (4) The wildlife area is closed to the public between 10 pm and 4 am. 

 (5) Domestic sheep and goats are prohibited. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
