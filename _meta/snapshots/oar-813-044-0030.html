Oregon Housing and Community Services Department

Chapter 813

Division 44
HOME OWNERSHIP ASSISTANCE PROGRAM OF THE OREGON HOUSING FUND

813-044-0030
Distribution of Funds

The maximum amount of an award to an organization under the Home Ownership Assistance Program is subject to determination by the Housing Stability Council.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 456.555
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 458.620 & 458.655
