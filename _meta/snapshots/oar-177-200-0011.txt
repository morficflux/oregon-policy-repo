 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 GENERAL VIDEO LOTTERYSM GAME RULES 
 177-200-0011 
 Accuracy of Wagers 
 
 It is the sole responsibility of a player to verify the accuracy of a wager placed on a Video LotterySM game terminal by the player. The Lottery is not responsible for any wager placed in error. The Lottery will not cancel wagers or provide refunds. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.215 & 461.217
