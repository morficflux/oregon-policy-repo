 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 FURBEARING AND UNPROTECTED MAMMAL REGULATIONS 
 635-050-0047 
 Set-Back Restrictions 
 
 On state or federal lands, except when authorized by the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife, no traps or snares may be set on land:  (1) Within 50 feet of any public trail;  (2) Within 300 feet of any trailhead that is designated and maintained as such by the public land management agency and is accessible to vehicular traffic;  (3) Within 300 feet of any public campground or picnic area designated and maintained as such by the public land management agency on the most current official map of the agency; or  (4) Within 500 feet of the center of the mouth of any Oregon Department of Transportation wildlife crossing structure located between US Highway 97 mile posts 149 and 153. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
