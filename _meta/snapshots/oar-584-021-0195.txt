 Teacher Standards and Practices Commission 
 Chapter 584 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 SCHOOL NURSE CERTIFICATES 
 584-021-0195 
 Procedures for Consideration of Other Cases 
 
 In a case not covered by OAR 584-021-0180 through 584-021-0190, the Executive Director must refer the application to TSPC for action. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 342 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183 & 342.455 –342.495
