 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 115 ALLOCATION OF MONIES TO DEFRAY COSTS OF MAINTAINING ACTIVE PROTECTIVE DEVICES AT RAILROAD-HIGHWAY CROSSINGS 
 741-115-0080 
 Vegetation Control at Grade Crossings 
 
 (1) The railroad shall control vegetation on its right-of-way for a distance of 250 feet in each direction from the edge of the crossing surface and for a distance of 50 feet in each direction from the centerline of the nearest track or to the edge of the railroad’s right-of-way, whichever is less, so that the vegetation does not obstruct motorists’ view of approaching trains.  (2) The public authority shall control vegetation on its right-of-way within the SSD and within its right-of-way. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 824.202 & 824.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 824.212 
 
 History:
