 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 GENERAL LICENSING REQUIREMENTS 
 918-030-0060 
 Application — Reapplying After Failed Examination 
 
 (1) An applicant who fails an examination may reapply for the license at any time as provided in OAR 918-030-0020. There is no waiting period to reapply after a failed exam.  (2) An applicant who reapplies is not required to re-qualify for examination or provide work history information unless the requirements for the license have changed since the applicant originally applied for the license.  (3) Exam retakes are scheduled no less than 30 days from the date of the failed exam. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.117 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.117
