Oregon State Marine Board

Chapter 250

Division 20
LOCAL AND SPECIAL RULES

250-020-0280
Boat Operations in Multnomah County

(1) Benson Lake: A person must not use a motor to propel a boat on Benson Lake.

(2) Bybee Lake: A person must not use an internal combustion engine to propel a boat.

(3) Sauvie Island Wildlife Area:

(a) A person must not operate a boat within the Sauvie Island Wildlife Area from October 10 to January 20 unless they possess a valid Sauvie Island Hunt Permit. This restriction does not apply to the Gilbert River from a point 100 feet upstream of the Gilbert River Dock downstream to the mouth, as marked.

(b) A person must not operate a motorboat in excess of a slow–no wake speed on any waters within the Sauvie Island Wildlife Area.

(4) Smith Lake: A person must not use an internal combustion engine to propel a boat.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 830.110 & ORS 830.175
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 830.110, ORS 830.175 & ORS 830.195
