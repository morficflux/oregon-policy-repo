 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 FIRE STANDARD COMPLIANT (REDUCED IGNITION PROPENSITY) CIGARETTES 
 837-035-0180 
 Modification of Packaging Markings 
 
 (1) Any proposed modifications to packaging markings must be submitted to the OSFM for approval before use.  (2) Violations of this section are subject to a civil penalty as referenced in OAR 837-035-0320. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 476.755 - 476.806 & 476.995
