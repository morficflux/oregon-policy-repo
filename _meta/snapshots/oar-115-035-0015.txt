 Employment Relations Board 
 Chapter 115 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 UNFAIR LABOR PRACTICE COMPLAINTS IN PUBLIC EMPLOYMENT 
 115-035-0015 
 Withdrawal of Complaint 
 
 Unless the respondent agrees, a complainant may not withdraw a complaint after issuance of a Recommended Order without a showing of good cause. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 243
