 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 RECYCLING AND WASTE REDUCTION 
 340-090-0005 
 Purpose
 
 
 OAR 340-090-0005 to 0510 establish the minimum requirements for providing the opportunity to recycle. These rules also describe the standards for waste prevention, reuse, recovery, and recycling programs in Oregon. The rules are adopted under the authority of ORS 459.045, 459A.025 and 468.020. These rules relate to the requirements of ORS 459.015, 459.250, 468.862 and Chapter 459A. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 459A.025, 459.045 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 459A.005, 459A.010, 459A.025 & 459A.575
