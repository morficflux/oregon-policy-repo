 Chief Education Office 
 Chapter 705 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ACHIEVEMENT COMPACTS 
 705-010-0065 
 End-of-Year Reports 
 
 (1) For terms of achievement compacts that are carried forward in identical form from one fiscal year to the next, an education entity’s report of results in a subsequent year’s achievement compact shall represent its report of final results for a given fiscal year.  (2) For terms of achievement compacts that are not carried forward in identical form from one fiscal year to the next, the education entity shall report its results in conjunction with its data reports for the Oregon Report Card or in separate reports within 120days after the close of the fiscal year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2012 OL Ch. 36 Sec. 14 (Enrolled SB 1581) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2012 OL Ch. 36 Sec. 14 (Enrolled SB 1581)
