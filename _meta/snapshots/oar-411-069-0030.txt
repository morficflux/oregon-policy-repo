 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 69 LONG TERM CARE ASSESSMENT 
 411-069-0030 
 Entities Subject to the Long Term Care Facility Assessment
 
 
 Each long term care facility in Oregon is subject to the long term care facility assessment except for long term care facilities operated by the Oregon Department of Veterans' Affairs. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, 410.070 & 411.060 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.750, OL 2003 Ch. 736 & OL 2013 ch. 608
