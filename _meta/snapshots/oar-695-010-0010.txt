 Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board 
 Chapter 695 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 RESTORATION GRANTS 
 695-010-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 The Board shall provide grants, as funds are available, for watershed projects that protect or restore watershed functions. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 541.906 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541.890 - 541.969 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OWEB 4-2004, f. 11-2-04, cert. ef. 2-1-05
