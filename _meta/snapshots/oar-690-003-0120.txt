 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 PUBLIC RECORDS ACCESS AND REPRODUCTION 
 690-003-0120 
 Scope 
 
 (1) With some exceptions prescribed by law, every person has the right to inspect public records of a state agency, including the Department. The Department may take reasonable measures to ensure the integrity of public records and to maintain office efficiency. Public access to view and copy public records is limited by reasonable restrictions and other such exemptions from disclosure that may be prescribed by law or rule.  (2) These rules do not apply to a person who obtains Department public records by using a Department computer, or any other computer, to download public records from the Department’s web page. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341, 536.025 & 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341, 537.173 & 537.626
