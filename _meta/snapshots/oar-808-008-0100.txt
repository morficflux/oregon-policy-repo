 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 ARBITRATION BY THE AGENCY 
 808-008-0100 
 On-Site Investigation, Settlement Discussions 
 
 (1) At the discretion of the agency the arbitration hearing may be preceded by an on-site meeting or settlement discussions.  (2) At the discretion of the arbitrator, the arbitration may be preceded by settlement discussions.  (3) The arbitrator may request that the agency conduct an on-site meeting prior to arbitration. The agency may grant or deny the request at its discretion. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.310 - 183.500, 670.310 & 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183 & 671.703(3)
