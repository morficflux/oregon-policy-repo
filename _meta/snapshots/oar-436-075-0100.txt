 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 RETROACTIVE PROGRAM 
 436-075-0100 
 Assessment of Civil Penalties 
 
 Under ORS 656.745 the director may assess a civil penalty against an insurer for failure to comply with these rules. Penalty orders will be issued under ORS 656.447 and 656.704 and are subject to review under OAR 436-075-0008. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.745 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.204, 656.726, 656.745 & 656.447
