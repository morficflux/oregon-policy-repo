 Employment Department 
 Chapter 471 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 HIGHER APPEALS 
 471-041-0095 
 Case Consolidation and Voting 
 
 Cases involving multiple parties, similar facts or similar issues may be consolidated. Cases are considered in panels of two or three EAB members. A member shall disqualify himself or herself from participation in any case in which the member has a private interest in the outcome, or a bias likely to impair his or her impartial review. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 657.610 & 657.685 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 657.685(3) & 657.685(6)
