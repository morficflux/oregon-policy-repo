 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 300 GUN RELIEF PROGRAM 
 859-300-0110 
 Public Meetings Law 
 
 (1) All gun relief hearings shall be open to the public in accordance with the Public Meetings Law.  (2) Deliberations of the Panel shall not be open to the public. For the purposes of this subsection, the term “public” does not include PSRB employees or staff. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387(1), OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.387(1), OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853))
