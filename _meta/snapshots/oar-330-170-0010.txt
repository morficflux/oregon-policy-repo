 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 170 BIOMASS PRODUCER OR COLLECTOR TAX CREDIT 
 330-170-0010 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 (1) OAR chapter 330, division 170 establishes the procedure and criteria for certifying tax credits under ORS 315.141 and 469B.403.  (2) These rules apply to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2016. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 315.141 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 315.141 & 469B.403
