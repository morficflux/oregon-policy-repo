 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 ADMINISTRATIVE PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE 
 309-012-0100 
 Purpose and Statutory Authority 
 
 (1) Purpose. These rules establish the amount of earned income the Division excludes when calculating ability-to-pay for cost of care at a mental health institution. The purpose of this earned income exclusion is to reduce the disincentive to work for patients and residents.  (2) Statutory Authority. These rules are authorized by ORS 413.042 and carry out the provisions of ORS 179.770. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.770 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.610 – 179.770 
 
 History:
