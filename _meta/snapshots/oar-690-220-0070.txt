 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 220 ABANDONMENT OF WATER SUPPLY WELLS 
 690-220-0070 
 Abandonment of Artesian Wells 
 
 In addition to OAR 690-220-0040 and 690-220-0050, the flow of artesian water supply wells to be abandoned shall be confined or restricted by cement grout applied under pressure, or by the use of a suitable well packer, or a wooden plug placed at the bottom of the confining formation immediately above the artesian water-bearing zone. Cement grout or concrete shall be used to effectively fill the well to land surface. (See Figure 220-4, 1986.)  [ED. NOTE: Figures referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
