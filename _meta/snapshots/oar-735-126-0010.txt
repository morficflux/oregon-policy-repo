 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 126 SAFETY EQUIPMENT REQUIREMENTS 
 735-126-0010 
 Fire Extinguisher 
 
 (1) Each vehicle used to transport workers shall be equipped with either a two pound dry chemical type, or a four pound carbon dioxide type, or a one quart vaporizing type fire extinguisher. Such extinguishers must be approved by Underwriter Laboratories, Inc., or other acceptable testing agency, or by the inspecting authority.  (2) Toxic vaporizing liquid type fire extinguishers such as those containing carbon tetrachloride or chlorobromethane will not be permitted on motor vehicles transporting workers. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 802.010 & 820.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 820.010 - 820.030
