 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 GENERAL REGISTRATION PROVISIONS 
 735-030-0015 
 Classification of Vehicles for Purposes of Registration Fees 
 
 DMV will use definitions found in the Oregon Vehicle Code to determine classifications for the purposes of imposing vehicle registration fees found in ORS 803.420. DMV will use the following definitions for the types of vehicles listed below, as no definition exists in the Oregon Vehicle Code: 

 (1) A “passenger vehicle” means a car, pick-up, van, SUV or other motor vehicle, designed and used to transport people, with a combined weight of 8,000 pounds or less, and not otherwise provided for in ORS 803.420 or ORS 821.320. 

 (2) A “utility or light trailer” means any trailer having a loaded weight of 8,000 pounds or less, except trailers for hire or rent, travel trailers, special use trailers and fixed loads. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.010 & 803.420 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 803.420 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DMV 24-2017, adopt filed 12/19/2017, effective 01/01/2018
