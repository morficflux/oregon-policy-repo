 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 69 INVESTOR OWNED UTILITY INSTITUTIONAL BUILDINGS PROGRAM 
 330-069-0075 
 Operating Area 
 
 ODOE shall run this program in the Oregon service areas of these IOUs:  (1) CP National;  (2) Idaho Power Company;  (3) Pacific Power and Light Company;  (4) Portland General Electric Company. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOE 5-1985, f. & ef. 6-17-85
