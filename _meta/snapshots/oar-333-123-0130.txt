 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 123 THERAPEUTIC RADIATION MACHINES 
 333-123-0130 
 Superficial Electronic Brachytherapy: Refresher Training 
 
 Authorized users, qualified medical physicists, and superficial electronic brachytherapy operators must complete at least four hours of annual continuing education specific to superficial electronic brachytherapy and retain copies of the continuing education records in accordance with OAR 333-100-0057 and be made available to the Authority for inspection. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PH 72-2020, adopt filed 10/09/2020, effective 10/09/2020
