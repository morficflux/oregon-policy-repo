 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 MODEL RULES FOR AGENCY DECLARATORY RULINGS 
 137-002-0050 
 Presiding Officer’s Proposed Declaratory Ruling 
 
 (1) Except when the presiding officer is the decision maker, the presiding officer shall prepare a proposed declaratory ruling in accordance with OAR 137-002-0060 for consideration by the decision maker.  (2) When a proposed declaratory ruling is considered by the decision maker, the parties and agency staff shall have the right to present oral argument to the decision maker. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.410 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.410
