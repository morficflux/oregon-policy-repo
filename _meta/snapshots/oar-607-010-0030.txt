 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Tall Fescue Commission - Chapter 607 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENTS 
 607-010-0030 
 First Effective Date of Assessment
 
 
 (1) The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and OAR 607-010-0020(1), applies to and covers any sale made on and after September 9, 1969, by a producer to such first purchaser of Tall Fescue Seed grown in Oregon, regardless of the time it was or is grown. 

 (2) The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and OAR 607-010-0020(2) applies to and covers any sale made on and after July 1, 2019, by a producer to such first purchaser of Orchardgrass Seed grown in Oregon, regardless of the time it was or is grown. 

 (3) “Sale” for the purpose of this rule means the date that title to the Tall Fescue Seed or Orchardgrass Seed passes from the seller to the purchaser. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 and HB 3401 (2019) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576 and HB 3401 (2019)
