 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 52 TRANSFERS AND RESPONSIBILITIES BETWEEN OREGON YOUTH AUTHORITY AND DEPARTMENT OF CORRECTIONS 
 291-052-0130 
 AICs and YICs Delivered Directly to the Oregon Youth Authority
 
 
 (1) The same day an AIC or YIC arrives at a youth correction facility, OYA staff must forward copies of the AIC’s or YIC’s judgments, statements of imprisonment, detainers, county misconduct documents, fingerprints, photograph, and any other information received to DOC. 

 (2) DOC must verify the AIC or YIC meets the qualifying criteria to be placed in a youth correction facility as outlined in OAR 291-052-0100(3). 

 (3) Designated DOC staff must coordinate with designated OYA staff to complete the AIC’s or YIC’s DOC intake process. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.124, 179.040, 420.011(2) & (3), 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
