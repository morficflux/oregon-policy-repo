 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 VEHICLES, DRIVER, EQUIPMENT: EQUIPMENT REQUIRED AND CONDITION OF VEHICLES 
 740-100-0040 
 Chains/Traction Devices 
 
 All motor carriers are required to carry and use chains and traction tires as provided in OAR chapter 734 division 17 rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 823.011 & 825.252 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 815.140
