 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 LICENSING - PROCEDURES FOR ESTABLISHING, OPERATING, DISCONTINUING AND SUSPENDING LICENSES AND AGENTS 
 635-010-0012 
 Service Fees 
 
 (1) The Department and its agents or contractors may charge a $2.00 fee for the reprinting of hard copy hunting and angling tags as well as for the mailing of hunt unit maps ordered through the electronic licensing system. 

 (2) The Department may charge shipping fees, up to the true cost of shipping, for special orders, large mailings, or other similar instances where shipping fees exceed $10. Exception: For landowner preference and related documents, the fee is referenced in OAR 635, division 075. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.146 & ORS 497.032
