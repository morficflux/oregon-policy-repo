 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 POLYCHLORINATED BIPHENYLS (PCBS) 
 340-110-0040 
 Marking of PCB and PCB Items 
 
 The provisions of 40 CFR 761.40 through 761.59 are applicable only as they relate to items removed from service for disposal. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 459 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466
