 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ASSESSMENT OF PROPERTY FOR TAXATION 
 150-308-0680 
 Contents of Department’s Journal 
 
 The department’s journal shall contain:  (1) A schedule of hearings conducted by the director during review of the tentative roll;  (2) The date the review is completed;  (3) The change property ratio for centrally assessed property;  (4) The name of each company assessed a late filing penalty and the amount of the penalty.  (5) The name of each company receiving a waiver of the late filing penalty from the director; and  (6) Any other information pertaining to the review of the tentative roll that the director deems appropriate. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 308.605
