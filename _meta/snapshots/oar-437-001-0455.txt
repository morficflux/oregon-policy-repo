 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 RULES FOR THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE OREGON SAFE EMPLOYMENT ACT 
 437-001-0455 
 Application for Consultative Services 
 
 Employers may make a verbal or written request for consultative services. A request must:  (1) Be made by an employer to a representative of the Division;  (2) Identify the employer and the location where the consultation is desired; and  (3) Define the specific problem or hazard, or other reason for the request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001-654.295
