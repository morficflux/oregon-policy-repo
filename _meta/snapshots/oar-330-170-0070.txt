 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 170 BIOMASS PRODUCER OR COLLECTOR TAX CREDIT 
 330-170-0070 
 Minimum Discount Value 
 
 The minimum discounted value of a tax credit issued under ORS 315.141 is 90 percent of the amount of the tax credit. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 351.141 & 469.791 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 351.141 & 469.791
