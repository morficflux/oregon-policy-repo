 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 ADOPTION; DEFINITIONS; CONSTRUCTION; NOTICES REQUIRED 
 438-005-0011 
 Effective Date; Applicability 
 
 These rules are effective January 1, 2006 and shall apply to all cases pending before the Hearings Division and the Board under the provisions of ORS Chapter 656 on and after that date. These rules are also applicable to cases pending before the Hearings Division and the Board arising under ORS Chapter 655. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.726(5)
