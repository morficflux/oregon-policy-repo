 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Employees' Benefit Board - Chapter 101 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 PEBB-PROVIDED HEALTH BENEFIT PLAN FOR  LIQUOR CONTROL COMMISSION AGENTS 
 101-060-0005 
 Definitions 
 
 The following definitions will apply as used in OAR chapter 101 division 60:  (1) “Eligible employee” means a retail sales agent, appointed under ORS 471.705, who is subject to an agency agreement with the Oregon Liquor Control Commission to sell distilled spirits in the Commission’s agency stores. A temporary liquor agent is not considered an eligible employee for PEBB administrative purposes.  (2) “Newly eligible employee” means an agent who has signed a contract with an effective date of July 1, 1985 or later. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 243.061 - 243.302 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 243.061 - 243.302
