 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 175 FOOD HANDLER TRAINING 
 333-175-0081 
 Successful Completion of Food Handler Training Program 
 
 (1) In order to receive a certificate of program completion, a food handler must pass the written assessment with a minimum score of 75%.  (2) If a person successfully completes a food handler training program and pays the appropriate fee, the program provider shall issue a certificate of program completion. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 624.570 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 624.570
