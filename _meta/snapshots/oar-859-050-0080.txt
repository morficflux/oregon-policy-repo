 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD HEARING PROCEDURES 
 859-050-0080 
 Board Decisions 
 
 (1) The Board will provide findings and notify the parties and other interested individuals and organizations, as appropriate.  (2) The board may issue its decisions orally on the record at the hearing, and will do so unless the Board has reason to delay issuing its findings. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.346
