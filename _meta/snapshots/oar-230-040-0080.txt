 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 CONTROL OF TICKET SALES: REVENUE 
 230-040-0080 
 Ticket Stubs
 
 
 Under no circumstances shall a ticket holder be passed through the gate without having the ticket separated from the stub, or be allowed to occupy a seat unless in possession of the ticket stub. A promoter must keep an auditable record of all tickets printed, sold, complimentary tickets issued, unsold and overprint tickets for an event. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.113
