 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0607 
 Funding: FFA Summer Duty Extended Contract Grant 
 
 (1) The Department of Education shall determine for each fiscal year the portion of the funds available for the FFA Summer Duty Extended Contract Grant. 

 (2) Prior to the allocation in each fiscal year, the Department of Education shall: 

 (a) Identify qualifying secondary school districts that have an approved Agriculture Science and Technology Program of Study; and 

 (b) Identify the maximum allocation available for individual teachers for FFA Summer Duty Extended Contract Grants for each fiscal year. 

 (3) Funds received by a school district under this section must be separately accounted for and must be used in accordance with the school district’s approved calendar of FFA activities. 

 (4) Any funds not claimed within a grant cycle will be reallocated into the FFA Summer Duty Extended Contract Grant fund and will be made available for the next grant cycle or returned to the state general fund at the end of the biennium.  

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2019 OL Ch. 588, Sec. 3 (Enrolled HB 2444) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2019 OL Ch. 588, Sec. 3 (Enrolled HB 2444) 
 
 History:
