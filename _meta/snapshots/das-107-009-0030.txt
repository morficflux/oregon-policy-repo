policy and related procedure.                             IS
This policy provides direction to state agencies for the advancement of business equity, inclusion and
engagement in procurement activities and identifies those with the authority and responsibility to implement the

                                                             IO
POLICY

                                                       R
Business Equity, Inclusion and Engagement in Public Procurement refers to the process of intentionally
including businesses owned by minorities, women, service-disabled veterans and emerging small businesses
to participate in procurement activities and to promote business engagement.
                                        ER
It is the policy of Oregon state government to provide equal access and opportunity to all Oregon businesses
by facilitating equity and fairness in the procurement activities administered by state agencies, boards and
commissions.

APPLICABILITY
                             ND
                        U
This policy applies to all state agencies, boards and commissions within the Executive Branch, as defined by
ORS 174.112. State agencies, cities, counties, and other public entities outside the Executive Branch are
encouraged to develop, implement, and participate in accordance with this policy and related procedure as
appropriate and to the extent allowable by law.

The policy is intended to function in addition to other existing legal responsibilities related to the procurements
of state agencies, boards or commissions.

DEFINITIONS

All capitalized terms in this policy are defined in OAR 125-246-0110. Capitalized terms not defined in the
aforementioned OAR, are defined as set forth below.

Agencies means all state agencies, boards and commissions within the Executive Branch, as defined by ORS
174.112.

COBID Certified Firms means qualified COBID firms certified by COBID and listed in the COBID Vendor
Directory.

Engagement is a value-added set of strategies, activities and partnerships designed to support and promote
economic prosperity by providing valuable information, tools and resources to businesses interested in
contracting with public agencies.

Equity means seeking to ensure fair treatment, equality of opportunity, and fairness in access to information
and resources for all Oregon businesses.

Inclusion is actively inviting the participation of Oregon businesses, with intentional invitation expanding to
COBID Certified Firms.                                                    N
                                                       EV
Provider means collectively or in the alternative, the Supplier, Contractor, Consultant or Business.
