 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 EMPLOYEE LEAVE LAWS 
 839-009-0300 
 OFLA: Postings
 
 
 (1) Each covered employer must display the Bureau of Labor and Industries Family Leave Act notice. The notice must be displayed in each building or worksite in an area that is accessible to and regularly frequented by employees. Failure to post the Family Leave Act notice is an unlawful practice as provided in ORS 659A.001. 

 (2) Electronic posting of the Family Leave Act notice is not sufficient to satisfy posting requirements, but may supplement worksite posting. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 651.060 & ORS 659A.805 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 659A.150 - 659A.186
