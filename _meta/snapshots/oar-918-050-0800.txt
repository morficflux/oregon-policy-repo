 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-050-0800 
 Permit Surcharges 
 
 (1) A one-percent surcharge will be assessed upon the total permit fees collected pursuant to ORS 455.210(4)(c).  (2) Effective January 1, 2008, a four percent surcharge will be assessed upon the total permit fees collected pursuant to ORS 455.210(4)(d). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.044 & 455.210 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.044 & 455.210
