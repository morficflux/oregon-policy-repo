 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 TRANSPORTATION OF RADIOACTIVE MATERIAL 
 345-060-0030 
 Reporting and Emergency Response 
 
 The carrier of any radioactive material shall immediately notify local emergency response authorities and the Oregon Emergency Response System (within Oregon call 1-800-452-0311, outside Oregon call 1-503-378-6377) of any of the following:  (1) Vehicle accidents regardless of whether radioactive material has been damaged or dispersed;  (2) Loss of any radioactive material;  (3) Tampering with or obstruction of any shipments. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 & 469.607 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.603 - 469.615
