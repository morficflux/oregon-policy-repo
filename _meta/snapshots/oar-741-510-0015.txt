 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 510 OREGON RAILROAD HAZARDOUS MATERIALS TRANSPORTATION RULES 
 741-510-0015 
 Adoption of Federal Regulations 
 
 The Department of Transportation adopts the rules of the United States Department of Transportation in effect on the date this rule is filed with the Secretary of State (anticipated June 18, 2015) in Title 49, Code of Federal Regulations, Parts 107 through 180, Hazardous Materials Regulations, insofar as those rules apply to railroads and railroad shippers. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 823.061 & 824.086 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 824.080 - 824.092 
 
 History:
