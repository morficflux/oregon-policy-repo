 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 38 VETERAN SERVICES GRANT 
 274-038-0300 
 Purposes of Grant Funds 
 
 
 Grant funds may be used only for purposes set forth in ORS 406.142(2). 
  

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406.030 & ORS 406.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 406.142 (2017 HB 2891)
