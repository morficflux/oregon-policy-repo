 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 VIDEO LOTTERY TERMINALS 
 177-100-0095 
 Procurement of Video LotterySM Game Terminals 
 
 (1) Only the Lottery may possess and operate Video LotterySM game terminals in Oregon.  (2) The Lottery may select and procure Video LotterySM game terminals as necessary by contracting with manufacturers approved pursuant to OAR chapter 177, division 35, and may provide the terminals to Video LotterySM retailers. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV, § 4(4) & ORS 461 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461
