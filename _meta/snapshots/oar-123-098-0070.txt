 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 98 INDUSTRIAL SITE READINESS ASSESSMENT PROGRAM 
 123-098-0070 
 Reporting 
 
 (1) Copies of final reports to be submitted to the Department within 30 days of receipt by Public Entity; and  (2) Annual progress report from Public Entity of all activities conducted or completed by program funding, and current budget to be submitted to Department within 60 days following the end of the Fiscal Year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 & 285B.635-285B.642 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285B.635-285B.642 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OBDD 8-2014, f. 4-30-14, cert. ef. 5-1-14
