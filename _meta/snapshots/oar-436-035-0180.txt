 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 DISABILITY RATING STANDARDS 
 436-035-0180 
 Conversion of Toe Values to Foot Value 
 
 (1) If the only findings are in the toes, it is not possible to convert the toe findings to a loss in the foot unless there are impairment findings in the foot. Each toe must be converted to the foot separately. After converting to the foot, each converted value is added.  (2) If there are impairment findings in the foot and impairment findings in the great toe, the following table is used to convert losses in the great toe to losses in the foot: [Table not included.
