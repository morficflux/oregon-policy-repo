 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 LICENSING 
 812-003-0321 
 Notification of Change of Non-Exempt or Exempt License Status
 
 
 (1) When a contractor’s license status changes from nonexempt to exempt or from exempt to nonexempt, the contractor must notify the board. The contractor must file a Notification of Changed Contractor License Status within 10 days of the effective date of the change. 

 (2) There is no charge to file the Notification under this rule. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 701.235 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701.035
