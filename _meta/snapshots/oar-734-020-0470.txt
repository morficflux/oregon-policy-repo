 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 TRAFFIC CONTROL 
 734-020-0470 
 Traffic Signal Spacing Requirement 
 
 The desirable spacing of signalized intersections on statewide and regional highways is 1/2 mile. The STRE may approve the installation of a traffic signal at locations where 1/2-mile spacing is inappropriate or infeasible due to:  (1) Topography;  (2) Existing or proposed road layout;  (3) Identified traffic crash pattern;  (4) Unique physical constraints; or  (5) Existing or proposed land use patterns. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 366.205 & 810.200 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 810.200 & 810.210
