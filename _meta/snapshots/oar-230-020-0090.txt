 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 LICENSES AND APPLICATIONS 
 230-020-0090 
 Basis for Granting License
 
 
 The superintendent shall not issue a promoter's license to a person unless the superintendent is satisfied that the applicant is the real party in interest, and intends to arrange, conduct, or hold such events itself. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 & 463.165 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.165
