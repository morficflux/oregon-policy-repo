 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2483 
 Written Affirmation 
 
 (1) When timely and meaningful consultation has occurred as described in OAR 581-015-2480, the public agency must obtain a written affirmation signed by the representatives of participating private schools.  (2) If the representatives do not provide the affirmations within a reasonable period of time, the school district must forward the documentation of the consultation process to the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.041, 343.045 & 343.055; 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 343.041, 343.055 & 34 CFR 300.135 
 
 History:
