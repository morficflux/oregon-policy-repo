 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 TRADE PRACTICES 
 836-080-0775 
 Severability 
 
 If any provisions of OAR 836-080-0750 to 836-080-0775 or their application to any person or circumstance is held invalid for any reason, the invalidity shall not affect the other provisions or any other application of 836-080-0750 to 836-080-0775 that can be given effect without the invalid provisions or applicant. To this end all provisions of 836-080-0750 to 836-080-0775 are declared to be severable. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 746.075, 746.110 & 746.240
