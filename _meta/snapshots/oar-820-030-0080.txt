 Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying 
 Chapter 820 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 THE PRACTICE OF LAND SURVEYING 
 820-030-0080 
 Survey Monument Marking 
 
 A monument set by a registered professional land surveyor to mark or reference a point on a property or land line, or to mark or reference a geodetic control survey point, must be durably and visibly marked or tagged with the registered business name (Oregon only) or the letters “L.S.” followed by the registration number (Oregon only) of the surveyor in charge or, if the monument is set by a public officer, the monument must be marked with the official title of the office. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 672.255 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.002 - 672.325 
 
 History:
