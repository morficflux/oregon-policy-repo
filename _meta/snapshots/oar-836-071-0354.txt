 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 INSURANCE LICENSING 
 836-071-0354 
 Designated Agent 
 
 As used in OAR 836-071-0355 to OAR 836-071-0400, “designated agent” means any named individual who is under contract with a vehicle rental company and who is authorized by the vehicle rental company to sell insurance under the authority of the limited license of the vehicle rental company. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244, 744.852 & 744.858 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 744.852, 744.856 & 744.858
