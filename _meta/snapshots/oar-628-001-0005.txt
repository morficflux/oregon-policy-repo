 Oregon Forest Resources Institute 
 Chapter 628 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 628-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, promulgated by the Attorney General effective January 1, 2006, are hereby adopted as rules of procedure of the Oregon Forest Resources Institute. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.645 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341 
 
 History:
