 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 324 OIL AND GAS TAX 
 150-324-0010 
 Applicability Limited to Natural Gas Production 
 
 OAR 150-324-0010 to 150-324-0035 are applicable to natural gas production within the State of Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 324.050 
 
 History:
