 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 629-001-0035 
 Immediate Review by Agency 
 
 (1) Matters referred to the agency for immediate review, as provided for in OAR 137-003-0640 in the Attorney General’s Model and Uniform Rules, shall be transmitted to the State Forester.  (2) Rulings on requests for immediate review may be made by the State Forester or the State Forester’s delegate. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.016(4), 527.687(3) & 527.715 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.310 - 183.550 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOF 5-2002, f. & cert. ef. 7-1-02
