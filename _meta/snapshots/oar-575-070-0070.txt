 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 OREGON TEACHER CORPS LOAN PROGRAM 
 575-070-0070 
 Authorized Deferment of Repayment 
 
 (1) A borrower under contract to teach at least half-time in a public elementary or secondary school in Oregon may defer the beginning of the repayment period for up to three years, subject to annual verification of employment status.  (2) A borrower who can document his/her inability to locate suitable employment may defer the beginning of the repayment period for up to three years, subject to annual verification efforts to locate employment.  (3) No borrower may receive more than three years of authorized deferment of repayment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329.757 - 329.780
