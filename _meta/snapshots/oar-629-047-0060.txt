 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 47 ENFORCEMENT POLICY 
 629-047-0060 
 Fire Prevention Enforcement Limitations 
 
 Only authorized fire wardens who have been designated a Fire Warden — Class A, by the forester on or after the original effective date of this rule, may issue a Fire Prevention Warning or a Fire Prevention Citation for violations involving the fire prevention requirements of ORS 477.510, 477.565, 477.615, 477.625, 477.635, 477.650, 477.655, 477.660, 477.665, 477.670, and 477.695. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 477.980 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 477.980 - 477.993
