 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ANIMAL INDUSTRY GENERAL 
 603-010-0053 
 Definition 
 
 As used in ORS Chapter 607, “swine” means a breed of domestic pig that is used for food production, but does not include any breed used primarily as a pet (e.g., pot-bellied pig). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 607.005 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 7-1997, f. & cert. ef. 6-10-97
