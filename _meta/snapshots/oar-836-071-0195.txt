 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 INSURANCE LICENSING 
 836-071-0195 
 Revocation of Registration of a School; Reinstatement 
 
 (1) The registration of an insurance school providing pre-examination training may be revoked by the Director if the Director determines that:  (a) The insurance training program as registered is not being taught; or  (b) Students who have not maintained a satisfactory attendance record or have not completed course work have been certified by the school for the insurance producer license examination.  (2) A school whose registration is revoked may apply for reinstatement. The school must demonstrate to the Director's satisfaction that the school has taken appropriate action to correct the conditions that were the basis of the revocation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 & 744
