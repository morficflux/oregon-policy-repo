 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 256 MOTOR VEHICLES 
 340-256-0200 
 Certification of Pollution Control Systems: County Designations
 
 
 Clackamas, Columbia, Jackson, Marion, Multnomah, Washington and Yamhill counties are hereby designated by the Environmental Quality Commission as counties in which all motor vehicles registered therein, unless otherwise exempted by statute or by rules subsequently adopted by the Commission, must be equipped with a motor vehicle pollution control system and must comply with motor vehicle emission standards adopted by the Commission. 

 [NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.] 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468A.390 & ORS 468A.360 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.390
