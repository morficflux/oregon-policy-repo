 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 APPROPRIATION AND USE OF WATER FOR HYDROELECTRIC POWER AND STANDARDS FOR HYDROELECTRIC APPLICATIONS 
 690-051-0040 
 Preliminary Permit not Required 
 
 No License or Permit shall be issued within six months of either approving the Preliminary Permit or acceptance of the License or Permit application. A Preliminary Permit is unnecessary where the project is a minor project. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025, 536.027, 537 & 543
