 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 VEHICLES, DRIVER, EQUIPMENT: EQUIPMENT REQUIRED AND CONDITION OF VEHICLES 
 740-100-0055 
 Commercial Vehicle Inspection Reciprocity 
 
 In general, vehicles displaying a valid Oregon Commercial Vehicle Inspection Decal are not subject to re-inspection. However, if an obvious defect is noticed on a vehicle with a current decal, nothing prevents a party from re-inspecting that vehicle. Maximum reciprocity will be granted to inspections conducted pursuant to the North American Standard Inspection procedures by other jurisdictions. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 825.232 & 825.252 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 810.560, 825.210 & 825.250
