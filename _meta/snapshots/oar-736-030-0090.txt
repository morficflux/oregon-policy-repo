 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 SPECIAL REGULATIONS AFFECTING OCEAN SHORES ADJACENT TO COASTAL CITIES 
 736-030-0090 
 Beach Lifeguard Authority 
 
 Within the city limits of Cannon Beach, city lifeguards shall have the authority to restrict the launching of inflatable devices by individuals, on a case-by-case basis, when necessary for public safety or the safety of an individual. No person shall disregard a city lifeguard’s instructions relating to the launching of inflatable devices within the city limits of Cannon Beach. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.124 & 390.660 
 
 History:
