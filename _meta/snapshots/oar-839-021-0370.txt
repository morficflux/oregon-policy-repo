 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 RULES REGULATING THE EMPLOYMENT OF MINORS IN OREGON 
 839-021-0370 
 Records Required to Be Made Available by Employers of Minors in the Entertainment Industry 
 
 All employers must make available to representatives of the Wage and Hour Division of the Bureau, records necessary to determine whether the employer is complying with OAR 839-021-0300 to 839-021-0375. Such records include, but are not limited to, the records described in 839-021-0365. Such records must be made available to such representatives for inspection and transcription during normal business hours. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 651.060(4) & 653.261 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  S.B. 135, 77th Leg., Reg. Ses. (Or.2013) & ORS 653
