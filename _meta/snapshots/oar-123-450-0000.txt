 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 450 OREGON ARTS PROGRAM GRANTS 
 123-450-0000 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) “Commission” means the Oregon Arts Commission.  (2) “Executive Director” means the administrator of the Arts Program of the Oregon Business Development Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 359 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 359
