 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 78 TRANSPORTATION OF OVERLENGTH LOGS, POLES, PILING, AND STRUCTURAL MEMBERS 
 734-078-0005 
 Scope 
 
 These rules shall apply to and govern the issuance of permits by the Department of Transportation, for the movement of vehicles transporting logs, poles, piling and structural members which, when loaded, have overall lengths in excess of those permitted by Oregon Revised Statutes or by OAR Chapter 734, Division 71. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184, 366 & 818 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.220
