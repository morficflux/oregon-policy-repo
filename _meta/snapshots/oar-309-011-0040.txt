 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 ADMINISTRATIVE PRACTICES 
 309-011-0040 
 Purpose and Statutory Authority 
 
 (1) Purpose. These rules describe the organization and responsibilities of the Mental Health Advisory Board.  (2) Statutory Authority. These rules are authorized by ORS 413.042 and carry out the provisions of ORS 430.050. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 430.050
