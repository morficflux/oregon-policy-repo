 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 ENERGY SUPPLIER REPORTING REQUIREMENTS 
 330-020-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to specify the form and content of certain information to be furnished annually to the Department of Energy by utilities regarding energy demand. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.080
