 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 145 EMERGENCY SOLUTIONS GRANT PROGRAM (ESG) 
 813-145-0026 
 Applicant Eligibility
 
 
 (1) ESG services shall be made available to households who meet eligibility requirements as outlined in the ESG Manual, including but not limited to: 

 (a) An income within limits for applicable ESG services as defined in the ESG Manual; and 

 (b) Are homeless or at risk of homelessness. 

 (2) Specific requirements and documentation to provide proof of eligibility for ESG services are detailed in the ESG Manual. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.555 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  24 CFR 576
