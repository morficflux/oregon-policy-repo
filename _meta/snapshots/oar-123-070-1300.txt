 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 FIRST-SOURCE HIRING AGREEMENTS 
 123-070-1300 
 Administration for Lottery-Funded Projects 
 
 (1) For the lottery-funded programs listed in OAR 123-070-1150(1)(a), the Department shall take anticipatory actions that are necessary and appropriate to implement the requirement of the First Source Agreement in accordance with ORS 461.740, which may include but are not limited to requiring a Benefited Business to submit a copy of the First Source Agreement, or otherwise ensuring that one has been entered into, before the grant or loan is ultimately awarded.  (2) The responsibilities consistent with section (1) of this rule may be delegated by the Department to a grantee that is the direct recipient of lottery-derived funds and that provides the grant, loan or substantial benefits to the Benefited Business. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 & 285A.110(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.740
