 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 RAILROAD-HIGHWAY CROSSINGS — GENERAL 
 741-100-0040 
 Display of U.S. DOT Inventory Number 
 
 The U.S. DOT crossing number shall be displayed at all public highway-rail crossings. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 824.202 & 824.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 824.200
