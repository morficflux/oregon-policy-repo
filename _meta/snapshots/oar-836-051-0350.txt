 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 LIFE, INDIVIDUAL AND GROUP; ANNUITIES 
 836-051-0350 
 Waiver of Premium 
 
 (1) An insurer may offer a waiver of premium as part of the accelerated benefit provision in the absence of a regular waiver of premium provision.  (2) When an accelerated benefit is included in an individual term policy or an individually-paid group certificate, the accelerated benefit provision must include a waiver of premium provision for any remaining face amount.  (3) A term rider shall not be affected by the payment of the accelerated benefit if premiums continue to be paid for the rider. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & 743.154 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 743.154
