 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 MANUFACTURED DWELLINGS 
 918-500-0520 
 Amendments to the Manufactured Dwelling Installation Specialty Code 
 
 The Oregon Manufactured Dwelling Installation Specialty Code is adopted and amended pursuant to OAR chapter 918, division 8. Amendments adopted are placed in this rule, showing the section reference, a descriptive caption, and a short description of the amendment.  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.185, 455.020 & 455.110 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.110
