 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 VIDEO LOTTERY TERMINALS 
 177-100-0000 
 Video LotterySM Game System 
 
 The Director may operate a Video LotterySM game system using Video LotterySM game terminals approved under this Division. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV, § 4(4) & ORS 461 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.215 & 461.217
