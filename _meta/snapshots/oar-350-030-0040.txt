 Columbia River Gorge Commission 
 Chapter 350 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 ENFORCEMENT 
 350-030-0040 
 Answer 
 
 (1) The alleged violator shall file an answer within 14 days of receipt of a notice of violation but it must be received by the Director within the 14 days allowed.  (2) The answer shall agree or disagree with all factual matters and shall affirmatively allege any and all affirmative claims or defenses and the reasoning in support thereof. The answer may include proposed measures for resolution of the matter through 350-030-0050–350-030-0060 or 350-030-0070 and any reason the Commission should modify the penalty recommended. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.150 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq.
