 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Policy and Analytics - Chapter 409 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 HEALTH EVIDENCE REVIEW COMMISSION MEDICAL TECHNOLOGY ASSESSMENTS 
 409-060-0140 
 Evidence-based Guidelines 
 
 The EbGS shall develop evidence-based guidelines based on one or more existing guidelines which may involve the consideration of additional research. Evidence-based guidelines shall be developed according to the process described in OAR 409-060-0120 except as described in this section. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 414.695 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  414.695 & 414.698
