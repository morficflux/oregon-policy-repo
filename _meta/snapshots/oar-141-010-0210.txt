 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 GENERAL 
 141-010-0210 
 Permit Requirement and Jurisdiction 
 
 (1) A permit is required to conduct any geological or geophysical survey on lands subject to these rules. Lands subject to these rules include tidal submerged lands and all state-owned submerged and submersible lands lying west of a line ten miles easterly of the 124th Meridian (see ORS 274.710).  (2) No permit shall be issued under these rules for surveys using dynamite, TNT, or other forms of explosives. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 274 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 274.735
