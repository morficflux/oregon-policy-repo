    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-11


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    GRAHAM FIRE IN JEFFERSON COUNTY                                                               |


    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Graham Fire” is burning in Jefferson County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Graham Fire is
    beyond local capabilities, Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by Don Colfels, Jefferson County Fire Defense Board Chief. The
    State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510 ef seq., I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the Graham Fire in Jefferson
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 6:20 p.m. on June 21, 2018, and I now confirm them
    with this Executive Order.                                                                        |


    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:                                             |

    1.      The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
            fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
            Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the                      |
            Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
            responding to Graham Fire, burning near the town of Culver, may be
            redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.


    2.      This emergency is declared only for the Graham Fire threatening structures
            in Jefferson County near the town of Culver and Lake Billy Chinook.

    3.      This order was made by verbal proclamation at 6:20 p.m. the 21st day of               |
            June 2018 and signed this LL day of June, 2018, in Salem, Oregon.                 |
