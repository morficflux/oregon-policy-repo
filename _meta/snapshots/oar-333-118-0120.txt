 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 118 TRANSPORTATION OF RADIOACTIVE MATERIAL 
 333-118-0120 
 General License: Plutonium Beryllium Special Form Material 
 
 A general license is issued to any licensee of the Authority to transport fissile material in the form of plutonium-beryllium (Pu-Be) special form sealed sources, or to deliver Pu-Be sealed sources to a carrier for transport, if the material is shipped in accordance with 10 CFR Part 71.23. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
