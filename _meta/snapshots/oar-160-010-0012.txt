 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 BUSINESS REGISTRY SECTION 
 160-010-0012 
 Distinguishable on Record 
 
 For purposes of the reservation, registration, or use of a name under ORS Chapters 58, 60, 62, 63, 65, 67, 70, 128, 554, and 648, a name is distinguishable on the records of the Secretary of State Business Registry office from the name of any other active organized entity, and from a reserved or registered name, if:  (1) Each name contains one or more different letters or numerals, or has a different sequence of letters or numerals, except that adding or deleting the letter “s” to make a word plural, singular, or possessive shall not cause a name to be distinguishable;  (2) One of the key words is different;  (3) The key words are the same, but they are in a different order; or  (4) The key words are the same, but the spelling is creative or unusual.  (5) The difference in key words is between how a number is expressed, as a numeral, Roman numeral, or word representing a numeral. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 56, 58, 60, 62, 63, 65, 68, 70, 128, 183, 554 & 648 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 58.085, 60.094, 62.131, 63.094, 65.094, 68.735, 70.010, 128.580; 554.005 & 648.051 
 
 History:
