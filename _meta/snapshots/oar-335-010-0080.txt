 Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology 
 Chapter 335 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 RECORD KEEPING 
 335-010-0080 
 Storage, Maintenance, and Retention of Records 
 
 (1) Clinical and billing records must be maintained for seven (7) years.  (2) All records, including clinical records, must be stored and maintained so that the records are safeguarded, readily retrievable, and open to inspection by the representatives of the Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 681.420(5) & 681.460 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 681.420 
 
 History:
