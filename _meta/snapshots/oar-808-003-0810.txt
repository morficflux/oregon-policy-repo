 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 LICENSES 
 808-003-0810 
 Conventional Examination Testing Requirements
 
 
 (1) As used in OAR Chapter 808,  Division 003 the conventional exam as required in ORS 671.571 shall be the landscape construction professional license exam that is offered by PSI and the LCB Prep Course   & Exam offered by the Small Business Development Center. An applicaion approved for testing may schedule testing at an approved testing center. 

 (2) An applicant must pay the exam fee directly to the approved exam provider as stated in OAR 808-003-0710(2). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.570
