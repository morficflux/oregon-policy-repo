 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 507 UMATILLA BASIN PROGRAM 
 690-507-0630 
 Butter Creek CGWA: General Requirements 
 
 (1) Except as specified in OAR 690-507-0650(3) and 690-507-0670(7), the use of water from the basalt groundwater reservoir within the Butter Creek Critical Groundwater Area shall be limited to the sustainable annual yield.  (2) The Commission delegates to the Director the authority to implement these rules.  (3) Water from the basalt groundwater reservoir in the Butter Creek Critical Groundwater Area shall be used for irrigation only during the irrigation season. The irrigation season begins on the 15th of March and ends on the 1st of November.  (4) The Department shall not accept any new applications for appropriation of water from the basalt groundwater reservoir within the Butter Creek Critical Area. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 537.515, 537.525, 537.545 & 537.730 - 537.745 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 537.535
