 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 810 GENERAL 
 441-810-0200 
 Applicability of Rules, Definition 
 
 (1) OAR 441-810-0200 to 441-810-0250 apply to the practices of a collection agency that enters into an agreement with an obligee to collect child support payments as provided in ORS 25.020.  (2) For the purposes of OAR 441-810-0200 to 441-810-0250, the term “debtor” includes the debtor’s spouse, parent (if the debtor is a minor), guardian, executor, or administrator. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 697.085 & Ch. 421, Sec. 4 & 2003 OL 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 25.020 & Ch. 421, Sec. 4 & 2003 OL 
 
 History:
