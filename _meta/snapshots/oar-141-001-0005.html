Department of State Lands

Chapter 141

Division 1
PROCEDURAL RULES

141-001-0005
Model Rules for Rulemaking

Pursuant to ORS 183.341, the Department of State Lands and the State Land Board adopt by reference the Attorney General's Model Rules for Rulemaking (OAR 137-001) in effect January 1, 2023.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 183.341 & ORS 273.045
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.333 & ORS 183.341
