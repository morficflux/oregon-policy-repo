 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 APPLICATIONS; REQUIREMENTS; RESTRICTIONS; PROHIBITED CONDUCT 
 845-006-0390 
 Transportation by Licensed Retailer from Licensed Wholesaler Premises 
 
 A licensed malt beverage or wine retailer may transport the malt beverages, cider, or wine the retailer purchases from a licensed wholesaler from the wholesaler’s premises to the retailer’s premises. The purchase price of such malt beverages, cider, or wine shall be the price listed pursuant to OAR 845-010-0210. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 471, including 471.030, 471.730(1) & (5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 471.305 & 471.398
