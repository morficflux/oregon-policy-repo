 Teacher Standards and Practices Commission 
 Chapter 584 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 COMMISSION SANCTION 
 584-050-0005 
 Criteria for Granting Licenses 
 
 (1) The Executive Director may issue licenses, certificates or registrations, grant reinstatements, and renew licenses, certificates or registrations when each of the following conditions exists:  (a) All requirements established by law and rules have been met;  (b) The applicant has attained at least eighteen years of age and has furnished evidence satisfactory to TSPC of fitness to serve as an educator; and  (c) The Executive Director deems that an applicant’s response to any application character questions does not adversely affect his or her ability to serve as an educator.  (2) The Executive Director may delay action and refer the application to an investigator when a response to any application character question needs further investigation. The results of the investigation will be reported to the Commission once the investigation is completed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 342 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 342.120 - 200 & 342.400
