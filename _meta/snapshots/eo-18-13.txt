    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-13


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    SUBSTATION FIRE IN SHERMAN AND WASCO COUNTIES


    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:


    The fire known as the “Substation Fire” is burning in Sherman and Wasco
    Counties.


    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Substation Fire is      |
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection   |
    was requested by Sherman County Fire Defense Board Chief Glenn Fluhr, Wasco               |
    County Fire Defense Board Chief Robert Palmer, and the Wasco County
    Commissioners. The State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510 ef seq., I have determined that a threat to life,
   ~ safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the Substation Fire in Sherman
    and Wasco Counties and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
    firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 10:21 p.m. on July 17, 2018, and I now confirm them
    with this Executive Order.


    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


    I.      The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
            Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to Substation Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
           Marshal.


    2.     This emergency is declared only for the Substation Fire threatening
           structures in Sherman and Wasco Counties.
