Oregon Department of Emergency Management

Chapter 104

Division 80
9-1-1 EMERGENCY COMMUNICATIONS  SYSTEM PROGRAM

104-080-0190
Funding the Emergency Communications System

(1) The Department distributes Emergency Communications Account monies to 9-1-1 jurisdictions quarterly, pursuant to ORS 403.240.

(2) The Department shall maintain a current list of 9-1-1 jurisdictions eligible to receive and expend distributed 9-1-1 tax funds from the Emergency Communications Account.

(3) The Department shall make payments for costs of the emergency communications system on behalf of a 9-1-1 jurisdiction as referenced in ORS 403.240 (3) (a), (b) and (c).

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 403.120; 2015 HB 2426
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 403 & 2015 HB 2426
