 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 SAFETY CODE FOR ELEVATORS 
 918-400-0620 
 General Policies Regarding Provisional Permits 
 
 (1) Provisional permits shall be issued only when requested by or agreed to by the contractor, owner or operator and the division, as relevant.  (2) When a provisional permit is issued, the division shall place documentation in the file that it considered whether any immediate hazard to health or safety existed and determined that none existed.  (3) Provisional permits shall be issued to allow corrections of items that do not meet code requirements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 460.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 460.055
