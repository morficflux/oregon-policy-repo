 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 LANDS: DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE LANDS 
 635-008-0117 
 Knight Park/Salmon River Access (Lincoln County)
 
 
 The Knight Park/Salmon River Access area is open to fish and wildlife oriented public use activities. In addition to the provisions in OAR 635-008-0200, the following restriction to public use of Department land at Knight Park/Salmon River Access applies: Parking is prohibited between 10 pm and 4 am. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
