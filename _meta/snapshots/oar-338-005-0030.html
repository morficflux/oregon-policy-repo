Oregon Health Authority

Health Licensing Office, Environmental Health Registration Board - Chapter 338

Division 5
REGISTRATION: GENERAL ADMINISTRATION

338-005-0030
Fees

(1) Applicants and registrants are subject to the provisions of OAR 331-010-0010 and 331-010-0020 regarding the payment of fees, penalties and charges.

(2) Fees established by the Health Licensing Office are as follows:

(a) Application:

(A) Registration — environmental health specialist: $150.

(B) Registration — waste water specialist: $150.

(C) Registration by reciprocity: $150.

(D) Trainee registration — environmental health specialist: $150.

(E) Trainee registration — waste water specialist: $150.

(b) Examinations:

(A) Oregon Environmental Health Specialist Trainee Laws and Rules exam: $50.

(B) Written — waste water specialist: $250.

(c) Original issuance of registration (including by reciprocity):

(A) Environmental health specialist: $150 for one year.

(B) Waste water specialist: $150 for one year.

(C) Trainee registration — environmental health specialist: $150 for one year.

(D) Trainee registration — waste water specialist: $150 for one year.

(d) Renewal of registration:

(A) Environmental health specialist: $150 for one year.

(B) Waste water specialist: $150 for one year.

(C) Environmental health specialist trainee extension: $150 (one-year increments)

(D) Waste water specialist trainee extension: $150 (one-year increments)

(e) Other administrative fees:

(A) Late renewal of registration: $40.

(B) Replacement registration, including name change: $25.

(C) Affidavit of Licensure: $50.

(D) Administrative processing fee: $25.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.560, ORS 700.005-700.240, ORS 700.995 & Senate Bill 844 (2025)
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.560, ORS 700.005-700.240, ORS 700.995 & Senate Bill 844 (2025)
