 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 82 OYSTER, CLAM AND MUSSEL LEASES 
 603-082-0080 
 Annual Fees and Taxes 
 
 (1) Persons using state lands for cultivating oysters, clams or mussels shall pay annual cultivation fees and use taxes quarterly to the department. Fees and taxes become delinquent 30 days after the end of the quarter. Use taxes shall be in the amount established by ORS 622.290(1).  (2) Annual cultivation fees and use taxes shall be assessed in lieu of property taxes, lease fees or rental charges for the use of lands upon which oysters, clams or mussels are grown and harvested. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 622.290 
 
 History:
