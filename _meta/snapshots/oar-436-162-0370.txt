 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 162 ELECTRONIC DATA INTERCHANGE; PROOF OF COVERAGE 
 436-162-0370 
 Proof of Coverage Terminations 
 
 When the insurer cancels coverage before the expiration of the policy term, the insurer must:  (1) Provide written notice to the employer under ORS 656.427(1) and (3);  (2) Retain a record of the written notice sent to the employer under ORS 656.427, and proof of mailing of that notice, for inspection by the division; and  (3) Provide notice to the director under ORS 656.427 by EDI. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  : ORS 656.264, 656.427 & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.427
