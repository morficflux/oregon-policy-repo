 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 36 VETERANS' EDUCATIONAL BRIDGE GRANT PROGRAM 
 274-036-0270 
 Waiver of Rules 
 
 Subject to the limitations of the law, and at its sole discretion, the Director of the Department of Veterans’ Affairs may waive all or part of these administrative rules. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406.005(4) & ORS 406.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 408.115 & ORS 408.125
