 Oregon Government Ethics Commission 
 Chapter 199 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 STATEMENT OF INTEREST 
 199-020-0020 
 Filing Deadlines
 
 
 Effective in 2016, Statements of Economic Interest electronically filed online with the Commission by April 15, regardless of whether the 15th falls on a holiday or weekend, are considered timely.  

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 244.290 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 244.050
