Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 51
GENERAL GAME BIRD REGULATIONS

635-051-0000
Purpose

(1) The purpose of these rules is to establish dates, areas and other restrictions for hunting game birds pursuant to ORS Chapter 496.

(2) The document entitled “2026-2027 Oregon Game Bird Hunting Regulations,” is incorporated by reference into these rules.

[Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
