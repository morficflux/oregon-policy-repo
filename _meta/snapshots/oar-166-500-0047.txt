 Division 500 OREGON ADMINISTRATIVE RULES FILING REQUIREMENTS 
 166-500-0047 
 Statutory Minor Correction (SMC) Filing Requirements 
 
 (1) SMC filings, in addition to the requirements outlined in ORS 183.335(7), must include the following components: 

 (a) Caption; 

 (b) Rule number and title; 

 (c) Final rule text, which must comply with OAR 166-500-0055; 

 (d) Associated statutory/other authority and statutes/other implemented, as defined in OAR 166-500-0010; 

 (e) Rule summary; and 

 (f) If applicable, a PDF of all rule attachments containing items such as charts, drawings, forms, graphs, maps, seals, tables, and other non-text information that is directly related to the rule and is not included in the rule text. See OAR 166-500-0060. 

 (2) Indicate the subsection(s) of ORS 183.335(7) that authorizes the change. 

 (3) Changes that are also applied through the statutory minor correction process are: 

 (a) Rule title changes; and 

 (b) Correcting a rule’s internal section numbering as allowed by ORS 183.360(2). 

 (4) An agency may file a statutory minor correction without first filing a notice of proposed rulemaking. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.355 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.360, ORS 183.355 & ORS 183.335
