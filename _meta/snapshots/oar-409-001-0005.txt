 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 409-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure
 
 
 The Oregon Health Authority, Health Policy and Analytics Division adopts the Attorney General’s Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure applicable to rulemaking, effective July 2016. 

 [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the Office of the Attorney General or the Oregon Health Authority.]
