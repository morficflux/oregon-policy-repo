 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 73 102"-WIDE COMMERCIAL VEHICLES — COMBINATIONS NOT SUBJECT TO OVERALL LENGTH RESTRICTIONS 
 734-073-0085 
 Statutory Weight Exemptions not Applicable to Interstate Highways 
 
 Except when operating under the terms of a variance permit, the weight exemptions authorized by ORS 818.030(8) and (9) do not apply to vehicles operating on any interstate highway. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 810.050, 818.030 & Ch. 510 & OL 1993 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.030 
 
 History:
