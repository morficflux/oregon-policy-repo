 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 COMMUNITY RIGHT-TO-KNOW SURVEY AND COMPLIANCE PROGRAMS 
 837-085-0060 
 Hazardous Substance Report — Substance Determinations
 
 
 A covered employer, owner, or operator must identify and evaluate all substances and wastes manufactured, generated, used, stored, possessed, or disposed of at their facilities to determine if they are reportable on the survey. The definition of hazardous substance in OAR 837-085-0040(31) shall be used. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.367 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.317
