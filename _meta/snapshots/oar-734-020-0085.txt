 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 TRAFFIC CONTROL 
 734-020-0085 
 Parking Regulations 
 
 (1) At defined areas requiring parking regulation, the Chief Engineer shall install signs using the legend “NO OVERNIGHT PARKING — PARKING (Between 1:00 a.m. and 5:00 a.m.) PROHIBITED.” Such signs shall be installed at locations visible to a driver and frequently enough at any one area to properly advise a driver of the parking restriction.  (2) Emergency parking shall be permitted in areas of regulated parking. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 366, 390 & 811 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 810.030 & 810.160
