 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 145 TREATMENTS OF SPECIFIC ASSETS 
 461-145-0175 
 Family Abuse Prevention Act (FAPA) Payments 
 
 (1) Family Abuse Prevention Act (FAPA) payments are court-ordered payments to victims of domestic violence made under authority of ORS 107.718(1)(h). A payment is considered available when actually received by the victim of abuse.  (2) For all programs covered by this chapter of rules, the first $2,500 is excluded. The excess above $2,500 is counted as a resource. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.816 & 412.049 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.816 & 412.049
