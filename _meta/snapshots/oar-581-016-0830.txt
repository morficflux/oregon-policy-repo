 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 16 OREGON SCHOOL FOR THE DEAF 
 581-016-0830 
 Related and Support Services 
 
 The Oregon School for the Deaf shall provide those related services which may be specified in a student’s Individualized Educational Program (IEP) in order for him/her to have access to educational program; i.e., such services as:  (1) Speech pathology and audiology;  (2) Psychological services;  (3) Physical therapy;  (4) Occupational therapy;  (5) Medical diagnostic services and specific health care; and  (6) Counseling and guidance. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343 & 346 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 346.010 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 EB 31-1989, f. & cert. ef. 11-2-89
