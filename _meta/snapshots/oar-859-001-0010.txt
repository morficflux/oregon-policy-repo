 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD RULEMAKING PROCEDURE 
 859-001-0010 
 Cost of Administrative Rules 
 
 Copies of administrative rules are available electronically at no charge on the Secretary of State’s website: http://sos.oregon.gov/archives/pages/oregon_administrative_rules.aspx. PSRB staff can also email copies of proposed administrative rules to interested parties. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161 & 183 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.387
