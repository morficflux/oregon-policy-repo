 Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board 
 Chapter 695 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 ENGAGEMENT GRANTS 
 695-015-0020 
 Definitions
 
 
 “Engagement Project” means a project whose purpose is to communicate and engage with landowners, organizations, and the community about the need for, feasibility, and benefits of a specific eligible restoration or acquisition project or program that leads to development of eligible projects within an identified geography. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 541.906 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541.956
