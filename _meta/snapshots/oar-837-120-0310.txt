 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 HAZARDOUS MATERIALS EMERGENCY RESPONSE SYSTEM 
 837-120-0310 
 Definitions
 
 
 (1) “Applicant” means any individual or organization considering itself capable of developing training programs as required in OAR 437-002-0100, 29 CFR 1910.120(q)(6)(iii) and (iv) that wishes to apply for State Fire Marshal approval of its program(s) under this rule. 

 (2) “Training Program” means an individual course, class, or instruction on a specific topic or subject area or a composite of individual courses, classes or instructions addressing all subject areas identified in OAR 837-120-0340(1). 

 (3) “Approval” and “Approved” means only that the State Fire Marshal has identified an applicant’s training program as having met the eligibility requirements identified in OAR 837-120-0320(1) for expenditure of state funds for training regional hazardous materials emergency response teams organized in accordance with ORS 453.374 and 453.380. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.367 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.374 - 453.390
