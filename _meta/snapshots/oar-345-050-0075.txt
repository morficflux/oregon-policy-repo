 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 RADIOACTIVE WASTE MATERIALS 
 345-050-0075 
 Alternate Technology 
 
 A person shall not locate a waste disposal facility at a site un­less there is no available alternative disposal technology that would better protect the health, safety, and welfare of the public and the environment. In making this finding, the Council shall con­sider proven, demonstrated technology, including but not limited to existing hazardous waste and radioactive waste disposal site technologies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.375
