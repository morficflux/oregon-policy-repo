 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 225 BOILERS AND PRESSURE VESSELS 
 918-225-0650 
 Practical Experience Requirement 
 
 Inspectors shall have at least the following practical experience in activities listed by ORS 480.565(1):  (1) Chief Inspector, ten years experience;  (2) Deputy or Special Inspector, experience required by the bylaws of the National Board for a commission as a National Board Inspector. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 480.565 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.565 
 
 History:
