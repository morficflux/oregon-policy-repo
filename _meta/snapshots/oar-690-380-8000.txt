 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 380 WATER RIGHT TRANSFERS 
 690-380-8000 
 Temporary Transfers
 
 
 Any person who holds a water use subject to transfer may request that the Department approve: 

 (1) For a period of not to exceed five years, a temporary transfer of the place of use and, if necessary to convey the water to the new temporary place of use, point of diversion or appropriation; or 

 (2) For a period of not to exceed 25 years, a temporary transfer in the Deschutes River Basin of place of use, type of use, and point of diversion or appropriation if necessary to convey water to the new temporary place of use, of all or a portion of a water right. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025 & ORS 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 536.050, ORS 540.520, ORS 540.523 & ORS 540.585
