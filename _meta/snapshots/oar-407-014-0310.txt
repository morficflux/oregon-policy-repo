 Department of Human Services 
 Chapter 407 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIALITY 
 407-014-0310 
 Information Access 
 
 The organization or user shall utilize the Department access control process for all requested and approved access. The Department shall notify the user of each approval or denial. When approved, the Department shall provide the user with a unique login identifier to access the network and information system or information asset. The Department may authorize the use of a generic login identifier.. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 182.122
