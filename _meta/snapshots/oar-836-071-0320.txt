 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 INSURANCE LICENSING 
 836-071-0320 
 Managing General Agents; Designation of Associations of Actuaries 
 
 For purposes of ORS 744.313, the Director determines the following associations of actuaries to have established adequate professional standards for membership:  (1) The American Academy of Actuaries.  (2) The Casualty Actuarial Society.  (3) The Society of Actuaries.  (4) The Canadian Institute of Actuaries. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244, 744.300, 744.306, 744.313 & 744.314 
 
 
 History:
