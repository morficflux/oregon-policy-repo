 Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying 
 Chapter 820 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 DEFINITIONS 
 820-005-0005 
 Board 
 
 "Board" means the Oregon State Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying provided by ORS 672.240. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 182.454, 182.456, 670.310, 672.240 & 672.255 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.002 - 372.325
