 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 LANDS: DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE LANDS 
 635-008-0100 
 Fish Lake Management Area: Harney County
 
 
 The Fish Lake Management Area is open to public use unless otherwise excluded or restricted by the following rules: 

 (1) Discharging of firearms is prohibited except as authorized during game bird and game mammal seasons. 

 (2) Camping is prohibited except on areas designated for that use. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
