 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 WORKERS WITH DISABILITIES PROGRAM 
 436-040-0002 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to establish guidelines for the administration of the Workers with Disabilities Program established to encourage the employment or re-employment of workers with disabilities. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.628, §286, ch. 70 & OL 2007
