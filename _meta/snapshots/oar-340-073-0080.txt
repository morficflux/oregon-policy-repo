 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 73 CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS 
 340-073-0080 
 Non-water-Carried Waste Disposal Facilities, Materials, and Construction: Construction of Gray Water Waste Disposal Sumps 
 
 A gray water waste disposal sump must consist of a receiving chamber, settling chamber, and either a seepage chamber or absorption trench. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 454.625 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 454.610, 454.615 & 454.775
