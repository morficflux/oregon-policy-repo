 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 PURCHASING GROUPS AND RISK RETENTION GROUPS 
 836-028-0013 
 Permitted Insurers 
 
 A purchasing group may not purchase insurance from a risk retention group or an insurer for members of the purchasing group in this state:  (1) Unless the risk retention group is registered in this state.  (2) Unless the insurer is authorized to transact insurance in this state or is an eligible nonadmitted insurer under ORS 735.415. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731, 735 & 746 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 735.335 
 
 History:
