 Health Licensing Office, Board of Cosmetology - Chapter 817 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 LICENSING AND BUSINESS OPERATIONS 
 817-007-0175 
 Application Requirements for Freelance Authorization 
 
 To obtain a freelance authorization a practitioner must meet the requirements of OAR 331 Division 30 and submit: 

 (1) A completed application form prescribed by the Office, which must contain the information listed in OAR 331-030-0000 and be accompanied by payment of the required fees. 

 (2) Proof of holding an active practitioner certificate. 

 (3) A current copy of the Assumed Business Name (ABN) filing if applicant is operating under an assumed business name when applying for a freelance authorization. An ABN is not required if business includes the real and true name of the owner. Refer to Secretary of State, Corporations Division under ORS 648. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 690.165, ORS 676.615 & ORS 690.123 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 690.123
