 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 FURBEARING AND UNPROTECTED MAMMAL REGULATIONS 
 635-050-0140 
 Raccoon Harvest Seasons
 
 
 (1) Open season: November 15 through March 15 of the following year. 

 (2) Open area: Entire state. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
