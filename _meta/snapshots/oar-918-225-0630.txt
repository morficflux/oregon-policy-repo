 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 225 BOILERS AND PRESSURE VESSELS 
 918-225-0630 
 Special Fee for Dryer Permits 
 
 The operating permit fee for dryer rolls for a paper machine is $5.50 per roll, if the machine is under the inspection requirements of ORS 480.600(2). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 480.600 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.600
