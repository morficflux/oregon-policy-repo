 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 33 AGRICULTURAL LAND 
 660-033-0145 
 Agriculture/Forest Zones
 
 
 (1) Agriculture/forest zones may be established and uses allowed pursuant to OAR 660-006-0050; 

 (2) Land divisions in agriculture/forest zones may be allowed as provided for under OAR 660-006-0055; and 

 (3) Land may be replanned or rezoned to an agriculture/forest zone pursuant to OAR 660-006-0057.; and 

 (4) A county in eastern Oregon shall apply either OAR chapter 660, division 6 or 33 standards for siting a photovoltaic solar power generation facility in an agriculture/forest zone based on the predominant use of the tract on January 1, 2024. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, ORS 197.040, ORS 197.230 & ORS 197.245 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.040, ORS 197.213, ORS 197.215, ORS 197.230, ORS 197.245, ORS 197.283, ORS 197.700, ORS 197.705, ORS 197.720, ORS 197.740, ORS 197.750 & ORS 197.780
