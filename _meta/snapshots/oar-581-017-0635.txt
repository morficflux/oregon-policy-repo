 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0635 
 Reporting: SSA Summer Program Grant 
 
 Each year, a recipient of an SSA Summer Program Grant must report on the grant in the manner and form required by the Department. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 327.341 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 327.341 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ODE 3-2020, adopt filed 03/20/2020, effective 03/20/2020
