 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 MODEL HIGHER EDUCATION COMMUNITY SERVICE VOUCHER PROGRAM 
 575-085-0020 
 Administration 
 
 (1) Role of the Commission:  (a) Development of appropriate administrative rules and procedures;  (b) Allocation of funds to institutions;  (c) Approval of organizations where participants will be placed and of services which participants will perform;  (d) Reimbursement of participating institutions for vouchers redeemed by participants at that institution.  (2) Role of Participating Institutions:  (a) Allocation of vouchers among various academic departments within the institution;  (b) Selection of participants;  (c) Placement of participants in authorized service opportunities;  (d) Redemption of vouchers presented by participants;  (e) Awarding of academic credit for services performed;  (f) Record keeping and reporting. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  SB 81, Sec. 40 - 44, & 128(9) & 1993 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.427 - 348.433 
 
 History:
