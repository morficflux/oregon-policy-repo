 Occupational Therapy Licensing Board 
 Chapter 339 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 IMPLEMENTATION AND ADMINISTRATION OF THE OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY PRACTICE ACT 
 339-010-0015 
 License Examinations Approved by the Board 
 
 (1) Pursuant to ORS 675.240(1)(b) and (2), the Board adopts the current certification examination and passing score of the National Board For Certification in Occupational Therapy as the examination to determine the fitness of applicants for practice as an occupational therapist.  (2) Pursuant to ORS 675.250(3), the Board approves the current certification examination and passing score of the National Board For Certification in Occupational Therapy as the examination to determine the fitness of applicants for practice as an occupational therapy assistant. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.230, 675.240, 675.250, 675.300 & 675.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.210(4), 675.240(1) & (2), 675.250(2) & (3), 675.300(1)(a) & 675.320(11)
