 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 306 QUALIFYING ELECTRICAL PRODUCTS FOR SALE OR DISPOSAL IN OREGON 
 918-306-0530 
 Fees and Procedures 
 
 (1) Applicants requesting product approval by special deputies shall pay hourly fees in OAR 918-309-0070 for “other inspections” or any successor rule. If it is determined by the division testing or evaluation through a laboratory or engineering firm is needed, the person requesting special deputy certification shall pay the company doing the testing or evaluation directly.  (2) Inspection fees shall be paid prior to inspection. If the exact amount cannot be determined in advance, an amount sufficient to pay the estimated fee shall be deposited with the division prior to inspection. Any portion of the deposit not required for the fee shall be refunded to the applicant upon written request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.760 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.760
