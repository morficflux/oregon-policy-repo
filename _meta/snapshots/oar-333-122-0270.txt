 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 122 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR INDUSTRIAL X-RAY MACHINE OPERATIONS 
 333-122-0270 
 General Requirements for All RGD: Location of Documents and Records 
 
 Each registrant must maintain current copies of the following documents and records sufficient to demonstrate compliance at each facility: 

 (1) A current radiation machine certificate of validation; 

 (2) Records of equipment problems identified in daily checks and six months inspections of equipment as required by OAR 333-122-0230; 

 (3) Records of alarm system and entrance control checks required by OAR 333-122-0320. 

 (4) Records of dosimeter readings as required by OAR 333-122-0190. 

 (5) Operating and emergency procedures as required by OAR 333-122-0140. 

 (6) Radiation survey instrument calibration records for instruments used at the site as required by OAR 333-122-0210; and 

 (7) Survey records required by OAR 333-122-0226 for the period of operation at the site. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807
