 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Potato Commission - Chapter 658 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ASSESSMENTS 
 658-010-0040 
 Penalties 
 
 (1) In addition to the penalties prescribed in ORS 576.991, any person who delays transmittal of funds beyond the time set by a commodity commission shall pay a penalty of 10 percent of the amount due and shall also pay one and one-half percent interest per month on the unpaid balance of the assessment. A commission may waive the penalty and interest upon showing of good cause.  (2) If any person responsible for the transmittal of assessment moneys to a commodity commission fails to relinquish assessment moneys collected, the person shall pay a penalty equal to twice the amount of the unrelinquished assessment moneys. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576.355 and ORS576.365 
 
 History:
