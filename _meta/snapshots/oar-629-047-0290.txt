 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 47 ENFORCEMENT POLICY 
 629-047-0290 
 Enforcement Policy for ORS 477.993(3) — Unauthorized Entry Into a Fire Scene 
 
 (1) A Fire Prevention Warning shall be issued for any unauthorized entry into a fire scene secured in accordance with ORS 477.365(1)(f), if the violator entered the secured area by accident or unintentionally.  (2) A uniform citation for a Class A Misdemeanor shall be issued and processed for any unauthorized entry into a fire scene secured in accordance with ORS 477.365(1)(f), if the violator entered the secured area intentionally or after having been warned to avoid entry. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 477.980 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 477.365(1)(f) & 477.993(3) 
 
 History:
