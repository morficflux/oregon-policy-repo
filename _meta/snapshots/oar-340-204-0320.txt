 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 204 DESIGNATION OF AIR QUALITY AREAS 
 340-204-0320 
 Priority Sources 
 
 For the purposes of division 224, priority sources are identified as follows:  (1) In the Lakeview sustainment area, uncertified residential wood fuel-fired devices. The offset values for replacement of uncertified residential wood fuel-fired devices are specified in OAR 340-240-0560.  (2) In any other area, DEQ may identify priority sources during a specific permit action based on the sources addressed in the emission reduction strategies that were included in the attainment or maintenance plans for the area. The offset value for priority sources identified under this section must be determined by DEQ. The offset values for replacement of uncertified residential wood fuel-fired devices in OAR 340-240-0560 may only be used if DEQ determines that the values reasonably apply to the geographical area in question.  NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025 & 468A.035
