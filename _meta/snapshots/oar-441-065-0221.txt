 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 REGISTRATION OF SECURITIES 
 441-065-0221 
 Application and Information Requirements for a SOAR Offering 
 
 (1) An application to register securities filed under the SOAR Offering shall include the following: 

 (a) A completed Form 440-2013 (a form for the registration of limited or non-public offerings); 

 (b) A copy of the written disclosure materials required by OAR 441-065-0222; 

 (c) A non-refundable registration fee as set in OAR 441-065-0001; 

 (d) Unless the offering is being sold through a licensed broker-dealer or by a person exempt from the licensing requirements, at least one completed salesperson application on FINRA Form U-4 and a per person non-refundable license fee as set in OAR 441-175-0002; 

 (e) An escrow, or similar arrangement, agreement; 

 (f) Pro forma financial statements that include a description and documentation of any material assumptions and demonstrate when the issuer expects to be profitable; and 

 (g) Any additional or supplementary materials requested by the Director. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 59.065, 59.085 & 59.285 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 59.065 & 59.085 
 
 History:
