 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2800 
 Termination of Eligibility — EI/ECSE 
 
 (1) A school district must evaluate a child with a disability in accordance with OAR 581-015-2775 and 2790 before determining that the child is no longer a child with a disability.  (2) The contractor or subcontractor must provide written notice under OAR 581-015-2745 when a team determines that a child is no longer eligible for EI or ECSE services. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.475 & 343.513 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 343.475 & 343.513
