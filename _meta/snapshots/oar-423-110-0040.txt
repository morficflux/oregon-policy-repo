 Oregon Department of Education 
 Youth Development Division - Chapter 423 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 Community Investment 
 423-110-0040 
 Site Visits 
 
 If practicable, the division shall make a reasonable effort to visit a site where activities funded by community investments are conducted at least once a biennium 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 417.847 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 417.847 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 YDD 2-2018, adopt filed 11/05/2018, effective 11/05/2018
