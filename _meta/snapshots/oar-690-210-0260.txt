 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 210 WELL CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS 
 690-210-0260 
 Openings in the Casing 
 
 There shall be no opening in the casing wall between the top of the casing and the bottom of the required casing seal except for pitless adapters, measurement access ports, and grout nipples installed in conformance with these standards. In no case shall holes be cut in the casing wall for the purpose of lifting or lowering casing into the well bore unless such holes are properly welded closed and watertight prior to placement into the well bore. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
