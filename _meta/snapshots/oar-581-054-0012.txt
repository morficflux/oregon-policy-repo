 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 54 EXTRA-CURRICULAR SCHOOL TRANSPORTATION GRANTS 
 581-054-0012 
 Application Process 
 
 (1) The Oregon Department of Education shall award all available moneys available for the School Transportation grants each year. 

 (a) The Department shall notify eligible applicants of the application process and due dates, and make available necessary guidelines and application forms. 

 (b) The application will be available on the first day of September each year. 

 (c) Applications will be due by the 15th day of October each year. 

 (2)(a) Applications must include the total amount of eligible transportation costs. 

 (b) If a district chooses to transfer a portion of their grant funds to a public charter the charter school must provide to the district an accounting of the total amount of eligible student transportation costs for which the charter school received grant funds, and the district must include this in their application. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Sec. 2, Ch. 62, OL 2018 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Sec. 2, Ch. 62, OL 2018 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ODE 40-2018, adopt filed 12/10/2018, effective 12/10/2018
