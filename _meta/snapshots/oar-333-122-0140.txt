 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 122 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR INDUSTRIAL X-RAY MACHINE OPERATIONS 
 333-122-0140 
 General Requirements for All RGD: Operating and Emergency Procedures 
 
 (1) Operating and emergency procedures must include instructions in the following when required by the authority: 

 (a) Appropriate use of radiation machines so that no person is likely to be exposed to radiation doses in excess of the limits established in division 120; 

 (b) Methods and occasions for conducting radiation surveys; 

 (c) Methods for posting and controlling access to radiation areas; 

 (d) Individual monitoring program to include operations of the monitoring equipment; 

 (e) The inspection, maintenance, and operability checks of radiation machines and survey instruments; 

 (f) Steps that must be taken immediately by X-ray operators in the event a pocket dosimeter is found to be off-scale or electronic personal dosimeter reads greater than 2 milliseiverts (200mrem); 

 (g) The procedure(s) for identifying and reporting defects and noncompliance, as required by OAR 333-122-0230; 

 (h) The procedure for notifying appropriate person(s) in the event of an accident or incident; and 

 (i) Maintenance of records. 

 (2) The registrant must maintain copies of current operating and emergency procedures in accordance with OAR 333-122-0151, OAR 333-122-0251 and OAR 333-122-0270. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807
