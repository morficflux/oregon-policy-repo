 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 53 ELDERLY RENTAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (ERA) 
 813-053-0030 
 Applicant Eligibility
 
 
 (1) ERA services shall be made available to very low income elderly households that are homeless or unstably housed. 

 (2) A household member’s duration of Oregon residency or immigrant status  shall not be considered as part of the eligibility criteria. 

 (3) Specific requirements and documentation to provide proof of eligibility for ERA services are detailed in the SHF Manual. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 458.375 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  458.620 & 458.650
