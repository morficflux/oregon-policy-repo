 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 190 EMPLOYMENT PROGRAM COMPONENTS 
 461-190-0401 
 JOBS Plus Program Applicability
 
 
 The JOBS Plus program (see OAR 461-001-0025) is a component (see OAR 461-001-0025) of the JOBS program and is subject to the provisions of ORS chapter 411 and the following rules (OAR 461-190-0401 to 461-190-0426). Except as provided under OAR 461-190-0416, individuals who participate in the JOBS Plus program (participants) become employed and receive wages from a JOBS Plus employer rather than cash benefits from the TANF program, SNAP benefits, and unemployment insurance benefits. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060 & 411.816 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060
