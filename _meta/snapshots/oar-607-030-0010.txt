 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Tall Fescue Commission - Chapter 607 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 COMMISSION MAKE UP, TERMS, QUALIFICATIONS,  TERM LIMITS, REMOVAL FROM OFFICE 
 607-030-0010 
 Number of Commissioners, Terms
 
 
 The Oregon Tall Fescue Commission will consist of nine (9) members appointed by the Director of the Oregon Department of Agriculture for a term of three (3) years or, if for a term following a prior term for that person, ending three (3) years from the date of expiration of any prior term. The initial term of office for producer 7 will end June 30, 2020; thereafter, the length of term will be three (3) years. No commissioner will serve for more than 2 consecutive, full terms of office. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 and HB 3401 (2019) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576 and HB 3401 (2019)
