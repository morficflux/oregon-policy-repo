 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 MOTOR CARRIER ELECTRONIC COMMERCE AND ONLINE TRANSACTIONS 
 740-015-0010 
 Purpose
 
 
 Chapter 740, division 15 rules are adopted by CCD to: 

 (1) Specify the procedures and requirements under which a motor carrier may, by electronic means, conduct business with CCD; and 

 (2) Promote the development and use of electronic transactions between CCD and its customers while providing service that is efficient, secure, convenient and responsive. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 802.012 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 802.012
