 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 61 THIRD PARTY TESTING FOR CLASS C DRIVER LICENSE 
 735-061-0450 
 Eligibility Check and Results Entry 
 
 (1) Prior to administering the drive test, the third party examiner must use DMV’s website, in accordance with OAR 735-018-0020, to verify the drive test applicant meets all requirements set forth in OAR 735-061-0420. 

 (2) If the applicant does not meet qualifications for testing as set forth in OAR 735-061-0420, the third party examiner must refer the applicant to DMV and must not conduct the drive test. 

 (3) If the applicant meets qualifications for testing as set forth in OAR 735-061-0420, the third party examiner may administer the test. 

 (4) After completing the drive test, the third party examiner must: 

 (a) Provide the applicant with a receipt for the test; 

 (b) Enter the drive test results using electronic means determined by DMV within 24 hours of the completion of the test; and 

 (c) Comply with provisions set forth in OAR 735-018-0020 for electronic transactions. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 802.010, 802.012 & 802.600 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 802.012 & 802.600
