 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS 
 635-065-0730 
 Shooting Hours 
 
 It is unlawful to hunt any game mammals from one-half hour after sunset to one-half hour before sunrise. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496
