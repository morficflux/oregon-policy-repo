 Oregon Health Authority 
 Equity and Inclusion Division - Chapter 950 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 TRADITIONAL HEALTH WORKERS 
 950-060-0000 
 Purpose
 
 
 These rules establish the criteria for training, certification, and enrollment of traditional health workers (THW) in a registry maintained by the Oregon Health Authority (Authority). THWs include community health workers, personal health navigators, peer wellness specialists, peer support specialists, and birth doulas not otherwise regulated or certified by the State of Oregon. These rules also establish curriculum requirements and procedures for Authority approval of programs seeking to train Oregon’s traditional health workers. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042, 414.635 & 414.665 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  414.665 & ORS 414.635
