 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 VEHICLES, DRIVER, EQUIPMENT: EQUIPMENT REQUIRED AND CONDITION OF VEHICLES 
 740-100-0045 
 North American Standard Level I and V Commercial Vehicle Inspection Procedures 
 
 Inspections must be performed by North American Standard Level I certified inspectors. The term “Level 1 certified inspectors” means the government employee conducting inspections must meet the certification requirements found in Title 49, Code of Federal Regulations, Part 385, Subpart C. Level I and V commercial vehicle inspections must be conducted in accordance with the North American Standard Inspection Procedures and all applicable policies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 825.232 & 825.252 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 810.560, 825.210 & 825.250
