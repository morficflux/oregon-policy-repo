 Oregon Department of Education 
 Fair Dismissal Appeals Board - Chapter 586 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 586-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 183.341, the Board adopts the Attorney General's Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, in effect on January 1, 2012, except those rules pertaining to contested cases.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Department of Education.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
