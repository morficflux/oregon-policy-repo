 Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying 
 Chapter 820 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 REGULATION 
 820-015-0026 
 Failure to Comply with Continuing Professional Development Requirements 
 
 Any registrant who fails to satisfy the Continuing Professional Development requirements in OAR 820-010-0635 will be subject to suspension of or refusal to renew the registrant's license. Failure to satisfy the Continuing Professional Development requirements will not be the sole basis for license revocation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 672.255 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.002 - 672.325
