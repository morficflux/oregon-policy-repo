 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 UTILITY REGULATION 
 860-021-0000 
 Applicability of Division 21 
 
 The rules contained in this division apply to energy utilities and large telecommunications utilities, as defined in OAR 860-021-0008. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040, 759.030, 759.040 & 759.045
