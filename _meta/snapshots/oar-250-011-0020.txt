 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 STEERING AND SAILING RULES 
 250-011-0020 
 General; Division Application 
 
 (1) This division applies to all vessels upon waters of this state shoreward of the navigational demarcation lines dividing the high seas from harbors, rivers, and other inland waters.  (2) All vessels operating on the high seas seaward of the navigational demarcation lines fall under the authority of the International Rules, 72 COLREGS, as amended November 19, 1989. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830.110 & ORS 830.175 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.335 & ORS 830.340 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSMB 5-2015, f. 6-26-15, cert. ef. 7-1-15
