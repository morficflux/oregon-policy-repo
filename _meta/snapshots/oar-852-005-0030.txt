 Board of Optometry 
 Chapter 852 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 BOARD ADMINISTRATION 
 852-005-0030 
 Contracting
 
 
 The Oregon Board of Optometry adopts by reference portions of the State of Oregon’s contracting policies. These rules are contained in the Oregon Administrative Rules, chapter 125, divisions 20, 300. The Board’s financial policies will govern any other contracting activities. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.350 & 683 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 670.350 Sec. 3 (4) & ORS 182.460
