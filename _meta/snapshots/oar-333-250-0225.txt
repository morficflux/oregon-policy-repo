 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 250 AMBULANCE SERVICE LICENSING 
 333-250-0225 
 Denial of License Application
 
 
 If the Oregon Health Authority intends to deny a license application, it shall issue a Notice of Proposed Denial of License Application in accordance with ORS chapter 183. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 682.017 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 682.017 - 682.117 & 682.991
