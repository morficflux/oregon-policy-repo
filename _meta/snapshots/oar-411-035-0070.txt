 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 K-STATE PLAN ANCILLARY SERVICES 
 411-035-0070 
 Eligibility for Consumer Transition Services 
 
 (1) Eligibility for transition services covered through the K-State Plan are restricted to consumers transitioning from a nursing facility or the Oregon State Hospital, as defined in OAR 309-091-0005(16), into a community-based or in-home program.  (2) Consumers transitioning from an acute care hospital directly to a community-based or in-home program are not eligible for transition services under this rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.050, 410.040, 410.090, 410.210 to 410.300 & 441.520
