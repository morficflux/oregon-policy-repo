 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 STUDENT INVESTMENT ACCOUNT 
 581-014-9999 
 Definitions for Student Investment Account: Third Grade Reading Proficiency 
 
 “Third-grade reading proficiency rate” means the percentage of students in the third grade who are determined to be proficient or above based on Oregon’s statewide summative assessments in English Language Arts (ELA). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Sec. 10, Ch. 122, OL 2019 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Sec. 10, Ch. 122, OL 2019 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ODE 152-2019, adopt filed 12/16/2019, effective 12/16/2019
