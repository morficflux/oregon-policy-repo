 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 680 RESOURCE SITE INVENTORY AND PROTECTION PROCESS RULES 
 629-680-0300 
 Purpose 
 
 ORS 527.710(3)(a)(D) requires the Board to identify and protect significant wetlands. The purpose of these rules is to identify significant wetlands types on forestlands. Significant wetlands require site-specific protection and written plans. Protection rules for significant wetlands are in OAR 629-645-0000 through 629-645-0050. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 527.710 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 527.715
