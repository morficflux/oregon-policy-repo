 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 COUNSELING AND TREATMENT SERVICES 
 413-060-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules define eligibility criteria for family planning services, describe the services to be provided, and the responsibilities SOSCF has to provide these services. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  HB 2004 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 435.205 - 435.235 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SCF 6-1995, f. 12-22-95, cert. ef. 12-29-95
