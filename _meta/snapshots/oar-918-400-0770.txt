 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 SAFETY CODE FOR ELEVATORS 
 918-400-0770 
 Withdrawal of Exceptions 
 
 (1) Exceptions granted under ORS 460.085(3) can be withdrawn if reasonable safety can no longer be maintained:  (a) Because of changes of facts, use or code requirements; or  (b) If the elevator is placed out of service for longer than one year.  (2) The elevator owner or operator shall be notified of an intent to withdraw an exception and of a right to a contested case hearing upon request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 460.085 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 460.085
