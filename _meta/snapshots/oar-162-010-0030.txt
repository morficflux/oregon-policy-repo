 Audits Division - Chapter 162 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 MINIMUM STANDARDS FOR AUDITS OF OREGON MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS 
 162-010-0030 
 Contracts
 
 
 Municipal Audit Law requires audits be performed pursuant to a contract between the independent accountant and the governing body, or managing or executive officer, of the municipal corporation. Laws governing contracts are prescribed in the Orego n Public Contracting Code, ORS Chapters 279A, 279B, and 279C. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 297.465 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 297.465
