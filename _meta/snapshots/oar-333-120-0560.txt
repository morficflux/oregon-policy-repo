 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION 
 333-120-0560 
 Waste Disposal: Compliance with Environmental and Health Protection Regulations 
 
 Nothing in chapter 333 divisions 100 through 123 relieves the licensee or registrant from complying with other applicable Federal, State, and local regulations or rules governing any other toxic or hazardous properties of materials that may be disposed of under division 333-120. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
