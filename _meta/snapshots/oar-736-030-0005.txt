 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 SPECIAL REGULATIONS AFFECTING OCEAN SHORES ADJACENT TO COASTAL CITIES 
 736-030-0005 
 Prohibition of Horses and Other Livestock 
 
 Horses and other livestock are prohibited on the ocean shore between the south city limits of Rockaway and northward to the mouth of the Nehalem River in Tillamook County. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.124 & 390.660 
 
 History:
