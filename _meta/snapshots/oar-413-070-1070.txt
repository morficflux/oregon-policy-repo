 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 SUBSTITUTE CARE 
 413-070-1070 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 413-070-1070 to 413-070-1090 is to describe the requirements and responsibilities of the Department when placing a child or young adult who is in the care or custody of the Department in a congregate care residential setting. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 & ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 419B.354 
 
 History:
