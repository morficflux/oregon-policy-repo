 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 POLYCHLORINATED BIPHENYLS (PCBS) 
 340-110-0077 
 Permits 
 
 (1) The procedures and requirements of divisions 105 and 120 shall be followed by an applicant for a PCB disposal facility permit.  (2) The procedures of divisions 106 and 120 will be followed when issuing permits required by this Division. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 459 & 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.275 & 466.295
