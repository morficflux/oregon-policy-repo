 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 91 OREGON BUSINESS RETENTION AND EXPANSION PROGRAM 
 123-091-0001 
 Purpose 
 
 This division of administrative rules clarifies, specifies and establishes procedures, standards and criteria for operation of and making loans to businesses from the Oregon Business Retention and Expansion Program (OBEP). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075, 285B & OL Ch. 549, Sec. 1-8 & 10-11 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285B, OL Ch. 549, Sec. 1-8 & 10-11 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OBDD 4-2012, f. 3-30-12, cert. ef. 4-2-12
