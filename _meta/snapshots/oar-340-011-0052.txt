 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 RULES OF GENERAL APPLICABILITY AND ORGANIZATION 
 340-011-0052 
 Rulemaking: Temporary Rules 
 
 The Commission may adopt temporary rules and file the same, along with supportive findings, pursuant to ORS 183.335(5) and 183.355(2) and the Attorney General’s Model rule OAR 137-001-0080. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.025 & 183.335
