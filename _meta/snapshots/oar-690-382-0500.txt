 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 382 GROUNDWATER REGISTRATION MODIFICATIONS 
 690-382-0500 
 Fee Refunds
 
 
 If an applicant for a modification to change the point of appropriation abandons the original well identified in the claim according to well construction standards within one year of the Department's recognition of the modification, the applicant may request a refund of $100 of the fee paid under OAR 690-382-0400(18)(b). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025, ORS 536.027 & ORS 537.610 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 537.610 & ORS 540.505-540.532
