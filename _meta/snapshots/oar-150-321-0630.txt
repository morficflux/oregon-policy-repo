 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 321 TIMBER TAXES 
 150-321-0630 
 Establishing the Identity of the Taxpayer for Severance Tax 
 
 For purposes of establishing the identity of the taxpayer for the severance tax, OAR 150-321-0010 applies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 & 321.609 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 321.700
