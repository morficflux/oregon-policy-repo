 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 67 IMPLEMENTING THE INSTITUTIONAL CONSERVATION PROGRAM 
 330-067-0055 
 Requests for TA Grants 
 
 Institutions shall submit a completed Request for Services Form. If ODOE determines the institution is eligible for a TA grant, the institution shall:  (1) Complete and submit an EUE for each building for which a TA grant is requested;  (2) Complete and submit an ODOE Partnership Agreement; and  (3) Any other information required by ODOE or which the applicant wants considered, such as a hardship request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.040 & 469.165 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469
