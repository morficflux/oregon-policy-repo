 Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology 
 Chapter 335 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 LICENSING 
 335-060-0050 
 License Certificate 
 
 A licensee shall not make any alteration on official documents issued by the board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 681.420 & 681.460 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 681.250 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SPA 1-2001, f. & cert. ef. 3-12-01
