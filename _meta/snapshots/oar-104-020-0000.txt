 Oregon Department of Emergency Management 
 Chapter 104 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 EARTHQUAKE PREPAREDNESS DRILLS 
 104-020-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of the rule is to provide guidance for all private, state, local government agencies and non-governmental employers with 250 or more full-time employees to conduct mandatory annual earthquake drills for employees located within office buildings. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Ch. 366 & OL 2001 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Ch. 366 & OL 2001
