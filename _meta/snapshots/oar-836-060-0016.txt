 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 INSURANCE POLICIES (ORS CHAPTER 743) 
 836-060-0016 
 Policy Forms and Related Material 
 
 (1) Permissible Forms. Credit insurance shall be issued only in the forms described in ORS 743.373.  (2) Rates and Forms Filing Requirements. All credit insurance policy forms, certificates of insurance, notices of proposed insurance, applications for insurance, endorsements and riders to be delivered or issued for delivery in this state and the schedules of premium rates pertaining thereto shall be filed with the Director as required by ORS 743.015. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731, 742, 743 & 746 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 743.015 & 743.373
