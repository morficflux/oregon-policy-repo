 Board of Geologist Examiners 
 Chapter 809 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 RENEWALS, RESTORATIONS 
 809-015-0010 
 Nonrestoration 
 
 (1) After five consecutive years of non-renewal, a registration can no longer be restored.  (2) A person past the 5 year restoration window must re-apply as a new applicant, meeting all current Board requirements for registration. This includes but is not limited to passage of national and specialty examination required by the Board unless previously passed as verified by the Board.  (3) Upon application to the Board for a new registration, a previous registration number shall not be reinstated. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 672.585 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.585
