 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-6765 
 Adaptive Management 
 
 (1) The Department shall use adaptive management principles within its statutory authority in support of achieving the expectations outlined in the guiding principles of this plan.  (2) The Commission will monitor implementation of the plan with an initial review in 2014 and undertake a comprehensive review at the end of the transition period. If the guiding principles are not being met, efforts will be made to determine why and the Commission will direct the Department to identify and evaluate alternative or additional management actions necessary to meet the principles. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & 506.129 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DFW 152-2012, f. 12-27-12, cert. ef. 1-1-13
