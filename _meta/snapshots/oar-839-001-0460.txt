 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 WAGE COLLECTION MATTERS 
 839-001-0460 
 Payment in Case of a Dispute over the Amount of Wages Due at Termination of Employment/Employee Rights and Remedies 
 
 (1) In case of a dispute over the amount of wages due and payable at termination of employment, the employer shall pay the amount of wages that the employer concedes to be due and payable to the employee. Such payment shall be made unconditionally and within the time set out in OAR 839-001-0420, 839-001-0430 and 839-001-0440.  (2) An employee may use all remedies available to the employee for the balance of wages that the employee claims are due and payable. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 652.165 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 652.160
