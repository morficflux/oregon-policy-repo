 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 47 PRIVATE HUNTING PRESERVE RULES 
 635-047-0005 
 Time, Place, and Manner of Hunting Privately Owned Game Birds 
 
 (1) The time and length of season allowed any given hunting preserve shall be determined on the basis of potential conflict with wild populations, provided that no open season shall be allowed between April 1 and July 31 of any year.  (2) The manner of taking shall comply with Oregon Laws and be consistent with recognized standards. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.112, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.112, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
