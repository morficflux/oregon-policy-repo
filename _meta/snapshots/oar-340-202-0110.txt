 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 202 AMBIENT AIR QUALITY STANDARDS AND PREVENTION OF SIGNIFICANT DETERIORATION INCREMENTS 
 340-202-0110 
 Ambient Air Quality Standards: Particle Fallout 
 
 The particle fallout rate as measured by an Oregon standard method at a location approved by DEQ must not exceed:  (1) 10 grams per square meter per month in an industrial area.  (2) 5.0 grams per square meter per month in an industrial area if visual observations show a presence of wood waste or soot and the volatile fraction of the sample exceeds 70 percent.  (3) 5.0 grams per square meter per month in residential and commercial areas.  (4) 3.5 grams per square meter per month in residential and commercial areas if visual observations show the presence of wood waste or soot and the volatile fraction of the sample exceeds 70 percent. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025 & 468A.035
