 Oregon Board of Physical Therapy 
 Chapter 848 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 BOARD ADMINISTRATION 
 848-005-0005 
 Name of Board 
 
 As used in OAR 848, “Physical Therapist Licensing Board” has the same meaning as “Oregon Board of Physical Therapy”. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2019 Oregon Laws Chapter 43, 2019; HB2092 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2019 Oregon Laws Chapter 43, 2019; HB2092 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PTLB 5-2019, adopt filed 12/20/2019, effective 01/01/2020
