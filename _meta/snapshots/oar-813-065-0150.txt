 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 MANUFACTURED DWELLING PARKS AND MARINAS 
 813-065-0150 
 Notice of Tax Credit Eligibility Upon Closure of Park; Sample Form
 
 
 For the notice of tax credit and right to appeal that a facility owner(s) or landlord's agent(s) of a facility is required by ORS 90.650 to give to a tenant when a facility is closed, the MMCRC establishes the sample form designated for the notice on the Department’s website. The sample form is also available upon request from the Department. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 90.650 & 90.645 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 90.650 & 90.645
