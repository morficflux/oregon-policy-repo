 Division 80 LOWER WILLAMETTE RIVER MANAGEMENT PLAN 
 141-080-0105 
 Lower Willamette River Management Plan 
 
 The 1992 Lower Willamette River Management Plan as promulgated by the State Land Board and the Division of State Lands is hereby adopted by reference.  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]
