 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 WATER SUPPLY WELL CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS: INTRODUCTION, GENERAL STANDARDS, AND DEFINITIONS 
 690-200-0025 
 Special Area Standards 
 
 If at any time, the Commission finds that different or supplemental standards are required for the safe development of ground water from any aquifer or area, special area standards for the construction and maintenance of water supply wells within such areas may be adopted as rules by the Commission. In the absence of such special area standards, these rules constitute the sole administrative standards of the Water Resources Department governing construction, conversion, maintenance, alteration, and abandonment of water supply wells. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027, 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795
