 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 113 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR WIRELINE SERVICE OPERATIONS AND SUB-SURFACE TRACER STUDIES 
 333-113-0310 
 Precautionary Procedures in Logging and Subsurface Tracer Studies: Subsurface Tracer Studies 
 
 (1) Protective gloves and other appropriate protective clothing and equipment must be used by all personnel handling radioactive tracer material. Precautions must be taken to avoid ingestion or inhalation of radioactive materials.  (2) No licensee shall cause the injection of radioactive material into potable aquifers without prior written authorization from the Authority. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
