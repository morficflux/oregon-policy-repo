 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 FOREST ACTIVITIES 
 437-007-1335 
 Vehicle Operation 
 
 (1) The operation of vehicles must comply with the requirements of OAR 437-007-0520 through 437-007-0570.  (2) All equipment hauled on a vehicle must be adequately secured when the vehicle is in motion.  (3) Vehicles must be brought to a full stop before personnel disembark. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSHA 2-2005, f. 5-27-05, cert. ef. 6-1-05
