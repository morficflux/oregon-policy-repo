 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 910 ELECTROLOGY 
 331-910-0045 
 Practical Examination Retake Requirements
 
 
 A student or trainee may retake a practical examination through a HECC OCS authorized to teach the Electrology Approved Curriculum listed under OAR 331-910-0005 or by a supervisor who is authorized to teach the Training Program listed in OAR 331-910-0007. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  676.568, 676.615, 690.350, 690.365, 690.370 & 690.405 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  690.350, 690.365, 690.370 & 690.405
