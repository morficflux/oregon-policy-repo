 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 250 BIAS PROVIDERS CONTRACTING WITH PUBLIC BODIES - DISCLOSURES AND DETERMINATIONS 
 860-250-0005 
 Applicability and Waiver 
 
 (1) These rules apply to broadband Internet access service providers engaged in the provision of broadband Internet access service to a public body, as set forth in Oregon Laws 2018, Chapter 88, Section 1(5), and any participant in a proceeding involving a determination made by the Commission under Oregon Laws 2018, Chapter 88, Section 1. These rules become effective on January 1, 2019. 

 (2) Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any Division 250 rule for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS Ch. 183, 756, 2018 OL Ch. 88 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2018 OL Ch. 88, Sect. 1 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PUC 9-2018, adopt filed 12/27/2018, effective 01/01/2019
