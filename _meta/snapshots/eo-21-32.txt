    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 21-32


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    COUGAR PEAK FIRE IN LAKE COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Cougar Peak Fire” is burning in Lake County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Cougar Peak Fire
    are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
    protection was requested by the Lake County Fire Defense Board Chief and the
    State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Cougar Peak Fire in Lake
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    The Office of the State Fire Marshal Blue Incident Management Team have entered
    into unified command with Pacific Northwest Team 8. The Office of State Fire
    Marshal Blue Incident Management Team were briefed at 8:00 pm on September 8,
    2021


    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    IL,     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
            fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
            Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
            Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
            responding to the Cougar Peak Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
            Marshal.


    Di      This conflagration is declared only for the Cougar Peak Fire threatening
            structures in Lake County.

    ioe)     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 11:30 am on the 8th day of
           . September 2021, and signed this 10" day of September 2021, in Salem,
            Oregon.
