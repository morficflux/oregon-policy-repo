 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 PREFERRED WORKER PROGRAM 
 436-110-0352 
 Worksite Modification — Worker Activated 
 
 Conditions for use of worksite modification assistance by the worker are as follows:  (1) Modifications may be provided for requests received within three years from the date of hire;  (2) A worker may use worksite modification assistance as often as necessary but only once per employer, with each use is limited to $35,000; and  (3) A worker can use a second worksite modification with the same employer for a new job if the majority of the job duties have changed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) & 656.622 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.622
