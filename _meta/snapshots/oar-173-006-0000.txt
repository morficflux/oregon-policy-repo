 Oregon 529 Savings Board 
 Chapter 173 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 ELIGIBILITY 
 173-006-0000 
 Account Owner 
 
 Any person, including a custodian under UGMA/UTMA, who is legally able to contract under applicable state law and who meets federal and state legal requirements governing the network, is eligible to establish an account. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.853(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.841 & 348.873
