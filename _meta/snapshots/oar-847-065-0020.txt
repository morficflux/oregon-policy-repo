 Oregon Medical Board 
 Chapter 847 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 HEALTH PROFESSIONALS’ SERVICES PROGRAM 
 847-065-0020 
 Participation in Health Professionals' Services Program
 
 
 Effective July 1, 2010, the Board must participate in the Health Professionals’ Services Program and may refer eligible licensees to the HPSP in lieu of or in addition to discipline. Only licensees who meet the eligibility criteria may enter into the HPSP. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.190, 677.265 & 676.200 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.190, 676.185, 676.200 & 676.194
