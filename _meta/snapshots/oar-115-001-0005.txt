 Employment Relations Board 
 Chapter 115 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 115-001-0005 
 Model Rules of the Attorney General 
 
 The Employment Relations Board adopts only the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure specifically identified in these rules.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedures is available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341, 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341, 240 & 243
