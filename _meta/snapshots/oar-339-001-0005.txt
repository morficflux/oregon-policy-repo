 Occupational Therapy Licensing Board 
 Chapter 339 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 339-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Practice and Procedure 
 
 The following Model Rules of Procedure promulgated by the Attorney General of the State of Oregon in effect January 1, 2008 are adopted by the Board by Reference. These rules apply to rulemaking and to the conduct of contested cases respectively:  (1) OAR 137-01-0005; 137-001-0007; 137-001-0008; 137-001-0009; 137-001-0011; 137-001-0018; 137-001-0030; 137-001-0040; 137-001-0050; 137-001-0060; 137-001-0070; 137-001-0080 and 137-001-0085.  (2) OAR 137-003-0501 to 137-003-0700.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Occupational Therapy Licensing Board.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 675.320(11), (13) & (14)
