 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 POST-ACKNOWLEDGEMENT AMENDMENTS 
 660-018-0060 
 Who May Appeal 
 
 Eligibility to appeal a local government decision to adopt a change to a comprehensive plan or land use regulation is governed by ORS 197.620, 197.830, and Oregon Laws 2011, chapter 280, section 6. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.040 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.610-197.845; OL 2011, Ch 280 & § 6
