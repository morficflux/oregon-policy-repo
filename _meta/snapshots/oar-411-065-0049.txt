 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 SPECIALIZED LIVING SERVICES CONTRACTS 
 411-065-0049 
 Reimbursement 
 
 (1) The total monthly rate for specialized living services must not exceed the amount approved for each resident in accordance with the terms of the contract signed by the Department.  (2) Any add-on costs for nursing services must not exceed the RN Contract fee rate. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.070
