 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 170 BED AND BREAKFAST FACILITIES 
 333-170-0080 
 Construction 
 
 In Bed and Breakfast Facilities, only new and replacement walls and ceilings (or their coverings), constructed after the effective date of these rules need comply with OAR 333-150-0000 sections 6-201.11, 6-201.16 and 6-201.17 provided, however, that all walls and ceilings (and their coverings) must be in good repair and maintained in a clean and sanitary condition. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 624.041 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 624.041
