 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 AURORA STATE AIRPORT OPERATION REGULATIONS 
 738-050-0040 
 Helicopters 
 
 (1) Approaches and departures shall not exceed 700' MSL.  (2) Land and take-off only in areas designated for helicopter use. Training activities may be conducted on runway using fixed-wing traffic pattern provided that no conflict with fixed-wing traffic is generated.  (3) Avoid hovering near fixed-wing aircraft.  (4) Remain clear of persons, vehicles and buildings so as not to endanger or interfere with persons or property. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184, 835 & 836 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 836.095 & 836.505
