 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Clover Seed Commission - Chapter 664 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 COMMISSION MAKE-UP, TERMS, QUALIFICATIONS, TERM LIMITS, REMOVAL FROM OFFICE 
 664-015-0010 
 Number of Commissioners, Terms
 
 
 The Oregon Clover Commission will consist of ten commissioners appointed by the Director of the Oregon Department of Agriculture for a term of three years or, if for a term following a prior term for that person, ending three years from the date of expiration of any prior term. The second term for handler 2 will end June 30, 2026; thereafter, the length of term will be three (3) years.   N o commissioner will serve for more than two full consecutive terms of office. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 & 2003 OL Ch. 604 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576 & 2003 OL Ch. 604
