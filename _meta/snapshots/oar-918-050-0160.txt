 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-050-0160 
 Statewide Fee Methodology for Phased Projects 
 
 A municipal plan review fee for a phased project is based on a minimum phasing fee, determined by the municipality, plus 10 percent of the total project building permit fee not to exceed $1,500 for each phase. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.048 & 455.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.046 & 455.055
