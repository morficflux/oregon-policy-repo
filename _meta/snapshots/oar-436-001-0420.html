Department of Consumer and Business Services

Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436

Division 1
PROCEDURAL RULES, ATTORNEY FEES, AND GENERAL PROVISIONS

436-001-0420
Attorney Fees Under ORS 656.262(11) (Late Payment of Compensation)

In cases in which the director orders a fee to be paid under ORS 656.262(11):

(1) OAR 438-015-0110 applies.

(2) The director may use the matrix in OAR 436-001-0410 as a guide in determining the amount of the fee.

(3) The director must consider the proportionate benefit to the worker when determining the amount of the fee.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 656.726(4)
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 656.262
