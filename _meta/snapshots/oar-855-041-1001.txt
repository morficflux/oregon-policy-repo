 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 OPERATION OF PHARMACIES 
 855-041-1001 
 Definitions
 
 
 “Drug room” is a drug storage area registered with the board which is secure and lockable. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689.205 & ORS 689.522 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 689.155 & ORS 689.522
