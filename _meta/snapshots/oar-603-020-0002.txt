 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 TAX CREDIT ADMINISTRATION 
 603-020-0002 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 (1) OAR chapter 603, division 20 establishes the procedure and criteria for certifying tax credits under ORS 315.176, ORS 315.184 and ORS 469B.403. 

 (2) These rules apply to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2018. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 315.176 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 315.176 & 469B.403
