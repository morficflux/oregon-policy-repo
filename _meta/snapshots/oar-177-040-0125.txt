 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 RETAILER CONTRACT 
 177-040-0125 
 Termination of Retailer Contract When An Applicant Does Not Qualify As A Key Person 
 
 When the Director determines that an applicant does not qualify as a key person, that is grounds for termination of the retailer contract associated with the applicant. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.300
