 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 86 WETLAND CONSERVATION PLAN 
 141-086-0180 
 Purpose 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 196.674 pertaining to the Statewide Wetlands Inventory (SWI), these rules establish a system for uniform wetland identification and comprehensive mapping. These rules also establish wetlands inventory standards for cities or counties developing a wetland conservation plan (WCP) pursuant to 196.678. A Local Wetlands Inventory (LWI) is developed for all or a portion of a city or county according to the standards and guidelines contained in these rules (OAR 141-086-0180 through 141-086-0240). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.674 - 196.681 & 196.692 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.668 - 196.692
