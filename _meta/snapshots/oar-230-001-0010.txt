 Department of State Police 
 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 230-001-0010 
 Filing of Documents
 
 
 All correspondence relating to the activities of the Commission and all documents required to be filed with the Commission shall be directed to: The Oregon State Athletic Commission Administrator.   The most up-to-date contact information may be found at: https://www.oregon.gov/osp/programs/osac/pages/publicmeetings.aspx 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.330 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.330 & ORS 463.113
