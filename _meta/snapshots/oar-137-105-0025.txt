 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 105 NON-PARTICIPATING MANUFACTURERS 
 137-105-0025 
 Model Escrow Agreement 
 
 (1) The model escrow agreement set forth in OAR 137-105-0025(2) is provided for use by tobacco product manufacturers who are required to execute a qualified escrow agreement. A tobacco product manufacturer that executes the model escrow agreement set forth in OAR 137-105-0025(2) is deemed to have satisfied the requirement of ORS 180.415(2)(b) that it use a form of escrow agreement that has been reviewed and approved by the Attorney General.  (2) [Model Escrow Agreement] [Table not included.
