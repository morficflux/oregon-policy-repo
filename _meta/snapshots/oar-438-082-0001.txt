 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 82 RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE FOR APPEALS UNDER THE COMPENSATION ACT FOR VICTIMS OF CRIME 
 438-082-0001 
 Statutory Authority; Adoption Procedures 
 
 (1) The statutory authority for the adoption of these rules is ORS 656.726(5) and 147.155.  (2) These rules are adopted in accordance with ORS Chapter 183 and the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure applicable to rulemaking functions. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.310 - 183.410, 147.155(5) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 147.155 & 656.726(4) 
 
 History:
