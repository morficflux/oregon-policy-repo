 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 305 GENERAL ADMINISTRATION OF REVENUE LAWS 
 150-305-0430 
 Method of Distribution from Check-off Contributions and Reimbursement of Administrative Expenses
 
 
 For programs which have been approved for charitable contribution checkoff by the Oregon Legislature or the Oregon Charitable Checkoff Commission, the department will maintain a record of contributions received and distribute the respective amounts, minus administrative expenses to each entity once a month. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 305.747
