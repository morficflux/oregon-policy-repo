     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-20


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     ELK LANE FIRE IN JEFFERSON COUNTY


     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, | find that:

     The fire known as the “Elk Lane Fire” is burning in Jefferson County.


    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Elk Lane Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by the Jefferson County Fire Defense Board and the State Fire
     Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Elk Lane Fire in Jefferson
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
     personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 8:40 a.m. on August 5, 2024, with the Department of
    the State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management
    Team will be entering into unified command with the Central Oregon Fire
    Management Service Type 3 Incident Management Team. The Oregon State Fire
    Marshal Red Incident Management Team will be briefed at 2:00 pm on August 5,
    2024, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.


     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:
     IE     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
            the Elk Lane Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

    Or      This conflagration is declared only for the Elk Lane Fire threatening
            structures in Jefferson County.


     3s     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 8:40 am on the 5'" day of
            August 2024 and signed this 5" day of August 2024, in Salem, Oregon.
