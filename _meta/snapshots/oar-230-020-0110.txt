 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 LICENSES AND APPLICATIONS 
 230-020-0110 
 Appearance Before the Administrator
 
 
 Any person applying for a promoter's license or renewal thereof, may be required to appear in person at the request of the Administrator. The Administrator shall review and investigate all applications. The Administrator shall file a signed written recommendation for grant or denial of the application for a license or renewal of a license, along with a report of such investigation of the applicant with the superintendent. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 & 463.165 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.035 & 463.165
