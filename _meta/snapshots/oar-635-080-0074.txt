 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0074 
 Warner Unit 
 
 The Warner Unit, number 74, is that area beginning at Lakeview; north east on U.S. Highway 395 to Fort Bidwell-Warner Valley Road north of Abert Lake; south on Fort Bidwell-Warner Valley Road through Plush and Adel to Oregon-California state line; west along state line to U.S. Highway 395 at New Pine Creek; north on U.S. Highway 395 to Lakeview, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
