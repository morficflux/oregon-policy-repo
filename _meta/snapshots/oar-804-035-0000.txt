 Landscape Architect Board 
 Chapter 804 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 BUSINESS REGISTRATION 
 804-035-0000 
 Business Registration Purpose 
 
 The rules in Division 35 implement ORS 671.318 by setting forth registration requirements for businesses providing landscape architecture services in Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 671.318 & 671.415 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.318 
 
 History:
