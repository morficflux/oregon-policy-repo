 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 VOLUNTEER SERVICES/STUDENT INTERNS 
 291-015-0135 
 Utilization 
 
 (1) Volunteers shall not be placed in positions of authority over department employees or contractors.  (2) Volunteers shall not perform professional services requiring certification or licensing unless the volunteer program manager or designee verifies the validity of the license.  (3) Volunteers shall be treated with the same respect as staff and recognized as having unique roles that differ from, but are complimentary to staff roles.  (4) Volunteers shall follow department rules and policies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
