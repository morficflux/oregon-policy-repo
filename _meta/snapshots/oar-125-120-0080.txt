 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 RENTING OR LEASING OFFICE QUARTERS 
 125-120-0080 
 Subleases by an Agency 
 
 (1) Facilities Division must review and approve all subleases by or for all agencies subject to the Leasing Rules.  (2) Any agency that has office space that is not needed for agency purposes, which the agency believes it is feasible to backfill, shall inform the Division. The Division will provide assistance to the agency in back filling the surplus space. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.340 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 276.428
