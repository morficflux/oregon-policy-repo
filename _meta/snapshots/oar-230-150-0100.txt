 Department of State Police 
 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 150 AMATEUR ATHLETIC ORGANIZATIONS 
 230-150-0100 
 Staffed Ambulance Presence 
 
 (1) The following must be present on-site while a bout is in progress, unless approved by the Administrator: 

 (a) A minimum of one (1) advanced life support ambulance, fully equipped in accordance with ORS 463; 

 (b) Ambulance staff, minimum of two (2), one (1) being a certified paramedic, able to provide advanced life support patient care; 

 (c) The ambulance staff must be present directly outside the fenced area with the equipment required to provide immediate emergency medical care while a bout is in progress.  

 (2) This rule does not apploy to amateur boxing sanctioned by USA Boxing. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OL2017 CH235 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  OL2017 CH235 
 
 History:
