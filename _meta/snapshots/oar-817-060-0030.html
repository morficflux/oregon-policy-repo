Oregon Health Authority

Health Licensing Office, Board of Cosmetology - Chapter 817

Division 60
CHEMICAL USE AND STORAGE

817-060-0030
Use, Handling and Disposing of Chemicals

(1) Chemicals used for providing services to clients must be mixed in a dispensing area, which has adequate ventilation away from open flame or other sources of potential ignition.

(2) All chemicals must be disposed of in accordance with their manufacturer’s instructions and according to local and state environmental requirements.

(3) Authorization holders are prohibited from:

(a) Using cosmetic products containing hazardous substances, which have been banned by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration for use in cosmetic products;

(b) Using products in a manner that is not approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration;

(c) Using any product containing compounds or substances characterized as hazardous or harmful to humans by Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) and/or random product testing see OAR chapter 817 division 060; and

(d) Using an open flame at the workstation during any phase of chemical service.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 676.605, 676.615, 690.165 & 690.205
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 676.605, 676.615, 690.165 & 690.205
