 Public Utility Commission 
 Oregon Board of Maritime Pilots - Chapter 856 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 Miscellaneous 
 856-025-0055 
 Periodic Tariff Review Process 
 
 (1) On a biennial basis beginning in 2019, the Board shall conduct a review of the current tariff in effect for the prior 24 months for all pilotage grounds. 

 (2) The tariff review shall include a reading by all Board members of: 

 (a) Pilot Organization financial reports as specified in OAR 856-025-0050; and 

 (b) The Board’s previous year’s Annual Report. 

 (3) The tariff review shall include the Board making available to the public: 

 (a) Pilot Organization financial reports as specified in OAR 856-025-0050; 

 (b) A notation of the current target net pilot income, as adjusted pursuant to any automatic adjustments ordered, in a cover letter. 

 (4) The tariff review shall not include Board commentary, inquiry, or analysis. 

 (5) The tariff review shall not include tariff adjustments based on approved capital improvements or expenses authorized by the Transportation Oversight Committee. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 776.115; ORS 776.115(7) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 776.115; ORS 776.115(7)
