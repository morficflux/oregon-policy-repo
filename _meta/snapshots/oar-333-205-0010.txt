 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 205 TRAUMA SYSTEM HOSPITAL DESIGNATION IN TRAUMA AREA # 1 
 333-205-0010 
 Designation
 
 
 (1) The designation method of selecting Level I trauma system hospitals shall be implemented in accordance with the provisions of OAR 333-200-0090(2) and (5), 333-200-0235, and 333-200-0245. 

 (2) Written notification of the trauma system hospital designation shall be provided to the applicant by the Oregon Health Authority (Authority). An applicant shall have 30 days from the receipt of notification of non-designation to file a request with the Authority for reconsideration. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431A.060, ORS 431A.065 & ORS 431A.090
