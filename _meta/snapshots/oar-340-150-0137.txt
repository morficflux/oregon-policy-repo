 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 150 UNDERGROUND STORAGE TANK RULES 
 340-150-0137 
 UST Systems with Field-Constructed Tanks and Airport Hydrant Fuel Distribution Systems 
 
 (1) On or after October 1, 2020, owners and permittees of field-constructed tanks and airport hydrant fuel distribution system tanks must comply with OAR 340-150-0137(2) and the training requirements for UST operators in this rule. 

 (2) EQC incorporates by reference into this division Title 40 C.F.R., Part 280, Subpart K (October 13, 2015). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  466.705 - 466.835, 466.994 & 466.995 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  466.706, 466.710 & 466.746 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 188-2018, adopt filed 05/14/2018, effective 06/01/2018
