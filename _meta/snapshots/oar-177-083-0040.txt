 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 83 LUCKY LINES GAME RULES 
 177-083-0040 
 Cancellation of Tickets 
 
 (1) General: A player may cancel a Lucky LinesSM ticket for a single drawing or consecutive drawings. To cancel a ticket, a player must follow the procedure in OAR 177-046-0060.  (2) Refund: In the event that a ticket is canceled in accordance with OAR 177-083-0040(1) and 177-046-0060, the player shall be entitled to a refund from the retailer equal to the purchase amount shown on the player’s ticket. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 461 & OR Const., Art. XV & § 4(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.210
