 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 MEDICAL MARIJUANA 
 333-008-3000 
 Medical Marijuana Records: Medical Marijuana Confidentiality
 
 
 The Oregon Health Authority may only disclose identifying information concerning applicants or registrants in accordance with the following statutes, unless otherwise required by law: 

 (1) ORS 475C.856 (Database of information related to production, processing and dispensing). 

 (2) ORS 475C.859 (Database of information related to cardholders). 

 (3) ORS 475C.862 (Confidentiality of personally identifiable information). 

 (4) ORS 475C.865 (Disclosure of personally identifiable information upon revocation or suspension of registration). 

 (5) ORS 475C.698. 

 (6) ORS 475C.853. 

 (7) ORS 475C.868. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.856 - 475C.865 & 475C.919 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.856 - 475C.865 & 475C.919
