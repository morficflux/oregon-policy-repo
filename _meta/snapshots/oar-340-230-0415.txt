 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 230 INCINERATOR REGULATIONS 
 340-230-0415 
 Adoption of Federal Plan by Reference 
 
 The federal plan for hospital, medical, and infectious waste incineration units constructed on or before December 1, 2008, in 40 CFR Part 62 Subpart HHH, is by this reference adopted and incorporated herein. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 8-2015, f. & cert. ef. 4-17-15
