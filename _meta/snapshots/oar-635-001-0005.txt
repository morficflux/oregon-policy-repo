 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 ADMINISTRATION 
 635-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure
 
 
 The Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, promulgated by the Attorney General and in effect January 1, 2024 are hereby adopted as the rules of procedures of the Fish and Wildlife Commission and the Department of Fish and Wildlife. 

 [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Department of Fish and Wildlife.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
