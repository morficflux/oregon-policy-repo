 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 76 ISSUANCE OF PERMITS ALLOWING TOW VEHICLES TO TOW OVERSIZE DISABLED VEHICLES OR COMBINATIONS OF VEHICLES ON STATE HIGHWAYS 
 734-076-0175 
 Approved Routes
 
 
 (1) When removing a load or vehicles from an emergency: 

 (a) While removing a vehicle from the initial emergency, tow vehicles or load recovery vehicles may operate on all state highways as directed by an enforcement officer or the road authority. 

 (b) When not otherwise directed by an enforcement officer or the road authority, tow vehicles may operate over any route approved for the disabled vehicle(s) prior to the incident; 

 (c) When not otherwise directed by an enforcement officer or the road authority,  load recovery vehicles may operate over approved routes and at the lengths authorized on Group Map 1 or Route Map 7 or those routes shown on the continuous trip permit or single-trip permit issued to the disabled vehicle. Vehicle(s) may not exceed weight restrictions shown on Route Map 8 and Attachment 100A. 

 (d) Group Map 1, revised April 2021, Route Map 7, revised May 2020, Route Map 8, revised November 2023, and Attachment 100A, revised November 2023 are by reference made part of Division 76 rules, and are available from the Commerce and Compliance Division, Over-Dimension Permit Unit. 

 (2) The Chief Engineer may add additional highway routes to those approved for operation or delete from the approved routes any highway or section of highway when continued operation of the units is not in the public’s best interest. 

 (3) This rule does not authorize operation over highways, streets, or roads not under the jurisdiction of the Department of Transportation. For such operations, separate permission must be obtained from the appropriate authority. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 823.011 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.200 & 818.220
