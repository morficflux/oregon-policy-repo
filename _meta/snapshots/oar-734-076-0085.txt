 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 76 ISSUANCE OF PERMITS ALLOWING TOW VEHICLES TO TOW OVERSIZE DISABLED VEHICLES OR COMBINATIONS OF VEHICLES ON STATE HIGHWAYS 
 734-076-0085 
 Permit Duration
 
 
 Permits issued under these Division 76 rules are for a single trip within a period of time not to exceed 10 days, or continuous trips for up to one year from the effective date. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 818.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.170
