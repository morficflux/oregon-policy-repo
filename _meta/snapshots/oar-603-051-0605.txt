 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 PRODUCE STANDARDS 
 603-051-0605 
 Grades 
 
 (1) Oregon No. 1 shall consist of plums or prunes of one variety which are well formed, mature, but not soft or shriveled, and which are free from decay and sunscald, and from damage caused by cracks, heat injury or internal browning, growth cracks, sunburn, split pits, russeting, scars, drouth spots, or gum pockets, dirt or other foreign materials, disease, insects, mechanical, or other factors which affect the quality of the processed fruit. Plums or prunes unless otherwise specified shall be not less than 1-3/16 inches in diameter.  (2) Oregon No. 2 shall consist of plums or prunes of one variety which meet the requirements of Oregon No. 1 grade, except for size. The minimum diameter shall be not less than 1-1/8 inches. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 616 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 632.900 - 632.980 
 
 History:
