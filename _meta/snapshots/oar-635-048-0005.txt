 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 48 HUNTER/TRAPPER EDUCATION 
 635-048-0005 
 Hunter Education Certificate 
 
 (1) Anyone younger than 18 years of age shall have proof of hunter education in their possession when hunting on land other than their own property or that of their parent or legal guardian, except youth participating in the Mentored Youth Program. Proof of hunter education means a certificate or license that includes hunter education number.  (2) For people younger than 18 years of age, hunter education training must include both a classroom (physical or virtual) component and a field day. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 & 497 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 497.360
