 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 102 OREGON ESSENTIAL INDIGENOUS ANADROMOUS SALMONID HABITAT 
 141-102-0000 
 Purpose
 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 196.810(1)(b), these rules: 

 (1) Further define "essential indigenous anadromous salmonid habitat (ESH)"; 

 (2) Designate ESH in consultation with the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife (ODFW) and in consultation with other affected parties; 

 (3) Establish the process to amend ESH designations as new information becomes available; 

 (4) Develop and implement a process for responding to requests to review the accuracy of an ESH designation and mapping; and 

 (5) Require an authorization from the Department for activities involving the fill or removal of any amount of material in ESH unless the activity is exempt. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.810 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.800 - 196.990 & 390.805 - 390.925
