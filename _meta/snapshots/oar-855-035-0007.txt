 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 OPERATION OF NONPRESCRIPTION AND MEDICAL DEVICE, EQUIPMENT AND GAS (MDEG) OUTLETS 
 855-035-0007 
 Sales 
 
 Sales by drug outlets except itinerant vendors shall be made only from the premises at the location registered by the Board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475.035 & 689.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 689.305, 689.315 & 689.325
