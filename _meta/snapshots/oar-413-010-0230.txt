 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 CLIENT RIGHTS 
 413-010-0230 
 Attorney Interview 
 
 When an attorney asks to interview a child, the following shall apply:  (1) An attorney of record representing the child may interview the child privately. However, if the attorney or the child requests, a Child Welfare staff member shall be present during the interview.  (2) An adversarial attorney shall not be permitted to interview a child unless the child’s legal guardian consents to the interview and the child’s own attorney is also present. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005 
 
 History:
