 Oregon Board of Naturopathic Medicine 
 Chapter 850 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 DISCIPLINE 
 850-050-0130 
 Change of Name and Address 
 
 —Each Board licensee must notify the Board in writing within 30 days of any change of the licensee’s name, residence address, practice location, or mailing address. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 685 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 685.100 & 685.110
