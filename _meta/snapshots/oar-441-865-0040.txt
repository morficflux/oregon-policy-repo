 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 865 RECORDS AND REPORTS 
 441-865-0040 
 Borrower and Investor Complaints 
 
 (1) Copies of all written complaints by borrowers and investors shall be maintained in a separate complaint file by the individual's name in alphabetical order and shall contain at a minimum a copy of the original complaint and response.  (2) Copies of correspondence related to the complaints and a written disposition of the complaint by an officer, director or control person of the company shall be maintained in the complaint file or in the borrower or investor file of the mortgage banker or mortgage broker.  (3) Complaints unrelated to borrower or investor transactions need not be retained. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 86A.112 & 86A.136 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 86A.112
