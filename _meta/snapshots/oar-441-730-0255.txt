 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 730 CONSUMER FINANCE 
 441-730-0255 
 Payoff Information to be Furnished to Borrower Upon Request 
 
 (1) When a borrower requests the payoff information on a loan and specifies a payoff date, the licensee must promptly, but in no case later than three business days, provide the requested information.  (2) When a borrower does not specify a payoff date, the licensee must calculate the payoff amount for a date no later than 10 days after the date of the request, and the amount must be provided within three business days of the borrower’s request. When a licensee provides a payoff amount, it must also advise the borrower, verbally or in writing, that interest will continue to accrue past the payoff date if the loan is not paid in full. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 725.505 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 725.360
