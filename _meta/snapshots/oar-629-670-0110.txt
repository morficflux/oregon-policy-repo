 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 670 FOREST PRACTICES ADMINISTRATION — ENFORCEMENT AND CIVIL PENALTIES 
 629-670-0110 
 Enforcement Action 
 
 The State Forester may initiate enforcement action by issuing and serving to the responsible person, persons, or corporation either a:  (1) Written statement of unsatisfactory condition under OAR 629-670-0115, 629-670-0120, or 629-670-0125; or  (2) Citation under OAR 629-670-0130 and ORS 527.680. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 527.710 & 526.016 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 527.680, 527.683, 527.990 & 527.992
