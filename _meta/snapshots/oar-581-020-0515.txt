 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 SCHOOL IMPROVEMENT AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT 
 581-020-0515 
 Oregon Classroom Content 
 
 (1) Content should be aligned with Oregon’s Common Curriculum Goals, Content Standards, and Essential Learning Skills. Adoption of content for instruction is governed by school Districts subject to Oregon Revised Statutes and Oregon Administrative Rules.  (2) OVSD will offer content aligned with state standards. The use of OVSD resources requires districts to conform to OAR division 22 Standards for public elementary and secondary schools. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 319.840 & 326.051 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 319.840
