 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-3820 
 J. C. Boyle Reservoir 
 
 Policies and objectives for fish management in J. D. Boyle Reservoir of the Klamath River Basin.  (1) Policies:  (a) Redband trout in J. C. Boyle Reservoir shall be managed for natural production under the Wild Fish Management Option;  (b) No hatchery reared fish shall be stocked in J. C. Boyle Reservoir.  (2) Objectives:  (a) Maintain protection of genetic diversity, adaptiveness and abundance of wild redband trout;  (b) Provide a consumptive fishery for warmwater game fish. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.162, 506.109 & 506.129 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FWC 56-1997, f. 9-4-97, cert. ef. 9-15-97
