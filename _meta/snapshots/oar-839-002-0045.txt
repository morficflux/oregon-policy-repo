 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 839-002-0045 
 Investigative Subpoenas for Civil Rights Division and Wage and Hour Division: Subpoena Ad Testificandum 
 
 (1) A subpoena ad testificandum may be issued to any person when the conditions set out in 839-002-0030(2) or 839-002-0030(3) have been met.  (2) The subpoena ad testificandum must give the person a reasonable time for preparation and travel to the place of attendance and the place of attendance must be in a suitable place in the vicinity to which testimony is applicable. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 651.060, 658.220 & 659A.800 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279C, 651, 652, 653, 658 & 659A
