 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS 
 808-002-0872 
 Retaining Walls 
 
 Landscape "retaining walls" as used in ORS 671 and this chapter is a structure designed to a height exceeding six (6) inches from the lowest finish grade and constructed to resist the lateral pressure of soil, when there is an actual, or desired change in ground elevation that exceeds the angle of repose of the soil. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 671.760 & ORS 670.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.520 & ORS 671.540 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LCB 5-2019, adopt filed 09/24/2019, effective 09/24/2019
