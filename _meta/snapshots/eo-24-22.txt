     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-22


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     DIXON FIRE IN DOUGLAS COUNTY


     Pursuant to my authority as Governor ofthe State of Oregon, | find that:

     The fire known as the “Dixon Fire” is burning in Douglas County.


     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Dixon Fire are
     beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
     was requested by the Douglas County Fire Defense Board Chief and the State Fire
     Marshal concurs with that request.


     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Dixon Fire in Douglas
     County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
     personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.


     These findings were made at 8:16 pm on August 10, 2024, with the Department of
     the State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Green Incident Management
     Team will be entering into unified command with the Oregon Department of
     Forestry Incident Management Team 3. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Green
     Incident Management Team will be briefed at 10:30 am on August 11, 2024, and |
     now confirm them with this Executive Order.


     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


     I.     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
            the Dixon Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

     2.     This conflagration is declared only for the Dixon Fire threatening structures
             in Douglas County.


     3%     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 8:16 pm on the 10" day of
            August 2024 and signed this 12'" day of August 2024, in Salem, Oregon.
