 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 81 NONTRANSPLANT ANATOMICAL RESEARCH RECOVERY ORGANIZATIONS 
 333-081-0040 
 Denial, Suspension or Revocation of License 
 
 (1) A license for a NARRO may be denied, suspended or revoked by the Division if a licensed NARRO has failed to comply with Oregon Laws 2013, chapter 356, sections 2 and 3, OAR 333-081-0080 or for violations of laws, regulations or administrative rules that governs how it obtains, processes and distributes donor material.  (2) If the Division intends to suspend or revoke a NARRO license, it shall do so in accordance with ORS 183.411 through 183.470. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 438.710 & 438.720 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 438.705-438.720 & 438.994 
 
 History:
