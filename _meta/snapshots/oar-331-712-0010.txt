 Division 712 RESPIRATORY THERAPIST AND  POLYSOMNOGRAPHIC TECHNOLOGISTS LICENSING BOARD — EXAMINATIONS 
 331-712-0010 
 Polysomnography Approved Examinations
 
 
 The Board has approved these examinations for qualification as a licensed polysomnographic technologist: 

 (1) The RPSGT “registration examination” administered by the BRPT; 

 (2) The SDS examination administered by the NBRC; and 

 (3) The Oregon Laws and Rules examination for polysomnography administered by the Office. 

 
 NOTE: An applicant is responsible for direct payment to the organization of all application, examination, national certification or other fees associated with any examination. 
 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.615, ORS 688.815, ORS 676.607 & ORS 688.830 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.615, ORS 688.815, ORS 676.607 & ORS 688.830
