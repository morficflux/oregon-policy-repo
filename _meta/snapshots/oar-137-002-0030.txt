 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 MODEL RULES FOR AGENCY DECLARATORY RULINGS 
 137-002-0030 
 Notice of Declaratory Ruling Hearing 
 
 The notice of hearing for a declaratory ruling shall:  (1) Be accompanied by a copy of the petition requesting the declaratory ruling and by a copy of any petition for intervention if copies of these petitions have not previously been served on the party;  (2) Set forth the time and place of the proceeding; and  (3) Identify the presiding officer. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.410 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.410
