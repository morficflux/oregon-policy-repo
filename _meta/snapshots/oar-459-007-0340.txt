 Oregon Public Employees Retirement System 
 Chapter 459 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 EARNINGS AND INTEREST DISTRIBUTION 
 459-007-0340 
 Crediting Earnings for an OPSRP Pension Program Cash Out of Small Benefits
 
 
 No earnings or distribution interest from the effective date of retirement to the date of distribution shall be credited to the amount of a cash out of small benefits under ORS 238A.195. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 238A.450 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 238A.120 & 238A.195 
 
 History:
