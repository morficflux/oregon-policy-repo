 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 VETERANS’ LOANS 
 274-020-0335 
 Appraisal of Property 
 
 (1) An appraisal shall be made to assist in establishing the loan value only after a complete application has been received.  (2) On farms, the loan value allowable on the principal home unit portion of the property shall not exceed the amount needed for a maximum home loan. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406.030, 407.115 & 407.225(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 407.115 & 407.225
