 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 AIRCRAFT DEALER’S LICENSE 
 738-060-0070 
 Penalties 
 
 Violations of ORS 837.075 or this rule are subject to the penalties established under ORS 837.100 and 837.990. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184, 835 & 837 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 837.990
