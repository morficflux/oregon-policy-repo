 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 250 GENERAL CONFORMITY 
 340-250-0070 
 Frequency of Conformity Determinations 
 
 (1) The conformity status of a federal action automatically lapses 5 years from the date a final conformity determination is reported under OAR 340-250-0050, unless the federal action has been completed or a continuous program has been commenced to implement that federal action within a reasonable time.  (2) Ongoing federal activities at a given site showing continuous progress are not new actions and do not require periodic redeterminations so long as the emissions associated with such activities are within the scope of the final conformity determination reported under OAR 340-250-0050.  (3) If, after the conformity determination is made, the federal action is changed so that there is an increase in the total of direct and indirect emissions above the levels in OAR 340-250-0020(4), a new conformity determination is required. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A.035 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.035
