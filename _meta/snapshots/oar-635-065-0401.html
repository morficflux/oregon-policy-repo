Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 65
GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS

635-065-0401
Deadline for Purchase of General Season Tags

Except as provided in OAR 635-065-0015 (10):

(1) No buck deer general season western Oregon any legal weapon tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, October 3, 2026.

(2) No buck deer general season archery tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, August 29, 2026.

(3) No black bear general season fall tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, October 3, 2026.

(4) Black bear additional general season fall tags may be purchased anytime during the bear hunting season, after a black bear general season fall tag has been purchased. An unused bear tag must be in the hunter’s possession at the time they are hunting.

(5) No general season cougar tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, October 3, 2026.

(6) Additional general season cougar tags may be purchased anytime during the cougar hunting season, after a general season cougar tag has been purchased. An unused cougar tag must be in the hunter’s possession at the time they are hunting.

(7) No general season elk eastern Oregon Rocky Mountain second season tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, November 7, 2026.

(8) No general season elk western Oregon Coast first season tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, November 14, 2026.

(9) No general season elk western Oregon Coast second season tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, November 21, 2026.

(10) No general season elk west Cascade tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, November 7, 2026.

(11) No elk general archery season tag shall be issued after 11:59 pm, Pacific Time, August 29, 2026.

(12) No General Season Antlerless Elk Damage Tag shall be issued after legal shooting hours end on March 31, 2026.

 

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
