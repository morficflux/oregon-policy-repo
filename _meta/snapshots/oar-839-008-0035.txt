 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 PAY EQUITY 
 839-008-0035 
 Individual Acts of Remuneration as Distinct Violations 
 
 An employer commits an unlawful compensation practice each time an employee is remunerated in violation of ORS 652.220. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 659A.805 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 652.220 & ORS 659A.875 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BLI 22-2018, adopt filed 11/19/2018, effective 01/01/2019
