 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 515 MANUFACTURED HOUSING INSTALLER LICENSING AND EDUCATIONAL PROVIDER APPROVAL 
 918-515-0060 
 Education Provider Scope of Work 
 
 Approved Education Providers may:  (1) Provide training and continuing education under the Division approved course curriculum to persons applying for license as a manufactured dwelling and cabana installer, limited installer, limited skirting installer and manufactured dwelling installation inspector; and  (2) Charge fees for conducting training. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.176, 446.400 & 446.405 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.395
