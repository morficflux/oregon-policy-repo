 Board of Geologist Examiners 
 Chapter 809 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 COMPLIANCE 
 809-055-0010 
 Contested Case Proceedings 
 
 The State Board of Geologist Examiners adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules for Contested Case Proceedings, OAR 137-003-0501 to 137-003-0700, as the rules of procedure for contested cases. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341 & 670.310(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.310 - 183.550 & 670.325 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BGE 1-2002, f. & cert. ef. 2-6-02
