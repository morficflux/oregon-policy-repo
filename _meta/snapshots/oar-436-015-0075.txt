 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 MANAGED CARE ORGANIZATIONS 
 436-015-0075 
 Worker Exams
 
 
 When the MCO schedules a worker exam that includes a psychological evaluation, the appointment letter must:  
(1) Inform the worker that a psychological evaluation is part of the exam; and 
(2) State the reason for the psychological exam. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  656.726(4) & ORS 656.260 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.260
