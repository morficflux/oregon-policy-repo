 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 77 TRANSPORTATION OF FOOD PROCESSING PLANT BY-PRODUCTS FROM WHICH THERE IS FLUID LEAKAGE 
 734-077-0015 
 Authorized Routes 
 
 It is intended that highway routes authorized in the permit will in general be those routes requested in the application. As authorized under ORS 818.220(1)(c), however, the Department of Transportation may, at its discretion, amend permits to designate alternate routes if the amount or character of fluid leakage is such that an alternate route would be in the public interest. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184 & 366 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.230
