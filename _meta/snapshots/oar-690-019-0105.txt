 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 19 DROUGHT MITIGATION 
 690-019-0105 
 Fee Refund 
 
 The Director may refund all or part of any fee submitted to the Department pursuant to OAR 690-019-0100. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.700 - 536.780 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 536.700 - 536.780
