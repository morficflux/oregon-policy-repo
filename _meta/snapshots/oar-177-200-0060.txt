 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 GENERAL VIDEO LOTTERYSM GAME RULES 
 177-200-0060 
 Requirements for Percentage Payout
 
 
 The maximum payout percentage for the Lottery’s Video LotterySM games is 96 percent. Extended play games may exceed this number. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Or Const, Art XV, § 4(4)(a) & ORS 461.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Or Const, Art XV, § 4(4) & ORS 461.220
