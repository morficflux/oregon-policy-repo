 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 27 SALE BY WEIGHT OR MEASURE 
 603-027-0650 
 Application 
 
 This regulation shall apply to all classes of devices and/or equipment as covered in National Institute of Standards and Technology Handbooks 44, 105-1, 105-2, and 105-3.  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 618 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 618.156 
 
 History:
