 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 BUSINESS REGISTRY SECTION 
 160-010-0610 
 Filing Format 
 
 (1) The notice of intent filed with the Secretary of State shall use a format approved by the Secretary of State, Corporation Division.  (2) The Corporation Division shall use an electronic method of filing the notice of intent and any amendments or cancellations thereof. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 56.022 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2007 SB 684 
 
 History:
