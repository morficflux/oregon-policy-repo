 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 262 HEAT SMART PROGRAM FOR RESIDENTIAL WOODSTOVES AND OTHER SOLID FUEL HEATING DEVICES 
 340-262-0900 
 Materials Prohibited from Burning 
 
 No person may cause or allow any of the following materials to be burned in a solid fuel burning device, fireplace, a trash burner or any other device described in ORS 468A.485(4)(b):  (1)(a) Garbage;  (b) Treated wood;  (c) Plastic or plastic products;  (d) Rubber or rubber products;  (e) Animal carcasses;  (f) Products that contain asphalt;  (g) Waste petroleum products;  (h) Paint;  (i) Chemicals;  (j) Products containing lead, mercury or other heavy or toxic metals;  (k) Materials containing asbestos; and  (l) Particleboard.  (2) Paper or paper products, except for paper used to kindle a fire.  NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.460 - 468A.515 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 2-2011, f. 3-10-11, cert. ef. 3-15-11
