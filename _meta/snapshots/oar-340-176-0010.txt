 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 176 UNDERGROUND STORAGE TANK LOAN GUARANTEE 
 340-176-0010 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) The definitions in OAR 340-172-0010 and the following definition apply to these rules.  (2) “Collection Expenses” means out of pocket expenses, attorney fees, administrative expenses, filing fees, recording fees, and other expenses related to collection of unpaid loan monies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.706 & OL 1989 & Ch. 1071
