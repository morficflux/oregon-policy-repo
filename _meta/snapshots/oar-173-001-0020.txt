 Oregon 529 Savings Board 
 Chapter 173 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 NOTICE OF PROPOSED RULEMAKING AND  MODEL RULES OF PROCEDURE 
 173-001-0020 
 Policies and Procedures of the Office of the Treasurer 
 
 The policies and procedures of the Office of the State Treasurer in regard to personal services contracting and purchasing goods and services, to the extent not inconsistent with the Act or OAR chapter 173, are adopted as the policies and procedures of the board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 36.224 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 36.224, 36.228, 36.230 & 36.232
