 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 73 SPECIAL INTEREST SEASONS 
 635-073-0080 
 Controlled Muzzleloader Deer Hunt Regulations
 
 
 Tags shall be issued by a controlled hunt drawing following the procedures established in OAR chapter 635, division 060. Except as provided in OAR-065-0015, a hunter successful in drawing one of the controlled muzzleloader deer tags shall not hunt in any general deer season or other 100 series hunts. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
