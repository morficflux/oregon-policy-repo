 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 OPERATION OF PHARMACIES 
 855-041-1110 
 Tamper-resistant Prescription
 
 
 When the use of a tamper-resistant prescription is required by any federal or state law or rule, the term “tamper-resistant” shall have the meaning as defined in OAR 855-006-0005. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  689.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 689.155
