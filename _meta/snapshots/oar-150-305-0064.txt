 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 305 GENERAL ADMINISTRATION OF REVENUE LAWS 
 150-305-0064 
 Accrual of Interest After Waiver 
 
 (1) The Department of Revenue will notify the taxpayer in writing if interest is waived under ORS 305.145(1). The taxpayer must pay the amount of the assessment within the appropriate appeal period. If the taxpayer does not pay the balance in full within the applicable appeal period, interest will accrue from that time on the balance due as provided in ORS 314.400(4).  (2) If the balance due is based on a Notice of Assessment the appeal period is 90 days from the date on the notice. If the balance due is based on a Magistrate decision, the appeal period is 60 days from the date of thedecision. If the balance due is based on a Tax Court Judgment, the appeal period is 30 days from the date of the judgment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 305.145
