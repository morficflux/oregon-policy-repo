 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. Oregon State Hospital (OSH) will comply with federal and state regulations
      regarding medical equipment to maintain a safe environment for patients, staff,
      and others. This policy establishes the OSH system for medical equipment
      maintenance, monitoring, and reporting medical equipment failure.
   B. This policy applies to all staff.

II. POLICY
   A. Before staff may use a piece of medical equipment, they must be deemed
      competent to use the equipment in accordance with discipline-specific
      requirements or standards of practice.
   B. Staff must report incidents related to medical equipment in accordance with
      applicable provisions of this policy and procedures, OSH Policy 5.012, “Injury or
      Illness Reporting”, OSH Policy 1.003, “Incident Reporting”, and all related
      procedures and attachments, and other applicable policies or regulations.
   C. OSH follows all applicable regulations, including federal and state statutes and
      rules; Oregon Department of Administrative Services (DAS), Shared Services,
      and Oregon Health Authority (OHA) policies; and relevant accreditation
      standards. Such regulations supersede the provisions of this policy unless this
      policy is more restrictive.
   D. Staff who fail to comply with this policy or related policy attachments or protocols
      may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including dismissal.

III. DEFINITIONS
   A. "Medical equipment" in this policy means a device that assists in the diagnosis,
      monitoring or treatment of a medical condition.
   B. “Staff” includes employees, volunteers, trainees, interns, contractors, vendors,
      and other state employees assigned to work at Oregon State Hospital (OSH).
