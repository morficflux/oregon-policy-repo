 Department of State Police 
 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 CONDUCT OF BOUT - BOXING 
 230-080-0400 
 Boxing 
 
 (1) All unarmed combat sports events held in the State of Oregon must be conducted in accordance with ORS Chapter 463 and the applicable rules set forth in OAR Chapter 230.  

 (2) The provisions of this section do not apply to events held on land controlled by an Oregon Indian Tribe unless governed by an intergovernmental agreement between the Oregon State Athletic Commission and an Oregon Indian Tribe. 

 (3) All boxing events must be held in a ring, no boxing bouts will be permitted to be held in a fenced area. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.113, ORS 463.025, ORS 463.035 & ORS 463.500 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SAC 8-2018, adopt filed 01/09/2018, effective 01/09/2018
