 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 VETERANS’ LOANS 
 274-020-0300 
 Cancellation of Application 
 
 (1) An applicant may cancel his application for a state veterans' loan at any time prior to receipt of the loan proceeds.  (2) The Director may cancel any application if the applicant fails to comply with any of the conditions pertaining to the loan.  (3) The Director may destroy any application 25 months after the veteran applicant is notified of action taken on an application (whether credit was approved or adverse action was taken). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 406.030, 407.115, 407.135, 407.145, 407.275, 407.305 & 407.375 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 406.030, 407.115, 407.265 & 407.385
