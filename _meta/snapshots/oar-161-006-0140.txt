 Appraiser Certification and Licensure Board 
 Chapter 161 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 ORGANIZATION, ADMINISTRATION AND PROCEDURES 
 161-006-0140 
 Registry 
 
 The Board shall, compile and publish on the Board website a registry of the names and addresses of all persons both licensed and certified to perform real estate appraisals. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 674.305(8) & 674.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 674.310
