 Oregon Board of Dentistry 
 Chapter 818 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 STANDARDS OF PRACTICE 
 818-012-0006 
 Qualifications - Administration of Vaccines 
 
 (1) A dentist may administer vaccines to a patient of record. 

 (2) A dentist may administer vaccines under Section (1) of this rule only if: 

 (a) The dentist has completed a course of training approved by the Board; and 

 (b) The vaccines are administered in accordance with the “Model Standing Orders” approved by the Oregon Health Authority (OHA). 

 (3) The dentist may not delegate the administration of vaccines to another person. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 679 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OBD 2-2019, adopt filed 10/29/2019, effective 01/01/2020
