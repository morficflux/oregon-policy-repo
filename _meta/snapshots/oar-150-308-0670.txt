 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ASSESSMENT OF PROPERTY FOR TAXATION 
 150-308-0670 
 Contents of the Utility Assessment Roll 
 
 For each company assessed, the department will include on the assessment roll according to the best information available, the following information:  (1) The name of the company;  (2) The name of a company contact or authorized agent;  (3) The last known address of the company or authorized agent;  (4) A general property description;  (5) The assessed value (AV), real market value (RMV), AV exceptions, and the RMV of exceptions;  (6) The values apportioned to each county where the property is located, including code areas, locations, county reference number, miles of track, wire or pipe, and values per mile, as applicable;  (7) Any penalty assessed under ORS 308.030;  (8) Any other information the department deems necessary for the counties to perform their duties as it pertains to the value determined for utility companies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 308.560
