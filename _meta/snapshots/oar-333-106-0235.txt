 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 106 X-RAYS IN THE HEALING ARTS 
 333-106-0235 
 Fluoroscopic X-ray Systems Requirements: Mobile Fluoroscopes 
 
 In addition to the other requirements of OAR 333-106-0201 through 333-106-0245 of these rules, mobile fluoroscopes shall provide intensified imaging. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
