 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 118 STATE AGENCY ENERGY SAVINGS PROGRAM 
 330-118-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 OAR 330-118-0000 through 330-118-0090 allows state agencies to retain and spend 50 percent of the net savings from energy projects pursuant to ORS 469.752 to 469.756. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.752 - 469.756 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.752 - 469.756 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOE 1-1992, f. & cert. ef. 4-30-92
