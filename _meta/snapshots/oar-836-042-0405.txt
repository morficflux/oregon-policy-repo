 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 RATES AND RATEMAKING 
 836-042-0405 
 Definitions 
 
 As used in OAR 836-042-0400 to 836-042-0430:  (1) “Casualty Insurance” as defined in ORS 731.158 and as used in 836-042-0400 to 836-042-0430 means liability or casualty insurance as used in 737.600(3)(f).  (2) “Rating System” means a collection of rating plans to be used by an insurer, rules for determining which rating plans are applicable to an insured, a classification system and other rules used by an insurer for determining contractual consideration for an insured. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 & 737 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 737.600(3)(f)–(g) 
 
 History:
