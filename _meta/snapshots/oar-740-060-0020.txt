 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 TRANSPORTATION OF HOUSEHOLD GOODS 
 740-060-0020 
 Inventories 
 
 (1) An inventory of items will be prepared when requested by the shipper or when household goods are received into storage in transit or permanent storage.  (2) The carrier must list any damage or unusual wear.  (3) Shippers must be allowed to note in writing on the inventory documents any disagreement with entries regarding damage or unusual wear noted by the carrier. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 825.232 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.202, 825.204 & 825.224
