 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 CLINICAL LABORATORIES 
 333-024-0385 
 Health Screen Testing: Testing Site Schedule
 
 
 The health screen testing service shall, in writing, notify the Oregon Health Authority, Oregon State Public Health Laboratory (OSPHL) of the proposed testing site schedule. The schedule must be received at the OSPHL, at least 15 days prior to testing at each site. All changes and cancellations regarding this schedule must be reported to the OSPHL prior to testing date. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 438.010, ORS 438.060 & ORS 438.150 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 438.150
