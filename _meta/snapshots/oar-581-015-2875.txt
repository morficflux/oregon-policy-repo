 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2875 
 Discipline of Children with Disabilities for ECSE 
 
 Contractors or subcontractors must follow the rules and procedures in OAR 581-015-2400 through 581-015-2445 for discipline of children with disabilities age three through the age of eligibility for public school with the following definition exceptions:  (1) "General curriculum" means appropriate activities engaged in by typical children of the same age;  (2) "IEP" means IFSP; and  (3) "Day" means calendar days excluding weekends, holidays, and ECSE program vacation days.  (4) "School district" means contractors or subcontractors. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.475 & 343.531 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 343.475 & 343.531
