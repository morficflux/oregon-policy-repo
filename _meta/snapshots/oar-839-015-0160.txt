 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 RULES REGULATING LABOR CONTRACTORS 
 839-015-0160 
 Procedure for Obtaining a Duplicate License 
 
 In the event a license is lost or stolen, the licensee shall submit a written request for a duplicate license. The licensee shall state the reasons for the request and the circumstances of the loss or theft. The new license will indicate the word "DUPLICATE" on the license above the number. The written request shall be made to the Bureau of Labor and Industries, Wage and Hour Division, License Unit, 3865 Wolverine Street, NE, Bldg. E-1, Salem, OR 97305. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 164, 165, 651, 658 & 962 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 658.405 - 658.503
