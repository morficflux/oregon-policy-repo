 Employment Department 
 Chapter 471 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 UNEMPLOYMENT INSURANCE TAXES 
 471-031-0110 
 Action at Law, Verification, Liens, Distraint Warrants 
 
 The Assistant Director for UI Programs, Manager of Tax, or other person designated by the Director is authorized for and on behalf of the Director to:  (1) Verify all pleadings filed in actions at law, and to perform such acts necessary in the prosecution of such actions at law.  (2) Execute and file lien claims and distraint warrants for taxes, interest, and penalty due and owing the Unemployment Compensation Trust Fund and the Employment Department Special Administrative Fund.  (3) The effective date for implementing this rule shall be retroactive to June 23rd, 2002. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 657 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 657.457 & 657.504 - 657.575
