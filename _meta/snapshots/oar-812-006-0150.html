Construction Contractors Board

Chapter 812

Division 6
RESPONSIBLE MANAGING INDIVIDUAL, PRE-LICENSURE TRAINING, AND TESTING

812-006-0150
Pre-Licensure Training Requirements

(1) Except as provided in OAR 812-006-0160 and 812-006-0162, the pre-licensure training required in ORS 701.122 shall cover the subjects listed in OAR 812-006-0250.

(2) Pre-licensure training shall consist of at least 16 hours.

(3) Except as provided in:

(a) OAR 812-006-0160 and 812-006-0162 pre-licensure training must be provided by a training provider approved by the agency as provided in OAR 812-006-0200; and also,

(b) OAR 812-006-0164, where pre-licensure training is provided pursuant to an interagency agreement with an Oregon community college Small Business Development Center, pre-licensure training must be provided by a training provider approved by the agency as provided in OAR 812-006-0160 or OAR 812-006-0200.

(4) A person seeking to take the pre-licensure training shall:

(a) Pay any fees required by the pre-licensure training provider; and

(b) Provide approved U.S. government-issued picture identification to the pre-licensure training provider.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, 701.122, 701.235 & OR Laws 2018, Ch 46, Secs 1,2
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.122 & OR Laws 2018, Ch 46, Secs 1,2
