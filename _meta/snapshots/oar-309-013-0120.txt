 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 13 ACCOUNTING AND BUSINESS PRACTICES 
 309-013-0120 
 Purpose and Statutory Authority 
 
 (1) Purpose. These rules establish a Division procedure for audits of community mental health programs, mental health organizations and their subcontractors and vendors and any service provider agreeing to offer services through direct contract with the Division. These rules also establish basic record keeping standards for programs subject to audit under these rules, establish procedures for appealing audit findings, and set out a process to implement the findings of the final audit report.  (2) Statutory Authority. These rules are authorized by ORS 179.040, 413.042 & 430.640 and are promulgated to enable the Division to carry out its responsibilities under ORS 414.018 to 414.024 and 430.610 through 430.695. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 413.042 & 430.640 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 414.018 & 430.610–430.695
