 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 ATTORNEY FEES 
 438-015-0045 
 Attorney Fees When a Claimant Requests a Hearing on Extent of Temporary Disability 
 
 If the Administrative Law Judge awards additional compensation for temporary disability benefits, the Administrative Law Judge shall award a reasonable assessed attorney fee. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.307, 656.388, 656.593 & 656.726(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.383, 656.386(2) & 656.388(3)
