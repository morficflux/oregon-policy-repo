 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 836-010-0130 
 Statutory Authority; Purpose; Applicability 
 
 (1) OAR 836-010-0130 to 836-010-0145 are adopted pursuant to the general rulemaking authority of the Commissioner in ORS 731.244 to aid in the effectuation and enforcement of 731.438.  (2) The purpose of OAR 836-010-0130 to 836-010-0145 is to prescribe title plant standards for title insurers.  (3) OAR 836-010-0130 to 836-010-0145 applies to all authorized title insurers. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 731.438 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 IC 1-1978, f. 3-27-78, ef. 4-1-78
