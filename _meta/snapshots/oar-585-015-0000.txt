 Commission for the Blind 
 Chapter 585 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 BUSINESS ENTERPRISE PROGRAM RULES 
 585-015-0000 
 Introduction 
 
 These rules govern the Business Enterprise Program of the Oregon Commission for the Blind. They are intended to supplement the governing law of the Randolph-Sheppard Act, 20 USC sections 107 to 107f and ORS 346.510 to 346.570. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  HB 3253 (2017) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 346.510 to 346.570 & Randolph-Sheppard Act, 20 USC sections 107 to 107f 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CFTB 4-2017, adopt filed 12/21/2017, effective 12/21/2017
