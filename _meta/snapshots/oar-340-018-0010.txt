 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 STATE AGENCY COORDINATION PROGRAM 
 340-018-0010 
 Policy 
 
 It is the Commission’s policy to coordinate the Department’s programs, rules and actions that affect land use with local acknowledged plans to the fullest degree possible. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.180 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 36-1990, f. & cert. ef. 8-28-90
