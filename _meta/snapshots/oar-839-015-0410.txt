 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 RULES REGULATING LABOR CONTRACTORS 
 839-015-0410 
 Records Availability 
 
 Labor Contractors will make available to representatives of the Wage and Hour Division records necessary to determine compliance with ORS 658.405 to 658.475 and these rules. Records will be made available upon request of such representatives. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 651 & 658.407(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 658.405 - 658.503
