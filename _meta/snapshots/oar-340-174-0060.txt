 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 174 UNDERGROUND STORAGE TANK INSURANCE COPAYMENT PROGRAM 
 340-174-0060 
 Requirement of Financial Responsibility 
 
 (1) The applicant shall provide proof of financial responsibility for cleanup of releases from the underground storage tank and any third party damages resulting from releases from the underground storage tank as required in OAR 340, division 150 and federal regulation 40 CFR 280 and 340-174-0065.  (2) The proof of financial responsibility shall be documented by an insurance coverage policy provided by an insurer containing the information listed in OAR 340-174-0070(1)(e)(A)–(F). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 466.706 - 466.895 & 466.995 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  1991, Ch. 863 & Sec. 38 - 43a
