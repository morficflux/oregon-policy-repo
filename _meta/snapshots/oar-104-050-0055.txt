 Oregon Department of Emergency Management 
 Chapter 104 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 OREGON SEISMIC REHABILITATION GRANTS PROGRAM 
 104-050-0055 
 Project Ineligible Activities 
 
 Project ineligible activities include, but are not limited to:  (1) The demolition and rebuild of an existing critical public building.  (2) Rehabilitation to a building located in the Tsunami Inundation Zone as defined in OAR 104-050-0010.  (3) Rehabilitation of a building located in the flood zone, with no previous mitigation activities completed.  (4) Partial rehabilitation of a building that does not holistically address all known seismic deficiencies, as defined in OAR 104-050-0010. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 401.300 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 401.300
