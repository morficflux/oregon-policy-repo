 Oregon Board of Physical Therapy 
 Chapter 848 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 MINIMUM STANDARDS FOR PHYSICAL THERAPY PRACTICE AND RECORDS 
 848-040-0175 
 Standards for Screening Services
 
 
 (1) “Physical therapy screening” means: 

 (a) The process of determining whether a person or animal would benefit from a physical therapy evaluation or referral to another health care professional; and 

 (b)  The process of determining whether a current patient has a new or separate condition that can be addressed by physical therapy treatment or intervention. 

 (2) A physical therapist or physical therapist assistant may conduct or perform a physical therapy screening of a person who is not currently a physical therapy patient. 

 (3) Only a physical therapist may conduct or perform a physical therapy screening of a current patient. 

 (4) A physical therapist or physical therapist assistant shall not delegate the performance of a physical therapy screening to an aide. 

 (5) A screening is not a physical therapy treatment or intervention and does not require or involve performance of an initial evaluation, preparation of a plan of care, or creation of a patient treatment record. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 688.160 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 688.160(6)(g)
