 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 225 BOILERS AND PRESSURE VESSELS 
 918-225-0565 
 Reporting of Immediate Hazards to Health or Safety 
 
 Deputy and special inspectors shall report immediate hazards to health or safety concerning boilers or pressure vessels to the chief inspector immediately by telephone and also that a notice of defective condition was issued and that the hazardous operations were stopped. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 480.545 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.545
