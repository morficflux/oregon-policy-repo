 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 385 DISTRICT WATER RIGHT TRANSFERS 
 690-385-5300 
 District Permanent Transfer of Water Right for Nonuse Application Map Requirements 
 
 A district permanent transfer application map shall meet the standards of OAR 690-385-2200. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025 & 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 540.574 & 540.580
