 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 246 GENERAL PROVISIONS FOR PUBLIC CONTRACTING 
 125-246-0135 
 Solicitation Templates; Contract Forms and Templates; Accountability for Advice (Director Verification)
 
 
 (1) See OAR 137-046-0140. 

 (2) Regardless of OAR 137-046-0140, a State Contracting Agency, in Solicitation documents, must comply with SB 471 (Chapter 294, 2019) regarding conflict minerals. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070
