 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 OREGON AFFORDABLE HOUSING TAX CREDITS: AFFORDABLE HOUSING PROJECT CERTIFICATION 
 813-110-0050 
 Waiver
 
 
 The Director may waive or modify any requirements of these Program rules unless such waiver or modification would violate applicable state statute or federal regulations. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 317.097 & ORS 456.515 - 456.725 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 317.097
