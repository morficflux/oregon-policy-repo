 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 HOSPICE PROGRAM LICENSURE 
 333-035-0290 
 Approval of Accrediting Organizations
 
 
 (1) An accrediting organization must request approval by the Authority to accredit hospice programs in Oregon. 

 (2) An accrediting organization shall request approval in writing and shall provide, at a minimum: 

 (a) Evidence that it is recognized as a CMS-approved accrediting organization; or 

 (b) Documentation of program policies and procedures that the accreditation meets standards and conditions established for hospice programs by CMS; 

 (c) Accreditation history; and 

 (d) References from a minimum of two hospice programs currently receiving services from the organization. 

 (3) If the Authority finds that an accrediting organization’s qualifications are equal to or exceed state licensing requirements in Oregon, the Authority may enter into an agreement with the accrediting organization permitting it to accredit hospice programs in Oregon. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 443.860 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 443.860
