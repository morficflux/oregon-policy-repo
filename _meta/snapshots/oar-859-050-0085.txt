 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD HEARING PROCEDURES 
 859-050-0085 
 Notification of Right to Appeal 
 
 (1) At the conclusion of a Board hearing, the Board Chair or acting Chair will provide the person and his or her attorney with written notification advising of the right to appeal on an adverse decision within 60 days from the date an order is signed and the right to an attorney is indigent.  (2) If the person refuses to sign the Notification of Appeal, the person’s attorney/PSRB staff will sign the Notification of Appeal attesting that the person refuses to sign it. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.385
