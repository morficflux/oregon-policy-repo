 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2880 
 Private Placement — ECSE 
 
 Contractors or subcontractors must follow the rules and procedures in OAR 581-015-2450 through 581-015-2515 for ECSE children placed by their parents in private schools. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.475 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 343.475 & 343.495
