 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 COMMERCIAL SHELLFISH AND MARINE INVERTEBRATE FISHERIES 
 635-005-0250 
 Commercial Shellfish and Intertidal Animal Harvest Permit Fee
 
 
 The annual fee for the Commercial Shellfish And Intertidal Animal Harvest Permit is set pursuant to ORS 508.116. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 508.116
