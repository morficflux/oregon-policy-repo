 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 COUNCIL MEETINGS AND COMMUNICATIONS 
 345-011-0000 
 Authority and Purpose 
 
 The purpose of the rules in this division is to provide procedures for the orderly conduct of meetings of the Council. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 469.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192 & 469.460
