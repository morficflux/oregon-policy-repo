 Board of Chiropractic Examiners 
 Chapter 811 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 RULES GOVERNING APPROVAL OF SCHOOLS 
 811-020-0011 
 List of Approved Schools and Programs
 
 
 The list of approved schools and programs shall be made available upon request as recognized, endorsed and as published by the Council on Chiropractic Education and approved by the Board. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 684 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 684.050 & 684.155(5)
