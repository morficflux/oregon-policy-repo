 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 LICENSING - PROCEDURES FOR ESTABLISHING, OPERATING, DISCONTINUING AND SUSPENDING LICENSES AND AGENTS 
 635-010-0009 
 Document Pricing 
 
 Statutory document pricing and Agent fees are listed in ORS 497.022 and 497.061.  Fees established by rule can be found in OAR 635-008, 635-043, 635-046, 635-047, 635-060, 635-067 and 635-090 as well in regulation publications. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 497.022 & ORS 497.061 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 497.022 & ORS 497.061 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DFW 160-2018, adopt filed 10/18/2018, effective 01/01/2019
