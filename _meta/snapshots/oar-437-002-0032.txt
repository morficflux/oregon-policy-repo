 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 GENERAL OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH RULES 
 437-002-0032 
 Ramps and Runways 
 
 (1) Scope and application. This rule applies to the safe use of ramps and runways for vehicles in addition to the rules in Division 2/D.  (2) The employer must ensure ramps and runways for vehicles:  (a) Have adequate width and evenness for safe operation of equipment.  (b) Are provided with timber guards of not less than nominal 6-inch by 6-inch material set on nominal 3-inch blocks, or the equivalent, placed parallel to and secured to the sides of the ramp or runway. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295
