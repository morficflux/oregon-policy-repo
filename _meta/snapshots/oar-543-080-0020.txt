 State Library 
 Chapter 543 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 PUBLIC LIBRARIES 
 543-080-0020 
 Annual Reports
 
 
 (1) Not later than October 31 of each year, public libraries shall submit an annual report to the State Library using a form furnished by the State Library. 

 (2) The report shall cover the previous fiscal year. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 357 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 357.520
