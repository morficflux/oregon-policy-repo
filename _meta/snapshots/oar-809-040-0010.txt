 Board of Geologist Examiners 
 Chapter 809 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 EXAMINATION PROCEDURES 
 809-040-0010 
 Scoring
 
 
 The minimum passing score for each the fundamental and the practical sections of the national examination shall be 70 percent. The passing score of the engineering geologist examination shall be 70 percent. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310(1), 672.555, 672.565, ORS 672.575 & 672.585 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.575, ORS 672.555(2)(c) & 672.565(2)
