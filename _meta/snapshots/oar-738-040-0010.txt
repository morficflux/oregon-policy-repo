 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 SEAPLANE OPERATION 
 738-040-0010 
 Purpose and Statutory Authority 
 
 To regulate seaplane operations on certain waters of this state to ensure the safe operation of such aircraft in relation to marine craft and persons using the same waters:  (1) ORS 184.619 pertaining to rulemaking authority.  (2) ORS 835.035 pertaining to the general public interest, safety and the development and promotion of aeronautics.  (3) ORS 835.080 directing the State Aviation Board to adopt rules governing seaplane safety and operations on state waters. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 835.035 & 835.080 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 835.035, 835.080 & 835.085
