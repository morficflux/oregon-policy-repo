 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 POLE AND CONDUIT ATTACHMENTS 
 860-028-0180 
 Removal of Occupant Pole Attachments 
 
 (1) If the pole occupant fails to meet the time limitations set out in OARs 860-028-0120, 860-028-0130, 860-028-0140, or 860-028-0150 by 180 or more days, then the pole owner may request an order from the Commission authorizing removal of the pole occupant's attachments. Nothing in this section precludes a party from pursuing other legal remedies.  (2) This rule does not apply to a pole occupant that is a government entity. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040, 757.035, 757.270-290, 759.045 & 759.650-675
