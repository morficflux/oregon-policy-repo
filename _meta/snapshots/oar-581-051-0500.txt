 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 SCHOOL FOOD AND NUTRITION SERVICES 
 581-051-0500 
 Purpose of Rules
 
 
 These rules are adopted to assist National School Lunch Program, School Breakfast Program, Child and Adult Care Food Program, Summer Food Service Program, and USDA Donated Food Program Organizations in the development of contracts for Food Service Management Companies. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.051, 7 CFR 210.21, 7 CFR 210.16, 7 CFR 220.16, 7 CFR 220.7(d), 7 CFR 226.22, 7 CFR 226.21, 7 CFR 225.17, 7 CFR 225.15(m), 7 CFR 250.53 & 2 CFR 200
