 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 OREGON VETERANS’ HOME 
 274-040-0031 
 Applications 
 
 (1) Applications for assistance from the Covered Care Program account shall be made in such manner and detail, and on such forms, as the Department shall determine.  (2) Applications generally will be prioritized for consideration based on the date of completed receipt by the Department. The Department may, however, consider applications in such other order and at such other times as deemed reasonable. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406.050, 408.360, 408.365 & 408.368 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 408.365 & 408.368 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DVA 9-2006, f. & cert. ef. 7-27-06
