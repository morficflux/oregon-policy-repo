 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 37 INSTRUCTIONAL ASSISTANTS 
 581-037-0025 
 Training of Instructional Assistants
 
 
 Districts employing instructional assistants in any capacity shall provide or arrange for suitable training to prepare them to perform such functions as they may be assigned. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.051 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 326.051
