 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office, Board of Cosmetology - Chapter 817 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 SAFETY AND INFECTION CONTROL 
 817-010-0110 
 Walls and Ceilings 
 
 Walls and ceilings must be clean and free of condensation, mildew and excessive spots, or peeling paint. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 690 
 
 
 History:
