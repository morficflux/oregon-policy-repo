 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 GENERAL OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH RULES 
 437-002-0283 
 Additional Protective Clothing Requirements During Hot Work Activities
 
 
 (1) Require employees to completely cover their skin with a double layer of clothing or equivalent to prevent burns or other damage by ultraviolet light. 
(2) Do not allow employees to wear easily ignited, highly flammable clothing, such as is made from synthetic materials. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2), 654.035 & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 through 654.295
