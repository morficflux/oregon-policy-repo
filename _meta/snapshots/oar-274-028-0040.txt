 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 VETERANS’ HOME IMPROVEMENT LOAN PROGRAM 
 274-028-0040 
 Title Insurance 
 
 Each loan shall be covered by a title insurance policy as designated by the director at the borrower’s expense. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 407.115, 407.177, 407.179 & 407.181 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 407.115, 407.177, 407.179 & 407.181 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DVA 6-1997, f. & cert. ef. 10-22-97
