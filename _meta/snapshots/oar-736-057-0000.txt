 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 57 Certified Local Government and Preserving Oregon Grant Programs 
 736-057-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 This division establishes the State Parks and Recreation Department procedures and criteria for distributing federal Historic Preservation Fund monies to Certified Local Governments and awarding the Preserving Oregon Grant Program Funds to qualified applicants. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.124 & 358.617 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 358.475, 358.605 & 358.612(2) and (12) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PRD 3-2020, adopt filed 02/21/2020, effective 02/21/2020
