 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 77 TRANSPORTATION OF FOOD PROCESSING PLANT BY-PRODUCTS FROM WHICH THERE IS FLUID LEAKAGE 
 734-077-0007 
 Authorized Commodities 
 
 Permits shall be issued only for the following:  (1) Vehicles transporting food processing plant by-products to be used for livestock feed or fertilizer from which there is a fluid leakage; or  (2) Vehicles transporting agricultural products from which there is fluid leakage, while the vehicles are enroute from the place of harvest to a place where the products will be processed, stored or sold. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184, 366 & 818 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.230 
 
 History:
