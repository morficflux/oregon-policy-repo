 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 123 RULES FOR GRANTING EASEMENTS ON NON-TRUST LANDS 
 141-123-0080 
 Transfer of Easements 
 
 (1) An easement in good standing is transferable. Prior written consent of the Department is required prior to any transfer of the easement. No transfer may increase the burden on the estate or detract from the value of the underlying state land.  
(2) The Holder of an easement wanting to transfer their easement must submit to the Department: 
(a) Notice of proposed transfer on a form provided by the Department at least 60 calendar days prior to the date that the transfer is to occur; and 
(b) Non-refundable transfer processing fee of $750 payable to the Department. 
  

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 273.045 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.761, 274.040, 274.720, 376.620, 530.050, 530.490 & 758.010
