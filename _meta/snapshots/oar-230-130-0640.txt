 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 130 CONDUCT OF BOUT - MUAY THAI/ KICKBOXING 
 230-130-0640 
 Announcing Winners 
 
 At the termination of each bout, the winner shall be announced by the announcer and the referee shall raise the winner’s hand. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SAC 13-2018, adopt filed 01/09/2018, effective 01/09/2018
