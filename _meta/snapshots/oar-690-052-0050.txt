 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 52 DECOMMISSIONING RULES FOR NON-FERC PROJECTS 
 690-052-0050 
 Expedited Process
 
 
 If the Department decides to conduct the decommissioning using the expedited process, within 120 days of making that decision, the Department shall develop an initial draft plan considering available information and comments received. Upon completion of the plan, the Department shall distribute the plan to state agencies for comment according to OAR 690-052-0080. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027 & ORS 543A.300(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 543 & ORS 543A
