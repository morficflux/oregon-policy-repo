 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 GENETIC INFORMATION AND PRIVACY 
 333-025-0155 
 Retention of Genetic Information for the Purpose of Newborn Screening Procedures 
 
 The Oregon Health Authority may retain the blood samples of newborns collected for the control of metabolic diseases, as provided in ORS 433.285, for up to one year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 433.285, 192.535 & 192.537 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.535 & 192.537
