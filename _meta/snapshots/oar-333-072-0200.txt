 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 72 HEALTH CARE PRACTITIONER REFERRALS 
 333-072-0200 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to establish notice requirements for patient choice and financial interest as required in ORS 441.098 when health practitioners refer patients for diagnostic testing or health care treatment or services. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 441.098 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 441.098 
 
 History:
