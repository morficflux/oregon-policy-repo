 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 SAFETY CODE FOR ELEVATORS 
 918-400-0220 
 Attorney General Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure, as adopted by the division in OAR 918-001-0010, apply in all contested cases. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341
