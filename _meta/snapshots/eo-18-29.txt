Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-29

                    AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 16-07


    Executive Order 16-07 was issued on March 29, 2016.


    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:
    On page one of Executive Order 16-07, the fourth paragraph is amended to provide
    as follows:


       In the event the Governor is not available immediately following an emergency,
       the emergency powers bestowed to the Governor as described in ORS 401.168
       are delegated to the Secretary of State, then to the State Treasurer, including the
       activation of the Governor’s Disaster Management Framework.


    Additionally, on page two of Executive Order 16-07, the first sentence of Section 1
    is amended to provide as follows:


       The Governor’s Disaster Cabinet (GDC) is hereby established and may provide
       recommendations to the Governor regarding statewide priorities, allocation of
       limited state emergency resources, and use of monies and appropriations of
       funds as described in ORS 401.168(3) to help Oregon effectively respond to
       and recover from disasters.
