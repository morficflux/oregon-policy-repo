 Department of Human Services 
 Vocational Rehabilitation Services - Chapter 582 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND REVIEW OF DETERMINATIONS OF REHABILITATION SERVICES 
 582-020-0095 
 Civil Action 
 
 Any party who disagrees with the reviewing official’s final order may bring a civil action with respect to the disputed action. The action may be brought in any state court of competent jurisdiction or in a district court of the United States of competent jurisdiction without regard to the amount in controversy. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 344.530 & 344.590 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 344.530 & ORS 344.540 & 344.590 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 VRS 3-2020, adopt filed 02/21/2020, effective 03/06/2020
