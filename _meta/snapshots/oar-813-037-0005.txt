 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 37 AFFORDABLE HOUSING LAND ACQUISITION REVOLVING LOAN PROGRAM (LAP) 
 813-037-0005 
 Purpose and Objectives 
 
 OAR Chapter 813, Division 37 is established to enact the Affordable Housing Land Acquisition Revolving Loan Program or Land Acquisition Program (LAP) as authorized in ORS 456.502. LAP is also subject to OHCS’s general rules, including but not limited to OAR chapter 813, division 5, and subject to OHCS’s general procedures for public contracts and procurements found in OAR chapter 813, division 6. The purpose of LAP is to provide financial assistance to eligible organizations to purchase land for affordable housing development. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.502 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.502
