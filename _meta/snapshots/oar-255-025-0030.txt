 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 ADJUSTED COMMITMENT DATE 
 255-025-0030 
 Effect of Inoperative Time on Prison Terms
 
 
 Inoperative time shall not count towards the completion of a prison term or an incarceration term for violation of parole or post-prison supervision conditions. In resetting the release date, the inoperative time shall be added to the term as provided in OAR 255-050-0015. The Board shall notify the inmate of its action. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144.050 & 144.140 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 144.050 & 144.140
