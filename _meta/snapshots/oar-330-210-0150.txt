 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 210 CONSERVATION ENERGY INCENTIVE PROGRAM 
 330-210-0150 
 Compliance and Pass-through 
 
 (1) All participants in this program are subject to OAR 330-230. If the tax credit is subject to recertification under OAR 330-210-0110, only that portion of the tax credit that has been certified or recertified may be transferred at the five-year present value rate in OAR 330-230-0130.  (2) The department may periodically inspect energy conservation projects and related documents during the five-year term of the tax credit. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.040 & 469B.306 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469B.270 - 469B.306 & 315.331
