 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 160 ESTABLISH A RENEWABLE ENERGY CERTIFICATE SYSTEM FOR THE OREGON RENEWABLE PORTFOLIO STANDARD (RPS) 
 330-160-0037 
 Confidential Treatment of Information 
 
 A generator representative may request confidential treatment of information provided to the Department pursuant to OAR 330-010-0005 through 330-010-0030. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469A.130 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469A.130 - 469A.145 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOE 1-2014, f. & cert. ef. 2-10-14
