 Oregon Department of Emergency Management 
 Chapter 104 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 OREGON SEISMIC REHABILITATION GRANTS PROGRAM 
 104-050-0020 
 Eligible Applicants 
 
 The following are eligible to apply for a Seismic Rehabilitation Grant, except those determined to be ineligible by the Department because of nonperformance under a prior Seismic Rehabilitation Grant contract:  (1) All hospital buildings with acute inpatient care facilities, fire stations, police stations, sheriffs’ offices, other facilities used by state, county, district or municipal law enforcement agencies.  (2) Kindergarten through grade 12 public schools, community colleges, education service districts and institutions of higher education buildings with a capacity of 250 or more persons that are routinely used for student activities and are owned by the State Board of Higher Education, a school district, an education service district, a community college district or a community college service district. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 401.300 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 401.300
