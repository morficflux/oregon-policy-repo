 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 62 ENERGY CONSERVATION STANDARD FOR EXISTING DWELLINGS 
 330-062-0040 
 Mandatory Weatherization Standards for Oregon Department of Veterans' Affairs Home Loans Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 330-062-0045, pursuant to ORS 407.055, is to adopt mandatory minimum weatherization standards for approval of loans by the Oregon Department of Veterans’ Affairs on Homes constructed before July 1, 1974, as defined in ORS 407.010. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 407, 469 & 470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 407.055 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOE 9-1982, f. & ef. 7-1-82
