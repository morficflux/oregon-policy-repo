 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Processed Vegetable Commission - Chapter 647 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 647-001-0000 
 Notice Rule for Rulemaking 
 
 Prior to adoption, amendment or repeal of any rule the Oregon Processed Vegetable Commission shall give notice of the intended action:  (1) In the Secretary of State’s Bulletin referred to in ORS 183.360 at least 21 days before the effective date of the rule;  (2) By mailing a copy of the notice to persons on the Oregon Processed Vegetable Commission mailing list established pursuant to ORS 183.335(7) at least 28 days before the effective date of the rule; and  (3) By mailing or furnishing a copy of the notice to:  (a) The Associated Press;  (b) The Capital Press; and  (c) Capitol Press Room. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576.325 - 576.575 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341(4) 
 
 History:
