 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 900 CLIMATE AND OCEAN CHANGE POLICY 
 635-900-0013 
 Climate and Ocean Change Key Principles for Coordination 
 
 (1) The Department should collaborate and partner with other agencies, tribes, stakeholders, and academics to achieve successful implementation of this policy. 

 (2) Collaborations and partnerships should span scientific expertise, stakeholder interests and regulatory authorities. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, ORS 506.036, ORS 506.109 & ORS 496.138 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, ORS 506.036 & ORS 506.109 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DFW 90-2020, adopt filed 07/14/2020, effective 07/14/2020
