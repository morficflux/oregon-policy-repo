 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 87 NURSING FACILITIES/LICENSING —  PHYSICAL ENVIRONMENT 
 411-087-0330 
 Employee Rooms 
 
 (1) Rooms Required. The facility shall have an employee lounge and room(s) for conferences, meetings and inservice training. This requirement may be met with a multi-purpose room, but must be in addition to space required for residents.  (2) Exceptions. Facilities continuously licensed since January 1, 1992 shall be exempt from section (1) of this rule unless otherwise provided by OAR 411-087-0010. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 & 441.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 441.055 & 441.615 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SSD 22-1991, f. 12-31-91, cert. ef. 1-1-92
