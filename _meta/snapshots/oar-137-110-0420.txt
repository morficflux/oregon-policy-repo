 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 OREGON FORECLOSURE AVOIDANCE PROGRAM 
 137-110-0420 
 Grantor Request for Resolution Conference 
 
 A grantor may request a resolution conference either:  (1) Through a housing counselor who may request a resolution conference on behalf of the grantor by using the service provider website to certify that the grantor is more than 30 days in default on the obligation that the residential trust deed secures or, if the grantor is not in default, that the grantor has a financial hardship that the housing counselor believes may qualify the grantor for a foreclosure avoidance measure.  (2) By submitting a request to the service provider electronically via e-mail or the service provider website, by facsimile, or by mail. The request shall include a certification by a housing counselor that the grantor is more than 30 days in default on the obligation that the residential trust deed secures or, if the grantor is not in default, that the grantor has a financial hardship that the housing counselor believes may qualify the grantor for a foreclosure avoidance measure.
