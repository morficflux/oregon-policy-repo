 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 STATE PARK MASTER PLANNING 
 736-018-0060 
 Management of State Park Forests: Objectives 
 
 (1) Protect the natural qualities of sensitive forest resources.  (2) Manage forests to control fire and destructive pests, improve growth and vigor, rehabilitate damaged areas, and create desirable conditions.  (3) Manage forests for safe, attractive, and compatible recreation opportunities.  (4) Revenue generation is not an objective of park forest management, except in areas designated through publicized processes as being surplus to park needs. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.121 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PR 12-1986, f. & ef. 7-29-86
