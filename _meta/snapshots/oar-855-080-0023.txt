 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 SCHEDULE OF CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES 
 855-080-0023 
 Schedule III
 
 
 Schedule III consists of the drugs and other substances by whatever official, common, usual, chemical, or brand name designated, listed in 21 CFR 1308.13 (v. 04/01/2025).  

 [Publications: Publications referenced are available for review at the agency.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689.205 & ORS 475.973 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475.035
