 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 SPECIAL FOREST PRODUCTS; WOODCUTTING PERMITTING ON STATE FOREST LANDS 
 629-028-0020 
 Criteria for Fee Charge; State Forest Land Woodcutting Permits 
 
 (1) In determining when to charge a permit fee, and the amount of such fee, the Forester shall consider the following:  (a) The amount of demand for woodcutting permits in local areas or districts;  (b) The amount of wood available for cutting and any costs incurred in making it accessible for permit use, such as yarding, bunching, posting, marking, and road improvement or maintenance;  (c) The possible impact of administration of the woodcutting permit program upon the accomplishment of other activities necessary to the management of the lands.  (2) If any of the above criteria or combination of criteria under the free permit system cause significant impact in the administration of general State forest land programs, the Forester may implement the fee permit system in a district or area. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 530 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 530.115 & 530.500
