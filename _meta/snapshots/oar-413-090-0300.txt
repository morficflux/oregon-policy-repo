 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 SUBSTITUTE CARE — PAYMENTS 
 413-090-0300 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 413-090-0300 to 413-090-0380 is to describe how payments for special and/or extraordinary needs may be used to benefit a child or young adult in the custody of the Department in foster care, family and professional shelter care, residential group care, or non-reimbursed placement such as SAIP and SCIP. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005
