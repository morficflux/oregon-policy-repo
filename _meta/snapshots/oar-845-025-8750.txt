 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 RECREATIONAL MARIJUANA 
 845-025-8750 
 License Surrender
 
 
 A licensee may request the Commission to accept the surrender of a license. The license remains in effect until the Commission accepts the surrender. If the Commission accepts the surrender, the Commission will notify the licensee of the date of acceptance. The licensee must cease all license privileges from on this date through the remainder of the licensing period. The licensee must apply for and receive a new license before engaging in any licensed activities. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.017 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.017
