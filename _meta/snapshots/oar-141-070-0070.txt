 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 LEASING OF ONSHORE STATE-OWNED OIL AND GAS RIGHTS 
 141-070-0070 
 Rejection 
 
 The Director may reject any application that is not in compliance with these rules or that is not in the public interest. If rejected, all fees shall be returned to applicant. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 273 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.780 - 273.790 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LB 3-1988, f. & cert. ef. 9-19-88
