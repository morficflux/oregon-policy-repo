 Citizens' Initiative Review Commission 
 Chapter 710 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 ADMINISTRATION 
 710-005-0005 
 Commission Budget 
 
 The Citizens’ Initiative Review Commission hereby adopts by reference the Citizens’ Initiative Review Commission 2015–2017 Biennium Budget of $243,250 covering the period from July 1, 2017 through June 30, 2019. The Chair of the Commission, in consultation with the Commission, will amend budgeted accounts as necessary within the approved budget of $243,250 for the effective operation of the Commission. The Commission will not exceed the approved 2017–2019 Biennium Budget without amending this rule and holding a public hearing thereon as required, by ORS 182.462(1). The budget can be found on the Commission’s website.
