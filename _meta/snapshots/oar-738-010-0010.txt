 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 RATES AND CHARGES FOR STATE-OWNED AIRPORTS 
 738-010-0010 
 Purpose of Rule and Statutory Authority 
 
 The purpose of this rule is to establish fair, reasonable and nondiscriminatory rates and charges for all users of Oregon State-owned airports. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 835.035, 835.040 & 835.112 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 835.035, 835.040, 835.112 & 836.055
