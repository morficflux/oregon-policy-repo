 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 PRIVACY OF PROTECTED INFORMATION 
 411-005-0045 
 Release of Alcohol and Drug and Mental Health Reports 
 
 (1) Unauthorized use, disclosure and redisclosure of alcohol and drug treatment information and mental health information is prohibited.  (2) A client has the right to restrict to what entity or person and for what purpose treatment information may be disclosed.  (3) The client’s right to limit use and disclosure of alcohol and drug and mental health information must be communicated to the client. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.140 & 411.300 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.140 & 410.150 & 179.505
