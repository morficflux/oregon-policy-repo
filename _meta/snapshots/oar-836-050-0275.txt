 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 836-050-0275 
 Credit Unions as Associations; Group Life Insurance 
 
 A credit union organized under ORS Chapter 723, a credit union authorized to conduct business as a credit union in this state under 723.042 or a federal credit union the principal office of which is located in Oregon is considered to be maintained primarily for purposes other than the procurement of insurance. Such a credit union may qualify as an association for the purposes of being the policyholder of a group life insurance policy if:  (1) The credit union is authorized to provide insurance to its members under the laws under which the credit union is organized; and  (2) The Director determines that the credit union otherwise satisfies the requirements of ORS 743.351(1). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 & 743 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 743.303 & 743.351
