 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. This policy provides guidelines for Oregon State Hospital (OSH) distribution of
      payroll, paychecks, and paystubs.
   B. This policy applies to all staff.

II. POLICY
   A. OSH adopts Shared Services policy DHS|OHA-040-007, “Payroll Management,”
      and related procedures, including ODHS|OHA-040-007-02, “Payroll Distribution.”
   B. Paper payroll checks will be held at Reception until picked up by the employee or
      designee, or until returned to the Payroll Office, in accordance with ODHS|OHA-
      040-007-02.
   C. OSH follows all applicable regulations, including federal and state statutes and
      rules; Oregon Department of Administrative Services (DAS), Shared Services,
      and Oregon Health Authority (OHA) policies; and relevant accreditation
      standards. Such regulations supersede the provisions of this policy unless this
      policy is more restrictive.
   D. Staff who fail to comply with this policy or related policy attachments or protocols
      may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including dismissal.

III. DEFINITIONS
   A. “Reception” means the front reception desk of the Salem and Junction City
      campuses of Oregon State Hospital.
