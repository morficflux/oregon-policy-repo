 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 113 UNIVERSAL WASTE MANAGEMENT 
 340-113-0000 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 (1) The purpose of the Division is to establish universal waste management standards for handlers, transporters and destination facilities of universal wastes.  (2) Persons must consult 40 CFR Part 273, which is incorporated by reference in OAR 340-100-0002, and associated Federal Register preambles, in addition to division 113 of these rules to determine all applicable universal waste management requirements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 4465.009 & 466.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 465.003, 465.009, 466.075 & 466.105
