 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 365 RENT GUARANTEE PROGRAM (RGP) 
 813-365-0015 
 Manual 
 
 The Rent Guarantee Program Operations Manual (RGP Manual) with the requirements and standards therein, dated May 24, 2023, is incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules, by reference. The RGP Manual may be accessed online at the OHCS website. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.555 & 456.608(10) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 458.505 & 456.607 to 456.609
