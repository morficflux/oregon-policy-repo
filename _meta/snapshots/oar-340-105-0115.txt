 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 105 MANAGEMENT FACILITY PERMITS 
 340-105-0115 
 Interim Status 
 
 The provisions of 40 CFR 270.70 to 270.73, pertaining to interim status, are not included in the State’s hazardous waste management program.  NOTE: State requirements applicable to existing hazardous waste management facilities are identified in rule 340-105-0010 and include provisions analogous to those of 40 CFR 270.71 and 270.72. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 459 & 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.030 
 
 History:
