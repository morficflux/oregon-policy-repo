 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 MANAGEMENT AND LEASING OF RANGELAND FORAGE 
 141-110-0145 
 Livestock Trespass 
 
 (1) Any unauthorized livestock found on state land will be considered in trespass.  (2) The Department will identify and notify the owner of all livestock found in trespass.  (3) The Department may pursue all appropriate legal remedies to assure that the Department is fairly compensated by the livestock owner for the impacts (including resource damages) of the trespassing livestock and that future trespass is deterred. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 273.045 & 273.051 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.805, 273.815 & 273.825 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DSL 7-2009, f. & cert. ef. 10-20-09
