 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 SCHOOL GOVERNANCE AND STUDENT CONDUCT 
 581-021-0300 
 A Parent or Eligible Student’s Request for Amendment of a Student’s Education Records 
 
 (1) If a parent or eligible student believes the education records relating to the student contain information that is inaccurate, misleading, or in violation of the student’s rights of privacy or other rights, he or she may ask the educational agency or institution to amend the record.  (2) The education agency or institution shall decide whether to amend the record as requested within a reasonable time after the agency or institution receives the request.  (3) If the educational agency or institution decides not to amend the record as requested, it shall inform the parent or eligible student of its decision and of his or her right to a hearing under OAR 581-021-0310. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.565 & 34 CFR § 99.20 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 326.565
