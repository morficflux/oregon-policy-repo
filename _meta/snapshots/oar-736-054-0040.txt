 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 54 HISTORIC CEMETERY GRANTS 
 736-054-0040 
 Permits to Restore, Maintain and Preserve an Abandoned Cemetery 
 
 Purpose 

 This division establishes the procedures, criteria and requirements that the Oregon Parks and Recreation Department (OPRD) will use in issuing a permit to restore, maintain, and preserve an abandoned cemetery. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 226.640(6) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 226.640 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PRD 7-2018, adopt filed 04/20/2018, effective 04/20/2018
