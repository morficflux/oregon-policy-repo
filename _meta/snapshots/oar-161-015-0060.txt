 Appraiser Certification and Licensure Board 
 Chapter 161 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 APPLICATION AND EXAMINATION PROCESS 
 161-015-0060 
 Examinations
 
 
 (1) The Board may administer its own appraiser examinations or it may contract with an examination service to provide and conduct the examinations. The examinations shall be compliant with the requirements of the Appraiser Qualifications Board of the Appraisal Foundation. 

 (2) Examination and re-examination fees, set by the testing agent, shall be paid by the applicant directly to the testing agent. 

 (3) The applicant may be required to pay a separate fee directly to the proctor. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 674.305(8) & 674.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 674.310
