 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 PREFERRED WORKER PROGRAM 
 436-110-0320 
 Preferred Worker Card 
 
 (1) The division will issue a preferred worker card to eligible workers. The card identifies a worker as being eligible to offer an employer Preferred Worker Program assistance.  (2) The division will issue a preferred worker card:  (a) Automatically at the time of claim closure based upon insurer submission of preferred worker information as specified in OAR 436-110-0240(4)(a); or  (b) When the division determines the worker is eligible for the Preferred Worker Program.  (3) The division may inactivate a preferred worker card if:  (a) The card was issued in error; or  (b) Any reason for ending Preferred Worker Program eligibility as specified in OAR 436-110-0310(5) applies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.622 & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.622
