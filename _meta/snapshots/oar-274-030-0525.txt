 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 STATE FUNDS FOR COUNTIES AND VETERANS' ORGANIZATIONS 
 274-030-0525 
 Fiscal Division of Funds for Veterans’ Organizations
 
 
 The Department shall not disburse more than one-half of the funds available for disbursement to veterans' organizations under ORS 406.310 during the first 12 months of the Department’s fiscal biennium. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 406.030, 406.310 & 406.330
