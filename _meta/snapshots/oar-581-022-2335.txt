 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 STANDARDS FOR PUBLIC ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY SCHOOLS 
 581-022-2335 
 Daily Class Size 
 
 A school district shall maintain class sizes and teacher assignments which promote effective practices consistent with the outcomes expected of each instructional program. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.051 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 326.051
