 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Raspberry and Blackberry Commission - Chapter 611 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 Per Diem Compensation 
 611-040-0030 
 Reimbursement for Hiring a Substitute 
 
 (1) As used in OAR 611-040-0020, "other expenses" includes expenses incurred by a member of the Commission in employing a substitute to perform duties, including personal duties, normally performed by the member, which the member is unable to perform because of the performance of official duties and which, by the nature of such duties, cannot be delayed without risk to health or safety. 

 (2) The amount that a member may be reimbursed for expenses incurred in employing a substitute must not exceed $25 per day pursuant to ORS 292.495(3). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 292.495 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 292.495 & ORS 576.206(7)
