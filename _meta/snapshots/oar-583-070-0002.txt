 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Degree Authorization - Chapter 583 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 OVERSIGHT OF POSTSECONDARY ACCREDITING BODIES 
 583-070-0002 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 Effective January 13, 2015. This rule implements ORS 348.603 in establishing standards and procedures for the evaluation and approval of postsecondary accrediting bodies seeking to operate in or from Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.603 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.603
