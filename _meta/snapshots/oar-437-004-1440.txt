 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 AGRICULTURE 
 437-004-1440 
 Required Postings 
 
 Post signs reading, “No Smoking or Open Flame,” in all areas:  (1) For fueling;  (2) For receiving or dispensing flammable or liquids;  (3) For use or storage of flammable liquids; or  (4) Where there may be flammable or explosive gases, vapors, mists, dust, fibers or flyings.  NOTE: Signs reading “FLAMMABLE — KEEP FIRE AWAY” will also be in compliance with this rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295
