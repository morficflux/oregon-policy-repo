 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 53 HEALTH BENEFIT PLANS 
 836-053-1500 
 Purpose; Statutory Authority; Applicability 
 
 (1) OAR 836-053-1500 to 836-053-1510 are adopted for the purpose of implementing sections 1 to 4, chapter 575, Oregon Laws 2015 and section 7, chapter 26, Oregon Laws 2016.  (2) The requirements set forth in OAR 836-053-1500 to 836-053-1510 apply to prominent carriers. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244; sec 1 - 4, ch 575, OLs 2015, sec 7, ch 26 & OL 2016 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Sections 1 & 3, ch. 575, OL 2015, sec 7, ch. 26 & OL 2016
