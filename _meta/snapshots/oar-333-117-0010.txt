 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 117 REGULATION AND LICENSING OF NATURALLY OCCURRING RADIOACTIVE MATERIALS (NORM) 
 333-117-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 This division establishes radiation protection standards for the possession, use, transfer and disposal of naturally occurring radioactive materials (NORM) not subject to regulation under the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.775 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 HD 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 1-8-91
