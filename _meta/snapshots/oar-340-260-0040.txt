 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 260 REFRIGERANT RECYCLING AND OZONE-DEPLETING SUBSTANCE REQUIREMENTS 
 340-260-0040 
 Federal Regulations Adopted by Reference 
 
 (1) Except as provided in Section (2) of this rule, 40 CFR Part 82 (July 1, 1994) is by this reference adopted and incorporated herein for major sources only, for purposes of implementing a stratospheric ozone protection program that meets the requirements of title VI of the Clean Air Act.  (2) Where “Administrator” or “EPA” appears in 40 CFR Part 82, “Department” shall be substituted, except in any section of 40 CFR Part 82 for which a federal rule or delegation specifically indicates that authority will not be delegated to the state.  (3) Where a discrepancy is determined to exist between OAR 340-260-0010 through 340-260-0030 and 40 CFR Part 82, 40 CRF Part 82 will apply. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A.310(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468.310
