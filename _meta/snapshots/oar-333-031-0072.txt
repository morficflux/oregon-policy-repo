 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF RECREATION PARKS 
 333-031-0072 
 Special Conditions 
 
 The Administrator may exempt any requirements of the rules for toilets, waste water disposals and spacing, to meet special short-term campground needs either arising annually during hunting season or other similar special situations if he finds that public health will not be endangered. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.330 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.330
