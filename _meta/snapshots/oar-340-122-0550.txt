 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 122 HAZARDOUS SUBSTANCE REMEDIAL ACTION RULES 
 340-122-0550 
 Solid Waste Orphan Site Account: Grants and Loans 
 
 DEQ may provide local government units with solid waste Orphan Site Account funds in the form of:  (1) A grant for remedial action costs exceeding the maximum amount collected by surcharge or equivalent funding; or  (2) A loan for remedial action costs up to the amount raised by surcharge or equivalent funding. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 459.045, 459.236 & 459.311 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 459.005, 459.236, 459.311, 465.200-455 & 465.900 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 7-1993, f. & cert. ef. 4-27-93
