 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 SCRATCH-ITSM TICKET GAME OPERATING RULES 
 177-050-0020 
 Scratch-It Ticket Price 
 
 The price of a Scratch-itSM ticket shall be at least $1, except to the extent of any discounts authorized by the Commission. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.240
