 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 CERTIFICATES, PERMITS, LICENSES AND DOCUMENTS 
 740-035-0040 
 Effect of Pending Investigation or Litigation on Applications for Authority 
 
 Unless the Department otherwise orders, no application for certificate or permit, for extension, transfer or modification thereof will be processed if:  (1) The applicant, certificate or permit is under formal investigation, other than a pending safety investigation, upon complaint filed with or by the Department; or  (2) The certificate, permit, or application is the subject of pending court litigation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 823.011 & 825.125 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.102 - 825.110 & 825.240
