 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 HISTORIC PRESERVATION OFFICER 
 736-050-0210 
 State Advisory Committee on Historic Preservation: Statutory Authority 
 
 ORS 358.617 requires the State Historic Preservation Officer to adopt rules to carry out the duties and functions of the officer, including rules governing cultural resource management programs. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 358.617 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 358.617 
 
 History:
