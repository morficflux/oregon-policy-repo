 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 306 PROPERTY TAX GENERALLY 
 150-306-0090 
 Confidentiality of Appraisals of Industrial Property Made by and Contained in Department of Revenue Files The files of industrial property, appraised by the Department of Revenue and contained in DOR files, will be safeguarded in the manner stated in OAR 150-308-0440.
