 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 270 OREGON POLST (PHYSICIAN ORDERS OR PORTABLE ORDERS FOR LIFE-SUSTAINING TREATMENT) REGISTRY 
 333-270-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 (1) These rules establish a registry (the Oregon POLST Registry) within the Oregon Health Authority for the collection of POLST forms and the dissemination of information from POLSTs to help ensure that medical treatment preferences for individuals nearing the end of the individual’s life are honored.  (2) The Registry may be operated and maintained by a contractor of the Authority. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 127.666 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 127.663-127.684 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PH 12-2009, f. & cert. ef. 12-3-09
