 Mortuary and Cemetery Board 
 Chapter 830 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 UNPROFESSIONAL CONDUCT 
 830-050-0000 
 Failure to Comply with Rules 
 
 (1) Failure to comply with ORS Chapter 692 and these rules shall be considered unprofessional conduct.  (2) It is considered unprofessional conduct for any person or licensee to directly or indirectly cause any other person or licensee to be in violation of any section of applicable rule or law. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341 & 692.320 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 692.320
