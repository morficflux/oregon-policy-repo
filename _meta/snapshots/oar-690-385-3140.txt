 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 385 DISTRICT WATER RIGHT TRANSFERS 
 690-385-3140 
 Temporary Change in Point of Diversion or Appropriation 
 
 (1) As provided in ORS 540.570, a temporary change in point of diversion or appropriation may be made in combination with, or as necessary to facilitate, a change in place of use.  (2) A change in point of diversion is restricted to the same source of surface water. A change in point of appropriation is restricted to the same aquifer. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025 & 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 540.570 
 
 History:
