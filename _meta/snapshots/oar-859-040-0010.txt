 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD ADMINISTRATIVE MEETINGS 
 859-040-0010 
 Quorum and Decisions 
 
 The presence of at least three members of the Board—in person or by remote means—constitutes a quorum: Three concurring votes (affirmative or negative) are required for the Board’s Adult Panel to make a decision. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.385
