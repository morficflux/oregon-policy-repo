 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 310 SUBSIDIZED DEVELOPMENT VISITABILITY 
 813-310-0080 
 Classifying Exemptions 
 
 For administrative purposes including without limitation, for purposes of justification, record keeping and analysis of exemptions, the Department may classify exemptions into the following categories, or combination of such categories:  (1) Topography.  (2) Funding conflicts.  (3) Undue cost.  (4) Undue constraint.  (5) Initial project rejection (see OAR 813-310-0095).  (6) Community and design standards.  (7) With regard to the availability of the community powder room, the provision of adaptable powder rooms with grab bars in each visitable unit. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 456.514 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.506 - 456.514 
 
 History:
