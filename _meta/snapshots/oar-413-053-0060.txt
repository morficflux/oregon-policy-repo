 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 53 STRENGTHENING, PRESERVING AND REUNIFYING FAMILIES PROGRAM (SPRF) AND SPRF FLEXIBLE FUNDS 
 413-053-0060 
 Outcome Measures 
 
 (1) All SPRF services have client-focused functional outcome measures. 

 (2) SPRF outcomes are delineated by service category and are in the SPRF Outcomes table, dated November 2017, which is adopted as Exhibit 1 and incorporated by reference into this rule. A printed copy may be obtained from the Department.
