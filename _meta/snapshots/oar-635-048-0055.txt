 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 48 HUNTER/TRAPPER EDUCATION 
 635-048-0055 
 Trapper Examination 
 
 An examination covering the course material must be successfully completed in order to qualify for a trapping brand number. Testing will take place under he auspices of Department of Fish and Wildlife personnel and at prearranged locations. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 & 497 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496 & 497 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FWC 15-1987, f. & ef. 4-15-87
