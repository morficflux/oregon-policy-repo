 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 234 EMISSION STANDARDS FOR WOOD PRODUCTS INDUSTRIES 
 340-234-0540 
 Board Products Industries (Veneer, Plywood, Particleboard, Hardboard): Testing and Monitoring
 
 
 All source tests must be done using the DEQ Source Sampling Manual. [NOTE: The DEQ Source Sampling Manual is published with OAR 340-200-0035.] 

 (1) Veneer dryers, wood particle dryers, fiber dryers and press/cooling vents must be tested using DEQ Method 7. 

 (2) Air conveying systems must be tested using DEQ Method 8. 

 (3) Fuel burning equipment must be tested using DEQ Method 5. When combusting wood fuel by itself or in combination with any other fuel, the emission results are corrected to 12% CO2. When combusting fuels other than wood, the emission results are corrected to 50% excess air. 

 [NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 468A.025 & 468A.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  468A.025 & 468A.070
