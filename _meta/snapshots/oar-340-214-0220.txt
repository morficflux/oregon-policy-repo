 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 214 STATIONARY SOURCE REPORTING REQUIREMENTS 
 340-214-0220 
 Emission Statements for VOC and NOx Sources: Submission of Emission Statement 
 
 The owner or operator of any facility meeting the applicability requirements stated in OAR 340-214-0200 must submit annual Emission Statements to DEQ. The Emission Statement for the preceding calendar year is due to DEQ no later than the due date for the annual permit report specified in the source’s ACDP or Oregon Title V Operating Permit.  NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A.050, 468A.070 & 468A.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.050, 468A.070 & 468A.310
