 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 COUNSELING AND TREATMENT SERVICES 
 413-060-0420 
 Eligibility for Services 
 
 Children under the age of 18 years, in the care, custody, and control of the State Office for Services to Children and Families, who have been adjudicated for sex offenses. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  HB 2004 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  HB 2004 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SCF 6-1995, f. 12-22-95, cert. ef. 12-29-95
