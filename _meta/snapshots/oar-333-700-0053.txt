 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 700 LICENSING STANDARDS FOR OUTPATIENT RENAL DIALYSIS FACILITIES 
 333-700-0053 
 Complaints 
 
 (1) Any person may make a complaint verbally or in writing to the Division regarding an allegation against an outpatient renal dialysis facility of a violation of any health care facility licensing law or condition for coverage.  (2) The identity of a person making a complaint shall be kept confidential.  (3) An investigation may be carried out as soon as practicable after the receipt of a complaint in accordance with OAR 333-700-0057.  (4) If the complaint involves an allegation of criminal conduct or an allegation that is within the jurisdiction of another local, state, or federal agency, the Division shall refer the matter to that agency. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 441.015 & 441.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 441.025 & 441.057 
 
 History:
