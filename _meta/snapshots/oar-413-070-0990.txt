 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 SUBSTITUTE CARE 
 413-070-0990 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 413-070-0990 to 413-070-1060 is to describe the responsibilities of the Department to determine the appropriate use of placement with a fit and willing relative as a permanency plan for a child or young adult in the care or custody of the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 & 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.010, 411.141 & 418.005 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CWP 17-2015, f. 9-28-15, cert. ef. 10-1-15
