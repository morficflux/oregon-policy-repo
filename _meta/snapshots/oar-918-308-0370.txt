 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ELECTRICAL PROGRAM MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-308-0370 
 Exit Interview 
 
 (1) Immediately following completion of a comprehensive review, the division shall conduct an exit interview with the building official or designated representative.  (2) The exit interviewer shall provide:  (a) The general result of the review;  (b) Formal notification of any items requiring immediate attention; and  (c) Specific information to the extent such is available. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.855 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.855
