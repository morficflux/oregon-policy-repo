 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 PUBLIC DISPLAY OF FIREWORKS IN OREGON 
 837-012-1130 
 Type I Violations 
 
 (1) Type I violations are subject to penalties ranging from $0 to $75 a day depending upon instance and in accordance with OAR 837-12-1050(3).  (2) Examples of Type I violations include but are not limited to:  (a) Failure to post "No Smoking" signs at the retail fireworks sales stand;  (b) Failure to provide required fire extinguishing equipment at the retail fireworks sales stand;  (c) Failure to maintain a clean, orderly area within 20 feet of the retail sales area;  (d) Failure to keep a copy of the retail sales permit at the retail sales stand while the stand is open;  (e) Possession of illegal fireworks worth less than $50. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.090 & 480.110 - 480.165 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.110 - 480.165
