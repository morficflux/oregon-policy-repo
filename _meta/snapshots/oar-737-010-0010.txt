 Department of Transportation 
 Transportation Safety Office - Chapter 737 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 VEHICLE EQUIPMENT AND SAFETY STANDARDS 
 737-010-0010 
 Minimum Safety Standards for Low-Speed Vehicles
 
 
 (1) As the minimum vehicle safety standards for low-speed vehicles, TSO adopts the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards (FMVSS) applicable to low-speed vehicles as set forth in 49 CFR, Part 571.500 (October 1, 2008). 

 (2) The vehicle safety equipment requirements for low-speed vehicles described under this rule apply to original and replacement equipment. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 802.010, 815.010, 815.030 & 2009 OL Ch 865 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 815.010, 815.030 & 2009 OL Ch 865
