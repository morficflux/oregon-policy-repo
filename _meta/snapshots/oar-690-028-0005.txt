 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 SURFACE WATER REGISTRATIONS  AND THE ADJUDICATION PROCESS 
 690-028-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules provide for the filing of registration statements for pre-1909 uses of water in areas not yet adjudicated, and federal and Indian reserved rights. They explain the process by which the Director will make determinations on surface water registrations, federal reserved water rights, and spring registrations. These rules also explain the adjudication process for making final determinations of the water rights. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 539
