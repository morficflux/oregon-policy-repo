 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 270 ZERO-EMISSION AND ELECTRIC VEHICLE REBATES 
 340-270-0500 
 Allocation of Rebate Funding 
 
 DEQ will allocate at least 20 percent of available moneys deposited per biennium into the Zero-Emission Incentive Fund for rebates under the Charge Ahead Program. The amount required to be allocated for the Charge Ahead Program rebates from the Zero-Emission Incentive Fund shall be reduced, but not below zero, by the amount deposited from any other source in the Charge Ahead Zero-Emission Incentive Fund. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 2017 Or. Law Ch. 750 Sec. 148-157, House Bill 4059 (2018), Sec. 18-21 & House Bill 2592 (2019), Sec. 35-37 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2017 Or. Law Ch. 750 Sec. 148-157, House Bill 4059 (2018), Sec. 18-21, House Bill 2592 (2019), Sec. 35-37 & 2021 Or. Laws, Ch. 95, §§ 4a, 5, 6, 8 and 9
