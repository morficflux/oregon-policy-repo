 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 EGGS 
 603-022-0055 
 Display of Assigned Permit Number on Container 
 
 (1) The Department will assign each egg handler a permit number to be placed on egg containers that they first sell in Oregon. If the egg handler is located outside of Oregon and has been assigned a permit number by another state, the Department will accept that number as the assigned permit number.  (2) The assigned permit number must appear on the end or side of the consumer egg container in bold face letters at least 1/8 inch in height. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 632 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 632.811
