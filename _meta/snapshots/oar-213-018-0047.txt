 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 OFFENSE SUBCATEGORIES 
 213-018-0047 
 Manslaughter II (ORS 163.125) 
 
 (1) CRIME CATEGORY 9: Manslaughter II shall be ranked at Crime Category 9 if the manslaughter resulted from the operation of a motor vehicle and the driver of the motor vehicle was driving while under the influence of intoxicants.  (2) CRIME CATEGORY 8: Manslaughter II shall be ranked at Crime Category 8 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 9. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667 & 2003 OL Ch. 815 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667 & OL Ch. 815 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CJC 2-2003, f. 12-31-03, cert. ef. 1-1-04
