 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 112 REQUIREMENTS FOR MICROWAVE OVEN USE AND SERVICE 
 333-112-0035 
 Records 
 
 The licensee shall maintain records showing the results of leakage measurements on each microwave oven serviced. The records shall be maintained for inspection by the Authority and shall be so filed as to be readily available for review. The records shall contain at least the following information:  (1) The name of the individual who performed the service.  (2) The date the service was completed.  (3) The maximum leakage levels before and after servicing as applicable.  (4) The location of the maximum leakage levels. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.745 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
