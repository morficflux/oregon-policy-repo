 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Medical Assistance Programs - Chapter 410 
 
 
 
 
 Division 141 OREGON HEALTH PLAN 
 410-141-5155 
 ASSET VALUATION AND PERMITTED INVESTMENTS: Approval by Board 
 
 (1) The investment policy shall be approved by the CCO’s Board or a committee thereof charged with the duty of investing the funds of the CCO. 

 (2) Deposits shall be made in banks or banking institutions approved by the CCO’s Board. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042, 414.572, 414.591 & 414.605 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 414.570-414.686 & 415.001-415.430
