 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 735-001-0100 
 Refunds 
 
 DMV will issue a refund of moneys received in excess of the amount legally due:  (1) If DMV determines a person has made an overpayment of more than $10; or  (2) Upon written request from a person who made an overpayment, or the person's legal representative, if the request is submitted to DMV within three years of the date that DMV received the overpayment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 293.445, 802.010 & 802.110 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 293.445 & 802.110
