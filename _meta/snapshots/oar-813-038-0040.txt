 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 38 PREDEVELOPMENT LOAN PROGRAM 
 813-038-0040 
 Waiver
 
 
 Subject to applicable law, the Department may waive or modify any requirements of OAR 813, division 038, as it deems appropriate for the public interest. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341, 456.555(2) & 456.625(12) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.561, 456.574, 456.620 & 456.625
