 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS 
 808-002-0920 
 Work Period 
 
 "Work Period" as used in OAR 808-004-0320 and 808-004-0600 means the time period from the date a landscape contracting business accepts a payment, offers a written or verbal proposal which is later accepted as a contract or enters into a contract or begins construction, whichever occurs first, until the date the work is completed as defined in OAR 808-002-0280 or the landscape contracting business ceases work. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.325 - 183.410, 670.310 & 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.710
