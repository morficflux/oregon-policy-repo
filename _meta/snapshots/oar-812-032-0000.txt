 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 32 HOME ENERGY ASSESSOR 
 812-032-0000 
 General Definitions 
 
 The following definitions apply to OAR 812-032-0000 to 812-032-0150:  (1) “Certificate” means the authorization issued by the board to an individual home energy assessor.  (2) “License” means the construction contractor license issued by the board under ORS 701.046 to a business offering or providing home energy performance scores, including, but not limited to, a home energy performance score contractor. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 701.235 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701.527–701.536
