 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 310 WATER RIGHT APPLICATION PROCESSING 
 690-310-0050 
 Map to Accompany Application for Water Use Permit
 
 
 Maps submitted as part of an application for water use permit shall meet the standards in OAR chapter 690, division 305. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 537.140 & ORS 537.615
