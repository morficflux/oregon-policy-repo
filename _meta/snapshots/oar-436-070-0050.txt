 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 WORKERS' BENEFIT FUND ASSESSMENT 
 436-070-0050 
 Assessment of Civil Penalties
 
 
 The director may assess a civil penalty under ORS 656.745(2) against an employer that does not comply with ORS chapter 656, OAR 436-070, or an order of the director. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.745
