 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 325 FRANCHISES 
 441-325-0050 
 Relationship to Securities Law 
 
 A franchisor must meet the requirements of ORS 59.005 to 59.451, 59.991 and 59.995 if any part of a franchise offering may be considered a security. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 650.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 59.015
