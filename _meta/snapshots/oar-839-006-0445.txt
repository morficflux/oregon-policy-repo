 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 INJURED WORKERS; DISABILITY; VETERANS AND PERSONS IN UNIFORMED SERVICES 
 839-006-0445 
 Eligibility for Employment Preference
 
 
 There are no limitations on the number of times a person can claim the preference described in ORS 408.225 to 408.237. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 659A.805 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 408.225 - 408.237
