 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 LIVESTOCK AUCTION MARKETS 
 603-012-0024 
 Miscellaneous Auction Market Requirements 
 
 (1) Livestock specifically consigned for “slaughter only” by the seller shall be so designated by the livestock auction yard operator at the time of brand inspection or time of consignment for sale in a manner approved by the Department. The Deputy State Veterinarian shall be advised of this designation by the livestock auction market employees or by the Department al brand inspector. The Deputy State Veterinarian shall be responsible for the preparation of Form 1-27 on all of such livestock. (Placards notifying patrons of the designation and meanings of “S” markings and “slaughter only” shall be posted at each livestock auction yard.)  (2) Female bison over 24 months of age shall be tested for brucellosis at the consignor’s expense unless consigned for slaughter only. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 596, 597 & 599 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 599.490 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 1-1981, f. & ef. 1-9-81
