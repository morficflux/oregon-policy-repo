 Department of State Police 
 Chapter 257 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 ALCOHOL BREATH TESTING 
 257-030-0050 
 Definitions 
 
 As used in these rules, “Chemical Test” and “Chemical Analysis” both mean a quantitative analysis for alcohol by means of direct or indirect measurement of physicochemical technique performed on a sample of breath. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335 & 813.160 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 813.160 
 
 History:
