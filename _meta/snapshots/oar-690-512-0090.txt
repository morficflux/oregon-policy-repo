 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 512 MALHEUR LAKE BASIN PROGRAM 
 690-512-0090 
 Whitehorse and Willow Creeks 
 
 Willow Creek and tributaries, and Whitehorse Creek and tributaries are withdrawn from future appropriations except as described in the order of the Water Resources Commission effective April 24, 1992. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.410 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 536.410 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 2-2016, f. 4-14-16, cert. ef. 4-15-16
