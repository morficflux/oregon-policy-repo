 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office, Board of Certified Advanced Estheticians - Chapter 819 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 Board of Certified Advanced Esthetics Renewal Requirements 
 819-025-0000 
 DISPLAY OF AUTHORIZATION AND POSTING REQUIREMENTS 
 
 (1) A certification holder must post advanced esthetic certificate in public view. 
 (2) A certification holder may temporarily conceal the address printed on the certification document with a covering that is removable. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.615 & ORS 676.655 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.655 
 
 History:
