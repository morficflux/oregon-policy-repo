 Landscape Architect Board 
 Chapter 804 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 EXAMINATION PROCEDURES 
 804-020-0045 
 Notice of Score
 
 
 Examination candidates will receive notification of examination scores directly from the Council of Landscape Architectural Boards (CLARB). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 671.335 & 671.415 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.335
