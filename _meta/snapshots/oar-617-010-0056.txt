 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Dairy Products Commission - Chapter 617 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 RELATING TO REPORTS AND ASSESSMENTS TO BE PAID TO THE COMMISSION 
 617-010-0056 
 Exemptions from Reporting and Assessment 
 
 (1) "Casual Sales" or on-farm sales of milk by a producer as provided for in Title 49 section 621.012 shall be exempt from Commission reporting and assessment requirements.  (2) "Casual Sales" or on-farm sales of milk by producer not meeting the criteria of Title 49 section 621.012 are not exempt from Commission reporting and assessment requirements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576
