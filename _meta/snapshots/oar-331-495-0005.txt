 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 495 DISCIPLINE AND ENFORCEMENT 
 331-495-0005 
 Investigative authority 
 
 The Office may initiate and conduct investigations relating to the practice of lactation consultation, and may take appropriate disciplinary action in accordance with the provisions of 676.612 and 676.992. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565 & ORS 676.665-676.689 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.560, ORS 676.565 & ORS 676.665-676.689
