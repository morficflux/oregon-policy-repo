 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Salmon Commission - Chapter 646 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENTS DUE THE OREGON SALMON COMMISSION 
 646-010-0025 
 Effective Dates of Assessment 
 
 The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser or handler, as required by ORS 576.155 and OAR 646-010-0010(1), or the reports required under OAR 646-010-0015, applies to and covers any purchases by him or delivery on or after July 1, 1984, of troll caught ocean salmon landed in Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSC 2-1984, f. & ef. 6-20-84
