 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 EMPLOYER/INSURER COVERAGE RESPONSIBILITY 
 436-050-0205 
 Notice of Self-Insurer’s Personal Elections
 
 
 When a self-insured employer elects to provide coverage for a nonsubject worker under ORS 656.039, the employer must notify the director in writing of the election and of any cancellation of the election within 30 days of the effective date. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.039
