 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 OPERATION OF NONPRESCRIPTION AND MEDICAL DEVICE, EQUIPMENT AND GAS (MDEG) OUTLETS 
 855-035-0025 
 Seasonal Nonprescription Drug Outlets 
 
 (1) Seasonal nonprescription drug outlets are defined as those outlets who either by location or weather are restricted to a seasonal demand for services.  (2) Seasonal nonprescription drug outlets shall be exempt from delinquent fees for nonprescription registration if renewals are paid no later than June 1 of current year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475.035 & 689.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 689.325
