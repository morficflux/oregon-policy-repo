 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 38 DIRECT ACCESS REGULATION 
 860-038-0470 
 Attachments to Poles and Conduits Owned by Public, Telecommunications, and Consumer-Owned Utilities 
 
 Pole and conduit attachments shall comply with the rules set forth in OAR chapter 860, division 028. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040, 757.270 - 290, 757.600 - 667 & 759.650 - 675 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PUC 23-2001, f. & cert. ef. 10-11-01
