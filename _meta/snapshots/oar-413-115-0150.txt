 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 115 APPLICATION OF THE INDIAN CHILD WELFARE ACT (ICWA) 
 413-115-0150 
 Full Faith and Credit
 
 
 The Department must give full faith and credit to the public acts, records and judicial proceedings of any Indian tribe regarding any Indian child -custody proceeding to the same extent the Department gives full faith and credit to Indian tribe s. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 & ORS 409.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005 & ORS 419B.100
