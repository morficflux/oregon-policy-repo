 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 316 PERSONAL INCOME TAX GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 150-316-0610 
 Road Construction Worker’s Travel Expenses 
 
 (1) As used in ORS 316.806(1), the term “construction job site” includes a roadway.  (2) As used in ORS 316.806(2), the term “structure” includes a road or railway. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 316.806
