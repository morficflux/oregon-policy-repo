 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-0709 
 Hyatt Lake 
 
 Hyatt Lake shall be managed for hatchery and natural production under two alternatives from the Warmwater Fish Plan and Oregon’s Trout Plan: Trout — Basic yield; Bass — Basic yield. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496, 506 & 508 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496, 506 & 508 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FWC 117-1990, f. & cert ef. 10-15-90
