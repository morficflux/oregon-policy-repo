 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES AND SYSTEMS 
 333-200-0275 
 Authority Responsibilities
 
 
 When requested, the Authority shall provide statistical reports in formats prescribed by the Authority in consultation with the STAB, to the STAB and ATAB Quality Improvement Committees within 90 days of the close of the calendar quarter following receipt of the data submitted pursuant to OAR 333-200-0265(8). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431A.065
