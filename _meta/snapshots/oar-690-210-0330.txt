 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 210 WELL CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS 
 690-210-0330 
 Unhydrated Bentonite 
 
 Unhydrated bentonite used in construction of casing seals for water supply wells shall be specifically designed for sealing wells and be within industry tolerances for dry western sodium bentonite. Bentonite shall be free of polymers that promote bacterial growth. Placement of the bentonite shall conform to the manufacturers specifications and result in a seal that is free of voids or bridges. Powdered bentonite and bentonite grout or slurry shall not be used as an annular seal material. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795
