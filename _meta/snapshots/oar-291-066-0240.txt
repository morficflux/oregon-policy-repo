 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 66 LEAVE REVOCATION HEARINGS 
 291-066-0240 
 Revocation Hearings Record 
 
 (1) The revocation hearings record shall include: 

 (a) A Hearings Report Summary; 

 (b) A written statement of alleged violations; 

 (c) Supporting materials, including documentary evidence admitted; 

 (d) A signed Notice of Rights/Decisions about Rights; 

 (e) The short-term transitional or nonprison leave conditions; 

 (f) A notice of time and place of hearing; 

 (g) An audio recording of the advisement of rights and the hearing; 

 (h) The supervising officer’s report, including recommended dispositions; 

 (i) The history of supervision, local sanctions and modifications; and 

 (j) If any, the written waiver of the AIC’s right to three business days’ notice of the hearing. 

 (2) The Hearings Officer shall retain the recording used in subsection (1)(g) of this rule in accordance with established retention schedules. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075, 421.168 & 421.500 to 421.512 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075, 421.168 & 421.500 to 421.512
