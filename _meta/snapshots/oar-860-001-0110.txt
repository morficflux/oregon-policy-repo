 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 GENERAL 
 860-001-0110 
 Appeal to the Commission from Ruling of Administrative Law Judge 
 
 (1) A party may request that the ALJ certify an ALJ’s written or oral ruling for the Commission’s consideration. A party must request certification of a ruling within 15 days of the date of service of the ruling or date of the oral ruling.  (2) The ALJ must certify the ruling to the Commission under OAR 860-001-0090 if the ALJ finds that:  (a) The ruling may result in substantial detriment to the public interest or undue prejudice to a party;  (b) The ruling denies or terminates a person’s participation; or  (c) Good cause exists for certification. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 756.040 & 756.060 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040 & 756.500 – 756.575 
 
 History:
