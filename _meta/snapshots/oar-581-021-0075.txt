 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 SCHOOL GOVERNANCE AND STUDENT CONDUCT 
 581-021-0075 
 Distribution 
 
 (1) School district boards, or designated representatives, shall attempt to give widest possible distribution to their rules of pupil conduct and discipline in order that students may understand the expectations of the district.  (2) School districts shall make reasonable attempts to give a copy of their current rules to each student, and a copy of the current rules shall be posted in a prominent place in the schools of the district.  (3) School districts shall make these rules available to the general public upon request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 339 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 336.635 
 
 History:
