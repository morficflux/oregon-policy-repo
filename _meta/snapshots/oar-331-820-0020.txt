 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 820 RENEWAL 
 331-820-0020 
 Certification Renewal
 
 
 (1) A certification is subject to the provisions of OAR Chapter 331, Division 30 regarding the renewal of a certification. 

 (2) Certification renewal is valid for one year. 

 (3) To avoid delinquency penalties, certification renewal must be made prior to the certification becoming inactive. The certification holder must: 

 (a) Submit a renewal application form; 

 (b) Attest to having obtained required annual continuing education, whether the certification is current or inactive; 

 (c) Pass a state criminal background check pursuant to OAR 331-030-0004; and 

 (d) Pay the renewal fee. 

 (4) A certification may be inactive for up to three years. When renewing after entering inactive status, the certification holder must: 

 (a) Submit a renewal application form; 

 (b) Attest to having obtained required annual continuing education, whether the certification is current or inactive; and 

 (c) Pass a state criminal background check pursuant to OAR 331-030-0004 ; and 

 (d) Pay delinquency and certification renewal fees; 

 (5) A certification that has been inactive for more than three years is expired and the certification holder must reapply and meet the current requirements. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410, 2019 House Bill 2472 & ORS 676.560 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.565, ORS 675.360-675.410, 2019 House Bill 2472 & ORS 676.560
