 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 315 WATER RIGHT PERMIT EXTENSIONS 
 690-315-0100 
 Department Action on Extension Applications by Holders of Municipal Water Supply Permits
 
 
 If the Department finds an applicant has submitted a completed application as required under OAR 690-315-0070, the Department shall process the application as established under OARs 690-315-0050 and 690-315-0060. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025 & ORS 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 537.230, ORS 537.248, ORS 537.630 & ORS 539.010
