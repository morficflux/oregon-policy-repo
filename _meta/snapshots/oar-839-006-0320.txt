 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 INJURED WORKERS; DISABILITY; VETERANS AND PERSONS IN UNIFORMED SERVICES 
 839-006-0320 
 Rights of Persons with Disabilities With Respect to Places of Public Accommodation: Provision of Auxiliary Aids 
 
 (1) Places of public accommodation must provide auxiliary aids and services when necessary to ensure equal access to offered goods, privileges, services or facilities.  (2) Places of public accommodation are not required to provide auxiliary aids that would result in significant difficulty or expense, or in a fundamental alteration in the nature of the offered goods, privileges or services. This is to be determined on a case-by-case basis. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 659A.805 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 659A.103 & 659A.142
