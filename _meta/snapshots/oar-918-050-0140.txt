 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-050-0140 
 Statewide Fee Methodology for Residential Fire Suppression Systems 
 
 Stand-alone and multipurpose fire suppression system permit fees shall each be calculated as separate flat fees based on the square footage of the structure with graduated rates for dwellings with 0 to 2000 square feet, 2001 to 3600 square feet, 3601 to 7200 square feet, and 7201 square feet and greater. The permit fee shall be sufficient to cover the costs of inspection and plan review. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.048 & 455.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.046 & 455.055
