 Department of State Police 
 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 CHAMPIONS 
 230-100-0040 
 Advertising Appearance of Champion or Contender 
 
 No person shall advertise a boxer in Oregon as a champion or contender in any manner which is false or misleading. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.113 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BWC 1-1988, f. 3-22-88, cert. ef. 3-29-88
