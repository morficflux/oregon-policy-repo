 Columbia River Gorge Commission 
 Chapter 350 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 CONFLICT OF INTEREST 
 350-014-0002 
 Application 
 
 Nothing in these rules is intended to affect:  (1) Any other statute or rule requiring disclosure of economic interest by a public official or public employee.  (2) Any statute or rule prohibiting or authorizing specific conduct on the part of any public official or public employee. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.150 & RCW 43.97 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.150, RCW 43.97 & 16 U.S.C. 544 et seq.
