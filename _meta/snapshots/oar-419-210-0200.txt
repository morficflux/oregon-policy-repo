 Office of Training, Investigations and Safety 
 Chapter 419 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 210 CHILD-IN-CARE ABUSE INVESTIGATIONS 
 419-210-0200 
 Confidentiality 
 
 
 Information gathered and records and reports compiled during an OTIS investigation are confidential and may be disclosed only as provided in ORS 419B.035. The identity of the person reporting abuse may not be disclosed. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 & 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.050, ORS 443.400 to 443.455, 409.185, 409.225, 418.015, 419B.005 - 419B.050 & 419B.035
