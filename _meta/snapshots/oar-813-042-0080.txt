 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 HOUSING DEVELOPMENT GRANT PROGRAM 
 813-042-0080 
 General Administrative and Monitoring Requirements 
 
 (1) The Department may perform such reviews or field inspections as it deems necessary to ensure Program compliance. The Department may require that a Recipient take such remedial actions as described in this rule and OAR 813-042-0090.  (2) Financial records, supporting documents, and all other pertinent records shall be retained by a HDGA Recipient for five years after the project is complete, or after any litigation or audit claim is resolved, whichever is later. The Department shall have access to all books, accounts, documents, records and other property belonging to or in use by the Recipient which relate to the use of HDGA funds. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.515 - 456.720 & 458.600 - 458.630 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.555
