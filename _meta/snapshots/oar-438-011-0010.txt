 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 BOARD REVIEW; THIRD PARTY ORDERS 
 438-011-0010 
 Applicability 
 
 These rules apply to all cases in which a party or parties request Board review of an order of an Administrative Law Judge pursuant to ORS 656.289, 656.291, 656.295 and 656.307 and to cases in which a party requests a decision of the Board under the third party law, 656.576 through 656.596. These rules do not apply to proceedings before the Board on its own motion pursuant to 656.278 and proceedings before the Board after remand from an appellate court. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.307, 656.388, 656.593 & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.295, 656.307, 656.587 & 656.594
