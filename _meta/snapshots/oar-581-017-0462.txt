 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0462 
 Reporting 
 
 Recipients of the CTE Teacher Preparation Program grant must report on the grant to the Department of Education. The report must include metrics developed by the Department of Education. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2015 OL Ch. 763 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3072) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2015 OL Ch. 763 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3072) 
 
 History:
