 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 ADJUSTED COMMITMENT DATE 
 255-025-0035 
 Adjusted Commitment Date for Parole Violations 
 
 The adjusted commitment date for parole violators with new convictions or new commitments shall be calculated as outlined in OAR 255-075-0078. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 144 
 
 History:
