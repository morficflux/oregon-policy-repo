 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 REGULATION OF FARMWORKER CAMP OPERATORS 
 839-014-0195 
 Alternative Methods of Examination 
 
 (1) An applicant for whom English is a second language may bring an interpreter to the examination to translate the examination questions. An interpreter may not assist an applicant in answering any question, but may translate the answers.  (2) Alternative methods of examination will be provided upon written request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 164, 165, 651, 658.730(1) & 962 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 658.730(1)(b) 
 
 History:
