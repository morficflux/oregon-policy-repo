 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 APPLICATIONS; REQUIREMENTS; RESTRICTIONS; PROHIBITED CONDUCT 
 845-006-0469 
 Full On-Premises Sales License with Additional On-Premises Sales Privilege 
 
 (1) Any Full On-Premises Sales licensee holding at the same licensed premises another license allowing on-premises consumption of alcohol must comply with the food service requirements of the Full On-Premises Sales license regardless of which license is used to serve alcohol for on-premises consumption.  (2) Violation of any section of this rule is a Category III violation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 471, including 471.030, 471.040, 471.730(1) & (5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 471.175
