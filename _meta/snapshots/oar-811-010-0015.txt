 Board of Chiropractic Examiners 
 Chapter 811 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 GENERAL RULES AND LICENSING 
 811-010-0015 
 Filing Addresses
 
 
 Each licensee and certificate holder shall provide their current business and mailing addresses, including all practice locations, with the OBCE. Each licensee and certificate holder shall also provide their current electronic mail address. Licensees and certificate holders shall notify the OBCE in writing, giving the new mailing and electronic mail addresses within 30 calendar days of any change. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 684 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 684.054
