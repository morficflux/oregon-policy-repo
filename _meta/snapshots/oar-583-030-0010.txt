 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Degree Authorization - Chapter 583 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 STANDARDS AND PROCEDURES FOR SCHOOLS  REQUIRED TO OBTAIN AUTHORIZATION TO OFFER ACADEMIC DEGREES IN OREGON, OR REQUIRED TO ESTABLISH EXEMPTION 
 583-030-0010 
 Exemptions
 
 
 The standards and procedures in these rules shall not apply to schools exempt under ORS 348.597 or those entities listed in 348.594 (3)(b). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.604 & 348.606 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.604, ORS 348.597, 348.605 & ORS 348.594
