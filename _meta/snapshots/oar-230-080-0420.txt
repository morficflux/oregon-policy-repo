 Department of State Police 
 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 CONDUCT OF BOUT - BOXING 
 230-080-0420 
 Approved Beverages 
 
 Only unopened, factory-sealed containers of bottled water are permitted for the treatment of dehydration of an unarmed combat sports competitor between rounds. Honey, electrolyte solutions, glucose, sugar, sports drinks or any other substance mixed with the water or sports drink is prohibited. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.113 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SAC 8-2018, adopt filed 01/09/2018, effective 01/09/2018
