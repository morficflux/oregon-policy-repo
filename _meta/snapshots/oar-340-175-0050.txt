 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 175 UNDERGROUND STORAGE TANK GRANT PROGRAM 
 340-175-0050 
 Additional Information Required on the Financial Assistance Application 
 
 In addition to the requirements of OAR 340-172-0040, the financial assistance application shall include:  (1) Where the UST project work is partially or fully completed:  (a) A description of the completed UST project work including the date the UST was placed into service;  (b) Cost of completed UST project work; and  (c) A description of all UST project work still to be done to meet new UST standards, including estimated cost and schedule of work.  (2) A signed agreement meeting the requirements of OAR 340-175-0055. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OL 1993 & Ch. 661 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  OL 1991, Ch. 863 & Sec. 7
