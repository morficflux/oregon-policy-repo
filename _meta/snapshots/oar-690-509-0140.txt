 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 509 POWDER BASIN PROGRAM 
 690-509-0140 
 Pine Creek Subbasin Reservation
 
 
 Ten thousand (10,000) acre-feet of unappropriated water of Pine Creek and tributaries above Long Branch, tributary to the Snake River, are reserved for multipurpose reservoirs to be constructed in the future. The priority date of the reservation is November 6, 1992. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027 & ORS 537.249 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 537.358, 536.300, ORS 536.310, ORS 537.249 & ORS 537.356
