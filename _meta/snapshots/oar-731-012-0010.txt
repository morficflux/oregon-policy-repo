 Department of Transportation 
 Chapter 731 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 REDUCTION OF VEHICLE-CARRYING CAPACITY 
 731-012-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 This division implements ORS 366.215. The purpose of this division is to define terms, identify a review process and facilitate communication and development of consensus during this review process. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 366.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 366.215 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOT 4-2013, f. & cert. ef. 8-26-13
