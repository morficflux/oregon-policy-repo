 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 COMMERCIAL FISHERIES: GEAR, LICENSES, POUNDAGE FEES, RECORDS AND REPORTS 
 635-006-1005 
 Introduction to Restricted Participation Systems Organization
 
 
 (1) In 2012, many commercial fishing regulations were reorganized with significant parts of the Restricted Participation Systems regulations moved to their related fishery sections in division 4 and division 5 of the Fish & Wildlife Oregon Administrative Rules. As a result of the reorganization, only regulations regarding commercial gillnet and troll salmon fisheries remain in the Restricted Participation Systems section. These two fisheries are organized by topic. For each topic, the applicable law for each fishery is either set forth or, where the statute provides detail, the statute is cited. 

 (2) The topics covered by these rules are: 

 (a) Requirement for Permit — 635-006-1015 (Requirement for Permit); 

 (b) Eligibility Requirements for a Permit — 635-006-1035 (Eligibility Requirements for a Permit); 

 (c) Evidence of Eligibility — 635-006-1050 (Evidence of Limited Entry Permit Eligibility); 

 (d) Revocation and Refusal to Issue Permits — 635-006-1060 (Revocation and Refusal to Issue Permits); 

 (e) Review of Denials — 635-006-1065 (Review of Denials (Restricted Participation Systems)); 

 (f) Renewal of Permit — 635-006-1075 (Renewal of Limited Entry Permit); 

 (g) Lottery for Certain Limited Entry Fisheries — 635-006-1085 (Lottery for Certain Limited Entry Fisheries); 

 (h) Transferability of Permits — 635-006-1095 (Transferability of Permits); 

 (i) Commercial Fishery Permit Boards — 635-006-1200 (Commercial Fishery Permit Boards); 

 (j) Agency Representation by Employee — 635-006-1210 (Agency Representation by Employee). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & ORS 506.129
