 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 820 DEBT BUYERS 
 441-820-0150 
 Records 
 
 All debt buyers shall maintain and preserve records concerning business operations, transactions, and customer complaints and responses for no less than 4 years after the last action on any consumer account. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2017 Or Laws ch 625 §§ 8, 13, 15 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2017 Or Laws ch 625 §§ 4 - 10 
 
 History:
