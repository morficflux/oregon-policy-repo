 Board of Nursing 
 Chapter 851 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 AGENCY FEES 
 851-002-0035 
 Clinical Nurse Specialist Schedule of Fees
 
 
 (1) Initial License — $250. 

 (2) Renewal without Prescriptive Authority — $175. 

 (3) Surcharge to Support the Prescription Monitoring Fund — $70. 

 (4) Renewal with Prescriptive Authority — $205. 

 (5) Prescriptive Authority Initial Application — $75. 

 (6) Delinquent Fee — $100. 

 (7) Temporary License for Active Armed Forces Military Spouse or Domestic Partner — $50 . 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 678.150 & ORS 678.410 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 678.410 & ORS 431A.880
