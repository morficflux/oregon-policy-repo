 Oregon Board of Maritime Pilots - Chapter 856 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE 
 856-001-0000 
 Notice of Proposed Rule
 
 
 Prior to the adoption, amendment or repeal of any rules, the Board shall give notice of the intended action by mailing or furnishing a copy of the notice to: 

 (1) The Associated Press; 

 (2) The following groups: 

 (a) Columbia River Pilots 

 (b) Columbia River Bar Pilots; 

 (c) Yaquina Bay Pilots; 

 (d) Coos Bay Pilots Association; 

 (e) Columbia River Steamship Operators Association, Inc.; 

 (f) Capitol Press Room. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & ORS 776 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.025, ORS 183.335 & ORS 776.135
