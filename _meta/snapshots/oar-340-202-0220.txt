 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 202 AMBIENT AIR QUALITY STANDARDS AND PREVENTION OF SIGNIFICANT DETERIORATION INCREMENTS 
 340-202-0220 
 Prevention of Significant Deterioration Increments: Ambient Air Ceilings 
 
 No concentration of a pollutant may exceed:  (1) The concentration permitted under the national secondary ambient air quality standard;  (2) The concentration permitted under the national primary ambient air quality standard; or  (3) The concentration permitted under the state ambient air quality standard, whichever concentration is lowest for the pollutant for a period of exposure.  NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan as adopted by the EQC under OAR 340-200-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025 & 468A.035
