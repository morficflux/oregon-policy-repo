 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 PROPHYLACTICS AND CONTRACEPTIVES 
 855-070-0010 
 Labeling and Storage of Prophylactics and Contraceptives 
 
 (1) The use of detachable slip labels or removable ink stamps listing names and addresses of manufacturer, brand name, and expiration date do not meet the labeling requirements of ORS 435.090.  (2) As of December 31, 1994, all prophylactics and contraceptives shall bear an expiration date. Prophylactics and contraceptives bearing an expiration date shall not be sold or otherwise distributed beyond that date.  (3) Prophylactics and contraceptives shall be stored, displayed, or sold from an area removed from excessive extremes of temperatures which may affect the quality of the products. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689.155 & 689.205
