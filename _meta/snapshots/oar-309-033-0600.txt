 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 33 CIVIL COMMITMENT PROCEEDINGS 
 309-033-0600 
 Statement of Purpose and Statutory Authority
 
 
 (1) Purpose. These rules prescribe standards and procedures for community hospitals, nonhospital facilities and residential facilities relating to obtaining informed consent to treatment from a person under civil commitment, and for the administration of significant procedures without obtaining the informed consent of a person under civil commitment. 

 (2) Statutory authority. These rules are authorized by ORS 426.385 and carry out the provisions of 426.005 through 426.395. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & 426.385 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 426.005 - 426.395
