 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 ADULT PROTECTIVE SERVICES — GENERAL 
 411-020-0070 
 APS Consultation
 
 
 (1) APS consultation is the process by which APS provides specialized information or assistance, enhanced referral, or technical assistance via electronic means, including telephone, fax, or e-mail, to assist in harm reduction. 

 (2) APS consultation, as an alternative to assessment or investigation, is only appropriate when the reported concern does not meet eligibility criteria for abuse or self-neglect. 

 (3) The local office must maintain a record of reports resolved by APS consultation. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.010, 410.020, 410.040, 410.070, 411.060, 411.116, 443.450, 443.765, 443.767 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 124.050 - 124.095, 409.010, 410.020, 410.040, 410.070, 411.116, 443.767
