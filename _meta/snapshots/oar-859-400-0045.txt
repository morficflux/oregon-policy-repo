 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 SEX OFFENDER CLASSIFICATION/RELIEF FROM REGISTRATION/RECLASSIFCATION 
 859-400-0045 
 Requests for Reclassification/Relief 
 
 (1) Beginning on December 1, 2018, registrants who meet certain criteria may petition the PSRB to reclassify them to a lower classification level and/or request relief from the obligation to report as a sex offender as provided in ORS 163A.125.  (2) The PSRB will develop administrative rules in preparation for implementation of a reclassification and relief program no later than October 1, 2018. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387(1) & 163A.010 - 163A.025 & 163A.035 - 163A.125 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 163A.125
