 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 OREGON TEACHER CORPS LOAN PROGRAM 
 575-070-0040 
 Selection of Recipients 
 
 (1) Recipients shall be selected by the Commission.  (2) Priority in the selection process shall be given to eligible students (under the criteria in OAR 575-070-0030) who also meet at least one of the following criteria:  (a) He/she is a member of a racial minority as defined in ORS 279.059;  (b) He/she is a prospective teacher in a "scarce endorsement area", as defined by the Oregon Teacher Standards and Practices Commission;  (c) He/she expresses willingness to teach in remote and difficult to serve school districts in Oregon, as defined by the Commission. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329.757 - 329.780
