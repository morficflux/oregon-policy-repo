 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 SAFETY CODE FOR ELEVATORS 
 918-400-0200 
 Notice of Rule Making 
 
 The following persons shall be provided all elevator-related rulemaking notices at no charge:  (1) All active Oregon licensed elevator contractors;  (2) All Oregon state elevator inspectors; and  (3) International Union of Elevator Constructors, Locals 19 (Seattle), 23 (Portland) and 38 (Salt Lake City). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.335
