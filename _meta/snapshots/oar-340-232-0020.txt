 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 232 EMISSION STANDARDS FOR VOC POINT SOURCES 
 340-232-0020 
 Applicability 
 
 (1) All new and existing sources inside the following areas must comply with the applicable requirements in this division:  (a) Portland-Vancouver Air Quality Maintenance Area;  (b) Medford-Ashland Air Quality Maintenance Area;  (c) Salem-Keizer Area Transportation Study (SKATS) Area.  (2) VOC sources located outside the areas cited in section (1) are exempt from the requirements in this division.  NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025
