 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 LOCAL AND SPECIAL RULES 
 250-020-0013 
 Boat Operations in Baker County
 
 
 (1) Anthony Lake: A person must not use an internal combustion engine to propel a boat. 

 (2) Balm Creek Reservoir: A person must not use an internal combustion engine to propel a boat. 

 (3) Brownlee Reservoir: A person must not operate a motorboat in excess of a slow-no wake speed from a point 200 feet upstream of the west launching ramp to a point 200 feet downstream of the east launching ramp at Hewitt County Park. 

 (4) Higgins Reservoir: A person must not use an internal combustion engine to propel a boat. 

 (5) Phillips Lake: A person must not operate a motorboat in excess of a slow-no wake speed in the area known as the Union Creek Inlet, beginning at a point approximately 500 feet south of the Union Creek Boat Launching Ramp and proceeding easterly across the inlet to a point on the opposite shore as marked. 

 (6) Unity Reservoir: A person must not operate a boat below the buoy line near the spillway of Unity Dam. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830.110 & ORS 830.175 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.110, ORS 830.175 & ORS 830.195
