 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 560 ABBREVIATED CERTIFICATE OF NEED REVIEW, DELAY OF REVIEW, WAIVER OF REVIEW FOR FACILITIES, AND EXPEDITED REVIEW 
 333-560-0100 
 Facility Waiver Limitations 
 
 A certificate of need facility waiver does not authorize a hospital to develop a new inpatient hospital facility or provide new long-term care services. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431.120(6), 442.315 & 442.342 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431.120(6), 442.315 & 442.342 
 
 History:
