 State Library 
 Chapter 543 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 READY TO READ GRANTS 
 543-040-0015 
 Authorized Activities
 
 
 Ready to Read Grants made under the provisions of this division shall be used only to establish, develop, or improve public library early literacy services for children from birth to 5 years of age, and/or to provide the statewide summer reading program for children from birth to 14 years of age. The grants may not be used to replace funds already appropriated by local governments from local sources. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 357.026 & ORS 357.760 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 357.740 & ORS 357.750
