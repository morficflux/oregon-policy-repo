 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 RULES OF GENERAL APPLICABILITY AND ORGANIZATION 
 340-011-0580 
 Contested Cases: Petitions for Reconsideration or Rehearing 
 
 (1) A participant is not required to seek either reconsideration or rehearing of a final order prior to seeking judicial review.  (2) Any petition for reconsideration or rehearing must be received by DEQ within 60 days of service of the final order. Unless specifically set forth in this rule, the procedures for petitions for reconsideration or rehearing are those in OAR 137-003-0675.  (3) A petition for reconsideration or rehearing does not stay the effect of the final order.  (4) The director, on behalf of the commission, shall issue orders granting or denying petitions for reconsideration and rehearing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.480 & 183.482
