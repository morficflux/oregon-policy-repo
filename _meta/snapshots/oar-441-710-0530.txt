 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 710 CREDIT UNIONS (IN GENERAL) 
 441-710-0530 
 Accrual Accounts, When Required 
 
 Each credit union with assets in excess of $5 million shall use accrual accounting. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 723.102 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 723.116
