 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 AMUSEMENT RIDE AND DEVICE SPECIALTY CODE 
 918-200-0100 
 Bungee Jumping 
 
 Except for jumps from towers or devices specifically engineered and designed for reverse jumping or catapulting, “bungee catapulting,” “negative jumping,” “reverse jumping,” “launching,” “tandem, double or multiple jumping,” or “sandbagging” shall not be permitted. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 460.355 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 460.355
