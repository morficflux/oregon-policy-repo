 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 ACCEPTANCE AND VERIFICATION OF INFORMATION SUBMITTED BY ELECTRONIC MEANS 
 735-018-0000 
 Authority and Purpose 
 
 (1) OAR chapter 735, division 18 rules are adopted by DMV under the authority of ORS 802.012.  (2) The purpose of these rules is to:  (a) Specify the procedures and requirements under which a person or organization may, by electronic means, submit information to DMV that is customarily provided on paper forms; and  (b) Promote the development and use of electronic transactions between DMV and its customers while providing service that is efficient, secure, convenient and responsive. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.012 & 803.460 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 802.012, 802.560, 803.200, 803.220, 803.360, 803.370, 803.450, 803.460, 807.420 & 807.560 
 
 History:
