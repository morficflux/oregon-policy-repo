 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 170 FIREFIGHTER CRIMINAL DEFENSE EXPENDITURES 
 629-170-0030 
 Funding 
 
 Prior to authorizing expenditures for costs and reasonable attorney fees pursuant to ORS 477.128, the State Forester must determine the source of funding to be used and the amount that may be available for such expenditures. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.041 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 477.128 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOF 2-2016, f. 6-10-16, cert. ef. 7-1-16
