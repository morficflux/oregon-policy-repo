 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 OPERATION OF OREGON BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT FUND 
 123-017-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards and criteria for the making of loans from the Oregon Business Development Fund. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.110 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285B.050 - 285B.098
