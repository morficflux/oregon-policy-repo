 Real Estate Agency 
 Chapter 863 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 863-001-0005 
 Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Real Estate Agency adopts by reference the Attorney General's Uniform and Model Rules OAR 137-001-0005 through 137-005-0070 bearing the effective date of January 1, 2008.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Real Estate Agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341, 183.502 & 696.385 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341 & 183.502
