 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 RIGHT OF WAY AND REAL PROPERTY 
 734-035-0100 
 Property Sales to Political Subdivisions 
 
 (1) Political subdivisions, as defined in ORS 271.005, and non-profit housing providers/housing authorities, shall be afforded the first opportunity, after other state agencies, to purchase surplus real property.  (2) The Department may require at the time of sale or transfer of real property to a political subdivision that the property shall be for use of a public purpose or benefit, and not be for resale to a private purchaser. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184, 271 & 366 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 270.100 
 
 History:
