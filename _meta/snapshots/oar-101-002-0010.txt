 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Employees' Benefit Board - Chapter 101 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 POWERS OF THE BOARD 
 101-002-0010 
 Conduct of Meetings of the Board 
 
 (1) The Board will select one of its appointed voting members as chairperson and another voting member as vice chairperson.  (2) Meetings will be conducted by and will be under the control of the chairperson of the Board. In the absence of the chairperson, the vice chairperson or other Board member designated by the chairperson in the absence of the vice chairperson will preside. All meetings of the Board will be conducted in the matter prescribed by and in accordance with the Oregon Public Meetings Law, ORS 192.610 to 192.690. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 243.061 - 243.302 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.310 - 550, 192.660, 243.061 - 302 & 292.051
