 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0000 
 Wildlife Units 
 
 For the purpose of managing Oregon's wildlife, the State has been divided into separate described areas. These areas are called wildlife units. Whenever reference is made to a unit by name or designated number in any rule of the department, the area description provided in division 080 shall apply, unless otherwise specified by the given rule. Areas described within a Wildlife Unit as a subunit are also described by a number which includes the Wildlife Unit number as its last two digits. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
