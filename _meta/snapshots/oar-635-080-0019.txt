 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0019 
 McKenzie Unit 
 
 The McKenzie Unit, Number 19, is that area beginning at Lebanon, east on U.S. Highway 20 to Pacific Crest Trail; south along Pacific Crest Trail to State Highway 58; northwest on State Highway 58 to Interstate Highway 5; north on Interstate Highway 5 to State Highway 34; east on State Highway 34 to Lebanon, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
