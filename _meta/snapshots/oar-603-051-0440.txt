 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 PRODUCE STANDARDS 
 603-051-0440 
 Oregon Box Packs 
 
 (1) All bulbs packed under these regulations shall be packed in clean, fairly bright boxes, in accordance to approved methods. The boxes shall be well nailed with cleats at ends. There shall be a layer of granulated peat or other suitable packing material on the top and bottom of the pack and also between each layer. All boxes shall be tightly packed at the time of packing, but contents shall not show unnecessary bruising because of over-filled package.  (2) Packed bulbs in any one box shall not have more than 10 percent below the minimum size marked on the box. The number of bulbs in any individual box shall not vary more than two percent below the number marked on the box and not more than one percent in lots of 10 boxes or more. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & 632.900 - 632.980 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 632.900 - 632.980 
 
 History:
