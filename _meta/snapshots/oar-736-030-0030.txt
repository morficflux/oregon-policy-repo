 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 SPECIAL REGULATIONS AFFECTING OCEAN SHORES ADJACENT TO COASTAL CITIES 
 736-030-0030 
 Prohibition of Windsailing 
 
 No person shall windsail on any beach within the city limits of Cannon Beach and Seaside except by permit from the city. A windsail is a sail propelled 3 or 4 wheeled cart. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.124 & 390.660 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PR 11-1994, f. 11-29-94, cert. ef. 12-1-94
