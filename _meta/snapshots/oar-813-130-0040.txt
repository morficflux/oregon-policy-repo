 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 130 HELP PROGRAM 
 813-130-0040 
 Eligible Costs for HELP Funds 
 
 Project costs eligible for HELP assistance are costs that promote housing affordability and include, but are not limited to:  (1) Development hard costs, such as the actual costs of constructing or rehabilitating rental housing;  (2) Costs of acquiring improved or unimproved real property;  (3) Pre-development costs which have been pre-approved by the department;  (4) Soft development costs associated with the construction, acquisition, or rehabilitation, including fees and interest studies; and  (5) Other uses identified in the HELP manual. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.555 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.555, 456.559 & 456.625
