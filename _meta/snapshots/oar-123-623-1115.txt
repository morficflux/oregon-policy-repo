 Division 623 STRATEGIC INVESTMENT PROGRAM 
 123-623-1115 
 Specially Preserved Rural Areas
 
 
 On and after October 5, 2015, the following remain rural areas under ORS 285C.600 pursuant to section 2, chapter 518, Oregon Laws 2015: 

 (1) Any area inside Clackamas Rural SIZ #1, designated September 24, 2010, and sponsored by Clackamas County and the cities of Canby, Estacada, Happy Valley, Molalla and Sandy; and 

 (2) Tax lot 2900 in the southeast quarter of section 21 of Township 1 North, Range 2 West of the Willamette Meridian (1N221-2900) in Washington County. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 & 285C.615(7) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285C.600 & OrLaws 2015 ch. 518 §2
