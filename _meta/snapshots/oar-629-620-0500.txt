 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 620 CHEMICAL AND OTHER PETROLEUM PRODUCT RULES 
 629-620-0500 
 Disposal of Chemical Containers 
 
 Operators shall dispose of chemical containers in accordance with the Department of Environmental Quality’s disposal requirements. Operators may apply flushing solution resulting from cleaning of chemical containers to the operation area. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 527.710 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 527.715 & 527.765 
 
 History:
