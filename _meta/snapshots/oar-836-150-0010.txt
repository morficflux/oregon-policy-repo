 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 150 OREGON REINSURANCE PROGRAM 
 836-150-0010 
 Purpose and Statutory Authority 
 
 The purpose of OAR 836-150-0010 to 836-150-0060 is to administer the Oregon Reinsurance Program established in the Department of Consumer and Business Services for the purposes of stabilizing the rates and premiums for individual health benefit plans and providing greater financial certainty to consumers of health insurance in this state. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & 2017 Or Laws ch 538 sec 19 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2017 Or Laws ch 538 sec 18-21 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ID 38-2018, adopt filed 12/19/2018, effective 01/01/2019
