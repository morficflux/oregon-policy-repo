 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0048 
 Heppner Unit 
 
 The Heppner Unit, number 48, is that area beginning at Heppner; northeast on State Highway 74 to U.S. Highway 395; south on U.S. Highway 395 to Long Creek-Monument Road; northwest on Long Creek-Monument Road to North Fork John Day River at Monument; southwest along North Fork John Day River to State Highway 19 at Kimberly; northwest on State Highway 19 to State Highway 207; north on State Highway 207 to Heppner, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
