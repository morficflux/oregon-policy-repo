 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 VEHICLE WEIGHT AND DIMENSION LIMITS — PERMITS 
 734-070-0035 
 Permit Fees 
 
 (1) The Department shall charge a fee of $8 for each permit issued by the Department under ORS 818.200.  (2) The Department shall charge a fee of $2 for each permit issued by a third party agent. The $2 fee shall be collected by the private contractor and remitted to the Department.  (3) Approved permits issued by a third party agent may include an administrative fee to be retained by the agent as authorized by ORS 818.270 not to exceed $5 for each permit issued to a vehicle in addition to the $2 fee charged by the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 818.270 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.200, 818.220 & 818.270
