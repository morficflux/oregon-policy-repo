 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 CERTIFICATES, PERMITS, LICENSES AND DOCUMENTS 
 740-035-0140 
 Vehicle Additions, Deletions or Alterations 
 
 Motor carriers must immediately apply for amendment of vehicle registration listing on forms furnished by the Department when:  (1) Any motor vehicle has been reconstructed; or  (2) Any motor vehicle has been materially altered, modified, or added to in body or seating arrangement or carrying capacity; or  (3) The declared combined weight of the motor vehicle is increased or decreased; or  (4) Any motor vehicle has been withdrawn from service. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 823 & 825 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.450
