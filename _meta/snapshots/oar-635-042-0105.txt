 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 COLUMBIA RIVER SYSTEM COMMERCIAL FISHERIES 
 635-042-0105 
 Shad Sales
 
 
 Shad may be taken for commercial purposes during authorized commercial fisheries in the Columbia River and Select Areas downstream of Bonneville Dam with the same fishing gear authorized for the taking of salmon. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.119 & ORS 496.118 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.129 & ORS 507.030
