 Department of Human Services 
 Vocational Rehabilitation Services - Chapter 582 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 VENDOR SELECTION POLICIES 
 582-080-0040 
 Conflict of Interest Regarding Vendor Selection 
 
 No employee or representative of OVRS may take any action that would result in the person’s private financial benefit, nor shall the action benefit any member or any business with which he/she or any member of his/her household is associated. All OVRS employees must give notice to their immediate supervisor of all potential conflicts of interest. If the supervisor agrees that there is a conflict of interest, the supervisor shall send the matter through supervisory channels to the appointment authority for resolution. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 344.530 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 344.511 - 344.690 & 344.710 - 344.730
