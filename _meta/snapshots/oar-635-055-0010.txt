 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 FALCONRY LICENSES, PERMITS AND REQUIREMENTS 
 635-055-0010 
 License Required to Practice Falconry 
 
 (1) Before a falconer may participate in hunting any wildlife, he/she shall possess a valid Oregon falconry license, a hunting license, and any permit or stamp that is required.  (2) Nonresident falconers hunting in Oregon shall have a valid falconry license from a state having a federally approved falconry program, a nonresident hunting license, and any permit or stamp that is required. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.112, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.112, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
