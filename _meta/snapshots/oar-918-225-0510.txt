 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 225 BOILERS AND PRESSURE VESSELS 
 918-225-0510 
 Installation of Buried Vessels 
 
 Under no circumstances shall any pressure vessel subject to the Boiler and Pressure Vessel Laws and these rules be buried underground or located in an inaccessible place. Where necessary to install a vessel underground, it shall be enclosed in a concrete or masonry pit with removable cover so that inspection of entire shell and heads of the vessel can be made. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 480.545 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.545
