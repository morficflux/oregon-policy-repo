 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 33 INVESTMENTS (ORS 733.510 TO 733.780) 
 836-033-0120 
 Purpose and Authority; Definition 
 
 (1) OAR 836-033-0120 and 836-033-0130 implement ORS 733.695 by establishing which obligations are not of investment grade and regulating the acts and practices of insurers with respect to the concentration of such obligations.  (2) For purposes of this rule and OAR 836-033-0130, “obligation” has the meaning given that term in ORS 733.540. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244, 733.010 & 733.695 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 733.695 
 
 History:
