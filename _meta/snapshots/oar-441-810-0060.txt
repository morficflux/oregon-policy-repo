 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 810 GENERAL 
 441-810-0060 
 Assumed Business Name 
 
 Before registration or renewal of registration of a collection agency business:  (1) An individual using an assumed business name shall be registered with the Secretary of State.  (2) A corporation must be registered to do business in Oregon with the Secretary of State. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 697 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 697.0031(1)
