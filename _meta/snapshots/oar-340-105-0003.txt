 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 105 MANAGEMENT FACILITY PERMITS 
 340-105-0003 
 Considerations Under Federal Law 
 
 The provisions of 40 CFR 270.3, and the Remedial Action Plan provisions under 40 CFR 270.2, 270.11(d), 270.42, 270.68, 270.73(a) and 270.79–270.230 are deleted. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 465.009 & 466.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 465.003, 465.009, 466.075 & 466.105
