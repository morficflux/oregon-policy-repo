 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 16 CONTROL OVER THE EXPORT OF UNPROCESSED TIMBER FROM COMMON SCHOOL FOREST LAND AND OTHER BOARD-MANAGED LANDS 
 141-016-0040 
 Log Branding and Marking Requirements 
 
 All unprocessed state timber or logs originating from state timber sales shall be branded with an assigned and registered brand before removal from the sale area. Unless prevented by the size or condition of the wood one end of all logs originating from state timber sales shall be hammer branded and one end shall be painted with a paint type and color determined by the State Forester or Director. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 273.045 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.521 
 
 History:
