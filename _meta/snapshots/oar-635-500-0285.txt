 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-0285 
 Malheur Basin Fish Management — Organization of Rules 
 
 Administrative rules for the Malheur Basin are organized as follows:  (1) OAR 635-500-0290 through 635-500-0300 apply to all waters of the Malheur Basin.  (2) OAR 635-500-0305 through 635-500-0325 apply to specific areas of the Malheur Basin and appear in order beginning in headwater areas and proceeding downstream, with reservoirs listed last. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 & 506 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496 & 506
