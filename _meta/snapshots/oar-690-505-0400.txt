 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 505 DESCHUTES BASIN PROGRAM 
 690-505-0400 
 Ground Water Classification 
 
 Ground water in the Deschutes Basin is classified for any beneficial use. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027, 536.220, 536.300 & 536.340 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.835 & Ch. 659 2001 OL HB 2184 
 
 History:
