 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 COMMERCIAL SHELLFISH AND MARINE INVERTEBRATE FISHERIES 
 635-005-0305 
 Bay Clam Dive Fishery Defined 
 
 "Bay clam dive fishery" means the commercial fishery for bay clams (including: cockle clams, Clinocardium nuttallii; butter clams, Saxidomus gigantea; gaper clams, Tresus capax; and softshell clams, Mya arenaria) from subtidal areas in Oregon estuaries using dive gear. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & 506.129
