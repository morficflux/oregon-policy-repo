 Mortuary and Cemetery Board 
 Chapter 830 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 RESPONSIBILITIES OF LICENSED FACILITIES, INSPECTIONS, PREPARATION ROOM REQUIREMENTS, OWNERSHIP CHANGES, AND INDIGENT DISPOSITION REIMBURSEMENT 
 830-040-0005 
 Contract Requirements 
 
 (1) It is the responsibility of each licensed facility entering into contracts, either at need, prearrangement or preconstruction, for death care goods and services to have printed (in a minimum 10-point print) at the bottom of each contract "This facility is licensed and regulated by the Oregon Mortuary and Cemetery Board" followed immediately by the current area code and phone number of the Oregon Mortuary and Cemetery Board.  (2) Each licensed facility must ensure that all contracts (at need, prearrangement or preconstruction) for death care goods and services have the registered business name and physical location of the facility printed, in a minimum 10-point font, on the front of the contract. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 692.320 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 692.320
