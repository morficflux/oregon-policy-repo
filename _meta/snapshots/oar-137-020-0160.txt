 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 MISLEADING PRICE REPRESENTATIONS 
 137-020-0160 
 Sales Practices 
 
 (1) A retailer may not limit the price advertised for a particular grade of motor vehicle fuel to a customer purchasing or receiving goods or services in addition to the motor vehicle fuel except for full services. For purposes of this rule, a customer does not include customers who purchase ODOT diesel.  (2) The location at which any grade of motor vehicle fuel is dispensed or at which any condition is applicable will not be changed except for a bona fide reason and will not be changed within 60 days of another change except for an emergency or legal necessity.  (3) Violation of OAR 137-020-0150 and this rule is a violation of the Unlawful Trade Practices Act, ORS 646.608(1)(u). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 646 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 646.608(1)(u) & 1985 c.751 (2)
