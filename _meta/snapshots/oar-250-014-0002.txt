 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 MARINE FACILITY PROGRAM RULES 
 250-014-0002 
 Eligibility 
 
 (1) The Boating Facility Grant Program is available to eligible public project sponsors.  (2) The Clean Vessel Act and Boating Infrastructure Grant programs are available to eligible public and private project sponsors.  (3) The Maintenance Assistance Program is available to eligible public project sponsors excluding federal agencies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830.110 & 830.150 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.150
