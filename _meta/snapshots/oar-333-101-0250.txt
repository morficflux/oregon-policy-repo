 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 101 REGISTRATION OF RADIATION MACHINES, GENERAL LICENSE RADIOACTIVE MATERIALS, LICENSING OF RADIATION SERVICES, AND ACCREDITATION OF HOSPITAL RADIOLOGY INSPECTORS 
 333-101-0250 
 Hospital Radiology Inspector Accreditation Revocation 
 
 The Authority may condition, suspend, revoke, or refuse to renew accreditation of a radiology inspector for the following reasons:  (1) Knowingly falsifying information included on the inspection report form supplied by the Authority.  (2) Substantially failing to comply with Authority procedures.  (3) Failing to meet Authority accuracy requirements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.790 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 History:
