 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Dungeness Crab Commission - Chapter 645 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENTS AND PENALTIES FOR NON-PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENTS 
 645-010-0010 
 Assessments 
 
 Any person who is a first purchaser as defined in ORS Chapter 576, shall deduct and withhold an assessment at the rate of 1% of the cash value of the raw product of Dungeness Crab. This assessment deduction shall be made upon the incoming revenue thereof, for Dungeness Crab taken by crab fishing and delivered in Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576.305 - 576.355 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576.305
