 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 BARBERS AND HAIRDRESSERS GRANT PROGRAM 
 575-035-0051 
 Fund Management
 
 
 (1) Funds from the Department of Commerce for the establishment of the B & H Grant program will be transferred to the Oregon Student Assistance Fund to be invested by the State Treasurer. 

 (2) Only the investment earnings from the fund shall be used for B & H Grants. 

 (3) Grants will be awarded each year, funds permitting, beginning on October 1, 1986. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.290 & ORS 348.505
