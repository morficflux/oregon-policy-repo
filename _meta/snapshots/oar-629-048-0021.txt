 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 48 SMOKE MANAGEMENT 
 629-048-0021 
 Necessity of Safeguarding Public Health 
 
 (1) Excessive smoke, no matter its source, can pose a serious health risk to the public, especially vulnerable populations. 

 (2) To help minimize the health risks to the public from prescribed burning, the program encourages prescribed burning emissions be minimized and smoke intrusions be avoided. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 477.013, ORS 477.562, ORS 526.016 & ORS 526.041 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 477.013, ORS 477.515 & ORS 477.562 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOF 1-2019, adopt filed 02/15/2019, effective 03/01/2019
