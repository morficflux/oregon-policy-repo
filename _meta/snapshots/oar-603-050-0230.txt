 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 PLANT INDUSTRY GENERAL 
 603-050-0230 
 Penalties 
 
 Upon a determination that a person has willfully violated any of the provisions of OAR 603-050-0210 to 603-050-0225, the Department may suspend, revoke, or refuse to nenew the registration of the official identification number assigned under OAR 603-050-0210(1). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 632 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 632.460 & 632.905 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 19-1977, f. & ef. 10-5-77
