 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE FOR CONTESTED CASES UNDER THE OREGON SAFE EMPLOYMENT ACT 
 438-085-0550 
 Ex Parte Communications 
 
 (1) Except for communications made in the context of a mediation or other settlement proceeding, there shall be no ex parte communication, concerning the merits of any pending case, with the assigned Administrative Law Judge.  (2) In the event a prohibited ex parte communication occurs, the assigned Administrative Law Judge shall place on the record a statement of the substance of the prohibited communication and shall notify the parties of the communication and of their right to rebut such communication. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335 & 656.726(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.025 & 654.078
