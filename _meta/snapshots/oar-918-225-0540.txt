 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 225 BOILERS AND PRESSURE VESSELS 
 918-225-0540 
 Scope of Special Inspector’s Authority 
 
 (1) General inspection requirements are in ORS 480.560.  (2) The type of inspections a special inspector can do is set out in ORS 480.570.  (3) The special inspector’s enforcement powers are in ORS 480.660.  (4) Appeals from special inspector actions are under ORS 480.660(4). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 480.560, 480.570 & 480.660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.560, 480.570 & 480.660 
 
 History:
