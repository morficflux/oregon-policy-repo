 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 44 HOME OWNERSHIP ASSISTANCE PROGRAM OF THE OREGON HOUSING FUND 
 813-044-0000 
 Purpose and Objectives
 
 
 The purpose of OAR chapter 813, division 44 is to establish the Home Ownership Assistance Program (HOAP) to implement the purposes of the Home Ownership Assistance Account of the Oregon Housing Fund described in ORS 458.655. The purpose of HOAP is to expand access to homeownership for Oregonians earning at or below area median income. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.555 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 458.620 & 458.655
