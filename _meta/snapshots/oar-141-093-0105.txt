 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 93 ADMINISTRATIVE RULES GOVERNING THE ISSUANCE AND ENFORCEMENT OF GENERAL PERMITS WITHIN WATERS OF THIS STATE 
 141-093-0105 
 Application Requirements and Completeness Review for Authorizing Projects under a General Permit
 
 
 (1) Authorization Required. Unless otherwise specified, a separate application must be submitted to the Department before starting any project covered by a GP. 

 (2) Required Information. A complete, signed application must be submitted on forms provided by the Department. The application must contain all applicable information set forth in OAR 141-085-0550. An approved, unexpired wetland delineation is required when wetlands are proposed for impact, unless otherwise approved by the Department. 

 (3) Applicant Signature Required. The applicant signature is required. As used in this section, “applicant” means a person who has the authority and responsibility to fully execute the terms and conditions of an authorization issued under these rules. The applicant becomes the authorization holder. The OAR 141-085-0510(1) definition of “applicant” does not govern use of the term in this Division. 

 (4) Fee. Any person proposing to conduct an activity under a GP must pay all applicable fees in accordance with OAR 141-085-0545, unless otherwise specified in a specific GP. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.600 - 196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990
