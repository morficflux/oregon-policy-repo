 Oregon 529 Savings Board 
 Chapter 173 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 ADMINISTRATION 
 173-005-0005 
 Executive Director 
 
 The Executive Director is responsible for the day to day operations of the network and for carrying out such duties and responsibilities as assigned by the board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.853(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.853(2)
