 Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying 
 Chapter 820 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 REGULATION 
 820-015-0005 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) “Complainant” means a person who submits a complaint to the board.  (2) "Respondent" means a person or firm that is the subject of a complaint. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 672.255 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.002 - 672.325
