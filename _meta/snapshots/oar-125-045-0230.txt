 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 DISPOSITION AND ACQUISITION OF REAL PROPERTY INTERESTS 
 125-045-0230 
 Right of First Refusal 
 
 (1) Prior to proceeding with the Transaction Process described in 125-045-0214, the Disposing Agency will determine, with the advice of the Division, whether any party is entitled to a Right of First Refusal.  
(2) If no contractual Right of First Refusal exists, the Disposing Agency may, in its discretion, grant a Right of First Refusal to the parties described in ORS 270.010(2) via written agreement with the party or parties. 
(3) Any Right of First Refusal will be governed by the conditions set forth in the agreement. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 270.010, 270.015(2), 270.100(1)(d) & 270.110, 270.135-270.140 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 270.010-270.015, 270.100 & 270.110, 270.135-270.140
