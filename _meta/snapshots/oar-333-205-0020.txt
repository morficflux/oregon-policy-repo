 Division 205 TRAUMA SYSTEM HOSPITAL DESIGNATION IN TRAUMA AREA # 1 
 333-205-0020 
 Hospital Resource Criteria
 
 
 Trauma system hospitals shall meet or exceed the standards for Hospital Resources as set forth in OAR 333-200-0090, Exhibit 4. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431A.060, 431A.065 & 431A.090
