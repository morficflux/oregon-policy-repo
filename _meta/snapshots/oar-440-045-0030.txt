 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Director's Office - Chapter 440 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 PREMIUM ASSESSMENT 
 440-045-0030 
 Payment Manner and Intervals 
 
 The manner and intervals for payments of the Workers' Compensation Premium Assessment is delegated to the Workers' Compensation Division. OAR 436 division 085 prescribe such manner and intervals. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 705.135 & 656.612 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.612 & 656.614
