 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 ADOPTION 
 413-120-0960 
 Disclosure to the Prospective Adoptive Parents
 
 
 The Department must provide the prospective adoptive parents in an outgoing Convention adoption all the child summary and medical history in both the original format and translated into the primary language of the prospective adoptive parents. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 417.262, 417.265 & 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 417.262, 417.265 & 418.005
