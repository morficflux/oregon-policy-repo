 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 32 AGGRAVATED MURDER 
 255-032-0027 
 Inmate's Right to Review Record; Exceptions 
 
 Except as provided in OAR 255-015-0010, all exhibits to be considered by the Board shall be disclosed to the inmate's attorney or the inmate, if proceeding pro se, within a reasonable period of time before the hearing:  (1) Exhibits not available prior to the hearing shall be made available to the inmate's attorney or to the inmate, if not represented, at the hearing.  (2) All material relevant and pertinent to issues before the Board shall be made a part of the record.  (3) Any material not made part of the record shall be separated and a statement to that effect shall be placed in the record. The board shall follow the criteria for denial or disclosure of records set out in OAR 255-015-0010. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335, 192.410 – 192.505, 144.025(3) & 144.050
