 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 DELEGATION OF PROGRAMS TO LOCAL JURISDICTIONS 
 918-020-0010 
 Relationship of Delegation Rules to Statute 
 
 These rules establish standards for:  (1) A procedure for cooperation between local municipalities and the Oregon State Fire Marshal; and  (2) The notice of assumption of any portion of the plan review program, required by ORS 455.148 and 455.150, and shall include a procedure for cooperation with the State Fire Marshal or the State Fire Marshal’s designee, to consider a uniform fire code in the plan review process. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.150 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.150
