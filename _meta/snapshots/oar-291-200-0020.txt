 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 PRISON ADVISORY COMMITTEE 
 291-200-0020 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) Affected Communities: Those cities and counties in which a Department of Corrections facility is sited or exists, or which due to their proximity to a Department of Corrections facility commit extensive resources to the ongoing operation of the facility.  (2) Facility: An institution or facility operated by the Department of Corrections which physically houses inmates.  (3) Superintendent: Any person within the Department of Corrections who reports to the Assistant Director for Operations Division or Institutions Administrator and has the responsibility for the delivery and coordination of program operations in a specific facility. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
