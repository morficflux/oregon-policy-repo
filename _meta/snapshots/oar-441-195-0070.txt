 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 195 RECORDS AND REPORTS 
 441-195-0070 
 Discretionary Authority 
 
 (1) No broker-dealer, or any associated person, shall exercise any discretionary power or authority for any customer unless such customer has given prior written authorization to exercise such power or authority to a stated associated person or persons.  (2) This rule shall not apply to transactions in which the broker-dealer’s discretion is limited to the price at which or the time when an order given by a customer for the purchase or sale of a definite amount of a specified security shall be executed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 59 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 59.135 & 59.205
