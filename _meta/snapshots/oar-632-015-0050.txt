 Department of Geology and Mineral Industries 
 Chapter 632 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 INFORMATION AND SEISMIC TEST HOLES 
 632-015-0050 
 Filing of Reports 
 
 (1) Report on Completion. Within 45 days after the completion of the plugging of an information hole or seismic shot hole, the owner or operator must file a report with the department setting forth in detail the method used in plugging the well or wells. The report must be in a form acceptable to the department.  (2) The drilling records:  (a) Must be in narrative form and include a map showing the location and depth of each shot hole or information hole so that it can be located; and  (b) Must indicate all work that has been performed in compliance with the permit issued for the seismic program or information hole operation.  (3) Within 60 days after plugging, the permittee must file an affidavit with the department, setting forth the location of the information hole or seismic program and the method used to protect such water-bearing formation, if any, that was penetrated during the program or operation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 516.090 & 520.095 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  520.027, 520.095 & 520.097
