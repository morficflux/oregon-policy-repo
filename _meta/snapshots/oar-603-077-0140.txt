 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 77 FIELD BURNING RULES 
 603-077-0140 
 Emergency Burning Cessation 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 468A.610 and upon finding of danger to public health or safety, the Commission or the Department of Environmental Quality may order temporary emergency cessation of all open field burning in any area of the Willamette Valley. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.585
