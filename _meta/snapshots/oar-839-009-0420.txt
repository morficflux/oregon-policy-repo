 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 EMPLOYEE LEAVE LAWS 
 839-009-0420 
 OMFLA: Relationship to FMLA 
 
 To the extent the employee’s need for OMFLA leave is also covered by the Qualifying Exigency entitlements of the federal Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) under 29 CFR §825.126, the employer may run OMFLA leave and FMLA leave concurrently. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 659A.093(6) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 659A.090 – 659A.099
