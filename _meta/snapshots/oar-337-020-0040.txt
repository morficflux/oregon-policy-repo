 Board of Medical Imaging 
 Chapter 337 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 BIRTHDAY MONTH RENEWAL 
 337-020-0040 
 Requirements for Prorating Fees
 
 
 License and permit renewal fees will be prorated only if the applicant can demonstrate to the Board that the applicant did not practice medical imaging in Oregon during the time the license or permit was expired. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 688 & ORS 688.555(1)
