 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 MEDICAID HOME DELIVERED MEALS 
 411-040-0050 
 Local Office Responsibility and Oversight 
 
 (1) The Department shall establish, authorize, purchase, and monitor the standards for Medicaid paid home delivered meals.  (2) Case managers must authorize Medicaid home delivered meal services.  (3) Subsequent yearly nutrition education:  (a) May be determined by local Medicaid home delivered meal providers; and  (b) Will be monitored by the Medicaid case manager at least annually.  (4) Provision of the home delivered meals reduces the need for reliance on paid staff during some meal times, so meal preparation hours in the service plan must be reduced.  (5) The Department shall make payments to Medicaid paid home delivered meal providers for all home-delivered meals provided to participants. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070, 411.060 & 411.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.070 
 
 History:
