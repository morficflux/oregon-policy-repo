 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 RESPONSIBLE MANAGING INDIVIDUAL, PRE-LICENSURE TRAINING, AND TESTING 
 812-006-0450 
 Pre-Licensure Experience
 
 
 (1) An individual may qualify as a responsible managing individual by experience under ORS 701.005(16)(c)(B) as follows: 

 (a) The individual must be listed on the agency’s license records before July 1, 2000, as having been a sole proprietor, partner, venturer, member, corporate officer, trustee, or responsible managing individual of a business licensed under ORS chapter 701 before July 1, 2000; and 

 (b) The license of the business described in section (1) of this rule has not lapsed, or if lapsed, has lapsed for not more than the 24 month period prior to the date of the application; or 

 (A) If the individual is listed on multiple license records as a sole proprietor, partner, venturer, member, corporate officer, trustee or responsible managing individual, the licensed business with which the individual is currently associated must not have lapsed for more than the 24-month period prior to the date of the application; and 

 (B) The individual must have been similarly and continuously associated with one or more licensed contractors during the time period beginning before July 1, 2000, until the date of the application. 

 (c) The individual must have been listed as a sole proprietor, partner, venturer, member, corporate officer, trustee, or responsible managing individual of the business described in subsection (a) or (b) of this rule within the 24 month period prior to date of the application. 

 (2) An individual may qualify as a responsible managing individual by experience under ORS 701.005(16)(c)(B) if the individual has the experience required by Section 2, Oregon Laws 2018, chapter 46, and has passed the test required by ORS 701.122. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 701.122, 701.235 & OR Laws 2018, Ch 46, Secs 1,2 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701.005, 701.122 & OR Laws 2018, Ch 46, Secs 1,2
