 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 321 TIMBER TAXES 
 150-321-0540 
 Check Scaling at the Point of First Measurement 
 
 (1) The department must be given access to any location where timber is first measured at any time the site is open for business in order to inspect or check scale the grading and measuring practices for timber subject to ORS 321.005 to 321.185, 321.560 to 321.600 and 321.700 to 321.754.  (2) Scalers or companies controlling the sites must make all pertinent information for the check scale available to the department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 & 321.609 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 321.609
