 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 62 SIGNS IDENTIFYING CULTURAL AND HISTORICAL FEATURES 
 734-062-0125 
 Signs Identifying Viticultural Features: Installation 
 
 (1) The Department may furnish, erect, and maintain all signs that have been approved for installation.  (2) The Department shall notify applicant promptly when an application has been approved or denied, and when a sign has been installed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 366.205, 366.450, 810.200 & 810.210 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 366.205, 366.450, 810.200 & 810.210 
 
 History:
