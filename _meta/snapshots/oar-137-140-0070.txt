 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 COLLECTION, RETENTION, PRESERVATION AND CATALOGING OF BIOLOGICAL EVIDENCE 
 137-140-0070 
 Retention Periods for Biological Evidence 
 
 (1) Custodians shall retain biological evidence for any covered offense for the periods specified in ORS 137.707.  (2) Custodians should confirm with the local district attorney whether a person convicted of a covered offense has served the person’s sentence.  (3) Custodians shall use the form provided in Appendix A to seek early disposal of biological evidence.  (4) Custodians shall not dispose of biological evidence without a signed order from a court.
