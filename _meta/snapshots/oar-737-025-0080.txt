 Department of Transportation 
 Transportation Safety Office - Chapter 737 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 OREGON SAFE ROUTES TO SCHOOL FUND 
 737-025-0080 
 Project Selection and Awarding Education Grants
 
 
 (1) TSO will review the applications to determine if: 

 (a) The applicant and the project are eligible for a SRTS Fund grant; and 

 (b) The project proposal complies with the appropriate standards or practices for the work described. 

 (2) The Safe Routes to School Advisory Committee will evaluate and rank eligible applications and submit funding recommendations to the OTSC. 

 (3) TSO staff will present SRAC funding recommendations for projects to the OTSC in order to award grants based on the criteria established in OAR 737-025-0070. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 184.740, 184.741 & 184.742 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 184.741 & 184.742
