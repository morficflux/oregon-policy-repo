 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 VETERAN SERVICE OFFICER CERTIFICATION PROGRAM 
 274-031-0005 
 Minimum Certification Standards 
 
 (1) Each certification test will require a passing grade as determined by ODVA. If a passing grade is not achieved, the test will be readministered approximately 90 days thereafter.  (2) Newly appointed and reestablished VSOs must complete the minimum training standards for certification within the first 18 months of appointment.  (3) The Director, or designee, will assign a specific value to each training as outlined in 274-031-0003. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406.452 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 406.050 & 406.452 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DVA 1-2011, f. & cert. ef. 3-24-11
