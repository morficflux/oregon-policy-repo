 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 VETERANS’ LOANS 
 274-020-0290 
 Second Loans 
 
 Before a second loan may be granted:  (1) The repayment shall have been satisfactory; and  (2) The amount shall be limited to the balance of the original or restored loan right. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 407.205 & 407.265
