 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 62 DRIVER LICENSING 
 735-062-0023 
 Proof of Legal Name, Real ID 
 
 (1) If a first-time Real ID applicant’s legal name is different from the name in the document submitted to prove identity and lawful status pursuant to OAR 735-062-0022, the applicant must provide proof of the current legal name sufficient to establish that the applicant is the same person named in the document submitted as proof of identity and lawful status. 

 (2) There must be sufficient connection between information shown on the identity and lawful status document and the proof of the applicant’s current legal name for DMV to determine that the applicant is the person named in the identity and lawful status document. Examples of connecting information include first name, middle name or middle initial, previous surnames used, date of birth, place of birth, social security number or mother’s maiden name. 

 (3) DMV may accept an identity document listed in OAR 735-062-0020 as proof of the applicant’s current legal name. 

 (4) DMV may use the applicant’s DMV record to help establish a connection between the identity document and the document used to establish identity and lawful status. 

 (5) If the applicant’s proof of legal name does not establish the applicant is the same person named in the document submitted as proof of identity and lawful status, or the applicant’s identity document or DMV record do not show the person’s current legal name, the applicant must provide documentary proof of a legal name change. Documentary proof of an applicant’s legal name change may include, but is not limited to: 

 (a) An official government-issued marriage document; 

 (b) A divorce decree; 

 (c) A certificate of registered domestic partnership; 

 (d) A judgment of dissolution or annulment of marriage or domestic partnership; 

 (e) An adoption decree; 

 (f) A court decree; or 

 (g) An order or judgment legally changing the applicant’s name. 

 (6) An applicant for a renewal or replacement Real ID must provide proof of legal name if the applicant’s name has changed since the last issuance of a Real ID.  Proof of legal name must be established in accordance with sections (3), (4) and (5) of this rule. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 802.010 & 807.455 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 807.455, 6 CFR §37.13
