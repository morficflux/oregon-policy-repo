 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 380 LICENSURE OF ART THERAPISTS 
 331-380-0020 
 APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR INITIAL LICENSURE AS A LICENSED ART THERAPIST 
 
 The Office will enter all licensed art therapist authorization holders and their status into its licensure database, which is available to the public. An individual applying for initial licensure as a licensed art therapist must: 

 (1) Meet the requirements of OAR 331 Division 30. 

 (2) Submit a completed application form prescribed by the Office, which must contain the information listed in OAR 331-030-0000. 

 (3) Be at least 18 years old. 

 (4) Have received at least a master’s degree from a program accepted by the ATCB. 

 (5) Arrange for official documentation to be sent to the Office from the ATCB proving that the applicant meets the requirements to be certified as an ATR; or 

 (6) Possess the qualifications to be credentialed as an ATR. 

 (7) Pass a fingerprint-based nationwide criminal records check pursuant to OAR 331-030-0004. 

 (8) Pay all fees. 

 (9) Beginning January 1, 2028, applicants must submit proof of having two years of supervised mental health practice as a PLAT as described in HB 3761 (2025). Applicants must: 

 (a) Submit proof of having a PLAT from the Office for 24 months with no outstanding disciplinary action; or 

 (b) Arrange for official documentation from the ATCB that the individual had a ATR-P or ATR for 24 months prior to date of application. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 681.740-681.758, 2019 Senate Bill 29 & 2025 House Bill 3761 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 681.740-681.758, 2019 Senate Bill 29 & 2025 House Bill 3761
