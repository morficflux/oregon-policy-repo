 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Raspberry and Blackberry Commission - Chapter 611 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENT 
 611-010-0025 
 Effective Dates of Assessment 
 
 The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser or handler, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and OAR 611-010-0010(1), or the reports required under 611-010-0015, applies to and covers any purchase by him or delivery on or after July 6, 1987, of caneberries grown in Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576.325 & 576.335
