 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 SUPPORTIVE SERVICES 
 413-050-0625 
 Target Population 
 
 To be eligible for Targeted Case Management services, a child or young adult in the care and custody of the Department must meet the following criteria: 

 (1) Be under age 21; 

 (2) Be Medicaid eligible; 

 (3) Reside in an in-home setting , foster home, group home, residential care facility and Public Institutions as defined in 42 CFR 435.1010, or in the Department's Independent Living Program paid for and under the responsibility of the Department; 

 (4) Reside in Oregon; 

 (5) Be a citizen or legal permanent resident of the United States; and 

 (6) Be ineligible for Title IV-E funding. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005
