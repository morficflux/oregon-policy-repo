 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 160 WILDLIFE MANAGEMENT PLAN 
 635-160-0010 
 Plan Objectives for Elk Management 
 
 The tables entitled “2016 Rocky Mt Elk Winter Population and Bull Ratio Management Objectives (MO)” and “2016 Roosevelt Elk Winter Population and Bull Ratio Management Objectives (MO)” are incorporated by reference as part of the Oregon Elk Management Plan. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.162 & 496.164 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146, 496.162 & 496.164
