 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 CRIMINAL RECORDS CHECK AND FITNESS  DETERMINATION RULES 
 125-007-0200 
 Statement of Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to provide uniform administrative rules to streamline criminal records check processes state-wide, unless otherwise provided by law. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 181A.170, 181A.195, 181A.215, 184.340 & 184.365 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 181A.170, 181A.195 & 181A.215
