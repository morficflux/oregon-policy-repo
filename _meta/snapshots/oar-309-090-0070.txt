 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 FORENSIC MENTAL HEALTH EVALUATORS AND EVALUATIONS 
 309-090-0070 
 Contested Case Hearing
 
 
 If the Authority denies, revokes, non-renews, suspends, or imposes a condition on certification, the Authority shall provide notice of the applicant’s right to a hearing pursuant to the provisions of ORS Chapter 183.
