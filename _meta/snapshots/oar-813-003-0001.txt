 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY 
 813-003-0001 
 Purpose and Objectives 
 
 OAR 813, division 003, is promulgated to carry out the provisions of ORS 291.055(c) and 456.562, which relate generally to the Department’s authority to license, share or otherwise provide for the use by a Person of intellectual property acquired or developed by the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 456.555 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 291.055 & 456.562 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OHCS 4-2004, f. & cert. ef. 11-23-04
