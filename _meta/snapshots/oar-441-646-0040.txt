 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 646 IDENTITY THEFT 
 441-646-0040 
 Safeguarding of Personal Information 
 
 Protecting the security, confidentiality and integrity of personal information extends to:  (1) Electronic and paper methods of storing personal information;  (2) Electronic, paper and verbal methods of transmitting personal information; and  (3) All methods of disposing of personal information. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 646A.626 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 646A.622 
 
 History:
