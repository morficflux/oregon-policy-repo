 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 76 ASPIRE PROGRAM 
 575-076-0005 
 Purpose and Scope
 
 
 This rule implements the Access to Student assistance Programs In Reach of Everyone (ASPIRE) Program. The program encourages all Oregon students to see education, training, and career pathways beyond high school as an option. The program provides information about financial aid, education and training, and career pathway options to students, ASPIRE site staff, community volunteers and families for this purpose. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348 & ORS 348.500 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.500
