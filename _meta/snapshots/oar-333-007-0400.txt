 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 MARIJUANA AND HEMP TESTING 
 333-007-0400 
 Standards for Pesticides Compliance Testing
 
 
 (1) A marijuana item or industrial hemp-derived vapor item required to be tested for pesticides must be tested by a laboratory for all the analytes listed in Exhibit A, Table 3, incorporated by reference. 

 (2) A batch fails pesticide testing if a laboratory detects the presence of a pesticide above the action levels listed in Exhibit A, Table 3 in any sample, including a duplicate: 

 (a) During an initial test where no reanalysis is requested; or 

 (b) Upon reanalysis as described in OAR 333-007-0450(1). 

 (3) The Authority will review and update, if necessary, the analytes listed in Exhibit A, Table 3, at least every two years.
