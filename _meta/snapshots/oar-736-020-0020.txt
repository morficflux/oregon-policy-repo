 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 BEACH CONSTRUCTION/ALTERATION STANDARDS 
 736-020-0020 
 Recreation Use Standards 
 
 The following recreation use standards shall be applied, where applicable, to each application for an ocean shore permit.  (1) Recreation Use — The project shall not be a detriment to public recreation use opportunities within the ocean shore area except in those cases where it is determined necessary or legally required to protect sensitive biological resources such as state or federally listed species.  (2) Recreation Access — The project shall avoid blocking off or obstructing public access routes within the ocean shore area except in those cases where it is determined necessary or legally required to protect sensitive biological resources such as state or federally listed species. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.124 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.605 - 390.770 & 390.990 - 390.995
