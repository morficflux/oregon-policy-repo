 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 LICENSES 
 808-003-0820 
 Scoring of Conventional Examinations and LCB Prep Course & Exam
 
 
 (1) Each conventional exam section shall be scored separately. 

 (2) Based on 100 percent, the passing score for a conventional exam and the LCB Prep Course & Exam shall be 75 percent or higher for each section. 

 (3) Except as provided in OAR 808-003-0730 a passing score for a conventional exam section and the LCB Prep Course & Exam shall be valid until the third December 31st following passage of that exam section. An applicant who fails to pass all the sections required for a particular license phase and obtain that license phase by the third December 31st following passage of any exam section shall retake that section. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.570
