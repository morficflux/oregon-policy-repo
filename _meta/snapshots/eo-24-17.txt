     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-17


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     BATTLE MOUNTAIN COMPLEX FIRE IN GRANT AND UMATILLA
     COUNTIES


     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, | find that:

     The fires known as the “Battle Mountain Complex” which contains the Snake,
     Owens, and Monkey Creek fires, are burning in Grant and Umatilla counties.

     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Battle Mountain
     Complex Fire are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and
     structural fire protection was requested by the Grant County Fire Defense Board
     and the State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.


     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Battle Mountain Complex
     Fire in Grant and Umatilla counties and the threat exceeds the firefighting
     capabilities of local firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have
     invoked the Emergency Conflagration Act.


     These findings were made at 2:20 pm on July 21, 2024, with the Department of the
     State Fire Marshal, to provide structural protection for portions of the fires in Grant
     and Umatilla counties, and | now confirm them with this Executive Order.

     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

     IF     The Department ofthe State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
            the Battle Mountain Complex Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
            Marshal.


     2.     This conflagration is declared only for the Battle Mountain Complex Fire
            threatening structures in Grant and Umatilla counties.

     33,    This order was made by verbal proclamation at 2:20 pm on the 21st day of
            July 2024, and signed this 22" day ofJuly 2024, in Salem, Oregon.
