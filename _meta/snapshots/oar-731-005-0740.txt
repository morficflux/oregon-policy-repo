 Department of Transportation 
 Chapter 731 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 PUBLIC CONTRACT RULES; HIGHWAY AND BRIDGE PROJECTS 
 731-005-0740 
 Documentation of Award
 
 
 (1) Basis of Award. After award, ODOT shall make a record showing the basis for determining the successful Offeror part of ODOT's solicitation file. 

 (2) Contents of Award Record. ODOT's record shall include: 

 (a) Bids. 

 (b) Completed Bid tabulation sheet; and 

 (c) Written justification for any rejection of lower Bids. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 279A.050 & 279A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279C.430
