 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 STATEWIDE RULES 
 250-010-0430 
 Chemical Analyses 
 
 (1) The chemical analysis of a person's breath for alcohol can be performed using breath testing equipment as approved under OAR 257-030-0040  (2) Accuracy testing of approved breath testing equipment shall be performed in accordance with OAR 257-030-0170.
