 Board of Nursing 
 Chapter 851 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ADMINISTRATION 
 851-010-0015 
 Vacancies in Office 
 
 A Board Officer vacancy shall be filled by election occurring during the next public day of a regularly scheduled board meeting. The elected Board Member shall fulfill the term of the vacancy. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 678.150 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 678.150
