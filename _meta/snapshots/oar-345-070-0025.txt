 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 CONFIDENTIAL TREATMENT OF SECURITY PROGRAM INFORMATION 
 345-070-0025 
 Releases of Non-Confidential Information 
 
 In reviewing information submitted to them under ORS 469.530, the Council and Director shall jointly determine whether specific portions of such information are not within the definition of "security program" in OAR 345-070-0015 and shall promptly arrange for the physical segregation and public availability of all information not entitled to confidential treatment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.530 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.530
