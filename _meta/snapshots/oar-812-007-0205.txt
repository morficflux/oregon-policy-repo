 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 LICENSING OF INDIVIDUALS AND FIRMS ENGAGED IN LEAD-BASED PAINT ACTIVITY 
 812-007-0205 
 Qualified Owner or Employee Required for Lead-Based Paint Activities — Contractors 
 
 (1) A lead abatement contractor must, at all times, have at least one owner or employee who is a licensed lead supervisor.  (2) A lead inspection contractor must, at all times, have at least one owner or employee who is a licensed lead inspector or a licensed lead risk assessor. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 701.235 & 701.515 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701.510 & 701.515 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CCB 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 2-1-10
