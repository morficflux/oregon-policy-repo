 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 650 RECREATION PARKS AND ORGANIZATIONAL CAMPS 
 918-650-0060 
 Special Rules for Organizational Camps 
 
 Sleeping Spaces. Permanently installed beds or bunks must have:  (1) A minimum of 30 inches of lateral separation between beds and a minimum of 30-inch vertical separation between tiers of beds or between the top tier and the ceiling.  (2) A maximum of two tiers of bunks.  (3) Not less than ten inches of space between the floor and the underside of the beds. In lieu of such space, a bed may have a continuous base which is attached to the floor. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.020, 455.110 & 455.680 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.680
