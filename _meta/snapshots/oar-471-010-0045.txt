 Employment Department 
 Chapter 471 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES GENERAL AND INTERNAL RULES 
 471-010-0045 
 Witness Qualification 
 
 For the purposes of ORS Chapter 657 any witness appearing for the Employment Department, who is an employee of the department:  (1) Is considered to be a duly authorized agent of the director; and  (2) Is considered, by virtue of their employment, to be qualified to testify in an administrative hearing as to the actions taken by the department that resulted in the proceeding in which they are testifying. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 657.610 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 657.610 
 
 History:
