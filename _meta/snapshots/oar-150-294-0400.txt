 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 294 LOCAL BUDGET 
 150-294-0400 
 Estimating Tax Revenue for Biennial Budgets 
 
 (1) When estimating the amount of tax revenue in a biennial budget, follow the procedure in ORS 294.368 for both years of the ensuing budget period and then add the two single-year amounts to get the biennial total.  (2) Each year during the biennial budget period, when certifying the tax levy for the ensuing year, use the single-year estimate for the corresponding year for the purposes of complying with ORS 310.060. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 294.368
