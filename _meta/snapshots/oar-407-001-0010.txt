 Department of Human Services 
 Chapter 407 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 407-001-0010 
 Delegation of Rulemaking Authority 
 
 Any officer or employee of the Department of Human Services who is identified on a completed Delegation of Authority form signed by the Director or Deputy Director of the Department and filed with the Secretary of State, Administrative Rules Unit, is vested with the authority to adopt, amend, repeal, or suspend administrative rules as provided on that form until such delegation is revoked by the Director or Deputy Director of the Department, or the person leaves employment with the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.325, 409.050, 409.120 & 409.130
