 Oregon Youth Authority 
 Chapter 416 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 430 Sensitive Case Descriptors 
 416-430-0000 
 Purpose
 
 
 (1) Certain types of youth in custody behavior exhibited prior to or following placement in OYA custody are identified as sensitive and cause a sensitive case descriptor to be attached to a case file. This rule defines the process by which such descriptor will be attached and removed from a case file. 
(2) These rules apply to all youth in custody who are committed to the legal or physical custody of OYA, and placed in OYA facilities or under OYA supervision on parole/probation status in the community. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 420A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 420.011, 420A.010 & 420A.125
