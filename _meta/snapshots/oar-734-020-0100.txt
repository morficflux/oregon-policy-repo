 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 TRAFFIC CONTROL 
 734-020-0100 
 Definitions for Freeway Median Crossovers 
 
 (1) “Freeway” means a fully access controlled throughway.  (2) “Median” means the space between inside shoulders of the separated one-way roadways of a freeway.  (3) “Crossover” means a surfaced roadway crossing the median and located generally at right angles to, between and connecting the inside or median shoulders of the separate through roadways of a freeway. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184 & 366 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 810.030 
 
 History:
