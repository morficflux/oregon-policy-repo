 Oregon State Treasury 
 Chapter 170 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 ADMINISTRATION AND PROCUREMENT 
 170-002-0160 
 Offer Preparation 
 
 Any Offeror submitting an Offer must sign the Offer, and include any information, forms or other documents required by the Solicitation Document. An Offeror must sign any correction or erasure in its Offer prior to the Closing date and time as permitted by the Solicitation Document. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 279A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279A.065 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OST 1-2018, adopt filed 02/27/2018, effective 03/02/2018
