 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 OREGON RURAL REHABILITATION PROGRAM 
 813-015-0045 
 Equal Opportunity 
 
 No person receiving Program Funding under OAR chapter 813, division 15, shall make any distinction or restriction or discriminate against any purchaser, occupant or lessee, or prospective purchaser, occupant or lessee, relating to the sale, rental, lease or occupancy of real property, because of race, color, creed, sex, marital status or national origin. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.515 - 456.725 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.515 - 456.725 & 566.310 - 566.360 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OHCS 19-2006, f. & cert. ef. 11-15-06
