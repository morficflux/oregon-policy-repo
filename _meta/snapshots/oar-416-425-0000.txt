 Oregon Youth Authority 
 Chapter 416 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 425 OFFENDER TRANSFERS IN CLOSE CUSTODY FACILITIES TO MENTAL HEALTH FACILITIES 
 416-425-0000 
 Purpose
 
 
 These rules prescribe procedures by which offenders in Oregon Youth Authority (OYA) close custody facilities may be transferred to a state mental hospital or a facility designated by the Oregon Health Authority (OHA) for evaluation and treatment. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 420A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.471, 179.473, 179.478, 420.500, 520.505 & 420.525
