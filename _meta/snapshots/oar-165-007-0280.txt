 Secretary of State 
 Elections Division - Chapter 165 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 CONDUCT OF ELECTIONS 
 165-007-0280 
 Spanish Language Ballots
 
 
 A county elections official may choose to prepare and make available an official ballot translated into Spanish for any election. If a county chooses to prepare and make available a Spanish language official ballot the following procedures must be followed: 

 (1) Not later than the 70th day before any election the county elections official must notify the Division in writing of the official’s decision to prepare and make ballots available in Spanish. The notice must include the method by which Spanish ballots will be made available to electors and the method by which those ballots voted and returned by the last day to receive ballots will be tallied. 

 (2) To guarantee consistency of translated material the county elections official must use the Secretary of State’s certified vendor for translation; and 

 (3) The cost of translation must be paid directly to the vendor by the county. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 246.150, ORS 254.465 & ORS 254.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 247, ORS 254 & HB 3291 (2021)
