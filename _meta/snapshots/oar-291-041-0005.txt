 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 SEARCHES (INSTITUTIONS) 
 291-041-0005 
 Authority, Purpose, and Policy 
 
 (1) Authority: The authority for this rule is granted to the Director of the Department of Corrections in accordance with ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, and 423.075.  (2) Purpose: To ensure safety and security of the Department of Corrections facilities.  (3) Policy: In order to maintain proper control over persons under its supervision and to ensure the safety and security of its facilities, it is the policy of the Department of Corrections, that any person employed by or volunteering with the department or Oregon Corrections Enterprises, or any person visiting or living in a Department of Corrections facility may be subject to search at any time in accordance with the procedures outlined in this rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
