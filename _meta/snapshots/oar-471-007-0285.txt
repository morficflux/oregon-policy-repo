 Chapter 471 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 CRIMINAL RECORDS CHECK AND FITNESS DETERMINATION RULES 
 471-007-0285 
 Agency Representation 
 
 The Director shall designate an authorized representative of the department to participate in hearings conducted pursuant to OAR 125-007-0270. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 181A.195 & 657.610 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 181A.195
