 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 APPLICABILITY OF THE PERMANENT DISABILITY RATING STANDARDS 
 438-010-0010 
 Effective Date for Applying Disability Standards 
 
 In applying the disability rating standards at hearing and on review, the Administrative Law Judge and the Board shall apply the disability rating standards as required by OAR 436-035-0003. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.283(7) & 656.295(5)
