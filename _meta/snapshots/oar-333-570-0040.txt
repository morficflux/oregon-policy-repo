 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 570 CERTIFICATE OF NEED REVIEW REQUIREMENTS AND PROCEDURES 
 333-570-0040 
 Additional Information 
 
 Following receipt of a complete certificate of need application, the division may request or accept additional information only if such information is necessary for the division to make an equitable and informed decision through application of applicable rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431.120(6) & 442.315 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431.120(6) & 442.315 
 
 History:
