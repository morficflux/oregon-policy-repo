 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 COUNSELING AND TREATMENT SERVICES 
 413-060-0020 
 Family Planning Services 
 
 Family planning services provided by the State Office for Services to Children and Families include:  (1) Referrals to appropriate family planning resource for consultation and treatment; or  (2) Counseling and information regarding avoiding unwanted pregnancy, termination of pregnancy, maintaining the desired family size and the spacing of children. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  HB 2004 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 435.205 - 435.235 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SCF 6-1995, f. 12-22-95, cert. ef. 12-29-95
