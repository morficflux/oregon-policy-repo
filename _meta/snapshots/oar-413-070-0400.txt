 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 SUBSTITUTE CARE 
 413-070-0400 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 413-070-0400 to 413-070-0490 is to describe the responsibilities of the substitute caregiver and the Department when a child or young adult placed in substitute care by the Department is prescribed or administered psychotropic medication. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 & 418.517 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 109.640, 109.675, 418.005 & 418.517
