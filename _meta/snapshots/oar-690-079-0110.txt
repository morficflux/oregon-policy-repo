 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 79 RESERVATIONS OF WATER FOR FUTURE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT 
 690-079-0110 
 Appeal of Director’s Decision 
 
 The agency requesting the reservation may file a request for Commission review of the Director’s rejection within 60 days of notification of rejection of a reservation request. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025, 536.027, 536.220, 536.300, 536.310, 537.338 & 537.356 - 537.358 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 9-1992, f. & cert. ef. 7-1-92
