 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 THIRD PARTY ADMINISTRATORS 
 836-075-0010 
 Completion of Application 
 
 The Director may reject a third party administrator license application if it has not been completed by the 90th day after its filing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244, 744.303, 744.635, 744.704, 744.706, 744.712 & 744.726 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 744.706 
 
 History:
