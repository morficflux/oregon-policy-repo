 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 SECURITIES TRANSACTIONS EXEMPT FROM REGISTRATION 
 441-035-0240 
 Waivers 
 
 Issuers may seek a waiver from the following requirements by submitting a request in writing demonstrating why a waiver should be granted and an analysis of why a waiver will not weaken the investor protection components of these rules. Issuers may seek waivers from the requirements of: 

 (1) The provisions under OAR 441-035-0080(6) (concerning number of employees); 

 (2) The provisions under OAR 441-035-0090(8) (meeting with a business technical service provider); 

 (3) Types of securities allowed under OAR 441-035-0090(9). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 59.035 & 17 CFR § 230.147A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 59.035
