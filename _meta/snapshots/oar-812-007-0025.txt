 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 LICENSING OF INDIVIDUALS AND FIRMS ENGAGED IN LEAD-BASED PAINT ACTIVITY 
 812-007-0025 
 Construction Contractors Board Lead-Based Paint Activities Fund 
 
 (1) Civil penalties imposed under ORS 701.995 will be deposited to the Construction Contractors Board LBP Activities Fund.  (2) The board will use the monies in the Construction Contractors Board LBP Activities Fund for lead poisoning prevention, including consumer and industry outreach, public education, and enforcement activities. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 701.235 & 701.515 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701.515, 701.520 & 701.995 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CCB 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 2-1-10
