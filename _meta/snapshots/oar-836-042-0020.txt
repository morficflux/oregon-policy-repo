 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 RATES AND RATEMAKING 
 836-042-0020 
 Insurers Must Demonstrate Statistical Reporting Ability 
 
 (1) Any insurer filing a workers’ compensation insurance exposure base, classification or classification system, must demonstrate how statistical experience data will be converted for purposes of statistical reporting in accordance with the uniform statistical plan prescribed by OAR 836-042-0045.  (2) Any filing by an insurer of a workers’ compensation insurance exposure base, classification or classification system which does not meet the requirements of section (1) of this rule shall be determined not to satisfy the requirements of ORS Chapter 737 and shall be disapproved. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 737.325(1)-(2), 737.325(4) & 737.230 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 IC 4-1982, f. 1-27-82, ef. 7-1-82
