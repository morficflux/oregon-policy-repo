 Real Estate Agency 
 Chapter 863 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REAL ESTATE MARKETING ORGANIZATION RULES 
 863-010-0660 
 Onsite Inspection of Nonresident Real Estate Marketing Organizations
 
 
 The Real Estate Commissioner may delegate any necessary onsite inspection of nonresident real estate marketing organizations to an agency of the state or foreign country in which the nonresident real estate marketing organization is located and which is found by the Commissioner to be the appropriate local regulatory agency. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Sec. 4(1), Ch. 217, OL 1995 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 696.627
