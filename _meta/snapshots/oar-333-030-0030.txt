 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 ORGANIZATIONAL CAMP RULES 
 333-030-0030 
 Required Fees
 
 
 Every applicant for an Organizational Camp license shall pay a fee of $100 unless another fee has been specified by the local public health authority. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.330 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.321
