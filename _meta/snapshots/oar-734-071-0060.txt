 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 LENGTHS OF VEHICLES, LOADS, AND COMBINATIONS OF VEHICLES IN OPERATION WITHOUT NEED OF SPECIAL PERMIT 
 734-071-0060 
 Pickup Truck Allowance
 
 
 A pickup truck may tow a single truck trailer that exceeds 40 feet in length, but does not exceed 53 feet in length if: 

 (1) The pickup truck is not carrying a load other than the towed vehicle; 

 (2) The coupling device for the trailer is a ball to socket hitch located above and not behind the tire tread of the rearmost axle of the pickup truck; 

 (3) The trailer has operating brakes on all wheels; and 

 (4) The load on the truck-trailer shall not extend past the rear of the trailer by more than five feet. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  184.619, 810.050 & 818.200 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 810.060
