 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 BUSINESS REGISTRY SECTION 
 160-010-0620 
 Signatures 
 
 (1) If the owner is a legal entity, the person signing the notice must be an officer of the entity and must identify the person's title.  (2) The signatures filed in the notice of intent will be electronic signatures, as provided in ORS Ch. 84. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 56.022 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2007 SB 684 
 
 History:
