 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 350 GENERAL GUARANTEE PROGRAM 
 813-350-0060 
 Fees 
 
 (1) A nonrefundable fee of $250.00 must be submitted with each request for a loan and/or lease guarantee.  (2) A lender, sponsor or leasing company receiving a loan or lease guarantee will be charged, and must pay, an annual fee of one percent of the guaranteed amount. Payment shall be remitted to the Department not less than annually as designated by the Department. All fees are nonrefundable.  (3) Line of credit guarantee fees will be based by the Department, and charged, on the total credit line.  (4) The Department may charge other fees, as determined necessary or appropriate by the Department to cover actual and anticipated costs of a guarantee including, without limitation, its issuance and its evaluation, or to reduce the financial risk to the Department of making a guarantee. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 456.555(2) & 456.625(12)(16) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.515 - 456.725 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OHCS 2-2003, f. & cert. ef. 5-1-03
