 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 CERTIFICATES, PERMITS, LICENSES AND DOCUMENTS 
 740-035-0250 
 Penalty for Failure to Participate 
 
 Failure to participate in the program as required by ORS 825.402 subjects a motor carrier to citation action as provided in 810.530 and civil action as provided in 825.950. For the purposes of 810.530, "requirements for attendance" has the same meaning as "participate in the program" described in 825.402 and OAR 740-035-0200. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 469, 823, 824 & 825 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.137
