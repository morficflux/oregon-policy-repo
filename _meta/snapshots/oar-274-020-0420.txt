 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 VETERANS’ LOANS 
 274-020-0420 
 Director’s Decisions Control in All Controversies 
 
 (1) The Director shall make all determinations as to the applicant’s eligibility for a loan.  (2) The Director shall make all determinations, based upon data and information in the file, as to whether the property offered is acceptable security and whether or not a loan shall be made.  (3) The Director’s decision shall be final in all matters pertaining to eligibility and the making of a loan. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 406.030, 407.115, 407.125 & 407.225
