 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 224 NEW SOURCE REVIEW 
 340-224-0500 
 Net Air Quality Benefit Emission Offsets: Net Air Quality Benefit for Sources Locating Within or Impacting Designated Areas 
 
 OAR 340-224-0510 through 340-224-0540 are the requirements for demonstrating net air quality benefit using offsets.  NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-020-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 468A.025, 468A.035, 468A.040, 468A.050, 468A.055 & 468A.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A 
 
 History:
