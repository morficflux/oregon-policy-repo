 Department of Public Safety Standards and Training 
 Chapter 259 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 61 INVESTIGATOR LICENSING STANDARDS AND REGULATIONS 
 259-061-0100 
 Exam Results 
 
 (1) The minimum passing score for the exam is 86%.  (2) The exam may be administered to an applicant no more then three consecutive times. If the applicant does not pass the exam on the third attempt, the applicant must wait one year from the date the last exam was taken to re-take the exam. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 703.415, 703.425, 703.430, 703.435, 703.445, 703.450, 703.460, 703.465 & 703.480 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 703.401 - 703.995 
 
 History:
