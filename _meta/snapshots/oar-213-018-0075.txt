 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 OFFENSE SUBCATEGORIES 
 213-018-0075 
 Extortion (ORS 164.075) 
 
 (1) CRIME CATEGORY 7: Extortion shall be ranked at Crime Category 7 if the offender threatened to cause physical injury to some person (ORS 164.075(1)(a)).  (2) CRIME CATEGORY 6: Extortion shall be ranked at Crime Category 6 if the offender threatened to report immigration status or suspected immigration status to law enforcement (ORS 164.075(1)(e)).  (3) OTHERWISE RATE AS A PROPERTY OFFENSE: Extortion shall be ranked as a Property Offense in Crime Categories 2, 3, 4, 5 or 6 as may be appropriate if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 7 or 6 as described above. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667 – 137.669 & HB 4128 (2016)
