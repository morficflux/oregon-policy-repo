 Appraiser Certification and Licensure Board 
 Chapter 161 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 520 REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS 
 161-520-0055 
 Change of Address 
 
 (1) An appraisal management company must maintain on file with the Board a current physical and mailing address, and notify the Board within thirty (30) calendar days of any change in either the physical or mailing address.  (2) A forwarding address is effective as a current mailing address when the Board receives notice of the forwarding address by the United States Postal Service. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.355, 674.305 & 674.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 674.305(7), 674.310(2), 674.205, 674.215, 674.230, 674.245 & 674.250
