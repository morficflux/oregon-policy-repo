 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 CONDITIONAL RELEASE OF ADULTS 
 859-070-0035 
 Out-of-State Conditional Release Order 
 
 The Board may consider and approve a conditional release plan to have the patient reside out of state. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.336 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PSRB 1-1985, f. 1-3-85, ef. 1-15-85
