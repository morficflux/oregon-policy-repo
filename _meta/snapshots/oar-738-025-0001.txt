 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 AIRPORT FUEL FACILITIES 
 738-025-0001 
 Purpose of Rule 
 
 The purpose of this rule is to establish a procedure for permitting nonretail facilities that dispense aviation fuels at airports. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 835.112 & 480.390 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.390
