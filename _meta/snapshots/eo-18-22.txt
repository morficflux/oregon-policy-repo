    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-22


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    STUBBLEFIELD AND OTHER FIRES IN GILLIAM AND WHEELER
    COUNTIES


    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:


    The fire known as the “Stubblefield Fire” is burning in Gilliam County, and a
    weather event started several other fires in Gilliam County and Wheeler County.


    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Stubblefield Fire
    and other fires in Gilliam and Wheeler Counties are beyond local capabilities.
    Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection was requested by Fire
    Chief Hinton and Gilliam County Fire Defense Board Chief Goff. The State Fire
    Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510 et seq., I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the Stubblefield Fire, and other
    fires in Gilliam and Wheeler Counties, and that the threat exceeds the firefighting
    capabilities of local firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have
    invoked the Emergency Conflagration Act.


    The findings related to the Stubblefield Fire were made at 2:58 p.m. on August 17,
    2018. The findings related to the additional fires in Gilliam and Wheeler Counties
    were made at 8:07 p.m. on August 19, 2018. I now confirm those findings with
    this Executive Order.


    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


    ie     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain these fires.
           Resources responding to the Stubblefield Fire may be redistributed to other
           fires burning in Gilliam and Wheeler Counties by the State Fire Marshal.


    2,     This emergency is declared for the Stubblefield Fire and other fires
           threatening structures in Gilliam and Wheeler Counties.
