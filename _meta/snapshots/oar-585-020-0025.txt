 Commission for the Blind 
 Chapter 585 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 EQUIPMENT POLICY 
 585-020-0025 
 Maintenance of Equipment
 
 
 (1) Commission may issue equipment for use by clients and provide for its maintenance while Commission retains ownership of the equipment. 

 (2) If available, service or maintenance agreements may be considered when appropriate, depending on the job environment and other related factors. Counselors should look first to purchase equipment which has at least a one-year warranty. 

 (3) Whenever available, used equipment in Commission’s inventory will be issued to clients. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 346.150 & ORS 346.180 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 346.180
