 Division 112 COUPLING DEVICES/HITCHES 
 735-112-0000 
 Vehicle Coupling Devices, Hitches and Safety Chains
 
 
 The Transportation Safety Office of the Department of Transportation adopts the Society of Automotive Engineers Standard, Number SAE J684f, relating to Automotive Type Trailer Coupling Devices, Hitches and Safety Chains.
