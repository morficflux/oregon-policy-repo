 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 300 GUN RELIEF PROGRAM 
 859-300-0200 
 Standard and Burden of Proof 
 
 (1) The standard of proof at the relief hearing shall be whether petitioner has demonstrated by clear and convincing evidence that the petitioner will not be likely to act in a manner that is dangerous to public safety and that granting the relief would not be contrary to the public interest.  (2) The burden of proof shall be on the petitioner. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387(1); OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.387(1); OL 2009 & Ch. 826 (HB 2853)
