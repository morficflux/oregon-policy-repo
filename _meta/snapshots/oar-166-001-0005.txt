 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 166-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The State Archivist adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, dated July 1, 2014.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Archives Division.]
