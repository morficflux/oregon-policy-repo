 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 61 REGIONAL INFRASTRUCTURE FUND 
 123-061-0010 
 Purpose
 
 
 These rules establish the criteria and process for selecting Projects to be funded by the Regional Infrastructure Fund. The Oregon Business Development Department administers the Regional Infrastructure Fund for the purpose of providing Grants and Loans to Local Governments and Tribes for infrastructure Projects including long-range planning, research and design. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 & OL 2013 c.786 §3 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285B.551 & OL 2013 c.786 §3
