 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 CULTURAL PARTICIPATION GRANTS 
 123-500-0160 
 Distribution of Funds 
 
 The Arts Program, under the direction of the Cultural Trust Board, shall distribute the amount disbursed from the Trust for Cultural Development Account under ORS 359.426(3)(c) to the core partner agencies as follows:  (1) 20 percent of the amount disbursed under ORS 359.426 (3)(c) for joint efforts by the core partner agencies in fostering cooperative cultural projects, including but not limited to cultural education, cultural tourism and other cultural activities; and  (2) 80 percent of the amount disbursed under ORS 359.426(3)(c) to the core partner agencies for the purposes described in 359.444. The Cultural Trust Board shall determine the amount or percent of available funds that each core partner agency shall receive under this paragraph. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 359.416 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 359.400 - 359.444 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OBDD 6-2010, f. 2-25-10, cert. ef. 3-1-10
