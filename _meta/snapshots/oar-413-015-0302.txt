 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 INTRODUCTION TO CPS RULES 
 413-015-0302 
 Purpose of Cross Reporting
 
 
 The purpose of the cross report is to share reports of alleged abuse between Child Welfare and law enforcement agencies. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005, ORS 419B.017 & ORS 409.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005, ORS 419B.017 & ORS 419B.015
