 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 205 WATER SUPPLY WELL CONSTRUCTION: STANDARDS AND LICENSING 
 690-205-0035 
 Continuing Education Committee 
 
 A Continuing Education Program and Continuing Education Committee are established under Chapter 496, Oregon Laws 2001 (ORS 537.765). The duties of the Well Constructors Continuing Education Committee are to review and approve continuing education courses and assign continuing education credits. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795
