 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 PUBLIC HEALTH PREPAREDNESS 
 333-003-0100 
 Volunteer Registry: Scope
 
 
 The rules in OAR 333-003-0100 to 333-003-0210 pertain to the registration and deployment of health care providers and support and administrative personnel to provide health care, public health and supportive services and to the designation of emergency health care centers under ORS 401.651 to 401.670 during a declared emergency. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 401.651 - 401.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 401.651 - 401.670
