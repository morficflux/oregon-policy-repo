 Occupational Therapy Licensing Board 
 Chapter 339 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 IMPLEMENTATION AND ADMINISTRATION OF THE OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY PRACTICE ACT 
 339-010-0023 
 License Renewals
 
 
 Each applicant for license renewal shall submit to the Board on or before May 31 of each even numbered year, a completed license renewal application and appropriate renewal fee. The renewal fees are non-refundable. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.320(10)(11) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.336 & 675.337
