 Secretary of State 
 Audits Division - Chapter 162 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 MINIMUM STANDARDS FOR AUDITS OF OREGON MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS 
 162-010-0300 
 Investments 
 
 Compliance with legal requirements, (as contained in ORS Chapter 294), pertaining to the investment of public funds shall be disclosed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 297.465 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 297.465
