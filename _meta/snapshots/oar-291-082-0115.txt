 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 82 ASSESSMENT, ASSIGNMENT, AND SUPERVISION OF ADULTS IN CUSTODY FOR WORK ASSIGNMENTS AND UNFENCED MINIMUM HOUSING 
 291-082-0115 
 Initial Work Housing Assignment Level Evaluation (WHALE) 
 
 (1) A WHALE shall be completed on all inmates who have been classified as Level 1 or Level 2 in order for the inmate to be considered for a community work assignment or for housing in an unfenced facility.  (2) The assigned counselor shall initiate the automated WHALE, review for accuracy of information imported from the CIS, and enter any additional information gleaned from file review or other sources.  (3) The WHALE shall be forwarded to the Correctional Rehabilitation Manager for approval. An evaluation is not considered official until approved by the Correctional Rehabilitation Manager or designee.  (4) All approved WHALE actions are historically recorded and maintained in the CIS system. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 421.445, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 421.425, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
