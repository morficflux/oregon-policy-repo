 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 MINIMUM STANDARDS FOR AIRPORTS 
 738-020-0045 
 Hearings 
 
 The procedure for hearings and appeals in connection with the grant, denial, or revocation of a site approval or license shall be as provided in The Attorney General's Model Rules of Practice and Procedure under the Administrative Procedure Act, and ORS Chapter 183.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the Office of the Attorney general or the Department of Aviation.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 835.035, 836.100, 836.110 & 836.115 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.430, 183.435, 836.100, 836.110 & 836.115
