 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 PUBLIC REAL PROPERTY NEAR RAIL INFRASTRUCTURE NOTIFICATION 
 741-040-0030 
 Notification Required 
 
 Unless an exception applies pursuant to OAR 741-040-0040, at least 30 days before listing or placing any Real Property for sale, exchange or conveyance, a Political subdivision shall notify the Rail Division Administrator of its intent to undertake such listing or placement if the Real Property is within:  (1) 100 feet of a Railroad Right of Way;  (2) 500 feet of an At-Grade crossing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & Ch. 446 OL 2011 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 271.310 
 
 History:
