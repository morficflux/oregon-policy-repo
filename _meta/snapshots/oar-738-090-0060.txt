 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 AIRPORT LISTINGS 
 738-090-0060 
 Removing an Airport from Listings 
 
 Airports shall be removed from the listing of airports established in OAR 738-090-0030 only upon:  (1) Request of the airport owner; or  (2) Closure of the airport for a period of more than three years. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 836.610 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 836.610 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AERO 1-1999, f. & cert. ef. 3-25-99
