 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 113 UNIVERSAL WASTE MANAGEMENT 
 340-113-0010 
 Applicability 
 
 In addition to provisions under 40 CFR 273.1, the following wastes are subject to universal waste management standards but are not “Universal Wastes” as defined in OAR 340-113-0020(4): Waste pesticides as defined in OAR 340-109-0010(2)(a), and pesticide residues as defined in OAR 340-100-0010, that are collected and managed as part of any pesticide collection program that has notified the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.325 - 183.335, 465.009, 466.020 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.015, 466.020 & 466.075
