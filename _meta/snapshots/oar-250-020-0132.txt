 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 LOCAL AND SPECIAL RULES 
 250-020-0132 
 Boat Operations in Harney County
 
 
 (1) Burns Gravel Pond (adjacent to Highway 78): A person must not operate a motorboat in excess of a slow-no wake speed. 

 (2) Delintment Lake: A person must not operate a motorboat in excess of a slow-no wake speed. 

 (3) Fish Lake: A person must not use a motor to propel a boat. 

 (4) Krumbo Reservoir: A person must not use an internal combustion engine to propel a boat. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830.110 & ORS 830.175 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.110, ORS 830.175 & ORS 830.195
