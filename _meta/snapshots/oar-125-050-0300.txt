 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 STATE SURPLUS PROPERTY 
 125-050-0300 
 Eligibility of State Agencies, Political Subdivisions and Non-Profit Organizations 
 
 Prior to offering Surplus Property for public sale, the State Surplus Property Program must make Surplus Property available to the following:  (1) State Agencies;  (2) Political subdivisions of the State; and  (3) Any non-profit organization qualified to acquire federal donation property pursuant to OAR 125-035-0045 or determined by the Department to be eligible under criteria established by the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 283.060, 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279A.260
