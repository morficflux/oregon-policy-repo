 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 IMPLIED CONSENT HEARING PROCEDURES 
 735-090-0051 
 Subpoena of Witness 
 
 Under no circumstance will DMV or OAH compel a witness to attend and testify at a hearing that determines the validity of a suspension of driving privileges unless the witness is served with a subpoena at least 72 hours prior to the time of the hearing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341, 184.616, 184.619, 802.010, 813.410 & 813.440 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 813.410 & 813.440
