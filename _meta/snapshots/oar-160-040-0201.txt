 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE — ARTICLE 9 
 160-040-0201 
 Time for Filing a Continuation Statement 
 
 (1) First day permitted. The first day on which a continuation statement may be filed is the date six months before the lapse date of the current financing statement.  (2) Last day permitted. The last day on which a continuation statement may be filed is the date upon which the related financing statement lapses. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 79.0526 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 79.0515
