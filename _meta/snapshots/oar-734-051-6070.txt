 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 HIGHWAY APPROACHES, ACCESS CONTROL, SPACING STANDARDS AND MEDIANS 
 734-051-6070 
 Delegation 
 
 (1) For OAR 734-051-6010 through 734-051-6070, the director delegates authority to the department’s right of way manager or the manager’s designee to:  (a) Determine the department’s offer of remedies, and  (b) Agree to any settlement that includes providing administrative remedies.  (2) The actions in section (1) of this rule must occur prior to the final order in a contested case conducted under OAR 734-051-3110. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 374.310–374.314, 374.345 & 374.355 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 374.300–374.360, §27, ch. 330 & OL 2011
