 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 256 MOTOR VEHICLES 
 340-256-0220 
 Compliance With Oregon Low Emission Vehicle Program
 
 
 Model years 2009 and newer vehicles that have 7,500 or fewer miles must meet the requirements of Chapter 340, Division 257 to qualify for a Certificate of Compliance. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 468A.010, 468A.015, 468A.025, 468A.360 & 468A.380 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468.020 & 468A.365 
 
 History:
