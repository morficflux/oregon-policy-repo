 Oregon Racing Commission 
 Chapter 462 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 PARI-MUTUEL RULES AND REGULATIONS 
 462-200-0010 
 Pari-Mutuel Rules and Regulations 
 
 All rules of pari-mutuel wagering herein contained (OAR 462-200-0010 through 462-200-0620) shall apply to horse races and greyhound races alike. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 462.270(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 462.270(3) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
