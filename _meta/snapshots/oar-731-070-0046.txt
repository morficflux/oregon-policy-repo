 Department of Transportation 
 Chapter 731 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 OREGON INNOVATIVE PARTNERSHIPS PROGRAM 
 731-070-0046 
 Solicitation Documents
 
 
 (1) In a solicitation for proposals, ODOT will specify requirements for proposal content, and for criteria and procedures used to evaluate selected proposals. These requirements, criteria and procedures will comply with the requirements of ORS 367.800 to 367.826. 

 (2) Nothing in this rule is intended to limit the scope of ODOT’s discretion or authority to develop proposal and evaluation criteria and processes for any project as long as those criteria and processes comply with the requirements of ORS 367.800 to 367.826. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 367.824 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 367.800 - 367.824
