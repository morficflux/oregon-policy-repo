 Department of State Police 
 Chapter 257 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 ALCOHOL BREATH TESTING 
 257-030-0120 
 Approved Breath Testing Equipment 
 
 The following breath testing equipment is approved under OAR 257-030-0110 for performing chemical analysis of a person’s breath: The Intoxilyzer 8000: Correlation studies performed with the Intoxilyzer 8000 by Oregon State Police technician(s) are incorporated as Appendix 2: Studies Performed with the Intoxilyzer® 8000.  [ED. NOTE: Appendices referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335 & 813.160 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 813.160
