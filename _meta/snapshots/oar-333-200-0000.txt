 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES AND SYSTEMS 
 333-200-0000 
 Purpose
 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to establish the procedures and standards for the development and maintenance of a comprehensive statewide trauma system as set forth under ORS 431A.050 through 431A.105. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431A.050 - 431A.105
