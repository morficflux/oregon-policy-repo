 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 SUBSTITUTE CARE — FUNDING ELIGIBILITY 
 413-100-0000 
 Purpose
 
 
 The purpose of these rules, OAR 413-100-0000 to 413-100-0345, is to describe the Department's responsibilities and criteria for making the following determinations, which are used to ensure proper federal reimbursement: 

 (1) Title IV-E eligibility for children and young adults whose placement and care are the responsibility of the Department or another public agency with which the Department has made an agreement which is in effect; 

 (2) Title IV-E eligibility for children who are candidates for foster care; 

 (3) Title IV-E eligibility for pregnant or parenting foster youth;  

 (4) Adoption Assistance Title IV-E eligibility; and 

 (5) Guardianship Assistance Title IV-E eligibility. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.010, ORS 409.050 & ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.010, ORS 409.050 & ORS 418.005
