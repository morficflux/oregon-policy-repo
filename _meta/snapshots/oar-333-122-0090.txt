 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 122 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR INDUSTRIAL X-RAY MACHINE OPERATIONS 
 333-122-0090 
 General Requirements for All RGD: Generator Cabinet 
 
 Each X-ray generator shall be supplied with a protective cabinet which limits leakage radiation measured at a distance of five centimeters from its surface such that it is not capable of producing a dose exceeding 0.25 millirem (2.5 µSv) in one hour. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807
