 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 EARLY HEARING DETECTION AND INTERVENTION AND CONGENITAL CYTOMEGALOVIRUS SCREENING 
 333-020-0130 
 Facility Requirements to Provide Hearing Screenings in Newborns
 
 
 (1) In all mandated facilities, each newborn shall receive a hearing screening, consistent with the Hearing Screening Protocol, prior to discharge from the facility. 

 (2) A mandated facility may not refuse to conduct a hearing screening due to an inability of the parent or guardian to pay for the procedure. 

 (3) In all non-mandated facilities where a hearing screening is not performed, each newborn’s parent or guardian shall be provided with information furnished by the Oregon Health Authority (Authority) including, but not limited to: 

 (a) A statement indicating that newborn hearing screening is important to determine the presence or absence of hearing loss and is considered standard of care; and 

 (b) A list of Authority recommended screening facility locations and contact information. 

 (4) A non-mandated facility that chooses to conduct hearing screenings must follow guidelines and follow-up requirements established for a mandated facility identified in these rules. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 433.321 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 433.321
