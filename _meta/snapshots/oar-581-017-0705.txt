 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0705 
 Latino/a/x and Indigenous Student Success Plan Grant Program: Funding 
 
 (1) Applicants must submit a detailed budget that explains how Grant funds will be used. 

 (2) Administrative costs, which include indirect costs, will be allowed as a percentage of the Grant funds disbursed as follows: 

 (a) Up to five percent for school districts, educational service districts, early learning hubs, or post-secondary institutions of education; or 

 (b) Up to fifteen percent for Community-Based Organizations, Tribes, or providers of early learning services. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 329.845 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329.845
