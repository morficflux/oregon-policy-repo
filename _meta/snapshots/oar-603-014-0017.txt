 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 LIVESTOCK IDENTIFICATION AND THEFT PREVENTION 
 603-014-0017 
 Location of Herd or Breed Identification Markings 
 
 As provided by ORS 604.021(2), the following areas and markings may be used for herd or breed identification:  (1) The gaskins of cattle, on either side, may be used for herd identification markings. Such identification markings shall consist of three letters or figures, or any combination thereof, and may be placed either vertically or horizontally. No designs or connected figures may be used.  (2) The necks of horses, on either side, may be used for breed association identification markings. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 604 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 604.021
