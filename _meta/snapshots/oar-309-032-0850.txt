 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 32 COMMUNITY TREATMENT AND SUPPORT SERVICES 
 309-032-0850 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules prescribe standards and procedures for regional acute care psychiatric services for adults. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & 430.640 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 430.630 & 430.640
