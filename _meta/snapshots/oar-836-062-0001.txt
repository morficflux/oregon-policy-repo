 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 62 VENDOR’S SINGLE INTEREST POLICIES AND MOTOR VEHICLE PHYSICAL DAMAGE ONLY POLICIES 
 836-062-0001 
 Statutory Authority; Effective Date 
 
 (1) OAR 836-062-0001 to 836-062-0010 are adopted pursuant to the general rulemaking authority of the Director under ORS 731.244.  (2) Not later than December 15, 1989, each insurer shall file with the Director a listing of all policy forms that comply with OAR 836-062-0005 or 836-062-0010. Any form to which 836-062-0005 or 836-062-0100 applies but does not comply with either rule becomes disapproved for use in Oregon and may not be used after that date unless it has been resubmitted to and approved by the Director. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 742.005(2), 742.023(1)(f) & 746.240 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ID 9-1989, f. & cert. ef. 10-23-89
