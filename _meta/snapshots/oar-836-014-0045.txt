 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 ALTERNATIVE INSURANCE ORGANIZATIONS 
 836-014-0045 
 Unfair Trade Practice 
 
 Failure of a legal service organization to comply with OAR 836-014-0001 to 836-014-0045 is an unfair trade practice under ORS 746.240. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 & 750 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 750.240 & 750.325(3) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ID 4-1990, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-90
