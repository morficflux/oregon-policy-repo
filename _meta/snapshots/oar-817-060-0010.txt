 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office, Board of Cosmetology - Chapter 817 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 CHEMICAL USE AND STORAGE 
 817-060-0010 
 Client Protection 
 
 (1) All safety procedures which prevent eye, nail, hair, or skin injury to clients or damage to the clothing of clients must be followed when administering chemical services.  (2) Client protection regulations must be met in accordance with ORS 654, OAR 437-division 2, General Occupational Safety and Health Rules, 29 CFR 1910.1030, and 29 CFR 1910.1200. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 690.165 & 690.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654, 690.165 & 690.205
