 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 RECYCLED WATER USE 
 340-055-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules (OAR 340-055-0005 to 340-055-0030) prescribe requirements for the use of recycled water for beneficial purposes. The purpose of this division is to protect the environment and public health in the State of Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 468.705 & 468.710 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468B.015 & 468B.020
