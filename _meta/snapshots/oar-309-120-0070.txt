 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 PATIENT TRANSFERS 
 309-120-0070 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules prescribe procedures by which offenders in Oregon Youth Authority (OYA) close custody facilities may be transferred to a state mental hospital or a facility designated by the Addictions and Mental Health Division for evaluation and treatment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 179.473, 420.500 & 420.505 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 179.473, 420.500 & 420.505
