 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 66 LEAVE REVOCATION HEARINGS 
 291-066-0270 
 Continuance on Short-Term Transitional or Nonprison Leave 
 
 (1) The Releasing Authority may continue an AIC on short-term transitional or nonprison leave and order modification of sanction time to be served. 

 (2) The Releasing Authority may continue an AIC on short-term transitional or nonprison leave and order administrative sanctions. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075, 421.168 & 421.500 to 421.512 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075, 421.168 & 421.500 to 421.512
