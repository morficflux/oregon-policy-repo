 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 TRADE PRACTICES 
 836-080-0335 
 Gifts 
 
 (1) A title company may give a plant or flowers to an intermediary in connection with an open house of a new or substantially remodeled office of the intermediary if the net cost to the title company is $50 or less.  (2) A title company may give a thing of value to an intermediary as a condolence if the net cost to the title company is $50 or less. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 746.045, 746.055, 746.160 & 746.240
