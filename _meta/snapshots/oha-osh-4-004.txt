 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. The Oregon State Hospital (OSH) Patient Benevolent Fund (the Fund) is an
      administrative trust fund intended to benefit patients with no other resources.
      This policy establishes eligibility and requirements regarding the Fund.
   B. This policy applies to all staff.

II. POLICY
   A. The Fund may only be used:
      1. for direct patient benefit, and
      2. for individual patients, and
      3. when all other resources are exhausted, and
      4. for personal needs that are not a direct treatment expense.
   B. Exceptions may be made for a patient who is able to work or has a higher
      resource level with the expectation that the patient will repay the Fund.
   C. To maintain the Fund, payments may not exceed the interest the Fund
      generates.
   D. The Business Office must provide a quarterly report of the Fund amount and
      funds expended in the quarter to the Fund Administrator.
   E. Staff must follow Procedures A and other instructions provided by Volunteer
      Services to process the Fund requests.
