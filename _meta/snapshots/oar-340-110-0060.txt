 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 POLYCHLORINATED BIPHENYLS (PCBS) 
 340-110-0060 
 Treatment and Disposal 
 
 (1) Sections (2)–(4) of this rule are added to the provisions of 40 CFR 761.60(a).  (2) “PCB disposal facility” includes a facility for treatment or disposal of PCB or PCB items.  (3) No person shall treat or dispose of PCB or PCB items except at a PCB disposal facility permitted by the Department.  (4) No person shall establish, construct or operate a PCB disposal facility without a permit issued by the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 459 & 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.025, 466.030 & 466.255
