 Oregon Board of Naturopathic Medicine 
 Chapter 850 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 GENERAL 
 850-010-0170 
 State Welfare Cases 
 
 Naturopathic physicians may accept welfare cases under the medical plan adopted by the Welfare Commission, April 26, 1946. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 685 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 NE 2, f. 6-7-59
