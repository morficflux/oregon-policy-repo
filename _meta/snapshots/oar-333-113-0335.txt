 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 113 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR WIRELINE SERVICE OPERATIONS AND SUB-SURFACE TRACER STUDIES 
 333-113-0335 
 Precautionary Procedures in Logging and Subsurface Tracer Studies: Tritium Neutron Generator Target Source 
 
 (1) Use of a tritium neutron generator target source, containing quantities not exceeding 1,110 GBq (30 curies) and in a well with a surface casing to protect fresh water aquifers, is subject to the requirements of this division except 333-113-0010, 333-113-0135 and 333-113-0501.  (2) Use of a tritium neutron generator target source, containing quantities exceeding 1,110 GBq (30 curies) or in a well without a surface casing to protect fresh water aquifers, is subject to the requirements of this division except 333-113-0135. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
