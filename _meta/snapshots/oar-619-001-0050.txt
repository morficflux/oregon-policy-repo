 Oregon Wine Board 
 Chapter 619 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 619-001-0050 
 Development of a Draft Annual Budget 
 
 (1) Each fiscal year, the Oregon Wine Board will prepare a draft annual budget for the next fiscal year.  (2) The draft annual budget will itemize the anticipated expenditures for each element of the corresponding draft annual plans, as described above in 619-001-0040, and will use classifications of expenditures and revenues required by ORS 291.206(1). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576.766(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576.768(2)(b) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OWB 1-2005, f. 12-5-05, cert. ef. 12-15-05
