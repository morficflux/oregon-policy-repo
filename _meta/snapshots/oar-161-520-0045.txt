 Appraiser Certification and Licensure Board 
 Chapter 161 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 520 REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS 
 161-520-0045 
 Change in Business Name 
 
 An appraisal management company must submit to the Board a notice of business name change within thirty (30) calendar days of the change. Such notification shall be in writing on a form prescribed by the Board along with the following:  (1) A corrected surety bond as required by ORS 674.210;  (2) A copy of the Secretary of State business registration with the company’s new name; and  (3) Applicable fees as established in OAR 161-510-0010. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.355, 674.305 & 674.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 674.305(7), 674.310(2), 674.205, 674.215, 674.230, 674.245 & 674.250
