 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 76 SPECIFIC STANDARDS FOR THE SITING OF NUCLEAR POWER FACILITIES IN OREGON 
 345-076-0050 
 Federal Waste Depository 
 
 To issue a site certificate for a nuclear power facility, the Council must find that there is an adequate federal repository for the disposal of high-level radioactive waste as described in ORS 469.595. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.300 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 EFSC 1-2007, f. & cert. ef. 5-15-07
