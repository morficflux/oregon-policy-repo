 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 536 IN-HOME CARE AGENCIES 
 333-536-0015 
 Review of License Application
 
 
 In reviewing an application for an agency or subunit license, the Authority shall: 

 (1) Confirm receipt of required fee and determine that the initial application is complete; 

 (2) Conduct a survey in accordance with OAR 333-536-0041; and 

 (3) Assess compliance with these rules and ORS 443.305 through 443.355. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 443.340 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 443.315 & 443.340
