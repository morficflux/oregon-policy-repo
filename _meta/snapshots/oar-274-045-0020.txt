 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 POST VIETNAM ERA VETERANS’ HOME LOAN PROGRAM 
 274-045-0020 
 Who May Apply for Loan 
 
 (1) A loan shall be made only to an individual veteran.  (2) Joint loans or loans to a cooperative shall not be valid. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 407 
 
 History:
