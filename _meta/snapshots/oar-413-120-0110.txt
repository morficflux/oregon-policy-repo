 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 ADOPTION 
 413-120-0110 
 Values 
 
 (1) Creating a legal child-parent relationship is the final goal of adoption.  (2) Achieving permanency for the child is important. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  HB 2004 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 109.307, 109.309 & 109.316 
 
 History:
