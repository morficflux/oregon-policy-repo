 Oregon 529 Savings Board 
 Chapter 173 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 OPENING AN ACCOUNT; APPLICATION; PARTICIPATION AGREEMENT; GENERAL ADMINISTRATION 
 173-008-0000 
 Opening an Account 
 
 (1) For all Network Plans, to open an account, an applicant must complete the appropriate plan application, and designate a contribution method as required by the appropriate plan.  (2) For all Network Plans, an applicant must select from the investment options offered by a plan. After an account has been opened, the account owner may change the investment options selected for the account as permitted by the Code. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.853(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.857(1), 348.860(1) & 348.853(2)
