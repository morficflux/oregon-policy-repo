 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 HUMAN IMMUNODEFICIENCY VIRUS 
 333-022-1160 
 CareAssist: Termination from CAREAssist 
 
 (1) The Authority may terminate a client or restrict benefits for any of the following:  (a) Failure to continue to meet eligibility requirements;  (b) Submitting false, fraudulent or misleading information to the Authority in order to obtain or retain benefits;  (c) Placement in a custodial institution, such as a state or federal prison, that is legally obligated to provide medical services; or  (d) Failure to notify the Authority of changes in accordance with OAR 333-022-1100.  (2) The Authority must provide a notice of termination to a client in writing in accordance with ORS 183.415.  (3) An individual who is found to have provided false, fraudulent or misleading information to the Authority may not reapply for CAREAssist benefits for six months following the issuance of a final order of termination and may be required to repay the Authority for benefits provided. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042, 431.250 & 431.830 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431.250 & 431.830 
 
 History:
