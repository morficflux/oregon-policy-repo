 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 MEDICAL MARIJUANA 
 333-008-0595 
 Transfers from Commission Licensees 
 
 (1) PRMGs, patients, and designated primary caregivers may receive immature marijuana plants from a Commission licensed marijuana producer registered under ORS 475C.137 in accordance with OAR chapter 845, division 25. 

 (2) Any transfer under this rule is subject to: 

 (a) Grow site plant limitations under OAR 333-008-0025, 333-008-0520 to 333-008-0560; and 

 (b) Possession limits under OAR 333-008-0080. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.137 & ORS 475C.919 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.137
