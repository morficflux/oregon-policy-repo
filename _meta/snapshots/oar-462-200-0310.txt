 Oregon Racing Commission 
 Chapter 462 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 PARI-MUTUEL RULES AND REGULATIONS 
 462-200-0310 
 Off-Track Wagering; Authorized; Executive Director’s Authority 
 
 (1) The Oregon Racing Commission authorizes the conducting of off-track wagering which meets the criteria set forth in these rules.  (2) Except as otherwise directed by the commission the executive director is hereby authorized to approve the selection, the operation of off-track facilities, and to approve contractual agreements which comply with the requirements of the commission for the conduct of off-track wagering. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 462.700 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 462.700 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
