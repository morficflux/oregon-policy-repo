 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0291 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of the Career Pathway Grant is to fund activities related to career and technical education programs of study that are pathways to high wage high demand jobs. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2015 OL Ch. 763 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3072) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2015 OL Ch. 763 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3072) 
 
 History:
