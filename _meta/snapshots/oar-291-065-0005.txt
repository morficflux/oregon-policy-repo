 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 PAROLE AND PROBATION OFFICER DUTIES 
 291-065-0005 
 Authority and Purpose 
 
 (1) Authority: The authority for this rule is granted to the Director of the Department of Corrections in accordance with ORS 137.610, 137.630, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 and 423.075.  (2) Purpose: The purpose of this rule is to specify the duties of parole and probation officers of Department of Corrections, as required by ORS 137.630 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.610, 137.630, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.610, 137.630, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
