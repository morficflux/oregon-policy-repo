 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ELECTRICAL PROGRAM MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-308-0430 
 Appeal of Program Revocation 
 
 A municipality served with a notice of proposed program revocation under ORS 479.855 may ask for a contested case hearing under ORS Chapter 183 and the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.855 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.855
