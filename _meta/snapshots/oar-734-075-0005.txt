 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 MOVEMENT OF OVER-DIMENSION MOBILE HOMES AND MODULAR BUILDING UNITS 
 734-075-0005 
 Scope 
 
 OAR Chapter 734, Division 75 regulates the transportation of mobile homes, modular building units and chassis units on highways under the authority of the Oregon Department of Transportation. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 810.606, 184.619 & 823.011 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.200 & 818.220
