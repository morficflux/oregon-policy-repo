 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 CONSTRUCTION CARVE-OUT PROGRAMS 
 436-140-0006 
 Administration of Rules 
 
 Any orders issued by the division in carrying out the director's authority under ORS Chapter 656 and these rules are considered orders of the director. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) & 656.174 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.170, 656.172 & 656.174 
 
 History:
