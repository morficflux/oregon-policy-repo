 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 ELDERLY & DISABLED HOUSING PROGRAM 
 813-030-0031 
 Standard Underwriting Criteria for Program Loans and Program Contributions
 
 
 The Department will underwrite each program loan pursuant to underwriting criteria that shall be established by the Department and updated from time to time in accordance with acceptable risk tolerance criteria for a direct lending program operated by the State of Oregon. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 90.800 - 90.840, 91.886, 183.335, 456.515 - 456.723 & 458.210 - 458.650 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  456.515 - 456.723
