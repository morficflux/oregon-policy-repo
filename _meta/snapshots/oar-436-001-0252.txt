 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES, ATTORNEY FEES, AND GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 436-001-0252 
 Stay of Director and Administrative Review
 
 
 The director may stay director review, administrative review, or referral of a matter if there is another pending matter concerning a claim that may affect the outcome of the matter within the director’s jurisdiction. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.704
