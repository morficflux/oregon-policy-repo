 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-6745 
 Chum 
 
 (1) Transition Period (2013–January 31, 2017).  (a) Assign commercial fisheries a sufficient share of the ESA-impact for chum to implement off-channel and mainstem fisheries targeting other salmon species.  (b) Prohibit the retention of chum salmon in recreational and commercial fisheries.  (2) Long Term (February 1, 2017 and Beyond).  (a) Assign commercial fisheries a sufficient share of the ESA-impact for chum to implement off-channel and mainstem fisheries targeting other salmon species.  (b) Prohibit the retention of chum salmon in recreational and commercial fisheries. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & 506.129
