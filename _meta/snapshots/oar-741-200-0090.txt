 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 APPLICATIONS 
 741-200-0090 
 Expiration of Authority to Construct Crossings 
 
 Authority to construct a crossing expires two years from the effective date of the Order granting such authority unless the authorized construction is substantially in progress within that period or within such additional period as may have been provided by Order of the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011, 824.202 & 824.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 824.204
