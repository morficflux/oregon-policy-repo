 Department of Human Services 
 Vocational Rehabilitation Services - Chapter 582 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND REVIEW OF DETERMINATIONS OF REHABILITATION SERVICES 
 582-020-0025 
 Notice of Action and Notice of Procedural Rights 
 
 The Notice of Proposed Action must include the following: 

 (1) The date the Notice of Proposed Action is mailed; 

 (2) A short and plain statement about the action the Program intends to take, including citations to the regulations, rules, statutes or policies involved; 

 (3) Statement about the reason for the action; 

 (4) Statement indicating whether and under what circumstances an order by default may be entered; 

 (5) A description of the procedure and time to request a hearing. 

 (6) Statement of the authority of the Program; and, 

 (7) The information provided in the Vocational Rehabilitation Dispute Resolution Rights. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 344.530 & 344.590 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 344.511 ‐ 344.690
