 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 ATTORNEY FEES 
 438-015-0030 
 Attorney Fees When There is No Hearing 
 
 If an attorney is instrumental in obtaining compensation for a claimant without a hearing before an Administrative Law Judge, a reasonable attorney fee may be approved or assessed. The amount of the fee shall be determined by an Administrative Law Judge or by agreement of the parties. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.386(1)(2)
