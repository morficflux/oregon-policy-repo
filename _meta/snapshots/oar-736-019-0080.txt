 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 19 LAND ACQUISITION AND EXCHANGE 
 736-019-0080 
 Sources of Funding 
 
 The Department will:  (1) Purchase lands with appropriations granted by the Legislature.  (2) Use proceeds from land sales to purchase other lands pursuant to ORS 390.121(3).  (3) Accept donated private funds and donated lands.  (4) Seek state, federal and private grants for land purchases when appropriate.  (5) Employ land exchanges when the land traded away is less suitable for open space and recreation than the land received. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.121 & 390.124 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.112 & 390.121
