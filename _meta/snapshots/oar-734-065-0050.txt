 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 ADVERTISING SIGNS ATTACHED TO TRANSIT SHELTERS OR BENCHES 
 734-065-0050 
 Removal 
 
 All signs granted permits under these rules are subject to removal procedures in accordance with ORS 377.775. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 377.729 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 377.725
