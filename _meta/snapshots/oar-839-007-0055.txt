 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 OREGON SICK TIME 
 839-007-0055 
 Substantial Equivalency
 
 
 An employer’s own sick leave, paid vacation, paid personal time off, or other paid time off policy is substantially equivalent to sick time required under ORS 653.601 to 653.661 when such a policy provides for at least the same number of sick time hours an employee would earn under ORS 653.606 and complies with all other minimum requirements as listed in ORS 653.601 to 653.661. These requirements include but are not limited to provisions related to when employees can use sick time; the rate of accrual; the regular rate of pay; qualifying absences; conditions of notice and documentation; and employment protections. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 653.601-653.661 & ORS 651 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 653.601-653.661 & OL Ch. 520 (2017)
