 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 GENERAL LICENSING REQUIREMENTS 
 918-030-0000 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 OAR 918, division 30 establishes the general licensing requirements and procedures for individuals and businesses licensed by the appropriate board or by Building Codes Division. These rules apply to individuals and businesses required to be licensed under provisions of ORS chapters 447.110 to 447.156, 460.005 to 460.175, 479.510 to 479.945, 480.510 to 480.670 and 693. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.117 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.335 & 455.117
