 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 SHELLFISH SANITATION 
 603-100-0040 
 Growers and Harvesters 
 
 (1) Growers and Harvesters must deliver shellfish intended for human consumption to a shellfish shipper certified by the Oregon Department of Agriculture within 24 hours of harvest.  (2) Growers and Harvesters may only sell shellfish intended for human consumption to shellfish shippers certified by the Oregon Department of Agriculture. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & 622.180 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 622.180 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOA 1-2007, f. & cert. ef. 1-2-07
