 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS 
 635-065-0101 
 Buck Deer Seasons
 
 
 (1) A person hunting deer during the general buck season in the open area listed under the western Oregon deer season described in OAR 635, division 068 shall have on their person a General Any Legal Weapon Western Oregon Tag. 

 (2) A person hunting in a season authorized by a controlled hunt tag shall have on their person the deer tag required for that area. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
