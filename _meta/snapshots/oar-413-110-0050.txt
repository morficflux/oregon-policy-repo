 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 PRE-ADOPTION SERVICES 
 413-110-0050 
 Effect on a Release and Surrender 
 
 A Legal Risk Placement shall be considered a placement for the “purpose of adoption” in accordance with ORS 418.270, subsection (4), which specifies when a release and surrender given by a parent or guardian shall be considered irrevocable. These rules shall apply to any release and surrender accepted prior to, concurrent with or subsequent to the Legal Risk Placement. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005
