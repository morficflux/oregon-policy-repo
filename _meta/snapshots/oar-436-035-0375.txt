 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 DISABILITY RATING STANDARDS 
 436-035-0375 
 Abdomen 
 
 Use the following classifications when impairment has resulted from a permanent and palpable defect in the supporting structures of the abdominal wall:  (1) Class 1: 5% for a slight protrusion at the site of the defect with increased abdominal pressure that is readily reducible; or occasional mild discomfort at the site of the defect, which limits the worker in one or more activities of daily living (ADL).  (2) Class 2: 15% for frequent or persistent protrusion at the site of the defect with increased pressure that is manually reducible; or frequent discomfort, which limits the worker from heavy lifting, but does not hamper some ADL.  (3) Class 3: 25% for persistent, irreducible, or irreparable protrusion at the site of the defect and there is a limitation in the worker’s ADL. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.005, 656.214, 656.268 & 656.726
