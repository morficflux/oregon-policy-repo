 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 103 HAZARDOUS WASTE MANAGEMENT 
 340-103-0030 
 Immediate Action 
 
 In addition to the requirements of 40 CFR 263.30, a transporter who has discharged hazardous waste must report the discharge to the Oregon Emergency Management Division (Phone 800-452-0311). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 466 & 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.020 & 466.635 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DEQ 8-1985, f. & ef. 7-25-85
