 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 DAM SAFETY 
 690-020-0047 
 Design Requirements for New Dams or to Increase Dam Height: Geosynthetics 
 
 Geosynthetics shall not be used as the sole element employed to perform a Dam safety function. Redundant design features are required whenever geosynthetics are used for Dam safety functions. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027, 540.488 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 540.449, 540.488
