 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 209 PUBLIC PARTICIPATION 
 340-209-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of this division is to specify the requirements for notifying the public of certain permit actions and providing an opportunity for the public to participate in those permit actions.  NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 468.065 & 468A.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468.065, 468A.035, 468A.040 & ORS 468A.310
