 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 DISTRIBUTION OF ALL-TERRAIN VEHICLE FUNDS TO PUBLIC AND PRIVATELY OWNED LAND MANAGERS, ATV CLUBS AND ORGANIZATIONS 
 736-004-0110 
 Fees 
 
 (1) There is no fee for issuance of the original ATV Safety Education Card.  (2) There is no fee for issuance of an ATV Safety Education Card that adds an endorsement code.  (3) The replacement fee for an ATV Safety Education Card is $8.00. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.570 & 390.575 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.570, 390.575 & 821.174
