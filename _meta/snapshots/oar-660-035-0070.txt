 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 FEDERAL CONSISTENCY 
 660-035-0070 
 Consistency for Federal Assistance to State and Local Governments 
 
 (1) When reviewing applications for federal assistance to state and local governments for consistency with the enforceable policies of the OCMP, the department shall conform to the requirements and procedures provided in 15 CFR Part 930, Subpart F.  (2) For review of federal assistance to state and local governments, the applicant agency must submit to the department the materials described in 15 CFR §930.94.  (3) The department’s review period for consistency certifications for federal assistance to state and local governments shall be 60 days. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.040 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.435
