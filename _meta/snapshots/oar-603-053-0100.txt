 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 53 INSPECTION FEES 
 603-053-0100 
 Fees and Charges for Hop Sampling and Analytical Services 
 
 The following fees and charges are established for hop sampling and analytical services:  (1) Lot inspection — $1.25 per bale (Minimum Charge — $35 per lot);  (2) 17¢ per bale additional for brewers value;  (3) Supplemental Certificate — $6 per certificate. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190, 570.450, 632 & 633 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 632.940 & 632.945
