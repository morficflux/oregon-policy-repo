    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-14


    EXTENDING THE DURATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-07
    (PROHIBITING ON-PREMISES CONSUMPTION OF FOOD OR DRINK)


    Executive Order 20-07, prohibiting on-premises consumption of food or drink, was
    issued on March 17, 2020. On page 3 of Executive Order 20-07, paragraph 8 is
    amended to provide as follows:


           This Executive Order remains in effect until terminated by the Governor.
           The Governor will continue assessing the ongoing public health emergency,
           and will modify or rescind provisions of this Executive Order, as
           appropriate.


    Done at Salem, Oregon, this 7" day of April, 2020.
