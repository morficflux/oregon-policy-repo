 Oregon Public Employees Retirement System 
 Chapter 459 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 PUBLIC EMPLOYER 
 459-009-0098 
 Employer Contribution Rate 
 
 In determining the amounts to be paid to PERS by a public employer, if a current employer contribution rate does not exist for an employer and they employ fewer than five active members on their payroll, PERS will apply the system-wide average rate as the employer contribution rate. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 238.650 & Sections 35 and 37, chapter 355, Oregon Laws 2019 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 238.225 & ORS 238A.220 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PERS 3-2021, adopt filed 02/01/2021, effective 02/01/2021
