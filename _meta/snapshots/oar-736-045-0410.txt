 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 OREGON NATURAL AREAS PROGRAM RULES 
 736-045-0410 
 Dedication and Management of a State Natural Area (Reserve): Publicity
 
 
 The department and the Institute for Natural Resources may develop and make available to private landowners, researchers, agencies and interested persons information about a state natural area or state natural area reserve and appropriate descriptive material. However, the department and the Institute for Natural Resources will avoid publicity which would tend to encourage the general public to visit a state natural area or state natural area reserve in greater numbers than its carrying capacity. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.124(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.563 - 273.591 & 352.808(2)(f).
