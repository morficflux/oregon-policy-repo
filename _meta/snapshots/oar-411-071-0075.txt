 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 PRIVATE ADMISSION ASSESSMENT 
 411-071-0075 
 Record Keeping 
 
 Certified programs must maintain records for three calendar years of the following materials:  (1) Completed assessment forms for each individual assessed;  (2) Personnel records for all employees engaged in performing admission assessments;  (3) Billing and financial records required by the program's contract with the Department; and  (4) Any other information as required by the Department and necessary for the implementation and enforcement of ORS 410.505 to 410.595 and these rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.530 & 410.535
