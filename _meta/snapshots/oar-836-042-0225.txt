 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 RATES AND RATEMAKING 
 836-042-0225 
 Criteria for Grouping; Criteria for Substantially Similar Occupations Within Organization; Open Enrollment Required 
 
 An insurer may combine for rating purposes the experience of a group of employers which it covers for workers’ compensation insurance only if:  (1) All employers in the group are members with full rights and privileges of the organization;  (2) The group satisfies all requirements of ORS 737.316. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & 737.316 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 737.316
