 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 LOW-INCOME HOME ENERGY ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (LIHEAP) 
 813-200-0050 
 Coordination with Home Energy Suppliers
 
 
 (1) Subgrantees must execute a contract with a home energy supplier in order for the home energy supplier to receive an energy assistance payment under LIHEAP. 

 (2) Subgrantees must use a contract template provided or approved by OHCS in fulfillment of its obligation under subsection (1) above. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.555 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 458.505
