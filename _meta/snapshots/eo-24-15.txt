     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-15


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     LONE ROCK FIRE IN GILLIAM, MORROW, WHEELER COUNTIES


     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

     The fire known as the “Lone Rock Fire” is burning in Gilliam, Morrow, and
     Wheeler counties.


     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Lone Rock Fire
     are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
     protection was requested by the Gilliam and Morrow County Fire Defense Board
     and the State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Lone Rock Fire in Gilliam,
     Morrow, and Wheeler counties and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities
     of local firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the
     Emergency Conflagration Act.


     These findings were made at 8:45 a.m. on July 15, 2024, with the Department of
     the State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management
     Team will be entering into unified command with Northwest Team Two. The
     Oregon State Fire Marshal Red Incident Management Team will be briefed at
     10:00 am on July 16, 2024, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

     Il     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding
            to the Lone Rock Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

     2      This conflagration is declared only for the Lone Rock Fire threatening
            structures in Gilliam, Morrow, and Wheeler counties.

     3,     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 8:45 a.m. on the 15" day of
            July 2024, and signed this 15" day ofJuly 2024, in Salem, Oregon.
