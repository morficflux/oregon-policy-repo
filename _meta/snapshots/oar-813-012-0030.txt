 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 MULTI-FAMILY RENTAL FINANCING PROGRAM 
 813-012-0030 
 Eligible Rental Housing Projects
 
 
 (1) The Multifamily Rental Financing: Permanent Loan program manual dated July 13, 2026, is incorporated into this Division 12 by reference.  
(2) Through its Multifamily Rental Financing: Permanent Loan program manual, dated July 13, 2026, the Department shall establish a method by which the Department will evaluate each project to ensure program and departmental standards are met. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 90.800 - 90.840, 90.630, 183.335, 456.515 - 456.723 & 458.210 - 458.650 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.625
