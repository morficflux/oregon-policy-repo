 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 CRIMINAL INTELLIGENCE UNIT 
 137-090-0020 
 Abbreviations 
 
 (1) CIU: Criminal Intelligence Unit.  (2) CIUS: Criminal Intelligence Unit Supervisor.  (3) CJD: Criminal Justice Division.  (4) AIC: Attorney in Charge of the Organized Crime Section.  (5) CIU/AAG: Assistant Attorney General assigned to the Criminal Intelligence Unit. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 180 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 180.610(2), 180.610(3) & 180.610(4)
