 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 311 COLLECTION OF PROPERTY TAXES 
 150-311-0750 
 Assessment District’s Responsibility to Collect Payments 
 
 (1) The Department of Revenue, upon being notified of the disqualification of a special assessment deferral account, will confirm the deferral account balance with the assessment district.  (2) The assessment district is responsible for collecting the deferred special assessment installments, along with any accrued interest, lien recording and releasee fees, and forwarding those amounts to the Department of Revenue. The district must remit any payment it collects in full to the department no later than August 15 of the year following the year of the disqualifying event or promptly after receipt. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 311.725
