 Oregon Health Authority 
 Chapter 943 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 INDIVIDUAL RIGHTS 
 943-005-0050 
 Data Collection 
 
 (1) OEI shall maintain records related to reports of alleged discrimination and harassment including data on the:  (a) Number of reports received.  (b) Types of discrimination or harassment alleged.  (c) Location of the alleged discrimination.  (d) Resolution status of individual reports.  (e) Number of requests for reconsideration.  (2) OEI shall establish reporting standards for designees.  (3) OEI shall retain all collected reporting records for at least three years. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 413.042 
 
 History:
