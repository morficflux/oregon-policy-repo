 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 SEAPLANE OPERATION 
 738-040-0025 
 Equipment 
 
 All seaplanes must be equipped as required by the regulations of the Federal Aviation Administration specifically for seaplanes that are in effect on November 1, 1996. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 835.035 & 835.080 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 835.035, 835.080 & 835.085
