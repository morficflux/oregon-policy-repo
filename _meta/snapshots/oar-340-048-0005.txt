 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 48 CERTIFICATION OF COMPLIANCE WITH WATER QUALITY REQUIREMENTS AND STANDARDS 
 340-048-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to describe procedures for processing applications for certification pursuant to Section 401 of the Clean Water Act, 33 USC § 1341, and ORS 468B.035 through 468B.047. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468b.035 & 33 USC 1341
