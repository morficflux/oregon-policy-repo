 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 61 FOOD SERVICES PROGRAMS 
 291-061-0140 
 Thawing Potentially Hazardous Foods 
 
 Potentially hazardous foods shall be thawed:  (1) In refrigerated units at a temperature not to exceed 40 degrees Fahrenheit;  (2) Unwrapped and under potable running water of a temperature of 70 degrees Fahrenheit or below with sufficient water velocity to agitate and float off loose food particles and thawed liquids into the overflow; or  (3) In a microwave oven, but only when the food will be immediately transferred to conventional cooking facilities as part of a continuous cooking process or when the complete cooking process occurs uninterrupted in the microwave oven; or  (4) As part of a conventional cooking process. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
