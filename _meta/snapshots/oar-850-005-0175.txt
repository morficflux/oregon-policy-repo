 Oregon Board of Naturopathic Medicine 
 Chapter 850 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 ADMINISTRATION OF THE BOARD 
 850-005-0175 
 Board Terms 
 
 The term for each member of the Board will be three years, with no more than three Board member terms expiring in the same year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  685.125 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  685.160
