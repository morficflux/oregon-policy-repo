Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 19
NORTHEAST ZONE SPORT ANGLING REGULATIONS

635-019-0090
Inclusions and Modifications

The current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations provide requirements for the Northeast Zone. However, additional regulations may be adopted in this rule division from time to time and to the extent of any inconsistency, they supersede the current annual Oregon Sport Fishing Regulations.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138 & ORS 496.146
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.162
