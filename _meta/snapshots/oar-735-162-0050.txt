 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 162 SNOWMOBILES INSTRUCTORS/OPERATORS 
 735-162-0050 
 Requirements 
 
 To be certified as a Snowmobile Safety Education Course instructor, the applicant must:  (1) Be at least 21 years of age;  (2) Have a high school education or the equivalent;  (3) Have been recommended by a local snowmobile club or knowledgeable person from the snowmobile industry or the snowmobile association;  (4) Have a valid Oregon driver’s license; and  (5) Have not been convicted of a felony or misdemeanor involving fraud, dishonesty, or moral turpitude as defined under Oregon Statutes during the three years prior to the date of application. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 802.010, 821.150, 821.160 & 821.190 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 821.160
