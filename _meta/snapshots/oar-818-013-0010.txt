 Oregon Board of Dentistry 
 Chapter 818 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 13 HEALTH PROFESSIONALS’ SERVICES PROGRAM 
 818-013-0010 
 Procedures for Board Referrals 
 
 (1) When the Board receives information involving a licensee who may have a substance related disorder, mental disorder, or both types of disorders, the Board staff will investigate and complete a non-identifying confidential report to be presented at a Board meeting.  (2) The Board will consider all relevant factors before determining whether to refer a licensee to the HPSP, including but not limited to licensee’s disciplinary history; the severity and duration of the licensee’s impairment; the extent to which licensee’s practice can be limited or managed to eliminate danger to the public; and the likelihood that licensee’s impairment can be managed with treatment.  (3) If a licensee meets referral criteria and the Board approves entry into the HPSP, the Board will provide a written referral to HPSP. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676, 679 & 680 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.185, 676.190, 676.195, 676.200 & 676.140(e)
