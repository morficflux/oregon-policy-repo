 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 26 SALVAGED VESSEL SUBACCOUNT 
 250-026-0050 
 Use of Contractors 
 
 Enforcement agencies must follow all applicable local and state laws, ordinances, and rules with respect to contracting for services. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830.110 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.948 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSMB 9-2014, f. 5-12-14, cert. ef. 6-1-14
