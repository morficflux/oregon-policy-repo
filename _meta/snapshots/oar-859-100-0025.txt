 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 RESPONSIBILITY OF STATE AND COMMUNITY MENTAL HEALTH PROGRAMS FOR ADULTS 
 859-100-0025 
 Discharge and Conditional Release Plans 
 
 The Legislature has provided that the Mental Health and Developmental Disability Services Division is responsible for preparation of discharge or conditional release plans. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.336 & 161.390 
 
 History:
