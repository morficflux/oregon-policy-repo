 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 230 STANDARDS AND PROCEDURES FOR LOW-TEMPERATURE GEOTHERMAL PRODUCTION  AND INJECTION WELLS AND EFFLUENT  DISPOSAL SYSTEMS 
 690-230-0060 
 Identification of Intended Well Use (Well Log) 
 
 Any low-temperature geothermal production or injection well shall be clearly identified as such on the water supply well report filed with the Water Resources Department under ORS 537.765. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
