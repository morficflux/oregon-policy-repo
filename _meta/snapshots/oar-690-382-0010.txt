 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 382 GROUNDWATER REGISTRATION MODIFICATIONS 
 690-382-0010 
 Purpose
 
 
 (1) The rules in OAR chapter 690, division 382 establish the requirements and procedures to be used by the Department to evaluate an application to modify a groundwater certificate of registration. The rules describe the requirements for changing the point of appropriation, place of use, or character of use of a certificate of registration. 

 (2) No change in point of appropriation, place of use, or character of use of a certificate of registration may be made except as provided under these rules and OAR Chapter 690, Division 380. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025, ORS 537.610 & ORS 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 537.610 & ORS 540.505-540.532
