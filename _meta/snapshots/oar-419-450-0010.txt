 Office of Training, Investigations and Safety 
 Chapter 419 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 450 HOMELESS, RUNAWAY, AND TRANSITIONAL LIVING SHELTERS 
 419-450-0010 
 Homeless, Runaway, and Transitional Living Shelters: What Law Applies
 
 
 (1) These rules, OAR 419-450-0010 to 419-450-0120, regulate a child-caring agency that provides residential services for homeless or runaway youth, pregnant or parenting girls, or other children in care working towards independent living. 

 (2) A child-caring agency that provides residential services for homeless or runaway youth pregnant or parenting girls, or other children in care working towards independent living must also comply with OAR 419-400-0005 to 419-400-0310. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, ORS 418.005, ORS 418.215 & ORS 418.240 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.205 - 418.310
