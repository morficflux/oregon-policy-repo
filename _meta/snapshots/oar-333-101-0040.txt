 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 101 REGISTRATION OF RADIATION MACHINES, GENERAL LICENSE RADIOACTIVE MATERIALS, LICENSING OF RADIATION SERVICES, AND ACCREDITATION OF HOSPITAL RADIOLOGY INSPECTORS 
 333-101-0040 
 Expiration of Notice of Registration
 
 
 (1) Except as provided by OAR 333-101-0045(2) each notice of registration shall expire at the end of the specified day in the month and year stated therein. 

 (2) An X-ray machine registration shall terminate if the X-ray machine is relocated for use in a physical surrounding other than the physical surrounding it occupied when originally registered. 

 (3) X-ray machine registrations are not transferrable to another person and shall terminate upon the transfer of the X-ray machine. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.761 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
