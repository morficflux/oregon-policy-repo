 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 738-001-0006 
 Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 (1) Pursuant to ORS 183.341(2), the Oregon Department of Aviation adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, OAR chapter 137, divisions 1 through 5 to govern its rulemaking and contested cases or equivalent proceedings.  (2) Pursuant to ORS 183.341, the Oregon Department of Aviation adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, OAR chapter 137, divisions 46 through 49 to govern its procurement and public contracting.  (3) Sections (1) and (2) above adopt the Model Rules in effect on the date this rule is filed with the Secretary of State. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 835.035 & 835.112 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341 & 279A.050
