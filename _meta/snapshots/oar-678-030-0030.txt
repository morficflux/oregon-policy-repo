 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Wheat Commission - Chapter 678 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 COMMISSION TERMS, MAKE-UP, AND QUALIFICATIONS 
 678-030-0030 
 Term Limits 
 
 Each grower member and public member of the commission shall be appointed to a four-year term. Should a vacancy occur, the Director of the ODA would appoint a replacement for the remainder of the term. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 578 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 578
