 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 RENTING OR LEASING OFFICE QUARTERS 
 125-120-0100 
 Lease Renewal 
 
 (1) Approximately eighteen months before an agency's lease expires, Division will notify the agency in writing and request the agency's plans for office quarters upon expiration of its lease.  (2) The agency shall respond within thirty days of receipt of the notice and inform Division of its plans for office quarters. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.340 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 276.428
