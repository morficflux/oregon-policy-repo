 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 105 REVIEW OF LOAN REQUESTS BY THE SMALL SCALE LOCAL ENERGY PROJECT ADVISORY COMMITTEE 
 330-105-0035 
 Notice of Adverse Decision 
 
 The Secretary will give prompt notice of any adverse decision and the reasons for such decision. Notice will be sent to the applicant and Committee members. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469 & 470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 470.090
