 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office, Board of Direct Entry Midwifery - Chapter 332 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 GENERAL ADMINISTRATION 
 332-010-0006 
 Duties of Officers 
 
 (1) The chairperson must preside at all meetings. The chairperson must confer with the Office on matters that come up between meeting dates, and matters that need to be placed on the agenda for Board meetings. The chairperson may order or reorder the agenda.  (2) In the absence of the chairperson from a meeting or a portion of a meeting, the Board must vote to elect another Board member to run the meeting.  (3) Decisions will be made by a vote of the Board and carried out with a motion and second and vote by majority. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.615 & 687.475 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 687.425, 687.475 & 687.493
