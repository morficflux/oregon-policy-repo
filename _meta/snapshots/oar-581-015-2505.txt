 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2505 
 Separate Classes Prohibited 
 
 A public agency may not use IDEA funds for classes that are organized separately on the basis of school enrollment or religion of the students if:  (1) The classes are at the same site; and  (2) The classes include students enrolled in public schools or ECSE programs and students enrolled in private schools. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.041, 343.045 & 343.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 659.150 & 34 CFR 300.143
