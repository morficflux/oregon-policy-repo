 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 BOARD REVIEW; THIRD PARTY ORDERS 
 438-011-0022 
 Expedited Review 
 
 (1) For purposes of OAR chapter 438, division 011, compensation is deemed stayed under ORS 656.313 if the employer/insurer has filed a request for Board review on or after July 1, 1990 of an Administrative Law Judge’s order and compensation is due and unpaid under the order for medical services, temporary disability benefits, permanent total disability benefits, or to a surviving spouse prior to remarriage, to children or dependents that accrued before the date of the order, or permanent disability benefits.  (2) Board review of Administrative Law Judge orders in cases described in section (1) of this rule will be expedited. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.313(1)(6) & 656.726(4)
