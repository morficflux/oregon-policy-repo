 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 850 MORTGAGE LENDING GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 441-850-0045 
 Displaying mortgage lender licensing information 
 
 For licenses issued or renewed through Nationwide Multistate Licensing System (NMLS) for periods on or after January 1, 2020, licensees can meet the requirements of ORS 86A.166 by posting a form provided by the Department of Consumer and Business Services completed with current and valid NMLS licensing information. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 86A.136 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 86A.166 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FSR 16-2019, adopt filed 12/16/2019, effective 01/01/2020
