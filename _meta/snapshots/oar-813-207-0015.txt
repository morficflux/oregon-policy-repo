 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 207 STATE HOME OIL WEATHERIZATION PROGRAM (SHOW) 
 813-207-0015 
 Manuals 
 
 The State Home Oil Weatherization Program Manual (SHOW Program Manual) with the requirements and standards therein, dated December 1, 2024, is incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules, by reference. The SHOW Program Manual may be accessed online at the OHCS website.
