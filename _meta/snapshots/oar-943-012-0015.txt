 Oregon Health Authority 
 Chapter 943 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 RESTRICTING ACCESS TO OREGON HEALTH AUTHORITY PREMISES AND EMPLOYEES 
 943-012-0015 
 Continuation of Eligible Services 
 
 (1) An individual whose access has been restricted by the Authority shall continue to be provided services for which the individual meets program eligibility requirements by an alternate and effective method of communication as determined by the Authority.  (2) Alternate methods may include telephone, electronic mail, written communication, meeting at a designated secure site, or through the individual's representative. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 413.042 & 654.010
