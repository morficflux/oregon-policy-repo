 Department of Public Safety Standards and Training 
 Chapter 259 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 CONTESTED CASES 
 259-005-0015 
 Rules of Procedures
 
 
 Pursuant to the provisions of ORS 183.341, the Board and Department adopt the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure applicable to contested cases under the Administrative Procedures Act as amended and in effect July, 2014. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 181A.410, ORS 181A.870, ORS 703.230 & ORS 703.465 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 181A.410, ORS 703.230, ORS 181A.630, ORS 181A.640, ORS 181A.650, ORS 181A.880 & ORS 703.470
