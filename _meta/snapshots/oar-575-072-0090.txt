 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 72 OREGON NURSING LOAN PROGRAM 
 575-072-0090 
 Conditions of Award 
 
 To receive disbursements of a loan made under the Oregon Nursing Loan Program a student must:  (1) Be enrolled in an eligible program of study at an eligible institution;  (2) Be making satisfactory academic progress as defined by the institution attended;  (3) Not be in default on any federal Title IV loan or owing a refund on federal Title IV funds previously disbursed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.115 & 348.117 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.105 - 348.117
