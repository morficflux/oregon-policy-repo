 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 48 HUNTER/TRAPPER EDUCATION 
 635-048-0050 
 Trapper Education Course of Study 
 
 The course of study will include but is not limited to appropriate skills, knowledge and values relating to responsible trapping. The Department will supply study materials for the course. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 & 497 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496 & 497 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FWC 15-1987, f. & ef. 4-15-87
