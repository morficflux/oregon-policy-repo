 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 185 TRUST AND AGENCY (T & A) ACCOUNTS AND CLIENT PAY-IN 
 461-185-0020 
 Authorization of T&A 
 
 (1) The client, or their guardian or conservator, authorizes establishment of a T&A account on a form prescribed by the Department.  (2) Spouses must each sign the authorization form to have their funds administered in trust.  (3) Clients not able to sign their name may make a mark. Two people must witness the signing and give their addresses. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, 411.060 & 411.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.050, 411.060 & 411.070 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AFS 80-1989, f. 12-21-89, cert. ef. 2-1-90
