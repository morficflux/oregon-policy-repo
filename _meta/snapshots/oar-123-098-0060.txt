 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 98 INDUSTRIAL SITE READINESS ASSESSMENT PROGRAM 
 123-098-0060 
 Uncommitted Annual Program Allocation 
 
 Uncommitted funds from annual allocations will be added to the following annual allocation and made available in procedures defined within these rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 & 285B.635-285B.642 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285B.635-285B.642 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OBDD 8-2014, f. 4-30-14, cert. ef. 5-1-14
