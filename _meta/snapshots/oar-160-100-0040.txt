 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 NOTARIES PUBLIC 
 160-100-0040 
 Administrative Services Fees 
 
 The Secretary of State shall charge the following fees for performing the administrative services indicated:  (1) $10 for each apostille or authentication certificate for a public official, including a notary public.  (2) $10 for each notary public certificate of good standing.  (3) $10 for each notarial act performed by a Corporation Division notary public.  (4) $50 for each list of new notaries public.  (5) $50 for each standard list of all active notaries public. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 194.360 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 194.360
