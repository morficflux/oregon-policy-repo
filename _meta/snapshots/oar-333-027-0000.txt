 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 27 HOME HEALTH AGENCIES 
 333-027-0000 
 Purpose
 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to establish the standards for licensure of home health agencies. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 443.085 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 443.014 - 443.090
