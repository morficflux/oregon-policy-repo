 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 AMUSEMENT RIDE AND DEVICE SPECIALTY CODE 
 918-200-0055 
 Permit Fee 
 
 (1) The fee for an annual operating permit for rides and devices shall be $25.  (2) The fee for reinspection by the Division shall be $60 per hour, including associated travel time. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 460.355 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 460.355
