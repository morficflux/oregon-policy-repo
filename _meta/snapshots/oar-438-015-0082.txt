 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 ATTORNEY FEES 
 438-015-0082 
 Timely Payment of Attorney Fees 
 
 (1) An approved attorney fee shall be paid within the time required for payment of the compensation out of which the approved fee is to be paid.  (2) An assessed attorney fee shall be paid within 14 days of the date the order authorizing the fee becomes final. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.307, 656.388, 656.593 & ORS 656.726(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.382(1), 656.386(1)(2) & 656.388
