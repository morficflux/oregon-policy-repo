 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 135 TOXIC USE REDUCTION AND HAZARDOUS WASTE REDUCTION REGULATIONS 
 340-135-0090 
 Information Access 
 
 (1) Toxics users must keep the reduction plan and environmental management system on site in accordance with ORS 465.015(6), and available for review by the Department in accordance with 465.018(4).  (2) Reduction plans and environmental management systems are confidential, except as provided for in (1) of this section and by ORS 465.015(6).  (3) The Department will make implementation summaries available to the public by placing them on the World Wide Web in accordance with ORS 465.018(5). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192.501, ORS 192.502, 65.003 - 465.037 & 466 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 465.015 & 465.018
