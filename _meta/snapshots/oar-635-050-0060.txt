 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 FURBEARING AND UNPROTECTED MAMMAL REGULATIONS 
 635-050-0060 
 Times, Places, Bag Limits, Species, Sex, and Manner of Taking
 
 
 Each species of furbearing or unprotected mammal may be hunted or trapped only during the times and in the places, quantities, and manner specifically provided for in OAR 635-050-0015 through 635-050-0300. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
