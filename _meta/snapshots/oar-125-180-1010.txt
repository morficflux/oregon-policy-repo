 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 180 SHARED SERVICES FUND 
 125-180-1010 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules implement procedures for transferring moneys from the Shared Services Fund pursuant to ORS 285C.635 and 285C.639. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.340, 285C.615(7) & 285C.635(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285C.600 - 285C.626 & 285C.635 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DAS 1-2013, f. & cert. ef. 7-31-13
