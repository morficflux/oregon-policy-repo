 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0060 
 Minam Unit 
 
 The Minam Unit, number 60, is that area beginning at the town of Minam; southeast on State Highway 82 to head of Wallowa Lake; southwest on West Fork Trail to Hawkins Pass; southwest along summit of Eagle Mountains to Granite Butte; northwest along summit separating Big Minam and Catherine Creek drainages past Burger Butte and Meadow Mountain to Cartwheel Ridge Trail 1907; West on Cartwheel Ridge Trail to Lodgepole Trail to Moss Springs; north on Point Prominence Road to the boundary of Wallowa-Whitman National Forest; north and east along National Forest boundary to Minam River; north along Minam River to State Highway 82 at the town of Minam, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
