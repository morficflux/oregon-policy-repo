 Oregon Board of Physical Therapy 
 Chapter 848 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 PHYSICAL THERAPIST AIDES 
 848-020-0010 
 Eligibility Standards 
 
 A physical therapist aide shall:  (1) Be at least 18 years of age.  (2) Have received a high school diploma or equivalent. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 688.160 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 688.160 & 688.210
