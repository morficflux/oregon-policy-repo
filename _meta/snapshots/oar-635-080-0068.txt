 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0068 
 Whitehorse Unit 
 
 The Whitehorse Unit, number 68, is that area beginning at Follyfarm; southeast on State Highway 78 to U.S. Highway 95; northeast on U.S. Highway 95 to Jordan Valley; east on Jordan Valley-Silver City Road to Oregon-Idaho state line; south and west along state line to Denio; northeast on county road 201 through Fields and Andrews to Follyfarm, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
