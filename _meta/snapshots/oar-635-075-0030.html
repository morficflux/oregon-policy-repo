Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 75
LANDOWNER HUNTING PREFERENCE AND OUTFITTERS AND GUIDES TAG ALLOCATION

635-075-0030
Tag Limitations

(1) Tags issued in the above described allocation process are for nonresident hunters only. Outfitter and Guides and their immediate families (as described in OAR 635-045-0002) or their employees are not eligible to receive or use tags described in this allocation process for personal use, when that Outfitter and Guide made the original application.

(2) Tags issued under this allocation process are valid only when the hunter is being guided by the Outfitter and Guide or their employee who made the original application as described above in an "Outfitter and Guide Hunt" as defined in 635-045-0002. 

(3) Tags shall not be transferred among Outfitter and Guides or among hunters and the Outfitter and Guide who drew the tag must have a federally issued special use permit for the area where they are guiding when guiding on federal land.

(4) Tags issued to an Outfitter and Guide will be revoked if at any time the Marine Board revokes or denies a registration or certification. Refunds for tags will be issued to Outfitter and Guides only.

(5) When the commission reduces allocated tags, refunds for tags only will be issued to Outfitter and Guides who were not selected in the reallocation process.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 497.112 & 496.151
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 497.112 & 496.151
