 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 665 SPECIFIED RESOURCE SITE PROTECTION RULES 
 629-665-0200 
 Resource Sites Used by Threatened and Endangered Species 
 
 The following resource sites used by threatened or endangered species are sensitive to forest practices: Northern spotted owl nesting sites. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 527.710 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 527.715
