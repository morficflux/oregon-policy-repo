 Oregon Health Authority 
 Chapter 943 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 PUBLIC CONTRACTING AND PROCUREMENT 
 943-060-0000 
 Application 
 
 These rules (OAR 943-060-0000 to 943-060-0120) govern public contracting of the Oregon Health Authority and are subject to ORS chapters 279A, 279B, and 279C. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 279A.065, 413.033 & 413.042 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OHA 5-2012, f. & cert. ef. 8-1-12
