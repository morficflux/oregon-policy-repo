 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 RELEASE TO POST-PRISON SUPERVISION OR PAROLE AND EXIT INTERVIEWS 
 255-060-0020 
 Out-of-State Parole Release Hearing Procedures 
 
 An inmate in the Department of Corrections’ custody who is housed in an out-of-state facility may receive a teleconference exit interview in conformance with rule 255-060-0006. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144.098 & 144.125 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 144.098 & 144.125
