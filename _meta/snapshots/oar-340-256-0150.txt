 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 256 MOTOR VEHICLES 
 340-256-0150 
 Visible Emissions: Opacity Method of Measurement
 
 
 Only a trained person may make the opacity observation for purposes of OAR 340-256-0100 through 340-256-0140. A trained person may use a Ringelmann Smoke Chart or DEQ approved opacity equipment to measure emission opacity. 

 [NOTE: Publications referenced are available from the agency.]
