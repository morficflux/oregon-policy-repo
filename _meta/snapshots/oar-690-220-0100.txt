 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 220 ABANDONMENT OF WATER SUPPLY WELLS 
 690-220-0100 
 Obstructions and Possible Contaminants 
 
 All obstructions or debris which may interfere with effective sealing operations shall be removed from the water supply well to be abandoned. Any foreign matter capable of causing ground water contamination shall be removed prior to placing any sealing material. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
