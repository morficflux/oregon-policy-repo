 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 29 SALE OF FOREST PRODUCTS FROM LANDS OWNED OR MANAGED BY THE STATE BOARD OF FORESTRY 
 629-029-0115 
 Availability of Award Decision 
 
 (1) Contract documents. A signed contract document may be sent to the successful bidder.  (2) Notification to unsuccessful bidders. Unsuccessful bidders generally will not be notified that a contract has been awarded. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.041 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 530.050 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FB 1-1997, f. 9-4-97, cert. ef. 1-1-98
