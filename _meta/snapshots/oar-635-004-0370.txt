 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 COMMERCIAL FISHERIES OTHER THAN SALMON OR SHELLFISH 
 635-004-0370 
 Organization of Coastal Pelagic and Smelt Species Rules 
 
 (1) Administrative rules contained in OAR 635-004-0375 though 635-004-0379 shall apply to all fisheries in the Coastal Pelagic and Smelt Species section, and are in addition to and not in lieu of division 004 General Regulations contained in 635-004-0200 through 635-004-0265. The Coastal Pelagic and Smelt Species Section includes regulations for the Sardine, Inland Waters Herring, Yaquina Bay Roe-Herring, Pacific Ocean Herring, Anchovy and Smelt Fisheries.  (2) Market squid are managed under the Coastal Pelagic Species Fishery Management Plan and through the regulations adopted by reference in OAR 635-004-0375. However, market squid are managed as a shellfish when landed in Oregon, and are subject to regulations in the Squid Fishery Section in division 5. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & 506.129
