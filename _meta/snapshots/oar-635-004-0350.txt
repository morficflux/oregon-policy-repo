 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 COMMERCIAL FISHERIES OTHER THAN SALMON OR SHELLFISH 
 635-004-0350 
 Harvest Guidelines and Landing Caps
 
 
 (1) Upon attainment of a harvest guideline in the Black Rockfish / Blue Rockfish / Nearshore Fishery, the Department shall initiate consultation to determine if additional regulatory actions are necessary to achieve management objectives. 

 (2) The following commercial harvest guidelines include the combined landings and other fishery related mortality by all Oregon commercial fisheries in a single calendar year: 

 (a) Black rockfish: 84.1 metric tons; 

 (b) Blue rockfish and deacon rockfish combined: 14.4 metric tons; 

 (c) Other Nearshore Rockfish combined: 15.0 metric tons; 

 (d) Cabezon: 30.5 metric tons; and 

 (e) Greenling: 95.3 metric tons. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & ORS 506.129
