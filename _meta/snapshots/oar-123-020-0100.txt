 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 FORMATION OF PORTS 
 123-020-0100 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 This division of administrative rules describes the steps and necessary elements for the Commission to approve the formation of a Port, as required under ORS 285A.627(2). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285A.627
