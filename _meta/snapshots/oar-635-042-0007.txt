 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 COLUMBIA RIVER SYSTEM COMMERCIAL FISHERIES 
 635-042-0007 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) For the purpose of Chapter 679, Oregon Laws 1979, "Columbia River gillnet salmon fishery" means commercial salmon gillnet fishing in the Columbia River below Bonneville Dam and all tributaries thereof, including Youngs Bay, wherein commercial salmon gillnet seasons are authorized by Department rules.  (2) "Commission" means the Oregon Fish and Wildlife Commission.  (3) "Department" means the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife.  (4) "Director" means the Director of the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.325, 496.118 & 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.129 & 507.030
