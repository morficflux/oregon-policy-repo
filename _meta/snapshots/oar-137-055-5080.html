Department of Justice

Chapter 137

Division 55
OREGON CHILD SUPPORT PROGRAM

137-055-5080
Adding Interest

(1) The administrator will not establish interest on support arrears.

(2) For enforcement purposes, the administrator will:

(a) Add interest to a case balance if a specific amount has been established in a judgment or order entered by a court; 

(b) Add interest to a case balance if the parties stipulate in writing that an amount should be added; or

(c) Add interest to the case balance on a case with a controlling order from another jurisdiction if the other jurisdiction provides a certified interest calculation consistent with its laws governing the computation and accrual of interest.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 180.345
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 25.167, 25.540, 82.010 & 110.611
