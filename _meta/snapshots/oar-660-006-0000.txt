 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 GOAL 4 FOREST LANDS 
 660-006-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 (1) The purpose of this division is to conserve forest lands as defined by Goal 4 and to define standards for compliance with implementing statutes at ORS 215.700 through 215.799.  (2) To accomplish the purpose of conserving forest lands, the governing body shall:  (a) Designate forest lands on the comprehensive plan map as forest lands consistent with Goal 4 and OAR chapter 660, division 6;  (b) Zone forest lands for uses allowed pursuant to OAR chapter 660, division 6 on designated forest lands; and  (c) Adopt plan policies consistent with OAR chapter 660, division 6.  (3) This rule provides for a balance between the application of Goal 3 "Agricultural Lands" and Goal 4 "Forest Lands," because of the extent of lands that may be designated as either agricultural or forest land. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.040, 197.230 & 197.245 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.040, 197.230, 197.245, 215.700, 215.705, 215.720, 215.740, 215.750, 215.780 & Ch. 792 & 1993 OL
