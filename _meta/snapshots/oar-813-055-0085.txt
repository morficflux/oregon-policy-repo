 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 GENERAL HOUSING ACCOUNT 
 813-055-0085 
 Charges 
 
 (1) An applicant requesting General Housing Account funds shall pay an application fee or charge as may be required by the department.  (2) An applicant or owner of a multifamily affordable housing development project that receives GHAP assistance shall pay a monitoring fee or charge as may be required by the department.  (3) The applicant or owner of a project that receives GHAP assistance shall pay such other charges with respect to the department’s anticipated costs and expenses of administration as the department may require. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.555 & 458.665 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.515 to 456.725 & 458.665
