 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 92 MINIMUM ENERGY EFFICIENCY STANDARDS FOR STATE-REGULATED APPLIANCES AND EQUIPMENT 
 330-092-0040 
 Determination of Compliance
 
 
 The Department may require the manufacturer to provide test results or other documentation verifying that a product meets Oregon's minimum energy efficiency standards for that category of equipment. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.040, ORS 469.255 & ORS 469.261 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.229–469.261
