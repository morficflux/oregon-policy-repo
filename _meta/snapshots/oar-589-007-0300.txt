 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Community Colleges and Workforce Development - Chapter 589 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 PROGRAMS 
 589-007-0300 
 Veterans Programs 
 
 Each community college requesting approval for the training of veterans and other eligible persons will contact the State Approving Agency (SAA) for veterans' administration. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 341.506 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 341.506
