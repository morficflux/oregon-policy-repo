 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 MODEL HIGHER EDUCATION COMMUNITY SERVICE VOUCHER PROGRAM 
 575-085-0050 
 Participant Eligibility 
 
 (1) A participant must be a resident of the State of Oregon as defined by the institution.  (2) A participant must be enrolled or accepted for enrollment as un undergraduate at an institution participating in the Higher Education Community Service Voucher Program. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  SB 81, Sec. 40 - 44, & 128(9) & 1993 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.427 - 348.433
