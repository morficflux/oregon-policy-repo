 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 82 VARIANCE PERMITS ISSUED FOR NON-DIVISIBLE LOADS AND ROAD USE ASSESSMENT FEES 
 734-082-0006 
 Acceptance of Permit 
 
 The receipt of a permit issued under these Division 82 rules by the permittee and initiation of any movement authorized by the permit is considered acceptance of all descriptions, terms and conditions contained in the permit provisions and requirements. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 818.200 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.220 & 818.225
