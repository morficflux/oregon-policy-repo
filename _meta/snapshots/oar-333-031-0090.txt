 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF RECREATION PARKS 
 333-031-0090 
 Civil Penalties 
 
 In accordance with ORS 446.348, the Assistant Director for Health will use the following schedule to impose civil penalties for violations of rules for recreation parks and picnic parks:  (1) Violations of any requirements stated in any part of OAR 333-031-0004, 333-031-0005, 333-031-0006 and 333-031-0110 are Class I violations subject to a civil penalty in the amount of not less than $75 nor more than $1,000 for each and every violation.  (2) Violations of any requirements stated in any part of OAR 333-031-0007, 333-031-0008, 333-031-0012, 333-031-0018, 333-031-0059, and 333-031-0066 are Class II violations subject to a civil penalty in the amount of not less than $50 nor more than $750 for each and every violation.  (3) Violations of any of the requirements stated in any part of OAR 333-031-0014, 333-031-0020, 333-031-0060, 333-031-0062, 333-031-0064 and 333-031-0068 are Class III violations subject to a civil penalty in the amount of not less than $25 nor more than $350 for each and every violation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.348 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.347 & 446.348 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 HD 9-1986, f. & ef. 6-12-86
