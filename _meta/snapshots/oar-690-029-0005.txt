 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 29 WARM SPRINGS WATER RIGHTS NEGOTIATIONS 
 690-029-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 This rule establishes the methods by which the Water Resources Department (Department) provides opportunity for public notice and comment regarding negotiations between the Director of the Water Resources Department (Director) and the representatives for the Warm Springs Tribes and federal government, as trustee for the tribes, held to establish the scope and attributes of the water right claimed by the tribes, pursuant to ORS 539.300, et seq. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 539 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 23-1988, f. & cert. ef. 12-14-88
