 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 RULES REGULATING THE EMPLOYMENT OF MINORS IN OREGON 
 839-021-0315 
 Special Hours Variances for Entertainment Industry Employers
 
 
 Employers, including employers registered under OAR 839-021-0320, must apply for a special hours variance when the contemplated employment will exceed the maximum hours prescribed in OAR 839-021-0335. Employers must address a letter application to the Bureau of Labor and Industries setting out the full and complete circumstances of the proposed employment and the reasons why a special hours variance is being requested. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 651.060, ORS 653.040, ORS 653.261 & ORS 653.305 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 653.261, ORS 653.305, ORS 653.307 & ORS 653.400
