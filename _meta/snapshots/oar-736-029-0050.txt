 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 29 MARINE RESERVE AND MARINE PROTECTED AREA RULES 
 736-029-0050 
 Penalties 
 
 A violation of OAR 736-029-0040 is as an ocean shore violation subject to fines as provided in OAR 736-021-0050. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.050 & 390.124 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PRD 3-2010, f. 2-3-10, cert. ef. 6-30-11
