 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 VIDEO LOTTERY TERMINALS 
 177-100-0130 
 Duties of Manufacturers 
 
 Manufacturers, their employees, representatives, and agents shall:  (1) Promptly report to the Lottery any violation or any facts or circumstances that may result in a violation of ORS Chapter 461 or these rules.  (2) Provide immediate access to all records and the entire physical premises of the business for inspection at the request of the Lottery or its auditors.  (3) Not conduct any advertising or promotional activities in Oregon (or directed at Oregon residents) that are false or misleading regarding Video LotterySM games.  (4) Promptly report to the Lottery their knowledge or suspicion of any gray machine located within Oregon.  (5) Attend all trade shows or conferences as required by the Lottery. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV, § 4(4) & ORS 461 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.215, 461.217 & 461.400
