 Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology 
 Chapter 335 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 335-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Board adopts the Attorney General’s Model Rules, Oregon Administrative Rule Chapter 137, division one, division four and Oregon Administrative Rules 137-003-0501 to 137-003-0700.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 681.420(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
