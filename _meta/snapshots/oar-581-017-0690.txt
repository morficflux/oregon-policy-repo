 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0690 
 American Indian/Alaska Native Student Success Plan Grant Program: Reporting 
 
 Grant recipients must report project or program outcomes in the manner and form required by the Office of Indian Education. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 329.843 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329.843 
 
 History:
