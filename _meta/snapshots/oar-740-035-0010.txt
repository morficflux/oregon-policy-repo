 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 CERTIFICATES, PERMITS, LICENSES AND DOCUMENTS 
 740-035-0010 
 Applications for Authority or Change of Authority 
 
 (1) All applications for a certificate or permit or for the extension, contraction, abandonment or other change in operating authority or for adding vehicles to certificate or permit must be made on forms approved by the Department.  (2) Additional filing requirements and attachments to or in support of applications are specified in OAR 740-035-0150. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 825.232 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.100, 825.102, 825.110 & 825.202
