POLICY:            5.001 DRUG AND ALCOHOL-FREE WORKPLACE

CONTACT:           LINDSEY GUY                                 EFFECTIVE DATE: APRIL 1, 2026
PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY:
To establish OSH as a drug and alcohol-free workplace in accordance with OHA policy,
define who this policy applies to, define situations requiring drug and/or alcohol screening,
the procedures for doing so, and how the outcome of screening is managed

SUMMARY OF CONTENT CHANGE(S):
Updates are minimal and primarily focused on revising language to improve clarity and
accessibility. Substantive changes are located in Procedures B and include:

Pre-employment drug tests are now scheduled through an online portal. Workflows have
been developed in collaboration with Minnie McCloud-Walker, Michael Meadows, both
from OHA Human Resources, and their teams. For cause/reasonable suspicion testing will
now be conducted onsite, rather than requiring staff to be transported offsite.

Updates also include incorporating current procedures for pre-employment and for-cause
(reasonable suspicion) evaluation and screening. These changes do not alter how positive
drug test results are handled.

REASON FOR CHANGE(S):
To improve clarity and accessibility of policy language and to align procedures with current
operational practices.

REQUIRED ACTION/SPECIFIED STAFF:
All staff must adhere to the requirements outlined in this policy.
