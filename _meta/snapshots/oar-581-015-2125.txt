 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2125 
 Interpretation of Evaluation Data 
 
 In interpreting evaluation data for the purpose of determining if a child is a child with a disability under OAR 581-015-2130 through 581-015-2180, and the educational needs of the child, each team must:  (1) Draw upon information from a variety of sources, including but not limited to, aptitude and achievement tests, teacher recommendations, physical condition, social or cultural background and adaptive behavior; and  (2) Ensure that information obtained from all these sources is documented and carefully considered. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.041 & 343.157; 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 343.157, 343.045, 343.146, 343.155 & 34 CFR 300.306
