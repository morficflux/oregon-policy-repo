 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES, ATTORNEY FEES, AND GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 436-001-0440 
 Time Within Which Attorney Fees Must Be Paid
 
 
 Attorney fees under OAR 436-001-0400 to 436-001-0438 must be paid within 14 days of the date the order addressing the fees becomes final. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.262, ORS 656.268, ORS 656.277, ORS 656.383 & ORS 656.385
