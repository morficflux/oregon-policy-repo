629-041-0540
Klamath-Lake Forest Protection District Boundary

The boundary of the Klamath-Lake Forest Protection District is as follows: Beginning at the point where the common boundary of Jackson County and Klamath County, as set forth in ORS 201.150 and 201.180, intersect with the southern boundary of the state of Oregon, as set forth in ORS 201.005, in or near section 18, township 41 south, range 5 east, Klamath County;
