 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 PRIVATELY FUNDED AWARD PROGRAMS 
 575-060-0040 
 Disbursement 
 
 (1) Unless otherwise specified by the donor, the Commission shall disburse funds to an institution on behalf of an awardee on an academic term basis.  (2) An institution shall not disburse funds to an awardee who ceases to meet conditions of award identified by the Commission.  (3) An institution shall return undisbursed funds to the Commission within 30 days of receipt. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.520 - 348.530 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.520 - 348.530 
 
 History:
