 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Sweet Cherry Commission - Chapter 669 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 COMMISSION MAKE-UP, DUTIES OF OFFICERS AND REIMBURSEMENT OF EXPENSES 
 669-030-0010 
 Number of Commissioners, Terms
 
 
 The Oregon Sweet Cherry Commission will consist of 11 commissioners appointed by the Director of the Oregon Department of Agriculture for a term or three years or, if for a term following a prior term for that person, ending three years from the date of expiration of any prior term. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2003 OL Ch. 604 & ORS 576
