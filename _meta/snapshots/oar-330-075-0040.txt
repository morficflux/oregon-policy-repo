 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 ENERGY SUPPLIER ASSESSMENT RULES 
 330-075-0040 
 Proprietary Information 
 
 An energy resource supplier may request that information submitted pursuant to OAR 330-075-0010 to 330-075-0035 be treated by the Director as proprietary information and maintained in confidence. Any such request shall be made at the time the information is submitted. Such request shall comply with the procedure set forth in 330-010-0005 to 330-010-0030. The Director’s decision on the request will be made in accordance with 330-010-0030. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.421
