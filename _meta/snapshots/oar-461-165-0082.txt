 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 165 ISSUING BENEFITS 
 461-165-0082 
 SNAP Cash-Out 
 
 SNAP households in Clackamas, Columbia, Multnomah, and Washington counties receive SNAP benefits in cash if all persons in the filing group are 65 years of age or older or are eligible to receive SSI benefits under Title XVI of the Social Security Act. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.816 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.816 
 
 History:
