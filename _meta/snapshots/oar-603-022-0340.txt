 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 EGGS 
 603-022-0340 
 Weight Classes 
 
 (1) The weight classes for Oregon Consumer Grades for Shell Eggs shall be as indicated in Table 5 and shall apply to all consumer grades.  (2) A lot average tolerance of 3.3 percent for individual eggs in the next lower weight class is permitted as long as no individual case within the lot exceeds five percent.  [ED NOTE: Tables referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & 632.811 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 632.811
