 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 47 MENTAL HEALTH TREATMENT PROGRAM (AIC ASSIGNMENT AND TRANSFER) 
 291-047-0110 
 Extension of Transfer 
 
 (1) If the Department of Human Services determines that the administrative commitment must exceed 180 days in order to stabilize the inmate, the administrative commitment must be renewed in a subsequent administrative commitment hearing held in accordance with these rules.  (2) Notwithstanding this rule, an administrative commitment may not continue beyond the term of incarceration to which the inmate was sentenced. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 179.473, 179.475, 179.477, 179.478, 179.479, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075, OL 2005 & Ch. 439 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 179.473, 179.475, 179.477, 179.478, 179.479, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075, OL 2005 & Ch. 439
