 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 RESPONSIBILITY OF STATE AND COMMUNITY MENTAL HEALTH PROGRAMS FOR ADULTS 
 859-100-0005 
 Compliance 
 
 State or local mental health programs shall comply with any order of the Board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137, 161, 192 & 428 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.336 & 161.346 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PSRB 1-1985, f. 1-3-85, ef. 1-15-85
