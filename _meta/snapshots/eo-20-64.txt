    EXECUTIVE ORDER 20-64

    RESCINDING EXECUTIVE ORDER 20-61

    On November 2, 2020, in Executive Order 20-61, I declared a state of emergency
    for Multnomah County, to address and ensure appropriate coordination of law
    enforcement resources to respond to the threat of civil disturbance.

    Following consultation with the incident commanders regarding the situation on the
    ground, and pursuant to my authority under ORS 401.204(1), I hereby proclaim and
    order that Executive Order 20-61 is rescinded, and that the state of emergency
    declared therein is terminated, effective at 12:00 noon, November 8, 2020.

    Done at Salem, Oregon, this 8" day of November, 2020.
