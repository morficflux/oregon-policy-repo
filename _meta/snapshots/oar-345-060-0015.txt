 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 TRANSPORTATION OF RADIOACTIVE MATERIAL 
 345-060-0015 
 Vehicles, Operator, Equipment 
 
 The carrier shall maintain all aspects of vehicles, operators and equipment in accordance with OAR 740, division 100. These Oregon Department of Transportation rules reference the requirements of 49 CFR 390 through 397 in effect as of the date of this rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 & 469.607 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.603, 469.605 & 469.607
