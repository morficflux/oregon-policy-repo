 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 470 ABATEMENT OF BUILDINGS DAMAGED BY EARTHQUAKE 
 918-470-0100 
 Staying of Order Under Appeal 
 
 Except for vacation orders according to OAR 918-470-0070, enforcement of any order issued under these rules shall be stayed while the appeal is pending. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 401.539 & 455.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 401.539 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BCD 9-1994, f. 3-30-94, cert. ef. 5-1-94
