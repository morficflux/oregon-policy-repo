 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-1020 
 Coho Salmon 
 
 (1) The following operating principle applies to coho salmon in the Willamette Coast Range subbasins:  (2) Maintain natural projection of coho salmon in the Willamete Coast Range subbasins consistent with achieving the desired staus for winter steelhead and sping Chinook populations identified in the Upper Willamete River Conservation and Recovery Plan for Chinook Salmon and Steelhead (OAR 635-500-6600). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119, 506.129 & 506.720
