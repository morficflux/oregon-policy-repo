 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 SAFETY CODE FOR ELEVATORS 
 918-400-0730 
 Reclassification of Elevators 
 
 Elevators shall be reclassified only if the elevator meets, exceeds or is altered to at least the current requirements for the new classification and is so used. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 460.085 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 460.085
