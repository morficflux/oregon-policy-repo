 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 LICENSING - PROCEDURES FOR ESTABLISHING, OPERATING, DISCONTINUING AND SUSPENDING LICENSES AND AGENTS 
 635-010-0150 
 Resident Senior Citizen Licenses 
 
 Resident senior citizen licenses issued before January 1, 2000, pursuant to ORS 497.102(f) and 497.121(h) are valid for as long as the licensee is a resident of Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 & 497 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496 & 497
