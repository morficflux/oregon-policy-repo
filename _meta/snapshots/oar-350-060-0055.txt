 Columbia River Gorge Commission 
 Chapter 350 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 APPEALS FROM COUNTY ORDINANCES 
 350-060-0055 
 Respondents’ Notice of Appearance 
 
 Within 14 days after filing of a Notice of Appeal, a respondent shall file at the Commission office and serve a “Notice of Appearance” stating whether the respondent is willing to attempt to resolve the case through alternative dispute resolution means, and whether the respondent opts out of email service of all documents. Note that the respondent must affirmatively opt out of email service. This Notice of Appearance shall not be used to argue merits of the appeal. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.150 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015, 16 U.S.C. § 544c(b) & 16 U.S.C. § 544m(a)(2)
