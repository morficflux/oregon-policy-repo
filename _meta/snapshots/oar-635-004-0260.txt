 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 COMMERCIAL FISHERIES OTHER THAN SALMON OR SHELLFISH 
 635-004-0260 
 Far Offshore Fishery Trip Limit 
 
 Except for the licensing requirements provided in OAR 635-004-0245, there are no trip limits or landing restrictions for fish or fish products taken in the far offshore fishery. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.162, 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.138, 496.162, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129
