 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 250 WATER DISTRIBUTION 
 690-250-0030 
 Appropriator Obliged to Maintain a Functional Point of Diversion 
 
 The watermaster shall have no obligation to increase flows of surface water to reach the elevation of a diversion or to overcome friction losses caused by clogged diversion facilities of the person calling for water. The appropriator shall be responsible for diversion and conveyance of water from the natural source to the place of use. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 540 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 540 
 
 History:
