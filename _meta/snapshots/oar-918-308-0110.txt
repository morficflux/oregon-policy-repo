 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ELECTRICAL PROGRAM MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-308-0110 
 Code Interpretation Dispute Resolution 
 
 Municipalities shall provide:  (1) A reasonable method to resolve conflicts in code interpretation within the municipality; and  (2) Information concerning appeal rights to the division under ORS 479.853. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.855 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.855
