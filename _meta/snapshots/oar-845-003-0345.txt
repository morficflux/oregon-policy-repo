 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 CONTESTED CASE PROCEDURES 
 845-003-0345 
 Public Attendance and Representation of Parties 
 
 (1) Contested case hearings are closed to non-participants and may only be attended by participants.  (2) Representation of parties is governed by OAR 137-003-0550. The Commission does not allow the use of authorized representatives described in 137-003-0555 in its contested case hearings. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341(2), 471.730(5) & (6) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341(2) 
 
 History:
