 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 95 COLUMBIA RIVER BASIN WATER DEVELOPMENT LOAN PROGRAM 
 690-095-0085 
 Issuance of Bonds 
 
 Bonds may be issued at the request of the director and administered as provided in ORS chapter 286A and 541.780 to 541.840. Bonds may be issued prior to and after the execution of any loan contracts entered into with respect to the bonds. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027 & 541.646 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541-600 - 541.646 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 12-14-10
