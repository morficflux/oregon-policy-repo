 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 NOTICE OF MEETINGS OF THE STATE LAND BOARD 
 141-005-0060 
 Responsibility for Notices 
 
 The Director of the Department of State Lands shall be responsible for the giving of all notices required by these rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192 & 273 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.035
