Office of the Governor
State of Oregon

  EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-16


  OREGON HISTORIC TRAILS ADVISORY COUNCIL


  WHEREAS, the Oregon Historical Trails Advisory Council was created by executive order
  in 1998 to promote Oregon’s 16 designated historic trails.                  ;


  WHEREAS, the creation of the Council was the first step in the development of the Oregon
  Historical Trails Program, which established Oregon as the nation’s leader in developing
  historic trails for their educational, recreational, and economic values.


  WHEREAS, the Council and its members—as well as others across Oregon—have
  successfully fostered statewide and local programs and organizations that support and
  advocate for Oregon’s historic trails.


  WHEREAS, the Council has fulfilled its purpose, as set forth in Executive Order No. 98-16.


  NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


     1.   Executive Order No. 98-16, issued on August 11, 1998, is declared concluded and
          rescinded.


    2. The Oregon Historical Trails Advisory Council shall be disbanded as of December
          31, 2018.


          Done at Salem, Oregon, this re day of i, 2018.
