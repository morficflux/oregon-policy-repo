 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Employees' Benefit Board - Chapter 101 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 101-001-0005 
 Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure
 
 
 The Attorney General’s Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedure Act, dated 2024, are adopted as rules of procedure of the Public Employees’ Benefit Board and are made a part of OAR chapter 101. 

 [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedures is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Oregon Health Authority, Public Employees’ Benefit Board.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 243.061 - 243.302 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.310 - 183.750
