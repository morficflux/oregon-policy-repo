 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 GENERAL 
 141-010-0010 
 Sealed Bids Required 
 
 The leasing of offshore tracts for the production of oil, gas, and sulphur shall be by sealed bids. Bids shall be submitted on forms supplied by the Board, or image copies thereof. To be considered, bids shall be received at the Department of State Lands, 775 Summer St. NE, Ste. 100, Salem, OR 97301-1279, before the time designated in the public notice of the offer to lease. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 274 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 274.735 
 
 History:
