 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 GENERAL OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH RULES 
 437-002-0363 
 Oregon Amendment 
 
 1910.1028(j)(1)(ii) is amended to read (wording in brackets is deleted): 1910.1028(j)(1)(ii) The employer shall ensure that labels or other appropriate forms of warning are provided for containers of benzene within the workplace. [There is no requirement to label pipes.] The labels shall comply with the requirements of CFR 1910.1200(f) and in addition shall include the following legend:  Danger Contains Benzene  Cancer Hazard 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 APD 13-1988, f. & ef. 8-2-88
