 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 54 EXTRA-CURRICULAR SCHOOL TRANSPORTATION GRANTS 
 581-054-0008 
 Eligibility 
 
 (1) School districts are eligible for School Transportation grants. 

 (2) A school district may receive an award only for the following eligible transportation costs: 

 (a) For which the school district does not receive any amount in distributions from the State School Fund under ORS 327.013 or any amount related to transit activities, and 

 (b) That are incurred by the school district for the provision of transportation to students in grades 9 through 12 on: 

 (A) A school bus, as defined in ORS 801.460; or 

 (B) A school activity vehicle, as defined in ORS 801.455. 

 (3) School districts may transfer grant funds by contract with a public charter school or private alternative educational program for eligible transportation costs set forth in subsection (2). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Sec. 2, Ch. 62, OL 2018 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Sec. 2, Ch. 62, OL 2018
