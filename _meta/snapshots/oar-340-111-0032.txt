 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 111 USED OIL MANAGEMENT 
 340-111-0032 
 Used Oil Storage 
 
 In addition to the provisions in 40 CFR 279.22, used oil generators shall comply with sections (1) and (2) of this rule:  (1) Used oil shall be stored following applicable state and local Fire Marshal regulations.  (2) Containers and tanks used to store used oil shall be closed, covered or located under cover to prevent rainwater from coming in contact with the used oil. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192, 465.009, 466.015, 466.020, 466.075, 466.090, 468.020 & 646 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 459A.590, 466.020 & 466.075 
 
 History:
