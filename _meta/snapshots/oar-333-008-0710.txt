 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 MEDICAL MARIJUANA 
 333-008-0710 
 OMMP Monitoring, Investigation, and Enforcement: Grow Site Inspections
 
 
 The Authority may inspect the following to ensure compliance with ORS 475C.792, 475C.795 and 475C.806, and any rule adopted under ORS 475C.792, 475C.795 and 475C.806: 

 (1) The marijuana grow site of a person designated to produce marijuana by a patient; and 

 (2) The records of a person designated to produce marijuana by a patient. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.792 & 475C.894 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.792 & 475C.894
