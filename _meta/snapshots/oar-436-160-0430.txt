 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 160 ELECTRONIC DATA INTERCHANGE; MEDICAL BILL DATA 
 436-160-0430 
 Medical Bill Data Changes 
 
 (1) Changes to medical bill information must be submitted according to the standards referenced in OAR 436-160-0004.  (2) The Unique Bill ID will be used to match cancellations, corrections, and replacements to the original bill. Failure to match on this data element will result in a rejected transaction.  (3) The insurer must correct and resubmit any transactions rejected for which law or rule requires filing, reporting, or notice to the director. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.264
