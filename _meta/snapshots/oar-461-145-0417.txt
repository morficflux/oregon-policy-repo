 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 145 TREATMENTS OF SPECIFIC ASSETS 
 461-145-0417 
 Railroad Retirement Payments; OSIP, OSIPM, and QMB 
 
 Railroad Retirement payments made by the Railroad Retirement Board are counted as unearned income. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.083, 411.404, 411.816 & 412.049 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.083, 411.404, 411.816 & 412.049 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SSP 44-2016, f. 12-7-16, cert. ef. 1-1-17
