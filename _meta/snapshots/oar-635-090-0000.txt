 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 ACCESS AND HABITAT PROGRAM 
 635-090-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules guide the implementation of the Access and Habitat Program authorized under Oregon Laws 1993, Chapter 659. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.300 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.300 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FWC 17-1994, f. & cert. ef. 3-10-94
