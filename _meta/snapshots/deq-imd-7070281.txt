Subject:       Revisions to the Regulatory Mixing Zone Internal Management Directive,
               Parts 1 and 2

This memorandum transmits Version 2.0 of the Regulatory Mixing Zone Internal Management
Directive (RMZ IMD), Parts 1 and 2 and is effective immediately.

The Regulatory Mixing Zone IMD was first issued in 2007. The current revisions are the first
significant revisions since that time. The need for some of the revisions was identified in the
Compliance Schedule Settlement Agreement, and was confirmed by regional staff.

The following individuals were principally involved in the revisions: Sonja Biorn-Hansen, Steve
Schnurbusch and Ranei Nomura.

The specific changes made to the document are described in Appendices E and D of parts 1 and
2 respectively.
