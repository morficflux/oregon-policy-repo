 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 LICENSES 
 808-003-0740 
 Translation of Exam 
 
 (1) Pursuant to ORS 671.570(3) & ORS 671.617(2) all exams will be given in the English language, unless otherwise requested as required in subsection (2) of this rule.  (2) An applicant must notify the exam provider if they wish to take any section of the exam in Spanish. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 671 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.570 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LCB 2-2016, f. & cert. ef. 5-23-16
