 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 PUBLIC DISPLAY OF FIREWORKS IN OREGON 
 837-012-1100 
 Penalty Adjustments
 
 
 (1) The assessment or adjustment of penalties for amounts other than those set by OAR 837-12-1050 may be done only by the State Fire Marshal through a hearings process either formally or informally. 

 (2) The assessment of penalties not in conformance with OAR 837-12-1050 may be made only after considering: 

 (a) The gravity and magnitude of the violation; 

 (b) The person's previous record; 

 (c) Such other considerations as the State Fire Marshal may consider appropriate. 

 (3) During a formal hearing or informal conference, the Department of the State Fire Marshal may modify or adjust the citation, cited violations, or penalties assessed in order to meet the requirements of these rules and to ensure uniformity and consistency in their application statewide. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.090 & 480.110 - 480.165 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 480.110 - 480.165
