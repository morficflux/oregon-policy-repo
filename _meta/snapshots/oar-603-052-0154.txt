 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 52 PEST AND DISEASE CONTROL 
 603-052-0154 
 Control Area 
 
 A control area as authorized by ORS 570.405 to 570.415 is hereby created for the control of Apple Maggot, San Jose Scale and Codling Moth within the boundaries of Wasco County, Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 570 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 561.190, 561.510 - 561.600, 570.305, 570.405 & 570.410 - 570.415 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 13-1981, f. 7-24-81, ef. 8-7-81
