 Division 225 BOILERS AND PRESSURE VESSELS 
 918-225-0435 
 Amendments to the Oregon Boiler and Pressure Vessel Specialty Code
 
 
 The Oregon Boiler and Pressure Vessel Specialty Code is amended pursuant to OAR chapter 918, division 8. Amendments adopted for inclusion into the Oregon Boiler and Pressure Vessel Specialty Code are placed in this rule.
