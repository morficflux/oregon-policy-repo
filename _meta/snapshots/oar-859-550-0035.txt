 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 550 JUVENILE PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD GENERAL PROCEDURES 
 859-550-0035 
 Motion Practice 
 
 Any party bringing a motion before the Board shall submit five copies of the motion and supporting memorandum of law to the Board and one to the opposing party no later than 10 days prior to the hearing date in which the motion will be heard. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387, OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.346 & 419C.532 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PSRB 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 9-28-10
