 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0043 
 Biggs Unit 
 
 The Biggs Unit, Number 43, is that area beginning at Arlington, south on State Highway 19 to Fossil; southwest along State Highway 218 to Shaniko; north on U.S. Highway 97 to Grass Valley; southwest on State Highway 216 to Tygh Valley; north on U.S. Highway 197 to the Columbia River; east along the Columbia River to Arlington, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496
