 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 COMMERCIAL SHELLFISH AND MARINE INVERTEBRATE FISHERIES 
 635-005-0355 
 Catch Limits 
 
 (1) In Tillamook Bay, the annual commercial landing caps for clams harvested by the bay clam dive fishery are as follows: 

 (a) 110,000 pounds for cockles; 

 (b) 260,000 pounds for gaper clams; and 

 (c) 260,000 pounds for butter clams. 

 (2) When any of the annual commercial clam landing caps specified in section (1) of this rule are reached, the commercial clam fishery for that species in that particular estuary will close for the remainder of the calendar year. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.119 & ORS 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109
