 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS 
 635-065-0655 
 Unit Boundary Requirements 
 
 Any person hunting in a season which is defined by a unit or units description shall abide by unit boundaries as described by the Oregon Wildlife Unit Map and descriptions under OAR 635, division 080. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 496 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183 & 496 
 
 History:
