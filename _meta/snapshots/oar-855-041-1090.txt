 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 OPERATION OF PHARMACIES 
 855-041-1090 
 Registration: Change of Business Name (Both Retail and Institutional Drug Outlets)
 
 
 A pharmacy must notify the board a minimum of 15 days prior to any change of business name of a pharmacy. The change must be reported by filing a new application for which no fee is required. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475.035 & ORS 689.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 689.205
