 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 69 LOTTERY RAFFLE GAME RULES 
 177-069-0020 
 Cancellation of Lottery Raffle Tickets 
 
 A Lottery raffle ticket once issued cannot be cancelled by a player or a retailer. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV & Sec. 4(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.210, 461.220, 461.240, 461.250 & 461.260 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LOTT 10-2008, f. 11-21-08, cert. ef. 12-1-08
