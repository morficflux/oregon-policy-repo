 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 162 ELECTRONIC DATA INTERCHANGE; PROOF OF COVERAGE 
 436-162-0040 
 Recognized Filing Date 
 
 (1) An electronic record is considered filed on the date it is delivered to the director only if the division issues a Transaction Accepted acknowledgment.  (2) Rejected transactions are not considered filed and do not satisfy proof of coverage requirements until they are corrected, resubmitted, and accepted by the division. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.264 & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.419, 656.423 & 656.427
