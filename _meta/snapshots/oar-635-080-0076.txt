 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0076 
 Silver Lake Unit 
 
 The Silver Lake Unit, number 76, is that area beginning at Silver Lake; southeast on State Highway 31 to Forest Road 29, 18 miles south of Summer Lake; west on Forest Road 29 to Forest Road 28; west on Forest Road 28 to Forest Road 30; west on Forest Road 30 to Pike's Crossing; downstream north and southwest along Sycan River to Powers Crossing (Teddy Powers Meadow); northwest on road 44 to Yamsey Guard Station at head of Williamson River; north and west along Williamson River to Silver Lake Road; east on Silver Lake Road through Bear Flat to State Highway 31; east on State Highway 31 to Silver Lake, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
