 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 IMPLIED CONSENT HEARING PROCEDURES 
 735-090-0130 
 Error of the Department
 
 
 In accordance with the definition of "Error of the Department" specified in OAR 735-090-0000(2), in a hearing that determines the validity of a suspension of driving privileges under ORS 813.410, if hearsay evidence is used to establish the required elements under ORS 813.410(7) and a petitioning party presents substantial evidence that contradicts the hearsay evidence, DMV will rescind the suspension and OAH will continue the hearing pursuant to 813.440(1)(c) and subpoena the hearsay witness to the continued hearing. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 802.010 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 813.410 & 813.440
