 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 930 PREARRANGEMENT AND PRECONSTRUCTION PLANS 
 441-930-0255 
 Reporting by Limited Operations Certified Provider 
 
 A limited operations certified provider must report the following activities to the director within 15 days of the occurrence:  (1) Transfer of any contract to another certified provider; or  (2) Sale of any new prearrangement or preconstruction contract. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 97.926 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 97.933 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FCS 14-2010, f. 12-30-10, cert. ef. 1-1-11
