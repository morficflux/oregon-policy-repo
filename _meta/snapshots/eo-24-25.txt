     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-25


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     SHOE FLY FIRE IN WHEELER COUNTY


     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, | find that:

     The fire known as the “Shoe Fly Fire” is burning in Wheeler County.


     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Shoe Fly Fire are
     beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
     was requested by the Wheeler County Fire Defense Board and the State Fire
     Marshal concurs with that request.


     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Shoe Fly Fire in Wheeler
     County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
     personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.


     These findings were made at 12:09 pm on September 6, 2024, with the Department
     of the State Fire Marshal. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Blue Incident
     Management Team will be coordinating resources with the Oregon Department of
     Forestry Team |. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Blue Incident Management Team
     will be briefed at 5:30 pm on September 6, 2024, and I now confirm them with this
     Executive Order.


     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


     I.     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
            the Shoe Fly Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

     2.     This conflagration is declared only for the Shoe Fly Fire threatening
            structures in Wheeler County.


     3;     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 12:09 pm on the 6" day of
            September 2024 and signed this 7" day of September 2024, in Salem,
            Oregon.
