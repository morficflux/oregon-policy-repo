 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 57 PESTICIDE CONTROL 
 603-057-0216 
 Pesticide Residue Tolerances in Foods 
 
 The rules governing residue tolerances of pesticides permitted on raw agricultural products and the exemptions from such tolerances, adopted by the United States Environmental Protection Agency and in effect as of the effective date of this rule, are hereby adopted by the Department as the rules governing residue tolerances of pesticides permitted on raw agricultural products and exemptions from such tolerances in the State of Oregon. Said federal rules are set forth in Title 40 Code of Federal Regulations (CFR), Chapter 1, Part 180. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 634 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 634.306 & 634.042
