 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 130 STANDARDIZED-COSTS SCHEDULES 
 837-130-0010 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) “Unprotected Area” shall mean an area outside the boundaries of recognized public or private fire protection.  (2) “Transportation Route” shall mean a roadway, waterway, railroad right-of-way or airplane route against which no taxes or assessments for fire protection are levied by the municipality, fire district, or fire protection agency. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 476.280 & 478.310 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FM 1-1993, f. 5-13-93, cert. ef. 6-1-93
