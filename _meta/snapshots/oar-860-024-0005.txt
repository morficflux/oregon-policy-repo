 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 SAFETY STANDARDS 
 860-024-0005 
 Maps and Records
 
 
 (1) Each utility shall keep on file current maps and records of the entire plant showing size, location, character, and date of installation of major plant items. 

 (2) Upon request, each utility shall file with the Commission an adequate description or maps to define the territory served. Maps must include all recently identified High Fire Risk Zones. All maps and records which the Commission may require the utility to file shall be in a form satisfactory to the Commission Staff. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, ORS 756 & ORS 757 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040 & ORS 757.020
