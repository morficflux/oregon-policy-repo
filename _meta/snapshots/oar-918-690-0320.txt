 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 690 PLUMBING ADMINISTRATION AND DEFINITIONS 
 918-690-0320 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these regulations is to provide minimum standards to safeguard life or limb, property, and public welfare, by regulating and controlling the design, construction, quality of materials, use and occupancy, location and maintenance, of all plumbing systems and plumbing fixtures in all buildings or structures within this state, medical gas and medical vacuum systems, solar heating and cooling systems and certain other plumbing systems and plumbing fixtures specifically regulated herein. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 447.010 & 447.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 447.010 & 447.020
