 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 34 LOG SCALING AGREEMENTS AND SCALING FACILITY REQUIREMENTS 
 629-034-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of this rule is to:  (1) Require a TPSO to enter into an agreement with the State before the TPSO is authorized to scale log loads sold under state timber sale contracts;  (2) Outline scaling facility requirements which will promote consistent, accurate scaling of state log loads; and  (3) Protect the interest of the people of Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 530 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 530.010, 530.020 & 530.050 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FB 7-1988, f. & cert. ef. 9-9-88
