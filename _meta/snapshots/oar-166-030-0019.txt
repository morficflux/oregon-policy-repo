 Secretary of State 
 Archives Division - Chapter 166 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 RECORDS MANAGEMENT PROCEDURES PROGRAMS 
 166-030-0019 
 Written Policies 
 
 State Agencies must develop and implement written policies that set forth the agency’s use, access, retention and ownership of public records. These policies, including any subsequent amendments, must be submitted to the State Archivist for approval per ORS 192.018. Model policies are available from the State Archivist. Although not required, the State Archives recommends the use of such policies by all agencies, including local governments. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192 & ORS 357 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 357.855 & 357.895
