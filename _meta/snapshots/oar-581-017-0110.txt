 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0110 
 Eligibility 
 
 The Department of Education shall allocate funds for the Expanded Reading Opportunities Grant to schools based on the following eligibility criteria:  (1) Schools must be a designated Focus or Priority School.  (2) Schools must serve students in at least one grade from kindergarten through grade 3.  (3) Schools must not have received a SIG grant.  (4) Schools must use the funds in alignment with their CAP.  (5) Schools must use the funds to extend or expand reading opportunities by:  (a) Providing adult support;  (b) Offering programs during non-school hours; or  (c) Increasing access to technology. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2013 OL Ch. 660 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3232) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2013 OL Ch. 660 & Sec. 1 (Enrolled HB 3232)
