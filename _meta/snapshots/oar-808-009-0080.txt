 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 CONTESTED CASE HEARINGS AND APPEAL COMMITTEE 
 808-009-0080 
 Administrative Law Judge 
 
 A contested case hearing may be held before an administrative law judge of the agency. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 670.310 & 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183 & 671
