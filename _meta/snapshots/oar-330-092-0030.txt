 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 92 MINIMUM ENERGY EFFICIENCY STANDARDS FOR STATE-REGULATED APPLIANCES AND EQUIPMENT 
 330-092-0030 
 Effect of Listing
 
 
 To be sold, offered for sale, or installed in Oregon on or after the effective dates described in OAR 330-092-0015, Oregon-regulated products must be listed per the requirements of OAR 330-092-0025. Products that are not listed according to these rules may not be sold, offered for sale, or installed in Oregon on or after the applicable effective date. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.040, ORS 469.255 & ORS 469.261 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.229–469.261
