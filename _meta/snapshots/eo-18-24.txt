    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-24


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    HUGO FIRE IN JOSEPHINE COUNTY


    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Hugo Fire” is burning in Josephine County.


    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Hugo Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by Josephine County Fire Defense Board Chief Johnson. The State
    Fire Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510 et seg., I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the Hugo Fire in Josephine
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 12:16 a.m. on September 3, 2018 and I now confirm
    them with this Executive Order.


    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


    1.     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to Hugo Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.


    2.     This emergency is declared only for the Hugo Fire threatening structures
           near the town of Hugo in Josephine County.


    3.     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 12:16 a.m. the 3rd day of
         - September 2018 and signed this Gh day of September, 2018, in Salem,
           Oregon.
