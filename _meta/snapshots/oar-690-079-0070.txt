 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 79 RESERVATIONS OF WATER FOR FUTURE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT 
 690-079-0070 
 Notice of Reservation Request 
 
 Within 30 days of receiving a request for reservation that is deemed complete by the Director, the Director shall notify:  (1) The Department of Fish and Wildlife, Department of Environmental Quality, Parks and Recreation Department, Economic Development Department, other affected federal and state agencies and the planning department(s) of affected local governments;  (2) Affected Indian tribes; and  (3) All persons on the Department’s weekly mail list and reservations mail list. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197, 536.025, 536.027, 536.220, 536.300, 536.310, 537.338 & 537.356 - 537.358
