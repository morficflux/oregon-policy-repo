 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 91 CHARGES FOR COPIES AND SERVICES 
 141-091-0020 
 Fees Waived 
 
 Charges may be waived in the following instances:  (1) Routine requests from state, local, and federal agencies;  (2) When charges would total $.50 or less;  (3) Copies of current leases, permits, etc., requested by lessee, permittee, mortgagor, their insurance company, financial institution, etc.;  (4) Documents needed by applicants for leases and permits, etc., such as administrative rules, lease forms, application forms. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192 & 273 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192 & 273 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LB 11-1982, f. & ef. 12-20-82
