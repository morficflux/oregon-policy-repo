 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 93 ADMINISTRATIVE RULES GOVERNING THE ISSUANCE AND ENFORCEMENT OF GENERAL PERMITS WITHIN WATERS OF THIS STATE 
 141-093-0235 
 Maintenance Drainage - Authorized Activities
 
 
 Maintenance of Drainage. The annual removal of up to one hundred cubic yards of material below OHW or HMT for jurisdictional waterways for maintaining existing drainage to protect agricultural land and the placement of up to one hundred cubic yards of material in converted wetlands as a result of the project, when upland disposal is not practicable. Placement of material in converted wetlands must not result in creation of upland. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.692 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.816
