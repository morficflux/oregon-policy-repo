 Department of State Police 
 Chapter 257 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 SEX OFFENDER REGISTRATION 
 257-070-0130 
 Internet Notification of Level Three Sex Offenders
 
 
 The Department shall utilize the sex offender registry public website to make information available to the public regarding level three sex offenders currently recorded on the Registry and not otherwise except from publication unless or until: 

 (1) The Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision notifies the Department that the level three designation for the sex offender is incorrect; 

 (2) The level three sex offender is assessed and classified by the Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision as either a level one or level two sex offender; 

 (3) The offender is removed from the Registry; or 

 (4) The offender becomes exempt from publication due to coming under the supervision of the Psychiatric Security Review Board. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 163A.215 & ORS 163A.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 163A.005-163A.235
