 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 BOARD ADMINISTRATION; PUBLIC RECORDS; PARKING 
 629-010-0030 
 Agendas 
 
 The Secretary shall send an agenda together with the minutes of previously held committee meetings and the minutes of the last Board meeting to all members of the Board prior to each Board meeting. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.310(1), 192.610 - 192.710, 526.016(4) & 526.041(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.610 - 192.710 & 526.016(4)
