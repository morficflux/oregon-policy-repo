Oregon Health Authority

Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309

Division 39
STANDARDS FOR THE APPROVAL OF PROVIDERS OF NON-INPATIENT MENTAL HEALTH TREATMENT SERVICES

309-039-0500
Purpose and Scope

These rules apply to certifications of provider organizations that render non-inpatient mental health treatment services. The certifications exist solely for the purpose of qualifying for insurance reimbursement. Agencies that contract with the Oregon Health Authority (OHA), subcontract with OHA, or contract with a Community Mental Health Program are not eligible for the “non-inpatient” certification.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.042 & 743A.168
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 743A.160 & 743.168
