 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 SPECIAL FOREST PRODUCTS; WOODCUTTING PERMITTING ON STATE FOREST LANDS 
 629-028-0025 
 Requirements of Permittees of Woodcutting Permits on State Forest Lands 
 
 Each permittee shall observe the following requirements:  (1) Permittee shall have the permit in possession when cutting or removing firewood.  (2) The firewood is for personal use and permittee shall not sell, bargain, or otherwise obtain monetary or material profit from the fuelwood obtained through a permit  (3) Permittee shall perform the cutting and removal of firewood in compliance with the laws of the State of Oregon, and requirements of the Forester for the protection and conservation of the lands. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 530 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 530.500
