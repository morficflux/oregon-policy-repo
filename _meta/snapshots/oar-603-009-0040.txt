 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 OREGON AGRICULTURAL OPPORTUNITIES FUND 
 603-009-0040 
 Application Procedures 
 
 (1) Application forms for the competitive statewide grants program will be made available and distributed by the Department.  (2) Each applicant shall submit its proposal for funding on the approved application form, including attachments as necessary. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 576 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576.013 
 
 History:
