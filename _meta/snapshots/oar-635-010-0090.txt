 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 LICENSING - PROCEDURES FOR ESTABLISHING, OPERATING, DISCONTINUING AND SUSPENDING LICENSES AND AGENTS 
 635-010-0090 
 Lost Prepaid Documents
 
 
 An agent is responsible for lost prepaid license, permit or parking documents. No refunds will be issued to the agent, except as indicated in OAR 635-010-0025. The agent must immediately notify the Department and the local State Police office of any loss. 

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 & 497 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496 & 497
