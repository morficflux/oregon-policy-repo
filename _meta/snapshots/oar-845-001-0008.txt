 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 845-001-0008 
 Confidentiality and Inadmissibility of Mediation Communications
 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 36.224, OAR 137-005-0052 is hereby adopted by reference as the permanent rule for mediation confidentiality. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 36.224 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 36.224, 36.228, 36.230 & 36.232
