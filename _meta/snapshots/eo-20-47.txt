    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-47


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    NORTH CASCADE COMPLEX FIRE IN CLACKAMAS COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “North Cascades Complex Fire” is burning in Clackamas
    County.


    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the North Cascades
    Complex Fire are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and
    structural fire protection was requested by the Clackamas County Fire Defense
    Board, and the State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510-.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the North Cascades Complex Fire
    in Clackamas County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
    firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 6:30 am on September 9, 2020 with Office of State
    Fire Marshal and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    li     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to the North Cascades Complex Fire may be redistributed by the
           State Fire Marshal.


    2,    | This conflagration is declared only for the North Cascades Complex Fire
           threatening structures in Clackamas County.

    ay     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 6:30 am on the 9" day of
           September, 2020 and signed this 15" day of September, 2020, in Salem,
           Oregon.
