 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 VOCATIONAL ASSISTANCE TO INJURED WORKERS 
 436-120-0187 
 Optional Services 
 
 (1) Optional services are services provided to:  (a) A worker who is not eligible for vocational assistance; or  (b) A worker who is eligible for vocational assistance, in excess of the services described in these rules.  (2) Optional services are provided at the discretion of the insurer.  (3) The insurer may not use optional services to circumvent the intent of these rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.340(9) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.340(7)
