 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS 
 808-002-0100 
 Definitions Generally 
 
 Except where the context requires otherwise, the definitions in this division govern the interpretation of ORS 671.510 to 671.710, 671.955 and OAR chapter 808. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.325 - 183.410, 670.310 & 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.510 - 671.990
