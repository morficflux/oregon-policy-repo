 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF RECREATION PARKS 
 333-031-0020 
 Supervision 
 
 (1) The management shall maintain buildings, grounds, rental units, spaces, and furnishings in good repair and appearance and in clean condition.  (2) Either the owner, an operator, a resident manager, a night clerk, or other such supervisor shall be available on the premises while it is open for use. In lieu thereof, there shall be posted on the premises the name and location of a representative who will be responsible for the operation of the establishment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.330 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.330
