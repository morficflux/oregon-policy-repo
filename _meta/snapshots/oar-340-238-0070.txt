 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 238 NEW SOURCE PERFORMANCE STANDARDS 
 340-238-0070 
 Compliance
 
 
 Compliance with standards set forth in this division shall be determined by performance tests and monitoring methods as set forth in the Federal Regulation adopted by reference in OAR 340-238-0060. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025
