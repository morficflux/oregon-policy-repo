 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 69 LONG TERM CARE ASSESSMENT 
 411-069-0170 
 Sunset Provision
 
 
 The long term care assessment applies to long term care facility gross revenue received on or after June 2003 and before July 1, 2032. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, 410.070 & 411.060 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.750, OL 2003, ch 736 & OL 2018, ch 66
