 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 VETERANS AND WAR MEMORIAL GRANTS 
 736-017-0015 
 Director Authority 
 
 (1) In addition to those considered by the committee, the director may recommend eligible projects to the commission for grant funding. The director may recommend grants for construction and restoration.  (2) The director may recommend funding eligible projects either in whole or in part.  (3) The director may establish minimum or maximum grant award amounts each funding cycle.  (4) The director may establish funding priorities or other criteria for each funding cycle. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.124 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.180(1)(d) 
 
 History:
