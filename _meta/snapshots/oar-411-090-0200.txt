 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 NURSING FACILITIES/LICENSING - VENTILATOR ASSISTED PROGRAM (VAP) 
 411-090-0200 
 Exceptions 
 
 Requests for exceptions to these rules shall be reviewed by the Department and processed in accordance with these rules and the licensing rules in OAR chapter 411, divisions 85 through 88. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, 410.070, 441.055, 441.615 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.050, 410.070, 441.055, 441.615 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 APD 9-2019, adopt filed 01/31/2019, effective 02/01/2019
