 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 CRIMINAL INTELLIGENCE UNIT 
 137-090-0140 
 Security 
 
 Because security and protection of the materials in the criminal intelligence file is of utmost importance, the following procedures shall be observed:  (1) Policy: All CIU employees shall be thoroughly familiar with access and dissemination policies of this chapter. All other persons authorized to access criminal intelligence information as provided in these rules shall agree to follow procedures regarding information access, security, and dissemination which are consistent with these rules.  (2) Access: Direct access to the CIU’s criminal intelligence files is limited to CIU file section employees, the CIUS and personnel of criminal justice agencies as approved by the CIUS. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 180 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 180.610(2), (3), (4) & 28 CFR §23.20
