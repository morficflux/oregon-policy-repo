 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES, ATTORNEY FEES, AND GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 436-001-0430 
 Attorney Fees Under ORS 656.262(12) (Late Payment of DCS Proceeds)
 
 
 The matrix for determining the amount of the attorney fee under ORS 656.262(12) is in OAR 436-060, Appendix C (OAR 436-060-0400). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.262
