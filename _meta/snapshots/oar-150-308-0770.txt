 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ASSESSMENT OF PROPERTY FOR TAXATION 
 150-308-0770 
 Real and Personal Manufactured Dwellings to be Assessed in Like Manner 
 
 All manufactured dwellings are assessable.  (1) Under ORS 308.875, the owner of a personal property manufactured dwelling need not file a personal property return on the structure.  (2) The personal property assessment cancellation provided in ORS 308.250 does not apply to such dwellings.  (3) They shall be assessed at 100 percent of real market value. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 308.875
