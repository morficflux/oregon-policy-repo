 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 36 WATER UTILITIES 
 860-036-2100 
 Form and Filing of Financial Transaction Applications 
 
 (1) All applications submitted to the Commission must be filed electronically with the Commission's Filing Center. To file an application, attach the application and all required documents to an electronic mail message addressed to the Commission's Filing Center at puc.filingcenter@state.or.us. Procedural rules regarding filing and service of documents are found in OAR Chapter 860, Division 001.  (2) Applications must be filled out completely.  (3) Any required financial statements must include the most current data available. The Income Statement must be for the most recent 12-month period. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756 & 757 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040 & 756.105 
 
 History:
