 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 310 FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT 
 413-310-0210 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) “Fraud”: Intentional acts, claims, statements, or payments resulting in misrepresentation and/or willful withholding of material facts for the purpose of deceiving.  (2) “Unintentional Error”: Errors in payments or billings made without intent to deceive. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 293.235 - 293.245 & 42 CFR 433
