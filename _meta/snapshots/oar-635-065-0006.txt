 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS 
 635-065-0006 
 General License Requirement 
 
 A person may obtain and possess only one valid annual hunting license per calendar year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 496 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183 & 496
