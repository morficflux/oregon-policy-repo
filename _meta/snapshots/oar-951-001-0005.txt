 Oregon Film and Video Office 
 Chapter 951 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 951-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure, OAR 137-001-0007 through 137-001-0080; and 137-004-0010 through 137-004-0800 as adopted by the Attorney General of the State of Oregon under the Administrative Procedures Act, effective October 1, 2001, are adopted as the rules of procedure for rulemaking and declaratory rulings for the Oregon Film & Video Office. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 284.300 - 284.315 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 284.300 - 284.315 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FVO 2-2004, f. 3-12-04, cert. ef. 4-15-04
