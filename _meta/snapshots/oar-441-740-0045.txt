 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 740 PAWNBROKERS 
 441-740-0045 
 Advertising 
 
 (1) A licensed pawnbroker must use its Oregon license number in any paid advertising.  (2) Without a pawnbroker license, a business may not use an assumed business name or advertising of a business that would lead the public to believe the business is a licensed pawnbroker, including use of "pawn," "hawk," "hock," or any synonym of any of those terms, in the assumed business name or in advertising referring to the business, whether used as a single word or part of a word. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 726.260 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 726.040 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FCS 11-2008, f. & cert. ef. 11-13-08
