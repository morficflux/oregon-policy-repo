 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 OCEAN CHARTER VESSELS 
 250-015-0002 
 Applicability 
 
 (1) This division is applicable to all charter boats, carrying passengers for hire, on waters of this state.  (2) The rules in this Division are in addition to and not in lieu of any other applicable federal laws or regulations. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830.110 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.430 - 830.460
