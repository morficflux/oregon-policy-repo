 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 WATER SUPPLY WELL CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS: INTRODUCTION, GENERAL STANDARDS, AND DEFINITIONS 
 690-200-0046 
 Perched Ground Water
 
 
 Wells drawing water from perched zones must be constructed to prevent the waste of this type of ground water. (See Figure 200-2).
