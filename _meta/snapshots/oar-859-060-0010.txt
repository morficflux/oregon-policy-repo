 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 PRINCIPAL ISSUES BEFORE THE ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD 
 859-060-0010 
 Primary Concern: Protection of Society
 
 
 In determining whether a person should be committed to a state hospital, conditionally released or discharged, the Board shall have as its primary concern the protection of society. The Board shall not discharge a person whose qualifying mental disorder may, with reasonable medical probability, occasionally become active, and when active, render the person a danger to others. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.336, 161.341, 161.346 & 161.351
