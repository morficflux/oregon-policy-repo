    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 22-20


    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    CEDAR CREEK FIRE IN LANE COUNTY


    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “Cedar Creek Fire” is burning in Lane County.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from Cedar Creek Fire are
    beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire protection
    was requested by the Lane County Fire Defense Board Chief and the State Fire
    Marshal concurs with that request.


    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Cedar Creek Fire in Lane
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 1:11 p.m. on September 9, 2022. The Office of State
    Fire Marshal Red Incident Management Team is mobilizing to the Cedar Creek
    Fire. They will also enter into unified command for the Cedar Creek Fire. [ now
    confirm these findings with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    1.     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to the Cedar Creek Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
           Marshal.


    Ds     This conflagration is declared only for the Cedar Creek Fire threatening
           structures in Lane County.


    3      This order was made by verbal proclamation at 1:11 p.m. on September 9,
           2022, and signed the same day, in Salem, Oregon.
