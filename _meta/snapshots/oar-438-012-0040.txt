 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 BOARD’S OWN MOTION JURISDICTION 
 438-012-0040 
 Action by Board after Insurer Recommendation 
 
 Except as provided in OAR 438-012-0050, within a reasonable time after receipt of the insurer’s recommendation and supporting evidence and any additional evidence and argument from the claimant the Board may:  (1) Issue its order based upon the evidence and argument submitted by the parties;  (2) Request additional evidence from one or more of the parties; or  (3) Refer the matter to the Hearings Division for an evidentiary hearing and recommended findings of fact and conclusions. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.278(1) & 656.726(4)
