 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 27 DOMESTIC INSURERS; ORGANIZATION; CORPORATE PROCEDURES 
 836-027-0140 
 Enterprise Risk Report
 
 
 The ultimate controlling person of an insurer required to file an enterprise risk report under ORS 732.569 shall furnish the required information on Form F. Form F is set forth on the website of the Department of Consumer and Business Services at dfr.oregon.gov. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 732.705 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 732.517 - 732.592
