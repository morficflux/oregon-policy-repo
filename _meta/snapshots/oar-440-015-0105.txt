 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Director's Office - Chapter 440 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 CONFLICT OF INTEREST 
 440-015-0105 
 Definitions 
 
 For the purpose of these rules:  (1) “Department” means the Department of Consumer and Business Services.  (2) “Director” means the director of the Department.  (3) “Designated position” means a position in the Department listed or designated under OAR 440-015-0110(2). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 705.135 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 244 & 705.135 
 
 History:
