 Division 305 GENERAL ADMINISTRATION OF REVENUE LAWS 
 150-305-0200 
 Adjustments Included in Deficiency Notice When Federal Audit Report Received 
 
 See OAR 150-314-0224 which explains what adjustments can be included in a Notice of Deficiency when a federal audit report is received.
