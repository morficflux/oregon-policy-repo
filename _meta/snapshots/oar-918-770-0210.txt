 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 770 PLUMBING PRODUCT APPROVALS 
 918-770-0210 
 Submission of Plumbing Products for Approval by Special Deputy 
 
 Persons seeking approval of plumbing products by a special deputy shall provide the special deputy with a sample of the product for which approval is sought or a site-specific location where the product may be reviewed. The applicant shall also provide the special deputy with design or shop drawings, engineering data and any other test data that the special deputy determines to be reasonably necessary to evaluate the plumbing product for which approval is sought. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 447.152 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 447.152
