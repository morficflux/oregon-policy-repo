 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 LICENSING 
 812-003-0190 
 New Bond, Letter of Credit or Cash Deposit Required for Change in Entity 
 
 (1) If an entity licenses as a sole proprietorship, partnership, limited liability partnership, limited partnership, joint venture, corporation, limited liability company, business trust or any other entity and seeks to change the licensed entity to one of the other entity types, the application must be accompanied by a new:  (a) Bond separate from the bond held for the previous entity;  (b) Letter of credit separate from the letter of credit held for the previous entity; or  (c) Cash deposit separate from the previous cash deposit held for the previous entity.  (2) Riders to existing bonds changing the type of entity bonded will be construed as a cancellation of the bond and will not be otherwise accepted. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 701.068, 701.088 & 701.235 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701.068 & 701.088
