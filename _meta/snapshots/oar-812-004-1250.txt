 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 COMPLAINTS 
 812-004-1250 
 Payments From the Bond for Court Costs, Complaint Processing Fee, Interest and Other Costs 
 
 (1) For complaints filed under ORS 701.145, the agency may include the following costs in the amount of a judgment that is subject to payment by a surety or financial institution:  (a) Court costs;  (b) Interest;  (c) Costs, other than attorney fees, to pursue litigation or the complaint; or  (d) Service charges or fees.  (2) For complaints filed under ORS 701.145, the agency’s determination of payment due from a surety or financial institution may not include amounts arising out of claims for anything other than construction work involving negligence, improper work or breach of contract. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 701.235 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  OAR 701.068, 701.088, 701.133, 701.145 & 701.146
