 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS 
 635-065-0750 
 Disguising Sex, Waste, and Sale 
 
 It is unlawful:  (1) To disguise the sex or kind of any wildlife while in the field or in transit from the field.  (2) To waste any game mammal or parts thereof, except that the meat of cougar is considered inedible and need not be salvaged. The hide of a cougar must be sealed and retained.  (3) To sell or offer for sale, barter, or exchange, any game mammals or parts thereof except deer, elk, and antelope hides, and shed or legally taken cervid antlers used for handcrafted items and cervid antlers which have been handcrafted as described in OAR 635-200-0050. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
