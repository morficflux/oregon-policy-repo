 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 130 ENERGY EFFICIENT DESIGN AND OPERATION FOR STATE AGENCY FACILITIES 
 330-130-0055 
 Procedure for Leased Buildings 
 
 The department, in consultation with the agencies, shall establish guidelines for incorporating energy efficiency requirements into lease agreements of 10 years or more to be phased in as current leases expire or as agencies enter into new agreements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Ch. 26 & OL 2008 HB 3612 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 276.900 - 276.915
