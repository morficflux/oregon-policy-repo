 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 VEHICLES, DRIVER, EQUIPMENT: EQUIPMENT REQUIRED AND CONDITION OF VEHICLES 
 740-100-0130 
 Limitations Regarding Transportation of Standees 
 
 A for-hire carrier of passengers may transport standees only if:  (1) The motor vehicle is equipped with devices specifically designed to provide stability for each standee.  (2) A standee does not block required exits or create undue crowding.  (3) No standee is transported a distance greater than 25 miles. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 823 & 825 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.210 & 825.252
