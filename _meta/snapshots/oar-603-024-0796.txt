 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS OF IDENTITY, LABELING AND OTHER REGULATIONS RELATING TO FLUID MILK AND DAIRY PRODUCTS 
 603-024-0796 
 Nondairy Frozen Dessert Mix: Identity 
 
 Nondairy Frozen Dessert Mix is the food, in a dry or concentrated form, used in the manufacture of a nondairy frozen dessert, which is prepared in accordance with the requirements of OAR 603-024-0795. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 621 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 621.311 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 8-1985, f. & ef. 9-20-85
