 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 FOREST ACTIVITIES 
 437-007-0730 
 Loading Machines 
 
 (1) Grapple arms or other positive means of keeping logs on the forks must be used on forklift type log handling and loading machines.  (2) Log loading machines must be equipped with an audible signaling device of a different tone than other signaling devices in the area. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSHA 5-2003, f. 6-2-03, cert. ef. 12-1-03
