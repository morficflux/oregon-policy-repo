 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 ELIGIBILITY GROUPS 
 461-110-0430 
 Filing Group; REF, REFM
 
 
 In the Refugee Assistance (REF) and Refugee Assistance Medical (REFM) programs: 

 (1) The filing group consists of an adult  (see section (2) of this rule) applicant and the following members of the household group  (see OAR 461-110-0210): 

 (a) The spouse (see OAR 461-001-0000) of an applicant. 

 (b) Each dependent child (see OAR 461-001-0000) of an adult in the filing group. 

 (2) For purposes of this rule, an "adult" means an individual who is not a dependent child (see OAR 461-001-0000) in a TANF filing group (see OAR 461-110-0330). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, 411.060, 411.070, 411.116, 412.006 & 412.049 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.010, 409.050, 411.060, 411.070, 411.116, 411.060, 411.070, 411.116, 412.006, 412.049, 45 CFR 400, 409.010, 412.049 & 412.006
