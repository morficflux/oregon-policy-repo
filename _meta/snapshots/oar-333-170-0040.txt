 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 170 BED AND BREAKFAST FACILITIES 
 333-170-0040 
 Employee Change Rooms 
 
 Bed and Breakfast Facilities shall be exempt from the provisions of OAR 333-150-0000 sections 6-305.11, 6-403.11 (B) and 6-501.110 provided, however, that no person shall change clothes, store clothing or personal effects in any area used for the storage or preparation of food or for utensil washing or storage. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 624.041 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 624.041
