 Department of Human Services 
 Chapter 407 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 12 RESTRICTING ACCESS TO DEPARTMENT OF HUMAN SERVICES PREMISES AND EMPLOYEES 
 407-012-0015 
 Continuation of Eligible Services 
 
 (1) An individual whose access has been restricted by the Department will continue to be provided services for which the individual meets program eligibility requirements by an alternate and effective method of communication as determined by the Department.  (2) Alternate methods may include telephone, electronic mail, written communication, meeting at a designated secure site, or through the individual’s representative. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.050 & 654.010 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DHSD 11-2007, f. 11-30-07, cert. ef. 12-1-07
