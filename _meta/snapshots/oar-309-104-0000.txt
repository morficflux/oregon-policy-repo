 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 104 TELEPHONE USE BY PATIENTS AND RESIDENTS IN STATE INSTITUTIONS 
 309-104-0000 
 Purpose and Statutory Authority 
 
 (1) Purpose. These rules prescribe policy and procedures for the use of telephones by patients and residents in state institutions.  (2) Statutory Authority. These rules are authorized by ORS 179.040 and 413.042 and carry out the provisions of 426.385 and 427.031. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040 & 413.042 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 MHD 28-1982, f. 12-28-82, ef. 1-28-83
