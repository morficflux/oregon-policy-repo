 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 58 FEEDS 
 603-058-0260 
 Certificates of Free Sale 
 
 (1) To enhance trade with foreign countries the department will provide certificates of free sale when requested by firms. Certificates are provided at no cost. Multiple feed products may be put on each certificate.  (2) Certificates will be provided within 30 calendar days of initial request. Product must be registered to sell in Oregon when the certificate request is made. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 633.006-089, 633.992, 561.605 & 561.620 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DOA 13-2011, f. & cert. ef. 8-12-11
