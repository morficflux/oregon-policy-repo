 Secretary of State 
 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 BUSINESS REGISTRY SECTION 
 160-010-0014 
 Prohibitions 
 
 (1) An entity identifier, except for “company” and abbreviations thereof, cannot be used with an assumed business name, unless all the registrants on the assumed business name are entities identified in the name.  (2) The Secretary of State Business Registry office shall not approve requested names that imply in any way that the business is an agency of the state, or any of its political subdivisions, without proof of authorization to register such a name. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 56, 58, 60, 62, 63, 65, 68, 70, 128, 183, 554 & 648 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 58.085, 60.094, 62.131, 63.094, 65.094, 68.735, 70.010, 128.580; 554.005 & 648.051
