 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 101 IDENTIFICATION AND LISTING OF HAZARDOUS WASTE 
 340-101-0030 
 Chemical Agent Munitions and Chemical Agent Bulk Items 
 
 Notwithstanding any otherwise applicable provisions of 40 C.F.R. Parts 260 to 268, 270, or other provisions of these rules, chemical agent munitions and chemical agent bulk items in storage as of the effective date of this rule are residues, and listed hazardous wastes assigned the appropriate waste codes in OAR 340-102-0011(2)(c)(A)(i) and (ii). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 466.005, 466.010 - 466.035, 466.625 & 466.630 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.205 - 466.225, 466.605 - 466.680, 468.005 - 468.075 & 468.090 - 468.140
