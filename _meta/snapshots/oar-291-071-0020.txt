 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 THERAPEUTIC RESTRAINTS (USE OF) 
 291-071-0020 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) Clinical Record: The record of mental health treatment provided to an inmate. This record is maintained as part of the inmate’s individual medical record.  (2) Inmate: Any person under the supervision of the Department of Corrections who is not on parole, probation or post-prison supervision status.  (3) Therapeutic Restraint: A type of restraint applied to an inmate for medical or mental health treatment and designed to limit an inmate’s movements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 History:
