 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 CONSTRUCTION 
 437-003-0015 
 Drinking Water 
 
 (1) Potable water means water meeting the bacteriological and chemical quality requirements prescribed in the OAR chapter 333, division 61, Public Water Systems, of the Oregon State Health Division.  (2) In addition to and not in lieu of any provisions in 1926.51(a), drinking water containers shall be constructed of materials that maintain water quality, shall be refilled daily or more often as necessary, shall be kept covered, and shall be regularly cleaned. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295
