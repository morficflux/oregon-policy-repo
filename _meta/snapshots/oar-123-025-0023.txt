 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 PORT PLANNING AND MARKETING FUND 
 123-025-0023 
 Grant Awards and Match 
 
 (1) The maximum grant is $50,000 or 75% of the total project cost, whichever is less.  (2) Grants will be awarded only when there are sufficient funds available in the fund. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285A.654 - 285A.660
