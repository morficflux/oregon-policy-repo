 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 RULEMAKING; ATTORNEY GENERAL’S MODEL RULES 
 690-001-0005 
 Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure, Adoption
 
 
 The Water Resources Commission adopted the January 1, 2008 edition of the Attorney General's Uniform and Model Rules for Rulemaking. The Water Resources Department and the Water Resources Commission must follow the Uniform and Model Rules in all matters, except where a different procedure is prescribed by statute. 

 [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the agency.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025, 536.027 & 183.341 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
