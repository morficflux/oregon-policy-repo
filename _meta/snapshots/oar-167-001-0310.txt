 Secretary of State 
 Business Services Division - Chapter 167 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 167-001-0310 
 Pre-Closing Withdrawal of Offers 
 
 Notwithstanding OAR 137-047-0440(2)(a) and prior to Closing, Offeror may modify or withdraw its Offer via OregonBuys, or subsequent system, using the system functionality. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Oregon Constitution, Article VI, Sections 2 and 5; ORS 279A.065 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BSD 2-2019, adopt filed 12/02/2019, effective 12/02/2019
