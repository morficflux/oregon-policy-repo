 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 ADOPTION; DEFINITIONS; CONSTRUCTION; NOTICES REQUIRED 
 438-005-0005 
 Statutory Authority 
 
 These rules are adopted under the Board's general rulemaking authority of ORS 656.726(5) to provide rules of practice and procedure for hearing and review proceedings under 656.001 to 656.990, for exercising its continuing authority under 656.278 and providing for the payment of attorney fees in cases under the Workers' Compensation Law pursuant to 656.388. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.726(5)
