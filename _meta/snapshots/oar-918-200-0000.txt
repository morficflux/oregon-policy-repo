 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 AMUSEMENT RIDE AND DEVICE SPECIALTY CODE 
 918-200-0000 
 General Title 
 
 OAR 918, division 200 shall be known and may be cited as the “Amusement Ride and Device Specialty Code.”  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 460.355 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 460.355
