 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 46 MODEL RULES GENERAL PROVISIONS RELATED TO PUBLIC CONTRACTING 
 137-046-0200 
 Notice to Governor’s Policy Advisor for Economic and Business Equity 
 
 In accordance with ORS 200.035, State Contracting Agencies shall provide timely notice of all Procurements and Contract Awards to the Governor’s Policy Advisor for Economic and Business Equity if the estimated Contract Price exceeds $10,000. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 279A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 200.035, OL 2015 & ch 325 (HB 2716)
