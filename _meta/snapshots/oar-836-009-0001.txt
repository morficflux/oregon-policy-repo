 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 FEES AND CHARGES 
 836-009-0001 
 Purpose
 
 
 The purpose of rules in OAR chapter 836, division 9 is to establish assessments, fees and charges for administering the regulatory program of the Division of Financial Regulation, Department of Consumer and Business Services. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335, 293.445 & 731.804 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.335(7) & 731.804(1)-(2)
