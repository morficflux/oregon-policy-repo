 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION 
 333-120-0740 
 Reports to Individuals Exceeding Dose Limits 
 
 When a licensee or registrant is required, pursuant to the provisions of OAR 333-120-0720 or 333-120-0730, to report to the Authority any exposure of an identified occupationally exposed individual or an identified member of the public to radiation or radioactive material, the licensee or registrant must also provide the individual a report on his or her exposure data included in the report submitted to the Authority. This report must be transmitted at a time no later than the transmittal to the Authority. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
