 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 VEHICLE WEIGHT AND DIMENSION LIMITS — PERMITS 
 734-070-0100 
 Continuous Operation Variance Permits; Purpose 
 
 The Department of Transportation is required by ORS 818.205 to establish a program for issuing continuous operation variance permits that would allow a motor carrier to obtain one permit that is valid for every participating jurisdiction in which the motor vehicle will travel. A road authority that does not participate may not issue a continuous operation variance permit offered by this program. The purpose of OAR 734-070-0100 through 734-070-0140 is to provide conditions for participation, program guidelines, and specify responsibilities, liabilities and fees of participants in the Continuous Operation Variance Permit Program. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 818.205 & 818.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.200, 818.205 & 818.220
