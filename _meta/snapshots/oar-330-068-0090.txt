 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 68 INSTITUTIONAL BUILDINGS PROGRAM 
 330-068-0090 
 Payment Levels 
 
 (1) TAS: The Department shall reimburse the institution for actual costs for a TAS not to exceed $0.0108 multiplied by the institution’s electric energy use expressed in kWh/yr, or not to exceed an amount equal to the total estimated cost of those ECMs recommended in the TAS and approved by the Department, whichever is smaller. The Department reserves the right to question and request adjustments of costs for a TAS which are judged to be unreasonable:  (2) ECM:  (a) The Department shall reimburse the institution actual costs for an ECM. Such costs shall not exceed $0.292 multiplied by the estimated first year kWh savings, minus the estimated first year dollar savings. These savings are those associated with the energy use reduction determined by multiplying the building’s retail electric energy rate by its estimated annual kilowatt hour savings indicated by the TAS for such ECM;  (b) For ECM projects costing more than $10,000 but less than $200,000, progress payments may be made for ECMs upon 30 percent, 60 percent, and 100 percent completion, unless otherwise agreed to by Bonneville;  (c) For ECM projects costing more the $200,000, progress payments may be made for ECMs upon 30 percent, 60 percent, 80 percent, and 100 percent completion, unless otherwise agreed to by Bonneville. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469
