 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 PREVENTIVE MEDICINE 
 333-021-0700 
 Form for Reporting Written Request and Consent to Performance of Artificial Insemination 
 
 The form, referred to as Exhibit 2 and by this reference made a part hereof, is adopted as the form for reporting written request and consent to performance of artificial insemination pursuant to ORS 677.365.  [ED. NOTE: Exhibits referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 677.365
