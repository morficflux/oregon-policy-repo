 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 522 DESCHUTES BASIN WATER MANAGEMENT 
 690-522-0020 
 Applicability
 
 
 The following rules apply to final orders, permits, and certificates issued pursuant to OAR 690, Division 505 after September 27, 2002, including but not limited to the change of mitigation water into mitigation credits. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027 & ORS 537.746 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Or Laws 2011, ch 694, ORS 537.746 & Or Laws 2005, ch 669 
 
 History:
