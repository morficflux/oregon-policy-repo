 Oregon Medical Board 
 Chapter 847 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 PODIATRIC MEDICINE 
 847-080-0035 
 Approved Colleges of Podiatric Medicine 
 
 Colleges of podiatric medicine approved by the Board are only those approved by the American Podiatric Medical Association Council on Podiatric Medical Education. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 677.265 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 677.265 & 677.820
