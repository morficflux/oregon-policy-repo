 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 46 VACCINES FOR CHILDREN (VFC) PROGRAM AND VACCINE ACCESS PROGRAM (VAP) 
 333-046-0080 
 Record Keeping
 
 
 Clinics must keep all records related to the Vaccines for Children (VFC) program or Vaccine Access Program (VAP) for a minimum of three years and make them available to the Oregon Health Authority for review upon request. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & 431.250 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 413.042 & 431.250
