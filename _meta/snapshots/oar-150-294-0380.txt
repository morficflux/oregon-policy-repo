 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 294 LOCAL BUDGET 
 150-294-0380 
 Negative Resources 
 
 A municipal corporation when estimating its budget resources shall not show negative amounts. Resource amounts should be the net amount that the municipal corporation anticipates to receive. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 294.361
