 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 ADOPTION 
 413-120-0880 
 No Delay in Placement
 
 
 (1) The Department may not delay placement of a child for adoption with an adoptive resource based on any criteria listed in the following subsections: 

 (a) Geographic location; or 

 (b) Race, color, or national origin of the child or the adoptive resource. 

 (2) An adoptive resource who believes that the Department violated the prohibition under section (1) of this rule may file a civil rights complaint and request a review under OAR 413-010-0400 to 413-010-0480. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005
