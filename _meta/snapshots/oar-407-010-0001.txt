 Department of Human Services 
 Chapter 407 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 PRE-EMPLOYMENT/EMPLOYMENT SCREENING 
 407-010-0001 
 Policies and Procedures to Guide Use of Information on OYA and DHS Employees Who are Subjects of a CPS Assessment 
 
 The DHS Office of Human Resources will develop policies and procedures to guide use of information provided by CPS supervisors in relation to the requirements of OAR 413-015-0405(9). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005 
 
 History:
