 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 CHAMPIONS 
 230-100-0030 
 Determination of Title
 
 
 The Superintendent may once a year publish state professional unarmed combat sports champions in each weight class. A championship may be lost by default, forfeit, or inability to make the weight, but a championship can only be won in a contest. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.113 & ORS 463.025
