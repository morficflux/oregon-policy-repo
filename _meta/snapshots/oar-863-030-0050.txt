 Real Estate Agency 
 Chapter 863 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 OREGON SUBDIVISION CONTROL LAW 
 863-030-0050 
 Definitions 
 
 As used in OAR 863-030-0050 to 863-030-0080, unless the context requires otherwise:  (1) “Commissioner” means the Real Estate Commissioner.  (2) “Agency” means the Real Estate Agency.  (3) “Accommodation,” “assessment,” “common expenses,” “developer,” “facility,” “managing entity,” “negotiate,” “purchaser,” “owner,” “seller,” “timeshare,” “timeshare agreement,” “timeshare period” and “timeshare plan” have the meaning given those terms in ORS 94.803. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 92, 94 & 696 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 94.803
