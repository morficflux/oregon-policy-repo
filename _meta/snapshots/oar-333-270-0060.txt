 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 270 OREGON POLST (PHYSICIAN ORDERS OR PORTABLE ORDERS FOR LIFE-SUSTAINING TREATMENT) REGISTRY 
 333-270-0060 
 Confidentiality of Registry Information 
 
 (1) Registry information may only be released to:  (a) Persons and facilities described in OAR 333-270-0050 or 333-270-0080; and  (b) Authorized researchers.  (2) Registry information may only be released for the purposes specified in OAR 333-270-0050 or 333-270-0080.  (3) All information collected or developed by the Registry that identifies or could be used to identify a patient, health care provider or facility is confidential and is not subject to civil or administrative subpoena or to discovery in a civil action, including but not limited to a judicial, administrative, arbitration or mediation proceeding.  (4) Only the minimum amount of information needed to accomplish the intended purposes shall be released under this rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 127.666 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 127.663-127.684 
 
 History:
