Board of Pharmacy

Chapter 855

Division 41
OPERATION OF PHARMACIES

855-041-1145
New Containers

Each pharmacy must dispense a drug in a new container that complies with the current provisions of the Poison Prevention Packaging Act in 16 CFR 1700 (v. 01/01/2025), 16 CFR 1701 (v. 01/01/2025), and 16 CFR 1702 (v. 01/01/2025).

[Publications: Publications referenced are available for review at the agency.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 689.205
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 689.155
