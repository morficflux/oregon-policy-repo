 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 112 REQUIREMENTS FOR MICROWAVE OVEN USE AND SERVICE 
 333-112-0025 
 Training of Service Personnel 
 
 The licensee shall ensure that each technician who services microwave ovens in his or her employ is instructed in the test procedures, compliance criteria and all requirements of this division. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 History:
