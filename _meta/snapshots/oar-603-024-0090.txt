 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS OF IDENTITY, LABELING AND OTHER REGULATIONS RELATING TO FLUID MILK AND DAIRY PRODUCTS 
 603-024-0090 
 Standard of Quality 
 
 Imitation milk products when sold or offered for sale in the final delivery container shall not contain more than 20,000 bacteria per milliliter, 10 coliform per milliliter, and the temperature shall not exceed 45°F 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & 621.418 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 621.418 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 910(16-69), f. 12-5-69, ef. 1-1-70
