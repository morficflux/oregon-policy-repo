 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0696 
 Latino/a/x and Indigenous Student Success Plan Grant Program: Establishment 
 
 (1) There is established the Latino/a/x and Indigenous Student Success Plan Grant Program to support Community-Based Organizations, early learning hubs, providers of early learning services, school districts, educational service districts, Tribes, and post-secondary institutions of education, who are working to design, implement, improve, expand, or otherwise revise programs and services for Latino/a/x and/or Indigenous students. 

 (2) Programs and services provided under a Grant must address one or more of the strategies or objectives included in the Latino/a/x and Indigenous Student Success Plan. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 329.845 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329.845
