 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 LICENSING - PROCEDURES FOR ESTABLISHING, OPERATING, DISCONTINUING AND SUSPENDING LICENSES AND AGENTS 
 635-010-0111 
 Licensing Provisions Applicable to Members of the Uniformed Services
 
 
 Members of the uniformed services of the United States, as defined in ORS 497.006, may purchase resident documents by attesting to ownership of military identification showing the person is a member of the uniformed services stationed in Oregon. Members must also be able to provide the information upon request to the Department or law enforcement. Spouses and dependent children, as defined in ORS 497.006, are residents for licensing purposes.  For exceptions reference OAR 635-060-0040 and OAR 065-0015. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496, 497 & 498 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496, 497 & 498
