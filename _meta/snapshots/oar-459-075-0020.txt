 Oregon Public Employees Retirement System 
 Chapter 459 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 OPSRP PENSION PROGRAM 
 459-075-0020 
 Termination of OPSRP Pension Program Membership
 
 
 (1) As of July 1, 2020, withdrawal from the OPSRP Pension Program is no longer available. 

 (2) A member who withdraws from the Individual Account Program (IAP) under ORS 238A.375 terminates membership in the OPSRP Pension Program as of the effective date of IAP withdrawal. 

 (3) A member who withdraws from the IAP forfeits any service performed by the member before the date of the separation and may not use that service for any purpose including, but not limited to, establishing membership under ORS 238A.100, vesting under ORS 238A.115, and the accrual of retirement credit under ORS 238A.140, 238A.150, or 238A.155. 

 (4) In the event the member’s IAP withdrawal is canceled under OAR 459-080-0020(7) and the member’s membership is restored, the member’s OPSRP Pension Program membership will also be restored. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 238A.450 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 238A.120 & OL 2007 Ch. 52
