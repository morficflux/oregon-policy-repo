 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 335 RAILROAD CABOOSE AND LOCOMOTIVE SANITATION FACILITY AND WATER STANDARDS 
 741-335-0100 
 Incinerator Toilets 
 
 (1) Toilets that function by intense heat shall be installed and maintained to consume waste with the least amount of odor possible.  (2) Incinerator toilets shall be vented to the outside in such a manner that the crew working area is free of objectionable odor.  (3) Clear and concise instructions for use of incinerator toilets shall be provided. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 823.011 & 824.068 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 824.068
