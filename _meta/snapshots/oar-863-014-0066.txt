 Real Estate Agency 
 Chapter 863 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 REAL ESTATE BROKER LICENSING 
 863-014-0066 
 Licensee Name Change 
 
 A licensee may apply for a name change by submitting to the Agency:  (1) An online name change application available through the Agency’s website and payment of the fee authorized under ORS 696.270; and  (2) Legal proof of the name change, which must be in the form of an official record such as a marriage certificate, divorce certificate, or a court order/decree. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 696.385 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 696.020 & 696.022
