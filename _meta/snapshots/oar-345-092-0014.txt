 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 92 STANDARDS FOR THE SITING OF URANIUM MILLS IN OREGON 
 345-092-0014 
 Mandatory Site Certificate Conditions 
 
 In addition to any other site certificate conditions that the Council may impose, the Council shall impose a site certificate condition that requires the certificate holder to design, build, operate and retire a uranium mill in accordance with the design standards contained in OAR 345-092-0031(l), (5) and (6), 345-092-0040(1), (2) and (3)(c) and 345-092-0050 and in accordance with any representations made in satisfaction of 345-092-0031(6) and (7) and 345-092-0040(3)(a) and (d). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.556 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.553
