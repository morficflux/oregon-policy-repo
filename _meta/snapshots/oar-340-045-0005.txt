 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 REGULATIONS PERTAINING TO NPDES AND WPCF PERMITS 
 340-045-0005 
 Purpose
 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to prescribe limitations on discharge of wastes and the requirements and procedures for obtaining NPDES and WPCF permits from the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468B 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468.065, 468B.015 & 468B.030
