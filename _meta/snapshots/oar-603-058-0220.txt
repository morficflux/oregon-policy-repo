 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 58 FEEDS 
 603-058-0220 
 Certain Mammalian Proteins are Prohibited in Ruminant Feed
 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 633.045(1), the Department adopts the requirements of Title 21, Code of Federal Regulations parts 589.2000 and 589.2001 effective as of the date these rules are promulgated. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 663.067 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 633.006-.089, 633.992, 561.605 & 561.620 
 
 History:
