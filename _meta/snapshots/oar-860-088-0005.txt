 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 88 COMMUNITY SOLAR PROGRAM RULES 
 860-088-0005 
 Scope and Applicability of Community Solar Program Rules 
 
 (1) OAR 860-088-0005 through 860-088-0190 establish rules governing implementation of a community solar program under Oregon Laws 2016, chapter 28, section 22.  (2) Upon request or its own motion, the Commission may waive any of the Division 088 rules for good cause shown. A request for waiver must be made in writing, unless otherwise allowed by the Commission. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OL 2016, ch. 28 & sec. 22 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  OL 2016, ch. 28 & sec. 22
