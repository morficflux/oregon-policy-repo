 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 145 TREATMENTS OF SPECIFIC ASSETS 
 461-145-0185 
 Floating Homes and Houseboats 
 
 (1) Floating homes and houseboats are treated in the same manner as real property under OAR 461-145-0420.  (2) Floating homes and houseboats are subject to OAR 461-145-0220 and 461-145-0250 if applicable. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.816 & 412.049 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.816 & 412.049 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SSP 15-2006, f. 12-29-06, cert. ef. 1-1-07
