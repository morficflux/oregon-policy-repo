 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 URBAN RESERVES 
 660-021-0060 
 Urban Growth Boundary Expansion
 
 
 All lands within URAs established pursuant to this division shall be included within an urban growth boundary before inclusion of other lands, except where an identified need for a particular type of land cannot be met by lands within an established urban reserve. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.040 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197A.245
