 Oregon Department of Education 
 Fair Dismissal Appeals Board - Chapter 586 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 CONDUCT OF HEARINGS 
 586-030-0076 
 Appeals 
 
 (1) Any party may appeal the final order of the panel by filing a petition in the Court of Appeals in accordance with the statutory timelines following the date the final order is served or within the statutory timeline after denial of a petition for rehearing or petition for reconsideration.  (2) The record will be made available to the parties for inspection, upon reasonable advance request and at a reasonable time and place, if there is an appeal of the final order. The record shall include the following items:  (a) All pleadings, motions and intermediate rulings;  (b) Evidence received or considered;  (c) Stipulations;  (d) Correspondence from and to parties or their legal counsel;  (e) A statement of any ex parte communications on a fact in issue made to a panel member;  (f) Any portion of the hearing record transcribed for the Panel  (g) The reporting/recording firm telephone number and address; and  (f) The Final Order.  (3) All communications between legal counsel and the Panel members or Board representatives shall not be made available. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 342.805 - 342.930 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 342.805 - 342.930
