 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 650 WATER PROTECTION RULES: RIPARIAN MANAGEMENT AREAS AND PROTECTION MEASURES FOR LAKES 
 629-650-0005 
 Written Plans for Operations Near Large Lakes 
 
 An operator shall submit a written plan to the State Forester before conducting an operation that requires notification under OAR 629-605-0140 and that is within 100 feet of a large lake. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 527.710 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 527.710 & 527.670 2) 
 
 History:
