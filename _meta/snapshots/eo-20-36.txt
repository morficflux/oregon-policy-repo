    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-36

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
    WHITE RIVER FIRE IN WASCO COUNTY

    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The fire known as the “White River Fire” is burning in Wasco County.
    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the White River Fire
    is beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
    protection was requested by Wasco County Fire Defense Board Chief Palmer and
    the State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

    In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
    safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the White River Fire in Wasco
    County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
    personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
    Conflagration Act.


    These findings were made at 6:20 a.m. on August 28, 2020 with Office of State
    Fire Marshal and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    1.     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
           fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
           Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
           Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
           responding to White River Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
           Marshal.


    2,     This conflagration is declared only for the White River Fire threatening
           structures near the town of Pine Grove in Wasco County.          .
