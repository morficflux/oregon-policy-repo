 Oregon Wine Board 
 Chapter 619 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 619-001-0030 
 Definitions 
 
 For purposes of these rules, the following definitions apply.  (1) “Board” means the Oregon Wine Board established pursuant to ORS 576.753.  (2) “Director” means the director of the Oregon Economic and Community Development Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576.766(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576.768(2)(b) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OWB 1-2005, f. 12-5-05, cert. ef. 12-15-05
