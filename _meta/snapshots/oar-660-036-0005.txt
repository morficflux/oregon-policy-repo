 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 36 OCEAN PLANNING 
 660-036-0005 
 Territorial Sea Plan: Use of the Territorial Sea for the Development of Renewable Energy Facilities or Other Related Structures, Equipment or Facilities
 
 
 The Land Conservation and Development Commission adopts as part of the Oregon Coastal Management Program, and herein incorporates by reference, an amendment to the Territorial Sea Plan Part Five: Use of the Territorial Sea for the Development of Renewable Energy Facilities or Other Related Structures, Equipment or Facilities, that the Commission approved as modified on May 24, 2019.
