 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 CRITICAL INCIDENT REVIEW TEAM (CIRT) AND DISCRETIONARY REVIEWS 
 413-017-0095 
 Discretionary Review 
 
 When a CIRT is not required under OAR 413-017-0060 and ORS 419B.024, and the Department has received a report of abuse that has resulted in a child fatality or serious physical injury of a child , the Department director or designee has the discretion to order an internal review of the incident. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 & ORS 419B.024 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005 & ORS 419B.024 
 
 History:
