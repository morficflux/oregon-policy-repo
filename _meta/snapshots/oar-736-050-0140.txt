 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 HISTORIC PRESERVATION OFFICER 
 736-050-0140 
 Appeal Process
 
 
 (1) An owner of historic property enrolled in the Special Assessment Program for Historic Property prior to July 1, 2024, may appeal all decisions made by the SHPO to the Historic Assessment Review Committee (HARC). The HARC must establish and may amend procedures for an appeal at a noticed public hearing, effective at the body’s next public hearing. 

 (2) An owner , county assessor, or governing body aggrieved by SHPO determination regarding a property applying for or enrolled in the Special Assessment Program for Historic Property after July 1, 2025, may appeal all decisions made by the SHPO as a contested case under the provisions of ORS 358.495(5). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 358.545 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 358.495
