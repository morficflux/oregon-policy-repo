 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 266 FIELD BURNING RULES 
 340-266-0110 
 Emergency Burning Cessation 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 468A.610 and upon finding of danger to public health or safety, the Commission may order temporary emergency cessation of all open field burning in any area of the Willamette Valley. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.610
