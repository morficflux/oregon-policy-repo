 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Ryegrass Growers Seed Commission - Chapter 657 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENT 
 657-010-0020 
 Reports and Payment of Assessment Moneys 
 
 First purchasers and handlers must submit completed and signed assessment reports on commission approved forms or formats. Assessment reports shall include all purchases by or deliveries to a first purchaser or handler of Ryegrass seed. Quarterly assessment reports and assessments are due to the commission office and must be postmarked by the last day for the reporting month specified below. Quarterly assessments shall be reported as follows:  (1) January, February, March assessments postmarked by April 30th;  (2) April, May, June assessments postmarked by July 31st;  (3) July, August, September assessments postmarked by October 31st;  (4) October, November, December assessments postmarked by January 31st. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576
