 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 93 DEATH ROW HOUSING UNIT 
 291-093-0007 
 Housing Assignment
 
 
 (1) The SPM Committee may reclassify and assign an AIC with a sentence of death to appropriate housing, which may include the Death Row Housing Unit or a Death Row status cell or other housing. 

 (2) All cell moves, within the housing type designated by the SPM Committee, shall be approved by the housing unit officer in charge and the FUM or designee. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 423.075, ORS 179.040, 423.020 & 423.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 423.075, ORS 179.040, 423.020 & 423.030
