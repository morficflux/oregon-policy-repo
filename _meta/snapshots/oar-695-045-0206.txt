 Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board 
 Chapter 695 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 LAND ACQUISITION GRANTS 
 695-045-0206 
 Access 
 
 OWEB, its contractors and cooperating agencies will be provided legal and sufficient access to Property Interest acquired with OWEB grant funds, for the purpose of monitoring and enforcing OWEB’s Conservation Easement rights and completing inspections and evaluations required under ORS 541.906(2)(c)(A). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 541.906 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541.906 & ORS 541.932(9) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OWEB 1-2019, adopt filed 04/29/2019, effective 05/01/2019
