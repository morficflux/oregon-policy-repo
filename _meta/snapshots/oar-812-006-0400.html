Construction Contractors Board

Chapter 812

Division 6
RESPONSIBLE MANAGING INDIVIDUAL, PRE-LICENSURE TRAINING, AND TESTING

812-006-0400
Pre-Licensure Training and Testing Period

(1) The pre-licensure training and testing required under ORS 701.122 (1) and (3) will be valid for 24 months from the date the responsible managing individual passed the test.

 (2) Section (1) of this rule does not apply to an RMI that meets the experience requirements under OAR 812-006-0450.

(3) Section (1) of this rule does not apply to an RMI that meets the training and testing requirements of OAR 812-006-0160 and 812-006-0310.

(4) Pre-license training and testing pursuant to OAR 812-006-0164 will be valid for 24 months from the date the Responsible Managing Individual (RMI) successfully completes and passes the entire Contractor Pre-License Business, Law, and Project Management Training Insitutue course developed by an Oregon community college Small Business Development Center , under an Intergovernmental Agreement pursuant to OAR 812-006-0164.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 670.310, 701.122 & 701.235
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 701.122
