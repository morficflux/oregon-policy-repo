 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 215 MAINTENANCE, REPAIR AND DEEPENING OF WATER SUPPLY WELLS 
 690-215-0016 
 Maintaining Well Setback Requirements 
 
 Within the boundaries of their own property, property owners are responsible for maintaining the applicable minimum setback distances for any well on their property. Refer to OAR 690-210-0030 for current minimum setback distances. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 536.090 & 537.505 - 537.795 
 
 History:
