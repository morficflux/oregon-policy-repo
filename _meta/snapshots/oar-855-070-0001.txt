 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 PROPHYLACTICS AND CONTRACEPTIVES 
 855-070-0001 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) Prophylactic means a drug, device or medical preparation intended for or having special utility in the prevention of a sexually transmitted disease, or in the prevention of conception.  (2) Contraceptive means a drug, device or medical preparation intended for the prevention of conception.  (3) Male condom means a prophylactic or contraceptive device in the form of a sheath which completely covers the penis with a closely fitting membrane.  (4) Female condom means a prophylactic or contraceptive device in the form of an intravaginal pouch that consists of a sheath with a flexible ring on each end. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689.155 & 689.205 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PB 1-1995, f. & cert. ef. 4-27-95
