 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 310 FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT 
 413-310-0300 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules describe the actions which will be taken by the State Office for Services to Children and Families Accounting Services Section when “writing off” accounts receivable balances owed to the agency. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  HB 2004 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 293.235 & 293.240 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SCF 6-1995, f. 12-22-95, cert. ef. 12-29-95
