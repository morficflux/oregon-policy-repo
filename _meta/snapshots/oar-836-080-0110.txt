 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 TRADE PRACTICES 
 836-080-0110 
 Applicability 
 
 OAR 836-080-0105 to 836-080-0155:  (1) Apply to all authorized insurers, whether stock, mutual, or reciprocal, and to the State Accident Insurance Fund.  (2) Apply to those forms of property and casualty insurance known by custom as “commercial lines”; and do not apply to other lines or forms of insurance. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 & 746 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 746.015(1), 746.075, 746.160(3) & 746.240 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 IC 69, f. & ef. 7-20-76
