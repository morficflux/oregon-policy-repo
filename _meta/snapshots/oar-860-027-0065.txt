 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 27 BUDGETS, FINANCE, ACCOUNTING AND ANNUAL REPORTS 
 860-027-0065 
 Uniform System of Accounts for Steam Heat Utilities — Class A, B, and C 
 
 The Uniform System of Accounts for Public Utilities and Licensees, Part 101, Chapter 1, 18 Code of Federal Regulations (April 1, 2001, edition) is hereby adopted and prescribed by the Commission for each steam heat utility.  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the Agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756 & 757 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.105, 757.120, 757.125 & 757.135
