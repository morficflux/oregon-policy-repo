 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 39 ADMINISTRATION OF FIRE PREVENTION PROGRAMS 
 837-039-0056 
 Exempt Jurisdiction Committee 
 
 (1) An Exempt Jurisdiction Committee should meet bi-annually to make recommendations to the State Fire Marshal on rules, policies and the program relating to their exempt status.  (2) Committee members shall also review new Exempt Jurisdictions application when requested by the State Fire Marshal. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 476.030 & 837-039-0060 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSFM 2-2017, f. 6-26-17, cert. ef. 7-1-17
