 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 126 SAFETY EQUIPMENT REQUIREMENTS 
 735-126-0020 
 Securing of Tools, Equipment, Etc. 
 
 (1) Racks, boxes, holsters, or equivalent means shall be provided and arranged so passengers and driver will not be endangered by tools or equipment being transported, loaded, or removed. Tools and equipment are preferably placed or arranged so they are accessible from the outside of the vehicle.  (2) Tools and materials shall be secured in the racks and boxes provided. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 802.010 & 820.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 820.010 - 820.030
