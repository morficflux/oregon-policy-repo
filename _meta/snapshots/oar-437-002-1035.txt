 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 GENERAL OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH RULES 
 437-002-1035 
 Oregon Rule for Sharps Injury Log 
 
 The requirement to establish and maintain a sharps injury log applies to any employer who is required to maintain an Exposure Control Plan. The sharps injury log must be maintained for 5 years. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSHA 10-2001, f. 9-14-01, cert. ef. 10-18-01
