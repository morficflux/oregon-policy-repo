 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 294 LOCAL BUDGET 
 150-294-0840 
 Duties of the Governing Body of a Council of Government 
 
 The council of governments notifies the public of the time and place of a public hearing on a budget document as approved by the budget committee. The council of governments must pass a resolution adopting a budget document as approved by the budget committee or as modified by the council of governments. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 294.920
