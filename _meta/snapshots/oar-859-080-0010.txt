 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD ORDER OF REVOCATION 
 859-080-0010 
 Reasonable Grounds for Revocation 
 
 Reasonable grounds for revocation of a conditional release include:  (1) The person has violated terms of the conditional release plan;  (2) The person’s mental health has changed;  (3) The person has absconded from jurisdiction;  (4) The community resources required by the conditional release order are no longer available. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.336 
 
 History:
