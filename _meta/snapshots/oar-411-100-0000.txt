 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 SELF-SUFFICIENCY TRUST FUND AND THE DISABILITY TRUST FUND 
 411-100-0000 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to provide for the administration and implementation of ORS 410.730 and 410.732, relating to the establishment of the Self-Sufficiency Trust Fund and the Disability Trust Fund. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.730 and 410.732 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.730 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SSD 4-1991, f. & cert. ef. 2-1-91
