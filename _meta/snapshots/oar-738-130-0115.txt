 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 130 STATE AGENCY COORDINATION PROGRAM 
 738-130-0115 
 Referral of Compatibility Disputes to the Land Conservation and Development Commission 
 
 If a compatibility conflict persists after pursuing the compatibility procedures listed in OAR 738-130-0045 through 738-130-0115, the Department shall request that the Land Conservation and Development Commission make a compatibility determination in accordance with 660-030-0070(7) through (12). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 835.035 & 835.112 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.180 
 
 History:
