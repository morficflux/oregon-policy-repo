 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 INDOOR CLEAN AIR ACT, NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION AT CHAIN RESTAURANTS AND RETAIL SALE OF TOBACCO PRODUCTS AND INHALANT DELIVERY SYSTEMS 
 333-015-0140 
 Oregon Menu Labeling Act: Verifiable and Accurate Information 
 
 (1) A chain restaurant may not make available to customers any typical nutrient values that are substantially inaccurate or that the restaurant knows or should know to be false or misleading.  (2) A chain restaurant may be found by the Oregon Public Health Division to have substantially inaccurate nutrient values if the chain restaurant failed to use one (or more) of the acceptable methods for determining nutrient values described in these rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2009 OL Ch. 314 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2009 OL Ch. 314
