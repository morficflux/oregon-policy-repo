 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 47 PRIVATE HUNTING PRESERVE RULES 
 635-047-0032 
 Transportation of Killed Birds 
 
 Birds killed on a hunting preserve may be cleaned, wrapped, and packaged for transport. These birds shall be accompanied by a transportation form provided by the preserve operator stating preserve name, signature, address, and hunting license number of the hunter. In addition, species, number, sex (pheasants only) and harvest date of the birds being transported shall be included. Birds not cleaned, wrapped, and packaged shall be transported with a fully feathered head attached. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.112, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.112, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 History:
