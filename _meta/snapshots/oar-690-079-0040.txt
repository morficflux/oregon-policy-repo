 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 79 RESERVATIONS OF WATER FOR FUTURE ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT 
 690-079-0040 
 Priority Date 
 
 The order establishing the reservation shall fix the date on which the request for reservation was filed with the Department as the reservation priority date. A request for reservation will be deemed filed on the date it is received by the Department provided that either:  (1) The request contains all the information required under OAR 690-079-0060; or  (2) Any additional information the Department deems necessary to complete the application is received by the Department within 30 days of its request for additional information, or within such additional time as the Department provides, not to exceed one year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197, 536.025, 536.027, 536.220, 536.300, 536.310, 537.338 & 537.356 - 537.358
