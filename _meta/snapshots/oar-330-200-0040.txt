 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 RENEWABLE ENERGY DEVELOPMENT GRANTS 
 330-200-0040 
 Fees 
 
 The department adopts the following schedule of fees as provided by ORS 469B.259 for applicants. All fee payments are non-refundable, despite the results of the department’s review.  (1) Applicants must submit a fee of $500 with their initial application.  (2) Applicants selected for technical review will be required to pay an additional technical review fee prior to that review. The fee amount is equal to the qualifying system cost multiplied by 1.25 percent.  (3) Applicants requesting amendments must submit a fee of $300 with their amendment request.  (4) If an applicant fails to pay fees timely as required by this rule, the department may reject the pending application and discontinue the review. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.040, 469B.259 & 469B.265 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469B.250 - 469B.265
