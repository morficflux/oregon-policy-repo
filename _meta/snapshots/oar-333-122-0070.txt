 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 122 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR INDUSTRIAL X-RAY MACHINE OPERATIONS 
 333-122-0070 
 General Requirements for All RGD: Warning Lights 
 
 (1) An easily visible warning light labeled with the words " X-RAY ON" , or words having a similar intent, shall be located near any switch that energizes an X-ray tube and shall be illuminated only when the tube is energized. 

 (2) On equipment installed after January 1, 1978, warning lights shall have fail-safe characteristics. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807
