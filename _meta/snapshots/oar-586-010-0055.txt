 Oregon Department of Education 
 Fair Dismissal Appeals Board - Chapter 586 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 POLICY 
 586-010-0055 
 Parliamentary Procedure 
 
 Roberts Rules of Order shall be the parliamentary procedure for the conduct of all meetings of the Board except as amended by these policies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 342
