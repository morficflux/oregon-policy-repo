 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 CLIENT RIGHTS 
 413-010-0480 
 Judicial Review 
 
 These rules (OAR 413-010-0400 to 413-010-0480) do not create a contested case, as defined by ORS 183.310, subject to judicial review under 183.482. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.194 & 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.192 & 418.005
