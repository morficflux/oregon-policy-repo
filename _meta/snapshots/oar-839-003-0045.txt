 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 CIVIL RIGHTS COMPLAINT PROCEDURES 
 839-003-0045 
 Withdrawal of Complaint: Employment and Public Accommodation 
 
 (1) This rule does not apply to housing discrimination complaints under ORS 659A.145 or 659A.421 or the federal Fair Housing Act (42 U.S.C. 3601–3614a). The withdrawal of a housing discrimination complaint is addressed in OAR 839-003-0210.  (2) An aggrieved person may voluntarily withdraw a complaint at any time by giving the division written notice of the aggrieved person’s decision to withdraw. If the aggrieved person wants a federal "right to sue letter," the aggrieved person must provide a written request to EEOC or to the division. If the aggrieved person makes the request to the division, the division will forward the request to EEOC. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 659A.805 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 659A
