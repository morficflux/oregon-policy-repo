 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 314 INCOME TAXATION GENERALLY  GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 150-314-0294 
 Estimated Tax: Affiliated Corporations 
 
 (1) If two or more affiliated corporations file a consolidated state return as described in ORS 317.710 through 317.725, each shall be jointly and severally liable for the filing and payment of the estimated tax liability. Estimated tax shall be made on a consolidated basis.  (2) See OAR 150-314-0317 for an explanation of how to compute an underpayment of estimated taxes for corporations filing a consolidated Oregon return. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 314.505
