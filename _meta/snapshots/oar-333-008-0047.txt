 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 MEDICAL MARIJUANA 
 333-008-0047 
 Interim Addition or Change of Person Responsible for a Marijuana Grow Site or Grow Site Address
 
 
 (1) If a patient is adding a PRMG and grow site address at any time other than when applying for a new or renewal registry identification card, or if a patient is changing a PRMG or grow site address at any time other than when submitting a renewal application for a patient identification card, the patient must: 

 (a) Submit a PRMG and grow site registration change application, on a form prescribed by the Authority, that includes all the information and documentation specified in the form and required under OAR 333-008-0020(4); and 

 (b) Pay the fee required in OAR 333-008-0021 unless the PRMG is a patient growing only for him or herself. 

 (2) A PRMG and grow site registration change application shall be reviewed in accordance with OAR 333-008-0025 and approved or denied in accordance with OAR 333-008-0033 or 333-008-0037. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.783, 475C.789, ORS 475C.792 & 475C.919 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.783, 475C.789 & 475C.792
