 Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board 
 Chapter 695 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 WATERSHED ENHANCEMENT PROGRAM 
 695-004-0020 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) “Board” means the Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board created under ORS 541.900.  (2) “Director” means the Executive Director of the Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board or the Executive Director’s designee.  (3) “OWEB” means the Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board state agency. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 541.906 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541.890 - 541.969 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OWEB 3-2008, f. 11-14-08, cert. ef. 1-1-09
