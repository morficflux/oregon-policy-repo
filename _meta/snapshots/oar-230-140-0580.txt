 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 CONDUCT OF BOUT - MMA 
 230-140-0580 
 When a Competitor is Deemed Down 
 
 A competitor is deemed down or grounded whenever any part of the body other than the hands and sole of the feet are in contact with the floor . 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.113 & ORS 463.025
