 Oregon Health Authority 
 Chapter 943 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 PUBLIC CONTRACTING AND PROCUREMENT 
 943-060-0050 
 ORS 190 Agreements 
 
 (1) The Authority, when it enters into an agreement under ORS 190.110, 190.420, or 190.485, or an agreement under ORS 190.112 or 660.342, shall post a summary of the agreement on an applicable electronic procurement system within the 30-day period immediately following the effective date of the agreement.  (2) The summary shall include:  (a) Names of the parties to the agreement;  (b) Date of the agreement;  (c) Subject matter of the agreement; and  (d) The agency through which a person may obtain a copy of the agreement. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 279A.065, 279A.070, 413.014, 413.033 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279A.140(2)(h)
