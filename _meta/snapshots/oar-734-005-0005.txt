 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 OREGON COORDINATE SYSTEMS 
 734-005-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of this administrative rule is to define the Oregon Coordinate System, consisting of three mapping projection coordinate systems that are authorized for use in the State of Oregon. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & Ch.179 OL 2011 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 209.130, 209.155, 209.250, 390.770 & Ch.179 OL 2011
