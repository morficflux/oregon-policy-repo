 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 WAGE COLLECTION MATTERS 
 839-001-0140 
 Determination of the Day; Failure to Make Determination for Regulations Pertaining to Maximum Hours of Work in Mills, Factories, Manufacturing Establishments 
 
 (1) The employer shall establish the beginning time and the ending time of the day to be used by the employer for purposes of complying with these rules.  (2) Once established, the beginning time of the day shall not be changed unless the change is intended to be permanent and is not designed to circumvent the provisions of ORS 652.020.  (3) If the employer fails to establish the beginning and ending times of day as provided for in section (1) of this rule, the Bureau shall consider the day to begin at the time the individual employee commences work and to end 24 consecutive hours after the work began. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 651.060(4) & 652 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 652.020 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BL 8-1990, f. & cert. ef. 5-11-90
