 Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board 
 Chapter 695 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 ENGAGEMENT GRANTS 
 695-015-0100 
 Board Funding Decision 
 
 (1) The Board may fund a project in whole or in part.  (2) Projects not funded may be resubmitted during application submission periods prescribed by the Board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 541.906 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541.956 
 
 History:
