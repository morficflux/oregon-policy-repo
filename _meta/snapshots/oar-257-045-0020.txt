 Department of State Police 
 Chapter 257 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 VEHICLE TOW HEARING PROCESS 
 257-045-0020 
 Scope of Hearing 
 
 (1) The owner of the vehicle, a person entitled to possession of the vehicle, or a person with a recorded interest on the title of the vehicle, may request a hearing to contest the validity of the towing and custody of the vehicle.  (2) The owner of the vehicle, a person entitled to possession of the vehicle, or a person with a recorded interest on the title of the vehicle, may request a hearing to contest the reasonableness of the charges for towing and storage only if the department used its own personnel, equipment and facilities in conducting the tow or in storing the vehicle. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OL 2009 & ch. 371 (HB 2738) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  OL 2009 & ch. 371 (HB 2738)
