 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 COMMERCIAL FISHERIES OTHER THAN SALMON OR SHELLFISH 
 635-004-0430 
 Sardine Catching Vessel 
 
 (1) Only a sardine catching vessel may pump fish onboard, directly from the pursed seine of another sardine catching vessel, and lawfully land that catch.  (2) For the purposes of this rule, “Sardine catching vessel” means a vessel holding a valid Sardine Permit pursuant to OAR 635-004-0385 that lawfully deployed purse seine gear during the current year and pumped resultant catch onboard the same vessel for delivery to a port of landing or fish dealer. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & 506.129
