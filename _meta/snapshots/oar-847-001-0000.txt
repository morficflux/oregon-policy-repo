 Oregon Medical Board 
 Chapter 847 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 847-001-0000 
 Notice of Proposed Rule
 
 
 Prior to adoption, amendment or repeal of any permanent rule, the Oregon Medical Board must give notice of the intended action: 

 (1) In the Secretary of State's Bulletin referred to in ORS 183.360 at least 21 days before the effective date of the intended action; 

 (2) To persons on the Oregon Medical Board's list established pursuant to ORS 183.335(8) at least 28 days before the effective date of the rule; and 

 (3) To persons specified in ORS 183.335(15) at least 49 days before the effective date of the rule. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 677.265 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.335, 183.341 & 677.275
