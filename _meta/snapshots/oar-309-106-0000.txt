 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 106 VISITATION OF PATIENTS AND RESIDENTS IN STATE INSTITUTIONS 
 309-106-0000 
 Purpose and Statutory Authority
 
 
 These rules provide the policies and procedures concerning visitation of patients at Oregon State Hospital . 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 413.042 & 179.360 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.321 & 426.385
