 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 348 HOST HOME PROGRAMS AND SETTINGS FOR CHILDREN WITH INTELLECTUAL OR DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES 
 411-348-0370 
 Personal Property 
 
 (1) A program provider must prepare and maintain an accurate individual written record of personal property that has significant or monetary value to each child as determined by a documented ISP team or parent or guardian decision. 

 (2) The record must include: 

 (a) The description and identifying number, if any; 

 (b) Date of inclusion in the record; 

 (c) Date and reason for removal from the record; 

 (d) Signature of in-residence caregiver or staff making each entry; and 

 (e) A signed and dated annual review of the record for accuracy. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, 427.104, 441.715, 443.450 & 443.455 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 427.104, 441.705-441.720, 441.740, 441.745, 443.384, 443.392, 443.400-443.445, 443.450, 443.455, 443.880, 443.881 & 443.991
