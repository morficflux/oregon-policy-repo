 Oregon 529 Savings Board 
 Chapter 173 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 CONTRIBUTIONS 
 173-009-0015 
 Maximum Contribution Limit 
 
 (1) Additional contributions to an account for a designated beneficiary are prohibited when the total account balance for all accounts for that designated beneficiary under the network exceeds the amount established by the Board as required by and in accordance with the Code.  (2) For the ABLE Plan, additional contributions to an account for a designated beneficiary are prohibited when the total annual contributions for that designated beneficiary exceeds the amount established by the Board as required by and in accordance with the Code. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.853(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.857(4) & 348.853(2)
