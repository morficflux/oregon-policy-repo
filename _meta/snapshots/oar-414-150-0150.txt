 Department of Early Learning and Care 
 Chapter 414 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 150 CHILD CARE PROGRAM FOR TARGETED POPULATIONS UNDER THE CHILD CARE DEVELOPMENT BLOCK GRANT 
 414-150-0150 
 Exception
 
 
 (1) Specific exception to any section of these rules may be granted for good and just cause by the Department. 

 (2) The exception must be requested in writing to the Department and show how the intent of the rule(s) will be met. All exceptions will remain on file. 

 (3) No exception will be granted which may jeopardize the health, safety, and well-being of any child in care. 

 (4) The granting of an exception shall not constitute a precedent for any other care provider or client family. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 329A.010 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329A.500 & ORS 329A.010
