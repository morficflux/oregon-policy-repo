 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 CLINICAL LABORATORIES 
 333-024-1050 
 Newborn Screening: Religious or Philosophical Exemption from Newborn Testing 
 
 (1) A parent or legal guardian may opt not to have their infant screened in accordance with these rules because of religious or philosophical beliefs opposed to such testing. To claim such an exemption, the parent or legal guardian must complete an Objection to Newborn Screening Blood Test on behalf of the infant on a form prescribed by the Oregon State Public Health Laboratory. 

 (2) The parent or legal guardian must sign the Objection to Newborn Screening Blood Test. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & ORS 433.110 - 433.770 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 433.110 - 433.770
