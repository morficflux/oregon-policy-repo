 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 SCHEDULE OF CONTROLLED SUBSTANCES 
 855-080-0075 
 Orders for Schedule I and II Controlled Substances 
 
 Controlled substances in Schedules I and II must be distributed by a registrant to another registrant only pursuant to an order form or electronic order in compliance with 21 USC 828 (v. 02/26/2026) and 21 CFR 1305.01 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.02 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.03 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.04 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.05 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.06 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.07 (v. 04/01/2025); 21 CFR 1305.11 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.12 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.13 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.14 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.15 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.16 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.17 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.18 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.19 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.20 (v. 04/01/2025); 21 CFR 1305.21 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.22 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.23 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.24 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.25 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.26 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.27 (v. 04/01/2025), 21 CFR 1305.28 (v. 04/01/2025), and 21 CFR 1305.29 (v. 04/01/2025).
