 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 145 TREATMENTS OF SPECIFIC ASSETS 
 461-145-0520 
 Stocks, Bonds and Other Securities 
 
 (1) Except as provided in sections (2) and (3) of this rule, the equity value (see OAR 461-001-0000) of mutual funds, and securities, including stocks, bonds, educational savings bonds, and certificates of deposit (CDs), is counted as a resource.  (2) In the OSIPM and QMB-DW programs, the value of a savings bond issued by the United States Department of the Treasury is excluded during the minimum retention period.  (3) In all programs except the OSIPM and QMB-DW programs, the value of a savings bond issued by the United States Department of the Treasury is excluded during the minimum retention period if the owner has received a denial of a request for a hardship waiver based on financial need.  (4) Interest and dividends on items covered by section (1) of this rule are treated as provided in OAR 461-145-0108. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 329A.500, 409.050, 411.060, 411.070, 411.404, 411.706, 411.816, 412.014, 412.049, 413.085 & 414.685 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329A.500, 409.010, 411.060, 411.070, 411.404, 411.706, 411.816, 412.014, 412.049, 413.085 & 414.685
