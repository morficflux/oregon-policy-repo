 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 52 OREGON FARM DIRECT NUTRITION PROGRAM 
 333-052-0100 
 Oregon Health Authority Responsibilities
 
 
 The Oregon Health Authority (Authority) must: 

 (1) Administer the Oregon Farm Direct Nutrition Program (Program) in accordance with 7 CFR 248 (Farmers’ Market Nutrition Program) and 7 CFR 249 (Senior Farmers’ Market Nutrition Program); 

 (2) Distribute or facilitate distribution of Farm Direct vouchers to participants; 

 (3) Assure payment to farmers for properly redeemed Farm Direct vouchers; 

 (4) Assure that training is provided to farmers who are new to the Program; 

 (5) Assure that Program signs are provided to all authorized farmers; and 

 (6) Monitor authorized farmers for compliance with Program rules and agreements, and if necessary, impose sanctions. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.500, ORS 411.813, 7 CFR 248 & 7 CFR 249 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 413.500, ORS 411.813, 7 CFR 248 & 7 CFR 249
