 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 SECURITIES TRANSACTIONS EXEMPT FROM REGISTRATION 
 441-035-0050 
 Isolated Issuer Transaction 
 
 (1) An exemption is granted pursuant to ORS 59.035(15) for any isolated issuer transaction not involving a public offering.  (2) For purposes of this rule, an “isolated issuer transaction” shall include sales by or on behalf of an issuer to three or fewer persons during any 24-month period. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 59.035 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 59.035 
 
 History:
