 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 34 SMALL TELECOMMUNICATIONS UTILITIES AND COOPERATIVES 
 860-034-0630 
 Information to be Included With a Request for an Exception Filed Under OAR 860-034-0620 
 
 Information included with filing a request for an exception, at a minimum, shall:  (1) State the account(s) for which the small telecommunications utility or Type 2 cooperative seeks the exception;  (2) State the number of calendar years for which the small telecommunications utility or Type 2 cooperative seeks the exception; and  (3) Include an engineering analysis, economic analysis, or both, or other support for the exception. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756 & 759 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040, 759.220 & 759.225
