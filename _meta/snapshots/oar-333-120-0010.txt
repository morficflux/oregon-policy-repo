 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION 
 333-120-0010 
 General Provisions: Scope 
 
 The rules in this division apply to persons licensed or registered by the Authority to receive, possess, use, transfer, or dispose of licensed radioactive material or registered devices. The limits in this division do not apply to doses due to background radiation, to exposure of patients to radiation for the purpose of medical diagnosis or therapy, or to voluntary participation in medical research programs. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
