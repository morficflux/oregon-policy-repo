 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 730 CONSUMER FINANCE 
 441-730-0060 
 Loans Not to Be Payable on Demand; Exception 
 
 (1) Except as provided by section (2) of this rule, a loan shall not be made payable on demand.  (2) A loan may provide that, if there is a default under the note or collateral security agreement, the loan may become due and payable immediately or on demand. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 725 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 725.050
