 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 560 HEARINGS BEFORE THE JUVENILE PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD 
 859-560-0050 
 Status Hearing 
 
 (1) The Board may hold a hearing on its own motion at any time to review the status of a youth to determine whether modification, revocation, any other change in conditional release or a status is warranted.  (2) The Board may order the youth to appear at a status hearing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387, OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.336, 161.346, 419C.532, 419C.538, 419C.540, 419C.542 & 419C.544
