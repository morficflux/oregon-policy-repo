 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 PROCEDURES FOR RESPONSES TO PAROLE AND POST-PRISON SUPERVISION CONDITIONS VIOLATIONS FOR OFFENDERS UNDER THE JURISDICTION OF THE BOARD OF PAROLE AND POST-PRISON SUPERVISION OR LOCAL SUPERVISORY AUTHORITY 
 255-075-0006 
 Method of Hearing 
 
 The Hearing Officer may conduct hearings by teleconference or video conference. The Hearing Officer shall conduct hearings in person or by video conference in the following situations:  (1) The alleged violations are contested and the offender or the offender’s attorney shows that the witness’s credibility, including observation of the witness’s demeanor is necessary;  (2) Physical exhibits may be part of the record and viewing the exhibits is essential, and the exhibits can not be viewed in some other manner;  (3) There are unusual circumstances not covered by this section, determined at the Hearing Officer’s discretion. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144.035(5) & 144.343(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 144.096, 144.098, 144.102, 144.106, 144.108, 144.346 & Ch. 525 OL 1997 (Enrolled SB 156)
