 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 122 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR INDUSTRIAL X-RAY MACHINE OPERATIONS 
 333-122-0210 
 General Requirements for All RGD: Records of Personnel Monitoring 
 
 Each registrant must maintain the following exposure records specified in OAR 333-122-0190. 

 (1) Direct reading dosimeter readings and yearly operability checks required by OAR 333-122-0190(2) and OAR 333-122-0190(3) for three years after the record is made; 

 (2) Reports received from an individual monitoring device processor until the Authority terminates the license or registration; and 

 (3) Records of estimates of exposures because of readings beyond the visible scale demarcation of personal direct reading dosimeters, or lost or damaged dose measuring devices, until the Authority terminates the license or registration. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807
