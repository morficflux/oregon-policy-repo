 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 HAZARDOUS WASTE MANAGEMENT 
 340-100-0020 
 Petitions, General 
 
 (1) Any person may petition the Department to approve an equivalent testing or analytical method or may petition the Commission to exclude a waste produced at a particular facility. This rule sets forth general requirements which apply to all such petitions.  (2) Persons submitting petitions shall comply with the requirements of 40 CFR 260.20.  (3) After evaluating all public comments, the Department or Commission as appropriate will make a decision to grant or deny the petition. Persons commenting on the petition will be notified and the decision placed in the public record. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 459 & 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.020 & 468.020 
 
 History:
