 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 507 UMATILLA BASIN PROGRAM 
 690-507-0700 
 Butter Creek CGWA: Violation Policy 
 
 Whenever the Department has reason to believe a violation of a rule in OAR 690, division 507 has occurred, it shall investigate. If a violation has occurred, the Director may take enforcement action. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536 & 537 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 9-1990, f. & cert. ef. 6-25-90
