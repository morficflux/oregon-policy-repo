 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 54 EXTRA-CURRICULAR SCHOOL TRANSPORTATION GRANTS 
 581-054-0004 
 Establishment 
 
 (1) There is established the School Transportation Grant. 

 (2) The starting date of eligible transportation costs for this grant program shall be July 1, 2018. 

 (3) The School Transportation Grant is established as a reimbursement grant. Funding will be released based on actual expenditures as calculated in ORS 581-024-0016. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Sec. 2, Ch. 62, OL 2018 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Sec. 2, Ch. 62, OL 2018 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ODE 40-2018, adopt filed 12/10/2018, effective 12/10/2018
