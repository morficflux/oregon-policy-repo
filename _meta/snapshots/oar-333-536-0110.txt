 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 536 IN-HOME CARE AGENCIES 
 333-536-0110 
 Violations
 
 
 In addition to non-compliance with any law that governs an agency, it is a violation to: 

 (1) Refuse to cooperate with an investigation or survey, including but not limited to failure to permit Authority staff access to the agency, its documents or records; 

 (2) Fail to implement an approved plan of correction; 

 (3) Refuse or fail to comply with an order issued by the Authority; 

 (4) Refuse or fail to pay a civil penalty; 

 (5) Fail to comply with rules governing the storage of records following the closure of an agency; 

 (6) Fail to report suspected abuse of elderly persons as defined in ORS 124.050; 

 (7) Fail to return a license as provided in OAR 333-536-0035; 

 (8) Operate without a license. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 443.340 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 443.315 & 443.340
