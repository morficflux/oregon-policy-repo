 Department of Early Learning and Care 
 Chapter 414 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 460 OREGON PRENATAL TO KINDERGARTEN 
 414-460-0065 
 Collect Data
 
 
 Programs must collect data which documents the numbers of families served in each of the criteria categories listed in OAR 414-460-0060. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 329.175 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329.175 & ORS 329.195
