 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 74 ISSUANCE OF PERMITS FOR COMBINATIONS OF VEHICLES HAVING GROSS WEIGHTS IN EXCESS OF 80,000 POUNDS 
 734-074-0025 
 Permit Duration 
 
 Permits issued under Division 74 rules are for a single trip within a period of time not to exceed 10 days, or continuous trips for up to one year from the effective date. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 818.220 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.200 & 818.220
