 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 HUMAN IMMUNODEFICIENCY VIRUS 
 333-022-1020 
 CareAssist: Eligibility
 
 
 To qualify for the CAREAssist program an individual must: 

 (1) Be HIV positive or have AIDS; and 

 (2) Reside in Oregon; and 

 (3) Have a monthly income based on family size which is at or below 550 percent of the FPL. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042, 431.250 & 431.830 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431.250 & 431.830
