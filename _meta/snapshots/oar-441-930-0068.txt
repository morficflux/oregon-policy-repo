 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 930 PREARRANGEMENT AND PRECONSTRUCTION PLANS 
 441-930-0068 
 Annual Report 
 
 Each master trustee is responsible for and shall file an annual report with the director by April 1 of each year on forms provided by the director. The report shall cover the preceding calendar year and shall include information on trusted funds:  (1) The actual value at the beginning and end of the calendar year;  (2) Deposits and withdrawals;  (3) Income earned and fees paid;  (4) Taxes paid for beneficiaries;  (5) Gains and losses; and  (6) The balance of all trust accounts as of December 31. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 97.926 & 97.935 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 97.935 
 
 History:
