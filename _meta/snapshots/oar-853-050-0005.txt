 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office, Long Term Care Administrators Board - Chapter 853 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 CONTINUING EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS 
 853-050-0005 
 Continuing Education Requirements for Residential Care Administrators 
 
 (1) To maintain an authorization, residential care facility administrators must complete a minimum of 20 hours of continuing education every authorization year. 

 (2) Credit hours obtained in excess of those required for the one-year reporting period cannot be carried forward or used to reinstate an inactive authorization. 

 (3) At least 1 hour of continuing education every other year must be in cultural competency coursework: 

 (a) Approved by the Oregon Health Authority under ORS 413.450; or 

 (b) That meets the skills requirements established by the Oregon Health Authority under ORS 413.450 by rule; 

 (4) Section (3) does not apply to authorization holders who are: 

 (a) Retired and not practicing the profession in any state; 

 (b) Not practicing the profession in this state; or 

 (c) Residing in this state, but not practicing in the profession in any state. 

 (5) Continuing education for residential care administrators must address subject matter related to the following domains: 

 (a) Leadership skills; 

 (b) Building requirements; 

 (c) Fire and life safety; 

 (d) Quality improvement processes; 

 (e) Human resources management; 

 (f) Health services; 

 (g) Role of the nurse and Oregon Nurse Delegation Act; 

 (h) Person-centered care principles; 

 (i) Memory care standards; 

 (j) Compliance and governance; 

 (k) Adult abuse protection and investigations; 

 (l) Oregon Revised Statutes and Oregon Administrative Rules. 

 (6) Documentation supporting compliance with continuing education requirements must be maintained for a period of two years following renewal and must be available to the Office upon request. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 678.000-678.820, ORS 678.990, 2018 House Bill 4129 & 2019 House Bill 2011 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 678.000-678.820, ORS 678.990, 2018 House Bill 4129 & 2019 House Bill 2011
