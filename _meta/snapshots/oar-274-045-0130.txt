 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 POST VIETNAM ERA VETERANS’ HOME LOAN PROGRAM 
 274-045-0130 
 Modification of Mortgage 
 
 (1) A request for modification of a mortgage must be made in writing by the borrower.  (2) The borrower and the Director shall agree in writing to the terms of the modification, and it shall be recorded in the mortgage records in the county where the security is located. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 407 
 
 History:
