 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 PRIVATE EMPLOYMENT AGENCY MATTERS 
 839-017-0040 
 Fee Paid Positions 
 
 (1) When an employment agency deals in both fee paid positions and in positions for which the applicant for employment pays the charge for service, and when the agency intends to assist individuals exclusively with referrals and placements in fee paid positions, the agency must enter into a written agreement with each individual applicant for employment. This agreement may be similar to that as provided in Appendix 2. If the applicant subsequently desires referrals to applicant fee paid positions, the agency must enter into a new contract in conformity with OAR 839-017-0011 to 839-017-0045 before any such referral is made.  (2) Written disclosure to the applicant for employment that is provided for in section (1) of this rule shall not be required when the position for which the applicant for employment is applying or being recruited will pay the applicant for employment no less than $30,000 per year on a salary basis.  [ED. NOTE: Appendices referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 658.210 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 658.005 - 658.245
