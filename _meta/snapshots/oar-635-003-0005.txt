 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 COMMERCIAL FISHERIES: OCEAN COMMERCIAL SALMON FISHERY 
 635-003-0005 
 Area 
 
 As used in this division, the Pacific Ocean includes that area of the Columbia River seaward of a straight line from the knuckle of the south jetty on the Oregon shore to the inshore end of the north jetty on the Washington shore. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 & 506 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496 & 506
