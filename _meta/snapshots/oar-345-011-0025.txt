 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 COUNCIL MEETINGS AND COMMUNICATIONS 
 345-011-0025 
 Consideration of Matters Not on Agenda 
 
 A Council member or the Council Secretary may, with the approval of a majority of Council members, raise matters at a meeting that the Council Secretary did not place on the agenda. The Council shall not act on a matter not on the agenda unless a majority of the members present agree that the matter is so substantial and of such immediate concern that the Council should not defer action until the next regular Council meeting. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.460
