 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 COMMERCIAL SHELLFISH AND MARINE INVERTEBRATE FISHERIES 
 635-005-0555 
 Incidental Catch Limits 
 
 (1) All groundfish, Dungeness crab and salmon, as defined in OAR 635-005-0240, taken in any commercial Tanner, Oregon hair and scarlet king crab fishery must be returned to the water immediately.  (2) All groundfish and salmon, as defined in OAR 635-005-0240, taken in any commercial red rock or box crab fishery must be returned to the water immediately. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & 506.129
