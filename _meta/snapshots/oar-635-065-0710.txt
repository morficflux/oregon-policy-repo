 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS 
 635-065-0710 
 Handguns — Requirements
 
 
 Hunters shall use: 

 (1) Any centerfire handgun .22 caliber or larger that is not fully automatic to hunt pronghorn antelope, black bear, cougar, or deer. 

 (2) Any centerfire handgun .24 caliber or larger that is not fully automatic to hunt bighorn sheep, Rocky Mountain goat, or elk. 

 (3) Any handgun that is not fully automatic to hunt western gray squirrels. 

 (4) Hunters shall not use military or full metal-jacket bullets in original or altered form. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
