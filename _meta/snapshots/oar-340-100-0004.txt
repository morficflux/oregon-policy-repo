 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 HAZARDOUS WASTE MANAGEMENT 
 340-100-0004 
 Table of Contents, Divisions 100 to 120 
 
 The following Divisions including the incorporation of regulations in 40 C.F.R. Parts 260 to 268, 270 and 124, comprise the Oregon hazardous waste management program:  DIVISION — SUBJECT:  100 — Hazardous Waste Management System: General.  101 — Identification and Listing of Hazardous Waste.  102 — Standards Applicable to Generators of Hazardous Waste.  103 — Standards Applicable to Transporters of Hazardous Waste.  104 — Standards for Owners and Operators of Hazardous Waste Treatment, Storage and Disposal Facilities.  105 — Management Facility Permits.  106 — Permitting Procedures.  109 — Management of Pesticide Wastes.  110 — Polychlorinated Biphenyls (PCBs).  120 — Additional Siting and Permitting Requirements for Hazardous Waste and PCB Treatment and Disposal Facilities.  124 — Standards Applicable to Dry Cleaning Facilities and Dry Stores.  142 — Oil and Hazardous Materials Emergency Response Requirements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 459, 466.020, 466.075, 466.105, 466.195 & 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.020, 466.075, 466.105 & 466.195
