 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 380 WATER RIGHT TRANSFERS 
 690-380-2300 
 Changes in Character of Use
 
 
 Water may only be used for the authorized purposes in the water right except as provided under ORS 540.510(3) and (8) and 540.520(8) and (9) or as approved by the Department through a water right transfer, certificate of registration modification under OAR chapter 690, division 382, instream lease or instream transfer under OAR chapter 690, division 77, allocation of conserved water under OAR chapter 690, division 18, or pursuant to OAR 690-380-2340. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025 & ORS 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 540.510 - 540.532
