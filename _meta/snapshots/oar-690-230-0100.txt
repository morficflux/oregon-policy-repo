 Water Resources Department

 Division 230 STANDARDS AND PROCEDURES FOR LOW-TEMPERATURE GEOTHERMAL PRODUCTION  AND INJECTION WELLS AND EFFLUENT  DISPOSAL SYSTEMS 
 690-230-0100 
 Additional Standards for Low-Temperature Geothermal Injection Wells 
 
 Procedures required to inject effluent into a low-temperature geothermal injection well shall not cause failure of the well casing and/or seal materials or other components of the well structure, including but not limited to, movement, displacement or fracturing of the overburden. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
