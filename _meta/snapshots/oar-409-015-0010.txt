 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Policy and Analytics - Chapter 409 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 HEALTH CARE FACILITY FINANCIAL REPORTING 
 409-015-0010 
 Report Forms 
 
 (1) All health care facilities shall file the required reports and data on forms provided or approved by the Authority.  (2) The Authority adopts and incorporates by reference the Patient Revenue and Unreimbursed Care form, Form FR-3.  (3) The Authority shall not accept obsolete forms.  [ED. NOTE: Forms referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 442.405, 442.420 & 442.425 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 442.425
