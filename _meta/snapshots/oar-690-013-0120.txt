 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 13 PETITION TO CHANGE DEPARTMENT RECORDS WITHIN WATER DISTRICTS 
 690-013-0120 
 Supplemental Permits 
 
 When supplemental permits apply to lands having perfected water rights, the supplemental permit shall be appurtenant to the land listed in the petition. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Ch. 1000 & OL 1989 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Ch. 1000 & OL 1989 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 1-1990, f. & cert. ef. 1-11-90
