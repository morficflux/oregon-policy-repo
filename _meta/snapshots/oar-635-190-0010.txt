 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 190 WILDLIFE MANAGEMENT PLAN 
 635-190-0010 
 Plan Objectives for Mule Deer Management
 
 
 The revised “2024 Mule Deer Population Management Objectives” (2026) and the revised “2024 Buck Ratio Management Objectives” (2026) function as a replacement to Table 1, “2024 Mule Deer Population Management Objectives” and Table 2, “2024 Buck Ratio Management Objectives” respectively, in Appendix 1 of the 2024 Oregon Mule Deer Management Plan and are incorporated here by reference into administrative rule.
