 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 300 PENALTIES 
 740-300-0035 
 Violation of ORS 825.100(2) — Penalties 
 
 Except as otherwise ordered by the Department in a particular case, any person who violates ORS 825.100(2) or any rule or order related thereto, in addition to any other penalties authorized by law, shall be subject to a penalty of $3,000. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 825.950 & Ch. 433 OL 2009 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.100 & 825.950
