 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES AND SYSTEMS 
 333-200-0060 
 Area Trauma Advisory Board Appointments
 
 
 (1) Each ATAB shall consist of at least 15 members who shall be broadly representative of the trauma area as a whole. 

 (2) Appointments to the ATABs shall be in accordance with the provisions of ORS 431A.070. 

 (3) Terms of appointment for ATAB members shall be as follows: 

 (a) Terms shall be for a period of three years; 

 (b) Vacancies shall be filled by appointment by the Authority in concurrence with the Governor; 

 (c) Members may serve unlimited terms at the discretion of the Authority; and 

 (d) Members are subject to removal with cause by the Authority. 

 (4) ATABs may recommend to the Authority that: 

 (a) Membership be expanded in order to improve coordination of the area trauma system; and 

 (b) Provider specialty positions are considered for ATAB appointments. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431A.065 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431A.070
