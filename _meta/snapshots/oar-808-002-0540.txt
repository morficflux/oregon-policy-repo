 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS 
 808-002-0540 
 Last-Known Address of Record
 
 
 "Last-known address of record" for a landscape construction professional, or a landscape contracting business, as used in ORS 671.603(2), means the mailing address provided by the landscape construction professional or landscape contracting business in writing to the agency. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & ORS 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.603
