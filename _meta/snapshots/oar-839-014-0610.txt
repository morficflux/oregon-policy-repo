 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 REGULATION OF FARMWORKER CAMP OPERATORS 
 839-014-0610 
 Emergency Access to Telephone
 
 
 (1) When employees occupy farmworker housing that is owned or controlled by the employer, the employer shall ensure that employees occupying the housing have reasonable access to an operating telephone at any time for emergency use. The telephone may be a pay telephone, at the discretion of the employer. 

 (2) Employers shall allow employees access to a telephone pursuant to the provisions of this rule, upon request of the employee. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 164, 165, 651, 658 & 659A.262(9) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 659A.253(3)
