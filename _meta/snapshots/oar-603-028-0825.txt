 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 MEAT PRODUCTS AND ESTABLISHMENTS 
 603-028-0825 
 Construction and Equipment 
 
 (1) A custom processing establishment is subject to the requirements specified in OAR 603-025-0020, 603-025-0030 and 603-025-0150.  (2) These requirements are not in lieu or any requirements specified in the Federal Wholesome Meat Act and those contained in the CFR Title 9, Chapter 3, Part 308.  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561, 616 & 619 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 603.085, 619.031 & 619.046
