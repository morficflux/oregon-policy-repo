 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 WILDLIFE MANAGEMENT PLANS: WILDLIFE DIVERSITY PLAN 
 635-100-0113 
 Threatened and Endangered Species Technical Committees 
 
 The director may appoint a technical committee to evaluate the information contained in a petition or staff report to either list, reclassify or remove a species from the state list. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.004, 496.171, 496.172, 496.182, 496.192 & 498.026 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.004, 496.171, 496.172, 496.182, 496.192 & 498.026 
 
 History:
