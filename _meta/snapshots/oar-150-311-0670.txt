 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 311 COLLECTION OF PROPERTY TAXES 
 150-311-0670 
 Deferred Taxes Paid by the Department 
 
 (1) When the department makes a deferral account payment to the county, the department will pay 97% of the amount of the tax assessed, and the county must credit that amount as full payment of such taxes, regardless of whether the department’s payment is before, on or after the payment deadline.  (2) The county may not charge the department interest on any deferral account payment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 311.676
