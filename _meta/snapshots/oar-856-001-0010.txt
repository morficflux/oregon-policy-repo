 Oregon Board of Maritime Pilots - Chapter 856 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 RULES OF PRACTICE AND PROCEDURE 
 856-001-0010 
 Organization
 
 
 (1) The Board of Maritime Pilots, created by ORS Chapter 776, is responsible for licensing of pilots in the State of Oregon and setting of pilotage rates. 

 (2) Information can be obtained from the Board’s office at 800 N.E. Oregon Street, Suite 507, Portland, OR 97232, telephone 971-673-1530. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 776 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 776.115
