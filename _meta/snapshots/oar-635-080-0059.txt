 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0059 
 Snake River Unit 
 
 The Snake River Unit, number 59, is that area beginning at confluence of Imnaha and Snake rivers; southeast along Snake River to Thirty-two Point Creek; southwest along Thirty-two Point Creek to Summit Road; southwest and northwest on Summit Road to North Fork Dry Creek; southwest along North Fork Dry Creek and northwest along Dry Creek to Imnaha River; north along Imnaha River to Snake River, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
