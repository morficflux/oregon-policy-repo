 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-0269 
 Summer Steelhead 
 
 (1) The following policy applies to summer steelhead in the McKenzie River Subbasin: Summer steelhead will be managed for production and harvest of hatchery fish — Option (1)(c) OAR 635-007-0525 of the Wild Fish Management Policy.  (2) In accordance with this policy, it is the objective of the Department to:  (a) Provide an average annual sport catch of 1,200 adult summer steelhead produced from a maximum release of 120,000 smolts;  (b) Reduce the potential impact of summer steelhead on the production of native trout and spring chinook;  (c) Develop a brood stock from adults returning to the McKenzie River to produce smolts for the McKenzie and Middle Fork Willamette Subbasins. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496 & 506 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496 & 506 
 
 History:
