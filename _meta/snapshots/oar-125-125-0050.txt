 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 125 STATEWIDE FACILITY PLANNING PROCESS 
 125-125-0050 
 Purpose, Application, and Authority
 
 
 These rules are adopted under ORS 276.056 and 276.227. They set forth the Statewide Facility Planning Process for state agencies and the duties of the Capital Planning Commission and Capital Projects Advisory Board, which assist the Department with the process. State agencies subject to these rules are required to provide information about their facilities and projects to the Department. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 276.227 & 276.056 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 276.227 & 276.056
