 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 215 MAINTENANCE, REPAIR AND DEEPENING OF WATER SUPPLY WELLS 
 690-215-0040 
 Casing and Sealing Wells after Disturbance 
 
 (1) If during the installation of casing, liner pipe, seals, packers, or during repair or deepening of a water supply well, the pre-existing casing is withdrawn, or moved as to compromise the annular seal, the well shall be cased and sealed in accordance with the rules set forth in OAR 690-210.  (2) If the annular seal is not compromised when cleaning out a water supply well or installing liner pipe, the water supply well shall not require re-casing or re-sealing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
