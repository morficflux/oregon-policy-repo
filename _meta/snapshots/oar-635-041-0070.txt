 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 41 COLUMBIA RIVER SYSTEM TREATY INDIAN FISHERIES 
 635-041-0070 
 Walleye Taken Incidentally to Commercial Season 
 
 (1) Walleye taken by net during any authorized commercial fishing season may be sold in accordance with OAR 635-006-0225.  (2) Notwithstanding OAR 635-011-0100, walleye may be retained for personal use. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496, 506 & 507 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496, 506 & 507
