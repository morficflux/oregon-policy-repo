 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 ADMINISTRATIVE PRACTICES 
 309-011-0120 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules prescribe standards to be implemented by the Addictions and Mental Health (AMH) Division in order to establish the Self-Determination Policy as used in ORS 430.071. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & 430.078 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 430.078 
 
 History:
