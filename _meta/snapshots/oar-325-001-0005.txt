 Oregon Patient Safety Commission 
 Chapter 325 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 325-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Patient Safety Commission adopts, in their entirety, the current Attorney General's Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 442.820 & 442.831 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
