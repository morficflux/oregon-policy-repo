 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 PRIVATE EMPLOYMENT AGENCY MATTERS 
 839-017-0476 
 Time for Maintenance of Records 
 
 (1) Except as provided in section (2) of this rule, all records of the employment agency will be maintained for a period of three years.  (2) Copies of all job advertisements with the information required by OAR 839-017-0477(6) thereon shall be maintained for a period of one year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 658.210 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 658.078
