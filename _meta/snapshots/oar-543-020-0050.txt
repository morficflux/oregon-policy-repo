 Chapter 543 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 GOVERNMENT INFORMATION SERVICES 
 543-020-0050 
 Scope 
 
 OAR chapter 543, division 20, applies only to the Government Information Services and to carry out the provisions of Chapter 328 (2015 Oregon Laws) 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 357.008(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 357.028, 357.029, 357.031 & 357.115
