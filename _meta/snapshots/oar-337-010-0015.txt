 Board of Medical Imaging 
 Chapter 337 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 LICENSURE 
 337-010-0015 
 Qualifications of Radiation Therapist
 
 
 In addition to those qualifications set out in ORS 688.475, applicants for licensure as a radiation therapist must submit evidence of current credentialing from the American Registry of Radiologic Technologists (ARRT) in radiation therapy. The Board will verify current credential status through review of the ARRT directory. If the applicant is not listed in the current ARRT directory, the applicant must request written verification of current credential and have the current credential submitted directly to the Board by the ARRT. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 688 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 688.455
