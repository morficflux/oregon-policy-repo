 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 93 ADMINISTRATIVE RULES GOVERNING THE ISSUANCE AND ENFORCEMENT OF GENERAL PERMITS WITHIN WATERS OF THIS STATE 
 141-093-0185 
 Jackson County Vernal Pools - Expiration
 
 
 This GP will expire on January 1, 2026 or when a combined total of 60 acres of vernal pool wetlands (up to 300 acres of vernal pool habitat complex) or other associated waters have been impacted, whichever occurs first. Upon expiration, the GP may be reviewed and modified or reissued. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.600 – 196.692 & 196.795 – 196.990 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.600 – 196.692 & 196.795 – 196.990
