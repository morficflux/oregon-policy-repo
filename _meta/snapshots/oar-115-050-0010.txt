 Employment Relations Board 
 Chapter 115 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 REVIEW AND ENFORCEMENT OF STATE PERSONNEL RELATIONS ARBITRATION AWARDS 
 115-050-0010 
 Written Exceptions 
 
 (1) A written exception to an arbitration award, pursuant to ORS 240.086(2), must:  (a) be filed with the Board within 14 days of the date of the award;  (b) specify which paragraph(s) of ORS 240.086(2) is (are) claimed as the basis for the exception, with a clear and concise statement of the facts on which the exception is based; and  (c) be accompanied by a copy of the award.  (2) Any response to the exceptions must be filed within 14 days of the date that the exceptions are filed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  240.086(3) & 243.766(7) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 240.086(2)
