 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 PRIVATE ADMISSION ASSESSMENT 
 411-071-0027 
 Confidentiality of Assessment Information 
 
 (1) Any records, forms, or information collected during the assessment process that identify an individual by name or address must be confidential and subject to the Department's rules on confidentiality set forth in OAR 411, division 005.  (2) Certified programs must not release information obtained during the assessment process to any person or entity not authorized by law to receive such information without the written consent of the individual or the individual's legal guardian. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.535 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.505 - 410.545
