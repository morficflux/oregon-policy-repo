 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 CLIENT RIGHTS 
 413-010-0714 
 Notice and Review when the Perpetrator is a Department Employee
 
 
 When the perpetrator is a Department employee, the Department will follow the Child Welfare Procedure Manual for notice and review outlined in “CPS Assessment and Founded CPS Disposition Review for Department Employees". 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 & ORS 409.050 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005
