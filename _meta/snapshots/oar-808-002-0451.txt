 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS 
 808-002-0451 
 Improper Work 
 
 "Improper"  work as used in ORS 671.690(2)(c) and ORS 671.695(1)(b) and (3)(b) means not suited to the circumstances, design or given job site.  

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & ORS 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.690 & ORS 671.695 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LCB 6-2018, adopt filed 09/17/2018, effective 09/18/2018
