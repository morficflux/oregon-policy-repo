 Oregon Health Authority 
 Chapter 943 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 PRIVACY AND CONFIDENTIALITY 
 943-014-0460 
 Authority Compliance Methods 
 
 The Authority may comply with these rules:  (1) By incorporating the business associate requirements contained in this rule into its contracts with business associates or by referencing these rules.  (2) By entering into a memorandum of understanding that accomplishes the objectives of these rules and meets the business associate requirements of the Privacy Rule and Security Rule, if the business associate is a government entity.  (3) By executing an amendment or rider that contains the contract provisions required by these rules or references to these rules and that amends the Authority’s contract with the business associate. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.505, 192.553, 192.556 - 192.581, 413.032, 413.042 & 414.065
