 Board of Geologist Examiners 
 Chapter 809 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 CERTIFICATION OF SPECIALTY 
 809-005-0005 
 Certification for Geologist-in-Training 
 
 To be certified as a geologist-in-training, the person shall pass an examination and pay the required fees. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 192 & 672 
 
 
 History:
