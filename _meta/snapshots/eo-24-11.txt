     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24-11


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     LARCH CREEK FIRE IN WASCO COUNTY


     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:


     The fire known as the “Larch Creek Fire” is burning in Wasco County.


     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Larch Creek Fire
     are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
     protection was requested by the Wasco County Commissioners and the State Fire
     Marshal concurs with that request.


     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Larch Creek Fire in Wasco
     County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
     personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.


     These findings were made at 8:06 pm on July 9, 2024, with the Department of the
     State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Green Incident Management
     Team will be entering into unified command with the Central Oregon Fire
     Management Service type 3 team. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Green Incident
     Management Team will be briefed at 11:00 pm on July 9, 2024, and I now confirm
     them with this Executive Order.


     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


     Ik,    The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
            the Larch Creek Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

     De     This conflagration is declared only for the Larch Creek Fire threatening
            structures in Wasco County.


     3      This order was made by verbal proclamation at 8:06 pm on the 9"" day of
            July 2024, and signed this 10" day of July 2024, in Salem, Oregon.
