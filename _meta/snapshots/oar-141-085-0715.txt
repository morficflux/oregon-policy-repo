 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 ADMINISTRATIVE RULES GOVERNING THE ISSUANCE AND ENFORCEMENT OF REMOVAL-FILL AUTHORIZATIONS WITHIN WATERS OF OREGON INCLUDING WETLANDS 
 141-085-0715 
 Mitigation for Temporary Impacts
 
 
 Applicants for projects that involve temporary impacts to waters of this state must provide a rehabilitation plan for rectification of temporary impacts. Rectification must include re-establishment of pre-existing contours and pre-existing vegetation. A monitoring plan to confirm the reestablishment of waters of this state, or reestablishment of vegetation may be required. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.600-196.692 & ORS 196.825 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.600-196.692 & 196.795 - 196.990
