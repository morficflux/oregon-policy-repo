 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 53 ELDERLY RENTAL ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (ERA) 
 813-053-0000 
 Purpose and Objectives
 
 
 The purpose of OAR chapter 813, division 53 of the administrative rules is to implement the Elderly Rental Assistance Program (ERA). ERA is administered through ORS 458.375. The purpose of ERA is to fund local homeless prevention programs that assist very-low-income elderly households, who are unstably housed, to attain housing stability. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 458.375 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 458.620, 458.650 & 458.610
