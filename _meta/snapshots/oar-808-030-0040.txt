 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 OWNER/ MANAGING EMPLOYEE, COURSE, AND TESTING 
 808-030-0040 
 Course Material and Subjects
 
 
 (1) The course required under ORS 671.595 must consist of the following subjects:¶ 
(a) License Requirements.;¶ 
(b) Insurance/Bonding.;¶ 
(c) Employment Law.;¶ 
(d) Contracts.;¶ 
(e) Supervision.;¶ 
(f) Claims/Dispute Resolution.;¶ 
(g) Lien Law.;¶ 
(h) Building Codes/Permits.;¶ 
(i) Safety/Hazard Communication.;¶ 
(j) Communications; and¶ 
(k) Estimating.¶ 
(2) The course material may consist of the Laws, Rules & Business Practices Guide. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & ORS 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.595
