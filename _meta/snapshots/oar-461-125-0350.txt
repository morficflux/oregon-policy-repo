 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 125 BASIS OF NEED 
 461-125-0350 
 Old Age as the Basis of Need; OSIP and OSIPM 
 
 Only a person who has attained the age of 65 years may be eligible for the OSIP-OAA or OSIPM-OAA program. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.404 & 411.706 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.404 & 411.706
