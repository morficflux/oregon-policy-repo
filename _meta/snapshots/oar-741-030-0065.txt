 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 STANDARDS TO DETERMINE PROJECT ELIGIBILITY AND APPLICATION PROCECURES FOR SHORTLINE RAILROAD REHABILITATION TAX CREDITS 
 741-030-0065 
 Fees for Certification 
 
 (1) When submitting an application for preliminary certification, applicants must include a payment of $250 to the Department. 

 (2) When submitting an application for final certification, applicants shall include a payment not to exceed one half of one percent (0.5%) of the estimated tax credit. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 823.011, 315.593 & 315.597 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 315.591 - 315.603
