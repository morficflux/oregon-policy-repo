 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 522 DESCHUTES BASIN WATER MANAGEMENT 
 690-522-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 The following rules are intended to operate in conjunction with the Deschutes Basin Ground Water Mitigation Rules in OAR Chapter 690, Division 505, and the Deschutes Basin Mitigation Bank and Mitigation Credit Rules in OAR Chapter 690, Division 521. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027 & ORS 537.746 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 537.746, Or Laws 2005, ch 669 & Or Laws 2011, ch 694 
 
 History:
