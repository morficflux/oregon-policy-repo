 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 715 RESPIRATORY THERAPIST AND POLYSOMNOGRAPHIC TECHNOLOGISTS LICENSING BOARD — LICENSURE; RENEWAL 
 331-715-0000 
 License Display and Posting Requirements
 
 
 (1) A licensee must show proof of valid license with the Office upon request or post the license document in public view at the licensee’s primary workplace; and 

 (2) A licensee must carry the license identification card (pocket card) with them, or post in plain view, the official license anytime services are being provided. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.568, ORS 676.615 & ORS 688.830 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.568, ORS 676.615 & ORS 688.830
