 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 QUALITY DEVELOPMENT AND LAND USE COORDINATION 
 123-008-0020 
 Compliance with Planning Goals 
 
 (1) The Department shall achieve Planning Goal compliance whenever possible by taking actions that are compatible with the applicable acknowledged comprehensive plan of a county or city government and land use regulations of this state and local zone ordinances.  (2) However, if a situation arises that necessitates direct goal findings, because of potential or actual incompatibility under a local comprehensive plan or other reasons, as described in OAR 660-030-0065(3), the Department shall adhere to the following procedures, as formally as appropriate:  (a) Confirm that a situation exists requiring the Department to adopt direct goal findings of compliance with one or more of Planning Goals;  (b) Identify which Planning Goals or Goal requirements the Department must address;  (c) Consult directly with affected jurisdictions;  (d) Request interpretative guidance as needed from the Department of Land Conservation and Development or the Department of Justice;  (e) Rely on any relevant goal interpretations for state agencies adopted in accordance with OAR chapter 660, whenever applicable; and  (f) Adopt any necessary findings to ensure compliance with the Planning Goals. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285B.075(5) & 285A.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.180, 285A & 285B
