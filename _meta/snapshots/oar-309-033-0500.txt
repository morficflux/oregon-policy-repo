 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 33 CIVIL COMMITMENT PROCEEDINGS 
 309-033-0500 
 Statement of Purpose and Statutory Authority 
 
 (1) Purpose. These rules prescribe standards for the approval of facilities that provide involuntary care, custody and treatment to persons in protective custody, in custody and on diversion.  (2) Statutory authority. These rules are authorized by ORS 426.395, and 426.005 through 426.395 and carry out the provisions of ORS 426.005 through 426.395. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & 426.060 - 426.500 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 426.005 - 426.395 
 
 History:
