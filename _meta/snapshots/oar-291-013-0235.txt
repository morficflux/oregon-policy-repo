 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 13 USE OF FORCE 
 291-013-0235 
 Reviews 
 
 In addition to the requirements of OAR 291-013-0140, use of force reviews shall be required as follows:  (1) A preliminary review is required whenever an employee unholsters a firearm, but does not point the firearm at an individual.  (2) A full review is required whenever an employee unholsters a firearm and points the firearm at an individual. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
