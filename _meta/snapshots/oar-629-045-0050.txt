 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 FORESTLAND CLASSIFICATION RULES 
 629-045-0050 
 Formal Written Order Requirements 
 
 A committee shall file the formal written order required by ORS 526.328(2) with the county clerk(s) within thirty days of its adoption by the committee. Any delay in meeting this thirty day requirement shall be promptly reported in writing to the State Forester. The State Forester may thereafter extend, in writing, the thirty day limitation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.041, Sec. 11 & Ch. 69 OL 2009 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 477.205, 477.230 & 526.305 - 526.370
