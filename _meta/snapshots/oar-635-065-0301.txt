 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 65 GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS 
 635-065-0301 
 General Black Bear Season
 
 
 Any person hunting bear during the general black bear season in the open area listed under general black bear season described in OAR chapter 635, division 066 shall have on their person an unused general season black bear tag or an unused Additional Bear Tag. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
