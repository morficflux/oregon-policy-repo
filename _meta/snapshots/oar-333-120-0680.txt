 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 STANDARDS FOR PROTECTION AGAINST RADIATION 
 333-120-0680 
 Records of Testing Entry Control Devices for Very High Radiation Areas 
 
 (1) Each licensee must maintain records of tests made under OAR 333-120-0240(1)(i) on entry control devices for very high radiation areas. These records must include the date, time, and results of each such test of function.  (2) The licensee must retain the records required by section (1) of this rule in accordance with OAR 333-100-0057. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
