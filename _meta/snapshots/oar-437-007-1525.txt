 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 FOREST ACTIVITIES 
 437-007-1525 
 Belayed (Snubbing) Climbing System 
 
 (1) The belay line must be a dynamic rope with a minimum 5400 pounds breaking strength.  (2) Do not use a body belay.  (3) Do not use a chest harness as the tie-in point.  (4) Follow the requirements of Appendix 7-K.  [ED. NOTE: Appendix referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSHA 3-2008, f. 3-7-08, cert. ef. 7-1-08
