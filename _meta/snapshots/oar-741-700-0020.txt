 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 700 UNIFORM SYSTEM OF ACCOUNTS FOR RAILROADS 
 741-700-0020 
 Class I Railroads and Subsidiary Railroad(s) Operating in Oregon 
 
 Annual reports will be submitted on the most current forms approved by the Department. Reports shall include:  (1) The Surface Transportation Board prescribed wage statistics Forms A and B;  (2) Freight Commodity Statistics; and  (3) Annual report including state supplemental pages. Reports for each data based year shall be filed on or before May 1 of the next year unless additional time is granted. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 823 & 824 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 824.030
