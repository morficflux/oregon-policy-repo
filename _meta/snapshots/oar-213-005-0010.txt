 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 SENTENCING 
 213-005-0010 
 Modification of Probationary Terms 
 
 (1) A sentencing judge may shorten or terminate a probationary sentence or transfer supervision to bench probation upon a finding that supervision is no longer necessary to accomplish the purposes of the imposed sentence.  (2) Modification of a probationary sentence which results in shortening the term, termination, or transfer to bench probation shall not require a hearing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667 - 137.669
