 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 CONSTRUCTION CARVE-OUT PROGRAMS 
 436-140-0050 
 Duties and Responsibilities of Employer 
 
 (1) An employer or group of employers that participates in a construction carve-out program shall comply with coverage requirements under ORS 656.017.  (2) The participating employer or group of employers shall report all claims made under the program to the insurer as with other claims.  (3) The participating employer or group of employers shall comply with the terms of the collective bargaining agreement and construction carve-out program. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) & 656.174 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.170, 656.172 & 656.174 
 
 History:
