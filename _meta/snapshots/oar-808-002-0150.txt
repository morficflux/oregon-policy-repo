 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS 
 808-002-0150 
 Backflow Assembly 
 
 “Backflow assembly,” as used in ORS 671.615, means the combination of a backflow device and two manual control valves on either side of the device, and the plumbing in between. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.615 & 671.617 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LCB 6-2005, f. 12-30-05, cert. ef. 1-1-06
