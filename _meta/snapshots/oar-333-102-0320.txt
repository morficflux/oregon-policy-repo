 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 102 LICENSING OF RADIOACTIVE MATERIAL 
 333-102-0320 
 Amendment of Licenses at Request of Licensee 
 
 Application for amendment of a license must be filed in accordance with OAR 333-102-0190 and must specify the respects in which the licensee desires the license to be amended and the grounds for such amendment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 & 453.665 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
