Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 60
GAME MAMMAL AND GAME BIRD CONTROLLED HUNT REGULATIONS

635-060-0022
Wait Period for Successful Bighorn Sheep and Rocky Mountain Goat Applicants

(1) A person successful in obtaining a 500 series ram hunt tag shall never reapply for any 500 series ram hunt tag.

(2) A person successful in obtaining a 900 series Rocky Mountain goat tag shall never reapply for any 900 series hunt tag.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
