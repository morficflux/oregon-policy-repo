 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 250 WATER DISTRIBUTION 
 690-250-0140 
 Special Problem Area Regulation 
 
 The watermaster may prohibit the diversion or use of water by anyone who has failed to comply with a Commission rule or order pursuant to a declaration of a serious water management problem area under ORS 540.435. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 540 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 540 
 
 History:
