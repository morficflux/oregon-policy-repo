 Oregon Department of Education 
 Fair Dismissal Appeals Board - Chapter 586 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 POLICY 
 586-010-0035 
 Record of Meetings 
 
 The minutes of all meetings and proceedings of the Board shall be prepared and maintained by the executive secretary and shall be approved by the Board. Minutes of the Board shall be available for inspection by any person at the office of the executive secretary during regular business hours. Copies may be obtained upon written request to the executive secretary. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183
