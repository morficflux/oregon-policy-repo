Maintenance of General Ledger and Object Tables

        .101      Statewide Financial Management Services (SFMS) Operations is responsible for maintaining the
                  general ledger and comptroller object tables in the Relational Statewide Accounting and
                  Reporting System (R*STARS). Statewide Accounting and Reporting Services will provide advice
                  and assistance to SFMS Operations to ensure that general ledger and comptroller object
                  definitions are in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles.


Agency Management’s Responsibility

        .102      Agency fiscal management is responsible for ensuring that fiscal staff make appropriate decisions
                  regarding the use of general ledger accounts and comptroller objects, in order to ensure the
                  accuracy and consistency of statewide financial reporting.
