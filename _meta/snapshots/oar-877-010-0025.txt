 Board of Licensed Social Workers 
 Chapter 877 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 BOARD DUTIES AND PROCEDURES 
 877-010-0025 
 Board Files
 
 
 Board records will be maintained pursuant to the requirements established by the Oregon Secretary of State, Archives Division, Board and Commission Records, OAR 166-350-0005 and 166-350-0010. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.510 - 675.600 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.510-675.600 & ORS 675.990
