 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Wheat Commission - Chapter 678 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENTS 
 678-010-0010 
 Applications of Assessment Rate to Wheat or Barley Mixtures 
 
 Any person who is a first purchaser or lien holder as defined by ORS 578.010, shall deduct and withhold the assessment as required by OAR 678-010-0030 on the gross weight of all grain mixtures that contain wheat, barley, or both that was grown in this state and sold through commercial channels, unless the grain mixture has been certified by the Federal Grain Inspection Service. If the grain mixture has been certified to contain a percentage of wheat, barley, or both, then the first purchaser or lien holder shall deduct and withhold the assessment as required by OAR 678-010-0030 on the percentage by weight of wheat, barley, or both that was grown in this state and sold through commercial channels. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 578 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 578
