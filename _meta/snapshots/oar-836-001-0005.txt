 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 GENERAL DEFINITIONS 
 836-001-0005 
 Insurance Code Definitions Adopted 
 
 The definitions given in the Oregon Insurance Code govern the meaning of terms used in administrative rules adopted by the Insurance Commissioner, except where the context otherwise requires. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 731.052 et seq. & 731.244 
 
 History:
