 Real Estate Agency 
 Chapter 863 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REAL ESTATE MARKETING ORGANIZATION RULES 
 863-010-0620 
 Term of License
 
 
 (1) A new real estate marketing organization license shall be for a period of not more than 13 months and a renewal of such license shall be for a period of not more than 12 months from the expiration of the prior license. 

 (2) A real estate marketing organization's license expires on June 30 each year. A licensee may not engage in real estate marketing activity after a license expires. 

 (3) The Agency may renew a real estate marketing organization's license when the Agency has received the following: 

 (a) The renewal fee authorized by OAR 863-010-0640. 

 (b) An online renewal. 

 (4) If the licensee fails to renew a license by the end of the renewal period but wishes to engage in real estate marketing activity, the licensee must submit a new initial license application pursuant to OAR 863-010-0610 and pay all required fees. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Sec. 4(1), Ch. 217 & OL 1995 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 696.606
