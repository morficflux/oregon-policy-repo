 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 DISABILITY RATING STANDARDS 
 436-035-0450 
 Immune System 
 
 (1) When exposure to physical, chemical, or biological agents has resulted in the development of an immunological response, impairment of the immune system is valued as follows:  (a) Class 1: 3% when the reaction is a nuisance but does not prevent most regular work related activities.  (b) Class 2: 8% when the reaction prevents some regular work related activities.  (c) Class 3: 13% when the reaction prevents most regular work related activities.  (2) An allergy is considered to be an immunologic state and is ratable under this rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.005, 656.214, 656.268 & 656.726
