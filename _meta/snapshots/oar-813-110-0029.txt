 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 OREGON AFFORDABLE HOUSING TAX CREDITS: AFFORDABLE HOUSING PROJECT CERTIFICATION 
 813-110-0029 
 Supplemental Application Charge
 
 
 A sponsoring entity of a project under the OAHTC program shall pay a supplemental application charge, as established by OHCS from time to time, when the sponsoring entity requests additional resources for a project that has already been funded under the OAHTC program. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 317.097 & ORS 456.515-456.725 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 317.097
