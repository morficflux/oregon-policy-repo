 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 COLUMBIA RIVER SYSTEM COMMERCIAL FISHERIES 
 635-042-0130 
 Smelt Season 
 
 Smelt may not be taken for commercial purposes from the Columbia River at any time. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.325, 506.109 & 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.129 & 507.030
