 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 CONSTRUCTION 
 437-003-0027 
 Applicable Rules 
 
 Whenever any employee is exposed to noise in the workplace, the requirements of OAR chapter 437, division 2/G, 1910.95, Occupational Noise Exposure shall apply.  NOTE: §1926.52 was not adopted by the Department. In Oregon, 437-003-0027 applies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295
