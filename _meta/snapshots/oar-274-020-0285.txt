 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 VETERANS’ LOANS 
 274-020-0285 
 Additional Loans 
 
 A veteran may receive this type of subsequent loan to replace an existing home unit on the existing ODVA security with a new home unit when the total funds do not exceed statutory limits. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 407.205 & 407.265
