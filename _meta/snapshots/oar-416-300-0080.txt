 Oregon Youth Authority 
 Chapter 416 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 300 Parole and Revocations 
 416-300-0080 
 Placement after Parole Revocation
 
 
 Parole revocation may result in an adjudicated youth’s placement in any youth correction facility. The adjudicated youth may contest the placement through the grievance process established in OAR chapter 416, division 20. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 420A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 420.045, 420A.115 & 420A.120
