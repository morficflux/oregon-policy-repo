 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 GENERAL OCCUPATIONAL SAFETY AND HEALTH RULES 
 437-002-2038 
 Dates 
 
 This rule is effective March 12, 2018, except:  (1) Change rooms and showers required by 437-002-2032 must be provided by March 11, 2019; and  (2) Engineering controls required by OAR 437-002-2029 must be implemented by March 10, 2020. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 – 654.295 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSHA 3-2017, f. 7-7-17, cert. ef. 3-12-18
