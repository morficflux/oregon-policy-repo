 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 910 ELECTROLOGY 
 331-910-0030 
 Approved Examination for Electrology
 
 
 (1) Written examination: The Office recognizes approved written examination for electrology. 

 (2) Practical examination: The Office recognizes and approves the Practical Examination Objectives and Scoring Criteria implemented on April 15, 2023, as the approved practical examination. 

 (3) The Oregon Laws and Rules examination related to safety and infection control requirements implemented on April 15, 2023, for electrology administered by the Office. 

 (4) The practical examination listed in subsection (2) of this rule must be administered at a HECC OCS authorized to teach the Electrology Approved Curriculum implemented on April 15, 2023, listed in OAR 331-910-0005 or by a supervisor who is authorized to provide the Electrology Training Program, listed in OAR 331-910-0007. 

 (5) The Office may review any approved or administered examination, including written objectives and practical examination assessments, upon request. 

 (6) If the examination the applicant took prior to applying for Oregon licensure is substantially equivalent to the examinations listed in this rule, the Office may waive an examination, pursuant to ORS 690.405. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  676.615, 690.365, 690.370, 690.405 & 676.568 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  690.365, 690.370 & 690.405
