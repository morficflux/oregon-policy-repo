 Board of Massage Therapists 
 Chapter 334 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 334-001-0020 
 Contracting and Procurement 
 
 (1) It is the policy of the Board to conduct its procurement efforts to ensure a process that promotes fairness, integrity, security, and honesty to maximize revenue and achieve the best value for the Board.  (2) These rules are used to simplify, clarify and modernize the public contracting of the Board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  SB 1127 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Section 5(4)
