 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 180 SHARED SERVICES FUND 
 125-180-1020 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) “Average Eligible Worker” means the average worker reported by Business Oregon as a new hire or retained employee on a Strategic Investment Program (SIP) project.  (2) “Eligible Project” is a project described in ORS 285C.600(2) for which the Oregon Business Development Commission has reported new hires or retained employees. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.340 & 285C.615(7) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285C.600 - 285C.626 
 
 History:
