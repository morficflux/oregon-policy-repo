 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 APPRENTICESHIP AND TRAINING COUNCIL 
 839-011-0530 
 Apprenticeship and Training Council Subpoenas — Subpoena Ad Testificandum 
 
 (1) A subpoena ad testificandum may be issued to any person when the conditions set out in 839-011-0105(1) or 839-011-0515(2) have been met.  (2) The subpoena ad testificandum must give the person a reasonable time for preparation and travel to the place of attendance and the place of attendance must be suitable place in the vicinity to which testimony is applicable. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 660.120(1)
