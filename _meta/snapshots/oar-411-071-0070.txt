 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 PRIVATE ADMISSION ASSESSMENT 
 411-071-0070 
 Orientation Requirement 
 
 (1) Management and supervisory personnel responsible for the admission assessment activities of the program applying for certification must participate in orientation or training sessions conducted by the Department.  (2) All personnel of the certified program, who will be performing admission assessments, must participate in the earliest available orientation or training session conducted by or approved by the Department on the admission assessment process, the forms designated by the Department and the continuum of long term care options available. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.535 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.505 - 410.545
