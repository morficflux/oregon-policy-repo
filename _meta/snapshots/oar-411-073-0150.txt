 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 73 NURSING FACILITIES/MEDICAID — REMEDIES 
 411-073-0150 
 Change of Ownership 
 
 (1) A facility may not avoid a remedy on the basis it underwent a change of ownership.  (2) If a facility has undergone a change of ownership the Division will not restart the count of repeated substandard quality of care surveys unless the new owner can demonstrate to the satisfaction of the Division that the poor past performance is no longer a factor. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.070 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 SSD 11-1995, f. 9-29-95, cert. ef. 10-1-95
