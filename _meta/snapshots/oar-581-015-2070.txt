 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2070 
 Nonacademic Services 
 
 (1) School districts must take steps, including the provision of supplementary aids and services determined appropriate and necessary by the child’s IEP team, to provide nonacademic and extracurricular services and activities in a manner to afford children with disabilities an equal opportunity for participation in those services and activities.  (2) Nonacademic and extracurricular services and activities may include meals, recess periods, counseling services, athletics, transportation, health services, recreational activities, special interest groups or clubs sponsored by the school district, referrals to agencies that provide assistance to individuals with disabilities, and employment of students, including both employment by the school district and assistance in making outside employment available. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.041, 343.045 & 343.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 343.045 & 34 CFR 300.107
