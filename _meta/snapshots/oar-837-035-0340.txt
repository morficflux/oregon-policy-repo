 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 FIRE STANDARD COMPLIANT (REDUCED IGNITION PROPENSITY) CIGARETTES 
 837-035-0340 
 Procedures, Hearings and Judicial Review 
 
 (1) Hearings are conducted according to ORS 183.413 through 183.470.  (2) The Attorney General may bring action for the OSFM to:  (a) Seek injunctive relief to prevent or end a violatio;  (b) Recover civil penaltie;  (c) Recover attorney fees and other enforcement costs and disbursements. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 476.755 - 476.806 & 476.995
