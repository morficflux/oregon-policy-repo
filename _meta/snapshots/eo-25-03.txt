       EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25-03


       DETERMINATION OF STATE OF EMERGENCY IN TILLAMOOK,
       CLACKAMAS, POLK, LINCOLN, LANE, COOS, CURRY, DOUGLAS,
       JACKSON, WASCO, LAKE, UMATILLA, WHEELER, GRANT, AND
       WALLOWA DUE TO HEAVY RAIN AND FLOODING


       Pursuant to ORS 401.165, I find that a threat to life, safety, property, and significant
       damage to the state roads on the federal-aid highway system beginning December 16,
       2024 through January 6, 2025 due to heavy rainfall and flooding from the atmospheric
       river caused disaster conditions at multiple sites in Tillamook, Clackamas, Polk, Lincoln,
       Lane, Coos, Curry, Douglas, Jackson, Wasco, Lake, Umatilla, Wheeler, Grant, and
       Wallowa Counties requiring significant efforts and expense to clean up and repair the
       damage.


       IT IS ORDERED AND DIRECTED:


           1.   Pursuant to ORS 401.165, and based on the facts described above, an emergency
                has occurred, and a state of emergency is declared for the above-listed counties
                from December 16, 2024, and continuing through January 06, 2025.


          2.    The Oregon Department of Transportation shall provide appropriate assistance
                and seek federal resources to affect repair and reconstruction of $26 million
                dollars or more in damages and impact to the federal aid highway system in the
                above-listed counties.


          3.    This declaration is limited to the above-listed counties. It is limited to the
|               provision of assistance by the Oregon Department of Transportation to repair and
                reconstruct eligible roadways that are part of the federal highway aid system in
                the above-listed counties. This declaration is not to be construed as a
                comprehensive declaration or proclamation of emergency for other purposes.

                Done at Salem, Oregon, this 6'" day of February, 2025.
