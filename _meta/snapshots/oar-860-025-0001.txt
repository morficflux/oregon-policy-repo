 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 REGULATIONS TO PREVENT DUPLICATION OF FACILITIES 
 860-025-0001 
 Definitions for Territory Allocated to Electric Utilities, Gas Utilities, and Large Telecommunications Utilities 
 
 For purposes of this Division, except when a different scope is explicitly stated:  (1) “Electric utility” means an electric utility as defined in ORS 757.600(13).  (2) “Gas utility” means a public utility as defined in ORS 757.005 that supplies natural gas.  (3) “Large telecommunications utility” means any telecommunications utility as defined in ORS 759.005 that is not partially exempt from regulation under ORS 759.040.  (4) “Utility service” means utility service as defined for electric and gas utilities in ORS 758.400(3) and telecommunications utility service as defined in ORS 759.500(3). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756, 757 & 759 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040, 757.005, 758.400, 759.005 & 759.500
