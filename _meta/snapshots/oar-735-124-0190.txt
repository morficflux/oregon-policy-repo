 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 124 CONSTRUCTION OF VEHICLES 
 735-124-0190 
 Horn 
 
 Every vehicle shall be equipped with a horn or horns in good working order and capable of emitting sounds audible under normal conditions from a distance of not less than 200 feet. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 815.230 & 820.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 820.010 - 820.030
