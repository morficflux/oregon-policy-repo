 Department of Early Learning and Care 
 Chapter 414 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 GENERAL AND INTERNAL RULES 
 414-050-0000 
 Hearings and Rulemaking Procedures
 
 
 The Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure effective December 9, 2003 shall be applicable to rulemaking functions and to all contested case hearings of the Child Care Licensing Division of the Department of Early Learning and Care. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.430 & ORS 329A.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 326.430
