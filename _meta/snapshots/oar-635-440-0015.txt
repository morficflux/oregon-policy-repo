 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 440 COLUMBIA RIVER FISHERIES TRANSITION PROGRAM 
 635-440-0015 
 Distribution of Funds by County 
 
 Funds received by a county program from the Department may only be used to reimburse the following expenses or losses:  (1) Compensation to permit holders for documented economic harm  (2) Compensation to permit holders for gear changes.  (3) Compensation to the county for allowable expenditures necessary to implement the county program during the calendar year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 509.230 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 508.775 - 508.796 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DFW 160-2014, f. & cert. ef. 12-8-14
