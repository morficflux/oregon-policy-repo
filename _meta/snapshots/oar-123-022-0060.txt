 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 SMALL BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT CENTERS 
 123-022-0060 
 Purpose
 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to provide procedures, standards, and criteria for making grants under ORS 285B.168 and 285A.340 to 285A.349 to community colleges, community college service districts, and other allowed entities to assist in the improvement and formation of small business development centers. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  285B.165 - 285B.180 & ORS 285A.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285A.340 - 285A.349 & 285B.165 - 285B.180 
 
 History:
