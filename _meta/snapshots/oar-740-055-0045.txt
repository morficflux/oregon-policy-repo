 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 RECORDS, REPORTS, AND ACCOUNTING FEES AND TAXES 
 740-055-0045 
 Determination of Mailing Date 
 
 For reports and payments transmitted through the United States Postal Service on which the post-office cancellation mark on the envelope or wrapper containing the report or payment is omitted or is not legible, the department will consider the report or fee deposited in the United States mail as five (5) postal delivery days prior to the date received by the department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 823.011 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.225, 825.472 & 825.555 
 
 History:
