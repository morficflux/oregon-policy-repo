 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 ADULT FOSTER HOMES 
 309-040-0455 
 Enjoinment of AFH Operation
 
 
 The Division may commence an action to enjoin the operation of an AFH pursuant to ORS 443.775(5): 

 (1) When an AFH is operated without a valid license; or 

 (2) After notice of revocation, non-renewal, or suspension has been given, a reasonable time for placement of residents in other facilities has been allowed, and such placement has not been accomplished. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 443.705 - 443.825
