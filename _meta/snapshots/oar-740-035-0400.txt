 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 CERTIFICATES, PERMITS, LICENSES AND DOCUMENTS 
 740-035-0400 
 Commissions Paid to Agents 
 
 Motor carriers are forbidden to pay commissions or grant consideration to their agents upon charges for motor carrier services performed for such agents. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 823 & 825 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.224
