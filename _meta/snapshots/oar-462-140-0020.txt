 Oregon Racing Commission 
 Chapter 462 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 DUTIES 
 462-140-0020 
 Mutuel Manager 
 
 (1) The mutuel manager shall be in charge of the mutuel department and shall enforce all pari-mutuel rules of the commission.  (2) The mutuel manager shall ensure that minors are not allowed to wager.  (3) The mutuel manager shall ensure that the wagering facilities and pari-mutuel clerks present a neat appearance and that the pari-mutuel clerks conduct themselves in an orderly, courteous and professional manner. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 462.270(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 462.270 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 RC 3-2000, f. 3-27-00, cert. ef. 5-1-00
