 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 74 ISSUANCE OF PERMITS FOR COMBINATIONS OF VEHICLES HAVING GROSS WEIGHTS IN EXCESS OF 80,000 POUNDS 
 734-074-0090 
 Permit Requirements for Triple Trailer Operators 
 
 (1) The third cargo carrying unit of a triple trailer combination cannot be a liquid cargo tank. 

 (2) A carrier operating under Division 74 rules must report total miles driven in Oregon while operating triple trailer combinations to the CCD in the form it requests. Triple trailer annual mileage reports must be received by the CCD by March 31 of the year following the year the miles were driven. Failure to timely report triples miles may result in cancellation or non-renewal of a motor carrier’s triple trailer permits. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 818.200 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.200 & 818.220
