 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 122 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR INDUSTRIAL X-RAY MACHINE OPERATIONS 
 333-122-0301 
 X-ray and Hybrid Gauges: Surveys 
 
 (1) Radiation surveys, as required by OAR 333-120-0200, of all X-ray and Hybrid gauges sufficient to show compliance with OAR 333-122-0101 shall be performed: 

 (a) Upon installation of the equipment; 

 (b) When reviewing safety devices every six months to insure their proper operation; 

 (c) When evaluating annual exposure or dose surveys and personal radiation exposure monitoring devices to ensure that operations are conducted safely; 

 (d) Following any change in the initial arrangement, number or type of local components in the system; 

 (e) Following any maintenance requiring the disassembly or removal of a local component in the system; 

 (f) Any time a visual inspection of the local components in the system reveals an abnormal condition; 

 (g) Whenever individual monitoring devices show a significant increase over the previous monitoring period or when the readings are approaching the radiation dose limits specified in OAR 333-120-0100. 

 (2) Records of all reviews and surveys shall be maintained in accordance with OAR 333-122-0240 for inspection by the Authority. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807
