 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 810 GENERAL 
 441-810-0020 
 Duration of Registration 
 
 All registrations shall be issued on the date all registration qualifications are met. Registrations issued before November 1st shall expire at the end of the calendar year. Registrations issued on or after November 1st shall expire at the end of the following calendar year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 697 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 697.031(3)
