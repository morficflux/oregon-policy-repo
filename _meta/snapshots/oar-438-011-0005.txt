 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 BOARD REVIEW; THIRD PARTY ORDERS 
 438-011-0005 
 Request for Board Review 
 
 (1) The time for and manner of filing a request for Board review of a Administrative Law Judge's order are set forth in ORS 656.289, 656.295, and OAR 438-005-0046.  (2) Copies of a request for Board review of an Administrative Law Judge's order shall be simultaneously served on all parties to the proceeding and to their attorneys, if represented by an attorney.  (3) The request should recite the name of the claimant, the WCB case number, the identity of the party requesting review and should contain a brief statement of the reason review is requested.  (4) The request should also recite whether payment of compensation will be stayed under ORS 656.313. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.289(3) & 656.295(1)(2)
