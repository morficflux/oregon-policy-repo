 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 SUBSTITUTE CARE — FUNDING ELIGIBILITY 
 413-100-0432 
 Payments for Services Provided in Emergency Situations When a Child or Young Adult is Not Enrolled in Title XIX Medicaid 
 
 Payment may be made for emergency medical services only for a child or young adult in the Department’s care or custody. The payment is made from the local office’s “Other Medical” budget. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CWP 12-2014, f. & cert. ef. 6-12-14
