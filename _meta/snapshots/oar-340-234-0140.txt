 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 234 EMISSION STANDARDS FOR WOOD PRODUCTS INDUSTRIES 
 340-234-0140 
 Wigwam Waste Burners: Existing Administrative Agency Orders 
 
 The provisions of OAR 340-234-0100 supersede any specific existing agency orders directed against specific parties or persons to abate air pollution.  NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A .025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025
