 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 86 WETLAND CONSERVATION PLAN 
 141-086-0228 
 Review and Approval Process 
 
 (1) A draft of all the LWI products required in OAR 141-086-0210 through -0225 of these rules must be provided to the Department (if the inventory was not developed by the Department) and the local government(s) for review.  (2) The local government must provide opportunity for public review of and comment on the draft LWI products.  (3) Public and local government comments on draft LWI products must be provided to the Department. The Department will request in writing from the party responsible for preparing the LWI any revisions or additions required in order for the LWI to be approved.  (4) The Department will review final products to ensure that all changes requested by the Department have been adequately addressed.  (5) If the final LWI products meet the requirements in these rules, the Department will send a letter of approval to the local government. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 273.045 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.668 - 196.686 & 196.692
