 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 MARIJUANA AND HEMP TESTING 
 333-007-0370 
 Sampling Personnel Requirements; Sampling Recordkeeping
 
 
 (1) Only individuals employed by a laboratory with an ORELAP accredited scope item for sampling under these rules may take samples. 

 (2) Sampling may only be conducted at a licensee’s or registrant’s premises or the licensee or registrant may transport the entire batch to a laboratory with an ORELAP accredited scope item for sampling under these rules. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.544 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.544
