 Oregon Racing Commission 
 Chapter 462 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 DUTIES 
 462-140-0030 
 Vendor 
 
 Vendors must have a list of products they sell attached to the license application and a current copy of all state permits and licenses to dispense such products. The list of products and any changes to that list must be approved by a commission veterinarian and/or stewards. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 462.270(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 462.270
