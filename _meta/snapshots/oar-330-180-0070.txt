 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 180 GREENHOUSE GAS EMISSIONS STANDARD 
 330-180-0070 
 Exemptions 
 
 The requirement for a governing body to report exemptions to the department as required by ORS 757.528(7) must be in writing and may be submitted electronically or by regular mail. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 757.522 - 757.538 & 469.040 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 757.522 - 757.538 
 
 History:
