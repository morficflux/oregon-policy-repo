 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 380 WATER RIGHT TRANSFERS 
 690-380-4030 
 Protests and Hearings
 
 
 (1) Except as described subsection (2) of this rule, protests of, requests for party status, and contested case proceedings concerning proposed final orders are governed by Or Laws 2025, ch 575 and OAR chapter 690 division 002. Proposed final orders shall become final if no protest is filed or by default as provided in OAR 690-002-0235. 

 (2) Within 30 days after the publication in the department’s weekly public notice any person may file a protest against the proposed final order. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025 & ORS 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.310 - 183.550, ORS 536.050, ORS 540.530 & Or Laws 2025, ch 575
