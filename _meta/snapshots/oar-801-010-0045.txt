 Oregon Board of Accountancy 
 Chapter 801 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 FEES, APPLICATIONS, REQUIREMENTS, PROCEDURES, AND P.C.’S 
 801-010-0045 
 Uniform Certified Public Accountant Exam
 
 
 CPA exam rules. The Board Recognizes the rules of conduct for CPA examination candidates as published in the CPA Exam Candidate Guite. The Board may deny credit for any or all sections of the exam and may prohibit candidates from retaking the exam for any of the following reasons: 

 (1) Conduct that violates the CPA Examination Rules of Conduct, 

 (2) Violation of the confidentiality provisions of the CPA Examination Rules of Conduct, which shall result in denial of credit for the candidate's scores on the CPA exam and the candidate shall be prohibited from retaking the CPA exam for a period of ten years. 
 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 673.410 & 673.060 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 673.060 & 673.410
