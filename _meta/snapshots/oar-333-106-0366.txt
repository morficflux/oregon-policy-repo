 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 106 X-RAYS IN THE HEALING ARTS 
 333-106-0366 
 Computed Tomography X-ray Systems: Dose Limits 
 
 The CTDIvol for the following CT examinations on standard phantoms shall not exceed the dose limits listed below:  (1) Adult head, 80 mGy;  (2) Adult abdomen, 30 mGy;  (3) Pediatric abdomen (five years of age or 40 pounds, 20 mGy; and  (4) Pediatric head, 40 mGy. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807 
 
 History:
