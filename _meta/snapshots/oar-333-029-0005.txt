 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 29 TRAVELERS’ ACCOMMODATION RULES 
 333-029-0005 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules adopted pursuant to the provisions of ORS 446.330, prescribe the requirements for the construction and operation of travelers’ accommodations and hostels. They are for the purpose of protecting the health and welfare of persons using those facilities. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.330 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.315 & 446.310 - 446.375
