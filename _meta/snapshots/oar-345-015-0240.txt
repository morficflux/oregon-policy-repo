 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 PROCEDURES GOVERNING COUNCIL AND DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY PROCEEDINGS, INCLUDING SITE CERTIFICATE HEARINGS 
 345-015-0240 
 The Decision-Making Record 
 
 The decision-making record on an application for a site certificate includes the decision record for the Department of Energy’s proposed order and the record of the contested case proceeding. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.370
