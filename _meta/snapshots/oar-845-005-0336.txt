 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 CRITERIA FOR ISSUANCE AND MAINTENANCE OF LICENSES 
 845-005-0336 
 Certificate of Authority Holder’s Sales Employees 
 
 For the purposes of ORS 471.162(3), a Certificate of Authority holder is a licensee of the Commission authorized to sell and ship malt beverages and wine to licensed Oregon wholesalers. Employees of a Certificate of Authority holder may sell alcoholic beverages on the employers behalf to licensed Oregon wholesalers. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 471, including 471.030, 471.040, 471.730(1) & (5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 471.162(3) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OLCC 19-2000, f. 12-6-00, cert. ef. 1-1-01
