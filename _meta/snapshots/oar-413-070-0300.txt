 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 SUBSTITUTE CARE 
 413-070-0300 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 413-070-0300 to 413-070-0380 is to prescribe conditions that must be met for the Department to remove a refugee child from home. These rules also establish the Refugee Child Welfare Advisory Committee (RCWAC) and set the criteria for its operations and duties. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 & 418.945 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005 & 418.925 - 418.945
