 Board of Nursing 
 Chapter 851 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ADMINISTRATION 
 851-010-0035 
 Meetings 
 
 Additional meetings may be held when necessary. Meeting dates are approved by the Board. The agenda for Board members to review shall be sent to them at least twelve days prior to the regular meeting date. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 678.150 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 678.150
