 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 160 ELECTRONIC DATA INTERCHANGE; MEDICAL BILL DATA 
 436-160-0440 
 Monitoring and Auditing Insurers 
 
 (1) The director may monitor and conduct periodic audits of medical bill data to ensure compliance with ORS chapter 656 and these rules.  (2) All records maintained or required to be maintained must be disclosed upon request by the director. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.252, 656.254, 656.264, 656.455 & 656.726
