 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 OFFENSE SUBCATEGORIES 
 213-018-0065 
 Sodomy I (ORS 163.405) 
 
 (1) CRIME CATEGORY 10: Sodomy I shall be ranked at Crime Category 10 if one or more of the following factors was included in the commission of the offense:  (a) The offender used or threatened to use a weapon;  (b) The offender caused or threatened to cause serious physical injury;  (c) The victim was under the age of twelve; or  (d) The victim was incapable of consent by reason of mental defect, mental incapacitation or physical helplessness.  (2) CRIME CATEGORY 9: Sodomy I shall be ranked at Crime Category 9 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 10. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667 - 137.669 
 
 History:
