 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 306 PROPERTY TAX GENERALLY 
 150-306-0200 
 Application Filing by Telephonic Facsimile (FAX) 
 
 Application Filing by Electronic Means  The Department of Revenue or any county may accept any application for property tax exemption or special assessment by telephonic facsimile (FAX) or other electronic means according to the policies and procedures of the receiving agency. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 & 306.265 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 306.265
