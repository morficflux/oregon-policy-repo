 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 AIRCRAFT DEALER’S LICENSE 
 738-060-0060 
 Individual Registration 
 
 Nothing in this rule or in ORS 837.075 prohibits the registering of a dealer’s aircraft individually under ORS 837.040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184, 835 & 837 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 837.075 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 1AD 1-1983, f. & ef. 2-14-83
