 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 LOCKSMITH CERTIFICATION 
 812-030-0250 
 Application, Renewal and Certificate Fees 
 
 (1) The application fee for all certificates is $60.  (2) The fee for the test required under OAR 812-030-0010 is $60.  (3) The fee for issuance of an initial two-year certificate is $60.  (4) The fee for renewal of a two-year certificate is $60.  (5) All fees are non-refundable and non-transferrable. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 701.235 & 701.485 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701.480 & 701.485 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CCB 3-2010, f. & cert. ef. 2-1-10
