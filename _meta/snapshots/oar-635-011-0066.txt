 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 SPORT FISHING REGULATIONS FOR FISH, SHELLFISH, AND MARINE INVERTEBRATES: STATEWIDE ANGLING REGULATIONS 
 635-011-0066 
 Authority to Take Fish
 
 
 In accordance with ORS 496.162 and 506.129, Department staff and designated representatives may take fish and shellfish necessary for Department programs in the times, places and manner necessary to collect broodstock for STEP and hatchery programs, take for scientific purposes, collect excess hatchery returns, trap and haul juvenile and adult fish, monitor and evaluate various species and stocks of fish, cull for conservation purposes, and for such other purposes that implement Commission approved basin and species plans. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.162 & ORS 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.162 & ORS 506.129
