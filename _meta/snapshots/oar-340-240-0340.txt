 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 240 RULES FOR AREAS WITH UNIQUE AIR QUALITY NEEDS 
 340-240-0340 
 La Grande Urban Growth Area: Hardboard Manufacturing Plants 
 
 No person may cause or permit the total emissions of particulate matter from all sources within a hardboard plant, other than press/cooling vents, in excess of 0.25 pounds per 1,000 square feet of hardboard produced on a 1/8" basis of finished product equivalent.  NOTE: These rules are included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 & 468A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025
