 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2435 
 Requirements of an Interim Alternative Educational Setting 
 
 An interim alternative educational setting under OAR 581-015-2415 and 581-015-2425 must:  (1) Be determined by the child’s IEP team; and  (2) Enable the child to:  (a) Continue to participate in the general curriculum, although in another setting; and  (b) Progress toward achieving the goals in the child's IEP. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.041, 343.045 & 343.155 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 343.155 & 34 CFR 300.530
