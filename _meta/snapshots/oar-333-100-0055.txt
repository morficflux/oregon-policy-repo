 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 CONTROL OF RADIATION IN OREGON – GENERAL REQUIREMENTS 
 333-100-0055 
 Records 
 
 Each licensee and registrant must maintain records showing the receipt, transfer, and disposal of all sources of radiation. Additional record requirements are specified elsewhere in these rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
