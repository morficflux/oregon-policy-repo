 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 66 LEAVE REVOCATION HEARINGS 
 291-066-0160 
 Rejection of Waiver 
 
 If the Releasing Authority or designee is not satisfied that the AIC knowingly and intelligently waived his or her right to a revocation hearing or revocation hearing rights, or if additional information is needed before making its decision, it may order a new hearing, to be conducted by the Hearings Officer. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075, 421.168 & 421.500 to 421.512 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075, 421.168 & 421.500 to 421.512
