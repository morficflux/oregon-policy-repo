 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Addiction Services - Chapter 415 
 
 
 
 
 Division 52 RECOVERY HOMES FOR PEOPLE IN RECOVERY FROM ALCOHOL AND DRUG ABUSE OR DEPENDENCY 
 415-052-0100 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules prescribe standards for providing financial assistance in the form of loans under 42 U.S.C. 300x-25 to support the establishment of recovery homes for people in recovery from alcohol and drug abuse or dependency. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 413.042 & 430.256 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 90.100 - 90.459, 105.105 - 105.168, 430.265 - 430.920 & 279B 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ADS 2-2009, f. & cert. ef. 12-3-09
