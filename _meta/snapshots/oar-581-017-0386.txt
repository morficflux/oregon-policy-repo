 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0386 
 Eligibility 
 
 (1) The following entities shall be eligible to receive the K–12 Biliteracy Pathways Grant:  (a) School districts  (b) Public charter schools; and  (c) Consortium of school districts, public charter schools or an Education Service District (ESD). Each consortium must have at least one school district or public charter school as a member.  (2) A single grant proposal may include more than one eligible applicant. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.051 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 336.079
