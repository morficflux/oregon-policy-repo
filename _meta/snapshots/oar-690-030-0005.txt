 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 CONTESTS IN THE ADJUDICATION OF WATER RIGHTS 
 690-030-0005 
 Rules of the Water Resources Director 
 
 The following rules, OAR 690-030-0005 to 690-030-0090, have been adopted by the Water Resources Director to govern the practice and procedure relative to contests before him. A substantial compliance with the rules is required. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536 & 543 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 3, f. & ef. 2-18-77
