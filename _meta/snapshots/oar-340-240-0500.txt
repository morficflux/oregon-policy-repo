 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 240 RULES FOR AREAS WITH UNIQUE AIR QUALITY NEEDS 
 340-240-0500 
 Klamath Falls Maintenance Area: Applicability
 
 
 OAR 340-240-0500 through 340-240-0630 apply in the Klamath Falls Nonattainment Area beginning January 1, 2013. Upon EPA approval of the maintenance plan and redesignation, these rules will continue to apply to the Maintenance Area. 

 [NOTE: This rule is included in the State of Oregon Clean Air Act Implementation Plan that EQC adopted under OAR 340-200-0040.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468A 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025
