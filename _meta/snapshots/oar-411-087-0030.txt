 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 87 NURSING FACILITIES/LICENSING —  PHYSICAL ENVIRONMENT 
 411-087-0030 
 Waivers for Physical Environment Requirements 
 
 (1) Request. Any request for a waiver of these building requirements (OAR 411, division 087) shall comply with OAR 411-085-0040 general waiver requirements, except as otherwise provided by this rule.  (2) Duration. The Division may grant waivers for building requirements for a period not to exceed ten years; however, such waiver may be rescinded if the Division determines continuance of the waiver has a potential adverse impact on resident well-being, privacy or dignity. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070 & 441.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 441.055 & 441.615 
 
 History:
