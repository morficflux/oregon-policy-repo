 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 FOOD ESTABLISHMENT STANDARDS AND STANDARDS FOR RETAIL FOOD SERVICE ACTIVITIES 
 603-025-0110 
 Labeling Placement 
 
 (1) The open date shall be affixed, stamped, or imprinted in a size, manner, and style clearly and easily legible and visible to the consumer.  (2) The open date shall not overlay other labeling matter or be of such a color as to be difficult to distinguish from such other labeling matter. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & 616.835 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 616.835
