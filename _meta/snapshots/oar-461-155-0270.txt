 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 155 INCOME AND PAYMENT STANDARDS, AND SPECIAL NEEDS 
 461-155-0270 
 Room and Board Standard; Community-Based Care
 
 
 For an individual residing in a community-based care facility (see OAR 461-155-0630(1)), the room and board standard is $773. An individual residing in a community-based care facility must pay room and board. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.704, 411.706, ORS 409.050, 411.404, 413.085 & 414.685 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.704, 411.706, ORS 409.010, 411.404 & P.L. 92-336
