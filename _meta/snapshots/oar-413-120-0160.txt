 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 ADOPTION 
 413-120-0160 
 Right to Use Expedited Process
 
 
 The adoption worker shall inform the adoptive resource in writing of their right to use this expedited process, using form CF 438, Legalizing the Adoption. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 419B.529
