 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 RECREATIONAL MARIJUANA 
 845-025-2140 
 Registrant to Patient Transfers 
 
 A medical grow site registered with the Commission must follow all applicable requirements of OAR 845-025-7700 for transfers to patients or designated primary caregivers. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.017 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.871
