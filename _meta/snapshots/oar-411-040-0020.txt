 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 MEDICAID HOME DELIVERED MEALS 
 411-040-0020 
 Client Eligibility and Responsibilities 
 
 (1) To be eligible for Medicaid home delivered meals a participant must:  (a) Be Medicaid eligible per 411-015-0100, and be receiving Medicaid long term services and supports in their own home;  (b) Be home-bound;  (c) Be unable to do meal preparation on a regular basis without assistance; and  (d) Not have natural supports available that are willing and able to provide meal preparation services.  (2) To remain eligible for home delivered meals, participants are responsible for payment of their specified monthly client liability amount so home-delivered meals can be authorized.  (3) If the participant is determined ineligible for Medicaid home delivered meals, but needs food assistance, the case manager shall refer the participant to the nearest Aging and Disability Resource Connection for assistance. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.070, 411.060 & 411.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.070
