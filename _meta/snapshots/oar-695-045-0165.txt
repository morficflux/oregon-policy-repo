 Oregon Watershed Enhancement Board 
 Chapter 695 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 LAND ACQUISITION GRANTS 
 695-045-0165 
 Application and Subsequent Grant Processing and Agreement Requirements 
 
 (1) Land acquisition grant applications must be submitted on the most current form that conforms with the process prescribed by the Board.  (2) The Board may consider proposals that are received for properties that were acquired by the applicant after the previous application deadline.  (3) In the event of any conflict between these requirements and requirements identified in OAR 695-005, the land acquisition requirements in this division will take precedence. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 541.906 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541.932(9) 
 
 History:
