 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Systems Division: Behavioral Health Services - Chapter 309 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 PATIENT TRANSFERS 
 309-120-0285 
 Releases from a State Mental Hospital
 
 
 An adult in custody who is transferred to a state mental hospital may be discharged and transferred back to a Department of Corrections facility for one of the following reasons: 

 (1) Completion of treatment; 

 (2) He/she could receive mental health services within the Department of Corrections, and there was a mutually agreed upon continuity of care plan developed by the state mental hospital and the Administrator of the Department of Corrections Counseling and Treatment Services Unit/designee; or 

 (3) He/she does not meet the requirements to continue treatment at a state mental hospital. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 179.473, 179.479 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.471-179.486 & 179.495-179.508
