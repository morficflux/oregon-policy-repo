 Department of Geology and Mineral Industries 
 Chapter 632 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 OIL AND GAS RULES 
 632-010-0235 
 Exceptions 
 
 The department may grant exceptions to OAR 632-010-0225 and 632-010-0230 after holding a hearing, based on the geology, productivity, topography, enhancement requirements, or environmental protection. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 520 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 520.095
