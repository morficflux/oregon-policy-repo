 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 MANUFACTURED DWELLING PARK 
 813-007-0005 
 General Purpose
 
 
 OAR chapter 813, division 007 is adopted to implement and administer ORS 90.732, 90.734, 90.736, and 90.738 for the purpose of the regulation of manufactured dwelling parks and marina communities. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2005 OL Ch. 619, Sec 2-4, 2009 OL Ch. 816, Sec 9, 2011 OL Ch. 503, Sec 21 & ORS 456.555 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2009 OL Ch. 816, Sec 9, 2011 OL Ch. 503 & ORS 456.555
