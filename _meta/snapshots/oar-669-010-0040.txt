 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Sweet Cherry Commission - Chapter 669 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENTS 
 669-010-0040 
 Effective Dates of Assessment 
 
 The assessment to be withheld and paid by a first purchaser or handler, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and 669-010-0020(4) of the reports required under 669-010-0025, applies to and covers any purchase by him or her or delivery on or after June 1, 1989, of any sweet cherries grown in Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576
