 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 CRIME SERIOUSNESS SCALE 
 213-017-0001 
 Crime Category 11
 
 
 The following offenses are classified at crime category 11 on the Crime Seriousness Scale: ORS 163.095  — ATTEMPT AGGRAVATED MURDER  — (A). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667 & 2003 OL Ch. 453 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667 - 137.669 & SB 1013 (2019)
