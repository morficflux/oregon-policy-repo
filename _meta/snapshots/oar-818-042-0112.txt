 Oregon Board of Dentistry 
 Chapter 818 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 DENTAL ASSISTING 
 818-042-0112 
 Expanded Function Preventive Dental Assistants (EFPDA) 
 
 The following duties are considered Expanded Function Preventive Duties and may be performed only after the dental assistant complies with the requirements of 818-042-0113: Polish the coronal surfaces of teeth with a brush or rubber cup as part of oral prophylaxis to remove stains providing the patient is checked by a dentist or dental hygienist after the procedure is performed, prior to discharge. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 679 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 679 
 
 History:
