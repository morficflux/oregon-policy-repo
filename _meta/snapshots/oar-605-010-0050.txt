 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Beef Council - Chapter 605 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 COLLECTION OF ASSESSMENT, ENFORCEMENT 
 605-010-0050 
 Cancellation of Uncollectible Assessments 
 
 The Council may cancel an uncollectible assessment consistent with ORS 293.240. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 577.125(1)(e) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 577.532, House Bill 2656, ORS 577.512, 577.520, 577.290(2), 577.525, 576.355, 576.365 & 576.392 
 
 History:
