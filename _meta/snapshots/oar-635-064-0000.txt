 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 64 PRIVATELY HELD EXOTIC AND GAME MAMMALS 
 635-064-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules define the prohibition of hunting privately held exotic and game mammals. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012 & 496.138 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012 & 496.138 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DFW 26-1999, f. & cert. ef. 4-26-99
