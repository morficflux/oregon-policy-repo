 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 JUNKYARDS AND AUTO WRECKING YARDS 
 734-040-0020 
 Screening Regulations 
 
 Screening by means of plantings, fences, or other appropriate means shall be in accordance with a design approved by the Engineer:  (1) For New Junkyards:  (a) Shall be located off the state highway right of way;  (b) Shall be constructed and maintained by the person owning or operating the junkyard in accordance with a design approved by the Engineer.  (2) For Existing Junkyards:  (a) Shall be located on the state highway right of way or in areas outside the right of way acquired for such purposes by the Commission;  (b) Shall be constructed and maintained by the Commission using funds available to the Commission. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 377 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 377.625 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 HC 1278, f. 5-27-72
