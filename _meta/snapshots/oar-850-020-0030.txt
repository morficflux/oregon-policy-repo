 Oregon Board of Naturopathic Medicine 
 Chapter 850 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 APPROVAL OF NATUROPATHIC SCHOOLS OF MEDICINE 
 850-020-0030 
 Revocation of Approval 
 
 Approval obtained under ORS 685.060 may be revoked for proper cause by the Board at its discretion, after a hearing. Such hearing shall be held in accordance to Model Rules of Procedure applicable to contested cases. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 685.125 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 685.010
