 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 145 TREATMENTS OF SPECIFIC ASSETS 
 461-145-0810 
 Deemed Assets; Overview 
 
 For a sponsored noncitizen:  (1) Deemed assets are the portion of the income and resources of an individual not in the financial group (see OAR 461-110-0530) used to determine eligibility and benefit level for a sponsored noncitizen who is a member of the financial group.  (2) To be eligible for benefits, a sponsored noncitizen must provide necessary information about each individual whose assets are deemed to the sponsored noncitizen who is a member of the financial group.  (3) To determine countable assets for deeming, use the policy for the program for which the financial group is applying. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.816, 412.006 & 412.049 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.816, 412.006 & 412.049
