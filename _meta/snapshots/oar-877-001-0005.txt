 Board of Licensed Social Workers 
 Chapter 877 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES AND DEFINITIONS 
 877-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The board adopts the model rules applicable to rulemaking, OAR 137-001-0005 to 137-001-0100, effective on January 1, 2008.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Board of Licensed Social Workers.]
