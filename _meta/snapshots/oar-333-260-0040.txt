 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 260 COUNTY AMBULANCE SERVICE AREA PLANS 
 333-260-0040 
 Boundaries 
 
 (1) The entire county must be included in a plan. One or more ASAs may be established in a plan. The county or contiguous counties are solely responsible for establishing all ASA boundaries within the county’s jurisdiction.  (2) A map showing ASA boundaries and response time zones must be included in the plan, along with a narrative description of each ASA.  (3) A map depicting all “9-1-1,” fire district and incorporated city boundaries within the county must be included in the plan.  (4) The plan must describe the major alternatives considered, if any, for reducing the effects of artificial and geographical barriers on response times. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 682.205, 682.215, 682.275, 682.315, 682.325, 682.335 & 682.345 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 682.205, 682.215, 682.275, 682.315, 682.325, 682.335 & 682.345
