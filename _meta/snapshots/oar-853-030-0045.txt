 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office, Long Term Care Administrators Board - Chapter 853 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 NURSING HOME ADMINISTRATORS AUTHORIZATIONS 
 853-030-0045 
 Nursing Home AIT Program Preceptor Duties 
 
 A registered Preceptor must: 

 (1) Train only one AIT at a time. 

 (2) Meet one-on-one with the AIT for at least four hours per month to discuss the strengths, progress, and competency development needs of the AIT, and to suggest methods of improvement. In-person consultation must be documented on a form prescribed by the Office. 

 (3) Sign the Certificate of Training completion forms. 

 (4) Notify the Office within 10 business days if they are no longer supervising the AIT. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 678.710-678.820, ORS 678.990 & 2018 House Bill 4129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 678.710-678.820, ORS 678.990 & 2018 House Bill 4129
