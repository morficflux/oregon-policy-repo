 Oregon Medical Board 
 Chapter 847 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 RULES FOR LICENSURE OF VOLUNTEER CAMP PHYSICIANS 
 847-028-0030 
 Application
 
 
 (1) When applying for a license to volunteer medical services at a camp operated by a nonprofit organization, the physician shall submit to the Board the completed application, fees, documents, letters, and any other information required by the Board for physician (MD/DO) licensure as stated in OAR 847, division 020. 

 (2) A physician applying for a license to volunteer medical services at a camp operated by a nonprofit organization who has not completed the process within a 6 month consecutive period shall file a new application, documents, letters and pay a full filing fee as if filing for the first time. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 677.265 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 677.100 & 677.265
