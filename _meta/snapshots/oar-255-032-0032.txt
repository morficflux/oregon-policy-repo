 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 32 AGGRAVATED MURDER 
 255-032-0032 
 Continuance of Hearings: Cancellation of Hearings
 
 
 Requests for continuances or cancellations of a murder review hearing shall be submitted and reviewed as provided in OAR 255-030-0046. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144.050, 144.120 & 183.341(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 144.050
