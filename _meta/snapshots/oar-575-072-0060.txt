 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 72 OREGON NURSING LOAN PROGRAM 
 575-072-0060 
 Renewal of Awards 
 
 Subject to available funding, awards made under this program are renewable in subsequent academic years provided that:  (1) The awardee makes timely application in the manner specified annually by the Commission;  (2) The awardee continues to be enrolled in an eligible program at an eligible institution;  (3) The awardee demonstrates satisfactory performance in his or her program of study;  (4) The awardee continues to demonstrate financial need. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348.115 & 348.117 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.105 - 348.117
