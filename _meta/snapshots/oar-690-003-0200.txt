 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 PUBLIC RECORDS ACCESS AND REPRODUCTION 
 690-003-0200 
 Certification of Copies of Records 
 
 Certification of both hard copies and electronic copies of records will be provided for the fee identified in ORS 536.050. The Department will certify only that on the date copied, the copy was a true and correct copy of the original record. The Department cannot certify as to any subsequent changes or manipulation of the record. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192.430, 536.025 & 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.440 & 536.480 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 6-2006, f. & cert. ef. 10-6-06
