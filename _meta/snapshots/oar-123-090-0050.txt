 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 STRATEGIC RESERVE FUND 
 123-090-0050 
 First Source Hiring 
 
 Any firm receiving an award of more than $100,000 through the Strategic Reserve Fund shall enter into a first source agreement in accordance with division 70 of this chapter of administrative rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285B.266
