 Employment Department 
 Chapter 471 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 UNEMPLOYMENT INSURANCE TAXES 
 471-031-0080 
 Tax Compromise Policy 
 
 (1) It shall be the policy of the Director to compromise tax claims pursuant to the provisions of ORS 657.515(7) where it appears that such action would be in the best interests of all parties involved and the statutory criteria for a settlement has been met.  (2) The effective date for implementing this rule shall be retroactive to June 23rd, 2002. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 657 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 657.515 & 657.517
