 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 REGULATION OF FARMWORKER CAMP OPERATORS 
 839-014-0220 
 Deposit in Cash or Negotiable Securities 
 
 As used in ORS 658.735 and in these rules “a deposit in cash or negotiable securities” means a single financial instrument which yields no less than the amount required pursuant to OAR 839-014-0210 in cash immediately upon demand. If the deposit is one which is subject to a penalty for early withdrawal, then the deposit must be in an amount sufficient to satisfy the penalty and yield no less than the amount required under 839-014-0210 in cash. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 658.407(3), 658.415(14) & 658.820(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 658.735(1)
