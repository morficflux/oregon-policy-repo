Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 8
LANDS: DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE LANDS

635-008-0052
Definition

For purposes of OAR 635-008-0051 through 635-008-0053:

(1) "Permit" means a vehicle permit that is issued as evidence of a grant of authority to park a motor-propelled vehicle in a designated parking fee area within a designated Wildlife Area.

(2) "Parking" means a vehicle not in motion.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
