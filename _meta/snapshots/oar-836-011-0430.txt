 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 ANNUAL STATEMENTS AND REPORTS BY INSURERS 
 836-011-0430 
 Scope and Authority 
 
 (1) OAR 836-011-0430 to 836-011-0460 apply to all domestic insurers and to all domestic health care service contractors under ORS 750.055 and multiple employer welfare arrangements under 750.333. For purposes of OAR 836-011-0430 to 836-011-0460, “insurer” includes health care service contractors and multiple employer welfare arrangements.  (2) OAR 836-011-0430 to 836-011-0460 are adopted under the authority of ORS 731.244, 731.574 and 733.210. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & 731.574 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 731.574 & 733.210 
 
 History:
