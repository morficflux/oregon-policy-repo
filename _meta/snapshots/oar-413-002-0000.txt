 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS 
 413-002-0000 
 Minimum Qualifications For Persons Conducting Investigation or Making Determination Regarding a Child 
 
 A person who conducts an investigation under ORS 419B.020, makes a determination that a child must be taken into protective custody under ORS 419B.150 or makes a determination that a child should not be released to the child’s parent or other responsible person under ORS 419B.165(2) must possess either: 

 (1) A bachelor’s degree in human services or a field related to human services; or 

 (2) A bachelor’s degree unrelated to human services and either: 

 (a) One year of human services related experience; or 

 (b) Completion of coursework equivalent to certification consistent with Oregon Caseworker Competency; or 

 (3) An associate degree and either: 

 (a) Two years of human services related experience; or 

 (b) One year of human services related experience and related training, coursework or certification consistent with Oregon Caseworker Competency. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, ORS 418.005 & ORS 419B.021 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 409.050, ORS 418.005 & ORS 419B.021
