 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ELECTRICAL PROGRAM MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-308-0200 
 Amendment of Plans by All Municipalities with Electrical Programs 
 
 All municipalities providing electrical inspections shall submit:  (1) Ordinance amendments and intended effective dates and change of electrical fees, regardless of whether fees are adopted by ordinance, at least 45 days prior to the adoptive date.  (2) Amendments and intended effective dates regarding the operating plan at least 30 days prior to implementation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.855 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.855
