 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 140 GENERAL FINANCIAL ELIGIBILITY 
 461-140-0260 
 Disqualification Due to a Resource Transfer; SNAP 
 
 In the SNAP program:  (1) A financial group disqualified due to the transfer of a resource (described in OAR 461-140-0210 or 461-140-0220) is disqualified from receiving benefits starting on the date of the denial of benefits or on the effective date of the closure. The disqualification applies to each individual in the financial group during the month that the disqualifying transfer of resources occurred.  (2) The duration of the disqualification is based on the amount of uncompensated value (see OAR 461-140-0250) as provided in the following chart: [Table not included. See ED. NOTE.]  [ED. NOTE: Tables referenced are available from the agency.]
