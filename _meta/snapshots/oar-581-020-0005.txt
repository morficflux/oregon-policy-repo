 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 SCHOOL IMPROVEMENT AND PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT 
 581-020-0005 
 Pertaining to School Improvement and Professional Development 
 
 A school Improvement and Professional Development Program has been established to encourage the following:  (1) The development of educational goals for individual schools and school districts;  (2) The assessment of the educational progress of school programs and students;  (3) The expansion of professional growth and career opportunities for Oregon teachers; and  (4) The restructuring of the school workplace to provide teachers with responsibilities and authority commensurate with their status as professionals. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 896 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 329.695 
 
 History:
