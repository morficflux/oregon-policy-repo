 Oregon Racing Commission 
 Chapter 462 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 PARI-MUTUEL RULES AND REGULATIONS 
 462-200-0040 
 Days Racing Permitted
 
 
 Any race meeting properly licensed under ORS Chapter 462 by the Oregon Racing Commission may conduct racing with pari-mutuel wagering on any day of the week. No wagering or actual running of a race shall be permitted after midnight on any day except in the case of emergency. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 462.270(3) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 462.270(3)
