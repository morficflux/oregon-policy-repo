 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 730 CONSUMER FINANCE 
 441-730-0200 
 Action on Precomputed Loan; Rebate Required 
 
 (1) When a licensee brings an action against a borrower on a precomputed loan, if the action comes to judgment prior to the due date of the final installment, the licensee must tender a rebate to the borrower of interest unearned as of the date of the judgment. The rebate must be computed in accordance with ORS 725.340(2)(c) as if the loan were prepaid in full on the date of the judgment.  (2) Rebate of any deferral charge must be determined on the US Actuarial Rule.  (3) Licensees may collect prejudgment interest awarded by the court, but may not estimate interest based upon an estimate of the judgment date. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 725.320 & 725.505 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 725.340
