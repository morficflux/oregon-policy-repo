 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 ENERGY CONSERVATION SERVICES 
 330-060-0090 
 Oil Audit Standards: Auditor Qualifications 
 
 The person performing the energy audit must be able to determine whether the applicable energy conservations measures meet the standards set forth in these rules and the specifications and to be able to effectively communicate recommendations to the customer. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.675
