 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 LANDS: DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE LANDS 
 635-008-0065 
 Carver Fishing Access: Clackamas County
 
 
 In addition to the provisions in OAR 635-008-0200, at the Carver Fishing Access (Clackamas County): 

 (1) Public use is prohibited between the hours of 10 pm and 4 am daily. 

 (2) Open fires are prohibited. 

 (3) Trapping is prohibited. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138 & 496.146
