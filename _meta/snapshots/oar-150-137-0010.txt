 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 137 CRIMINAL FINE AND ASSESSMENT ACCOUNT DISTRIBUTION 
 150-137-0010 
 Criminal Fine Account Distribution 
 
 (1) Monthly, the department will distribute moneys available in the Criminal Fine Account after final deposits into the account for the calendar month have been made by the Oregon Department of Revenue and Oregon Judicial Department.  (2) The department will distribute to the General Fund all moneys remaining in the Criminal Fine Account after distributing the monthly allocations to funds and programs referenced in Oregon Laws 2011, Chapter 597, Section 53. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100; 137.300 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.300
