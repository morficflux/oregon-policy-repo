 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 SCHOOL GOVERNANCE AND STUDENT CONDUCT 
 581-021-0430 
 The Distribution of Rules Relating to Student Records 
 
 (1) The State Board of Education shall distribute the administrative rules regarding student education records to all school districts.  (2) School districts shall make those rules available to the public schools in the district and to the public. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.565 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 326.565
