 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 218 OREGON TITLE V OPERATING PERMITS 
 340-218-0060 
 State-Enforceable Requirements 
 
 DEQ will specifically designate as not being federally enforceable any terms and conditions included in the permit that are not required under the FCAA or under any of its applicable requirements. Terms and conditions so designated are subject to the requirements of OAR 340-218-0040 through 340-218-0220, other than those contained in 340-218-0070. All terms and conditions in an Oregon Title V Operating Permit are enforceable by DEQ. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020, 468A.025, 468A.040 & ORS 468A.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A
