 Division 135 SPECIFIC PROGRAM REQUIREMENTS

 Forms; Request for Notice of Transfer or Encumbrance; Termination of Request for Notice of Transfer or Encumbrance; Notice of Transfer or Encumbrance 
 
 (1) The forms set forth in this rule are adopted in accordance with ORS 93.268, 205.246, and 411.692.  (2) Request for Notice of Transfer or Encumbrance Form; [Form not included. See ED. NOTE.]  (3) Termination of Request for Notice of Transfer or Encumbrance Form; [Form not included. See ED. NOTE.]  (4) Model Form — Notice of Transfer or Encumbrance Form; [Form not included.
