 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 TRADE PRACTICES 
 836-080-0425 
 Applicability; Definitions 
 
 (1) OAR 836-080-0425 to 836-080-0440 apply to personal insurance as defined in ORS 746.600.  (2) "Application" means an action by a prospective insured that, if accepted by the insurer, would establish a contract of insurance.  (3) “Consumer,” “consumer reporting agency,” “credit history” and “insurance score” have the meanings given those terms in ORS 746.600. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 746.015 & 746.240
