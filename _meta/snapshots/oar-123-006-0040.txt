 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 6 PROCEDURES FOR CONTRACTS ENTERED INTO WITH THE BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT DEPARTMENT 
 123-006-0040 
 Contracts Must be Authorized Prior to Performance 
 
 All Contracts, including any amendments, must be in writing and fully executed before any Work or Services may be performed or payment made. Contractors are not entitled to payment for any Work or Services performed prior to such execution. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285A.075 & 279.070
