 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 FORMATION OF PORTS 
 123-020-0105 
 Definitions 
 
 For the purposes of these rules additional definitions may be found in Procedural Rules, OAR 123-001. As used in this division of administrative rules, unless the context requires otherwise:  (1) "Applicant" means an entity that may legitimately seek and propose the formation of a new Port, and that submits a request for the Commission's approval.  (2) "Port" means a municipal corporation organized under ORS Chapter 777 or 778, which may be known as a "port authority" or "port district." 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285A.627
