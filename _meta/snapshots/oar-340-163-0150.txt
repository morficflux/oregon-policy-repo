 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 163 REGISTRATION AND LICENSING REQUIREMENTS FOR HEATING OIL TANK SOIL MATRIX CLEANUP SERVICE PROVIDERS AND SUPERVISORS 
 340-163-0150 
 Fees
 
 
 (1) Heating oil tank service providers must pay a non-refundable license fee for a twelve (12) month license as listed in Table 1. 

 (2) Heating oil tank supervisors must pay a non-refundable license fee for a twenty-four (24) month license according to the following schedule: 

 (a) $150 for license applications received on or before December 31, 2019; 

 (b) $200 for license applications received January 1, 2020 or later. 

 (3) Supervisors taking qualifying examinations the department administers must pay an examination fee equal to the cost of administering the examination. 

 (4) Examination study guides will be made available to the public for the cost of production. Copyrighted reference materials, which may have separate costs charged by the specific organization, are not included with study guides. 

 (5) The department will provide replacement licenses, including name change requests, for a $10 fee. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 465.200 - 465.320, 466.706 - 466.995 & 466.868 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.706, 466.750 & 466.868
