 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 WILDLIFE UNITS 
 635-080-0012 
 Wilson Unit 
 
 The Wilson Unit, number 12, is that area beginning on the north shore line at the mouth of Tillamook Bay; north along Pacific Ocean coastline to north shoreline of Nehalem Bay; northeast along north shoreline of Nehalem Bay and North Fork Nehalem River to State Highway 53 bridge; northeast on State Highway 53 to U.S. Highway 26 at Necanicum Junction; southeast on U.S. Highway 26 to Timber-Glenwood Highway; south on Timber-Glenwood Highway to Glenwood; southwest on State Highway 6 to Wilson River at Mills Bridge; west along Wilson River and north shoreline of Tillamook Bay to mouth, point of beginning. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
