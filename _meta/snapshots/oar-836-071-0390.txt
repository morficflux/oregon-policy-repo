 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 INSURANCE LICENSING 
 836-071-0390 
 Statewide Filing Officer 
 
 (1) Each limited licensee must appoint as the statewide filing officer of the licensee an executive or manager of the licensee who is located in this state. The statewide filing officer shall serve as the in-state representative and contact person for the limited licensee.  (2) The limited licensee shall notify the Director in writing of any change in the appointment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 744.858 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 744.852 & 744.856
