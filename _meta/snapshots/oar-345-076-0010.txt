 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 76 SPECIFIC STANDARDS FOR THE SITING OF NUCLEAR POWER FACILITIES IN OREGON 
 345-076-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 The rules in this division supplement the general standards in OAR chapter 345, division 22, for the siting of energy facilities and apply to any nuclear-fueled thermal power plant or nuclear power plant, as defined in ORS 469.300 and their related and supporting facilities. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.470
