 Oregon Board of Maritime Pilots - Chapter 856 
 
 
 
 
 Division 19 Incidents, Complaints and Discipline 
 856-019-0020 
 Failure to Report
 
 
 The license of any pilot who fails, neglects, or refuses to report to the Board or U.S. Coast Guard, as required by these rules for a period of ten days after the date required may be suspended; and if the pilot fails to report for a period of 30 days, the pilot shall be suspended. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 776 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 776.115
