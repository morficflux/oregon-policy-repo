 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 44 CHARITABLE PHARMACIES 
 855-044-0005 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) “Charitable Pharmacy” means a facility registered with the Oregon Board of Pharmacy for the purpose of receiving and distributing donated drugs.  (2) “Point-of-Contact” means an individual designated by a charitable pharmacy who serves as the primary contact person for the charitable pharmacy and who is responsible for managing the charitable pharmacy at that location. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 689.772 & 689.774 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BP 6-2010, f. & cert. ef. 6-29-10
