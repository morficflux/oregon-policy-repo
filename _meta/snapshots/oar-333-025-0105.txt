 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 GENETIC INFORMATION AND PRIVACY 
 333-025-0105 
 Scope 
 
 (1) OAR 333-025-0100 to 0165 apply to all genetic research subject to the law of the State of Oregon.  (2) All genetic research must comply with the applicable standards set forth in the Federal Common Rule. Additional protections for subjects of research are authorized by ORS 192.531 et seq. and these rules. These rules set state standards that are in addition to, and not intended to alter, any requirements under the Federal Common Rule or the Federal Privacy Rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192.531–192.549 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.531–192.549
