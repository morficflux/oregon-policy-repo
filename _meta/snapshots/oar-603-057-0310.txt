 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 57 PESTICIDE CONTROL 
 603-057-0310 
 Time Period 
 
 The restrictions and conditions on the use or application of the high volatile esters of 2,4-D specified in OAR 603-057-0301 shall apply for the period of April 1 to September 1 of each year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190, 634.306 & 634.322 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 634.306 & 634.322 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 1072(18-75), f. & ef. 12-5-75
