 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 308 ELECTRICAL PROGRAM MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-308-0120 
 Permit Sales Office 
 
 A municipality shall establish at least one office within each noncontiguous service area where electrical permits can be obtained directly or have self-issuing or temporary permit procedures for all regular work days, which excludes weekends and holidays. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.855 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 479.855
