 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 249 GENERAL PROVISIONS RELATED TO PUBLIC CONTRACTS FOR CONSTRUCTION SERVICES 
 125-249-0110 
 Policies 
 
 In addition to the policies of the Code as set forth in ORS 279A.015, the policy on competition as provided in 279C.300 applies to this division, except as provided in 279C.335. The policy on least-cost for Public Improvements applies as described within 279C.305. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 279A.065(5)(a) & 279A.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 279C.300 & 279C.305 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DAS 4-2004, f. 11-23-04, cert. ef. 3-1-05
