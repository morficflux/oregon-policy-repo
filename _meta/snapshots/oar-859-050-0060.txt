 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 ADULT PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD HEARING PROCEDURES 
 859-050-0060 
 Deliberations 
 
 Board deliberations are confidential and not open to the public. For purposes of this rule, “the public” does not include PSRB employees. PSRB staff may attend Board deliberations. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.346
