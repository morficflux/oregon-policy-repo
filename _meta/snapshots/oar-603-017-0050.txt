 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 REFRIGERATED LOCKERS 
 603-017-0050 
 Chart Rotating Device 
 
 Chart rotating device shall be fitted with perforating pins preventing rotation except by means of the electric clock in the case. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 628 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 628.350 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 286, f. 7-9-47, ef. 7-10-47
