 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 61 FOOD SERVICES PROGRAMS 
 291-061-0071 
 Inspections 
 
 (1) Annual inspections will be requested of the Oregon Health Division, State Fire Marshal or Occupational Safety and Health Administration. These will be coordinated with the safety and sanitation officer.  (2) Weekly inspections shall be made of all food services areas and equipment. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 421.614, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 421.614, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
