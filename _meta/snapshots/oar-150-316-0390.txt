 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 316 PERSONAL INCOME TAX GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 150-316-0390 
 Deductions Allowed on Either the Inheritance Tax Return or the Fiduciary Income Tax Return 
 
 Certain deductions may not be taken on both the Oregon inheritance tax return (Form IT-1) and the Oregon fiduciary income tax return (Form 41). See OAR 150-118-0040 for details. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 316.272
