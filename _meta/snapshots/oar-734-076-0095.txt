 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 76 ISSUANCE OF PERMITS ALLOWING TOW VEHICLES TO TOW OVERSIZE DISABLED VEHICLES OR COMBINATIONS OF VEHICLES ON STATE HIGHWAYS 
 734-076-0095 
 Cancellation of Permit
 
 
 A permit issued under these Division 76 rules may be canceled at any time by the granting authority for any of the reasons stated in ORS 818.220. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 818.170
