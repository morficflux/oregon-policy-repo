 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 133 MARRIAGES AND DOMESTIC PARTNERSHIP SOLEMNIZATION CEREMONIES (AIC) 
 291-133-0035 
 Inmate-to-Inmate Marriages and Domestic Partnership Solemnization Ceremonies 
 
 (1) An applicant satisfying the eligibility requirements of OAR 291-133-0015, except subsection (5)(d), may be permitted to marry or enter a domestic partnership with another applicant provided they reside in the same facility.  (2) The department will not transport inmates for the purpose of participating in a marriage or domestic partnership solemnization ceremony. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.285, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.285, 179.040, 423.020, 423.030, 423.075 & 2007 OL Ch. 99
