 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 535 NEW CONSTRUCTION AND ALTERATIONS OF EXISTING HOSPITALS 
 333-535-0000 
 Applicability
 
 
 OAR 333-535-0000 through 333-535-0015 shall apply to all hospitals not licensed or for which plans have not been approved on the effective date of these rules for major alterations and new construction. Major alteration has the meaning given that term in OAR 333-500-0010. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 441.060 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 441.060
