 Division 500 OREGON ADMINISTRATIVE RULES FILING REQUIREMENTS 
 166-500-0040 
 Permanent Filing Requirements 
 
 (1) Permanent filings, in addition to the requirements outlined in ORS 183.355, must include the following components: 

 (a) Agency approved date; 

 (b) Effective date; 

 (c) Caption; 

 (d) Rule number and title; 

 (e) Final rule text which must comply with OAR 166-500-0055; 

 (f) Associated statutory/other authority and statutes/other implemented, as defined in OAR 166-500-0010; 

 (g) Rule summary provided for each rule; and 

 (h) If applicable, a PDF of all rule attachments containing items such as charts, drawings, forms, graphs, maps, seals, tables, and other non-text information that is directly related to the rule and is not included in the rule text. See OAR 166-500-0060. 

 (2) Agencies adopting or amending rules incorporating published standards by reference may omit copies of the publications if applicable, under ORS 183.355(2). Agencies must explain how the public may obtain referenced items. 

 (3) An agency must file a notice of proposed rulemaking before a permanent filing can be submitted, as ORS 183.335 requires unless ORS 183.335(10) or ORS 183.341(1) applies. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.355 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.360, ORS 183.335 & ORS 183.355
