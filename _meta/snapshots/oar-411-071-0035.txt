 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 PRIVATE ADMISSION ASSESSMENT 
 411-071-0035 
 Certification Process 
 
 (1) Any hospital, agency, program, or Area Agency on Aging must obtain certification from the Department before providing admission assessment services.  (2) The certification issued to a program is not valid for use by any other program.  (3) Certification is valid for the length of the contract unless revoked or suspended by the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 410.505 - 410.545 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 410.530
