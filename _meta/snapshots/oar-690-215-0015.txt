 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 215 MAINTENANCE, REPAIR AND DEEPENING OF WATER SUPPLY WELLS 
 690-215-0015 
 Accessibility to Well for Reconditioning, Repair or Abandonment 
 
 To enable drilling equipment future access to the water supply well for reconditioning, repair, or abandonment, the property owner should maintain a minimum five-foot separation distance between the well and any permanent structure. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
