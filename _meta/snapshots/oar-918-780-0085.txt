 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 780 PLUMBING FEES, PERMITS, INSPECTIONS 
 918-780-0085 
 License Required 
 
 Whenever any plumbing installation is being made by an unlicensed person contrary to the provisions of the Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code or OAR chapter 918, division 695, the building official must stop the installation through written notice. The building official may serve the notice on any person engaged in such an installation, or by posting a copy of the written notice at the site of the installation. No person may proceed with the installation until authorized by the building official.  [Publications: Publications referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 447.010 & 447.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 447.010 & 447.020 
 
 History:
