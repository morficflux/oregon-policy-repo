 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 83 SOLID WASTE PLANNING AND RECYCLING GRANT RULES 
 340-083-0010 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 These rules implement:  (1) ORS 459A.120(2) as amended by Section 7, Chapter 662, Oregon Laws 2015, to provide grants for activities to reduce the environmental and human health impacts of materials at all stages of their life cycles; and  (2) ORS 459A.110 as amended by Section 6a, Chapter 662, Oregon Laws 2015 to provide partial rebates of solid waste tipping fees to distressed counties. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 45A.025 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 459A.120
