 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 WILDLIFE MANAGEMENT PLANS: WILDLIFE DIVERSITY PLAN 
 635-100-0193 
 Lower Columbia River Coho Monitoring 
 
 (1) De-listing decisions and the operation of fishery and hatchery programs requires monitoring and evaluation sufficient to measure risks and describe outcomes. ODFW shall implement such a monitoring program as sufficient funds become available.  (2) As funding becomes available, ODFW shall intensify its monitoring of juvenile and adult coho such that the data generated are directly comparable with coho information collected by ODFW from populations on the coast of Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.004, 496.171, 496.172, 496.182, 496.192 & 498.026 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.004, 496.171, 496.172, 496.182, 496.192 & 498.026
