 Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying 
 Chapter 820 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 THE PRACTICE OF LAND SURVEYING 
 820-030-0005 
 Definitions 
 
 “Professional service” or “professional services,” as used in ORS 672.005, means providing labor that does not produce a tangible commodity, but that requires a high level of training and proficiency. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 672.005 & 672.255 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.002 - 672.325 
 
 History:
