 Corporation Division - Chapter 160 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 NOTARIES PUBLIC 
 160-100-0120 
 Description of Official Stamping Device 
 
 (1) Stamping device, as used in ORS 194.215(17) and 194.295, does not include an ink-less embosser or crimper.  (2) A stamping device must be capable of affixing or logically associating the official stamp such that the record to which the official stamp is so affixed or associated may be copied, filmed, scanned, or otherwise legibly reproduced. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 194.290-295 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 194.290-295
