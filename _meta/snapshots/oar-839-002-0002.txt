 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 839-002-0002 
 Rulemaking: Proposed Rule Notice 
 
 Prior to the permanent adoption, amendment or repeal of any rule of the Bureau of Labor and Industries, the bureau will give notice of intended action:  (1) In the Secretary of State's Bulletin, referred to in ORS 183.360, at least 21 days prior to the rule's effective date.  (2) To persons on the bureau's mailing list and email list established pursuant to ORS 183.335(8).  (3) To the legislature, by mailing a copy of the notice to the legislators specified in ORS 183.335(15) at least 49 days before the effective date of the rule.  (4) To the general public, by posting the notice on the bureau's Website. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.335
