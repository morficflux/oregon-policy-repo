 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 83 SOLID WASTE PLANNING AND RECYCLING GRANT RULES 
 340-083-0080 
 Application Procedures 
 
 An applicant must provide a complete application for each grant applied for. Application must be made on a form DEQ provides. Each application must include:  (1) Applicant’s contact information;  (2) Description of the project and the expected outcomes;  (3) Workplan and schedule for project completion;  (4) Complete budget, including breakdown of costs;  (5) Signature of applicant's responsible official;  (6) Any other information DEQ requires. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 45A.025 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 459A.120
