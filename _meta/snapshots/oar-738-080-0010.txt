 Oregon Department of Aviation 
 Chapter 738 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 AIRCRAFT REGISTRATION 
 738-080-0010 
 Purpose and Statutory Authority
 
 
 To regulate the registration of aircraft and UAS in Oregon: 

 (1) ORS 835.035 and ORS 835.112 pertaining to rulemaking authority. 

 (2) ORS 835.035 and ORS 837.005 pertaining to the general public interest, safety, and the development and promotion of aeronautics. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 835.035 & 835.112 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 835.035, 835.112 & 837.015
