 Department of Forestry 
 Chapter 629 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 FOREST RESOURCE TRUST 
 629-022-0200 
 Implementing the Project Plan; Payments 
 
 (1) The Landowner must implement the Practices in the Project plan and notify the State Forester when an Approved Practice or an Approved Consulting Service, or a part of a Practice or service, described in the Project plan is complete.  (2) To request a Direct Cost Payment, the Landowner must submit written invoices for the Incurred Cost of the completed Practice or consulting service notified in section (1) of this rule.  (3) The State Forester must disburse Trust funds to the land owner as Direct Cost Payments when the State Forester certifies that a Practice is complete to the specifications and within the Budgeted Costs approved in the Project plan in OAR 629-022-0140.  (4) Actual Trust costs must be used to determine final payback amounts described in OAR 629-022-0300 through 629-022-0320. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.700 - 526.730 & 526.745 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 526.695 - 526.775
