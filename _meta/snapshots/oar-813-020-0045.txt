 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 BOND RESIDENTIAL LOAN PROGRAM & FLEX LENDING FIRSTHOME PRODUCT 
 813-020-0045 
 Manual
 
 
 The Oregon Bond and Flex Lending FirstHome Guideline Manual with the requirements and standards therein, is incorporated into and adopted as part of this division of administrative rules, by reference. The program manual may be accessed online at the OHCS website. The manual is dated August 29, 2025.
