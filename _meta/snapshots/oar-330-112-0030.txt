 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 112 ENERGY EFFICIENCY AND SUSTAINABLE TECHNOLOGY LOAN PROGRAM 
 330-112-0030 
 Sustainable EnergyTerritories 
 
 (1) The boundaries for sustainable energy territories shall comply with ORS 470.530(3) (a), (b), and (c) and ORS 470.555  (2) Territory boundaries may be set by the director as necessary to accomplish the goals of the program. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 470.500 - 47.0715, 2009 OL Ch. 753 & HB 3675 (2010) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 470.500 - 470.715, 2009 OL Ch. 753 & HB 3675 (2010)
