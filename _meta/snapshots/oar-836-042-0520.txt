 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 42 RATES AND RATEMAKING 
 836-042-0520 
 Supporting Data 
 
 Supporting actuarial data shall accompany every filing of a commercial liability insurance rate, rating plan and rating system. The data shall be in sufficient detail to:  (1) Demonstrate compliance with ORS 737.207; and  (2) Demonstrate the statistical significance of differences or correlations relevant to rating plan definitions and rate differentials. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731 & 737 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 737.207(4) & 737.320(6)
