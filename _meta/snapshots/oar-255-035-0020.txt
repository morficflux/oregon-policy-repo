 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 APPLICATIONS OF THE GUIDELINES TO ESTABLISH A PRISON TERM 
 255-035-0020 
 Board Bound by Court Order 
 
 (1) The Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision does not have the authority to run a sentence concurrently or consecutively to an out-of-state jurisdiction, but is bound by the final judgment order issued by the Oregon courts.  (2) The Board does not have the authority to convert a court ordered indeterminate sentence for a crime committed prior to November 1, 1989 to a Sentencing Guidelines determinate sentence. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.120, 137.122, 137.123, 144.050, 144.079 & 144.783 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 144
