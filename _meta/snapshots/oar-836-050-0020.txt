 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 836-050-0020 
 Notice of Rejection 
 
 A policyholder or certificate holder who elects to reject the transfer and novation of the policy under an assumption reinsurance agreement to which ORS 742.150 applied must give notice indicating rejection to the transferring insurer on a pre-addressed, postage-paid response card provided by the transferring insurer in the notice of transfer as required in OAR 836-050-0010 and Exhibit 1 to that rule, or in another written notice by the policyholder or certificate holder.  [ED. NOTE: Exhibits referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244, 742.156 & 742.158 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 742.156 & 742.158
