 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 115 PUBLICLY SUPPORTED HOUSING PRESERVATION 
 813-115-0001 
 Purpose and Objectives 
 
 The rules of OAR chapter 813, Division 115, describe a required process for Publicly Supported Housing Contract Preservation. This process is defined in ORS 456.766 through 456.813 and 456.828 which provides a framework to help preserve the affordability restrictions of properties that entered into these restrictions to receive federal, state, or local government assistance. This process is also subject to OHCS’ general rules (OAR 813-005).  

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.766, ORS 776-781 & 456.819 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.766-819, 456.828 & Senate Bill 973 (2025)
