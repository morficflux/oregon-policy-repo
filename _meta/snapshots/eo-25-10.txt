     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25-10


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     COLD SPRINGS FIRE IN UMATILLA COUNTY


     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, | find that:

     The fire known as the “Cold Springs Fire” is burning in Umatilla County.

     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Cold Springs Fire
     are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
     protection was requested by the Umatilla County Fire Defense Board Chief and the
     State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.


     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Cold Springs Fire in
     Umatilla County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
     firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.


     These findings were made at 6:45 pm on July 2, 2025, with the Department of the
     State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Green Incident Management
     Team will take command on July 3, 2025. The Oregon State Fire Marshal Green
     Incident Management Team will be briefed at 9:00 a.m. on July 3, 2025, and I now
     confirm them with this Executive Order.

     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

     IF     The Department of the State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
            the Cold Springs Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

     Dee    This conflagration is declared only for the Cold Springs Fire threatening
            structures in Umatilla County.


     3,     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 6:45 pm on the 2" day of
            July 2025 and signed this 3 day of July 2025, in Warrenton, Oregon.
