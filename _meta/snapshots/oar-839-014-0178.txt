 Bureau of Labor and Industries 
 Chapter 839 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 REGULATION OF FARMWORKER CAMP OPERATORS 
 839-014-0178 
 Grading of Examinations/Notice of Score 
 
 (1) The passing score for each examination shall be 75 percent.  (2) The License Unit shall notify the applicant by mail of the examination scores. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 164, 165, 651, 658 & 962 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 658.705 - 658.850
