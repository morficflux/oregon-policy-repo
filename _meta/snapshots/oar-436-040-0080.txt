 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 WORKERS WITH DISABILITIES PROGRAM 
 436-040-0080 
 Third Party Recoveries 
 
 (1) If a third party recovery is made prior to a claim qualifying for Workers with Disabilities Program relief, compensation recovered shall be credited against the compensation of the claim prior to any request for reimbursement.  (2) The Workers with Disabilities Program shall be a party to any third party recovery on a claim if payment from the program has been made prior to the third party recovery as provided in ORS 656.591 and 656.593(1)(c). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.628, §286, ch. 70 & OL 2007
