 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 VOLUNTEERS IN SERVICE TO OREGON 
 575-075-0030 
 Conditions of Voucher Use 
 
 A VISTO voucher may:  (1) Be used for any academic period of a fiscal or academic year.  (2) Be used only for payment of undergraduate tuition and fees and are limited in use to a maximum of $5,250 in any academic year.  (3) Not be used to pay tuition and fees for a course of study leading to a degree in theology, divinity or religious education.  (4) Be used only by the VISTO volunteer who received it in recognition of his or her voluntary service. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.405 - 348.425
