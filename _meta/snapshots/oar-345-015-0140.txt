 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 PROCEDURES GOVERNING COUNCIL AND DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY PROCEEDINGS, INCLUDING SITE CERTIFICATE HEARINGS 
 345-015-0140 
 Review by the Department of Energy 
 
 The Department of Energy shall review the notice of intent (NOI) and the comments and recommendations received by the final date for submission of comments specified in the public notice described in OAR 345-015-0110 and in the memorandum described in 345-015-0120. The Department shall send copies of any written comments or recommendations to the applicant. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.330
