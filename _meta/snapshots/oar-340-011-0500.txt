 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 RULES OF GENERAL APPLICABILITY AND ORGANIZATION 
 340-011-0500 
 Contested Cases: Contested Case Proceedings Generally 
 
 Except as otherwise provided in OAR 340, division 011, contested cases will be governed by the Rules of the Office of Administrative Hearings, specifically OAR 137-003-0501 through 0700. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341 & 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
