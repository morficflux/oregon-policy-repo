 Department of Human Services 
 Vocational Rehabilitation Services - Chapter 582 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 RATES OF PAYMENT — MEDICAL 
 582-075-0020 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) "Brief narrative," as used in division 075, means a document from a treating vendor that summarizes client treatment to date and current status; responds briefly to 3–5 specific questions posed by OVRS, if any; and is usually one or two pages.  (2) "Complete narrative," as used in division 075, means a document from a treating vendor that describes an extended client history, addresses six or more specific topics, and is usually three or more pages.  (3) "Vendor, " as used in division 075, means an entity that provides goods and/or services at the request of OVRS. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 344.530(2) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 344.511 - 344.690
