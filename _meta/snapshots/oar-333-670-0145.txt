 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 670 RECONSIDERATION OF CERTIFICATE OF NEED DECISION 
 333-670-0145 
 Applicability of OAR 333-670-0140 
 
 OAR 333-670-0140 shall apply to reconsideration hearings requested or held on or after the effective date of this rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431.120(6) & 442.315 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431.120(6) & 442.315
