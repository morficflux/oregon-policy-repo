 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 NETWORK OF QUALITY TEACHING AND LEARNING 
 581-018-0327 
 Definitions: Small/Rural Grant
 
 
 The following definitions apply to 581-018-0327 to 581-018-0336 

 (1) “Common Core State Standards (CCSS)” means a coherent progression of learning expectations in English language arts and mathematics designed to prepare K–12 students for college and career success. CCSS were adopted by the Oregon State Board of Education in 2010. 

 (2) “Educator Effectiveness” means expectations for educators defined by the Core Teaching Standards established in Senate Bill 290 to improve student academic growth. Teacher effectiveness is defined by the Model Core Teaching Standards and administrator effectiveness is defined by the Oregon Educational Leadership/Administrator Standards adopted by the State Board of Education. 

 (3) “Network” means the Network of Quality Teaching and Learning established by ORS 342.950. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 342.950 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 342.950
