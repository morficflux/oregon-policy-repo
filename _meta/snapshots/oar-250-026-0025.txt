 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 26 SALVAGED VESSEL SUBACCOUNT 
 250-026-0025 
 Availability of Funds 
 
 (1) Enforcement agencies are encouraged to verify the availability of funds in the Salvaged Vessel Subaccount prior to incurring salvage, towing, storage and disposal expenses.  (2) The Board will retain up to 20 percent of the Salvaged Vessel Subaccount until the beginning of the third quarter of the final year of each biennium; at which time, the funds could be disbursed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830.110 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.948 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSMB 9-2014, f. 5-12-14, cert. ef. 6-1-14
