 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 90 SPORTS ACTION 
 177-090-0000 
 Purpose and Disclaimer 
 
 (1) Purpose: These rules establish the procedures and requirements for playing Sports Action, a lottery game operated by the Oregon State Lottery.  (2) Disclaimer: Sports Action is not associated with, sponsored, or authorized by the National Football League (NFL). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.213
