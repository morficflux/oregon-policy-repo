 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 COMMUNITY RIGHT-TO-KNOW SURVEY AND COMPLIANCE PROGRAMS 
 837-085-0230 
 Compliance Audit Warrants
 
 
 If a compliance auditor is denied entry, the State Fire Marshal may obtain an inspection warrant, as provided for in ORS 476.155. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.367 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.317(8)
