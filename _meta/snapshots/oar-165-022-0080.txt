 Elections Division - Chapter 165 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 COUNTY VOTERS’ PAMPHLET 
 165-022-0080 
 Billing Electoral Jurisdictions for County Voters’ Pamphlet 
 
 The formula used to determine the apportioned expenditure and revenue allocations for the county voters’ pamphlet as related to election costs is outlined in OAR 165-020-0050, 165-020-0055, and 165-020-0060, Billing for Local Elections. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 246.150, 251.305 & 251.325 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 251.365
