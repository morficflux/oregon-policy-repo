 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 SCHOOL FOOD AND NUTRITION SERVICES 
 581-051-0520 
 Scope of Rules
 
 
 These rules apply to the solicitation, award, renewal, and execution of contracts between Sponsors and FSMCs within this State. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.051, 7 CFR 210.21, 7 CFR 210.16, 7 CFR 220.16, 7 CFR 220.7(d), 7 CFR 225.17, 7 CFR 225.15(m), 7 CFR 226.22, 7 CFR 226.21, 7 CFR 250.53 & 2 CFR 200 subpart D
