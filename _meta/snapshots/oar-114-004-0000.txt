 Long Term Care Ombudsman 
 Chapter 114 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 DESIGNEES 
 114-004-0000 
 Appointment of Designees 
 
 (1) The Ombudsman shall review each designee applicant approved by a local screening committee. The Ombudsman shall approve the applicant if the applicant meets the qualifications of a designee as set forth in OAR 114-004-0010.  (2) Once the Ombudsman approves the applicant, the applicant must meet the requirements set forth in OAR 114-004-0020(1).  (3) Upon successful completion of the requirements set forth in OAR 114-004-0020(1) by the designee applicant, the Ombudsman shall appoint the applicant as a designee. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 441 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LTCO 1-1989, f. 1-19-89, cert. ef. 2-1-89
