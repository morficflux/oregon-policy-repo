 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 GINSENG MANAGEMENT PROGRAM 
 603-060-0080 
 Civil Penalty 
 
 (1) In addition to any other liability or penalty provided by law, the Director of Agriculture may impose a civil penalty on a person for violation of any of the provisions of 603-060. The civil penalty for a first violation shall be a fine of not more than $1,000. Upon a second violation, the department may impose a fine of not more than $2,000.  (2) All penalties recovered under this chapter shall be deposited by the State Treasurer in the Department of Agriculture Service Fund. Such moneys are continuously appropriated to the department for the purpose of carrying out the ginseng management program. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & OL 1997 & Ch. 9 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  OL 1997 & Ch. 9
