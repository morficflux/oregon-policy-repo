 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 314 INCOME TAXATION GENERALLY  GENERAL PROVISIONS 
 150-314-0325 
 Estimated Tax: Computation of Underpayment 
 
 (1) Underpayment charges shall be assessed on the last filed return received before the due date for such return. That return shall be considered the “original return,” and the tax due shall be used as the basis for computing the underpayment charges.  (2) Once underpayment charges are assessed on the original return, an amended return, reducing the tax liability, shall not reduce the underpayment charges. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 314.525
