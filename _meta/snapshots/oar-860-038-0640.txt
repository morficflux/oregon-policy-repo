 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 38 DIRECT ACCESS REGULATION 
 860-038-0640 
 Compliance Filings 
 
 By June 1 of each odd numbered year, an electric company must file a verified report prepared by an independent third-party regarding the electric company’s compliance with OAR 860-038-0500 through 860-038-0620 for the prior two calendar years. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756 & 757 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040 & 757.600 - 757.667 
 
 History:
