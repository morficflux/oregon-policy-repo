 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Mint Commission - Chapter 642 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENT 
 642-010-0015 
 Reports and Payments of Assessment Moneys 
 
 First purchasers and handlers must submit completed and signed assessment reports on commission approved forms or formats. Assessment reports shall include all purchases by or deliveries to a first purchaser or handler of Mint. Monthly assessment reports and assessments are due in the commission office and must be postmarked by the last day of the month following the reporting month. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576
