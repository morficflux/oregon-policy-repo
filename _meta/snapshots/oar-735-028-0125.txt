 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 ODOMETERS 
 735-028-0125 
 Fee for Providing Secure Forms
 
 
 (1) An agent may charge the end user a service fee authorized under ORS 803.124 for the service of providing secure forms. A service fee may not exceed 200 percent of the agent’s cost to purchase secure forms from the vendor or a person described in OAR 735-028-0160. In addition to the service fee, an agent may charge an end user the actual distribution costs for the delivery of secure forms to the end user. 

 (2) Upon request, a secure forms agent must provide DMV a breakdown of the actual distribution costs associated with providing secure forms to the end user. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 802.010 & 803.124 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 803.124 & 49 CFR Part 580
