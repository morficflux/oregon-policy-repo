 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 85 SEX OFFENDER NOTIFICATION LEVELS 
 255-085-0061 
 Classification and Final Decision
 
 
 (1) The classifying agency will apply the results of a risk assessment or review by reviewing the documents relied upon for the assessment, and any relevant documents from a review, and will place the person into a notification level. 

 (2) Following a Board decision, the Board shall send notice of the Board's order to the registrant and any Board registered victims. 

 (3) The classifying agency shall send notice to the Department of State Police of the registrant’s notification level. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 163A.100, ORS 163A.105, ORS 163A.110, ORS 163A.115, ORS 163A.125, ORS 144.050 & ORS 144.140 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 163A.100, ORS 163A.105, ORS 163A.110, ORS 163A.115, ORS 163A.125, Section 7, Chapter 708, Oregon Laws 2013, as amended by Section 27, Chapter 820, Oregon Laws (2015), SB 767 (2017) & HB 2320 (2015)
