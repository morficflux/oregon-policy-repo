 Land Use Board of Appeals 
 Chapter 661 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 RULES OF PROCEDURE FOR APPEALS 
 661-010-0038 
 State Agency Briefs
 
 
 A state agency that wishes to file a brief under ORS 197.830(8) shall file the brief within the time required for respondent’s brief. A state agency brief shall have yellow front and back covers. A state agency brief shall follow the form prescribed for the petition for review, but need not contain the final decision and may, if the state agency is arguing in favor of the respondent’s position, follow the form prescribed for the respondent’s brief. A state agency brief shall be accompanied by a filing fee of $100. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.820(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.830(8)
