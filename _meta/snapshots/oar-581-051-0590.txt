 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 SCHOOL FOOD AND NUTRITION SERVICES 
 581-051-0590 
 Meal Quality 
 
 The nutrition standards of Section 210.10, 210.10a, 220.8a, 225.16, and 226.20 of Title 7, Code of Federal Regulations shall apply to all programs under these rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 326.051, 7 CFR 210.21 & 7 CFR part 3015 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Federal Regulations 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ODE 6-1999, f. & cert. ef. 1-12-99
