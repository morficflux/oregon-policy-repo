 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 GROUNDWATER QUALITY PROTECTION 
 340-040-0210 
 Wellhead Protection: Appeal Procedure 
 
 Final Department decisions regarding certification, recertification, or decertification are not subject to contested case hearing rights but are reviewable under ORS 183.484. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468 & 468B 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468 & 468B 
 
 History:
