 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 GENERAL LICENSING REQUIREMENTS 
 918-030-0020 
 Application Process 
 
 (1) An applicant must apply for a license on a division form or by completing an online application available through the division’s Web site.  (2) In order to be approved the application must include:  (a) Applicable examination and application fees; and  (b) Verification of training, work experience or other documentation submitted in the manner established by OAR 918-030-0030.  (3) Nothing in chapter 918, division 030 prevents an applicant for a license from faxing or scanning and e-mailing documents.  (4) An applicant may not sit for an examination or receive a license unless the division approves the application.  (5) An applicant required by statute or appropriate rule to pass a written examination must score at least 75 percent correct. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.117 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.117
