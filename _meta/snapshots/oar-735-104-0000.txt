 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 104 WINDOWS AND GLAZINGS 
 735-104-0000 
 Glazing Materials
 
 
 The Transportation Safety Office of the Oregon Department of Transportation (Transportation Safety) adopts Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) Number 205, Glazing Materials, regarding glazing materials that may be used in vehicle windows and windshields. This standard is as found in the Code of Federal Regulations, Title 49, Part 571. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 815.030 & 815.040 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 815.040
