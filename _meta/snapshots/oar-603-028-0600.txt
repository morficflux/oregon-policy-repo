 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 28 MEAT PRODUCTS AND ESTABLISHMENTS 
 603-028-0600 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) In addition to the definitions set forth in ORS Chapter 603, a custom meat establishment is subject to the definitions set forth in OAR 603-025-0010.  (2) “Mobile custom slaughtering establishment” means a custom slaughtering establishment consisting of a truck, trailer, or other vehicle, and the equipment and utensils situated therein or thereon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561, 616 & 619 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 603.085, 619.031 & 619.046
