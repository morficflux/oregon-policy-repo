 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 47 CARBON MONOXIDE ALARMS AND DETECTORS 
 837-047-0135 
 Exemption 
 
 A landlord who rents a space for a manufactured dwelling or who rents moorage space for a floating home as defined in ORS 830.700, but does not rent the manufactured dwelling home or floating home is exempt from these rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476.725 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 476.725 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSFM 7-2010, f. 12-1-10, cert. ef. 12-28-10
