 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 375 ART THERAPY PROGRAM GENERAL ADMINISTRATION 
 331-375-0020 
 FEES 
 
 (1) An applicant or authorization holder is subject to the provisions of OAR 331-010-0000 through OAR 331-010-0070, including OAR 331-010-0010 and OAR 331-010-0020 regarding the payment of fees, penalties and charges. 

 (2) Fees established by the Office pursuant to ORS 676.592 are: 

 (a) Licensed art therapist: 

 (A) Application: $150. 

 (B) License: $50 – valid for one licensure year. 

 (C) Renewal: $50 – valid for one licensure year. 

 (b) Licensed certified art therapist: 

 (A) Application: $150. 

 (B) License: $50 – valid for one licensure year. 

 (C) Renewal: $50 – valid for one licensure year. 

 (c) Provisional licensed art therapist: 

 (A) Application: $150 

 (B) License: $50 – valid for one licensure year. 

 (C) Renewal: $50 – valid for one licensure year. 

 (d) Fingerprint fee for background check: $50 

 (e) Replacement: $25. 

 (f) Late fee: $40 for each year of inactive status up to three years. 

 (g) Affidavit of licensure, as defined in OAR 331-030-0040: $50. 

 (h) Administrative fee: $25. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 681.740-681.758, 2019 Senate Bill 29 & 2025 House Bill 3761 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 681.740-681.758, 2019 Senate Bill 29 & 2025 House Bill 3761
