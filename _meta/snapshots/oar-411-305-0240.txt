 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 305 FAMILY SUPPORT SERVICES 
 411-305-0240 
 Standards for Providers 
 
 (1) Independent providers, provider agencies, and general business providers paid with family support funds must be qualified as provider types as described in OAR 411-450-0070, excluding adult foster home providers.  (2) Provider agencies paid with family support funds must meet the standards described in OAR 411-450-0080.  (3) Provider agencies for respite and community inclusion may also be licensed under ORS 446.330. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, 417.346, 427.104 & 430.662 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 417.340-417.344, 417.346-417.350, 427.005, 427.007, 427.104, 430.610, 430.620 & 430.662-430.670
