 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 LICENSES 
 808-003-0710 
 Fees
 
 
 (1) Conventional exam fee: The fee amount is determined by PSI. 

 (2) The LCB Prep Course & Exam fee: The fee amount is determined by the Small Business Development Center (SBDC). 

 (3) Practical skills exam fee: Exterior Technician Exam taken through the National Association of Landscape Professionals (NALP) is paid directly to NALP. The fee amount is determined by NALP. 

 (4) Practical skills business class fee: $100. 

 (5) Retake fees. An applicant must pay the fees in subsection ( 1) (2) & (3 ) of this rule if an applicant wants to retake any sections of the test. 

 (6) Expiration of application. An applicant must pay the application fee in as stated in OAR 808-003-0130(5) if the application has expired as stated in OAR 808-003-0030. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 671.760 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.561 & ORS 671.570
