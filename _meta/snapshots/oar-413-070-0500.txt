 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 SUBSTITUTE CARE 
 413-070-0500 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 413-070-0500 to 413-070-0519 is to describe the Department's responsibility to seek legal permanency for a child or young adult in the legal custody of the Department and the use of a permanency committee. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.005
