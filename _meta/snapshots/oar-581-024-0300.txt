 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 STANDARDS FOR EDUCATION SERVICE DISTRICTS 
 581-024-0300 
 Advisory Committees 
 
 (1) Except as required in section (2) of this rule, the district board may appoint local advisory committees to represent the interests of areas within the district and to advise the district board on matters of interest within the area.  (2) If two or more component school district boards adopt resolutions petitioning the district board to establish a local advisory committee, the district board shall establish such a committee.  (3) A local advisory committee shall represent more than one component school district board.  (4) The district board shall adopt policies concerning the composition, number of members, term of office, procedures for appointment, and duties and responsibilities of each advisory committee.  (5) Nothing in this rule prevents district boards from appointing ad hoc advisory committees whenever the board deems it appropriate. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 334.025(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 334.025
