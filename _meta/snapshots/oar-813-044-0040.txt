 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 44 HOME OWNERSHIP ASSISTANCE PROGRAM OF THE OREGON HOUSING FUND 
 813-044-0040 
 Application Procedure and Requirements
 
 
 An organization may apply for a grant from the Home Ownership Assistance Program under ORS 458.655, upon an open solicitation for a Request for Application (RFA), as determined by OHCS. An organization may apply for a grant under this rule by submitting to OHCS a completed application and documents as required in the Request for Applications (RFA). 

   

   

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.555 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 458.620 & ORS 458.655
