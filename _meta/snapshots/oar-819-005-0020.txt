 Health Licensing Office, Board of Certified Advanced Estheticians - Chapter 819 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 BOARD OF CERTIFIED ADVANCED ESTHETICIANS, GENERAL ADMINISTRATION 
 819-005-0020 
 VACANCIES IN OFFICE 
 
 If the chairperson or vice chairperson are unable to complete their term, the Board must elect another chairperson or vice chairperson. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.650, ORS 676.655 & ORS 676.615 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.650 & ORS 676.655 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BCAE 2-2018, adopt filed 06/28/2018, effective 06/28/2018
