 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 110 POLYCHLORINATED BIPHENYLS (PCBS) 
 340-110-0003 
 Definitions 
 
 (1) The definitions of the following sections are added to 40 CFR 761.3.  (2) The definitions of OAR 340-100-0010.  (3) For the purpose of this Division:  (a) “Agency’s Regional Administrator in the EPA Region in Which the PCBs are Located” means the Department;  (b) “Agency” means the Department;  (c) “Approve” means permit;  (d) “Approved” means permitted;  (e) “Approval” means permit;  (f) “Assistant Administrator for Pesticides and Toxic Substances” means the Department;  (g) “Appropriate Regional Administrator” means the Department;  (h) “Chemical Waste Landfill” means PCB landfill;  (i) “Environmental Protection Agency” and “EPA” means the Department;  (j) “Initial Report” means application;  (k) “Receive Written Approval” means obtain a permit;  (l) “Regional Administrator” means the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 459 & 468 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.005
