 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 460 STRUCTURAL AND ENERGY EFFICIENCY SPECIALTY CODES 
 918-460-0040 
 Fire and Life Safety Plan Review Fee 
 
 When a fire and life safety plan review is conducted under ORS 479.155, the fee shall be 40 percent of the structural permit fee. This fee is in addition to the 65 percent structural plan review fee. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.210 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.210
