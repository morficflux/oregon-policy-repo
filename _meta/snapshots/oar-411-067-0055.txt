 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 67 CONTINUING CARE RETIREMENT COMMUNITY 
 411-067-0055 
 Residency Agreement 
 
 (1) The provider and the prospective resident must sign and date a residency agreement before CCRC services begin.  (2) A copy of the agreement must be provided to the resident.  (3) The residency agreement must list in boldface type the percentage of the entrance fee to be refunded and the manner in which the percentage of the entrance fee is calculated. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 101.150 & 410.090 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 101.030, 101.050 & 101.080
