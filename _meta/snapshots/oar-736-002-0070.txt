 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 ADMINISTRATIVE ACTIVITIES 
 736-002-0070 
 Crimes Considered
 
 
 The Department with consider a conviction of any crimes or offenses as provided in OAR 125-007-0270. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  390.131, 390.140, 181A.195, 390.200 & ORS 390.124 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  181A.195 & 390.200
