 Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying 
 Chapter 820 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 DEFINITIONS 
 820-005-0056 
 Person 
 
 "Person" as used in ORS 672.007(1)(c), (2)(c), and (3)(c) and ORS 672.045 subsections (1), (2), (4), (7), and (8), means individuals, corporations, associations, firms, partnerships, limited liablility companies, and joint stock companies. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, ORS 672.007, ORS 672.045 & ORS 672.255 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.002 - 672.325 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BEELS 7-2018, adopt filed 07/11/2018, effective 07/11/2018
