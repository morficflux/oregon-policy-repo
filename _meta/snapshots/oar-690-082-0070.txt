 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 82 ACCEPTANCE OF APPLICATIONS FOR WATER USES IN ADDITION TO CLASSIFIED USES 
 690-082-0070 
 Normal Application Process 
 
 Any application accepted for filing under these rules shall be processed in the same manner as all other applications to appropriate water, as provided in OAR 690, divisions 1 and 11. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 536 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 2-1988, f. & cert. ef. 1-19-88
