 Office of Training, Investigations and Safety 
 Chapter 419 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 440 FOSTER CARE AGENCIES 
 419-440-0010 
 What Law Applies
 
 
 These rules, OAR 419-440-0010 to 419-440-0210, regulate a child-caring agency licensed as a foster care agency. A foster care agency must also comply with OAR 419-400-0005 to 419-400-0310. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050, ORS 418.005, ORS 418.215 & ORS 418.240 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 418.205 - 418.325
