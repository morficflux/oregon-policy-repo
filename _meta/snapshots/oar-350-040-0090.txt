 Columbia River Gorge Commission 
 Chapter 350 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 REVISION OF URBAN AREA BOUNDARIES 
 350-040-0090 
 Consultation 
 
 In considering amendments to urban area boundaries, the Gorge Commission shall consult with the Forest Service, both states, the six Gorge counties, all four Indian tribal governments and agencies or organizations that have a specific interest. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 196.150 & RCW 43.97.015 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 196.150, RCW 43.97.015 & 16 U.S.C. § 544 et seq. 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CRGC 1-1999, f. & cert. ef. 10-14-99
