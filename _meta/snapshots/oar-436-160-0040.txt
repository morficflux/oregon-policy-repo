 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 160 ELECTRONIC DATA INTERCHANGE; MEDICAL BILL DATA 
 436-160-0040 
 Recognized Received Date 
 
 An electronic record is received when:  (1) The record enters the division’s designated information processing system;  (2) All the required data elements and electronic records are in the form and format specified in these rules in the proper sequence; and  (3) The record can be fully processed by the division's information processing system. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.264 & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 84.043 & 656.264
