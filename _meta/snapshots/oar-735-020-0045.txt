 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 GENERAL TITLE PROVISIONS 
 735-020-0045 
 Form of Titles and Ownership Records 
 
 Notwithstanding provisions of law that allow for titles in a form other than a certificate of title, except as otherwise provided by rule:  (1) All Oregon titles and salvage titles shall be issued in the form of a certificate of title.  (2) Primary ownership record, as defined in ORS 801.402, shall be in the form of a document as provided in OAR 735-020-0010 through 735-020-0030.  (3) Transitional ownership record, as defined in ORS 801.562, shall be in the form of a document as provided in OAR 735-020-0015 through 735-020-0040. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 801.402, 801.562 & Ch. 233 & OL 1993 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 803.012 
 
 History:
