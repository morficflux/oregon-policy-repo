 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 LICENSING OF INDIVIDUALS AND FIRMS ENGAGED IN LEAD-BASED PAINT ACTIVITY 
 812-007-0323 
 License Surrender 
 
 A certified LBP renovation contractor may request that the board accept the surrender of its license.  (1) The license remains in effect until the board accepts the surrender.  (2) If the board accepts the surrender, the board will notify the licensee of the date the license terminates.  (3) The board will not accept the surrender if an investigation of or disciplinary action against the licensee is pending.  (4) The licensee must cease renovating target housing or child-occupied facilities from the date the license terminates through the remainder of the license period.  (5) The board will not reinstate the surrendered license. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 701.235 & 701.515 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701.510 & 701.515
