 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 3 LICENSES 
 808-003-0960 
 Transfer and Conversion of Exam Scores
 
 
 (1) To be considered equivalent to passing the Plants & Turf and Design, Grading & Drainage conventional exam sections, an applicant must pass the following practical skills exam sections: 

 (a) Common Core; and 

 (b) Softscape Installation. 

 (2) To be considered equivalent to passing the Irrigation conventional exam section, an applicant must pass the following: 

 (a) Common Core; and 

 (b) Irrigation. 

 (3) An applicant must pass all practical skills exam sections of the practical skills exam for each section as stated in subsections (1) and (2) of this rule to be considered equivalent to the LCB conventional examination. 

 (4) The agency will grant credit to an applicant who has passed the specific sections of the practical skills exam as stated in OAR 808-003-0920 prior to submitting an application for licensing to the agency if: 

 (a) The agency is able to obtain the scores from the exam provider; and 

 (b) The passing scores are valid as required in OA R808-003-0930. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.561
