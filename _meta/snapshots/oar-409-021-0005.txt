 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Policy and Analytics - Chapter 409 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 ACCESS OF DATA AND FEES FOR DATA SEARCH 
 409-021-0005 
 Introduction 
 
 This division defines the access to the health data collected from various sources and stored at the Office for Oregon Health Policy and Research. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 442.420(3)(d) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.410 - 192.440 & 442.420(3)(d)
