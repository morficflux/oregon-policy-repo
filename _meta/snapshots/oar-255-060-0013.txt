 Board of Parole and Post-Prison Supervision 
 Chapter 255 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 RELEASE TO POST-PRISON SUPERVISION OR PAROLE AND EXIT INTERVIEWS 
 255-060-0013 
 Postponement Order 
 
 Any order regarding the postponement of parole release shall be sent to the prisoner and shall set forth:  (1) The facts and specific reasons for the decision and the individual votes of the Board members.  (2) Notice of the right to administrative appeal pursuant to the procedures of division 80. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 144 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PAR 2-1990, f. & cert. ef. 4-5-90
