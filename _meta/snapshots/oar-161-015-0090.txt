 Appraiser Certification and Licensure Board 
 Chapter 161 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 APPLICATION AND EXAMINATION PROCESS 
 161-015-0090 
 Re-examination
 
 
 An applicant may take the exam up to three times within six months of the date of the Board’s approval to take the examination. Any applicant who has failed to successfully pass the examination after six months or three attempts, whichever occurs first, may be required to furnish the ACLB Administrator with evidence of additional study as determined by the Administrator before submitting a new application and being approved to take subsequent examinations. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 674.305(8) & 674.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 674
