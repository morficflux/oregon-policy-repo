 Oregon Public Employees Retirement System 
 Chapter 459 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 ADMINISTRATION 
 459-005-0615 
 Minimum Retroactive Payment 
 
 PERS may not issue a retroactive payment for underpaid monthly benefits or lump sum distributions if the total amount of the underpayment is less than $5. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 238.650 and 238A.450 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 238.601 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PERS 4-2012, f. & cert. ef. 3-28-12
