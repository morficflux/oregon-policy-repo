 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 305 GENERAL ADMINISTRATION OF REVENUE LAWS 
 150-305-0206 
 Written Objections: Procedures 
 
 (1) When the department receives written objections, a department representative shall review the objections and try to resolve the disagreement. The representative shall then reach a decision regarding the written objections.  (2) Once a decision has been reached the department shall advise the taxpayer of the decision by letter. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 305.265
