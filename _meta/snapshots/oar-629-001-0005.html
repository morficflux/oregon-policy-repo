Department of Forestry

Chapter 629

Division 1
PROCEDURAL RULES

629-001-0005
Model Rules of Procedure

The Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, promulgated by the Attorney General effective January 1, 2024 are hereby adopted as the rules of procedures of the Board of Forestry and the State Forester.

[ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Board of Forestry.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 526.016(4) & 526.041
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
