 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 ACCOUNTING AND INVESTMENTS (ORS CHAPTER 733); REHABILITATION AND LIQUIDATION OF INSURERS (ORS CHAPTER 734) 
 836-031-0600 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of OAR 836-031-0600 to 836-031-0690 is to prescribe:  (1) Requirements for statements of actuarial opinion to be submitted in accordance with ORS 733.304 and for memoranda in support thereof;  (2) Rules applicable to the appointment of an appointed actuary.  (3) Guidance as to the meaning of “adequacy of reserves.” 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & 733.304 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 733.304
