 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 175 FOOD HANDLER TRAINING 
 333-175-0091 
 Reciprocity and Equivalency 
 
 (1) A food handler certificate of program completion is valid statewide.  (2) Any person who has a current certification from an Authority-approved food manager training program or is registered as an Environmental Health Specialist or Environmental Health Specialist Trainee as required in ORS Chapter 700 need not obtain a food handler certificate of program completion.  (3) To be accepted in lieu of a food handler certificate of program completion, a food manager certification must be renewed every five years. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 624.570 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 624.570
