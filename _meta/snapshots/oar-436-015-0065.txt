 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 MANAGED CARE ORGANIZATIONS 
 436-015-0065 
 Monitoring and Auditing 
 
 (1) The director will monitor and conduct periodic audits of an MCO as necessary to ensure compliance with the MCO certification and performance requirements.  
(2) All records of an MCO and its individual panel providers must be disclosed upon the director’s request. These records must be legible and cannot be kept in a coded or semi-coded manner unless a legend is provided for the codes. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.260 & ORS 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.260 
 
 History:
