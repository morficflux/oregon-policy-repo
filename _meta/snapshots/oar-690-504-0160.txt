 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 504 HOOD BASIN PROGRAM 
 690-504-0160 
 Fifteenmile Creek Subbasin Reservation 
 
 Unappropriated water in the Fifteenmile Creek subbasin is reserved as follows:  (1) Five thousand (5,000) acre-feet of unappropriated water of Fifteenmile Creek or its tributaries upstream of the City of Dufur water supply reservoir are reserved for storage in multipurpose reservoirs. The priority date of the reservation is November 6, 1992.  (2) Three thousand five hundred (3,500) acre-feet of unappropriated water of Larch Creek or its tributaries are reserved for storage in multipurpose reservoirs. The priority date of the reservation is November 6, 1992. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 537 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 537.249 & 537.356 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 8-1996, f. & cert. ef. 11-14-96
