 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0663 
 Early Indicator and Intervention System: Eligibility 
 
 (1) The following entities shall be eligible to receive an Early Indicator and Intervention Systems Grant: 

 (a) School districts; 

 (b) Public charter schools; 

 (c) The Oregon School for the Deaf; 

 (d) Youth Correctional Education Programs; 

 (e) Juvenile Detention Educational Programs; 

 (f) Long Term Care and Treatment Educational Programs or 

 (g) Consortia of two or more eligible entities . 

 (2) An education service district may serve as fiscal agent for a consortia or eligible entity . 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 327.367
