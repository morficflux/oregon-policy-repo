 Board of Pharmacy 
 Chapter 855 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 43 PRACTITIONER DISPENSING 
 855-043-0730 
 Community Health Clinic (CHC) - Storage of Drugs
 
 
 All drugs, including drug samples, must be stored according to the manufacturer’s published guidelines and be stored in appropriate conditions of temperature, light, humidity, sanitation, ventilation, and space. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 689.205 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 689.305
