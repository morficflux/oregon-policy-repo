 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 MOTOR CARRIER ELECTRONIC COMMERCE AND ONLINE TRANSACTIONS 
 740-015-0090 
 Electronic Records
 
 
 CCD records pertaining to electronic transactions conducted via an authorized system site are subject to all requirements for processing and records maintenance as described in OAR 740, division 55. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619 & 802.012 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 802.012 & 825.515
