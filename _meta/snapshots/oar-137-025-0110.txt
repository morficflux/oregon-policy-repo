 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 BINGO/RAFFLES/MONTE CARLO 
 137-025-0110 
 Operator Lists
 
 
 Each Class A or B bingo licensee shall submit to the Department, on a form prescribed by the Department, a list of the names, address and dates of birth of all employees who conduct bingo operations on behalf of the licensee. An initial list shall be submitted on or before the date the licensee begins conducting bingo operations pursuant to these rules. An updated list of employees shall be filed once every 90 days for Class A licensees and annually for Class B licensees. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 464 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 464.250
