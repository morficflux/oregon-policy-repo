 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 CASE MANAGEMENT — PROGRAM ELIGIBILITY 
 413-030-0310 
 Eligibility for Adoption Program 
 
 To be eligible for the program classification of adoption, a child must be in a living situation that is officially considered an adoptive placement. The following are considered eligible for the program classification:  (1) Children fully free for adoption and placed in an approved adoptive home are classified as adoption from the time the placement is approved until the adoption is completed or disrupted.  (2) Children receiving courtesy supervision for an adoptive placement initiated in another state through the Interstate Compact. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 109.305 – 109.310, 418.005 & 418.270 – 418.285
