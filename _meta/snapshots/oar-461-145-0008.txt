 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 145 TREATMENTS OF SPECIFIC ASSETS 
 461-145-0008 
 Alaska Permanent Fund Dividend 
 
 (1) The Alaska Permanent Fund Dividend is issued annually to eligible Alaskan residents who apply for the payment. Out-of-state residents, except military personnel and students who claim Alaska as their residence, are not eligible unless they resided in Alaska and filed for the payment before leaving the state.  (2) Alaska Permanent Fund Dividend payments are counted as lump-sum income (see OAR 461-140-0120). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.816 & 412.049 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.700, 411.816 & 412.049
