 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 MUNICIPAL ADMINISTRATION 
 918-050-0030 
 Standard Tri-County Regional Processes 
 
 All jurisdictions within the Tri-County region shall use uniform processes for permit application, plan review, permit issuance, and recording inspections as approved by the division, including, but not limited to:  (1) Minor labels;  (2) Issuing permits when no plan review is required;  (3) Recording inspections;  (4) Partial permits;  (5) Deferred submittals;  (6) Over-the-counter permits that require plan review; and  (7) Plan review issue resolution. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.048 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.046
