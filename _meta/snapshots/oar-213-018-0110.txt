 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 OFFENSE SUBCATEGORIES 
 213-018-0110 
 Unlawful Operation of Weaponized Unmanned Aircraft System (ORS 837.365(2)) 
 
 Unlawful Operation of Weaponized Unmanned Aircraft System shall be ranked as follows: 

 (1) CRIME CATEGORY 8: If offender intentionally, knowingly, or recklessly operated an unmanned aircraft system in a manner that caused the system to function as a dangerous weapon  and the operation caused serious physical injury to another. 

 (2) CRIME CATEGORY 6: If offender intentionally, knowingly, or recklessly operates an unmanned aircraft system: 

 (a) To fire a bullet or projectile; or 

 (b) In a manner that causes the system to function as a dangerous weapon. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667 & HB 3047 (2017) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CJC 3-2018, adopt filed 08/06/2018, effective 08/06/2018
