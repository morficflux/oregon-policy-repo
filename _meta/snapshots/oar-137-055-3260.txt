 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 OREGON CHILD SUPPORT PROGRAM 
 137-055-3260 
 Correction of Mistakes in Orders
 
 
 (1) Clerical mistakes in final orders or money awards issued by the administrator pursuant to ORS 25.501 to 25.556 and errors therein arising from oversight or omission may be corrected by the administrator at any time within 60 days of the issuance of the order. The corrected order must be clearly marked "Corrected Order" and must contain notice to the parties of appeal rights as provided by ORS 25.513. 

 (2) The corrected order must be served on the parties by regular mail at their contact addresses. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 25.505 & 180.345 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 25.501 - 25.556
