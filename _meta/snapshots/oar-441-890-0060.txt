 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 890 LICENSING OF MORTGAGE SERVICERS 
 441-890-0060 
 Preexisting Duty to Service a Mortgage Loan 
 
 The revocation, suspension, or failure to obtain and maintain a license does not impair or affect the servicer’s obligations under a preexisting lawful contract with a lender or borrower. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2017 Or Laws ch 636 § 16 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2017 Or Laws ch 636 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FSR 3-2018, adopt filed 04/16/2018, effective 04/17/2018
