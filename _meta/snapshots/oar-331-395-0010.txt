 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 395 DISCIPLINE AND ENFORCEMENT 
 331-395-0010 
 INVESTIGATIVE AUTHORITY 
 
 The Office may initiate and conduct investigations relating to the practice of art therapy and may take appropriate disciplinary action in accordance with the provisions of ORS 676.612 and ORS 676.992. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 681.740-681.758 & 2019 Senate Bill 29 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 681.740-681.758 & 2019 Senate Bill 29 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 HLO 9-2019, adopt filed 08/28/2019, effective 12/01/2019
