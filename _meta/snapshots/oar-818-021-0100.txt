 Oregon Board of Dentistry 
 Chapter 818 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 EXAMINATION AND LICENSING 
 818-021-0100 
 Competency Examination 
 
 (1) The Board may require any licensee to pass the Board examination or require any licensee to participate in a practice review as set forth in section (2) of this rule.  (2) One or more consultants will review the licensee’s treatment of patients selected at random and report observations to the Board.  (3) If the results of the examination or practice review provide evidence that the licensee lacks competency, the Board may refuse to renew the licensee’s license or take disciplinary action against the licensee. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 679 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 679.250(9)
