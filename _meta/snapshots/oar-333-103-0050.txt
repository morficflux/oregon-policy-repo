 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 103 RADIATION PROTECTION SERVICES FEES 
 333-103-0050 
 Fees for Accredited Hospital Radiology Inspectors 
 
 (1) Each accreditation for a radiology inspector shall be subject to an accreditation fee of $264.  (2) Each accreditation issued by the Authority for a radiology inspector shall be subject to a biennial renewal fee of $264.  (3) Each accreditation shall expire in the second year on the last day of the month of issuance unless renewed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.757 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.757
