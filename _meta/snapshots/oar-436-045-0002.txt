 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 REOPENED CLAIMS PROGRAM 
 436-045-0002 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to establish guidelines for administering disbursements made from the Reopened Claims Program established to reimburse compensation paid as a result of awards made by the Board or voluntary claim reopenings pursuant to ORS 656.278. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.625 & 656.726 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.625 & 656.726
