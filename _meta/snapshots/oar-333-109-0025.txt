 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 109 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR INDUSTRIAL AND MANUFACTURING USE OF PARTICLE ACCELERATORS 
 333-109-0025 
 Shielding and Safety Design Requirements 
 
 (1) A qualified expert, acceptable to the Authority, must be consulted in the design of a particle accelerator installation and called upon to perform a radiation survey when the accelerator is first capable of producing radiation.  (2) Each particle accelerator installation must be provided with such primary and/or secondary barriers as are necessary to assure compliance with OAR 333-120-0100 and 333-109-0030. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 – 453.807
