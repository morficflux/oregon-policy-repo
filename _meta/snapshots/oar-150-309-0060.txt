 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 309 EQUALIZATION OF PROPERTY TAXES 
 150-309-0060 
 Records Included in Journal of Governing Body
 
 
 The following records from sessions of the property value appeals board shall be made a part of the journal of the county governing body: 

 (1) The date the members were appointed; 

 (2) The positions to which the members were appointed; 

 (3) A record of the date the board convened and the date the board adjourned. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 309.072
