 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 160 REGISTRATION AND LICENSING REQUIREMENTS FOR UNDERGROUND STORAGE TANK SERVICE PROVIDERS 
 340-160-0150 
 Fees 
 
 (1) The nonrefundable application fee for a service provider license is $600.  (2) The nonrefundable application fee for a supervisor license is $150. This fee covers up to four supervisor license categories, if the expiration date is the same for all license categories. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 466.746 & 466.750 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 466.750
