 Department of Energy 
 Chapter 330 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 ENERGY SUPPLIER ASSESSMENT RULES 
 330-075-0045 
 Retention of Supporting Documentation by Energy Resource Suppliers 
 
 Supporting documentation used by the energy resource supplier in compiling the information submitted pursuant to OAR 330-075-0010 to 330-075-0045 shall be kept available by the energy resource supplier for inspection by the Department for three years from the date of submission. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 469 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.421
