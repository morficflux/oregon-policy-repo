 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS OF IDENTITY, LABELING AND OTHER REGULATIONS RELATING TO FLUID MILK AND DAIRY PRODUCTS 
 603-024-0494 
 Examination Grade Required for Licensing 
 
 (1) The examination shall have a total of 100 possible points, 50 of which shall be on the written examination, and the other 50 points on the practical examination.  (2) A total grade of at least 80 out of the 100 possible points is required to pass the examination for a dairy operators’ lifetime license.  (3) A score of 40 or more is required for a passing grade on the written or practical examination. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 621 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 621.266
