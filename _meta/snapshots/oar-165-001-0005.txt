 Elections Division - Chapter 165 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 165-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure, OAR 137-001-0007 through 137-002-0060 as adopted by the Attorney General of the State of Oregon under the Administrative Procedures Act, effective January 1, 2008, are adopted as the rules of procedure for rulemaking and declaratory rulings for the Elections Division, Secretary of State.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Elections Division.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
