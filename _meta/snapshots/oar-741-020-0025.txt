 Department of Transportation 
 Rail Division - Chapter 741 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 STANDARDS TO DETERMINE PROJECT ELIGIBILITY AND APPLICATION PROCEDURES FOR GRANTS OR LOANS UNDER THE INDUSTRIAL SPUR PROGRAM 
 741-020-0025 
 Application Submission Periods 
 
 (1) The Department will announce periods for submitting applications for funding from the Industrial Rail Spur Fund, as funding is available.  (2) Project applications will be reviewed for compliance with the requirements in OAR 741-020-0040 and as prescribed in 741-020-0050.  (3) Projects not funded may be resubmitted during application submission periods prescribed by the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619 & 823.011 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 367.070 & Sec. 10 & Ch. 741 OL 2003 
 
 History:
