Department of Consumer and Business Services

Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836

Division 10
GENERAL PROVISIONS

836-010-0013
Additional Filing Requirements for Transitional Health Benefit Plans

(1) Under section 5, chapter 80, Oregon Laws 2014, a transitional health benefit plan must comply with the Insurance Code as of December 31, 2013.

(2) In addition to the requirements of OAR 836-010-0000, 836-010-0011, and 836-010-0021 when submitting a filing for a rate change to a transitional health benefit plan, the insurer shall comply with the guidance for the applicable plan year as provided in Exhibits 1, 2 and 3 of this rule.

(3) The additional filing requirements set forth in this section apply only to 2015, 2016 and 2017 transitional plans.

[ED. NOTE: Exhibits referenced are available from the agency]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 731.244 & 743.018
Statutes/Other Implemented: 2014 OL Ch. 80 & Sec. 5 (Enrolled SB 1582)
