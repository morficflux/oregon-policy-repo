 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 ATTORNEY FEES 
 438-015-0095 
 Attorney Fees in Third-Party Cases 
 
 Unless otherwise ordered by the Board after a finding of extraordinary circumstances, an attorney fee not to exceed 33-1/3 percent of the gross recovery obtained by the plaintiff in an action maintained under the provisions of ORS 656.576 through 656.596 is authorized. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.307, 656.388, 656.593 & 656.726(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.593(1)(a) & 656.388(3)
