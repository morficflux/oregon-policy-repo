 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 OREGON NATURAL AREAS PROGRAM RULES 
 736-045-0320 
 Oregon Register of Natural Heritage Resources: Location and Maintenance of Register 
 
 The department will maintain the Oregon Register of Natural Areas at the department’s office in Salem, OR. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.124(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.581(1)
