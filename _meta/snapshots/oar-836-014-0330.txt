 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 14 ALTERNATIVE INSURANCE ORGANIZATIONS 
 836-014-0330 
 Unfair Trade Practices 
 
 (1) Violation of any provision of OAR 836-014-0270, 836-014-0280, 836-014-0290, 836-014-0300 or 836-014-0310 is an unfair trade practice for purposes of ORS 746.240.  (2) The provisions of OAR 836-014-0200 to 836-014-0330 are in addition to applicable provisions of ORS Chapter 746, governing trade practices. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 746.240
