 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 446 DEALER LICENSING 
 441-446-0200 
 Supplemental or Corrected Licenses 
 
 A supplemental or corrected license shall have the same expiration as the limited or dealer license. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.666 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.666 & 446.716
