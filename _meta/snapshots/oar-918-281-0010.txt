 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 281 CERTIFICATION OF ELECTRICAL INSPECTORS 
 918-281-0010 
 Continuing Education 
 
 Electrical inspectors must obtain continuing education as outlined in OAR 918-098-1450. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 479.650 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.720 & 479.680
