 Land Conservation and Development Department 
 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 23 PROCEDURES AND REQUIREMENTS FOR COMPLYING WITH GOAL 5 
 660-023-0230 
 Scenic Views and Sites 
 
 (1) For purposes of this rule, “scenic views and sites” are lands that are valued for their aesthetic appearance.  (2) Local governments are not required to amend acknowledged comprehensive plans in order to identify scenic views and sites. If local governments decide to amend acknowledged plans in order to provide or amend inventories of scenic resources, the requirements of OAR 660-023-0030 through 660-023-0050 shall apply. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 197 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.040 & 197.225 - 197.245
