 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 FISH MANAGEMENT PLANS 
 635-500-1720 
 Nongame Fish 
 
 (1) The policy for nongame fish in the Lahontan subbasins is that the following indigenous species and their respective waters shallbe managed to maintain self-sustaining populations: Borax Lake chub in Borax Lake; Alvord chub in the Alvord Lake subbasin, except for Borax Lake; and Lahontan redside, Tahoe sucker, mountain sucker, and speckled dace in the Quinn River subbasin.  (2) The objective for nongame fish in the Lahontan subbasins is to improve and maintain population health (e.g., abundance, multiple age classes, and genetic fitness) of all indigenous nongame species in the Alvord Lake and Quinn River subbasins. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 496.172, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162, 496.172, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129
