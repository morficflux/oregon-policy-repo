 Teacher Standards and Practices Commission 
 Chapter 584 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 SCHOOL NURSE CERTIFICATES 
 584-021-0202 
 Criteria for Denial of Certification Based on Conviction for Crimes 
 
 An applicant for the initial Oregon certificate or for reinstatement of an Oregon certificate that has been lapsed for three years or more must submit to a fingerprint check. If the applicant has been convicted of any of the crimes listed in ORS 342.143 or if the applicant has made a false statement about a conviction, the applicant shall be denied certification. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 342 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 342.175 – 342.190 & 342.455 – 342.495
