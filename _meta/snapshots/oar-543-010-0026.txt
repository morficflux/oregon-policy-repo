 State Library 
 Chapter 543 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ADMINISTRATION 
 543-010-0026 
 Copying Charges 
 
 The State Library Board authorizes the State Library to charge current rates for the following types of copying. The fee schedule will be available to the public in a State Library policy.  (1) Photocopies from print publications.  (2) Photocopies from microfilm.  (3) Copies of photographs using a library approved vendor.  (4) Print-outs from electronic resources. Copying charges for state employees and officials on state business are covered by its agency assessments. Fees based on current rates may be charged for copying beyond reasonable levels. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 357.026(1) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 357.026(1)
