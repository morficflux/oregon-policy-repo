 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 CONTESTS IN THE ADJUDICATION OF WATER RIGHTS 
 690-030-0035 
 Amendment of Statement of Contest 
 
 The statement of contest may be amended, prior to the date fixed for the hearing, by the filing of such amended statement of contest with the Water Resources Director at least 10 days prior to the date fixed for the hearing of said contest. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536 & 543 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 3, f. & ef. 2-18-77
