 Oregon State Marine Board 
 Chapter 250 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 250-001-0010 
 Availability of the Rules 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 830.110(11), the laws and rules of the Marine Board shall be available at the office of the State Marine Board. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 830 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 830.110 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 MB 1-1960, f. & ef. 2-4-60, Renumbered from 250-010-0020
