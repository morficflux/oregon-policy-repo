 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 OFFENSE SUBCATEGORIES 
 213-018-0038 
 HIT AND RUN (VEHICLE) (ORS 811.705)
 
 
 (1) CRIME CATEGORY 8: Hit and Run shall be ranked at Crime Category 8 if a person suffers serious physical injury or dies as a result of the collision. 

 (2) CRIME CATEGORY 6: Hit and Run shall be ranked at Crime Category 6 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 8. 

 (3) The crime categories above as applied to the offense of Hit and Run (Vehicle) as set forth in HB 4055 (2018) are effective January 1, 2019.
