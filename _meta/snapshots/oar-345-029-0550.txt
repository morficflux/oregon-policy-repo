 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 29 NOTICE OF VIOLATION, CIVIL PENALTIES, REVOCATION OR SUSPENSION 
 345-029-0550 
 Enforcement Conference 
 
 If requested by the responsible party in its response to the Pre-Enforcement Notice provided under OAR 345-029-0520(2), the Director must provide the responsible party an opportunity for an enforcement conference to present information regarding the alleged violation and to discuss any corrective actions taken or proposed. The Director may use information discussed at the conference in determining the appropriate enforcement action. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.085, 469.540 & 469.992 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 EFSC 1-2021, adopt filed 02/26/2021, effective 02/26/2021
