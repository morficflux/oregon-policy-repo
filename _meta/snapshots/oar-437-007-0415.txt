 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Oregon Occupational Safety and Health Division - Chapter 437 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 FOREST ACTIVITIES 
 437-007-0415 
 Explosives and Blasting Agents 
 
 (1) The storage, transportation, handling, and use of explosives and blasting agents must be in accordance with OAR chapter 437, division 3, subdivision U, Blasting and The Use of Explosives.  (2) Explosives and blasting agents must be handled only by qualified, designated personnel.  (3) Explosives and blasting agents must not be transported in:  (a) The driver’s compartment.  (b) Any passenger-occupied area of a machine or vehicle.  (4) Explosives must not be hauled on any vehicle while it is engaged in transporting workers.  EXCEPTION: This rule does not prohibit the driver and one qualified person from riding in a vehicle in which explosives are being hauled. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 654.025(2) & 656.726(4) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 654.001 - 654.295 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OSHA 5-2003, f. 6-2-03, cert. ef. 12-1-03
