 Department of Transportation 
 Commerce and Compliance Division - Chapter 740 
 
 
 
 
 Division 105 WELDED REPAIR OF LOG TRUCK REACHES; EQUIPMENT: LOGS, POLES OR PILING 
 740-105-0120 
 Approved Repair Splice Configuration Diagram
 
 
 The reach splice, including the weld, must be constructed in substantial compliance with the Department’s approved splice configuration. A diagram of the approved splice configuration is available through the office of the Commerce and Compliance Division, 455 Airport Road SE, Building A, Salem OR 97301. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 823.011, 825.210 & 825.252 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 825.210 & 825.252
