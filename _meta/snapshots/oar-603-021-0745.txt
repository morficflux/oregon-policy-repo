 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 FOOD SAFETY DIVISION — BAKERIES 
 603-021-0745 
 Vehicles and Transportation 
 
 Vehicles used to transport all products and materials shall be maintained in a clean condition to aid in protecting the product from contamination. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561.190 & 635.045 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 635.045 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 AD 851(23-67), f. & ef. 9-13-67
