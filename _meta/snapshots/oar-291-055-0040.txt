 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 INTENSIVE MANAGEMENT UNIT 
 291-055-0040 
 Property 
 
 (1) Any personal property, as defined in the Department of Corrections rule on Personal Property Control and Disposition (Inmate), OAR 291-117, not permitted in the cell of any Intensive Management Unit inmate will be properly protected in a designated property room. Upon release from IMU, the inmate shall check his/her personal property and sign the property sheet.  (2) Intensive Management Unit inmates will be permitted to retain basic personal health items (dentures, prescribed eye wear, hearing aids and approved prosthetic devices). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
