 Real Estate Agency 
 Chapter 863 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 REAL ESTATE PROPERTY MANAGER LICENSING 
 863-024-0000 
 Application and Purpose
 
 
 (1) This division sets forth the requirements and process for licensing real estate property managers, as that term is defined in ORS 696.010. 

 (2) The purpose of this division is to specify the requirements for obtaining a real estate property manager license. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 696.385 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 696.022
