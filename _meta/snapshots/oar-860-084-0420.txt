 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 84 SOLAR PHOTOVOLTAIC PROGRAMS 
 860-084-0420 
 Compliance with Pilot Program Requirements 
 
 (1) The participant agrees to the confidential release of information from participant surveys and pilot program applications to the organizations listed in section (2) of this rule.  (2) Each electric company must send a list of all reserved and contracted systems that have completed the release of confidential information to the Energy Trust of Oregon, the Oregon Department of Revenue, or the Oregon Department of Energy, upon request by each organization. Data in this list must include the following minimum information:  (a) Installation location of system;  (b) Nameplate capacity of installed system;  (c) Name, business name, and business address of contractor installing system;  (d) Financer of system;  (e) In-service date;  (f) Date of certification of compliance; and  (g) Customer account number. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 757.360 - 757.380 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 757.360 - 757.380
