 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 DECONTAMINATION OF ILLEGAL DRUG MANUFACTURING SITES 
 333-040-0170 
 Advice and Consultation 
 
 Between the dates of scheduled training for contractors as set forth in ORS 453.888, the Division shall be available to consult with contractors, as well as those planning to become contractors, on information pertinent to illegal drug manufacturing sites, including but not limited to chemicals found at such sites and their toxicity, new or revised decontamination procedures, personal protective equipment and applicable federal regulations and state rules. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.864 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.855 - 453.912
