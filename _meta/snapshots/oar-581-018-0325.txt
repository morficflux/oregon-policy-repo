 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 NETWORK OF QUALITY TEACHING AND LEARNING 
 581-018-0325 
 Reporting
 
 
 The Department of Education shall develop district reporting requirements for allocation of funds for Educator Effectiveness and CCSS implementation. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  Sect. 1, ch. 661 & OL 2013 (Enrolled HB 3233) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Sect. 1, ch. 661 & OL 2013 (Enrolled HB 3233)
