 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 17 STRATEGIC INVESTMENTS 
 581-017-0356 
 Eligibility 
 
 The Department of Education shall allocate funds for the Regional Promise Program grant to consortia that consist of at least three school districts, at least one education service district, and at least one public post-secondary institution. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 327.800 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 327.820
