 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 95 COLUMBIA RIVER BASIN WATER DEVELOPMENT LOAN PROGRAM 
 690-095-0075 
 Conditions to Disbursement of Loan Funds 
 
 The director shall withhold payment of funds under a loan contract until any necessary federal and state environmental impact approval processes have been completed for the project and any relevant approvals and permits from any regulatory authority pertaining to the construction or operation of the project have been obtained. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027 & 541.646 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 541-600 - 541.646 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 12-14-10
