 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 7 FISH MANAGEMENT AND HATCHERY OPERATION 
 635-007-0548 
 Training of Fish Hatchery Personnel 
 
 (1) Fish Division, regional managers, or hatchery managers shall develop training programs for staff to assure awareness of and compliance with hatchery program management plans, to keep staff abreast of new scientific and technological developments and to encourage and support staff career development.  (2) Each hatchery shall establish a training schedule for its staff and maintain training records. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012 & 496.138 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.171, 496.172, 496.176, 496.182, 496.430, 496.435, 496.445, 496.450 & 496.455
