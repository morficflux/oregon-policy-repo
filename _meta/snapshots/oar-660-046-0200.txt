 Chapter 660 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 46 Middle Housing in Medium and Large Cities 
 660-046-0200 
 Purpose of Middle Housing in Large Cities 
 
 OAR 660-046-0205 through OAR 660-046-0235 are intended to measure compliance with ORS 197.758 and Goal 10 Housing for Large Cities. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.040 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.758 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LCDD 18-2020, adopt filed 12/29/2020, effective 12/31/2020
