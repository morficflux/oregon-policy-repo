 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 520 ORGANIZATION OF THE JUVENILE PSYCHIATRIC  SECURITY REVIEW BOARD 
 859-520-0005 
 Membership and Terms 
 
 The Juvenile panel of the Psychiatric Security Review Board shall consist of five members as constituted in ORS chapter 419C. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.385, 161.387; OL 2007 & Ch. 889 § 6 (SB 328) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.385(1)-(7) & 161.387(1) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PSRB 2-2010, f. & cert. ef. 9-28-10
