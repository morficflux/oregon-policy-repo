 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 CONTESTS IN THE ADJUDICATION OF WATER RIGHTS 
 690-030-0010 
 Statement of Contest, When to Be Filed 
 
 Every statement of contest must be filed within the period prescribed by statute; and no statement of contest will be received or considered which is not filed with the Water Resources Director within the time permitted by law. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536 & 543 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 3, f. & ef. 2-18-77
