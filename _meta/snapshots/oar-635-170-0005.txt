 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 170 WILDLIFE MANAGEMENT PLAN 
 635-170-0005 
 Program Objectives 
 
 The objectives of the Black Bear Management Plan are:  (1) Recognize the black bear as an important part of Oregon’s fauna, valued by many Oregonians.  (2) Maintain healthy black bear populations within the state into the future.  (3) Conduct a management program that maintains healthy populations of black bear, and recognizes the desires of the public and the statutory obligations of the Department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.146 & 496.162
