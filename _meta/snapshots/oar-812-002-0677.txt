 Construction Contractors Board 
 Chapter 812 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS 
 812-002-0677 
 Specialty Contractor 
 
 “Specialty contractor” means a contractor who performs work on a structure, project, development or improvement and whose business operations:  (1) Require the use of two or fewer unrelated building trades or crafts; or  (2) If a residential contractor, require the use of more than two unrelated building trades or crafts that the specialty contractor supervises, subcontracts to be performed or performs, provided that the sum of all contracts (including labor and materials) on any single property does not exceed $2,500. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 701.235 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 701 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CCB 17-2010, f. 12-22-10, cert. ef. 1-1-11
