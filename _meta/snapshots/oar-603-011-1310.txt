 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 11 LIVESTOCK HEALTH AND SANITATION 
 603-011-1310 
 Import Requirements – Captive Wildlife and Cervids
 
 
 In addition to the requirements of OAR 603-011-1100 and all other import requirements, species of captive wildlife and cervids prohibited by the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife (ODF&W) may not be imported. If a special exemption is granted and signed by the Director of ODF&W, Oregon Department of Agriculture may issue an import permit, if the animal(s) meet health requirements. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 561 & 596 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  596.020 & ORS 596.341
