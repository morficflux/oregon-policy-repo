 Department of Geology and Mineral Industries 
 Chapter 632 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 OIL AND GAS RULES 
 632-010-0132 
 Tubing 
 
 All wells must be equipped with tubing. Production must be exclusively through tubing, unless otherwise approved by the department. The bottom of tubing on flowing wells may not be higher than 100 feet above the top of the producing horizon, or as otherwise approved by the department. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 520 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 520.095
