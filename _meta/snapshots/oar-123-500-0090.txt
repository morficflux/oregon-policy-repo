 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 500 CULTURAL PARTICIPATION GRANTS 
 123-500-0090 
 Eligibility 
 
 (1) Each Oregon county, and each federally-recognized Indian Tribe, may establish a Cultural Coalition responsible for developing, implementing and monitoring a local cultural plan.  (2) The Cultural Trust will award Cultural Participation Grant funds annually to each cultural coalition working within a cultural plan that has been approved by the Cultural Trust Board.  (3) In accordance with its plan, the Cultural Coalition may decide how these funds are allocated locally. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 359.416 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 359.400 - 359.444
