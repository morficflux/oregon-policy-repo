    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-18


    AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER 18-15 AND EXTENDING THE
    GARNER COMPLEX FIRE DECLARATION


    On July 21, 2018, the Emergency Conflagration Act was invoked and an
    emergency was declared for the Garner Complex Fire in Jackson and Josephine
    Counties. That declaration was memorialized in Executive Order 18-15, issued on
    July 26, 2018.


    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


    Pursuant to ORS 476.580, Executive Order 18-15 is amended to extend the
    emergency declaration to the following fires:


    1.      The Taylor Creek Fire, burning in Josephine County near the communities
            of Merlin and Grants Pass


    2       The Klondike Fire, burning in Josephine County near the communities of
            Selma and Cave Junction


    3;      The Sugar Pine Fire, burning in Jackson and Douglas County in close
           proximity to the South Umpqua Complex                                        ‘


    Due to the number of fires burning in Josephine, Jackson, and Douglas Counties,
    the Oregon State Fire Marshal is empowered by this Order to deploy resources to
    assist in structural protection on an as-needed basis until threats to structures
    subside and the Fire Marshal demobilizes from the region.
