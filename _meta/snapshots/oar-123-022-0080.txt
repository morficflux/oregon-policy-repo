 Oregon Business Development Department 
 Chapter 123 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 22 SMALL BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT CENTERS 
 123-022-0080 
 Eligibility Criteria
 
 
 To be eligible for a grant, an entitle must be: 

 (1) a community college district, or a community college service district; or 

 (2) a public university listed in ORS 352.002; or 

 (3) a service provider offering small business development services in a specific geographic area that is not covered by entities described in (1) or (2). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 285A.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 285A.340 - 285A.349 & 285B.165 - 285B.180
