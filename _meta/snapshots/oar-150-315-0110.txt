 Department of Revenue 
 Chapter 150 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 315 PERSONAL INCOME TAX CREDITS 
 150-315-0110 
 Scholarship Tax Credit 
 
 The form required by this section should not be attached to the tax return, but must be kept with the taxpayer’s records. Upon audit or examination, the information must be made available to the department to verify any credit claimed under this section.  [ED. NOTE: Forms referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 305.100 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 315.237
