 Board of Examiners for Engineering and Land Surveying 
 Chapter 820 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 DEFINITIONS 
 820-005-0057 
 NCEES Record 
 
 An NCEES Record is a verified compilation of information, for which NCEES is the repository, that an applicant may submit to a state licensing board as part of the licensure application process. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, ORS 672.107 & ORS 672.255 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 672.002 - 672.325 
 
 History:
