 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 LANDS: DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE LANDS 
 635-008-0197 
 Woodburn Ponds (Marion County)
 
 
 In addition to the provisions in OAR 635-008-0200, it is unlawful at Woodburn Ponds to: 

 (1) Discharge any firearm. 

 (2) Operate any motor-propelled craft, except craft propelled by electric motors. 

 (3) Use the area for any purpose between one hour after sunset and one hour before sunrise. 

 (4) Trapping is prohibited. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 496.012 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.138, 496.146 & 496.012
