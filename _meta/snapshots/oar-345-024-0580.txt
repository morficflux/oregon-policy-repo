 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 SPECIFIC STANDARDS FOR SITING FACILITIES 
 345-024-0580 
 Monetary Offset Rate
 
 
 The monetary offset rate is $6.40 per ton of carbon dioxide emissions. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 & ORS 469.503 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.503
