 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 COMMERCIAL SHELLFISH AND MARINE INVERTEBRATE FISHERIES 
 635-005-0387 
 Catch Limits
 
 
 (1) In Netarts Bay, the commercial landing cap for cockles harvested by the intertidal fishery is 22,000 pounds. 

 (2) In Tillamook Bay, the commercial landing cap for cockles harvested by the intertidal fishery is 90,000 pounds. 

 (3) When the commercial cockle landing cap specified in sections 1 or 2 of this rule is reached, the intertidal cockle fishery will close in that bay for the remainder of the calendar year. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.036, 506.119 & 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109 & 506.129
