Oregon Health Authority

Public Health Division - Chapter 333

Division 52
OREGON FARM DIRECT NUTRITION PROGRAM

333-052-0110
Monitoring

(1) The Oregon Health Authority (Authority) must monitor farmers for compliance with applicable laws and rules, including on-site investigation of randomly selected farmers.

(2) The Authority may conduct covert compliance buys of Farm Direct-authorized farmers for compliance with the Authority rules and regulations.

 

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 413.500, ORS 411.813, 7 CFR 248 & 7 CFR 249
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 413.500, ORS 411.813, 7 CFR 248 & 7 CFR 249
