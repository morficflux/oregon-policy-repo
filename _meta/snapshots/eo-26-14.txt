     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 26-14


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     OLIVE BUTTE FIRE IN GRANT COUNTY



     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

     The fire known as the “Olive Butte Fire” is burning in Grant County.

     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Olive Butte Fire
     are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
     protection was requested by the Grant County Fire Defense Board Chief and the
     Grant County Judge and the State Fire Marshal concurs with that request.

     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476.610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to the fire known as the Olive Butte Fire in Grant
     County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local firefighting
     personnel and equipment. Accordingly, | have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.


     These findings were made at 6:06 pm on July 11, 2026, with the Department ofthe
     State Fire Marshal, the Oregon State Fire Marshal Green Incident Management
     Team will be in unified command with Northwest Team 12 on July 12, 2026. The
     Oregon State Fire Marshal Green Incident Management Team will be briefed at
     12:00 p.m. on July 12, 2026, and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

         1. The Department ofthe State Fire Marshal shall mobilize fire resources
            statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense Board Chiefs for
            the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the Emergency
            Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources responding to
            the Olive Butte Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire Marshal.

        2. This conflagration is declared only for the Olive Butte Fire threatening
            structures in Grant County.


        3. This order was made by verbal proclamation at 6:06 p.m. on the 11" day of
            July 2026 and signed this 12" day of July 2026, in Salem, Oregon.
