 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 440 FEES 
 331-440-0000 
 Fees
 
 
 (1) Applicants and licensees are subject to the provisions of OAR 331-010-0010 and 331-010-0020 regarding the payment of fees, penalties and charges. 

 (2) Fees established by the Office: 

 (a) Application: 

 (A) License: $350. 

 (B) License by reciprocity: $450. 

 (C) Temporary license: $50 

 (b) Examination: 

 (A) Oregon laws & rules: $50. 

 (B) Written: $350. 

 (C) Practical: $650. 

 (c) Original issuance: 

 (A) License: $350 

 (B) Temporary license: $50 

 (d) Renewal: 

 (A) License: $350 

 (B) Temporary license: $50 

 (e) Delinquency fee: $40 for each year in an inactive status, for up to three years. 

 (f) Replacement of license, including name change: $25. 

 (g) Duplicate license document: $25 per copy with maximum of three. 

 (h) Affidavit of licensure: $50. 

 (i) An additional $25 Administrative Processing fee will be assessed if a NSF or non-negotiable instrument is received for payment of fees, penalties and charges. Refer to OAR 331-010-0010. 

 (3) Individuals who renew online will receive a $150 discount. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 676.592, ORS 676.586, ORS 680.515, ORS 676.615, ORS 680.527 & ORS 680.565 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 676.592, ORS 680.515, ORS 680.527 & ORS 680.565
