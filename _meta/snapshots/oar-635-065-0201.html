Department of Fish and Wildlife

Chapter 635

Division 65
GAME MAMMAL GENERAL SEASONS AND REGULATIONS

635-065-0201
Elk Seasons

(1) A person hunting bull elk during either the first or second period of the general bull season in the open area listed under Rocky Mountain elk seasons in the Big Game Regulations shall have on their person a corresponding Rocky Mountain elk any legal weapon tag.

(2) A person hunting bull elk during general bull season in the open areas listed under the Coast elk seasons in the Big Game Regulations shall have on their person a corresponding Coast elk any legal weapon tag.

(3) A person hunting bull elk during the general bull elk season in the open area listed under the West Cascade elk season in the Big Game Regulations shall have on their person a General West Cascade elk any legal weapon tag.

(4) A person hunting antlerless elk during the general antlerless elk season in an open area listed under the General Season Antlerless Elk Damage Tag shall have on their person a General Season Antlerless Elk Damage Tag.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162 & ORS 496.012
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 496.138, 496.146, 496.162 & ORS 496.012
