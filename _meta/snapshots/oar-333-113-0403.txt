 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 113 RADIATION SAFETY REQUIREMENTS FOR WIRELINE SERVICE OPERATIONS AND SUB-SURFACE TRACER STUDIES 
 333-113-0403 
 Radiation Surveys and Records: Radioactive Contamination Control 
 
 (1) If the licensee detects evidence that a sealed source has ruptured or licensed materials have caused contamination, the licensee must initiate immediately the emergency procedures required by 333-113-0205.  (2) If contamination results from the use of licensed material in well logging, the licensee must decontaminate all work areas, equipment, and unrestricted areas.  (3) During efforts to recover a sealed source lodged in the well, the licensee must continuously monitor, with an appropriate radiation detection instrument or a logging tool with a radiation detector, the circulating fluids from the well, if any, to check for contamination resulting from damage to the sealed source. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 453.635 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 453.605 - 453.807
