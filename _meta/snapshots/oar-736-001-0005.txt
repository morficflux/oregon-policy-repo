 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 736-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Oregon Parks and Recreation Commission hereby adopts the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act as amended and effective October 3, 2001.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or State Parks & Recreation Department.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.124 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341(1)
