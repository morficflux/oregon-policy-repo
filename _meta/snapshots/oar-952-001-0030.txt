 Oregon Utility Notification Center 
 Chapter 952 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 Oregon Utility Notification Center 
 952-001-0030 
 Permits to Notify Excavator of Requirements of Law 
 
 Any entity authorized to issue permits for construction which requires excavation must include on such permits the language set out in OAR 952-001-0020. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 757.552 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 757.552
