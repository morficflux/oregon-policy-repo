 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 47 PRIVATE HUNTING PRESERVE RULES 
 635-047-0030 
 Length of Season 
 
 The department shall determine the length of the season during which the hunting preserve shall operate, except that the shooting season on any preserve shall not be open between April 1 and July 31. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.570, 497.102, 497.156 & 497.248 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.012, 496.138, 496.570, 497.102, 497.156 & .248
