 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 36 WATER UTILITIES 
 860-036-2220 
 Application for Waiver of Requirements under OAR 860-036-2210 
 
 The Commission will not waive the requirements of OAR 860-036-2210 for any rate-regulated water utility transactions exceeding 0.1 percent of the previous calendar year’s Oregon utility operating revenues unless the transaction can be demonstrated in advance to be fair and reasonable and not contrary to the public interest. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 & 756 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040, 757.015 & 757.495 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PUC 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-17
