 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 CANNABIS OFFENSE SUBCATEGORIES 
 213-021-0002 
 Crime Category 9 
 
 (1) ORS 475B.359 – ARSON INCIDENT TO THE MANUFACTURE OF A CANNABINOID EXTRACT I – (A): If offense did not represent a threat of serious physical injury (CC 10) and the economic loss is greater than $50,000; otherwise CC 8 or 7. 

 (2) ORS 475B.367 – CAUSING ANOTHER PERSON TO INGEST MARIJUANA – (A): As specified in ORS 475B.367. 

 (3) ORS 475B.371 – ADMINISTRATION TO ANOTHER PERSON UNDER 18 YEARS OF AGE – (A). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667, ORS 475B.367 & ORS 475B.371 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667 & SB 302 (2017) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CJC 3-2018, adopt filed 08/06/2018, effective 08/06/2018
