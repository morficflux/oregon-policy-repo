 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 750 PLUMBING CODES 
 918-750-0100 
 Scope of the Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code 
 
 (1) The Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code is applicable and uniform throughout the state and in all municipalities.  (2) Unless a local amendment request is approved by the division under ORS 455.040, a municipality may not enact or enforce any ordinance, rule or regulation in conflict with the Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code.  (3) The Oregon Plumbing Specialty Code applies to all plumbing installations as addressed in ORS 447.020 and ORS chapter 455 including one- and two-family dwellings of three stories or less within the scope of the Oregon Residential Specialty Code.  [Publications: Publications referenced are available for review at the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 447.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 447.020
