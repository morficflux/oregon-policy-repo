 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Blueberry Commission - Chapter 670 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 REPORTING AND PAYMENT OF ASSESSMENTS DUE THE OREGON BLUEBERRY COMMISSION 
 670-010-0025 
 Effective Date of Assessment 
 
 The assessment to be withheld and paid by a producer, first purchaser or handler, as required by ORS Chapter 576 and subsection (1) of Section 2, or reports required under Section 3, applies to and covers any sales, purchases or delivery on or after June 1, 1986, of blueberries grown in Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 576 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 576.325 & 576.335 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OBC 1-1986, f. 5-28-86, ef. 6-1-86
