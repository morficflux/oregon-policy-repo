 Employment Department 
 Chapter 471 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 UNEMPLOYMENT INSURANCE TAXES 
 471-031-0065 
 Employer Elections to Cover Multi-State Workers 
 
 (1) Employer elections to cover Multi-State workers shall be in accordance with the procedures, terms, and requirements of the Interstate Reciprocal Coverage Agreement.  (2) The Manager of Tax is authorized to approve or disapprove reciprocal coverage elections. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 657 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 657.035, 657.425 & 657.755 - 657.775
