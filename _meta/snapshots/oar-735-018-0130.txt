 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 ACCEPTANCE AND VERIFICATION OF INFORMATION SUBMITTED BY ELECTRONIC MEANS 
 735-018-0130 
 Authorization to Provide Name and Address to the Director of Veterans’ Affairs 
 
 An authorization to provide the name, residence or mailing address, and electronic mail address of a member or veteran of a uniformed service to the Director of Veterans’ Affairs as described in OAR 735-010-0250 may be submitted to DMV by means of an electronic transaction through DMV’s website. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.619, 406.073, 802.010, 802.012 & 803.460 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 406.073, 802.012, 802.560, 803.200, 803.220, 803.360, 803.370, 803.450, 803.460, 807.420 & 807.560
