 Oregon Utility Notification Center 
 Chapter 952 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 Oregon Utility Notification Center 
 952-001-0001 
 Adoption of Model Rules 
 
 The Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act, which became effective January 1, 2012, are by this reference adopted as rules of administrative procedure of the Board of Directors for the Oregon Utility Notification Center and are controlling except as otherwise required by statute or rule. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 757.552 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341 & 757.552 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OUNC 2-2013, f. & cert. ef. 11-14-13
