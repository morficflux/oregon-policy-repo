 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 162 SNOWMOBILES INSTRUCTORS/OPERATORS 
 735-162-0000 
 Qualifications 
 
 Any person who does not have an operator’s license issued under ORS Chapter 807 may be issued a certificate to operate a snowmobile if they have taken a Snowmobile Safety Education Course that has been approved by the Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division of the Department of Transportation. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 802.010, 821.150, 821.160 & 821.190 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 821.160
