 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 13 FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE 
 845-013-0110 
 Wholesale-Retail Relations; Prohibited Conduct 
 
 (1) No manufacturer or wholesaler may buy food, beverages or anything of value on a retailer licensee’s premises for customers who are not his/her personal acquaintances.  (2) No manufacturer or wholesaler may provide or offer assistance through a group of trade association of breweries, wineries, distilleries or wholesalers to a person to obtain a retail license. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 471 & 472, 471.030, 471.730(1) & (5), 472.030, 472.060(1) & (2)(d) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 471 & including 471.398
