 Oregon Housing and Community Services Department 
 Chapter 813 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 360 HOUSING CHOICE LANDLORD GUARANTEE PROGRAM (HCLGP) 
 813-360-0045 
 Reporting and Recordkeeping 
 
 (1) OHCS will retain and keep accessible all HCLGP records for such time as required by applicable law and state records retention requirements. 

 (2) OHCS will maintain reports, data and financial information of HCLGP administration in accordance with OHCS policy. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 456.555 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 456.375 to 456.390 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 OHCS 44-2019, adopt filed 12/31/2019, effective 12/31/2019
