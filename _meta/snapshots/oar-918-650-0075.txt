 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Building Codes Division - Chapter 918 
 
 
 
 
 Division 650 RECREATION PARKS AND ORGANIZATIONAL CAMPS 
 918-650-0075 
 Existing Parks 
 
 (1) Parks or camps existing prior to September 18, 1987 may have their existing use continued if the use was legal at the time of construction, provided that this continued use is not a threat to life, health, property, and general welfare of the public and is maintained in a safe and healthful condition.  (2) Any alteration to a recreation park or organizational camp must comply with the requirements of ORS Chapter 446 and these rules for new parks or camps. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 455.020, 455.110 & 455.680 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 455.680
