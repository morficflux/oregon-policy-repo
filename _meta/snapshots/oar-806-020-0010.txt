 State Board of Architect Examiners 
 Chapter 806 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 RULES OF PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT 
 806-020-0010 
 Truthful Statements to the Board
 
 
 A registered architect or architectural firm shall be truthful in statements or written responses to the Board or its representatives on matters pertaining to professional conduct. The signature of an architect or architectural firm in all matters relating to Professional Conduct certifies to the best of the architect or architectural firm’s knowledge, information, and/or belief that the information furnished is true and that no information has been withheld. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310, 671.090 & 671.125 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.010 – 671.220
