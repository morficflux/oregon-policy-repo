Board of Medical Imaging

Chapter 337

Division 1
PROCEDURAL RULES

337-001-0005
Model Rules of Practice

Pursuant to ORS 183.341, the Oregon Board of Medical Imaging adopts the Attorney General’s Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure in effect on January 1, 2023. These rules are controlling except as otherwise required by statute or rule.

[ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Board of Radiologic Technology.]

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 688.555
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 183.341
