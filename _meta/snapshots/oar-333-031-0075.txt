 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 31 OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE OF RECREATION PARKS 
 333-031-0075 
 Fees
 
 
 (1) Every applicant for a Recreation Park license shall pay a base fee of $100 plus $3 for each space from 1-50, $2 for each space from 51-100, and $1 for each space above 100 unless another fee has been specified by the local public health authority. 

 (2) Every applicant for a Picnic Park license shall pay a fee of $100 unless another fee has been specified by the local public health authority. 

 (3) All licenses under ORS 446.310 to 446.350 terminate and are renewable on December 31 of each year. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 446.321 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 446.320
