 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 INSURANCE LICENSING 
 836-071-0146 
 Individual Insurance Producer License Expiration Date 
 
 (1) A license issued to an individual insurance producer expires biennially in the month of the individual’s birthday anniversary.  (2) For the purpose of making the transition to renewal according to birth date month as provided in this rule, a license of an individual insurance producer that would have expired on or after the effective date of this rule according to ORS 744.072 expires instead in the birth date month next following the former expiration date. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 & 744.072 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 744.072 
 
 History:
