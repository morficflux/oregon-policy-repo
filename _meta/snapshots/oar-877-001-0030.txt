 Board of Licensed Social Workers 
 Chapter 877 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES AND DEFINITIONS 
 877-001-0030 
 Confidentiality and Inadmissibility of Mediation Communications 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 36.224, the Oregon Board of Licensed Social Workers adopts by reference OAR 137-005-0052, Confidentiality and Inadmissibility of Mediation Communications, and OAR 137-005-0054, Confidentiality and Inadmissibility of Workplace Interpersonal Dispute Mediation Communications, as promulgated by the Attorney General. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.600 & 36.224 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.600 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 BLSW 4-2017, f. & cert. ef. 7-25-17
