 Department of Environmental Quality 
 Chapter 340 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 230 INCINERATOR REGULATIONS 
 340-230-0230 
 Crematory Incinerators: Compliance 
 
 A crematory incinerator installed on or after March 13, 1993, must demonstrate within 180 days of startup compliance with OAR 340-230-0200(1) by:  (1) Conducting a source test for particulate matter emissions in accordance with OAR 340-212-0120 through 340-212-0140; or  (2) Submitting the results of testing performed on a crematory incinerator that the Department agrees is comparable to the incinerator in question. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 468.020 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 468A.025
