 Mortuary and Cemetery Board 
 Chapter 830 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 EXAMINATION AND LICENSURE 
 830-020-0050 
 Administrative Fees 
 
 (1) The Mortuary and Cemetery Board may charge a fee reasonably calculated to reimburse the agency for costs of providing and conveying copies of public records. A schedule of administrative fees will be approved as necessary by the Board and will be made available to the public.  (2) All fees and charges must be paid before public records may be made available for inspection or copies provided. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 692.160 & 692.320 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 692.160
