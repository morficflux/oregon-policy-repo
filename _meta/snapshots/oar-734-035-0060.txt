 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 RIGHT OF WAY AND REAL PROPERTY 
 734-035-0060 
 Determination that Property is Surplus 
 
 The following criteria may be used to determine real property is surplus: The property is no longer needed by the agency for public purposes. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184, 273 & 366 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 270.110 
 
 History:
