 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 603-001-0155 
 Organizational Structure 
 
 In order to comply with the provisions of ORS 183.330(1) requiring each agency to publish a description of its organization, a chart of the department’s organizational structure is set forth as Appendix 1.  [ED. NOTE: Appendices referenced are available from the agency.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 561, 192 & 603 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.440, 192.450, 561.040 & 561.260
