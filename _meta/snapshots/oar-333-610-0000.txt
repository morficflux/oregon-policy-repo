 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 610 DEMONSTRATION OF NEED FOR LONG-TERM CARE SERVICES 
 333-610-0000 
 General
 
 
 To demonstrate need for nursing facility beds, an applicant must satisfy the criteria specified in the Certificate of Need Application Instructions (OAR chapter 333, division 580). Where appropriate, responses to these instructions shall be based on the methodology of this division (OAR chapter 333, division 610). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431.120 & 442.315 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 431.120 & 442.315
