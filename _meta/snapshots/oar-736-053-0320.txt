 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 53 HERITAGE COMMISSION GRANT AND COORDINATION PROGRAMS OREGON MUSEUM GRANT PROGRAM 
 736-053-0320 
 Application Procedure 
 
 To apply for an event to be designated a statewide anniversary commemoration, an application must be submitted by the specified deadline. Applications must meet the format and criteria prescribed by the Commission. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 358.585 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 358.595 (2)(f) 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PRD 5-2019, adopt filed 11/25/2019, effective 11/25/2019
