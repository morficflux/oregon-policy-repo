 Department of Transportation 
 Delivery and Operations Division - Chapter 734 
 
 
 
 
 Division 35 RIGHT OF WAY AND REAL PROPERTY 
 734-035-0120 
 Determination of Most Advantageous Bid 
 
 (1) The decision of the Department on the question of the most advantageous bid shall be final and conclusive.  (2) The Department shall reserve the right to accept or reject any bid. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184 & 366 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 270.135 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 HWY 9-1990, f. & cert. ef. 5-24-90
