 Department of State Lands 
 Chapter 141 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 ONSHORE MINERALS PROSPECTING AND LEASING RULES 
 141-071-0495 
 Availability 
 
 State-owned lands are available for mineral leases if:  (1) A certified or proven discovery has been made under an exploration permit; or  (2) A nearby discovery warrants inclusion of the state land in a unit agreement or in competitive bidding as provided in these rules; or  (3) The lands have been formally classified by the State Land Board, or the Division, for minerals leasing. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 273 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 273.780 - 273.790 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 LB 44, f. & ef. 8-16-77
