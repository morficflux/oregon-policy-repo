 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 CLINICAL LABORATORIES 
 333-024-0260 
 Alpha-Fetoprotein Testing and Other Serum and Amniotic Fluid Based Markers for Congenital/Genetic Defects: Purpose
 
 
 (1) OAR 333-024-0260 through 333-024-0265 shall apply to the assay of Maternal Serum Alpha-Fetoprotein (MSAFP), Amniotic Fluid Alpha-Fetoprotein (AFAFP), Serum Human Chorionic Gonadotropin, Unconjugated Estriol, Amniotic Fluid Acetylcholinesterase or other related markers for the purposes of congenital/genetic screening. 

 (2) All sections of Clinical Laboratory OAR 333-024-0005 through 333-024-0055 shall apply. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 438.040 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 438.040
