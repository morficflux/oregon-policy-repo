 Psychiatric Security Review Board 
 Chapter 859 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 400 SEX OFFENDER CLASSIFICATION/RELIEF FROM REGISTRATION/RECLASSIFCATION 
 859-400-0150 
 Motion Practice 
 
 Any party bringing a motion before the Panel shall submit five copies of the motion and memorandum of law to the Panel at least 10 days prior to the date of the hearing in which the motion will be heard. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 161.387(1) & ORS 163A.125 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 161.387(1) & ORS 163A.125 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PSRB 3-2018, adopt filed 11/28/2018, effective 12/01/2018
