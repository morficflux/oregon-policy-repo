 Board of Nursing 
 Chapter 851 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 62 STANDARDS FOR CERTIFICATION OF THE NURSING ASSISTANT AND MEDICATION AIDE 
 851-062-0080 
 Oregon CMA Certification 
 
 A CMA must have a current Oregon CMA certificate and be listed on the Oregon CNA Registry prior to performing medication aide duties. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 678.442 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 678.442
