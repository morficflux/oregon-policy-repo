 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS 
 808-002-0775 
 Piping 
 
 “Piping” as used in ORS 671.540(1)(m), (n) & (o) and OAR 808-002-0480 means water distribution pipe, of any type or composition, including connecting fittings, used in an irrigation system. Piping does not include valves, control wires, sprinkler heads, emitters, nozzles, controllers or other elements used in an irrigation system. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.310, 671.670 & 2009 OL Ch. 483 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.520 & 671.540 
 
 History:
