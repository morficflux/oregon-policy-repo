 Department of Administrative Services 
 Chapter 125 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 700 INTERNAL AUDITING 
 125-700-0010 
 Purpose 
 
 The Oregon Department of Administrative Services is responsible for adopting rules setting standards and policies for internal audit functions within state government under authority provided in ORS 184.360(3). The rules include, but are not limited to:  (1) Standards for internal audits that are consistent with and incorporate commonly recognized industry standards and practices; and  (2) Policies and procedures that ensure the integrity of the internal audit process. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.360 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 184.360(3)
