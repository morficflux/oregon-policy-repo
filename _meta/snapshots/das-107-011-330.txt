 Vending Services in State Office Buildings             ______________________________________________________________
 and Grounds Controlled by the Department               Berri Leslie, DAS Director

PURPOSE

To provide clear policies relating to vending services and food carts in state buildings and grounds which are
owned, leased or controlled by the Department of Administrative Services (DAS), according to ORS 346.510 to
346.570 and OAR 125-080-0000.

The Business Enterprise Program (BEP) is administered by the Oregon Commission for the Blind (OCB) under
the Randolph-Sheppard Vending Stand Act, which covers federal properties, and ORS 346.510 to 346.570. This
state law provides that OCB shall operate vending facilities in or on any "public buildings or properties," defined
as any building, land or other real property, owned, leased or occupied by any department or agency of the
state of Oregon.

Each year, BEP conducts site surveys in governmental buildings or properties to determine the viability of
installing a food service or vending operation at the location to provide self-employment opportunities to
Oregonians who are legally blind and wish to run their own food concession businesses. This survey is sent to
agency buyers and procurement specialists through the OregonBuys network.

APPLICABILITY

Applies to vending facilities in state office buildings and grounds owned, leased or controlled by DAS.

DEFINITIONS

Food cart: a mobile food-dispensing facility also referred to as a food concession business, such as the food
cart deployed in Willson Park near the Capitol building.

Oregon Commission for the Blind (OCB): means the commission established under ORS 346.130 to 346.140.

Operator: a person who is blind, has seriously impaired vision or a vending facility manager responsible for the
day-to-day conduct of the vending facility operation under a contract with DAS under ORS 346.510 to 346.570.

Qualified rehabilitation facility: those organizations defined under ORS 279.835 to 279.855.

State office building premises under the jurisdiction of the department: any office building, land or other real
property owned, leased or occupied by DAS. It does not include single agency-leased facilities which are
approved but not being operated by DAS.

Vending machine: a manual or coin-operated machine or a similar device used for vending articles, including
machines or devices that accept electronic payment.

Vending facility:
   • Shelters, counters, shelving, display and wall cases, refrigerating apparatus and other appropriate
      auxiliary equipment that are necessary or customarily used for the vending of articles, including an
      established mix of healthy vending items approved by OCB and the agency, department or political
      subdivision charged with maintaining the public building or property where the vending facility is
      located;
   • Vending machines; or
   • Cafeterias, snack bars or food carts used for the dispensing of foodstuffs and beverages. This type of
      facility must comply with policy 107-011-331 and OAR 125-80-0005

GENERAL INFORMATION

(1)    For all available vending facility locations, OCB shall be given the first right of refusal on the contract
       based on the terms and conditions set by DAS, according to ORS 346.520 and 346.530. Qualified
       rehabilitation facilities shall have a second right of refusal on the contract according to ORS 279.835 to
       279.855. If more than one qualified rehabilitation facility is competing, the decision to award a contract
       is based on competitive bidding among them. Any vending facility or prospective locations not
       contracted with OCB or qualified rehabilitation facility may be selected by competitive bids.

(2)    No person, firm or state agency may place any vending facility on any state office building premises
       under the jurisdiction of DAS without specific authorization issued by DAS’ Enterprise Asset
       Management Division, Operations and Maintenance Unit. Contact the help desk at
       facilities.helpdesk@das.oregon.gov for information regarding placement of vending machines:

       Department of Administrative Services
       Enterprise Asset Management, Operations and Maintenance
       1240 Ferry Street SE
       Salem, OR 97301-4288
       Phone: 503-378-3664

(3)    DAS may determine fees for each class of vending facilities which will at least pay for the cost of
       space, utilities and administration. Such fee schedules may be changed by DAS as needed. DAS may
       permit such payments to be made quarterly.

(4)    Authorizations issued by DAS for placing vending facilities may be in the form of a contract which will
       specify terms and conditions governing the operation of vending facilities.

(5)    DAS reserves the right to reject any bids or proposals. Contract awards are based on appropriate
       qualification standards, contract terms and considerations offered to DAS by vendors.

(6)    All food cart vendors shall comply with all applicable laws of the state and ordinances of the local
       government jurisdictions pertaining to the vending of foods.
