 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 581-001-0110 
 Mediation Confidentiality
 
 
 Pursuant to ORS 36.224 and OAR 137-005-0050, the Oregon Department of Education adopts by reference OAR 137-005-0052 Mediation Confidentiality as promulgated by the Attorney general effective as of November 13, 2018. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 36.224 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 36.224, 36.228, 36.230 & 36.232 
 
 History:
