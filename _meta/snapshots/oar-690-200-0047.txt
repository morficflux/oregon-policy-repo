 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 200 WATER SUPPLY WELL CONSTRUCTION STANDARDS: INTRODUCTION, GENERAL STANDARDS, AND DEFINITIONS 
 690-200-0047 
 Unattended Wells 
 
 All wells, when unattended during construction, shall be covered to protect public health and safety. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
