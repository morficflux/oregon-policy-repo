 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 REPORTING REQUIREMENTS OF THE OREGON DEATH WITH DIGNITY ACT 
 333-009-0020 
 Record Review/Annual Report 
 
 (1) The Authority shall annually review records maintained pursuant to this Act.  (2) The Authority shall generate and make available to the public an annual statistical report of information collected under this Act. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 127.800 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 127.800 - 127.897
