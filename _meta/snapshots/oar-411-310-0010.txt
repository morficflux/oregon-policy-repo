 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 310 DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES COMMUNITY HOUSING 
 411-310-0010 
 Statement of Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to:  (1) Prescribe the operational procedures for the Developmental Disabilities Community Housing Program and the Community Housing Fund; and  (2) Implement and describe the acquisition, construction, rehabilitation, maintenance, and disposal of Community Housing established under the authority of ORS 427.330 to 427.345. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 & 410.070 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 427.330 - 427.345
