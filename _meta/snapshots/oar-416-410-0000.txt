 Oregon Youth Authority 
 Chapter 416 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 410 Close-custody Population 
 416-410-0000 
 Purpose
 
 
 The Oregon Youth Authority (OYA) establishes the following criteria for admission to close-custody facilities, including the designation of Public Safety Reserve (PSR) beds and county bed allocations. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 420A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 420.014 & 420.011
