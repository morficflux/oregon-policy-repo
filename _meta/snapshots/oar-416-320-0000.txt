 Oregon Youth Authority 
 Chapter 416 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 320 Arrest Orders and All Points Bulletins 
 416-320-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules describe the process OYA staff will use to issue and cancel arrest orders and All Points Bulletins. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 420A.025 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 420.905 - 420.915 
 
 History:
