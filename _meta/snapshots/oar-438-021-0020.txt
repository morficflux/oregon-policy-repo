 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Board - Chapter 438 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 BOARD MEETINGS 
 438-021-0020 
 Consultation of the Board 
 
 (1) The Chairperson shall consult with the Board on all matters requiring such consultation under ORS 656.001 to 656.990 during meetings as set forth in these rules.  (2) These consultations shall be made prior to any decision by the Chairperson. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.726(5) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.726(5) 
 
 History:
