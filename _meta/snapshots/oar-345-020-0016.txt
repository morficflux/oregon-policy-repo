 Department of Energy 
 Energy Facility Siting Council - Chapter 345 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 NOTICE OF INTENT 
 345-020-0016 
 Amendment of Notice of Intent
 
 
 (1) The applicant may amend the notice of intent (NOI). The applicant must submit to the Department an electronic version of the amended NOI in a non-copy-protected format acceptable to the Department. The applicant must provide a printed copy of all or part of the amended NOI to any person on the distribution list as directed by the Department. 

 (2) Submission of an amended NOI does not extend the expiration date of the NOI. The applicant, however, may petition the Council to extend the duration of the NOI as provided in OAR 345-020-0060. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 469.470 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 469.330
