 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 61 THIRD PARTY TESTING FOR CLASS C DRIVER LICENSE 
 735-061-0460 
 Customer Feedback Process 
 
 (1) At the conclusion of the testing process, a third party examiner must give an applicant a Class C Third Party Testing Feedback form (DMV form 735-7414), regardless of whether the applicant passed or failed the drive test. 

 (2) The Class C Third Party Testing Feedback form is designed to give the applicant necessary information to provide DMV with feedback regarding the experience of testing with a testing business. The form provides contact information for the Class C Third Party Testing Program at DMV including a mailing address, a fax number and a method to submit the form to DMV electronically. 

 (3) DMV may contact an applicant to get additional information or investigate a complaint raised by an applicant. 

 (4) If DMV requires a testing business to respond to a particular applicant’s complaint, the testing business must do so within 10 calendar days. 

 (5) DMV may compile data from the Class C Third Party Testing Feedback forms to evaluate the effectiveness of the Class C Third Party Testing Program. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616, 184.619, 802.010 & 802.600 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 802.600 
 
 History:
