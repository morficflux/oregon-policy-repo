 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 ARBITRATION BY THE AGENCY 
 808-008-0300 
 Arbitration in the Absence of a Party 
 
 (1) Except as provided in section (2) of this rule, an arbitration may proceed in the absence of a party who, after due notice, fails to appear. An award shall not be made solely on the default of a party. The arbitrator may require the attending party to submit such evidence as the arbitrator may require for the making of an award.  (2) Notwithstanding section (1) of this rule, an arbitrator may dismiss a claim without an evidentiary hearing if the party making the claim fails to appear after due notice and without good cause. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.310 - 183.500, 670.310 & 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183 & 671.703(3)
