 Secretary of State 
 Audits Division - Chapter 162 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 MINIMUM STANDARDS FOR AGREED-UPON PROCEDURES OF OREGON MUNICIPAL CORPORATIONS 
 162-040-0085 
 Other Financial or Statistical Information (OI) 
 
 The report may include such other financial or statistical information as desired by the municipal corporation, including financial statements or schedules relating specifically to programs funded wholly or partially by other governmental agencies. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 297.465 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 297.465
