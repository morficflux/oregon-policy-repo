 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 4 CLAIMS 
 808-004-0350 
 Procedure if Information on Statement of Claim is Incomplete 
 
 The agency may refuse to accept a claim if:  (1) The Statement of Claim does not meet the requirements of OAR 808-004-0340; and  (2) The claimant fails to provide the missing information in response to a written request from the agency for the information within 30 days. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 670.310 & 671.670 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671.703
