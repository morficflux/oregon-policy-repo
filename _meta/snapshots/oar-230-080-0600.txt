 Department of State Police 
 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 CONDUCT OF BOUT - BOXING 
 230-080-0600 
 Wiping Gloves
 
 
 Before a competitor resumes competition after having been knocked or having fallen or slipped to the floor, the referee shall wipe any accumulated resin from the competitor’s gloves with a damp towel or on the referee's shirt. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463.113 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.113
