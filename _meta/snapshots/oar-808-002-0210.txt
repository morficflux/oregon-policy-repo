 Landscape Contractors Board 
 Chapter 808 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 DEFINITIONS AND STANDARDS 
 808-002-0210 
 Claimant 
 
 "Claimant" means a person who files a claim against a landscape contracting business under ORS 671.695. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 671 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 671
