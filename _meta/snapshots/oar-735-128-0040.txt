 Department of Transportation 
 Driver and Motor Vehicle Services Division - Chapter 735 
 
 
 
 
 Division 128 DRIVER’S DUTIES 
 735-128-0040 
 Refueling of Vehicles 
 
 (1) Smoking in the vicinity of vehicles being refueled is prohibited.  (2) Refueling while motor is running or when within close proximity to any open fires or flame lights is prohibited.  (3) Refueling of Class “A” and “B” vehicles shall be done when vehicles are not occupied. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 184.616 & 820.030 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 820.030 & 820.070
