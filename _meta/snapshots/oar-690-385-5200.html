Water Resources Department

Chapter 690

Division 385
DISTRICT WATER RIGHT TRANSFERS

690-385-5200
District Permanent Transfer of Water Right for Nonuse Applications

(1) Each district permanent transfer of water right for nonuse application shall contain the standard information required by OAR 690-385-2000; and

(2) Include the following:

(a) A copy of the notice required under OAR 690-385-5100 mailed by the district to the user;

(b) A copy of any objections to the proposed transfer received by the district within the 30-day time period following the mailing of notice under OAR 690-385-5100; and

(c) A copy of findings, determinations and conclusions resulting from any hearing held by the district to consider unresolved objections received in response to the notice required under OAR 690-385-5100.

Statutory/Other Authority: ORS 536.025 & 536.027
Statutes/Other Implemented: ORS 540.574 & 540.580
