 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 55 OREGON CHILD SUPPORT PROGRAM 
 137-055-2320 
 Requirement for Services — Obligor Bankruptcy Situations
 
 
 (1) The administrator will have access to an attorney admitted to federal court practice to handle situations of obligor bankruptcy, or contract with suitable counsel so admitted. 

 (2) For the purposes of this rule, "suitable counsel" means any of the following: 

 (a) That portion of the Oregon Department of Justice (DOJ) designated to handle bankruptcy situations; or 

 (b) Any Oregon county district attorney's office with staff counsel admitted to federal court practice to handle situations of obligor bankruptcy; or 

 (c) Private counsel so admitted, provided that such private counsel complies with the administrative rules and procedures of the Oregon Child Support Program that apply to situations of obligor bankruptcy, and with applicable DOJ policies regarding representation. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 180.345 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 25.080
