 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 50 HISTORIC PRESERVATION OFFICER 
 736-050-0112 
 Requirement for Owner Expenditure
 
 
 (1) Under ORS 358.487(2)(a)(A), the 10 percent expenditure requirement may include the market value of donated materials, services, and professional labor. Nonprofessional volunteer labor may also be included and must be valued at the state minimum wage. 

 (2) An owner of specially assessed historic property must submit documentation to the Oregon SHPO to demonstrate that the 10 percent expenditure requirement is met, including the date, description, and service provider details (name, address, and contact information) for all materials, services, and labor , and any other information requested by the SHPO. The documentation must demonstrate compliance with the SHPO approved Preservation Plan . Furnishings and appliances do not count toward the expenditure commitment . 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 358.545 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 358.487
