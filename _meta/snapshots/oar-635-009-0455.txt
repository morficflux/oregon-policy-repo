 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 9 SALMON AND TROUT ENHANCEMENT PROGRAM AND RIPARIAN LANDS TAX INCENTIVE PROGRAM 
 635-009-0455 
 Cooperative Salmon Hatchery Program Ongoing Projects 
 
 (1) OAR 635-009-0420 through 635-009-0435 do not apply to cooperative projects existing as of December 31, 1996, as approved on or before that date.  (2) Any increases in releases for projects excluded under section (1) of this rule shall occur only in compliance with these rules (OAR 635-009-0400 through 635-009-0455). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138 & 506.119 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.146 & 506.124 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FWC 15-1997, f. & cert. ef. 3-10-97
