 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 INSURANCE LICENSING 
 836-071-0405 
 Statutory Purpose and Authority 
 
 (1) OAR 836-071-0405 to 836-071-0425 apply to an owner who issues, sells or offers for sale insurance coverage to occupants at a self-service storage facility.  (2) OAR 836-071-0405 to 836-071-0425 are adopted pursuant to the authority in section 8, chapter 280, Oregon Laws 2013 and ORS Chapter 744, for the purpose of implementing sections 2 to 8, Chapter 280, Oregon Laws 2013. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244; sect. 8, ch. 280 & OL 2013 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Sects. 2 to 8, ch. 280 & OL 2013 
 
 History:
