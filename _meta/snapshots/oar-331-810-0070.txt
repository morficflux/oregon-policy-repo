 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 810 CERTIFICATION 
 331-810-0070 
 Certified Sexual Offense Therapist Intern Supervision 
 
 A CSOTI must: 

 (1) Enter into a formal contract prescribed by the Board with a CCSOT that specifies: 

 (a) The duties of a CSOTI; 

 (b) The scope and focus of the supervision; and 

 (c) Frequency and durations of meetings between the supervisor and the CSOTI to review the certified sexual offense therapist intern’s professional performance. 

 (2) Notify the Office in writing within 10 business days of ending the supervision contract; 

 (3) Identify themselves as a CSOTI on all materials relating to the provision of sexual-abuse-specific treatment. The supervisor's name and designation “supervisor” must be included on these materials; 

 (4) Maintain supervision records for a minimum of 5 years after the last day of supervision. Upon request, you must make the records available to the Office. 

 (5) Obtain at least: 

 (a) Two hours of direct supervision per month. At least one hour must be individual supervision if the certificate holder has 45 or fewer hour in the month of direct clinical contact with clients; or 

 (b) Three hours of direct supervision per month. At least 90 minutes must be individual supervision if the certificate holder has more than 45 hours in the month of direct clinical contact with clients. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.365-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.365-675.410 & 2019 House Bill 2472
