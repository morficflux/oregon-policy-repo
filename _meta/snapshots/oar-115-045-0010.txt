 Employment Relations Board 
 Chapter 115 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 45 APPEALS, HEARINGS AND INVESTIGATIONS IN THE STATE SERVICE UNDER THE STATE PERSONNEL RELATIONS LAW 
 115-045-0010 
 Regular Employee Appeals from Suspension, Reduction in Pay, Demotion and Dismissal Actions 
 
 An appeal under ORS 240.560 must contain a detailed statement specifying:  (1) The action being appealed;  (2) The reasons why appellant believes the action was not in good faith for cause or was taken for political, religious or racial reasons, or because of sex, marital status or age; and  (3) The corrective action being requested. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 240.086(3) & 243.766(7) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 240
