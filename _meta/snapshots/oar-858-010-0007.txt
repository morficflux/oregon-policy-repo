 Mental Health Regulatory Agency 
 Oregon Board of Psychology - Chapter 858 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 858-010-0007 
 Notice of Proposed Rule
 
 
 Prior to the adoption, amendment, or repeal of a permanent rule, the Board of Psychology shall give notice of the proposed adoption, amendment, or repeal: 

 (1) In the Secretary of State's Bulletin pursuant to ORS 183.360; 

 (2) By mailing or emailing a copy of the notice to individuals on the Board's mailing list established pursuant to ORS 183.335(8); 

 (3) By mailing or emailing a copy of the notice to the following individuals, organizations, or publications: 

 (a) All licensees of the Board; 

 (b) Oregon Psychological Association; and 

 (c) All applicants for licensure. 

 (4) Prior to the adoption, amendment, or repeal of any rule of the Board relating to continuing education, the Board shall additionally mail a copy of the notice to the State Higher Education Coordinating Commission. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 675.010 - 675.150 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 675.110
