    EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-41

    INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT STATEWIDE
    IN LIGHT OF EXTREME FIRE DANGER FROM HOT, DRY, WINDY
    CONDITIONS


    Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:

    The State of Oregon is currently experiencing multiple large fires and heightened
    danger of new significant fires due to hot, dry conditions and extremely high winds.

    The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the multiple fires
    currently burning and anticipated new fires are currently and are expected to
    continue to be beyond local capabilities. In addition, because of multiple fires
    burning simultaneously, local areas may experience a significant reduction in
    available firefighting resources. In this situation, statewide coordination is
    necessary to ensure efficient deployment of available firefighting resources. The
    State Fire Marshal concurs with this assessment.

    I have previously issued a number of incident-specific executive orders invoking
    the emergency conflagration act, and will continue to do so, as necessary. In
    Executive Order 20-35, I also declared an emergency pursuant to ORS 401.165 et
    seq. due to imminent threat of wildfire, and that declaration authorizes the
    mobilization of state resources, among other actions, to fight fire. However, the
    wildfire threat facing Oregon is unprecedented. Many of Oregon’s critical
    firefighting resources are local entities, the threat of fire is rapidly escalating,
    statewide coordination of all resources is necessary at this time, and officials need
    additional flexibility to deploy and redeploy resources, beyond areas with an active
    fire.


    Thus, in accordance with the Emergency Conflagration Act, ORS 476.510-
    476.610, I have determined that a threat to life, safety, and property exists due to
    the conditions described above, and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities
    of local firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the
    Emergency Conflagration Act statewide.

    These findings were made at 4:00 p.m. on September 8, 2020 with Office of State
    Fire Marshal and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.

    NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:

    1.      The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
            fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
