 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 COMMERCIAL SHELLFISH AND MARINE INVERTEBRATE FISHERIES 
 635-005-0750 
 Revocation and Refusal to Issue Weathervane Scallop Permits 
 
 The Commercial Fishery Permit Board may revoke and refuse subsequent issuance of a Weathervane Scallop Permit pursuant to ORS 508.855. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 506.036, 506.109, 506.119 & 506.129 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 506.109, 506.129 & 508.867 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 DFW 76-2012, f. 6-28-12, cert. ef. 7-1-12
