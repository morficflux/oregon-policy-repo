 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 SPECIAL EDUCATION 
 581-015-2240 
 Requirement for Least Restrictive Environment 
 
 School districts must ensure that:  (1) To the maximum extent appropriate, children with disabilities, including children in public or private institutions or other care facilities, are educated with children who do not have a disability and  (2) Special classes, separate schooling or other removal of children with disabilities from the regular educational environment occurs only if the nature or severity of the disability is such that education in regular classes with the use of supplementary aids and services cannot be achieved satisfactorily. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 343.041, 343.045 & 343.055 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 343.045, 343.155 & 34 CFR 300.114
