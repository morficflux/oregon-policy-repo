 Board of Chiropractic Examiners 
 Chapter 811 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 RULES OF PROCEDURE 
 811-001-0005 
 Model Rules of Procedure
 
 
 Pursuant to the provisions of ORS 183.341, the Board of Chiropractic Examiners adopts the Attorney General's Uniform and Model Rules of Procedure under the Administrative Procedures Act July 2014, these rules shall be controlling except as otherwise required by statute or rule. 

 [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General’s Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or Board of Chiropractic Examiners.] 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341
