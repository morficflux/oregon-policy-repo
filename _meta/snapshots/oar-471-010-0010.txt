 Employment Department 
 Chapter 471 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES GENERAL AND INTERNAL RULES 
 471-010-0010 
 Rules Procedure
 
 
 Prior to the adoption, amendment, or repeal of any permanent rule, the Employment Department shall give notice of the proposed adoption, amendment, or repeal: 

 (1) In the Secretary of State's Bulletin referred to in ORS 183.360 at least twenty-one (21) days prior to the effective date. 

 (2) By providing a copy of the notice to persons on the Employment Department’s mailing list established pursuant to ORS 183.335(7) at least twenty-eight (28) days prior to the effective date. 

 (3) By providing a copy of the notice to the legislators specified in ORS 183.335(15) of this section at least 49 days before the effective date. 

 (4) By providing a copy of the notice to news agencies, to a newspaper of statewide circulation, and other members of the media who have requested notification. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.335, 657.260, 657.265 - 657.270, 657.335, 657.610 & Ch. 729 & OL 1993 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.335 & 183.360
