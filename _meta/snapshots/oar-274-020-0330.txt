 Department of Veterans' Affairs 
 Chapter 274 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 VETERANS’ LOANS 
 274-020-0330 
 Legal Description of Property Offered as Security 
 
 (1) Property offered as security must have an adequate legal description from which the boundaries of the property may be located.  (2) The Director may require a survey to ascertain the boundary lines and location of all permanent improvements on the property. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 406 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 407.225 
 
 History:
