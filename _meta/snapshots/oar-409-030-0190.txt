 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Policy and Analytics - Chapter 409 
 
 
 
 
 Division 30 HEALTH PROFESSION STUDENT CLINICAL TRAINING ADMINISTRATIVE REQUIREMENTS 
 409-030-0190 
 Screening Standards
 
 
 Table 1 in OAR 409-030-0150 provides detailed information related to required screenings for students’ clinical placements. Required screenings consist of: 
(1) Tuberculosis (OAR 409-030-0200); 
(2) Substance abuse or misuse (OAR 409-030-0210); and 
(3) State and nationwide criminal background check (OAR 409-030-0220).
