REGIONALLY SIGNIFICANT INDUSTRIAL AREAS

966-100-0500
Central Douglas Industrial Area

(1) The Central Douglas Industrial Area was approved by the ERRC as a designated RSIA on September 28, 2012.

(2) The Central Douglas Industrial Area located in Douglas County, consists of 328.12 acres and is comprised of the following:
