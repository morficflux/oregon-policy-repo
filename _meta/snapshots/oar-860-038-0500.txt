 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 38 DIRECT ACCESS REGULATION 
 860-038-0500 
 Code of Conduct Purpose 
 
 The Code of Conduct rules (OAR 860-038-0500 through 860-038-0640) govern the interactions and transactions among the electric company, its Oregon affiliates, and its competitive operations. The Code of Conduct is designed to protect against market abuses and anti-competitive practices by electric companies in the Oregon retail electricity markets. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756 & 757 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040 & 757.600 - 757.667
