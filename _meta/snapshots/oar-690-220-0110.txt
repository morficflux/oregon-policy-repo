 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 220 ABANDONMENT OF WATER SUPPLY WELLS 
 690-220-0110 
 Removal of Well Casing During Abandonment 
 
 If the casing of a water supply well is removed during abandonment, the well shall be plugged and sealed in accordance with OAR 690-220-0030 through 690-220-0050 and shall be filled with sealing materials as the casing is removed. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 536, 537 & 540
