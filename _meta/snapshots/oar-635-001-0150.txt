 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 ADMINISTRATION 
 635-001-0150 
 Purpose 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to establish the process and requirements for hunters to be awarded controlled hunt preference points (preference points) as an alternative to cash for providing information to the Oregon State Police (OSP) and Oregon Hunters Association (OHA) “Turn-In-Poachers” (TIP) program, pursuant to ORS 497.112. The program is intended to encourage hunters to report wildlife violations they witness involving antelope, bear, cougar, deer, elk, moose, mountain goat, mountain sheep or wolf. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496.138, 496.146, ORS 496.012, 496.162 & 497.112 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496.138, 496.146, ORS 496.012, 496.162 & 497.112 
 
 History:
