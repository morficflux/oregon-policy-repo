 Oregon Liquor and Cannabis Commission 
 Chapter 845 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 25 RECREATIONAL MARIJUANA 
 845-025-5580 
 Marijuana Worker Renewal Requirements
 
 
 (1) An individual must renew his or her marijuana worker permit every five years by submitting a renewal application, on a form prescribed by the Commission and the applicable fee specified in OAR 845-025-1060. 

 (2) Renewal applications will be reviewed in accordance with OAR 845-025-5520 and 845-025-5540. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.269 & 475C.273 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.269 & 475C.273
