 Board of Nursing 
 Chapter 851 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 2 AGENCY FEES 
 851-002-0050 
 Certified Medication Aide Schedule of Fees 
 
 
 (1) Certification by Examination — $60. 

 (2) Certification for RN or LPN — $20. 

 (3) Renewal — $15. 

 (4) Reinstatement — $30. 

 (5) Re-Examination — $25. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 678.150 & ORS 678.410 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 678.410
