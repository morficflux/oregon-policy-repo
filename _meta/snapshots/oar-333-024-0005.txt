 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 CLINICAL LABORATORIES 
 333-024-0005 
 Purpose
 
 
 These rules (OAR 333-024-0005 through 333-024-0055 and 333-024-0260 and 333-024-0265) are for the purpose of carrying out ORS chapter 438 to ensure the quality of medical laboratory work in order to protect the health and welfare of the people of the State of Oregon by establishing a regulatory program for clinical laboratories. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 438.450 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 438.450
