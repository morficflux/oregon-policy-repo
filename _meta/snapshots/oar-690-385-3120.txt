 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 385 DISTRICT WATER RIGHT TRANSFERS 
 690-385-3120 
 Temporary Change in Type of Use of a Water Right to Store Water 
 
 A change in the type of use of a water right to store water (reservoir right) may be made provided:  (1) The district receives the written consent of the operator of the reservoir; and  (2) The district is a lessor or co-lessor in a proposed instream lease pursuant to OAR 690-077-0077. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.025 & 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 540.570 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 9-2004, f. & cert. ef. 11-16-04
