 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Workers' Compensation Division - Chapter 436 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 WORKERS’ COMPENSATION BENEFITS OFFSET 
 436-100-0002 
 Purpose of Rules 
 
 The purpose of these rules is to establish requirements and procedures for offsetting permanent total disability benefits against social security disability benefits. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 656.209 & 656.727 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 656.209
