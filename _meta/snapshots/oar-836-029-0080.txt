 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 29 CAPTIVE INSURERS 
 836-029-0080 
 Conflict of Interest 
 
 (1) Each captive insurer licensed in Oregon shall adopt a conflict of interest statement for officers, directors and key employees. The statement shall disclose that the individual has no outside commitments, personal or otherwise, that would divert the person from the duty to further the interests of the captive insurer the person represents but this shall not preclude a person from being a director or officer in more than one insurance captive insurer.  (2) Each officer, director and key employee shall file a yearly disclosure with the board of directors. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244, 2012 OL Ch. 84 & Sec. 4 (Enrolled SB 1547) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2012 OL Ch. 84 & Sec. 2-22 (Enrolled SB 1547)
