 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 505 HOSPITAL ORGANIZATION AND MANAGEMENT 
 333-505-0007 
 Health Care Practitioner Credentialing 
 
 Each hospital shall comply with the health care practitioner and telemedicine provider credentialing requirements in accordance with OAR chapter 409, division 045. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 441.056 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 441.056 & ORS 441.222
