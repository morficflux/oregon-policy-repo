 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Finance and Securities Regulation - Chapter 441 
 
 
 
 
 Division 810 GENERAL 
 441-810-0140 
 Civil Penalties 
 
 Civil penalties shall be $400 for the first offense and $1,000 for each subsequent offense. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 697 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 697.095
