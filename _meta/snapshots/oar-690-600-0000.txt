 Water Resources Department 
 Chapter 690 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 600 WATER CONSERVATION, REUSE AND STORAGE GRANT PROGRAM 
 690-600-0000 
 Purpose 
 
 These rules establish procedures for the Oregon Water Resources Department in accepting applications and considering proposals for funding under the provisions of Senate Bill 1069 (Chapter 13, 2008 Laws). The Water Conservation, Reuse, and Storage Grant Program includes funding for project planning studies performed to evaluate the feasibility of developing a water conservation, reuse, or storage project. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 536.027 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2008 OL Ch. 13 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WRD 1-2008, f. 6-4-08, cert. ef. 6-6-08
