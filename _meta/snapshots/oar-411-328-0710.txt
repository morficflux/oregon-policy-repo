 Department of Human Services 
 Aging and People with Disabilities and Developmental Disabilities - Chapter 411 
 
 
 
 
 Division 328 SUPPORTED LIVING PROGRAMS FOR ADULTS WITH INTELLECTUAL OR DEVELOPMENTAL DISABILITIES 
 411-328-0710 
 Vehicles and Drivers 
 
 (1) A provider that owns or operates a vehicle that transports individuals must:  (a) Maintain the vehicle in safe operating condition;  (b) Comply with the laws of the Department of Motor Vehicles;  (c) Maintain insurance coverage on the vehicle and all authorized drivers; and  (d) Carry a first aid kit in the vehicle.  (2) A driver operating a vehicle to transport individuals must meet all applicable requirements of the Department of Motor Vehicles. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 409.050 & 430.662 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 430.610, 430.630 & 430.670
