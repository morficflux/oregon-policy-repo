 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 INSURANCE LICENSING 
 836-071-0185 
 Qualification of Agents Selling Variable Life Insurance, Including Variable Annuities
 
 
 A person shall not solicit, place or procure variable life insurance contracts or policies, including variable annuities, unless the person is licensed as authorized by OAR 441-175-0120 or by OAR 441-175-0130. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 744.062
