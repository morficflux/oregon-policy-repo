 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 39 NET METERING RULES 
 860-039-0075 
 Public Utility Not to Limit Net Metering Systems 
 
 A public utility will not limit the cumulative generating capacity of net metering systems in any manner except as expressly ordered by the Commission under ORS 757.300(6). 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756 & 757 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 756.040 & 757.300 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PUC 8-2007, f. & cert. ef. 7-27-07
