 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 MEDICAL MARIJUANA 
 333-008-2170 
 General Requirements for Medical Marijuana Processing Sites and Dispensaries: Inspections
 
 
 (1) The Authority must conduct a routine inspection of every registrant at least every year. 

 (2) The Authority may conduct a complaint inspection at any time following the receipt of a complaint that alleges a registrant or registrant representative is in violation of ORS 475C.815, ORS 475C.815 to 475C.827, 475C.833 to 475C.843, 475C.544, 475C.604, 475C.612, OAR chapter 333, division 7 or these rules. 

 (3) The Authority may conduct an inspection at any time if it believes, for any reason, that a registrant or registrant representative is in violation of ORS 475C.815 to 475C.827, 475C.833 to 475C.843, 475C.544, 475C.604, 475C.612, OAR chapter 333, division 7 or these rules. 

 (4) The Authority may inspect the following to ensure compliance with ORS 475C.815 to 475C.827, 475C.833 to 475C.843, 475C.544, 475C.604, 475C.612, OAR chapter 333, division 7 or these rules: 

 (a) The premises of a proposed marijuana processing site or dispensary, or registered marijuana processing site or dispensary; and 

 (b) The records of a registered marijuana processing site or dispensary. 

 (5) Registrant representatives must cooperate with the Authority during an inspection. 

 (6) If an individual at a registered dispensary or processing site fails to permit the Authority to conduct an inspection or if the Authority requires access to a dispensary or processing site and cannot obtain permission the Authority may seek an administrative warrant authorizing the inspection pursuant to ORS 431A.010. 

 (7) The Authority may purchase, possess or seize a marijuana item as necessary for the Authority to determine compliance with ORS 475C.815 to 475C.827, 475C.833 to 475C.843, 475C.544, 475C.604, 475C.612, OAR chapter 333, division 7 or these rules. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 431A.010, 475C.815, 475C.833 & 475C.919 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.815 & 475C.833
