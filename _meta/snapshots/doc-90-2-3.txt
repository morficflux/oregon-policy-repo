I.     PURPOSE

       The purpose of this policy is to provide institution security staff, Religious Services staff and
       volunteers with a list of items that have been approved for Religious Services volunteers to
       bring inside the secure perimeter of institutions without an equipment list or authorizing memo.

II.    DEFINITIONS

       A.   Religious Property: Items that are used or worn as part of an inmate’s spirituality or
            cultural practice.

III.   POLICY

       A.   Attachment 1 is a list of the currently authorized religious items that carded Religious
            Services volunteers are approved to bring into all institutions.

       B.   Items not covered in the attachment require prior approval from the security manager,
            functional unit manager, or designee. Volunteers shall check with the institution chaplain
            when there is a question about what is approved.

       C.   Specific volunteers may also be approved to bring in more specialized items, such as
            tobacco, wine (up to 4 ounces), sweat lodge building and maintenance materials, and
            audiovisual equipment not on the attached list.

            1.    In such cases, Religious Services staff will obtain written approval from the
                  institution security manager, functional unit manager, or designee.

            2.    Once written approval for special items has been given it will be entered into the
                  computerized volunteer database w/w volunteers (VIMS) and remain operative until
                  removed from the database.


IV.    IMPLEMENTATION

       Attachment 1 may be updated by Religious Services in consultation with institution security
       managers without revising the policy. The updated attachment will be distributed as necessary.
