 Department of Human Services 
 Child Welfare Programs - Chapter 413 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 ADOPTION 
 413-120-0142 
 Purpose
 
 
 ORS 419B.529 sets forth conditions under which an adoptive resource is not required to file a petition for adoption. These rules establish the Department’s responsibilities in the process for legalizing an adoption through the juvenile court where a petition for adoption is not required. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 418.005 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 419B.529
