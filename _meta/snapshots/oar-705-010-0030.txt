 Chief Education Office 
 Chapter 705 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 10 ACHIEVEMENT COMPACTS 
 705-010-0030 
 Distribution of Compacts to Education Entities 
 
 (1) The Board shall distribute achievement compacts to all education entities no later than 120 days prior to the date by which the achievement compact must be completed as set forth in 705-010-0035.  (2) Distribution may be done by electronic means. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  2012 OL Ch. 36 Sec. 14 (Enrolled SB 1581) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  2012 OL Ch. 36 Sec. 14 (Enrolled SB 1581)
