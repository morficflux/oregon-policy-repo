 Oregon Criminal Justice Commission 
 Chapter 213 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 18 OFFENSE SUBCATEGORIES 
 213-018-0080 
 Theft by Receiving (ORS 164.095) 
 
 (1) CRIME CATEGORY 5: Theft by Receiving shall be ranked at Crime Category 5 if the offender was acting as part of an organized fencing operation.  (2) CRIME CATEGORY 3: Theft by Receiving shall be ranked at Crime Category 3 if it cannot be ranked at Crime Category 5. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 137.667 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 137.667 - 137.669 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 CJC 1-1999, f. & cert. ef. 11-1-99
