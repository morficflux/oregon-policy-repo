 Department of the State Fire Marshal 
 Chapter 837 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 130 STANDARDIZED-COSTS SCHEDULES 
 837-130-0000 
 Purpose and Scope 
 
 (1) The purpose of these rules is to adopt by reference standardized-costs schedules for fire protection agency response to emergency incidents in unprotected areas and on transportation routes.  (2) These rules are to assist fire protection agencies and local government officials in the application of ORS 476.290 and 478.310. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 476.290 & 478.310 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 476.030 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FM 1-1993, f. 5-13-93, cert. ef. 6-1-93
