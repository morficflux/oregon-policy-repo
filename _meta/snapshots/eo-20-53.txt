     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-53


     AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER 20-47, REGARDING THE NORTH
     CASCADES COMPLEX FIRE


     The Emergency Conflagration Act was invoked for a group of fires that were
     labeled collectively as the North Cascades Complex Fire, in Clackamas County,
     and that declaration was later memorialized in Executive Order 20-47, issued on
     September 15, 2020.


     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


     Pursuant to ORS 476.580, Executive Order 20-47 is amended to extend the
     emergency declaration to include all of the constituent fires ultimately declared part
     of the North Cascades Complex Fire, and to modify the declared starting date and
     time that the conflagration to be September 8, 2020 at 4:19 pm. Executive Order
     20-47 otherwise continues without modification.


     Done at Salem, Oregon, this 25" day of September, 2020.
