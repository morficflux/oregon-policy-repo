 Oregon Forest Resources Institute 
 Chapter 628 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 CRITERIA FOR PRODUCER CLASS QUALIFICATIONS; PRODUCER CLASSIFICATIONS 
 628-020-0020 
 Producer Classifications 
 
 Producer classifications shall consist of the following:  (1) Producers of under 20 million board feet per year;  (2) Producers of at least 20 million board feet per year, but under 100 million board feet per year;  (3) Producers of at least 100 million board feet per year. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 526.645(6) 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 526.600, 526.610, 526.615 & 526.620 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 FRI 2-1995, f. & cert. ef. 10-17-95
