 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 80 CRIME VICTIMS’ COMPENSATION 
 137-080-0015 
 Notice of Establishment of an Escrow Account 
 
 In the case where a victim of crime is deceased, the notice to be published by the Department for five years from the establishment of an escrow account, under Section 3, Chapter 552, Oregon Laws 1985, shall advise dependents of such victims of the escrow moneys’ availability to satisfy judgments for damages suffered as a result of the crime. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 147 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 147.275 
 
 History:
