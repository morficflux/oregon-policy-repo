 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 60 INSURANCE POLICIES (ORS CHAPTER 743) 
 836-060-0046 
 Supervision of Credit Insurance Operations 
 
 (1) Once every rate review period established under OAR 836-060-0041(4), each insurer transacting credit insurance in this state shall be responsible for conducting a thorough review of creditors with respect to the insurer's credit insurance business with such creditors, to assure compliance with the Oregon Insurance Code and 836-060-0000 through 836-060-0060.  (2) The insurer shall maintain written records of these reviews for review by the Director. The records shall:  (a) Review and report the creditor's activities according to OAR 836-060-0011(5); and  (b) Verify the prompt and timely payment of claims and accuracy of refunds. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 731.244 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 743.377, 743.378, 743.380 & 746.160
