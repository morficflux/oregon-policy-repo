 Oregon Health Authority 
 Chapter 943 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 1 PROCEDURAL RULES 
 943-001-0000 
 Model Rules of Procedure 
 
 The Oregon Health Authority adopts the Attorney General Model Rules applicable to rulemaking, effective January 1, 2010, with the exception of OAR 137-001-0080.  [ED. NOTE: The full text of the Attorney General's Model Rules of Procedure is available from the office of the Attorney General or the Oregon Health Authority.] 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183.341 & 413.042 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183.341 & 413.042
