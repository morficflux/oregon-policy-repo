 Economic Recovery Review Council 
 Chapter 966 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 100 REGIONALLY SIGNIFICANT INDUSTRIAL AREAS 
 966-100-0100 
 Purpose 
 
 This division of administrative rules describes regionally significant industrial areas approved and designated by the Economic Recovery Review Council. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 197.723 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 197.723 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 ERRC 1-2013, f. & cert. ef. 1-2-13
