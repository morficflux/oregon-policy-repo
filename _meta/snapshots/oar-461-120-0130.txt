 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 120 GENERAL NONFINANCIAL ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS 
 461-120-0130 
 Declaration of Citizenship or Noncitizen Status
 
 
 An individual required to meet the citizenship and noncitizen status requirements of OAR 461-120-0110 must report the individual's citizenship and noncitizen status to the Department. An adult member of the filing group or an authorized representative (see OAR 461-115-0090 and 461-115-0140) must sign a statement under penalty of perjury attesting to this status for each member of the filing group. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.404, 411.706, 411.816, 412.014, 412.049, 412.124 & 414.231 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.070, 411.404, 411.706, 411.816, 412.014, 412.049, 412.124 & 414.231
