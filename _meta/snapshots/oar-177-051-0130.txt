 Oregon State Lottery 
 Chapter 177 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 51 LOTTERY PROMOTIONS, GIVEAWAYS, AND PLAYER LOYALTY PROGRAMS 
 177-051-0130 
 Miscellaneous 
 
 (1) Cancellation of Promotions: The Director may cancel or postpone any promotion at any time in the exercise of the Director’s sole discretion, with or without prior notice.  (2) Conflicting Provisions: In the event of a conflict between the Lottery’s rules in division 51 and the terms and conditions of any promotion, these rules control. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  OR Const. Art. XV, Sec. 4(4) & ORS 461 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 461.200
