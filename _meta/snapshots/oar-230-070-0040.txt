 Department of State Police 
 Oregon State Athletic Commission - Chapter 230 
 
 
 
 
 Division 70 RING, FENCED AREA AND EQUIPMENT 
 230-070-0040 
 Ringside Physicians
 
 
 (1) Medical personnel shall have an unobstructed view of the ring or fenced area at all times. 

 (2) Medical Personnel must be seated at a table nearest a stair entrance to the ring or fenced area. 

 (3) The promoter shall furnish chairs for assigned medical personnel. 

 (4) There must be 3 additional seats positioned behind medical personnel for ambulance personnel and injured competitor for additional medical observation. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 463 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 463.037 & 463.113
