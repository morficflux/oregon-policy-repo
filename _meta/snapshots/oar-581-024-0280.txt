 Oregon Department of Education 
 Chapter 581 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 24 STANDARDS FOR EDUCATION SERVICE DISTRICTS 
 581-024-0280 
 Auxiliary Services 
 
 Each district choosing to engage in auxiliary services with components or other agencies shall do so consistent with district goals and shall document planning and assessment procedures for each such service. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 334.005, 334.125, 334.145(3), 334.175, 334.185 & 334.195 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 334.005, 334.125, 334.145, 334.175, 334.185 & 334.195
