 Oregon Health Authority 
 Health Licensing Office - Chapter 331 
 
 
 
 
 Division 650 LICENSURE 
 331-650-0050 
 Office-Administered Practical Examination Requirements 
 
 Before an individual can apply to take the Office-administered practical examination, they must: 

 (1) Complete the training program pursuant to 331-640-0005; or 

 (2) Meet the requirements in ORS 694.065 (1)(b) or (1)(c). 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 694.015-694.170 & ORS 694.991 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 694.015-694.170 & ORS 694.991
