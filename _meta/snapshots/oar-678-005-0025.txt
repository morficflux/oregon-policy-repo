 Department of Agriculture 
 Oregon Wheat Commission - Chapter 678 
 
 
 
 
 Division 5 SCREENING AND SELECTION PROCEDURES FOR PERSONAL SERVICE CONTRACTS ENTERED INTO BY THE OREGON WHEAT COMMISSION 
 678-005-0025 
 Maintenance of RFP Mailing List 
 
 The Commission Office shall maintain a Request for Proposals (RFP) Mailing List consisting of persons, businesses, organizations and other entities which have indicated the desire to be notified of contracting opportunities that are available. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 578 
 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 WC 1-1991, f. & cert. ef. 7-11-91
