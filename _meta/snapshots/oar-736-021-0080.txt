 Parks and Recreation Department 
 Chapter 736 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 GENERAL OCEAN SHORE STATE RECREATION AREA RULES 
 736-021-0080 
 Livestock and Farming 
 
 (1) A person may not harass livestock or interfere with lawfully permitted farming activities or facilities, including fencing.  (2) A person may not conduct non-permitted farming activities on the ocean shore state recreation area. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 390.660 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 390.635 & 390.660
