 Teacher Standards and Practices Commission 
 Chapter 584 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 21 SCHOOL NURSE CERTIFICATES 
 584-021-0210 
 Reinstatement of Expired School Nurse Certificates 
 
 (1) For one year after expiration, a Professional Nurse Certificate may be reinstated upon payment of the required late application fee. Applicable renewal requirements must also be met. (See OAR 584-021-0150 for renewal requirements.)  (2) The applicant also must hold a current registered nurse license issued by the Oregon State Board of Nursing. The additional preparation must be completed within the three-year period prior to application for reinstatement. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 342 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 183, 342.175 – 342.190 & 342.455 – 342.495
