 Higher Education Coordinating Commission 
 Office of Student Access and Completion - Chapter 575 
 
 
 
 
 Division 75 VOLUNTEERS IN SERVICE TO OREGON 
 575-075-0005 
 Residency 
 
 A VISTO volunteer must be a U.S. Citizen (or be in the United States for other than a temporary purpose and intend to become a permanent resident) and maintain a residence in the State of Oregon. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 348 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 348.405 - 348.425 
 
 History:
