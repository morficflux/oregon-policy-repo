 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Employees' Benefit Board - Chapter 101 
 
 
 
 
 Division 20 ENROLLMENT RULES 
 101-020-0018 
 Declining Benefits 
 
 (1) An active eligible employee who declines PEBB core benefits waives the employee’s right to the benefit amount and enrollment in any PEBB sponsored plans.  (2) An eligible employee may decline benefits at the time of hire or meeting eligibility, consistent with a qualifying midyear plan change event, or during the open enrollment period.  (3) An eligible employee who previously declined benefits may enroll in benefit plans consistent with a qualifying midyear plan change event or during the open enrollment period. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 243.061 - 243.302 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 243.061 - 302
