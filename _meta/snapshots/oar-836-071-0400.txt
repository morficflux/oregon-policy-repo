 Department of Consumer and Business Services 
 Insurance Regulation - Chapter 836 
 
 
 
 
 Division 71 INSURANCE LICENSING 
 836-071-0400 
 Applicable Insurance Code Statutes 
 
 (1) Provisions of the Insurance Code that apply to the limited licensing program established by ORS 744.850 to 744.858 include but are not limited to the following: 744.007(6), 744.013, 744.014, 744.016, 744.018, 744.024, 744.028, 744.033, and 744.037.  (2) A limited licensee is not subject to ORS 744.225 and 744.227 and are not otherwise required to treat moneys paid by renters for insurance in a fiduciary capacity or to hold the moneys in a separate trust account. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 744.858 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 744.856 & 744.858
