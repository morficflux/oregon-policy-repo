 Department of Agriculture 
 Chapter 603 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 57 PESTICIDE CONTROL 
 603-057-0425 
 Household Pesticide Use Information 
 
 According to Chapter 1059, Oregon Laws 1999, Section 4 (5), the Department is to develop a mechanism(s) to identify the use of pesticides by households. The following rules shall apply;  (1) The Department shall utilize a survey. Such a survey shall have the following characteristics:  (a) Be statistically valid such that information obtained from participating households can be used to accurately estimate pesticide use by all households;  (b) Gathers information similar in content to information reported by pesticide users; and  (c) Includes the entire state.  (2) The Department may utilize the assistance of other agencies, including federal, state and local, and of private entities in developing, implementing and maintaining a mechanism to identify pesticide use by households. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 634, 561.190 & Ch. 1059 OL 1999 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  Ch. 1059 OL 1999
