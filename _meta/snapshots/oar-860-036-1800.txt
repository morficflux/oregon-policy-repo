 Public Utility Commission 
 Chapter 860 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 36 WATER UTILITIES 
 860-036-1800 
 Service Territory Allocation Definitions 
 
 For purposes of service territory allocation OAR 860-036-1800 through 860 036-1850:  (1) "Allocated territory" means an approved area with boundaries set out by Commission order granting an exclusive service territory.  (2) "Community water supply system" has the meaning provided by ORS 758.300.  (3) "Water utility" has the meaning provided in ORS 758.300. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 183, 756, 757 & 758 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 758.300-758.320 
 
 History: 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 PUC 1-2017, f. & cert. ef. 1-24-17
