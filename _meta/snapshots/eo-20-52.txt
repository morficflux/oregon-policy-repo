     EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20-52


     INVOCATION OF EMERGENCY CONFLAGRATION ACT FOR THE
     TWO FOUR TWO FIRE IN KLAMATH COUNTY


     Pursuant to my authority as Governor of the State of Oregon, I find that:


     The fire known as the “Two Four Two Fire” is burning in Klamath County.


     The resources necessary for protecting life and property from the Two Four Two
     Fire are beyond local capabilities. Assistance with life, safety, and structural fire
     protection was requested by the Klamath County Fire Defense Board and the State
     Fire Marshal concurs with that request.


     In accordance with ORS 476.510-476-610, I have determined that a threat to life,
     safety, and property exists due to a fire known as the Two Four Two Fire in
     Klamath County and the threat exceeds the firefighting capabilities of local
     firefighting personnel and equipment. Accordingly, I have invoked the Emergency
     Conflagration Act.


     These findings were made at 1:00 am on September 8, 2020 with Office of State
     Fire Marshal and I now confirm them with this Executive Order.


     NOW THEREFORE, IT IS HEREBY DIRECTED AND ORDERED:


     Ie     The Oregon State Police and the Office of State Fire Marshal shall mobilize
            fire resources statewide and coordinate with all appropriate Fire Defense
            Board Chiefs for the use of personnel and equipment in accordance with the
            Emergency Conflagration Act to suppress and contain this fire. Resources
            responding to the Two Four Two Fire may be redistributed by the State Fire
            Marshal.


     2.     This conflagration is declared only for the Two Four Two Fire threatening
            structures in Klamath County.


     3;     This order was made by verbal proclamation at 1:00 am the 8th day of
             September 2020 and signed this 25"" day of September, 2020, in Salem,
            Oregon.
