 Department of Justice 
 Chapter 137 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 79 ADDRESS CONFIDENTIALITY PROGRAM 
 137-079-0110 
 Authority and Purpose
 
 
 These rules set out guidelines for the operation of the Address Confidentiality Program (ACP) set forth in ORS 192.820 through 192.868. This includes the designation of Application Assistants; the application process to participate in the ACP; the certification of a program participant; ongoing participation and termination of participation in the ACP; the responsibility of public agencies to use the substitute address provided by the ACP; conditions under which a participant’s actual address may be disclosed or participation in the ACP may be verified; service of process on a participant; and other aspects of program operation. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 192.860, 2005 OL, Ch. 821 & SB 850 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 192.820-192.868, 2005 OL, Ch. 821 & SB 850
