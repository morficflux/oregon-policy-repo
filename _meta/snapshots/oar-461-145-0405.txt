 Department of Human Services 
 Self-Sufficiency Programs - Chapter 461 
 
 
 
 
 Division 145 TREATMENTS OF SPECIFIC ASSETS 
 461-145-0405 
 Plan for Self-support 
 
 (1) This rule covers two types of plans for self support.  (a) A plan for self support approved by the Social Security Administration.  (b) A plan for self support approved by the Department (see OAR 461-135-0708).  (2) Assets listed in an approved plan for self support are excluded. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 411.060, 411.816 & 412.049 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 411.060, 411.816 & 412.049
