 Department of Corrections 
 Chapter 291 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 15 VOLUNTEER SERVICES/STUDENT INTERNS 
 291-015-0015 
 Recruitment 
 
 (1) To recruit a volunteer or student intern, a position description proposal will be written and submitted to the appropriate manager for approval.  (2) A position description proposal will be submitted by the manager to the volunteer coordinator who will secure a decision concerning approval.  (3) Volunteers and student interns who best meet program needs will be recruited from all ethnic, cultural, and economic segments of the community.  (4) Any volunteer or student intern who leaves the program will return his/her ID card to the volunteer coordinator. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 179.040, 423.020, 423.030 & 423.075
