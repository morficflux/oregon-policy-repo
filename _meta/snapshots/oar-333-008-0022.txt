 Oregon Health Authority 
 Public Health Division - Chapter 333 
 
 
 
 
 Division 8 MEDICAL MARIJUANA 
 333-008-0022 
 Proof of Residency
 
 
 (1) In order to be approved for a registry identification card, an applicant must be a resident of Oregon. 

 (2) If an applicant for a registry identification card does not have a valid Oregon driver license or Oregon identification card, the applicant must demonstrate that they are a resident of Oregon by submitting sufficient documentation such as but not limited to a current lease agreement or current utility bill that has the applicant’s name and address. 

 (3) Residency must be maintained by patients while registered with the Authority. 

 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 475C.783, 475C.792 & 475C.919 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 475C.783
