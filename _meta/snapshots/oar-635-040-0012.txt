 Department of Fish and Wildlife 
 Chapter 635 
 
 
 
 
 
 Division 40 SALMON HATCHERIES — PRIVATE 
 635-040-0012 
 Release Limits 
 
 Each permit shall specify release limits. If limits were not specified in permits already issued, the Department has specified, in writing, applicable release limits for those permits, and any transferee is bound by those specified limits. 
 
 
 
 
 Statutory/Other Authority:  ORS 496, 506 & 508 
 
 
 Statutes/Other Implemented:  ORS 496, 506 & 508 
 
 History:
