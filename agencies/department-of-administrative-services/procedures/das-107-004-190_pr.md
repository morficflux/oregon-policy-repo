## Full text

RESPONSIBLE ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE
USAGE PROCEDURE
NUMBER: 107-004-190_PR
EFFECTIVE DATE: June 16, 2026
DIVISION: Enterprise Information Services (State CIO)
POLICY OWNER: Privacy and Artificial Intelligence
INTERNAL OR STATEWIDE PROCEDURE: Statewide
LAST REVIEWED DATE: June 16, 2026
SUPERSEDES: Updated Interim Guidance on Generative AI Usage and Access memo
(Feb.10, 2026)
REFERENCE/AUTHORITY:

   •   ORS 276A.203; 276A.206
   •   Responsible Artificial Intelligence Usage Policy 107-004-190
APPROVED SIGNATURE:

_____________________________________________
Terrence Woods, State Chief Information Officer

Statement
This procedure establishes the core activities agencies will use to implement
the statewide Responsible Artificial Intelligence (AI) Usage policy.

Applicability
This procedure applies to state agencies as defined in ORS 174.112, and includes any
board, commission, department, office or other entity within the Oregon executive
department. The following agencies are excluded:

   •   Secretary of State
   •   State Treasurer

The procedure applies to generative and agentic AI systems used to conduct the business
of the state, including both standalone systems and components embedded in other
software.

Forms and Instructions
The following supporting materials may be required or used under this procedure:
     •   Agency AI Adoption Strategy Template and Instructions
     •   AI Risk Management Framework
     •   AI Use Form
     •   User disclosure mechanism
     •   User feedback
     •   Human in the loop documentation
     •   Higher-risk use cases requirements

Definitions
Refer to the Responsible Artificial Intelligence Usage Policy (107-004-190).

Procedure
Create an AI Adoption Plan for the Agency
 STEP RESPONSIBILITY ACTION
 1        Agency             Establish an internal process for creating an AI Adoption Plan
                             following the AI Adoption Strategy template provided.

 2                           Create an AI Adoption Plan that is aligned with and/or
                             included within the agency’s strategic/business plan, IT
                             strategic plan, and data governance plan.

For proposed new uses of AI, evaluate risks and submit the AI Use Form as part of the
Information Technology Investment Oversight Policy and Procedure
 STEP RESPONSIBILITY ACTION
 1        Agency             Review the proposed AI use through the agency’s internal
                             governance, risk evaluation, and approval processes.

 2                           Apply the AI Risk Management Framework to your potential AI
                             use case. Refer to the questions in the AI Use Form,
                             to identify risks and answer questions around safeguards,
                             testing, monitoring, and oversight.

 STEP RESPONSIBILITY ACTION
 3      Agency             Ensure the proposed use has the following requirements
                           and/or standards identified:
                              • Generative AI model and system card disclosure
                              • Use of non-public data for model training prohibited
                              • User disclosure mechanism
                              • User feedback
                              • Human in the loop documentation
                              • Additional requirements for elevated-risk or higher-
                                  risk uses

 4                         Complete and submit the AI Use Form as a supplemental
                           form to the IT investment intake process.

 5      EIS                Initial review by EIS. For use cases categorized as higher-risk,
                           EIS will prescribe additional requirements. Refer to Higher-
                           risk use cases requirements for additional information.

 6      Agency             Complete additional requirements for higher-risk use cases,
                           if required by EIS.
