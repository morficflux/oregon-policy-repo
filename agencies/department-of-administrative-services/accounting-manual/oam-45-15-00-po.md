# Payroll accounts reimbursement-policy (OAM 45.15.00.po)

## At a glance

OAM chapter "45 - Payroll", document 45.15.00.po: Payroll accounts reimbursement-policy.
Effective 2001-08-15 per the OAM listing of record.

> "Agencies served by the Oregon State Payroll Application (OSPA) will reimburse the Joint Payroll Account and the Mass Transit Account for their respective payroll costs by pay day for each payroll processing run. 45.15.00.PO.1"
