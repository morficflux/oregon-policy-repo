 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. This policy describes Oregon State Hospital (OSH) policies and procedures for
      staff returning to work following an injury.
   B. This policy applies to all staff.

II. POLICY
   A. OSH adopts Department of Administrative Services (DAS) policy 50.020.03,
      “Reinstatement and Reemployment of Injured Workers,” DAS policy 50.020.05,
      “Early Return to Work of Injured Workers,” and Shared Services procedure
      DHS|OHA 080-002-01, “Early Return to Work of Injured Workers.”
   B. OSH follows all applicable regulations, including federal and state statutes and
      rules; Oregon Department of Administrative Services (DAS), Shared Services,
      and Oregon Health Authority (OHA) policies; and relevant accreditation
      standards. Such regulations supersede the provisions of this policy unless this
      policy is more restrictive.
   C. Staff who fail to comply with this policy or related policy attachments or protocols
      may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including dismissal.

III. DEFINITIONS
   A. “Staff” includes employees, volunteers, trainees, interns, contractors, vendors,
      and other state employees assigned to work at Oregon State Hospital (OSH).
