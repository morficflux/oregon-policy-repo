# Medical Record Maintenance and Transportation (OSH Policy 2.014)

## At a glance

OSH Policy 2.014 — Medical Record Maintenance and Transportation. Oregon State Hospital (Oregon Health Authority). Effective JULY 31, 2024.

## Full text

                OREGON STATE HOSPITAL
                             POLICY ATTACHMENT
PROCEDURES B:   Medical Record Transportation                        POLICY : 2.014

POINT PERSON:   Health Information Manager

APPROVED:       Interim Superintendent                        DATE: JULY 31, 2024

SELECT ONE:

  RESPONSIBLE                              PROCEDURES
PERSON/GROUP
Staff           1. To request approval to remove protected health information
                   (PHI) from OSH grounds, complete the form, “Request to
                   Remove Documents”.
                2. Submit the completed form to the supervisor.
                3. If permission is obtained, send a copy of the completed form
                   with the supervisor’s signature to Health Information Manager.
                4. Follow all provisions of OSH policy 2.014, “Medical Record
                   Maintenance and Transportation” and DAS policy 107-004-100,
                   “Transporting Information Assets.”
                5. Maintain control of PHI during transport.
                6. Before removing PHI, complete a desk log itemizing the date
                   and scope of the information being transported, and the patient
                   medical record number.
                7. Keep the desk log in the secure work environment, available for
                   supervisor review.
                8. Destroy the log after the medical record has been returned to
                   OSH.
                9. If PHI is lost, report the loss in accordance with regulation and
                   Oregon Health Authority policy and related procedures.
Supervisor      1. Review request to remove PHI and respond in writing with
                   approval or denial.
                2. If needed, review the desk log.
