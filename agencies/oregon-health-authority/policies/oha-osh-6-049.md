                        OREGON STATE HOSPITAL
                                  POLICY AND PROCEDURE
SECTION 6:          Patient Care                                                      POLICY:4.016

SUBJECT:            Admission of Patients with
                    Medical Problems

APPROVED:           Sara Walker, MD                                          DATE: JUNE 24, 2024

                    Interim Superintendent

      Medical Problems” as it has been absorbed into policy 6.058 “Admissions.”
