 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. Oregon State Hospital (OSH) strives to improve workplace safety and attempts to
      mitigate staff injuries by providing safety grant funding for safety-related projects,
      services, and equipment as approved by the OSH Central Safety Committee
      (CSC).
   B. This policy applies to all OSH staff.

II. GUIDELINES
   A. OSH provides safety grant funds through reimbursements received from the
      State Accident Insurance Fund's (SAIF) "Oregon Employer-at-Injury Program
      (OEIP)" and Oregon Workers' Compensation Division's "Prefer Workers
      Program."
   B. OSH provides safety grants through the CSC to help improve staff safety through
      the following initiatives:
      1. Safety Grant Project;
      2. Safety Grant Program;
      3. Safety Grant Initiative.
   C. This safety grant fund may not be used as a budgetary supplement, but only to
      address safety issues not provided for in the current biennium fiscal budgets.

         E. OSH follows all applicable regulations, including federal and state statutes and
            rules; Oregon Department of Administrative Services (DAS), Shared Services,

         F. Failure to comply with this policy or related policy attachments or protocols may
