## Full text

                OREGON STATE HOSPITAL
                            POLICY ATTACHMENT
PROCEDURES A:   Wanding                                             POLICY : 8.041

POINT PERSON:   Director Security

APPROVED:       Sara Walker, MD                          DATE: JANUARY 15, 2025
                Interim Superintendent

SELECT ONE:

  RESPONSIBLE                             PROCEDURES
 PERSON/GROUP
Staff who are   1. Appropriate staff must be present prior to conducting a
trained to         wanding in accordance with this policy.
conduct         2. All wandings must occur within view of the video surveillance
wandings           system.
                3. Security staff conducting a wanding must follow department
                   protocols.
                4. Move the wanding device within close proximity (around 1 inch
                   from the person) over the patient’s clothing and body.
                5. If a metal item is detected during the wanding, determine the
                   cause or source of item per security protocols. Do not
                   physically touch the patient during a wanding. If physical touch
                   is required to determine the cause or source, follow
                   Procedures B for a pat down.
                6. If a patient refuses wanding, request a registered nurse (RN)
                   to assess the patient for reason to conduct a pat-down.
Registered      1. If a patient refuses wanding, assess the patient to determine
Nurse (RN)         whether a pat-down is necessary.
                2. If a pat-down is considered to be necessary, follow Procedures
                   B, “Pat-down.”
