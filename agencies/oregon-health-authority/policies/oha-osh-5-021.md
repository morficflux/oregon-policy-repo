 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. This policy establishes requirements and procedures for background checks of
      staff at Oregon State Hospital (OSH).
   B. This policy applies to all staff.

 II. POLICY
   A. OSH adopts Shared Services policy, DHS-060-010 "Background Checks," and
      applicable associated Oregon Administrative Rules (OAR) and Oregon Revised
      Statutes (ORS) detailed in the DHS policy.
   B. OSH staff must follow procedures associated with this policy, and related
      protocols, for subject individual background check processes specific to OSH.
   C. All OSH staff will undergo a background check every two years to comply with
      OAR Chapter 309, Division 35.
    D. OSH follows all applicable regulations, including federal and state statutes and
       rules; Oregon Department of Administrative Services (DAS), Shared Services,
       and Oregon Health Authority (OHA) policies; and relevant accreditation
       standards. Such regulations supersede the provisions of this policy unless this
       policy is more restrictive.
    E. Staff who fail to comply with this policy or related policy attachments or protocols
       may be subject to disciplinary action.
