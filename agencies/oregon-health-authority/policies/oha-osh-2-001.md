 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. This policy explains how Oregon State Hospital (OSH) works to stop the spread
      of infections. It ensures that OSH follows all laws, rules, and accreditation
      standards about infection prevention and employee health.

   B. This policy applies to everyone at OSH - Staff, patients, and visitors.

II. GUIDELINES
   A. The Infection Prevention Department maintains the OSH Infection Control
      Program. This program outlines how OSH prevents infections, protects patients
      and staff, and ensures a safe, clean environment.
   B. OSH Staff, patients, and visitors must follow the infection control procedures
      described in this policy and protocols.
   C. Infection Control Program:
      The OSH Infection Control Program is managed by the Medical Department.

   D. Preventing Outbreaks:
      As directed by the Chief Medical Officer (CMO) or their designee, medically
      necessary preventative measures must be followed to protect patients and avoid
      an outbreak of highly infectious diseases with associated morbidity and mortality.

   E. When Staff are Sick:
      Staff who show signs of an infectious disease must follow OSH policy 2.003,
      "Infectious Disease Management for Staff."
