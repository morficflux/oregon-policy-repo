 I.      PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
         A. This policy tells Oregon State Hospital staff what to do when they get test results
            or lab values that show something serious or dangerous. These results need
            quick action to keep patients safe.
         B. This policy applies to all staff who receive a critical laboratory value or diagnostic
            procedure resu It.

II.      POLICY

         A. If a test or result has Critical values or results of a test or procedure must be
            addressed in a timely and organized manner, staff must:
            1. Follow Procedure A, "Critical Values or Results Response" for handling
                critical values or results.
            2. Refer to Attachment A for definitions of critical values or results.
         B. Oregon State Hospital (OSH) follows all applicable regulations, including federal
            and state statutes and rules; Oregon Department of Administrative Services
            (DAS), Shared Services, and Oregon Health Authority (OHA) policies; and
            relevant accreditation standards. Such regulations supersede the provisions of
            this policy unless this policy is more restrictive.
         C. Failure to comply with this policy or related policy attachments or protocols may
            be subject to disciplinary action.
