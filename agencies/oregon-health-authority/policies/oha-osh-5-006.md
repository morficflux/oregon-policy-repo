I.    PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
      A. The Oregon State Hospital (OSH) celebrates staff service and rewards
         extraordinary achievements in furthering the hospital’s mission, vision, and
         values.
      B. This policy applies to staff at OSH.

II.   POLICY
        A. OSH Executive Team
               1. The Superintendent and CFO/COO will create a budget from funds made
                  available under Oregon Department of Administrative Services
                  (DAS) policy 50.040.01, “Employee Performance Recognition
                  Program” and other agency funding for the employee recognition activities
                  included in this policy.
               2. OSH Executive Team is responsible for reviewing, approving, and providing
                  appropriate support for employee recognition activities included in this
                  policy.
        B. OSH Employee Recognition Committee, LLC
               1. The Employee Recognition Committee (ERC) will coordinate or perform
                  some or all the staff
                  recognition activities included in this policy with appropriate
                  agency support.
               2. The ERC may submit requests to OSH Executive Team for budgetary

          3. The ERC may raise funds for its own staff recognition activities

   C. Recognition of Years of Service
          1. Staff will be recognized for years of state service at 2 years, 5 years,
             and in 5-year increments thereafter.
   D. Recognition Program
          1. In line with provisions provided by DAS 50.040.01, the agency has
             created a Recognition Program to acknowledge and reward the notable
             achievements of individuals or teams that exemplify one or more parts
             of the OSH mission, vision, or values or for improving quality of services
             provided to OSH patients, external stakeholders, and internal partners.
                     i. Prizes may be monetary or non-monetary, may not exceed
                        $50.00, and are subject to taxation.
                 ii. To be eligible for the Recognition Program the nominee must
                     be staff of the Oregon State Hospital.
                iii. Staff may only receive a Recognition Program award (either
                     an individual or a team award) one time per calendar year.
          2. Individual Recognition Award
                i.     Any staff may submit an Individual Recognition Award nomination slip.
         3. Team Recognition Award
                i.     Managers can submit a completed Team Recognition Award form,
                       found on OWL, to nominate work teams for a Team Recognition
                       Award.
   E. Additional Staff Recognition Events
         1. The agency realizes that there are numerous other opportunities to
            acknowledge the hard work, dedication, professionalism, and resilience
            of its staff that are not covered by the specific events listed above.
         2. As such, the agency will allow the ERC to submit a written request to
            hold a Staff Recognition Event otherwise not covered above.
                  i. The ERC may submit a proposal for the event it would like to
                     hold to OSH Executive Team on an as needed basis.
                           1) The proposal must identify:
                                 a. What the event is and how it recognizes OSH
                                    staff.
                                 b. How the event is going to be structured.
                                 c. The communication plan for the event.
                                 d. Requested budget and resource support.
                 ii. Approval of an ERC submitted proposal for a Staff
                     Recognition Event will mean the event is agency sponsored,

III.     DEFINITIONS
         A. “Staff” includes employees, volunteers, trainees, interns, contractors, vendors,
            and other state employees assigned to work at Oregon State Hospital (OSH).
         B. “ERC” is an acronym that stands for OSH Employee Recognition Committee,
            LLC. OSH Employee Recognition Committee, LLC may also be referenced to as
            "OSH ERC" or "OSH Employee Recognition".
