 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. Oregon State Hospital may have law enforcement presence on campus for a
      variety of operational and safety-related purposes. This policy establishes
      guidelines for staff response to routine and non-routine law enforcement
      interactions to ensure safety, protect patient rights, and maintain compliance with
      applicable laws and regulations.
   B. This policy applies to all staff who interact with law enforcement.

II. GUIDELINES
   A. OSH will respond to all law enforcement presence on its campuses in a manner
      that prioritizes safety, preserves patient rights, and ensures compliance with
      applicable laws and regulations.
   B. When law enforcement is present at OSH for routine, scheduled activities, staff
      shall coordinate with the Administrator and follow established procedures.
   C. When law enforcement is present for non-routine or emergent situations, staff will
      immediately notify the Administrator and shall not independently grant access to
      patients, patient care areas, or protected information unless authorized.
   D. Oregon State Hospital (OSH) follows all applicable regulations, including federal
      and state statutes and rules; Oregon Department of Administrative Services
      (DAS), Shared Services, and Oregon Health Authority (OHA) policies; and
