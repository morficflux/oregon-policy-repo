 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. This policy concerns the required notifications to other entities that Oregon State
      Hospital (OSH) must complete regarding patients committed to OSH for arson or
      arson-related activity.
   B. This policy applies to staff within the Social Work department.

II. POLICY
   A. In accordance with Oregon Revised Statute 476.730, regardless of where a
      patient discharges, OSH will notify the State Fire Marshall and Oregon State
      Police (OSP) before a patient committed to OSH for arson or arson-related
      activity is discharged.
       1. This includes patients who are civilly committed due to arson or arson-related
          activity, but not actually criminally charged with said activity.
       2. This policy does not apply to patients who have prior history of arson or
          arson-related activity but are not currently committed to OSH for said activity.
   B. OSH will also immediately notify the State Fire Marshall and OSP when the
      patient goes on unauthorized leave.
   C. The designated social worker must complete notifications as indicated in the
      applicable Social Work Department protocol.
   D. OSH follows all applicable regulations, including federal and state statutes and
      rules; Oregon Department of Administrative Services (DAS), Shared Services,
