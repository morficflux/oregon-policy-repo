                         OREGON STATE HOSPITAL
                                    POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

                         Executive Summary of Policy Changes
            Contact the person noted below with questions about the policy or related procedures.

POLICY :            2.008 PRIVACY AND INFORMATION SECURITY OF PATIENT INFORMATION

CONTACT:                                                                                  FEBRUARY 12,
                    HEALTH INFORMATION MANAGER                      EFFECTIVE DATE:
                                                                                          2026

PURPOSE OF THIS POLICY:
 This policy establishes Oregon State Hospital’s procedures regarding the privacy and
security of patient information

SUMMARY OF CONTENT CHANGE(S):
Updated policy to remove unnecessary references

REASON FOR CHANGE(S):
3-year review

REQUIRED ACTION:
Tier 0 – all staff read the updated policy, but no follow-up action is required.

WHO IS AFFECTED BY THE CONTENT CHANGE(S):
All staff

NOTE: OSH personnel are responsible to know and abide by all applicable policies as indicated in OSH
Policy and Procedure 1.001, “Policy System at OSH”.
Current documents may be found in I:\OSH Policies & Procedures and on the OSH Policies and
Procedures intranet page.
Email questions to “OSH Policies and Procedures”.
