## Full text

                       OREGON STATE HOSPITAL

SECTION 3:         Operations                                                POLICY: 3.002

SUBJECT:           Electrical Equipment and
                   Resource Conservation

POINT PERSON:      Director of Facilities and Support
                   Operations

APPROVED:          Jim Diegel                                        DATE: MARCH 9, 2026

                   Interim Superintendent

SELECT ONE:      C New policy                           E: Minor/technical revision of
                                                           existing policy

                 C Reaffirmation of existing policy     C Major revision of existing policy

 I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. This policy explains how to save energy and use electrical equipment wisely in
      state buildings. It also covers personal appliances in work areas, break rooms,
      and other spaces.
   B. This policy applies to all Staff.

II. GUIDELINES
   A. Oregon State Hospital follows the Department of Administrative Services (DAS)
      policy 107-011-010 "Resource Conservation."
   B. Oregon State Hospital (OSH) follows all rules and laws from the federal
      government, the state, the Department of Administrative Services (DAS), Shared
      Services, and the Oregon Health Authority (OHA). If those rules are stricter than
      this policy, the stricter rules apply.
   C. Staff who do not follow this policy or related rules may face discipline.

      NONE
