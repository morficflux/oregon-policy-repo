I. PURPOSE AND APPLICABILITY
   A. This policy and its attached procedures concern the right of patients to vote, and
      Oregon State Hospital (OSH)’s procedures and guidelines regarding patient voter
      registration and voting activities.
   B. This policy applies to all staff and patients.

II. POLICY
   A. Any person admitted to Oregon State Hospital (OSH) and who is a citizen of the
      United States must be offered the opportunity to register to vote within ten days
      of admission. Every patient who is registered to vote will receive a ballot after
      their 18th birthday in accordance with Oregon Revised Statute (ORS) 247.016.
   B. A patient’s right to vote may not be restricted unless provisions of ORS 137.281
      apply.
   C. Each patient must be offered the opportunity to register and vote, be made aware
      of the deadlines to vote, and return their ballot and receive their ballot from the
      county elections office.
   D. OSH will assume the cost of postage for patient voter registration forms to be
      mailed to county elections offices.
   E. OSH employees must provide assistance to patients completing forms upon their
      request. Pursuant to ORS 247.208, An employee who assists a patient with their
      voter registration may not suggest a political preference or party allegiance,
      discourage a patient from registering to vote, lead any patient to believe that the
