PURPOSE

This procedure outlines the necessary actions to request or carry out an exception to, or temporarily rescind,
suspend or otherwise amend a statewide or DAS internal policy in alignment with State Policy 107-001-002,
Exceptions to Policies.

APPLICABILITY

All statewide policies and DAS internal policies except for provisions within those policies specifically required
by law or rule.

DEFINITIONS

Division Administrator is an individual appointed by the DAS Director in accordance with ORS 184.315 to
administer an administrative division or staff office of DAS.

PROCEDURE

This section outlines in detail the entire procedure, how it is to be applied, how exceptions are to be remedied.
The main purpose of this section is to avoid confusion by setting forth in detail what is expected in specific
situations.

 RESPONSIBILITY            STEP     ACTION
 State Agency Director       1      Identify circumstances where an exception to statewide policy or
 or DAS Division                    applicable DAS internal policy is necessary to maintain or establish the
 Administrator                      efficient and effective conduct of state operations.

                             2      Submit a written request for an exception to the appropriate DAS Division
                                    Administrator including, but not limited to, the name and number of the
                                    specific policy; the reason for the request; the specific circumstances
                                    requiring an exception; an explanation indicating how the exception will
                                    comply with all applicable statutes, contracts and rules.

 DAS Division                3      Review request for exception and make recommendation to the DAS
 Administrator                      Director for approval or denial. Approval or denial may be based upon the
                                    following criteria or other criteria deemed to be an appropriate
                                    consideration by the Division Administrator: The exception would result in
                                    a cost savings or cost avoidance; a decrease in state/agency liability; or
                                    consistency of the proposed exception with applicable statutes and rules.

 DAS Director or             4      Approve or deny the exception request in writing and provide a copy to the
 Deputy Director                    DAS Policy Coordinator.

 DAS Policy                  5      Maintain files documenting policy exception requests and approvals or
 Coordinator                        denials thereof.

 DAS Division                6      Review statewide and DAS internal policies every two years for potential
 Administrator                      revision based on patterns of exception requests and amend as
                                    appropriate.
