## How to find the right standard

- **"What controls must my agency implement?"** — the IT Control Standards; find your
  control family in the [family index](eis-css-it-control-standards/_index.md).
- **"What is the statewide security plan we must comply with?"** — the Statewide
  Information Security Plan (and note OAR 128-030-0010 defines the "State Information
  Security Plan" broadly as a compilation of statutes, rules, policies, and plans).
- **Program-level objectives and roles:** the 2023 Program Plan.
