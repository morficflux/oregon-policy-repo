# Department of Administrative Services (DAS) — agency map

DAS is the central administrative agency of Oregon's executive branch. Agency-scoped
knowledge bodies live here; jurisdiction-wide law (ORS/OAR/EOs) lives at repo root.

## Knowledge bodies

| Directory | Contents |
|---|---|
| [policies/](policies/_index.md) | DAS statewide policies (107-xxx series, HR 50-xxx, procedures `_PR`) |
| [accounting-manual/](accounting-manual/_index.md) | Oregon Accounting Manual (OAM) — Office of the State Controller |
| [standards/](standards/_index.md) | EIS/CSS statewide IT & security standards (referenced out from policy) |

## Organization (for scoping and CODEOWNERS)

Chief Operating Office · Office of Strategic Initiatives and Enterprise Accountability ·
Chief Financial Office (SARS, SFMS, OSC) · Chief Human Resources Office · Enterprise Goods
and Services · Enterprise Asset Management · **Enterprise Information Services (EIS)** —
led by the State CIO; contains Cyber Security Services (CSS), Data Center Services, Data
Governance and Transparency, Project Portfolio Performance, Shared Services, Strategy and
Design, Administrative Services.

To add another agency, clone this directory shape under `agencies/<agency>/` — no schema
changes required.
