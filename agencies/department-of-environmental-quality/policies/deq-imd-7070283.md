# Use of Significant Figures and Rounding Conventions in Water Quality Permitting - Transmittal Memo (DEQ Internal Management Directive)

## At a glance

DEQ Internal Management Directive — Use of Significant Figures and Rounding Conventions in Water Quality Permitting - Transmittal Memo. Oregon Department of Environmental Quality.

## Full text

State of Oregon
Department of Environmental Quality                                       Memorandum
                                                                            Date: June 1, 2012

To:           DEQ Water Quality Staff

From:         Greg Aldrich
              Interim Administrator, Water Quality Division

Subject:      Revisions to the IMD entitled “The Use of Significant Figures and Rounding
              Conventions in Water Quality Permitting”

This memorandum transmits Version 1.2 of the Internal Management Directive (IMD) entitled
“The Use of Significant Figures and Rounding Conventions in Water Quality Permitting”. It is
effective immediately.

The specific changes made to the document are described in Appendix A of the document.

The following individuals were principally involved in the revisions: Sonja Biorn-Hansen and
Scott Hoatson.

The specific changes made to the document are described in Appendices E and D of parts 1 and
2 respectively.

Cc: Water Quality Managers
   Division Administrators
