# Department of Environmental Quality (`department-of-environmental-quality`) — index

Knowledge bodies for Department of Environmental Quality. **Non-authoritative copies.**

- [Policies](policies/_index.md)

DEQ regulates air, land, and water quality under ORS chapter 468 (and its many program
chapters — 454, 459, 465, 468A/B, 566...) and OAR chapter 340. Its internal policy body is a
single "Internal Management Directives" (IMD) series spanning the Air Quality, Water Quality,
Land Quality, and Cross-Agency divisions — staff-facing interpretive guidance, explicitly
non-binding (each carries a disclaimer that it "does not constitute rulemaking").
