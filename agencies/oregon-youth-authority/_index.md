# Oregon Youth Authority (`oregon-youth-authority`) — index

Knowledge bodies for Oregon Youth Authority. **Non-authoritative copies.**

- [Policies](policies/_index.md)

OYA is Oregon's juvenile-corrections agency, operating close-custody youth correctional
facilities and community juvenile-justice programs under OAR chapter 416 and statutory
authority spanning ORS chapters 419A/419C (juvenile code) and 420/420A (youth corrections).
Its policy stack is a single numbered "Policy Statement" series organized into parts
(0 = Mission/Values, I = Administrative Services, II = Facility Operations, III = Field/
Community Services), each policy carrying its own effective date and a related-policy
cross-reference list in its own header.
