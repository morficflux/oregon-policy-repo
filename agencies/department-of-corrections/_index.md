# Department of Corrections (`department-of-corrections`) — index

Knowledge bodies for Department of Corrections. **Non-authoritative copies.**

- [Policies](policies/_index.md)

The Department of Corrections (DOC) operates Oregon's state prisons and supervises parole,
probation, and community corrections. It is created by **ORS 423.020** and headed by a
director appointed by the Governor (**ORS 423.075**); its administrative rules are **OAR
chapter 291** (in this repo under [`rules/291/`](../../rules/291)). DOC publishes a numbered
**departmental policy series** (e.g. 10.1.2) as individual PDFs — the operational layer
beneath its rules — grouped by functional area (the leading number; each policy's issuing
division is in its frontmatter). Those policies are ingested here; see
[policies](policies/_index.md) for the per-division breakdown.
