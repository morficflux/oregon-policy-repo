PURPOSE

This procedure outlines tasks of Executive Branch agencies to comply with Statewide Policy 107-004-180
(Information Technology Readiness) when requesting information technology (IT) budget or IT project funding,
participating in prioritization and contributing to statutory reporting.

APPLICABILITY

Refer to the Information Technology Readiness Policy (107-004-180) for applicability of this procedure.

FORMS/ATTACHMENTS/INSTRUCTIONS

   •   IT Readiness Workbook
   •   Prioritization business case template
   •   Budget category descriptions
   •   Attachment A: Transmittal form

DEFINITIONS

Refer to the Information Technology Readiness Policy (107-004-180).

PROCEDURE

IT Project Requests

 RESPONSIBILITY                STEP          ACTION
 Assistant State Chief             1.        ASCIO/SIPMs deliver presentations to program areas regarding
 Information Officer                         IT Readiness policy and procedure.
 (ASCIO) / Senior IT
 Portfolio Manager
 (SIPM)

 Agency                            2.        Meet with SIPM on IT project requests’ alignment with Enterprise
                                             Information Resources Management (IRM) Strategy and agency
                                             IT strategic plan.

                                             Meet with ASCIOs on all other IT funding requests’ alignment with
                                             IRM Strategy and agency IT strategic plan.

                                             Submit draft request for IT budget and IT project(s) by April 30 of
                                             each even-numbered year utilizing the IT Readiness Workbook.
                                             Submit through the EIS form.

                                             The IT Readiness Workbook must be accompanied by a draft
                                             prioritization business case for each IT project exceeding
                                             $1,000,000. Additionally, the project prioritization worksheet in the
                                             IT Readiness Workbook should reflect each IT project exceeding
                                             $1,000,000.

                                             Note: Budget category descriptions are to facilitate consistent
                                             categorization of IT expenditures.

 ASCIO/SIPM                        3.        Meet with agencies to provide and discuss feedback on IT budget
                                             request(s), IT project request(s), and draft prioritization business
                                             case(s).
                                             CFO Analyst is included in meeting.

 Agency                            4.        By June 1 of even-numbered years, submit updated IT Readiness
                                             Workbook through the EIS form. Updated workbooks may
                                             address feedback from EIS. Updated workbooks may not include
                                             new requests not already identified in the draft request.

                                             The updated IT Readiness Workbook must be accompanied by a
                                             transmittal form signed by the agency head and agency IT leader
                                             role (Attachment A).

                                             Upload updated prioritization business case(s) (amended
                                             business case for continuing investments) in the Project Portfolio
                                             Management (PPM) system.

                                             Submit new project investments as an “idea”. Submit continuing
                                             project investments in a folder labeled “project prioritization”.

                                             Note: Agencies without access to the PPM system should work
                                             with their SIPM.

 SIPM/ASCIO                        5.        Meet with agency to discuss prioritization scoring differences.
                                             CFO Analyst is included in the meeting.

                                             requests.

                                             Develop recommended prioritization of program area IT project
                                             requests.
