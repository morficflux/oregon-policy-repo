Effective 2003-07-28 per the OAM listing of record.

> **[VERBATIM]** "This policy provides guidelines to agencies concerning when meals and refreshments may be paid for with State funds. Applicability of this policy is limited to non-travel business meals and refreshments. Non-travel business would include meetings, training sessions, conferences, or other agency-sponsored events to conduct official state business.
