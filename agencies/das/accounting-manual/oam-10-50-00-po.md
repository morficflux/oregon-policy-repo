# Capital and non-capital assets-policy (OAM 10.50.00.po)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** OAM chapter "10 - Internal control", document 10.50.00.po: Capital and non-capital assets-policy.
Effective 2001-07-01 per the OAM listing of record.

> **[VERBATIM]** "It shall be the policy of the State to hold in State ownership no more real property than is necessary to conduct official business, with allowance for reasonably foreseeable demands of the future. The acquisition, sale, exchange, lease, retention, and management of State-owned real property shall be subject to a statewide plan. The plan will encourage the transfer through sale or lease of property already in State ownership to private ownership…"
