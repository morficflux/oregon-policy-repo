# Sick Leave Balances at Retirement (OAM 45.06.00)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** OAM chapter "Payroll", document 45.06.00, issued by DAS State Controller's Division.
Effective 2007-03-01.

> **[VERBATIM]** "When a state employee retires, the employing agency shall report all accumulated unused sick leave to the Public Employees Retirement System (PERS). Thereafter, the unused sick leave is no longer available to a retiree who is reemployed or rehired, in any appointment or position, by the State."
