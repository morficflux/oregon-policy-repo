# Regular Pay Days (OAM 45.05.00)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** OAM chapter "Payroll", document 45.05.00, issued by DAS State Controller's Division.
Effective 2007-03-01.

> **[VERBATIM]** "Payrolls will be processed by Oregon Statewide Payroll Services (OSPS) to ensure compliance with ORS 652.120, that no more than 35 days elapse between paydays. Paydays are the first and fifteenth day of each calendar month."
