# Dual Update Access to PPDB and OSPA (OAM 45.30.00)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** OAM chapter "Payroll", document 45.30.00, issued by DAS State Controller's Division.
Effective 2007-06-29.

> **[VERBATIM]** "As a matter of internal control, no individual will be authorized to have general update authority for employee records in both the Position and Personnel Data Base (PPDB) system and the Oregon State Payroll Application (OSPA)."
