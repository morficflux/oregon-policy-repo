# Employee assigned property-policy (OAM 10.55.00)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** OAM chapter "10 - Internal control", document 10.55.00: Employee assigned property-policy.
Effective 2002-02-02 per the OAM listing of record.

> **[VERBATIM]** "This policy provides guidance for maintaining records of State property assigned to state employees, contractors, or volunteers. Policy Standards .102 Agency management is responsible for establishing procedures to issue and inventory property assigned to employees. State-owned property that may be assigned to state employees includes but is not limited to:  Cell phones  Pagers  Palm pilots  Keys and key cards  Hand tools  Laptop computers…"
