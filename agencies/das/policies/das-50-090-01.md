# Managing Improper Governmental Conduct (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.090.01)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Encourages reporting of known or suspected improper governmental conduct
and describes how the state manages such reports (Oregon's whistleblower-protection
implementation, ORS 659A.200–224). Effective 01/01/2025 (supersedes the 2023 version);
owned by the CHRO Policy Unit.

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Oregon state government is committed to providing employees and
> volunteers with an environment that encourages and enables reporting of known or
> suspected improper governmental conduct. This policy addresses reporting such conduct
> and how state government manages reports."

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All employees, including temporary employees, and volunteers where not
> in conflict with an applicable bargaining agreement."

**[SUMMARY]** Defines "abuse of authority" and related terms. (Source: Definitions
section.)
