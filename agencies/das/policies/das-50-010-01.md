# Discrimination and Harassment Free Workplace (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.010.01)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Commits Oregon state government to a discrimination- and harassment-free
work environment, and sets out prohibited conduct and reporting/investigation procedures.
Effective 03/03/2025 (supersedes the 2022 version); the longest of the HR policies in
this batch (8 pages). Owned by the CHRO Policy Unit.

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Oregon state government as an employer is committed to a discrimination
> and harassment free work environment. This policy outlines types of prohibited conduct
> and procedures for reporting and investigating prohibited conduct."

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All employees, including limited duration and temporary employees,
> board and commission members, elected officials, volunteers, interns, others working in
> an agency and prospective employees unless this policy conflicts with an applicable
> coll…" *(elision: source line wraps mid-word "collective bargaining agreement")*
