# Working Remotely (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.050.01)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Encourages remote work where viable, defining central and alternate
workplace concepts. Effective 3/21/2025 (supersedes the 2024 version); owned by the CHRO
Policy Unit. Cross-references [DAS 107-011-100](das-107-011-100.md) (Space Design and
Utilization), which in turn cites this policy for hybrid-work planning.

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Oregon state government encourages working remotely where it is a
> viable option that benefits both the employee and the agency."

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All employees where not in conflict with an applicable collective
> bargaining agreement."

**[SUMMARY]** Defines "Central Workplace" (the agency-assigned position location) and
"Alternate Workplace" concepts. (Source: Definitions section.)
