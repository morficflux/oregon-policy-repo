# ADA and Reasonable Accommodation in Employment (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.020.10)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Implements the ADA's employment mandate for Oregon state government:
removing barriers for qualified people with disabilities and providing equal employment
opportunity. Effective 3/21/2025 (supersedes the 2019 version); owned by the CHRO Policy
Unit. This is the employment-side counterpart to
[DAS 107-011-150](das-107-011-150.md) (Service Animals, a facilities/Title II–III policy
that explicitly defers employee accommodation requests to this document).

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Oregon state government follows the clear mandate in state law and the
> Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) of 1990, as amended by the ADA Amendments Act of
> 2008, to remove barriers that prevent qualified people with disabilities from enjoying
> the same employment opportunities that are available to people without disabilities.
> Oregon state government provides equal access and equal opportunity in employment. Its
> agencies do not discriminate based on disability."
