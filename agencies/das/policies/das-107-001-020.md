# Public Records Management (DAS Statewide Policy 107-001-020)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Sets minimum standards for agency public-records management policies and
provides a Model Public Records Management Policy. Effective June 1, 2025 (supersedes the
2019 version); approved by DAS Director Berri Leslie; 12 pages.

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "This policy supports statewide compliance with ORS 192.018 by
> establishing minimum standards for state agency policies relating to the internal
> management of public records and providing a Model Public Records Management Policy
> (model policy) for state agency reference or adoption."

**[SUMMARY]** Detailed requirements (agency policy content, retention, custodianship,
model policy text) are in the source PDF's later pages; consult the source for the model
policy itself.
