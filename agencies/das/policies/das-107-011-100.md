# Space Design and Utilization (DAS Statewide Policy 107-011-100)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Sets size and design guidelines for Oregon state government's owned and
leased office facilities, prioritizing business needs and flexible workspace design.
Effective May 1, 2024 (supersedes the 2019 version); approved by DAS Director Berri
Leslie; owned by Enterprise Asset Management / Administration.

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "This policy sets size and design guidelines for managing and operating
> office spaces in Oregon state government's owned and leased facilities. It prioritizes
> business needs and offers flexibility for developing workspaces that support right-sized
> office spaces for all types of administrative work."

**[SUMMARY]** A companion resource, "Workspaces Reimagined for Oregon's Hybrid
Workforce," offers technical and qualitative planning documents. (Source: Purpose
section.)
