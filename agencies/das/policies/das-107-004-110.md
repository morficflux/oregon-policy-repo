# Acceptable Use of State Information Assets (DAS Statewide Policy 107-004-110)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** The statewide acceptable-use policy for state information assets, binding
every authorized user of state systems. Also serves as cited authority for Policy
107-004-140 (Privileged Access). Effective 01-01-2010. An older-format policy; its
applicability clause still cites OAR 125-800-0020 (renumbered to 128-030-0020 effective
5/1/2026).

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "The purpose of this policy is to inform authorized users of state agency
> information … assets of the appropriate and acceptable use of information, computer
> systems and devices." *(elision: the source's two-column layout interleaves the
> "PURPOSE:" margin label at the ellipsis)*

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All individuals who have been granted access to state agency
> information-related technology or systems, including but not limited to, “User” as
> defined in the Definitions section below."

**[SUMMARY]** Applies to all Executive Branch agencies per ORS 174.112, with exceptions
per ORS 182.122/182.124 and OAR 125-800-0020(3)(a)–(b) and (4) for higher education, the
State Lottery, Secretary of State, State Treasurer, and Attorney General; other agencies
may opt in. (Source: Applicability section.)
