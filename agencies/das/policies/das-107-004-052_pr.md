# Cyber and Information Security Procedure (DAS 107-004-052_PR)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** The companion procedure to Policy 107-004-052: the concrete EIS and agency
actions for complying with the policy — CSS assessments (schedule, findings and
recommendations, plans of action and milestones) and the offshore/foreign-services
exception request process. New document, effective February 17, 2026, same date as the
policy revision.

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "This procedure describes Enterprise Information Services (EIS) and
> agency responsibilities and actions that must be followed to comply with Statewide
> Policy 107-004-052, Cyber and Information Security."

### Applicability & definitions

> **[VERBATIM]** "Refer to Statewide Policy 107-004-052, Cyber and Information Security,
> for applicability of this procedure."

### Exhibits

**[SUMMARY]** CSS Assessment Schedule; Attachment A: CSS assessment finding and
recommendations template; Attachment B: Plan of Action and Milestones template;
Attachment C: Request to use offshore or foreign services. (Source: Exhibits section.)
