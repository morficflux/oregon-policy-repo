# Data Governance (DAS Statewide Policy 107-004-160)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Establishes the statewide data-governance approach: a baseline framework
and accountability structure agencies use to build internal data governance programs.
New policy effective February 9, 2022; approved by Terrence Woods, State CIO; owned by
EIS Data Governance and Transparency (which also owns 107-004-050, Information Asset
Classification).

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "Data and information are strategic assets of the state and must be
> actively governed in order to preserve and enhance their value. This policy sets forth a
> statewide approach to data governance and establishes a baseline framework and
> accountability structure for agencies to use in establishing internal data governance
> programs."

### Applicability

**[SUMMARY]** All state agencies as defined in ORS 276A.230 (Executive Branch boards,
commissions, departments, divisions, offices), with the standard exclusions (Secretary of
State, State Treasurer, Attorney General as to ORS 276A.303, etc.). (Source:
Applicability section.)
