# Information Technology Asset Inventory & Management (DAS Statewide Policy 107-004-010)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Requires agencies to run IT Asset Management programs and report IT asset
information to DAS. Policy 107-004-052 points here for the asset-inventory element of
agency security programs (note: 107-004-052 cites it as "Statewide Policy 107-004-010,
Information Technology Asset Inventory and Management"). Effective 09-08-08; approved by
DAS Director Scott Harra. An older-format policy (pre-2020 template).

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "This policy establishes standard methods and timeframes that state
> agencies will use … to manage, collect, and report their information technology (IT)
> asset information. Agencies will establish IT Asset Management programs and procedures
> to track the acquisition, deployment, management, and disposition of all IT assets under
> their control." *(elision: the source's two-column layout interleaves the "PURPOSE:"
> margin label at the ellipsis)*

**[SUMMARY]** Agencies also report planned IT investments to the DAS Enterprise
Information Strategy and Policy Division (EISPD). Attachments include the statewide IT
asset inventory submission schedule, glossary, mandatory attributes table, and exception
process. (Source: Purpose and Attachments sections.)

### Applicability

**[SUMMARY]** Every Oregon Executive Branch agency, board, or commission paid wholly or
partly from State Treasury funds, except the Public Defense Services Commission, higher
education, Secretary of State, State Treasurer, and State Lottery. (Source: Applicability
section.)
