# Capitol Mall Area Parks and Grounds, Use of (DAS Statewide Policy 107-011-310)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Regulates use of the Capitol Mall area parks and grounds owned or
controlled by DAS. Effective June 20, 2001 (one of the oldest policies in this repo's
corpus; supersedes a 1998-era "125-6-" numbered policy); approved by C. David White,
Administrator, Facilities.

## Key provisions

### Purpose & applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "To specify policies and procedures to regulate the use of the Capitol
> Mall area parks and grounds which are owned or controlled by the Department."

**[SUMMARY]** Forms referenced: Event Agreement form, Premise Permit form (available from
the Enterprise Asset Management website). (Source: Purpose/Forms sections.)
