# Violence-Free Workplace (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.010.02)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Commits Oregon state government to a violence-free work environment.
Effective 11/1/2024 (supersedes the 2019 version); owned by the CHRO Policy Unit.

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Oregon state government is committed to a violence-free work
> environment."

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All employees, including temporary employees, and others working in the
> agency, where not in conflict with an applicable collective bargaining agreement."
