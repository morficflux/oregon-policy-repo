# Sustainable Acquisition and Disposal of Electronic Equipment (DAS 107-011-050_PR)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** The disposal-of-electronics procedure cross-referenced by
[DAS 107-004-051](das-107-004-051.md) (Controlling Portable and Removable Storage
Devices) for disposal of electronic storage media. Adopts EPEAT sustainability standards
per Executive Order 06-02. Effective March 9, 2017 (supersedes 2012's Policy
#107-009-0050); owned by Surplus Property.

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "This procedure directs state agencies in acquiring and disposing of
> electronic equipment; it supports the Governor's Executive Order 06-02, Sustainability
> for the 21st Century, and adopts the Electronic Products and Acquisition Technology
> (EPEAT) standards."

**[SUMMARY]** Procedure objectives: prevent hazardous materials from entering the waste
stream; consider end-of-life cost in acquisition decisions; maximize re-use of universal
waste and materials. (Source: Purpose section.)

## Authority & references

- Rests on: Executive Order 06-02; ORS 279, 279A.075, 279A.250–279A.280; OAR 125-050,
  125-246-0800; EPEAT criteria (per the procedure's REFERENCE/AUTHORITY header)
- Related: [DAS 107-004-051](das-107-004-051.md) — cited there as the disposal companion
  for removable storage devices
