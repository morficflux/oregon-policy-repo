# Privileged Access to Information Systems (DAS Statewide Policy 107-004-140)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Governs granting and use of privileged (administrator-level) access to
State Data Center information systems. Unusual among the 107-004 series in resting partly
on another policy (107-004-110, Acceptable Use) as cited authority. Effective July 10,
2013; approved by DAS Director Michael Jordan.

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "This policy establishes the process and expectations for granting and
> using privileged … access to the information systems of the State Data Center (SDC).
> Privileged access … enables users to take actions that may affect computing systems,
> network communication, or the accounts, files, data, or processes of other users."
> *(elisions: the source's two-column layout interleaves the "PURPOSE:" margin label at
> the ellipses)*

**[SUMMARY]** Privileged access is typically granted to system/network administrators,
account-administration staff, and others whose duties require special privileges.
Attachment A is the Privileged Access Request form. (Source: Purpose and Attachments
sections.)

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "This policy applies to all SDC users and contractors, and staff and
> contractors of customer-agencies who require, request, and acquire privileged access to
> the SDC's information systems."
