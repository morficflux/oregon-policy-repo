# Service Animals (DAS Statewide Policy 107-011-150)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Guides how "service animal" applies under Titles II and III of the ADA in
facilities owned, operated, or leased by DAS. Effective May 20, 2025 (supersedes the 2018
version); owned by Operations and Maintenance. Explicitly does not cover Title I (employee
workplace accommodation requests, which fall under HR policy 50-020-10).

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "To provide guidance on the term “service animal” as it applies to Titles
> II and III of the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and where service animals are
> allowed in facilities owned, operated or leased by the Department of Administrative
> Services (DAS). This policy does not apply to Title I of the ADA. Employee requests to
> use a service animal in the workplace fall under the State HR Policy 50-020-10, ADA and
> Reasonable Accommodation in Employment."
