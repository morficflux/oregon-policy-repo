# Employee Health and Wellness (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.010.06)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Commits Oregon state government to promoting employee health and wellness.
Effective 01/01/2025 (supersedes the 2019 version); owned by the CHRO Policy Unit.

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Oregon state government is committed to promoting and supporting health
> and wellness of all state employees. State employees are state government's most
> valuable resource. Healthy, empowered and engaged employees are integral to the
> effective delivery of state services."

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All employees, including limited duration and temporary employees,
> board and commission members, volunteers, and others working in an agency, where not in
