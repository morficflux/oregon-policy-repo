# Weapons in the Workplace (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.010.05)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Prohibits weapons in the state workplace and sets reporting procedures.
Effective 3/21/2025 (supersedes the 2019 version; note the source's own
`50-010-05.Prior.pdf` is the superseded copy — never ingest that as current). Owned by
the CHRO Policy Unit. Cross-references OAR 125-155-0510(1)(g), a state-vehicle-use rule.

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Oregon state government is committed to providing a safe and secure
> environment for employees and visitors. This policy outlines prohibited conduct and
> procedures for reporting a violation of this policy."

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All employees, including limited duration and temporary employees,
> board and commission members, volunteers, and others working in an agency, unless this
> policy conflicts with an agency's collective bargaining agreement."

**[SUMMARY]** Includes a Risk Management Active Shooter reference attachment. (Source:
Attachments section.)
