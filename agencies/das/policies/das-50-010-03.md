# Maintaining a Professional Workplace (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.010.03)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Sets expectations for mutual respect and professional conduct among
managers, employees, and volunteers. Effective 03/03/2025 (supersedes the 2019 version);
owned by the CHRO Policy Unit.

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Mutual respect between and among managers, employees and volunteers is
> integral to the efficient conduct of business within Oregon state government. All
> individuals work together to create and maintain a work environment that is respectful,
> professional and free from inappropriate workplace behavior."

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All employees, including temporary employees and volunteers, and others
> working in the agency."

**[SUMMARY]** Includes a Complaint Form attachment (hosted at
oregon.gov/das/HR/Documents/). (Source: Attachments section.)
