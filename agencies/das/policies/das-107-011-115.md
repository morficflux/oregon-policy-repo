# Siting State-owned and Leased Office Space (DAS Statewide Policy 107-011-115)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Sets criteria for siting state-owned and leased office space, implementing
several executive orders in cooperation with the Department of Land Conservation and
Development and local municipalities. Effective June 10, 2025 (supersedes a 1998 policy
numbered under the old "125-6-" scheme); owned by Real Estate Services. Referenced by
[DAS 107-011-100](das-107-011-100.md) as its companion siting policy.

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "To set forth criteria for siting state owned and leased office space.
> This policy establishes criteria for the implementation of Executive Orders listed above
> in cooperation with the Department of Land Conservation and Development and local
> municipalities. It is intended to encourage and promote the development and
> re- development of central business districts, downtowns and other central areas in
> support of economic prosperity and revitalization throughout the state of Oregon"
