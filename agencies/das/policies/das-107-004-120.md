# Cyber and Information Security Incident Response (DAS Statewide Policy 107-004-120)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** The statewide incident-response policy, operationalizing ORS 276A.306
(information security incidents and assessments). Effective November 16, 2020
(supersedes the 2008 version); approved by Terrence Woods, State CIO. Policy 107-004-052
requires agencies to establish incident identification and reporting processes as
outlined in the Information Security Incident Response Plan.

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "This policy defines Oregon state government's approach to cyber and
> information security incident response. Effective incident response helps protect the
> availability, integrity and confidentiality of state information assets."

### Applicability

**[SUMMARY]** All state agencies as defined in ORS 276A.230 (Executive Branch boards,
commissions, departments, divisions, offices); excludes Secretary of State, State
Treasurer, the Attorney General (as to ORS 276A.303 DOJ authority), State Lottery, and
higher education. (Source: Applicability section.)
