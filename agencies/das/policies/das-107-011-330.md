# Vending Services (DAS Statewide Policy 107-011-330)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Governs vending services and food carts in state buildings and grounds
owned, leased, or controlled by DAS, implementing the Randolph-Sheppard Vending Stand Act
via the Oregon Commission for the Blind's Business Enterprise Program. Effective
6/10/2025 (supersedes a 1998 version); owned by Operations and Maintenance.

## Key provisions

### Purpose

> **[VERBATIM]** "To provide clear policies relating to vending services and food carts in
> state buildings and grounds which are owned, leased or controlled by the Department of
> Administrative Services (DAS), according to ORS 346.510 to 346.570 and OAR 125-080-0000."

**[SUMMARY]** The Business Enterprise Program (BEP), administered by the Oregon
Commission for the Blind under the Randolph-Sheppard Vending Stand Act, covers federal
properties and is given priority under this Oregon law for state facilities. (Source:
Purpose section.)
