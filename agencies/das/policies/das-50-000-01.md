# Drug Free Workplace (DAS Statewide HR Policy 50.000.01)

## At a glance

**[SUMMARY]** Commits Oregon state government to a drug-free workplace. Effective
11/01/2024 (supersedes the 2020 version); owned by the Chief Human Resources Office (CHRO)
Policy Unit.

## Key provisions

### Policy statement

> **[VERBATIM]** "Oregon state government is committed to a drug-free workplace that
> encourages a safe, healthy and productive work environment."

### Applicability

> **[VERBATIM]** "All employees, including temporary employees, and others working in the
> agency, where not in conflict with an applicable collective bargaining agreement."

**[SUMMARY]** Includes an FAQ attachment and definitions (e.g., "controlled substance"
per Section 202 of the Controlled Substances Act). (Source: Attachments/Definitions
sections.)
