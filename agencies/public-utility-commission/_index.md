# Public Utility Commission (`public-utility-commission`) — index

Knowledge bodies for Public Utility Commission. **Non-authoritative copies.**

- [Policies](policies/_index.md)

PUC regulates investor-owned utilities and certain telecom/water/pipeline services under
ORS chapters 756–759. It publishes a single governance document — its Internal Operating
Guidelines — describing how open meetings, rulemakings, and contested cases are run;
no numbered policy series.
