# Oregon Military Department (`oregon-military-department`) — index

Knowledge bodies for Oregon Military Department. **Non-authoritative copies.**

- [Policies](policies/_index.md)

OMD administers the Oregon National Guard and state emergency-management functions under
ORS chapter 396 (and OAR chapter 125 for state-workforce/vehicle rules it shares with DAS).
Its "Employee Resources > Policies" page mostly re-links DAS statewide HR policies and bare
forms rather than publishing its own body; its genuinely agency-authored policies carry a
distinct "AGP" (Adjutant General Personnel) numbering.
